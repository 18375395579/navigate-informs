Title: Resource Management in Web Hosting Centers for Energy Conservation

Author: Natarajan Gautam, Associate Professor, Texas A&M University, 235A Zachry, Mailstop 3131, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, gautam@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yiyu Chen, Cesar Rincon Mateus

Year: 2007

Abstract: Web hosting services and data centers consume a phenomenal amount of electric power to run farms of servers. In this talk we present effective resource management strategies to reduce this energy consumption. We take advantage of the fact that user requests vary tremendously both spatially and temporally. Using a statistical model to predict user-request patterns and a queueing model for performance measures, we formulate and solve an optimization problem for resource management.
