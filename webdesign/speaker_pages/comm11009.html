Title: Assortment Planning in Retail Chains

Author: Narendra Agarwal, Operations & Management Information Systems Santa Clara University, Santa Clara, CA, United States of America, NAgrawal@scu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Many of the recent paper on assortment planning fail to capture an important phenomenon ?category effect of key items. We formulate a model to capture category effects of items in addition to substitution, and develop an LP based approximation methodology to solve it. We use a numerical case study to illustrate key insights about assortment planning.
