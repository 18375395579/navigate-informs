Title: Reliability Analysis and Modeling of the U.S. Marine Corps Medium Tactical Wheeled Vehicle in OIF

Author: Matt Reuter, Head, Logistics Operations Analysis Branch, USMC / Installations and Logistics Department, HQMC, 2 Navy Annex, Washington, DC, 20380-1775, United States of America, matthew.reuter@usmc.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: An analysis of the reliability of the Medium Tactical Vehicle Replacement (MTVR) in Operation Iraqi Freedom. Analysis and modeling of this vehicle's failure modes are conducted to understand the vehicle's usage and performance. Nonparametric and parametric statistical methods are utilized, with failure mode recurrence data, to measure reliability.
