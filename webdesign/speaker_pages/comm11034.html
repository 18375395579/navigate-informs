Title: Models and Algorithms for Effective Emergency Supply Planning
Author: Zhihong Shen, University of Southern California, 3715 McClintock Avenue, GER-240,, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, shenz@usc.edu
Coauthor(s): Fernando Ordonez, Maged Dessouky
Year: 2007
Abstract: A key ingredient in an effective response to an emergency is the prompt availability of the necessary supplies at the emergency sites. Many unique features require the creation of new models of planning in order to develop a rational deployment strategy. We model the disbursement of medical supplies in an emergency circumstance into a facility location and routing problems. Simulation results show the benefits of this formulation based on LA county data.
