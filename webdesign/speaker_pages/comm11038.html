Title: Horizontal Cooperation in Vehicle Routing Problems with Environmental Criteria

Author: Javier Faulin, Full Professor, Public University of Navarra, Los Magnolios Blg. First floor, Pamplona, 31006, Spain, javier.faulin@unavarra.es

Coauthor(s): Alejandro García del Valle, Angel Juan, Scott Grasman

Year: 2013

Abstract: This contribution presents horizontal cooperation in road transportation and supports this praxis as a way of reducing distribution costs and CO2 emissions. The methodology is based on a set of well-known benchmarks for the multi-depot VRP. We analyze environmental impacts in collaborative and non-collaborative scenarios making comparisons between costs in road transportation with and without those environmental costs. Finally, this methodology is illustrated with the results of a case study.
