Title: On Finding Compact Tree-Based Representations from Observed Dynamic Data
Author: Michel Minoux, Professor, University of Paris, 4 Place Jussieu, Paris Cedex 05, F-75252, France, michel.minoux@lip6.fr
Year: 2006
Abstract: Many dynamic optimization problems require models of time-dependent data (such as temperature, prices on markets, etc.) based on past observations or on anticipated 'scenarios'. We discuss 0-1 integer formulations of the problem of inferring, from given lists of scenarios, compact tree-based models representing such dynamic processes.
