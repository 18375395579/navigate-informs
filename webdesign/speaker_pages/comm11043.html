Title: P-MATCH and QUBIT: Methods to Extract Critical Information in Free Text for System Health Management

Author: Anne Kao, Technical Fellow, Boeing, P.O. Box 3707, MC 7L-43, Seattle, WA, 98124, United States of America, anne.kao@boeing.com

Coauthor(s): Stephen Poteet, David Augustine

Year: 2012

Abstract: In data sources for system health management, free text data often provides critical information. Analysts need to extract detailed information with high recall from very noisy text data. We will use airplane health management to illustrate how to extract critical information from free text data and better support decision making in system health management. Our methods use text analytics and visual interactive techniques to leverage analysts?knowledge to improve system health management.
