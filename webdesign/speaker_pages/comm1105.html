Title: Data Reconstruction for Identity Disclosure Protection
Author: Xiaobai (Bob) Li, College of Management, University of Massachusetts Lowell, One University Ave., Lowell, MA, 01854, United States, xiaobai_li@uml.edu
Coauthor(s): Dan Zhu
Year: 2007
Abstract: We propose a data reconstruction approach for privacy protection in data mining. The proposed approach uses a set of techniques, including aggregation, swapping, and instance selection via genetic algorithms, to reconstruct data to achieve k-anonymity protection in predictive data mining.
