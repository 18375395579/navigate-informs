Title: Determining OSPF Routing Policies in the Presence of Active Queue Management
Author: Jiaxin Liu, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, Canada, j69liu@uwaterloo.ca
Coauthor(s): Stanko Dimitrov, Laura Sanita
Year: 2013
Abstract: We propose a mathematical model to derive OSPF routing policies in the presence of router active queue management. We discuss the computational complexity of the proposed general model. A modified model is proposed to ensure edge weights are realizable and feasible. We conclude by showing the derived routing policies tend to perform better than those used in a real world network.
