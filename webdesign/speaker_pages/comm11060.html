Title: Strategic Modeling to Support Investment Decision-making in New Technology
Author: Joe Santoro, Dow Chemical, 31 Neudorfstrasse, Horgen, 8810, Switzerland, GSANTORO@dow.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: A model of the Biodiesel industry is used to understand the future development of the glycerine industry. Insights from the simulation are used to develop long term strategy to support a multi million dollar investment in new technology and a new chemical plant.
