Title: Mathematical Model and Agent-based Model: A Comparative Study on the Performance of Sepsis

Author: Chih-Hang(John) Wu, Associate Professor, Kansas State University, 2037 Durland Hall, Manhattan, KS, 66506, United States of America, chw@ksu.edu

Coauthor(s): Zhenzhen Shi, David Ben-Arieh

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a system dynamics mathematical model and multi-agent-based model (MABM), which model and simulate the innate immunity and adaptive immunity of sepsis, especially getting insights into the time and intensity of intervention of anti-inflammatory cytokines in sepsis evolution. By comparing the results from both models, we conclude that MABM is more realistic approach to simulate sepsis progression.
