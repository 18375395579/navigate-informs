Title: Identifying and Visualizing Nonlinear Variation Patterns in Multivariate Manufacturing Data

Author: Feng Zhang, Fairchild Semiconductor, 333 Western Avenue, MS 10-29, South Portland, ME, 04106, United States of America, feng.zhang@fairchildsemi.com

Coauthor(s): Dan Apley

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose a model for representing nonlinear variation patterns and a method for blindly identifying the patterns, based on a sample of multivariate measurement data, with no prior knowledge of the nature of the patterns. The identification method is based on principal curve estimation, in conjunction with a suitable data preprocessing step for high dimensional data. Experimental results show that interactive visualization of identified variation patterns is effective in diagnosing the root causes that contribute to process variability.
