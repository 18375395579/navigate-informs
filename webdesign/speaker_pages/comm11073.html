Title: Engineering Driven Force Model Enhancement for the Laser Assisted Micro Milling (LAMM) Process

Author: Chia-Jung Chang, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, Main Building, Office 217, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, cchang43@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Mukund Kumar, Roshan Vengazhiyil, Shreyes N. Melkote

Year: 2012

Abstract: LAMM is capable of generating three dimensional microscale features in hard metals. An engineering-driven force model that captures the effect of thermal softening due to laser heating has been developed. We proposed a systematic way to estimate unknown model parameters with experimental force measurements. This model provides accurate force predictions over a range of production conditions in a timely manner which facilitates the LAMM process optimization to yield the maximal force reduction.
