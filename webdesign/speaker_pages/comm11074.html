Title: Resource Allocation to a Portfolio of Modernization Technologies for the National Airspace System
Author: Senay Solak, School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, ssolak@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Paul Collopy, Ellis Johnson, John-Paul Clarke
Year: 2006
Abstract: We develop common performance assessment measures and analyze correlation levels for a portfolio of National Airspace System modernization technologies. We then propose a portfolio optimization model to determine the optimal allocation of available resources to the technologies in consideration. In addition, the computational difficulty of the proposed model is analyzed, and appropriate solution methods are described.
