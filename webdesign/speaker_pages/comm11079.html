Title: Organ Preservation and Viability Evaluation in Transplant

Author: Ran Jin, Virginia Tech, Grado Dept of Industrial and Systems, Engineering, 111 Durham Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, jran5@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Tingting Huang, John Robertson

Year: 2013

Abstract: Organ transplant helps many critically-ill patients regain control of their lives. However, fewer than 20% of patients who need a transplant actually receive one. Many potentially useful organs are discarded because of limited means to evaluate the viability and potential long-term function of organs. In this research, we develop a viability evaluation index based on biopsy image data. We validate the organ viability index based on the study of isolated, perfused pig livers.
