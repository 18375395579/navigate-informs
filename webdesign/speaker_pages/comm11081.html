Title: EPA’s Application of a Detailed Power Sector Model for Analysis of GHG Cap and Trade Proposals

Author: Misha Adamantiades, Senior Analyst, US Environmental Protection Agency, 1200 Pennsylvania Avenue, 6204J, Washington, DC, 20460, United States of America, Adamantiades.Mikhail@epa.gov

Year: 2008

Abstract: This session will focus on U.S. EPA’s application of the Integrated Planning Model, a detailed LP model of the U.S. power sector, in recently completed analyses of legislative proposals to reduce domestic emissions of GHGs. The session will highlight key modeling results, model assumptions, approach, and impacts of the analyses.
