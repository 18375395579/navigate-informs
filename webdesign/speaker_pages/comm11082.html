Title: Information Quality and Customer Acquisition: A Risk Management Approach for Private Label Cards
Author: Pantea Alirezazadeh, PhD Student, University of Connecticut, 205C, crawford, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, Pantea.Alirezazadeh@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Fidan Boylu, Ram Gopal, Paulo Goes
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this paper we analyze risks from information deficiencies in credit application and ensuing financial implications. We first employ the use of record matching software and design optimal configuration to minimize financial costs. Next we explore the option of procuring additional information to improve accuracy of record matches. We develop models to decide conditions under which it is optimal to exercise the option of information procurement.
