Title: Energy Storage Systems, Stochastic Optimization and Robust Control

Author: Alberto Lamadrid, Assistant Professor, Lehigh University, 621 Taylor Street, room 451, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, ajlamadrid@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Tim Mount, Ray Zimmerman, Carlos Murillo-Sanchez

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper formulates an energy storage problem, in the context of high penetration of renewable energy sources (RES), as used by System Operators in their daily operations. Our suggested model has an electricity network system planner seeking to maximize the welfare of consumers and producers, procuring energy and ancillary services using a security constrained Optimal Power Flow (SC-OPF) and reproducing the uncertainty in the system by using a Markovian transition probability matrix.
