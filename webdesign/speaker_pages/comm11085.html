Title: Discrete-time Dynamic Traffic Assignment Models for Managed Lanes

Author: Qipeng (Phil) Zheng, Ph.D. Student, University of Florida, Industrial & Systems Engineering, 303 Weil Hall, ISE, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, zqp@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Siriphong Lawphongpanich, Yafeng Yin

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk, we present discrete-time traffic assignment models for managed lanes such as HOV and HOT lanes. Unlike discrete-time models for general networks, our models have a simple network structure and their upper bounds can be obtained by solving mixed-integer optimization problems using, e.g., Benders decomposition. Also, numerical results will be presented.
