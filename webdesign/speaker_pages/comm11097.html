Title: Integrated Topology Control and Routing in Wireless Sensor Network Design
Author: Hui Lin, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, College Station, TX, linhui@tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Halit Uster
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present models employing alternative objectives for integrated topology control and routing in wireless sensor networks. The models attempt to promote energy efficiency with the purpose of prolonging network lifetime. We develop efficient heuristics reinforced with exact methods and cut generations. We provide computational results demonstrating that employing combined objectives based on energy usage and remaining energy characteristics helps to significantly extend the network lifetime.
