Title: Non-linear Modeling of Customer Satisfactions

Author: Nobuhiko Terui, Professor, Tohoku University, Kawauchi, Aoba-ku, Sendai, Japan, terui@econ.tohoku.ac.jp

Coauthor(s): Taemyung Chun, Kosuke Ogawa, Satoshi Shibata

Year: 2013

Abstract: Focusing on nonlinear relationship between customer satisfaction and customer loyalty which has been discussed in the literature, we propose non-linear modeling of customer satisfaction index(CSI). We empirically show that nonlinear model is better than linear model, and non-linear patterns are different across industries according to the competitive environment in a market and consumer's degree of involvement.
