Title: Internet Routing Under Active Congestion Control
Author: Stanko Dimitrov, University of Michigan - Ann Arbor, 2812 IOE Building, 1205 Beal Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109-2117, United States, sdimitro@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Marina A. Epelman, Dushyant Sharma
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present a mathematical model of Internet routing that incorporates congestion control techniques such as random early detection (RED). We show that several model instances are NP-hard and present numerical results comparing current routing policies to the routing policies corresponding to the optimal solution of our model.
