Title: Mining Weather Influence on Transit Travel Behaviour

Author: Bruno Agard, Assistant Professor, École Polytechnique de Montréal, C.P. 6079, succ. Centre-ville, Montréal, QC, H3C 3A7, Canada, bruno.agard@polymtl.ca

Coauthor(s): Catherine Morency, Martin Trépanier

Year: 2006

Abstract: The authors propose to link information contained in Smart Card Automated Fare Collection Systems with meteorological data to see if weather influences the use of public transportation. To better understand these impacts, a 10-month period of city bus network data (6 millions boardings) is analyzed using various data mining techniques.
