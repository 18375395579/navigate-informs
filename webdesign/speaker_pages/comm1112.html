Title: Data Transformations for Terminal Airspace Queuing Models

Author: David Lovell, Associate Professor, University of Maryland, 1173 Martin Hall, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, lovell@eng.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrew Churchill, Michael Ball, Avijit Mukherjee, Amedeo Odoni

Year: 2007

Abstract: It is common to model terminal airspace as a queuing system in order to determine performance metrics such as delay. The input data for such models, when taken from the most common databases, are confounded with a large variety of other influences, and model performance typically suffers as a result. We show some techniques for filtering and remediating these influences, and the subsequent performance gains that can be expected.
