Title: Moneyball and the Mavs.

Author: Wayne Winston, Professor, Indiana University, 3453 Maritime Court, Bloomington, IN, 47401, United States, winston@indiana.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We will describe our player and lineup evaluation system that has been used for Dallas Mavericks for 6 seasons. Over that time period the Mavs won more regular season games than any other NBA team. We will pay particular attention to how the coaching staff uses our data.
