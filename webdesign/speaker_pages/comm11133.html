Title: A Statistical Analysis of Ambulance Travel Times
Author: Dawit Zerom, Associate Professor, California State University, Fullerton, Mihaylo College of Business,P.O.BOX 6848, Fullerton, CA, 92834, United States of America, dzerom@Exchange.FULLERTON.EDU
Coauthor(s): Susan Budge, Armann Ingolfsson
Year: 2008
Abstract: An important component of virtually any model of emergency service operations (an analytic model or optimization model) is the travel time of vehicles, especially of those en route to a call. In this paper, we present a semi-parametric statistical model for estimating travel times for emergency services using unique data from ambulance operations of the city of Calgary, Canada.
