Title: Equivalence of Two-parameter and Measure-valued Fluid Models for G_t/GI/N+GI Queues

Author: Weining Kang, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, 1000 Hilltop Circle, Baltimore, MD, 21250, United States of America, wkang@umbc.edu

Coauthor(s): Guodong Pang

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, we show the equivalence of two fluid models for G_t/GI/N+GI queues, the two-parameter fluid model by Whitt (2006) and the measure-valued fluid model by Kang and Ranaman (2010). We also explore connections between fluid models tracking residual times and fluid models tracking elapsed times.
