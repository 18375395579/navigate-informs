Title: Continuity Properties of Equilibria in Some Fisher and Arrow-Debreu Market Models
Author: Lei Wang, Georgia Tech, 500 Northside Circle, #ii2, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States of America, wanglei@math.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Vijay Vazirani
Year: 2008
Abstract: Following up on the work of Megiddo and Vazirani \cite{MV.2007}, who determined continuity properties of equilibrium prices and allocations for perhaps the simplest market model, Fisher's linear case, we do the same for: 1. Fisher's model with piecewise-linear, concave utilities 2. Fisher's model with spending constraint utilities 3. Arrow-Debreu's model with linear utilities 4. Arrow-Debreu's model with piecewise-linear, concave utilities.
