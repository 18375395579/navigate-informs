Title: A Distribution-Free Multivariate Phase I Location Control Chart
Author: Allison Jones-Farmer, Auburn University, 415 W. Magnolia Ave, Suite 420, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States of America, joneall@auburn.edu
Coauthor(s): Richard Bell, Nedret Billor
Year: 2012
Abstract: A proper Phase I analysis is essential to the success of Phase II monitoring. This research develops a Phase I location control chart for multivariate processes. The resulting in-control reference sample can then be used to estimate the parameters for Phase II monitoring. The proposed method is compared to the Hotelling's T2 Phase I chart. The proposed method is shown to perform significantly better under non-normality.
