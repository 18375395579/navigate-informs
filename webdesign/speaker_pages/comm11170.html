Title: Effective Use of Hospital Stroke Units

Author: Natalia Yankovic, Columbia University, Decision. Risk and Operations, Columbia Business School, Nerw York, NY, 10027, United States of America, ny2106@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Linda Green

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present models for evaluating the impact of alternative bed placement policies for hospital inpatients suffering from ischemic strokes. Specifically, we develop a tandem queuing model to study the impact of the criteria used to admit a patient to a stroke unit as well transfer policies for moving patients from the stroke unit to a neurological unit.
