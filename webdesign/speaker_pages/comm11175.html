Title: Determining Optimal Therapy for Diabetic Patients. Evidence from the U.S. Department of Veteran Affairs

Author: Vishal Ahuja, PhD Candidate, University of Chicago, Booth Business School, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States of America, vahuja@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): John Birge

Year: 2012

Abstract: We investigate various treatments for Type 2 Diabetes in adults using data from the Veterans Health Administration. We analyze dynamic patterns of glucose lowering therapies, and evaluate the risks of associated diabetes outcomes. We propose a predictive model to evaluate effectiveness of various glucose-lowering therapies and recommend optimal treatment based on patient characteristics. The paper addresses confounding measures such as physician bias and unobservable patient characteristics.
