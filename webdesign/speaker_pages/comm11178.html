Title: Optimizing the Mobility and Safety of Walk-and-Ride Systems

Author: Hugh Medal, Assistant Professor, Mississippi State University, 260 McCain Engineering Building, Mississippi State University, MS, 39762, United States of America, hugh.medal@msstate.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiaopeng Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the problem of jointly locating sidewalks and bus shelters in order to maximize both mobility and safety for walk-and-ride transit users. Our models, which include both hub location and network design decisions, investigate two objectives: travel time and safety. We employ Benders's decomposition and branch-and-price strategies along with a fast heuristic. A set of computational experiments, using data from Starkville, MS, investigate the robustness and tractability of the models.
