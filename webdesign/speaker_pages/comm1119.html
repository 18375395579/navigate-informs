Title: Return on Service Amenities
Author: Roland Rust, Distinguished University Professor, U. of Maryland, Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, rrust@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Rebecca Hamilton, Michel Wedel, Chekitan Dev
Year: 2013
Abstract: We build a model of how service amenities produce financial return. We apply the model in the context of hotel amenities, using a large-scale customer database provided by a major hotel chain, and employ hierarchical Bayesian estimation to estimate the model. The estimation results are then used to evaluate return on investment for two service amenities, for each of several hotel brands.
