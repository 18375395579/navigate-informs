Title: On Solving Large-Scale Multiple Criteria Evaluation Problems

Author: Pekka Korhonen, Professor, Helsinki School of Economics, Runeberginkatu 22-24, Helsinki, 00100, Finland, Pekka.Korhonen@hse.fi

Coauthor(s): Jyri Ruutu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We deal with multiple criteria evaluation problems having few criteria and a huge number (> 500 000) of alternatives. The approach, originally developed by Korhonen (1988) and further by Korhonen and Karaivanova (1999), is interactive and uses a reference direction as a tool for obtaining preference information from a decision maker. We present numerical results concerning the performance of the algorithm. In addition, we propose some ideas to further improve its performance.
