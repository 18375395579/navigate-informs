Title: Social TV Recommendation System
Author: Shawndra Hill, Assistant Professor, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19103, United States of America, shawndra@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Adrian Benton
Year: 2012
Abstract: The approach that we have developed introduces a novel use of social media text for product recommendations, in this case, television show recommendations. We compare our approach to commonly used recommendation systems and show how one might combine the approaches to outperform any alone.
