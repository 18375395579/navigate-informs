Title: Panel Econometrics for Experimental Data: Implications for Innovation and Entrepreneurship Research

Author: Anna Kostanovskaya, Ph.D Student, Humboldt-Universitaet zu Berlin, Spandauer Str. 1, Berlin, 10178, Germany, kostanovskaya@wiwi.hu-berlin.de

Year: 2007

Abstract: The paper develops the idea why one may want to use panel econometrics for the analysis of experiments in behavioural economics: randomisation problems due to the potential influence of unobserved variables and the subjects?heterogeneity can be handled. Two econometric analyses based on laboratory experiments dealing with R&D decisions are presented.
