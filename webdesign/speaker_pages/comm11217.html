Title: Multiscale Adaptive Basis Function Modeling of Spatiotemporal Signals

Author: Hui Yang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, huiyang@usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Gang Liu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Mathematical models facilitate the characterization and recognition of underlying biological patterns. However, there are practical issues pertinent to model efficacy, robustness, and generality. This paper presents a multiscale adaptive basis function approach to model space-time cardiac pathological behaviors, using the best matching projections of characteristic waves onto a dictionary of nonlinear basis functions. The model performance is experimentally evaluated with real-world signals.
