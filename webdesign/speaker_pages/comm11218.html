Title: Integrating Multicriteria Analysis and Geographic Information Systems: An Application of Spatial ANP

Author: Valentina Ferretti, PhD, Politecnico di Torino, Corso Castelfidardo 30/A, Torino, 10138, Italy, valentina.ferretti@polito.it

Coauthor(s): Giulio Mondini

Year: 2012

Abstract: Spatial Multicriteria Evaluations are rapidly gaining traction for planning and policy-making as useful tools to make decision processes effective and reliable. The contribution proposes a Spatial Multicriteria approach based on the integration of Geographic Information Systems and the Analytic Network Process for the development of a land suitability analysis in the context of undesirable facilities location problems. The integrated approach is able to cope with complex decision problems.
