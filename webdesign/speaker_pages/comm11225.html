Title: A Demand Distribution-free Analysis of Process Flexibility Design
Author: Tianhu Deng, University of California-Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, dthacyj@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Z. Max Shen
Year: 2012
Abstract: We discuss a distribution-free analysis of flexibility design problems. We investigate the worst-case performance among all demand distributions that have the given mean and variance. The analysis draws implications about the first and second order effects of plant capacity, demand mean and standard deviation.
