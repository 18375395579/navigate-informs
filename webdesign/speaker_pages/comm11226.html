Title: A Northwest and Delta Airlines Merger Analysis
Author: Dan Larsen, Student, University of Cincinnati, 707 W Martin Luther King Dr, #711E, Cincinnati, OH, 45220, United States of America, asel_dan@msn.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: This past April, Northwest and Delta Airlines have announced their intent to merge. Is keeping their seven existing hubs realistic? By utilizing open source software (namely, COIN-OR) I wish to evaluate an optimal network structure for the combined airline, given current fuel prices. The data source is public data provided by the DOT.
