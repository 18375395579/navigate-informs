Title: An Augmented Lagrangian Framework for Risk Parity Optimization

Author: Xi Bai, PhD Candidate, Lehigh University, 461 Montclair Ave., Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, xib210@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Katya Scheinberg

Year: 2013

Abstract: The risk-parity optimization problem aims to find such portfolio that the contributions of risk are equally weighted. In this talk, we form a general model for risk-parity portfolio construction and propose an augmented Lagrangian framework to solve it. Numerical experiments indicate the effectiveness of our technique in terms of both speed and accuracy.
