Title: Probabilistic Graphical Modeling based on L1 Penalized Likelihood for Cancer Gene Network

Author: Sung Won Han, Research Scientist, New York University, 650 First Avenue, New York, NY, United States of America, SungWon.Han@nyumc.org

Coauthor(s): Hua Zhong, Gong Chen, Anton Belousov, Laurent Essioux

Year: 2013

Abstract: Directed acyclic graphic model has been suggested to model the interactions among random variables where directed edges represent the causal influence of components of the system. This presentation discusses the estimation of the structure of the directed acyclic graph by the L1-penalized likelihood approach. We formulate the problem by a linear structure with latent variables under the assumption that the variances of latent variables are unequal and unknown, and a natural ordering is unknown.
