Title: Improving the Transfer Baggage Operation at American Airlines

Author: Tuell Green, American Airlines, 4333 Amon Carter Boulevard, MD 5358, Fort Worth, TX, 76155-2664, United States, tuell.green@aa.com

Coauthor(s): June Ma, Alejandro Scalise

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present an approach using simulation and optimization to analyze and improve the connecting baggage operation of American Airlines at DFW airport. We will include a description of the simulation model and some simulation results, an overview of the optimization approach and some sample test cases, and some observations regarding the transition from laboratory to daily operations.
