Title: Approximation Metaheuristics for Assortment Problems with Multiple Quality Levels
Author: Mark McElreath, Research Assistant, Clemson University, 110 Freeman Hall, Clemson University, Clemson, SC, 29634-0920, United States of America, mmcelre@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Maria Mayorga, Mary Beth Kurz
Year: 2008
Abstract: While research on assortment planning has yielded useful analytical results, it has also left open computational questions. In particular, when products are differentiated both horizontally and vertically the optimal solution has only been partially characterized. We investigate three metaheuristics (using a common representation) and evaluate performance over several problem instances.
