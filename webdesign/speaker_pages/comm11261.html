Title: Survival of the Fittest: Firm Evolution During the DotCom Era
Author: Jacquelyn Rees, Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, jrees@purdue.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Evolutionary models have been used to simulate various organizational processes, such as firm structure, markets, and decision processes. In particular, genetic algorithms, (GAs) have been used a both a model and a metaphor for change over time of such structures. In this research, we fit eCommerce firm data to a specific instance of a GA in order to examine the evolutionary course of firms over the so-called “DotCom?era.
