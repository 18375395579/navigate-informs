Title: Option Pricing with Artificial Neural Networks and Adaptive Neuro Fuzzy Inference Systems
Author: Nagendra Nagarur, Associate Professor, Binghamton University, SSIE Department, Binghamton University, Binghamton, NY, 13902, United States of America, nnagarur@binghamton.edu
Coauthor(s): Domnic Ojala
Year: 2008
Abstract: Two heuristic systems, Artificial Neural Networks and Adaptive Neuro Fuzzy Inference Systems, are developed to estimate option prices. The models are tested on S&P-500 Index options. The results are compared to those obtained by Black-Scholes method. A detailed error analysis is carried out comparing the relative efficiencies of all the three methods.
