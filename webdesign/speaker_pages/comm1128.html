Title: Evaluating the Impact of Uncertainty on the Value Chain Optimization of a Forest Biomass Power Plan

Author: Nazanin Shabani, University of British Columbia, Vancouver, BC, Canada, nazanin.shaebani@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Taraneh Sowlati

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper Monte Carlo simulation is used to assess the impact of uncertainties in biomass availability and cost, moisture content (MC), higher heating value (HHV) and electricity price on the profit of a biomass power plant. The deterministic optimization model considers biomass procurement, storage, electricity production and ash management. It is a mixed integer non-linear model with monthly time-steps over a one-year time horizon.
