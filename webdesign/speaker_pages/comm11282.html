Title: Limiting Extraimmunization in a Childhood Immunization Schedule

Author: Shane Hall, Assistant Professor of Operations Research, Air Force Institute of Technology, AFIT/ENS, Bldg 641, 2950 Hobson Way, WPAFB, OH, 45433, United States of America, SHANE.HALL@afit.edu

Coauthor(s): Edward Sewell, Sheldon H. Jacobson

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation discusses discrete optimization models for designing childhood immunization vaccine formularies that limit extraimmunization. Such models allow public health policy makers and pediatricians to evaluate the combinatorial explosion of vaccine choices spawned by the development of combination vaccines.
