Title: Accounting for Multivariate Parameter Uncertainty in Large-scale Simulations

Author: Canan Gunes, PhD Student, Tepper School of Business, 5000 Forbes Ave., Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, canalp.canan@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Bahar Biller

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a large-scale stochastic simulation with correlated inputs and develop a Bayesian framework that provides point estimates and confidence intervals for performance measures by accounting for stochastic and multivariate input uncertainties. We demonstrate the importance of the joint representation of stochastic and multivariate input uncertainties for multi-product inventory simulations.
