Title: A Variational Framework for Revenue Optimization and Demand Forecasting

Author: Alexander Kushkuley, Oracle, 10 Van De Graaff Drive, Burlington, MA, United States of America, alex.kushkuley@oracle.com

Coauthor(s): Su-Ming Wu, Maria Wang

Year: 2013

Abstract: The problem of retailer revenue optimization is usually solved in a three step cycle of model formulation, parameter estimation and subsequent revenue functional maximization. We investigate a general variational framework that is well suited for all three stages of this cycle. In particular, a bootstrap methodology that combines optimization with parameter estimation into one “optimize/re-estimate?procedure is evaluated on real data samples.
