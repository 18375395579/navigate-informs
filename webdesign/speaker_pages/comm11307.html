Title: Queuing Network Models for Cycle Times of Autonomous Vehicle based Storage and Retrieval Systems

Author: Ananth Krishnamurthy, University of Wisconsin-Madison, ME 3258 Department of ISyE, 1513 University Avenue, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, ananth@engr.wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Li Zhang, Charles Malmborg, Sunderesh Heragu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Autonomous vehicle storage and retrieval System (AVSRS) are a relatively new technology. AVSR systems use vehicles and lifts for horizontal and vertical movements respectively. We propose a new class of queuing network models for AVSR systems that uses fork/join synchronization stations to precisely model transaction, vehicles and lift interactions.
