Title: State Processes and their Role in Design and Implementation of Financial Models

Author: Dmitry Kramkov, Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15217, United States, kramkov@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We evaluate on state processes and their role in design and implementation of financial models. The concrete topics will include 1) The classification of financial models on identical from the point of view of implementation. 2) Pricing of path dependent derivatives 3) Choices of easily implementable multi-factor models.
