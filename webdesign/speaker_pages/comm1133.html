Title: Two-stage Controlled Fractional Factorial Screening for Simulation Experiments
Author: Bruce Ankenman, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Rd., Evanston, IL, United States, ankenman@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Hong Wan
Year: 2007
Abstract: Factor screening with statistical control of Type I error and Power makes sense for simulation experiments since they have random error, but can be run automatically. Here, a fractional factorial design in combination with a two-stage procedure for controlling power is proposed and compared to other available methods.
