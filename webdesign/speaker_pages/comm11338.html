Title: SUITS: A Framework for Sustainable IT Services

Author: Parijat Dube, IBM, TJ Watson Research Center, Hawthorne, NY, United States of America, pdube@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Genady Ya. Grabarnik, Laura Shwartz

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce the Sustainable IT Services (SUITS) framework to develop models for operational performance of an IT Service Provider in a global environment with carbon taxation and carbon credit trading markets. The models are used to formulate a revenue optimization problem for the IT Service Provider in this environment. The model solutions can provide guidance for designing operational level of different IT components and for creating effective strategies for trading in carbon markets.
