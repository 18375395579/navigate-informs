Title: The End of California’s Oil Rigs: Decision Analysis Finds a Win-win Solution

Author: Surya Swamy, Consulting Decision Analyst, Lumina Decision Systems, 26010 Highland Way, Los Gatos, Ca, 94117, United States of America, surya@lumina.com

Coauthor(s): Max Henrion

Year: 2012

Abstract: Controversy arose about how to decommission California's 27 offshore oil and gas platforms, now at the end of their productive lives. The California Ocean Sciences Trust (OST) sponsored a study to evaluate options, including complete removal and converting rigs to artificial reefs. A multi-attribute decision analysis framework using Analytica assisted stakeholders, including fishermen, divers, oil companies, and state agencies, resulting in an innovative solution preferred by most stakeholders.
