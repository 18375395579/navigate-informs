Title: Systemic Approach to Forecasting Emerging Technologies

Author: Guillermo Rueda, Ph.D Student, Intel, Hillsboro, OR, 97124, United States, grueda@pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Dundar Kocaoglu

Year: 2007

Abstract: A combination of several methods is being used as a way to overcome for the lack of historical information when forecasting emerging technologies. A methodology has been defined as a way to handle multiple factors and their relative importance combining the use of different methods such as the Delphi method, bibliometric analysis, utility curves, scenarios and composite indicators.
