Title: ESPAM: An Oligopolistic, Generation Expansion Model Which Incorporates Carbon Emissions Trading

Author: Pedro Linares, Professor, Universidad Pontificia Comillas, Alberto Aguilera 23, Madrid, 28015, Spain, Pedro.Linares@iit.icai.upcomillas.es

Coauthor(s): Luis Lapiedra, Mariano Ventosa, F. Javier Santos

Year: 2006

Abstract: The model combines detailed system operation and the modeling of the economic market equilibrium and a formulation for the resolution of the emissions permit and tradeable green certificates market equilibriums. It is formulated as a LCP which allows solving simultaneously the optimization problem for each firm considering all the markets. It has been implemented in GAMS and applied to the Spanish system.
