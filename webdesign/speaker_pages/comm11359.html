Title: Mining Censored Data and Occurrence Time Prediction
Author: Young Ryu, University of Texas at Dallas, School of Management, SM33, 800 West Campbell Road, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, ryoung@utdallas.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: There exist few data mining models utilizing censored observations. Presented are mathematical models and a neural net model for event occurrence time prediction with a mixture of complete and right censored observations.
