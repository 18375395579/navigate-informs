Title: Managing Time-of-Day Electricity Usage with Static and Dynamic Pricing Segments

Author: Shivaram Subramanian, IBM Research, 1101 Kitchawan Road, TJ Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, United States of America, subshiva@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Soumyadip Ghosh, Roger Lederman

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a novel predictive-control approach that allows an electricity provider to monitor and proactively manage peak and off-peak residential intra-day electricity usage in an emerging smart energy grid using time-dependent dynamic pricing incentives. We develop a forecast model for intra-day shares of a total daily load, which is then embedded within a global optimization framework for managing the daily electricity usage.
