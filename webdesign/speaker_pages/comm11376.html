Title: Measuring Operational Performance of Selected FAA Weather Programs through Data Analysis

Author: Dan Citrenbaum, Operations Research Analyst, Federal Aviation Administration, 800 Independence Ave, Washington, DC, 20591, United States of America, daniel.citrenbaum@faa.gov

Year: 2008

Abstract: The CIWS and the ITWS are two of FAA's weather systems expected to improve NAS performance in convective weather. These two products, which assist controllers and traffic managers with better forecasting capabilities, have been estimated to deliver benefits, i.e.,delay savings. This paper overviews our validation using data analysis versus modeling. These data validations compare the difference in operational performance duing the pre-acquisition period and the post-acquisition period.
