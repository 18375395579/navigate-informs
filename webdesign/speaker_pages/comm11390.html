Title: Game-theoretic Analysis of Attack and Defense in Coupled Cyber-physical Network Infrastructures

Author: Fei He, University at Buffalo, 330 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, feihe@buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Jun Zhuang, Nageswara S. V. Rao

Year: 2012

Abstract: Critical infrastructures rely on cyber and physical components that are both subject to natural, incidental or intentional degradations. In this paper we use game-theoretical approach to study the interaction between the attacker and the defender in the coupled cyber-physical network infrastructure. Both analytical and numerical results are presented to illustrate the models. Our research provides some novel insights into the survival and operations of such infrastructures.
