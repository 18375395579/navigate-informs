Title: A Method to Generate Image for SPC by Point Cloud Data

Author: Ketai He, Associate Professor, University of Science and Technology Beijing, No. 30, Xueyuan Rd., Haidian Dist., Beijing, 100083, China, ketaih@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Jaime Camelio, Fadel Megahed

Year: 2012

Abstract: Image analysis is an important method of statistical process control in manufacturing. It is difficult to obtain suitable images for defect detection and analysis when products have irregular surfaces. Three-dimension surface scanner can get the surface information, but the data is difficult to analyze directly due to massive data points. We propose a method to generate image by point cloud data from surface scanner, which can be used in defect analysis.
