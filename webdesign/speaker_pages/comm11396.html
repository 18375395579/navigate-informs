Title: Modeling Patient Follow-ups in a Primary Care Clinic

Author: Yichuan Ding, Stanford University, 14 Comstock Circle, Apt 10, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, y7ding@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Chinghua Chen, Mayank Sharma

Year: 2012

Abstract: In an outpatient setting, patients arriving for a single appointment may unexpectedly find that they must return for one or more follow-up appointments. This paper develops a Markov decision process (MDP) to model this phenomenon when the regular patients have to book their visits in advance. We derive an approximately optimal policy to this MDP and evaluate its performance by simulations.
