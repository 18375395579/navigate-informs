Title: Mixture Distribution Scenarios for Investment Decisions with Downside Risk

Author: Gautam Mitra, Brunel University, CARISMA, Uxbridge, United Kingdom, Gautam.Mitra@brunel.ac.uk

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose an integrated model for portfolio choice, where downside risk is considered explicitly in both scenario generation and optimization. Scenarios are generated using a mixture distribution of asset returns where lower tails and dependence are captured using copula functions while upside returns are modeled via multivariate GARCH. The model is an extended mean-variance approach, with a CVaR constraint. Computational results are presented for portfolios of ETFs of equity indices.
