Title: Managing Complexity and Uncertainty in Offshore Outsourced New Product Development Projects
Author: Saikat Chaudhuri, Assistant Professor of Management, University of Pennsylvania, The Wharton School, 3730 Walnut Street, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, saikatc@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Abhijit Mandal
Year: 2013
Abstract: Motivated by its rising prominence, we investigate to what extent the offshore outsourcing approach can effectively transfer to complex and uncertain knowledge-intensive work like R&D, by developing a simulation model based on a sample of such projects by a leading vendor. We contrast the global delivery model with a consulting model, and find that each approach is more efficacious at handling different types of tasks, bearing implications for notions of firm boundaries and organizational forms.
