Title: On Performance of Fiber Delay Lines in Optical Packet Switching Systems
Author: Yavuz Gunalay, Bahcesehir University, Faculty of Business, Cıragan Cad. No:4-6, İSTANBUL, 34100, Turkey, yavuz.gunalay@bahcesehir.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Nail Akar
Year: 2008
Abstract: An optical packet switching system which has N input-output fibers and W wavelength channels for each fiber is considered. In such systems, fiber delay lines (FDL) can be deployed to resolve contention. The behavior of the FDL is analyzed and packet drop rates are evaluated using a Markov Chain model.
