Title: Second Order Models and Traffic Data from Mobile Sensors

Author: Hongcheng Liu, PhD Candidate, Pennsylvania State University, 240 Leonhard, University Park, PA, United States of America, hql5143@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Benedetto Piccoli, Terry Friesz, Tao Yao, Ke Han

Year: 2013

Abstract: A data-enabled computational method is proposed for highway traffic when the second-order Phase Transition Model is employed. Such model, in contrast to first-order models, captures higher-order variations inherent in traffic stream. We use the NGSIM dataset to illustrate the unique insight of how the estimation of first- and second-order traffic quantities deteriorates as the sampling frequency and probe penetration rate change.
