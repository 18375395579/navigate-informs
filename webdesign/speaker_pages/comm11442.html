Title: Optimizing Pediatric Vaccine Formularies
Author: Sheldon Jacobson, Professor, University of Illinois, 201 N. Goodwin Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States, shj@uiuc.edu
Coauthor(s): Edward Sewell, Shane Hall
Year: 2006
Abstract: This presentation introduces the Vaccine Formulary Selection with Limited Budget Problem for obtaining the optimal childhood immunization vaccine formulary. The model that is formulated allows public health policy makers and pediatricians to evaluate the combinatorial explosion of vaccine choices spawned by the development of combination vaccines.
