Title: Dynamic Parameter Selection for Simulation Experiments
Author: Dashi Singham, Naval Postgraduate School, 1411 Cunningham Road, Monterey, United States of America, dsingham@nps.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We propose a method for dynamically changing simulation experimental settings while the model is running to determine run length. One method is to pre-determine the input parameters as functions of the simulation output, the other is to continually adjust input choices based on the observed output in a feedback control loop. We explore these ideas in the context of stopping rules for confidence interval procedures.
