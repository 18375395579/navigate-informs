Title: Predictive Analytics for Solid Organ Transplant Operations

Author: Diwakar Gupta, University of Minnesota, 111 Church Street S. E., Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, guptad@me.umn.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Operations Research professionals have much to contribute to the policy debate concerning how best to prioritize allocation of organs to wait list candidates. This talk will present efforts to improve Simulated Allocation Models (SAMs), which are simulation based predictive modeling tools, for the purpose of policy evaluation and policy development.
