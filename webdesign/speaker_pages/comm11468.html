Title: Monitoring Wafer Geometric Quality using Additive Gaussian Processes

Author: Nan Chen, Assistant Professor, National University of Singapore, 1 Engineering Drive 2, Singapore, 129789, Singapore, isecn@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Linmiao Zhang, Kaibo Wang

Year: 2013

Abstract: Wafer geometric quality is an important quality characteristic in the semiconductor industry. However, it is difficult to monitor the geometric quality due to the challenges raised from the complexity of the data structure. In this article, we propose an additive Gaussian process (AGP) model to approximate the standard wafer geometric profile while quantifying the normal deviations. Based on the AGP model, two statistical tests are developed to monitor the conformity of the wafers.
