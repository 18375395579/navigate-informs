Title: Reverse Logistics and Warranty Matching in a Large Consumer Electronic Retailer

Author: Andre Calmon, Massachusetts Institue of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Bldg. E40-149, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, acalmon@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen Graves

Year: 2012

Abstract: We describe, model and optimize the reverse logistics of our industrial partner, a Fortune 500 company that sells consumer electronics. Based on real data, we introduce a discrete-time model that captures the dynamics of this reverse logistics system. In addition, using an approximate dynamic programming approach, we propose a closed-loop data-driven policy for managing inventory. The performance of this policy is analysed theoretically and through numerical experiments.
