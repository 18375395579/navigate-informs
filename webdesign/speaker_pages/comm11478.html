Title: Evaluating the Performance of Brazilian Financial Managers with the AHP

Author: Luiz F. Autran M. Gomes, Professor, Ibmec/RJ, Av. Presidente Wilson, 118, Room 1110, Rio de Janeiro, 20030020, Brazil, autran@ibmecrj.br

Coauthor(s): Renata Andrade

Year: 2012

Abstract: The key objective of this paper is to show, through a case study, how managers working for a financial organization in Brazil were evaluated in their performance by using the AHP method. Based on the success of the study the organization has decided that the AHP method should definitely be considered as the analytical tool to be utilized in their future performance evaluation processes.
