Title: Multivariate Statistical Control of Unsynchronized Batch Processes

Author: Flavio Fogliatto, Prof., UFRGS, Av Osvaldo Aranha, 99/5o Andar, Porto Alegre, 90040-120, Brazil, ffogliatto@producao.ufrgs.br

Coauthor(s): Ndeye Niang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a new quality control strategy for unsynchronized batch processes. In our proposition, data arising from such batch processes are completed using a straightforward scheme to preserve all information on the variability in batch profiles along the time axis. The completed data set is reduced using a three-way method and monitoring of batch performance is accomplished directly on principal plane graphs, from which non-parametric off-line and on-line control charts are derived.
