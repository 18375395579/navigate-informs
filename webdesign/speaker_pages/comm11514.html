Title: An Online Travel Time Prediction System with a Decomposed Network Simulator

Author: Xiaorong Lai, University of Maryland, Department of Civil Engineering, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, xrlai@wam.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Pei-Wei Lin, Ying Liu, Gang-Len Chang

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study presents the framework and application of an online travel time prediction system with a decomposed network simulator. The system circumvents the constraints in real-time operations, and develops some effective models for data filtering, mainline volume prediction, ramp volume estimation and online simulation. The field study conducted in the Maryland Eastern Shore region (between Ocean City and Bay Bridge) has indicated the potential of the system for online travel time prediction.
