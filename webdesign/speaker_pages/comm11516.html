Title: Impact Assessment of the Integration of Advanced Concepts and Vehicles into the NextGen

Author: Almira Ramadani Williams, Associate Fellow, CSSI, Inc., 400 Virginia Ave, SW, Washington, DC, 20024, United States of America, awilliams@cssiinc.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: A metric set and performance evaluation framework were developed to enable understanding the impact of integration of advanced concepts and vehicles into the NextGen. The performance metrics and framework support analyses of procedures, safety assurance and system-level performance, and enable understanding of trade-offs between safety assurance, system performance and environmental constraints.
