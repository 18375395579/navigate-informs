Title: A Continuum Model for Signalized Junctions and What It Means for the LWR Model on Networks

Author: Yuqi Sun, Industrial and Manufacturing system Engineering, the Pennsylvania State University, 240 Leonhard, University Park, United States of America, yus139@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ke Han, Tao Yao, Terry Friesz

Year: 2013

Abstract: One way of approximating on-and-off signal control is by introducing a priority parameter and converting the problem setting to a continuum regime. We provide rigorous analysis on the convergence and error bound for such a continuum model in connection to the LWR model with spillback. We also show circumstances where such approximation is erroneous. This work provides theoretical support to a class of approximate models with reduced complexity and computational burden.
