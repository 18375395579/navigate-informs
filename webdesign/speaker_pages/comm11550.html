Title: Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Networks with Outsourcing Under Price and Quality Competition

Author: Dong Li, Doctoral Student, University of Massachusetts Amherst, 121 Presidents Drive, Amherst, MA, 01003, United States of America, dongl@som.umass.edu

Coauthor(s): Anna Nagurney, Ladimer Nagurney

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a pharmaceutical supply chain network model with outsourcing under price and quality competition in equilibrium and dynamic versions. This model allows for the determination of the optimal product flows of in-house and outsourcing activities and provides the firm the optimal make-or-buy and contractor selection decisions. We state the variational inequality formulation and the dynamic adjustment processes of the model, and present several numerical examples with sensitivity analysis.
