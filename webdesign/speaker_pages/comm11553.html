Title: Pandemic Flu Response

Author: Ali Ekici, Ph.D Student, H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States, aekici@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Pinar Keskinocak, Julie Swann, Shaudi Hosseini, Randeep Ramamurthy

Year: 2007

Abstract: Given the instances of Asian flu over the past few years, experts think that a pandemic might happen in the near future. Epidemiologists have warned that one in every three people could be infected, many of them requiring hospitalization. We constructed a model to predict the number of people infected, using data for state of Georgia. We developed optimization models to design networks to distribute food to affected populations, and analyzed the number and location of facilities to be set up.
