Title: Analysis of Destructive Degradation Test for a Product with Random Degradation Initiation Time

Author: Ye Zhang, University of Arizona, 1557 N Santa Rita Ave, Tucson, AZ, 85719, United States of America, yezhang1@email.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Haitao Liao

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we develop two delayed degradation models based on destructive degradation test data to evaluate the reliability of a product with a random degradation initiation time. Fixed-effects and random-effects gamma processes are considered, respectively. EM algorithm is implemented to calculate the MLE in both models. Monte Carlo approach is used to obtain the confidence intervals.
