Title: Pricing Managed Lanes Using Simulation
Author: Robert Phillips, Nomis Solutions, San Bruno, CA, United States of America, robert.phillips@nomissolutions.com
Coauthor(s): Caner Gocmen, Garrett van Ryzin
Year: 2013
Abstract: Managing toll lanes for profit is becoming increasingly common. We consider the problem of finding revenue-maximizing tolls for a managed lane operator -- that is an operator who can charge a toll for some lanes on a highway while the others remain free to use. We present some structural results on optimal tolls and use a simulation to evaluate the performance of different tolling schemes such as myopic, static, and dynamic tolling.
