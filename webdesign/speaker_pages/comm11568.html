Title: A Real Option Approach for Make-and-Sell-or-Buy Decisions of Web Services

Author: Marcel Mueller, Research Assistant, University of Innsbruck, Universit‰tsstraﬂe 15, Innsbruck, 6020, Austria, marcel.mueller@uibk.ac.at

Coauthor(s): Steffen Zimmermann, Bernd Heinrich

Year: 2013

Abstract: Characteristics of web services like loose coupling and standardized interfaces give non-software firms the option to sell internally developed web services on the web service market. We propose an analytical and a simulation model based on real options theory to value such a sell option ex-ante and enhance traditional make-or-buy approaches to a make-and-sell-or-buy approach for web services. Thereby, we reveal that this option may have considerable impact on traditional make-or-buy-decisions.
