Title: LINKS Simulations - Exploiting Web Resources for Maximum Teaching and Learning Impact With Integrative Competitive Simulations

Author: Randall G. Chapman, LINKS Simulations, Winter Garden, FL, United States of America, chapman@LINKS-simulations.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: How can an instructor teach effectively and efficiently with an integrative, large-scale, team-based, competitive business/management simulation? By exploiting the simulation’s web-based teaching and learning resources! This presentation explores web-based resources for supporting instructors and their students throughout LINKS simulations events.
