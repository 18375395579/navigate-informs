Title: Real Time Probabilistic Weather
Author: George Hunter, Senior Scientist, Sensis Corporation, 1700 Dell Avenue, Campbell, CA, 95008, United States, george.hunter@sensis.com
Coauthor(s): Kris Ramamoorthy, Benjamin Boisvert
Year: 2006
Abstract: This presentation summarizes JPDO research efforts in improving probabilistic weather modeling, evaluation and use in traffic flow management decision support tools. We present an equity-based traffic flow management solution and ProbTFM, a realtime traffic flow management evaluation platform.
