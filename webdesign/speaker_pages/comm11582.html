Title: The Exponomial Choice Model

Author: Aydin Alptekinoglu, Associate Professor of Supply Chain Management, Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA, United States of America, aydin@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): John Semple

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a new discrete choice model and explore its implications for pricing and assortment planning. Greatly facilitated by the fact that the loglikelihood function is concave in the unknown parameters, we estimate our model and compare it with multinomial logit on real choice data.
