Title: Finding Fakes: Counterfeit Product Detection at United States Border Crossings

Author: Gregory DeYong, Assistant Professor, University of Michigan-Flint, 2145 Riverfront Center, 303 E. Kearsley Street, Flint, MI, 48502, United States of America, gdeyong@umflint.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: The problem of counterfeit products continues to plague manufacturers. Using data from the U.S. Customs and Border Patrol, we analyze the effectiveness of border crossing points using data envelopment analysis and apply game theory to suggest interdiction strategies.
