Title: Data-driven Dynamic Networks of HIV Transmission

Author: Benjamin Armbruster, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, Il, United States of America, armbruster@northwestern.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We describe how to create a consistent and accurate simulation of how a sexual network changes over time using ego-centric network data collected from a longitudinal survey. We then add HIV transmission to the model and use it to address various questions of interest. The data is from a survey of young men who have sex with men in Chicago. We describe how to overcome the key challenge: determining an accurate model for the formation of new partnerships.
