Title: Evaluating Pediatric Access to Proton Therapy Using Discrete Event Simulation

Author: Lauren Cipriano, Research Scientist, Institute for Technology Assessment, 101 Merrimac Street, 10th Floor, Boston, MA, 02114, United States, Lauren@mgh-ita.org

Coauthor(s): Paul Nguyen, James Stahl

Year: 2007

Abstract: Proton beam radiation has proven a useful therapy for treating rare diseases such as sarcomas, eye tumors, and select head and neck and pediatric cancers. There is growing evidence that it may also be useful for treating more common cancers such as those of the prostate. We consider the potential demand from pediatric cancer patients in New England. We present a discrete event simulation for evaluating access to this limited medical resource.
