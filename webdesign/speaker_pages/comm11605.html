Title: Multicommodity Variable Upper Bound Flow Models

Author: Deon Burchett, PhD Student, University of Florida, 406-A Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, deonlb@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Jean-Philippe Richard

Year: 2013

Abstract: Variable upper bound flow models are well-studied in the MIP literature. Their study has yielded inequalities that are useful in computation. We consider a multicommodity extension of variable upper bound flow models. We perform a polyhedral study of this extended model, investigating novel aspects of its structure while also examining its relationship to the single-commodity case. In particular, we identify a large family of inequalities that generalizes well-known flow cover inequalities.
