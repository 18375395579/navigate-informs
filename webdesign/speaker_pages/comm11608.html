Title: The GDEA Model of Fundamental Analysis Under Expert Information and Its Application to U.S. Markets

Author: Xin Zhang, Ph.D. Candidate, University of Tennessee, College of Business Administration, Management Science, Knoxville, TN, 37996, United States of America, xzhang3@utk.edu

Coauthor(s): Chanaka Edirisinghe

Year: 2007

Abstract: Stock selection based on fundamental analysis of public firms is addressed. A generalized DEA model is developed to analyze firms' financial statements over time to determine a relative financial strength indicator that is highly correlated with stock returns. The model uses expert information in input/output selection. The correlation maximization binary model is solved via a two-step heuristic algorithm. Computational evidence is presented with over 800 public firms from 9 US sectors.
