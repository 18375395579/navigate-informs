Title: On Track?Modeling the Strategic Railway Crew Scheduling at Deutsche Bahn (Part I)

Author: Dominik Malcherek, University of Cologne, Department of Information Systems, and Operations Research (WINFORS), Cologne, 50969, Germany, d.malcherek@uni-koeln.de

Coauthor(s): Ulrich Derigs, Simon Schäfer

Year: 2008

Abstract: An essential step in the strategic planning process at railway companies is the optimal allocation of crew bases and suitable staff sizes. We present concepts of the crew scheduling problem at Deutsche Bahn and a corresponding mathematical model. Our experiences are based on a joint research project with Deutsche Bahn, Germany’s largest railway company.
