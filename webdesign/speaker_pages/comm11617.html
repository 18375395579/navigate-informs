Title: Dynamic Portfolio Choice with Learning

Author: Andrew Lim, Professor, National University of Singapore/U.C. Berkeley, NUS Business School, Singapore, Singapore, andrewlim@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Poomyos Wimonkittiwat

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present an approach for dynamic utility maximization when asset prices are modulated by a hidden Markov model with unknown transition matrix and observation statistics that combines Gibbs' sampling with approximations of the cost-to-go function in the dynamic programming. Optimality bounds are also presented.
