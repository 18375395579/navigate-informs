Title: Modeling of PSA Dynamics of Prostate Cancer Patients Receiving Neoadjuvant Hormone Therapy Prior to Radiation Therapy

Author: Mariel S. Lavieri, PhD Student, University of British Columbia, 2053 Main Mall, Vancouver BC V6T 1Z2, Canada, Mariel.Lavieri@sauder.ubc.ca

Coauthor(s): Martin Puterman, Steven Shechter, Scott Tyldesley, Tom Pickles

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk describes the use of the Kalman filter to update knowledge about PSA kinetics of high-intermediate and high risk prostate cancer patients that receive neoadjuvant hormone therapy prior to radiation therapy. Initial beliefs are based on population characteristics. Estimates of the time to nadir, and nadir level (which might be linked to maximal tumor regression) are updated as new information becomes available. The model could be used as input in the monitoring and treatment decision making process of individual prostate cancer patients as well as a tool to help schedule radiation therapy across patients.
