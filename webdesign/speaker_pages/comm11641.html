Title: Operations, Disruptions and Recovery
Author: Goran Stojkovic, Senior Operations Research Analyst, Jeppesen, 1800 McGill College Avenue, Suite 1930, Montreal, QC, H3A 3J6, Canada, Goran.Stojkovic@jeppesen.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: In operations disruption management, multiple parties are changing data simultaneously and the preferred approach to solve the disruption recovery problem usually varies by user. The lack of industry best practices and standards makes it hard to evaluate generated solutions and perform convincing benchmarks. We propose a standard benchmarking process based on an automated testing environment and a Recovery Performance Indicator (RPI) model in order to show the added value to the customer.
