Title: Application of Derivative-free Optimization to Influenza Epidemic Vaccination

Author: Judy Chen, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, DH A103, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, judyc@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Nick Sahinidis, Panagiotis Vouzis

Year: 2013

Abstract: Vaccination is the principal strategy to reduce the morbidity and mortality caused by the influenza virus. In this work, a derivative-free optimization method is used in combination with an epidemic simulator to study and optimize the vaccination strategy under limited vaccine supply.
