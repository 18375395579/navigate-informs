Title: A Finite-mixture Modeling Approach to the Management of Transportation Infrastructure Systems

Author: Pablo Durango-Cohen, Assistant Professor, Northwestern University, pdc@northwestern.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Maintenance optimization models rely on the specification of a perfect deterioration model. This can lead to inadequate characterizations due to population heterogeneity or epistemic uncertainty, and consequently to the implementation of inefficient policies. We propose a framework where deterioration is represented by a finite-mixture model. Policies are obtained by solving a partially-observable. Numerical examples illustrate the convergence and effectiveness of the approach.
