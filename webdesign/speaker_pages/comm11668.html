Title: Using Simulation Methods to Guide Decision Making of the Kentucky Cabinet for Health and Family Services
Author: Russell Harpring, University of Louisville, Louisville, KY, United States of America, russ.harpring@gmail.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: The purpose of this study is to find potential improvements to the intake process at the Kentucky Cabinet for Health and Family Services (KCHFS) main office in Louisville using simulation software. KCHFS deals with hundreds of clients every day, with each client in the system having different needs. By simulating the process with Arena Software, experiments can be run with real-time data and solutions found to complex problems involving process flow, staffing, and layout.
