Title: Reexamining Discrete Approximations to Continuous Distributions

Author: Robert Hammond, The University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Stateion, Austin, TX, United States of America, robertkh@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Eric Bickel

Year: 2012

Abstract: We analyze the performance of several discretization methods, extending the work of Keefer and Bodily (1983) by expanding their analysis of discrete approximations of continuous probability distributions using the Pearson distribution system, and adding some additional commonly used methods. We also propose some three-point approximations tailored to specific distribution types.
