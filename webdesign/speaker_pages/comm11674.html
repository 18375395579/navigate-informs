Title: Tree-MARS Models

Author: Subrat Sahu, University of Texas at Arlington, 415 South Oak Street #109, Arlington, TX, 76010, United States of America, subrat@exchange.uta.edu

Coauthor(s): Ching-feng Lin, Victoria Chen

Year: 2008

Abstract: Abstract: We present a tree-MARS model that first fits a regression tree model, then uses multivariate adaptive regression splines (MARS) to refine the model at the terminal nodes of the tree. Comparisons are made to the tree-regression model that uses a similar concept, but only permits the use of linear regression at the terminal nodes. The tree-MARS model is used to represent state transitions for a complex pain management dynamic programming application.
