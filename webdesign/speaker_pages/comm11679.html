Title: Multi-period Optimization Model for a Household and Optimal Insurance Design

Author: Norio Hibiki, Associate Professor, Keio University, 3-14-1 Hiyoshi, Kohoku-ku, Yokohama, 223-8522, Japan, hibiki@ae.keio.ac.jp

Year: 2007

Abstract: We discuss optimization models to obtain an optimal investment and insurance strategy for a household. In this paper, the optimization model is formulated with term life insurance which variable insurance money is received. Next, we propose a model to decide optimal life and medical insurance money at each time. We examine the models to illustrate optimal financial plans with some practical examples.
