Title: Active Learning for Decision-Making
Author: Maytal Saar-Tsechansky, Assistant Professor, Department of Information, Risk, and Operations Management, McCombs School of Business, University of Texas-Austin, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, maytal@mccombs.utexas.edu
Coauthor(s): Foster Provost
Year: 2006
Abstract: As businesses strive to cater to the preferences of individual consumers, they often employ predictive models to customize their targeting decisions. However building accurate models to support decisions requires information about consumers?preferences that often is costly to acquire. We develop a new approach for information acquisition that targets decision-making specifically. Empirical evaluations demonstrate that the method significantly improves targeting decisions.
