Title: Multi-voxel Pattern Analysis of fMRI Data with Mutual-information based Feature Selection

Author: Fatma Gumus, PhD Student, SUNY Binghamton, Binghamton, NY, 13902, United States of America, fgumus1@binghamton.edu

Coauthor(s): Chun-An (Joe) Chou

Year: 2013

Abstract: Recent studies have pointed out that multi-voxel pattern analysis (MVPA) of fMRI data is a promising technique to explain the distributive working nature of the brain. However, the disparity between the number of features and available observations is made difficult to reach easy and meaningful results. In this work, mutual information based feature selection is applied to reduce scale of the problem. Experimental results for several classifiers are shown for different experimental settings.
