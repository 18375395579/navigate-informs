Title: Sources of Innovation Failures
Author: Kingshuk Sinha, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, ksinha@umn.edu
Coauthor(s): Ujjal Mukherjee
Year: 2013
Abstract: An integrative framework is proposed that relates ‘technology life cycle?of a product to the ‘stages of product development and manufacturing process?in identifying causes of innovation failures. The constituent hypotheses of the framework are empirically tested through analysis of a “big?panel data set on medical device recalls.
