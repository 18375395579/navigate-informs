Title: Computational Issues in the Analysis of Call Center Arrivals
Author: Refik Soyer, George Washington University, Decision Sciences Dept Funger Hall 415, School of Business, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, soyer@gwu.edu
Coauthor(s): Murat Tarimcilar, Fabrizio Ruggeri
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this talk we consider analysis of call center arrivals that are mostly linked to individual advertisements. We discuss the problem of prediction for aggregate arrivals based on advertisement specific data and illustrate how these predictions can be developed in a Bayesian analysis. We also address the issue of how to deal with call arrival data that are not 100% linked to ads. We consider this as a missing data problem and develop a data augmentation algorithm for the Bayesian analysis.
