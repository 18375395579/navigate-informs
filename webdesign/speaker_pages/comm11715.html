Title: Network-wide Statistical Modeling and Prediction of Computer Traffic

Author: George Michailidis, University of Michigan, 1085 South University Ave., 439 West Hall, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, gmichail@umich.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Network engineers are interested in monitoring traffic across all links of their network. However, resource constraints and limited access, makes it impossible to directly measure traffic on all the links. We introduce a physically interpretable, probabilistic model of network-wide traffic that can be used in conjunction with easily obtainable measurements to provide more accurate prediction than is possible with only the inexpensive measurements.
