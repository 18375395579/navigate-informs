Title: An Ensemble Approach for Model Bias Prediction
Author: Zhimin Xi, Research Assistant Professor, University of Michigan, 4901 Evergreen Rd, Dearborn, MI, 48128, United States of America, zxi@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Yan Fu, Ren-Jye Yang
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper presents an ensemble approach for model bias prediction in order to correct predictions of computational models. The basic idea is to first characterize the model bias of computational models, then correct the model prediction by adding the characterized model bias. The ensemble approach is composed of two prediction mechanisms: 1) response surface of model bias, and 2) Copula modeling. Advantages of both mechanisms are utilized with an accuracy based weighting approach.
