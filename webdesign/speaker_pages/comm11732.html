Title: Network Cooperation Models for Irregular Warfare

Author: Chris Arney, Professor, United States Military Academy, Department of Mathematics, West Point, NY, 10996, United States of America, david.arney@usma.edu

Coauthor(s): Kathryn Coronges

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a complexity-based modeling framework that merges network science and social network analysis with recent results in cooperative game theory and information science to simulate, design, and understand military systems and doctrine. This framework embraces the complexity of understanding the challenging issues associated with complex adaptive systems and military operations. We apply our framework to scenarios and important military operational issues.
