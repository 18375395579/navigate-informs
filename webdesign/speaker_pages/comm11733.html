Title: A Competitive Model of Price Dispersion in Online Retailing
Author: Jan Stallaert, Associate Professor, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States, jan.stallaert@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): John Zhang, Sulin Ba
Year: 2007
Abstract: We propose a competitive model, based on online retailers?differentiation, to explain price dispersion. We empirically test the predictions of this model and find that the model is a viable alternative to the hedonic price model. In addition, our competitive model is able to predict and explain observations that are seemingly inconsistent with a hedonic model.
