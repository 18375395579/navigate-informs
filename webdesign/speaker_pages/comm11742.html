Title: Multi Attribute Utility Functions with Incomplete Preference Information

Author: Luis Montiel, PhD, The University of Texas as Austin, 1 University Station, Austin, TX, 78703, United States of America, lvmontiel@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Eric Bickel

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a new method to explore multi-attribute utility functions when the decision maker's preferences are incompletely specified. We consider the multi-linear utility function and use a new simulation procedure called JDSIM to perform sensitivity analysis over the space of all possible utilities that match partially specified preferences. Finally, we present a case of study and illustrate the power of this procedure under relative elicitation.
