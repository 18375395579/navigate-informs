Title: ParkPGH: Pittsburgh’s Predictive Parking App

Author: Tayo Fabusuyi, Lead Strategist, Numeritics, 5907 Penn Ave, Suite 313, Pittsburgh, PA, 15206, United States of America, Tayo.Fabusuyi@numeritics.com

Coauthor(s): Robert Hampshire, Katsunobu Sasanuma

Year: 2012

Abstract: ParkPGH is a smart parking solution that provides information on the availability of parking spaces within Pittsburgh’s downtown. The application uses an event calendar, historical data, multiple regression model and a Bayesian updating process in making both long and short term predictions about parking availability. Parking information is delivered through channels that include websites, SMS text, voice, iPhone app and a mobile version of the website that is optimized for other smartphones.
