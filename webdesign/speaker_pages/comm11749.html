Title: Workload Balancing Problem for Nurses: A Multi-Objective Approach

Author: Ilgin Poyraz Acar, Anadolu University, Department of Industrial Engineering Fa, Anadolu University, Eskisehir, Turkey, ipoyraz@anadolu.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Aydin Sipahioglu, Steven E. Butt

Year: 2012

Abstract: In addition to using patient acuity measures, this work considers incorporating travel distances into the construction of a nurse’s patient assignments based on the use of unit-derived workload scores and distance scores. It is shown that the conic scalarization approach can be successfully used for solving these multi-objective nurse-to-patient assignment problems.
