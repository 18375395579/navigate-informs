Title: Predicting the Impact of a Proposed Securities Exchange Regulation
Author: Waraporn Tongprasit, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, United States of America, waraporn@stanford.edu
Coauthor(s): Benjamin Van Roy
Year: 2012
Abstract: We propose an approach to counterfactual analysis of securities exchanges that can be applied to predict the impact of proposed regulatory changes. We conduct an empirical case study in which we predict the impact of a historical tick size change on exchange efficiency and compare predictions to what can be inferred from subsequent data.
