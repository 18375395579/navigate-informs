Title: Success Factors from Major Decision Analysis Studies
Author: Gregory Parnell, Professor of Systems Engineering, United States Military Academy, Department of Systems Engineering, West Point, NY, 10996, United States of America, gregory.parnell@usma.edu
Coauthor(s): Terry Bresnick
Year: 2008
Abstract: One of the best ways to improve decision analysis practice is to learn from successes and failures. We describe a decision analysis framework that includes the major techniques of the field. Next, we identify lessons learned from several major decision analysis studies that span the decision analysis techniques included in the framework.
