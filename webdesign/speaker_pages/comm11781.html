Title: Using Probabilistic Soft Logic (PSL) to Model Group Behavior in Collective Action

Author: Bert Huang, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, United States of America

Coauthor(s): Lise Getoor

Year: 2013

Abstract: Probabilistic soft logic (PSL) is a rich modeling framework that supports data-driven discovery of patterns of interaction in social media. The use of PSL to model collective behaviors in civil unrest and election events shows how different groups frame events and develop relationships between them.
