Title: Fractals-based Statistical Process Control (SPC)

Author: Irad Ben-Gal, Tel-Aviv University, Tel Aviv, Israel, bengal@eng.tau.ac.il

Coauthor(s): Noa Ruschin Rimini, Oded Maimon

Year: 2013

Abstract: In recent years, with the overall increase in data availability there is a growing need for new monitoring techniques that can cope with such data complexity. For this purpose, we suggest a new statistical process control (SPC) approach for data-rich environments. The proposed approach is based on the theory of fractal geometry.The fractal model can be used for anomaly detection, pattern analysis and knowledge discovery.
