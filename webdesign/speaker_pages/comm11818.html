Title: Operational Analytics and Their Application in Application Management Service (AMS)

Author: Ying Li, Research Staff Member, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, yingli@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Ta-Hsin Li, Jeaha Yang, Rong Liu, Juhnyoung Lee

Year: 2013

Abstract: In AMS business, organizations could spend as much as 80% of their application budgets on maintenance-related activities, which are faithfully captured by application-based problem tickets. We will discuss a set of recently developed operational analytics for mining the wealthy information contained in these tickets including ticket volume and resolution time trend analysis, backlog monitoring, resource utilization analysis, volume forecasting, group right-sizing and what-if scenario analysis.
