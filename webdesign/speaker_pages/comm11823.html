Title: Queuing Models for Mobile Rack-based Order-pick Systems

Author: Debjit Roy, Assistant Professor, Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad, Wing 3C, Vastrapur, India, debjit@iimahd.ernet.in

Coauthor(s): Shobhit Nigam

Year: 2013

Abstract: Movable storage shelves (pods) that can be lifted and brought to the order-pick station by robotic vehicles can improve flexibility and productivity in picking activities. Closed queuing network models provide a rich framework for rapidly evaluating the throughput performance of such systems with alternate pick-area, vehicle sharing, and pod storage strategies.
