Title: Evaluating Impacts of Staffing Policies on Patient Flows in an Emergency Department using Simulation

Author: Yong Hong Kuo, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, SEEM Dept,Chinese Uni. of Hong Kong,, Shatin, New Territories, Hong Kong, NT, Hong Kong - PRC, yhkuo@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Benedetta Lupia, Omar Rado, Colin Graham, Janny Leung

Year: 2012

Abstract: We studied impacts of staffing policies on patient flows in an Emergency Department in Hong Kong. We built a simulation model of the Emergency Department, which captures all relevant treatment processes, complicated arrival rates and adjustable staff deployment. To tackle a challenge of data incompleteness, we estimated required parameters in our model with the help of meta-heuristics. We will also present some insights and suggestions for operational improvements by using our model.
