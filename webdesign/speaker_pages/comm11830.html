Title: HIV Epidemic in Far Western Nepal: A Mathematical Model and Control

Author: Naveen Vaidya, PhD Student, Dept of Maths and Stats, York University, 4700 Keele Street, Toronto, ON, M3J1P3, Canada, nvaidya.anyol@gmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: We develop a mathematical model of the dynamics of HIV epidemic in far western Nepal. For specific conditions, we calculate the basic reproduction number, disease-free equilibrium and endemic equilibrium. Our model analysis and simulation results suggest that returning-migrants group from India have to be targeted for developing preventive programs. We also formulate an optimal control problem and provide a better strategy based on the results of our model.
