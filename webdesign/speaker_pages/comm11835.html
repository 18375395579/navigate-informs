Title: Inventory Financing and Trade Credit

Author: Qi Wu, University of Texas at Austin, 2110 Speedway Stop B6500, Austin, TX, 78703, United States of America, qi.wu@phd.mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Kumar Muthuraman, Sridhar Seshari

Year: 2012

Abstract: Trade credit is one of the common ways to finance inventory and is often offered via an early payment discount. In this talk, we present a model of trade credit, embedded in a stochastic inventory control problem. We compute the optimal joint inventory, cash management and trade credit decisions, based on which, we show that trade credit helps with smoothing cash flows and making better operational decisions.
