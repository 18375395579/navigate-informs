Title: Global Operational Logistics Decision Support System

Author: Paul Grant, Systems Engineer, SAS Institute, Boston, MA, United States of America, Paul.Grant@sas.com

Coauthor(s): Jodi Blomberg

Year: 2007

Abstract: Applying this crisis action planning tool, military analysts optimize their supply chains incorporating complexities of combat operations. Pentagon decision makers can create, change, and compare optimization scenarios in a point-and-click environment. This optimization model runs on the newest solvers available in SAS/OR? and can be deployed as a BI environment via an easy to use interface so military planners can explore results with embedded GIS capabilities and OLAP cubes.
