Title: Chasing Shadows: Assessing the Terrorist Threat
Author: Gary Ackerman, Research Director, National Consortium for the Study of Terrorism and Responses to Terrorism, 3300 Symons Hall, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, gackerman@start.umd.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Of the three main legs of terrorist risk assessment - threat, vulnerability and consequence - threat aspects are often underdeveloped in homeland security analysis tools based on traditional risk analysis. This presentation will introduce several types of threat assessment tools in order to illustrate how threat assessment can be integrated into the larger task of comprehensive risk assessment and how novel insights from the social sciences can inform analyses of terrorism risk.
