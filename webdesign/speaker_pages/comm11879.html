Title: Correcting Partition Dependence and Carryover Biases in Subjective Probabilities: A Web Survey

Author: Venkat Prava, PhD Student, The Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, MD, United States of America, vprava1@jhu.edu

Coauthor(s): Melissa Kenney, Robert Clemen, Ben Hobbs

Year: 2013

Abstract: A model-based approach to correcting partition dependence (PD)and carryover bias in subjective probabilities is tested using responses from a web-based questionnaire involving 1500 subjects. We model assessed probabilities as a convex combination of anchors and individual’s unbiased probabilities. We found that ordering of questions can be used to decrease carryover and questionnaire format can be used to reduce PD bias.
