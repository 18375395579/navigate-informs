Title: Solving the Problem of Plaster Waste Destination in Buildings Sites Structured by VFT

Author: Luciana Hazin, Assistant Professor, Federal University of Pernambuco, Recife, Brazil, alencarlh@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Ana Paula Costa

Year: 2013

Abstract: Value-focus thinking is a methodology that addresses how values can be used to improve the decision making process. It was applied in the problem of plaster waste destination in the building sites in Brazil. It was possible for the parties involved in the problem to clarify their objectives and specify more precisely the consequences and constraints for the decision problem. With the problem structured, an additive model was applied using a new procedure for elicitation.
