Title: Adaptive Route Choice Models

Author: Song Gao, Assistant Professor, University of Massachusetts Amherst, 16B Marston Hall, 130 Natural Resources Road, Amherst, MA, 01003, United States of America, songgao@ecs.umass.edu

Coauthor(s): Emma Frejinger, Moshe Ben-Akiva

Year: 2007

Abstract: We specify and estimate adaptive route choice models that explicitly capture travelers route choice adjustments according to real-time information in a stochastic time-dependent network. Two types of adaptive route choice models are studied: a sequence of path choice models at every intermediate decision node; and a routing policy choice model where the alternatives correspond to decision rules at the origin rather than simple paths. The models are estimated based on a Swedish GPS data set.
