Title: A Robust and Efficient Dynamic Traffic Assignment Solution Procedure for Large-scale Applications

Author: Yi-Chang Chiu, Assistant Professor, University of Arizona, 1209 E 2nd St. Room 206, Department of Civil Eng.and Eng. Mech., Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, chiu@email.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Brenda Bustillos, Eric Nava

Year: 2008

Abstract: Most simulation-based dynamic traffic assignment (DTA) models are limited spatially and temporally due to computational resource requirements. The concept of rolling epoch is introduced to perform temporally scalable DTA modeling procedure. Model developments and numerical results are presented and discussed in this talk.
