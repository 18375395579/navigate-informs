Title: Revenue Management with Stochastic Programming
Author: Julia L. Higle, The Ohio State University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Columbus, OH, United States of America, higle.1@osu.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present revenue management models based on stochastic programming constructs. Our model is one that combines capacity-based calculations to obtain price-based policies, while explicitly recognizing the stochastic nature of customer demand in the various market segments. Data requirements and computational implications will be discussed.
