Title: Adaptive B-spline Knots Selection using Multi-resolution Basis Set

Author: Nan Chen, Assistant Professor, National University of Singapore, 1 Engineering Drive 2, Singapore, 129789, Singapore, isecn@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Yuan Yuan, Shiyu Zhou

Year: 2013

Abstract: B-splines are commonly used in the Computer Aided Design (CAD) and signal processing to fit complicated functions. However, how to place the knots appropriately in B-spline curve fitting remains a difficult problem. In this paper, we proposed a two-stage knots placement method to place knots adapting to the curvature structures of the unknown function. We demonstrated the effectiveness using numerical studies on multiple representative functions.
