Title: Scalable Approach to Joint Location and Dispatching Decisions for Emergency Medical Service Systems
Author: Maria Mayorga, Clemson University, Raleigh, NC, United States of America, mayorga@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Hector Toro-Diaz, Laura McLay
Year: 2013
Abstract: Emergency Medical Service (EMS) systems provide medical care to emergency patients. Jointly optimizing location and dispatching decisions can improve equitability measures in EMS systems. However, existing methods are not scalable. We incorporate linear approximations to the hypercube model and allow for multiple vehicles at a station. We developed a hybrid method which uses tabu-search with a non-deterministic tabu-list. The model is tested on two real world data sets.
