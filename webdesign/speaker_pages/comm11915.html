Title: Nonlinear Modeling of Spiral Wave Formation and Turbulent Patterns in High-dimensional Systems
Author: Yun Chen, University of South Florida, 4202 E. Fowler Ave, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, yunchen@mail.usf.edu
Coauthor(s): Hui Yang
Year: 2013
Abstract: Real-world dynamical systems often exhibit complex spiral wave dynamics and turbulent patterns. Many previous investigations focused on such space-time patterns on the regular 2D surfaces. This paper presents a dimensionality reduction approach for modeling nonlinear reaction-diffusion mechanisms in high-dimensional systems. This present investigation shows great potentials for characterizing and quantifying space-time dynamics in high-dimensional systems.
