Title: Global Supply Chain Networks Sustainability: Corporate Social Responsibility and Risk Management

Author: Jose Cruz, Assistant Professor, University of Connecticut, School of Business, 2100 Hillside Road Unit 1041, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States, Jose.Cruz@business.uconn.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper develops a dynamic framework for the modeling and analysis of global supply chain networks sustainability. We model the multicriteria decision-making behavior of the various decision-makers. We explore the dynamic evolution of the product flows, the associated product prices, as well as the levels of social responsibility activities on the network until an equilibrium pattern is achieved. We illustrate the model and computational procedure with several numerical examples.
