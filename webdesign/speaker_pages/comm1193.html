Title: Optimal Pricing for Student Housing

Author: Jian Wang, VIce President, The Rainmaker Group, 4550 North Point Parkway, Suite 400, Alpharetta, GA, 30022, United States of America, jwang@letitrain.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: The application of revenue management to conventional multi-family housing has been successful, but it is new to student housing. In this presentation, we describe some unique characteristics of student housing, and point out the drawbacks of prevalent pricing methods. We then propose an optimal pricing model, and illustrate the application to a real case.
