Title: A Stochastic Dynamic Programming Approach for Transmission Investments
Author: Enzo Sauma, P. Universidad Catolica de Chile, Ave. Vicuña Mackenna #4860, Santiago, RM, Chile, esauma@ing.puc.cl
Year: 2007
Abstract: We reformulate a previously-developed static model of transmission investments in restructured electricity systems using a stochastic dynamic framework. In particular, we study the optimal transmission investment strategy of a network planner based on equilibrium models characterizing the dynamic competitive interaction among oligopolistic generation firms.
