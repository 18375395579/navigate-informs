Title: Automatic Signal Segmentation for Multiple Embedded Operations Monitoring Using Wavelets Analysis

Author: Yong Lei, Industrial and Operations Engineering, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States, ylei@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Judy Jin

Year: 2007

Abstract: In complex systems, available sensing signals are usually the combined responses of multiple embedded operations, which generate a high dimensional nonlinear profile data, and cause challenges in finding failed individual operations. In this research, wavelet analysis is used to achieve automatic signal segmentation with the enhanced diagnosability of individual operation conditions.
