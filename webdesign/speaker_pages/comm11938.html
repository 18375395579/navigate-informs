Title: Human Decision-making Model Based on Enhanced BDI Framework

Author: Young-Jun Son, Associate Professor, The University of Arizona, Systems and Industrial Engineering, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States, son@sie.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Seungho Lee

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a novel human decision-making model based on the enhanced BDI (belief, desire, intention) framework. The model is capable of generating plans in real-time, supporting both reactive and proactive decision making, and maintaining situation awareness. Bayesian belief network is employed for the belief module. Decision field theory, a cognitive approach to human decision making, is employed for the intention module. SOAR architecture is employed for the learning capability of the model.
