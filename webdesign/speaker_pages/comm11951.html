Title: A Probabilistic Model for Assessing the Mortality Risk in Post-operative Patients
Author: Dmytro Korenkevych, 372 Maguire Village, Apt. 1, Gainesville, FL, 32603, United States of America, dmitriy@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Petar Momcilovic, Panos Pardalos
Year: 2012
Abstract: We used a probabilistic model based on Bayesian learning and optimization techniques to estimate the mortality risk in post-operative patients. The model incorporates discrete and continuous risk factors and provides a probabilistic score associated with the risk. The feature selection procedure was applied in order to discard irrelevant risk factors and 70/30 cross-validation analysis was performed to estimate the accuracy of the model.
