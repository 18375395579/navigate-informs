Title: A Simulation Study of an Outpatient Cancer Clinic

Author: David Claudio, Assistant Professor, Montana State University, 220 Roberts Hall, Bozeman, 59717, United States of America, david.claudio@ie.montana.edu

Coauthor(s): Mahmudur Rahman, Annali Huggins

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Bozeman Deaconess Cancer Center provides infusion services to a sector of the state of Montana. It has 7 different types of patient flow, different process times and different schedule times for each resource. The objective of this study was to identify how to simulate such a complex system. The simulation process of cancer center began with a simple model and complexity was added incrementally. As the model increased in complexity, it became more representative of actual system.
