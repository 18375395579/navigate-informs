Title: Dependencies Among Multiple Quality Responses: Characterization and Aggregation for Prediction
Author: Leman Esra Dolgun, Anadolu University, Industrial Engineering Department, Eskisehir, 26555, Turkey, ledolgun@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Gülser Köksal, Nimetullah Burnak
Year: 2012
Abstract: We present results of a structured survey with several quality control experts to characterize preferential and statistical dependencies among multiple quality responses. We then consider aggregation of these responses via Choquet integral for predictive modeling. The method is tested for different dependence situations identified through the survey. We elaborate on design of the reference alternatives to control the number of comparisons required to capture the expertise of the decision maker.
