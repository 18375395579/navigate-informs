Title: Designing a User Friendly System for Planning under Uncertainty with Stochastic Programming (SP)

Author: Linus Schrage, University of Chicago, 5807 S. Woodlawn, Chicago, IL, United States of America, linus.schrage@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): Mustafa Atlihan, Kevin Cunningham, Gautier Laude

Year: 2012

Abstract: Perhaps the most daunting challenge of SP is providing a user interface that is not daunting to the user. We describe the approach we have used in the SP component of the LINDO API optimization library. The two major tasks are eliciting from the user: 1) sequencing of decisions and random events, and 2) distributions of random variables, including dependencies. This library is accessible via the spreadsheet add-in What’sBest! and the modelling languages GAMS and LINGO.
