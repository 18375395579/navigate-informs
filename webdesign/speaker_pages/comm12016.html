Title: Agent-based Modeling and SImulation for Logistics Markets and Co2 Reduction
Author: Han La Poutre, Professor, CWI, Kruislaan 413, 1098 SJ, P.O. Box 94079, Amsterdam, 1090 GB, Netherlands, Han.La.Poutre@cwi.nl
Coauthor(s): Valentin Robu, Pieter Jan 't Hoen, Han Noot
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present research results on the reduction of CO2 emission by intelligent truck usage, as part of the Dutch research program Energy, Ecology, and Technology. We describe market-based, automated techniques for cargo allocation to trucks and transportation companies. We present agent-based models and interactive simulation systems. We model the players by agents and design bidding strategies. We also describe how to get robustness in the results of adaptive simulation systems.
