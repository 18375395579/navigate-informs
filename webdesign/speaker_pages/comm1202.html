Title: An Integer Program Model for United States Postal Service (USPS) Test Mail Induction

Author: Mariah Jeffery, Managing Consultant, IBM Global Business Services, 12902 Federal Systems Park Dr, Fairfax, VA, United States of America, mjeffery@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Noriah din Daily, Hua Ni

Year: 2008

Abstract: IBM contracts with the USPS to report its on time performance by service standards by creating test mail and tracking it through the network. An integer programming model is used for developing mail induction schedules. The problem structure, unique challenges encountered, history of the model, and performance with several optimization packages will be discussed.
