Title: Transportation Fuel Portfolio Design under GHG Emission Regulations
Author: Yuche Chen, University of California, Davis, One Shields Avenue, Davis, CA, 95616, United States of America, ychchen@ucdavis.edu
Coauthor(s): Yueyue Fan, Sonia Yeh
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this talk, we will present a bi-level optimization model to simulate the behavior of fuel manufactures and blenders under certain emission regulations in a carbon credit trading market. A case study in California will be included to test the applicability of the model and to draw policy implications.
