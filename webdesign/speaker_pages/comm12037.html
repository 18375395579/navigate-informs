Title: A Probabilistic Stationary Speed-density Relation Based on Newell's Simplified Car-following Model

Author: Saif Eddin Jabari, IBM, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, sgjabari@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Jianfeng Zheng, Henry X. Liu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper presents a method for deriving probabilistic macroscopic traffic flow relations from Newell's simplified car-following model. The probabilistic nature of the model allows investigating the impact of driver heterogeneity on macroscopic relations of traffic flow. The physical features of the model are verified analytically. An empirical study is carried out to obtain distributions of individual driver parameters and these are used to perform calculations with the probabilistic macroscopic relations.
