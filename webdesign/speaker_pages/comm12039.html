Title: Evaluation of City-production Integration Based on Factor Analysis and Cluster Analysis
Author: Lin Su, Tongji University, School of Economic and Management, French Center A403, Shanghai, China, yjshsl@163.com
Coauthor(s): Xia Wang, Bing Guo, Xin Zheng, Xue Li
Year: 2013
Abstract: The level of city-production integration is essential for urban development; however the current research mainly focuses on the definition of the concept. In this paper, we took nationwide high-tech zone of the major cities as cases by using the combination of factor analysis and cluster analysis to quantitatively evaluate and classify the level of city-production fusion, and finally proposed policy recommendations.
