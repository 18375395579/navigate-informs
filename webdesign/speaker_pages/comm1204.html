Title: Overview of MATREX
Author: Tom Hurt, US Army RDECOM, Fort Belvoir,, VA, United States of America, tom.hurt@us.army.mil
Coauthor(s): Chris Metivier
Year: 2008
Abstract: The Modeling Architecture for Technology, Research, and EXperimentation (MATREX) Program is a key distributed modeling and simulation environment in the US Army. It provides a unifying M&S architecture, supporting tools, and infrastructure to ease integration and use of multi-resolution multi-fidelity live, virtual, and constructive applications. The MATREX program's adaptable environment and tools support Systems of Systems design and development from requirements through test and integration.
