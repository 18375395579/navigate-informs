Title: Opportunistic Condition Based Maintenance Using Equivalent Age Model

Author: Ali Aldubaisi, PhD Student, Auburn University, 3301 Shelby Center, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States of America, aza0050@tigermail.auburn.edu

Coauthor(s): Fadel Megahed

Year: 2013

Abstract: Equivalent age models are very flexible since they do not require the life distributions to be shape invariant. However, they have only been proposed to model the aging rate of a single machine and the available literature on developing maintenance strategies based on equivalent age is preliminary. Thus, the objective of our work is to allow for multiple machine configurations with dependencies, while triggering maintenance based on multiobjective optimization routines and experimental results.
