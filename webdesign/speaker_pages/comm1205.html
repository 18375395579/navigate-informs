Title: A Novel Machine Learning Methodology for Sentiment Analysis
Author: Shyamal Krishna, Hewlett-Packard, Platform Services, Global Analytics, India
Coauthor(s): Debayan Bose, Siddharth Arun
Year: 2013
Abstract: The classical lexicon-approach used for sentiment analysis is limited in its application as it requires numerous case specific-rules to understand the usage of words for expressing opinions. Here ‘Support-Vector-Machines? a machine-learning based technique, is used to automatically generate sentiments and underlying themes based on a sample provided by the user.
