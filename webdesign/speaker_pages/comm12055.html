Title: Comparative Analysis of Capital Asset Pricing (CAPM) Betas Based on Tail Risk Measures
Author: Konstantin Pavlikov, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, kpavlikov@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Stan Uryasev
Year: 2012
Abstract: The paper considers Capital Asset Price (CAPM) for tail risk measures: Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR) and Conditional Drawdown-at-Risk. We compared CAPM Beta based on Standard Deviation with Betas based on CVaR and CDaR. We ranked the performance of the hedge fund stiles using these new Betas.
