Title: Effective Compression, Clustering, and Pattern Discovery for Binary Data Sets
Author: Haibing Lu, Assistant Professor, Santa Clara University, 500 El Camino Real, Leavey School of Business, Santa Clara, CA, 95053, United States of America, hlu@scu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper presents a framework for effective compression, clustering, and pattern discovery for binary datasets, using weighted rank-one binary matrix factorization.
