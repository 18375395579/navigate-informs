Title: Valuing Administrative Solutions to Handle Patient Volume Uncertainty in Nurse Staffing/Scheduling

Author: Kibaek Kim, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, Kibaek.Kim@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Sanjay Mehrotra

Year: 2013

Abstract: Uncertain patient volume fluctuation complicates nurse staffing and scheduling decisions. Moreover, limited budget for nursing positions compels hospital operations managers to efficiently utilize nursing staffs without lose of care quality. We present a decision support model to evaluate the benefit of flexing the nurse staffing levels for different care units and nurse float pool. We further analyze the cost implications of using part-time nurses and float pool nurses.
