Title: Long-Term Water Allocation Models under Uncertainty
Author: Guzin Bayraksan, The Ohio State University, Integrated Systems Engineering, Columbus, OH, United States of America, bayraksan.1@osu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present long-term, multi-period generalized network water allocation models under water demand and supply uncertainty. We discuss different ways to incorporate uncertainty in the model, resulting in different classes of stochastic programs. We also discuss how these uncertainties can be tied to regional climate models. We apply our models to allocate Colorado River water to a southeast region of Tucson, Arizona and present computational results.
