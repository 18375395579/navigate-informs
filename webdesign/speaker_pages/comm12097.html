Title: Developing Project Management Skills Through Senior Design

Author: Anita Vila-Parrish, Teaching Assistant Professor, North Carolina State University, Campus Box 7906, Raleigh, No, 27695, United States of America, arvila@ncsu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk will be focused on a project management framework used at North Carolina State University in the Edward P. Fitts Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering's senior design course. We will discuss how project management and lean six methodologies are used to provide a structure to the course, evaluate student progress, and develop a sought after skill set for graduates who go on to OR/ISE careers.
