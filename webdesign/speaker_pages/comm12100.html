Title: An Optimization Model to Improve Resource Utilization in a Cancer Clinic

Author: Anali Huggins, Montana State University, 220 Roberts Hall, Bozeman, MT, 59717, United States of America, anali.huggins@msu.montana.edu

Coauthor(s): David Claudio

Year: 2013

Abstract: The objective of this research is to improve resource utilization at an outpatient Cancer Clinic. An optimization model was developed to maximize infusion chair utilization. The model takes into consideration the balance of human resource workload. This yields a more realistic model that takes into account constraints in physical and mental demand.
