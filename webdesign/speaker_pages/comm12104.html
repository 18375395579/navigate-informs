Title: Optimization and Risk Analysis of Distributed Energy Systems

Author: Shaya Sheikh, Case Western Reserve University, 12000 Fairhill Rd. Apt 406, Cleveland, OH, 44120, United States of America, sxs697@case.edu

Coauthor(s): Behnam Malakooti

Year: 2012

Abstract: Integrated Energy problems representing variety of commonly used energy systems are presented. The presented models are flexible to changes in demand, energy price, and risk attitude of energy providers. A robust and simple approach is used for understanding and measuring risk of energy providers. Computational results show that problems with thousands of entities can be solved efficiently within reasonable time.
