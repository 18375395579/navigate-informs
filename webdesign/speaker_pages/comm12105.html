Title: A Dynamic Model for Multi-criteria Resource Allocation at the Finnish Road Administration
Author: Pekka Mild, Researcher, Helsinki University of Technology, Systems Analysis Laboratory, P.O. Box 1100, TKK, 02015, Finland, pekka.mild@tkk.fi
Coauthor(s): Ahti Salo
Year: 2007
Abstract: Resource allocation decisions in road management have far-reaching impacts on asset values and future service levels. We present an award-winning decision model to help senior managers at the Finnish Road Administration allocate limited appropriations among different kinds of road products. The model gives budget ranges for each product for a 30-year period, based on annual deterioration of asset values, use of incomplete preference information, and computation of optimal maintenance portfolios.
