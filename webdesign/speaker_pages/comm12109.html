Title: Design of Experiments for Simulation Optimization
Author: Hong Wan, Assistant Professor, School of IE, Purdue Univ., 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, hwan@purdue.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this paper, we will discuss efficient design of experiments for simulation optimization. We have proposed a DOE-based simulation optimization framework that has nice convergence property. Based on the framework, we will discuss different designs that are suitable for simulation optimization.
