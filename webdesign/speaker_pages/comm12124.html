Title: Signal Transduction Network Inference from Double Causal Experimental Evidence

Author: Bhaskar DasGupta, Associate Professor, University of Illinois at Chicago, 851 South Morgan Street, Chicago, IL, 60607, United States of America, dasgupta@bert.cs.uic.edu

Coauthor(s): Reka Albert, Riccardo Dondi, Sema Kachalo, Eduardo Sontag, Alex Zelikovsky, Kelly Westbrooks, Ranran Zhang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We introduce a new method of combined synthesis and inference of biological signal transduction networks. We validate the biological usability of our approach by successfully applying it to a previously published signal transduction network and by using it to synthesize and simplify a novel network corresponding to activation induced cell death in large granular lymphocyte leukemia.
