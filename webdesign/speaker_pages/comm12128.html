Title: Predicting Sequential Health Events

Author: Benjamin Letham, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Operations Research Center, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, bletham@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): David Madigan, Cynthia Rudin

Year: 2012

Abstract: Using a database of sequences of health events, we wish to learn a predictor that can use a patient's current medical history to predict future health events. We use ideas from supervised ranking to develop a framework for sequential predictions that allows for a variety of modeling choices. We apply our method to health event data from a clinical trial and demonstrate good performance.
