Title: Decision Analysis Publication Award

Author: James Dyer, Professor, University of Texas-Austin, MSIS Dept, 1 University Sta Stop B6500, Austin, TX, 78712-0212, United States of America, j.dyer@mccombs.utexas.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Decision Analysis Publication Award is given annually to the best decision analysis article or book published two years earlier. To be considered for this year’s award, a work must have been published during 2006. The intent of the award is to recognize the best publication in decision analysis, broadly defined -- including theoretical work on decision analysis methodology, descriptions of applications, and/or experimental studies.
