Title: Determining Reserve Zone Requirements from the Energy and Ancillary Service Co-optimization
Author: Yonghong Chen, Consulting Engineer, MISO, 720 City Center Drive, Carmel, IN, 46077, United States of America, ychen@misoenergy.org
Coauthor(s): Paul Gribik
Year: 2012
Abstract: A new approach was proposed and implemented at MISO to address the reserve deliverability issue. MISO enhanced the co-optimization process so that it can evaluate the impact that deploying cleared reserves on a zonal basis would have on specified transmission constraints. Reserve zone requirements are solved to meet both market-wide reserve requirements and deliverability requirements. MISO is looking at a more comprehensive solution in which procurement of reserves is modeled on a nodal basis.
