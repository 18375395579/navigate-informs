Title: Simulation Toolset for Experimental Environment Research (STEER)
Author: Thomas Jones, NPRST-2222, Memphis, TN, thomas.jones4@navy.mil
Year: 2008
Abstract: STEER is an agent-based simulation platform that uses multiple incentive structures to align individual readiness and resource allocation decisions with organizational Navy objectives. Using an integrated system of agent based simulation-optimization analysis tools, STEER is designed to quantify the impact of varying market-based personnel policies on individual, unit, and enterprise behavior.
