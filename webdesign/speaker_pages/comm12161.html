Title: Revenue Management Planning at Taiwan High Speed Rail
Author: Shaw Ching Chang, Institute for Information Industry, Taipei, Taiwan - ROC, Scchang@iii.org.tw
Coauthor(s): Kwo Chen Wang, Jason C. H. Chen, Wen-Chyuan Chiang
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper describes a multi-leg multi-fare yield management model with consideration of leg-OD based inventory control and service classes. The model captures the operating style of Taiwan High Speed Rail Corporation in which the company adopts it as the basis for its yield management system and data mining.
