Title: Control Strategy for Mitigating Supplier Disruption Effects on a Manufacturing System

Author: Rajesh Krishnamurthy, PhD Candidate, Oklahoma State University, EN 322, School of Industrial Engineering & Mgmt, Stillwater, OK, 74078, United States of America, rajesh.krishnamurthy@okstate.edu

Coauthor(s): Charlene Yauch

Year: 2008

Abstract: Suppliers can create disruptive effects on a manufacturing system, for example through diminishing deliveries over time or in the extreme case going out of business. In this study, simulation model experiments are used to analyze these disruptions and how they can be managed through multi-sourcing.
