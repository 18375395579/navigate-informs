Title: Multiple Criteria Risk Measures for Cybersecurity
Author: Lawrence Bodin, Professor Emeritus, University of Maryland, R. H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, LBodin@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Martin Loeb, Lawrence Gordon
Year: 2008
Abstract: Most procedures that have been developed for assessing risk for Cybersecurity base the analysis on a single criterion. However, assessing risk for Cybersecurity is best viewed in terms of multiple criteria. In this paper, we describe a process for carrying out this multi-criteria analysis and illustrate this process with several examples.
