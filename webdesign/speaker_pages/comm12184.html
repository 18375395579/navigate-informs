Title: Fast Optimal DOE Using Search Engine Technology

Author: Ning Zheng, Graduate Teaching Associate, The Ohio State University, 1971 Neil Avenue, 210 Baker Systems, Columbus, OH, 43210, United States, ningsean@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Theodore Allen

Year: 2006

Abstract: Computational speed has limited the criteria that can be used for generation to be fairly irrelevant to objectives, e.g., D-efficiency. However, if designs can be pre-tabulated, google type search engines promise to instantly generate designs from the most relevant criteria imaginable. We explore this and exploitation of continuous design mathematics. Also, we consider benefits for planning experiments for generalized least squares based analysis.
