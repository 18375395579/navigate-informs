Title: Analyses of Lagrangian Combined with Approximate Dynamic Programming

Author: Diego Klabjan, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, IEMS, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, d-klabjan@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Frank Schneider

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Lagrangian framework can be combined in several ways with approximate dynamic programming algorithm, e.g., Lagrangian can be used for each myopic problem or outside of approximate dynamic programming. We provide various algorithms, compare their respective bounds, and analyze convergence.
