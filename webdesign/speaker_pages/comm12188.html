Title: Research on CPV(Cost Per View) Online Advertising Pricing Model

Author: Haifei Yu, Northeastern Unversity, Shenyang, 110819, China, hfyu@mail.neu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Shoufeng Ji

Year: 2013

Abstract: For CPM pricing model is unable to identify the browsing quality of online advertisements, a novel CPV pricing model is supposed, which is based on the exposure time of advertisements. MATLAB simulation and analysis of factors that influence the advertiser’s and publisher’s model (CPV or CPM) preference are performed. The three factors are orderly ranked by their influence power on the model preference as the browsing time mean, variance, and the browsing quality standard.
