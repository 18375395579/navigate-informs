Title: Providing Decision Support for the Management of Pediatric Asthma Exacerbation in the ED

Author: Szymon Wilk, Post-doctoral Fellow, University of Ottawa, Telfer School of Management, 136 Jean-Jacques Lussier St, Ottawa, ON, K1N 6N5, Canada, wilk@management.uottawa.ca

Coauthor(s): Ken Farion, William Elazmeh, Wojtek Michalowski, Stan Matwin, Dympna O'Sullivan

Year: 2007

Abstract: Asthma exacerbation is prevalent medical reason for children to be brought to the ED. Its clinical management follows complex care pathway that is difficult to evaluate without a comprehensive clinical DSS. We are developing the MET-A3Support-Asthma clinical DSS using a multi-agent framework. This talk describes two agents: a triage agent for early assessment of asthma severity and an evidence-based agent for retrieval of patient-specific evidence from an on-line repository.
