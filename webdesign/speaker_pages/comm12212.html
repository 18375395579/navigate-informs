Title: Integer Programming Models of Pricing Problems with Probabilisitic Choice

Author: Romy Shioda, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue, Waterloo, ON, Canada, rshioda@math.uwaterloo.ca

Coauthor(s): Tuncel Levent

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present mixed-integer programming formulations of several product pricing models using probabilistic customer choice models. In these models, the probability that a customer segment would purchase a given product depends on their reservation prices and the product prices. We present some mathematical properties of these problems and computational experiments.
