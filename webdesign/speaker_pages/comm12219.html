Title: A Metamodel Approach to Simulate Material Handling in Wafer Fabs

Author: Yan Wang, Assistant Professor, University of Central Florida, 4000 Central Florida Blvd., Orlando, FL, 32816, United States of America, wangyan@mail.ucf.edu

Coauthor(s): Ola Batarseh, Dima Nazzal

Year: 2008

Abstract: Simulation is used to model automated material handling systems (AMHS) in wafer fabs. The models produce accurate assessments of performance but are time-consuming to construct and execute. We present a metamodel that provides fast and reasonably accurate estimates of the AMHS response time to move requests. The metamodel is based on a new reliable simulation mechanism that takes essential simulation parameters as intervals to incorporate uncertainties associated with dependency relationships.
