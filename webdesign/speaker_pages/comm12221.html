Title: Developing Federal Resource Allocation Strategies to Mitigate Cross-regional Pandemic Outbreaks
Author: Alex Savachkin, Industrial Engineering, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave, Tampa, FL, United States of America, savachki@eng.usf.edu
Coauthor(s): Tapas Das, Diana Prieto, Andres Uribe
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a simulation based optimization methodology to develop strategies for deploying scarce federal resources (vaccines, anti-viral drugs, medical personnel, temporary hospital beds) during the spread of cross-regional pandemic outbreaks. The model is implemented on a four-city (Tampa, Miami, Jacksonville, and Orlando) outbreak and the effectiveness of the resulting allocation strategies is compared with that from existing governmental distribution policies.
