Title: Predictive Models for Re-Enrollment Management in Higher Education Industry

Author: Tao Wu, Operations Research Scientist, University of Phoenix, 4025 S Riverpoint Pkwy, CF-K301, Phoenix, AZ, 85040, United States of America, tao.wu@phoenix.edu

Coauthor(s): Roger Gung

Year: 2012

Abstract: We developed Logistic Regression models in predicting the likelihood that a withdrawn student will re-enroll with academic success. Using students?geographical, academic, and financial information as predictive drivers, the models achieve a 0.84 C-statistic value on average on performance. The models have been rolled out with re-enrollment management team at University of Phoenix.
