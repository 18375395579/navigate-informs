Title: An Integrated Operating Room Scheduling System

Author: Shailendra Jain, Distinguished Technologist & Project Manager, Hewlett-Packard Laboratories, 1501 Page Mill Road, Bldg 1U, MS 1132, Palo Alto, CA, 94304, United States of America, Shailendra.Jain@hp.com

Coauthor(s): Haiyan Wang, Sharad Singhal, Cipriano Santos, Jaap Suermondt, Dr. Karl Sylvester, Enis Kayis, Meghna Patel, Dr. RJ Ramamurthi

Year: 2012

Abstract: The operating room is a critical resource in the hospital, and OR scheduling is a challenging and critical problem. We report on a joint project with Lucile Packard Children’s Hospital to develop an OR planning and scheduling system, which is based on optimization and simulation models.
