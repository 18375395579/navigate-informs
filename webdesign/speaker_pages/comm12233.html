Title: Joint Force Mix Analysis in OSD(PA&E)
Author: John Duke, Operations Research Analyst, Northrop Grumman Corporation, 713 Fontaine St., Alexandria, VA, 22032, United States of America, john.duke.ctr@osd.mil
Year: 2007
Abstract: Over the course of the Operational Availability series of studies, the Simulation and Analysis Center (SAC), OSD (PA&E), in collaboration with the Joint Staff, CoComs, and Services, has conducted joint force mix analysis in order to assess how well joint force structure sources the full range of military operations required by the US defense strategy. The purpose of this briefing is to update analysts on the state of the models used to conduct this analysis.
