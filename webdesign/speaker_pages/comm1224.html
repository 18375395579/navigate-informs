Title: Abductive and Inductive Reasoning in Service-system Decision Making

Author: Ralph Badinelli, Professor, Virginia Tech, Dept. of Business Information Technology, Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, ralphb@vt.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper begins with a description of the Viable Systems Approach (VSA) as a framework for modeling service systems. The process of abductive/inductive reasoning guides the epistemology and decision-making of agents in a viable service system. We investigate the use of fuzzy logic and fuzzy control systems to model abductive/inductive reasoning. We define different forms of viability and establish conditions for viability based on the performance of fuzzy controls.
