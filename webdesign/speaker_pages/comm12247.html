Title: A Run-to-Run Controller for Product Surface Quality Improvement

Author: Lulu Bao, PhD Candidate, Tsinghua University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Beijing, 100084, China, baoll11@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Kaibo Wang

Year: 2013

Abstract: The surface quality of silicon wafers has a great impact on the subsequent processes. The geometric requirement for the wafer surface is smooth and flat; the thickness is expected to fall within certain specification limits. To achieve the requirement through control, we build a Gaussian-Kriging model, considering the spatial correlation of observations on the wafer, to link the wafer quality with the process variable, and then develop a new run-to-run controller to generate the optimal recipe.
