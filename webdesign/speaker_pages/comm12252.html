Title: Foundations of Social Network Ad Optimization
Author: John Turner, Assistant Professor, University of California - Irvine, The Paul Merage School of Business, Room SB 338, Irvine, CA, 92697-3125, United States of America, john.turner@uci.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We introduce revenue optimization models for placing ads in social networks, motivated by the connectivity structure of the underlying graph. We discuss some pros and cons of the underlying models, and illustrate our approach using real social graphs.
