Title: Tractable Functional Response Modelling with Processes with Non-Separable Covariance Functions

Author: Matthew Plumlee, Ph.D. Student, The Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, mplumlee@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Roshan Vengazhiyil

Year: 2012

Abstract: Gaussian Process models have become increasingly popular to model functional responses. However, fitting these models requires computationally expensive matrix inversion or decomposition, a problem exacerbated with functional responses. Separability and stationarity assumptions on the covariance function of the GP model to ease the computational burden. This work proposes a method to model these functions without the separability assumption while maintaining the computational advantages.
