Title: Market Demand Analysis by using the Market-data Envelopment Analysis Model, The Case Study: Analysis

Author: Mehdi Ahmadpanah, smapanah@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Maryam Ghanizadeh

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper introduces a market demand analysis model using the Market-Data Envelopment Analysis technique. M-DEA is a new approach that initially uses the AHP technique to extract the main and affecting parameters on different regions?demand and then according to the extracted parameters, it assesses market demand in each region. Among the characteristics of this model is that the effective parameters are considered as a combination to evaluate the market volume of different regions.
