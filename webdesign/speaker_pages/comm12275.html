Title: Multivariate Process Capability Analysis with Non-Parametric Bootstrap Method

Author: Wenzhen Huang, Assistant Professor, University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, Dartmouth, MA, United States of America, whuang@umassd.edu

Coauthor(s): Ankit Pahwa, Zhenyu (James) Kong

Year: 2008

Abstract: A method is proposed to analyze multivariate process capability (MPC). For a MP process the widely used Cp, Cpk for individuals do not provide complete conformance information. Yield is used for simultaneous conformity in MPC evaluation. A non-parametric bootstrap method is developed, i.e., approximating the pdf by sampled data and a kernel model and sampling the pdf for yield estimation. A case study compares the estimated yield from 50 samples with the benchmark from a Beta distribution.
