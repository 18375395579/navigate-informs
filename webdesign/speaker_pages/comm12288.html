Title: Planning for Disruptions in Facility Location Models

Author: Kaiyue Zheng, University of Wisconsin-Madison, Industrial & Systems Engineering, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, kzheng23@wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Laura McLay

Year: 2013

Abstract: To hedge against the risk of interdictions to facility systems, planners may fortify the existing network to make it more reliable, or design a network that is resilient to disruptions. We present models addressing both topics, including a defender-interdictor location model with uncertain futures, and a location design model with heuristic and optimal interdictions. Algorithms are discussed for both models.
