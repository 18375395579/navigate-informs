Title: The 2012 Update to the Airport Capacity Benchmarks
Author: Jennifer Gentry, Lead Analyst, CAASD/MITRE Corporation, 7515 Colshire Avenue, McLean, VA, 22102-7539, United States of America, jenniferg@mitre.org
Year: 2012
Abstract: The FAA has updated its Airport Capacity Benchmark Report. The report, first published in 2001 and updated in 2004, presents benchmark capacity ranges derived from Facility Reported Rates and modeled capacities. The 2012 update was motivated by changes in aviation, construction of new runways, and improved modeling techniques.
