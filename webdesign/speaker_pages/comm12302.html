Title: Optimal Investment in Credit Derivatives Portfolio under Contagion Risk

Author: Agostino Capponi, Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, MD, United States of America, acappon1@jhu.edu

Coauthor(s): Lijun Bo

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the optimal portfolio problem of a power investor who wishes to allocate her wealth between several credit default swaps (CDSs), a stock, and a money market account. We model contagion risk using a Markovian model with interacting default intensities. We establish a lattice dependence structure between the HJB equations associated with the default states of the portfolio. We assess the impact of default contagion on size and directionality of the investor strategy.
