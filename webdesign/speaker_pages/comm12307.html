Title: Network-Based Facility Location for Community Health Centers
Author: Christina Scherrer, Assistant Professor, Southern Polytechnic State University, 1100 S. Marietta Parkway, Atlanta, GA, 30060, United States, cscherre@spsu.edu
Coauthor(s): Paul Griffin, Julie Swann
Year: 2006
Abstract: We determine the best location and medical service offerings for Community Health Centers (CHCs) using a network-based facility location approach. We expand upon an earlier model to consider weighted disease objectives and compare locations chosen by our model to existing locations based on several public health measures. We also use the model to analyze different healthcare access and coverage policy mechanisms for cost and effectiveness.
