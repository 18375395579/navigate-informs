Title: A Solution to the Group Decision Problem
Author: Ralph Keeney, Fuqua School of Business, 101 Lombard St, #704W, San Francisco, CA, 94111, United States of America, keeneyr@aol.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: All previous attempts to develop a decision analysis model for group decisions made the implicit assumption that group members had the same decision frame for their joint decision problem. Accounting for member's different frames, a general prescriptive model for group decisions is developed consistent with standard decision analysis assumptions.
