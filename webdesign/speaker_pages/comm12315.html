Title: Risk-sensitive Reinforcement Learning

Author: Abhijit Gosavi, University at Buffalo, SUNY, 317 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, United States, agosavi@buffalo.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Concepts such as variance and downside risk have been used by economists as measures of risk. In the field of artificial intelligence, the use of risk measures is a relatively new development. We present some reinforcement learning algorithms that employ risk measures, along with some numerical results from industrial problems.
