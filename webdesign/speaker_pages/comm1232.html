Title: Using Large-scale Traffic Simulation Models to Inform the Design of Vehicle Sharing Networks

Author: Carolina Osorio, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, 1-232, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, osorioc@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Krishna Selvam, Cynthia Barnhart, Virot Chiraphadhanakul, Erica Klampfl, Yimin Liu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We develop an optimization framework to inform the design of vehicle sharing networks. The framework embeds an urban traffic simulation model, which provides a detailed description of how modal shifts impact both link and network-wide traffic metrics. We use the framework to identify locations for vehicle sharing stations that lead to maximal improvements in network-wide metrics.
