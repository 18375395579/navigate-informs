Title: The Fjord Motor Custom Fleet Pricing Exercise
Author: Robert Phillips, Nomis Solutions, robert.phillips@nomissolutions.com
Coauthor(s): J. Michael Harrison
Year: 2006
Abstract: Students are given data on 4000 past bids for fleet sales to police departments and corporations. Maximum likelihood is used to fit logit bid-response models at three levels of refinement. A second layer of Solver application gives the optimal pricing strategy under each bid-response model.
