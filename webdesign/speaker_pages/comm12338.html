Title: Assignment of Network Services Considering Risk

Author: Abdullah Konak, Assistant Professor, Penn State - Berks, Tulpehocken Road, Reading, PA, 19610, United States, konak@psu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Most networks today use the client-service paradigm to provide services to users in distributed manner. In this model, an entity that uses a service called client and an entity that provides service(s) is called server. This work introduces the problem of assigning network services to network nodes to minimize a risk measure in the case of catastrophic events.
