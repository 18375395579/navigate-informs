Title: Copulas-based Dependent Decision Lattice Approach
Author: Tianyang Wang, Assistant Professor, Colorado State University, Finance and Real Estate Department, Fort Collins, CO, United States of America, tianyang.wang@business.colostate.edu
Coauthor(s): Joe Hahn, James Dyer
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper we present a general framework of a copulas-based dependent decision lattice approach for correlated uncertainties. The proposed dependent decision lattice model allows multiple dependent uncertainties with arbitrary marginal distributions to be represented in a recombining tree structure. This general framework significantly reduce the size of the tree for practical applications in decision analysis, real options valuations, and financial derivatives valuation.
