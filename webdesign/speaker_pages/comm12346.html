Title: Linear Stochastic Systems Model for Counter-Insurgency Strategies

Author: Patrick Driscoll, Professor, U.S. Military Academy, Department of Systems Engineering, Building 752, Mahan Hall, West Point, NY, 10996, United States, patrick.driscoll@usma.edu

Coauthor(s): Niki Goerger

Year: 2006

Abstract: We introduce a linear dynamic model that uses the evolving effects of system inter-dependencies on state and process conditions to suggests effective alternative counter-insurgency strategies based on stochastic intertemporal effects.
