Title: Demand Forecasting in Service Industries

Author: Kin Ming Kam, Graduate student, The University of Texas at Arlington, 500 West First Street, Arlington, TX, 76019, United States of America, kinming.kam@mavs.uta.edu

Coauthor(s): Li Zeng

Year: 2013

Abstract: Demand forecasting is a critical problem in service planning and management. This study develops a Bayesian dynamic modeling approach for demand forecasting and compares it to the popular ARIMA modeling methods using data from transportation and healthcare services.
