Title: Canadian Energy Supply and Demand with ENERGY 2020: Scenarios and Sensitivity Cases
Author: Matthew Hansen, Market Analyst, National Energy Board, 444-7th Ave SW, Calgary, AB, T3C 0T1, Canada, Matthew.Hansen@neb-one.gc.ca
Year: 2012
Abstract: The National Energy Board periodically releases long-term projections of Canadian energy supply and demand as part of its Energy Futures series. Since 2007, the ENERGY 2020 model has been central to this analysis. This presentation will describe how the National Energy Board uses ENERGY 2020 to create the scenarios and sensitivity cases featured in the Energy Futures reports.
