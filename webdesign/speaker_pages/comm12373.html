Title: A Bayesian Approach to Output Analysis of Limit Standards

Author: Roy Creasey, Assistant Professor, Longwood University, 201 High Street, Farmville, VA, 23901, United States of America, creaseyrr@longwood.edu

Coauthor(s): Preston White

Year: 2007

Abstract: Limit standards have been defined as probabilistic requirements that specify confidence on the maximum proportion of simulation runs yielding values of an output variable above or below a desired threshold. Typical analysis methods utilize the binomial distribution. This research investigates the relationship between the binomial and beta distributions, and develops a Bayesian sequential approach to analysis of limit standards.
