Title: Optimal Resource Allocation for Multi-level Preventive Care for Type 2 Diabetes
Author: Karca Aral, PhD Candidate, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77300, France, karca.aral@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Stephen E. Chick, Alfons Grabosch
Year: 2013
Abstract: We propose a mathematical model for T2 Diabetes that comprehends the interactions of multiple interventions including primary, secondary, and tertiary prevention. We use the model to design an optimal preventive care policy. We compare the model outcomes with real-life recommendations, and provide insights on multi-level chronic disease prevention.
