Title: US Army Equipment Sourcing Decisions for Arforgen Reset

Author: Jeff Eaton, Manager, Operations Research, Concurrent Technologies Corporation, 9570 Regency Square Blvd., Suite 400, Jacksonville, FL, 32225, United States of America, eatonj@ctc.com

Coauthor(s): Jake Enholm

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation will review the assumptions and methodology used to create the Prototype Logistics Innovation Agency’s RESET Equipment Sourcing model. This model aims to provide an equipment sourcing plan that seeks to meet unit readiness goals in the ARFORGEN process across multiple types of equipment sources including procurement, Depot maintenance, and equipment pools.
