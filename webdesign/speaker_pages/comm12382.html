Title: Modelling Army Force Generation

Author: Josh Klimas, Operations Research Analyst, Center for Army Analysis, 6001 Goethals Rd, Ft. Belvoir, VA, 22060, United States of America, joshua.klimas@us.army.mil

Year: 2007

Abstract: The Army manages its force structure through the Army Force Generation process. The Center for Army Analysis first developed the MARATHON simulation to analyze Army Force Generation for the 2005 Quadrennial Defense Review. MARATHON is now used for a wide range of analytical efforts concerning force sizing and management. This presentation describes Army Force Generation, the modeling approach that we employ in MARATHON, and some results from our analyses.
