Title: Efficient Learning of Donor Retention Strategies for the American Red Cross

Author: Bin Han, University of Maryland, College Park, MB, United States of America, danielh@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Ilya Ryzhov, Boris Defourny

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a new sequential decision model for allocating campaign budget for the American Red Cross. The outcome--attributes relation is modeled using linear regression. We derive the first simulation procedure for learning unknown regression parameters and sampling noise. We apply convex approximation with a quantization procedure and semidefinte programming relaxation to reduce the computational complexity. Simulation experiments demonstrate the efficient performance of the approximation.
