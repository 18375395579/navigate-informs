Title: Prediction Aided Bed Management Decision Support System
Author: Bex Thomas, Researcher, GE Global Research, 1 Research Circle, Niskayuna, NY, 12019, United States of America, thomasb@ge.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Bed management is an important function of any hospital with a major impact on patient care, patient flow and ultimately the hospital operating margin. We demonstrate the use of simulation based capacity prediction models to proactively manage bed assignments.
