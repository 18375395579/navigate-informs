Title: Predictive Environmental Analysis System

Author: Jayant Kalagnanam, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, P.O. Box 218, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, jayant@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Stuart Siegel, Sayan Ranu, Jun Zhang, Lun Zhong

Year: 2013

Abstract: We highlight the use of spatio temporal data for building predictive models for air quality, dengue, and weather. The sources of data are multi modal such as radar, air quality and weather monitoring stations. The aggregation this information semantically to build statistical and decision models is discussed.
