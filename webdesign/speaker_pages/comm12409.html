Title: Quantitative Intelligence Analysis
Author: Christopher Smith, PhD Student, U.S. Army/ University of Virginia, 1044 Autumn Hill Ct, Crozet, VA, 22932, United States of America, christopher.m.smith@us.army.mil
Coauthor(s): William Scherer, Stephen Carr
Year: 2012
Abstract: A methodology is proposed to supplement the subjective “value?of intelligence with an objective metric of “impact?of intelligence on network models. This metric would measure the impact of a piece of intelligence on a network’s metric, being used to answer analyst intelligence questions or gain insight on a network. Our methodology suggests that on an intelligence driven network, each document can bring information in the form of unique links and nodes which can be measured quantitatively.
