Title: Estimating Choice Models from Airline Data

Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States, gjv1@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Gustavo Vulcano

Year: 2006

Abstract: We look at the results of an empirical study of estimating choice models from available airline data. We discuss both estimation methods and how the estimated choice models can be used to improve revenue management.
