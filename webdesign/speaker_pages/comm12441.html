Title: Forecasting Promotional Sales using Linear Regression Models

Author: Usha Ramanathan, PhD Candidate, Nottingham University Business School, Jubilee Campus, Wollaton Road, Nottingham, NG81BB, United Kingdom, lixur@nottingham.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Bart MacCarthy, Luc Muyldermans

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study CPFR arrangements between a large soft drink manufacturer and major retailers in the UK. The manufacturer’s sales are strongly driven by the promotions executed in the retail stores. We develop a conceptual reference demand model, which lists the important factors and events that explain demand. These factors are used in multiple linear regression forecasting models to predict the weekly retailers?sales.
