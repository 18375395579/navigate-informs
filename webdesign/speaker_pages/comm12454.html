Title: Exploiting Network Characteristics and Driver Knowledge for Effective Traffic Routing

Author: Srinivas Peeta, Professor, Purdue University, Civil Engineering, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, West Lafayette, IN, 47907-2051, United States, peeta@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Alexander Paz

Year: 2007

Abstract: Network and behavioral characteristics are exploited looking for opportunities to enhance the effectiveness of a behavior-consistent traffic routing framework while testing the sensitivity of the model to the use of different control paradigms. Different information-based control paradigms are formulated and experiments are conducted to compare the effectiveness and stability of the behavior-consistent framework.
