Title: The Use of Spies in Strategic Situations

Author: Scott Provan, Professor, Univiversity of North Carolina, Dept. of Stat. and Operations Research, CB#3260, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599, United States of America, Scott_Provan@UNC.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The use of spies is a valuable tool in strategic situations, and it is important to gain a deeper understanding of the underlying framework of spying. We study the use of spies in 2-person matrix games. We formulate the context of a spying in such games, and model a number of situations involving single or multiple types of spies, spies for one or both sides, and the use of decoy strategies to mislead spies. Computational studies indicate the advantage of using spies under various scenarios.
