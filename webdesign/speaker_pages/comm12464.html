Title: A Mathematical Model for Evaluating the System Impact of EV Demand

Author: Yueyue Fan, Associate Professor, University of California-Davis, One Shield Ave., Davis, CA, United States of America, yyfan@ucdavis.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, we will present a network model that captures the spatial interdependence of existing power supply and demand. This model will be used to evaluate the economic and environmental impacts of increasing EV demand in a California case study.
