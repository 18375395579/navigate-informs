Title: Group LASSO-based Variable Selection Method for Data Envelopment Analysis

Author: Song Irene, Columbia University, Columbia University, 120 Street, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, irene.j.song@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Tony Qin

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, we propose a data-driven variable selection method based on group least absolute shrinkage and selection operator (LASSO) for data envelopment analysis (DEA). We test our method against two of the most widely-used variable selection methods - efficiency contribution measure (ECM) and regression-based test - in DEA using Monte Carlo simulations and report on its performance, which compares favorably to that of the other two methods.
