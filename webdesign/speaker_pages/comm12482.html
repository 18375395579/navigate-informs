Title: Application of Online DTA Framework for Weather-related Traffic Management

Author: Hani S. Mahmassani, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, masmah@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Ying Chen, Jiwon Kim, Lan Jiang

Year: 2012

Abstract: Weather-related traffic management through variable message signs, speed control and active demand management is an important application for predictive traffic estimation and prediction based on online DTA deployments. A framework and associated methodology is presented and demonstrated through several applications.
