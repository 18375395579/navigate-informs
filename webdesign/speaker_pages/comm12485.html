Title: Recommendations Using Information from Multiple Association Rules: A Probabilistic Approach

Author: Abhijeet Ghoshal, University of Minnesota, 19th Av. S., Minneapolis, United States of America, abhijeet@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Sumit Sarkar, Syam Menon

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we propose a method to improve the quality of recommendations made using association rules. This is accomplished by combining rules when possible using a technique strongly grounded in probability theory. Combining rules requires identification of the best combination of rules. We use a maximum-likelihood framework to compare alternative combinations. We empirically show that our proposed approach performs better than several existing rule based and non-rule based approaches.
