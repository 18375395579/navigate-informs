Title: Transformation of HP Business Model through Advanced Analytics and Operations Research

Author: Suresh Subraman, Hewlett-Packard, San Diego, CA, United States of America, Suresh.Subramanian@hp.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: So that Hewlett-Packard customers can shop anywhere anytime, HPDirect.com developed an ECommerce value chain across financial, marketing, warehouse planning and operations departments. Key drivers impacting online traffic were identified and quantified by time series forecasts and regression models. Their holistic customer targeting engine doubled purchase conversion rates and increased order sizes up to 30% -thanks to Bayesian modeling, Markov analysis and Linear Discriminant analysis.
