Title: An Ensemble Approach to the Netflix Prize
Author: Brian Almquist, Management Sciences Department, The University of Iowa, S204 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52241, United States, brian-almquist@uiowa.edu
Coauthor(s): Nick Street, Xin Ying Qiu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Netflix is offering cash prizes for improvements to their method for predicting customer movie ratings, based on a huge training corpus. We present a machine learning approach to the problem using only a customer's ratings of other movies. We generalize across movies with a knowledge-transfer method based on predictive ensembles.
