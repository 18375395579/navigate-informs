Title: Estimating Multiple Objectives in Data Mining
Author: John Sparks, University of Illinois, Chicago, Information and Decision Sciences, Chicago, IL, 60607, United States, jspark4@uic.edu
Coauthor(s): Sid Bhattacharyya
Year: 2007
Abstract: Many data-mining situations require estimation of multiple objectives. For instance, predict which prospects will become customers, and which customers will provide more value. This study compares the performance of different statistical methods of modeling for multiple objectives using six different files from the database marketing arena.
