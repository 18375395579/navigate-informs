Title: Optimal Learning with Local Parametric Models

Author: Bolong Cheng, Princeton University, Sherrerd Hall, Princeton, NJ, 08540, United States of America, bcheng@princeton.edu

Coauthor(s): Warren Powell

Year: 2013

Abstract: We are interested in maximizing a general function where observations are noisy and may be expensive. We derive a knowledge gradient policy, which chooses measurements which maximize the expected value of information, while using a locally parametric belief model which uses linear approximations with radial basis functions. Our technique accounts for correlation within adjacent subsets of the alternatives and does not pose any a priori assumption on the global shape of the underlying function.
