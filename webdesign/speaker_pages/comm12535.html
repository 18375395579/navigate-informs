Title: Water Management: Optimal planning and allocation of renewable natural resource
Author: Karthik Murali, PhD Candidate, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 350 Wohlers Hall, Urbana, IL, 61820, United States of America, kmurali4@illinois.edu
Coauthor(s): Nicholas Petruzzi, Michael Lim
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study the optimal supply management problem for the manager of a renewable aquifer. The manager must determine the optimal level of water transfers between municipalities and investment in water production technology. Using a stylized model, we determine the optimal policy for a profit or welfare-maximizing manager, and compare their impacts using the Triple Bottom Line framework. We obtain managerial insights and draw implications for policy related to sustainable water management.
