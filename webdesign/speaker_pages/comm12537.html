Title: Estimating Costs of Self-sustaining Logistics Networks

Author: Eva Regnier, Naval Postgraduate School, 699 Dyer Road, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, eregnier@nps.edu

Coauthor(s): Jay Simon

Year: 2012

Abstract: New logistics networks in disaster response and undeveloped military theaters may need to be self-sustaining. We present methods for 1) designing a distribution network based on simple planning scenarios and 2) estimating costs to deliver fuel and other supplies, for use in evaluating conservation and alternative energy investments.
