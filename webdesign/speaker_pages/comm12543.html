Title: High-frequency Trading in the Presence of Long-term Stochastic Dependencies

Author: Alexander Galenko, Graduate Student, University of Texas at Austin, Department of Mechanical Engineering, 1 University Station C2200, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, agalenko@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Ivilina Popova, Elmira Popova

Year: 2007

Abstract: We model the long term stochastic dependencies via co-integration and prove a necessary and sufficient conditions theorem. This theoretical result is then used to construct a trading strategy which performance will be presented.
