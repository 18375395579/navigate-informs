Title: Decision Analysis in the Oil and Gas Industry: From Exploration to Development and Production

Author: Lev Virine, Schlumberger, 600, 322-11 Avenue SW, Calgary, AB, Canada, lvirine@slb.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Strategic decision-making in the oil and gas industry involves a comprehensive analysis of uncertainties starting from exploration and propagated to field development and production. The decision and risk analysis workflow implemented by Schlumberger includes analysis of uncertainties at various stages of geophysical and geological exploration, reservoir simulation using multiple scenarios, and decision analysis based on economic evaluation of reserves with uncertainties.
