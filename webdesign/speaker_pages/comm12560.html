Title: A Robust CVaR Portfolio Allocation Model Using Option Prices
Author: Luis Zuluaga, Assistant Professor, University of New Brunswick, PO Box 4400, Fredericton, NB, E3B 5A3, Canada, lzuluaga@unb.ca
Coauthor(s): Juan Vera, Javier Pena
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present a novel linear programming portfolio allocation model in which worst-case Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR) is used as the risk measure. The worst-case CVaR is computed by considering underlying asset price probability distributions that replicate the observed prices of vanilla options.
