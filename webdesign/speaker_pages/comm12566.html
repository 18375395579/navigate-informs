Title: Reducing Infection Transmission in the ICU: Using a System Level Model

Author: Shreyas Limaye, University of Washington, Industrial Engineering, Box 352650, Seattle, WA, 98116-2650, United States, shreyas@u.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Christina Mastrangelo

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study takes a systemic view at the operational level of ICU in order to reduce transmission of Hospital-associated infections. It suggests an integrated methodology that combines cognitive modeling, simulation, risk analysis and generalized linear models to improve the quality of healthcare provided at the Children’s Hospital, Seattle.
