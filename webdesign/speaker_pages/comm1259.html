Title: Social Influence Network Structures: Determination and Detection

Author: James Andrew Leinart, United States Air Force, 1570 Air Force Pentagon, Washington, DC, 20330-1570, United States of America, James.Leinart@pentagon.af.mil

Coauthor(s): Dick Deckro

Year: 2008

Abstract: A causal analysis approach to determining potential social influence network structures is presented. The method permits not only the identification of relationships between network individuals, but also the possibility of detecting hidden individuals. An illustrative example using Congressional members?voting records is provided.
