Title: Recent Advances on the Solution of the DMDGP
Author: Andrea Cassioli, Ecole Polytechnique, Route de Saclay, Palaiseau, 91128, France, cassioli@lix.polytechnique.fr
Coauthor(s): Leo Liberti, Antonio Mucherino, Carlile Lavor
Year: 2013
Abstract: The DMDGP is a subclass of the DDGP in which the pecularities of the geometric structure of molecule are exploited. The use of a discrete search algorithm, the interval Branch&Prune (iBP), allows to enumerate all the possible solution of the problem. We will present recent extensiona to the iBP framework: (1) the use of Clifford Algebra to improve the discretization of distances defined as continuous intervals; (2) the introduction of pruning devices specific for molecules.
