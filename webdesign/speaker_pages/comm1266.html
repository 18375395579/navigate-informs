Title: Modeling and Measuring Operational Process Risk

Author: Eric Cope, IBM Zurich Research Lab, Saeumerstrasse 4, Rueschlikon, 8135, Switzerland, erc@zurich.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Jochen Kuester, Dominik Etzweiler, Bonnie Ray, Lea Deleris

Year: 2008

Abstract: A large class of operational risks can be thought of as threats to business process objectives. We discuss how formal metamodels can incorporate risk-relevant information directly into business process models, and how those models can be transformed into quantitative models for estimating risk to process objectives. Various quantitative methods, including Bayesian networks and discrete-event simulation will be discussed, in the context of HR processes and IT infrastructure risk
