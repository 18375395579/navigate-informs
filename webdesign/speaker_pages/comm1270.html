Title: Understanding Client Preferences for Preventive Care

Author: Lijie Song, MIT-Zaragoza International Logistics Program, Edificio Náyade, 5 C/ Bari 55, PLAZA, Zaragoza, 50197, Spain, lsong@zlc.edu.es

Coauthor(s): Vedat Verter, Beste Kucukyazici

Year: 2012

Abstract: We studied a government-funded breast cancer screening program in Montreal, using stated preference discrete choice modeling as framework. This enables us to better understand the clients' preference concerning facility attributes. Incorporating our findings in a simulation model, we are able to generate managerial insights on how to improve the participation to such programs.
