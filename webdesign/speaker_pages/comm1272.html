Title: The Long Road to Online Conversion: A Model of Multi-channel Attribution

Author: Vibhanshu Abhishek, Assistant Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, 4800 Forbes Avenue, Hamburg Hall, Pittsburgh, Pe, 15213, United States of America, vibs@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: A conversion may be the result of a series of ads that were displayed to the consumer across multiple channels. In this paper, we address the problem of attribution using a unique data set from an online campaign. We present a HMM of an individual consumer's behavior based on the concept of a conversion funnel. We observe that different ad formats, affect the consumers differently based on the state they are in. We also present an attribution scheme based on the proposed model.
