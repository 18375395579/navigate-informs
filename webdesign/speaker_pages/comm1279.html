Title: Bounded Rationality and Forecast Information Sharing
Author: Fei Gao, University of Pennsylvania, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, United States of America, feigao@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Xuanming Su
Year: 2013
Abstract: We develop a model of bounded rationality based on iterative thinking. We apply the model to study forecast information sharing in supply chains. Our results shed light on how trust between supply chain partners forms and evolves over time.
