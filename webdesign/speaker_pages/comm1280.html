Title: Airline Customers?Searching and Purchasing Behavior
Author: Misuk Lee, PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of Industrial and Systems Engr., Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, mlee@gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Laurie Garrow, David Post
Year: 2008
Abstract: Markov models are use to analyze how individuals search for information about air ticket attributes and prices using data from a website that employs a unique strategy for selling tickets at low prices to travelers with a high degree of travel flexibility. Results show search intensity is highly dependent on the relative discount level and there is a high degree of correlation observed across screens, that is customers are seen to fixate on specific attributes of the search process.
