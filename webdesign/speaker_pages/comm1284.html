Title: A Cusum Control Chart for Detecting a Varying Shift in the Proportion of Failures

Author: Jonghyun Ryu, Purdue University, 315 N. Grant st., West lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, ryuj@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Hong Wan, Sujin Kim

Year: 2007

Abstract: A new cumulative sum (CUSUM) chart is developed for unknown future shifts in the proportion of failures. The chart is based on counts between failures which can be modeled by a geometric distribution. A geometric based model is widely used for monitoring healthcare procedures. To detect a varying shift, we estimate the current failure rate and update the CUSUM statistic continuously. The simulation results show its potential to perform more effectively than other well-established methods.
