Title: Autoregressive Approach to Synchronization among Multiple Components of Complex Integrated System
Author: Alla Kammerdiner, ISE, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, United States of America, alla.ua@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Lisa Maillart, Nagi Gebraeel
Year: 2008
Abstract: A novel approach for studying synchrony in complex integrated systems is proposed based on multivariate statistical analysis by modeling cointegrated vector autoregressive processes. The method is illustrated by applying it to multichannel electroencephalogram recordings.
