Title: IPOPT in a Software Package for Dynamic Optimization
Author: Yi-Dong Lang, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, lang@andrew.cmu.edu
Coauthor(s): Andreas Waechter, Lorenz Biegler
Year: 2006
Abstract: A software package, DynoPC, uses IPOPT as the NLP solver to solve discretized dynamic optimization problems simultaneously. It invokes ADOL-C to evaluate constraints, Jacobians and Hessians required by IPOPT, based on user-supplied models. The package has been updated according to new developments of IPOPT, and tested on challenging examples.
