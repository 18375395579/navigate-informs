Title: On-line Surface Defect Detection in Rolling Process for Quality Improvement
Author: Ran Jin, Research Assistant, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave., Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, jinr@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Jing Li, Jianjun (Jan) Shi
Year: 2007
Abstract: This research develops a method for surface defect detection based on the image data obtained from rolling production lines. This method first uses a multiscale Principal Component Analysis to remove the noisy texture of the images and then formulates a searching procedure to locate the defects.
