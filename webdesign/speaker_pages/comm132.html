Title: Nonstationary System Prediction using Dirichlet Process based Symbolic Transition Analysis
Author: Satish Bukkapatnam, Professor, Oklahoma State University, 322 Engineering North, Oklahoma State University, Stillwater, OK, United States of America, satish.t.bukkapatnam@okstate.edu
Coauthor(s): Changqing Cheng, Zhenyu (James) Kong, Zimo Wang
Year: 2013
Abstract: Multi-step clustering prediction is investigated using Dirichlet process based symbolic dynamic analysis. Symbolic sequence is constructed based on Dirichlet process clustering. Our case studies show that this approach outperforms conventional models for multi-step clustering prediction for non-stationary systems.
