Title: Fast Compartmental MC Simulation of Population Balance Models: Application to Nanoparticle Formation

Author: Roberto Irizarry, DuPont Electronic Technologies, Circleville, OH, United States of America, Roberto.Irizarry@dupont.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: A compartmental Monte Carlo (CMC) algorithm is introduced for the simulation of nanoparticle formation in spatially heterogeneous systems. The CMC is utilized to study large reactors using compartment models to simulate the fluid flow. The CMC ability to track single events was utilized to study the impact of highly turbulent zones on the final particle size distribution. The computational speed of this algorithm allows the solution of problems that may be intractable otherwise.
