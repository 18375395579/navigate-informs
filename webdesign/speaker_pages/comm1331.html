Title: Pricing Discretely Monitored Lookback Options in Levy Process Models
Author: Liming Feng, Assistant Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S Mathews Ave, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States, fenglm@uiuc.edu
Coauthor(s): Vadim Linetsky
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this talk, we present an efficient method for pricing discretely monitored lookback option in Levy process models. The method involves a sequential evaluation of Hilbert transforms. A discrete approximation with exponentially decaying errors is developed based on the Whittaker cardinal series for functions analytic in a strip in the complex plane.
