Title: Stochastic Optimization and Robust Ancillary Services Determination for Electricity System Operation

Author: Alberto Lamadrid, Assistant Professor, Lehigh University, 621 Taylor Street, room 451, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, ajlamadrid@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Tim Mount, Ray Zimmerman, Carlos Murillo-Sanchez

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper formulates a receding horizon framework, for System Operators in their daily operations, in the context of high penetration of renewable energy sources (RES). The model has an electricity network system operator seeking to maximize the welfare of consumers and producers, procuring energy and ancillary services in a robust manner using a security constrained Optimal Power Flow (SC-OPF).
