Title: Product/Service Quality and Loyalty in a Korean Auto Company

Author: Hosun Rhim, Korea University, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, hrhim@korea.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Hye Jeong Bae, Kwangtae Park

Year: 2013

Abstract: We measure service quality based on service process, and examine relationships among product/service quality, and product/service loyalty. We develop a construct to evaluate process based service quality. We collect data from customers of auto maintenance centers to measure perceived quality and loyalty. We test relationships between constructs using a structural equation model and regression analysis.
