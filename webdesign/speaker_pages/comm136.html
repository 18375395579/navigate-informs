Title: Semantic Reasoning Over Big Data for Condition Based Maintenance and Mission Based Forecasting

Author: Wes Regian, Principal Knowledge Engineer, Concurrent Technologies Corporation, 15091 Alabama Highway 20, Suite A, Madison, AL, 35756, United States of America, regianj@ctc.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: CBM as practiced today ignores impending mission characteristics. Brigade Aviation Maintenance Officers (BAMOs) on the other hand, predict parts needs for upcoming mission characteristics based on failures observed in the past under similar mission conditions. Semantic analysis offers the possibility of machine reasoning to combine knowledge of MBF with CBM ?supporting BAMO-like machine reasoning in combination with CBM capabilities.
