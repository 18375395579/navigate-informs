Title: Utilizing Anonymous Vehicle Location Data to Derive Freight Origin-destination Information
Author: Crystal Jones, Transportation Specialist, US DOT Federal Highway Administration, 1200 New Jersey Ave, Washington, DC, 20590, United States of America, Crystal.Jones@dot.gov
Coauthor(s): Jeffrey Short
Year: 2008
Abstract: This presentation details a public-private partnership that centers on collecting anonymous vehicle location data, processing and managing the data, and using it to support freight transportation planning. The presentation includes examples of how the resultant data can be used to derive trip length estimation on a facility, zone to zone travel times for a region, and tables that estimate external-external and external-internal trips for a state.
