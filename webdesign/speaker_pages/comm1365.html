Title: A New Statistical Learning Method for Mouse Brain Segmentation
Author: Min Zhang, Arizona State University, 699 S Mill Ave, Tempe, AZ, 85281, United States of America, mzhang33@asu.edu
Coauthor(s): Teresa Wu, Alexandra Badea, Rong Pan
Year: 2012
Abstract: We introduce a new statistical learning method, integrating Support Vector Machine (SVM) and Markov Random Field (MRF) approaches, to segment different transgenic mouse brain models Magnetic Resonance Microscopy (MRM) images. By using our model, more accurate, computational efficient and robust result can be achieved.
