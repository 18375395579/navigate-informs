Title: Discovery of Associated Patterns in the U.S. Patent Database using Text Mining Approaches
Author: Jeonghun Kim, Korea University, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, ppllkkoo@korea.ac.kr
Coauthor(s): Seoung Bum Kim
Year: 2012
Abstract: Patent is one of the largest sources of technological information. In this study, we elicit the associative patterns of patents by using various text mining algorithms. Real data were used to extract meaningful patterns from patent data.
