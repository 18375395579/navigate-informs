Title: Copula Based Independent Component Analysis

Author: Kobi Abayomi, Visiting Assistant Professor, Duke University, Department Of Statistics, Durham, NC, 27707, United States of America, kaa71@columbia.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a parametric version of Independent Component Analysis (ICA) via Copulas - families of multivariate distributions that join univariate margins to multivariate distributions. Our procedure exploits the role for copula models in information theory and offers a unified view of component analysis procedures, in particular, by parameterizing multivariate dependence.
