Title: Bidding Advisory Model for Combinatorial Auctions in Freight Transportation Incorporating LTL Schemes using Branch-and-Price

Author: Rodrigo Mesa-Arango, Purdue University, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, rmesaa@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Satish Ukkusuri

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper we present a bidding technique that can be used by a Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) company in order to submit optimal quotes in a combinatorial auction. The objective is to minimize the miles traveled by trucks. This problem is solved using a Branch-and-Price technique. Numerical results show the implications of using LTL strategies over the usual Truck-Load (TL) strategies presented in previous literature.
