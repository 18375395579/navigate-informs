Title: Clinical Workload Prediction Model for Patient-centered Medical Homes
Author: Issac Shams, Research Assistant, Wayne State University, 4815 4th Street, Detroit, MI, 48201, United States of America, er7671@wayne.edu
Coauthor(s): saeede ajorlou, Kai Yang
Year: 2013
Abstract: In current practices of medical homes, a clinical-based prediction frame is recommended because it can help match the portfolio capacity of primary care teams with the actual load generated by a set of patients. In this talk, by extending the hierarchical generalized linear model to include multivariate responses, we present a workload prediction model for care portfolio demands in a Bayesian framework.
