Title: Modeling Stochastic Programs in COIN FlopC++ and Smi

Author: Alan King, IBM Research, PO Box 218, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, kingaj@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Michal Kaut, Tim Hultberg

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present an overview of our proposal for modeling stochastic programs using the combined capabilities of the FlopC++ and Smi packages from COIN-OR.
