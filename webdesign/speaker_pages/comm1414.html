Title: A Systems Engineering Approach to Improve Operational Performance in a Colonoscopy Suite

Author: Bjorn Berg, Mayo Clinic, 200 First Street SW, Rochester, MN, 55905, United States of America, berg.bjorn@mayo.edu

Coauthor(s): Angela Bailey, Ahmed Rahman, Heidi Nelson, Hari Balasubramanian, Brian Denton, Keith Lindor

Year: 2007

Abstract: In our study we address the resource allocation and operational planning problem in a high volume colonoscopy delivery service using discrete event simulation. This involves determining several key aspects such as the optimal number of Endoscopists operating, the number of operating rooms to open each day, and the number of patients that can be served on any given day. Such an analysis allows the practice manager to determine the most cost-effective settings at which to operate the suite.
