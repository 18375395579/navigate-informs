Title: Introducing Decision Analysis Techniques to the Vaccine Research Center (VRC, NIAID, NIH)
Author: Ajoy Chakrabarti, Director, Vaccine Product Development, Contractor/MSD, Inc., Vaccine Research Center (VRC), NIAID, NIH Building 40, Room 2512, 40 Convent Drive, Bethesda, MD, 20892, chakrabartia@mail.nih.gov
Year: 2006
Abstract: Selective implementation of decision analysis approaches has helped structure VRC clinical development plans. I will use a vaccine development plan as a case study to compare & contrast public versus private sector (pharmaceutical company) uses of decision analysis to improve vaccine/drug development.
