Title: A Comprehensive Review of the Literature on Disaster Performance Analysis of Transportation Systems

Author: Reza Faturechi, University of Maryland, 1173 Glenn L. Martin Hall, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, reza@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Elise Miller-Hooks

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk provides a comprehensive review of the literature on transportation system performance in disaster. Performance characteristics considered include risk, vulnerability, reliability, robustness, flexibility, survivability and resilience. The works are classified based on a host of criteria, including applied measure (qualitiative vs. quantitative, assessment vs. management), conceptual approach, and methodology.
