Title: Mesoscopic Traffic State Estimation using GPS and Bluetooth Data

Author: Hao Lei, Phd Student, University of Utah, 110 Central Campus Dr, MCE 2000, Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States of America, hao.lei@utah.edu

Coauthor(s): Xuesong Zhou, Wen Deng

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk discusses how to use multiple data sources, including loop detector counts, AVI Bluetooth travel time readings and GPS location samples, to estimate mesoscropic traffic states on a homogeneous freeway segment. We extend Newell's three-detector method to approximate stochastic cumulative flow counts, by using a multinomial probit model and Clark's approximation method.
