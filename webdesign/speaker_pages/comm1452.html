Title: Building a Supply Chain Model for Strategic Demand Planning Model at a Pharmaceutical Company
Author: Michael Berliner, Operations Research specialist, SAS Denmark, Købmagergade 7-9, Copenhagen K, 1150, Denmark, michael.berliner@sdk.sas.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: During this talk a model for strategic supply chain optimization for pharmaceutical finished goods will be presented. The talk will include a description of the modelling process which was focused on finding an appropriate balance of complexity to allow for fast deployment while ensuring applicability. The talk will also present how the model was implemented using SAS/OR and SAS?BI environment for result reporting and exploration.
