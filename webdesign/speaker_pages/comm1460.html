Title: Deviation Measures: Theory and Applications

Author: Michael Zabarankin, Assistant Professor, Stevens Instiute of Technology, Castle Point on Hudson, Hoboken, NJ, 07030, United States, Michael.Zabarankin@stevens.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: During the last several years, significant progress has been made in the development of the theory and application of general deviation measures, introduced by Rockafellar, Uryasev and Zabarankin. The talk will discuss the framework of general deviation measures in the context of portfolio analysis, capital asset pricing model, market equilibrium, generalized linear regression, the theory of choice, maximum entropy principle, and probability inequalities.
