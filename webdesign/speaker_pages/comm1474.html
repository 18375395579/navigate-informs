Title: Data Mining to Aid Beam Angle Selection for Intensity-modulated Radiation Therapy

Author: Stuart Price, Robert H. Smith School of Business, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, United States of America, sprice@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Hao Howard Zhang, Bruce Golden

Year: 2012

Abstract: A good IMRT plan is a set of beam angles and intensities that deliver radiation to the tumor while minimizing damage to healthy tissue. Fully evaluating intensities and performance from angle sets is computationally intensive, we data mine evaluated plans to create a model to screen angle sets before evaluation.
