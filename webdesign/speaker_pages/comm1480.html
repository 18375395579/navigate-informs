Title: A Choice-based Non-parametric Method of Demand Modeling in Revenue Management

Author: Shadi Sharif Azadeh, Polytechnique Montreal, Apt 108, 50 Willowdale, Outremont, Montreal, No, H3T 1G1, Canada, shadi.sharifazadeh@polymtl.ca

Coauthor(s): Patrice Marcotte, Gilles Savard

Year: 2013

Abstract: In transportation companies, demand forecasting is crucial. To maximize revenue, these systems use historical data. However, due to booking limits, registered reservations do not represent the real demand. We represent a non-parametric approach which is able to model demand by using choice probabilities. Our proposed model is able to extract seasonal features of demand and customer utilities for a given product. The precision of results has been verified by both synthetic and real data.
