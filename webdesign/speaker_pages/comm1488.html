Title: Open-source Quality Assurance and Performance Analysis Tools

Author: Armin Pruessner, GAMS Development Corporation, 1217 Potomac St NW, Washington, DC, 20007, United States of America, apruessner@gams.com

Coauthor(s): Michael Bussieck, Steven Dirkse, Stefan Vigerske

Year: 2008

Abstract: Until recently, much of the math programming community has focused primarily on performance testing and benchmarking, while the general commercial environment has emphasized reliability over performance. We introduce a suite of tools to aid in both QA and performance analysis of solver software. We show how users can effectively use our tools to quality control their solver using a variety of quality metrics. In addition, we show how one can obtain benchmarking and performance information.
