Title: An Adaptive Step-down Procedure for Fault Variable Identification
Author: Myong K Jeong, Associate Professor, Rutgers University, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, mjeong@rci.rutgers.edu
Coauthor(s): Jinho Kim, Elsayed A. Elsayed, Abdelmajid Hamouda, Khalifa Al-Khalifa, Wook-Yeon Hwang
Year: 2013
Abstract: An adaptive step-down procedure using conditional T^2 statistics is proposed for fault variable identification. The procedure selects a variable having no significant evidence of a change at each step based on the variables that are selected in previous steps. The selected variables are useful to construct powerful conditional T^2 test statistics for identifying the shifted components of the mean vector when a shift occurs only in a few variables.
