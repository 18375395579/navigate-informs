Title: Approach for Evaluating Predictive Quality of Seizure Prediction Algorithms

Author: Alla Kammerdiner, Ph.D Student, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, alla@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Deng-Shan Shiau, Panos Pardalos

Year: 2007

Abstract: To show that a certain algorithm can be successfully applied to predict seizure occurrences, one must compare its predictive performance against the Naïve predictors (periodic predictor or random predictor). In this study, we develop the outcome measure that allows one to properly evaluate the quality of prediction. The proposed approach for assessing validity of predictive scheme is applied to test the performance of the test algorithm with respect to Naïve predictors on the test datasets.
