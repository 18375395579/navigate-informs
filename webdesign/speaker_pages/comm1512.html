Title: An Electronic Teaching Assistant for Basic Operations Management Methods

Author: Ralph Scheubrein, Freiberg University, Faculty of Business Administration, Lessingstrasse 45, Freiberg, 09596, Germany, ralph@scheubrein.de

Year: 2006

Abstract: Software packages are frequently used to support the teaching of quantitative methods. One didactic approach is to make the students implement the method considered in the lecture on their own. A popular type of software used for this purpose is the spreadsheet. ExcelTutor was designed to help students learn the necessary steps to compute the results of a method using a spreadsheet. ExcelTutor verifies the correctness of the final results and provides feedback on the time needed.
