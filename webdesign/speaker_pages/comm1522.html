Title: Decentralized Inventory Rebalancing for Large-scale Vehicle Sharing Systems

Author: David George, Monsanto Company, St. Louis, MO, United States of America, david.k.george@monsanto.com

Coauthor(s): Cathy Xia

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the joint control problem of dynamic vehicle repositioning and customer admission in a general vehicle sharing system. We present a decentralized approach, based on a closed queueing network model, that is able to be efficiently implemented in an agent-based manner with low-complexity computations. We provide an analysis of our algorithm's long-run performance, along with simulations.
