Title: The Efficiency of Academic Departments at Universidad de La Frontera: A Multivariate-DEA Analysis

Author: Karen Morales-Muñoz, Alumna Magíster en Sistemas de Gestión Integral de la Calidad, Universidad de La Frontera, Avenida Francisco Salazar 01145, Temuco, Chile, karenmoralesm@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Ana Moraga-Pumarino, Sonia Salvo-Garrido, Martha Ramírez-Valdivia

Year: 2013

Abstract: This work estimates the technical efficiency of 30 Academic Departments at Universidad de La Frontera by means of Data Envelopment Analysis. We use two scenarios to evaluate the effect different variables have on the performance of the Departments. The average efficiency score using a BCC-Output oriented model varies from 0.599 to 0.89. The main results imply that key variables are related to teaching and research. A Tobit model identifies the main factors that influence the efficiency score.
