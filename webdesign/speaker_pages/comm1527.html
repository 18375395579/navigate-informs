Title: An All-or-None Inspection Policy for Inaccurate Data Records

Author: Howard Hao-Chun Chuang, Assistant Professor, National Chengchi University, Dept. of Management Information Systems, No.64, Sec. 2, Zhinan Rd., Wenshan Dist., Taipei, 11605, Taiwan - ROC, hchuang@mis.nccu.edu.tw

Coauthor(s): Rogelio Oliva

Year: 2013

Abstract: We develop an all-or-none inspection policy to tackle data inaccuracy, which is a pervasive problem in practice. Some qualitative insights about the interaction between inspector fallibility, risk preferences, and optimal inspection decisions are derived from analytics assuming risk-neutrality and simulation under risk-aversion. Also, we propose a Bayesian hierarchical model to make statistical inferences about unobserved inspection errors that are critical to the inspection policy design.
