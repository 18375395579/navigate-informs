Title: GAMS/MPSGE Version 2

Author: Thomas Rutherford, Professor, University of Wisconsin Madison, AAE Department, 427 Lorch Street, Madison, WI, 54706, United States of America, rutherford@aae.wisc.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: While algebraic modeling languages are helpful, the precise representation of multisectoral models is tedious and error prone. GAMS/MPSGE provides a tabular, non-algebraic modeling format which automates calibration and reduces the scope for errors in model specification. A new open source implementation of MPSGE, written in GAMS, accommodates optimal taxation, embedded gams-theoretic models and models with growth, infrastructure or pollution externalities.
