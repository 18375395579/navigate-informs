Title: Combining Probability Forecasts

Author: Roopesh Ranjan, Graduate Student, University of Washington, BOX-354322, Dept. of Statistics, Seattle, WA, 98105, United States of America, roopesh@stat.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Tilmann Gneiting

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose a method for combining probability forecasts from different sources. The commonly used method of linearly combining probability forecasts has limitations, in that a weighted combination of distinct calibrated forecasts is necessarily uncalibrated.In view of this, we propose a recalibration method. We illustrate our findings with simulation examples and a case study on operational probability of precipitation forecasts.
