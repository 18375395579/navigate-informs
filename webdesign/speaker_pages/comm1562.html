Title: Intaver Institute Inc. - Intaver RiskyProject Software
Author: Lev Virine, Chief of Innovation, Risk Management and Decision Analysis, Intaver Institute Inc., 303, 6707 Elbow Drive S.W., Calgary, Alberta, T2V0E5, Canada, lvirine@intaver.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Intaver’s RiskyProject is enterprise project risk management and risk analysis software. RiskyProject is used for project and portfolio planning, scheduling, qualitative risk analysis and quantitative analysis using Monte Carlo simulations. The presentation demonstrates RiskyProject software: how to identify and manage project risks, perform risk analysis using Monte Carlo, and interpret the results of analysis
