Title: Medical Laboratory Network Design

Author: Yue Zhang, Assistant Professor, University of Toledo, 2801 W. Bancroft St., Toledo, OH, 43606, United States of America, Yue.Zhang@utoledo.edu

Coauthor(s): Derek Atkins

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research presents a system-optimal and two user-choice models for designing a network of medical laboratories in a centralized environment, where a central decision maker determines the optimal location and capacity of each open facility so as to maximize overall social welfare. We propose a common solution methodology along with the location-allocation framework. Through a case study, we discuss several interesting managerial insights.
