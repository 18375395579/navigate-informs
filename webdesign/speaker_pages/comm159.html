Title: Understanding the Pulse of the Workforce through Social Media Text Analytics

Author: Wermer Geyer, IBM Research Cambridge, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, Werner.Geyer@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Stephen Levy

Year: 2013

Abstract: The use of social media within the enterprise is increasing and with it the availability of unstructured text data containing the public voice of the workforce. We present an application that helps make sense of unstructured data by applying text analytics methods (topic extraction, sentiment analysis, etc.) Combined with structured data from corporate directories, we can effectively filter and segment social data based on demographics to provide targeted insights into the workforce.
