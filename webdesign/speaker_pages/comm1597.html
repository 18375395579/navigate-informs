Title: DDDAS-based Simulation for the Distributed Manufacturing Enterprise
Author: Young-Jun Son, Associate Professor, The University of Arizona, Systems and Industrial Engineering, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States, son@sie.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Parag Sarfare, Nurcin Koyuncu, Seungho Lee
Year: 2007
Abstract: We intend to augment the validity of simulation models in the most economic way using automated fidelity switching in a multi-fidelity model via incorporating dynamic data into the executing model, which then steers the measurement process for selective data update. Comprehensive architecture is first described, and four algorithms embedded within the real-time simulation model are then discussed. Finally, a prototype system is demonstrated for a distributed manufacturing enterprise.
