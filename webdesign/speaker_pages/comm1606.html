Title: A Classification Technique in Computational Biology

Author: Altannar Chinchuluun, Industrial and Systems Engineering Department, University of Florida, Weil Hall 303, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, altannar@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Mario Guarracino, Panos Pardalos

Year: 2008

Abstract: The problem of classifying data in spaces with thousands of dimensions have recently been addressed in the literature for its importance in biomedical and computational biology applications. An example of such applications is the analysis of gene expression data. We present a supervised learning technique based on incremental generalized eigenvalue classifier to obtain higher classification accuracy with a small training set.
