Title: Multiple Objective Decision Analysis for Systems Engineering

Author: Gregory Parnell, Professor of Systems Engineering, United States Military Academy, Department of Systems Engineering, West Point, NY, 12520, United States, gregory.parnell@usma.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present and illustrate a system decision process using multiple objective decision analysis for evaluation and improvement of system design alternatives. This methodology is developed in our new textbook used for our introductory course for all majors and three course sequencers in our Systems Engineering Department at West Point.
