Title: Model and Solution Algorithm for Schedule-based Transit Assignment
Author: Alireza Khani, University of Arizona, 1209 E. Second Street, Room 206A-1, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, akhani@email.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Mark Hickman
Year: 2013
Abstract: An optimization model is proposed for passenger assignment in schedule-based transit networks. The model uses logit route choice and takes into account transit vehicles capacity to model user behavior more realistically. A simulation-based assignment algorithm is developed to solve the model efficiently and application in a large network is provided.
