Title: Support Vector Machines Based on Tail Risk Measures in Financial Applications
Author: Peter Tsyurmasto, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, United States of America, tsyurmasto@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Stan Uryasev
Year: 2012
Abstract: We considered several well-known Support Vector Machines (SVM) for classification and proposed a new SVM based on Value-at-Risk (VaR). Computational experiments with several Financial applications showed that the SVM based on VaR is not sensitive to large outliers.
