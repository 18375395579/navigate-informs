Title: The Cost-effectiveness of Herpes Zoster Vaccine
Author: Mehdi Najafzadeh, PhD Student, University of British Columbia, 2545 West Broadway, Vancouver, BC, V6K 2E9, Canada, mehdin@interchange.ubc.ca
Coauthor(s): Eleni Galanis, David Patrick, Carlo Marra
Year: 2007
Abstract: The incidence and severity of herpes zoster and postherpetic neuralgia increase with age in association with a progressive decline in cell-mediated immunity to varicella-zoster virus (VZV). Vaccination against VZV decreases the incidence and severity of herpes zoster and postherpetic neuralgia among older adults. We used a discrete event simulation model to determine the cost-effectiveness of vaccinating against VZV in older adults in British Columba.
