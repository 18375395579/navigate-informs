Title: Parsimony vs RFID

Author: Metin Cakanyildirim, University of Texas at Dallas, School of Management SM 30, Richardson, TX, United States of America, metin@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Alain Bensoussan, Suresh Sethi, Celine Ho

Year: 2008

Abstract: One of the potential benefits of RFID tags is reducing inventory costs by using information-rich models. The benefit is the difference between the costs of an information-rich model and the cost of a parsimonious model. We illustrate numerical/approximation techniques to compute the costs.
