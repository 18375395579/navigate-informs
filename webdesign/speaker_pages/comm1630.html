Title: Provisioning for Cloud Computing Services via Non-parametric Adaptive Learning

Author: Yue Tan, PhD Candidate, The Ohio State University, 210 Baker Systems Bldg., 1971 Neil Ave, Columbus, OH, 43210, United States of America, tan.268@buckeyemail.osu.edu

Coauthor(s): Cathy Xia

Year: 2013

Abstract: The high volatility nature of the emerging cloud computing service market makes service demand highly uncertain. The traditional provisioning solutions employing classical demand estimation approaches can hardly guarantee convergence to the optimal offering. In our study, we present a non-parametric adaptive learning approach that provides much improved offering solutions. This learning approach could be used to calibrate the model and converge to the optimal.
