Title: Modeling Memetic Diffusion: Toward an Integrative Predictive Model

Author: Brian Spitzberg, Professor, San Diego State University, San Diego, CA, United States of America, spitz@mail.sdsu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Big data are being mined extensively, but there is little theory to guide such investigations. This presentation outlines a nested, multi-level model that adapts several theories to the task of providing a flexible yet predictive conceptual framework and vocabulary for these investigations.
