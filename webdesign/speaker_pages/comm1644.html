Title: Fluid Limit Approximations to Combinatorial Algorithms
Author: R. W. R. Darling, Applied Research Mathematician, National Security Agency, P. O. Box 535, Annapolis Junction, MD, 20701, United States, rwdarli@nsa.gov
Year: 2006
Abstract: The coding theory examples presented in Bruce Hajek's Markov Lecture will be used to illuminate the possibilities for building a parsimonious and accurate differential equations model, in cases where a simple combinatorial algorithm is applied to a large (usually sparse) data set. Suitable references and study materials will be suggested for those wishing to try fluid limits.
