Title: A Control Chart Integrating Out of Control Data Information for Complex Processes

Author: Chi Zhang, HKUST, Room 607D, Tower A, University Apartment, Kowloon, Hong Kong - PRC, czhangaf@ust.hk

Coauthor(s): Fugee Tsung

Year: 2013

Abstract: Currently conventional Statistical Process Control methods only make use of in control data. However, as data become easier to store, historical out of control (OC) data are provided and can be exploited as well, especially for processes with less prior information. We propose a general framework to monitor complex processes in which historical OC data are fully utilized and some data mining tools are employed. A simple diagnostic framework could be applied when the control chart signals.
