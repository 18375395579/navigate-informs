Title: CGE Model for Clean Electricity Standards: Impacts on the U.S. Electric Power Sector
Author: Ekundayo Shittu, Assistant Professor, Tulane University, 7 McAlister Dr., New Orleans, LA, 70118, United States of America, eshittu@tulane.edu
Coauthor(s): Ian Sue Wing
Year: 2012
Abstract: We develop a technology-detailed bottom-up CGE capacity expansion model with a macro-consistent calibration of activities via column disaggregation of the social accounting matrix. We address the congruency of technology substitution elasticity with clean electricity standards given a hybrid setting and EIA’s generator pool mapping.
