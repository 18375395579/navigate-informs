Title: Inventory Management Spreadsheet Tradeoffs between Supplier Shipping Costs and Lead Times

Author: Joel Oberstone, Professor, University of San Francisco, SOBAM, 2130 Fulton Street, San Francisco, 94117, United States of America, qba.teechur@gmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: An inventory spreadsheet simulation is presented for an introductory undergraduate course in quantitative business analysis. Although the template is a stand alone design——it does not employ add-ins——it still allows the student to manipulate key input parameters such as multiple order quantity-reorder point combinations along with uncertain consumer demand, supplier lead times and multiple price-break points.
