Title: A Day Ahead Electricity Market with Reserve Shortage Pricing

Author: Jichen Zhang, Department of Management Sciences, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, ON, N2L3G1, Canada, j36zhang@engmail.uwaterloo.ca

Coauthor(s): Samir Elhedhli, David Fuller

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a large-scale multi-period stochastic linear programming model for scheduling the electric energy in a day-ahead electricity market. The model considers various scenarios and integrates the idea of reserve shortage pricing based on reserve shortage level in real time. Numerical results are discussed.
