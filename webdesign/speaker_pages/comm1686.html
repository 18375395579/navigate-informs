Title: Computer Experiment for Data Center Thermal Management
Author: Zhiguang Qian, Assistant Professor, University of Wisconsin at Madison, zhiguang@stat.wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Yasuo Amemiya
Year: 2007
Abstract: Data centers are integrated IT systems consisting of multiple servers. Thermal modelling is a key in running and designing this type of system. Computational fluid dynamics techniques are widely used for simulating data center thermal properties under different configurations. Incorporating physical properties and structural analysis, we present some efficient multivariate analysis methods for modelling the multivariate responses from this experiment.
