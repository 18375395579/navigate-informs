Title: 2013 Decision Analysis Publication Award

Author: Canan Ulu, University of Texas at Austin, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, United States of America, Canan.Ulu@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Robin Dillon-Merrill, Robert Bordley, John Butler

Year: 2013

Abstract: This award is given annually to the best decision analysis article or book published in the second preceding calendar year (i.e. calendar year 2011 for consideration in 2013). The intent of the award is to recognize the best publication in decision analysis, broadly defined. This includes, but is not limited to, theoretical work on decision analysis methodology (including behavioral decision making and non-expected utility theory), descriptions of applications, and experimental studies.
