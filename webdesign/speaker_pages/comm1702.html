Title: Mining News Articles Using Unsupervised Learning

Author: Ronen Feldman, School Of Business Administration, Hebrew University, Jerusalem, ronenf@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Benjmain Rosenfeld

Year: 2007

Abstract: Web extraction systems attempt to use the immense amount of unlabeled text in the Web in order to create large lists of entities and relations. SRES is a Web relation extraction system that performs its task in a totally unsupervised way. SRES learns the extraction patterns from unlabeled text. In this paper we show how we can use SRES to mine large collections of business news articles.
