Title: Markdown Optimization Analytics for Retail
Author: Tugrul Sanli, Manager, SAS Institute Inc., SAS Campus Drive, Cary, 27513, United States of America, Tugrul.Sanli@sas.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: Clearance markdowns help retailers to adapt to inventory overstocking, make way for new merchandise, and increase customer traffic. Taking markdowns too often, at the wrong time, on the wrong items or at the wrong location can be costly. SAS Markdown Optimization enables retailers to develop and implement optimal pricing strategies. Our presentation will focus on the modeling and formulation of the business problem and solution methodologies used.
