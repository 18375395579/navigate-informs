Title: Lane Use Management Strategies
Author: Chaitanya Paleti, NexTRANS Research Center, Purdue University, 3000 Kent Avenue, West Lafayette, IN, United States of America, cpaletis@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study presents the results of micro-simulation based analysis to study the feasibility of lane use management strategies. Ramp metering, high occupancy vehicle (HOV) lanes and reversible lanes are three strategies analyzed using VISSIM for an I-65 corridor. Insights from the analysis are linked to the decision-making process of implementing the lane use management strategies.
