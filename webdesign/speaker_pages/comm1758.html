Title: Modeling the Emergence of Leadership Reliance Networks
Author: Sophia Sullivan, Research Assistant, Northwestern University, 1578 Oak Ave, Apt 1, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, sophiasull@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Leslie DeChurch, Noshir Contractor
Year: 2013
Abstract: There has been recent interest in understanding the emergence of leadership in organizations from a network perspective. This study will present a computational model for the emergence of leadership reliance ties and empirically validates the model using data from experiments conducted on multi-team systems.
