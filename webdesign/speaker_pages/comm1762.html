Title: Adaptive Inventory Control with Demand Feedback

Author: Justin Azadivar, University of California, Berkeley, Department of Industrial Engineering, jazad@ieor.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Max Shen, Peng Li

Year: 2008

Abstract: We examine inventory control and optimization methods for situations where the demand varies in response to previous inventory control decisions and demands. We model this using a satisfaction model, and extend to models based on separate communities with internal communication mechanisms.
