Title: An Agent-based System Model to Study Health Policy

Author: Shinyi Wu, Assistant Professor, University of Southern California, 3650 McClintock Ave., OHE 310R, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, shinyiwu@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Pai Liu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We developed an Agent-based model to study the US healthcare delivery and policy. Our model includes interacting agents representing the key stakeholders in the health system. The agents would respond to the policy by changing their behaviors. Using this model to study Accountable Care Organizations (ACOs), the results show non-linear patterns of provider behaviors in responding to gradients of payment model in accordant with providers?priorities, suggesting payers to expand its strategies.
