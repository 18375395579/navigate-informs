Title: Formulating the Data Flow Perspective for Business Process Management
Author: Xiaoyun Sun, University of Arizona, Eller College of Management, Tucson, AZ, United States, xiaoyun@u.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Jay Nunamaker, J. Leon Zhao, Olivia Sheng
Year: 2006
Abstract: Given a syntactically correct process sequence, errors can still occur during workflow execution due to incorrect data flow specifications. However, there are no formal methodologies are available for systematically discovering data flow errors in a workflow model. In this paper, we provide a data flow perspective for detecting data flow anomalies such as missing data, redundant data, and potential data conflicts.
