Title: Risk-Based Multiobjective Resource Allocation for Infrastructure Protection

Author: Don Kleinmuntz, Research Professor, University of Southern California, c/o Strata Decision Technology, 2001 S. 1st Street, Suite 200, Champaign, IL, 61820, United States, dnk@strata-decision.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: A comprehensive decision analysis approach for optimal resource allocation to counterterrorism measures is described. The approach incorporates sophisticated analyses of both risk and consequences of an attack, considering health and safety, economic impact, as well as more qualitative consequences. The approach is demonstrated using data from actual sites in California.
