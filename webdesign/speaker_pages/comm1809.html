Title: Decision Analysis Workflow for Upstream Petroleum Industry

Author: Lev Virine, Schlumberger, 600, 322-11 Ave S.W., Calgary, AB, T2R 0C5, Canada, lvirine@slb.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Consistent and comprehensive decision analysis process was proven to improve performance of oil companies. The proposed decision analysis workflow includes identification of uncertainties related to petroleum reservoirs, costs of wells and facilities, prices and markets. Decisions made on the stages of exploration, appraisal, development, and production are based on analysis of these uncertainties. Actual production performance management helps to improve the quality of new decisions.
