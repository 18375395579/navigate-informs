Title: Ranking Evaluation of Regression Models

Author: Claudia Perlich, Researcher, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Route 134, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States, perlich@us.ibm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: We suggest the use of ranking-based evaluation for regression tasks. In marketing, ranking is often the ultimate goal in building a regression model. Even when ranking is not the contextually correct performance metrics, our measures still have significant advantages: robustness against extreme outliers in the evaluation set; and interpretability. Our measures correspond closely to non-parametric correlation coefficients used in data analysis and both have interesting graphical representations.
