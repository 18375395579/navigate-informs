Title: A Unified View of Parallel Machine Scheduling with Interdependent Processing Rates

Author: Bahram Alidaee, Professor, The University of Mississippi, School of Business Administration, University, MS, 38677, United States of America, balidaee@bus.olemiss.edu

Coauthor(s): Haibo Wang, R. Bryan Kethley, Frank Landram

Year: 2008

Abstract: During the last several decades, variety of realistic scheduling models have been proposed in the literature. Many of these models are closely related. The purpose of this study is to present a unified view of these models using two related approaches. Several new complexity results also will be presented.
