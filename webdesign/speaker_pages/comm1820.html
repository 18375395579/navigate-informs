Title: Time Series Forecasting Methods- Is One Better?
Author: Syed Shahabuddin, Professor, Central Michigan University, 1306 E Preston, Mt Pleasant, MI, 48858, United States of America, shaha1s@cmich.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: There are many time series forecasting methods, e.g., moving average, Holt. Each one supposed to be used for different type of time series data. The question is: which one is better? My paper will evaluate the time series methods to determine which method does better for which type of data.
