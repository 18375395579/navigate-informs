Title: Using Competitive Analysis of Online Algorithms in Revenue Management: A Modeling Perspective

Author: Itir Karaesmen, University of Maryland, Robert H Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, United States of America, ikaraes@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingjie Lan, Michael Ball, Huina Gao

Year: 2008

Abstract: In our recent work, we successfuly used competitive analysis of online algorithms to design new distribution-free methods for capacity control in revenue management. This talk summarizes the modeling choices we made and the challenges encountered in extending our results.
