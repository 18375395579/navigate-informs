Title: Quality Analysis and Prediction Model based on Multivariate Data Analysis
Author: Yibo Ai, Assistant Professor, USTB, No.30 Xueyuan Road, Haidian District, Beijing, 100083, China, ybai@ustb.edu.cn
Coauthor(s): Bin Yang
Year: 2013
Abstract: The manufacturing process is a multivariate, complex process. The quality model of predicting and controlling are developed, based on the statistical dataset from the production process of the complex manufacturing process, which include the process control parameters, raw material parameters and the product quality parameters. The report is focused on new methods of quality modeling, prediction and production process control.
