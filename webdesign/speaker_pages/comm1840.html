Title: A Common Modeling Framework for Transportation and Production Planning Systems with Congestion
Author: Georgios Kalafatas, Purdue University, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, West Lafayette, IN, United States, gkalafat@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta, Reha Uzsoy
Year: 2006
Abstract: The cell transmission model and the material requirements planning are compared. Both have an exact graph structure for propagating flow (vehicles or SKUs) under capacity and congestion constraints. The modeling units (cell or facility) differentiate the spatio-temporal analysis. Common concepts, like the existence of a state variable, are highly indicative of the potential of a common modeling framework for transportation and production planning.
