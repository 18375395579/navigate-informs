Title: Resource Allocation for Service Supply Chains

Author: Ralph Badinelli, Professor, Virginia Tech, Department of BIT (0235), Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, ralphb@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Sheneeta White

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper we develop a productivity-based resource allocation model with various forms of technology functions. We evaluate the risks that are inherent in resource plans due to inaccuracy in the specification or estimation of technology functions. Adaptive planning approaches are suggested and evaluated.
