Title: Bayesian Mortgage Default Models and Reliability Analysis

Author: Refik Soyer, Professor, Department of Decision Sciences, The George Washington University, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, soyer@gwu.edu

Coauthor(s): Feng Xu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Bayesian point of view is rather new in the mortgage default literature. In this talk we present some recent Bayesian mortgage models for analysis of aggregate and individual loan level data. In so doing, we also discuss how these models can be used for loan maintenance using a decision theoretic approach.
