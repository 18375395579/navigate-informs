Title: The Effectiveness of Using a Web-based Applet to Teach Concepts of Linear Programming

Author: Christine Kydd, Professor, University of Delaware, Dept. of Business Administration, Lerner College of Business, Newark, DE, 19716, United States of America, chriskyd@udel.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper presents a web-based java applet that was used by instructors and students to graphically illustrate/learn fundamental concepts of LP models. It describes the results of a study which compares student performance of those who did use the applet vs. those who did not. Results show that the students who used the applet to learn about LP concepts performed significantly better than those who did not. Implications for using such techniques in the classroom are discussed.
