Title: Risk Analysis of DOE Energy R&D Programs
Author: Sam Baldwin, Chief Technology Officer, Department of Energy/EERE, 1000 Independence Ave., SW, Washington, DC, 20585, United States of America, Sam.Baldwin@ee.doe.gov
Year: 2008
Abstract: A methodology has been developed for quantitatively evaluating the technical risks of energy technology system R&D and the associated economic and environmental benefits. Key issues include: the probability assessment process; expert recruitment; energy technology system modeling; evaluation of alternative technology pathways; benefits analysis; decision analysis; and lessons learned.
