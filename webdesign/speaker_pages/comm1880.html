Title: Optimal Operations, Management, and Network Design for Personal Rapid Transit Systems
Author: Ryan Smith, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Newmark Civil Engineering Laboratory, Urbana, Il, 61801-2352, United States of America, smith103@illinois.edu
Coauthor(s): Yanfeng Ouyang
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper presents an integrated personal rapid transit planning model that uses a genetic algorithm and an embedded closed queuing network model to optimize the pod fleet size, station location, the number of berths at each station, and the strategy for empty pod reallocation. The model formulates agency investment and user cost as functions of design characteristics.
