Title: On the Effectiveness of Patenting Strategies

Author: Fabian Sting, Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University, Burgemeester Oudlaan 50, Rotterdam, Netherlands, FSting@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Jurgen Mihm, Tan Wang

Year: 2012

Abstract: What patenting strategy should a company pursue? What factors should the choice of strategy depend on? We build a coherent inventory of patenting strategies and we integrate it into a common coherent framework. Through a simulation model, we identify competitive dynamics as the most crucial contingency and within our framework we characterize the optimal patenting choices. Our research makes a step towards a contingency theory of patenting strategies.
