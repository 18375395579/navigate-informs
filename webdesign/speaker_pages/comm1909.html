Title: Presenting Business Statistics with Concise Managerial Statistics
Author: Kellie Keeling, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, Business Information Technology(0235), Pamplin 1007, Blacksburg, VA, 24061-0235, United States, kkeeling@vt.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Concise Managerial Statistics is an introduction to Business Statistics text integrating the use of computers with statistics, by describing the concepts and applications while giving users the opportunity to carry out computer-generated solutions using SPSS, Excel, and MINITAB. The text includes Excel Add-ins that expand Excel's power in graphing and regression analysis. The cases, chapter examples, and exercises focus on the interpretation of statistical graphs and statistical conclusions.
