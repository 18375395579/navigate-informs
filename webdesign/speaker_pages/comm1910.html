Title: A Methodology for Classification and Detection of Helicopter Bearing Faults

Author: David He, Associate Professor, University of Illinois at Chicago, davidhe@uic.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk, the development of a general methodology using wavelet analysis integrated with data mining approaches for classification and detection of helicopter swashplate bearing faults will be reported. A successful application of the developed methodology to recent seeded fault tests for US Army CH-47D and MH-47E swashplate bearings will be discussed.
