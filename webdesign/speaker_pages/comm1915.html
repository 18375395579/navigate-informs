Title: Informative Sensor and Feature Selection via Hierarchical Non-Negative Garrote

Author: Kamran Paynabar, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, United States of America, kamip@umich.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: In order to effectively extract the information from a distributed sensing system, it is essential to develop a systematic method that not only can select important sensors/signals, but also can extract a low dimension of interpretable features from the high-dimensional vector of a selected signal. For this purpose, we develop a new hierarchical regularization approach called hierarchical non-negative garrote. Performance of the method is evaluated through simulations and a case study.
