Title: Hidden Markov Models for Financial Engineering

Author: Mayur Thakur, Google Inc., 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA, 94043, United States

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk will describe how Hidden Markov Models (HMMs)---a stochastic model used widely in speech recognition systems---can be used to model noisy financial time series, such as stock market data. A key difference between our approach and previous work in time series prediction is that our approach is symbolic rather than numerical. We show that this approach yields relatively good performance---both in terms of accuracy of prediction and for algorithmic trading.
