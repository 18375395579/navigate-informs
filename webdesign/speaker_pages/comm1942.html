Title: Equity and Efficiency Analysis of Vehicle Usage Restriction, Ownership Quota, and Pricing Policies

Author: Shanjiang Zhu, Assistant Research Scientist, University of Maryland, 1173 Glenn L. Martin Hall, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, zhuxx120@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Longyuan Du, Lei Zhang

Year: 2012

Abstract: This research develops an analytical framework for analyzing and comparing transportation rationing policies, which consists of a mathematical model of joint household vehicle ownership and usage decisions and welfare analysis methods based on compensating variation and consumer surplus. This research extends previous research to consider multiple user groups on a network.
