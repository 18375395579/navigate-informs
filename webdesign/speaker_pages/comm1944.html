Title: Integrated Analytical Performance Evaluation Models of Warehousing Systems
Author: Manjunath Kamath, Professor, Oklahoma State University, School of Industrial Engr. & Mgmt., 322 EN, Stillwater, OK, 74078, United States, m.kamath@okstate.edu
Coauthor(s): Karthik Ayodhiramanujan
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop queueing approximations of warehouse operations that can handle features such as planned inventory and limited storage capacity. We present an approximate model of a shared server that stores and retrieves material from a finite capacity rack. We discuss how this shared-server model can be used as a building block to develop comprehensive warehouse performance evaluation models.
