Title: Incorporating Client Preferences into Preventive Care Network Design
Author: Lijie Song, Zayed University, Dubai, Dubai, United Arab Emirates, song.lijie@me.com
Coauthor(s): Yue Zhang, Vedat Verter, Beste Kucukyazici
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present a study on preventive care network design. We first estimate a population level participation function, then use stated preference discrete choice modeling to explore clients?preference concerning facility attributes. Next we apply results from econometrics analyses to an empirically based simulation model, and use meta-heuristics to demonstrate how to improve preventive care network performance by incorporating client preferences
