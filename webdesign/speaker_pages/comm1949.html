Title: A Decision Support Framework for Railway Capacity Expansion
Author: Yung Cheng Lai, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, B-118 NCEL, MC-250, 205 N. Mathews Ave., Urbana, IL, 61801, United States, lai3@uiuc.edu
Coauthor(s): Christopher Barkan
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop a decision support framework by using multicommodity flow techniques to determine how to allocate money for capacity expansion in the best possible way. Based on estimated future demands, the model can successfully determine the optimal solution regarding which subdivisions need to be upgraded with what kind of engineering options. The benefit of using the decision support framework is substantial for budget management of North American railroads.
