Title: Emergence and Decline of Design Gurus

Author: Haibo Liu, INSEAD, 1 Ayer Rajah Avenue 138676, Singapore, Singapore, HAIBO.Liu@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Jurgen Mihm, Manuel Sosa

Year: 2012

Abstract: This study examines the creative output of individual designers over time and disentangles knowledge and social factors that predict both the emergence and decline of design gurus. We define design gurus as top ranked designers based on the citations received by their design patents. We test our hypotheses by using Event History Analysis on the design patents filed in United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). Practical and theoretical implications for design management are discussed.
