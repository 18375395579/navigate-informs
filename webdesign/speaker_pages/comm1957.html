Title: Estimation of Long Queues using Mobile Sensors for Traffic Signals

Author: Peng Hao, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 108 8th Street, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, haop@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeff Ban

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop methods to estimate queue lengths of signalized intersections using mobile data when the queue exceeds the upstream data collection location. The proposed method is based on LWR theory which is reformulated for this particular scenario.
