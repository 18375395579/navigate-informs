Title: Choice Prediction with Consideration Set Clustering
Author: Nick Street, Associate Professor, University of Iowa, Dep. of Management Sciences, S232 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States, nick-street@uiowa.edu
Coauthor(s): Ding Yuan, Gary Russell
Year: 2007
Abstract: We previously developed a heuristic subset clustering method that was used to induce consideration sets, the set of products a customer considers before a purchase. In this work we use the resulting market segmentation as a preprocessing step for choice prediction. The resulting simplified choice models identify easy-to-predict segments.
