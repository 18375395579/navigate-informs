Title: Estimation and Monitoring of Spatial Defect Patterns
Author: Matthew Plumlee, PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, North Ave., Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, mplumlee@gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Jianjun Shi
Year: 2013
Abstract: The talk discusses a common scenario where each product is scanned for potential defects and a set of points representing the location of the associated defects is recorded. While individual defects occur sporadically on a single product, meaningful diagnostic analysis requires estimation of a spatial pattern of defects during production. Newly developed procedures using functional (nonparametric) statistics are illustrated using production data from a steel rolling mill.
