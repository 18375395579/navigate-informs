Title: Sales and Operations Planning: Using Optimization and Analytics in a Collaborative Process

Author: Tom Ervolina, Dr., IBM Research, Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, ervolina@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Pawan Chowdhary, Michael Geroulo, Feng Cheng

Year: 2008

Abstract: The S&OP process seeks to find a plan that optimizes business performance yet is also achievable. In practice, achievability is a blend of hard constraints and acceptable risks against soft constraints. In practice, the S&OP is a collaborative process where functional owners of each constraint and objective must reach an optimal plan through consensus. We will present a web services based framework that uses optimization and analytics to support this collaborative process.
