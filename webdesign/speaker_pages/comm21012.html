Title: A Particle Filtering Framework for Continuous Optimization: EDAs, CE, MRAS, and More

Author: Enlu Zhou, PhD Student, University of Maryland, College Park, 4305 Rowalt Dr., Apt. 301, College Park, MD, 20740, United States of America, enluzhou@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Fu, Steven Marcus

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose a framework for optimization problems based on particle filtering (also called the Sequential Monte Carlo method). The proposed framework incorporates local/gradient search into the global randomized search. Moreover, this framework provides a unifying view at three randomized methods for optimization: estimation of distribution algorithms (EDAs), the cross-entropy (CE) method, and model reference adaptive search (MRAS). It also sheds light on developing new optimization algorithms.
