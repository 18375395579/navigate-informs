Title: On the Global Linear Convergence of Alternating Direction Method

Author: Wei Deng, Rice University, 6100 Main St, MS-134, Rice U, CAAM Dept,, Houston, TX, 77005, United States of America, wei.deng@rice.edu

Coauthor(s): Wotao Yin

Year: 2012

Abstract: The alternating direction method (ADM) has proven to be effective for solving many optimization problems. However, its effectiveness has not been matched by a provably fast rate of convergence. We establish its global linear convergence under various scenarios and allow the subproblems to be solved faster and less exactly in certain manners. The analysis also provides some theoretical guidance for optimizing the ADM parameters.
