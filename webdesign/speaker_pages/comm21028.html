Title: A Branch-and-Price Algorithm for a Dynamic PCB Assembly System

Author: Mohammad Yazdani Sabouni, PhD Candidate, Oregon State University, School of Mech, Indust, and Mfgr Engr, 204 Rogers Hall, Corvallis, OR, United States of America, yazdanim@onid.orst.edu

Coauthor(s): R. Logen Logendran

Year: 2013

Abstract: A dynamic printed circuit board (PCB) assembly system is introduced in which the internal (machine) and external (kitting) setup times are integrated. The problem is NP-hard implying the need for lower bounds. A lower bound based on the idea of the branch-and-price (B&P) algorithm is developed. A branching strategy superior to the conventional branching in B&P is proposed. Nevertheless, the sub problems are NP-hard and several properties on the contribution of the sub problems are proposed.
