Title: Incremental Formulations for SOS1 Variables

Author: Sercan Yildiz, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Pittsburgh, 1048 Benedum Hall, 3700 O'Hara Street, Pittsburgh, PA, 15261, United States of America, SEY21@pitt.edu

Coauthor(s): Juan Pablo Vielma

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce a hierarchy of formulations for SOS1 variables that incorporates the incremental and logarithmic formulations as its extreme cases. We use these formulations to build MILP models for piecewise linear functions and present computational results that allow us to compare their relative effectiveness.
