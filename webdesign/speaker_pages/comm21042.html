Title: Decentralized Charging Scheduling for Electric Vehicles Using Mixed-Integer Nonlinear Programming
Author: Dzung Phan, Research Staff Member, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, phandu@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Jinjun Xiong, Soumyadip Ghosh
Year: 2012
Abstract: We propose a distributed decision-making scheme to model the charging of a collection of Electric Vehicles (EV). Our model introduces three characteristics to this problem: capacity constraints on the distribution grid, fair rationing of energy supply available under the capacity constraint, and discrete choice of EV charger settings. We study a distributed scheme based on the homotopy technique, which is able to efficiently solve the MINLP formulation.
