Title: A Branch-and-Cut Decomposition Algorithm for Solving General Chance-Constrained Mathematical Programs with Finite Support

Author: James Luedtke, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1513 University Ave, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, jrluedt1@wisc.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a new algorithm for solving finite support chance-constrained mathematical programs (CCMPs). The algorithm applies to any CCMP in which the random constraints are polyhedra, and hence includes the difficult cases of linear constraints with random coefficients and models with the opportunity to restore feasibility with recourse actions. The algorithm benefits from decomposition by scenario and the use of strong valid inequalities. Encouraging computational results are presented.
