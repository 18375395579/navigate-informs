Title: Non-stationary Stochastic Optimization

Author: Yonatan Gur, Columbia University, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, ygur14@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Omar Besbes, Assaf Zeevi

Year: 2013

Abstract: Many revenue management problems may be approached through stochastic approximation algorithms. We consider a family of sequential stochastic optimization problems in which the underlying objective function may exhibit a broad array of temporal changes. Our main results lead to a mapping from budget of variation to achievable performance. Furthermore, we introduce a meta-principle for constructing rate-optimal policies in various settings.
