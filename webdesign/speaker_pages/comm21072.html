Title: Robust Planning Optimization for Intensity Modulated Proton Therapy

Author: Behlul Saka, Research Scientist, Elekta, 13723 Riverport Dr Suite 100, Maryland Heights, United States of America, Behlul.Saka@elekta.com

Coauthor(s): Chunhua Men

Year: 2013

Abstract: Intensity Modulated Proton Therapy (IMPT) provides more degrees of freedom for designing dose distributions than photon IMRT. However, IMPT distributions are much more sensitive to uncertainties in radiologic path length and set-up errors. To mitigate the effects of these uncertainties, non-linear robust optimization models ranging from expected value optimization to worst-case optimization are developed. We present our results by testing the proposed models on patient cases.
