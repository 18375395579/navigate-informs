Title: Mechanism Design Problems in Advertising Exchanges
Author: Vahab Mirrokni, Senior Staff Research Scientist, Google Research NYC, 542 W 112th Street, New York, NY, 10025, United States of America, mirrokni@google.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this talk, I will discuss various mechanism design and stochastic optimization optimization problems in the context of advertising exchanges. In particular, I will present results about optimal revenue-sharing double auctions, optimal auctions in the presence of intermediaries, and revenue-maximizing auctions in the presence of budget constraints.
