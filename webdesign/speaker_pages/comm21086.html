Title: A Continuum Approximation Approach to the Dynamic Facility Location Problem

Author: Xin Wang, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 111 E Healey St Apt 202, Champaign, 61820, United States of America, wangxin1@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Yanfeng Ouyang, Michael Lim

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a continuum approximation framework to solve a dynamic facility location problem for a large-scale growing market. Using a tube-regulation procedure, we convert the CA solution back into discrete facility locations and illustrate the performance of our algorithm.
