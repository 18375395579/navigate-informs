Title: Multi-stage Stochastic Optimization and Cloud Computing Applied to Energy Systems
Author: Luiz Carlos Costa Jr, PSR, Praia de Botafogo 228 / 1701-A, Rio de Janeiro, RJ, 22250-145, Brazil, luizcarlos@psr-inc.com
Coauthor(s): Nora Campodonico, Luiz Mauricio Thom?, Mario Veiga Pereira
Year: 2012
Abstract: We describe the use of a distributed processing version of the stochastic dual dynamic programming algorithm (SDDP) to two large scale real-life applications: (i) transmission interconnections planning for 16 Latin American countries; and (ii) a spot price forecasting system for the Scandinavian electricity market (Nordpool). Both applications require stochastic scheduling of a very large number of hydro plants and thus the solution of stochastic DP problems with several hundred state variables.
