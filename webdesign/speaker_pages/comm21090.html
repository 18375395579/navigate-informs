Title: Optimal Preventive Maintenance Policy Under a Budget Constraint
Author: Inderjeet Singh, University of Texas at Austin, Department of Mechanical Engineering, 1 University Station, C2200, ETC 5.110, Austin, TX, 78705, United States, inderjeet@mail.utexas.edu
Coauthor(s): Ernie Kee, Elmira Popova
Year: 2007
Abstract: We design optimal preventive maintenance policy for a system of N items that minimizes total expected maintenance cost under a budget constraint. The problem has finite horizon and we consider constant inter preventive maintenance times for every item. The resulting nonlinear program is reformulated as a binary integer program and computation results are presented on a real problem from South Texas Project Nuclear Operating Company in Bay City, Texas, USA.
