Title: Facility Allocation by Using Weighted Support Vector Machines

Author: Emre Tokgoz, Faculty - Lecturer, University of Oklahoma, 601 Elm Ave. Room 423, Norman, OK, 73019, United States of America, Emre.Tokgoz-1@ou.edu

Coauthor(s): Samir Alwazzi, Theodore B. Trafalis

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this work we employ the Weighted Support Vector Machine (WSVM) approach to solve a facility allocation problem (FAP). The WSVM approach used in this work is a new solution technique that was not applied to allocate facilities previously; preliminary results are presented.
