Title: MILP Approaches for a Class of Mixed Integer Bi-level Programming Problems
Author: Mahdi Hamzeei, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1513 University Avenue, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, hamzeei@wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): James Luedtke
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a class of mixed integer bilevel programming problems motivated by a facility location problem with queueing delay considerations. We formulate a mixed-integer bilevel programming problem and derive mixed-integer linear programming approximations of the problem. We also investigate ways to strengthen the resulting MILP formulations. Numerical results will demonstrate the performance of the approach.
