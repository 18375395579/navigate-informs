Title: On a New Rotation-tour Network Model for Aircraft Maintenance Routing Problem

Author: Zhe Liang, Rutgers, 900 Davidson Rd. Apt 110, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, iseliangzhe@hotmail.com

Coauthor(s): Art Chaovaitwongse, Ellis Johnson, Huei Chuen Huang

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper, we present a new compact network representation of rotation tour, which is used in a new formulation for aircraft maintenance routing problem. The quality of the model was assessed on four test instances, and compared with the flight string model. The computational results show that the proposed model provides a very good linear programming relaxation and is able to obtain the optimal solutions to all test instances in very reasonable time.
