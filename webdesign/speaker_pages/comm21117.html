Title: Modeling Disjunctions of Polytopes, with Applications to Piecewise-Linear Functions

Author: Warren Adams, Clemson University, O-327 Martin Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States of America, wadams@clemson.edu

Coauthor(s): Frank Muldoon

Year: 2012

Abstract: We model the convex hull of the union of a collection of polytopes using a logarithmic number of binary variables. For special structures, we show how projections can lead to concise forms. These structures include knapsack constraints and SOS-d restrictions. The SOS-2 case allows for the handling of piecewise-linear functions, yielding new representations and motivating existing ones. Favorable computational experience is reported.
