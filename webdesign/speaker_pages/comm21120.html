Title: Oil Supply to Nepal- Steady State Inventory

Author: Stein W. Wallace, Professor, Molde University College, PO Box 2110, Molde, 6402, Norway, Stein.W.Wallace@himolde.no

Coauthor(s): Biju Kumar Thapalia

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a two-stage stochastic programming model for analyzing the steady state of inventory control in oil supply to Nepal. The focus will be on how we formulate this infinite horizon problem as a two-stage program.
