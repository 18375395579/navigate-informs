Title: Energy Infrastructure Investment over Finite Horizon

Author: Zhen Liu, Assistant Professor, University of Minnesota at Twin Cities, 206 Church St S.E., Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, zhenliu@umn.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the optimal time to construct a green plant of a firm with a portfolio of plants to maximize the expected profit. We formulate the problem as a mixed optimal stopping/control problem, and characterize the optimal strategies through numerical method.
