Title: Relaxations of Approximate Linear Programs for the Real Option Management of Commodity Storage

Author: Selvaprabu Nadarajah, PhD Candidate, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, snadaraj@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Nicola Secomandi, Francois Margot

Year: 2013

Abstract: The real option management of commodity conversion assets gives rise to intractable Markov decision processes (MDPs). This intractability is due primarily to the high dimensionality of a commodity forward curve in the MDP state. We develop a novel approximate dynamic programming approach based on relaxations of approximate linear programs. Focusing on natural gas storage, we provide theoretical and numerical support for the proposed approach.
