Title: A Model and Algorithm for Robust Optimization in Cancer Treatment Planning
Author: Stephen Wright, Professor, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1210 West Dayton Street, Madison, WI, 53706, United States, swright@cs.wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Arinbjorn Olafsson
Year: 2006
Abstract: We describe a formulation of IMRT cancer treatment planning that takes account of uncertainties in organ location and dose calculation, and present an algorithm for solving the resulting optimization problem efficiently.
