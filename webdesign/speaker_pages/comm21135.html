Title: Modeling and Numerical Solution of Portfolio Optimization Problems with Transaction Costs

Author: Zhen Liu, Assistant Professor, University of Missouri - Rolla, 1870 Miner Circle, Rolla, MO, 65409, United States, zliu@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): John Birge

Year: 2007

Abstract: In analogy to American option pricing, we study portfolio optimization problems through the Local Discontinuous Garlerkin Finite Element Method combined with an optimization method: We set up a buy-and-hold problem and then we find an optimal set of trades to move to an optimal portfolio whenever the current portfolio is far from the ideal. We give error bounds for the numerical method, with which we establish overall bounds for the problem and prove the convergence of this method.
