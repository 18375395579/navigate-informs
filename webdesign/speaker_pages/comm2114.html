Title: An Expected Value Constrained Two-stage Stochastic Unit Commitment with Uncertain Wind Power Output

Author: Chaoyue Zhao, cherryzhao09@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Yongpei Guan, Qianfan Wang, Jianhui Wang

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper proposes an expected value and chance constrained stochastic optimization approach to handle the wind power penetration problem for the reliability unit commitment run by ISOs/RTOs. We consider conventional thermal generator constraints while accommodating the wind output uncertainty. We use a Sample Average Approximation (SAA) method to transform the expected objective value, the expected value constraint and the chance constraint into a sample average reformulation.
