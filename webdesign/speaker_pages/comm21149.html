Title: Cutting Planes in SCIP

Author: Kati Wolter, PhD Candidate, Zuse Institute Berlin (ZIB), Takustr. 7, Berlin, 14195, Germany, wolter@zib.de

Year: 2008

Abstract: SCIP is a solver for constraint integer programming, which integrates constraint programming and mixed integer programming modeling and solving techniques. We introduce one component of the solver, namely the generation of cutting planes. The solver includes efficient separation algorithms for general cutting planes as well as for problem specific cutting planes.
