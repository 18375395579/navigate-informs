Title: Annealing Evolutionary Stochastic Approximation Monte Carlo for Global Optimization

Author: Faming Liang, Associate Professor, Texas A&M University, Department of Statistics, College Station, TX, United States of America, fliang@stat.tamu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a new algorithm, the so-called annealing evolutionary stochastic approximation Monte Carlo (AESAMC) algorithm as a general optimization technique, and study its convergence. A remarkable feature of AESAMC is that it possesses the self-adjusting mechanism and is thus not trapped by local energy minima. The numerical results indicate that AESAMC can potentially outperform simulated annealing and the genetic algorithm in function optimization.
