Title: Homogeneous Radiation Therapy Treatment Plans for Gamma Knife Perfexion

Author: Kimia Ghobadi, University of Toronto, 5 King's College Road, Tornto, ON, M5S 3G8, Canada, kimia@mie.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): David Jaffray, Mark Ruschin, Dionne Aleman

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this work the feasibility of fractionated radiation therapy (RT) for Gamma Knife Perfexion is studied. In RT, the targets are typically large and overlap with healthy organs, and require homogeneous dose coverage. We use a fast geometry-based algorithm to select isocentres to provide quality RT treatments. After isocentres selection, a sector duration optimization is used to find the final shot shapes. This approach was tested on seven cases and yielded clinically satisfactory treatments.
