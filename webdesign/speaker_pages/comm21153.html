Title: Reliable Infrastructure Location Design under Interdependent Disruptions
Author: Xiaopeng Li, Mississippi State University, Department of Civil and Environmental En, PO Box 9546, 235L Walker Hall, 501 Hardy, Mississippi State, MS, 39759, United States of America, xli@cee.msstate.edu
Coauthor(s): Fan Peng, Yanfeng Ouyang
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper proposes a facility location model under interdependent facility disruption risks. We propose a supporting station structure that decomposes complicated interdependent disruptions at service facilities into much simpler i.i.d. disruptions at supporting stations. We consolidate the reliable location design problem under an exponential number of disruption scenarios into a compact polynomial-size integer programming model. This model can be efficiently solved by commercial solvers.
