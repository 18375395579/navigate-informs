Title: Large-scale evacuation through network clustering

Author: Chrysafis Vogiatzis, Ph.D. Student, University of Florida, Weil 303, Gainesville, FL, 32608, United States of America, chvogiat@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Jose L. Walteros, Panos Pardalos

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, we present a way to decompose large-scale networks into smaller subproblems that can then be recombined to obtain a high-quality solution to the, inherently difficult, problem of evacuation. We discuss real-life examples, and provide computational results that prove the success of our efforts.
