Title: Personalized and Adaptive Diabetes Management: A Robust Optimization Approach

Author: Allison O'Hair, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Bldg. E40-149, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, akohair@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose an optimization approach to manage the blood glucose levels of type II diabetes patients by optimizing food and exercise choices. We use robust integer optimization in a dynamic and adaptive way to determine food preferences and mixed-integer optimization to select an optimal meal and exercise plan that minimizes individual blood glucose levels. We have implemented an online software using the proposed approach and report evidence of its strength.
