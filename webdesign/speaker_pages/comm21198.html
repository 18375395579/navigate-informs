Title: Innovative Mathematical Characterizations and Algorithms for Decision Tree Analysis

Author: Jitamitra Desai, Visiting Assistant Professor, Lehigh University, Dept of Industrial & Systems Engineering, 200 W Packer Ave, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States, jdesai@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Suvrajeet Sen

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper presents novel mathematical characterizations for representing decision trees. The advantage of using optimization techniques for solving decision tree problems, as opposed to using (traditional) backward recursion or stochastic dynamic programming methods is illustrated. Next, we extend this basic characterization to a compact path-based formulation and design a branch-and-price-and-cut algorithm for solving this problem. Finally, we present some preliminary computational results.
