Title: Random Search Methods for Continuous Simulation Optimization
Author: Andrei Prudius, Ph.D Student, Georgia Tech., Stewart School of ISyE, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, aprudius@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Sigrun Andradottir
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present several random search methods for solving continuous parameter simulation optimization problems. We show that our methods are globally convergent with probability one, and also provide some numerical results that demonstrate the performance of the proposed approaches.
