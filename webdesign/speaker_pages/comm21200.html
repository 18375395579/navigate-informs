Title: Reliability Constrained Multi-area Power Systems Adequacy Planning Using SP with SAA

Author: Panida Jirutitijaroen, Research Assistant, Texas A&M University, 400 Nagle St. Apt 102, College Station, TX, 77840, United States, pjirut@ece.tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Chanan Singh

Year: 2007

Abstract: We first address the challenges of the problem, namely, reliability evaluation and optimization. The problem is formulated as a two-stage recourse model where randomness in generation, transmission lines, and loads are considered. Reliability index is the expected cost of load loss as it incorporates duration and magnitude of load loss. Sample-average approximation technique, using Monte Carlo and Latin Hypercube sampling, is employed. The method is tested on an actual twelve-area power system.
