Title: Modeling and Solving an Integrated Workforce-Equipment-Production Planning Problem
Author: Gang Li, Assistant Professor, Bentley University, 175 Forest Street, Waltham, MA, 02452, United States of America, gli@bentley.edu
Coauthor(s): Tian He, Hongxun Jiang, Youming Pang
Year: 2012
Abstract: We built an optimization model to assist a company to make integrated decisions on equipment purchasing, workforce recruiting and training, and production scheduling. A genetic algorithm based decomposition approach, combing with preprocessing and network optimization, was shown effective in solving this large scale problem.
