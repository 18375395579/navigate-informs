Title: State-of-the-Optimization Using Xpress-MP v2006

Author: Alkis Vazacopoulos, Director, Dash Optimization Inc., 560 Sylvan Avenue, Englewood Cliffs, NJ, 07632, United States, av@dashoptimization.com

Coauthor(s): Gabriel Tavares, Richard Laundy

Year: 2006

Abstract: The state of the Xpress-MP v2006 Linear Programming (LP) and Mixed Integer Programming (MIP) solvers is analyzed in this study. Some new features of v2006 will be described including: the addition of in-tree cuts for parallel MIP, a feasibility pump heuristic, a 64 bit version, and improved solutions and preprocessing interfaces.
