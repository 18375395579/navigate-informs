Title: Global Optimization of Non-convex Piecewise Linear Regression Splines

Author: Nadia Martinez, The University of Texas at Arlington, P.O. Box 19017, Arlington, TX, 76019, United States of America, nadia.martinezcepeda@mavs.uta.edu

Coauthor(s): Jay Rosenberger, Victoria C Chen, Diana Martinez

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research presents a branch-and-bound algorithm to optimize a non-convex piecewise linear function generated by a modified version of Multivariate Adaptive Regression Spline (MARS) subject to constraints that include both linear regression models and piecewise linear MARS models. The method proposed is illustrated in an automotive crash safety system design case study and compared with an evolutionary optimization algorithm.
