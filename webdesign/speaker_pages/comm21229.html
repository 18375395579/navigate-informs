Title: The Static Stochastic Knapsack Problem with Normally Distributed Item Sizes

Author: Yasemin Merzifonluoglu, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, Industrial and Systems Engineering Dept, Blacksburg, VA, United States, yasemin@vt.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Static stochastic knapsack problem requires assigning items with random sizes to a knapsack with known and fixed capacity.When the realization of the sum of selected item sizes exceeds the capacity, a penalty cost is assessed.We seek to maximize the expected net value resulting from the assignment of items.We provide an exact solution method for a linear relaxation of the problem when item sizes are independent and normally distributed, which is used in a branch-and-bound algorithm.
