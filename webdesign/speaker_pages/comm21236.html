Title: Scheduling and Long-term Pricing of Electric Vehicle Charging in Parking Lots with Shared Resources
Author: Ajay Deshpande, IBM, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, ajayd@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Pavankumar Murali
Year: 2013
Abstract: With the growth in the use of electric vehicles (EVs), management of shared charging infrastructure under varying customer demands, schedules and time-varying electricity prices presents a novel servicing opportunity. We formulate the combined pricing-scheduling problem as a quadratic integer program and propose a tractable algorithm to find long-term charging permit prices for different arrival-departure-demand bundles and the corresponding charging schedules.
