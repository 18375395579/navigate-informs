Title: Ensemble Learning with S-plus

Author: Lixin Gong, Insightful Corporation, lgong@insightful.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: An on-going effort at Insightful Corp. will produce next generation classification and regression software based upon ensembles of decision trees. The software's modular design and extensible structure facilitates rigorous validation of promising approaches and further development of novel ensemble methods. We demonstrate preliminary results on protein mass spectrometry data. Future work will include parallel implementation and techniques for scaling ensemble learning to large datasets.
