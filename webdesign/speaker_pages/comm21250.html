Title: A Robust Optimization Approach for Adaptive Traffic Signal Control with Emission Constraints

Author: Hongcheng Liu, PhD Candidate, Pennsylvania State University, 240 Leonhard, University Park, PA, United States of America, hql5143@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Tao Yao, Ke Han, Terry Friesz

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider an adaptive signal control problem using the LWR model, with vehicle emission side constraints. We uncover, through numerical simulation, certain relationship between macroscopic traffic and emission quantities. Such relationship is combined with robust optimization techniques to reduce the problem to a mixed integer linear program, efficiently solvable with standard software.
