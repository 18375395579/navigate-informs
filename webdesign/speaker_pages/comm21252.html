Title: Preprocess or Not to Preprocess: That is the Question

Author: Endre Boros, RUTCOR, Rutgers University, 640 Bartholomew Road, Piscawatawy, NJ, 08854, United States, boros@rutcor.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Gabriel Tavares, Peter Hammer

Year: 2007

Abstract: A set of preprocessing tools based on roof-duality and its extensions is proposed in order to simplify and possibly decompose the Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization (QUBO) problem at hand. The number of variables simplified by the proposed method is maximum possible in the framework of standard linearizations of QUBO problems. Practical evidence of the usefulness of the method is illustrated in various applications (e.g., Ising model, VLSI, graph stability, medical imaging).
