Title: A New Approach to Hotel Pricing
Author: Dan Zhang, University of Colorado at Boulder, 995 Regent Dr, Boulder, CO, United States of America, dan.zhang@colorado.edu
Coauthor(s): Lawrence Weatherford
Year: 2012
Abstract: We show that a non-linear non-separable approximation architecture proposed in the recent work of Zhang (2011) can be generalized to dynamic pricing for network revenue management. This generalization leads to tighter bounds and better heuristic policies than widely used DAVN type approaches. We provide a case study with data from a major US hotel.
