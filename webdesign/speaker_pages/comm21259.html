Title: Bayesian Optimal Learning with Generalized Linear Model

Author: Si Chen, Princeton University, Sherrerd Hall, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, sichen@princeton.edu

Coauthor(s): Warren Powell

Year: 2013

Abstract: The knowledge-gradient policy is an optimal learning policy that maximizes the expected value of information in each time period. We extend the this policy to a broader class of prior distributions by introducing the generalized linear model(GLM). GLM unifies various statistical models, such as linear regression, logistic regression and Poisson regression. With proper sampling methods and numerical integration, we demonstrate that the knowledge-gradient algorithm for GLM is efficient.
