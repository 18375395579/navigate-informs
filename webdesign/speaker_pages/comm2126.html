Title: A Branch-and-Price Approach for Vehicle Routing with Soft Time Windows and Stochastic Travel Times
Author: Duygu Tas, TU/e Eindhoven University of Technology, School of Industrial Engineering, P.O. Box 513, Eindhoven, 5600 MB, Netherlands, D.Tas@tue.nl
Coauthor(s): Michel Gendreau, Tom van Woensel, Nico Dellaert, Ton de Kok
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study a vehicle routing problem with soft time windows and stochastic travel times. In our problem setting, we have for each node, except the depot, a known demand, a fixed service duration and a soft time window. Our objective is to minimize the sum of transportation costs and service costs. We apply a column generation procedure to solve our model. To generate an integer solution, we embed our column generation procedure within a branch-and-price method.
