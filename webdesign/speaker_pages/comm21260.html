Title: A Link-node Formulation for Dynamic Congestion Pricing
Author: Xuegan (Jeff) Ban, Assistant Research Engineer, CCIT, University of California, Berkeley, 2105 Bancroft Way, Berkeley, CA, United States, xban@berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Henry Liu
Year: 2007
Abstract: We formulate the dynamic congestion pricing as a bi-level problem. We then establish the solution existence condition for such a model, independent of the actual formulation of the underlying dynamic user equilibria. By applying the link-node dynamic user-equilibrium model we proposed recently, the toll pricing problem can be converted into a single level nonlinear programming problem defined on the decision variable and the destination-specific link inflows.
