Title: Competing output-sensitive frame algorithms for DEA
Author: Jose Dula, Associate Professor, Virginia Commonwealth University, School of Business, Richmond, VA, United States of America, jdula@vcu.edu
Coauthor(s): Francisco Lopez
Year: 2008
Abstract: Two output-sensitive procedures for identifying the extreme efficient DMUs (the ``frame) of the PPS are currently available: one by Dula (2007) and the other based on Ottmann et al. (2001). The two procedures are in dual spaces and differ enough to motivate the question as to how they compare in a fair competition. We derive an improved dualized version of the procedure in Ottmann et al. and compare it to the one by Dula using a well-structured, large-scale, problem suite.
