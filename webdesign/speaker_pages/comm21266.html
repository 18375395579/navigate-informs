Title: Mixed-Integer Linear Programming Models for Reliable System Design

Author: Kelly Sullivan, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering Center, Fayetteville, AR, 72701, United States of America, ksulliv@uark.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present an LP for computing the reliability of a broad class of systems consisting of independent components. This model can be extended to address a number of questions arising in reliability. To illustrate this, we extend the reliability-computing LP to derive a mixed integer linear program for the redundancy allocation problem. We present a brief computational study to demonstrate the viability of this approach.
