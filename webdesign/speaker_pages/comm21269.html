Title: Maximizing Sequence-submodular Functions and Its Application to Adwords Auctions

Author: Azarakhsh Malekian, student, University of Maryland, 4322 Rowalt Dr., apt 101, College Park, MD, 20740, United States of America, azarakhshm@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Saeed Alaei

Year: 2008

Abstract: We extend the notion of submodularity to sequence functions and analyze the greedy algorithm for maximizing such functions subject to a maximum length constraint on the solution sequence. Many combinatorial problems involving time fall in this category. We model the online ad allocation problem in this framework implying that the greedy gives a (1-1/e) approximation assuming that the distribution of queries over keywords do not change over time.
