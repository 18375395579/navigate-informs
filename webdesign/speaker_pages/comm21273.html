Title: A Hybrid Decomposition/ Genetic Algorithm for Minimax-regret Stochastic Programming

Author: Helene Tokou, Student, The University of Iowa, Department of Computer Science, Iowa City, United States of America, htokou@engineering.uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Dennis Bricker

Year: 2008

Abstract: A minimax-regret stochastic program with binary first-stage decisions is solved using a genetic algorithm, where the fitness function is an outer linearization of the maximum second-stage regret. This function is first approximated using cross-decomposition (a hybrid algorithm incorporating subproblems of Benders' decomposition and Lagrangian relaxation) and further refined during the GA. Computational experience is described.
