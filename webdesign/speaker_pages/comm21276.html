Title: A Linear Programming Algorithm for Steady-state Analysis of Reflected Brownian Motion
Author: Assaf Zeevi, Columbia University, NY, United States, assaf@gsb.columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Denis Saure, Peter Glynn
Year: 2007
Abstract: We describe a linear programming-based method for approximately solving the steady-state distribution of RBM in the orthant. The method is based on the basic adjoint relation and approximating the distribution (rather than the density) of the stationary measure. Other applications of the method will be discussed as well.
