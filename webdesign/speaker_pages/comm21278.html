Title: A Novel Algorithm for Stochastic Linear Programs with Conditional-value-at-risk Constraints

Author: Dharmashankar Subramanian, IBM Research, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, Yorktown Hts, 10591, United States of America, dharmash@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Pu Huang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a novel algorithm for stochastic linear and convex programs with Conditional-Value-at-Risk constraints. It iteratively constructs and solves a sequence of compact-sized linear, or convex, optimization problems. The problem size in each iteration is unaffected by the size of random samples, which makes it extremely efficient for large-scale problems. We prove that it converges to the true optimal solution. Applications include practical problems, such as, portfolio optimization.
