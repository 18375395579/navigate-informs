Title: Itinerary-based Control with Nesting and Upsell

Author: Bill Pun, Senior Operations Research Consultant, Sabre Holdings, 3601 Grapevine Mills Pkwy, Grapevine, TX, 76951, United States of America, Bill.Pun@sabre.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: We formulate a stochastic optimization problem that captures allocation nesting and customer upsell at the itinerary level. Approximate dynamic programming is applied to approximate the objective function by piecewise linear functions. The resulting allocation policy is tested on a real airline network and benchmarked against the randomized linear programming bid-price policy under various demand settings.
