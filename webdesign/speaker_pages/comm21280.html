Title: Solving Order-Preserving Submatrix Problem via Integer Programming

Author: Oleg Prokopyev, University of Pittsburgh, 1048 Benedum Hall, University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA, 15261, United States of America, prokopyev@engr.pitt.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrew Trapp

Year: 2007

Abstract: For a given data matrix, discovering the largest submatrix obeying an unknown linear ordering is known to be strongly NP-hard. Although in recent years several good heuristic methods have been presented to find this Order Preserving Submatrix (OPSM), they lack the guarantee of optimality. We discuss application of the problem in data mining and present exact solution approaches based on linear mixed 0-1 programming formulation.
