Title: Incorporating Equity Concerns into Vehicle Routing Problem for Bus Route Design
Author: Kelly Bertolaccini, University of Connecticut, 261 Glenbrook Rd, unit 3037, Storrs, Ct, 06269, United States of America, kelly.bertolaccini@engr.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Nicholas Lownes
Year: 2013
Abstract: This research develops a method for considering equity in vehicle routing for bus networks. Several objective functions reflecting different definitions of equity are used to formulate the VRP. The VRP formulations are applied to several networks and solved using a genetic algorithm. The results of using the equity objective functions are compared to the results of a cost minimizing function on different sized networks to examine the cost of designing for different definitions of equity.
