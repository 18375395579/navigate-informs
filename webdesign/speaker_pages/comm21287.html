Title: A Self-Organizing (Ghost Image) Algorithm for Fixed-Charge Network Flows

Author: Richard Barr, Southern Methodist University, P O Box 750123, 333 Caruth Hall, Dallas, TX, 75275-0123, United States of America, barr@smu.edu

Coauthor(s): M. Mehdi Amini, Fred Glover, Gary Kochenberger

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose a new self-organizing algorithm for fixed-charge network flow problems based on ghost-image processes. Our algorithm iteratively modifies an idealized representation of the problem embodied in a self-organizing ghost image, enabling all steps to be performed with a primal network flow algorithm. Extensive computational testing on fixed-charge transportation problems is presented although our algorithm is not specialized to this class.
