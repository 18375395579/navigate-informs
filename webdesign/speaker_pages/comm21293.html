Title: Solving Multistage Decision Problems by Discretization Method

Author: Yijing Li, University of Kansas, 1300 Sunnyside Ave., Lawrence, KS, 66045, United States of America, yjl@ku.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Large sequential decision problems with continuous probability distributions are usually intractable. The simplest case of multistage decision problems is the Markov Decision Process (MDP). In this paper, we propose using discretization method to solve MDPs. We can obtain optimal strategy by using interpolation. We will illustrate the method by some examples.
