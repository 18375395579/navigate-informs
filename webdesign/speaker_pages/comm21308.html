Title: An Improved NSGA-II for Dynamic Design of Multi-objective Reverse Logistics Network
Author: Shuang Li, School of Management, Xi'an Jiaotong University, P.O. Box 1850, No.28, Xianning West Road, Xi'an, 710049, China, lishuang615@stu.xjtu.edu.cn
Coauthor(s): Nengmin Wang
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper addresses the problem of multi-period, multi-objective reverse logistics network design for post-sale repair service. We propose a multi-objective linear mix-integer programming model with the objectives of minimizing the total reverse logistics cost and maximizing consumer satisfaction. To solve this multi-objective problem, an improved Non-dominated Sorting Genetic Algorithm II (NSGA-II) is developed. Its advantage over basic NSGA-II is validated by numerical examples.
