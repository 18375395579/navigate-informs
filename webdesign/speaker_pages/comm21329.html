Title: Short-term Operational Planning in Upstream Natural Gas Production Networks

Author: Ajay Selot, Chemical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 66-365, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, selot@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Paul Barton

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present an operational planning framework for upstream natural gas networks whose features are inspired by the Sarawak Gas Production System in Malaysia. It incorporates models of the production infrastructure and the contractual framework. Customized heuristics that exploit problem structure are required for global solution of the resulting nonconvex MINLP in a branch-and-bound framework. A strategy for efficient solutions of hierarchical multiobjective instances is also discussed.
