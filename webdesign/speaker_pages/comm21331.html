Title: Integer Models for Tactical Planning in Forestry
Author: Evelyn Richards, Associate Professor, Faculty of Forestry and Environmental Management, University of New Brunswick, Fredericton, NB, E3B6C2, Canada, ewr@unb.ca
Coauthor(s): Eldon Gunn
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present a system of integer programming models supplemented with local heuristics to solve tactical planning problems in a hierarchical forest management system. The models provide decision support to managers for assessment of spatial tradeoffs.
