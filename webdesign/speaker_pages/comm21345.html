Title: Managing Patient Admissions in a Neurology Ward

Author: Saied Samiedaluie, McGill University, Montreal, QC, Canada, saied.samiedaluie@mail.mcgill.ca

Coauthor(s): Vedat Verter, Dan Zhang, Beste Kucukyazici

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study patient admission policies in a neurology ward recognizing multiple types of patient and model it in an average cost dynamic programming framework. By solving the dynamic program, we are looking for the dynamic admission policy that provides the best care for all patients in light of limited bed availability. We present an integrated approach which combines queuing theory and approximate dynamic programming and compare the performance of our approach to other heuristic policies.
