Title: A Cutting Plane Algorithm for Convex Generalized Disjunctive Programming Models

Author: Francisco Trespalacios, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, ftrespal@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ignacio E. Grossmann

Year: 2013

Abstract: GDP problems are can be reformulated as MILP/MINLP models using either Big-M (BM) or hull reformulation (HR). The former yields a smaller problem while the later a tighter one. The tightness of (HR) can be further improved by using basic steps. In this work we propose a cutting plane algorithm for convex GDP that exploits the advantages of (BM), (HR) and basic steps. We solve the continuous relaxation of the (BM), and use the strong formulation of (HR) after basic steps to derive cutting planes
