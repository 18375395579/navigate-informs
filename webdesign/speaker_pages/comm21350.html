Title: Constraint-centered Search Heuristics to Solve Constraint Satisfaction Problems

Author: Gilles Pesant, University of Montreal, CRT, CP 6128, Quebec, Canada, pesant@crt.umontreal.ca

Coauthor(s): Claude-Guy Quimper, Alessandro Zanarini

Year: 2008

Abstract: Constraints have played a central role in constraint programming because they capture key substructures of a problem and efficiently exploit them to boost inference. This talk proposes to do the same thing for search. We introduce new search heuristics based on solution counting information at the level of individual constraints and describe efficient algorithms to compute such information for the alldifferent, regular, and knapsack constraints.
