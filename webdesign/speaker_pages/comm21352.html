Title: A Pattern-Driven Optimization Approach for Appointment Scheduling

Author: Michele Samorani, University of Alberta, School of Business, Edmonton, Canada, samorani@ualberta.ca

Coauthor(s): Bora Kolfal

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a clinic with stochastic service times, undifferentiated providers, heterogeneous patients, and 30% no-show rate. Due to the many stochastic inputs to consider, it is hard to solve the problem analytically. So, to find general solutions to the problem, we employ a pattern-driven optimization approach: first, we use simulation to find good schedules for different problem instances; then, we analyze these schedules through data mining to find patterns that are desirable in any schedule
