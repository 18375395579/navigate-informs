Title: Balancing Perfectly Periodic Service Schedules Quickly with Aggregation
Author: Jeffrey Herrmann, Associate Professor, University of Maryland-College Park, ent of Mechanical Engineering, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, jwh2@isr.umd.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Scheduling periodic services that have different period lengths seeks a schedule in which the workload is nearly the same in every time unit. This talk will discuss a new aggregation approach that separates the problem into subproblems for each period length, forms aggregate services, schedules these, and then creates a solution to the original instance. Computational experiments show that using aggregation generates high-quality solutions and reduces computational effort.
