Title: Extended Formulations for Stochastic Lot-sizing Problems
Author: Andrew Miller, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 3156 Engineering Centers Building, 1550 Engineering Drive, Madison, WI, United States of America, amiller@engr.wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Yves Pochet
Year: 2007
Abstract: We discuss strong MIP formulations of stochastic lot-sizing models obtained by integrating additional variables into the base formulation. Our results generalize the work of Krarup and Bilde, Eppen and Martin, and others, to the stochastic case. They also extend the work of the current authors and others on extended formulations for mixing sets. To the best of our knowledge, they provide the only known convex hull descriptions of stochastic lot-sizing problems having more than two periods.
