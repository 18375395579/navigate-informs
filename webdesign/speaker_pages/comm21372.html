Title: Gradient-based Adaptive Stochastic Search for Discrete Optimization

Author: Enlu Zhou, Assistant Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, University of Illinois, Urbana, 61801, United States of America, enluzhou@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Xi Chen

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose a stochastic search algorithm to solve discrete optimization problems by iteratively generating candidate solutions from a sequence of parameterized sampling distributions over the solution space. We convert the discrete optimization problem to an equivalent continuous problem on the parameter space so that a gradient method can be incorporated to update the parameter of the sampling distribution. We show the convergence of the algorithm via tools from stochastic approximation.
