Title: Scheduling the First Stage of an International Soccer Tournament with Fairness

Author: Murat Kurt, Ph.D Candidate, University of Pittsburgh, Department of Industrial Engineering, Pittsburgh, PA, 15261, United States, endmuratkurt@yahoo.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: International tournaments are one of the ways that are increasing the attractiveness of soccer, which is one of the most popular sports in the world. In this talk, fair allocation of the first-stage games of such tournaments to a predetermined set of cities is discussed, and to identify the fairest feasible allocation, that meet some other requirements imposed on the schedule, a method requiring the solution of a sequence of 0-1 integer optimization problems is proposed.
