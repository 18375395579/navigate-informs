Title: A Greedy Algorithm for Two-stage Stochastic Programs with Decision-dependent Probabilities

Author: Hugh Medal, Assistant Professor, Mississippi State University, 260 McCain Engineering Building, Mississippi State University, MS, 39762, United States of America, hugh.medal@msstate.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a two-stage stochastic program with a maximization objective and decision-dependent probabilities, i.e., the probability distribution is a function of the first-stage decisions. We show that for several discrete distributions, the objective function is directionally concave if the recourse function is submodular in the righthand side. This property implies that a continuous greedy algorithm with sampling has a worst-case guarantee of 0.63 times the optimal objective value.
