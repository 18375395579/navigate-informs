Title: Scheduling Flexible Flow Lines using Artificial Immune Algorithms
Author: Thashika Rupasinghe, Clemson University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Clemson, SC, 29634-0920, United States of America, rthashi@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Mary Beth Kurz
Year: 2008
Abstract: Similar to the other biologically-inspired techniques such as genetic algorithms, the field of artificial immune system is an emerging branch of computational intelligence. This study evaluates the effectiveness of immune algorithms compared to genetic algorithms in solving complex scheduling problems.
