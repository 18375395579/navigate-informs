Title: A Graph-based Formulation for the Shift Rostering Problem

Author: David S.W. Lai, Ph.D. student, Systems Engineering & Eng. Mgt. Dept., CUHK, Shatin, New Territories, Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, swlai@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Janny Leung

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper addresses a shift rostering problem where the work rules are expressed in terms of prohibited meta-sequences and resource constraints. A graph-based formulation is proposed where the feasible shift patterns are represented by the paths of a graph. The formulation size depends on the structure of the work-rule constraints and is independent of the number of staff. This is particularly beneficial when the work rules possess sufficient structure that results in a small formulation.
