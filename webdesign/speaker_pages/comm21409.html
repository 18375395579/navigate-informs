Title: Trailer Fleet Planning for Industrial Gas Distribution

Author: Yazhe Feng, Research Scientist, Air Liquide, 200 GBC Drive, Newark, DE, 19702, United States of America, Yazhe.Feng@airliquide.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Fleet planning is important to the financial and operational performance of industrial gas providers. This research considers vehicle purchase, relocation, and rental decisions to minimize total distribution costs and fleet investment costs over multiple time periods and multiple depots. A two-phase approach is proposed to solve the real-life problem efficiently. Numerical studies, conducted using an industrial dataset, demonstrate the effectiveness and efficiency of the two-phase approach.
