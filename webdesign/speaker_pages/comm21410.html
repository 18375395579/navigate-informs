Title: A Heuristic Approach for Solving Large 0-1 Integer Programs

Author: Ahmet Keha, Assistant Professor, Arizona State University, PO Box 875906, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, Ahmet.Keha@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Gerardo Trevino

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk we introduce an approach that can be used for finding near optimal solutions to large scale 0-1 integer programs. The approach solves the problem over a reduced feasible region that hopefully contains the optimal solution. We compare several ways to generate the reduced feasible region and give an approach to improve the reduced feasible region. We also give extensive computational results that show that our heuristic gives solutions that are very close to the optimal ones.
