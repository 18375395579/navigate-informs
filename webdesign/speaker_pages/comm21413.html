Title: Probabilistic Solution Discovery Heuristic for Multistate Series-Parallel Systems

Author: Jose Emmanuel Ramirez-Marquez, Assistant Professor, Stevens Institute of Technology, Castle Point on Hudson, Hoboken, NJ, 07030, United States, jmarquez@stevens.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: This research illustrates solution discovery heuristics for the problem of resource allocation for the optimization of network performance. Network problems are prevalent in every area of technology. Currently, solution approaches for network performance optimization are restricted to traditional operations research approaches or highly involved heuristics. The algorithms are expected to have a broad range of applicability such as: Financial, Manufacturing and Network Reliability.
