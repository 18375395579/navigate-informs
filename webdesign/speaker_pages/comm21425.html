Title: Influence of CO2 Cap and Trade Policy Parameters on Deregulated Electricity Networks
Author: Felipe Feijoo, University of South Florida, 4202 E. Fowler Avenue, ENB118, Tampa, Fl, 33620, United States of America, felipefeijoo@mail.usf.edu
Coauthor(s): Tapas Das
Year: 2012
Abstract: Our game theoretic model analyzes the impact of design parameters of CO2 cap and trade policies on the performance of deregulated electricity networks. The performances include emissions, LMPs, consumption, generation and allowances allocation, and profits. We formulate the allowance and electricity markets as a bi-level continuous optimization problem and solve it using a MPEC/EPEC approach.
