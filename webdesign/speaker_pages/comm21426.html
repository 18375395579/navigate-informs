Title: Agent-based Traffic Optimization Model for Rail Terminal Management
Author: Tie Shi, Student, Southwest Jiaotong University/ University of Utah, MCE Building 2135, 110 S. Central Campus Dr., Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States of America, tie.shi1986@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Xuesong Zhou
Year: 2013
Abstract: This talk presents an agent-based integer programming formulation to represent the terminal station operation process, and the goal is to reduce overall train waiting time and delay penalty in the complex terminal network. A Lagrangian relaxation based solution method is developed to decompose the original complex problem into a sequence of easy-to-solve linear programming problems.
