Title: Scheduling On-Line Advertisements Using Neural Network / Genetic Algorithm Based Metaheuristics
Author: Jason Deane, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, Pamplin Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24060, United States, jason.k.deane@cba.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Anurag Agarwal
Year: 2006
Abstract: On-line advertising is a very robust industry experiencing revenue growth in 12 of the last 13 quarters. One of the most challenging problems faced by industry participants is the ad scheduling problem. We introduce three new versions of this problem and test several heuristic and metaheuristic solution techniques.
