Title: An Active-set Approach using a Primal-dual Augmented Lagrangian for Large-scale Nonconvex Optimization in SAS

Author: Wenwen Zhou, SAS Institute, Inc., United States of America, Wenwen.Zhou@sas.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: SAS has recently developed and implemented multi-threaded Krylov-based interior-point and active set methods for large-scale nonconvex optimization. Both methods use the exact primal dual augmented Lagrangian merit function of P. E. Gill and D. Robinson. In this talk we focus on the active-set approach and will give some numerical results to demonstrate nice features about this approach and discuss some strategies used for improving convergence.
