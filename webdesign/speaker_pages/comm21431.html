Title: A Novel Heuristic to Solve Tanker Routing and Scheduling Problems

Author: Iftekhar Karimi, Professor, National University of Singapore, Department of Chem & Biomolecular Eng, 4 Engineering Drive 4, Singapore, Singapore, cheiak@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Hong Choon Oh

Year: 2008

Abstract: Tanker routing and scheduling problems have attracted extensive interest from researchers for more than half a century. To date, none of these researchers manage to offer efficient solution methodologies that meet the practical needs of industry practitioners. This paper aims to bridge the application gap by introducing a novel solution methodology that can efficiently solve tanker routing and scheduling problem with account of all key operating constraints.
