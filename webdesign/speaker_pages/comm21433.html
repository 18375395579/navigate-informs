Title: An Algorithmic Framework for Mixed Logit Assignment

Author: Patrice Marcotte, University of Montreal, Department of Computer Science and OR, C.P. 6128, Succursale Centre-Ville, Montreal, QC, H3C 3J7, Canada, marcotte@iro.umontreal.ca

Coauthor(s): Cinzia Cirillo, Fabian Bastin

Year: 2007

Abstract: The assignment procedure associated with mixed logit models involves the evaluation of a complex integral that is usually performed by simulation. In this presentation, we introduce alternative algorithms that exploit the decomposition of the problem into its deterministic and stochastic components, within the general framework of quasi Monte-Carlo methods. Applications to urban transportation and revenue management will be discussed.
