Title: Robust Stochastic Dominance Constraints

Author: Darinka Dentcheva, Professor, Stevens Institute of Technology, Castle Point on Hudson, Hoboken, NJ, 07030, United States of America, darinka.dentcheva@stevens.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrzej Ruszczynski

Year: 2008

Abstract: We introduce a new preference relation in the space of random variables, which we call robust stochastic dominance. We consider stochastic optimization problems where risk-aversion is expressed by a robust stochastic dominance constraint. These are composite semi-infinite optimization problems with constraints on compositions of measures of risk and utility functions. We develop necessary and sufficient conditions of optimality for such optimization problems.
