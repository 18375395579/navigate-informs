Title: Generalized Point Queue Model (GPQM) and Application to System Optimal Dynamic Traffic Assignment (SO-DTA)

Author: Tao Yao, Pennsylvania State University, 349 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, taoyao@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Terry Friesz, Ke Han

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Vickrey's point queue model involves an ODE with discontinuous right hand side, which makes it difficult to analyze and compute in continuous-time. We present a partial differential equation formulation of Vickrey's model. This formulation leads to an explicit solution of the ODE and a new computational method. We will also discuss the application of the GPQM to SO-DTA problems.
