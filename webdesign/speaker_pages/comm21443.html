Title: Optimal and Approximate Algorithms for Energy-efficient Routing of Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles

Author: Mahyar Movahed Nejad, PhD Candidate, Wayne State University, 4815 Fourth St., Detroit, MI, 48202, United States of America, mahyar@wayne.edu

Coauthor(s): Ratna Babu Chinnam

Year: 2013

Abstract: We introduce the problem of Energy-Efficient Routing for Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles as a new class of the Shortest Path Problem, and model it as both Integer Program (IP) and Dynamic Program (DP). We then prove that the problem is NP-complete, and design optimal and approximate algorithms to solve it. We demonstrate the performance of the proposed algorithms using road network data from Metro Detroit.
