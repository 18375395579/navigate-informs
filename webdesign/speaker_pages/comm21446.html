Title: Primal Heuristics for Mixed Integer Programs

Author: Timo Berthold, Zuse Institute Berlin (ZIB), Takustr. 7, Berlin, 14195, Germany, berthold@zib.de

Year: 2008

Abstract: In modern MIP-solvers like the state-of-the-art branch-cut-and-price-framework SCIP, primal heuristics play a major role in finding and improving feasible solutions at the early steps of the solution process. We give an overview about different categories of heuristics, present a new large-neighborhood-search heuristic and an improved version of the feasibility pump. Finally, we present recent computational results.
