Title: Propagating Knapsack Constraints in Sublinear Time

Author: Meinolf Sellmann, Assistant Professor, Brown University, sello@cs.brown.edu

Coauthor(s): Pascal Van Hentenryck, Eli Upfal, Irit Katriel

Year: 2007

Abstract: We develop an efficient incremental version of an existing cost-based filtering algorithm for the knapsack constraint. On a universe of n elements, m invocations of the algorithm require a total of O(n log n+mk log(n/k)) time. We show that the expected value of k is significantly smaller than n on several interesting input distributions. After a theoretical study, we introduce heuristic enhancements and demonstrate the new algorithm’s performance experimentally.
