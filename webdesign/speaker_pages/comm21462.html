Title: Solving the Minimum Energy Broadcasting Problem in Ad Hoc Wireless Networks by Integer Programming

Author: Keumseok Kang, Krannert School of Management, Purdue University, 403 W. State Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907-2056, United States, kangk@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Kemal Altinkemer, Sibel Salman

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the minimum energy broadcasting problem in ad hoc wireless networks. For this NP-hard problem, we propose a new integer programming formulation based on a set covering model and a Lagrangian relaxation procedure to solve the problem. We provide extensive computational tests.
