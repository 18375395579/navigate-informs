Title: An Integrated Disruption Recovery Approach with Passenger Recovery and Cruise Speed Decisions

Author: Ugur Arikan, Research Assistant, Middle East Technical University, Universiteler Mh, Dumlupinar Blv, No:1, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, arikanu@metu.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Sinan Gurel, M. Selim Akturk

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a new network-flow based model for the integrated airline disruption recovery problem. We consider detailed passenger recovery and cruise speed decisions along with other well-known aircraft and crew recovery actions. We propose a mixed integer conic quadratic programming formulation solved in reasonable CPU times for practical size problems.
