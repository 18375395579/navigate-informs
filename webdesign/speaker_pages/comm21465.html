Title: A New Heuristic for the Integrated Vehicle Routing and Container Loading Problem

Author: Hugo Yoshizaki, Associate Professor, University of São Paulo, Dept of Production Engineering, Av Prof Almeida Prado Trav 2 no 128, Sao Paulo, SP, 05508-900, Brazil, hugo@usp.br

Coauthor(s): Danilo Campos

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper presents a heuristic for the integrated problem of vehicle routing and container loading, which makes k-opt like moves to generate alternative sets of loads. These routes respect the unloading sequence of boxes three-dimensionally packaged into the container. This method uses Ngoi et al (1994) spatial matrix representation, extending it to the selection box strategy by Gehring et al (1990). The heuristic performs well both in quality of the results and in response time.
