Title: Robustness of Aircraft Rerouting Strategies: Coevolutionary Approach

Author: Rafal Kicinger, Metron Aviation, Inc., 45300 Catalina Court, Sterling, VA, 20166, United States of America, kicinger@metronaviation.com

Coauthor(s): Babak Khorrami, Joe Prete, Steven Penny

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation will introduce a coevolutionary framework for generating robust rerouting strategies for the NAS operating in inclement weather. It utilizes coevolutionary algorithms to concurrently search the solution spaces of rerouting strategies and hazardous weather scenarios. By using this approach, air traffic managers can achieve two key strategic objectives: identification of robust rerouting methods and vulnerabilities of the NAS with respect to certain weather conditions.
