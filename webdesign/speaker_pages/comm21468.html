Title: A Unified Method for Constrained Assortment and Pricing Problems Under the Nested Logit Model

Author: Ruxian Wang, Assistant Professor, The Johns Hopkins Carey Business School, 100 International Drive, Baltimore, MD, 21202, United States of America, ruxian.wang@jhu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a unified method for finding the optimal solution to the constrained assortment and pricing problems under the nested logit model. The efficient are uniquely determined by the desired adjusted markups, which are also linked to a common variable at the nested level. The product selection search space is reduced to the set of efficient assortments of polynomial size. Finally, the joint optimization can be reduced to a single-dimensional problem with jump-up discontinuity.
