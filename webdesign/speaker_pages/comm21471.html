Title: Constraint Programming in AIMMS

Author: Deanne Zhang, AIMMS, 500 108th ave ne, ste 1780, Bellevue, WA, 98005, United States of America, Deanne.Zhang@aimms.com

Coauthor(s): Gertjan de Lange

Year: 2013

Abstract: Constraint Programming is a paradigm useful in formulating and solving highly combinatorial problems in addition to MIP solvers. With the Constraint Programing functionalities in AIMMS, we are able to take advantage of both ?by combining the two methods together to solve a combinatorial problem. We will compare the different formulations, use different solving techniques, and analyze the results using the tools in AIMMS.
