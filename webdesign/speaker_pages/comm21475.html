Title: Solving Two Inverse Optimization Problems via Quadratic Branch-and-Bound

Author: Guanglin Xu, PhD student, The University of Iowa, 108 Hawkeye Court, Iowa City, IA, 52246, United States of America, guanglin-xu@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Alexandre Belloni, Sam Burer

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate two inverse optimization problems: (i) a particular partial inverse problem arising in instrumental variable quantile regression; and (ii) an inverse optimal value problem studied by Ahmed and Guan. We convert both to mathematical programs with complementary constraints, which are generally NP-hard. We then introduce a single branch-and-bound algorithm for solving (i) and (ii) globally. Finally, we present computational results comparing our algorithm with existing global solvers.
