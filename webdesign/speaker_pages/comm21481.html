Title: Acceleration Schemes for a Class of Variational Inequality Problems

Author: Yuyuan Ouyang, University of Florida, 358 Little Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, ouyang@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Yunmei Chen, Guanghui Lan

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a novel method to solve a class of deterministic and stochastic variational inequality (VI), and accelerate the solution through identification and special treatment of the potential functional components. With the identification, we incorporate a multi-step acceleration scheme into the prox-method by Nemirovski to solve the problem. We show that our method not only achieves optimal rate of convergence, but may also be of higher order than the optimal rate with respect to general VI.
