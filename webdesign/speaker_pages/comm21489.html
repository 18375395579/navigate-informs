Title: Background Check Screening for Long-term Care Workers

Author: Tiffany Radcliff, Assistant Professor, University of Colorado, 13611 E. Colfax Ave, Suite 110, Aurora, CO, 80045, United States of America, tiffany.radcliff@uchsc.edu

Coauthor(s): David West, Donna Hurd, Alan White

Year: 2008

Abstract: As part of the evaluation of a seven-state pilot demonstration program mandated by Congress, we mapped the processes that are used to conduct criminal history background screenings for long-term care workers. We found variation across states in terms of technology, screening criteria, program structure, and program processes. Several procedures and methods were associated with increased program efficiency. General themes will be discussed.
