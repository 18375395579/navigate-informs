Title: Use of Approximate Derivatives in the Filter-Trust-Region Method for Unconstrained Optimization

Author: Caroline Sainvitu, Facultés Universitaires Notre-Dame de la Paix, Rempart de la Vierge, 8, Namur, 5000, Belgium, caroline.sainvitu@math.fundp.ac.be

Coauthor(s): Philippe Toint

Year: 2006

Abstract: This talk will focus on defining a filter-trust-region method designed for solving unconstrained nonlinear optimization problems. The main idea is to let the filter play the major role when convexity is present and to fall back on the classical trust-region method only if things do not go well or if non-convexity is encountered. We will mainly analyze the use of approximate derivatives in the algorithm. Numerical experiments carried out on small-scaled unconstrained problems will be presented.
