Title: Decomposition Method for Mixed Integer Linear Bi-level
Author: George Saharidis, Research Associate, Rutgers University, 98 Brett Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08540, United States, saharidis@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Marianthi Ierapetritou
Year: 2007
Abstract: The majority of scientific results in bi-level optimization has focused in the linear version of the problem. The mixed integer linear bi-level problem (MILBP) continues to be a difficult task. We present a new approach in order to solve MILBP based on decomposition methodology.
