Title: Robust Optimization in Data Rich Environments
Author: Vishal Gupta, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, ORC, 77 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, vgupta1@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas, Nathan Kallus
Year: 2013
Abstract: The last decade witnessed an explosion in data availability. Motivated by this data rich paradigm, we propose a novel schema to utilize data to design uncertainty sets for robust optimization by using hypothesis tests. Models built with our new sets are computationally tractable, and their solutions enjoy a finite-sample probabilistic guarantee. Through applications in portfolio management and queueing, we show that our data-driven sets outperform conventional sets whenever data is available.
