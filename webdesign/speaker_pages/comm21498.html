Title: On Generating Cuts for Facial Solutions

Author: Sanjay Mehrotra, Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 sheridan road, Evanston, il, 60201, United States of America, mehrotra@iems.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Huanyuan Sheng

Year: 2007

Abstract: We will present methods for generating cuts for facial solution of integer programming problems. These cuts are generated from exploring lattices associated with the sub-faces of a given face. A lattice characterization of the cuts is also given.
