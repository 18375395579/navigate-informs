Title: An Interior Point Decomposition Algorithm for Solving Semidefinite Programs via Matrix Completion

Author: Kartik Sivaramakrishnan, Axioma, Inc., 8800 Roswell Road, Building B, Suite 295, Atlanta, GA, 30350, United States of America, kksivara@ncsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sanjay Mehrotra

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose a two stage interior point decomposition algorithm for solving semidefinite relaxations of maxcut, stable set, and box constrained QPs. In the 1st stage, we process the exisiting SDPs into equivalent problems in the block-angular form. In the 2nd stage, we solve the block-angular SDP in a parallel and iterative interior point decomposition framework. We will present our computational experiences with the algorithm on a parallel and distributed cluster of workstations.
