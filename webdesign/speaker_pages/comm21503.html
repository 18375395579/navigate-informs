Title: A Combined Sampling-and-bounding Approximation Method
Author: Guzin Bayraksan, University of Arizona, 1127 E James E. Rogers Way, Engineering Building #20, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, guzinb@sie.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Peguy Pierre-Louis, David Morton
Year: 2008
Abstract: A classic approximation scheme for stochastic programs calculates refined deterministic upper and lower bounds on the objective function value via Jensen’s and Edmundson-Madansky (EM) inequalities. In this talk, we present a method that replaces the computationally burdensome EM bound with sampling. We provide stopping rules that ensure the quality of the obtained solutions. We employ a control variate for variance reduction and present computational results.
