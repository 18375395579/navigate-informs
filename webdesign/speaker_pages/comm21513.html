Title: Orthogonal and Nearly Orthogonal Designs for Computer Experiments

Author: Boxin Tang, Professor, Simon Fraser University, boxint@stat.sfu.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: We construct a class of designs for computer experiments. Our designs include Latin hypercubes and two-level fractional factorials as special cases. The basic construction is simple, building larger designs based on a small design. If the base design is orthogonal, the resulting designs are orthogonal. We also present two generalizations: the first improves the projection properties of the basic method and the second produces designs with smaller correlations.
