Title: Gradient based Methods for Large-scale Statistical Computing

Author: Zhaosong Lu, Assistant Professor, Simon Fraser University, 250 - 13450 102nd Avenue, Surrey, BC, V3T 0A3, Canada, zhaosong@sfu.ca

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk, we first discuss two simple gradient methods, namely, Nestero's optimal method and two-point projected gradient method for a class of smooth and nonsmooth convex optimization problems. We then discuss their application to sparse covariance selection and principle component analysis. Finally, we compare their performance based on some randomly generated instances.
