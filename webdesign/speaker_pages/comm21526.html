Title: Primal Dual Schema for Capacitated Covering Problems with Lot-Sizing Applications
Author: Tim Carnes, PhD Student, Cornell University, 206 Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, tac45@cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): David Shmoys
Year: 2007
Abstract: LP-rounding algorithms for the min knapsack and one-demand facility location problems were shown in Carr et al. 2000, which made use of flow-cover inequalities. We strengthen these results by showing primal-dual schema with the same performance guarantee for these problems. Further, we extend this to the single-item lot-sizing problem with linear holding costs. We believe that this is a significant step for more general lot-sizing problems, and perhaps the capacitated facility location problem.
