Title: An Affine Arithmetic Method to Solve Stochastic Optimal Power Flow Problems with Uncertainties

Author: Mehrdad Pirnia, University of Waterloo, 200 University Ave. W, Waterloo, On, N2L3G1, Canada, mpirnia@uwaterloo.ca

Coauthor(s): Claudio Canizares, Kankar Bhattacharya, Alfredo Vaccaro

Year: 2013

Abstract: An affine arithmetic (AA) method is proposed to solve an optimal power flow problem with uncertain generation sources. In the AA-based OPF problem, all the state and control variables are treated in affine form to represent forecast, model error, and other sources of uncertainty. Furthermore, the proposed AA-based OPF problem is applied to the determination of operational margins of the thermal generators in grids with renewable generation.
