Title: Workforce Planning with Training and Learning

Author: Huan Jin, University of Iowa, Iowa City, IA, 52241, United States of America, huan-jin@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Barrett Thomas

Year: 2013

Abstract: Training is an effective way to expand workforce capacity using existing resources. Yet, incorporating the effects of training into optimization models that explicitly account for the effects of training on human learning complicates an already nonlinear integer program. We linearize the model via reformulation and develop a solution approach to overcome the significant increase in constraints and integer variables. Our results demonstrate the value of explicitly modeling training.
