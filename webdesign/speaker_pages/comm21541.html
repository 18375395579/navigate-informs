Title: Global Optimization with Non-polyhedral Convex Envelopes

Author: Sreekanth Rajagopalan, Graduate Student, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Doherty Hall 3110, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, sreekanr@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Aida Khajavirad, Nick Sahinidis

Year: 2013

Abstract: Central to the efficiency of deterministic global optimization algorithms is construction of sharp and cheap convex relaxations. In a recent work, Khajavirad and Sahinidis presented algebraic expressions for the convex envelopes of various functional forms that are building blocks of nonconvex optimization problems. We propose a cutting plane generation technique based on supporting hyperplanes of the proposed convex envelopes, and present extensive computational results.
