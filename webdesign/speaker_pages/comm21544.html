Title: Trust-search Methods for Unconstrained Optimization
Author: Philip Gill, University of California, San Diego, CA, United States of America, pgill@ucsd.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Recent research on interior methods has re-emphasized the role of sequential unconstrained optimization for the solution of very large nonlinear programming problems. We focus on the numerical linear algebra associated with a class of ``trust-search'' methods that combine the best features of line-search and trust-region methods for unconstrained optimization.
