Title: The Network Community Structure Detection Problem
Author: Jiaofei Zhong, Assistant Professor, University of Central Missouri, W.C. Morris Building, Room 222, Warrensburg, MO, 64093, United States of America, zhong@ucmo.edu
Coauthor(s): Zaixin Lu, Weili Wu, Zheng Gao, Weidong Chen, Yuanjun Bi
Year: 2013
Abstract: Many network systems of interest are rising from the real world, e.g. social networks. One typical issue is to find the community structures. We propose a community structure detection problem which aims to analyze the relationships among data via the network topology. We collect a series of unified definitions for community structures and formulate the community detection into an optimization problem to identify as many communities as possible, and develop a heuristic algorithm for it.
