Title: Integrated Supply Chain Design for Commodity Chemicals Production via Woody Biomass Fast Pyrolysis
Author: Yanan Zhang, Mechanical Engineering, Iowa State University, Ames, IA, 50011, United States of America, yananz@iastate.edu
Coauthor(s): Guiping Hu, Robert Brown
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study investigates the optimal production planning and facility locations for commodity chemicals production via woody biomass fast pyrolysis and hydroprocessing. The locations and capacities of distributed preprocessing hubs and centralized biorefinery facilities are optimized by a mixed integer linear programming (MILP) model. The objective is to maximize the net present value for a 20-years project based on this pathway. This approach is illustrated through a case study in Minnesota.
