Title: Bandits with Knapsacks

Author: Ashwinkumar Badanidiyuru, Phd Student, Cornell University, 312 Thurston ave, A34, Ithaca, NY, 14850, United States of America, ashwinkumarbv@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Aleksandrs Slivkins, Robert Kleinberg

Year: 2013

Abstract: We introduce a model for Multi-armed bandit problems with supply limits, called ``bandits with knapsacks, that combines aspects of stochastic integer programming with online learning. We present two learning algorithms whose reward is close to the information-theoretic optimum. Further, we illustrate the generality of the problem by presenting applications in a number of different domains including routing, scheduling and electronic commerce.
