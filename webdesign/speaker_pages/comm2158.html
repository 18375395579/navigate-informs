Title: Comparing Production Planning Policies for the Integration of Lot Sizing and Scheduling Decisions
Author: Martin Ravetti, Federal University of Minas Gerais, Avenida Antonio Carlos, 6627, Pampulha, Belo Horizonte, Brazil, gravetti.martin@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Bernardo Furtado, Maurício Souza
Year: 2012
Abstract: We deal with production planning problem integrating lot sizing and scheduling decisions. The problem is based on a real case and two integration procedures combining heuristics and exact approaches are compared. A set of instances considering a flowshop scenario is generated to test and compare the proposed metodologies.
