Title: Using Vector Quantization & the P-Median Problem in Radiotherapy
Author: Allen Holder, Trinity University Mathematics, One Trinity Place, San Antonio, United States, aholder@trinity.edu
Coauthor(s): Gino Lim, Josh Reese
Year: 2006
Abstract: We show that the discrete versions of quantization design and the P-median problem are the same. We use this connection to heuristically solve a combinatorial problem in radiotherapy design.
