Title: A Tabu Search Heuristic for an Integrated Production and Distribution System
Author: Claudio B. Cunha, Associate Professor, University of Sao Paulo, Av. Prof. Almeida Prado, Tr.2, n.83, Escola Politecnica - Cid. Universitaria, Sao Paulo, SP, 05508900, Brazil, cbcunha@usp.br
Coauthor(s): Andrei Znamensky
Year: 2008
Abstract: Motivated by the importance of integrating production and distribution aiming to reduce holding costs and lead times of products across a supply chain, we describe a two-phase tabu search heuristic to solve this problem, which also considers a Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) policy. Computational experiments for benchmark instances show that our heuristic is effective, and provides better solutions compared to previously reported results.
