Title: Enhancing Computational Techniques for Stochastic Linear Programs
Author: Udom Janjarassuk, Student, Lehigh University, 200 West Packer Avenue, Mohler Lab, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, udj2@lehigh.edu
Coauthor(s): Jeffrey Linderoth
Year: 2008
Abstract: We focus on solving large-scale two-stage stochastic linear programs with a trust-region enhanced, L-shaped decomposition method. Techniques for improving the computational efficiency of the algorithm, including warm-starts and dynamic cut aggregation will be discussed. Computational results will be given to demonstrate the effectiveness of the various techniques
