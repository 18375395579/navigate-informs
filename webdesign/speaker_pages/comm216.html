Title: A Receding Horizon Approach to the Trading of Autocorrelated Returns under Transaction Costs

Author: James Primbs, Associate Professor, University of Texas at Dallas, 800 W. Campbell Road, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, jap120030@utdallas.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of optimally trading returns that are autocorrelated when transaction costs and other constraints are present. We develop an efficient suboptimal receding horizon algorithm in which a quadratic program is computed at each rebalancing opportunity. Numerical results demonstrate the performance of the approach.
