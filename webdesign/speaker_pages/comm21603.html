Title: Adaptive Random Search with Discarding for Continuous Simulation-based Optimization

Author: Liujia Hu, PhD candidate, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Groseclose Bldg Room 344, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, lhu9@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Sigrun Andradottir

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose an Adaptive Search with Resampling and Discarding (ASRD) approach for solving stochastic optimization problems with uncountable feasible regions. ASRD is a framework for designing algorithms that are adaptive both in seeking new points and in keeping or discarding already sampled points. We present conditions under which ASRD is convergent almost surely and provide numerical results showing that ASRD is promising, especially for difficult problems with large numbers of local optima.
