Title: Gradient Based Methods for Large-scale Cone Program

Author: Zhaosong Lu, Assistant Professor, Simon Fraser University, 250 - 13450 102nd Avenue, Surrey, BC, V3T 0A3, Canada, zhaosong@sfu.ca

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk, we discuss various gradient methods for cone program whose underlying cones are product of nonnegative orthant, second-order and semidefinite cones. Their associated iteration computational costs are compared. We finally demonstrate the performance of these methods based on some randomly generated cone programming problems.
