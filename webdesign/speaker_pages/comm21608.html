Title: Convexification Techniques for Separable Polynomial Inequalities

Author: Mohit Tawarmalani, Professor, Purdue University, 100 S Grant Street, Room 4019, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, mtawarma@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Jean-Philippe Richard

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we develop techniques for convexifying separable polynomial covering inequalities over a hypercube using disjunctive arguments. We discuss various decomposition strategies for creating convex hulls. Then, we provide various linear and nonlinear valid inequalities in an explicit form. We discuss separation procedures for deriving other valid inequalities. Finally, we extend our results to quasi-convex knapsacks and to reverse convex constraints.
