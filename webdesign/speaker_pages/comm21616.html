Title: Preprocessing Stochastic Integer Programs with Stochastic Decomposition

Author: Suvrajeet Sen, Professor, University of Southern California, Daniel J. Epstein Department of ISE, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, S.sen@USC.edu

Coauthor(s): Yifan Liu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Stochastic Decomposition (SD) provides a relatively stable platform for solving stochastic linear programs with both discrete and continuous random variables. We report the performance of such a preprocessing step for decomposition-based branch-and-cut algorithms such as D2BAC.
