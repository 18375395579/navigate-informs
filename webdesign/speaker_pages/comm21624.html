Title: A Study of Robust Lot-sizing Problem
Author: Zhili Zhou, PhD student, School of Industrial Engineering University of Oklahoma, 202 W. Boyd St., Room 124, Norman, OK, United States of America, zhilizhou@ou.edu
Coauthor(s): Yongpei Guan
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this paper, we consider a lot-sizing problem in which severe events may happen such that the normal process will be disrupted. Our objective is to provide a robust schedule such that the total cost is minimized with the consideration of uncertain extreme events. A Minimax formulation is formulated and a corresponding algorithm is developed. Our preliminary study verifies the effectiveness of our approach. The applications of our model include production and inventory planning problems.
