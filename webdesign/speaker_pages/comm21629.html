Title: On the Convex Hull of some Nonlinear Sets
Author: Jean-Philippe Richard, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, richard@ise.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Mohit Tawarmalani, Trang Nguyen
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study how the convex hull of certain nonlinear sets can be obtained through lifting. In particular, for a bilinear set in three variables, we derive a complete (nonlinear) description of the convex hull in the space of original variables. We then provide constructive derivations of these inequalities. Next, we describe how lifting can be used to construct the convex hull of disjunctive sets. We illustrate the applicability of this result by deriving the nonlinear convex hull of simple sets.
