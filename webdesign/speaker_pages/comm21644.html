Title: Computational Methods for the Dynamic Traffic Information Update Problem under Vehicle-to-Vehicle Communications

Author: Yong Hoon Kim, Graduate student, Purdue University, 3000 Kent ave., West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, kim523@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study proposes an efficient vehicle-based graph theoretical approach to map the information flow to identify the dynamic traffic information update under V2V communications. The efficacy of the proposed model is demonstrated and compared with a information-based graph theoretical approach and a dynamic programming approach.
