Title: Transmission Switching for Improving Wind Power Utilization

Author: Jianhui Wang, Affiliate Professor, Auburn University, 9700 S. Cass Avenue, Bldg. 221, Argonne, IL 60439, USA, Argonne, IL, 60439, United States of America, jianhui.wang@anl.gov

Coauthor(s): Feng Qiu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study explores the possibility of using transmission switching, to change transmission network topology,for accommodating higher utilization of wind power. The uncertainty in wind power is modeled by scenarios, and the transmission switching problem is formulated as a two-stage stochastic integer program, minimizing thermal generator costs while maintaining a specified utilization level for wind power.
