Title: Gaussian Processes in Trust-Region Optimization Methods

Author: Patricia Hough, Sandia National Laboratories, PO Box 969, MS 9159, Livermore, CA, United States of America, pdhough@sandia.gov

Year: 2008

Abstract: Surrogate-based optimization methods have become popular choices for solving simulation-based optimization problems. We describe an interface between two open open source software packages, OPT++ and tgp, that allows the use Gaussian processes as surrogates in a trust-region method. We consider multiple sampling methods to guide surrogate construction at each iteration and examine the effects on computational efficiency of the algorithm for test functions and a mechanical deformation problem.
