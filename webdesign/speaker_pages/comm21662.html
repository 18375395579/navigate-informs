Title: Vehicle Classification using Mobile Sensors

Author: Zhanbo Sun, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 2162, 14th Street, 2nd Floor, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, sunz2@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeff Ban

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider staffing agents in which an initial forecast of the call volume is given at the beginning of the day. After an initial observation period, the manager has the ability to update the staffing level based on a revised forecast. The manager operates under QoS constraints which can be very general. The resulting problem is formulated as a two-stage stochastic program. When utilization is the metric, the resulting solution can be written in a closed form analogous to a newsvendor solution.
