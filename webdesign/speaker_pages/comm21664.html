Title: The Myth of Micro-Aggregation
Author: Rathindra Sarathy, Professor, Oklahoma State University, Spears School of Business, Stillwater, OK, 74078, United States, sarathy@okstate.edu
Coauthor(s): Krish Muralidhar
Year: 2006
Abstract: Micro-aggregation is often proposed as a simple but effective technique for masking numerical confidential data. In this presentation, we compare the performance of micro-aggregation with other procedures for masking numerical data. The results clearly show that, compared to other masking procedures, micro-aggregation generally provides lower data utility and/or higher disclosure risk and must be used with caution.
