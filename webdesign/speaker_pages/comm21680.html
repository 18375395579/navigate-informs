Title: Constructing Uncertainty Sets from Data

Author: Vishal Gupta, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Ave, E40-135, Operations Research Center, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, vgupta1@mit.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Modeling uncertainty via uncertainty sets and using robust optimization has become increasingly popular in recent years. In many applications, however, it is not clear how to construct an appropriate set. In this work we consider a nonparametric, data-driven approach to constructing uncertainty sets directly from historical samples of the uncertainty. Robust optimization problems using these sets remain tractable and enjoy strong probabilistic guarantees.
