Title: Robust Optimization for Simulation-based Problems
Author: Omid Nohadani, MIT, 77 Mass. Ave., Cambridge, MA, 02139, nohadani@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas
Year: 2008
Abstract: Most robust optimization techniques assume that underlying cost functions are given explicitly. We present a robust optimization method for problems with nonconvex cost functions and problems based on simulations such as PDE solvers and kriging metamodels. This technique can be employed for most real-world problems. With an actual engineering problem in nano-photonic design, we demonstrate a significant improvement of the robustness in a high-dimensional design space with a nonconvex objective function.
