Title: Capacity Investment in Electricity Markets: Perfect Competition
Author: Gul Gurkan, Associate Professor, Tilburg University, Warandelaan 2, Tilburg, 5037 AB, Netherlands, ggurkan@uvt.nl
Coauthor(s): Yves Smeers, Ozge Ozdemir
Year: 2007
Abstract: We analyze investment choices of firms using a two-stage game; capacities are installed in stage one and production takes place in an energy-only market at stage two. Under deterministic demand, we show equivalence of the two-stage game and a single optimization problem. Under demand uncertainty, we essentially have a two-stage stochastic game and an equilibrium point can be found by solving a two-stage stochastic program.
