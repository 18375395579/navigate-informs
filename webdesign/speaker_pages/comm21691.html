Title: Use of Energy Storages to Balance Regenerative Energy Supply and Consumers?Demand

Author: Debora Mahlke, Technical University of Darmstadt, Schlossgartenstr. 7, Darmstadt, 64289, Germany, mahlke@mathematik.tu-darmstadt.de

Coauthor(s): Alexa Epe, Andrea Zelmer, Alexander Martin, Hermann-Josef Wagner

Year: 2007

Abstract: We deal with the integration of offshore wind parks into power supply networks. As regenerative energy supply and consumers demand strongly fluctuate, energy storages and power plants are used to compensate the imbalance. We present a mixed-integer nonlinear model where nonlinear efficiencies, start-up costs, and switching processes are considered. A piecewise linear approximation of the nonlinearities yields a mixed-integer linear program. For its solution a Branch&Cut algorithm is presented.
