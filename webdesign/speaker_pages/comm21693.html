Title: Some New T-space Facets

Author: K. Chen, Georgia Tech, School of Mathematics, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, kchen@math.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Ellis Johnson, William Cook

Year: 2007

Abstract: We discuss the infinite group master polyedron and its connection to the generation of cutting planes for general mixed-integer programming. In 2003, Gomory and Johnson gave two different three-slope T-space facet constructions, both of which shared a slope with the corresponding Gomory mixed-integer cut. We give a new three-slope facet which is independent of the GMIC. We also give the first four-slope T-space facet construction.
