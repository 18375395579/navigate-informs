Title: On-demand Jets: An Approach for Solving the Acceptance/Rejection Problem

Author: Satish Ukkusuri, Assistant Professor, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 4032 JEC, Troy, NY, 12180, United States, ukkuss@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Wilfredo Yushimito

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk will discuss a new formulation for solving the scheduling problem of On-Demand jets considering the acceptance/rejection of requests. An important problem that has not been addressed so far is whether or not to accept a request which is consequent with initial scheduling. This paper proposes a simple heuristic that benefits from the savings that can be obtained by using hard time windows. Numerical results on test networks are presented.
