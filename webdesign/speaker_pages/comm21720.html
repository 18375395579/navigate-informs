Title: Robust Rankings for American College Football

Author: Sam Burer, Professor, University of Iowa, S346 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, United States of America, samuel-burer@uiowa.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a model for calculating robust rankings of college football teams. Using several seasons of data, we discover that the top-25 rankings of a well-known computer system are quite sensitive to (hypothetical) changes in the outcomes of games between very weak teams. So we propose a robust variant of the rankings based on computing an optimal solution of a difficult (but not impossible!) optimization problem. We confirm empirically that our rankings are considerably more robust.
