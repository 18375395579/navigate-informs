Title: Piecewise Linear Approximation of Bi-level Problems in Transportation

Author: Ximing Wang, University of Florida, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, X.Wang@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Siriphong Lawphongpanich, Yafeng Yin

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk proposes a piecewise linear approximation scheme for solving bi-level problems in transportation. The scheme allows a bi-level problem to be solved approximately as a linear integer program. The approximate solution can be further refined by adding additional linear pieces and solving the expanded linear integer program starting from a previous solution.
