Title: A Branch-and-Price-and-Cut Approach for Maximum Multiple Weighted Clique Problem

Author: Seyed Javad Sajjadi, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave, ENC 2400, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, ssajjadi@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Bo Zeng, Xiaoning Qian

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider deriving a collection of cliques with the maximum weight in a general network where both nodes and arcs are weighted, which can be potentially applied for the biomarker identification problem in bioinformatics. An algorithm integrating column generation, branch-and-bound and cutting plane methods is developed for efficient computing. We also present numerical results based on random networks and biological networks with real data.
