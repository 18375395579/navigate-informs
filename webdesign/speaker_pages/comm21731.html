Title: Probabilistic Constrained Problems with Compound Distributions

Author: Anh Ninh, Rutgers University, 640 Bartholomew Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, anhninh@rci.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Andras Prekopa

Year: 2013

Abstract: We prove that the compound Poisson distribution is log-concave, under some conditions for the distribution of the terms. Then we formulate and solve probabilistic constrained stochastic programming problems, for both the discrete and continuous distribution cases, regarding the compound random variables. Applications in insurance and finance will be mentioned.
