Title: New Algorithmic Framework for Lost Sales Models with Large Lead Times

Author: David Goldberg, Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, dgoldberg9@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Squillante, Dmitriy Katz, Mayank Sharma, Yingdong Lu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Lost sales inventory models are notoriously difficult to optimize due to the curse of dimensionality. We show that when lead times are large, a simple algorithm performs nearly optimally. The main insight is that when the lead time is large, no algorithm can ``beat the uncertainty, so even simple policies perform relatively well. Our main proof technique combines a novel coupling for suprema of random walks with arguments from queueing theory.
