Title: A Branch-and-Cut Algorithm for Convex MINLPs

Author: Serdar Yildiz, PhD Candidate, Lehigh University, 200 West Packer Ave, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, sey309@Lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Ashutosh Mahajan

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a branch-and-cut algorithm for solving convex mixed-integer nonlinear optimization (MINLP) problems. In contrast to the linearization-based approach of adding cutting planes to a linear relaxation of the MINLP, we add cuts directly to the continuous nonlinear-relaxation. We describe methods used to derive cuts from special combinatorial structures and nonlinear functions. Results obtained from computational experiments performed using the open source MINOTAUR toolkit are presented.
