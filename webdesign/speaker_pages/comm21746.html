Title: Advancing Branch-and-bound Techniques for Mixed-integer Multiobjective Programming

Author: Margaret Wiecek, Professor, Clemson University, Martin Hall O-208, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States of America, wmalgor@clemson.edu

Coauthor(s): Pietro Belotti, Banu Soylu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Properties of the Pareto set for mixed integer linear multiobjective programs (MOPs) are studied. Branch-and-bound (BB) rules for pure integer MOPs are reviewed and examined against their applicability for mixed integer MOPs. More effective components of a BB algorithm, namely a fathoming procedure and a data structure for Pareto solutions, especially tailored to mixed integer MOPs, are proposed and integrated into a BB algorithm.
