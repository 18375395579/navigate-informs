Title: A Path Relinking Algorithm with Ejection Chains for the Quadratic Assignment Problem

Author: Tabitha James, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, 1007 Pamplin Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, tajames@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Fred Glover, Cesar Rego

Year: 2007

Abstract: In a recent study we demonstrated the superiority of ejection chain (EC) methods over state-of-the-art very large scale neighborhood algorithms (VLSN) for the QAP. In addition, a path-relinking (PR) approach with simple neighborhood search has proved very effective in solving classical instances from QAPLIB. In the present study, we introduce a specialized approach that combines PR with EC neighborhoods for the solution of large QAP instances.
