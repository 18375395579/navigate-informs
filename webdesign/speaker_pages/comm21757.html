Title: Network Delay Inference from Additive Metrics

Author: Shankar Bhamidi, Postdoctoral Researcher, Dept of Mathematics, University of British Columbia, Dept of Mathematics, UBC, Room 121, 1984 Mathematics Road, Vancouver, BC, V6T 1Z2, Canada, shanky@stat.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Ram Rajagopal, Sebastien Roch

Year: 2008

Abstract: We use computational phylogenetic techniques to solve a central problem in inferential network monitoring. We design a novel algorithm for Multicast-based delay inference, i.e. the problem of reconstructing delay characteristics of a network from end-to-end delay measurements on network paths. Our inference algorithm is based on additive metric techniques used in phylogenetics.
