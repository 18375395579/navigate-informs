Title: Accelerating Cut Generation in D2 and D2-BAC Algorithms for Stochastic Mixed-Integer Programs
Author: Suvrajeet Sen, Professor, Department of Systems and Industrial Engineering, University of Arizona, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States, sen@sie.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Yang Yuan
Year: 2006
Abstract: The D2 and D2-BAC algorithms use strong disjunctive cuts in each iteration of the respective algorithms. However, these cuts can be somewhat time-consuming when the number of outcomes are very large. We show to speed-up the cut generation process. This capability is particularly critical for sampled decomposition algorithms for stochastic mixed-integer programs.
