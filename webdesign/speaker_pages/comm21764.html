Title: Distributed Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers

Author: Ermin Wei, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, 32-D640, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, erminwei@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Asuman Ozdaglar

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a network of agents that are cooperatively solving a global unconstrained optimization problem, where the objective function is the sum of privately known local objective functions of the agents. Recent literature for solving this problem focused on subgradient based methods. In this paper, we introduce a new distributed optimization algorithm based on Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers (ADMM) and show that this algorithm converges at the rate O(1/k).
