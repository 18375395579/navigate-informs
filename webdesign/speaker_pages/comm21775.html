Title: Parallel Optimization Algorithms for the Lifetime Prolonging Design of Wireless Sensor Networks

Author: Hui Lin, Texas A&M University, Industrial and System Engineering, College Station, TX, 77843-3131, United States of America, linhui@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Halit Uster

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present an integrated analytical modeling framework for wireless sensor networks design incorporating topology control and routing decisions. The model attempts to promote energy efficiency with the purpose of prolonging network lifetime.We develop efficient parallel heuristics reinforced with exact methods and cut generations. Computational evidence suggests that such framework and algorithm can provide very good solutions with significantly improved solution times compared to exact methods.
