Title: A Scenario Decomposition Approach for Solving Optimization Problems with Coherent Risk Measures

Author: Maciej Rysz, University of Iowa, 751 W. Benton St. Apt 23, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, maciej-rysz@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Pavlo Krokhmal

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider large-scale portfolio optimization problems involving higher-moment coherent risk measures. An efficient solution algorithm for decomposing non-redundant scenarios by solving a series of relaxation problems containing linear combinations of scenarios is proposed. Numerical case studies demonstrating significant improvements in computation time are presented.
