Title: A Lifted Linear Programming Branch-and-bound Algorithm for Mixed Integer Conic Quadratic Programs

Author: Juan Pablo Vielma, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, jvielma@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Shabbir Ahmed, George Nemhauser

Year: 2007

Abstract: We develop a linear programming based branch-and-bound algorithm for mixed integer conic quadratic programs, which is based on a higher dimensional or lifted polyhedral relaxation of conic quadratic constraints introduced by Ben-Tal and Nemirovski. We present results of computational experiments on a series of portfolio optimization problems and show that the algorithm significantly outperforms commercial and open source solvers based on both linear and nonlinear relaxations.
