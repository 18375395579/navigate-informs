Title: A Two-sided Laplace Inversion Algorithm with Computable Error Bounds and its Applications in Financial Engineering

Author: Steven Kou, Professor, Columbia University, 312 Mudd Building, Department of IEOR, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, sk75@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Ning Cai

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose an inversion algorithm for two-sided Laplace transforms with computable error bounds. The algorithm can achieve any desired accuracy. In many cases, the bounds decay exponentially, leading to fast computation. Therefore, the algorithm is especially suitable to provide benchmarks. Examples from financial engineering show that our algorithm is fast and easy to implement.
