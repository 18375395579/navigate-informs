Title: Impact Solver for Optimization Services

Author: Huanyuan Sheng, Northwestern University, Tech C231, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States, h-sheng@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Sanjay Mehrotra, Jun Ma

Year: 2006

Abstract: We improve our IMPACT solver for broader range of optimization problems. New algorithms are being developed and tested for large scale mixed nonlinear integer problems. Combining with Optimization Services(OS) protocols, we plan to provide a robust solver service for applications of mathematical programming.
