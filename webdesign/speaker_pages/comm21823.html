Title: Numerical Results Using AMIGO (A Mixed Integer Global Optimizer)

Author: Zsolt Ugray, Assistant Professor, Business Information Systems Department, USU, 3515 Old Main Hill, Logan, UT, 84321, United States, Zsolt.Ugray@usu.edu

Coauthor(s): János D. Pintér

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present some preliminary results for solving mixed integer global optimization problems using AMIGO (A Mixed Integer Global Optimizer).
