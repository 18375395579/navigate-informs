Title: Towards Efficient Higher-order Semi-definite Relaxations for Max-cut
Author: Miguel F. Anjos, Canada Research Chair, Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal, C.P. 6079, Succ. Centre-Ville, Montreal, QC, H3C 3A7, Canada, miguel-f.anjos@polymtl.ca
Coauthor(s): Elspeth Adams, Franz Rendl, Angelika Wiegele
Year: 2013
Abstract: The basic semidefinite relaxation for max-cut can be tightened by adding facet-defining inequalities for the metric polytope, or more generally valid inequalities for the convex hull of incidence vectors of cuts, known as the cut polytope. We start with the basic semidefinite relaxation intersected with the metric polytope, and iteratively refine this relaxation using a new class of cuts called polytope cuts. We present theoretical insights as well as computational results.
