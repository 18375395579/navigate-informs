Title: Robustness to Dependency in Portfolio Optimization using Overlapping Marginals

Author: Karthik Natarajan, Associate Professor, Singapore University of Technology and Design, 20 Dover Drive, Singapore, 138682, Singapore, natarajan_karthik@sutd.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Xuan Vinh Doan, Xiaobo Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we develop a distributionally robust portfolio optimization model to dependency structures. For a Frechet class of distributions with overlapping marginals, we show that the portfolio optimization problem is efficiently solvable with linear programming. Lastly, we use a data-driven approach using real financial data to identify the Frechet class of distributions and then optimize the portfolio over this class of distributions.
