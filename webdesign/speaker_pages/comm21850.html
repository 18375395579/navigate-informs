Title: Stability and Convergence in Large-scale Dynamic Traffic Assignment
Author: Stephen Boyles, Assistant Professor, The University of Texas, Austin, 1 University Station C1761, Austin, TX, United States of America, sboyles@mail.utexas.edu
Coauthor(s): C. Matthew Pool
Year: 2012
Abstract: This work reviews, implements and compares existing techniques for finding equilibrium solutions in simulation-based dynamic traffic assignment and proposes novel approaches. Numerical experiments assess the convergence and stability properties of these approaches in realistic-sized networks.
