Title: Payment Scheduling for a Project with Resource Constraints and Two Clients

Author: Joseph Szmerekovsky, Assistant Professor, North Dakota State University, Putnam Hall, PO Box 5137, Fargo, ND, 58105-5137, United States, joseph.szmerekovsky@ndsu.nodak.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The case of a project contractor with two clients is considered. A mathematical programming formulation for the irregular starting time costs problem with resource constraints is presented. The problem is solved to determine how the contractor allocates resources across the two clients' projects. In particular, the effect of the payment schedule chosen by each client is investigated.
