Title: Iterative Scenario Based Reduction Technique for Optimizing the Conditional Value-at-Risk
Author: Roberto Minguez, University of Castilla-La Mancha, Avda. Camilo Jose Cela s/n, Ciudad Real, CR, 13071, Spain, roberto.minguez@uclm.es
Coauthor(s): Raquel Garcia-Bertrand
Year: 2008
Abstract: In the last decades, several tools for managing risks in competitive markets, such us CVaR, have been developed. These techniques are primarily based on scenarios, which in case of large-scale models increase considerably the size of the problems to be solved according to the number of scenarios, resulting in intractable problems. An iterative scheme is proposed which allows obtaining the equivalent solution for a given number of scenarios but solving problems with a reduced number of scenarios.
