Title: Dynamic Programming for Efficient Container Inspection Policies
Author: Noam Goldberg, Rutgers University, 640 Bartholomew Rd, Piscataway, United States of America, ngoldberg@rutcor.rutgers.edu
Coauthor(s): Paul Kantor, Jonathan Word, Endre Boros
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider the problem of optimally combining any given set of stochastically independent diagnostic tests into an inspection system that maximizes detection subject to a budget constraint. Our inspection policies can be represented as mixtures of decision trees. We present a dynamic programming algorithm that enumerates all efficient, undominated, inspection policies. Our approach directly solves the bicriterion optimization problem, allowing for sensitivity analysis.
