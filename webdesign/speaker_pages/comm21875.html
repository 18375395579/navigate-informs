Title: Price Optimization Under Generalized Extreme Value Models
Author: Amr Farahat, Assistant Professor, Washington University in St. Louis, Olin Business School, St. Louis, MO, United States of America, farahat@wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Renyu (Philip) Zhang
Year: 2012
Abstract: We investigate the multi-product price optimization problem under the Generalized Extreme Value discrete choice model proposed by McFadden (1978). We present structural results leading to a tractable approach to solving this problem which exhibits the non-concavity issues inherent in its special case: the (nested) multinomial logit model.
