Title: Multi-Start Tabu Search and Diversification Strategies for the Quadratic Assignment Problem

Author: Tabitha James, Pamplin College of Business, Business Information Technology, Blacksburg, VA, United States, tajames@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Cesar Rego, Fred Glover

Year: 2006

Abstract: We introduce several multi-start tabu search variants and show the benefit of utilizing strategic diversification approaches within the tabu search framework for the QAP. Results on a set of instances from QAPLIB demonstrate the improvement of our TS multi-start variants over the classic tabu search approach widely used for the QAP. We also show that our new procedures are highly competitive with the best recently introduced methods from the literature, including more complex hybrid approaches.
