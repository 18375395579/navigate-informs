Title: Price Optimization under Two-stage Models

Author: Srikanth Jagabathula, Professor, NYU Stern School of Business, 44 West Fourth Street, KMC Suite 8-74, new york, NY, 10012, United States of America, sjagabat@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Paat Rusmevichientong

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of finding optimal prices under the following two-stage model of choice: each incoming customer samples a preference list, a willingness-to-pay, and chooses to purchase the most preferred product according to the sampled preference list among the products whose price is less than that of the sampled willingness-to-pay. This problem is provably hard in general. We propose a PTAS and an algorithm that can be shown to be efficient under reasonable conditions.
