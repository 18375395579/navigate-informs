Title: Hunting Drug Smugglers: An Optimal Search Problem in Continuous Time and Space
Author: Jesse Pietz, Naval Postgraduate School, 1411 Cunningham Road, Monterey, CA, United States of America, japietz@nps.edu
Coauthor(s): Johannes Royset
Year: 2012
Abstract: We consider search for moving targets in continuous time and space, and formulate a mixed-integer nonlinear program whose solution yields an optimal search plan. We highlight features of this highly challenging problem that lead to specialized algorithms and discuss solutions for application to drug smuggling interdiction operations.
