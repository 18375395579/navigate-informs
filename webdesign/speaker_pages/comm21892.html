Title: Automated Mechanism Design, with Applications to Auctions
Author: Yevgeniy Vorobeychik, Sandia National Labs, 7011 East Ave, Livermore, CA, 94550, United States of America, eug.vorobey@gmail.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: We present general methods for automated mechanism design when both the design, and player strategy spaces are constrained. We offer Bayesian and worst-case (robust) formulations, and present a technique based on stochastic search. Finally, we demonstrate the effectiveness of our methods in a variety of auction settings.
