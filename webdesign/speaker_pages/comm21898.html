Title: Real-Time Optimization with ROMeo & IPOPT

Author: Brian Baumrucker, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, bbaumruc@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Lorenz Biegler

Year: 2006

Abstract: The ROMeo (Rigorous On-line Modeling with Equation based Optimization) process modeling environment incorporates both reduced space SQP and IPOPT algorithms and is modified to deal with complementarity constraints. Numerical comparisons of the solvers will be presented and complementarity reformulations will be explored on a number of test problems.
