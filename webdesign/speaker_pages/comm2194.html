Title: A Reformulation-based Branch-and-bound for Non-convex Mixed-integer Nonlinear Programming

Author: Pietro Belotti, Tepper S. of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, United States, belotti@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): François Margot, Pierre Bonami, Lorenz Biegler, Andreas Waechter, Jon Lee, Ignacio Grossmann

Year: 2007

Abstract: We describe a spatial Branch-and-Bound for non-convex MINLP. At the core of the algorithm is a reformulation approach inspired by the work of Sherali and Adams. Bound tightening and special branch rules are applied to improve the lower bound, while an upper bound is sought with an NLP solver. The procedure is implemented as a specialized cut generator for the Bonmin MINLP solver, in the COIN-OR framework (www.coin-or.org). We present some preliminary results on real-world MINLP instances.
