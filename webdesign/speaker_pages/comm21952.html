Title: Integrated Warehouse-Inventory-Transportation Planning Under Uncertainty

Author: Nan Kong, Assistant Professor, Purdue University, Evanston, IN, United States of America, nkong@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Christopher Hagmann, Pratik Parikh

Year: 2013

Abstract: Warehouses play a vital role in mitigating variations in supply and demand and in providing value-added services in a supply chain. However, warehousing decisions are often not incorporated when developing distribution plans in practice. We develop a two-stage stochastic integer quadratically-constrained program for the problem of integrated warehouse-inventory-transportation planning under uncertainty. We apply a scenario decomposition approach and report preliminary computational findings.
