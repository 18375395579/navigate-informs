Title: Security-Constrained Optimal Power Flow with Sparsity Control and Efficient Parallel Algorithms

Author: Andy Sun, Assistant Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 755 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, xsun84@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Dzung Phan

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a new model for security-constrained optimal power flow problem, which can find a post-contingency corrective action with a minimum number of adjustments while achieving essentially the same minimum generation cost. We also propose a decomposition algorithm to solve the problem, which can be parallelized. Computational results are presented for several standard IEEE test systems and large-scale real-world power networks.
