Title: Truthful Bundle/Multi-unit Double Auction Mechanism Design

Author: Leon Chu, Marshall School of Business, University of Southern California, Bridge Hall 401, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, leonyzhu@usc.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We address the mechanism design problem for an exchange market with multiple buyers and sellers. Each buyer asks for a bundle of various commodities, and each seller provides multiple units of one commodity. We implement a novel padding technique to design the truthful double auction mechanisms under the multi-stage design approach. To the best of our knowledge, these are the first strategy-proof, individually rational, budget-balanced mechanisms for the specified exchange environment.
