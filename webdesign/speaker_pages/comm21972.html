Title: Price and Ore Uncertainty on the Ultimate Pit Problem

Author: Daniel Espinoza, Assistant Professor, University of Chile, Av. Republica 701, Santiago, 8370439, Chile, daespino@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Eduardo Moreno, Marcos Goycoolea

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, we will present ongoing research on stochastic or uncertain open pit planning, either on ore-grade estimation, or in price. We compare several standard risk models (VaR, CVaR and others) by using either the Sample Average Approximation (SAA) technique, and robust counterparts of some simple formulations. We will focus on the strengths and drawbacks of the proposed techniques, and intensive computational experiments for the analysis.
