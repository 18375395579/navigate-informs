Title: Robust SAA

Author: Nathan Kallus, PhD Student, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Ave, E40-149, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, kallus@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas, Vishal Gupta

Year: 2013

Abstract: The sample average approximation (SAA) approach to data-driven optimization generally enjoys convergence as the sample size grows infinitely, but lacks finite-sample performance guarantees that apply as generally. We present a robustification of the SAA using tools of statistical testing, gaining generic probabilistic guarantees without losing SAA's wide-applicability, convergence, and tractability. The convergence of guarantees suggests that they are not overly conservative.
