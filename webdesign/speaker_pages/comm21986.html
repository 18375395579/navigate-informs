Title: Approximate Dynamic Programming for Dynamic Ambulance Deployment
Author: Huseyin Topaloglu, Cornell University, Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, United States, ht88@cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Shane Henderson, Mateo Restrepo
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present algorithms for repositioning ambulances in an emergency medical service system. Our approach is based on approximating the value functions in the dynamic programming formulation of the problem, and using simulation- and regression-based techniques to fine-tune the approximations.
