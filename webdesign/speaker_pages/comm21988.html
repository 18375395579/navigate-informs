Title: The Trust Region Subproblem with Non-Intersecting Linear Constraints

Author: Sam Burer, Professor, University of Iowa, S346 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, United States of America, samuel-burer@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Boshi Yang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study an extended trust region subproblem in which the trust region intersects the unit ball with m linear inequality constraints. We show that a certain convex relaxation exhibits no gap as long as the linear constraints are non-intersecting in the unit ball.
