Title: A Robust Algorithm for Planning Ground Delay Programs Under Uncertainty

Author: Avijit Mukherjee, University of California, avijit@ucsc.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Hoffman, Michael Ball

Year: 2007

Abstract: A new algorithm, which considers both equity and efficiency goals, for planning ground delay programs under uncertainty is presented. Experimental results with realistic flight schedules and airport capacity indicates that the algorithm produces an efficient frontier of solutions compared to a distance-based flight exemption policy, which is a standard practice.
