Title: MIP Techniques for Product Pricing under a Reservation Price Model

Author: Tor Myklebust, University of Waterloo, 200 University Ave. W., Waterloo, ON, N2L 6S7, Canada, tmyklebu@csclub.uwaterloo.ca

Coauthor(s): Levent Tuncel, Malcolm Sharpe

Year: 2013

Abstract: We discuss techniques for solving instances of a certain product pricing problem based on reservation prices. We detail some primal heuristics, a column generation technique, several classes of cuts, and a preprocessing step. We will present results of some computational experiments with these techniques. Some of these techniques are problem-specific, but we will indicate generalisations to a class of bilinear MIPs where appropriate.
