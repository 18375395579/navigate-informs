Title: Convex Integer Minimization in Variable Dimension

Author: David Adjiashvili, Dr., ETH Zurich, ETH HG, Raemistrasse 101, Zurich, 8092, Switzerland, david.adjiashvili@ifor.math.ethz.ch

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a convex integer minimization problem in variable dimension. Given an integer matrix W with n columns and d rows, a convex function f over R^d and a polyhedron P in R^n, we would like to minimize f(Wx) subject to x in P, integer. Assuming d to be fixed, and W to be given in unary representation, we study classes of polyhedra P, for which the latter problem can be solved in polynomial time.
