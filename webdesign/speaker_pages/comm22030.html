Title: A Data-mining Guided Heuristic Approach for Sensor Distribution in Multi-station Assembly Processes

Author: Yuan Ren, Research Assistant, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Texas A&M University, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, renyuan@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yu Ding

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper presents a data mining guided evolutionary approach, which borrows strength from both data mining and evolutionary algorithm and compensates limitations of one another, and thus produces better solutions in less time. The proposed method is used to solve an optimal sensor distribution problem in multi-station assembly processes.
