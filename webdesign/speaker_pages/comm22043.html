Title: Managing Currency Supply in Mexico Banknote Supply Chain
Author: Yunxia Zhu, Assistant Professor, Rider University, Sweigart Hall 358, 2083 Lawrenceville Road, Lawrenceville, NJ, 08648, United States of America, yuzhu@rider.edu
Coauthor(s): Subodha Kumar, Chelliah Sriskandarajah, Sara Rodríguez Sánchez
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper studies transportation and holding of banknotes of multiple denominations in Mexico Banknote Supply Chain. We first provide a MIP formulation for the Regional Bank’s supply planning problem after proving that the First Fit Decreasing strategy can guarantee an optimal solution of the special bin-packing problem faced in this study. We also propose several polynomial-time algorithms for deriving either optimal or near-optimal solutions for the problem under different settings.
