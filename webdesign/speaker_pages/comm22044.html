Title: Open Source Software for Interfacing and Support of Large-scale Embedded Nonlinear Optimization

Author: Roscoe Bartlett, Senior Member of the Technical Staff, Sandia National Laboritories, PO Box 5800, Albuquerque, NM, 87185-1318, United States of America, rabartl@sandia.gov

Year: 2008

Abstract: It is difficult for embedded nonlinear optimization to impact important application areas. I will discuss the Thyra effort in Trilinos to develop the software infrastructure for interfacing embedded nonlinear optimization solvers while keeping well integrated with production simulation codes. I will present the concepts behind “abstract numerical algorithms?that underlie Thyra and describe a case study involving the semi-production code Charon and the optimization solver MOOCHO.
