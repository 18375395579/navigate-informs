Title: Structural Similarity Measures for the VRP
Author: Arne Lokketangen, Arne.Lokketangen@hiMolde.no
Coauthor(s): David Woodruff
Year: 2007
Abstract: A decision maker might be equally interested in a set of different, good solutions, than in just the optimal solution. There is thus a need to distinguish between good solutions, based on some other concept than quality alone. We produce measures of difference of the type proposed in the Psychology literature . We suggest several such functions for the class of VRP problems, basing our difference on the underlying structure of solutions.
