Title: Lot Sizing with Minimum Order Quantity

Author: Young Woong Park, Northwestern University, Industrial Engineering and Management Sc, Evanston, IL, United States of America, ywpark@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Diego Klabjan

Year: 2012

Abstract: Minimum order quantity is often present in practical lot sizing problems. We study special cases of the general problem from the algorithmic and mathematical programming perspective. We exhibit a polynomial algorithm when capacity is constant and minimum order quantities are ordered. A linear programming extended formulation is provided for the case with constant capacity and minimum order quantity.
