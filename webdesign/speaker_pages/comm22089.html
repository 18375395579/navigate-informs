Title: Optimal Speed Trajectory for Fuel Consumption Reduction at Signalized Intersection

Author: Xiaozheng He, University of Minnesota, 500 Pillsbury Dr SE, Minneapolis, MN, 55414, United States of America, hexxx069@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Henry Liu

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this study, we formulate optimal speed trajectories at signalized intersection as solutions to a multi-phase optimal control problem, which considers the impact from vehicle queues due to traffic lights. By applying the pseudospectral method, this multi-phase optimal control problem is reformulated into a nonlinear programming problem which can be solved effectively. We demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed model by an example.
