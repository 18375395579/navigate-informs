Title: New Rational Generating Function Techniques for Integer Programming
Author: Matthias Koeppe, Otto-von-Guericke-Universitaet Magdeburg, FMA-IMO, Universitaetsplatz 2, Magdeburg, 39108, Germany, mkoeppe@merkur.math.uni-magdeburg.de
Year: 2007
Abstract: A. Barvinok (1994) presented a polynomial-time algorithm to count the number of integer points in a polytope, when the dimension is fixed, using his technique of polynomial-size rational generating functions. We present new efficient primal variants of the algorithm. We also present new applications in Integer Programming, based on the construction of discrete relaxations, for which rational generating techniques are used to compute strong bounds or prove infeasibility.
