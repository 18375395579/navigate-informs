Title: Aversion to Health Inequalities in Healthcare Prioritisation: A Multiobjective Perspective

Author: Alec Morton, London School of Economics and Political Science, Houghton Street, London, WC2A 2AE, United Kingdom, a.morton@lse.ac.uk

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper we discuss the prioritisation of healthcare projects where there is a concern about health inequalities. Our analysis begins with a standard welfare economic model of healthcare resource allocation and we show how the problem can be reformulated as one of finding a particular subset of the class of efficient solutions to an implied multiobjective optimisation problem. We demonstrate our approach through a worked example of treatment for clinical depression.
