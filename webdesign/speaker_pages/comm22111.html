Title: The Relative Efficiency of New England VA Medical Facilities
Author: Aysun Sunnecti, Northeastern University, 360 Huntington ave, 334Snell Engineering, Boston, MA, United States of America, sunnetci.a@neu.edu
Coauthor(s): James Benneyan
Year: 2007
Abstract: We investigate the relative efficiency of 39 New England Veterans Affairs medical facilities at producing good access, clinical outcomes, satisfaction, and quality. We propose and compare several approaches for handling proportional data bound between 0 and 1 in constant-returns-to-scale DEA models, including an odds-ratio approach. 13 facilities are identified on the efficiency frontier.
