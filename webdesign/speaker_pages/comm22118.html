Title: Conic Mixed-Integer Programming Cuts
Author: Alper Atamturk, Associate Professor, University of California at Berkeley, 4141 Etcheverry Hall, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, atamturk@ieor.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Vishnu Narayanan
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present valid inequalities for conic mixed-integer programming for cutting off infeasible points of its continuous relaxation. We discuss aggregation and lifting techniques in this context.
