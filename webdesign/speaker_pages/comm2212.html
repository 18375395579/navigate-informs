Title: An Integrated Patient Transportation and Operating Room Scheduling Model

Author: Sepehr Nemati Proon, PhD Student, University of Pittburgh, 3700 O'Hara Street, Pittsburgh, PA, 15261, United States of America, sen12@pitt.edu

Coauthor(s): Oleg Shylo, Andrew Schaefer, Oleg Prokopyev

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a model that integrates the optimal transportation of outpatients from their residences to a hospital and back using a capacitated fleet of vehicles, and surgery scheduling in an operating suite. The objective is to minimize the overall time that patients spend on the road and in the hospital. We formulate this problem as a mixed integer programming and present a Branch and Price algorithm to solve it.
