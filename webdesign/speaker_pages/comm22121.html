Title: Approximate Dynamic Programming for Inventory Allocation Under Uncertainty

Author: Huseyin Topaloglu, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, 223 Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, huseyin@orie.cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Sumit Kunnumkal

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present two approximate DP approaches for an inventory allocation problem. The first one uses linear approximations of the value function and exploits the LP formulation of the underlying DP. The second one uses Lagragian relaxation to relax the constraints that link the decisions for different production plants.
