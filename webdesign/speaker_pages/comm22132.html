Title: Spatial Branch-and-bound Algorithms for MINLP
Author: Pietro Belotti, Tepper S. of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, United States, belotti@andrew.cmu.edu
Coauthor(s): François Margot, Ignacio Grossmann, Lorenz Biegler, Jon Lee, Pierre Bonami, Andreas Waechter, Laszlo Ladanyi
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present a spatial branch-and-bound approach for solving non-convex MINLPs. A reformulation-linearization technique and an NLP solver are used to obtain a lower- and an upper bound, respectively. We describe specific branching rules and bound tightening techniques that help solving several classes of MINLP problems. We then report on our experience on a large set of both MINLP and continuous NLP instances.
