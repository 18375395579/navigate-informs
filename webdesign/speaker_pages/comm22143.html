Title: Size Matters: Optimal Calibration of Shrinkage Estimators for Portfolio Selection
Author: Alberto Martin-Utrera, Mr, University Carlos III of Madrid, C/ Madrid, 126 - 28903, Getafe, Madrid, 28903, Spain, amutrera@est-econ.uc3m.es
Year: 2012
Abstract: We carry out a comprehensive investigation of shrinkage estimators in the context of portfolio optimization and we make several contributions to this field. First, we propose new shrinkage estimators for both the moments of asset returns and portfolio weights. Second, we propose novel calibration criteria to compute the optimal shrinkage intensity. Finally, we propose a parametric and a nonparametric approach to estimate the shrinkage intensity.
