Title: Improvements in SDPT3

Author: Michael J. Todd, Professor, Cornell University, 229 Rhodes Hall, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, mjt7@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Kim-Chuan Toh, Reha Tutuncu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We will describe recent enhancements incorporated into SDPT3-4.0-beta, a code for solving semidefinite-quadratic- linear programming problems. This version can handle complex data and log barrier terms in the objective function. It deals with unrestricted variables better than earlier versions, and can exploit low-rank constraint matrices. In addition, a homogeneous self-dual variant is provided.
