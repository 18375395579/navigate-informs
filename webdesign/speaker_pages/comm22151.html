Title: Global Optimization Applications in Biomedicine
Author: Panos Pardalos, Distinguished Professor, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, ISE Department, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, pardalos@cao.ise.ufl.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this talk we are going to focus on global optimization techniques (multi-quadratic 0-1 integer programming) in computational neurosciences and biclustering (nonlinear fractional 0-1 integer programming) based data mining approaches in cancer research.
