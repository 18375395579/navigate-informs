Title: Duality Between Coherent Risk Measures and Stochastic Dominance Constraints in Optimization

Author: Darinka Dentcheva, Professor, Stevens Institute of Technology, Castle Point on Hudson, Hoboken, NJ, 07030, United States of America, darinka.dentcheva@stevens.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrzej Ruszczynski

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider optimization problems with nonlinear second order stochastic dominance constraints formulated as relations of Lorenz curves. We demonstrate that mean-risk models with law invariant coherent risk measures appear as dual optimization problems to the problems with stochastic dominance constraints.
