Title: Tabu Search Heuristic for the Network Design Model with Lead Time and Safety Stock Considerations
Author: Kyoungsun Lee, Ph.D. student, Purdue University, 425 W. State Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907-2056, United States, lee430@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Leyla Ozsen
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper presents a two-phase tabu search heuristic for the single product network design model with lead time and safety stock considerations. We introduce the concept of indirect-potential cost savings to the move evaluation step. The proposed tabu search heuristic provides near optimum solutions with much less computational efforts than the other published heuristics.
