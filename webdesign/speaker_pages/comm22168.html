Title: A Consensus-building Mechanism for Setting Service Expectations in Air Traffic Flow Management

Author: Prem Swaroop, PhD Candidate, Robert H Smith School of Business, University of Maryland, 3330C, Van Munching Hall, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, pswaroop@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Ball

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a voting-based mechanism for airlines to provide strategic input to the Air Navigation Service Provider. This involves some interesting problems and technologies -- largely: voting, optimization models, integer programming and column-generation, multi-attribute valuation models. Through large experiments, we have shown that the proposed mechanism is fairly robust and has promise in addressing the stated problem.
