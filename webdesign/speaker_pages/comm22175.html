Title: Multidimensional Assignment Problems: Graph Theoretic and Integer Programming Heuristics
Author: Chrysafis Vogiatzis, Ph.D. Student, University of Florida, Weil 303, Gainesville, FL, 32608, United States of America, chvogiat@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Panos Pardalos
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Multidimensional Assignment Problem (MAP) is a well-known NP-hard problem, which is often used to model multi-sensor multi-target, and data association problems. In this presentation, we propose two novel decomposition schemes, employing the Augmented Lagrangian, and a graph-theoretic description of the problem to solve it heuristically. Computational results will be presented to contrast our results to the state-of-the-art.
