Title: Promotion Optimization with Multiple Vehicles

Author: Georgia Perakis, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Sloan School of Management, 100 Main Street, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, georgiap@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Maxime Cohen, Zachary Leung

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study promotion optimization with multiple vehicles, i.e. which items to promote, at what prices and with which vehicle (e.g. flyer and display). We consider demand models that are easy to estimate from data and capture interesting consumer behavior. We propose and study a decomposition approach where price optimization and vehicle selection are solved sequentially. We study the solution of each sub-problem and validate our model through supermarket data.
