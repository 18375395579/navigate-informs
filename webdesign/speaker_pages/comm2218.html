Title: Two-stage Robust Network Design with Uncertain Capacities
Author: Alper Atamturk, University of California, 4175 Etcheverry Hall MC 1777, Berkeley, CA, United States of America, atamturk@berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Muhong Zhang
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this talk we consider a two-stage robust network design problem with uncertain capacities. We give a characterization of the feasible region for the first stage and discuss the separation problem under a budget uncertainty set. Numerical experiments will be presented.
