Title: CVX: Software for Disciplined Convex Programming

Author: Michael Grant, 100 Dovetail Lane, Georgetown, TX, 78628, United States of America, mcg@algorithmguy.com

Coauthor(s): Stephen Boyd

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk we present an overview of CVX, a software framework developed in MATLAB for constructing and solving disciplined convex programs. Constraints and objectives are specified in standard MATLAB syntax. In this talk we will demonstrate the software and discuss some of its more novel features, such as the automated conversion of geometric programs to semidefinite programs.
