Title: Optimal Design of Mixed AC-DC Distribution Systems for Commercial Buildings

Author: Steffen Rebennack, Colorado School of Mines: Division of Economics and Business, 816 15th Street, Golden, CO, 80501, United States of America, srebenna@mines.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen Frank

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a mathematical program and global optimization algorithm which determine maximally energy efficient designs for mixed AC-DC building electrical distribution systems. The research approach precisely quantifies building electrical energy efficiency at a systems level. The results of two case studies validate the optimization algorithm and demonstrate that well designed mixed AC-DC building systems can achieve higher efficiency than either AC or DC distribution used alone.
