Title: Quantum Control of Multi-Particle Simulated Annealing

Author: Orcun Molvalioglu, Graduate Student, University of Washington, Industrial Engineering, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States, orcun@u.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Zelda Zabinsky, Wolf Kohn

Year: 2006

Abstract: We represent the state evolution of Multi-Particle Simulated Annealing as a quantum system using the Schrödinger equation. We investigate feedback control strategies for generating efficient particle trajectories. As a part of the multi-particle algorithm we design a meta-controller compatible with the quantum representation of the algorithm.
