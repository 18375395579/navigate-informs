Title: Pathfinding Problems with Uncertainty

Author: Hiroyuki Sato, PhD Candidate, Naval Postgraduate School, Operations Research Department, Monterey, CA, 93940, United States, hsato@nps.edu

Coauthor(s): Johannes Royset

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study pathfinding problems where objective and constraint functions are subject to uncertainty. The vehicle traveling along the path may also be subject to attrition. Our solution approach is based on Lagrangian relaxation and a specialized branch-and-bound technique. We illustrate our approach with several examples from military route planning.
