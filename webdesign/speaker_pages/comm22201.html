Title: A New Voronoi Heuristics to Devide a Network into Equal Sized Sub-networks

Author: Atsuo Suzuki, Professor, Nanzan University, 27 Seirei-cho, Seto, Ai, Japan, atsuo@nanzan-u.ac.jp

Coauthor(s): Takehiro Furuta, Atsuyuki Okabe

Year: 2007

Abstract: By the network Voronoi diagram and the linear programming technique, we introduce a new method which divides a road network into equal sized sub-networks in a reasonable time. For location analysis on the Euclidean plane, such as the cell count method, we divide a region into equal size sub-regions easily. On the other hand, on a network, to divide a road network into equal sized sub-networks is a challenging problem. We apply our method to actual road networks and show the effectiveness.
