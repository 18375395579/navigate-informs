Title: Integrated Production and Distribution Scheduling with Committed Delivery Dates
Author: Zhi-Long Chen, University of Maryland, Robert H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, United States of America, ZChen@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Weiya Zhong
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider an integrated production-distribution scheduling problem faced by a make-to-order manufacturer with a commit-to-delivery business mode. The shipping cost of an order decreases linearly with the delivery time used. The problem is to minimize the total shipping cost subject to the committed delivery dates constraints. The problem is strongly NP-hard. We propose a polynomial-time heuristic algorithm and show that its worst-case performance ratio is bound by 2 and this bound is tight.
