Title: Handling Constraints in Practical Optimization Problems

Author: Santosh Tiwari, PhD, Vanderplaats Research & Development, Inc., 41700 Gardenbrook, Suite 115, Novi, MI, 48375, United States of America, stiwari@vrand.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Most real-world optimization problems involve a large number of constraints. Quite often, many of these constraints are active at the optimum. In this talk, a discussion of constraint handling methods is presented. An overview of available tools and techniques, and associated methodology is also presented. Significant emphasis is placed on problems for which it is not possible to evaluate objectives if the constraints are violated. Also discussed is the issue of handling pass/fail constraints.
