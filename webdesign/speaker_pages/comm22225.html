Title: Improving Web-site Structure to Facilitate Effective User Navigation

Author: Min Chen, University of Texas at Dallas, 800 West Campbell Road, School of Management, SM33, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, min.chen@student.utdallas.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: It has been long recognized that designing usable Web sites is not a trivial task. In this paper, we propose an integer programming model to improve the navigation effectiveness of a Web site while minimizing unnecessary changes to its structure, as it bears business or organizational logics. Thus, our approach can be applied for Web site maintenance on a regular basis. The test results show that we can provide significant improvements over the site structure by introducing very few changes.
