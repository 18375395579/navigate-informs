Title: The SYMPHONY MIP Solver

Author: Ashutosh Mahajan, Lehigh University, 200 W Packer Ave, Bethelehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, asm4@Lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Ted Ralphs, Menal Guzelsoy

Year: 2008

Abstract: SYMPHONY is an open source callable library for solving mixed integer linear programs (MILPs) and has several advanced features such as the ability to warm start the solution process. This talk will focus on recent improvements in primal heuristics, preprocessing, branching and management of valid inequalities that enable it to solve MILPs faster. Computational results are provided to show the effect of these techniques on SYMPHONY's performance as a MILP solver.
