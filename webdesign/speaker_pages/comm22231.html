Title: Locating Replenishment Stations for Electric Vehicles: Application to Danish Traffic Data
Author: Oli Madsen, Professor, Technical University of Denmark, DTU Transport Bygningstorvet 115, Kgs. Lyngby, 2800, Denmark, ogm@transport.dtu.dk
Coauthor(s): Anders Norrelund, Min Wen, Allan Olsen, Gilbert Laporte
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper considers the problem of locating electronic replenishment stations for electric vehicles on a traffic network with flow-based demand. The objective is to optimize the network performance, e.g. to maximize the flow covered by a prefixed number of stations, or to minimize the number of stations needed to cover traffic flows. Two mixed integer linear programming formulations are proposed to model the problem. These models are tested on real-life traffic data collected in Denmark.
