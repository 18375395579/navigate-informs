Title: Integrating Protection against Intentional Attacks with Facility Location Problem

Author: Chi Zhang, Assistant Professor, Tsinghua University, Department of Industrial Engineering, #1 Qinghua Yuan, Beijing, China, czhang@tsinghua.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Jose Emmanuel Ramirez-Marquez

Year: 2013

Abstract: To preserve continued effective performance of facilities, protection against intentional attacks need to be considered while determining optimal facility location strategies. This interaction is modeled as a simultaneous game and solved by a new algorithm. Viewing minimizing the expected disruption- solution of the proposed game- as one of the objectives, facility location problem is modeled as a multi-objective optimization problem and an evolutionary algorithm is employed to solve it.
