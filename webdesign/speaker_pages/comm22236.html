Title: Conic Scalarization for Multi Objective Quadratic Assignment Problems

Author: Zehra Kamisli Öztürk, Assistant Professor Doctor, Anadou University, Engineering Faculty, Industrial Engineering Department, Eskisehir, 26555, Turkey, zehrakozturk@gmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: The multi objective quadratic assignment problem (mQAP) is an non-deterministic polynomial-time complete (NPC) problem with many real-world applications. In this work, we consider to compare the quality of solution sets generated by the conic scalarization technique and a multi objective genetic algorithm of NSGA-II for various mQAP test problems.
