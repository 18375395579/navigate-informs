Title: Portfolio Selection with Multiple Spectral Risk Constraints

Author: Carlos Abad, Columbia University IEOR, 500 W 120th St, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, ca2446@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Garud Iyengar

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose an iterative algorithm to efficiently solve the portfolio selection problem with multiple spectral risk constraints (CVaR is a special case of the spectral risk function). In each step, the algorithm solves a very simple separable convex quadratic program. We report numerical results that show that our proposed algorithm is very efficient, and is at least two orders of magnitude faster than the state of the art general purpose solver for all practical instances.
