Title: Robust Customized Pricing
Author: Elcin Cetinkaya, Doctoral Student, Lehigh University, 200 W. Packer Avenue, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, elcin.cetinkaya@lehigh.edu
Coauthor(s): Aurelie Thiele
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study robust revenue management problems when companies request bids for services but their price-response function is not known precisely. The company bidding for these contracts only knows the previous bids it submitted to those businesses and whether they were accepted or not. We show how to derive tractable mathematical models for this problem and provide insights into the optimal solution. We also document the performance of the approach in numerical experiments.
