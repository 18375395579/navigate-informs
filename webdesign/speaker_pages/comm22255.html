Title: Supply Chain Scheduling with Renewable and Non-renewable Resources

Author: Michael Pinedo, Professor and Chair, New York University, Stern School of Business, 44 West 4th Street, New York, NY, 10012, United States of America, mpinedo@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Lei Lei, Shengbin Wang, Kangbok Lee

Year: 2012

Abstract: When supply chain operations scheduling is subject to both renewable and nonrenewable resource constraints, solving the resulting problems to optimality, especially those involving additional constraints, becomes very difficult. We present three polynomial-time solvable cases, and also a heuristic algorithm for the general case.
