Title: On the Linear Transformation of Multiple Criteria Vectors

Author: Pekka Korhonen, Professor, Aalto University School of Business, POB 21210, 00076 Aalto, Helsinki, Finland, Pekka.Korhonen@aalto.fi

Coauthor(s): Abolfazl Keshvari

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study properties of the linear transformation of multiple criteria vectors in evaluation problems. In case of a non-singular transformation, no information is lost provided we transform the efficient frontier accordingly. The singular transformation is more complicated. Not all transformations are feasible. We discuss the principles and propose a method for a proper transformation. Numerical illustrations are used.
