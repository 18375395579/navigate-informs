Title: Cone Programming Relaxations for Complementarity Constraints

Author: Juan Vera, Post Doctoral Fellow, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, ON, N2L 3G1, Canada, jvera@engmail.uwaterloo.ca

Coauthor(s): Miguel Anjos

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present new theoretical results on new relaxations schemes for complementarity constrains. These relaxations can be casted as second-order cone programming problems or semidefinite programming problems, and are shown to be exact, i.e. to provide the global optimum, in some cases.
