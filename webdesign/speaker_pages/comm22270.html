Title: Maximization of a Strongly Unimodal Multivariate Discrete Distribution

Author: Mine Subasi, RUTCOR, Rutgers University, 640 Bartholomew Rd, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States, msub@rutcor.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Ersoy Subasi, András Prékopa

Year: 2006

Abstract: A dual type linear programming algorithm is presented to find the maximum of a strongly unimodal multivariate discrete distribution. Numerical examples are presented.
