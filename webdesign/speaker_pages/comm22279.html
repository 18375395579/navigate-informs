Title: MILP Based Hyper-box Enclosure Approach for Multi-group Data Classification
Author: Fadime Uney Yuksektepe, PhD Candidate, Koc University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Rumelifeneri Yolu Sariyer, Ýstanbul, 34450, Turkey, funey@ku.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Metin Turkay
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this talk, a novel mixed-integer programming based hyper-box enclosure method is presented for multi-group data classification problems. In order to overcome computational difficulties, a 3-stage mathematical programming based approach is developed for training part analysis of hyper-box enclosure method. The performance of this method is tested on different benchmark datasets.
