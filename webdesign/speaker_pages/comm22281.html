Title: Valuing Strategic Network Improvements for Highway BOT Projects Under User Equilibrium

Author: Jennifer Duthie, University of Texas, 6.506 ECJ Hall, Austin, TX, 78751, United States of America, jduthie@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Ivan Damnjanovic, S. Travis Waller

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper investigates the effects of network recourse on toll road revenues. The proposed model utilizes a two-stage stochastic network design formulation under user equilibrium constraints. Sensitivity analysis is conducted on an illustrative network where the bi-level design formulation is simplified with a closed-form solution of the user equilibrium constraint.
