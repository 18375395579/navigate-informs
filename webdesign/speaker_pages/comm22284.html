Title: Botticelli 2006: A TAC SCM Agent

Author: Victor Naroditskiy, Graduate Student, Brown University, Department of Computer Science, Box 1910, Providence, RI, 02912, United States, vnarodit@cs.brown.edu

Coauthor(s): Amy Greenwald

Year: 2006

Abstract: One of the challenges of the TAC SCM is integration of long-term procurement decisions and short-term selling decisions in the environment where product demand and component supply change all the time. We approximate the game-theoretic problem by an optimization problem. We then solve a tractable approximation to make long-term decisions and a smaller problem to make short-term decisions. This strategy was employed by Brown University’s entrant Botticelli, a finalist in 2006 TAC SCM.
