Title: Path-finding Algorithms for Complex Dynamic Systems

Author: Alvaro Maggiar, PhD Candidate, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, IEMS, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, alvaro.maggiar@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Irina Dolinskaya

Year: 2013

Abstract: The motion of unmanned vehicles subject to external factors, such as wind, results in complex dynamics that need to be taken into account for efficient path-planning. By formulating the problem of finding a fastest path between two configurations in direction-dependent media as a non-linear program, we are able to derive an algorithm that implements the results. We illustrate its application to naval and aerial vehicles.
