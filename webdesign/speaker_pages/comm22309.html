Title: Comparative Assessment of Alternative Approaches to Distribution System Design in Supply Chains
Author: Srinagesh Gavirneni, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, Johnson Graduate School of Management, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, sg337@johnson.cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Poornachandra Panchalavarapu, Ram Rachamadugu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Conventional methods in designing distribution systems are sequential in nature ?strategic design of the distribution network followed by the operational design. We propose an integrative approach which models and makes these decisions simultaneously. Using a large practical data set from industry, we explore how product characteristics such as value density and demand variability impact the sequential approach vis-?vis the integrative approach.
