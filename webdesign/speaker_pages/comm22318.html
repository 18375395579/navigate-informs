Title: Dynamic Pricing Through Adjustable Robust Optimization
Author: Georgia Perakis, Associate Professor, Sloan School, MIT, 50 Memorial Drive, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, georgiap@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Ruben Lobel
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a robust optimization approach to dynamic pricing for perishable products. The main issues in this talk are how to model demand uncertainty and how to optimize the pricing strategy. While the traditional robust optimization models are tractable, the pricing policies are static. This paper explores adaptable robust optimization approaches to the pricing problem. We analyze a variety of models and compare them through simulations.
