Title: Fast Approximation Schemes for Scheduling Problems Related to Positive Convex Half-Product
Author: Hans Kellerer, Professor, Universitat Graz, Statistik and Operations Research, Universitatstrabe 15, A-8010, Graz, Austria, hans.kellerer@uni-graz.at
Coauthor(s): Vitaly Strusevich
Year: 2012
Abstract: We address a version of the Half-Product Problem and its restricted version with a linear knapsack constraint. We focus on the development of fully polynomial-time approximation schemes with running times that depend quadratically on the number of variables. Applications to various scheduling problems are reported.
