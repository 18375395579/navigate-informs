Title: A Genetic Algorithm for Container Loading Problem

Author: Ali Haghani, Professor and Chairman, Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, University of Maryland, 1173 Glen L. Martin Hall, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, haghani@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Masoud Hamedi, Saini Yang

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper deals with the containership stowage planning problem. The problem is stacking containers on different bays of a containership that visits several ports during a voyage. Due to its NP-Complete nature, exact algorithms for this problem are impractical. A heuristic approach based on Genetic Algorithm is proposed to deal with real world cases. Computational experiments and analysis of results as well as suggestions for future research are presented.
