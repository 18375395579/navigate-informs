Title: Experiments with Solving Difficult Integer Programs on Distributed Computing Platforms
Author: Jeff Linderoth, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1513 University Avenue, Madison, WI, 53706-1572, United States, jtl3@lehigh.edu
Coauthor(s): James Ostrowski, François Margot, Greg Thain
Year: 2007
Abstract: We recount our experiences in solving very difficult mixed integer programs with the help of powerful computing platforms. Our recent progress on solving the football pool problem and on computing the incidence width of Steiner Triple Systems will be discussed.
