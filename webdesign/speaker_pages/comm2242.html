Title: Static vs Adjustable Solutions in Two-stage LPs under Constraint Uncertainty

Author: Vineet Goyal, Columbia University, New York, NY, United States of America, vgoyal@ieor.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas, Brian Lu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the performance of static solutions for two-stage robust packing linear programs with uncertain constraint coefficients and give a tight characterization of adaptivity gap. Computing an adjustable robust optimization problem is often intractable. We give a tight characterization for the optimality of static solutions and a tight approximation bound related to a measure of non-convexity of a transformation of uncertainty set. Our bound is significantly stronger than the symmetry bound.
