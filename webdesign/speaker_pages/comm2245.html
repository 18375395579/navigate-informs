Title: Large-Scale Quaratic Integer Programming Problems

Author: Panos Pardalos, Distinguished Professor, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, PO Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, pardalos@ufl.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk we are going to present computational results using exact algorithms and heuristics for large-scale quadratic 0-1 problems.
