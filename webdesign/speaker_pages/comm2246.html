Title: Solving Asymmetric Variational Inequalities via Convex Optimization

Author: Michele Aghassi, maghassi@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Georgia Perakis, Dimitris Bertsimas

Year: 2006

Abstract: Using duality, we reformulate the asymmetric variational inequality (VI) problem over a conic region as a single-level, and many-times continuously differentiable optimization problem. We give sufficient conditions for the convexity of this reformulation and identify a class of VIs, of which monotone affine asymmetric VIs over polyhedra are a special case, and can be solved using widely-available and commercial-grade convex optimization software.
