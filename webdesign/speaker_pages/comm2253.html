Title: High-performance Implementation of Positive Matrix Completion for SDPs
Author: Makoto Yamashita, Associate Professor, Tokyo Institute of Technology, 2-12-1-W8-29, Ookayama, Meguro-ku, Tokyo, Japan, Makoto.Yamashita@is.titech.ac.jp
Coauthor(s): Kazuhide Nakata
Year: 2013
Abstract: When SDPs (SemiDefinite Programs) have structural sparsity, the positive matrix completion method can exploit the sparsity and reduce the computation time. Nakata et al. implemented SDPA-C by embedding the completion method into the interior-point method. We replace the SDPA-C bottleneck with a more efficient formula due to the property of the inverse of variable matrix. Furthermore, with the enhancement of multiple-threading, the new SDPA-C successfully solves SDPs in a shorter time.
