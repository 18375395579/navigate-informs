Title: Markov Perfect Industry Dynamics with Many Firms

Author: Gabriel Y. Weintraub, Stanford University, Management Science and Engineering Department, Terman Engineering Center, 3rd Floor, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, gweintra@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): C. Lanier Benkard, Benjamin Van Roy

Year: 2006

Abstract: We propose an approximation method that dramatically reduces the computational complexity of solving dynamic models of imperfect competition. We provide conditions for which our method closely approximates an equilibrium for large markets. We develop bounds that can assess accuracy for any given applied problem. Experiments show how our method greatly expands the set of economic problems that can be analyzed. As an example we consider a model of price competition with investments in quality.
