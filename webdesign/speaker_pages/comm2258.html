Title: Application of Adaptive Network Design to Transit Fare Revenue Management

Author: Joseph Chow, Assistant Professor, Ryerson University, 350 Victoria Street, Toronto, ON, M5B 2K3, Canada, joseph.chow@ryerson.ca

Coauthor(s): Anchor Chin

Year: 2013

Abstract: An overview and review is given of the adaptive network design problem using approximate dynamic programming based on a real option solution method. Key research problems are highlighted, and feasibility of an application to transit fare revenue management with demand response is considered.
