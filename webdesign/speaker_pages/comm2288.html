Title: Impact of Optimization Methods for Spot Scanning Proton Therapy Treatment Planning

Author: Gino J. Lim, University of Houston, Department of Industrial Engineering, E206 Engineering Bldg 2, Houston, TX, 77204, United States of America, ginolim@uh.edu

Coauthor(s): Wenhua Cao

Year: 2012

Abstract: Spot scanning proton therapy has become available to cancer patients. However, the problem of optimizing intensities of scanning spots has rarely been explored. We compare two classes of optimization models (linear programming and quadratic programming) by examining their resulting plan quality and delivery efficiency using clinical patient cases with different cancer sites, including head and neck, prostate, and lung.
