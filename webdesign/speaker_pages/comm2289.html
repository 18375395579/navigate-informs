Title: Computational Study of Aggregation Procedure for Two-Step MIR Inequalities
Author: Joao Goncalves, IBM Research, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States, jpgoncal@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Oktay Gunluk
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study the application of the aggregation procedure for mixed-integer rounding (MIR) inequalities proposed by Marchand and Wolsey (2001) to the two-step MIR inequalities proposed by Dash and Gunluk (2003). We describe our implementation and present computational results.
