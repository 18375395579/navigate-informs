Title: Numerical Algorithms for Solving MPEC Models Used Analyzing the Potential Impacts of PHEVs on LMPs

Author: Anhua Lin, Assistant Professor, Middle Tennessee State University, 1301 East Main Street, Murfreesboro, TN, 37132, United States of America, alin@mtsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Lizhi Wang

Year: 2008

Abstract: The potential impacts of plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEVs) on locational marginal prices (LMPs) can be modeled as a special kind of mathematical programs with equilibrium constraints (MPEC). This type of MPEC problems are highly structured. In this paper we will present some theoretically sound MPEC algorithms (for solving small and medium size problems) and certain heuristic algorithms (for solving large ones) that can exploit the structure of these problems.
