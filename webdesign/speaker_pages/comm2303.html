Title: Visualization Tools for Branch-and-bound Algorithms

Author: Brady Hunsaker, University of Pittsburgh, 1036 Benedum Hall, Pittsburgh, PA, United States of America, hunsaker@engr.pitt.edu

Coauthor(s): Osman Ozaltin, Ted Ralphs

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present methods to visualize the behavior of branch-and-bound algorithms. Our implementation has interfaces for three open-source solvers: CBC, SYMPHONY, and GLPK. We give examples to demonstrate the potential usefulness of these methods in understanding the progress of the algorithm, understanding differences between instances, and learning about branch-and-bound algorithms.
