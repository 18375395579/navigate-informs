Title: Risk and Expectation-based Models and Algorithms for Insuring Critical Paths
Author: Siqian Shen, University of Florida, Weil Hall 303, P.O. Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, sshen@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Cole Smith, Shabbir Ahmed
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider a class of two-stage stochastic optimization problems arising in the protection of vital arcs in a critical path network, where one must trade off costs in insuring arcs with expected penalties from late completion times. We analyze different risk-level cases, provide various formulations, and employ RLT to remodel the problem to be amenable to solution via Benders decomposition. We employ SAA for scenario generation and demonstrate the computational efficacy of our approach.
