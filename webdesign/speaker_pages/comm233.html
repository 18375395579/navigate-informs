Title: Optimal Routes for Electric Vehicles Facing Uncertainty, Congestion, and Energy Constraints

Author: Matthew Fontana, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, mfontana@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Daniel Reich, Oleg Gusikhin, Perry MacNeille, Dimitris Bertsimas, Thomas Magnanti, Erica Klampfl, Ryan McGee

Year: 2012

Abstract: To address range anxiety for drivers of battery electric vehicles, we propose a robust optimization model to find optimal routes under uncertainty and congestion while considering constraints on total energy. We describe the physics of electric vehicles, the overall model, and our solution approach. We have implemented our approach using data from a Geographic Information System with very promising results.
