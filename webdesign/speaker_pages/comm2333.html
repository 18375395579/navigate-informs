Title: Multi-stage Stochastic Capacity Expansion using Bender’s Decomposition with Sobol Sampling

Author: Mort Webster, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, mort@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Berk Ustun

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a multi-stage stochastic capacity expansion model for electricity generation, with uncertainty in future carbon limits, fuel prices, and technology capital costs. Sobol sampling of the joint distribution of the uncertainty allows us to efficiently generate thousands of scenarios. Using this approach, we demonstrate that the first-stage optimal mix depends critically on interactions between the uncertainties in policy, fuel prices, technology costs, and the time horizon.
