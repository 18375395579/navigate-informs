Title: Network Design with Stochastic Arc Capacities

Author: Avinash Bhardwaj, University of California Berkeley, 450/60 Sutardja Dai Hall, University of California Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, 94720-1764, United States of America, avinash.bhardwaj@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Alper Atamturk

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present models and branch-and-cut algorithms to find a min cost fixed charge network with stochastic arc capacities. We derive strong valid inequalities for the convex hull of the solutions. We provide inequality extension and lifting algorithms to obtain the facets of the convex hull.
