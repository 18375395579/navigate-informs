Title: Polyhedral Studies for Vendor-Managed Inventory Models

Author: Ayse Nur Arslan, PhD Student, University of Florida, Department of Industrial and Systems, Engineering 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32608, United States of America, nur.arslan@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Jean-Philippe Richard, Yongpei Guan

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study a family of vendor-managed inventory models. We first present a polynomial time dynamic programming algorithm and the corresponding extended formulation. In addition, we perform a polyhedral study of the corresponding set. We obtain several families of facet-defining inequalities which provide the convex hull description for two periods.
