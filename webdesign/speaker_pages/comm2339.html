Title: Genetic Algorithms for Multiple Equipment Multiple Cover Facility Location-Allocation Problem

Author: Vaidyanathan Jayaraman, Associate Professor, University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL, 33124, vaidy@miami.edu

Coauthor(s): Anurag Agarwal, Rajesh Srivastava

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Multiple Equipment Multiple Cover Facility Location-Allocation (MEMCOLA)Problem attempts to minimize the amount of demand not covered within the maximal-service distance of an equipment or facility. The model considers multiple types of equipment such as primary/secondary, main/backup or general/specialty. Due to the NP-Hard nature of the problem, optimality is difficult to obtain. We formulate the model and propose some genetic algorithms for this problem.
