Title: Integer Programming Techniques for Cocircuits of Linear Matroids

Author: John Arellano, Rice University, 6100 Main St., Houston, TX, 77005-182, United States of America, jda2@rice.edu

Coauthor(s): Illya Hicks

Year: 2012

Abstract: I present a set covering problem (SCP) formulation of the matroid cogirth problem. Addressing the matroid cogirth problem for linear matroids can lead to significantly enhancing the design process of sensor networks by providing a means to evaluate the quality of the network. I briefly discuss existing methods developed to solve the matroid cogirth problem and the SCP. Computational results are provided to validate a branch-and-cut algorithm that addresses the SCP formulation.
