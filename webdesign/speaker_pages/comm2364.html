Title: Global Optimization of Problems with Edge-concave Intermediates

Author: Yash Puranik, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, ypp@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Aida Khajavirad, Nick Sahinidis

Year: 2013

Abstract: We describe a branch-and-cut implementation that relies on a highly efficient solution of linear separation problems for obtaining facet defining cuts for edge-concave functions. Standard test libraries are used to demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed approach.
