Title: An Interior-point Approach using a Primal-dual Augmented Lagrangian for Large-scale Nonconvex Optimization in SAS

Author: Joshua Griffin, SAS Institute, Inc., United States of America, jdgriffi@gmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: SAS has recently developed and implemented multi-threaded Krylov-based interior-point and active set methods for large-scale nonconvex optimization. Both methods use the exact primal dual augmented Lagrangian merit function of P. E. Gill and D. Robinson. In this talk we focus on the interior-point approach and will give some numerical results to demonstrate nice features about this approach and discuss some strategies used for improving convergence.
