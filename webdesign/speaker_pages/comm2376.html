Title: Designing Resilient Telecommunication Networks
Author: Michael Bartolacci, Associate Professor of IST, Penn State University - Berks, PO Box 7009, Reading, PA, 19610, United States, mrb24@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Abdullah Konak
Year: 2006
Abstract: The authors propose an approach for designing resilient telecommunication networks based on genetic algorithms. A methodology for estimating traffic efficiency is also proposed.
