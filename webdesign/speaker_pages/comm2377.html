Title: A Constraint Reduced IPM for Convex Quadratic Programming, with Application to SVM Training

Author: Jin Hyuk Jung, PhD Student, University of Maryland, Department of Computer Science, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, jjung@cs.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Andre Tits, Dianne O'Leary

Year: 2006

Abstract: We propose a constraint reduction scheme for a convex QP with m inequality constraints and n variables,m>>n. We solve the normal equations with a reduced matrix, H + A'_Q diag(z/s)_Q A_Q, for a well chosen index set Q (adaptively shrunk) thus reducing the computational cost per iteration by a factor close to |Q|/m. We prove global, and local quadratic, convergence. We apply our scheme to support vector machine (SVM) training (primal QP formulation), with highly promising results.
