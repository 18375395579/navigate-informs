Title: A Branch-and-price Algorithm for the Bin Packing Problem with Conflicts

Author: Samir Elhedhli, Associate Professor, Dept. of Management Sciences, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, ON, Canada, elhedhli@uwaterloo.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a branch-and-price algorithm for the bin packing problem (BPP) with conflicts, a variant of the BPP that takes item conflicts into account. The strength of the proposed approach relies on the fact that the branching constraints match the conflicting constraints, thus preserving the structure of the subproblems after branching. We provide an efficient algorithm where proper data structures are used, warm starting is exploited and valid inequalities are generated for the subproblems.
