Title: Min-max Regret Robust Optimization for Power System Planning

Author: Jianhui Wang, Affiliate Professor, Auburn University, 9700 S. Cass Avenue, Bldg. 221, Argonne, IL 60439, USA, Argonne, IL, 60439, United States of America, jianhui.wang@anl.gov

Coauthor(s): Bokan Chen, Lizhi Wang

Year: 2013

Abstract: Long-tern power system planning is constrained by many uncertain factors such as load profile, renewable energy penetration and output, availability of hydro power resources. In this talk, we will present a robust optimization algorithm for long-term power system planning to take into account these uncertainties. The objective of the algorithm is to propose a robust power system expansion plan that can be relatively less conservative while still robust to account for the worst-case scenarios.
