Title: Designing Wood Supply Chains under Uncertainty

Author: Marc-Andr?Carle, Universit?Laval, Québec, Canada

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk discusses the main sources of uncertainty arising in the design of wood products supply chain. These sources and their impacts on the supply chain are identified and described. A supply chain design approach based on scenario generation, SCN design generation and design evaluation and selection processes is outlined.
