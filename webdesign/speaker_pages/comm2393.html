Title: An Integrated Location, Inventory and Service Model

Author: Mark Daskin, Professor, Northwestern University, Department of IE/MS, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States, m-daskin@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Max Shen

Year: 2007

Abstract: We formulate a multi-objective facility location model that integrates location, transportation and inventory costs with customer service considerations. We discuss a genetic algorithm that identifies the cost-service tradeoff curve. Computational results comparing the algorithm to an exact approach are presented using a variety of test datasets.
