Title: Hierarchical Demand Response Bidding Framework in Wholesale Energy Markets

Author: Masood Parvania, Sharif University of Technology, Electrcical Engineering Department, Azadi Ave., Tehran, Iran, parvania@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Mahmud Fotuhi-Firuzabad

Year: 2013

Abstract: This presentation introduces a hierarchical DR bidding framework in wholesale energy markets. In the proposed framework, DR aggregators actively correspond with ISO and customers for maximizing DR. The DR aggregators utilize the proposed price-based self-scheduling model to optimize the DR offers. The ISO clears the day-ahead energy market considering energy offers of generating units and load reduction offers of DR aggregators. The ISO’s and aggregators?models are formulated as MILP problems.
