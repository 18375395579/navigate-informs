Title: A New Algorithm and Numerical Experiments for a Multiple-period, Multiple-product Inventory Problem

Author: Kai Luo, Assistant Professor, EUROMED, BP 921, LUMINY, MARSEILLE, France, kai.luo@euromed-management.com

Coauthor(s): RAMESH BOLLAPRAGADA, LAOUCINE KERBACHE

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate the cases where two capacitated suppliers produce two products for a limited-shelf-space retailer in a multiple period horizon. Demand is stochastic and unmet demand is assumed to be lost. We show that an uncapacitated transformed problem is L-nature-convex. We also provide an upper bound of the optimal solution for the original capacitated problem and a new algorithm to search for near-optimal solutions. The effectiveness of the algorithm is tested via numerical examples.
