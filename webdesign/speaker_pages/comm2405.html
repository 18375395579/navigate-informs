Title: A Mixed Integer Nonlinear Program with Complementarity Constraints for Reinforced Concrete Design

Author: Andres Guerra, Ph.D Candidate, Colorado School of Mines, 1500 Ilinois St, Golden, 80401, United States, aguerra@mines.edu

Coauthor(s): Alexandra Newman

Year: 2007

Abstract: A novel formulation aiming to achieve optimal design of reinforced concrete (RC) structures is presented here. The explicit optimization formulation incorporates, inter alia, stiffness correlation among all structural members via finite element analysis and complementarity constraints to model nonlinear material response. Optimal sizing and reinforcing for beam and column members in multi-bay and multi-story RC structures result in cost savings over typical-practice design solutions.
