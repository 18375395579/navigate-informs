Title: Imperfect Protection of Infrastructure Using Stochastic Programming with Endogenous Uncertainty

Author: Hugh Medal, Assistant Professor, Mississippi State University, 260 McCain Engineering Building, Mississippi State University, MS, 39762, United States of America, hugh.medal@msstate.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We examine common assumptions in infrastructure protection models: 1) perfect protection, 2) binary protection, and 3) binary infrastructure states. We employ a two-stage stochastic program with endogenous uncertainty and solve it using a greedy algorithm with a performance guarantee. We found that assumptions 1 and 2 together significantly affected model performance, and yet assumption 3 did not have a significant effect. We also found a significant interaction between assumptions 1, 2, and 3.
