Title: Mathematical Optimization Techniques for Multi-vehicle Motion Planning

Author: Pramod Abichandani, Drexel University, 3141 Chestnut Street, Philadelphia, United States of America, pramod@drexel.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Multi-Vehicle Motion Planning (MVMP) problems require the optimization of suitable performance measures under an array of complex and challenging constraints involving kinematics, dynamics, collision avoidance, and communication connectivity. We provide a step-by-step demonstration of how to formulate and experimentally validate a MINLP with second-order cone constraints that represents an MVMP problem. Finally, we discuss the advantages, technical challenges, and limitations of this framework.
