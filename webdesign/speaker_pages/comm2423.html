Title: Time Consistency in Efficiency for Discrete-time Cone Constrained Markets

Author: Duan Li, Patrick Huen Wing Ming Prof. of SEEM, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dept. of Sys. Eng. & Eng. Manag., Shatin, NT, Hong Kong - PRC, dli@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Xiangyu Cui

Year: 2013

Abstract: We derive the semi-analytical expressions of the global (pre-committed) efficient mean-variance policy for convex cone constrained markets and investigate analytically effects of portfolio constraints on time consistency in efficiency (TCIE), which further enables us to establish a general solution framework in constructing TCIE dynamic portfolio selection problem formulations by introducing suitable portfolio constraints.
