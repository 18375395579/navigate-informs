Title: Smooth Optimization Approach for Sparse Covariance Selection

Author: Zhaosong Lu, Assistant Professor, Simon Fraser University, 8888 University Drive, Burnaby, BC, V5A 1S6, Canada, zhaosong@sfu.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: Given a sample covariance matrix, we consider a maximum likehood problem penalized by 1-norm of entries of the inverse covariance matrix to perform sparse covariance selection. By exploiting the structure of this problem, we develop a smooth optimization approach that has better theoretical complexity, and outperforms substantially the existing methods in practice.
