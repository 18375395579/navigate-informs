Title: Robust Optimal Control with an Application to Inventory Management
Author: Changhyun Kwon, Assistant Professor, University at Buffalo, SUNY, Dept of Industrial & Systems Engineering, 438 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, chkwon@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): Terry Friesz, Matthew Rigdon
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a method for handling uncertainty in optimal control problems via a robust optimization approach. We extend prior work on robust optimal control by treating an objective function that is nonlinear in states, controls, and uncertain parameters. An application to inventory management is provided.
