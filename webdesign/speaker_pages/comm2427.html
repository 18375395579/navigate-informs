Title: Multiobjective Order Picking Problem
Author: Cansu Kandemir, PhD Student, Old Dominion University, Department of Engineering Management, Norfolk, VA, 23529, United States of America, ckand002@odu.edu
Coauthor(s): Ozgur Ozpeynirci
Year: 2012
Abstract: We work on the order picking problem (OPP) in a special warehouse with one picker. The single objective version of this problem is polynomially solvable. We address the multiobjective version and show that the algorithm designed for single objective OPP is not capable of finding all nondominated points. After analyzing the properties of nondominated points, we develop a pseudo-polynomial algorithm to find a nondominated point. We propose an approach to generate all nondominated points.
