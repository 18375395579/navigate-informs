Title: Effective Way to Generate Multiple Solutions for Medical Residents Scheduling

Author: Young-Chae Hong, University of Michigan, Department of Industrial and Operations, 1205 Beal Ave., Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, hongyc@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Ariella Rose, Micah Long, Amy Cohn, Elizabeth Perelstein

Year: 2013

Abstract: Given many rules and requirements, scheduling medical residents is a highly complicated task. Adding to this complexity, the objective function is ill-defined and multi-criteria. Instead of finding a unique optimal solution, our goal is to quickly find and present to the user a range of high-quality feasible solutions. We focus on the modeling of the problem and the algorithmic approach to generating multiple solutions.
