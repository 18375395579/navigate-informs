Title: Complementarity Formulations for Dynamic Equilibrium in Single Bottleneck Models

Author: Gitakrishnan Ramadurai, PhD Student, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, JEC 4002 Dept. of Civil and Env Engg., 110, 8th Street,, Troy, NY, 12180, United States, ramadg@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Satish Ukkusuri, Jong-Shi Pang, Jinye Zhao

Year: 2007

Abstract: Past studies have enriched our understanding of dynamic equilibrium in single bottleneck models significantly. However, none of them provide a rigorous game theoretic basis. Such a basis allows us to characterize theoretical results on equilibrium existence, uniqueness, and stability and provides new computational algorithms for computing equilibrium solutions efficiently. In this talk, we present complementarity formulations for the single and multiple user class single bottleneck models.
