Title: On Some Disjunctive Relaxations Related to Pseudo-boolean Optimization
Author: Michel Minoux, michel.minoux@lip6.fr
Year: 2007
Abstract: The use of the so-called 'elementary closure of Lift-and-Project' cuts has been shown to provide good bounds for some classes of quadratic pseudo-boolean problems such as MAX-2SAT (see Bonami & Minoux, 2006). We investigate further disjunctive relaxations for such problems, exhibit links to other known relaxations (Sherali-Adams, Lovasz-Schrijver) and provide some preliminary computational results.
