Title: Copositive Reformulation of Quadratically Constrained Quadratic Programs

Author: Luis F. Zuluaga, Professor, Lehigh University, 200 West Packer Ave., Bethlehem, United States of America, luis.zuluaga@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Javier Pena, Juan Vera

Year: 2013

Abstract: A line of recent research looks at how non-convex optimization problems can be reformulated as a copositive optimization problem; that is, a conic program over the cone of copositive matrices. In this talk we discuss the conditions under which a quadratically constrained quadratic program can be reformulated as a copositive optimization problem.
