Title: Convexification Techniques for Complementarity Constraints
Author: Mohit Tawarmalani, Professor, Purdue University, 100 S Grant Street, Room 4019, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, mtawarma@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Trang Nguyen, Jean-Philippe Richard
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper, we develop a hierarchy of relaxations for linear programs with complementarity constraints. The technique is similar to reformulation-linearization technique, but uses different product factors. We also derive an equate-and-relax procedure that generates cuts in the space of the original problem variables. As a special case, we strengthen the well-known simple cut and use it to derive cuts that simultaneously exploit multiple complementarity constraints.
