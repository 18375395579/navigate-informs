Title: Efficient Reinforcement Learning with Compact Value Function Approximations

Author: Zheng Wen, Stanford University, 274 Packard, Stanford, United States of America, zhengwen@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Benjamin Van Roy

Year: 2013

Abstract: We provide a provably efficient RL algorithm for large-scale RL problems with compact Q-function approximations. Our proposed RL algorithm is both sample efficient and computationally efficient. The main idea of our algorithm is to choose actions based on “optimism in the face of uncertainty?to hit a right balance between efficient exploration and efficient exploitation.
