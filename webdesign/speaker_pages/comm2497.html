Title: Simulation and Optimization

Author: Michael Fu, Professor, University of Maryland, College Park, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, mfu@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Chun-Hung Chen, Leyuan Shi

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the problem of optimizing a performance function from a simulation model. We begin by classifying different types of problems, and then provide anoverview of the major approaches, followed by a more in-depth presentation of three specific areas: efficient simulation budget allocation, stochastic gradient estimation, and the nested partitions method.
