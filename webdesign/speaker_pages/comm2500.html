Title: Advancing AND/OR Search for Optimization
Author: Rina Dechter, Professor, UC Irvine, Irvine, California, Irvine, CA, United States of America, dechter@ics.uci.edu
Coauthor(s): Radu Marinescu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Recently, Branch-and-Bound search algorithms were developed, exploring the AND/OR search graph for solving constraint optimization problems, demonstrating tremendous gain using problem decomposition (using AND nodes), equivalence (by caching) and irrelevance (via the mini-bucket heuristics). We show here additional improvements gained by exploiting constraint propagation, using initial upper bounds generated via stochastic local search and improving the quality of the guiding pseudo tree.
