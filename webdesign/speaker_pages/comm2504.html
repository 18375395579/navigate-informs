Title: Arbitrage Bounds on European Basket Options via Linear Programming
Author: Javier Pena, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, United States of America, jfp@andrew.cmu.edu
Coauthor(s): Luis Zuluaga, Juan Vera
Year: 2008
Abstract: We provide efficient linear programming formulations for computing sharp upper and lower bounds on the price of an European basket option, given prices of similar options and absence of arbitrage. Our approach yields closed-form solutions for special cases of the problem. It also enables the modeling of features such as options with negative weights and bid/ask spreads.
