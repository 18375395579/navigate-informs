Title: A Massively Parallel Version of the DIRECT Algorithm

Author: Layne Watson, Professor, Departments of Computer Science and Mathematics, Virginia Polytechnic Institute & State University, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States, ltw@cs.vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Ana-Maria Visan, Pengyuan Wang, Clifford Shaffer, John Tyson

Year: 2006

Abstract: The deterministic direct search algorithm DIRECT of D. Jones has proven surprisingly practical, especially when coupled with a local pattern search algorithm such as MADS by Audet and Dennis. Massively parallel implementations of these algorithms, necessary for realistic problems, are highly nontrivial. This talk will summarize the parallel DIRECT and MADS algorithms, place them in the context of global optimization, and describe recent applications in MDO and systems biology.
