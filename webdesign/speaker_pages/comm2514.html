Title: Reliable Location-routing Problem with Probabilistic Disruption

Author: Weijun Xie, University of Illinois, Urbana, IL, United States of America, wxie4@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Yanfeng Ouyang

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper presents a set covering based model for the reliable location routing problem with the existence of facility disruption risks. A variety of solution techniques (i.e., Lagrangian relaxation, Greedy, Interchange) is developed and analyzed. We also propose an alternative formulation including a continuum approximation model for this problem. Numerical study is conducted to test the performance of the proposed algorithms and draw managerial insights.
