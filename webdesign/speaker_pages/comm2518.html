Title: The 4/3 Conjecture for Fractional 2-Matching Instances of the Traveling Salesman Problem

Author: Caleb Fast, Rice University, PO Box 1892, Comp & Applied Math MS-134, Houston, TX, 77251, United States of America, calebfast@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk presents a 4/3 bound on the integrality gap of the Held-Karp relaxation of the Traveling Salesman Problem for certain fractional 2-matching cost instances. These instances have been conjectured to attain the integrality gap for the general problem. Thus, this work provides an approach to proving the 4/3 conjecture for the Held-Karp relaxation and a direction for improving on Christofides' 3/2 approximation heuristic.
