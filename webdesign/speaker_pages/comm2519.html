Title: The Do-Not-Exceed (DNE) Limit for Wind Generation

Author: Jinye Zhao, ISO New England, One Sullivan Rd, Holyoke, MA, United States of America, JZhao@iso-ne.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Due to the variability of wind generation, the ISO tries to identify DNE limits, which are the maximum wind generation ranges that the power system can accommodate without sacrificing system reliability. The problem is formulated as a robust optimization problem where the objective is to find the largest uncertainty set such that the system remains feasible under the worst case realization. Assuming an affine policy, the model is converted into a bilinear programming problem.
