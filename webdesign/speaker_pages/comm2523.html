Title: A Dual Ascent Procedure for the Design of Virtual Private Networks

Author: Ishwar Murthy, Professor, Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, 103 NF, IIM Bangalore, Bannerghatta Rd, Bangalore, 560076, India, ishwar@iimb.ernet.in

Coauthor(s): Sri Narasimhan

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper we present a new formulation and solution procedure for designing virtual private networks for corporate networks. We study the following problem: Given the locations of the corporate offices we would like to design a virtual private network that best connects the various corporate locations. We formulate an integer programming problem and develop a dual ascent procedure for solving large-scale problems approximately. Computational results are provided.
