Title: GIS-based Approach to Determine Feasible Locations for Urban Multimodal Interchanges

Author: Grisselle Centeno, Associate Professor, University of South Florida, Industrial & Mgmt Systems Engineering, 4202 E. Fowler Ave. ENB118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States, gcenteno@eng.usf.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Multimodal interchanges allow users to transfer between various transportation networks. This work combines mathematical programming with sources of geographic information to select feasible sites for the location of such interchanges. The discrete nature of location problems and the decisions to be made lead to computationally intensive algorithms to solve the resulting mathematical programming problem.
