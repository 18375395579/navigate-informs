Title: Random Number Generators and Open Source
Author: Jiheng Zhang, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 ferst dr, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, jrz@gatech.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Numerical experiments on stochastic systems normally involves random number generators. The quality of random numbers generated is very important in obtaining accurate insight from numerical results. We discuss some existing random number generators, and present a user friendly and open source package written by the the speaker.
