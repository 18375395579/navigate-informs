Title: Efficient Global Approximation of Generalized Nonlinear l1 Regularized Solution Paths

Author: Ming Yuan, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of ISyE, Gatech, 755 Ferst Dr NW, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, myuan@isye.gatech.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider efficient construction of nonlinear solution paths for general l1 regularization. Unlike the existing methods that incrementally build the solution path through a combination of local linear piecewise approximation and re-calibration, we propose an efficient global approximation to the whole solution path. The proposed methodology avoids high dimensional numerical optimization and is therefore faster and more stable in computation.
