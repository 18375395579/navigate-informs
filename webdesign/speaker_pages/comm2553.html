Title: Improving the Distribution System for the FKB Program at the Regional Food Bank of Oklahoma

Author: Emre Tokgoz, Faculty - Lecturer, University of Oklahoma, 601 Elm Ave. Room 423, Norman, OK, 73019, United States of America, Emre.Tokgoz-1@ou.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper presents the application of an optimization model based on a mixed-integer programming to solve a cost optimization problem for one of the programs of the RFBO; the Food for Kids Backpack program (FKBP). The objective of this program is to provide food to kids who are considered to be “chronically?hungry at no cost in Oklahoma. A myopic policy solution with an algorithmic approach to the cost distribution problem is followed.
