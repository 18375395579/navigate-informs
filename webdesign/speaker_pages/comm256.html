Title: Generalized MIR Inequalities: Facets for Group Polyhedra

Author: Kiavash Kianfar, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, 239B Zachry, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, kianfar@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yahya Fathi

Year: 2007

Abstract: We discuss the relationship between generalized mixed integer rounding (MIR) inequalities and integer group polyhedra. We show that generalized MIR inequalities are facet-generating for infinite group problems. We then use this result to prove that they can also generate facets for many finite group problems.
