Title: Strong Valid Inequalities for Bilinear Integer Knapsack Sets
Author: Kwanghun Chung, School of Industrial Engineering, Purdue University, 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, khchung@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Jean-Philippe Richard, Mohit Tawarlamani
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this talk, we present strong valid inequalities for bilinear integer knapsack sets that arise as a substructure in applications such as cutting stock and trim loss. In particular, using convex extensions, we show that the convex hull for the unbounded case can be expressed as the disjunctive hull of single-term unbounded bilinear integer knapsack sets. We incorporate the derived inequalities within a branch-and-cut framework and conduct computational experiments to investigate their strength.
