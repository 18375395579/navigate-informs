Title: Sequential MARS for Optimization Problems

Author: Diana Martinez, The University of Texas at Arlington, 415 Summit Ave Apt # 213, Arlington, TX, 76013, United States of America, diana.martinezcepeda@mavs.uta.edu

Coauthor(s): Victoria Chen, Jay Rosenberger

Year: 2012

Abstract: To find a solution for optimization problems, in some cases an iterative method to sequentially reduce the solution space is required. It is possible then to have different design configurations for testing that involve the estimation of a response value. MARS modeling technique has been used as a surrogate method in such cases. An algorithm that proposes different MARS sequential routines has been developed and tested.
