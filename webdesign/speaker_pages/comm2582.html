Title: A Heuristic Algorithm for Rescheduling Problems in Critical Chain Project Management
Author: Yan Zhao, HuaZhong University of Science and Technology, School of Management, LuoYu Road 1073, Wu han, China, zydxd@163.com
Coauthor(s): Nan fang Cui, Xue jun Hu
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper focuses on developing a two-step algorithm to solve problems lying in feeding buffer insertion in critical chain project management. First, three priority rules with a dynamic programming are proposed for rescheduling to solve resource conflictions. Then remaining problems of non-critical chain extension and critical chain break are removed by a heuristic algorithm through decreasing feeding buffer size gradually. Finally, a simulation experiment is set up to test the algorithm.
