Title: Capacitated Facility Location for Disbursement of Supplies in a Large-scale Emergency

Author: Fernando Ordonez, University of Southern California, 3715 McClintock Avenue, GER-240, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, fordon@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Pavankumar Murali, Maged Dessouky

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a model for the facility location problem with capacity constraints, with the objective being to minimize unmet demand. We then extend this problem to the scenario with stochastic demands, for which we propose a chance-constrained model. The performance of the locate-allocate heuristic combined with LP-rounding procedures is evaluated for both these problems for the case of locating emergency medical supplies in Los Angeles.
