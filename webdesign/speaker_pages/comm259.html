Title: An Efficient Benders Decomposition Algorithm for Chance-constraint MIP

Author: Yu An, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave, ENB 118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, yan2@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Bo Zeng, Ludwig Kuznia

Year: 2013

Abstract: Chance-constrained stochastic program with a finite support is often formulated using big-M method, which makes it computationally challenging. We adopt Benders decomposition strategy to develop our algorithm that does not assume a large big-M. Various improvement methods of Benders are also applied to achieve better performance. Numerical results will be presented.
