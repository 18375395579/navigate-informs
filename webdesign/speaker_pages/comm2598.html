Title: Decomposition Approach to Retail Markdown Optimization

Author: Andrew Vakhutinsky, Revionics, 2998 Douglas Blvd, Suite 350, Roseville, CA, 95661, United States of America, Andrew.Vakhutinsky@revionics.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: We formulate retail markdown optimization problem as mixed-integer linear programming problem, propose column-generation approach to solving it, and show how the problem can be decomposed when the budget constraints encompass large groups of products with very low demand transference between the groups. We discuss the results of our computational experiments with real data.
