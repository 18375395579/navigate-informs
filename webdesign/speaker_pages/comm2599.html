Title: Scheduling Summer Programs at the AF Academy via Integer Programming
Author: Andrew Armacost, Associate Professor of OR, US Air Force Academy, 2354 Fairchild Drive, Suite 6J100, USAFA, CO, 80840, United States, Andy.Armacost@usafa.af.mil
Coauthor(s): Johnathon Dulin
Year: 2006
Abstract: Each of the 4000 cadets at the AF Academy must be scheduled for multiple programs over their nine-week summer period. We develop and solve a binary integer program to generate a workable solution. This model, now being integrated into the campus-wide information system, dramatically reduces the number of unscheduled cadets. It also provides the human scheduler insight into how program enrollment levels affect schedule feasibility.
