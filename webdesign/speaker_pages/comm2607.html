Title: Enabling Congestion Avoidance in Stochastic Transportation Networks Under ATIS

Author: Ali Guner, PhD Candidate, Wayne State University, 4815 Fourth St., WSU Industrial Eng., Detroit, MI, 48201, United States, arguner@wayne.edu

Coauthor(s): Madhusalini Saripalle, Ratna Babu Chinnam, Alper Murat

Year: 2007

Abstract: In JIT manufacturing environments, on-time delivery is the key performance measure for dispatching and routing of freight vehicles. In this study, we propose exact and heuristic solution approaches for dynamic vehicle routing. Our basic model is a non-stationary stochastic shortest path problem which uses both real-time recurring congestion and incident information. We present computational results for several network scenarios.
