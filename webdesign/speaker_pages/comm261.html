Title: Exploiting Degeneracy in Sulum MIP Optimizer

Author: Bo Jensen, CEO, Sulum Optimization, Kaasvaenget 45, Middelfart, 5500, Denmark, bo.jensen@sulumoptimization.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: In the recent years the consequences of multiple optimal solutions in the root node LP has become increasingly interesting. Besides the notion of erratic effects on the solve process, ideas to exploit degeneracy in branch and cut have emerged. This talk will present in details the choices made in Sulum MIP optimizer, especially focusing on the cutting loop and how the interaction with degeneracy exploiting heuristics is beneficial.
