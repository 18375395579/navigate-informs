Title: Planning for Evacuation of Low-Mobility Groups: Model Framework and the Pickup Location Problem

Author: Mark Hickman, Associate Professor, University of Arizona, PO Box 210072, Tucson, AZ, 85721-0072, United States, mhickman@engr.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Chi Pak Chan

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider the challenges of using public vehicles to evacuate low-mobility persons in an emergency, including the problems of determining the location of evacuation sites and the subsequent vehicle routing. For the selection of evacuation sites, we provide a mathematical formulation and identify heuristic techniques to solve the problem. We also illustrate the solution technique on several test problems.
