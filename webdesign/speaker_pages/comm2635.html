Title: Belief Propagation and Linear Programming
Author: Mohsen Bayati, Microsoft Research, One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA, 98052, United States of America, bayati@stanfordalumni.org
Coauthor(s): Christian Borgs, Jennifer Chayes, Riccardo Zecchina
Year: 2008
Abstract: Belief propagation (BP) is an iterative, message-passing algorithm for making inference in graphical models. We study the relationship between the convergence and correcness of the BP and integrality of linear programming (LP) in the context of combinatorial optimizaiton problems. We show some theoretical results which shed more light on this often noted but poorly understood connection.
