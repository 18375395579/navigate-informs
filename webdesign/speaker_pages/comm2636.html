Title: True Upper Bounds on Bermudan Option Prices under Jump-diffusion Processes

Author: Fan Ye, University of Illinois, 104 S. Mathews Ave.,, 117 Transportation Bldg., Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, fanye2@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Enlu Zhou, Helin Zhu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Fast pricing of American-style options is difficult when the underlying asset prices follow some jump-diffusion processes. Based on the martingale duality approach we propose an algorithm to generate tight upper bounds on the Bermudan option price under the jump-diffusion setting, where an approximation of the optimal martingale is derived without nested simulation but preserves the martingale property. Numerical experiments are conducted to verify the efficiency of our proposed algorithm.
