Title: Optimizing the Layout of Proportional Symbol Maps

Author: Tallys Yunes, Department of Management Science, University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL, 33124-8237, United States of America, tallys@bus.miami.edu

Coauthor(s): Guilherme Kunigami, Pedro de Rezende, Cid de Souza

Year: 2012

Abstract: Proportional symbol maps help cartographers and geo-scientists visualize geo-positioned data, e.g. population and earthquakes. At specific locations, symbols are placed and scaled to make their areas proportional to the magnitude of the data. We present a novel IP model to draw opaque disks on a map while maximizing the visible border of all disks. We implement new decompositions and facets in a branch-and-cut algorithm, and are the first to optimally solve real instances of this problem.
