Title: Tight Bounds on Non-convex Separable Quadratic Knapsack Problems with Box Constraints

Author: Jaehwan Jeong, University of Tennessee, 1300 clinch ave apt3, Knoxville, TN, 37916, United States of America, jjeong3@utk.edu

Coauthor(s): Chanaka Edirisinghe

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a new method for lower/upper bounds on the global optima of non-convex separable quadratic knapsack problems with box constraints, an NP-hard problem. The lower bound is obtained via the dual problem, whose solution is used to construct a feasible solution upper bound. The algorithm runs in linear time, in contrast to the polynomial-time method of Vavasis (1992) yielding local optima. Experiments show that our method produces better upper bounds than Vavasis more efficiently.
