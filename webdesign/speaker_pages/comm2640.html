Title: Network Flow Under Information Provision
Author: Avinash Unnikrishnan, Graduate Research Assistant, The University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Sta. C1761, ECJ Hall 6.2, Austin, TX, 78731, United States of America, avinash@mail.utexas.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: A formulation for the user equilibrium problem where users learn the state of the outgoing arcs when they reach the upstream node is provided. A solution algorithm and numerical results will be presented. Formulation of the system optimal variant of the above problem will also be presented.
