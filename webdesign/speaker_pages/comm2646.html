Title: Aircraft Schedule Recovery with Crew Connection Protection

Author: Darren Hang, Optimization Specialist, Jeppesen, Odinsgatan 9, Gothenburg, SE-411 03, Sweden, darren.hang@jeppesen.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: The aircraft schedule recovery problem is solved daily by airlines.?The objective is to minimize the impact of disruptions on the airline schedule taking into account essential aircraft and crew constraints, as well as passenger values.?We present a fast metaheuristic algorithm for solving the aircraft schedule recovery problem.?It protects crew connections and produces structurally different solutions within seconds. Results on production data will be presented.
