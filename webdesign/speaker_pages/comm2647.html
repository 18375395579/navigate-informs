Title: Sulum Optimization - Optimization for Servers and Cloud Computing at Competitive Prices
Author: Bo Jensen, CEO, Sulum Optimization, Kaasvaenget 45, Middelfart, 5500, Denmark, bo.jensen@sulumoptimization.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Sulum Optimization Tools is a high performance optimization engine for solving network, linear, and integer problems. This talk will present the most recent improvements. In particular we will cover our new Cloud Solver and demonstrate how to solve optimization problems on remote servers or in the cloud with easy to manage software tools.
