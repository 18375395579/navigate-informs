Title: Scheduling a Large Open-pit Mining Operation
Author: Eduardo Moreno, Universidad Adolfo Ibañez, School of Engineering, Chile, eduardo.moreno@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Daniel Espinoza, Marcos Goycoolea
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a collection of medium to large sized open-pit copper mines in Chile. Given precedence relationships and capacity limits over time, we obtain provably good feasible solutions as fast as possible by incorporating a simple rounding heuristic, pre-processing techniques, and cutting planes in a branch and bound algorithm to solve a classical integer program. We present computational results using the CPLEX solver.
