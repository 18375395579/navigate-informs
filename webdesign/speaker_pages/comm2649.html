Title: Tensor Principal Component Analysis via Convex Optimization

Author: Shiqian Ma, Assistant Professor, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Room 508, William M.W. Mong Engineering, Shatin, N.T., Hong Kong - PRC, sqma@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Bo Jiang, Shuzhong Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We show that in a general tensor PCA problem, the tensor can be embedded into a symmetric matrix. We prove that if the tensor is rank-one, then the embedded matrix must be rank-one too, and vice versa. The tensor PCA problem can thus be solved by means of matrix optimization under a rank-one constraint. We propose two convex relaxations for the rank-one constrained problem. Our numerical results show that both relaxations produce rank-one solutions with high probability.
