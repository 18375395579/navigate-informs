Title: Approximation Algorithms for Stochastic Perishable Inventory Control Models
Author: Xiting Gong, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dept. of Systems Eng. and Eng. Mgmt., Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, xtgong@se.cuhk.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): Xiuli Chao, Cong Shi, Huanan Zhang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We develop two approximation algorithms for stochastic perishable inventory control models with an arbitrary fixed lifetime. Specifically, we develop an algorithm for the models with a general demand process and an algorithm for the models with an independent and non-decreasing demand process. We show that these two algorithms have worse-case performance guarantees between 2 and 3 and 2, respectively. Extensive numerical studies demonstrate near optimal performances for both algorithms.
