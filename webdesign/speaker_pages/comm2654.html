Title: Global Equilibrium Search Applied to the Unconstrained Binary Quadratic Optimization Problem
Author: Shylo Oleg, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, P.O. Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611-6595, United States, shylo@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Panos Pardalos, Oleg Prokopyev, Volodymyr Shylo
Year: 2007
Abstract: We describe heuristic approaches for solving the unconstrained binary quadratic optimization problem based on a global equilibrium search (GES) framework. We investigate performance of the proposed approaches and compare them with the best available solver on well-known benchmarks instances. The reported computational results indicate a high efficiency of the developed algorithm.
