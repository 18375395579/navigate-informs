Title: Computational Approaches to ATFM with Fairness Constraints

Author: Douglas Fearing, Ph.D. Student, MIT Operations Research Center, 77 Massachusetts Ave., Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, dfearing@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Constantine Caramanis

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk we analyze the national ATFM problem with fairness constraints derived from the industry-standard RBS approach. Prior research has shown that there are nice computational properties of the network ATFM model. With the introduction of fairness constraints, we find that many of these properties are lost. In order to regain computationally tractability, we develop two techniques that apply not only with fairness considerations, but also to the general network ATFM problem.
