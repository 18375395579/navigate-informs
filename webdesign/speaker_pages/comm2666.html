Title: Document Classification: A Many-Criteria Optimization Problem

Author: Pekka Malo, Assistant Professor, Aalto University School of Economics, Runeberginkatu 22-24, Helsinki, 00100, Finland, pekka.malo@aalto.fi

Coauthor(s): Ankur Sinha, Oskar Ahlgren, Pekka Korhonen, Jyrki Wallenius

Year: 2012

Abstract: Large dimensional problems are often encountered in information retrieval. The task of finding relevant documents can be viewed as a many-dimensional optimization problem, where each concept represents a dimension. In this study we propose a concept-based method for document classification. The procedure models the queries using a value function, which is learnt by solving a sequence of linear problems. The iterative nature of the process ensures that only relevant concepts are retained.
