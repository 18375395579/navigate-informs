Title: Robust Optimization of Relay Networks for Truckload Transportation

Author: Hector A. Vergara, Assistant Professor, Oregon State University, 204 Rogers Hall, Corvallis, OR, 97331, United States of America, hector.vergara@oregonstate.edu

Coauthor(s): Zahra Mokhtari

Year: 2013

Abstract: Relay networks (RNs) have been proposed as an alternative approach to dispatch loads in truckload trucking. They provide several benefits over traditional direct dispatching such as shorter tours for drivers. We study the strategic design of RNs considering operational limitations. We present a comprehensive review of different model formulations and solution methods for this problem and suggest a robust optimization approach to capture major sources of uncertainty that exist in practice.
