Title: QPPAL: A Friendly Solver for Large-Scale QPs

Author: Sven Leyffer, Computational Mathematician, Argonne National Laboratory, 9700 S. Cass Avenue, Argonne, IL, 60439, United States, leyffer@mcs.anl.gov

Coauthor(s): Michael Friedlander

Year: 2006

Abstract: Our new approach for solving quadratic programs (QPs) is based on gradient projection, augmented Lagrangian, and filter methods. Using gradient projection, we approximately minimize the augmented Lagrangian to estimates the optimal active set. This active set estimate is explored further by approximately solving an equality constrained QP. Global convergence is promoted through a filter. Our implementation is based on the large-scale toolboxes TAO and PETSc.
