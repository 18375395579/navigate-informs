Title: Convex Stochastic Minimization and Saddle Point Problems via Stochastic Mirror-Prox Algorithm
Author: Arkadi Nemirovski, School of Industrial and Systems Engineering Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, nemirovs@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Anatoli Juditsky, Claire Tauvel
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a novel Stochastic Mirror-Prox (SMP) algorithm for Convex Stochastic Nash Equilibrium problems (e.g., problems of convex minimization and of solving convex-concave games). Assuming the associated monotone operator possessing both smooth and nonsmooth components, we show that SMP exhibits optimal rates of convergence achievable under this hypothesis. We discuss applications of SMP to Stochastic Composite minimization, Semidefinite feasibility and Eigenvalue minimization.
