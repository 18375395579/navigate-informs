Title: Using AMIGO, a Mixed Integer Global Optimizer: Methodology and Numerical Examples
Author: Zsolt Ugray, Assistant Professor, Utah State University, 3515 Old Main Hill, Logan, UT, 84322-3515, United States of America, zsolt.ugray@usu.edu
Coauthor(s): Janos D. Pinter
Year: 2007
Abstract: We highlight recent developments of AMIGO, an Advanced Mixed Integer Global Optimizer. AMIGO combines solver strategies that work on general mixed integer problems with the LGO (Lipschitz Global Optimizer) solver suite for continuous global/local optimization. We discuss the algorithmic framework and show illustrative numerical examples.
