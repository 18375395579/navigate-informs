Title: Flexible Demand Management in the Smart Grid: Robust Solutions

Author: Yong Liang, University of California, Berkeley, 1117 Etcheverry Hall, Berkeley, United States of America, yongliang@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Z. Max Shen

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the flexible demand management problem with stochastic prices and demand arrivals. Decisions are made recursively over a certain number of periods. We first analyze the stochastic multi-objective optimization problem where the objectives are to minimize the expected total cost and discomfort. Then, we formulate and solve a distributional-robust optimization model. We show that decision makers will be potentially better off if confronted with stochastic prices.
