Title: Solving Mixed-Integer Semidefinite Programs
Author: Lars Schewe, Dr., FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg, Cauerstr. 11, Erlangen, 91058, Germany, lars.schewe@math.uni-erlangen.de
Coauthor(s): Sonja Mars
Year: 2012
Abstract: We present practical solution approaches to solve mixed-integer semidefinite programs: We show that by integrating standard semidefinite solvers into the mixed-integer solver SCIP, we can harness standard methods from integer programming. We present computational results on a variety of instances.
