Title: Reformulation-induced Cuts

Author: Frederik von Heymann, Delft University of Technology, Mekelweg 4, Delft, 2628CD, Netherlands, f.j.vonheymann@tudelft.nl

Coauthor(s): Karen Aardal, Andrea Lodi, Laurence Wolsey

Year: 2013

Abstract: Given a rational affine subspace, we can use a well-known technique to reformulate the problem of finding non-negative integer points in the subspace to the problem of finding elements of the kernel lattice of the equations describing the subspace, that are contained in a certain polytope. We compare how classical cutting planes translate between these formulations from a theoretical viewpoint, and give an indication of how this technique performs in practice.
