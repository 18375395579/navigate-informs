Title: Optimal Server Allocation in Closed Finite Queueing Networks

Author: J. MacGregor Smith, Professor, Dept. of Mechanical and Indust, 160 Givenors Drive, Amherst, Ma, 01003, United States of America, jmsmith@ecs.umass.edu

Coauthor(s): Ryan Barnes

Year: 2013

Abstract: Topological network design of closed finite queueing networks with finite buffers is an important part of the design and analysis of manufacturing and service systems. An effective queue decomposition methodology coupled with a nonlinear mixed integer optimization algorithm is employed to allocate servers to series, merge, and split configurations of closed network topologies.
