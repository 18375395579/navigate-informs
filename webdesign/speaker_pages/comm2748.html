Title: The Design and Implementation of BLIS

Author: Yan Xu, Optimization Developer, SAS Institute Inc, yax2@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Laszlo Ladanyi, Ted Ralphs, Matthew Saltzman

Year: 2007

Abstract: BLIS is one the libraried in COPS, which is a framework for implementing parallel algorithms based on tree search. BLIS is the layer for solving mixed-integer linear programs (MIPs) in parallel or sequential. We will give an overveiw of design and implementation BLIS and also present some computational results of solving MIPs and the vehicle routing problem.
