Title: Dynamic Resource Allocation Problem for Transportation Network Evacuation

Author: Xiaozheng He, Research Associate, Purdue University, NEXTRANS Center, 3000 Kent Ave, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, seanhe@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study proposes a mixed integer linear program (MILP) model to allocate movable resources dynamically to a transportation network to improve evacuation operational efficiency. To enable realism for practical operations, the proposed model involves constraints on resource reallocation time and minimum time at a location. By transforming the problem into a two-stage optimization problem, we develop a greedy-type heuristic algorithm to solve the MILP approximately but efficiently.
