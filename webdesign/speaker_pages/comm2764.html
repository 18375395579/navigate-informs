Title: A Comparison of Robust Optimization and Monte Carlo Tree Search for Dynamic Resource Allocation

Author: Velibor Misic, PhD Student, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Building E40, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, vvmisic@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas, J. Daniel Griffith, Vishal Gupta, Mykel Kochenderfer

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a stylized wildland fire control problem where one must dynamically deploy resources to suppress the fire and minimize cumulative damage. We apply two solution approaches to this high-dimensional Markov decision process (MDP): Monte Carlo tree search and robust optimization. Computational experiments show that the two methods obtain high quality solutions in operationally feasible computational times.
