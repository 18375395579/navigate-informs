Title: American Options under Stochastic Volatility

Author: Kumar Muthuraman, Assistant Professor, Purdue University, 3225, Humboldt St.,, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States, kumar@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Arun Chockalingam

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the problem of pricing American options when the volatility of the underlying asset price is stochastic. We consider a canonical setup that is general enough to encompass several stochastic volatility models proposed in literature. We describe the arising free-boundary problem and present a method to efficiently solve it. Examples illustrating the generality of the method are provided, together with comparisons against existing methods to validate our numerical results.
