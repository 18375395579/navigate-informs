Title: Large-scale MIPs for Procurement Auctions

Author: Olga Raskina, Lead Scientist, Emptoris, 200 Wheeler Road, Burlington, MA, 02476, United States of America, ORaskina@emptoris.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Deciding the best allocation of sourcing awards based on supplier pricing and performance while complying with corporate purchasing policies is one of the most difficult problems in strategic sourcing, with large dense MIPs in its core. We discuss some of the challenges we faced in implementing fast and robust solutions for this problem and the approaches we implemented to tackle them.
