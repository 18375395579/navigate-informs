Title: Rank Optimization by an Approximation to WMW Statistic and its Applications

Author: Kaan Ataman, The University of Iowa, S210 John Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52242-1000, United States, kaan-ataman@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Nick Street

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a linear programming formulation for ranking instances in the context of binary classification by an approximation to the Wilcoxon-Mann-Whitney statistic. Then we present and discuss applications of this LP based ranking algorithm in the domains of business and health care.
