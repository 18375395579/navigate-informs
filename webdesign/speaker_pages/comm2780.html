Title: Optimal Design of Flexible Systems with Exercising Delay

Author: KiHyung Kim, Purdue University, School of Industrial Engineering Purdue, 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907-2023, United States of America, kim1151@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Brandon Pope, Abhijit Deshmukh

Year: 2013

Abstract: Although the optimal control of flexible systems has received considerable attention, the optimal design of flexible systems remains in question. This paper focuses on the design problem by explicitly considering an oft-neglected aspect of flexibility, the exercising delay. We model the control of a flexible system as a delayed optimal stopping time problem, and solve the designer's problem of creating an optimally flexible system.
