Title: A Branch-and-Price-and-Cut Algorithm for Batch Annealing in Steel Industry
Author: Ying Meng, Northeastern University, The Logistics Institute, Shenyang, 110004, China, ymeng@tli.neu.edu.cn
Coauthor(s): Lixin Tang, Zhi-Long Chen
Year: 2012
Abstract: We consider a batching decisions problem arising in the batch annealing operations and propose a branch-and-price-and-cut solution algorithm. To strengthen the upper bound, in addition to new columns, some valid inequalities are added to the formulation in every iteration of the column generation process. A variable reduction strategy is also proposed to accelerate the algorithm. For some special cases, polynomial-time solution methods are proposed to solve the problem optimally.
