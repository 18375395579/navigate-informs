Title: Smoothed Online Convex Optimization

Author: Adam Wierman, Assistant Professor, Caltech, 1200 E. California Blvd., Pasadena, CA, 91125, United States of America, adamw@caltech.edu

Coauthor(s): Lachlan Andrew, Minghong Lin, Alan Roytman

Year: 2012

Abstract: Out work on sustainable data center design during the last few years has repeatedly led us to a class of problems we term smoothed online convex optimization (SOCO) problems. SOCO is a variant of the class of online convex optimization (OCO) problems, and is strongly related to the class of metrical task systems, each of which have been studied largely independently. In this talk I will discuss some recent progress toward unifying the results and algorithms from these communities.
