Title: A New Approach for Routing Courier Delivery Services with Urgent Demand
Author: Chen Wang, University of Southern California, University Park Campus, Los Angeles, CA, 90007, United States of America, wang20@usc.edu
Coauthor(s): Fernando Ordonez, Maged Dessouky
Year: 2012
Abstract: The courier delivery industry is faced with random demand, as well as urgent requests. An example of such application is the transportation of medical specimens. We propose better vehicle routing solutions that can efficiently satisfy random demand and urgent requests. We formulate a multi-trip vehicle routing problem, and devise a heuristic based on insertion and Tabu search. Simulations on randomly generated data and on real-world data show significant improvement from existing methods.
