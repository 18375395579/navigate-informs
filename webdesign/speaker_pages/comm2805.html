Title: An Outer Approximation Algorithm for the Robust Shortest Path Problem

Author: Mehrdad Shahabi, PhD Student, West Virginia University, 1227 Pineview Dr, Morgantown, WV, 26505, United States of America, mehrdad.shahabi@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Avinash Unnikrishnan

Year: 2013

Abstract: The mean standard deviation shortest path problem is reformulated as a conic quadratic program, and an outer-approximation algorithm based algorithm is developed. Numerical experiments show that the outer-approximation algorithm significantly outperforms standard integer programming algorithms. Preliminary results are shown demonstrating the efficiency of this algorithm for the mean variance case.
