Title: Experience in Solving Commercial Nonconvex MINLPs with KNITRO
Author: Todd Plantenga, Sr Software Developer, Ziena Optimization, Inc, 1801 Maple Ave, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, todd.plantenga@ziena.com
Coauthor(s): Richard Waltz
Year: 2007
Abstract: KNITRO, regarded as one of the best nonlinear optimization solvers for continuous problems, recently extended its scope to include problems with integer variables. We provide an overview of the new algorithm, and computational results on commercial MINLP applications. More information about KNITRO is available at www.ziena.com.
