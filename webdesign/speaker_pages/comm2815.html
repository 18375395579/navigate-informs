Title: A Systemic Approach to Assess Service Effectiveness within a Respiratory Care Department

Author: Dr. Robert Frie, Systems Analyst, Mayo Clinic, 200 1st Street SW, Rochester, MN, 55905, United States of America, zahrob@charter.net

Year: 2008

Abstract: The research study purpose was to assess, through systems analysis, service effectiveness within a large medical institution respiratory care team. The assumption was any service gaps in effectiveness would justify future patient service improvement programs for a large medical institution respiratory care team. The research design used the mixed methods design. The mix methods design consisted of a cross sections survey (quantitative design) and a case study (quantitative design.
