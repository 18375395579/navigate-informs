Title: Almost Sure Asymptotic Optimality in Online Routing Problems

Author: Michael R. Wagner, Assistant Professor, Department of Management, Cal State East Bay, 25800 Carlos Bee Blvd, Hayward, CA, 94542, United States of America, michael.wagner@csueastbay.edu

Coauthor(s): Patrick Jaillet

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider online routing problems such as the online TSP. The problems are online since the problem instance is revealed incrementally. We introduce probabilistic assumptions as a tool to study online algorithms that do not utilize the probabilistic information. Under very mild assumptions we show that many online algorithms are asymptotically optimal, with probability one.
