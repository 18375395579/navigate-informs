Title: Hard Wood Supply Chain Tactical Planning in a Furniture Company Using a Time Decomposition Approach
Author: Mustapha Ouhimmou, FORAC, CIRRELT, University Laval, Quebec, PQ, G1K7P4, Canada, mustapha.ouhimmou@cirrelt.ca
Coauthor(s): Sophie D'Amours
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the wood supply chain tactical planning problem of an integrated furniture company. The goal is to define manufacturing and logistics policies that allow the company to have a competitive level of service at minimum cost. Supply chain planning is formulated as a large MIP model. We solved small size MIP problems and propose a heuristic using a time decomposition approach for larger problems. Computational results are reported as well as an application to a real industrial case.
