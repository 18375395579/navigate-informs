Title: A Column Generation Heuristic for Traffic Grooming in Wavelength-Routed Optical Networks.

Author: Sanjeewa Naranpanawe, SAS Institute Inc., 100 SAS Campus Drive, Cary, NC, 27513, United States of America, Sanjeewa.Naranpanawe@sas.com

Coauthor(s): Chelliah Sriskandarajah, Rakesh Gupta, Milind Dawande

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the problem of traffic grooming in all-optical networks with the objective of maximizing traffic subject to constraints on the number of transceivers at each node, the link load, and the capacity of each lightpath. Based on the structural properties of the problem, we propose a heuristic based on column generation and summarize the results from an extensive computational study.
