Title: Greedy Bayesian Method for Sparse Vector Estimation with Performance Guarantee
Author: Zhenyu (James) Kong, Associate Professor, Oklahoma State University, 322 Engineering North, Stillwater, OK, 74078, United States of America, james.kong@okstate.edu
Coauthor(s): Kaveh Bastani, Wenzhen Huang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We propose a greedy Bayesian method for sparse vector estimation problems. The proposed algorithm benefits from the greedy approach of orthogonal matching pursuit (OMP) in determining the support set of sparse vector. Bayesian framework of the proposed method is adopted from relevance vector machine (RVM). Performance guarantee of the proposed method is obtained. We prove that the ideal risk of the proposed method outperforms the best known unbiased sparse estimator under certain conditions.
