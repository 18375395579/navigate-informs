Title: Pricing under the Multi-level Nested Logit Model

Author: Huseyin Topaloglu, Cornell University, Frank H T Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, ht88@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Guang Li, Paat Rusmevichientong

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider pricing problems under the multi-level nested logit model. We give a formulation of the multi-level nested logit model that works for any number of levels in the choice model. We describe a simple algorithm to find a set of prices that maximize the expected revenue and discuss convergence properties.
