Title: A Projected Gradient Algorithm for Network Equlibrium

Author: Michael Florian, Professor, University of Montreal, P.O.Box 6128, Station, Montreal, QC, H3C3J7, Canada, mike@crt.umontreal.ca

Coauthor(s): Isabelle Constantin

Year: 2008

Abstract: An efficient adaptation of the projected gradient algorithm for solving network equilibrium problems is presented. Comparison with the OBA method of Bar-Gera and other algorithms demonstrate the computational efficiency of the method on large scale problems.
