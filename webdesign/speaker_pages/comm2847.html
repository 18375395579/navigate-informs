Title: On Adaptive Trust-region Methods for Nonlinear Stochastic Programming

Author: Fabian Bastin, Assistant Professor, Universit?de Montreal, Department of Computing Science and Oper. Res., CP 6128, Succ Centre-Ville, Montreal, QC, H3C 3J7, Canada, bastin@iro.umontreal.ca

Year: 2008

Abstract: Sample average approximations are a popular approach to deal with complex optimization problems involving expectations. The accuracy is however related to the sample size, so that the numerical cost can be prohibitive. We examine numerical and theoretical improvments of a previoulsy proposed adaptive strategy, varying the number of draws from iteration to iteration, in the light of recent developments in the trust-region and stochastic programming litterature.
