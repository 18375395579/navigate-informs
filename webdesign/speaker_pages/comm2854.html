Title: Production Scheduling with Batching in Continuous Annealing Plant

Author: Lixin Tang, Northeastern University in China, The Logistics Institute, China, qhjytlx@mail.neu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Yang Yang

Year: 2013

Abstract: This presentation studies the steel coil scheduling problem with batching decision in multiple continuous annealing lines. The problem is formulated as a mixed integer linear programming model with uncertain processing time. To solve the problem, a hybrid method of optimization via simulation and branch-and-pricing algorithm is proposed to tackle the problem. Finally, the computational experiment results illustrate the efficiency of the proposed model and method.
