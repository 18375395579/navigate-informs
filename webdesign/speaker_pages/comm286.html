Title: A Global Optimization Algorithm for Reliable Network Design
Author: Jitamitra Desai, University of Arizona, 1127 E. James E. Rogers Way, 111, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States, jdesai@sie.arizona.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider the problem of designing reliable networks that satisfy typical network constraints by allocating resources to mitigate the arc failure probabilities so as to minimize the total cost of network design and resource allocation. The resulting model formulation turns out to be a nonconvex mixed-integer 0-1 program for which a globally convergent branch-and-bound procedure is designed. Computational results are reported for a hypothetical case and related extensions are also presented.
