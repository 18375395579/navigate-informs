Title: Molecular Structure Determination by a Discrete Geometric Approach

Author: Carlile Lavor, Dr., UNICAMP, IMECC - UNICAMP, Campinas, SP, 13083-859, Brazil, clavor@ime.unicamp.br

Year: 2013

Abstract: The problem of identifying 3D molecular structures by exploiting distance information is a Molecular Distance Geometry Problem (MDGP), which can be formulated as a nonconvex global minimization problem. Defining an artificial ordering on the atoms of the protein molecule, we can prove that the MDGP can be reformulated as a combinatorial problem and solved by an exact algorithm: the Branch and Prune (BP) algorithm.
