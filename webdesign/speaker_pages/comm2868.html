Title: Hybrid genetic algorithm to coordinate production and transportation in coal mines

Author: Jiu Song, Xi’an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, Shaanxi, 710049, P.R. China, Xi’an, China, jeroldsong@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Zhili Zhou

Year: 2013

Abstract: The coal production mainly consists of three stages in which various equipment need maintenance each day. The finished products will be transported by trains. The objective is to minimize the shutdown times of equipment and aversion to maintenance time considering multiple coal mines. We first develop a mixed integer linear programming model and then a hybrid genetic algorithm is proposed to solve it. Computational results show that our algorithm can produce good-quality solutions efficiently.
