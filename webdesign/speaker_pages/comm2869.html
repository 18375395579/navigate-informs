Title: On the Implementation of Interior Point Decomposition Algorithms for TS Stochastic Conic Programs

Author: Sanjay Mehrotra, Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60601, mehrotra@iems.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Gokhan Ozevin

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a practical primal interior decomposition algorithm for two-stage stochastic programming problems. This uses a sampling based preprocessing heuristic to generate a starting point; a starting barrier parameter; a method for step-length calculation without performing line searches; and a method for adaptive addition of scenarios over the course of the algorithm while generating scenarios using quasi-Monte-Carlo sampling. Results show significant savings for certain class of problems.
