Title: Forest Road Upgrading and Harvest Planning

Author: Patrik Flisberg, Creative Optimization, 16 Brocker Tay, Tokai, Cape Town, South Africa, pafli@mweb.co.za

Coauthor(s): Mikael Frisk, Mikael Ronnqvist

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem to integrate the upgrading of forest roads and harvest planning. The planning horizon is five years and includes several thousand harvest areas. This model becomes very large and various solution approaches is developed and tested. We compare with the traditional decoupled planning approach and show the benefit with the proposed method. The system is tested on case studies from Swedish forest companies.
