Title: Relaxations for Stochastic Unit Commitment
Author: John Birge, University of Chicago, Booth School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States of America, john.birge@chicagobooth.edu
Coauthor(s): Mihai Anitescu, Cosmin Petra
Year: 2013
Abstract: Stochastic unit commitment problems can be modeled as stochastic mixed-integer programs but these models can easily become intractable. This talk will discuss relaxations in which binary decisions are interpreted as probabilities and constraints are placed on variance.
