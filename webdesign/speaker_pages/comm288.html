Title: Optimal Draining of Fluid Networks with Parameter Uncertainty: Recourse Case

Author: Burak Buke, PhD Candidate, The University of Texas at Austin, OR/IE Program, 1 University Station C2200, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, bukeb@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): John Hasenbein, David Morton

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this work, we explore the makespan problem in fluid networks with parameter uncertainty. The initial controls are set without complete knowledge of the parameters. After some fixed time T, the controller is allowed to take a recourse action. This problem turns out to be a non-convex stochastic programming problem. We analyze the structural properties of this stochastic programming problem and discuss possible solution methodologies.
