Title: Designing Networks with Transit Time Constraints

Author: Sifeng Lin, University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Station, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, sifenglin@utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Prakash Mirchandani, Anant Balakrishnan

Year: 2013

Abstract: We address a multi-commodity fixed-charge network design problem with upper limits on the total transit time (or length) of each commodity’s origin-to-destination path. We consider alternative model formulations, propose valid inequalities to strengthen the models, outline problem reduction strategies, and discuss decomposition solution methods. Our computational results for a variety of test problems demonstrate the efficacy of the modeling enhancements and solution approach.
