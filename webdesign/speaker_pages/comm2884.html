Title: Ziena Optimization LLC - Using the KNITRO Tuner for Improved Performance
Author: Richard Waltz, Ziena Optimization LLC, 1801 Maple Ave, Suite 6305, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, waltz@ziena.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: KNITRO is the premier solver for nonlinear optimization problems. This software demonstration will highlight the new KNITRO Tuner forthcoming in KNITRO 9.0. The KNITRO Tuner will automatically tune and recommend non-default user option settings to obtain the best performance for difficult nonlinear optimization problems. Examples will be provided.
