Title: Extending Benders Decomposition to Integer Programs

Author: D.A. Tony Tarvin, PhD Student, Colorado School of Mines, 1500 Illinois Street, Golden, CO, 80401, United States of America, dtarvin@mines.edu

Coauthor(s): R. Kevin Wood, Alexandra Newman

Year: 2012

Abstract: We lay new groundwork for extending Benders decomposition to integer programs. Initially specializing to a set-packing problem, we relax a subset of the binary variables and solve the resulting mixed integer program with Benders decomposition. We assess methods for partitioning the binary variables into binary and relaxed, and explore means for recovering integer-feasible solutions to the original model. We provide preliminary results comparing our decomposition to standard branch-and-bound.
