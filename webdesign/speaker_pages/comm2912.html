Title: A Variational Inequality Model for Road Pricing with Environmental Constraints

Author: Henry Liu, University of Minnesota, Department of Civil Engineering, Minneapolis, MN, United States, henryliu@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiao-Zheng He

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk, we consider a road pricing problem with environmental capacity constraints, which has been proposed for a decade without appropriate model and solution methods due to the requirement of explicit functional information and high computational cost. We formulate this problem as a constrained Inverse Variational Inequality (IVI) that has been applied to link-based toll pricing problems recently. A revised proximal-point algorithm is adopted as the solution method.
