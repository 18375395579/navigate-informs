Title: Redundancy and Robustness Analysis of a Structured Linear Model Using Matroid Decomposition

Author: Jung Jin Cho, Post-Doc Research Associate, Texas A&M University, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, jjcho@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yu Ding, Yong Chen

Year: 2007

Abstract: A general linear model y = Xb + e, is widely used in many engineering and statistics applications. X is said to be structured if there exist dependent p rows in X, where X is an n by p matrix. The cogirth (the cardinality of a minimum cocircuit) of a linear matroid associated with X is very useful for a robustness study. We show applications of the cogirth of a linear matroid in engineering and statistics and develop a new algorithm using matroid decomposition.
