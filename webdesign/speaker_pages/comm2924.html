Title: Simulation-based Local Search for Large-scale Workforce Scheduling Problems

Author: Alex Fukunaga, Tokyo Institute of Technology, Ookayama 2-12-1, Meguro, Tokyo, Japan, fukunaga@is.titech.ac.jp

Year: 2007

Abstract: With millions of people working at call centers worldwide, workforce scheduling for call centers is an important, industrial problem. This talk will describe a simulation-based, local search approach to workforce scheduling. In contrast to traditional approaches that decomposes staff scheduling into a muti-stage problem, this approach is a monolithic approach which solves the problem in a single stage. This approach is used in a highly successful commercial system.
