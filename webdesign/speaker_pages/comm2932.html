Title: On the Performance of Affine Policy for Adaptive Optimization: A Geometric Perspective

Author: Hoda Bidkhori, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, bidkhori@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this presentation, we consider two-stage adjustable robust linear optimization problems with uncertain right hand side belonging to a convex and compact uncertainty set U. We provide an approximation bound on the ratio of the {optimal affine solution} to the optimal adjustable solution that depends on two fundamental geometric properties of U and provides deeper insight on the power of affine policies for this class of problems.
