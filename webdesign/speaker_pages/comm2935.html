Title: Exact Primal-dual Regularization of Linear Programs
Author: Dominique Orban, Professor, Ecole Polytechnique de Montréal, Math & Industrial Eng Department, 2900 Boulevard Edouard Montpetit, Montréal, H3T 1J4, Canada, dominique.orban@polymtl.ca
Coauthor(s): Michael Friedlander
Year: 2008
Abstract: We propose a primal-dual algorithm for linear programs (LPs) with explicit primal and dual regularization. The regularizations are exact in the sense that a solution of the original LP can be recovered independently of the regularization parameters. This regularization leads to a sequence of symmetric quasi-definite linear systems.
