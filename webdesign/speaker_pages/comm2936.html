Title: On the Recent Advances in the Mosek Interior-point Optimizer

Author: Erling Andersen, CEO, MOSEK ApS, Fruebjergvej 3 Box 16, Copenhagen, O, 2100, Denmark, e.d.andersen@mosek.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: The interior-point optimizer implemented in MOSEK is intended for solution of large scale sparse linear, conic, and convex problems. In this talk we will present the recent advantages in the implementation of interior point optimizer for linear problems. These advances includes an improved stopping criteria, better numerical stability and enhanced efficiency. Finally, we will present computational results.
