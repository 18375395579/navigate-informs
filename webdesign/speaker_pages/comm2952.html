Title: Enhanced Lift-and-project Cuts from the LP Simplex Tableau

Author: Egon Balas, Professor, Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, eb17@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Pierre Bonami

Year: 2008

Abstract: We revisit the Balas-Perregaard procedure for generating lift-and-project cuts from the LP simplex tableau, with new details and several generalizations. We discuss computational experience with several variants of the procedure.
