Title: Making Use of All Solutions to Measure to Progress of Formal Chip Design Verification

Author: Stefan Heinz, ZIB, Division Scientific Computing Department Optimization, heinz@zib.de

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present an application in the field of formal chip design verification for which it is essential to count all solutions of a constraint integer program (CIP) model. The CIP model is a MIP enriched by additional non-linear constraints. For this task we extended the CIP framework SCIP (http://scip.zib.de) and developed problem specific techniques to speed-up the counting process. Some of these methods are generalized to be applicable for arbitrary CIPs.
