Title: Robust Mechanism Design
Author: Chaithanya Bandi, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, cbandi@mit.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper, we revisit the auction design problem for multi-item auctions with budget constrained buyers by introducing a robust optimization approach to model (a) concepts such as incentive compatibility and individual rationality that are naturally expressed in the language of robust optimization and (b) the auctioneer's beliefs on the buyers' valuations of the items.
