Title: Voltage and Reactive Power Control Using Approximate Stochastic Annealing
Author: Eugene Feinberg, Stony Brook University, Department of Applied Mathematics, Stony Brook, NY, United States of America, eugene.feinberg@stonybrook.edu
Coauthor(s): Jiaqiao Hu, Eting Yuan
Year: 2013
Abstract: This talk discusses voltage and reactive power control for distribution networks. We propose to use an approximate stochastic annealing (ASA) algorithm for solving the voltage and reactive power control problem to minimize energy losses. Our testing results indicate that the ASA algorithm yields within a modest amount of computational time near-optimal solutions.
