Title: An Exact Algorithm for Power Grid Interdiction Problem with Line Switching

Author: Long Zhao, University of South Florida, 14249 Les Palms Circle, Tampa, FL, 33613, United States of America, longzhao@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Bo Zeng

Year: 2012

Abstract: As one of the most effective operations to mitigate deliberate outages or attacks, transmission line switching recently has been included in power grid interdiction model. This bi-level problem is a challenging nonconvex discrete optimization problem, and only a few recent heuristic procedures are available. In this talk, we describe a column-and-constraint generation algorithm to derive the global optimal solution. Numerical results confirm the quality of solutions.
