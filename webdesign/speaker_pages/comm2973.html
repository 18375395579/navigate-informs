Title: Auctions for Competitive Capacity Allocation and Scheduling

Author: Zhixin Liu, University of Michigan - Dearborn, zhixin@umd.umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Nicholas G. Hall

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a capacity allocation and scheduling problem, and design an ascending auction mechanism to allocate capacity to the various jobs. We introduce flexible time blocks as markets goods, demonstrate the effectiveness and efficiency of our auction mechanism, and develop fast solution procedures for the winner- and bid-determination problems. We show that flexible time block auctions provide efficient schedules, and investigate tradeoffs in the facility owner's choice of market good.
