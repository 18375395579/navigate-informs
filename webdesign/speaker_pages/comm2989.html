Title: A Conservative Approximation Approach to the Value-at-risk Minimization
Author: Jun-ya Gotoh, Chuo University, 1-13-27 Kasuga, Bunkyo-ku, Tokyo, 112-8551, Japan, jgoto@indsys.chuo-u.ac.jp
Coauthor(s): Yuichi Takano
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a conservative approximation approach to the VaR minimization problem when the underlying loss distribution is given by a finite number of scenarios. The proposed approach is formulated as a D.C. optimization problem, and a branch-and-bound global optimization algorithm is presented. Some comparative computational results are given, showing characteristics of the algorithms.
