Title: Optimal Design of Reliability Tests for Multiple Stresses

Author: Rong Pan, Assistant Professor, Arizona State University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States, Rong.Pan@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Huairui Guo

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk, we first review the optimal designs of single stress reliability tests and two-stress reliability tests, which is based on a degenerated single stress design. D-optimal design for multiple-stress reliability tests is then proposed for completed and censored data. Examples are provided for demostrating the proposed method.
