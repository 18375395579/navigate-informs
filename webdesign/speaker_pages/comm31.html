Title: Building Energy Analytics for Operational Energy Efficiency Improvement of Commercial Buildings

Author: Young Lee, Manager, Process and Energy Analytics, IBM Research, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, ymlee@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Lianjun An, Young Tae Chae, Raya Horesh, Rui Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: Analytics can play an important role in identifying energy saving opportunities in buildings by modeling and analyzing how energy is consumed in buildings. A set of analytics which can assist building operators in determining energy efficient operational settings of buildings through forecasting, simulation and optimization is presented.
