Title: Hierarchies and Decision Making Dynamics
Author: Jurgen Mihm, Assistant Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, jurgen.mihm@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Christoph Loch, Bernardo Huberman, Dennis Wilkinson
Year: 2007
Abstract: Through a series of analytic models as well as extensive simulations we explore the effect of different hierarchical setups and different decision making cultures on task outcomes and task completion timing.
