Title: Impact of Delay Announcement: An Empirical Study

Author: Qiuping Yu, student, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridon, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, yqp2009@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Gad Allon, Achal Bassamboo

Year: 2012

Abstract: Many service systems announce anticipated delay to the customers. In this paper, we explore the impact of such announcement. We study the data from a medium size call center where the customers are provided announcement concerning their delay. The data provides information about how long the customer stayed in system before they decided to abandon or entered service, whichever occurred first.
