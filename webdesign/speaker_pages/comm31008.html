Title: A Multimethodology Approach for Planning Development Projects in Vulnerable Communities

Author: Juan Felipe Henao Piza, Doctoral Researcher, Warwick Business School, The University of Warwick, Coventry, CV4 7AL, United Kingdom, Juan.Henao_Piza05@phd.wbs.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Ruth Davies, Alberto Franco

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this research a Multimethodology (MM) approach, which combines Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis with Problem Structuring Methods and Sustainable Livelihoods framework, is developed. In here we focus on the benefits of MM and its applicability in developing countries. The MM is validated through a case study in Colombia within the context of a governmental project. The MM proved to be successful in creating business solutions for people living in extremely poor conditions on a garbage dump.
