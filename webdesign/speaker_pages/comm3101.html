Title: A Tracking Study in Medical Wards using RFID
Author: Ziye Zhou, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dept of Syst. Engg. & Engg. Mgmt., Shatin, NT, Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, zhouzy@se.cuhk.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): Chun-Hung Cheng, Dorbin Tobun Ng
Year: 2012
Abstract: A large-scale tracking pilot study was conducted in two 40-bed Medical Wards in a Hong Kong hospital since Fall 2011 for 6 months. This work is to illustrate how the tracking data were analyzed to support data-driven activity analysis & person-to-person contact tracing in the hospital ward environment.
