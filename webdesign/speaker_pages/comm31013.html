Title: Assortment Localization

Author: Ramnath Vaidyanathan, PhD Student, The Wharton School, 500 Jon M Huntsman Hall, 3730, Walnut Street, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, ramnathv@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Marshall Fisher

Year: 2006

Abstract: Most retailers would like to vary their assortments by store, but lack an appropriate methodology. We describe an algorithm for analyzing sales history to determine store specific assortments that maximize a given profit function. We discuss application of this algorithm with several retailers.
