Title: Ant Swarm Reinforcement Learning for Formulating Online Promotion Strategies

Author: P.K. Kannan, Harvey Sanders Associate Professor of Marketing, Director, Center for Excellence in Service, Robert H. Smith School of Business, 3461 Van Munching Hall, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, PKannan@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Tuck Siong Chung

Year: 2006

Abstract: Online environments are characterized by dynamic preferences and response patterns that have to be learned and tracked over time in order to formulate appropriate strategies.
