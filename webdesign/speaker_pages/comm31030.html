Title: Using INFORMS Content Management System for Your Community Web Site Needs
Author: Shirley Mohr, INFORMS, Web Designer, Internet and New Media, Shirley.Mohr@INFORMS.org
Year: 2008
Abstract: This special training session for INFORMS Communities and Subdivision webmasters introduces INFORMS Content Management System (CMS) as the preferred solution for easily creating and updating web sites without the need for special software or programming knowledge. Any community or Subdivision that wishes to utilize a ready-made INFORMS template and intuitive tools to create and maintain their website should attend. The second part of this session will include training for current users of our CMS in advanced aspects of the system such as password protected areas, polls, and user feedback forms.
