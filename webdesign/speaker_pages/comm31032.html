Title: Competition with Search Engine Optimization and Sponsored Search
Author: Amit Mehra, Assistant Professor and ISB Research Fellow, Indian School of Business, Hyderabad, India, Amit_Mehra@isb.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: A search engine results page is the gateway for Internet traffic. Search engine optimization drives traffic through organic links while sponsored search drives traffic through sponsored links. Both types of advertising can be synergistic, but can also be substitutes. We analyze both types of advertising and offer insights into how both of these can be used together for maximum benefit.
