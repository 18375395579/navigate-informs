Title: The Service Information Transformation

Author: Ming-Hui Huang, Distinguished Professor, National Taiwan University, 1, Sec. 4, Roosevelt, Taipei, 10617, Taiwan - ROC, huangmh@ntu.edu.tw

Year: 2013

Abstract: We observe that IT is shaping service in two directions: IT transforms service that makes it more tangible (integrated solutions), separable (e-services), homogeneous (automation), and storable (cloud computing); whereas IT also renovates manufacturing sectors to be servitized (intangibility), engage customers deeper through participation (inseparability), customize individual customers?needs (variability), and incorporate time, not just price, as an important consideration (perishability).
