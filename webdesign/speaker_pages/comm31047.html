Title: Pricing and Discounting Against Real-world Competition

Author: Dave Decker, Revenue Manager, Extra Space Storage (NYSE:EXR), Salt Lake City, UT, 84121, United States of America, DDecker@extraspace.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: In competitive markets, there are no ideal market conditions. When building models to optimize/forecast demand by pricing/discounting there are many moving parts that, by necessity to achieve a working model, generally assumed out of the equation. We have found several interesting variables and tests that have shown us accurate and achievable discounting/pricing modes. We will be discussing findings in promotion sensitivity, competitive data gathering, and customer segmentation opportunities.
