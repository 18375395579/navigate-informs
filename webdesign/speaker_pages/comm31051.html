Title: Hurricane Preparedness and Response: How Can All Sectors Support a National Plan?
Author: Chuck Gregg, Hurricane and Earthquake Program Manager, DHS-FEMA Region 6, 800 North Loop 288, Denton, TX, 76205, United States, Chuck.Gregg@dhs.gov
Year: 2006
Abstract: A FEMA hurricane program manager explains what federal, state and local organizations are doing to improve hurricane preparedness and response. He also describes how these systems can benefit from new methods and technologies for everything from evacuation decision making to shelter plans. He also provides an update about national catastrophe planning and the DHS National Response Plan.
