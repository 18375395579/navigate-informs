Title: Measuring Pricing Performance in Travel & Transportation
Author: Brenda Barnes, Brenda A. Barnes, LLC, 163 Third Avenue #192, New York, NY, 10003, United States, brendaabarnes@gmail.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: Pricing practitioners use a variety of methodologies, tools and metrics to measure the outcomes and performance of their pricing decisions. An experienced airline and hospitality industry executive will discuss common techniques, challenges in measuring pricing performance, shortcomings in current tools, and oppportunities for future research and development.
