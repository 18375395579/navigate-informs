Title: The Insatiable Demand for Bandwidth and the Effect on Competition
Author: Joseph Bailey, Research Associate Professor, University of Maryland, Smith School of Business, 4315 Van Munching Hall, College Park, MD, 20742-1815, United States, joepbailey@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Justin Biegel
Year: 2007
Abstract: Is there monopolization in the Internet bandwidth market? While there are many tendencies towards monopolization, there are unique aspects of the Internet bandwidth market that may prevent full monopolization. Our data shows movement towards increased concentration in the Internet bandwidth market. We describe its impact on adjacent telecommunication markets.
