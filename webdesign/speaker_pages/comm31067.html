Title: A Dynamic Mechanism for Achieving Sustainable Quality Supply

Author: Jeannette Song, Professor, Duke University, 100 Fuqua Drive, Durham, NC, 27708, United States of America, jingsheng.song@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Fang Liu, Tracy Lewis

Year: 2013

Abstract: Several leading companies have realized the importance of sustainable quality supply and initiated programs to achieve it. One example is Starbucks' C.A.F.E. Practices. This paper investigates whether the guidelines in such programs provide the right incentives and information structures for all parties to participate, to achieve the intended long-term goals. The participation by the retailer includes her investment in supplier development.
