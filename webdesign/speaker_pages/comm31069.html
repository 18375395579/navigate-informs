Title: Competition in Sponsored Search Auctions: Do High Quality Firms Bid More?

Author: Siva Viswanathan, Assistant Professor, University of Maryland, 4313 Van Munching Hall, RH Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, sviswana@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Animesh Animesh, Vandana Ramachandran

Year: 2006

Abstract: Sponsored search mechanisms that enable low quality bidders to be listed at the top of the search listings can adversely affect consumer welfare. This study examines the performance of sponsored search mechanisms as measured by the quality of sellers who bid to be listed on Yahoo! and Google.
