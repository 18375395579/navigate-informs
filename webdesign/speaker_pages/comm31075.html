Title: Making Decisions with Models: Uncovering Perceptual and Cognitive Challenges
Author: Jenna Marquard, University of Wisconsin - Madison, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 1513 University Avenue, Madison, WI, 53706, United States, jlhetland@wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Stephen Robinson
Year: 2007
Abstract: Models can aid decision makers by providing structure to problems, but we must attend to how these models are presented to decision makers. Well known perceptual and cognitive challenges still affect decision makers who use models to guide their decision making.
