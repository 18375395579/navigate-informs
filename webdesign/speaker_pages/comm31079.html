Title: Exploring the Opportunities and Barriers to Rail/Truck Multimodal Freight
Author: Yuntao Guo, Purdue University, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, School of Civil Engineering, West Lafayette, In, 47907, United States of America, guo187@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study seeks to explore the opportunities and barriers of rail/truck multimodal freight transportation by using a survey based approach conducted over freight shippers, railway carriers and truck carriers. A clear understanding of these opportunities and barriers can provide indicators for potential of collaborative win-win and growth opportunities for all the sectors involved.
