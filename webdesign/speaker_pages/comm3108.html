Title: The Verdict is in: Toyota-based Process Improvements Work in Health Care
Author: Naida Grunden, Medical Communications Consultant, 1910 Ponderosa Drive, Bellingham, WA, 98229, naidag@comcast.net
Year: 2007
Abstract: For 6 years all around Pittsburgh, the nonprofit Pittsburgh Regional Health Initiative (PRHI) conducted pilot projects in various hospitals using a health-oriented form of TPS. PRHI has even developed its own curriculum and teaches Toyota-based process improvements to healthcare workers across the nation. Naida Grunden, former PRHI Communications Director now residing in Bellingham, details numerous examples in her talk.
