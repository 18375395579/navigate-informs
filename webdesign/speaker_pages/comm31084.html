Title: Software Architecture, Quality and Innovation
Author: Manuel Sosa, Associate Professor of Technology and Operations Management, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, manuel.sosa@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Jurgen Mihm, Tyson Browning
Year: 2008
Abstract: We study how software architecture relates to quality and innovation. By examining several java-based applications developed by the Apache open-source foundation, we analyze empirically the relationship between software architecture characteristics and the capability of the organization to uncover and fix bugs as well as to implement intended product changes.
