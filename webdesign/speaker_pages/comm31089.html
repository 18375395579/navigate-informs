Title: Consumer-driven Competition Sets
Author: Serguei Netessine, Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fountainbleau, 77305, France, Serguei.Netessine@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Jun Li
Year: 2012
Abstract: We use clickstream data from Expedia to construct and analyze hotel competition sets. We find that these competition sets are drastically different from the traditionally used attribute-based competition sets.
