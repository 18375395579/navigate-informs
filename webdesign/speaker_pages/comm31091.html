Title: Analyzing K-12 Education as a Complex System

Author: Pratik Mital, Ph.D. Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, pratik_mital@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Donna Llewellyn, Douglas Edwards, Roxanne Moore

Year: 2013

Abstract: Schools and school districts are complex, dynamic systems affected by numerous factors, specific to the particular environment. A framework is developed that describes the system in terms of both actors and the attributes of those actors and will become the foundation for identifying a subset of attribute combinations that allow for successful change in the system. An agent based model is developed that analyzes the barriers and enablers to school change and reform.
