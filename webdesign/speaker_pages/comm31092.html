Title: The Effect of Information on Bidding Aggressiveness in Auctions

Author: Chun Qiu, chun.qiu@mcgill.ca

Coauthor(s): Peter Popkowski Leszczyc

Year: 2008

Abstract: This study investigates the effect of information on bidding behavior in auctions. Several studies examine the impact of seller supplied information on auction outcomes and bidder valuations. We identify instances where, contrary to auction theory, bidders bid more aggressively when less information is provided.
