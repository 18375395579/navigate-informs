Title: Prize Amount and Entry Behavior in Innovation Contests

Author: Anant Mishra, Assistant Professor, George Mason University, 4400 University Drive, Fairfax, VA, United States of America, amishra6@gmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Cheryl Druehl, Jesse Bockstedt

Year: 2012

Abstract: Innovation contests are being widely used by firms to generate creative ideas and identify solutions to complex problems. Using a large panel dataset of unblind innovation contests from a popular online logo-design platform, we examine the interplay or prize amount and contestant's prior winning experience on their entry behavior in a contest.
