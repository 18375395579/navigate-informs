Title: Regulating Greed over Time for Yahoo! Front Page News Article Recommendations
Author: Cynthia Rudin, Assistant Professor, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, rudin@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Virot Chiraphadhanakul, Ed Su
Year: 2012
Abstract: I will describe our second prize entry in the ICML Exploration and Exploitation 3 Challenge, which is a recommender system for Yahoo! News Article recommendations, involving multi-armed bandits and time series techniques.
