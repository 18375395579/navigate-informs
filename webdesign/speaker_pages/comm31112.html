Title: Influence and Influential Users in Online Health Communities
Author: Kang Zhao, Asst. Professor, University of Iowa, S224 PBB, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, kang-zhao@uiowa.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: What characterize influence and influential users in online health communities? Using sentiment analysis of users?online interactions, this research revealed sentiment influence in threaded discussions. It proposed a novel metric to effectively identify influential users. Text mining was used to find the difference between influential and non-influential posts. Findings of the study can help to manage online health communities and advance our understanding of social influence.
