Title: Panel Discussion: NSF

Author: Ali Abbas, Assistant Professor, College of Engineering, University of Illinois-Urbana Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61822, United States, aliabbas@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Jacqueline Meszaros, Stephen Nash, Matthew Realff, George Hazelrigg

Year: 2006

Abstract: This session includes presentations by several program directors at NSF on visions for decision analysis related funding. Matthew Realff will talk about the Service Enterprise Engineering program, Jacqueline Meszaros about Decision Risk and Management Science, Stephen Nash on the Operations Research program and George Hazelrigg on Decision-Based Design . Talks will be followed by Q&A.
