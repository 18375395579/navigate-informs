Title: App Economy: Understanding Demand for Bestselling Apps on Mobile Platform
Author: Rajiv Garg, Carnegie Mellon University, 4800 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, rg@cmu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Smartphones and tablets have created a new market, which is highly dynamic with large volume of sales and consumption of software applications. We present a simple framework to use only publicly available data to infer rank-demand relationship for bestselling apps. With the sales-rank relationship we estimate the price elasticity for both iPhone and iPad platforms and present the impact of app characteristics on their demand.
