Title: George Dantzig at RAND, Berkeley, and Stanford

Author: Richard Cottle, Department of Management Science and Engineering, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 94305-4026, United States of America, rwc@stanford.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This talk will touch on highlights of the last 53 years of George Dantzig's professional life: his eight years as a research mathematician at the RAND Corporation, his six years at the University of California, Berkeley, his thirty years on active duty at Stanford University, and his nine productive years in retirement. The accent here will be on the continuity and scope of Dantzig's major research initiatives.
