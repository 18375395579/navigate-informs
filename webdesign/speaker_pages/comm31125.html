Title: Analysis of Sellers' Behavior in the eBay Marketplace

Author: Paulo Goes, Professor, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States, Paulo.Goes@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Yanbin Tu, Alex Tung

Year: 2006

Abstract: While the behavior of bidders has received widespread attention, sellers' behavior in online auctions has not been well studied in the literature. In this study, we find that new sellers have higher listing error rates, lower auction success rates, lower auction prices, lower positive feedback rates, higher negative feedback rates, and higher frustration rates than experienced sellers.
