Title: An Analysis of Inventory Financing Cost and Performance

Author: Sridhar Seshari, Professor, University of Texas at Austin, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, Sridhar.seshadri@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Qi Wu, Kumar Muthuraman

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper, we study the inventory performance of publicly listed firms between 1985 and 2010 and show how the effect of cost of capital should be accounted for in inventory decisions. We also examine how financing and inventory decisions jointly impact one another after controlling for various operational and financial factors.
