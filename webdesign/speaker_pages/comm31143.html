Title: The Impact of Baggage Fees on Airlines' Operations and Demand
Author: Mark Ferguson, Professor, University of South Carolina, 1705 College St, Columbia, SC, 29208, United States of America, mark.ferguson@moore.sc.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: In 2008, all the major U.S. airlines began charging fees for checked bags with the exception of Southwest. Using data collected from various sources, we study the impact that these fees had on the operational performance of the airlines as well as how it influenced customer demand.
