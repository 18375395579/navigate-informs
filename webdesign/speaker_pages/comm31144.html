Title: Assessing Professional Skills through Model of Domain Learning

Author: Sadan Kulturel-Konak, Professor of Management Information System, Pennsylvania State University, Tulpehocken Rd., PO Box 7009, Reading, PA, 19610, United States of America, sadan@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Abdullah Konak

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this study, a novel assessment framework based on a pedagogical theory, the Model of Domain Learning (MDL) is proposed to assess student development in various professional skills across STEM disciplines. The project aims to facilitate the integration of professional skills assessment into an overall program assessment plan. We have created and administered a pilot test measuring team-work communication skills of college students and findings of this test will be presented.
