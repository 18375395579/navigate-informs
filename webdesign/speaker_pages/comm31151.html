Title: How Cooperative can Firms be in Green Production?

Author: Fang Tian, Ph.D. Student, University of Southern California, 2723 S Hoover St, Apt 4, Los Angeles, CA, 90007, United States of America, tianfang@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Greys Sosic, Laurens Debo

Year: 2012

Abstract: As the growth in volume and complexity of consumer products, the end-of-life disposal has become a societal problem. Regarding to different products, we studied responsibilities of the government and manufacturers, identified the optimal/stable market structure to implement recycling. Our research balances the fixed cost constructing inverse channels and variable cost recycling products, and provides legislators suggestions on planning recycling schemes to pursue the optimal social welfare.
