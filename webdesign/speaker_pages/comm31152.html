Title: A Quantitative Framework for Value, Risk and Opportunity Management in Projects

Author: Tyson Browning, Assistant Prof. of Enterprise Operations, Texas Christian University, TCU Box 298530, Fort Worth, TX, 76129, United States of America, T.Browning@tcu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We provide an analytical framework for quantifying the value of a project, as well as the risk and opportunity it faces, as functions of its chosen goals, its stakeholders?desired values, its technological capabilities, and the prevailing uncertainties. We show how managers must think beyond a mere quantification of uncertainty to the expected costs and benefits of that uncertainty—i.e., risk and opportunity.
