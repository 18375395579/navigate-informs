Title: A New Methodology for Investigating Health Disparities

Author: Chester Spell, Associate Professor, Rutgers University Camden, School of Business, 227 Penn Street, Rm 331, Camden, NJ, 08102-1656, United States of America, cspell@camden.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Katerina Bezrukova, Alok Baveja

Year: 2013

Abstract: A challenge in understanding disparities in health between communities is evaluating the effect of multiple disparity-related factors (e.g., disease incidence, location, access to care). Using the emerging concept of macro-faultlines, or alignment of characteristics that individuals within a population share, we develop an innovative method that assesses 'total health disparity.? The goal is to address community-based disparities and preventive-health strategies in a more informed manner.
