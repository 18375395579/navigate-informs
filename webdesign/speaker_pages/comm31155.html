Title: Non-Partisan Political Redistricting

Author: Hayri Onal, Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 1301 W. Gregory Dr., 305 Mumford Hall, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, h-onal@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Kevin patrick

Year: 2013

Abstract: A linear integer programming model is developed to configure non-partisan political districts with population equality, compactness, contiguity, and community integrity considerations. An application to redistricting in Illinois will be presented using the 2000 and 2010 census data where approximately 3000 census tracts are assumed as spatial units.
