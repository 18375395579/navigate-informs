Title: A Panel Discussion on Academic-industry Collaboration
Author: Karen Smilowitz, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, Technological Institute M233, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60208-3119, United States of America, ksmilowitz@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Lawrence V. Snyder, Sean Willems, Ranga Nuggehalli, Jon Owen, Alan Erera
Year: 2013
Abstract: Please join us for a lively, open discussion of industry/academia research collaboration. Panelists from a mix of industry and academia will discuss collaboration strategies, focusing on best practices and pitfalls to avoid. Ph.D. students and junior faculty are encouraged to attend; panelists will discuss how to successfully initiate and implement collaborations, and how to turn collaborations into strong publications.
