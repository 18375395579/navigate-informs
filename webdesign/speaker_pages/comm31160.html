Title: Assessment of Productive and Environmental Efficiencies of Japanese Industries
Author: Mika Goto, Senior Research Economist, Central Research Institute of Electric Power Industry, 1-6-1, Otemachi, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo, 100-8126, Japan, mika@criepi.denken.or.jp
Coauthor(s): Akihiro Otsuka, Toshiyuki Sueyoshi
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study proposes a new use of DEA models to assess unified (operational and environmental) efficiency concerning Japanese manufacturing and non-manufacturing industries of 47 prefectures. Measuring their energy utilization and energy efficiencies, this study discusses their energy and environmental implications for development of a sustainable society.
