Title: Analysis of Exchange and Refund Behavior for U.S. Airlines

Author: Laurie Garrow, Assistant Professor, School of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, 790 Atlantic Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, laurie.garrow@ce.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Roger Parker, Dan Iliescu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We analyze airline passengers?exchange and refund behavior for multiple airlines using 2004 ticketing data from the Airline Reporting Corporation. Hazard models are used to analyze the probability a ticket will be exchanged and refunded and the timing of the event. Multiple markets allow exploration of differences in leisure and business segments and differences in competitive market structure.
