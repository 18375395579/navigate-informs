Title: Is Prison-based Substance Abuse Treatment Cost-beneficial?
Author: Katherine Hicks, RTI International, 3040 Cornwallis Rd, RTP, NC, 27709, United States of America, khicks@rti.org
Coauthor(s): Laura Dunlap, Alexander Cowell, Michael Mills, Vincent Keyes, Gary Zarkin, Steven Belenko, Kimberly Houser
Year: 2008
Abstract: We will present a discrete event model simulating the remaining lifetimes of a cohort of state prison inmates with respect to substance abuse and treatment, criminal activity, healthcare usage and productivity to estimate the lifetime benefits and costs of prison-based treatment and different policy scenarios. We will discuss results and interesting findings, as well as the challenges of developing models for which data is scarce.
