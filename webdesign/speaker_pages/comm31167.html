Title: IT Capability for Health Care Delivery: Is More Better?

Author: Kingshuk Sinha, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, ksinha@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Sriram Thirumalai, Asoke Dey

Year: 2013

Abstract: Electronic medical record (EMR) capability is the ability to leverage health IT to enable and link the clinical processes for an effective and efficient delivery of care. Findings of an empirical study will be reported that investigated (i) the performance implications of EMR capability and (ii) whether providers should commit to higher stages of EMR capability.
