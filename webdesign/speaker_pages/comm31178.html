Title: Ensemble Methods for Adaptove Logistic Models
Author: Douglas Clarkson, Chief Scientist, ScienceOps, 11711 Noth Creek Parkway, Suite D-113, Bothell, WA, 980118808, United States of America, clarkson@scienceops.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: Here we examine ensemble methods in adaptive logistic models with particular interest in dissimilarity measures, detecting outliers, variable importance and missing value imputation.
