Title: The Mortgage for the “House Rich Cash Poor?Retirees: Credit Risk & Insurance for Reverse Mortgages

Author: Gigi Yuen-Reed, Consultant, IBM Global Business Services, 12902 Federal Systems Park Dr., Fairfax, VA, 22033, United States, gigi.yuen@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Edward Szymanoski, James Enriquez

Year: 2007

Abstract: Reverse mortgages (RM) provide senior citizens cash payments and/or a credit line secured by their home’s equity. While the primary risk of traditional mortgage is loan default, RM’s risks are borrowers?mortality, mobility, and the liquidation value at move out. To provide a more efficient financial pricing structure for RM, we formulated a loan repayment model from historical data and developed a stochastic pricing model to incorporate the impact of house price appreciation and interest rate.
