Title: Tutorial: Transforming Military Supply Chains - Management Innovation in DoD
Author: Greg Parlier, Colonel, USA Retired, Institute for Defense Analyses, 255 Avian Lane, Madison, AL, United States of America, greg.parlier@meitechinc.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: This tutorial offers a practical approach for understanding the Army's extremely complex global logistics system. The new concept of management innovation as a strategic technology (MIST) is introduced and described. Cutting-edge supply chain theory and powerful analytical methods are applied to this seemingly intractable national security resource challenge which has remained on the Government Accountability Office's (GAO) high-risk list for two decades now.
