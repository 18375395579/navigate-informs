Title: Single versus Multisourcing: The Role of Effort Interdependence and Metric-Outcome Misalignment
Author: Sameer Hasija, INSEAD, Fontainbleau, France, sameer.hasija@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Shantanu Bhattacharya, Alok Gupta
Year: 2012
Abstract: Many factors influence the costs and benefits of single versus multisourcing. Keeping all other factors equal, in this paper we focus on the role of client-vendor effort interdependence and performance metric-outcome misalignment on informing a firm's sourcing strategy.
