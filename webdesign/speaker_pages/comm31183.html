Title: Performance of the Minimum Data Set (MDS-2.0) to Predict Risk of Falling in Nursing Home Residents
Author: Jenya Antonova, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1175 WARF Building, 610 Walnut Street, Madison, WI, 53726, United States, Jenya_Antonova@chsra.wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): David Zimmerman
Year: 2006
Abstract: Falling is a common widespread problem that can cause severe injury in the elderly. We developed and tested a model using data from the Minimum Data Set to identify factors that predict the probability of falling in nursing home residents. Results are reported.
