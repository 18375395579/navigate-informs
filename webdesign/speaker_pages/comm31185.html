Title: Panel: Outsourcing and/or Offshoring Analytics

Author: Peter Bell, Professor, Western University, Ivey School of Business, London, ON, N6A3K7, Canada, pbell@ivey.uwo.ca

Year: 2013

Abstract: More and more companies are now buying their analytics from domestic or offshore suppliers. Others operate 'captive' offshore analytics divisions. This panel will discuss the benefits and costs of these analytics acquisition strategies as well as the reasons for the dramatic growth in analytics outsourcing/offshoring.
