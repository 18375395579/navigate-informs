Title: Reliability - Research Directions and Curriculum Needs
Author: Yong Chen, University of Iowa, Iowa City, IA, United States, yongchen@engineering.uiowa.edu
Coauthor(s): Elsayed Elsayed, Marlin Thomas, Kailash Kapur, Way Kuo
Year: 2007
Abstract: The panelists will first present their own visions on promising directions and crucial needs in reliability research and education. A Q&A session and open discussion with the audience will be held following the presentations.
