Title: Progress Report for The Decision Education Foundation
Author: Carl Spetzler, CEO, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, cspetzler@sdg.com
Coauthor(s): Chris Spetzler
Year: 2012
Abstract: The Decison Education Foundation has developed on-line curriculum to teach decision skills to youth. It has piloted this curriculum in a blended (classroom and online) course design. In thissession we will share the lessons learned and how Decision Professionals can participate in bringing decision skills to their local schools or extracurricular programs.
