Title: Human Capacity Building in LMIC

Author: Yehuda Bassok, Professor, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, bassok@marshall.usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Sriram Dasu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We will present our experiences in building human capacity for managing health care delivery systems mainly in Cameroon. We work with local universities to train their student: medicine, pharmacy, nursing and health policy, to improve the performance of the health system without adding much resources but improving the efficiency of the system. We also run research and implementation project that use the concepts we have delivered in the class room to improve performance
