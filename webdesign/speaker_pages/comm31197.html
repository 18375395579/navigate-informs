Title: Industry Studies and Robustness in Airline Planning

Author: Amy Cohn, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Operations Engineering, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave., Ann Arbor, MI, 48105, United States of America, amycohn@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Belobaba

Year: 2008

Abstract: What is Industry Studies? And what role does it play in helping to design more robust airline plans? In this talk, we address these questions, focusing on our experience with carriers working to address the following key questions: How is robustness defined? How can it be measured? How should it be valued? How do we achieve it?
