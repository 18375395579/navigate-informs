Title: Kidney Exchange: Present and Future

Author: Tuomas Sandholm, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave., Pittsburgh, PA, 15232, United States of America, sandholm@cs.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): John Dickerson, Ariel Procaccia

Year: 2013

Abstract: I will share my experiences in designing and developing the algorithms for the UNOS nationwide kidney exchange. I will discuss the main challenges facing kidney exchanges, focusing on the two key dynamics: 1) various natural failures that cause most matches not to go to transplant, and 2) entry and exit of pairs and altruists. I will discuss results from several of our recent papers that tackle optimization under these dynamics.
