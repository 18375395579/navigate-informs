Title: Insurance Agency Productivity, Efficiency and Prospecting

Author: Mark Grabau, IBM, United States of America

Coauthor(s): Elizabeth Riczko

Year: 2012

Abstract: Insurance companies desire a fact-based, decision-making process for managing their agency distribution channel such that it is productive and profitable. We present an approach that combines Data Envelopment Analysis, Predictive Modeling, Collaborative Filtering, and Reporting to objectively meet that need. Results are discussed as well as areas for future research and on-going improvements.
