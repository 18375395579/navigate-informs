Title: Solar Value Chain Decision Analysis in Oil and Gas Rich Countries

Author: Thomas Seyller, Sr. Engagement Manager, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson St., Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, tseyller@sdg.com

Coauthor(s): Dan Cornew

Year: 2012

Abstract: We will explore how decision analysis can help oil and gas rich countries develop strategies around solar energy that maximize the total benefit to the nation ?including economic value and costs, social benefits such as job creation, and environmental impacts. Strategies cover a wide range of possible incentives and mandates throughout the solar value chain, from manufacturing to deployment.
