Title: Best Publication in Energy: An Approximate Dynamic Programming Approach to Benchmark Practice-Based
Author: Nicola Secomandi, Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, ns7@andrew.cmu.edu
Coauthor(s): Guoming Lai, Francois Margot
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper benchmarks real options methods used by practitioners to value and operate natural gas storage. I will focus on discussing the origin of this paper, how the underlying research unfolded over time, and additional research prompted by this work.
