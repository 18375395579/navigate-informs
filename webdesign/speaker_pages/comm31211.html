Title: Modeling Investments in Preparedness in a Cournot Duopoly Market
Author: Vicki Bier, Professor, University of Wisconsin - Madison, 3234 Mechanical Engineering Building, 1513 University Ave, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, bier@engr.wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Taher Jamshidi
Year: 2008
Abstract: This paper models the decision-making process of two firms in a Cournot duopoly about whether to invest in disaster preparedness. We consider both fixed and variable preparedness costs. Some cases result in multiple equilibrium strategies, where each firm wishes to bet on a different outcome (disaster or not).
