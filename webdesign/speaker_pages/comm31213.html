Title: Integrating Ethics into the OR/MS Classroom

Author: Matthew Drake, Assistant Professor, Duquesne University, A.J. Palumbo School of Business, Donahue Graduate School of Business, Pittsburgh, PA, 15282, United States, drake987@duq.edu

Coauthor(s): David Wasieleski, James Weber

Year: 2006

Abstract: The importance of ethics instruction has been recognized by accreditation boards and many companies in recent years. Many OR/MS instructors, though, have yet to integrate ethics into their classrooms, partly because they do not think they have adequate training in the subject. We discuss ways in which even the most ethically-novice instructors can introduce ethics topics into their classrooms.
