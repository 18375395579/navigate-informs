Title: A Location Model of an Eyeglass Lens Adjustment Problem

Author: Richard Francis, Professor Emeritus, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, francis@ise.ufl.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The problem is to find one (or several) best eyeglass lens adjustment(s) for an eye with double vision when the vision changes throughout the day. We model the problem as a well-known location problem, and mention some personal experience. Known theoretical results have some interesting applications, believed to be new.
