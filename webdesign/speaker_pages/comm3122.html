Title: Industry Studies and Healthcare Scheduling
Author: Amy Cohn, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Operations Engineering, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave., Ann Arbor, MI, 48105, United States of America, amycohn@umich.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: What is Industry Studies? What roles does it play in healthcare scheduling? How is healthcare different from other industries, in the role that Operations Research can play in solving challenging problems? We address these questions in the context of our work in scheduling healthcare personnel, focusing on both the algorithmic approach and the underlying problem definition.
