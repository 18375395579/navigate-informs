Title: New Product Development Management under Time Uncertainty

Author: Leonardo P. Santiago, Assistant Professor, Federal University of Minas Gerais, Escola de Engenharia, Av. Antonio Carlos, 6627 - Pampulha, Belo Horizonte, MG, 31270-901, Brazil, lsantiago@ufmg.br

Coauthor(s): Thiago Augusto Silva

Year: 2008

Abstract: The development time is one of the key factors that contribute to the new product development success. In this context, the objective of the present paper is to evaluate the development process of new technologies under time uncertainty. The novelty in our approach is to thoroughly blend the stochastic time with a formal approach to the problem.
