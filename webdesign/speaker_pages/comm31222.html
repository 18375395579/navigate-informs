Title: Impact of Website Functionalities on e-Retailer Sales: An Econometric Analysis

Author: Guanyi Lu, PhD Candidate, Mays Business School at Texas A&M University, 320N, 4217 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843-4217, United States of America, glu@mays.tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiaosong Peng, Howard Hao-Chun Chuang, Gregory Heim

Year: 2012

Abstract: Utilizing a three-year panel data set of top500 Internet retailers, we examine the effects of various web functionalities on retailer sales. The results suggest that only value-added web functionalities exhibit a direct and sizable effect on web sales; the effects of fundamental and customer-centered web functionalities appear to be non-substantial.
