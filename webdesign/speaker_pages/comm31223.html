Title: Dynamic Scheduling and Planning of In-game Advertisements
Author: Sridhar Tayur, SmartOps, 12 Federal Street, Suite 400, Pittsburgh, PA, 15212, United States, stayur@smartops.com
Coauthor(s): John Turner, Alan Scheller-Wolf
Year: 2007
Abstract: With the acquisition of Massive Incorporated by Microsoft, and of Adspace by Google, the competition for acquiring market leadership in this nacsent but fast growing space is well on its way. As the number of multi-player, internet based games, players and the number and types of advertisments grow, there is a need for optimization methods that are quick as well as effective.
