Title: DEA Model for Supply Chain Network Redesign
Author: Seungkee Baek, GRIPS, 7-22-1, Roppongi, Minatoku, Tokyo, Japan, seungkee@grips.ac.jp
Coauthor(s): Kaoru Tone
Year: 2006
Abstract: Well designed supply chain network will guarantee successful performance of supply chain members. We evauate relative efficiency of supply chain network using DEA and propose strategic approach for performance improvement.
