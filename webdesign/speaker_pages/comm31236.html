Title: Valuation of Fast Pyrolysis Pathway via Real Options Approach

Author: Yihua Li, Iowa State University, Ames, IA, United States of America, yihuali@iastate.edu

Coauthor(s): Guiping Hu, Chung-Li Tseng

Year: 2013

Abstract: Biofuels have the potential to substitute petroleum fuels and reduce the U.S. dependency on imported petroleum. In this study, the investment decision making for advanced biofuel producers is investigated. The production pathway under investigation is fast pyrolysis of corn stover and upgrading to fuels. Stochasticity in biomass supply and biofuel demand is taken into consideration. Real option approach is used to evaluate the investment strategies. A case study in Iowa is presented.
