Title: An Interactive Panel/Audience Discussion: Research and Publications
Author: Candace Yano, Professor, University of California, Industrial Engineering and OR, Haas School of Business, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, yano@ieor.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): David Simchi-Levi, Wallace Hopp, Martin Lariviere
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this interactive panel discussion, we will discuss the role of publishing in academia. The panel comprise of the editors of the leading academic journals in the OR/OM field. The Panel members will discuss the importance of academic publishing in different environments such as business schools and engineering departments. A large part of the panel will be dedicated to interactive discussion with the Audience. We plan to: 1) provide an overview of the publication outlets for Engineering and Business School faculty; 2) describe how the publishing process works; 3) present strategies for picking topics for publication; and 4) discuss the research/practice interface.
