Title: Centralized Topology Control and Enhanced Distribution Network LMPs for T&D Resilience
Author: Michael Caramanis, Professor, Boston University, Division of Systems Engineering, 15 Saint Mary's Street, Brookline, MA, 02446, United States of America, Mcaraman@bu.edu
Coauthor(s): Pablo Ruiz, Justin Foster
Year: 2012
Abstract: T&D infrastructure resilience is a key objective of efforts for the triple sustainability -- environmental, economic and energy security ?of massive clean energy generation integration. We will outline new directions towards this end encompassing (i) practical Dynamic Transmission Network Reconfiguration and (ii) expanded Distribution Network Locational Marginal Cost-based Pricing.
