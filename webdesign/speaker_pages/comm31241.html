Title: Assessing the Use of Alternative Aircraft Vehicles to Address Future Capacity Shortfalls

Author: Jeremy Eckhause, LMI, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, 22102, United States of America, jeckhause@lmi.org

Coauthor(s): Yuri Gawdiak, George Price, Robert Hemm

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Strategy, Architecture and Analysis Division of NASA’s Aeronautics Research Mission Directorate (ARMD SAA) evaluated several NextGen alternatives for the state of the air transportation system in 2025. To address potential capacity shortfalls at some airports, ARMD SAA analyzed the potential benefit of accommodating unsatisfied demand through the use of Civil Tiltrotor (CTR) and Cruise-Efficient Short Takeoff and Landing (CESTOL) aircraft. Methodology and performance results are presented.
