Title: Can Pythagoras Predict the Winners of the English Premier and Spanish Football Leagues?
Author: James Cochran, Louisiana Tech University, Ruston, LA, United States of America, jcochran@latech.edu
Coauthor(s): Cedric Luma
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Pythagorean method, developed and applied to Major League Baseball by Bill James over 30 years ago, has been extended to NFL and NCAA (American) football, NCAA and NBA basketball, and NHL hockey. We explore the application of James?Pythagoras method to the English Premier and Spanish Football Leagues, and consider the optimal functional form and parameters for such a model. We compare the results for the two leagues and look at changes across several seasons.
