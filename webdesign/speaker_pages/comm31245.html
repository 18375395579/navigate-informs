Title: An Empirical Analysis of Product Category Entry Decisions
Author: Necati Tereyagoglu, Assistant Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, Scheller College of Business, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, Necati.Tereyagoglu@scheller.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Cem Ozturk
Year: 2013
Abstract: Using a comprehensive store-level data set, we investigate retailer’s strategic decisions regarding which product categories to offer. In particular, we investigate how category entry decisions are related to company, customer and competition characteristics.
