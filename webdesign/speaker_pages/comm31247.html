Title: Structural and Geographic Shifts in Warehousing
Author: Anne Goodchild, Assistant Professor, University of Washington, Dept. of Civil and Environmental Eng., 121E More Hall, Seattle, WA, 98195-2700, United States of America, annegood@u.washington.edu
Coauthor(s): Derik Andreoli
Year: 2008
Abstract: The warehousing industry has experienced rapid growth and structural change over the last decade. In this paper we describe this change within the United States and compare this growth to geographic and industry factors. We discuss the transportation consequences of the rapid rise of super-warehouses or inland ports.
