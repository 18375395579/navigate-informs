Title: Impacts of Top Service Designers: Analysis of Golf Course Quality and Price

Author: Seung Jun Lee, PhD Student, Texas A&M University, Department of Information & Operations M, 320 Wehner Building 4217 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843-4217, United States of America, sjlee@mays.tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Gregory Heim, Michael Ketzenberg

Year: 2013

Abstract: Service firms leverage talents of top designers who built their excellence upon past design successes and prior competitive performances to design better experiences. Using data on golf courses and top golf course designers, we study whether top designers and lead user designers influence quality and pricing of golf courses. Empirical results provide insights about possible impacts of using top and lead user designers.
