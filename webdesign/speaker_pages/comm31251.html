Title: Work Life Balance - A Mom's Perspective
Author: Alice Smith, Professor and Chair, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, Auburn University, 207 Dunstan Hall, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States, aesmith@eng.auburn.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Combining an academic career, parenting and personal life is the topic. Achieving success with all while maintaining sanity, and even serenity, is the goal. Synergies of academic work with home life is an occasional, and welcome, byproduct.
