Title: Converting Real Time Group Agreements into Successful Implementations

Author: Ashley Carreras, Principal Lecturer, De Montfort University, The Gateway, Leicester, LE19BH, United Kingdom, acarreras@dmu.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Parmjit Kaur

Year: 2008

Abstract: We ran an event for a multinational company on the theme of Delivering Breakthrough Technology. A number of outcomes were sought from the event including the selection of four breakthrough technologies in two areas of their core business activity and the development of an improved process for the roll-out of new technology across a range of internationally located production units. This paper explores the factors that became evident in ensuring that the event was viewed as a success post event.
