Title: Upsell Effects in Network O&D Revenue Management
Author: Richard Ratliff, Sr. Research Scientist, Sabre, 3150 Sabre Dr., Southlake, TX, 76092, United States, Richard.Ratliff@sabre-holdings.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: This presentation will provide a practitioner's perspective on the issues involved in incorporating upsell and recapture effects in network O&D revenue management (including the impact of demand dependencies across fare classes).
