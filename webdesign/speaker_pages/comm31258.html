Title: Oil and Gas Industry: Career Opportunities in Optimization, Logistics and Data Analysis

Author: Cassandra McZeal, Senior Staff Mathematician, ExxonMobil, PO Box 2189, URC-URC-C307, Houston, TX, 77252-2189, United States of America, cassandra.m.mczeal@exxonmobil.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The increasing complexity of finding, developing, and managing energy sources brings many opportunities for innovation in the areas of optimization, logistics and data analysis. In this talk I will cover several challenges facing the industry and focus on a few example to demonstrate the need for professionals in multiple disciplines from optimization to data analysis.
