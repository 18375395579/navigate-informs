Title: Understanding Social Dynamics ?A Computational Approach

Author: Jaideep Srivastava, Professor, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, srivasta@cs.umn.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: First, we describe findings from the Virtual World Exploratorium; a multi-institutional, multi-disciplinary project which considers data from commercial MMOGs and VWs for social science. Second, we provide a survey of new approaches for behavioral informatics that are being developed by multi-disciplinary teams, and their successes. Third, we discuss some novel potential applications and their associated research issues. Finally, we present commercial applications of Social Analytics research.
