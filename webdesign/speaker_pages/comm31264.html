Title: Energy Efficiency in India’s Energy Intensive Industries: A Directional Distance Function Analysis
Author: Kankana Mukherjee, Associate Professor, Babson College, Economics Division, 231 Forest Street, Babson Park, MA, 02457, United States of America, kmukherjee@babson.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study utilizes the method of Data Envelopment Analysis and directional distance function to examine the energy efficiency of each of the energy intensive industries across the states in India over the period 2004-05 to 2007-08. Results show that significant potential for simultaneous increase in output and reduction in energy use exists, particularly for the paper and paper products industry and for the chemicals and chemical products industry.
