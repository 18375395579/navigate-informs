Title: Boeing Operations Research Project Examples
Author: Sharon Arroyo, The Boeing Company, PO Box 3707, MC 42-58, Seattle, WA, 98124, United States of America, sharon.f.arroyo@boeing.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Operations Research Group at Boeing partners with organizations across the company to improve results by applying operations research expertise to business opportunities. The group is engaged in improving processes in areas including supply chain, transportation, production, and communications systems. Insights into how the group collaborates and specific project examples will be given in this presentation.
