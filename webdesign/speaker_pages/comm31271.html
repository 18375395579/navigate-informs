Title: How to Make Professional Presentations

Author: Xuegang Ban, Assistant Professor, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY, United States of America, banx@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingyan Lou, Srinivas Peeta, Stephen Boyles

Year: 2013

Abstract: This panel discussion session aims to help young professionals including graduate students, post-doctoral researchers, and junior faculty member to improve their presentation skills. The panelists will share their own experiences and tips on how to make the best professional presentations. This will be followed by Q/A. Live discussions are expected among the panelists, and between the panel and the audience.
