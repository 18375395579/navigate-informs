Title: Performance Characteristics of Flowshops with Work Flow Control

Author: Steven Harrod, Assistant Professor, University of Dayton, 1143 Ashburton Dr., Dayton, OH, 45459, United States of America, a2soft@aol.com

Coauthor(s): John Kanet

Year: 2013

Abstract: Very often the terms pull and make to order are conflated and treated as synonymous. In this presentation we emphasize how this conflation is inaccurate, and by example demonstrate how important it is to describe the distinct combinations of push/pull and make to order/make to stock. Best practices are summarized by these combinations.
