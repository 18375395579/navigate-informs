Title: Analysis of Patient Triage in Urban Pediatric Emergency Departments
Author: Michael DeGuzman, Children's HealthCare of Atlanta, Michael.DeGuzman@choa.org
Coauthor(s): Eva K. Lee
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this talk, we describe the challenges of pediatric patient triage to correct level of acuity in the emergency departments. Two approaches: conventional 4-tier triage versus the 5-tier emergency severity index (ESI) will be described and contrasted. Preliminary work using OR approaches to improve proper patient categorization will be described.
