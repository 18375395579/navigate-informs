Title: Analysis of the Biological Clock Decision

Author: Dinah Vernik, Ph.D Candidate, Duke University, The Fuqua School of Business, 1 Towerview Rd, Box 311, Durham, NC, 27708, United States, dav2@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Ralph Keeney

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a prescriptive approach to a decision when a professional woman should begin trying to conceive her first child. The model incorporates separate objectives for a woman’s professional, social, and family aspects of life and integrates them into a quality of life function that includes the changing relative importance of these aspects with age over a woman’s life. Several illustrations are included to demonstrate insights that can be generated using the model.
