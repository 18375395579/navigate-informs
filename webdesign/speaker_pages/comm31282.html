Title: Technology Integration Framework for Early-stage NPD
Author: Neslihan Sener, Portland State University, PO Box 751, Portland, OR, United States, neslido@yahoo.com
Coauthor(s): Tugrul Daim
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper develops a framework for multi dimensional technology integration analysis at the early stages of product development. As a case, Intel Corporation's product development process is analyzed.
