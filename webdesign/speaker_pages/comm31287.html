Title: The Impact of Conformance and Experiential Quality on Healthcare Cost and Clinical Performance

Author: Claire Senot, The Ohio State University, 2100 Neil Ave, Columbus, United States of America, senot_1@fisher.osu.edu

Coauthor(s): Aravind Chandrasekaran, Peter T. Ward, Anita Tucker

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate the relationship between a dual focus on conformance and experiential quality in the process of care and hospitals?cost and clinical performance. We collect data for 3458 U.S. acute care hospitals over 3 years. Findings reveal a trade-off between clinical outcomes and cost efficiency.
