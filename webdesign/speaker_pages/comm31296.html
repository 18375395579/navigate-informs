Title: ENRE Award Session
Author: Juan Pablo Vielma, University of Pittsburgh, 1043 Benedum Hall, 3700 O'Hare Street, Pittsburgh, PA, 15261, United States of America, jvielma@pitt.edu

Abstract: This session includes presentations by the winners of the 2012 student travel award and best publications in energy, natural resources and the environment. In addition the following 2011 awards will be given: -Bistra Dilkina, Best Paper Award in Forestry Sponsored Sessions; -Rodolfo Carvajal, Best Paper Award in Environment and Sustainability Sponsored Sessions; - Gonzalo Munoz and Donal O'Sullivan, Best Paper Awards in Mining Sponsored Sessions.
