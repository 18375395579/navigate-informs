Title: JPDO Systems Engineering and Analysis Division Update

Author: Shahab Hasan, Program Manager, LMI, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, 22102, United States of America, shasan@lmi.org

Coauthor(s): Sherry Borener, Gregory Carr, Fred Wieland, George Hunter, Dan Goldner, Monica Alcabin, Michael Graham, Jeremiah Creedon, Michael Marcolini

Year: 2007

Abstract: The Joint Planning and Development Office's (JPDO) Systems Engineering and Analysis Division (SEAD) uses a breadth of modeling and simulation tools to assess the benefits of the NextGen air transportation system. SEAD analysts will discuss how our initial parametric benefits estimates have evolved into more detailed and specific analyses of operational improvements in the areas of capacity, efficiency, environmental performance, and safety.
