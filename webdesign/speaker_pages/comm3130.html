Title: Peptide Extraction Using Regular Expressions and Support Vector Machines

Author: Theodore B. Trafalis, Professor, The University of Oklahoma -- School of Industrial Engineering, 202 West Boyd room 124, Norman, OK, 73019, United States, ttrafalis@ou.edu

Coauthor(s): Robin C Gilbert

Year: 2007

Abstract: Short sequences of peptide are often contained only within the published medical literature rather than in databases and thus are not available until located and extracted. Herein we report the efficacy of using regular expressions and support vector machines to differentiate peptide sequences from the text that surrounds them. We have tested the system on over 10,000 journal articles. Our results suggest that this method will help in automated extraction of sequences from large texts.
