Title: Charity Auctions on Social Networks
Author: Mohammad Mahdian, Research Scientist, Yahoo! Research, 2821 Mission College Blvd, Santa Clara, CA, 95054, United States of America, mahdian@alum.mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Arpita Ghosh
Year: 2008
Abstract: Charitable giving is influenced by many social, psychological, and economic factors. A common way to encourage individuals to donate to offer to match their gift. Conitzer and Sandholm introduced auctions that allow individuals to match each other's contribution. We explore this idea in a social network setting, where agents care about the contribution of their friends. We give mechanisms that raise the largest individually rational amount, and analyze the equilibria of this mechanism.
