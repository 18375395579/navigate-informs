Title: Ideas to Action: Application of Quality Tools in Outpatient Healthcare

Author: Linda Laganga, Director of Quality Systems & Operational Excellence, Mental Health Center of Denver, 4141 East Dickenson Place, Denver, CO, 80222, United States of America, linda.laganga@mhcd.org

Coauthor(s): Cathie McLean, Paul Frankel, Craig Iverson, Jim Linderman, CJ McKinney, Wes Williams

Year: 2013

Abstract: We apply Tools of Quality to engage work teams in problem identification and solution implementation. We present recent projects, measurable results, and quantifiable value to the organization. Our Pareto analysis and utilization management tools identify costly service patterns and guide redistribution of resources to improve consumer outcomes and cost-effectiveness.
