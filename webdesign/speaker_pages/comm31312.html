Title: Results of a Decision Analysis of Alternate Modes of Dispensing Medications in LA
Author: Anke Richter, Associate Professor, Naval Postgraduate School, Defense Resources Management Institute, 699 Dyer Rd, Bldg 234, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, arichter@nps.edu
Coauthor(s): Sinan Khan
Year: 2007
Abstract: Mass prophylaxis planning for a large-scale public health emergency is heavily reliant on “Points of Dispensing?(POD) distribution sites. To assess the relative merits of alternate modes of dispensing in LA County, a decision analysis was conducted. Sensitivity analyses reveal that the choices from the public health point of view are robust. However, examining the problem from the law enforcement angle reveals potential areas of conflict with these fellow first responders.
