Title: Process Innovation and Competition in an Industrial Symbiotic System
Author: Yunxia Zhu, University of Texas - Dallas, 800 West Campbell Road, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, yunxia.zhu@utdallas.edu
Coauthor(s): Milind Dawande, Srinagesh Gavirneni, Vaidy Jayaraman
Year: 2012
Abstract: Inspired by a real-world example of a paper-sugar symbiotic complex, we study the impact on a firm's operational decisions from implementing an industrial symbiotic system. Our models capture both supply- and demand-side influences of implementation. We characterize the firm's optimal/equilibrium decisions, both in the presence and absence of the system, under monopoly as well as under Cournot competition. Our focus is on understanding the firm's “willingness?to implement the symbiotic system.
