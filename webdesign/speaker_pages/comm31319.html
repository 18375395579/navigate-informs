Title: New Non-nash Equilibria for Noncooperative Games

Author: Bill Corley, Professor, IMSE Department, Box 19017, Arlington, TX, 76019, United States of America, corley@uta.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: A new equilibrium embodying an intrinsic cooperative aspect is presented for noncooperative games. It addresses some well-known issues in game theory. For example, in contrast to the Nash equilibrium, it provides one-time sensible solutions for Prisoner's Dilemma and Traveler's Dilemma agreeing with experimental results. Existence of the new equilibrium is established, its implications are discussed, and applications are presented.
