Title: Collaborative Research: Stochastic Challenge

Author: Timothy Matis, Texas Tech University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Box 43061, Lubbock, TX, 79409, United States of America, timothy.matis@ttu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This presentation will describe the educational research activities associated with the NSF-sponsored project “Collaborative Research: Stochastic Challenge? a collaborative research project between the Texas Tech University, Missouri University of Science and Technology and the University of Texas ?Pan American. We will describe how these tools may be of use to Operations Research students and professionals interested in Telecommunications applications.
