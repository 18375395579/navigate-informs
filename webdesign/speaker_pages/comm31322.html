Title: The Peer-to-Peer Communication Paradigm and its Influence on the IT Industry

Author: Nicolas C. Liebau, Technische Universität Darmstadt, Merckstrasse 25, Darmstadt, He, 64283, Germany, nicolas.liebau@KOM.tu-darmstadt.de

Coauthor(s): Oliver Heckmann, Andreas Mauthe, Ralf Steinmetz

Year: 2006

Abstract: Applications following the Peer-to-Peer (P2P) communication paradigm are responsible for over 60% of the Internet traffic volume today. As the example of Skype shows, P2P systems potentially have drastic impact on future markets. In this paper, we discuss the P2P communication paradigm and evaluate its impact on the IT industry and eBusiness companies, on products, markets, and business models using case studies.
