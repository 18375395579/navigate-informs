Title: New Product Development (NPD): Impact of Design-Manufacturing Interface

Author: Debasish N. Mallick, Associate Professor, University of St. Thomas, Opus College of Business, 1000 LaSalle Ave #SCH 435, Minneapolis, MN, 55403, United States of America, dnmallick@stthomas.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We explore the issues at the interface between design and manufacturing activities and examine how these issues impact the new product development performance.
