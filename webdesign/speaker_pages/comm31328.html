Title: A Broker Agent for Smart Grid Balancing

Author: Daniel Urieli, PhD Candidate, The University of Texas at Austin, 2317 Speedway, Stop D9500, Austin, TX, United States of America, urieli@utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Stone

Year: 2013

Abstract: The 'Smart Grid' vision includes incorporating clean, renewable, distributed energy sources into the electricity grid. This creates major challenges in the form of (1) balancing electricity production and consumption at all times, under uncertain generation and consumption (2) encouraging customer participation. The talk will focus on a broker agent that aims at addressing these challenges. The broker is fully implemented and tested in the PowerTAC smart grid simulation environment.
