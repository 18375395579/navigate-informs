Title: Workforce Cross-training with Learning
Author: Scott Grasman, Professor and Head, Rochester Institute of Technology, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 81 Lomb Memorial Drive, Rochester, NY, 14623, United States of America, Scott.Grasman@rit.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: This work develops cross-training frameworks that incorporate worker learning. A mathematical modeling framework is presented along with key insights from the solution of practical cases. These insights lead to policies for workforce development.
