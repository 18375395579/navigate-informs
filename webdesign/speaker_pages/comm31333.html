Title: Logistics Aspects of Collecting and Processing Biomass Rest Flows
Author: Simme Douwe Flapper, Technische Universiteit Eindhoven, Den Dolech 2, Eindhoven, Br, Netherlands, S.D.P.Flapper@tm.tue.nl
Year: 2008
Abstract: After a short introduction into some specific issues related to the logistics of biomass rest flows, two case studies are briefly discussed. Topics for further research are indicated.
