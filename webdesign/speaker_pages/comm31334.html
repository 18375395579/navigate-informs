Title: Quality and Service Analytics Tool Box

Author: Ramachandran Chandrashehar, HP Global Analytics, India

Coauthor(s): Pavithra V., Ramani Sathyanarayana, Sharmila Samuel, Gayathri Arunkumar, K.V. Nathan, M. Govindakumar

Year: 2013

Abstract: The ‘Quality & Services analytics toolbox?with its 360 degree view of data and advanced analytics capabilities has helped to identify & statistically predict the drivers of product and service quality, which in turn have helped to cut costs and improve profitability in HP’s PC business in Asia.
