Title: Hybridization Noise Removal From cDNA Microarrays
Author: Chaitra Gopalappa, Ph.D Student, Dept. of IMSE, University of South Florida, 4202 E. Fowler Ave, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States, chaitra@eng.usf.edu
Coauthor(s): Steven Eschrich, Steven Enkemann, Tapas K. Das
Year: 2007
Abstract: Microarray technology offers a significant tool for harnessing the true potential of human genome discovery via development of efficient disease diagnosis and treatment planning tools. A major impediment in this process has been the presence of noise distorting the gene expression values. We present a multiresolution strategy for removal of hybridization noise in microarrays.
