Title: Maintenance Recovery in Passenger Aviation

Author: Amy Cohn, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, United States of America, amycohn@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Marcial Lapp, Sergey Shebalov

Year: 2012

Abstract: Preventative maintenance is an important aspect of passenger aviation and must be planned in advance. When flight operations are disrupted, this can disrupt the maintenance plan as well. We present models and algorithms for addressing this problem.
