Title: Does Multitasking Improve Productivity? Evidence from the Emergency Department

Author: Diwas KC, Emory University, 1300 Clifton Road, Atlanta, GA, 30322, United States of America, Diwas_Kc@bus.emory.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We examine the effect of multitasking on performance in an emergency department. By drawing on recent findings in the experimental psychology literature we develop several hypotheses for the effect on performance. We find that multitasking has implications for the service encounter, including patient flow and quality of care.
