Title: Evidence of Reference Prices in B2B Markets for Used Products
Author: Wedad Elmaghraby, Associate Professor, University of Maryland, RH Smith School of Business, Van Munching Hall Room 4352, College Park, MD, 20742-1815, United States of America, wedad_elmaghraby@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Ali Pilehvar, Anand Gopal
Year: 2013
Abstract: We empirically study the performance of online auctions for returned products. We find evidence of differential impact of reference prices, emerging from concurrent auctions, on bids submitted in an auction, based on a bidder's experience.
