Title: On Comparing Flexibility Structures in Parallel Queueing Systems

Author: Douglas G. Down, Associate Professor, McMaster University, downd@univmail.cis.mcmaster.ca

Coauthor(s): Sigrun Andradottir, Hayriye Ayhan

Year: 2007

Abstract: We discuss two issues in the problem of comparing flexibility structures in systems of parallel servers. The first is the role of the server's scheduling policy. The second is how one may incorporate the variability of underlying distributions when making comparisons.
