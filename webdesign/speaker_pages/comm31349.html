Title: Social Production of Content in the New Media

Author: Hong Xu, UT Austin, IROM Dept, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, 78712, Hong.Xu@phd.mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrew Whinston, Jianqing Chen

Year: 2008

Abstract: An issue with open communities, including online newspapers, blogs, discussion forums, etc, is how to motivate the public to produce desired content? In absent of the hierarchy structure that induces content in traditional media outlets, we propose a moderation mechanism and investigate its impact on new media bias.
