Title: Opportunities in Healthcare Systems Engineering

Author: Ronald L. Rardin, White Distinguished Professor, University of Arkansas, Industrial Engineering, Bell 4169, Fayetteville, AR, 72762, United States of America, rrardin@uark.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Healthcare delivery in the United States is in a crisis of inconsistent and sometimes dismal quality, safety and efficiency, with exploding cost. While engineering is at the heart of many of the dramatic advances in medicine, the engineering that has been done on healthcare delivery processes and operations has had more limited impact. This presentation reviews the vast array of opportunities in such healthcare systems engineering.
