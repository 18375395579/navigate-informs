Title: Joint Planning and Development Office Metrics for Evaluating NextGen Performance
Author: Jerry Creedon, Professor, Old Dominion University, 3750 Elkhorn Avenue, Norfolk, VA, 23529, United States of America, jcreedon@odu.edu
Coauthor(s): Yuri Gawdiak, George Price
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper summarizes a set of metrics for use in assessing projected performance of the Next Generation Air Transportation System (NextGen). The effort, guided by the six goals established in the 2004 Integrated National Plan for NextGen, formulated a concise set of metrics to assess the projected benefits of NextGen for each of several NextGen stakeholder groups.
