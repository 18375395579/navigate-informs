Title: Teaching Revenue Management in the Carnegie Mellon Tepper School of Business MBA Program

Author: Nicola Secomandi, Assistant Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, ns7@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: This talk illustrates the speaker’s experience teaching an MBA revenue-management elective at the Carnegie Mellon Tepper School of Business since 2004.
