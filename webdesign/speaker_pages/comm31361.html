Title: Quality of Group-Tacit Knowledge

Author: Zeynep Erden, ETH Zurich, SMI, KPL G13, Kreuzplatz 5, Zurich, Switzerland, zerden@ethz.ch

Coauthor(s): Georg von Krogh, Ikujiro Nonaka

Year: 2007

Abstract: Abstract: In this article we review some of the central elements in organizational knowledge creation theory, define „quality of group-tacit knowledge?and develop a model explaining different levels in the quality of group tacit knowledge. As the basis for our model development, we use the SECI model from organizational knowledge creation theory (Nonaka, 1994).
