Title: A Bayesian Approach for Risk-adjusted Quality Monitoring in Healthcare

Author: Li Zeng, University of Wisconsin - Madison, 1513 University Ave., Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, lzeng1@wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Shiyu Zhou

Year: 2008

Abstract: One challenge in healthcare outcome monitoring is the need of adjustment for patient case mix, i.e., taking the heterogeneity of base-line patient risk into account. This paper proposes a Bayesian approach which can not only implement risk-adjusted monitoring, but also can estimate the change point once a change is detected.
