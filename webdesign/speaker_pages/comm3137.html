Title: Developing the National Aviation Safety Strategic Plan

Author: Wendell Ricks, NASA, 1 N. Dryden St., MS 449, Langley Research Center, Hampton, VA, 23681, United States of America, Wendell.R.Ricks@nasa.gov

Coauthor(s): Stephen Darr

Year: 2008

Abstract: The U.S. National Aviation Safety Strategic Plan (NASSP) defines national goals, objectives, and strategies for aviation safety improvements. The NASSP was developed and vetted by the JPDO partner departments and agencies with close collaboration of industry stakeholders and academia subject matter experts. The planning for the NASSP included an analysis that drew upon the collective experience and expertise of the aviation community.
