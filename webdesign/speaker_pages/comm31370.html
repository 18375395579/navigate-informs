Title: Workforce Planning Models in Semiconductor Manufacturing
Author: Anirudha Kulkarni, Rochester Institute of Technology, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 81 Lomb Memorial Drive, Rochester, NY, 14623, United States of America, apk2932@rit.edu
Coauthor(s): Shrikant Jarugumilli, Naiping Keng, Scott Grasman
Year: 2013
Abstract: Workforce planning in semiconductor manufacturing facilities can be tricky considering the complex shift patterns and the business rules. In this presentation we discuss the various observations we encountered while implementing such models in real industry settings.
