Title: Continuous and Discrete Time Stochastic Control

Author: Daniel Mitchell, University of Texas, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, United States of America, daniel.mitchell@phd.mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Kumar Muthuraman

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a stochastic process in which a controller has the opportunity to issue control in continuous time at some cost. Additionally the controller can issue control for a different cost at discrete time intervals. We find an algorithm to solve for the optimal control and discuss applications in portfolio management.
