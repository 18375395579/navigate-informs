Title: Generation Investments in Competitive and Uncertain Markets

Author: Jorge Valenzuela, Associate Professor, Auburn University, 211 Dunstan Hall, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States, jvalenz@eng.auburn.edu

Coauthor(s): Pinar Kaymaz, Chan Park

Year: 2007

Abstract: Investments on new capacity of electricity generation involve decisions regarding size and location of new power plants. These decisions are subject to risk due to future conditions of the system. A generation expansion model that explicitly accounts for the stochastic nature of load and fuel costs in power markets is presented. The model is a Cournot competition where decisions are made at the same stage with bilateral contracts.
