Title: Unconstrained Demand ?Back to the Drawing Board?
Author: Montgomery Blair, Asst Vice President, Archstone-Smith, 9200 East Panorama Cir, Englewood, CO, 80112, United States of America, MBlair@archstonesmith.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: A key component to most modern revenue management systems is the forecast of unconstrained demand. The premier RM system in the apartment rental industry, LRO (Lease Rent Options), is no exception. This session will share some ideas, perspectives, testing initiatives, models, and thoughts on this important component from an application perspective. What is unconstrained demand ?really? What new data may become available to better model it and why is it so important?
