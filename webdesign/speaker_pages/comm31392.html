Title: Patterns of Aircraft Operations and Spare Parts Demands

Author: George Kuhn, Senior Research Fellow, LMI, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, United States of America, gkuhn@lmi.org

Year: 2007

Abstract: Research for the U.S. Army has suggested that identifiable regions of the operational spectrum exhibit distinctive patterns of aircraft usage and of associated Class IX maintenance parts demands. Research first reported in 2005 and 2006 is being extended and deepened. The question is whether improved forecasting of both aircraft usage and mixes and magnitudes of parts demands is possible.
