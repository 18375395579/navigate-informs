Title: Connections Between OR/MS and “Analytics?for Undergraduate Business Students

Author: Susan Palocsay, Professor, James Madison University, Computer Info Sys & Mgt Science Dept., Harrisonburg, VA, 22807, United States, palocssw@jmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Rick Mathieu, Ina Markham

Year: 2007

Abstract: The role of “business analytics?continues to evolve and expand as companies seek higher levels of performance and enhanced competitiveness. We identify skill sets needed for business undergraduates to pursue a career path in this field, based on recent industry reports and a sample of posted job positions. Our focus is on potential opportunities to raise the visibility and appeal of OR/MS courses in business school curricula.
