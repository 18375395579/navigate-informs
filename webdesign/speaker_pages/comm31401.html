Title: Panel Discussion: Meet the Program Managers

Author: Shiyu Zhou, Assistant Professor, University of Wisconsin - Madison, 1513 University Ave., Madison, WI, 53711, United States, szhou@engr.wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen Nash, John Ramthun, Debo Aichbhaumik, Wendy Martinez

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this panel discussion, program managers from several funding agencies will present the current research trend and priorities in federal government and private industries. After brief presentations by the panelists, an interactive discussion with audience will follow.
