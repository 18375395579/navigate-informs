Title: Performance Aspects of Collaborative Planning Forecasting and Replenishment (CPFR) Technologies

Author: Craig Hill, Assistant Professor, Georgia State University, Dept. of Managerial Sciences, 35 Broad Street, 10th Floor, Atlanta, GA, 30302, United States, chill4@gsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yusen Xia, Peter Zhang

Year: 2006

Abstract: The CPFR initiative is a relatively new management paradigm that supports supply chain members making joint decisions on their mutual supply chain issues. These issues and decisions include such things as inventory management and product design. This research uses empirical data to focus on the performance ramifications to companies that use this program.
