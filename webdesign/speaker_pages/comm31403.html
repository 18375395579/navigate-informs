Title: Double-sided Utility Copulas
Author: Ali Abbas, Associate Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Ave, Urbana, IL, 61822, United States of America, aliabbas@illinois.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: A double-sided utility copula matches the conditional utility assessments at all boundary values of the domain of the attributes and allows for an additional degree of freedom to vary the trade-off assessments among them. We should how to construct such utility copula functions and discuss methods for their assessment.
