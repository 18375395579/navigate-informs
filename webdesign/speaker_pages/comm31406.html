Title: Identifying Social Media Users Who Refuse to Evacuate in the Context of Forecasted Natural Disasters

Author: Hyun-Woo Kim, PhD Candidate / Graduate Teaching Fellow, Pennsylvania State University, 324 IST Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, hxk263@ist.psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrea Tapia, John Yen

Year: 2013

Abstract: Natural disasters like Superstorm Sandy mobilize people even before they hit certain areas. There are people who refuse to evaluate when they must due to insufficient information on forecasted threats. In this paper, we analyze Twitter data on Sandy and propose an approach to identify those people who are not willing to evacuate based on their postings on Twitter so local emergency management officials can keep their eyes on them when situations get worse.
