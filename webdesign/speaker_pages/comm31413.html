Title: Influence of OR Practices to Drive Corporate Strategic Choices at Procter & Gamble

Author: Glenn Wegryn, Associate Director, Product Supply Analytics, The Procter & Gamble Company, 2 Procter & Gamble Plaza, TN-5-328, Cincinnati, OH, 45202, United States of America, wegryn.gw@pg.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: The speaker will discuss the practical use of O.R. methods, including decision analysis, optimization and simulation, to drive high-value, strategic-level decisions for P&G. Examples will be drawn from supply chain integration in an acquisition, new manufacturing plant investment decisions and brand name decision-making.
