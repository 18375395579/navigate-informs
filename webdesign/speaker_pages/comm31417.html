Title: A Hard Take on a Soft Asset? A Test of an Operational Approach to Human Resource Management

Author: Serguei Netessine, Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fountainbleau, 77305, France, Serguei.Netessine@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Valery Yakubovich

Year: 2012

Abstract: We analyze empirically agent behavior at a virtual call center in which work is allocated according to performance-based hierarchy. We use a controlled experiment to disentangle the effect of this system on agent performance.
