Title: Games and Risks: Some National Security Issues and Comparison of Analytic Frameworks
Author: Elizabeth Pate-Cornell, Stanford University, Palo Alto, CA, United States of America, mep@stanford.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: An overview of studies in the Stanford engineering risk research group on the use of game analysis in risk assessment with applications to national security problems involving counter-terrorism, nuclear non-proliferation startegies and warnings from the intelligence community.
