Title: Impacts of Data Standards on Patient Risk in Healthcare
Author: Paiman Farrokhvar, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering, Fayetteville, AR, United States of America, pfarrokh@uark.edu
Coauthor(s): Nebil Buyurgan, Ron Rardin
Year: 2012
Abstract: Data standards can increase patient safety and decrease risk in terms of improved inventory accuracy, and recall and outdate management procedures. This simulation study investigates different inventory management models as well as recall and outdate management practices for fewer system errors and better accuracy. Different scenarios are compared to suggest the best practices.
