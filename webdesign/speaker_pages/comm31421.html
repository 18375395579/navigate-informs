Title: Mike Rothkopf's Approach to Teaching Analysis of Applications
Author: Dave Shanno, Professor, Rutgers University, RUTCOR, 640 Bartholomew Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, shanno@rutcor.rutgers.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Mike Rothkopf was my colleague at Rutgers for nineteen years. He was heavily involved in teaching how to analyze complex business problems and arrive at practical solutions, both through case studies and through practical experience. His involvement of associated faculty led to more balance between theory and applications in general classroom teaching.
