Title: Models of Behavior and Models for Models for Market Data Analysis

Author: Theodoros Evgeniou, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, Fr, France, theodoros.evgeniou@insead.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Marketing has been a key application area of data mining and machine learning. Meanwhile, researchers in consumer behavior and psychology have been developing theories of how people “actually?make decisions. However, there has been little interaction between these two fields. In this talk we discuss novel methods for analyzing consumer data that are based on principles from these two seemingly unrelated fields.
