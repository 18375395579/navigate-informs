Title: Digitization of Music: An Empirical Study of Dis-intermediation and the Long-tail Phenomenon

Author: Ramnath Chellappa, ram@bus.emory.edu

Coauthor(s): Shivendu Shivendu, Vallabhajosyula Sambamurthy, Benn Konsynski

Year: 2007

Abstract: Digitization of content has led to significant changes in the structure of the music industry. Theoretical research argues that record-labels are being increasingly displaced and consumers?music purchasing behavior has been fundamentally altered. Our work investigates the impact of content digitization through an empirical examination of the role of traditional players in this entertainment industry.
