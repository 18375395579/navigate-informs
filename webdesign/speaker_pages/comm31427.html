Title: Quality Uncertainty And Adverse Selection In Online Sponsored Search Markets

Author: Siva Viswanathan, Assistant Professor, University of Maryland, 4313 Van Munching Hall, RH Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, sviswana@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Vandana Ramachandran, Animesh Animesh

Year: 2006

Abstract: Yahoo! and Google employ different mechanisms to rank bidders?advertisements in their sponsored search listings. This provides an unprecedented opportunity to test earlier predictions relating quality and advertising. Using data from sponsored search auctions we examine the relationship between advertisers?quality and their advertising intensity. We find significant differences in the quality-advertising relationships across product categories as well as across the two market mechanisms.
