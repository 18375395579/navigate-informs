Title: Direct Solution of Systems of Linear Equations

Author: Joseph Elble, University of Illinois, Industrial Engineering, Urbana, IL, United States, elble@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Nick Sahinidis

Year: 2007

Abstract: The direct solution of Ax=b involves four main phases: ordering A for fill-in reduction and structural singularity detection, factorizing A into a product of two triangular matrices, solving for x, and optionally refining x to reduce computational error. All four phases are investigated, and multi-threading is utilized to improve performance.
