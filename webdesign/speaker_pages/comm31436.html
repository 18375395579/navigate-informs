Title: Can We Build Bayesian Networks From Unstructured Text?

Author: Lea Deleris, IBM, Paris, 75015, France, lea.deleris@ie.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Brian White, Lamia Tounsi

Year: 2012

Abstract: Bayesian networks (BNs) are most often build either from experts?opinions or when available using historical data. We explore a less common option: leveraging existing literature. Our research objective is to facilitate building BNs by leveraging Natural Language Processing techniques to extract relevant pieces of information. In particular, we seek to extract and aggregate dependence and independence information (for structure) and probability statements (for parameters).
