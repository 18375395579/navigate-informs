Title: Modeling Projects for Undergraduates

Author: Bob Bosch, Oberlin College, bobb@cs.oberlin.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: For the last 16 years, I've taught OR courses at Oberlin College, a top-tier liberal arts institution. I structure each course so that it culminates with a modeling project. In this talk, I will describe my experiences and present numerous examples. Several stellar projects have led to publications in refereed journals.
