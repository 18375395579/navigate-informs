Title: Operations Research and Non-profit Organizations
Author: Burcu Balcik, Postdoctoral Fellow, Norhwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, United States of America, burcub@u.washington.edu
Coauthor(s): Seyed Iravani, Karen Smilowitz
Year: 2008
Abstract: This talk reviews the unique characteristics of non-profit organizations, with particular reference to public, private, and community-based organizations. We discuss specific examples from the operations research literature that consider equity issues in non-profit organizations. Finally, we present areas of future work.
