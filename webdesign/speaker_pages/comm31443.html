Title: Maintenance Modelling: Tony Christer
Author: Maurice Kirby, Professor, The Management School, Lancaster University, Lancaster, La, LA1 4YX, United Kingdom, m.kirby@lancaster.ac.uk
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper assesses the contribution of Tony Christer as a significant OR researcher in the area of maintenance modelling. A leading figure within the British Operational Research Society, he received international acclaim for his work on the random nature of failures and the stochastical growth of hidden defects.
