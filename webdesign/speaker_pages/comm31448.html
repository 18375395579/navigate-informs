Title: Pointing to Value Priorities in Organizations through Problem Structuring Methods
Author: Leroy White, Reader in Management, University of Bristol, School of Economics, Finance and Managem, 8 Woodland Rd, Bristol, BS8 1TN, United Kingdom, leroy.white@bristol.ac.uk
Year: 2008
Abstract: It is now widely accepted that policy and strategy development requires both an appreciation of values and an ability to involve insights from stakeholders. This paper presents research about values and policy making. Developments in identifying more clearly value priorities of stakeholders will be presented, and alongside problem structuring methods (PSM) they provide the potential for new avenues for PSM research and practice.
