Title: Supporting Multi-disciplinary Team (MDT) Collaboration Through the EMR

Author: Harriet Nembhard, Pennsylvania State University, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, hbn2@engr.psu.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk aims to understand the role of non-clinical team members in a multi-disciplinary team and their utilization of electronic medical records. THe work is interview-based and we will discuss some of our findings.
