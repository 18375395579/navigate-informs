Title: Tools for Decision-making Under Deep Uncertainty
Author: Jaime Menoyo, University of Wisconsin-Madison, Madison, WI, jmmenoyo@wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Vicki Bier
Year: 2008
Abstract: Decision-making under risk and uncertainty is widely studied; however, there is always the opportunity to improve. I seek to describe how certain organizational structures and cultures fare in the face of deep uncertainty, and give an example of an organizational culture that performed well in a situation involving deep uncertainty.
