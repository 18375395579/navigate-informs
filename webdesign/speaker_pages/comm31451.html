Title: Risks in Lean Service

Author: Seong Dae Kim, Assistant Professor, University of Alaska Anchorage, 3211 Providence Drive, University Center Room 155, Anchorage, AK, 99508, United States of America, sdkim2@uaa.alaska.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: The concept of Lean is originated from Toyota Production System (TPS), which is manufacturing industry oriented. The key principles and techniques are now applied to service sector in many different ways. Reducing cost might be easy but reducing cost while keeping or improving quality is a challenge due to the two conflicting goals. This research will identify risks associated with seeking for lean service and recommend how to deal with them.
