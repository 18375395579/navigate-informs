Title: A Mathematical Model for Advanced Access Scheduling in Healthcare Systems
Author: Ramakrishnan Sundaram, Student, Florida International University, EC 3155, 10555 W. Flagler Street, Miami, FL, 33199, United States, rsund001@fiu.edu
Coauthor(s): Ronald Giachetti
Year: 2006
Abstract: This research addresses the problem of poor access to healthcare identified by the Institute of Medicine, as a major problem in US healthcare. To address this problem we present a mathematical model for Advanced Access Scheduling in Healthcare Systems. The decision is how many appointments to leave open versus advanced booking. The model considers two important health outcomes of access to care and continuity of care and is analyzed for single physician and multiple physician offices.
