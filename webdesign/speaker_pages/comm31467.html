Title: Emergency Care vs. Urgent Care

Author: Ozden Engin Cakici, Assistant Professor, American University, Kogod School of Business, 4400 Massachusetts Avenue, Washington, DC, 20016, United States of America, cakici@american.edu

Coauthor(s): Rajiv Dewan, Marshall Freimer

Year: 2013

Abstract: As the number of hospital emergency departments (ED) decline many urgent care centers (UCC) have been opening. We model a strategic relationship between an ED and a UCC with strategic patients. Our model takes into account patient wait times, transportation costs and copays incurred for each facility.
