Title: Inventory Control at Spiegel Grove

Author: Matt Drake, Assistant Professor, Duquesne University, School of Business, Pittsburgh, PA, United States, drake987@duq.edu

Coauthor(s): John Mawhinney

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this presentation we detail a classroom game designed to show students the impact of uncertainty related to transportation lead time and product quality on inventory control strategies in a serial supply chain.
