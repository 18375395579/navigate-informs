Title: Analysis of Prognostic Factors in Patients with Relapsed Ovarian Cancer

Author: Chi-Chang Chang, Chungshan Medical University, 110, Sec. 1, Chien-Kuo N. Rd., Taichung, 40100, Taiwan - ROC, changintw@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Newly diagnosed ovarian cancer is very responsive to first-line therapy, but the majority of patients will have disease relapse. We developed five machine learning approaches were considered to find important prognostic factors to predict the relapsed ovarian cancer. Our findings suggest that three most important prognostic factors (Pathologic Stage, Pathologic T, Cell Type) and to improve surveillance after treatment might lead to earlier detection of relapse.
