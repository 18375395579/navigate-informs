Title: Vertically Differentiated Product Line Design with Shared Capacity
Author: Muge Yayla-Kullu, UNC-Chapel Hill, CB3490 McColl Building, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599, United States of America, muge_yayla@unc.edu
Coauthor(s): Jayashankar Swaminathan, Ali K. Parlakturk
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the product line of a capacity constrained firm. The products differ in their quality and the consumers are heterogeneous in their willingness to pay for quality. We show that accounting for limited capacity can lead to critically different results from that of the existing literature and the capacity plays an important role in determining the optimal product line.
