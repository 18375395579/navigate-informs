Title: Consumers?Willingness to Pay for Recycled Versus New/Conventional

Author: Jonathan Linton, Power Corporation Professor, Universit of Ottawa, 55 Laurier Street, Ottawa, ON, Canada, linton@telfer.uottawa.ca

Coauthor(s): Leila Hamzaoui

Year: 2008

Abstract: An empirical study of the willingness to pay for product that contains recycled or reused product is conducted to obtain a better understanding of the factors that positively and negatively influence consumers willingness to purchase recycled/refurbished/remanufactured product instead of new product.
