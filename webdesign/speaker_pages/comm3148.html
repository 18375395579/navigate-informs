Title: Premium-call Policies for Mutual Insurance

Author: Xianghua Gan, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hung Hom, Hong Kong, China, lgtxgan@polyu.edu.hk

Year: 2007

Abstract: Mutual insurance (MI) is a type of insurance where those protected by the insurance (policyholders) also have certain ``ownership rights in the organization. MI has been the dominant insurance vehicle in marine insurance market for over 150 years. Assuming periodic premium collection, we find the optimal premium-call policy for the mutual insurance organization.
