Title: Strategic Analysis of Dual Channel Supply Chains with Digital Goods
Author: Yinliang(Ricky) Tan, University of Florida, PO Box 117169, Gainesville, Fl, 32611-7169, United States of America, tanyinliang@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Janice Carrillo
Year: 2012
Abstract: The advent of digital goods has made a significant impact on traditional markets for items such as videos and books. We study decision strategies facing retailers and publishers by formulating a dual channel model which includes distribution of both traditional and digital goods. Our results identify circumstances under which it is optimal for the retailer to sell only digital goods in the marketplace.
