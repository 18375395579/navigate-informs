Title: The Role of Decison Professionals in the Analytics Movement

Author: Carl Spetzler, CEO, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, cspetzler@sdg.com

Coauthor(s): Vince Barabba, Robert Bordley, Don Kleinmuntz

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Analytics Movement is hot. The movement marries big data and OR to create insight. In this session a panel of DA leaders will address questions, such as: Where does big data and the consultative role of a decision professionl meet? What is new for the decision professional? Is Business Analytics on top of big data naturally focused on near term and optimization of smaller decisions? How do we marry expert judgment with data based analytics?
