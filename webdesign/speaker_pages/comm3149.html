Title: Social Connectedness and Adoption of Online Music Sites
Author: Genevieve Bassellier, Associate Professor, McGill University, Desautels Faculty of Managemen, 1001 Sherbrooke St. W., Montreal, QC, H3A 1G5, Canada, genevieve.bassellier@mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Zachary Krastel, Jui Ramaprasad
Year: 2013
Abstract: In recent years a great diversity of models for online music sites has emerged. These models differ not only in terms of format, genre, and price of music offered, but also in the extent to which they support social connectedness amongst consumers and with artists. This survey-based study examines users?perceptions of features and their role in influencing adoption of online music sites.
