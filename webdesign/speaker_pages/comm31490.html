Title: BlueReach: Supporting Global Expertise Sharing through Synchronous Chat
Author: Kevin Singley, Research Staff Member, IBM Research, 19 SKYLINE DR, HAWTHORNE, NY, 10532, United States of America, ksingley@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Lei Kuang, Jung-Mu Tang, Jennifer Lai, Keith Grueneberg
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a case study of BlueReach, an expertise sharing application that uses synchronous chat to connect question askers with experts. BlueReach provides a browsable directory of expertise categories to allow an information seeker to locate the right kind of expert. BlueReach has been deployed globally and is currently being used in 23 different countries. We discuss how the solution evolved over time, and the generally high level of acceptance from users and volunteer experts.
