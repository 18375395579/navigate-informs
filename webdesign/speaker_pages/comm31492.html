Title: Centralized vs. Decentralized Ordering and Inventory Decisions: Designing a Field Experiment in Zambia
Author: Prashant Yadav, Professor of Supply Chain Management, MIT-Zaragoza International Logistics Program, Gomez Laguna 25, Zaragoza, AR, 50009, Spain, pyadav@zlc.edu.es
Year: 2008
Abstract: In multi-echelon supply chains for drug distribution, changing the loci of decision making around order management and inventory planning has significant system impact. We share experiences from the design of a randmomized experiment to test some key hypothesis.
