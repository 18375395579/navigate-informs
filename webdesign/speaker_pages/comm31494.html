Title: Relationship between Appointments of Sustainability Officers and Performance
Author: Manpreet Hora, Assistant Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, College of Management, 800 West Peachtree St. NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, Manpreet.Hora@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Ravi Subramanian
Year: 2012
Abstract: In recent years, firms have been creating and staffing senior-level environmental and sustainability positions. We investigate the association between announcements pertaining to appointments of senior-level environmental or sustainability officers and firm performance.
