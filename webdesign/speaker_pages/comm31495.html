Title: Incorporating Implementation Complexity in Periodic Vehicle Routing Problems
Author: Karen Smilowitz, Assistant Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, M233, EV3119, Evanston, IL, 60208, ksmilowitz@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Peter Francis, Michal Tzur
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this talk, we discuss methods to quantify implemenation complexity for periodic vehicle routing problems and show how these metrics can be used in routing decisions.
