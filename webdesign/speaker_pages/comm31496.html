Title: Multi-source Information Fusion for Residential Real Estate Ranking
Author: Keli Xiao, Stony Brook University, Stony Brook, NY, United States of America, keli.xiao@gmail.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Given the risk factors and volatility inherent in the real estate business, it is critical to provide rating capabilities for residential real estate. In this paper, we provide a residential real estate ranking system based on multi-source information fusion. Specially, in addition to traditional features, we aim at extracting the features from urban geography data, such as Point of Interests, as well as the features from human mobility patterns.
