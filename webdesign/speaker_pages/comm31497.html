Title: Information Systems and Sustained Competitive Advantage: A Resource-based Analysis
Author: Yan Chen, The School of Management, Xian Jiaotong University, PO Box 1647, The School of Management, Xian Jiaotong University, Xian, 710049, China, chenyan02@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Bjorn Johansson, Jun Tian
Year: 2008
Abstract: This paper discusses the relationships between information systems and sustained competitive advantages from the resource-based perspective. According to the resource-based analysis, we conclude that Information Systems Flexibility, IT-Business Relationship, and IT-Business Alignment Process are three major sources of sustained competitive advantages.
