Title: Service Science Editor's Report: Manuscript Statistics, Readership, and Strategic Directions
Author: Paul Maglio, Professor, University of California, Merced, 5200 N. Lake Road, Merced, CA, 95343, United States of America, pmaglio@ucmerced.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Service Science Editor-In-Chief Dr. Paul Maglio will review the state and direction of the newest INFORMS journal, Service Science. This session will summarize 2012 reviewing statistics for submitted manuscripts, existing readership, financial condition, and other data about the journal. The session will introduce the 2013-2014 Editorial Review Board (ERB). Dr. Maglio will present his vision for the strategic direction of Service Science, followed by a question-and-answer opportunity.
