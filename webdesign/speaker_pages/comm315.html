Title: A Practical Method for Overbooking and Fare-class Allocation with Censored Demand Data

Author: Itir Karaesmen, University of Maryland, Robert H Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, United States of America, ikaraes@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingjie Lan, Michael Ball

Year: 2008

Abstract: Extending our work on competitive analysis of online algorithms in revenue management, we show how fare-class allocation decisions can be combined with overbooking when an airline has good no-show information but has censored demand data.
