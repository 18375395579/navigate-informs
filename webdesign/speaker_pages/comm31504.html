Title: DARMA: Interpreting the Results of a Regression

Author: Evan Brott, Scientist, PROS Revenue Management, 3100 Main Street #900, Houston, TX, 77002, United States, ebrott@prosrm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Driver Apportionment for Regression Model Analytics (DARMA), a novel method for visualizing and analyzing the results of a regression, is presented. Each covariate is represented as causing a specific deviation from a filtered response; shifts in this response over time can then be attributed to sources other than the analyzed covariates. Examples and applications using real datasets are provided.
