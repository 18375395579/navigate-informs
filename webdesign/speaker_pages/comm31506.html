Title: An Internet Experiment on Bargaining in Networks
Author: Yashodhan Kanoria, PhD Candidate, Stanford University, 350 Serra Mall, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, ykanoria@stanford.edu
Coauthor(s): Cindy Chang, Andrea Montanari, Madison White
Year: 2012
Abstract: Exchange networks model the behavior of a set of players who need to reach pairwise agreements for mutual benefit, as in the labor market, the housing market and the 'market' for social relationships. We describe internet-based experiments on bargaining in networks, that are the largest such experiments to date. Our results include some of the first insights into the dynamics of bargaining.
