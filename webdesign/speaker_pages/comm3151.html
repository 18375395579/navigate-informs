Title: Increasing Marketing Engagement using Graph-augmented A/B Testing at MailChimp.com
Author: John Foreman, Data Scientist, MailChimp.com, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, john.foreman@mailchimp.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: MailChimp.com sends 5 billion email marketing newsletters per month; the site’s users leverage A/B testing to optimize their content. While a small business might not have the historical data to facilitate any other approach, most email addresses in MailChimp's system are subscribed to multiple newsletters. This subscription data yields an email address interest graph that can inform A/B testing. This talk will explore the initial results of this network-informed A/B testing concept.
