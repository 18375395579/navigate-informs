Title: Recruiter Selection Model and Implementation within the United States Army

Author: John Halstead, Assistant Professor, U.S. Military Academy, Department of Systems Engineering, West Point, NY, 10996, United States, john.halstead@usma.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: MG Bostick, Commanding General of the U. S. Army Recruiting Command, began a new method of selecting individuals from within the Army for recruiting duty. Previously, the Army assigned successful Non-Commissioned Officers, regardless of inherent sales and marketing skills, into the recruiting force. The new selection model applies statistical learning using NLSI and biographical data to determine the best Non-Commissioned Officers for recruiting duty.
