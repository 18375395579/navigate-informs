Title: Developing a Graduate-level Communication Curriculum for STEM Communities
Author: Donna Llewellyn, Director, Ctr for Enhancement of Teaching & Learning,GA Tech, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, donna.llewellyn@cetl.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Karen Head
Year: 2008
Abstract: Effective and appropriate written, oral, and electronic communication is especially critical for graduate students in the STEM fields. For our presentation we will describe how our graduate-level communication curriculum has been and continues to be developed to help our students better understand the conventions of academic and professional discourse.
