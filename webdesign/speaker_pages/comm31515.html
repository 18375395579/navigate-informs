Title: Intelligent-IEDs Application and Risk Acceptability Optimization in a Successful Conduct of War
Author: Anna Doro-on, Sr. Consultant/Project Engineer, Water, Tunnel & Infrastructure Concepts, Inc., 120 S. Euclid Avenue, Pasadena, CA, 91101, United States of America, anna@annaforhomelandsecurity.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: The Improvised Explosive Devices (IEDs) were used by terrorists against United States and Allies' military forces and responsible of deaths in Iraq and Afghanistan. This paper presents the application of intelligent-IEDs for the effectiveness of military operations and defense against the enemy, to decrease military casualties, protect infrastructures, minimize risk of terrorism, and maximize risk acceptability.
