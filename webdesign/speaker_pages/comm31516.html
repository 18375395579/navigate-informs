Title: Services Supply Chain Modeling

Author: Sean McGarraghy, College Lecturer, UCD School of Business, Management Information Systems, Quinn School of Business, Belfield, Dublin, Du, 4, Ireland, sean.mcgarraghy@ucd.ie

Coauthor(s): Terry Harrison

Year: 2007

Abstract: A fundamental part of any services supply chain implementation is having a flexible and extensive database of human resource attributes, and methods for acting on them. We describe a data modeling platform for services supply chains. We also map out key data needs to support stochastic optimization of services supply chains, and develop an approach to create the necessary probability distributions from existing data. We describe the implementation of this on an example stochastic attribute.
