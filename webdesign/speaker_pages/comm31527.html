Title: To Offshore or Not to Offshore? An Analysis of Risk and Benefits
Author: Letian Wang, Ph.D Student, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke Street West, Montreal, Quebec, H3A 1G5, Canada, lwang87@po-box.mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Shanling Li
Year: 2007
Abstract: This research considers a global capacity planning problem. A manufacturer has an option to build plant(s) in either domestic or foreign countries to meet global demand. With the uncertainty in foreign exchange rate or demand, we study the optimal capacity expansion and operational solutions by risk-neutral and risk-averse decision makers.
