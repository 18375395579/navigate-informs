Title: Linking Modeling & Simulation, Decision Analysis, and the Technology of Drug Formulation

Author: Lee Hodge, Pharsight Corporation, 5625 Dillard Dr., Suite 205, Cary, NC, 27519, United States of America, fhodge@pharsight.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Most pharmaceutical companies must turn chemical compounds into drugs that deliver that compound in the appropriate amount and at the right site, time, and duration. However, it is often unclear which formulation “knob settings?are optimal. This presentation will provide an example of linking a PD/PD modeling analysis to a formulation decision. This enabled a client to see if altering a drug’s formulation could improve net patient benefit and if it was worth further investment.
