Title: Power Utility Function for Entrepreneurs

Author: Manar El-Chammas, Stanford University, PO Box 19278, Stanford, CA, 94309, United States of America, manar@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Ibrahim Almojel, Jim Matheson

Year: 2008

Abstract: We discuss the power utility function in the context of entrepreneurial decision making. In this context we have prospects with large returns and very small probabilities, and the currency used tends to be equity instead of money. The power utility naturally lends itself to both. We define the certain equivalent multiplier (CEM) as the certain return equivalent to the deal. We solve for the value of information and control in terms of the CEM, and we give approximations based on few moments.
