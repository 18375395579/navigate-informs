Title: Ranking and Selection in a High-performance Computing Environment
Author: Eric Ni, Cornell University, 206 Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, United States of America, eric.ni.cao@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Shane Henderson, Susan Hunter
Year: 2013
Abstract: We explore ranking and selection methods implemented in a parallel computing setting. Our focus is on screening-based methods in a high-performance computing environment.
