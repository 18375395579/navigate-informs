Title: Frank’s M. Bass' Key Contributions to Choice Modeling and Stochastic Behavior
Author: Peter Popkowski-Leszcyc, Associate Professor of Marketing, University of Alberta, 4-20 F School of Business, Edmonton, AB, T6G 2R6, Canada, ppopkows@ualberta.ca
Year: 2007
Abstract: This talk will provide a brief overview of some of Frank M. Bass’s major achievements, and will focus on his key contributions in the area of choice modeling and stochastic behavior. An Introduction to this session will be provided by key members of the INFORMS and Marketing community.
