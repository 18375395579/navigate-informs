Title: Tutorial: Unifying Facility Restricted Location and Layout Theory

Author: Rakesh Nagi, Professor, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, 438 Bell Hall, University at Buffalo (SUNY), Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States, nagi@buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Rajan Batta

Year: 2006

Abstract: This tutorial aims at synthesizing a common theory base in the context of facilities location and facilities layout. The dichotomy has arisen due to the point or area treatment of the new facility. Participants will gain an appreciation of the common theory for location and layout problems, and how these results can help advance each field or bring them closer. It is suitable for students and researchers alike. We will draw upon research results of two NSF awards and associated papers.
