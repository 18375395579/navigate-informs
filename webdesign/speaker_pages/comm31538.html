Title: Innovation Inside: An Analysis of Internal Idea Competitions in Two Large IT Companies
Author: Sebastian Fixson, Babson College, Tomasso 226, Babson Park, MA, 02457, United States of America, sfixson@babson.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: To generate innovations, idea competitions have grown in number and sophistication in recent years. In this paper, we study internal idea competitions in two large companies. We compare and contrast the choices the two companies have made regarding a set of decision variables in the design of their idea competitions, and how those choices impacted the outcome.
