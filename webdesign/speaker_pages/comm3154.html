Title: RM - Business Function that owns Big Data
Author: Dev Koushik, Director, Revenue Optimization, InterContinental Hotels Group, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, dev.koushik@ihg.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Big Data as it is being portrayed in the media right now has existed in the world of Revenue Management right from the days of its inception. The business function of Revenue Management evolved from using data as a lever to make strategic pricing and inventory decisions in the travel and transportation industry. This presentation will make a case for why Revenue Management should lead the organization in this evolution through its expertise of managing business using data and science.
