Title: Examples of Operational Research in World Bank Health, Nutrition and Population Programs
Author: Jed Friedman, Development Research Group, The World Bank, 1818 H Street NW, Washington DC, DC, 20433, United States of America, jfriedman@worldbank.org
Coauthor(s): Edit Velenyi
Year: 2008
Abstract: The inclusion of operational research in the design of World Bank sponsored health programs is growing increasingly common. This talk will review the motivation, goals, and common designs of such research as well as present salient examples from on-going and planned work.
