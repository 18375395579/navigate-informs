Title: Valid Inequalities for Some Generalizations of the Continuous Mixing Set
Author: Manish Bansal, PhD Student, Texas A&M University, Emerging Technologies Building,, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77840, United States of America, bans1571@neo.tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Kiavash Kianfar
Year: 2012
Abstract: We present our progress in studying some interesting generalizations of the continuous mixing set previously studied by Van Vyve (2005). We present new valid inequalities for these sets and discuss their facet-defining properties.
