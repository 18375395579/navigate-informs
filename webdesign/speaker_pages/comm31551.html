Title: Catalyze Supports UK Ministry of Defence Training Rationalisation
Author: Carmen Carmona, Consultant, Catalyze, 1 The Old Diary, Bunstead Barns, Hursley, SO21 2LL, United Kingdom, CarmenCarmona@catalyze.co.uk
Coauthor(s): Patrick Warburton, Rex Mazonowicz
Year: 2012
Abstract: Catalyze designed and implement a two level stakeholder socio-technical process aiming to consistently assess 24 possible options, using a mix of financial, socio and technical criteria. The process allowed the wider stakeholders and MOD scrutiny community to be convinced of the integrity of the decision consequences and engage MOD Experts from all three armed services. (Navy, Royal Air Force and Army)
