Title: Practical Guide for Robust Linear Optimization

Author: Dick den Hertog, Professor, Tilburg University, Tilburg, Netherlands, D.denHertog@uvt.nl

Year: 2013

Abstract: It is remarkable that real-life applications of Robust Optimization is still lagging behind; there is much more potential for real-life applications than has been exploited hitherto. We pinpoint several issues that are important when applying Robust Optimization and that are often not well understood and even incorrectly applied by practitioners. We also provide a general recipe for Robust Linear Optimization that may be helpful for successful applications.
