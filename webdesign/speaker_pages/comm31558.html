Title: Mining Information from Deconstructed Social Networks
Author: Guido Zarrella, MITRE Corporation, 202 Burlington Rd, Bedford, MA, 01730, United States of America, jzarrella@mitre.org
Year: 2012
Abstract: Social media offers unprecedented insight into the attitudes of large populations, but the potential for misuse of data mining algorithms is high. We demonstrate that a deconstructed view of social networks can help to provide additional context, enabling a better understanding of patterns in the data.
