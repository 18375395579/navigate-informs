Title: Simulation of City Evolution for the Planning of Facility Networks

Author: Wenjun Yin, Dr., IBM China Research Lab, Zuanshi Build, Zhongguancun Software par, Beijing, BJ, 100084, China, yinwenj@cn.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Bin Zhang, Ming Xie, Jin Yan Shao, Jin Dong, Li Xia

Year: 2008

Abstract: The amounts and locations of facilities (e.g., bank branches, retail stores) are heavily dependent on the geographical and economic environments of the whole city. This paper uses a simulation-based technique to predict the evolution of city, concerning the main factors such as the distribution of residents and marketplace. The simulation results of the city evolution have potential to be beneficial to instruct the amount and location planning of facility networks.
