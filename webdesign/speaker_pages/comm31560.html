Title: Dynamic Coordinated Search and Rescue

Author: David Fajardo, davidf@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): S. Travis Waller, Avinash Unnikrishnan

Year: 2007

Abstract: We formulate an instance of the Dynamic Search and Rescue Problem in which vehicles have the ability to communicate information. We provide numerical results, as well as heuristic solution methods.
