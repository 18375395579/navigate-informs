Title: Unleashing The Power of Expert Knowledge for IT Services Sales with Graph Search
Author: Mu Qiao, Research Staff Member, IBM Almaden Research Center, 650 Harry Road, San Jose, CA, 95120, United States of America, mqiao@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Rama Akkiraju, Taiga Nakamura, Debdoot Mukherjee, Daniel Greenia, Shun Jiang
Year: 2013
Abstract: During the sales phase of IT services, the ability to rapidly identify experts allows deal teams to respond efficiently to a client's request for proposal. Interacting with experts can help improve knowledge sharing, which in turn can increase competitive advantage in bidding. We present a novel expert finding approach in complex IT service solution design domain, which extends the value of social graph by providing an efficient way to search through a large amount of complex and connected data.
