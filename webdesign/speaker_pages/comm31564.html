Title: Human Computer Interaction Frameworks: Challenges Associated with Designing myHealthImpact.org for HIV Dissemination
Author: Christina Harrington, Graduate Student, NC State U, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, cnharrin@ncsu.edu
Coauthor(s): Fay Cobb Payton
Year: 2012
Abstract: Current research trends highlight social implications as a research framework in user interface design. However, there is a need for a more human-centered design method to address consumer needs of certain social populations. Informed by focus-group data collected to develop a HIV/AIDS awareness website, this research aims to discuss current HCI frameworks in relevance to the needs of an identified user population.
