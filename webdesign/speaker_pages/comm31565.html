Title: Service Analytics Workbench

Author: Shun Jiang, R&D Engineer, IBM CRL, Diamond Building, ZGC Software Park 19, Dong Beiwang road, ShangDi, Beijing, China, jshun@cn.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Chunhua Tian, Rongzeng Cao, Wei Ding, Juhnyoung Lee

Year: 2008

Abstract: To provide consistent results, expedite the analysis process, and save efforts in assessing cost and benefit of IT services projects, it is important to leverage the commonality of individual projects. We present a tool, Service Analytics Workbench, which utilizes a questionnaire-based approach to project scoping and capturing the heterogeneity of various projects, and a rule-based approach to generating cost estimations. Additionally it provides other derivative analyses abilities.
