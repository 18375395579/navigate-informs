Title: Phase Transitions in Loss Networks
Author: Kavita Ramanan, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, United States, kramanan@math.cmu.edu
Coauthor(s): Brad Luen, Ilze Ziedins
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider Markov random field models of large loss networks, and characterise the behavior of their steady state distribution. In particular, we identify parameter values when so-called phase transitions occur, and describe their implications for performance of the network. This talk is based on various joint works with Ilze Ziedins and Brad Luen.
