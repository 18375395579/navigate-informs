Title: Architecture for Social Analytics in Smarter Commerce
Author: Bing Shao, Staff Researcher, IBM Research China, Haidian District, Beijing, P.R.C., Beijing, China, shaobing@cn.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Tao Qin, Hao Ji, Miao He, Changrui Ren, Jinfeng Li
Year: 2013
Abstract: The prosperity of social media is transforming the way of business and customer interaction. Social business insight elevates diagnosis and predictive capability on brand, product or service for commerce companies. We develop a reference architecture that focuses on the process and system level for the description of social analytics. This architecture can be used for integration with new and traditional business process workflows, aligned with the implementation of company social strategy.
