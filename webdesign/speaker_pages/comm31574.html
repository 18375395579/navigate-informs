Title: Panel on Humanitarian Supply Chain Management
Author: Luk N. Van Wassenhove, Professor, INSEAD, INSEAD Technology Management, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau Cedex, ID, 77305, France, luk.van-wassenhove@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Jo van Nunen, Laura Kopczak, Jarrod Goentzel, Eric Johnson, Hau L. Lee
Year: 2006
Abstract: This panel will discuss research priorities in humanitarian supply chain management based on interviews with humanitarian logisticians and our own work on humanitarian disaster preparedness and response.
