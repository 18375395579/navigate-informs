Title: Risk-Averse Bidding on Name-Your-Own-Price Auctions

Author: Christian Terwiesch, Associate Professor, University of Pennsylvania, 548 JMHH, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, terwiesch@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Il-Horn Hann, Ali Abbas

Year: 2006

Abstract: We discuss optimal repeat bidding strategies on Name-Your- Own-Price (NYOP) auctions, and the effects of bidders' attitude towards risk on the optimal bidding sequence. Using data from an internet site that allowed bidders to rebid indefinitely, we estimate bidders?risk aversion, change in risk aversion, and transaction costs.
