Title: Life Cycle Oriented Design of Capital Goods: The State of the Art in Theory and Practice

Author: Rob Basten, PhD Candidate, Twente University of Technology, PO Box 217, Enschede, 7500AE, Netherlands, R.Basten@utwente.nl

Coauthor(s): Marco Schutten, Matthieu van der Heijden

Year: 2006

Abstract: Increasingly, customers ask companies to take care of the complete life cycle of a product. This means that companies need techniques to optimize their designs for life cycle cost. A lot of design-for-X paradigms have surfaced for (parts of) the life cycle. We conducted a literature study and thorough case studies with four companies to see which (quantitative) techniques are available and how they are used in practice. We identified weaknesses in the techniques and new research opportunities.
