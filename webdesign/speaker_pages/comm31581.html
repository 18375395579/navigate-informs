Title: Behavior-driven Promotion Campaign Analytics
Author: Wei Sun, Research Staff Member, IBM Watson, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, Yorktown, 10598, United States of America, sunw@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Anshul Sheopuri, Pavankumar Murali
Year: 2013
Abstract: Promotion campaigns can be costly, and their performance must be understood and executed well to accomplish business goals. However, demand is highly dynamic, noisy and interrelated among a wide range of products. Given many business constraints (e.g., a finite campaign budget) and numerous marketing options, we propose a system to design more focused and behavior-driven campaigns by incorporating new sources of data such as such as customer sentiment, browsing history, social media etc.
