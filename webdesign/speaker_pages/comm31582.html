Title: Smart Grid and Operation of the Power System
Author: Guillermo Bautista, Manager, Market Validation and Quality, California ISO, 250 Outcropping Way, Folsom, CA, 95630, United States of America, bautista.guillermo@gmail.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: With emerging technologies, the traditional paradigm of operating and controlling the power system is evolving towards identifying and reacting to grid events much faster and accurately. In this presentations, several aspect of smart grid will be introduced, including tools to manage more efficiently the power grid, two-way communications, consumer-response curtailments such as demand response and smarter forecasting.
