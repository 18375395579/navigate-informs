Title: Locomotive and Freight Car Planning: Predicting the Future
Author: Evan Bell, CSXT, 500 Water Street, Jacksonville, FL, 32202, United States, Evan_Bell@CSX.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: Locomotive and freight car planning is a process that has three key drivers. -Supply -Demand -Utilization Each one of these drivers has many components that need to be forecasted into the future. This presentation will address the issues and possible solutions of the driver components and how the changing world around us affects our view going forward.
