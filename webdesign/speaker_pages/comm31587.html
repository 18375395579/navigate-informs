Title: Business Analytics, Information Technology, and Decision Analysis

Author: Don Kleinmuntz, Exec VP & Cofounder, Strata Decision Technology, 200 E Randolph St 49th Floor, Chicago, IL, 60601, United States of America, dnk@strata-decision.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Business Analytics is a huge wave that has overtaken a number of industries and is guiding the development of IT infrastructure in many others. There is a genuine opportunity to embed decision analysis tools and processes in that infrastructure and create tremendous impact.
