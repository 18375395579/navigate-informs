Title: A Survey of Quantum Games

Author: Julie Zhang, PhD Student, University of Florida, PO Box 117169, Gainesville, FL, 32611-715, United States of America, juheng@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Hong Guo, Gary Koehler

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper presents an overview and survey of a new type of game theoretic setting based on ideas emanating from quantum computing. (We provide a brief overview of quantum computing in the Appendix. Initial results suggest this view brings more flexibility and possibilities into decisions involving game theoretic considerations. Applications cover a broad spectrum of classical games as well as games in economics, finance and other areas.
