Title: Game Theory Models for Electricity Market Simulation

Author: Ettore F. Bompard, Associate Professor, POLITECNICO DI TORINO - Dipartimento di Ingegneria Elettrica, C.so Duca degli Abruzzi, 24, Torino, TO, Italy, ettore.bompard@polito.it

Coauthor(s): Yuchao Ma, Graziano Abrate

Year: 2007

Abstract: In oligopoly electricity markets, the network structure poses strict physical and operational constraints which provide very specific occasions of market power. Based on the individual producer’s surplus maximization problem nested with the pool typed market clearing, this paper provides a comparative analysis of the application of game theory models to network constrained electricity markets. The network impacts on market performances are addressed with reference to the IEEE30 bus test system.
