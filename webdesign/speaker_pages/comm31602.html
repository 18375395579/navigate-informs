Title: An Evaluation of Collaborative Research in College of Engineering: Social Network Approach

Author: Oguz Cimenler, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, ocimenle@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Kingsley A. Reeves

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research investigates how the structural aspects of the communication network constructed based on information from researchers?conversation (or advice) exchanges differ from the structural aspects of the network constructed based on information from researchers?collaborative output (e.g. co-authored publications, joint grant proposal, and joint patent applications). Results of this study have the potential to provide information for policy formulations in collaborative research.
