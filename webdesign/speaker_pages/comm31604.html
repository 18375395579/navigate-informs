Title: Modeling the Spread of Measles and Investigating the Effectiveness of Vaccination Strategies
Author: Pinar Keskinocak, Associate Professor, H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, pinar@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Daniel Park, Farminder Anand, Julie Swann, Tuba Yilmaz
Year: 2008
Abstract: We develop an agent-based simulation to model the spread of measles both geographically and over time. We then explore the effectiveness of different vaccination strategies given limited resources.
