Title: Improving a Recommender System for Child Adoptions

Author: Vincent Slaugh, Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business, Pittsburgh, PA, 15208, United States of America, vslaugh@cmu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We utilize an operations research framework to improve the effectiveness of a recommender system for children in state custody and adoptive families. Through a spreadsheet tool and managerial insights that have led to process redesigns, we have addressed key challenges related to the quality of the system’s information, the value of the recommendation algorithm, and the interaction among system participants.
