Title: A Big Data Approach to the Newsvendor Problem
Author: Gah-Yi Vahn, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, gvahn@london.edu
Coauthor(s): Cynthia Rudin
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present how one can incorporate big data to the newsvendor problem. We also present preliminary theoretical and empirical findings.
