Title: Productivity Effects of Information Diffusion in Email Networks

Author: Sinan Aral, Assistant Professor, NYU / MIT, 44 West 4th Street Room 8-81, New York, NY, 10012, United States, sinana@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Erik Brynyjolfsson, Marshall Van Alstyne

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine the diffusion of different types of information through email networks and the effects of information diffusion on productivity. We ask: What predicts the likelihood of becoming aware of strategic information sooner? Do different types of information diffuse differently? Does better access to information predict productivity? We find that different types of information exhibit different diffusion patterns and that access to information strongly predicts productivity.
