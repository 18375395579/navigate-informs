Title: Product Development Teaching in North America: A Status Report

Author: Sebastian Fixson, Babson College, Tomasso Hall 226, Babson Park, MA, 02457, United States of America, sfixson@babson.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Over the past 15 years many universities have introduced multi-disciplinary courses in product development (PD).?In this presentation, we report on the results of a survey of these courses across business, engineering, and design schools.?We discuss course features such as disciplinary course set-up, student and faculty involvement, course size, course age and evolution, and industry and practitioner participation.?We identify successful course designs, practices, and techniques.
