Title: Efficient Funding: Auditing in the Nonprofit Sector

Author: Feryal Erhun, Assistant Professor, Stanford University, MS&E, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, ferhun@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Natalie Privett

Year: 2007

Abstract: We take a contracting perspective to model the process of funders (foundations or the government) awarding grants and allocating funds between nonprofit organizations. We show that the effectiveness of the funding process and the nonprofit sector overall can be significantly improved through the use of efficient contracts and auditing. In fact, numerical analysis shows that the funder can potentially achieve nearly 35% improvement through auditing contracts.
