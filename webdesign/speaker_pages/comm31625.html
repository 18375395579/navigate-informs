Title: And Two More ...
Author: Lou Hafer, Simon Fraser University, Computing Science, 8888 University Drive, Burnaby, BC, V5A 1S6, Canada, lou@cs.sfu.ca
Year: 2007
Abstract: Previous talks in this session have discussed individual MIP solvers (BLIS, SYMPHONY, SCIP). This talk will consider two more solvers, cbc (from COIN-OR) and glpk (from the GNU Linear Programming Kit). The emphasis will be on usability (installation, interfaces, user and developer documentation, performance), with a quick tour of the underlying algorithms.
