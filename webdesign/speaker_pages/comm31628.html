Title: Innovation in the Delivery of Services

Author: Kamalini Ramdas, Deloitte Chaired Professor in Innovation & Entrepreneurship, London Business School, A215 Sussex Place, Regent's Park, London, United Kingdom, kramdas@london.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: I will present an overview of recent and ongoing research projects with multiple coauthors, focusing on innovation in the delivery of services. We consider major innovations in service delivery that simultaneously improve quality and reduce cost. We examine how these innovations alter four key areas of delivery: the service interaction, the service boundary, service tasks and the service location. I will present examples from healthcare and other service industries based on this work.
