Title: Value of Storage for Managing Wind Volatility: Stochastic Unit Commitment-based Models and Analysis
Author: Jeremy Hargreaves, Johns Hopkins University, DoGEE, Baltimore, MD, United States of America, jjh@jhu.edu
Coauthor(s): Sebastiaan Hers, Joost Van Stralen, Benjamin F. Hobbs, Christos Kolokathis
Year: 2008
Abstract: SDP and Lagrangian Relaxation models are developed for unit commitment and dispatch under random wind production and load. We compare their conclusions concerning the impact of increased wind penetration upon balancing energy costs and operating reserve requirements in the Netherlands, and contrast those results to those of a deterministic analysis.
