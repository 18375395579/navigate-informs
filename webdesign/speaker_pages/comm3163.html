Title: MARA Effectiveness Research: Decision Analyses in the Context of Strategy Development
Author: Martin Schilling, PhD Candidate, London School of Economics, Department of Operational Research, Houghton Street, London, NW3 1AH, United Kingdom, m.schilling@lse.ac.uk
Coauthor(s): Nadine Oeser
Year: 2006
Abstract: The evaluation of the effectiveness of socio-technical decision analyses in strategy development contexts is the research focus of MARA 2006. The across-project research results of MARA 2006 are the topic of this presentation.
