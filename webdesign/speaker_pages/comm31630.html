Title: Valuing Thermal Generation Units with Overfire Options and Maintenance Constraints

Author: Wei Zhu, First Choice Power, Dallas, TX, Wei.Zhu@firstchoicepower.com

Coauthor(s): Chung-Li Tseng

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper concerns overfire option of a gas turbine that allows the unit to burn more-than-usual amount of fuel to generate power exceeding its normal rated capacity. Exercising the overfire option may help to capitalize on more profits in the marketplaces than otherwise. This option, however, runs with a risk of potentially damaging or overstressing the turbine, which can be corrected by maintenances that are performed in longer times or higher frequency. The tradeoff of using this option is analyzed. The optimal strategy of exercising this option subject to price uncertainties is also studied.
