Title: Where Do We Want to Go in Practice?
Author: Irv Lustig, ILOG Incorporated, 25 Sylvan Way, Short Hills, NJ, 07078, irv@ilog.com
Coauthor(s): Scott Ellis
Year: 2006
Abstract: The evolving global economy, developments in technology, and changing managerial practices provide the OR/MS profession with new opportunities and challenges. OR/MS must change along with the world. In this session, the Roundtable examines pertinent developments challenges, and opportunities in the world of practice, and suggests appropriate responses.
