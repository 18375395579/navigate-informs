Title: Success and Failure of Continuous Process Improvement Teams in the Same Organization
Author: Ken Schultz, Associate Professor, Air Force Institute of Technology, 2950 Hobson Way, WPAFB, 45433, United States of America, Kenneth.Schultz@afit.edu
Coauthor(s): Robert Rabon, Alan Johnson
Year: 2013
Abstract: We look at some less tangible facets of organizational culture that lead to successful Continuous Process Improvement. We used structured interviews and supervisor rankings of success to evaluate a conceptual model proposed by Lander and Liker (2007).
