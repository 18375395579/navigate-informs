Title: Multiple Criteria Decision Analysis: Two Business Applications

Author: Gina Beim, Senior Consulting Engineer - Marketing, Pile Dynamics, Inc., 4535 Renaissance Parkway, Cleveland, OH, 44128, United States, gina@pile.com

Coauthor(s): Moren Levesque

Year: 2006

Abstract: Multiple Criteria Decision Analysis (MCDA) is an Operations Research methodology that help people and organizations make better decisions. It is most valuable in instances where tradeoffs, subjectivity, and accountability exist. We test the feasibility of using MCDA to aid in two business situations with no previous applications of this methodology: selection of a country where to launch a new venture, and selection of business plans to receive funding from a venture capital fund.
