Title: Past, Present and Future of the Fire Planning Analysis System

Author: Darek Nalle, US Forest Service, Washington, DC, United States of America, djnalle@fs.fed.us

Coauthor(s): Matthew Thompson

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Fire Planning Analysis system was created by the Federal government to inform the allocation and distribution of pre-suppression resources. The system is currently undergoing redesign. New research enhancements will be overviewed, with particular focus on optimization capabilities.
