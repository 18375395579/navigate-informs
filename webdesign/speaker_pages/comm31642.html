Title: Smart Service ?State of the Art (SOTA)

Author: Andreas Aldrian, System Design Software & Application Engineering, AVL LIST GMBH, Graz, Austria, andreas.aldrian@avl.com

Coauthor(s): Christian Rackl, Jens Poggenburg

Year: 2012

Abstract: Service orientation and quality is getting increasingly important for the competiveness of enterprises. The development of smart services was identified as a key factor of success. Within this context this paper shows an approach in identifying coherence and common structures between different Smart Services and to develop a consistent system for creating new ones. The aim of this work is to establish a simple method for developing new Smart Services.
