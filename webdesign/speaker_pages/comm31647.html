Title: Conceptual Model of Performance for Contract Manufacturers

Author: Craig Hill, Assistant Professor, Georgia State University, P.O. Box 4014, Atlanta, GA, 30302-4014, United States, chill4@gsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Burgess

Year: 2007

Abstract: This research presents a model for performance from a contract manufacturers?point of view. Included in this research are descriptions of types of contract manufacturer’s relationships within the supply chain and insights into possible managerial opportunities for performance enhancement.
