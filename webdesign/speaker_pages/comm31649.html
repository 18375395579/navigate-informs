Title: Innovative Applications in Analytics Award

Author: Pooja Dewan, General Director, Burlington Northern Santa Fe Corporation, 2400 Western Center, Fort Worth, TX, 76092, United States of America, Pooja.Dewan@bnsf.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The purpose of the third annual Innovative Applications in Analytics Award is to recognize creative and unique developments, applications or combinations of analytical techniques used in practice. The prize promotes the awareness of the value of analytics techniques in unusual applications, or in creative combination to provide unique insights and/or business value. These three sessions in the INFORMS annual meeting will be focused on having the 9 Semi-finalists of the award present their work to a panel of judges. At the end of the sessions, the judges will deliberate and determine the 3 Finalists that will present in the Spring INFORMS of 2014.
