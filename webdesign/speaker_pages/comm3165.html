Title: Modeling Biofuel Supply Chains with Real-world Geographies and Supply Uncertainty
Author: Yueyue Fan, Assistant Professor, University of California, Department of CEE, Davis, CA, 95616, United States of America, yyfan@ucdavis.edu
Coauthor(s): Nathan Parker
Year: 2007
Abstract: Design of biofuel infrastructure is dependent on the geography of uncertain supply and demand. A stochastic MINLP model is developed to design biofuel production and delivery infrastructures with real-world geographies and uncertainties.
