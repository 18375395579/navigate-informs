Title: The Web Services Marketplace: Examining Advertising Effectiveness

Author: Sandra Vannoy, Doctoral Student, UNCG, 479 Bryan School of Business and Econ, Greensboro, NC, 27402, United States of America, SAVANNOY@uncg.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: An electronic marketplace has emerged as a trading ground for buyers and sellers of business services delivered via Web Services technologies. In question is whether a mechanism is in place to enable buyers of business services to make effective choices among the services. In this research we develop the tools necessary to understand and conduct an examination of the effectiveness of advertising within a Web Services Marketplace, and we utilize those tools in conducting such an examination.
