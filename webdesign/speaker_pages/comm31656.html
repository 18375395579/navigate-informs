Title: Renewing, Building, and Maintaining Infrastructure in Basra, Iraq

Author: Dale Henderson, Assistant Professor, United States Military Academy, 272A Bowman Loop, West Point, NY, 10996, United States, Dale.Henderson@usma.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We describe our experiences as part of a military study team in Basra, Iraq in the summer of 2007. We assisted the provincial reconstruction team and the provincial technical directorates in assessing power, water, and sewerage infrastructure, in determining the best way to allocate investments in these areas, in developing and implementing maintenance operations practices, and in growing a pool of skilled engineers through the University of Basrah.
