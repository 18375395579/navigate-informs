Title: Behavioral Bidding Strategies
Author: Fredrik Odegaard, Assistant Professor, Richard Ivey School of Business, 1151 Richmond St. N, London, On, N6G4X3, Canada, fodegaard@ivey.uwo.ca
Year: 2012
Abstract: Auctions and bidding strategies have a rich and extensive research history. While theoretical results regarding optimal bidding strategies are well-established, there are also numerous empirical anomalies. In this paper we investigate certain simple bidding heuristics and compare those with observed online bidding behavior.
