Title: Mastering Uncertainty in Telecom New Ventures in Israel
Author: Christoph Loch, Professor of Technology Management, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, 77305 Fontainebleau, France, Christoph.LOCH@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Svenja Sommer
Year: 2008
Abstract: We describe the approaches used to respond to high uncertainty in a sample of 50 telecom software new venture companies in Israel. We find limitations in how project management in these companies is adapted to the challenges of uncertainty and complexity, and we document the barriers causing these weaknesses.
