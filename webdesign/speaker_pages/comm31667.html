Title: Information-theoretic Sensor Location Model for Traffic Condition Monitoring Applications

Author: Xuesong Zhou, Assistant Professor, University of Utah, 122 South Central Campus Dr., Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States, zhou@eng.utah.edu

Coauthor(s): Xing Tao, Peter Martin

Year: 2007

Abstract: An information-theoretic model is presented to characterize network-wide traffic monitoring systems that can utilize data from detectors, AVI readers and GPS probes. This talk will discuss the marginal value of probe data and how to locate traffic detectors and AVI readers so as to maximize the expected information gain.
