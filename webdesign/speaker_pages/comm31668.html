Title: Investment in Alternative Energy Technologies under Physical and Policy Uncertainty

Author: Afzal Siddiqui, University College London, Gower Street, London, United Kingdom, afzal.siddiqui@ucl.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Ryuta Takashima

Year: 2012

Abstract: Policymakers have often backed alternative energy technologies, e.g., nuclear power, due to their relatively low operating costs and emissions. However, they have also been quick to respond to public perceptions about the safety of such plants by suspending construction or even decommissioning existing facilities. We address public concerns about physical plant risks along with stochastic market prices for energy in modelling investment and decommissioning of alternative energy technologies.
