Title: Personalized Decision Support in The University of California Athena Breast Health Network
Author: Elissa Ozanne, UCSF Medical Center, 3333 California St, Suite 265, San Francisco, CA, 94118, United States of America, elissa.ozanne@ucsfmedctr.org
Coauthor(s): Laura Esserman, Beth Crawford
Year: 2012
Abstract: The Athena Breast Health Network is an innovative collaboration across the five University of California medical centers. In Athena, clinical care and research are integrated - allowing data collection, broad data access, and continuous feedback to inform and drive research and improvement in care. Athena launched with a screening and prevention initiative, which automates risk assessment and decision support for all women who receive a mammogram and complete an electronic health questionnaire.
