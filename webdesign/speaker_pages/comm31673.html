Title: Juggling Tasks: Mine, Yours and Ours...
Author: Reha Uzsoy, Professor, Purdue University, 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, uzsoy@ecn.purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Lucia Mendez
Year: 2006
Abstract: We show that the NP-hardness of simple scheduling problems achieves completely new heights when applied to families involving professional parents with children. We present some empirical observations from the field, and discuss directions for future research into this important open problem.
