Title: Who Will Control the United States Senate after the 2012 Election?

Author: Sheldon Jacobson, Professor, University of Illinois, 201 N. Goodwin Avenue (MC258), Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, shj@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Edward Sewell, Jason Sauppe, Steve Rigdon

Year: 2012

Abstract: Control of the United States Senate by a party provides critical legislative advantages. This presentation discusses an analytics approach to assessing who will achieve this objective. Forecasts for the 2012 Senate race are discussed. A web site electionanalytics.cs.illinois.edu has been created for people to follow these forecasts until election day.
