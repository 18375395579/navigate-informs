Title: What Is a Good Measure of Risk: The Controversies and a Different Set of Axioms
Author: Steve Kou, Associate Professor, Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, Columbia University, 312 S.W. Mudd, New York, NY, 10027, United States, sk75@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Xianhua Peng, Chris Heyde
Year: 2006
Abstract: Axioms-based coherent risk measures have been widely used in mathematical finance. In this paper we point out some controversies of the axioms. We then suggest a different set of axioms and derive risk measures based on the axioms.
