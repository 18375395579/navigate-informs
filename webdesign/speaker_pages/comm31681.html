Title: Tutorial on Portfolio Optimization

Author: John Birge, University of Chicago, Booth School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States of America, john.birge@chicagobooth.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Portfolio optimization is one of the oldest applications of OR. Despite its age, the topic still has substantial interest and opportunities for new research on the application and methods. This tutorial will provide an overview of work to date and possible future directions.
