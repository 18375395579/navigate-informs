Title: An Econometric Analysis of Black Friday Sales in Retail
Author: Nikolay Osadchiy, Assistant Professor, Emory University, Robert Goizueta Business School, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, nikolay.osadchiy@emory.edu
Coauthor(s): Vishal Gaur
Year: 2013
Abstract: Black Friday is one of the most anticipated sales events for the US retailers. Using the state of the art non-parametric method we estimate the mix of consumers during the Black Friday, whether they postpone purchases or buy in advance, as well as their distribution of preferences over products.
