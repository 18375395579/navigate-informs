Title: Mapping Competitive Intelligence in Pricing Applications

Author: Aihong Wen, Scientist, PROS Revenue Management, 3100 main st. Suite 900, HOuston, TX, 77025, United States of America, awen@prospricing.com

Coauthor(s): Tony Ruggiero

Year: 2008

Abstract: Leveraging the competitive intelligence in a pricing system could gain significant improvement on pricing solutions. However the competitive intelligence data may not, or most likely, will not have the same attributes and same values for attributes as a company’s own data. We developed a methodology to map the elements in these two data sets utilizing similarity indices. The methodology is robust in the sense that if an exact mapping is not available then the most similar match is identified.
