Title: Using Multi-Dimensional Plots to Examine Passenger Behavior and Applications to RM

Author: Judy Pastor, Principal, OR&DS, American Airlines, 4333 Amon Carter Boulevard, MD 5358, Fort Worth, TX, 76155-2664, United States, judy.pastor@aa.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: We will use trellis plots in the software package, R, to examine patterns in PNR attributes. Airline RM models can take advantage of these patterns to detect sell-up opportunities.
