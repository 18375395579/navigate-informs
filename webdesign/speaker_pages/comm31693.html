Title: Improving Drinking Water Quality in Pinellas County via Optimized Flushing

Author: Long Zhao, University of South Florida, 4202 East Fowler Avenue, ENC2400, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, longzhao@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Bo Zeng, Xiongfei Xie

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Pinellas County water distribution system is suffering from water aging that affects 500,000 residents. This problem results from decreased water consumption in recent years. In this presentation, simulated annealing will be applied to obtain an optimal fire hydrant flushing schedule and thus maintain water quality and system integrity in Pinellas County. This project is supported by USF graduate student challenge grant.
