Title: Social Link Recommendation
Author: Masoud Makrehchi, Associate Professor, University of Ontario, Institute of Technology, 2000 Simcoe Street North, Oshawa, ON, L1H7K4, Canada, Masoud.Makrehchi@uoit.ca
Year: 2013
Abstract: Several strategies for link recommendation and prediction are discussed. First, taxonomy of the topic is presented. Then, social data and features are briefly discussed. Several link prediction methods including (semi)supervised and unsupervised techniques, and also link prediction using hidden topics are detailed. Finally, some applications and examples are outlined.
