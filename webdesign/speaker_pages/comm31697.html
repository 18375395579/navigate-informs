Title: Pavement Management Decision Making through Dynamic Traffic Assignment and Dynamic Programming

Author: Yi-Chang Chiu, Assistant Professor, University of Arizona, 1209 E 2nd St. Room 206, Department of Civil Eng.and Eng. Mech., Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, chiu@email.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Jorge Villalobos

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a dynamic programming based decision approach in conjunction with the dynamic traffic assignment to highlight the need for the pavement management system to consider the traffic flow re-distribution due to major highway project in order to improve the prediction of future ESAL load, serviceability of highway pavements, and better resource allocation.
