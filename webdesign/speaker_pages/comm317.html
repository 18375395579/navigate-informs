Title: The Structure of Reduced bases for Kernel Lattices: Theoretical Analysis
Author: Frederik von Heymann, Delft University of Technology, Mekelweg 4, Delft, 2628 CD, Netherlands, f.j.vonheymann@tudelft.nl
Coauthor(s): Karen Aardal, Andrea Lodi, Laurence Wolsey
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this presentation we continue on the topic of the previous talk. Here we present the main ingredients of the theoretical analysis and give some computational examples.
