Title: Aviation System Reliability During Natural Disaster

Author: Yoonjin Yoon, Assistant Professor, KAIST, 291 Daehak-ro, Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, 305-701, Korea, Republic of, yoonjin@kaist.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Jiseon Lee

Year: 2013

Abstract: Increasing trend of natural disaster affects various modes of transportation and air transportation is no exception. In this study, we analyse the effect of volcanic ash to air transportation system performance at the national and worldwide level. We focus our analysis on Icelandic volcano ash event, and apply to the hypothetical case of Korea's Mt. Baekdoo eruption to assess the level of disruption to the Korean and nearby airspace.
