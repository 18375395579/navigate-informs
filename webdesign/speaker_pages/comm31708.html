Title: Impact of Payer-Provider Integration on Quality, Access, Cost, and Social Welfare

Author: Aaron Ratcliffe, Aaron_Ratcliffe@kenan-flagler.unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Ann Marucheck, Wendell Gilland

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a queuing game to analyze interaction of a profit-maximizing health provider which chooses quality and capacity and cost-minimizing insurer which sets its coinsurance rate. We compare quality, access, cost and social welfare between a competitive managed care model and integrated model where the provider insures its patient population.
