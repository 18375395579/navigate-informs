Title: Scheduling Engineering and Production Lots in Wafer Fabrication
Author: Reha Uzsoy, Professor, Purdue University, 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, uzsoy@ecn.purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Kristy Crist, Brian Kelly
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present a simulation study examining the performance of different scheduling policies for allocating machine capacity between production and engineering lots in a semiconductor wafer fab. Our results indicate the basic tradeoffs involved in this problem, and suggest a broader view of the problem is required for effective solutions.
