Title: Social Policy and Private Capital in Railway Investment

Author: Steven Harrod, Dr., University of Dayton, Department of MIS, OM, & DS, 300 College Park, Dayton, OH, 45469, United States of America, steven.harrod@udayton.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The dominant policy of North American railways since 1893 has been private capital funding of railway infrastructure, yet financial support has continued to other transportation modes, particularly roads. This presentation reviews the historic patterns of government transportation funding and the social forces that have shaped current transportation policy.
