Title: Effect of Government Subsidies on the Adoption of Resource Efficient Products

Author: Haoying Sun, Assistant Professor, Texas A & M University, 4217 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, hsun@mays.tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Steve Gilbert

Year: 2012

Abstract: We use the durable goods framework to study how various forms of government subsidy programs shift consumer’s adoption of resource efficient products and how this in turn affects consumption of scarce natural resources.
