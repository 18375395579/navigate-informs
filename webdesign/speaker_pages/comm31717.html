Title: Bidder Regret, Sniping, and the Sequential Auction Problem on eBay

Author: Adam I. Juda, PhD Candidate, Harvard University, Wyss Hall, Harvard Business School, Boston, MA, 02163, United States, ajuda@hbs.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Bidders on eBay have no dominant bidding strategy when faced with multiple auctions each offering the same item. An analysis of 10,151 bidders in 1,956 auctions for a Dell E193FP LCD monitor demonstrates a large number of bidding mistakes which are likely hampering the efficiency of the market system.
