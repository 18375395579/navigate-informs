Title: Enablers and Barriers for Producer Responsibility in the Photovoltaic Sector

Author: Maria Besiou, Kuehne Logistics University - THE KLU, Brooktorkai 20, Hamburg, 20457, Germany, Maria.Besiou@the-klu.org

Coauthor(s): Luk Van Wassenhove

Year: 2012

Abstract: This case-based research focuses on how the producer responsibility is implemented in the photovoltaic sector. Transferring the knowledge from the electrical and electronic equipment and the automotive sectors, two sectors with tradition on producer responsibility, factors affecting its implementation in the photovoltaic sector are identified. Then they are categorized into barriers, preventing its implementation, and enablers that will facilitate it.
