Title: A New Voxel-based Optimization Algorithm for Automatic Planning, Re-planning and Interactive Treatment

Author: Masoud Zarepisheh, University of California-San Diego, San Diego, CA, United States of America, mzarepisheh@ucsd.edu

Coauthor(s): Nan Li, Xun Jia, Feng Shi, steve jiang

Year: 2013

Abstract: The conventional treatment planning procedure is a time-consuming task that may need multiple iterations between the dosimetrists and clinicians. We develop a new algorithm to adjust the voxel weighting factors and reference doses automatically to navigate on the Pareto surface to find out a plan with a desirable DVH or dose distribution. The applications of the algorithm in automatic treatment planning, adaptive radiotherapy re-planning and interactive treatment planning would be presented.
