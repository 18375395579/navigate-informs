Title: Minimum Vertex Cover Problem for Coupled Interdependent Networks with Cascading Failures

Author: Alexander Veremyev, NRC Research Associate, Munitions Directorate, Air Force Research Laboratory, 101 West Eglin Blvd, Eglin AFB, United States of America, averemyev@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a generalization of the classical minimum vertex cover problem to the case of interdependent networks, i.e., identifying a minimum-cardinality set of nodes, whose deletion would e ectively disable both network layers through cascading failure mechanisms. We analyze the computational complexity, develop linear 0-1 formulations and discuss other related issues. Computational experiments are reported to illustrate the performance of the developed models.
