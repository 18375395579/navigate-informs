Title: Planning Strategies for Home Health Care Delivery
Author: Maciek Nowak, Associate Professor, Loyola University Chicago, 1 E. Pearson, Chicago, IL, 60611, United States of America, mnowak4@luc.edu
Coauthor(s): Mike Hewitt, Nisha Nataraj
Year: 2013
Abstract: In home health care, where a patient receives care for two to three months, continuity of care is very important. Previous research focusing on continuity of care uses planning horizons that are often a week or shorter. We demonstrate that considering a longer horizon in this setting has potential for savings. With inherent uncertainty in planning for such a long time horizon, a methodology is presented that anticipates future patient requests that are unknown at the time of planning.
