Title: Operations Analysis of a Precision Tracking Space System (PTSS) for Phased Adaptive Approach (PAA)

Author: Daniel Wilson, Senior Principal Systems Engineer, Raytheon Integrated Defense Systems, 225 Presidential Way, Mailstop 27/2317D, Woburn, MA, 01801, United States of America, Daniel.D.Wilson@raytheon.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Precision Tracking Space System (PTSS) is in concept development to provide tracking for the Phased Adaptive Approach (PAA). The Space Tracking Surveillance System (STSS) demonstrated the value of Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites to the Ballistic Missile Defense System (BMDS). Quality of Service (QoS) metrics are being used as analytics to develop traceability from performance specifications to the mission effectiveness, system risk, and affordability of alternative BMDS architectures.
