Title: Global Capacity Expansion under Uncertainty with Supply-Demand Imbalances

Author: Jeff Stonebraker, North Carolina State University, Poole College of Management, Raleigh, NC, United States of America, jsstoneb@ncsu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present an application of decision analysis to global production capacity planning for a proposed new biotechnology drug to treat hemophilia A. This decision analysis addressed global production capacity expansion under uncertainty when supply is limited due to manufacturing complexities, and regional supply-demand imbalances exist during the product-generation transition.
