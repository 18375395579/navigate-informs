Title: New Development in Google OR-Tools

Author: Laurent Perron, Google, 38 Avenue de l'Opéra, Paris, Fr, 75002, France, lperron@google.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Google's OR-Tools is a set of libraries that includes a constraint programming solver, a wrapper around linear solvers, and some dedicated graph and flow algorithms. Since the initial presentation, development has been very active and lots of new features have been added to the different solvers. We will take advantage of the INFORMS meeting to present these improvements.
