Title: Use of Operations Research Models to Plan Capacity at Norfolk Southern

Author: Wayne Mason, Senior Director of Strategic Planning, Norfolk Southern Corporation, 1200 Peachtree Street NE, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States, Wayne.Mason@nscorp.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Norfolk Southern and other North American railroads have been experiencing unprecedented growth in recent years. Facing the challenges of a growth industry, Norfolk Southern turned to Operations Research models and techniques to draw more capacity out of the existing operating plan and infrastructure. Furthermore, OR tools are being employed to identify the choke points in the NS transportation network and make recommendations to invest in new capacity.
