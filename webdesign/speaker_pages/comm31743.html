Title: The Influence of Information Architecture on Bidding Activity in Reverse Auctions
Author: Otto Koppius, RSM Erasmus University, Dept. of D&IS (T9-08), PO Box 1738, Rotterdam, ZH, 3000 DR, Netherlands, OKoppius@rsm.nl
Year: 2007
Abstract: In practice, reverse auctions are designed with a wide variety of information architectures that dictate what information is (not) revealed to bidders, such as the lowest bid, bidder's current ranking and total number of bids and bidders. We study the impact that this has on the activity in the auction.
