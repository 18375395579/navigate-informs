Title: A Data Envelopment Analysis Model to Asses the Efficiency of Inter-modal Transportation Systems

Author: Evangelos Kaisar, Assistant Professor, Florida Atlantic University, 777 Glades Rd., Boca Raton, FL, United States, evangelos@civil.fau.edu

Coauthor(s): Ramesh Teegavarapu, Elisabeth Gundersen

Year: 2007

Abstract: United States has become progressively more concerned about safety and security issues regarding the country’s transportation systems. In this research, DEA model is developed to assess the efficiency of the assorted transportation modes. The objective of the study is to identify the problems associated with the intermodal transportation system to enhance safety and security.
