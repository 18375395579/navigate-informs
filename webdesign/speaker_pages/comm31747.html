Title: Auctions Design for Online Display Advertising Exchanges
Author: Santiago Balseiro, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, srb2155@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Omar Besbes, Gabriel Weintraub
Year: 2012
Abstract: Ad Exchanges are emerging markets where advertisers may purchase display ad placements directly from publishers via a simple auction mechanism. Using the novel notion of a Fluid Mean Field Equilibrium we study key auction design decisions that publishers face in these markets. In particular, we provide sharp prescriptions regarding the reserve price, the allocation of impressions to the exchange versus an alternative channel, and the disclosure of viewers' information.
