Title: A Literature Survey: An All-hazards Approach to Disaster Management Planning
Author: Josey Chacko, Virginia Tech, 412 W Redwood Street, Baltimore, MD, 21201, United States of America, joseyc@vt.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: An exhaustive literature review of various quantitative models in the All-Hazards (AH) disaster management planning literature will be evaluated, highlighting the current gaps in this field.
