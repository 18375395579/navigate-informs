Title: Government Decisions and Real Options: Cases and Potential
Author: Scott Farrow, Professor & Chair, Department of Economics, University of Maryland, 1000 Hilltop Circle, Baltimore, MD, 21250, United States, Farrow@umbc.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Many Government program and project decisions involve uncertainty, irreversibility, and flexibility in timing. Examples include oil and gas leasing, regulations, and major procurements. In contrast to many private sector applications, market data are frequently lacking. Existing and potential applications are discussed.
