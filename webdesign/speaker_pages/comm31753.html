Title: Line of Sight Networks

Author: Alan Frieze, Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, af1p@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): R Ravi, Warren Debany, Jon Kleinberg

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study a new class of random graph which model communication within an environment with regularly spaced obstacles.
