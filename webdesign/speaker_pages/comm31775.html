Title: The Impact of Failure on Performance Over Time: The Case of Cardiac Surgery Operations

Author: Emmanouil Avgerinos, PhD Student, University College London, Gower Street, London, United Kingdom, emmanouil.avgerinos.10@ucl.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Bilal Gokpinar

Year: 2013

Abstract: In many knowledge-intensive service settings where human action is critical, failure is a reality. In this study, we examine the relationship between previous failures and subsequent operational performance both at the team and individual level. Using a large scale data from more than 6,000 cardiac surgeries in a European hospital over seven years, we investigate mechanisms through which task failure affects future productivity and performance.
