Title: A Multi-Criteria Decision Support System for R&D Project Selection and Portfolio Optimization

Author: Susan Palocsay, Professor, James Madison University, Computer Information Systems & Management Science Department, Harrisonburg, VA, 22807, United States, palocssw@jmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Anne Henriksen

Year: 2006

Abstract: To determine the optimal R&D portfolio, decision makers must consider positive interdependencies between projects such as the capability to share resources, research results, intellectual capital or investment costs. We have developed Popt, an Excel Solver/VBA DSS that uses goal programming to obtain a portfolio with the optimal combination of project merit and value and maximum portfolio synergy while meeting cost and strategy objectives.
