Title: Insights From Applying Decision Analysis to Homeland Security Problems
Author: Detlof von Winterfeldt, University of Southern California, Ronald Tutor Hall 316A, University Park, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, winterfe@sppd.usc.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Three recent decision analyses applied to homeland security problems created new insights in the use of decision analysis in adversarial situations. This presentation will discuss some of these insights.
