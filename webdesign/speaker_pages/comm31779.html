Title: Newsvendor Information Gathering and Decision Making: Observations from Verbal Protocol Analysis

Author: Srinagesh Gavirneni, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, Johnson Graduate School of Management, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, sg337@johnson.cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Alice Isen

Year: 2007

Abstract: From protocol analyses of audio recordings of subjects?thought processes, we report our observations on the information gathering and decision making efforts in a newsvendor setting. Many participants correctly identified the overage and underage costs, but failed to convert that into the optimal order quantity leading us to conclude that the mathematics involved is not as intuitive as commonly perceived. Subjects sought the basic information, but failed to seek relevant advanced information.
