Title: Design of a Logistics Platform for the Distribution of Fresh Produce

Author: Octavio Sanchez, Research Assistant, Arizona State University, Dept. of Industrial Eng., Tempe, az, 85287, United States of America, octavio.sanchez@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): J Rene Villalobos

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this presentation we discuss a case study of the design of a single logistics platform for fresh produce that has as its main objective the maximization of revenues of the farmer. One of the design factors to be discussed is the location of the platform in such a way that the most revenue can be realized subject to maximal driving constraints.
