Title: Using Propensity Scores to Evaluate an e-Health Technology for Depression Care

Author: Haomiao Jin, University of Southern California, 3715 McClintock Ave, GER 240, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, haomiaoj@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Chih-Ping Chou, Pey-Jiuan Lee, Shinyi Wu, Irene Vidyanti, Kathleen Ell

Year: 2013

Abstract: Diabetes-Depression Care-management Adoption Technology (DCAT) is an innovative e-Health technology to facilitate evidence-based depression care in primary care settings for underserved populations. To evaluate DCAT, a three-arm quasi-experiment clinical trial involving 1400 low-income, predominantly minority diabetes patients was conducted. Propensity score method was applied to adjust for sampling bias in the outcome analyses. Results show that DCAT significantly improved depression care.
