Title: Dynamic Learning in Resource Allocation for Survey Designs

Author: Melania Calinescu, VU University Amsterdam, Faculty of Sciences, De Boelelaan 1081, Amsterdam, 1081HV, Netherlands, melania.calinescu@vu.nl

Coauthor(s): Barry Schouten, Sandjai Bhulai

Year: 2012

Abstract: We explore a resource allocation model in a system de?ned by an extremely large number of stochastic parameters. The exploration- exploitation tradeo? is represented by the decision to allocate scarce resources between learning about the system parameters to improve future decisions and choosing actions that contribute most to the total expected reward. This work is motivated by the challenges survey organizations are faced with in the attempt to increase the quality of survey estimates.
