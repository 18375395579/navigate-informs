Title: Asymptotics for Optimal Investment and Consumption with Small Capital Gain Tax and Interest Rate
Author: Min Dai, Associate Professor, National University of Singapore, Department of Mathematics, Singapore, Singapore, matdm@nus.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Xinfu Chen
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider continuous-time optimal investment and consumption decision of a constant relative risk aversion investor who faces capital gain tax. We present an asymptotic expansion of the optimal strategy with small interest/tax rate.
