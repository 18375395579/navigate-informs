Title: Panel Discussion: Entrepreneurship in OR/MS - Lessons Learned

Author: James Kelly, CEO, OptTek Systems Inc., 1919 Seventh Street, Boulder, CO, 80302, United States, Kelly@opttek.com

Coauthor(s): Dennis Pegden, Ron Laughery, Mark Elder, James Franklin

Year: 2006

Abstract: Many members of INFORMS have worked in an entrepreneurial capacity. Some have started and/or grown companies on a full-time/ or part-time basis. The purpose of this session is to recount some of the stories associated with these activities and to discuss common experiences, obstacles, and strategies. This group has a particular focus on products and services related to simulation and optimization.
