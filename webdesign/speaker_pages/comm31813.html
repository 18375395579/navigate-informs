Title: The Medco Story: A Modern Marvel of Large Scale Mass Customization
Author: Art Onge, St. Onge Company, 1400 Williams Road, York, PA, 17402, astonge@stonge.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: Medco prepares and delivers over two million distinct prescriptions each week from its two facilities. How did St. Onge Company ?a consulting/engineering firm known for its creative and innovative business solutions ?employed mass customization principles to design an automated, error-free prescription delivery system with virtually no human involvement is discussed.
