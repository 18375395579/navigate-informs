Title: AMIGO: A New Solver for Mixed Integer Nonlinear Optimization

Author: Zsolt Ugray, Assistant Professor, Business Information Systems Department, USU, 3515 Old Main Hill, Logan, UT, 84321, United States, Zsolt.Ugray@usu.edu

Coauthor(s): János D. Pintér

Year: 2006

Abstract: We introduce AMIGO, an Advanced Mixed Integer Global Optimizer. AMIGO incorporates the LGO (Lipschitz Global Optimizer) solver suite for continuous global/local optimization. We present the algorithmic framework with illustrative numerical results.
