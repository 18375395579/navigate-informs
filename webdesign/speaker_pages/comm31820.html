Title: Volunteering, Social Capital and Professional Leadership

Author: Michael Trick, General Chair, INFORMS Anuual Meeting, Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15217, United States, trick@cmu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Service in organizations can come about through unexpected directions. I'll talk about how an inability to remember web addresses led to a year of being president of INFORMS, and the key social capital aspects involved.
