Title: WORMS: Advice on Promotion to Full from Associate Professor Panel
Author: Mary E. Kurz, Associate Professor, Clemson University, Industrial Engineering, 110 Freeman Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States of America, MKURZ@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Aleda Roth, Susan Sanchez, Beril Toktay, Julie Higle
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this panel, we will discuss the experiences, observations and receive advice from several successful female faculty housed in different types of departments and institutions, focusing on the transition from associate to full professor.
