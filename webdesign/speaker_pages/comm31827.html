Title: An Interactive Excel VBA Example for Teaching Statistics Concepts
Author: Weiyu Tsai, Assistant Professor, University of Utah, Department of Management, 1645 E. Campus Center Drive, Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States, mgtwt@business.utah.edu
Coauthor(s): Don Wardell
Year: 2006
Abstract: We developed a Visual Basic Application driven Excel spreadsheet that is built around one simple business scenario that allows students to integrate the Central Limit Theorem, interval estimation and hypothesis testing. Moreover, the example demonstrates several useful Excel statistical formulae. The results of an in-class open-book quiz showed that the scores of those using the interactive demo were superior to those exposed to more traditional techniques at the 10% significance level.
