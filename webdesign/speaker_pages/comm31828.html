Title: A Bayesian Framework for Assessment of Prediction Uncertainty in Scale-up

Author: Tirthankar Dasgupta, Harvard University, 1 Oxford St, Dept of Statistics, Cambridge, MA, 02138, United States of America, dasgupta@stat.harvard.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Reproducibility of experimental results under unknown, but perhaps predictable noise conditions is a challenge in scale-up operations. We propose a hierarchical Bayesian framework that allows augmentation of prior information about potential sources of variation in scale-up operations.
