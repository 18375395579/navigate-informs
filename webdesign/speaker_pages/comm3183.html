Title: Sustainable HealthCare Supply Chains

Author: Cigdem Gurgur, Indiana University, Purdue University Fort Wayne, Fort Wayne, IN, United States of America, gurgurc@ipfw.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Recent years have witnessed a profound interest in healthcare waste given its impact on costs and the environment. In this study we consider a hybrid simulation model that incorporates both productivity and sustainability related criteria in helping capacity management decisions.
