Title: Network Planning Workbench, An Analytical Tool for Monitoring and Improving Operating Performance
Author: Viraj Karnik, Manager Operations Research, Norfolk Southern Corporation, 1200 Peachtree St NE, MS 12-117, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States of America, viraj.karnik@nscorp.com
Coauthor(s): Ilya Lavrik
Year: 2012
Abstract: Business analytics improves decision making, it helps to optimize performance by providing new insights. In this talk we present NPW ?an analytical tool developed by NS Operations Research for Transportation Planners. NPW provides historical, real time, plan vs. actual comparison information. It is highly interactive, visual and provides user with ability to drill down and perform root cause analysis and decide on corrective actions.
