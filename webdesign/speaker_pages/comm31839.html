Title: Behavior-driven Social Network Mining and Analysis for Business Intelligence

Author: Feida Zhu, Singapore Management University, 80 Stamford Road, Singapore, Singapore, fdzhu@smu.edu.sg

Year: 2013

Abstract: A distinct feature characterizing social network data is the rich user behavior information. These interactive behavior information could collectively reveal tremendous insight into a wide range of social network patterns. In this talk, we discuss how longitudinal behavior data collected over a time span can be leveraged to facilitate a number of interesting social network mining and analysis tasks.
