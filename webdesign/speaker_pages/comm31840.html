Title: Sustainable Supply Chains: Contributions from MCDM

Author: Birsen Karpak, Professor of Management, Youngstown State University, One University Plaza, WCBA 3303, Youngstown, OH, 44555, United States of America, bkarpak@ysu.edu

Coauthor(s): Carol Lamb

Year: 2012

Abstract: Supply chains play a significant role in the global knowledge based economy. There has been numerous papers written and research done in the area of sustainable supply chain management (SSCM). The purpose of this paper is to explore contributions of MCDM to this domain. Taxonomy of the methodologies used will be given. Our initial research indicates that Analytical Hierarchy process (AHP)/ Analytical Network Process (ANP) are among the most applied methodologies of MCDM to this field.
