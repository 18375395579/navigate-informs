Title: Price Signaling in a Congested Environment
Author: Laurens Debo, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, laurens.debo@chicagobooth.edu
Coauthor(s): Senthil Veeraraghavan, Uday Rajan
Year: 2012
Abstract: Often, high-quality firms have long queues. A question is why these firms do not raise prices? In this talk, I will discuss the superior informational role that queues can play in conveying information about quality to uninformed consumes.
