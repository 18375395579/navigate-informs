Title: Bridging Laboratory and Field Behavior: Bargaining and Auctions in Controlled Experiments on EBay

Author: Gary Bolton, Smeal College of Business, Penn State University, 334 Business Building, University Park, PA, United States, gbolton@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Axel Ockenfels

Year: 2006

Abstract: We conducted a controlled field experiment on eBay to investigate first, whether basic laboratory results on equitable bargaining and competitive bidding surface in a naturally occurring market environment among experienced traders, and second, whether trading strategies observed in the experiment can be linked to trading patterns observed outside the experiment.
