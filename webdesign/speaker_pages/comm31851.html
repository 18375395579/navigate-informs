Title: Xpress-Mosel: Modeling Support for Distributed Computing in Physical and Virtual Networks

Author: Oliver Bastert, FICO, Starley Way, Birmingham, United Kingdom, OliverBastert@fico.com

Coauthor(s): Susanne Heipcke

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk presents the Xpress-Mosel environment and its functionality for distributed and remote modeling and solving of optimization problems. In particular, we describe new functionality for remote computing without any need for a local installation of Xpress that has recently been released. We discuss several examples of distributed applications implemented by some of our customers.
