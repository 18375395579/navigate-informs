Title: Building a Regional Earthquake Emergency Response Exercise from Scientific Foundations
Author: Anne Wein, Operations Research Analyst, US Geological Survey, Western Geographic Science Center, 345 Middlefield Rd., MS 531, Menlo Park, CA, 94025, United States of America, awein@usgs.gov
Year: 2008
Abstract: An emergency response exercise planned in So. California is based on an earthquake scenario produced by the United States Geological Survey and partners. The Multiple Hazards Demonstration Project integrated models and expert opinion to transform geophysical outputs into estimates of lifeline and building damages, and their consequences. The talk will provide an overview of the models, processes, and products, and highlight lessons learned, innovative features, results, and stakeholder benefits.
