Title: City Readiness and Emergency Response for Biodefense and Infectious Disease Outbreaks
Author: Eva Lee, Associate Professor and Director, Georgia Institute of Technology, Ctr for Operations Research in Medicine, Industrial & Systems Engineering, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States of America, evakylee@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Chien-Hung Chen, Hannah Smalley
Year: 2008
Abstract: City readiness and emergency response for biodefense and infectious disease outbreaks involves multi-level strategic and operational planning of the public health infrastructure. This talk will focus on protection of regional population through mass dispensing of prophyllaxic medication. In collaboration with CDC, we will discuss our experience in strategic stockpile,locations of dispensing facilities, optimal facility staffing and resource allocation, transportation, and dispensing modalities.
