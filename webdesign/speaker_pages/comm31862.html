Title: A Dynamic Approach to the Dose Optimization in Radiotherapy
Author: Minsun Kim, Student, University of Washington, 1959 N.E. Pacific, Seattle, WA, United States of America, mk688@u.washington.edu
Coauthor(s): Archis Ghate, Mark Phillips
Year: 2008
Abstract: We propose a novel treatment planning strategy that dynamically includes patients?radiobiological responses. The dose delivery is thus optimized not only spatially as in IMRT but also temporally. Preliminary results for simplified scenarios will be presented.
