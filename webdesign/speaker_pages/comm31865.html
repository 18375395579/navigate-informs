Title: Some Personal Observations Regarding an Academic Career in Operations Research

Author: Martin Wortman, Professor, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, College Station, TX, 77843-3131, United States, wortman@mwx.tamu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Over a 20 year academic career, there have been occasions upon which the author asked himself, What was I thinking? In this talk, we will review some of the approaches taken by the author to achieve a balanced and well ordered career that were not entirely successful.
