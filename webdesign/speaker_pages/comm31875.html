Title: PTC Overview, Risk Factors, and Research Needs

Author: Carl VanDyke, Partner, Oliver Wyman, One University Square, Suite 101, Princeton, NJ, 08540, United States of America, Carl.VanDyke@oliverwyman.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: An overview of PTC will be presented, including the base technological strategy. The different approaches to implementing PTC will be presented, and the reasons for why the overlay approach has been adopted for the U.S. will be explored. A number of key risk factors and research needs will also be explored.
