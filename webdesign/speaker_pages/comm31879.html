Title: Smart Services ?Implications and Approach for Agile Service Management

Author: Helmut Aschbacher, Professor, University of Applied Sciences CAMPUS 02, Koerblergasse 126, Graz, 8021, Austria, helmut.aschbacher@campus02.at

Coauthor(s): Ernst Kreuzer, Christian Kittl

Year: 2012

Abstract: Smart Services are described in the literature as an ICT technique that identifies customer needs and issues fast service responses for increased convenience. The implications of smart services are not yet well understood as it is a management concept which has the power to revolutionize the agile delivery of services. A four-tier development approach, design patterns and the ethical implications of Smart Services will be discussed in this presentation.
