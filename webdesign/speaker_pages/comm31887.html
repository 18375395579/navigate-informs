Title: Optimal Timing of New Product Introduction using Preliminary Market Testing

Author: Rene Caldentey, New York University, 44 West Fourth Street, New York, NY, United States of America, rcaldent@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Victor Araman

Year: 2012

Abstract: Introducing new products into the marketplace is a risky bet that manufacturers must continuously take. As a result, it is not uncommon to witness major manufacturers discontinuing a product shortly after its introduction. In this talk, we consider a seller who first tests the market and learns more about the demand before deciding whether or not to launch a product (or set of products). We also discuss current e-commerce practices that have increase companies' ability to do such market testing.
