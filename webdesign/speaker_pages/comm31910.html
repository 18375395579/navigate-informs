Title: Environmental and Economic Concerns in Time-dependent Vehicle Routing
Author: Yixiao Huang, Tsinghua University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, 100084, China, huangyx12@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn
Coauthor(s): Lei Zhao, Jean-Philippe Gross, Tom van Woensel
Year: 2013
Abstract: Traffic conditions vary in time during the day and also differ in regions within the city. Fuel consumption and carbon emissions are associated with both travel distances and loads of the delivery truck. In this talk, we study the time-dependent vehicle routing problem under environmental and economic concerns, together with a careful examination on the data under the logistics and traffic situations in Beijing.
