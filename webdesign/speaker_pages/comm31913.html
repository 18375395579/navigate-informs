Title: Numerical Algorithms Group - Mathematical Software to Address Your Business Needs

Author: Brian Spector, Technical Sales Specialist, NAG, 801 Warrenville Rd., Ste. 185, Lisle, IL, 60532, United States of America, spector@nag.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Do you need to make your numerical code more efficient and accurate but don’t know where to begin? We will demonstrate some of the 1,700 mathematical and statistical functions using the Numerical Algorithms Group (NAG) Library from MATLAB and Excel. Examples covered include optimization, regression, nearest correlation matrix, and more.
