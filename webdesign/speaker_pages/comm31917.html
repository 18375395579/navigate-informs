Title: Track Analysis and Anomaly Detection - Maritime (TAANDEM) for Maritime Domain Awareness (MDA)

Author: Bob Zarnich, Senior Manager, Metron, Inc, 11911 Freedom Dr, Reston, VA, 20190, United States of America, zarnich@metsci.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: A Maritime Domain Awareness (MDA) capability is sought by the Department of Homeland Security and the U.S. Navy. A key component is automated alerts of tracks inconsistent with “Normal behavior of commercial traffic. This presentation will provide an overview of the methods developed to address this capability. Of critical concern is defining normal behavior and choosing statistics oriented towards intuitive justification via graphical display while controlling the alert rate of the system.
