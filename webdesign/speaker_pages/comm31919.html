Title: Economics of Hosted Applications
Author: Narayan Raman, Lucent Technologies, 101 Crawfords Corner Road, Holmdel, NJ, 07733, United States, narayanraman@lucent.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: This study evaluates the benefit to a network operator of outsourcing the delivery of applications to consumer or enterprise end users. It investigates the impact of market- and supply-related uncertainties on the relative economics of the outsourcing decision.
