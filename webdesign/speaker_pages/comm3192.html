Title: Mathematical Programming: Turing-completeness and Applications to Algorithmic Analysis
Author: Leo Liberti, IBM Research, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, leoliberti@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Fabrizio Marinelli
Year: 2013
Abstract: We provide a new proof of the fact that Mathematical Programming is Turing-complete, and show how it can be useful in algorithmic analysis.
