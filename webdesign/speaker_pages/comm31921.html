Title: Traffic-Based Labor Planning in Retail Stores
Author: Howard Hao-Chun Chuang, Assistant Professor, National Chengchi University, 64 Sec. 2, Zhinan Rd., Wenshan District, Taipei, 11605, United States of America, hchuang@mays.tamu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: To improve retail staffing decisions, we perform a comprehensive empirical study using a proprietary panel dataset. We develop a response function to quantify the impact of labor and traffic on sales, and formulate a profit maximization problem to help retailers determine labor requirements. The heuristic performs closely to the optimal staffing levels and outperforms current staffing practices.
