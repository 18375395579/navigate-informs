Title: Two New Cases with Data Sets

Author: Itir Karaesmen, University of Maryland, Robert H Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, United States of America, ikaraes@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingjie Lan, Inbal Yahav

Year: 2008

Abstract: We will introduce two new cases with data sets. The first case is related to golf course revenue management. The second case is on group reservations at hotels where the data set can be used to analyze price segments. Copies of the cases can be obtained by contacting the author prior to the conference.
