Title: An Improved Tabu Search for Airport Gate Assignment
Author: Cheuk Lam Kwan, Graduate Student, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dept of Syst Engg & Engg Mgmt, The Chinese Univ of Hong Kong, Shatin, NT, 00000, Hong Kong - ROC, clkwan@se.cuhk.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): C.H. Cheng
Year: 2008
Abstract: We examine the use of tabu search for airport gate assignment. Features such as path relinking will be used to improve its performance. Computational study will also be presented.
