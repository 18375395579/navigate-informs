Title: Bike-Sharing System Design and Scheduling
Author: Mabel Chou, NUS Business School, Level 8, Biz1, Singapore, Singapore, mabelchou@nus.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Meilin Zhang
Year: 2012
Abstract: We develop practical OR models to support decision making in the design and scheduling of public car-sharing or bicycle-sharing systems. We develop a network flow model and discuss how to design the system so that the scheduling of the re-distribution can be more cost effective.
