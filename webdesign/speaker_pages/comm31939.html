Title: FHA Mortgage Insurance Program- Its Roles and Risks
Author: Gigi Yuen-Reed, Consultant, IBM, 14 Third Street SE, Washington, DC, 20003, United States of America, gigi.yuen@us.ibm.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: The mission of Federal Housing Authority (FHA) is to help borrowers get mortgage amounts they qualify for, and assist lenders by reducing their risks in issuing loans. FHA achieves this mission primarily by providing mortgage insurance. In this paper, we discuss the risk management approach of FHA and its role in alleviating the current mortgage crisis.
