Title: Game-Theoretic Learning Models in Traffic Assignment
Author: Tarun Rambha, The University of Texas at Austin, 301 E Dean Keeton St Stop C1761, Austin, United States of America, tarun.1988@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Kai Yin, Stephen Boyles
Year: 2013
Abstract: Fictitious play in atomic congestion games is known to converge to Nash Equilibrium. We explore other learning strategies in the context of traffic networks and examine the impact of travel time information and past experiences of users on the convergence of repeated play.
