Title: Optimizing Manufacturing Validation Builds

Author: Daniel Reaume, Staff Researcher, General Motors Operations Research, M/C 480-106-155, 30500 Mound Road, Warren, MI, 48090, United States of America, daniel.reaume@gm.com

Coauthor(s): Hallie Kintner

Year: 2007

Abstract: Manufacturing validation builds (MVBs) are an essential, but expensive, tool to ensure the launch of high-quality vehicles. We propose a hybrid model to optimize the number of such builds. The model recognizes the benefits of early issue detection and the fundamental differences between the automated and manual production processes involved in vehicle assembly.
