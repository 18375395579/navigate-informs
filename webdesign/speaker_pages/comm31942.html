Title: Implementation of CPOE Systems?An Empirical Analysis of the Antecedents and Impact on Performance
Author: Kingshuk Sinha, Curtis L. Carlson Family Foundation Professor, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, ksinha@umn.edu
Coauthor(s): Asoke Dey, Sriram Thirumalai
Year: 2008
Abstract: This talk will report the findings of a study on the implementation of computerized physician order entry (CPOE) systems used by physicians to enter orders for medications, diagnostic tests, and ancillary services. Key antecedents and performance implications of CPOE implementation are examined. The study sample contains 188 acute care hospitals.
