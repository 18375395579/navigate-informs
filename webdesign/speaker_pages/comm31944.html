Title: Cyber-armor: Analyzing the Risk of Vulnerabilities Introduced into a Supply Chain
Author: Marshall Kuypers, Stanford, 10124 Lindsay lane, Mokena, IL, 60448, United States of America, marshallkuypers@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Philip Martin, Jack Smith, Elisabeth Pate-Cornell, Katie Ward, Gregory Heon
Year: 2013
Abstract: The possibility of an intentional attack using a vulnerability introduced through a component’s supply chain has recently gained attention. Organizations are struggling with tough decisions regarding how to best evaluate the risk of different products that are an integral part to their network security. In this talk, we discuss several strategies for managing the risk of cyber-attacks and provide a case study on an enterprise style firewall.
