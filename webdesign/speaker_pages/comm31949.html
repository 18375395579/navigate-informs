Title: What About the People? Accounting for Indigenous Populations in SASO and Recovery Plans
Author: Paul Deason, Analysis Fellow, SAIC, 4815 Agave Dr., Las Cruces, NM, 88001-7592, United States, deasonp@zianet.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: The intent is to address the characteristics and qualifications in military sustainment and support planning representation to account for the needs of the encountered civilian population, including disaster recovery and humanitarian assistance and relief, and how to facilitate sustainable economic and cultural recovery.
