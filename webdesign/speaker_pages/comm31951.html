Title: Reaching Out to Juvenile Detention Centers

Author: Ali Abbas, Assistant Professor, College of Engineering, University of Illinois-Urbana Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61822, United States, aliabbas@uiuc.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We will discuss our experience reaching out with decision skills to teens in Juvenile Detention Centers in central Illinois and the lessons learnt. Topics of (i) Owning my decision, (ii) Decision fitness, and (iii) Framing my decisions were among the most important messages to convey.
