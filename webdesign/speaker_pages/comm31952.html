Title: Servitization: The Role of Technology in Services Demonstrated by Logistics

Author: Wei Xu, PhD Student and Teacher, University of Plymouth, School of Management Drake Circus, Plymouth, PL4 8AA, United Kingdom, wei.xu@plymouth.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Daniel Berg

Year: 2012

Abstract: The role of technology in services has gotten increased academic attention after years of neglect. Systems technology such as logistics, has enabled increasingly,the servitization of manufacturing.This paper explores the changes underway and prospects for future impacts.
