Title: Evaluation, Implementation, and Management of Outsourcing Initiatives
Author: Sean Handley, Doctoral Candidate, The Ohio State University, 2100 Neil Ave, 600 Fisher Hall, Columbus, OH, 43210, United States, handley_20@cob.osu.edu
Coauthor(s): WC Benton
Year: 2007
Abstract: Evidence from our preliminary research suggests that many organizations fail to realize the performance improvement expectations from their outsourcing efforts. In this research, we empirically analyze both manufacturing and business process outsourcing decisions to determine the management practices during the business case evaluation, implementation, and management stages that are related to successful outsourcing initiatives.
