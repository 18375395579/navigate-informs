Title: The Spreadsheet Guru Contest

Author: Rick Carter, Equation Consulting, 2650 S. Decker Lake Blvd, Salt Lake City, UT, United States of America, rcarter@equationconsulting.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: SPRIG (the Spreadsheet Productivity Research Interest Group) has sponsored a Spreadsheet Guru competition at each of the last two Spring INFORMS Analytics meetings. The contest, focusing on spreadsheet technique and speed, originated as an internal contest for Equation Consulting. Sample problems will be demonstrated and the future of the contest discussed.
