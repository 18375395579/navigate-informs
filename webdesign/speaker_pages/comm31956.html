Title: Fleet Management Coordination in Decentralized Humanitarian Operations

Author: Alfonso Pedraza Martinez, Indiana University, Kelley School of Business, Bloomington, IN, United States of America, alpedraz@indiana.edu

Coauthor(s): Sameer Hasija, Luk Van Wassenhove

Year: 2012

Abstract: Motivated by extensive field research we study incentive alignment to coordinate humanitarian operations. In large international humanitarian organizations efficiency/equity balancing headquarters assign operational capacity to equity-oriented programs. This creates a misalignment of incentives complicated by earmarked funding of vehicles. We propose an operational mechanism design for system coordination.
