Title: Big Data Means Nothing without Big Data Analytics

Author: Anand Srinivasan, Consultant, Revenue Analytics, 3100 Cumberland Blvd., Suite 1000, Atlanta, GA, 30339, United States of America, asrinivasan@revenueanalytics.com

Coauthor(s): Jon Higbie

Year: 2013

Abstract: Big Data is defined by 3Vs of data, Volume, Variety and Velocity. Technically, Big Data refers to storage and processing of large data, but in common usage, it refers to insights provided by the data. Industries like Revenue Management have a rich experience of crunching large datasets to better segment their customers and provide more targeted offerings to consumers. To best utilize Big Data capabilities, industries need to scale the 3 Vs of Big Data to match their execution capabilities.
