Title: A Conversation About Critical Thinking in OR Classes
Author: Natalia Santamaria, Smeal College of Business, Penn State, 471B Business Building, Penn State, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, nsantam@psu.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Dr.Rothkopf was my adviser while I was PhD student at Rutgers and during his time at Penn State. In this period of time I learned a great deal from him about how to be a good researcher and a good teacher also. In this presentation I'll share one of the conversations I had with him related to the importance of including critical thinking in OR classes.
