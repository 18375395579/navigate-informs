Title: Smartphone Application: A New Tool for Individual Drivers to Contribute to Sustainability
Author: Ying Wang, Associate Professor, Beihang University, 37 Xueyuan Road, Haidian District, New Main Building H1101, Beijing, 100191, China, yingwang@buaa.edu.cn
Year: 2013
Abstract: Smartphone applications have been developed very functional and used worldwide in recent years. Result from our questionnaire survey shows that many Chinese drivers select to use navigation apps on their smartphone rather than traditional portable GPS or the in-vehicle embedded GPS while driving. It suggests to develop novel smartphone applications as a new solution to promote sustainability in the transportation system.
