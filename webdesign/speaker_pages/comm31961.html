Title: Opportunities in Behavioral Service Operations

Author: Gad Allon, Professor, Northwestern University - Kellogg, 2001 Sheridan Rd., Evanston, IL, United States of America, g-allon@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: I will discuss models in service operations and empirical anomalies with emphasis on opportunities for behavioral research and experiments. Specific attention will be given to the areas of customer decision making, pricing, and delay announcements.
