Title: Building Decision Analysis Based Businesses

Author: Carl Spetzler, CEO, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, cspetzler@sdg.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: As an early member of the original Decision Analysis Group at SRI Internaional and then one of the founders of SDG, I have participated in and observed about a dozen of start-up companies based on the power of DA. In this session, I will share my observations from the successes and failures of these entrepreneurial efforts.
