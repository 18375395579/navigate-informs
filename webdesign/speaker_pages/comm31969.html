Title: NextGen Deployment Scenarios- Safety and Financial Considerations

Author: Richard Golaszewski, Executive Vice President, GRA, Incorporated, 115 West Av., Suite 201, Jenkintown, PA, 19046, United States of America, richg@gra-inc.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: The implementation of major changes to the air traffic control system has safety and financial implications for air traffic service providers and users. NextGen costs are expected to be signiificant for both operators and FAA. Changing technology and procedures also raises issues in terms of safety assurance. This presentation will explore the ways in which these considerations can best be managed, and will provide potential analytic frameworks for this.
