Title: Success Factors when Applying Decision Analyses- Lessons from the MARA Projects
Author: Cornelius Schaub, Director, Decision Institute / LSE, Karl-Liebknecht-Straﬂe 5, Berlin, 10178, Germany, c.schaub@decisioninstitute.eu
Coauthor(s): Nadine Oeser, Martin Schilling
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this talk, we explore success factors when applying MCDA in practice. Using the ten decision analyses carried out during the applied research project MARA in Argentina and Germany, we analyze the degree of relative success of each of the case studies and identify success factors which may account for these results.
