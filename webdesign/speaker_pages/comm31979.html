Title: Resource Allocation in the Pharmaceutical Development Process
Author: Christian Terwiesch, Associate Professor, University of Pennsylvania, 548 JMHH, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, terwiesch@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Karl Ulrich, Karan Girotra
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present a dynamic capacity allocation model that supports the portfolio planning process in the pharmaceutical industry.
