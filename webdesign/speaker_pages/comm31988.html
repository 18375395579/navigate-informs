Title: The Choice of Stochastic Process in Real Option Valuation

Author: Luiz Ozorio, Professor, IBMEC Business School, Av.Afranio de Melo Franco 149/403, Rio de Janeiro, RJ, 22430-060, Brazil, lmozorio@ibmecrj.br

Coauthor(s): Luiz Eduardo Brandão, Adrian Pizzinga, Carlos Bastian-Pinto

Year: 2012

Abstract: A main issue in valuation modeling is the choice of the stochastic process that describes the asset price performance. Particularly, in investment projects that show a high level of managerial flexibility in conditions of uncertainty the assumption of a specific process can have an impact on the project value and the investment rule. This work discusses the choice of stochastic process in real options valuation and useful tests and theoretical considerations to give support to this task.
