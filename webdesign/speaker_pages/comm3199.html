Title: Hospital Inpatient Design: Analysis and Insights
Author: Mabel Chou, NUS Business School, Level 8, Biz1, Singapore, Singapore, mabelchou@nus.edu.sg
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study the patient flow management in an inpatient department of a Singaporean hospital and discuss how to design the system to make the planning more cost effective. Our analysis generates a number of managerial insights for inpatient management.
