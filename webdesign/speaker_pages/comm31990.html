Title: Selling Wind Power in Mid-term Energy Auctions
Author: Javier Garcia-Gonzalez, Professor, Comillas University, Alberto Aguilera, 23, Madrid, 28015, Spain, javier_garcia_gonzalez@yahoo.es
Coauthor(s): Miguel Vazquez, Tomas Gomez
Year: 2008
Abstract: Displacement of fossil fuel generation technologies by intermittent renewable sources, and more specifically wind power, requires new methodologies in order to successfully provide base-load energy. We present a novel approach, focused on procurement of regulated demand.
