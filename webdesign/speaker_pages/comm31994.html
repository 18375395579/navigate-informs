Title: Capacity Implications of PTC Now and in the Future

Author: David Thurston, Vice President - Systems Engineering, Parsons Transportation Group, 1601 Market St, 9th Floor, Philadelphia, PA, 19103, United States of America, david.thurston@parsons.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: How PTC will be applied for the various types systems that are being built (I-ETME. ACSES and ITCS) and how each design impacts capacity will be discussed. The future evolution of these systems and their future impacts on capacity will also be examined.
