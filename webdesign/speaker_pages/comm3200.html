Title: Optimal Premium for Service Contracts
Author: Amit Mitra, Professor, Auburn University, College of Business, 419 Lowder Hall, Auburn, AL, 36849-5266, United States of America, mitraam@auburn.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Product manufacturing/assembly often takes place in multiple locations requiring service organizations to deliver raw materials, sub-assemblies, or assemblies from one location to another. Customers may purchase damage protection contracts based on product value. Here, we investigate the amount of premium to be charged for such contracts.
