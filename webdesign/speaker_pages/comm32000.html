Title: A Service-oriented Architecture for Emergency Healthcare Process Integration

Author: Vasso Koufi, Research Assistant, University of Piraeus, 80, Karaoli & Dimitriou St., Piraeus, GR, 18534, Greece, vassok@unipi.gr

Coauthor(s): Flora Malamateniou, George Vassilacopoulos, Mikaela Poulymenopoulou

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation concerns a prototype system that aims at integrating the processes carried out by both the ambulance service and the emergency department of a hospital to deal with emergency medical cases. The prototype system has been developed on the grounds of a service-oriented architecture and involves the Athens Ambulance Service (AAS).
