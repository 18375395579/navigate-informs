Title: Disaster Preparedness & Response at American Red Cross
Author: Rosemary Parnell, Red Cross, ParnellR@usa.redcross.org
Coauthor(s): Elizabeth Critchley
Year: 2008
Abstract: The disaster response of the American Red Cross includes shelter, food, health services and to assist people affected by disaster to to resume their normal daily activities independently. Logistics within the American Red Cross is responsible for all activities related to the procurements of goods and services, acquisition and release of facilities, availability of supplies and equipment, warehousing, transportation, and maintenance of the national fleet of disaster vehicles.
