Title: The Impact of Social Capital On Realizing a Trust-based Social Network

Author: Haoyan Sun, PhD student, University of Washington, Mackenzie 339, Seattle, WA, 98115, United States of America, haoyan@uw.edu

Coauthor(s): Yong Tan

Year: 2013

Abstract: How to build up trusts has been a central topic of research in social networks. In this paper, we base on social capital theory to investigate what factors can influence an individual’s trust network in an online community. Specifically, we propose that an individual’s (1)reputation of helpfulness, (2)activeness in the community, and (3)popularity in the network can increase his/her social capital, which will further win him/her trusts from the other network members.
