Title: Strategies for Public/Private Infrastructure Investment and Management
Author: John Gibson, VP Operations Research and Planning, CSX Transportation Inc., 500 Water Street, Jacksonville, FL, United States of America, John_Gibson@csx.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: In order to meet the projected growth in freight and passenger demand over the next 20-30 years, partnerships between freight railroads and public agencies will be very critical. The process used and the role of operations research and train simulation techniques in addressing such issues at CSX will also be described.
