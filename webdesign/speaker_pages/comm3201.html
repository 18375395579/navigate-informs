Title: Managing Offshore Software Projects: Antecedents of Project Performance and Customer Satisfaction
Author: Sriram Narayanan, narayans@kenan-flagler.unc.edu
Coauthor(s): Sridhar Balasubramanian, Jay Swaminathan
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study the antecedents of project performance and customer satisfaction in offshore software projects. We develop a conceptual model and validate our hypotheses using a structural equations model. In particular, we focus on the nature of task and type, duration and size of the software project.
