Title: Robust Risk-based Resource Allocation for Counter Terrorism

Author: Don Kleinmuntz, University of Southern California, c/o Strata Decision Technology, 2001 S. First St, Suite 200, Champaign, IL, 61820, United States, dnk@strata-decision.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: A method for allocating funds to counter terrorism measures under incomplete information is described. The method uses robust portfolio allocation methods that identify efficient portfolios when information on risk, benefits, and costs is limited. An example of allocation of funds to infrastructure protection measures is presented.
