Title: Understanding the G in GIS for Business
Author: Emil Boasson, Central Michigan University, Ronan 309, Mount Pleasant, MI, United States of America, boass1e@cmich.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: GIS is an emerging but growing area in IS/IT/MIS curriculum. A better understanding of G is needed when teaching GIS to business school students. A case is made to illustrate how GIS concepts and tools can be incorporated with other business courses such as finance, marketing, and international business.
