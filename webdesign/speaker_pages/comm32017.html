Title: Workforce Communication Program: Instruction That Works and Assessment Built from Executive Input

Author: Judith Norback, Dir. of Workplace & Academic Communication, Stewart School of ISyE, GA Tech, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, jnorback@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Tris Utschig, Rebecca E. Burnett, Garlie Forehand

Year: 2008

Abstract: Students benefit by learning to communicate as the workplace demands. This presentation will cover 1) the instruction, 2) self-report assessment showing the instruction works, and 3) a new scoring system for performance based on the literature and executives' as well as faculty input. Instructional and assessment materials will be shared.
