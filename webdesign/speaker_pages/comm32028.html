Title: Integration of Online and Offline Channels in Retail

Author: Antonio Moreno-Garcia, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, a-moreno@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Santiago Gallino

Year: 2013

Abstract: Increasingly, retailers are integrating their offline and online channels to reduce costs or to improve the value proposition they make to their customers. Using data from a leading online retailer of eyewear, we study the effect of opening brick and mortar showrooms to display inventory that can be ordered online.
