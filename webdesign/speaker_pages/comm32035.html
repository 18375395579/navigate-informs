Title: Dual Careers - Challenges and Creative Solutions
Author: Ana Muriel, Associate Professor, University of Massachusetts Amherst, 160 Governors Drive, Amherst, MA, 01003, United States, muriel@ecs.umass.edu
Coauthor(s): Rosemary Berger, Pinar Keskinocak, Anna Nagurney, Lawrence Snyder, Grace Lin
Year: 2007
Abstract: The panelists will share their experience and advice on the hard task of optimizing the dual careers of spouses while balancing work and family. A lively discussion should follow.
