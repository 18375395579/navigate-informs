Title: Organizing Remanufacturing Operations: A Transaction Cost Perspective

Author: Pinar Martin, PhD Student, SDA Bocconi, Operations and Technology, Management Dept, Milan, Italy, pinarmartin@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Daniel Guide

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study uses the transaction cost economics approach to build an analytical framework to understand the organizational choices for remanufacturing processes. A conceptual model is developed to test the relationships between the transactional attributes and vertical integration decisions in product recovery and to identify the strategic implications of these governance forms.
