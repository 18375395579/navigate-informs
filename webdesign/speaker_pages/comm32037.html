Title: Strategies for Enhancing the Quality and Quantity of Pedagogical Research in OR/MS: Panel Discussion

Author: Salwa Ammar, Professor of Managment Science, Le Moyne College, 1419 Salt Springs Road, Syracuse, NY, 13214, United States, Ammar@lemoyne.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen Powell, Ronald Wright, Donna Llewellyn, Armann Ingolfsson

Year: 2006

Abstract: Meaningful discussions of scholarly work on teaching and learning are now emerging in a variety of disciplines. The need for this body of knowledge is well accepted. Much debate is taking place on the appropriate form of credible scholarship, where and how it should be disseminated, as well as what defines its value. The panel in this session will lead a discussion on the status of pedagogical scholarship in OR/MS, reasons for its limited availability and strategies for its advancement.
