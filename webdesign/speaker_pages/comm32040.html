Title: Explore Equivalence of Accelerated Testing Plans Using a Principal Stress Decomposition Approach

Author: Haitao Liao, Assistant Professor, Wichita State University, 1845 Fairmount St., Industrial & Manufacturing Eng Dept, Wichita, KS, 67260-0035, United States, haitao.liao@wichita.edu

Coauthor(s): Elsayed Elsayed, Zhaojun Li

Year: 2007

Abstract: One challenge in ALT is to determine an efficient stress profile. We explore the equivalence of various ALT plans involving complex stress profiles. A stress decomposition approach is investigated, which decomposes a complex stress profile into principal stress elements. From the results, equivalent optimal test plans can be determined and optimized. The research finding is validated through a resistor ALT experiment involving both electrical and thermal stresses.
