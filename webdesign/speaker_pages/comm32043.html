Title: Knowledge Development and Knowledge Transfer in New Product Development Projects

Author: Wenli Xiao, University of San Diego, School of Business Administration, Olin Hall 335, 5998 Alcala Park, San Diego, CA, 92110, United States of America, wenlixiao@sandiego.edu

Coauthor(s): Cheryl Gaimon, Janice Carrillo

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce a dynamic model that characterizes linkages among three stages of engineering activities in an NPD project: prototyping, pilot line testing, and production ramp-up. The manager pursues knowledge development and transfer over time. Through knowledge transfer from a source to recipient stage, the manager enhances the recipient’s knowledge development capabilities. We examine how the effectiveness of knowledge development, prior knowledge, and feedback impact the manager’s strategies.
