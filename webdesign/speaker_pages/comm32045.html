Title: Integrating Heterogeneous Routine Hospital Data to Reduce Unnecessary Variations in Care at Emergency Departments
Author: Sudi Lahiri, University of Warwick, International Manufacturing Centre, Coventry, CV4 7AL, United Kingdom, S.Lahiri@warwick.ac.uk
Coauthor(s): Darek Ceglarek
Year: 2012
Abstract: Unnecessary variations in care in emergency departments result in patient diverting from care pathways and inefficiencies in healthcare systems including avoidable re-attendances and readmissions. Variation in care is also noted among patients with co-morbidities. This study integrates heterogeneous routine hospital data to comprehensively model patient journeys reducing healthcare system inefficiencies.
