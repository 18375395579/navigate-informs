Title: Why are Banks Still Opening More Branches? The Impact of On-line Channel on Offline Channel Capacity
Author: Mei Xue, Assistant Professor, Boston College, Walace E. Carroll School of Management, 140 Commonwealth Ave., Chestnut Hill, MA, 02467, United States, xueme@bc.edu
Coauthor(s): Lorin Hitt, Jun Zhang
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this paper we study the impact of offering virtual, self-service channel (e.g. Internet banking) on a service firm’s capacity investment in the existing physical, employee service channel (e.g. bank branches). In particular, we explore the issue of the “missing?of channel substitution. We first present a two-channel model and then test the major conclusions from the model using a large data set from retail banking.
