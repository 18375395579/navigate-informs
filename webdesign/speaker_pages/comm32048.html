Title: RFID in Containerization
Author: Panagiotis Tsilingiris, Management Science & Engineering Dept., Stanford University, Palo Alto, CA, United States, tsilipan@yahoo.com
Coauthor(s): Harilaos Psaraftis, Dimitrios Lyridis
Year: 2007
Abstract: This presentation addresses the issue of RFID utilization in containerization from a practical standpoint. To that end, we report the status and perspectives of RFID in container transportation and handling. Moreover, since new processes are considered indispensable to match the revolutionary characteristics of RFID, we describe container handing processes re-engineering. Finally, we discuss Management Science opportunities that have emerged within this new area of research.
