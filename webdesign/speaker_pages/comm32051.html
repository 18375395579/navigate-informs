Title: QSR-related Curriculum Development (I)
Author: Shiyu Zhou, Assistant Professor, University of Wisconsin - Madison, 1513 University Ave., Madison, WI, 53711, United States, szhou@engr.wisc.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Recent technological innovation and methodological development raise new challenges to the QSR-related curriculum. A panel of discussants will share their experiences and perspectives in teaching the QSR-related courses at both the undergraduate and graduate levels, and exchange with the audience the ideas and information that might be helpful to reshape the QSR curriculum.
