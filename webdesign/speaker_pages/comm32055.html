Title: Adversarial Classification

Author: Bowei Xi, Assistant Professor, Purdue University, xbw@stat.purdue.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Many data mining applications are faced with active adversaries. In all these applications, initially successful classifiers could degrade easily. We investigate the possibility of an equilibrium in this seemingly never ending game, where neither party has an incentive to change. We develop a game theoretic framework where the equilibrium behavior of adversarial learning applications can be analyzed, and provide a solution for estimating the equilibrium point.
