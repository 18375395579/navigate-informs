Title: Technology Strategy Using Business Roadmapping Model
Author: Sang Hyung Ahn, College of Business Adm., Seoul National University, Seoul, KR, Korea, Republic of, shahn@snu.ac.kr
Coauthor(s): Chang Won Lee, Sungmin Kang
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper presents a technology strategy using business roadmapping model in terms of product, market, and technology. The roadmapping model is utilized for analyzing current technology in an industry. This study suggests that decision-makers be able to gain strategic advantages by an business raodmapping modeling process planning in an industry and other similar settings. Correspondence:CW Lee
