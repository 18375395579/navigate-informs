Title: Decision Analysis Student Paper Award
Author: Jeffrey Stonebraker, Decision Sciences, A2RT -- Analysis Applications, Research and Technologies, Biomedical Data Sciences, GlaxoSmithKline, jeffrey.s.stonebraker@gsk.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: The Student Paper Competition Award is given annually to the best decision analysis paper by a student author, as judged by a panel of the Decision Analysis Society of INFORMS. Students who did not complete their Ph.D. prior to May 1, 2005 are eligible for this year’s competition.
