Title: Specifying Objectives for Decisions is Not Easy
Author: Ralph Keeney, Research Professor, Fuqua School of Business, Duke University, Box 90120, Durham, NC, 27708-0120, United States, keeney@duke.edu
Coauthor(s): Kurt Carlson, Samuel Bond
Year: 2006
Abstract: Our recent experiments indicate that many individuals cannot list an appropriate set of objectives for their own important decisions. Individuals identify only about half of their objectives. Furthermore, using their own priorities, they omit objectives as important as those they identify. Suggestions for improving the specification of objectives are given.
