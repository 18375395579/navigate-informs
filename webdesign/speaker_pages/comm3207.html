Title: Strategic Learning with Constrained Agents

Author: Fidan Boylu, Assistant Professor, University of Connecticut, One University Place, Stamford, CT, 06901, United States, Fidan.Boylu@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Gary Koehler, Praveen Pathak, Haldun Aytug

Year: 2007

Abstract: In Strategic Learning, the principal anticipates modification of attributes by agents. In many cases, agents are constrained on how much an attribute can be modified (modifications may need to belong to a set of possible changes like moves in checkers or chess). We explore attribute adjustment by constrained agents.
