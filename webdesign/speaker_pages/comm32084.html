Title: Physician Variation in Resource Utilization
Author: Jillian Berry, Doctoral Candidate, Harvard University, Soldiers Field, Boston, MA, 02478, United States of America, jberry@hbs.edu
Coauthor(s): Anita Tucker
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study the variation in physician resource utilization in two academic Emergency Departments (ED) and how this variation is affect by resource availability.
