Title: Social Enterprising Elderly Healthcare within Community Care Depots in Taiwan
Author: Ruey-Fa Lin, Feng Chia Univestiy, Taichung, Taiwan - ROC, rflin@fcu.edu.tw
Year: 2013
Abstract: The community care ideologies have been increasingly stressed for their convenience in providing effective, accessible, acceptable, affordable and efficient services in concerns for the aged. Inventing community care depots is characterized by socially value public services for almost a decade in Taiwan. Elderly people are being served and satisfied with the delivered provisions by different indigenous corporates within cultural contexts in their own civil society at large.
