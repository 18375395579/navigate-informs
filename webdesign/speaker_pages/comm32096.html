Title: Theoretical Approaches to Public Health: Women and HIV
Author: Jing Chong, Post-Doctoral, Pennsylvania State University, IST Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, chongjing79@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Lynette Kvasny
Year: 2012
Abstract: In order for women to gain more knowledge from the HIV/AIDS prevention information on government health websites and obtain the desired behavior change, they need to be able to find the information and need to receive culturally relevant information. In this paper, I will review the literature in public health education and communication strategies, and health-related information behavior. I will then develop a theoretical framework that synthesizes concepts from this literature.
