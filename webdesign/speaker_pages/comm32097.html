Title: A New Dual Representation of Entropic Risk Measures
Author: Andrew Lim, Associate Professor, IEOR Department, 4141 Etcheverry Hall, University of California, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, lim@ieor.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Peng Li, George Shantikumar
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present a new duality result for entropic risk measures which generalizes in a non-trivial way the well known relationship between relative entropy and exponential utility.
