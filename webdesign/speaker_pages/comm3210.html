Title: Enhancing Knowledge Base with Knowledge Transfer

Author: Si-Chi Chin, The University of Iowa, 3087 LIB, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, si-chi-chin@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): W. Nick Street

Year: 2012

Abstract: This presentation motivates the problem of Knowledge Base Enhancement (KBE), discusses the research methodology and proposes knowledge transfer network to describe the dynamic process of knowledge transfer. This interdisciplinary research crosses the study area of information filtering, machine learning, knowledge representation, and network analysis. It aims to explore and identify knowledge transfer methods to address the challenge of incomplete labelled data.
