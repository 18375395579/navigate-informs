Title: Pairing Decision Analysis and Analytics at Chevron
Author: Larry Neal, Chevron, 6001 Bollinger Cyn Rd, San Ramon, CA, 94583, United States of America, LarryNeal@chevron.com
Coauthor(s): Margery Connor
Year: 2013
Abstract: Major Capital Projects in the oil and gas industry are reaching extraordinary levels of size and complexity. Complexity in particular presents challenges modeling the investment alternatives in a manner consistent with good decision quality. Chevron's DA Community partners with our Analytics CoE to overcome these analytical barriers, incorporating discrete event simulation and other techniques to simplify the economic modeling yet maintain a high degree of transparency and decision quality.
