Title: Pricing CDO Tranches: Optimization approach
Author: Alex Veremyev, PhD Student, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, averemyev@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Stan Uryasev, Oleksandr Nakonechnyi
Year: 2008
Abstract: This research presents a framework for pricing Collateralized Debt Obligation tranches. It applies maximum entropy principle and its appropriate modifications for estimating default distribution implied by the market prices (spreads) of CDO tranches.
