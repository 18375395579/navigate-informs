Title: Evaluating an Automatic Data Sharing Tool for Municipal Governments

Author: Stacy Hobson, IBM T. J. Watson Research Center, 19 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, NY, 10532, United States of America, stacypre@us.ibm.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Coordination of data from multiple departmental applications are critical for municipalities to provide services to its citizens, yet the prevalence of silos between applications requires significant amounts of manual data sharing. We present our work in creating the Shared Data Manager (SDM) ?a tool to for loosely-coupled and customizable application integration and findings of our study on the use of the SDM as a means to improve data sharing and coordination within municipal governments.
