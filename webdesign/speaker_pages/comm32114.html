Title: Determining the Best Strategy for Mission Growth in Remote Locations

Author: Andrea Muchinsky, andrea.muchinsky@gmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: We used value focused thinking and multiple objective decision analysis to help an element of the U.S. Department of Defense decide on the best strategy for growing missions at locations far removed from headquarters operation. This analyses demonstrated the benefit of increasing mission capability along with the costs of new facilities and decentralized leadership.
