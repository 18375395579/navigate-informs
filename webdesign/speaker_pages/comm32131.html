Title: Medical Imaging Capacity and its Impacts on Energy and Sustainability

Author: Janet Twomey, Professor, Wichita State University, Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering, 1845 Fairmount St, Wichita, 67206, United States of America, janet.twomey@wichita.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: The research presented here highlights a new area for energy improvement in healthcare ?the energy and material use link to patient outcome decisions and alternatives.The outcomes of a study investigating the impact of medical imaging capacity decisions on energy and sustainability will be presented for a medical center and the nation’s healthcare system. Research sponsored by: NSF CMMI 1037961 and NSF CMMI 0946342.
