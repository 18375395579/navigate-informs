Title: The Value of Attaching the Correct Revenue to an Airline Booking
Author: Igor Cakulev, Senior Development Scientist, PROS, 3100 Main Street, Suite 900, Houston, TX, 77002, United States, icakulev@prosrm.com
Coauthor(s): Andrew Boyd
Year: 2006
Abstract: As a result of the way airline tickets are sold, carriers frequently use an approximation of the revenue they will pocket when they sell a ticket. This raises the question: what is the value of using the exact revenue? The business problem is described and ways to overcome it are discussed, and results are presented showing the achievable revenue improvements.
