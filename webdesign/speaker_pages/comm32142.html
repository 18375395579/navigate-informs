Title: Systems and Methodology for Environmental Compliance
Author: Pitipong Veerakamolmal, IBM Corporation, 125 Pleasant Street, #125, Brookline, MA, 02446, United States, pitipong@us.ibm.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: Electronics enterprises are facing mounting pressure to create “green?supply chains. Specifically, all OEMs selling products in the European Union most conform to the RoHS directive. This directive applies even if the product being sold is manufactured elsewhere. This presentation discusses a comprehensive solution through which manufacturers can meet the directive’s mandates.
