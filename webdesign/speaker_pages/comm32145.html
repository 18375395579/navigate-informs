Title: Three Keys to Successful Research Projects: Training, Teamwork, Timing
Author: Mike Veatch, Gordon College, Department of Mathematics, 255 Grapevine Rd, Wenham, MA, 01984, United States of America, Mike.Veatch@gordon.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: Our experience is that undergraduates can make significant contributions to O.R. research projects, spanning from software and numerical tests to model formulation, bounds, and derivations. Success hinges on picking highly motivated students, a carefully focused training program, building a project team, and creating sufficient time for student and advisor.
