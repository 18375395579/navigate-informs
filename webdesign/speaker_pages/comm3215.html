Title: IT Investments and the Structure of Production

Author: Fengmei Gong, University of Calgary, Haskayne School of Business, Calgary, Canada, fgong@ucalgary.ca

Coauthor(s): Barrie Nault

Year: 2012

Abstract: We examine the potential for determining how investments in information technology (IT) from an industry and its value chain partners affects the input-output structure of production.
