Title: Workforce Cross Training and the Learning Effect

Author: David Nembhard, Faculty Advisor, Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, dan12@psu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In the modern service and manufacturing work environments, where workers are often asked to become skilled at many things, there is a need to calibrate the amount of cross training. One possible view is to balance the benefits of multi-functionality against the training costs required to achieve it. Several models are discussed in the context of simulation results in various scenarios.
