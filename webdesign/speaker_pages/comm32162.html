Title: The Bundled Payments Care Improvement Initiative of the CMS: A Normative Analysis
Author: Mili Mehrotra, Assistant Professor, The University of Minnesota, 321 19th ave south, #321, Minneapolis, MN, 55406, United States of America, milim@umn.edu
Coauthor(s): Diwakar Gupta
Year: 2013
Abstract: CMS has introduced the Bundled Payments for Care Improvement Initiative. The initiative is a method of combining payment for all service providers involved in an episode of care to a single payment. This initiative encourages care coordination and removes financial incentives of providers to increase volume without improving quality of care under the fee-for-service payments. We analyze the efficacy of this initiative in achieving the CMS?goal to maximize the welfare of medicare enrollees.
