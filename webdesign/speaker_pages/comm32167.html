Title: Exploring Service Delivery in the United States Coast Guard
Author: Tad Drozdowski, US Coast Guard, Tad.F.Drozdowski@uscg.mil
Coauthor(s): Tugrul Daim
Year: 2008
Abstract: This paper proposes a framework for the Coast Guard to provide a better customer service with enhanced service provider-stakeholder interactions and better insights from front-line service providers and stakeholders
