Title: Modeling Predator UAV Operations
Author: Alan Johnson, Associate Prof of Logistics, Air Force Institute of Technology, AFIT/ENS, 2950 Hobson Way, Bldg 641, W-P AFB, OH, 45433-7765, United States, alan.johnson@afit.edu
Coauthor(s): Michael Kretser
Year: 2007
Abstract: Operation Iraqi Freedom accelerated the Predator Unmanned Aerial Vehicle's transition from development to operations, which precluded normal testing and capabilities refinement before use. As such, the interrelationships of Predator vehicle, logistics support, and ground control system componentry are not well understood. We present our progress and plans for simulating Predator flight operations and ground regeneration activities.
