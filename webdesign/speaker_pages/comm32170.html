Title: The Dynamics of Term Structure and Volatility of Freight Rates

Author: Amir H Alizadeh, Faculty of Finance, Cass Business School, London EC1Y 8TZ, UK, United Kingdom, a.alizadeh@city.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Nikos K Nomikos

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine the impact of the term structure of freight rates on the volatility of the spot market, using data from January 1999 to September 2006. The findings would be useful for investors in various diverse industries (ranging from banking and finance to utility companies and trading houses) who are interested in the expanding and dynamic freight derivatives market.
