Title: Benchmarking Warehouse Performance

Author: Andy Johnson, Assistant Professor, Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Texas A&M University, 241 Zachry, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, ajgcvt@yahoo.com

Coauthor(s): Leon McGinnis

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Internet-based Data Envelopment Analysis System (iDEAs) for System-based Self-assessment of Warehouse Operations is a tool available via the Internet. It allows a user to compare the performance of their warehouse to a database of warehouses in order to understand their current performance level. This presentation will describe the most recent results of a review of 390 warehouses, the trends identified, the relationships between performance and a variety of practices and attributes.
