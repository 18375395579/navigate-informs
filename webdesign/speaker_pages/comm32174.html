Title: Successful Business Execution - Strategic, Operational and Solutions Architecture Trifecta
Author: Ripal Goel, Principal Systems Engineer, Raytheon Integrated Defense Systems, 6620 Chase Oaks Blvd., Plano, TX, 75023, United States of America, Ripal_Goel@raytheon.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Increasing level of complexity in technical solutions, varying customer mission needs, and business goals with aggressive ROI, requires an integrated approach that allows for seamless business execution. Unfortunately, robust and smooth collaboration does not exist between the business, stakeholders and technical communities. The agile approach focuses on aligned architecture development. The trifecta and methodical approach influences business decisions, while providing best value solution.
