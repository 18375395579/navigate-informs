Title: Mentoring
Author: Cynthia Barnhart, Professor, MIT, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Room 1-235, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, barnhart@mit.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: As a Professor of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Engineering Systems, and Co-Director of the MIT's Operations Research Center and the MIT Center for Transportation and Logistics, I have had several mentoring experiences, both on the giving and receiving end. I will try to describe some of lessons I have learned, and my assessment of what works and what doesn't work.
