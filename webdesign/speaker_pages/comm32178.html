Title: BizSimz - Flexible Educational Simulations

Author: Chris Huston, Founder, BizSimz, 8 Sachem Circle, #5BizSimz, West Lebanon, NH, 03784, United States of America, chris.huston.t10@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: An overview of BizSimz simulations (FactorySim, CapacitySim, RmSim, FlexCap, SIP, BottleneckSim and QueueSim), discussing the options available for customizing the learning experience. See a demonstration of how to run the competitive simulations during a class session.
