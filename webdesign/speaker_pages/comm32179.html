Title: Exploring the trade-off between emission and travel time in daily transport related choices

Author: H. M. Abdul Aziz, Ph.D. Candidate, Purdue University, 150 Arnold Dr. Apt. 15, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, haziz@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Satish Ukkusuri

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research explores the mechanism of travel related short-term choices that involve trade-off between emissions and travel time. We investigate the heterogeneity in trade-off values at different traffic states and different choice contexts (route vs. departure time), and the heterogeneity across the individuals due to variation in taste and pro-environmental attitudes.
