Title: Modeling Distributed Customer Base Behavior under Uncertainty in a Reuse Environment

Author: Vijitashwa Pandey, RA, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews, 117 Transportation Building, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States, vijitashwa@yahoo.com

Coauthor(s): Deborah Thurston

Year: 2006

Abstract: Efficient environmentally conscious product design requires rigorous understanding of customer preferences with strict conformance to government regulations. We present a methodology for modeling preferences of a widely distributed customer base under uncertainty, in a reuse environment, to ensure that the government, manufacturer and the customers speak the same language.
