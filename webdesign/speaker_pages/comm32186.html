Title: Retirement Planning: The Effects of Health Care Costs
Author: Margret Bjarnadottir, MIT, 77 Mass Ave., MIT E40-149, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, margret@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas
Year: 2007
Abstract: Health care costs can intimately affect one's financial well-being. A big portion of one's lifetime health care costs are incurred during retirement, and therefore taking these costs into account when planning for retirement is critical. We propose a way to model the uncertainty of health care costs, and investigate the effects of health care costs on different investment and insurance strategies for retirement.
