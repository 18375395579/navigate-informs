Title: Operational Innovation through Continuous Automation
Author: Larisa Shwartz, IBM, Hawthorne, NY, United States of America, lshwart@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Genady Grabarnik
Year: 2013
Abstract: Modern service management focuses on the development of tools and methods for improving service’s efficiency and quality. It’s desirable to run a service in a fully automated environment, but hardly achievable. In this paper we discuss data driven Continuous Automation process: examine automatobility assessment process, which identifies activities of the next greatest impact; and review how to estimate and measure gains from a process automation of the divers service delivery environments.
