Title: Planning and Optimization of Postal Distribution Networks with TransIT

Author: Tore Gruenert, General Manager, GTS Systems and Consulting GmbH, Raiffeisenstr. 10, Herzogenrath, NW, 52134, Germany, gruenert@gts-systems.de

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk we present the decision support system TransIT. TransIT is an interactive planning and supervision system for vehicle routing and dispatching. TransIT is used in different fields of distribution planning and dispatching and combines state-of-the-art optimization technology with modern software technology for GPS routing, electronic mapping, and web and mobile communication.
