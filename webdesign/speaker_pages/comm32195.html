Title: Early Career Tips for Tenure and Promotion
Author: Mark Ferguson, Associate Professor, Georgia Tech, 800 W Peachtree St, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, Mark.Ferguson@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Serguei Netessine, Max Shen, Karen Smilowitz, Esma Gel
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this panel session, we will discuss the expectations that junior faculty in engineering and business schools face. The panelists will share their own experiences regarding the tenure process. The goal of the panel is to highlight the measures with respect to publications, service, teaching and time management issues, and provide effective strategies to be successful in the tenure process. Part of the panel will be dedicated to interactive discussion with the audience.
