Title: Innovative Applications in Analytics Award: History
Author: Michael Gorman, Professor, University of Dayton, 300 College Park, Dayton, OH, 45469, United States of America, michael.gorman@udayton.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Mike will recount the 2-year history of the Innovative Applications in Analytics Award, describing the philosophy of the award, and the history of submissions and finalists.
