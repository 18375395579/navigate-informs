Title: Energy Objectives for the U.S. Department of Defense

Author: Jay Simon, Naval Postgraduate School, 699 Dyer Rd, Monterey, CA, United States of America, jrsimon@nps.edu

Coauthor(s): Eva Regnier

Year: 2013

Abstract: Energy is a crucial issue to many organizations within the United States Department of Defense (DoD). However, these organizations provide differing objectives. We examine strategy and policy documents from DoD and related organizations to determine an appropriate set of objectives to evaluate potential decision alternatives related to energy.
