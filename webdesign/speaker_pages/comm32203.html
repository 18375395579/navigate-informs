Title: Scheduling the scheduling task: A time management perspective on scheduling

Author: Jan Fransoo, Professor, Eindhoven University of Technology, P.O. Box 513, Pav F4, Eindhoven, Netherlands, J.C.Fransoo@tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Jose Antonio Larco Martinelli, Vincent Wiers

Year: 2013

Abstract: Using a field study we develop a framework to describe the scheduler job’s from a time perspective, separating endogenous and exogenous factors. The framework provides information on how the scheduler may take decisions to be more responsive and time efficient.
