Title: How Much and How Heartfelt: The Relationship Between Buzz, Sentiment, and Sales

Author: Jonathan Schler, jonathan.schler@buzzmetrics.com

Coauthor(s): Kate Niederhoffer, Rob Mooth, Jonathan Gordon

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper we analyze the relationship between volume and sentiment of online discussions in relation to sales. Our research offers empirical evidence to classify the conditions under which buzz and sales are optimized. Potential implications offer guidance to manufacturers in strategizing their marketing effort for new initiatives.
