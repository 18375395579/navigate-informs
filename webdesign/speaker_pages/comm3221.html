Title: Overview of the 2006 DAAG conference in Baltimore, Maryland
Author: Jack Kloeber, Director, J&J Pharma Group Portfolio Management, 700 Route 202 South, Raritan, NJ, 08869, jkloeber@prdus.jnj.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: Each year the Decision Analysis Affinity Group organizes a 2-3 day conference for practitioners of Decision Analysis. This year’s venue was the beautiful Inner Harbor of Baltimore. The program was of high quality and was well attended, with over 80 participants from more than 25 companies. I will describe the DAAG charter and hit some of the highlights of the conference content, where all the presentations were focused on solving real decision analysis problems in these companies, including governmental and not-for-profit organizations.
