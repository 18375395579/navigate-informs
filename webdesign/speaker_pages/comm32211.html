Title: Who Benefits when Prescription Drug Manufacturers Offer Co-pay Coupons?
Author: Greg King, PhD Candidate, University of Michigan, Department of Industrial and Operations, Ann Arbor, MI, United States of America, gjking@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Xiuli Chao, Izak Duenyas
Year: 2012
Abstract: Prescription drugs have played a meaningful role in the rising cost of health care in the United States. In an effort to manage cost, insurance companies induce patients to choose less expensive medications by making them pay larger copayments for more expensive drugs. However, drug manufacturers have defended market positions in recent years by offering co-pay coupons. The goal of this paper is to understand the impact of co-pay coupons in a competitive environment with strategic patients.
