Title: Improving Global Healthcare Delivery through Robust Analytical Methods
Author: Jennifer Campbell, Clinton Health Access Initiative, United States of America, jcampbell@clintonhealthaccess.org
Coauthor(s): Elizabeth McCarthy, Paul Domanico
Year: 2013
Abstract: Clinton Health Access Initiative (CHAI) will introduce their work on global health issues related to evidence-based research and operations research. Members of the CHAI Applied Analytics Team will discuss their use of OR methods for improving healthcare delivery in resource limited settings.
