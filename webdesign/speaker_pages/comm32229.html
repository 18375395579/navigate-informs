Title: Modes of Experimentation to Facilitate NPD Knowledge Management

Author: Janice Carrillo, Associate Professor, University of Florida, PO Box 117169, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, janice.carrillo@cba.ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Cheryl Gaimon

Year: 2008

Abstract: We introduce an analytic model to assess the dynamic investments in prototyping, pilot line experimentation, and on-line experimentation as well as the time to release a new product to the market. We obtain insights concerning the interactions between these knowledge generating activities and the value of that knowledge for future products.
