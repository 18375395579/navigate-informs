Title: Teaching and Assessing Workforce Presentation Skills in Capstone Design and Intro to Statistics

Author: Judith Shaul Norback, Director of Workforce and Academic Communication, GA Tech Stewart School of Industrial & Systems Engineering, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, judith.norback@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Paul Griffin, Nicoleta Serban

Year: 2007

Abstract: The following information will be described about Georgia Tech's Stewart School of ISyE's workforce presentation instruction used in Capstone Design and introduction to Statistics: the development of the instruction from interviews of CEOs, managers, and IEs; its integration into the courses; and assessment results. Instructional materials will be shared.
