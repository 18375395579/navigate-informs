Title: The Value of Being Social: How Bloggers Attract Followers

Author: Hailiang Chen, City University of Hong Kong, Department of Information Systems, Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, chenhl.2007@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Yu Hu, Prabuddha De

Year: 2012

Abstract: In a blog community, for a blogger to build readership and gain popularity, ultimately, the key should be to write good quality content. However, from a marketing perspective, how much additional value does being social with her readers bring about to the blogger? This study intends to quantify the value of being social in the blogging context and to examine how bloggers should manage their conversations with readers.
