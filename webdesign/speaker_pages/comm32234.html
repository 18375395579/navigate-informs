Title: Creativity Operators and Idea Matrix
Author: Jianxi Luo, New York University - Poly, 5 Metrotech Center, LC401, Brooklyn, NY, United States of America, jluo@nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Victor Tang
Year: 2012
Abstract: Despite the importance of creativity to innovation and entrepreneurship, a formal model of idea generation and evaluation is lacking. In this paper, we present a formal introduction of creativity operators and formulate them. Then, we propose a formal method for assessing and comparing the creativity of ideas or solutions.
