Title: Optimal Control of Dosage Decisions in Controlled Ovarian Hyperstimulation

Author: Miao He, Graduate student, Tsinghua University, Beijing, 100084, China, hem03@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Lei Zhao, Warren Powell

Year: 2008

Abstract: We formulate an MDP model for the controlled ovarian hyperstimulation (COH) cycle in the IVF-ET therapy. We then study the impact of misclassification of patients?responsiveness to gonadotropin dosages on their treatment cycle outcomes.
