Title: Effectiveness of Location-based Advertising: A Randomized Field Experiment Comparing PC with Mobile

Author: Dominik Molitor, LMU Munich, Geschwister-Scholl-Platz 1, Munich, Germany, molitor@bwl.lmu.de

Coauthor(s): Philipp Reichhart, Martin Spann, Anindya Ghose

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this study, we examine the interplay between interplay between display rank, geographical distance and promotional discounts on smartphones and PCs. Using data from a large-scale randomized field experiment involving location-based promotions for smartphones and PCs, we are able to show that users?choices are negatively affected by the interplay between information on geographical distance and the display rank of the offer on the screen.
