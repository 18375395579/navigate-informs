Title: Internships, Co-ops and Student Projects: How Can You Get Real World Experience before Leaving the Graduate School Womb?

Author: Stephen Strauss, PTSM, AT&T Labs, 180 Park Avenue, Building 103 Room A009, Florham Park, NJ, 07932-1003, United States of America, sstrauss@att.com

Coauthor(s): Peter Norden, Jeff Camm, Jeffrey Alden, Michael Grant, Hena Bar

Year: 2007

Abstract: There are multiple benefits for getting real world experience before leaving graduate school. They include getting experience for your resume, sources for thesis and/or dissertation topics, help finding a job and information gathering for career decisions. Come and find out more.
