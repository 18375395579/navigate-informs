Title: Optimizing Warranty Returns Networks: The Case of a Consumer Electronics Manufacturer
Author: Baptiste Lebreton, Senior Research Fellow, INSEAD, TOM - 19, bd de Constance, Fontainebleau, MI, 77300, France, baptiste.lebreton@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Stephan Satijn, Atalay Atasu, Luk Van Wassenhove
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present the results of a recent project performed with an OEM in Western Europe. This project intended at identifying potentials to improve the trade-off between cost efficiency and customer satisfaction / reactivity in the warranty returns network. Next to the MIP-based approach used, we also address the issue of managing incentives and service level agreements in a decentralized network of LSPs.
