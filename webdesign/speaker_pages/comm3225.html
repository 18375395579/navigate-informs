Title: Service and Price Differentiation in Grid Computing
Author: Inbal Yahav, UMD, 4358 VMH, RH Smith School of Business, College Park, United States of America, iyahav@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Itir Karaesmen, Louiqa Rashid
Year: 2008
Abstract: Motivated by the grid computing, we analyze a service system with multiple types of customers and a shared resource. We investigate admissions control and scheduling decisions when customers are heterogeneuous in their service frequency, usage predictability, urgency of the service, delay penalties, and price sensitivity.
