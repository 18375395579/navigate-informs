Title: Anonymous Data Collection Protocol
Author: Rajeev Kumar, University Of Connecticut, Hill side Road, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States, Rkumar@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Ram Gopal, Robert Garfinkel
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop flexible, cryptography-based anonymous data collection protocol that can collect data from individuals without the use of a trusted third party. Our protocol is flexible in that it can be easily modified depending on the type of threat or desired level of security. The protocol is also robust against malicious participants.
