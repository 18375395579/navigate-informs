Title: Inventory Productivity and Financial Performance in the US Retail Sector
Author: Vishal Gaur, Assistant Professor, Leonard N. Stern School of Business, New York University, 44 West 4th Street, 8-72, New York, NY, 10012, United States, vgaur@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Marshall Fisher, Ananth Raman
Year: 2006
Abstract: We use public financial data on US retailers to investigate whether high inventory turnover is positively associated with financial performance as measured by contemporaneous and abnormal stock returns of firms.
