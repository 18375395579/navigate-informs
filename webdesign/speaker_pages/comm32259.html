Title: To Change China: Creating Cultural Change Through Wine Marketing in Taiwan

Author: Michael Turton, PhD Student, National Chengkung University, Da Hsueh Rd, Tainan, Taiwan - ROC, turton.michael@gmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Wine importers in Taiwan faced consumers who were ignorant of how to drink wine, and whose drinking culture was incompatible with wine consumption. How did foreign wine marketers change the way consumers in Taiwan perceived and used wine? This study will replicate of market survey work done in the late 1980s and early 1990s by foreign wine marketers. Individuals working in wine marketing in Taiwan will be interviewed, to document how changes in the drinking culture were carried out.
