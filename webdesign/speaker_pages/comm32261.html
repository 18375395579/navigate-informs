Title: Industry/University Collaboration: The Use of Design and Project Courses
Author: Karla Hoffman, Professor, George Mason University, Mail Stop 4A6, SEOR Department, 4400 University Drive, Fairfax, VA, 22030, United States, khoffman@gmu.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Seniors in systems engineering take a one-year design course, while all graduate students take a one-semester project course. We divide classes into teams working with an industrial partner. These courses have improved our student's analytic skills and enhanced our relationships with multiple companies. This talk describes the successes and challenges of running such courses.
