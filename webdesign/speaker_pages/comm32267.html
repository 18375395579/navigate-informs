Title: Building Collaborative Networks for the Fight Against Undernourishment with Decision Conferencing

Author: Andres Lartigue Debian, Fundación MARA, Marcelo T. de Alvear 1261 of 29, Buenos Aires, BA, Argentina, alartigue@binova.com.ar

Year: 2007

Abstract: Fundación MARA facilitated a series of Decision Conferences for a group of representatives that came from various organizations and companies from the agricultural sector in Argentina. An NGO who pursues this goal named CONIN, would serve as the base of a network committed in the fight against undernourishment. The DA methodologies applied on this project, still in progress, helped to build trust between all the participants and on a best way forward to achieve this goal.
