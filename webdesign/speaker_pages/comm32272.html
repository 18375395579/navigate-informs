Title: AESA: Decision Analysis in Search for Safer Skies
Author: David Rios Insua, Professor on Statistics and Decision Sciences, Royal Academy of Sciences, Calle Valverde, 22, Madrid, Spain, david.rios@urjc.es
Coauthor(s): Pablo Hernandez-Coronado, Esperanza Herraiz, Jorge Valero, Veronica Elvira
Year: 2012
Abstract: We describe the pioneering efforts of the Spanish Agency for Air Transportation Safety in using Decision Analysis within the State Safety Programme. We illustrate modeling issues in forecasting threats and their severities, identifying and communicating the most severe ones, allocating resources to mitigating them, and delivering safe sky policies in public-private partnership.
