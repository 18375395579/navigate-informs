Title: Product Development Performance Improvement Through Efficient Use of Prototypes

Author: Ryan Pereira, D.ENG Candidate, University of Michigan, 2822 Longview Ave, Rochester Hills, MI, 48307, United States, rpereira@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeff Liker

Year: 2007

Abstract: The primary purpose of this research is to understand how efficient use of prototypes can enhance PDP. There is a preference for virtual prototypes but we have also uncovered that excessive reliance on this tool can lead to poor quality. Literature review shows a gap in knowledge about how firms use prototypes. Our research will develop propositions on the behavior of successful PD firms.
