Title: Customer Oriented Learning in Entrepreneurial Firms

Author: Ilknur H. Tekin, Doctoral Student, Portland State University, P.O. Box 751, Eng. & Tech, Management, Portland, OR 97207, Ilknur@pdx.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper aims to study the interactions between the entrepreneurial firm and its customers from a learning perspective. The focus is on the impact of learning from the customers on the firm’s internal processes and its performance. The findings of an interview conducted with an entrepreneurial firm’s founder & CEO are presented in this paper. The paper is at the development stage, aiming to lead towards a detailed research paper on understanding the performance effects of learning from the customers in entrepreneurial firms.
