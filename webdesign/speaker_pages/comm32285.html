Title: Modeling the Operational and Delay Impact of Air Traffic Control Automation Tools

Author: Megan Smirti, University of California at Berkeley, NEXTOR, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, msmirti@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Xing Chen, Mark Hansen

Year: 2007

Abstract: This study examines the use and impact of Traffic Management Advisor (TMA) during its early stages of deployment at Chicago Center. The focus on early stage deployment creates a methodological challenge due to sporadic usage, possibly leading to spurious results if simple with/without comparisons are made. In an effort to isolate the impact of TMA, an alternate method is employed. We find evidence that TMA did not increase capacity but did reduce capacity variability.
