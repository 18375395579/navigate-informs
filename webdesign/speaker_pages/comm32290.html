Title: Inventory Performance of Auto Dealerships: The Role of Local Market Conditions

Author: Gerard Cachon, Professor of Operations and Information Management, University of Pennsylvania, 543 JMHH, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, cachon@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Marcelo Olivares

Year: 2006

Abstract: We collected detailed data on the particular vehicles held by dealerships. The goal of this research is to explain heterogeneity in dealership inventory holdings. Of particular interest is the role of local demographics, competition and brand on the propensity of dealerships to hold inventory.
