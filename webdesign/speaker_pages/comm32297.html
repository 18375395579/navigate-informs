Title: Early Detection of Bioterrorism: A Combined Social and Spatial Network Analysis

Author: Inbal Yahav, Assistant Professor, Bar Ilan University, Graduate School of Business, Ramat Gan, 52900, Israel, inbal.yahav@biu.ac.il

Coauthor(s): Sean Barnes, Bruce Golden, Edward Wasil

Year: 2012

Abstract: Bioterrorism is a significant threat to the US. The spread of certain agents may be difficult to distinguish from a seasonal flu outbreak. We simulate epidemic and bioterrorism outbreaks and propose a method for differentiating between the two scenarios when only a small proportion of the population becomes infected.
