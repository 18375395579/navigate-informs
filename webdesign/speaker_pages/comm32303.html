Title: Schedule Robustness, Block-times, and Turn-times
Author: Vinayak Deshpande, Purdue University, 4024 Rawls Hall, West Lafayette, IN, United States of America, vinayak@exchange.purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Milind Sohoni
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this paper we analyze airline schedule data with respect to flight block-times and aircraft turn-times. We study how these decisions affect the schedule's robustness.
