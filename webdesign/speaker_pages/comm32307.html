Title: Analysis of Nanoparticle Self-assembly Processes

Author: Chiwoo Park, Assistant Professor, Florida State University, 2525 Pottsdamer St., Tallahassee, FL, 32310-6046, United States of America, cpark5@fsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Taylor Woehl, James Evans, Nigel Browning

Year: 2013

Abstract: Precise morphology control of nanoparticles requires a full understanding of the nanoparticle growth mechanism. Recently, in-situ electron microscopy has shown promise for the direct observations of nanoparticle growth, but analyzing a large amount of in-situ observations is challenging. We present a method for analyzing the in-situ observations to determine the key characteristics of nanoparticle growth processes, growth/aggregation rates, and particle size distribution as a function of time.
