Title: OR in the Classroom: Helping the Homeless

Author: Laura McLay, Assistant Professor, Virginia Commonwealth University, Statistics & Operations Research, 1001 W Main Street, Richmond, VA, 23284, United States, lamclay@vcu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation summarizes the results and lessons learned from managing a real-world community service class project. The project partnered with a local agency that provides services for the homeless in Richmond, Virginia. It engaged graduate students studying multiobjective decision analysis in applying value focused thinking to the agency’s case management services.
