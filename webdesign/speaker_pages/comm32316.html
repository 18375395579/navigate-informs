Title: Strategic, Multimodal Communication to Analyze Critical and Complex Problems
Author: Rebecca E. Burnett, Dir. of Writing & Communication, School of Lit, Communicatn & Culture, GA Tech, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, Rebecca.Burnett@lcc.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Andrew Cooper
Year: 2008
Abstract: Communication is one critical component of effective decision-making. Simply put, professionals explore possibilities, analyze problems in complex contexts, interpret results, and implement solutions, all of which require strong communication skills. We describe our undergraduate communication curriculum, a strategic and multimodal approach that integrates written, oral, visual, electronic, and nonverbal communication.
