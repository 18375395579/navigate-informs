Title: Constraints Analysis

Author: Kevin James, Senior Scientist, NASA, Ames Research Center, Moffett Field, CA, 94035, United States, kjames@mail.arc.nasa.gov

Year: 2006

Abstract: This is related to the investment portfolio under NGATS, but would look at how various facets of the NAS constrain performance, starting with the current system and then applying NGATS improvements. The idea being to make sure JPDO is addressing the critical issues and in the right order.
