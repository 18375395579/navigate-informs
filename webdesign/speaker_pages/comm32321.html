Title: Credit Derivatives and Risk Aversion

Author: Siu-Tang (Tim) Leung, Princeton University, Dept. of OR&FE, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, siutang@Princeton.EDU

Coauthor(s): Ronnie Sircar

Year: 2008

Abstract: The recent turbulence in the credit markets highlights the need for an alternative pricing mechanism. A number of observations suggest that risk aversion may help explain some common market phenomena. We discuss a utility-based valuation model for credit derivatives, and analyze the effects of risk aversion and other factors on the prices.
