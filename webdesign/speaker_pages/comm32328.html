Title: Transportation 2030- Trends and Challenges
Author: Martin Savelsbergh, Professor, H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Georgia Tech, 765 Ferst Dr, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States, mwps@isye.gatech.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: The Transportation Science and Logistics Society is including a panel discussion on transportation trends and challenges in its cluster of presentations. Panel members will present their views on this topic followed by a general discussion. Panel members: Brenda Dietrich (Director, Mathematical Sciences, IBM TJ Watson Research Center & INFORMS President), Michael Meyer (Professor, Civil & Environmental Engineering, Georgia Tech), Chelsea White (Chair, Industrial & Systems Engineering, Georgia Tech).
