Title: Insights from Teaching Business Analytics to MBAs in a Flipped Style
Author: Stephen Powell, Dartmouth College, Amos Tuck School of Business, Hanover, NH, United States of America, Stephen.G.Powell@tuck.dartmouth.edu
Coauthor(s): Galit Shmueli
Year: 2013
Abstract: We have taught business analytics to MBAs in both traditional and flipped classroom styles, and in different settings. We will discuss the pros and cons of the flipped approach and its applicability to different schools and cultures.
