Title: Listening to the Field: Law Enforcement Needs for the OR Community
Author: John Hollywood, Operations Researcher, RAND Corporation, 1200 South Hayes Street, Arlington, VA, 22202, United States of America, johnsh@rand.org
Year: 2012
Abstract: We present the results of recent studies on law enforcement's needs for new technologies, including needs for OR analysis. We discuss how these needs are very different from traditional OR for policing, reflective how policing philosophies are changing from focusing on crime response to crime prevention.
