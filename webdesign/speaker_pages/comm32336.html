Title: Manifold Learning for Discovering Variation Patterns in Noncontact Dimensional Metrology Data

Author: Daniel Apley, Northwestern University, Dept. Industrial Engineering, Evanston, IL, 60208-3119, United States of America, apley@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): George Runger, Edwin Shi

Year: 2013

Abstract: Imaging and laser optical dimensional metrology systems are becoming increasingly common in manufacturing quality control. Although fitting parametric geometric dimensioning and tolerancing features is well established, there has been little prior research on how to utilize the data to discover the nature of nonparametric part-to-part variation patterns that have not been pre-identified and pre-modeled. We propose the use of manifold learning techniques to extract and visualize such information.
