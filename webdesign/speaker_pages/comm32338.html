Title: Using Integrated Disaster Simulation to Enhance Situation Awareness

Author: Shengnan Wu, Research Assistant, University of Pittsburgh, 1048 Benedum Hall, Industrial Engineering, Pittsburgh, PA, 15261, United States of America, SHW14@pitt.edu

Coauthor(s): Carey Balaban, Matthew Kelley, Bopaya Bidanda, Larry Shuman, Ken Sochats

Year: 2008

Abstract: The term situation awareness has appeared in the psychology literature since the 80s. It is complementary to decision making with imperfect information. Since computer simulation models are capable of looking into the future, they can be used to aid decision makers where information is often ambiguous, but improves as situational awareness improves, such as a disaster scene. This paper develops an integrated disaster simulation system for making appropriate decisions in these situations.
