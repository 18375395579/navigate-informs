Title: Optimized Tactical Traffic Planning: What is Required for Success?
Author: Frank Boyle, Union Switch & Signal Inc., 1000 Technology Drive, Pittsburgh, PA, 15219, United States, cfboyle@switch.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: US&S' Optimizing Traffic Planner (OTP) is a tactical traffic planning system that optimizes the movements of trains in real time. Successful development of such a system and its deployment across a class 1 railroad's network present significant challenges ranging from technical to practical to organizational, and even philosophical. This presentation is a recounting of the major hurdles one can expect when undertaking a project of this kind and how to clear them.
