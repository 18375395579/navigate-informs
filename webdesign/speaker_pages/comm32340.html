Title: A Dynamic Two-stage Model of HIV Transmission in MSM for Policy Evaluation

Author: Maria Vlasiou, Research Engineer II, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, vlasiou@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Paul Griffin, Tom Gift, Julie Swann

Year: 2007

Abstract: The role of bathhouses and sex clubs in HIV transmission is a cause of concern. We present a dynamic HIV epidemic transmission model that includes the acute and chronic stages of HIV and parameters for two interacting populations with different characteristics. We use data from the 1997 Urban Men's Health Study as parameters for the model to study the role that bathhouses and sex clubs play in the epidemic, and we discuss the effects of various policies regarding bathhouses.
