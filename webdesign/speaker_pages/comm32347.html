Title: An Introduction to Pedagogical Research - What it is and What it is not?

Author: Donna Llewellyn, Director, Georgia Tech, CETL, 225 North Avenue, Suite 4, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0383, United States, donna.llewellyn@cetl.gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This session will explore what people mean when they talk about pedagogical or educational research. What is involved in performing this type of research, how it differs from your content/technical research, and what resources are needed. The session will be the introductory session of a series of four sessions at this conference on pedagogical research.
