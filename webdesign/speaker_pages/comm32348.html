Title: Approximating the Crowd with CrowdSense
Author: Cynthia Rudin, Assistant Professor, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, rudin@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Seyda Ertekin, Haym Hirsh
Year: 2012
Abstract: We give an algorithm for approximating the crowd, which is to find the crowd's majority vote on an item without paying all of them.
