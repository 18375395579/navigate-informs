Title: Fairness in Operations: From Theory to Practice

Author: Nikos Trichakis, Assistant Professor, Harvard Business School, Boston, MA, United States of America, ntrichakis@hbs.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We analyze the effects that fairness considerations have on operational efficiency and vice versa. Our analysis allows managers to make informed decisions about how to trade off fairness and efficiency in an operational setting. We use our findings to propose ways of improving the kidney allocation system in the US. Simulation studies suggest that our proposals could lead to an increase of life year gains by 8%, without sacrificing fairness.
