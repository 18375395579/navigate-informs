Title: Structuring Preference Ranking Markets for Early Stage Emerging Technology Evaluation
Author: Li Chen, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road Unit 1041, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, li.chen@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Paulo Goes, James Marsden, John Zhang
Year: 2008
Abstract: Selection of early stage emerging technologies is a challenging decision. Recently, several large companies are interested in using information markets as preference aggregation and group decision-making tools to rank new technologies. We set up two internal preference markets with one market using an additional play-money incentive. Our analysis focus on participant and market behavior, dynamic preference aggregation, and market performance. Results indicate promising expectations.
