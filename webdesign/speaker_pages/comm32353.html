Title: Modeling Comorbidity as a Function of Age, Race and Gender

Author: Julie Simmons Ivy, North Carolina State University, ISE Department, Campus Box 7906, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, jsivy@ncsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Fay Cobb Payton

Year: 2008

Abstract: Our goal is understand and model the impact of comorbid conditions (concurrent chronic diseases) on patient outcomes. We develop path analyses to describe relationships between comorbid conditions, charges, procedures, length of stay, and disposition as a function of age, race, and gender for patients at a large urban academic hospital.
