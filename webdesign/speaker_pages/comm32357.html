Title: Finding Three Good Alternatives in a Multi-Criteria Decision Problem

Author: Robert Taft, Lead Scientist, NSWCDD, PO Box 1497, Dahlgren, VA, 22448, United States, robert.l.taft@navy.mil

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Pareto Boundary Explorer (ParBoX) is an architecture and implementation using mathematical programming, expert systems, and statistical discrimination to nominate a handful of significantly different non-dominated solutions to multi-criteria decision problems. Problems examined include notional theater level planning for cruise missile strikes and selecting a college semester schedule.
