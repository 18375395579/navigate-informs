Title: Educational Efficiency and Productivity in Pennsylvanian Schools

Author: John Ruggiero, University of Dayton, School og Business Administration, Dayton, OH, 45469-2251, United States of America, jruggiero1@udayton.edu

Coauthor(s): Shae Brennan

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper, we apply a public sector productivity model to analyze efficiency, environmental costs and productivity of Pennsylvanian schools. Recognizing the importance of exogenous socio-economic factors of production, we apply the model of Johnson and Ruggiero (2012) to decompose overall productivity.
