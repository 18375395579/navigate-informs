Title: Off-hour Deliveries: Theory and Implementation
Author: Jose Holguin-Veras, William H. Hart Professor, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 110 8th st, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, jhv@rpi.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: The presentation discusses the economic foundations of off-hour delivery programs. This is accomplished by modeling the behavior of receivers in response to financial incentives, and the ensuing behavior of the carrier in response to both pricing and the receiver decisions concerning off-hour deliveries.
