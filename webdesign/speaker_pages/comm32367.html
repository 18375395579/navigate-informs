Title: Using Virtualization Technologies to Enable a Mobile IaaS System for Tactical Edge Data Analytics
Author: Albert Barreto, Lecturer, Naval Postgraduate School, 1411 Cunningham Road, Suite GW3006, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, abarreto@nps.edu
Coauthor(s): Dan Boger, Charles Ibatuan
Year: 2013
Abstract: Lessons learned by NPS HFN teams sent to Katrina and Haiti have shown a gap existed in the ability to respond quickly. By deploying a mobile EOC with existing HFN comms and power, C2 is enhanced with advanced analytics. The EOC in a box runs VMware to host a complete Windows and VDI suite. C2 uses ArcServer with 10 TB of local geospacial data for predictive analysis. Additionally, USHAHIDI Crowdsourcing and Lighthouse Social Networking applicaiton devloped at NPS are hosted.
