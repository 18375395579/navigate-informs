Title: Panel Discussion: How to Have an OR Career

Author: Stephen Strauss, PTSM, AT&T Labs, 180 Park Avenue, Building 103 Room A009, Florham Park, NJ, 07932-1003, United States of America, sstrauss@att.com

Coauthor(s): Michael Berliner, Russ Labe, Michael Grant, Srinivas Bollapragada, Hena Bar

Year: 2007

Abstract: Panelists from a range of industries; experience; and educational levels and backgrounds will discuss how they got into OR, how they got some of their positions and what kind of career paths they have had.
