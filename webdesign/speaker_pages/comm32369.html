Title: Call Center: Customer Information Exchange and Knowledge Factory

Author: Vijay Mehrotra, Assistant Professor, San Francisco State University, Department of Decision Sciences, 1600 Holloway Avenue, San Francisco, CA, 94132, United States of America, drvijay@sfsu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: In any large-scale services operation, potentially valuable information can be captured through interactions with customers. Despite massive investments in CRM systems, the information in these databases is rarely ever utilized to create business value. In this paper, we describe some of the challenges associated with this kind of “information value chain?and describe a successful case study of creating such a value chain for a large multi-site software technical support operation.
