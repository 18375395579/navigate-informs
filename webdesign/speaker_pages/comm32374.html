Title: Semantic Web Integration for Healthcare with Clinical Decision Support System
Author: Clarence Augustine Tee, CEO/Professor, e-Oncologist, Stanford, 3505 El Camino Real, Palo Alto, CA, 94306, United States of America, catht@e-oncologist.com
Coauthor(s): Su Xie
Year: 2013
Abstract: Clinical decision support systems are receiving great attention today for improving quality of care. This paper proposes an integrated semantic web supported by key stakeholders to incorporate a comprehensive solution in bringing precision medicine closer to patients. We find that both patients and hospitals can potentially benefit tremendously by employing this novel architecture.
