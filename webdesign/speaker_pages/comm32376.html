Title: Recent Improvements in SAS/OR Optimization Solvers
Author: Imre Pólik, SAS Institute, 100 SAS Campus Dr, Cary, NC, 27513, United States of America, imre@polik.net
Coauthor(s): Menal Güzelsoy, Philipp Christophel, Yan Xu, Amar Narisetty
Year: 2013
Abstract: We will present the latest improvements in the SAS/OR linear and mixed-integer optimization solvers including symmetry-based techniques and presolver improvements.
