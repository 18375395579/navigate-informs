Title: Pricing in Dynamic Vehicle Routing Problems in an Auction Setting

Author: Hani Mahmassani, University of Maryland, 3128 J. H. Kim Building, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, masmah@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Patrick Jaillet, Miguel Figliozzi

Year: 2006

Abstract: We formulate a pricing problem in the context of a dynamic vehicle routing environment. We discuss some properties of this problem. In addition, we present and analyze extensive numerical results of carriers bidding in an electronic marketplace.
