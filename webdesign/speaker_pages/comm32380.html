Title: Chaos on a Discrete Assembly Line

Author: Donald Eisenstein, University of Chicago, Graduate School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States, don.eisenstein@ChicagoGSB.edu

Coauthor(s): John Bartholdi, Yun Fong Lim

Year: 2007

Abstract: A natural extension of the bucket brigade model of manufacturing is capable of chaotic behavior in which the instantaneous production rate is, in effect, random, even though the model is completely deterministic. This is, we believe, the first proven instance of chaos in discrete manufacturing.
