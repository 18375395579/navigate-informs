Title: Functional Coordination, Incentives and Decentralized Decision Processes in New Product Development
Author: Stylianos Kavadias, Associate Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 W. Peachtree St., NW, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, Stylianos.Kavadias@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Jeremy Hutchison-Krupat
Year: 2008
Abstract: The NPD project selection process involves specialized knowledge regarding consumer needs and technological capabilities. Very often the team members responsible for technology development lack the necessary expertise to make accurate market assessments, and resources responsible for ‘shaping?the market lack understanding of the technology. We investigate the impact of the firm’s incentive structure on the decisions of the functions and contrast these with the centralized decision.
