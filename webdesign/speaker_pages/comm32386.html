Title: A Survey of Research in Product Support
Author: Sal Agnihothri, Professor, School of Management, Binghamton University, SUNY, Binghamton, NY, 13902-6000, United States of America, agni@binghamton.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Product support (after-sales support) encompasses all the goods and services supplied by a manufacturer to maximize the trouble-free use of a product/service by the customer. Product support is an increasingly important factor in competition. In this talk we analyze critical issues, review past research, and outline future research opportunities in product support.
