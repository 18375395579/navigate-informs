Title: Multi-stage Deployment of the Army Theater Hospital

Author: Trisha Cobb, Naval Postgraduate School, 1 University Circle, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, tacobb@nps.edu

Coauthor(s): Nedialko Dimitrov

Year: 2013

Abstract: A conflict can change overtime from full combat operations, to insurgency suppression, to local force training. Each of these conflict types requires an Army Theater Hospital to handle inflows of different types of patients--soldiers, civilians, children, and elderly--at different rates. This study analyzes the configuration of an agile Army Theater Hospital that can adapt to changing conflict through modular asset deployment.
