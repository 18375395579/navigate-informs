Title: Social Link Recommendation
Author: Masoud Makrehch, Senior Research Scientist, Thomson Reuters, 333 Bay Street, Toronto, Canada, makrehchi@gmail.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this talk, several strategies for link recommendation and prediction are discussed. First, a taxonomy of the topic is presented. Then, social data and features are briefly discussed. Several link prediction methods including (semi)supervised and unsupervised techniques, and also link prediction using hidden topics are detailed. Finally, some applications and examples are outlined.
