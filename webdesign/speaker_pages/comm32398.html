Title: Trends and Challenges in Global Application Services Delivery

Author: Fausto Bernardini, Sr Mgr, Application Services Technologies, IBM Research, 19 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, NY, 10532, United States of America, fausto@us.ibm.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Global software development has become the norm for managing complex enterprise IT. At the same time, componentization and abstraction, ofetn expressed in Service Oriented Architectures (SOA) have proven their value in managing complexity in software methodologies. In this talk I will explore the current boundaries of the practice and research in the field, review some of the most promising new approaches to the problem, and lay out the next technical challenges.
