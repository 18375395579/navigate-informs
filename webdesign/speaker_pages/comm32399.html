Title: Addressing Safety Efforts in Management and Oversight of Interdependent Systems of Systems

Author: David Wichner, Associate, Booz Allen Hamilton, 528 Stannage Ave., Albany, CA, 94706, United States of America, davidwichner@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Organizational factors have been identified as a significant source of risk for decades. Yet even with the accumulated lessons learned, few solutions have been designed to help make systems of systems more comprehensible. What are the continuing and elusive challenges in achieving higher levels of aviation safety and reliability in the face of the current, excellent safety record? What are some possible solutions to provide ‘sense-making?tools for those who manage risk across an industry?
