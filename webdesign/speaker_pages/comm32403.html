Title: Panel Discussion: Complexity in Designing Service
Author: Ralph Badinelli, Professor, Virginia Tech, Dept. of Business Information Technology, Virginia Tech 0235, Blacksburg, VA, 23060, United States of America, ralphb@vt.edu
Coauthor(s): Ray Strong, Sechan Oh
Year: 2013
Abstract: This panel represents both an academic and an industry view of the challenges to service innovation that are posed by the inherent complexity of context-dependent service environments. Each panel discussant will make a short presentation on this topic. The moderator will then stimulate a discussion on several key questions about complexity in service. The audience will be encouraged to engage in questions and comments.
