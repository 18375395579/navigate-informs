Title: Innovation in Industrial Service Operations- How People Integration Matters

Author: Gerhard Gudergan, FIR at Aachen University, Pontdriesch 14/16, Aachen, Germany, Gerhard.Gudergan@fir.rwth-aachen.de

Year: 2008

Abstract: Why are some industrial service firms more successful in innovating then others? Organization and people based integration in particular are con-sidered to be a main success factor for reaching innovation capability. How-ever, the effects of organizational design and personal linkages on successful industrial service innovation are not sufficiently explored so far. Results from a large scale empirical study in the German industrial service industry and mana-gerial recommendations are presented.
