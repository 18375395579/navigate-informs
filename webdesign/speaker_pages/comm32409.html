Title: Decomposing the Impact of Workload on Patient Outcomes

Author: Michael Freeman, Judge Business School, University of Cambridge, Cambridge, United Kingdom, mef35@cam.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Nicos Savva, Stefan Scholtes

Year: 2012

Abstract: Previous work has shown that in-patient outcomes deteriorate at high workloads. In this paper we use a detailed dataset from the Delivery Unit of a large UK hospital to investigate the operational factors that drive this this deterioration. We discuss findings and implications for hospitals.
