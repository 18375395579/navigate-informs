Title: A Multivariate Diffusion Model for Social Groups

Author: Blake McShane, Doctoral Student, University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School, 3730 Walnut St., Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, mcshaneb@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Jonah Berger, Eric Bradlow

Year: 2008

Abstract: How does the type of people who own a particular product influence others?likelihood of purchase? How do these effects vary across products? We have acquired data on over 26m cars sold in the US over the past ten years. We model the relevant (potentially latent) social groups as well as whether the adoption of a given model by one social group can be explained by previous adoption by another. We also incorporate spatial effects in our model to address the local effects of social contagion.
