Title: Revenue Optimization in Higher Education Industry
Author: Roger Gung, Sr. Manager of Business Analytics and Optimization, University of Phoenix, 4025 S Riverpoint Pkwy, Phoenix, AZ, 85251, United States of America, roger.gung@phoenix.edu
Coauthor(s): Tao Kang
Year: 2012
Abstract: Scholarship program in higher education industry is considered as promotions in marketing, so as to boost sales. In this research, we developed a non-linear optimization approach, in conjunction with students?academic behavioral models, to determine the optimal scholarship program crtiera that intends to maximize the revenue while offering grants to hi-performing financially needy students.
