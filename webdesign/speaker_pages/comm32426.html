Title: The Effects of Online Review on Collaborative Service Provision
Author: Haoying Sun, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, 320 Wehner Building, Mays Business School, TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843-421, United States of America, hsun@mays.tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Lizhen Xu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study a collaborative service setting where the outcome depends on both the service provider’s and the client’s effort levels, and the provider can prescribe the client’s effort level. The market uses various signals, such as outcomes or/and online reviews, to update its belief about the provider’s type. Furthermore, the provider’s payoff depends on the market belief. We analyze how the availability of online reviews affects the effort levels of both parties and the outcomes accordingly.
