Title: Impact Analysis of Time-dependent Road Network Tolling on Travel Choices

Author: Jing Dong, Iowa State University, 394 Town Engineering, Ames, IA, 50011, United States of America, jingdong@iastate.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper studies the effect of usage-based vehicle charges on the behavior patterns of road users based on the revealed preference survey data. Travelers?response to time-dependent network-wide toll charges includes reduction in vehicle miles traveled, combination of multiple trips and departure time shift.
