Title: The Wisdom of Small Crowds

Author: Jack Soll, Associate Professor of Management, Duke University, The Fuqua School of Business, 100 Fuqua Drive, Durham, NC, 27708, United States of America, jsoll@duke.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We examine the “small crowd?strategy for combining point estimates, which ranks judges based on a cue to expertise (e.g., last period’s performance) and averages the opinions of the top five. Although the strategy ignores many opinions, it benefits from excluding the worst judges. We analyzed 90 datasets from the domains of economic forecasting and psychology experiments. Compared to other strategies, the small crowd strategy is robust; it performs well across a wide range of environments.
