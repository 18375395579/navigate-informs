Title: Mechanism Design to Harness Volatile Renewables
Author: Golbon Zakeri, Dr., University of Auckland, 70 Symonds street, Auckland, New Zealand, g.zakeri@auckland.ac.nz
Coauthor(s): Javad Khazaei, Shmuel Oren
Year: 2013
Abstract: We will describe 3 different market clearing mechanisms, one in current practice, one proposed to enhanced the utilization of renewables and a third variant. We will demonstrate the quivalence of these mechanisms under particular conditions, describe the resulting market equilibria and compare these mechanisms in terms of social welfare.
