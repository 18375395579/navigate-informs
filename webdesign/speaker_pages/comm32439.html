Title: Impact on Decision Analysis of Minimizing Data Error Margins and Distortion
Author: Orhan Güvenen, Professor, Bilkent and Paris Duphine Universities, Bilkent Unv. SAL, LC117, 06800 Bilkent, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, gorhan@bilkent.edu.tr
Year: 2012
Abstract: The increase in quantity of the information available to access does not automatically guarantee the increase in quality and authenticity of the information. Dominantly this information is open to information distortion and to non-negligible error margins. In decision analysis high error margins and information distortion can create a substantial biased structure and very high alternative costs, it is a necessary condition to minimize error margins,information distortion, endogenize ethics.
