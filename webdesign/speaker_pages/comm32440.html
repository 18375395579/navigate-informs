Title: A Dynamic Model for Sustainable Energy Policy Making in Spain

Author: Alvaro Lopez-Peña, Comillas P. University, IIT, Santa Cruz de Marcenado 26, Madrid, 28015, Spain, alvaro.lopezpena@iit.upcomillas.es

Coauthor(s): Pedro Linares, Ignacio Pérez-Arriaga

Year: 2012

Abstract: Today, there is an important need for sound sustainable energy policies. For assessing them, mathematical models are needed, which allow to represent energy systems?evolution over the long term under different policy assumptions (trajectories). We present a dynamic model applied to Spain, which aims at representing realistic trajectories. By realistic, we mean that it takes into account important aspects of energy systems such as imperfect markets, investor’s myopia and risk aversion.
