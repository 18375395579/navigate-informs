Title: Estimating Technology Functions in Service Operations

Author: Sheneeta White, PhD Candidate, Virginia Tech, 1007 Pamplin Hall (0235), Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, sheneeta@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Ralph Badinelli

Year: 2008

Abstract: The way in which professional services transform inputs into outputs is typically unknown. Consequently decision makers, at best, can only make estimates of the underlying technology function from measures of overall efficiency. The objective of this research is to examine the breadth of technology functions which can be consistent with DEA results and the sensitivity of estimates of technology functions to aggregate data analysis.
