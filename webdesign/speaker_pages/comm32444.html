Title: Information Distortion in Decision Support Systems and Respective Alternative Costs

Author: Orhan Guvenen, Professor/Institute Director, Bilkent University, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, gorhan@bilkent.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Murat Hakan Ozturk

Year: 2007

Abstract: Information Systems can be considered as the most important decision support tool for the decision makers in business, academia and politics. In this study we are trying to analyze how manipulations in information systems may affect decision-making processes. We will evaluate the respective alternative costs of manipulated information in decision making from the decision analysis perspective.
