Title: Assessing Product Service Systems (PSS): The Danish Maritime Industry

Author: Juliana Hsuan, Professor, Copenhagen Business School, Department of Operations Management, Solbjer Plads 3, Frederiksberg, DK-2000, Denmark, jh.om@cbs.dk

Coauthor(s): Tim McAloone, Line Maria Neugebauer

Year: 2013

Abstract: We apply a management tool to classify different integrated PSS within the context of Danish maritime industry. We investigate how a firm should organize its operations to manage the transition from being a manufacturing to a servitizing company, and to provide a guideline for strategic positioning and planning of the balance between product sales and service activities.
