Title: Spillover Effects of Foreclosures on Neighborhood Property Values

Author: Len Lin, Fannie Mae, Washington, DC, United States of America, len_lin@fanniemae.com

Coauthor(s): Eric Rosenblatt, Vincent Yao

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper extends previous studies into a formal theoretical model through pricing based on comparables. We theorize that the spillover effect of a foreclosure on neighborhood property values depends on two factors. One is related to housing cycle and the other varies by time of foreclosure and its distance from the subject property. Our empirical findings are consistent with our theory.
