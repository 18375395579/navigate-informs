Title: Value of Information for the Partially Observed MDP and Partially Observed Markov Game

Author: Yanling Chang, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, changyanling@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Erera, Chelsea White III

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate the impact of varying state observation accuracy on the value function of the POMDP and POMG, extend key results for the POMDP to the POMG, and show that POMG value function changes due to changes in state observation accuracy can be quite varied and can include discontinuities.
