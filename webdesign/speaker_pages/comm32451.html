Title: Global Resourcing: Today’s Reality and Tomorrow’s Challenges

Author: Kellar Nevill, Vice President, Global Delivery BTO HR, IBM, 11 Brook Hollow Lane, Trophy Club, TX, United States, knevill@us.ibm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Global resourcing has become prevalent in the services industry. If it can be digitized, it can be offshored. In this session we will discuss today’s issues that effect this practice and tomorrow’s challenges that are inevitable as the practice grows. How you hire, train, retain and enable qualified human capital.
