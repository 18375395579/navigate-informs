Title: Text Mining Approach to Find the Key Sentences in Large Documents
Author: Sugon Cho, Korea University, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, sugoncho@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Seoung Bum Kim
Year: 2012
Abstract: Finding the key sentences and paragraphs is an important and challenging problem. In recent years, the amount of text data has grown astronomically and this growth has produced a great demand for text summarization. In the present study we applied the text mining methods to find the key sentences from large documents.
