Title: A Center for Innovation in Logistics Systems

Author: Greg Parlier, Dr., SAIC, 255 Avian Lane, Madison, AL, 35758, United States, GREG.H.PARLIER@saic.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Fully engaged in the Global War on Terror, the US Army is also committed to a comprehensive and ambitious Transformation endeavor. An analytical architecture is presented consisting of four complementary modeling approaches collectively referred to as Dynamic Strategic Logistics Planning. An organizational construct is presented to accelerate and sustain continual improvement for Army logistics and supply chain management - a Center for Innovation in Logistics Systems.
