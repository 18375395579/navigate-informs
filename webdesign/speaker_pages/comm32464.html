Title: A Game Theoretic Approach to UxV Routing and the Price of Anarchy Associated with Decentralization

Author: Michael Couche, University at Buffalo, 342 Bell Hall, University at Buffalo, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, mjcouche@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The use of UxV’s is becoming increasingly popular in the military. We aim to analyze several two-player games under different conditions in order to route UxV’s. We will then compute the loss of information between these games when compared to a centralized routing framework to identify “best practice?rules when routing.
