Title: Efficiency vs. Equity in Dynamic Air Traffic Flow Management
Author: Amedeo Odoni, Professor, M.I.T., 77 Massachusetts Avenue 33-219, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, arodoni@MIT.EDU
Coauthor(s): Michael Hanowsky
Year: 2007
Abstract: The tradeoff between efficiency and equity/fairness presents a major challenge for ATFM optimization models. Recent work on dynamic ground delay programs under uncertainty highlights the issues involved. Efficient solutions may be strongly biased with respect to flight distance and, by implication, airline and aircraft type.
