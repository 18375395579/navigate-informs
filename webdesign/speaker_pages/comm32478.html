Title: Simulating the effects of PTC systems on railroad operations

Author: Ian Martin, Director - Operations Analysis, SYSTRA, 520 Eighth Avenue, Suite 2100, New York, NY, 10018, United States of America, imartin@systra.com

Coauthor(s): Ted Jones

Year: 2013

Abstract: This presentation will discuss the simulation of train operations under a system with and without positive train control (PTC). This paper will focus on the ACSES PTC overlay system being deployed on the Northeast Corridor (NEC) by Amtrak and the various commuter railroads operating in the Northeast on and adjacent to the NEC.
