Title: Impact of Physician Styles on Patient Flow in the ED
Author: Vera Tilson, Assistant Professor, University of Rochester, Simon School, Rochester, NY, United States, Vera.Tilson@simon.rochester.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Physicians have different preferences for sequencing their work in the ED. We develop a mathematical model to examine how these preferences affect patient flows in the ED: door-to-doctor time and door-to-door time as well as the utilization of physicians.
