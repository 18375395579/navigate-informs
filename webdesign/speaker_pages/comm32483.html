Title: Valuation of Technology Investment Using an Economic Market Clearing Mechanism
Author: Chris Johnson, Researscher, GE Research, 1 Research Circle, Niskayuna, NY, 12301, United States of America, johnsonch@crd.ge.com
Coauthor(s): Jason Black, Srinivas Bollapragada
Year: 2008
Abstract: Significant infrastructure investments are being made with uncertain payoffs due to an economic value proposition dominated by regulatory incentives. A method is presented to create a market clearing mechanism to determine price and volume estimates.
