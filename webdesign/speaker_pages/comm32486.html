Title: Performance of Pinch Gesture Operations in a 2-dimensional Synchronous Task on a Multi-touch Screen

Author: Cheng-Jhe Lin, Assistant Professor, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, No.43, Sec. 4, Keelung Rd., Da’an Dist.,, Taipei, 106, Taiwan - ROC, robert_cjlin@mail.ntust.edu.tw

Year: 2013

Abstract: Gestural operations on multi-touch screens have become popular but, the pinch gesture, one of the most frequently used gestures, have not been thoroughly studied for its time performance and accuracy. The study manipulates 4 influential factors: target distance, target width, moving direction and target allowance in a factorial experiment, to see their effects on the time and accuracy of a 2-dimensional synchronous task performed with the pinch gesture.
