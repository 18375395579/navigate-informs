Title: The Influence Disaster Severity and Outcome Attribution on Travel Behavior

Author: Heather Rosoff, Research Assistant Professor, University of Southern California, CREATE, RTH-315, MC-2902, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-2902, United States of America, rosoff@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Richard John

Year: 2013

Abstract: The decision to travel by plane following a disaster event depends on the individuals?decision making under uncertainty. We examine how air travel decision making is influenced by the severity of the event and attribution of the outcome.
