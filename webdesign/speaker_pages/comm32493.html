Title: Managing Demand under Inventory Uncertainty

Author: Shaoxuan Liu, University of California, Irvine, The Paul Merage School of Business, Irvine, CA, United States, sliu02@merage.uci.edu

Coauthor(s): Rick So, Fuqiang Zhang

Year: 2006

Abstract: We develop a mathematical model to analyze the impact of inventory uncertainty on the optimal marketing and inventory decisions. Our results provide useful information for evaluating the potential benefits of adopting RFID technology to improve inventory tracking accuracy under different operating environments.
