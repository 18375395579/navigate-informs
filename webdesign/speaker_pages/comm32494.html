Title: Research Needs of the Electricity Industry

Author: Richard O'Neill, Chief Economic Advisor, FERC, 888 First St NE, Washington, DC, 20426, United States of America, richard.oneill@ferc.gov

Year: 2012

Abstract: We have 20 years of experience with competition and open access. New technologies including solar, wind, batteries, and price-responsive demand continue to penetrate the market. The ISO market design needs to reflect the impact the new technologies. Improvements in IT include better measurement, communications and algorithms. Pricing needs to be better aligned with cost causation. We will discuss the research needed to improve market efficiency.
