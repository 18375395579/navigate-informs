Title: Reliability, Risk, and High-stakes Wagering
Author: Martin Wortman, Professor, Texas A&M University, Industrial & Systems Engineering, College Station, TX, 77843-3131, wortman@tamu.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: The analytical relationship between reliability, risk, and high-stakes wagering will be examined within the context of technology assessment. Particular emphasis will be given to predictive modeling and subsequent computational methods for identifying, from among competing alternatives, that alternative having the most favorable risk.
