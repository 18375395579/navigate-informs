Title: Servitization: Disentangling the Impact of Service Business Model Innovation on Manufacturing Firm Performance
Author: Ivanka Visnjic, Professor, ESADE Business School, Spain, ivanka.visnjic@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Bart Van Looy
Year: 2013
Abstract: As manufacturing businesses operate in an ever more competitive, global economy where products are easily commoditized, innovating by adding services to the core product offering has become a popular strategy. Contrary to the economic benefits expected, recent findings pinpoint implementation hurdles that lead to a potential performance decline, the so-called ‘servitization paradox?
