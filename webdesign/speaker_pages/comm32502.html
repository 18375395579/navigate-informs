Title: Alliances in Enterprise Systems Software Markets: A Social Network Approach
Author: Ramnath Chellappa, ram@bus.emory.edu
Coauthor(s): Nilesh Saraf
Year: 2007
Abstract: Enterprise Systems Software (ESS) is a multi-billion dollar industry that produces systems components to support a variety of business functions for a wide-range of vertical industry segments. Using social network analyses as the theoretical framework, our research builds on extant findings of research in economics of standards to empirically examine ESS firm competition.
