Title: The Quality of Medical Information on Wikipedia
Author: Gerald Kane, Assistant Professor, Boston College, 140 Commonwealth Ave, Chestnut Hill, MA, 02467, United States of America, gerald.kane@bc.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Wikipedia is rapidly becoming a major source of medical information on the Internet, but little is known about the quality and completeness of this information. Even less is known about who is generating this information and how it is generated. This study explores the quality of medical information on Wikipedia and the collaborative processes that surround it.
