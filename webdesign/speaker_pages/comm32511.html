Title: Efficiently Appraising Decision Situations

Author: Debarun Bhattacharjya, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Route 134, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, debarunb@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Ross Shachter

Year: 2013

Abstract: The appraisal stage is crucial in decision analysis, helping the decision maker understand the benefits of obtaining greater parameter precision and in exploring opportunities for improving their decision situation. Being able to do so efficiently is particularly crucial for large real-world problems. In this talk, I will describe the myriad capabilities of decision circuits in this regard, using several illustrative examples.
