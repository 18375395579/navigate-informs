Title: Does the Pitcher or the Batter Control Home Plate in a Major League Baseball Game?

Author: James Cochran, Louisiana Tech University, College of Administration & Business, PO Box 10318, Ruston, LA, 71055, United States, jcochran@cab.latech.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In baseball the question of who has more influence over the outcome of a plate appearance is critical - the answer may help teams assess value of players and decide at which positions to invest. We use regression analysis and data from the 04 MLB season - frequency of particular events (hits, homeruns, etc.) for individual pitcher/batter matchups and the frequency of the event for individual pitcher and hitters over the entire season - to assess relative influence of the pitcher and the batter.
