Title: George B. Dantzig Dissertation Prize
Author: Zeynep Aksin Karaesmen, Professor, Koc University, Rumeli Feneri Yolu Sariyer, Istanbul, 34450, Turkey, zaksin@ku.edu.tr
Year: 2013
Abstract: The George B. Dantzig Disseration Award recognizes dissertations that are relevant to the practice of operations research and the management sciences. The award is designed to support greater interaction between academia and the world of practice by awarding research that combines development of novel methodology and new techniques with creative application in a practical real-world setting. Problem scope and potential magnitude of impact of research are considered. In this session, the award finalists will present their research.
