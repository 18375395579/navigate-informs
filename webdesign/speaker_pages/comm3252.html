Title: Occurrence in Aviation Safety Management of South Korea: Survey on Various Occurrence Categories

Author: Yunah Noh, Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, 291 Daehak-ro, Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, Korea, Republic of, ynnoh@kaist.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Yoonjin Yoon

Year: 2013

Abstract: Successful implementation of the aviation Safety Management System (SMS) highly depends on well-defined set of occurrences which provides important foundation for key SMS tasks. It is commonly agreed that variations in the occurrence definition among organizations hinders integrative and effective implementation of SMS even at the national level. In this paper, we present detailed analysis on commonality and differences in several existing occurrence sets from various organizations.
