Title: Balancing R&D and Marketing Tension in Startup Firms
Author: Moren Levesque, Associate Professor, University of Waterloo, Waterloo, ON, Canada, levesque@waterloo.ca
Coauthor(s): Nitin Joglekar
Year: 2006
Abstract: When should the owner of a startup firm acquire working capital and how should she distribute this capital between R&D - that improves product quality ?and marketing expenses - that builds the sales up? The industry focus is software, where both R&D and marketing are significant fractions of overall expenses. We develop a model to study optimal resource acquisition and allocation across successive stages of startup growth.
