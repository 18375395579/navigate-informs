Title: Learning Bayesian Networks for Managing Inventory of Banner Advertisements

Author: Max Chickering, Mad Scientist, Microsoft Live Labs, Max.Chickering@microsoft.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Many online publishers sell space for banner advertisements on their web pages. These publishers often give impression guarantees that complicate the delivery system due to the need to manage inventory. I will describe a delivery system that allows targeting of both groups of pages (e.g., sports pages) and information about the people browsing the content (e.g., males from Seattle). The system learns a Bayesian network from web-traffic data to predict inventory of the targetable attributes.
