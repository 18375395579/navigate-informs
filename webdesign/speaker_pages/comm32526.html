Title: Modeling Chem-Bio Effects at Airports and Seaports

Author: Phil Collins, Sr. Scientist, BBN, 1337 Park Plaza #3, O'Fallon, IL, 62269, United States of America, pcollins@bbn.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: U.S. Transportation Command's Airport and Seaport Simulation Tools (AST/SST) have been modified to provide detailed APOD and SPOD modeling of chemical and biological effects and the resulting impacts on installation capabilities. This is accomplished as part of the Joint Operation Effects Federation (JOEF) which includes agent dispersion, deposition, exposure, and human effects models.
