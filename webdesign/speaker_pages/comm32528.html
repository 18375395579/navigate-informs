Title: Panel Discussion: The Industry Job Search
Author: David Kim, Associate Prof., Oregon State University, 121 Covell, Corvallis, OR, 97330, United States, david.kim@orst.edu
Coauthor(s): Stephan Biller, Alicia Wilson, Rajiv Saxena
Year: 2006
Abstract: The panel will discuss the industry interview process and do’s and don’ts associated with the job search. In addition to comments by current and former recruiters, time will be provided for questions and answers.
