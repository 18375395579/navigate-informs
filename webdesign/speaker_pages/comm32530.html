Title: High-Low or Low-High: Product Line Introduction Strategies

Author: Oded Koenigsberg, Columbia University, New York, NY, 10027, United States, ok2018@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Eyal Biyalogorsky, Debu Purohit

Year: 2006

Abstract: Determining the specifications of new products to be introduced into the market is one of the most important and difficult tasks. In cases of sequential introduction a manager has to decide whether to direct the initial efforts towards the introduction of a high end model packed with many features, or toward a lower end model. In this paper we derive the optimal introduction strategies under different market conditions and provide guidelines to managers how they should launch their product line
