Title: Pricing CDO Tranches Using Reduced-Form Default Model

Author: Alex Nakonechnyi, PhD Student, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, PO Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611-6595, United States, nakon@ufl.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: This research presents a framework for pricing Collateralized Debt Obligation tranches. It applies maximum entropy principle to estimating default distribution implied by the market prices (spreads) of CDO tranches. This default distribution is used to price other credit derivatives.
