Title: Supporting Strategy: An Empirical Study of UK Practitioners

Author: Frances O'Brien, Associate Professor, Warwick Business School, University of Warwick, Gibbet Hill Road, Coventry, CV4 7AL, United Kingdom, f.a.o-brien@warwick.ac.uk

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper reports the latest findings of a program of research into the current practice of supporting strategy development within the UK. The paper presents an analysis of interviews conducted with UK OR practitioners regarding how they support strategy development within their organisations. The analysis identifies the activities within a strategy process that are supported and the tools used to support these activities. The paper concludes with a conceptual framework highlighting the possible roles that OR can provide in supporting strategy development within organisations.
