Title: Service Technology, Pricing, and Process Economics in Retail Banking Competition

Author: Hosun Rhim, Professor, Korea University Business School, Anam-dong Seongbuk-gu, Seoul, 136-701, Korea, Republic of, hrhim@korea.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Uday Karmarkar, David Choi

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper presents a framework that models service technology, design, access and delivery processes, pricing and competition in information intensive services, in the specific context of retail banking. We analyze two alternative service delivery processes, conventional and electronic, and examine how they affect customer costs, process economics, market segmentation and competition.
