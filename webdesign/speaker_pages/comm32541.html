Title: Value of Flexibility in Oil and Gas Projects; The Case of Deferral Options

Author: Babak Jafarizadeh, Statoil, Svanholmen 8, Sandnes, 4313, Norway, bajaf@statoil.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The timing flexibility of investments may entail extra value for oil and gas companies. However, the extent of such value creation has not been previously explored in detail. In this paper, we discuss investments with long or short-term outcome realizations and conclude that the value of deferral options tend to be higher for decisions with an earlier outcome realization.
