Title: Recent Developments in Constraint Programming at ILOG

Author: Paul Shaw, pshaw@ilog.fr

Coauthor(s): Jean-Francois Puget, Jean-Charles Regin

Year: 2006

Abstract: ILOG Solver is the leading commercial constraint programming tool, used by industry and academia alike. In this talk, we present the recent developments in constraint programming at ILOG with respect to ease of use, search procedures and solver performance.
