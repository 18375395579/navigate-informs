Title: Strategic Asset Management of Equipment in a Multi-specialty Hospital
Author: Kingshuk Sinha, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, ksinha@umn.edu
Coauthor(s): Scott Bosch, Ujjal Mukherjee, Shoubhik Sinha, Alex Cherucheril
Year: 2013
Abstract: An empirically grounded framework is proposed for optimal maintenance and service of various classes of medical equipment in a multi-specialty hospital categorized by criticality and complexity of the equipment. Hardware, software and user errors underlying medical equipment failures over time are analyzed to gain insights into differential effects of learning.
