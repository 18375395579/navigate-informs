Title: Scalable Data Analytics with Uncertainty

Author: Yingdong Lu, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, yingdong@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Mark Squillante

Year: 2012

Abstract: We provide an overview of some IBM research efforts on Scalable Data Analytics. In particular, we discuss current research problems in the space of data analytics applications and workloads together with the solution methodologies being developed to address these problems
