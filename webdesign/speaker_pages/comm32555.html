Title: Chromosomal Mapping Identifies Patterns for Progression of Breast Cancer Molecular Subtypes

Author: Gabriela Alexe, Computational Biologist, PhD, The Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard, 7 Cambridge Center, Cambridge, MA, 02142, United States, galexe@broad.mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Shridar Ganesan, Daniel Scanfeld, Charles DeLisi, Arnold Levine, Gul S. Dalgin, Gyan Bhanot, Pablo Tamayo, Jill P. Mesirov

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this study we re-analyze gene expression data of matched pairs of ductal carcinoma in situ and invasive ductal carcinoma to discover specific patterns of progression for breast cancer molecular subtypes.
