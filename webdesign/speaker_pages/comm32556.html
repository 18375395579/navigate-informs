Title: Panel Discussion: Decision Analysis: Foundations in Mathematics, Science, and Engineering

Author: Ronald Howard, Professor and Director, Decisions and Ethics Center, Department of Management Science and Engineering, School of Engineering, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, rhoward@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Winkler, James Matheson, Jim Dyer, Ali Abbas, Gordon Hazen

Year: 2006

Abstract: Decision analysis has contributed to decision-making in business, medicine, engineering, and law. Panelists discuss its origins, its relation to other disciplines, and the most appropriate academic preparation for a career in decision analysis. The panelists will first make brief presentations, then discuss among themselves, and finally invite audience participation.
