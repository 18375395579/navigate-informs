Title: Patient Scheduling in a Mobile Health Care Setting with Patient No-Shows
Author: Karen Smilowitz, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, Technological Institute M233, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60208-3119, United States of America, ksmilowitz@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Martin Savelsbergh, Samuel Ellis
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present work motivated by an ongoing collaboration with a mobile asthma care provider. Our study focuses on the scheduling challenges caused by patient no-shows. We address this issue through an improved scheduling model that incorporates incentives. We present a comparison with alternate scheduling policies.
