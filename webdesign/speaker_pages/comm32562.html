Title: The State of Research in Air Cargo Capacity Management
Author: Mikhail Nediak, Queen's School of Business, 143 Union St., Kingston, ON, K7L 3N6, Canada, mnediak@business.queensu.ca
Coauthor(s): Yuri Levin, Tanya Levin, Jeff McGill
Year: 2008
Abstract: We discuss recent developments and current trends in air cargo capacity management research. In particular, we review air cargo industry, compare passenger and cargo capacity management, and formulate main challenges related to cargo. We also discuss our recent work on itinerary flexibility combined with uncertain capacity and network nature of the problem.
