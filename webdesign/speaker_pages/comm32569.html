Title: Business Activity Networks
Author: Andre Calmon, Massachusetts Institue of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, E62-364, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, acalmon@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Jorge Sanz
Year: 2013
Abstract: Leveraging data from multiple industries, we introduce the concept of a Business Activity Network, i.e., the network formed by the union of different business processes that exist within a service company. We study the statistical properties of these networks and propose generative models that describe the growth of activities within a company. In addition, we discuss the relationship between the structure of the Business Activity Network of a company and vulnerability towards external shocks.
