Title: Empirical Analysis of a Buy-online-Pickup-in-Store Implementation

Author: Antonio Moreno-Garcia, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, a-moreno@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Santiago Gallino

Year: 2013

Abstract: Increasingly, retailers are integrating their offline and online channels to reduce costs or to improve the value proposition they make to their customers. Using a proprietary dataset, we analyze the impact of the implementation of a buy-online-pickup-in-store project.
