Title: When Should Firms Pool Purchases from a Sole-source Supplier?
Author: Bin Hu, PhD Student, University of Michigan, Ross School of Business, Ann Arbor, United States of America, hub@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Izak Duenyas, Damian Beil
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider the strategic question of whether firms (or divisions of a firm) should or should not pool their purchases when facing a sole source monopolistic supplier. We develop a principal-agent model and develop insights that are surprisingly different than those in classical inventory pooling.
