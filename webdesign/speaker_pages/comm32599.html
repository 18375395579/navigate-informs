Title: Marketing Science and the Google Online Marketing Challenge Overview
Author: Jamie Murphy, Associate Professor, University of Western Australia, School of Business, M261, Stirling Highway, Crawley, WA, 6009, Australia, jmurphy@biz.uwa.edu.au
Year: 2008
Abstract: This lively session focusing on the Google Online Marketing Challenge, a global student competition, will interest marketing scientists in two complementary areas ?teaching and research. The inaugural Challenge comprises about 10,000 students in 400 universities and 40 countries. After a brief overview of the Challenge and search advertising, the panelists will share marketing science applications in search advertising and a meta-analysis of about 2,000 advertising campaigns in the Challenge.
