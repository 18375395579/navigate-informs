Title: Minimum Bid Increments and Last-Minute Bidding in Online Auctions

Author: Alex Rogers, University of Southampton, Southampton, S017 1BJ, United Kingdom, acr@ecs.soton.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Esther David, Jeremy Schiff, Nicholas Jennings

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the effects of minimum bid increments within online auctions (particularly eBay style auctions). We show the while last minute bidding (sniping) is an effective strategy against incremental bidders, in general, a bidder should attempt to be the first sniper to bid (i.e. it should 'snipe before the snipers').
