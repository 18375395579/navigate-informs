Title: Global Supply Chain Networks
Author: Nitish Jain, INSEAD, 1 Ayer Rajah Avenue, Singapore, 138676, Singapore, Nitish.jain@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Karan Girotra, Serguei Netessine
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study the evolution of global supply chain networks of publicly traded U.S. retail and wholesale firms. We construct these networks using a novel data-set that is compiled by processing over 10 million import transactions by U.S. firms from global suppliers.
