Title: Multicriteria Model for Price/Promotion Optimization
Author: Natalia Viktorovna, SAS Institute, SAS Campus Drive, Cary, 27513, United States of America, Natalia.Viktorovna@sas.com
Coauthor(s): Maarten Oosten
Year: 2013
Abstract: Marketing efforts are commonly used to drive customer demand. This research focuses on modeling the pricing as well as the selection and allocation of promotions, specifically those in retail industry (coupons, circulars, etc.) with a novel multicriteria approach. A known algorithm as NSGA-II is then used to solve the mathematical formulation.
