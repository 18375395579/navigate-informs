Title: Startup Strategies for Participation in Open Innovation Systems
Author: Lee Fleming, Professor, Harvard Business School, 485 Morgan Hall, Boston, MA, 02163, United States, lfleming@hbs.edu
Coauthor(s): Dave Waguespack
Year: 2006
Abstract: While research has shown why individuals contribute to open innovation communities, little research has considered why a firm would contribute. We investigate how firms should engage open innovation communities and demonstrate strong causal effects upon liquidity events for startups in telecommunications technology.
