Title: The Impact of Sector Dynamic Rules on the Choice of Management Strategy in Highly Uncertain Market and Technology Situation

Author: Olga Kokshagina, PhD Student, Centre for Scientific Management (CGS), CGS Mines ParisTech, 60 Boulevard Saint-Michel, Paris, 75272, France, olga.kokshagina@mines-paristech.fr

Coauthor(s): Pascal Le Masson, Patrick Cogez, Benoit Weil

Year: 2012

Abstract: Management strategy choice in high uncertainty is predefined by sector dynamics. There are sectors “pushed?by technology or “pulled?by market. But what happens in the situations when technology and markets are unknown (ex., semiconductors). Which strategy to adopt in these conditions? We create a simulation model to test the influence of different sector dynamics on the choice of management strategies. Our results show new strategies that minimize risks and exploration costs in double unknown.
