Title: An Application of a MCDM Model in a Supplier Selection Problem

Author: Adiel Almeida-Filho, Assistant Professor, Universidade Federal de Pernambuco, CAIXA POSTAL 7471, RECIFE, PE, 50630971, Brazil, atalmeidafilho@yahoo.com.br

Coauthor(s): Jônatas Almeida, Ana Paula Cabral, Adiel Almeida

Year: 2013

Abstract: This work presents an application of a model resolving criteria influence by flexible elicitation in a case study for a supplier selection problem. Supplier selection is naturally multicriteria decision problem that considers besides costs, strategic factors that influence the operation success. With the outsourcing trend growing along organizations this is a situation that arise frequently in the literature.
