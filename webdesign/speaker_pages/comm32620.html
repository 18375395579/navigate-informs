Title: Multi-stage Integer Programs in Power Networks
Author: Daniel Bienstock, Columbia University, dano@ieor.columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Abhinav Verma
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present continuing work on various models for the dynamics of a blackout. These models lead to difficult multi-stage mixed-integer programs. In particular, we are interested in optimal 'attack' problems which server as a paradigm for exposing hidden weaknesses in a network. Joint work with A. Verma.
