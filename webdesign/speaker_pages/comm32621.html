Title: LCA Methods for Estimating Supply-chain Contaminant Emissions for Construction Projects

Author: Seth Guikema, Assistant Professor, Johns Hopkins University, Department of Geography and, Environmental Engineering, Baltimore, MD, 21218, United States of America, sguikema@jhu.edu

Coauthor(s): Neethi Rajagopalan

Year: 2008

Abstract: Supply chains have been shown to account for a significant portion of total contaminant emissions from construction projects, and environmental assessments that ignore supply chain effects miss this portion of the environmental impact of these projects. However, life-cycle assessment for construction projects is challenging. This talk provides an overview and comparison of the two basic methods for incorporating supply chain effects into a life-cycle assessment for construction projects.
