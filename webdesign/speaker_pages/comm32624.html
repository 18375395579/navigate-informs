Title: Use of Socio-Technical Roadmapping in Transportation Infrastructure Planning

Author: Scott Grasman, Professor, Rochester Institute of Technology, 81 Lomb Memorial Drive, Rochester, NY, 14623, United States of America, segeie@rit.edu

Coauthor(s): Suzanna Long

Year: 2012

Abstract: This research creates a socio-technical roadmap for transportation infrastructure as part of a study that was used to create the Missouri State Rail Plan. The roadmap illustrates partnerships, processes, and infrastructure that comprise the socio-technical elements of a strategic rail network and showcases short-, medium-, and long-range planning decision points.
