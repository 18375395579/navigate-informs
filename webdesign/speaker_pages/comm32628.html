Title: Rethinking Teaching Decision Analysis

Author: Greg Parnell, Consultant, Innovative Decisions Inc., Vienna, VA, United States of America, gparnell@innovativedecisions.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Two years ago, I wrote the Wiley & Sons' Handbook of Decision Analysis with three colleagues with extensive consulting experience which was published in Mar 2013. As a result of the research and writing, I fundamentally changed my spring Decision Analysis course. I will present my insights and experience with the revised course.
