Title: Assessing the Integration of Wind Energy Producers in the Spanish Electricity Market
Author: Miriam Bueno-Lorenzo, Assistant Professor, Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Av Universidad, 30, Dpto. Ingeniería Eléctrica, Leganés (Madrid), 28911, Spain, mblorenz@ing.uc3m.es
Coauthor(s): Julio Usaola, M. Ángeles Moreno
Year: 2013
Abstract: This work aims to study the participation of wind energy producers in the Spanish electricity market. Hence a tool which maximizes the incomes of wind power producers in electricity markets is employed. Through a stochastic process a strategic bidding algorithm is designed. It takes into account the variables involved in the process, namely wind power forecasts, market prices, as well as their forecasts. Moreover, a risk analysis is included in the results.
