Title: Where Do Creative Interactions Come from? The Role of Tie Content and Social Networks
Author: Manuel Sosa, Assistant Professor of Technology and Operations Management, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, manuel.sosa@insead.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Why are some interactions more likely to generate novel and useful ideas than others? Integrating research on creativity, knowledge sharing, and social networks this work identifies how the creative interaction outcomes are influenced by the properties of the knowledge exchanged and the social networks surrounding the interaction. Hypotheses are tested in a sociometric study completed in a software development firm.
