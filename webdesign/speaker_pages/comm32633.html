Title: Social Network-based Marketing, Revisited
Author: Shawndra Hill, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, shawndra@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Adrian Benton
Year: 2013
Abstract: We compare the predictive performance of a social network-based recommendation engine across product and user categories. The prediction task is to predict which of more than 600 brands in 15 industry categories web users follow on Twitter. We explore various features of both users and brands and show how these features interact when making social network-based predictions.
