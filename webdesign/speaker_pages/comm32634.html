Title: Analyzing the Long-run Impact of the Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative
Author: Yihsu Chen, Assistant Professor, University of California, Merced, 5200 N. Lake Rd, School of Engineering, R262, Merced, CA, 95343, United States, yihsu.chen@ucmerced.edu
Coauthor(s): Karen Palmer, Benjamin Hobbs, Dallas Burtraw, Steve Gabriel, Soora Kim, Anthony Paul
Year: 2007
Abstract: The Regional Greenhouse Gas Initiative (RGGI) is a joint effort by several Northeast States to control for regional CO2 emissions. The objective is to reduce CO2 emissions from the power sector 10% below the current level by 2019 using a cap-and-trade program. This analysis sought to evaluate the competitiveness of the state power sector and any increases in electricity costs for consumers in Maryland.
