Title: Forecasting and Pricing for Small and Medium Enterprise Hotels in the Netherlands

Author: Dirk Sierag, CWI Amsterdam, Science Park 123, Amsterdam, 1098 XG, Netherlands, dirk@cwi.nl

Year: 2013

Abstract: Small and medium enterprise (SME) hotels face different forecasting and pricing challenges than big chain hotels, while most research is conducted on the latter. Therefore proper forecasting and pricing methods need to be developed for SME hotels. We use recent data of several Dutch SME hotels to explore the problems and opportunities for different types of hotels. Furthermore, different basic and advanced forecasting methods are applied to the data and the results are compared.
