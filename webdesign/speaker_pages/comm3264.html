Title: Transit Route Choice with Partial Arrival Information at Stops
Author: Peng Chen, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, chenp07@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Yu Nie, Qianfei Li
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper, we propose a method to determine the probability of boarding each line at a stop when arrival information of only a subset of available lines is provided to passengers. The impact of information availability will be illustrated with small numerical examples.
