Title: Optimizing Complex Decisions through Flexible Business Processes Management and Provenance

Author: Roman Vaculin, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, vaculin@us.ibm.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a framework for support of complex collaborative decision processes, such as team development project planning or acquisition, that integrates flexible declarative business process management techniques, human generated insights and analytics based insights. The approach enables insights into and optimization of complex collaborative decisions through the decision provenance and business process intelligence techniques, such as querying and mining of the provenance graphs and records.
