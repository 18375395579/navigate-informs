Title: Strategic Capacity Planning for New Patient Oncology Appointments
Author: Emma Liu, BC Cancer Agency, 600 West 10th Ave., Vancouver, BC, V5Z4E6, Canada, Emma.Liu@bccancer.bc.ca
Coauthor(s): Marianne Taylor, Antoine Saur?, Scott Tyldesly, Martin Puterman, Claire Ma
Year: 2013
Abstract: As a consequence of an increasing demand for on-going cancer care, a significant amount of oncologist’s time is spent on follow-up appointments with patients. This limits their availability and increases wait times for new patient appointments. This talk describes a British Columbia study of oncologists?patterns of practice intended to provide insights about long-term workload incurred by new patient admissions and how different patterns of practice affect new patient wait times.
