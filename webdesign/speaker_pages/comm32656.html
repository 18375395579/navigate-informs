Title: Joint Session QSR/DM: Panel Discussion: Funding Opportunities
Author: Hui Yang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave. ENB118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, huiyang@usf.edu
