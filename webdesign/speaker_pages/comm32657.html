Title: George B. Dantzig's Influence on My Research

Author: Arthur Veinott, Jr., Professor, Department of Management Science and Engineering, Stanford Universitry, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, veinott@stanford.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This talk will discuss George B. Dantzig's influence on several areas of my research. The areas include Leontief substitution systems, least d-majorized network flows, dynamic programming and supply chains. I will illustrate how my work benefited from his genius, generosity and warmth as a friend and colleague at Stanford for 40 years.
