Title: Revenue Optimization for Rental Industry
Author: Hossam Zaki, Vice President, Zilliant, Austin, TX, United States of America, hossam.zaki@zilliant.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: This presentation will provide insights and comparisons between the revenue management (RM) problem for the One-Way Truck Rental industry and other traditional RM problems (e.g. equipment rental, car rental, airline and air cargo). We will also describe the main components of a typical RM system and several design and deployment options.
