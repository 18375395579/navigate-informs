Title: Teaching Out Loud
Author: David Morton, Professor, The University of Texas at Austin, Graduate Program in Operations Research, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, morton@mail.utexas.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Rick Rosenthal advised students, and mentored young colleagues, in a fashion that sometimes resembled thinking out loud. His effectiveness was remarkable. This presentation will attempt to illustrate some ideas for teaching in this spirit.
