Title: A DEA Framework to Performance Risks: New Concepts and Applications to Supply Chain Management

Author: Chien-Ming Chen, PhD Candidate, Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University, PO Box 1738, Burg. Oudlaan 50, Rotterdam, 3000DR, Netherlands, cchen@rsm.nl

Year: 2008

Abstract: Performance variations of chain members have a far-reaching consequence for the entire supply chain. However, performance risks have been a neglected topic in the literature. Existing studies mostly focus on market related risks and the risk of supply disruption. I develop a framework comprising Data Envelopment Analysis (DEA) and simulation techniques to analyze performance variations under two general problem scenarios; I also provide some potential applications of the framework to SCM.
