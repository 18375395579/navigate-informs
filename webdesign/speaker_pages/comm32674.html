Title: Teaching Business Students to Give Managerial Talks of Analytical Work
Author: Thomas Grossman, University of San Francisco, tagrossman@usfca.edu
Coauthor(s): Judith Shaul Norback, Jill Hardin, Garlie Forehand
Year: 2007
Abstract: Two models and 11 skills useful for teaching students how to present analytical work to managers will be described. The relevant workforce presentation skills are based on the Workforce Communication Program at Georgia Tech's Stewart School of ISyE. The skills will be described along with preliminary assessment results. Instructional materials will be shared.
