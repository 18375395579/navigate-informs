Title: Panel Discussion: Service Industry -The Next Frontier for Technology Management
Author: Daniel Berg, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY, 12180, United States, bergd@rpi.edu
Coauthor(s): Dundar Kocaoglu, James C. Spohre, Arnold Reisman
Year: 2006
Abstract: Service industry represents 75% of the U.S. economy today; and its backbone is technology. Yet, while the manufacturing sector has improved impressively through proper application of technology management concepts and methodologies, the service industry has lagged behind manufacturing very badly in that context. This panel will explore the ways technology management can and should be applied to the health, financial, educational, government and other sectors of the service industry.
