Title: Integrating Fire and Forest Harvest Planning

Author: Mauricio Acuna, Programme 3 - Harvesting and Operations, CRC for Forestry, Hobart, Australia, mauricio.acuna@utas.edu.au

Coauthor(s): Wenbin Cui, David Martell, Cristian Palma, Andres Weintraub

Year: 2007

Abstract: FireSmart forest management is based on the assumption that the timing and location of forest access roads, harvesting, and silvicultural treatments can fragment forest landscapes and reduce fire spread potential. We developed a DSS (that includes fire ignition, spread and harvesting sub-models) that can help develop integrated fire/forest management plans to exploit such interactions. We describe our DSS and its application to a forest management unit in the province of Alberta, Canada.
