Title: Panel: 10 Years Anniversary for the QSR Section: Past, Current and Future

Author: Judy Jin, Associate Professor, Department of Industrial and Operations Engineering, University of Michigan, IOE 2855, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States, jhjin@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Jye-Chyi (JC) Lu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The panel is to review and access the QSR's formation and growth in the past 10years, and to identify opportunities and define the future directions of the QSR. The panel will consist of 4~5 speakers, who are selected to represent the QSR organization leaders and three focused research areas leaders in Quality, Statistics, and Reliability respectively.
