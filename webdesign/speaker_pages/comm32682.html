Title: Community-Level Peacebuilding

Author: Vicki Johnston, SAIC, 6725 Odyssey Drive, Odyssey II - Room 2039, Huntsville, AL, 35806, United States of America, vicki.v.johnston@saic.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Processes and Technology Insertion Strategies for Renewing Civic Security: Emphasizing a bottoms-up approach to Peace-Building at the local levels, this discussion will explore the high-level processes of facilitated, fully-represented community emergency planning and training activities.
