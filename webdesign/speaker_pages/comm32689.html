Title: Using Visual Analytics to Enhance the Understanding of Occupational Safety Data
Author: Yao-Te Tsai, PhD Student, Auburn University, 3323 Shelby Center, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States of America, yzt0007@tigermail.auburn.edu
Coauthor(s): Fadel Megahed, Stephen Swartz
Year: 2013
Abstract: Highway traffic safety has remained a leading public safety threat with significant economic impacts. We develop a visualized data-mining package to examine the factors that associate with fatal crashes on the federal highway system. A case study is then introduced to demonstrate the effectiveness of the tool. The result can be used for further statistical analysis.
