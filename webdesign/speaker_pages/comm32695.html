Title: Sequential Procedures for Large-Scale Ranking-and-Selection Problems in Parallel Simulation Schemes

Author: Jun Luo, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Add. 535 Fahua Zhen Rd., Shanghai, 200052, China, jluo_ms@sjtu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Yang Wu, Jeff Hong, Barry Nelson

Year: 2013

Abstract: Many sequential R&S procedures are designed to be implemented in a single-processor simulation environment. However, parallel computing, such as multi-core Desktops and many-core servers, is ubiquitous. What makes sequential procedures on multiple processors different from that on a single processor? is a critical question we intend to answer. We first identify the potential issues of simply executing existing procedures in parallel and then provide two approaches to address these problems.
