Title: The Role of “Men of Power?and “Men of Insights?
Author: Elif Akcali, Assistant Professor, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, PO Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611-6595, United States, akcali@ise.ufl.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: For successful industry-academia collaboration, it is important to establish contact with both “men of power?and “men of insights?in industry. While the men of power, i.e., the executives of corporate management, can ensure institutional support, the men of insights, i.e., the members of the middle management or engineers, can facilitate the transfer of institutional knowledge. In this talk, we will talk about some strategies on how to establish contact with both “men.?
