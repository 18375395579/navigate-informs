Title: Autonomous Agents in Future Energy Markets: The 2013 Power Trading Agent Competition
Author: Wolf Ketter, Associate Professor, Erasmus University, Rotterdam School of Management, Rotterdam, Netherlands, WKetter@rsm.nl
Coauthor(s): John Collins, Markus Peters
Year: 2013
Abstract: Power TAC is a rich, competitive, open-source simulation platform for future retail power markets built on real-world data and state-of-the-art customer models. Its purpose is to help researchers understand the dynamics of customer and retailer decision-making as well as the robustness of proposed market designs. Power TAC invites researchers to develop autonomous electricity broker agents and to pit them against in global competitions. We analyze successful brokers of the 2013 competition.
