Title: Technology Management Section Best Paper Winner Presentation
Author: Cheryl Druehl, George Mason University, Fairfax, VA, United States of America, cdruehl@gmu.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: Please join us to hear from the TMS Best Paper from 2007 Award Winners, including Professor Melissa Schilling and Professor Corey Phelps. They will present recent work and future directions in the domain. In addition, a special guest discussant, Linda Argote, will comment on their work and the field.
