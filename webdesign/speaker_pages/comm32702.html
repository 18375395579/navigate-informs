Title: Taking a Regional Approach to Emergency Management

Author: Eric Holdeman, EHoldeman@icfi.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: There is a system for emergency management for the nation. It has been rigorously tested over the years and some question its ability to function during catastrophic events. The challenge for emergency managers is blending the public-private efforts with the multi-level governmental response. Taking a regional approach is one solution to improving the current system of governance and organizational interaction.
