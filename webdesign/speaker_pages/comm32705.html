Title: Shared Transportation Systems: Bike Sharing
Author: Ashish Kabra, INSEAD, Fontainebleau, France, ashish.kabra@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Serguei Netessine, Francis de Véricourt, Karan Girotra
Year: 2013
Abstract: Shared urban transportation systems have the potential to reduce urban pollution, relieve traffic congestion and increase economic activity in cities, while offering the convenience of personally owned vehicles. We analyze data from over 25 major bike sharing systems and estimate customer adoption and use in response to system design, operation and performance. We use our estimates to identify station design and optimal launch strategies.
