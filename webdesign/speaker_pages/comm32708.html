Title: Game Theory for Security: Key Algorithmic Principles, Deployed Applications, Lessons Learned
Author: Milind Tambe, University of Southern California, Los Angeles, CA, United States of America, tambe@usc.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We will provide an overview of key principles of several game-theoretic applications developed by my research team at USC that are now deployed over multiple years with multiple security agencies: LAX police, Federal Air Marshals (FAMS), US coast guard in Boston and New York, and Los Angeles Sheriff Department.
