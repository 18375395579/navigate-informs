Title: Algorithmic Challenges in the Scheduling of Robotic Cells
Author: Neil Geismar, Texas A&M University, Mays Business School, College Station, TX, United States, ngeismar@yahoo.com
Coauthor(s): Milind Dawande
Year: 2007
Abstract: Despite much recent research in the scheduling robotic cells, some very important foundational problems remain open. We detail the state of the art in this field, describe the most significant open problems, suggest potential approaches to them, and report recent approximation results.
