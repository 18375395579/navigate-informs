Title: Competitive Strategies in Online Sponsored Search Markets
Author: Siva Viswanathan, University of Maryland College Park, 4313 Van Munching Hall, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, sviswana@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Animesh Animesh
Year: 2008
Abstract: While it is widely believed that an ad's rank in the sponsored-search listings is the key determinant of its performance, we examine the impact of additional factors - including the seller's positioning strategy, and its ability to differentiate itself from competition - on performance (CTR) of the seller's ad.
