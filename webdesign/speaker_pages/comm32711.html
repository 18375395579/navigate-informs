Title: Applying the Concept of Credit Guarantee on Dual Sourcing Model

Author: Cheng-feng Wu, PhD Student, National Taiwan University, 1, Sec. 4, Roosevelt Rd, Taipei, 106, Taiwan - ROC, d99741006@ntu.edu.tw

Coauthor(s): Po-Hua Chen, David Chiang, Jiuh-Biing Sheu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Capital shortage in SMEs causes them difficult to fulfill orders, resulting in poor performance of SC. Therefore, obtaining financial support for SMEs is a significant issue. This study attempts to examine the issue of trade guarantee mechanism from manufacturer’s perspectives with dual sourcing. This study provides some suggestions to the supply chain under the trade guarantee mechanism.
