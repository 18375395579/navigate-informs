Title: Stigma Entrusted Institutions and HIV/AIDS: The Role of Family, Churches, and Medical Institutions
Author: Johanne Laboy, Graduate Student, NC State U, Department of Communication, 201 Winston Hall, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, laboy@nc.rr.com
Coauthor(s): James Kiwanuka-Tondo, Fay Cobb Payton
Year: 2012
Abstract: One of the major challenges of HIV/AIDS interventions is stigma experienced by afflicted and affected family members. This paper analyzes the role played by family members, churches, and medical institutions in this complex issue.
