Title: Probability Management: An Overview
Author: Sam Savage, Consulting Prof., Stanford University, Dept. of MS&E, 326 Terman, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, savage@stanford.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Probability Management is an approach to creating enterprise wide stochastic models that may be consolidated, audited, and most importantly trusted by management. It is defined by a managerial component, the Chief Probability Office (CPO), an informational component, Stochastic Information Systems, and a technological component, Interactive Simulation. Live Computer demonstrations will include applications from Energy, Finance and Human Resources.
