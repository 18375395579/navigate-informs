Title: ICS Prize Session

Author:

Coauthor(s):

Year: 2008

Abstract: The winners of the 2008 ICS Prize and the 2008 ICS Student Paper Award present their award-winning papers. The 2008 ICS Prize winners are Robert Day and S. Raghavan for their paper: Fair Payments for Efficient Allocations in Public Sector Combinatorial Auctions, which was published in Management Science in 2007. The Student Paper Award Winner is Guanghui Lan for his paper: Efficient Methods for Stochastic Composite Optimization.
