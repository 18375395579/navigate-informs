Title: The Effect of Trade Credit in Supply Chains

Author: John Birge, Professor, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, john.birge@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): Song Alex Yang

Year: 2012

Abstract: Trade credit is the dominant source of short-term credit for most firms. In previous work, we described a model of firm interactions in which trade credit serves as a risk-sharing mechanism that improves chain efficiency. This talk discusses predictions from this model and empirical evidence.
