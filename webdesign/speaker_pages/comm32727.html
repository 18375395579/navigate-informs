Title: Hub and Hub Arc Location Problem Using Shipment Consolidation

Author: Wooseung Jang, Associate Professor, University of Missouri - Columbia, E3437 Lafferre Hall, Columbia, MO, 65211, United States of America, jangw@missouri.edu

Coauthor(s): Sean Carr

Year: 2008

Abstract: The shipment consolidation has become a key factor that affects the cost and service quality of a logistics operation. This research develops models and solution methodologies for the logistics network problem using shipment consolidation strategies. It is motivated by a diversified, worldwide manufacturer that has approximately 220 US branches.
