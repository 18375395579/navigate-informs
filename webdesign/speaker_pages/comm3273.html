Title: Efficiency and Sustainability in the Food Service Supply Chain

Author: Renzo Akkerman, Technical University of Denmark, Department of Management Engineering, Produktionstorvet 424, Kgs. Lyngby (Copenhagen), 2800, Denmark, renzo@dtu.dk

Coauthor(s): Martin Grunow, Yang Wang

Year: 2012

Abstract: The foodservice sector often uses industrially prepared meal elements in the preparation of meals. This presentation deals with the temperature-controlled production and distribution of these meal elements. The focus will be on the planning problems and on its sustainability assessment. This involves the inclusion of changing product temperature in planning decisions, and the integration of these planning decisions in sustainability assessment.
