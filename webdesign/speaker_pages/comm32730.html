Title: Operations Research in The Public Sector
Author: Christer Johnson, IBM Global Business Services, Fairfax, VA, christer.johnson@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Paul Thompson
Year: 2006
Abstract: Exciting opportunities for OR analysis have recently emerged in the public sector, including emergency management, homeland security and military logistics. Advances in computer technology and mathematical science are allowing OR to move into real-time decision-making. We discuss what the OR professional needs to successfully meet these challenges.
