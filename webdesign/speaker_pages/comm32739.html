Title: Panel Discussion: Integrated Operations and Marketing for Serving Emerging Markets

Author: Vish Krishnan, Professor, University of California - San Diego, Rady School of Management, 9500 Gilman Drive MC 0093, La Jolla, CA, 92093, United States, vkrishnan@ucsd.edu

Coauthor(s): Uday Karmarkar, Kamalini Ramdas, Cheryl Druehl, Sunder Kekre, Aleda Roth

Year: 2006

Abstract: Emerging markets present new opportunities and challenges for global firms. Their size and limited penetration present growth potential. However, infrastructural and budget constraints could challenge margins and even realization of growth. This panel seeks to discuss how integrating the operations and marketing decisions can offer firms the opportunity to realize the potential of emerging markets.
