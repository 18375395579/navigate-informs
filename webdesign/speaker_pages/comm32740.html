Title: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention: Advancing Public Health and Medical Preparedness

Author: Greg Burel, Center for Disease Control and Prevention, Washington, DC, United States of America, Greg.Burel@cdc.gov

Coauthor(s): Eva Lee

Year: 2012

Abstract: CDC public health experts teamed with operations researchers to devise sophisticated modeling and computational strategies to improve mass dispensing. With the powerful information decision-support suite, RealOpt? expanded capabilities are identified for those with limited resources and stressed environments. Over 4,000 public health and emergency directors can now make rapid and effective interventions for at-risk populations, lessening the subsequent disease mortality and save money.
