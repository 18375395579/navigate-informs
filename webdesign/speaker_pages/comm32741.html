Title: The Impact of Technological Developments on Research in Technology Management

Author: Cheryl Gaimon, Regents' Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, College of Management, 800 West Peachtree Street, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308-0520, United States of America, cheryl.gaimon@mgt.gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this session, TMS Distinguished Speaker Cheryl Gaimon will reflect on how recent major technological developments have impacted research in technology management. She will discuss the differences and similarities of the research that has evolved in manufacturing versus service domains. She will conclude by suggesting opportunities for future research with special attention to multidisciplinary themes.
