Title: Supply Chain Relationship Dependency, Relationship Strength, and Firm Performance
Author: Marcus Bellamy, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, marcus.bellamy@scheller.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Soumen Ghosh, Manpreet Hora
Year: 2013
Abstract: We investigate the dependency and strength of supply chain relationships and their association with firm performance. We develop and test hypotheses based on a unique dataset of supplier and customer relationships. Results from our empirical analysis suggest that the dependency and strength of supply chain relationships influence firm performance.
