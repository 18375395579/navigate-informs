Title: Analysis and Insights of Marketing Through Blogs
Author: Mohammad Rahman, The University of Kansas, 345 Summerfield, Lawrence, United States, rahman@ku.edu
Coauthor(s): Gilbert Karuga, Paulo Goes
Year: 2006
Abstract: Firms are realizing that including promotional messages in the blog content may be an effective way to market their products, in addition to banner ads on blogsites. Can firms buy out blogger support? Will bloggers mislead their audiences in response to sponsorship offers? Should blog readers continue to believe bloggers, even amidst uncertainty or deception? We model promotional blogging within a game-theoretic framework to answer these questions.
