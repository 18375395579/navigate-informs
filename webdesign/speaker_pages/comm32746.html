Title: Chemotherapy Appointment Scheduling at the BC Cancer Agency

Author: Martin Puterman, University of British Columbia, 2053 Main Mall, Vancouver, Canada, Martin.Puterman@sauder.ubc.ca

Coauthor(s): Pablo Santibanez, Ruben Aristizabal

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk reports the results of our three year effort to improve chemotherapy appointment scheduling practices at the BC Cancer Agency. In describes our process review, software development, implementation at the Vancouver Clinic, evaluation, ongoing improvement to address system changes and our experiences implementing the software at a second clinic.
