Title: Advances in Coin-SMI for Stochastic Integer and Decomposition Solvers

Author: Alan King, IBM Research, PO Box 218, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, kingaj@us.ibm.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this session we discuss technical advances in COIN-OR's Stochastic Modeling Interface to support mixed-integer programming and to support decomposition methods.
