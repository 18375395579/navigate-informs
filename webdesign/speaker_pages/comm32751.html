Title: Offshore Operations: Strategic Positioning to Address Skill Gaps

Author: Jeanette Harrison, Vice President, American Express Learning Network, American Express, 777 American Expressway, MS 05-01-4, Fort Lauderdale, FL, 33337, United States, jeanette.k.harrison@aexp.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Skills gap, industry growth and technological advances have in part driven companies to develop off shore operations. Offshore centers provide enormous opportunity but require careful planning and cultural awareness in order to obtain benefit from the world’s labor pool. This paper will address the cross cultural issues attendant to offshore operations.
