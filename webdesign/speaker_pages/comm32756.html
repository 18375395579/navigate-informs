Title: Grid Integration and R&D policy
Author: Erin Baker, University of Massachusetts, Amherst, MA, United States of America, edbaker@ecs.umass.edu
Coauthor(s): Noubara Djimadoumbaye
Year: 2012
Abstract: In previous work we have analyzed the optimal energy technology R&D portfolio in the face of climate change, considering solar PV, CCS, and nuclear. Here we consider how the results are impacted by two very different assumptions regarding the integration of solar PV onto the grid. We apply a mutli-model, decision making under uncertainty framework.
