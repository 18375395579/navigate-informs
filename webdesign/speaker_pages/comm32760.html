Title: Numerical Optimization, Second Edition, J. Nocedal and S. Wright

Author: Stephen Wright, Professor, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1210 West Dayton Street, Madison, WI, 53706, United States, swright@cs.wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Jorge Nocedal

Year: 2006

Abstract: Additions and improvements in the second edition of the book Numerical Optimization by J. Nocedal and S. Wright will be discussed. An outline of the contents and a sample of the approach used in the book will be presented.
