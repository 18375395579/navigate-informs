Title: Mechanism and Network Design with Loss-exposed Agents

Author: Changrong Deng, Duke University, Fuqua School of Business, 100 Fuqua Drive, Box 90120, Durham, NC, 27708-0120, United States of America, changrong.deng@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Alexandre Belloni, Sasa Pekec

Year: 2013

Abstract: A large number of networked agents compete for a single indivisible resource. Agents experience a loss if the resource is allocated in their proximity but not to them. Resource and loss values are privately held by the agents. In a large network, a no-allocation equilibrium with revenues higher than that of any mechanism that always allocates the resource could emerge. We provide optimal direct mechanisms when the resource value is public, and describe the revenue-maximizing network structures.
