Title: Optimizing Health Care Delivery in Sub-Saharan Africa
Author: Jacqueline Griffin, PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of Industrial & Systems Engineering, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, jgriffin3@mail.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Martin Savelsbergh, Paul Griffin
Year: 2008
Abstract: Providing access to essential medicines and health treatments is a major challenge in Africa. Using an integer programming model, we explore the trade off between optimizing for “outcome?and for “fairness?given a limited health care budget. We also examine how delivery strategies are affected by infrastructure and spatial characteristics of a region.
