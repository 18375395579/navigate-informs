Title: Please Comment Here - Initiated Participation and Willingness to Pay on Content Websites

Author: Lior Zalmanson, Tel Aviv University, Ramat Aviv, Tel Aviv, Israel, zalmanso@post.tau.ac.il

Coauthor(s): Gal Oestreicher-Singer

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate the effect of acts of initiated participation?calls to action made by website owners—on users' willingness to pay on content websites. We show that users who are given the calls of action in increasing order of effort are willing to pay more for a monthly subscription compared to users who are not exposed to such prompts. Calls given in any other order, do not yield significant results. Moreover, the “calls?do not interfere with users?appreciation of the content website.
