Title: Socio-technical Methodologies for Strategic Policy-making

Author: Martin Schilling, m.schilling@lse.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Cornelius Schaub, Nadine Oeser

Year: 2007

Abstract: Analytical-deliberative methodologies can aid politicians and civil servants with the formulation of strategic policies. In this session, we introduce a framework to classify these methodologies and discuss the potential contribution of decision analyses for strategic policymaking.
