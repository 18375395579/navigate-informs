Title: Practical Challenges in Optimizing Room Rates for Hotels and Gaming Resorts

Author: H. Ahmet Kuyumcu, President, Prorize, LLC, 12138 Madison Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30346, United States, akuyumcu@prorize.com

Coauthor(s): Amar R. Duggasani

Year: 2007

Abstract: There are many practical challenges in optimizing room rates for hotels and gaming resorts. These include, but not limited to, incorporation of demand uncertainty, rate uncertainty, customer value, price sensitivity, overbooking, groups, promotions, room upgrades, room cross-sells, competitive rates, and market level information. This presentation expplores some of these challenges in more detail.
