Title: Short-term Trading for a Wind Power Producer

Author: Juan Miguel Morales, PhD Student, University Castilla - La Mancha, Department of Electrical Engineering, Campus Universitario s/n, Ciudad Real, 13071, Spain, JuanMiguel.Morales@uclm.es

Coauthor(s): Antonio J. Conejo

Year: 2008

Abstract: A wind power producer participating in a pool-based electricity market should derive its bidding strategies for the day-ahead market as well as for other shorter-term adjustment and balancing markets. This paper provides a multi-stage stochastic programming methodology to derive optimal bidding strategies for such markets. Appropriate examples and case studies are analyzed. Adequate conclusions are provided.
