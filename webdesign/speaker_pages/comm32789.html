Title: Peak and Off-peak Strategies in Constrained Capacity Industries

Author: Steven Shugan, University of Florida Warrington College of Business Admin, 201 Bryan Hall Campus Box 117155, Gainesville, FL, United States of America, Steven.Shugan@cba.ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Aydin Alptekinoglu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Pricing theory suggests that during peak periods, service providers should improve profits by strategically raising prices and decreasing quality. However, we show when and why that is not the case.
