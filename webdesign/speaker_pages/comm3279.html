Title: Product Recalls in the Medical Device Industry: Exploration of the Sources and Financial Consequences

Author: Kingshuk Sinha, University of Minnesota, 321-19th Ave SE, Minneapolis, MN, United States, ksinha@csom.umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Sriram Thirumalai

Year: 2007

Abstract: This study addresses three key issues regarding product recalls in the medical device industry: first, it empirically assesses the financial consequences of recalls; second it examines the moderating effects of firm characteristics on the financial consequences; lastly, it explores the sources of recalls in this industry. The analyses are based on data from medical device firms over a four-year period (2002-2005).
