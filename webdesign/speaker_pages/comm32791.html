Title: Joining the CCS Club! Insights from a Northwest European CO2 Pipeline Project
Author: Stephane Tchung-Ming, Research Fellow, IFP Energies Nouvelles, 1 ?4 Avenue de Bois Préau, Rueil-Malmaison, 92852, France, stephane.tchung-ming@ifpen.fr
Coauthor(s): Olivier Massol
Year: 2012
Abstract: The large-scale diffusion of Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) technologies imposes the construction of a sizeable pipeline infrastructure. We analyze the conditions for a widespread adoption of CCS by a group of emitters using a common pipeline system. It clarifies how the regulatory constraints imposed to the pipeline operator influence the emitters' adoption. An application of this framework to the case of a European point-to-point pipeline is discussed. Policy recommendations are formulated.
