Title: QSR Best Student Paper Award

Author: Hong Wan, Assistant Professor, School of IE, Purdue Univ., 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, hwan@purdue.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This session includes presentations from four finalists for the QSR best student paper award. The four finalists are selected after reviewing all of the submitted papers for this competition. The results will be announced at the QSR business meeting.
