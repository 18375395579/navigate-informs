Title: Use of Lean 3P Tools to Optimally Expand Clinical Space at TRIA Orthopaedic Center

Author: Sunny Ray, Director, Quality Improvement, TRIA Orthopaedic Center, 8100 Northland Drive, Bloomington, MN, 55431, United States of America, Sunny.Ray@TRIA.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: TRIA Orthopaedic Center (Bloomington, MN) has grown in clinic volumes 234% since opening in 2005, and space constraints were a barrier to continued growth, patient access and physician recruitment. Lean 3P (production preparation process) tools were used to understand the needs of space expansion requirements, and design and sequence expansion.
