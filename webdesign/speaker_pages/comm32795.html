Title: Various Approaches to Business Cases for Enterprise-wide Analytics
Author: Margery Connor, Senior Technology Strategist, Chevron, 6001 Bollinger Canyon Road, San Ramon, CA, 94588, United States of America, MHCO@chevron.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: Developing business cases for individual project is challenging. What about justifying an enterprise-wide initiative to grow modeling and analytics capability across the enterprise? We will present various approaches used and the advantages and pitfalls of each. Be warned, we will not be presenting any ‘silver bullets?
