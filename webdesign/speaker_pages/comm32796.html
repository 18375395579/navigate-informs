Title: Supply Chain Disruption and Swift Knowledge Transfer
Author: Rebecca Clemons, Doctoral Student, Cleveland State University, 521 West Lakeshore Drive, Port Clinton, OH, 43452, United States of America, clemonsbec@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Raymond Henry
Year: 2013
Abstract: Many manufacturers experience situations where disruptions in supply require a new supplier to be brought into production quickly. In this situation it is important to transfer knowledge but time constraints may make typical knowledge transfer processes ineffective. The objective is to examine the critical factors that impact the swift transfer of knowledge within a supply chain.
