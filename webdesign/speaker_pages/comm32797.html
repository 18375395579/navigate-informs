Title: Multi-criteria Decision Analysis and Game Theory: Perspective on Terrorist Archetypes
Author: John Coles, PhD Student, University at Buffalo, 435 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, jbcoles@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): Jun Zhuang
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper provides a perspective on how game theory could be used to supplement a Multi-Attribute Utility Theory (MAUT) examination of terrorist actions and reactions. We present a quantitative, stakeholder-driven method to provide a clear understanding about immediate threats and long-term behavioral patterns, building on the current set of literature and proposing MAUT and game theory integration.
