Title: Hybrid Parallel Metaheuristics for Traveling Salesman Problems with Precedence Constraints
Author: Amelia Regan, University of California, Irvine, CA, United States of America, aregan@uci.edu
Coauthor(s): Dmitri Arkhipov, Ross Wagoner
Year: 2012
Abstract: Our work studies the effectiveness of implementations of several hybrid metaheuristics implemented on GPUs.
