Title: R&D Productivity, IT Investments, and Firm Performance
Author: Vish Krishnan, Professor, University of California, San Diego, CA, vkrishnan@ucsd.edu
Coauthor(s): Indranil Bardhan, Gokcen Arkali
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the interactions between R&D productivity, IT investments, and firm performance using archival data from 1998-2003 for a large cross section of 492 firms. Preliminary results suggest that IT spending has a significant moderating impact on the relationship between R&D and firm performance. These results shed new light on the relationship between R&D and firm performance.
