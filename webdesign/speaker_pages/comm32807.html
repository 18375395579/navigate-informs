Title: A Meta-analysis of the Trends and Directions of Research in Mass Customization

Author: Ashok Kumar, Grand Valley State University, kumara@gvsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Kathryn Stecke, Frank T. Piller

Year: 2007

Abstract: Over 1,500 publications have appeared since Davis (1987) coined the term mass customization in Future Perfect. We select approximately 100 publications based on their impact on the field and partition the concepts and decisions addressed in these papers into logical taxonomies and structures and set forth directions for future research.
