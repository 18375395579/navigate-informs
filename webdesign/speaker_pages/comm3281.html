Title: Operations Management and Strategic Outsourcing: Coordinating the Relationship for Success
Author: Bill Phifer, EDS Fellow, EDS, 1101 Shadow Wood Drive, Downingtown, PA, 19335, United States, bill.phifer@eds.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: The explosion of outsourcing of non-core business functions and its mixed results in operations management is explored, with a focus on the client and service provider relationship. Of primary concern is the ability of the business to effectively outsource appropriate functions, meet operational expectations, and maintain control. The eSourcing Capability Models?will be considered as best practices for clearly establishing and managing performance objectives, governance, and transparency.
