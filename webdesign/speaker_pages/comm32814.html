Title: Examining the Relationship Between Reviews & Sales: The Role of Social Information in E-Markets
Author: Anindya Ghose, New York University, aghose@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Batia Wiesenfeld, Chris Forman
Year: 2007
Abstract: User-generated product reviews have proliferated online, driven by the notion that consumers?purchsing decision is based on the positive or negative information about that product. Using research on information processing, we show evidence that in online markets, the social information reviewers provide about themselves is used to supplement or replace product information in shaping community members?purchase decisions and the value they attribute to reviews.
