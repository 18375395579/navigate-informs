Title: Piecewise Linear Approximation for Network RM

Author: Sumit Kunnumkal, Indian School of Business, AC4 Level 1 4116, ISB, Hyderabad, AP, India, Sumit_Kunnumkal@isb.edu

Coauthor(s): Kalyan Talluri

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate the piecewise-linear approximation for network RM with and without choice behavior. We show that it is tractable, and can be solved efficiently in many applications of practical interest.
