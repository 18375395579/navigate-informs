Title: Research Collaboration Netwroks and Innovation Output
Author: David Deeds, dxd054000@utdallas.edu
Coauthor(s): Irem Demirkan
Year: 2007
Abstract: We explore the evolution of co-authorship networks of the firms in the biotechnology industry. We use social network theory to develop a model of the structure and dynamics of interorganizational research collaboration networks. This paper also demonstrates how this evolution affects focal firms?successive performance in terms of the firms?innovative output.
