Title: Right Product, Right Price, Right Time ?A Framework for Profitable New Product Introduction
Author: Duru Ahanotu, Decision Architect, Rapt Inc., 625 2nd Street, 2nd Floor, San Francisco, CA, 94107, United States, duru.ahanotu@rapt.com
Coauthor(s): Michael Goldbach
Year: 2006
Abstract: New product introductions (NPIs) are the life blood of growth in many segments. In high-tech, products introduced within 18 months often contribute over 50 percent of revenue. While mispricing at introduction can have a devastating impact on margin, these pricing decisions are often the most uncertain due to lack of data. This session introduces a framework to improve NPI decisions by providing a typology of NPI decisions each calling for a different pricing strategy and analytical technique.
