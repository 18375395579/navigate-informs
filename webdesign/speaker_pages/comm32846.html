Title: Stochastic-endogenous Generation Capacity Expansion Equilibrium: Incompleteness and Remedies

Author: Gauthier de Maere d'Aertrycke, Research Analyst, GDF-Suez Energy, Brussels, Belgium, gdemaere@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Yves Smeers, Andreas Ehrenmann

Year: 2013

Abstract: The European power industry is currently facing risks concerning climate policies and demand growth. Those risks are impossible to trade and markets alone might not be sufficient to deliver the appropriate investment. To restore the investment signal, forward contracts and reliability options were proposed in the literature. We elaborate on the issue using a stochastic-endogeneous equilibrium model and analyze how the mechanisms help to complete the market in terms of welfare and investment.
