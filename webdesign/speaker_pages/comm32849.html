Title: Impact of Patient Transfers on Hospital Performance
Author: Diwas Kc, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, diwas@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Christian Terwiesch
Year: 2008
Abstract: Smaller hospitals frequently transfer their severe cases to larger regional hospitals. We examine the inter-hospital transfer patterns to examine whether hospital-level measures of performance are impacted by patient transfers. In particular, we estimate the externalities imposed on receiving hospitals through patient transfers.
