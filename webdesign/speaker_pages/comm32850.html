Title: Road Pricing and Regulation on a Large Mixed-Ownership Network

Author: Lei Zhang, Assistant Professor, Civil, Construction, and Environmental Engineering, Oregon State University, 220 Owen Hall, Corvallis, OR, 97331, United States, lei.zhang@oregonstate.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Stimulated by the revived interest in private roads, this paper develops a model to study the economic impact of mixed ownership structures in a large-scale network. Constrained by travel demand patterns and road supply costs, public and private road authorities adopt pricing and investment strategies to achieve their welfare and profit objectives respectively. Potential regulatory needs and strategies are also identified and their effectiveness analyzed.
