Title: Competition with Real Options
Author: Ozge Sahin, Assistant Professor of OMS, Ross School of Business, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, ozge@bus.umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Guillermo Gallego
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study duopoly competition of capacitated providers with real options under proportional rationing rule. We compare and contrast the revenues of the providers with and without options.
