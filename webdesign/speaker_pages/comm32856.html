Title: Improve IT Incident Handling based on SME Social Network Information
Author: Zhe (Jay) Shan, Assistant Professor, University of Cincinnati, 2925 Campus Green Dr Rm 509, Cincinnati, OH, 45221, United States of America, zhe.shan@uc.edu
Coauthor(s): Rong Liu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Due to the complexity of IT environment, the IT incident diagnosis at application level is very challenging. The traditional sequential approach leads huge cost of handling delay. In this paper, we propose to use the historical communication information among SMEs to identify their social network structure. Based on this network, we can identify an appropriate expert group for different kinds of incidents, which potentially improves the efficiency of incident handling.
