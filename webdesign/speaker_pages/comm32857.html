Title: Characterization of Internet Application Traffic and Network Capacity Sizing Implications
Author: Amie Elcan, Qwest Communications, 1801 California Street, Suite 2620, Denver, CO, 80202, United States of America, Amie.Elcan@qwest.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: Internet carrier traffic volumes have been increasing exponentially due to the availability of large digital media files from commercial web sites and from private sources through peer to peer file sharing clients. Other bandwidth intensive applications such as VoIP, short form video and online TV have gained popularity. Internet application usage trends will be presented as well as methods for sizing network link capacity to respond to traffic loads made possible by faster service speeds.
