Title: Factor-based Risk Decomposition
Author: Chen Chen, Doctoral Student, Columbia University, 325, S. W. Mudd Building, 500 W. 120th S, New York, United States of America, cc3136@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Garud Iyengar, Ciamac Moallemi
Year: 2012
Abstract: The risk of a system can arise from a variety of factors that influence future outcomes. We develop a statistical methodology to explain the risk exposure created by a set of factors, and to identify the factors which are most systemically important.
