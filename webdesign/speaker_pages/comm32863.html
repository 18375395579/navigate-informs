Title: A Framework for Data-driven Improvement of Healthcare

Author: Juergen Klenk, Principal, Booz Allen Hamilton, Inc., 8283 Greensboro Drive, McLean, VA, 22102, United States of America, klenk_juergen@bah.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: According to Erik Brynjolfsson of MIT’s Sloan School of Management, The problem is not that we don’t have enough data ?it’s that we have too much data and we need to make sense of it. In this presentation we will review a framework to generate value from data in Healthcare. We will illustrate its usability with a case study on mining EHRs to reduce hospital-acquired conditions such as sepsis, and close with additional examples on how Analytics can drive improvement of our Healthcare system.
