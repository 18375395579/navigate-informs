Title: Patient Flow Through an ICU

Author: Hsiao-Hui Lee, Ph.D Candidate, University of Rochester, Simon School, Rochester, NY, United States, hsiao-hui.lee@simon.rochester.edu

Coauthor(s): Gregory Dobson, Edieal Pinker

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper investigated how the OR surgery schedule, patient profile, and the utilization, affect the incidence of bumping a patient from the ICU to another ICU or the floor earlier than medically advisable. We present an aggregation-disaggregation algorithm to solve the ICU bumping problem as a large scale Markov chain.
