Title: Leading a Double Life
Author: Uday Karmarkar, Los Angeles Times Chair in Management and Policy, UCLA Anderson School, 110 Westwood Plaza, Box 951481, Los Angeles, CA, 90077, United States, uday.karmarkar@anderson.ucla.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: The difficulties of balancing research with education and practice are well known. There are a number of ways of achieving your personal goals with respect to those three areas, but most involve doing double duty, and working quite hard. Still the rewards and satisfactions are also substantial.
