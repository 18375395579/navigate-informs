Title: Expert Communication Networks for Market Change Predictions

Author: Daniel Romero, Northwestern University, 600 Foster Street, Evanston, IL, United States of America, d-romero@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Brian Uzzi

Year: 2013

Abstract: Research on public social media as heralds of critical events has rapidly accelerated. We conduct tests to examine whether public and private social network data provide precursor signals for system behavior. Focusing on a large social system, financial markets, we examined 22 million instant messages among 8,828 persons in a network of financial specialists. We find that the social network provides unique precursor signals of system behavior - changes in the Dow Jones Industrial Average.
