Title: Preliminary Exploration on Health Care Services in China

Author: Lei Zhao, Assistant Professor, Tsinghua University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Beijing, 100084, China, lzhao@tsinghua.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Li Zheng

Year: 2007

Abstract: In the past decades, the Chinese health care services systems have been undergoing a series of reformations. The efforts are still ongoing and difficulties remain. In this exploratory research, via hospital investigation and public information collection, etc., we tried to collect facts, data, and information regarding the current status of health care systems in China. We are to find out where and how we can help to improve by applying operations research tools.
