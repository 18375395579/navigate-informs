Title: Individual Dissatisfaction-reversion based Group Decision Making and its Application on Health Care
Author: Jian Hu, Assistant Professor, University of Michigan - Dearborn, 4901 Evergreen Rd., Dept. of IMSE, University of Michigan-De, Dearborn, MI, 48128, United States of America, jianhu@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Yung-wen Liu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study improving individual-satisfaction in group decision-making problems. A decision is conducted to fairly represent a social preference. This is illustrated by a medical decision problem for pregnant women to choose either chorionic villus sampling or amniocentesis.
