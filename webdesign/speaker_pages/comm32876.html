Title: Option Pricing: A Matroid Theory Perspective

Author: Farid AitSahlia, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Systems Engineering, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, farid@ise.ufl.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk I show how matroid theory provides a unifying framework for diverse approaches to option pricing theory in incomplete markets. Algorithmic implications are also discussed.
