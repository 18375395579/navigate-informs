Title: An Integrated Framework for Forecasting, Markdown and Replenishment Optimization
Author: Ulas Cakmak, Senior Scientist, Predictix, 1349 W. Peachtree Street NW, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States of America, ulas.cakmak@predictix.com
Coauthor(s): Emir Pasalic, Mokhtar Bazaraa, Zografoula Vagena
Year: 2013
Abstract: We discuss analytical and estimation challenges in Forecasting, Markdown and Replenishment Optimization in a retail environment. Different aspects of these problems and interactions between them are analyzed. A Cloud based solution implementation, where parallel processing allows us to solve complex optimization models, is presented.
