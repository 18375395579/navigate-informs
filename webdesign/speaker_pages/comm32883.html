Title: Doing Business in Second Life: e-Commerce in a 3D Online Environment

Author: Prabodh Shrestha, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, 1000 Hilltop Circle, Department of Information Systems, Baltimore, MD 21250, MD, United States of America, s.prabodh@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Dongsong Zhang

Year: 2008

Abstract: The general idea behind 3D e-Commerce is to enable users to experience online shopping as they do in a real-life store. Many existing 3D virtual worlds are currently lack of some critical components for successful e-Commerce. Some virtual economy is only limited to virtual items. In this paper, we analyze the 3D platform, demonstrate that current 3D virtual worlds need significant improvement in their current systems to propel a real world e-commerce to prosper in such environments.
