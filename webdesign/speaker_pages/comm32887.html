Title: Virtual Team Performance in Crowdsourcing Contests

Author: Indika Dissanayake, University of Texas at Arlington, 701 S. West Street, Arlington, TX, 76019, United States of America, indika.dissanayake@mavs.uta.edu

Coauthor(s): Jie Zhang, Bin Gu

Year: 2013

Abstract: During last few years, we witnessed many organizations find solutions to their vital business problems with the aid of online contests. Self-organized virtual teams participate and collaborate online in these contests and compete among themselves for monitory reward. Motivated by this phenomenon, this research investigates what impacts the performances of virtual teams in the context of crowdsourcing contests.
