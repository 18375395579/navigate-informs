Title: Analytically Establishing the Bounds the Alphorn of Uncertainty in Project Scheduling
Author: Peter D Simonson, Adjunct Professor, North Dakota State University, Richard H Barry Hall, P.O. Box 6050, Fargo, ND, 58108, United States of America, psimonson@mac.com
Coauthor(s): Joseph Szmerekovsky
Year: 2013
Abstract: The purpose of this paper is to establish the bounds of the “Alphorn of Uncertainty?for a project. If the duration of an activity is a random variable then the cost associated with that activity is also a r.v. and thus the range of cost outcomes at any give time can be bordered by an upper and lower bound. This range forms an “alphorn?getting wider as the duration of the project increases. Further, we investigate the complexity of calculating the Alphorn of Uncertainty exactly.
