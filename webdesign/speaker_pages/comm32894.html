Title: Business Innovation Case of Korea Aerospace Industries (KAI)

Author: Chang Won Lee, Department of Venture Management, Jinju National University, Jinju, KN, 660-758, Korea, Republic of, cwlee@cjcc.jinju.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): CW Lee

Year: 2007

Abstract: KAI is a leading corporation ushering in a new future for Korea's aerospace industry through continuous technology innovation and stronger worldwide competitiveness. A case study is utilized for analyzing current business innovation performance in terms of financial and non-financial aspects. This study suggests that the management can achieve strategic gains by appropriate business innovation along with a significant competitiveness of technology and business.
