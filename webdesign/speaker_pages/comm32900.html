Title: Tracking the Trend Line: Operations Research Challenges within the Trend Analysis Branch of JIEDDO

Author: Matthew Santoni, MAJ, JIEDDO, Joint IED Defeat Organization, 5000 Army Pentagon, Washington, DC, 20310-5000, United States of America, matthew.santoni@jieddo.dod.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Trends Analysis Branch of the Joint IED Defeat Organization is tasked with collecting and interpreting reporting on IED attacks. Providing this common operating picture presents three fundamental issues: managing free text fields, merging disparate data sources, and transformation into a standardized view. The author will interpret current trends and discuss potential solutions to the three data challenges listed above.
