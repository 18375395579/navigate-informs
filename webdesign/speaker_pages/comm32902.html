Title: Improving Accessibility to Radiation Therapy for Cancer Patients
Author: Mariel Lavieri, Student, University of British Columbia, 2053 Main Mall, Vancouver, BC, V6T 1Z2, Canada, Mariel.Lavieri@sauder.ubc.ca
Coauthor(s): Scott Tyldesley, Steven Shechter, John French, Tom Pickles, Martin Puterman
Year: 2006
Abstract: This talk describes a framework for modeling decision making for prostate cancer patients under constrained resource availability. We provide an overview of the proposed approach which will combine both clinical and operational decision making and then focus on patient specific decisions for regarding when to start radiation therapy.
