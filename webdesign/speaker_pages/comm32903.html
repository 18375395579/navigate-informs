Title: Understanding the Emotional Content Embedded in Tweets and Impact on Users' Social Structure Change
Author: Ivy Yuan, Indiana University, 503 S. Muller Parkway, Unit 311, Bloomington, IN, 47403, United States of America, yuanl@indiana.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Social media platform can be used to exchange personal opinions. For users with depression symptoms, it serves as a convenient way to help them exchange emotional support and maintain social connectivity. This research aims to explore the impact of emotional content in exchanged information on network structure evolvement. The findings of this study can be used to better understand network dynamics and provide insights to help Twitter users getting more emotional support in a more efficient way.
