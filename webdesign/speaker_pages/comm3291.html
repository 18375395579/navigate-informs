Title: Handling Risks of Failures for a Production Line
Author: Lin Li, Post-doctoral Research Fellow, S.M. Wu Manufacturing Research Center, University of Michigan, Mechanical Engin, 2250 G.G. Brown Bldg, 2350 Hayward St., Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, lilz@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Saumil Ambani, Jun Ni
Year: 2008
Abstract: In the present scenario of cost cutting and efficient operations,the tradeoff between risks and maximizing profits is often not well addressed. A production line exhibits several modes of failures, which cause a risk to the operations. Companies may wish to avoid, reduce or transfer risks to stay within this risk capacity. In this paper, various approaches of handling risks of failures are analyzed while trying to maximize profit under the constraints of risk capacity and maintenance resources.
