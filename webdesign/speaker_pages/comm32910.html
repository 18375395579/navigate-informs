Title: How to Fight Piracy: Differences between Making Pirated Products Less Available vs Less Attractive
Author: Antino Kim, PhD Student, University of Washington, Box 353200, MKZ 360, Seattle, WA, 98133, United States of America, kheehyon@uw.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: In light of the recent movements towards prosecuting online suppliers of pirated digital goods, we study the effect of such policies and make comparison to the ones that penalize the consumption of illegal copies.
