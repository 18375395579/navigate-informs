Title: HALT: Long Range Triage

Author: David Anderson, Assistant Professor, City University of New York Baruch, 55 Lexington Ave, New York, NY, 10010, United States of America, danderson@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Bruce Golden, Ryan Scilla, Edward Wasil, Mangla Gulati

Year: 2013

Abstract: Inter-Hospital Transfer Patients tend to be the most severe and resource intensive patients in large hospitals?internal medicine units. We develop a highly accurate tool based on simple characteristics to predict whether or not a patient will require an ICU bed within 48 hours of arrival.
