Title: Scenario Maps: Tools for Discovery and Design of Business Scenarios
Author: Yukio Ohsawa, Associate Professor, The University of Tokyo, ohsawa@q.t.u-tokyo.ac.jp
Year: 2007
Abstract: Awareness of possible future scenarios is essential for business decisions, where creating new values and reducing risks are desired. Scenario maps visualized based on business data are presented, as tools for creating values and managing risks on rare/novel events. The methods have been utilized for managing credit risks and for designing products/markets in real companies since our team started the project chance discovery in 2000.
