Title: A Theoretical Framework for Managing the NPD Portfolio: When and How to Use Strategic Buckets

Author: Raul Chao, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree Street, Atlanta, GA, United States, raul.chao@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Stylianos Kavadias

Year: 2006

Abstract: Developing the right new products is critical to firm success and is often cited as a key competitive dimension. This paper addresses the link between new product development (NPD) portfolio strategy and firm performance. We first develop a general theoretical framework for resource allocation and NPD portfolio strategy. We then apply our framework to analyze a popular practice in NPD portfolio management: the use of strategic buckets.
