Title: Research Frontiers in Healthcare Mass Customization
Author: Sagar Kamarthi, Northeastern University, MIE Department, Boston, MA, United States of America, sagar@coe.neu.edu
Coauthor(s): Abe Zeid, Tucker Marion
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this research, we investigate operational and strategic issues in healthcare mass customization (HMC) for cost effective and efficient personalization of diagnosis, care and cure. We amalgamate two innovative paradigms personalized medicine and mass customization to build HMC.
