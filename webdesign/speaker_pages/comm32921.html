Title: Teaching Practical Optimization Using GLPK
Author: Paul Brooks, VCU, Dept. of Stat. Sci. and O.R., PO Box 843083, Richmond, VA, 23284, United States of America, JPBrooks@vcu.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: This talk describes experiences teaching a master's-level course on Practical Optimization using GLPK, an open source optimization package. A survey of some of the resources available and the resources that are not available is given. Examples of assignments and projects and their use in illustrating real-world optimization problems are presented.
