Title: Patient Revisit Intervals in Primary Care
Author: Hessam Bavafa, Ph.D. Candidate, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, bavafa@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Christian Terwiesch, Sergei Savin
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study the impact of two innovative primary care delivery modes on system outcomes: patient health, physician panel size and physician compensation. The delivery modes we study are email communication between patients and physicians (e-visits) and non-physician provided care (i.e., nurse practitioners and physician assistants).
