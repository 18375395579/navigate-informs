Title: Panel: CONDIE Progress Report
Author: Lawrence Seiford, Professor and Chair, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109-2117, United States, seiford@umich.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: The Committee on the Next Decade in Industrial Engineering (CONDIE) was formed to help ensure that academia will be able address areas of critical need in both research and education over the coming decade. CONDIE will serve as a continuing forum for applied dialog and deliberate action focused on facilitation of a timely response to national need, while advancing the frontier of engineering research.
