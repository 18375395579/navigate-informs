Title: Distributed Content Curation on Social Networks

Author: Zeinab Abbassi, PhD Student, Columbia University, New York, NY, United States of America, za2153@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Nidhi Hegde, Laurent Massoulie

Year: 2013

Abstract: There has been an explosion of digital content in recent years on online platforms such as blogs and social networks, video sharing, and original content. We consider the problem on curating this vast catalogue of content in a way that aggregators or publishers can offer readers content that is of interest to them, with minimal spam. Under a game-theoretic model we obtain several results on the optimal content selection and on the eefficiency of distribution curation.
