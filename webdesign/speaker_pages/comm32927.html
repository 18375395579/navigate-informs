Title: A Statistical Process Monitoring Perspective on Big Data
Author: Fadel Megahed, Assistant Professor, Auburn University, 3301L Shelby Center, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States of America, fmegahed@auburn.edu
Coauthor(s): Allison Jones-Farmer
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper, we discuss several big data applications to highlight the opportunities and challenges for applied statisticians interested in surveillance and statistical process control. Our goal is to bring the research dialogue into better focus and encourage methodological developments for big data analysis in these areas.
