Title: Robust Capital Investment Decisions of Nonprofit Healthcare Organizations

Author: Shuyi Wang, Lehigh University, 217 W. Packer Ave. APT 209, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, shw210@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Aurelie Thiele

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study focuses on capital investment decisions of nonprofit healthcare organizations, such as buying a MRI machine. Instead of using a single monetary metric (Total Net Present Value) combining NPV and social value, we consider social value separately in a non-monetary but quantitative way, such as gain in expected quality-of-life years. We then analyze trade-offs between NPV and social outputs in the context of sustainable nonprofits.
