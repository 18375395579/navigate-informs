Title: Pricing End-to-End QoS Guaranteed Internet Services
Author: Aparna Gupta, Assistant Professor, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 110 8th Street, Troy, NY, 12180, United States, guptaa@rpi.edu
Coauthor(s): Lingyi Zhang
Year: 2006
Abstract: Risks underlying Quality of Service (QoS) guaranteed services on the Internet cannot be technologically eliminated. We develop appropriate models for end-to-end QoS guaranteed contracts and apply options-pricing based techniques for pricing the risk in these contracts.
