Title: Impact of News Media on Humanitarian Donations

Author: Maria Besiou, Assistant Professor, Kuhne Logistics University, Brooktorkai 20, Hamburg, Germany, Maria.Besiou@the-klu.org

Coauthor(s): Jennifer McCormick

Year: 2013

Abstract: News media play an important role during emergencies, being one of the first actors to arrive in the area of disaster, but may also be at fault for causing in-kind donations to arrive at emergency sites. These donations clog the stream of lifesaving materials that need to reach victims. It is believed that the mediatized disasters receive more funding. This research explores the use of news media volume as a potential indicator for donation volume.
