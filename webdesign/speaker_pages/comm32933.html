Title: The Multi-armed Bandit Problem with Commitments: Generalized Depreciation and Necessity of Restart Indices

Author: Wesley Cowan, Rutgers University, Dept. of Mathematics, 110 Frelinghuysen Rd., Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, cwcowan@math.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Katehakis

Year: 2013

Abstract: The multi-armed bandit problem is one of the most basic models for sequential decision problems with exploration–exploitation trade-offs. It has applications from clinical trials to data mining and machine learning. In this talk we present a new, nice proof for the celebrated Gittins index decomposition. We also present several extensions to the theory that are made possible with our approach.
