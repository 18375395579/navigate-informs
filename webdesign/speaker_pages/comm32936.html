Title: Real-time Sentiment Analysis with the ‘Hedonometer?
Author: Brian Tivnan, Senior Principal, The MITRE Corporation, 7500 Colshire Dr, McLean, VA, United States of America, btivnan@mitre.org
Year: 2012
Abstract: Building on earlier psychological research, the Hedonometer is an instrument to ‘remotely-sense?emotional states and levels, in real time or post hoc. Here, we provide an update on the development of the Hedonometer, its extension beyond native English to a multitude of languages and the recent development of a real-time capability to perform sentiment analysis.
