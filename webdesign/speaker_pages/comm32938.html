Title: A Behavioral Comparison of Scripted and Customized Service Delivery Processes Using Lab Experiments
Author: Liana Victorino, Ph.D. Candidate, University of Utah, David Eccles School of Business, 1645 E Campus Center Drive, Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States, liana.victorino@business.utah.edu
Coauthor(s): Rohit Verma
Year: 2007
Abstract: This study examines the use of scripted and customized service delivery approaches for face-to-face service encounters. A laboratory experiment using a series of video clips is conducted to explore the service quality, service design and operational performance effects of a standardized and customized approach to the service delivery process.
