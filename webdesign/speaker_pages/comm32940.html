Title: Analysis of Business Process Models in Enterprise Web Services

Author: Mabel T. Kung, Professor, Mihaylo College of Business and Economics, California State University at Fullerton, Fullerton, CA, 92834, United States of America, mkung@fullerton.edu

Coauthor(s): Ronald Armstrong

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper reports a series of process-based models for the development of e-Business using enterprise software applications. Our framework focuses on the guidelines for traditional users to identify the structural conflicts in integrating web services.
