Title: Professional Service Firms - Developing a Taxonomy for Workforce Planning

Author: Vincent Hargaden, Director, Master of Engineering Management Program, University College Dublin, Engineering & Materials Science Centre, Belfield, Dublin 4, Ireland, vincent.hargaden@ucd.ie

Coauthor(s): Jennifer Ryan

Year: 2013

Abstract: The area of workforce planning in service systems has gained significant attention recently, with researchers applying different approaches to modeling the various aspects of the human capital supply chain. In this paper, we develop a taxonomy for professional service firms which brings together all the stages of the human capital supply chain. This allows us to identify where and how models can be applied to support the workforce planning process.
