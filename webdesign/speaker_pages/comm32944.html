Title: Network Equilibrium and Design under Information Provision
Author: Avinash Unnikrishnan, Graduate Student, University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Station C1761, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, avinash@mail.utexas.edu
Coauthor(s): Travis Waller
Year: 2008
Abstract: The focus of this work is to determine analytical network equilibrium and design formulations which account for online information provision. Frank Wolfe based solution methods will be discussed where the sub-problems involve calculating online shortest paths.
