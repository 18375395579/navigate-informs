Title: Identifying and Prioritizing Shared Rail Corridor Technical Challenges
Author: Rapik Saat, Research Assistant Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 205 N Mathews, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, mohdsaat@illinois.edu
Coauthor(s): Brennan Caughron, Christopher Barkan
Year: 2012
Abstract: New passenger and high-speed rail developments in the U.S. may involve infrastructure sharing with existing freight railroads. It is important to safely and effectively accommodate any new passenger service while sustaining ongoing freight rail transportation efficiency and growth. This study identified important technical challenges, knowledge gaps and research needs related to shared freight and passenger rail corridors.
