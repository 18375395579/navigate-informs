Title: 2013 Ramsey Medal Award
Author: James Dyer, Professor, University of Texas at Austin, CBA 3.218, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, Jim.Dyer@mccombs.utexas.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Ramsey Medal of the Decision Analysis Society is awarded for distinguished contributions in decision analysis. Distinguished contributions can be internal, such as theoretical and procedural advances in decision analysis, or external, such as developing or spreading decision analysis in new fields. We will introduce the 2013 Ramsey Medal winner, followed by a presentation by the winner.
