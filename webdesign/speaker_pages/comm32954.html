Title: The Academics of OR Practice

Author: Karen Holness, PhD Student, University at Buffalo, 435 Bell Hall, Department of Industrial Engineering, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States, holness@eng.buffalo.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The interdisciplinary nature of OR warrants an investigation into the core skills required for successful OR project work. This research examines the key practitioner skills identified in the OR, MS and Industrial Engineering literature and correlates them with those obtained from our empirical questionnaire and interview studies.
