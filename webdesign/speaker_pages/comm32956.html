Title: Managing Customer Expectations and Priorities in Service Systems
Author: Qiuping Yu, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, QiupingYu2009@u.northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Gad Allon, Achal Bassamboo
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study in a service environment, how to manage customers' expectations and to prioritize customers appropriately to maximize the firm's profits. Specifically, we focus on a setting where the firm uses only delay announcements and study the opportunities and limitations of this mechanism. We are particularly interested in when and how the customers can be influenced by delay announcements.
