Title: The Effect of Electronic Medical Records on Hospital Performance

Author: Abby Swanson, Assistant Professor, Department of Health Administration & Policy, Medical University of South Carolina, 151 Rutledge Ave., Building B, PO Box 250961, Charleston, SC, 29425, swansonaj@vcu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yasar A. Ozcan

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study examines the prevalence and performance of Electronic Medical Records (EMRs) in acute care hospitals. Using logistic regression, organizational and environmental factors associated with hospital EMR use are identified. Data Envelopment Analysis and post hoc evaluations performed for comparison of hospitals with and without EMRs in efficiency and quality.
