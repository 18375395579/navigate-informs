Title: Reflections on the Field of Revenue Management

Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, 412 Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, gjv1@columbia.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: I will offer some personal reflections on my experience in the field of revenue management and why, after many years, it still continues to interest me. I'll also try to draw lessons learned from various successes and failures along the way, both in terms of my direct project experience and my observations of the field overall.
