Title: Performance Management and Optimization in Service Operations

Author: John McKenna, VP, Product Development, ac2 Solutions, Inc., 12 Crown Plaza, Suite 207, Hazlet, NJ, 07730, United States of America, johnsmckenna@ac2solutions.com

Coauthor(s): Turgut Aykin

Year: 2012

Abstract: Forecasting, Capacity Planning and Scheduling are planning processes that are based on forecasts and other assumptions regarding the future. Deviations from assumptions during operations are unavoidable. This presentation describes several methods for managing performance in “real-time?including performance, skills and routing optimization, adherence and activity management.
