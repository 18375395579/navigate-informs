Title: Humanitarian Staff Reassignment: Incorporating Centralized Utilities into Stable Matchings

Author: Mallory Soldner, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, msoldner@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Ozlem Ergun, Julie L. Swann

Year: 2012

Abstract: Humanitarian organizations regularly face the challenge of reassigning staff member to different positions and countries. We extend the classic Gale-Shapely stable matching model to include centralized utilities for matches for the overall good of the organization. Extensions for couples and decision-making over time are also investigated.
