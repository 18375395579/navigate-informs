Title: Assessing the Impact of Global Price Interdependencies

Author: Anke Richter, Associate Professor, DRMI Naval Postgraduate School, 699 Dyer Rd., Bldg. 234, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, arichter@nps.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Documented launch delays and the ensuing debate over their underlying causes have focused on assessment from the individual country perspective. Seen in a larger game theoretic framework, this may cause problems because while countries see an individual game, the pharmaceutical firm sees a repeated, linked game. A theoretical mixed integer linear model of the firm’s launch and pricing decisions is presented along with examples wherein international price dependencies most likely played a role.
