Title: The Trade-off Between Pregnancy Rate and OHSS Risk in In Vitro Fertilization-embryo Transfer

Author: Miao He, Tsinghua University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Beijing, 100084, China, hem03@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Lei Zhao, Yue Zhang

Year: 2007

Abstract: During the hyperstimulation process in In Vitro Fertilization-Embryo Transfer (IVF-ET), doctors need to decide on gonadotropin doses to balance the tradeoff between pregnancy rate and the risk of ovarian hyperstimulation syndrome (OHSS). Supported by clinical data, we use approximate dynamic programming models to capture the dynamic nature of the process and the variability of individual responses. Our goal is to provide doctors with decision supporting tools to balance the tradeoff.
