Title: Realizing the Vision of Social Radar

Author: Captain Dylan Schmorrow, Office of the Assistant Secretary of Defense (Research and Engineering), 4800 Mark Center Drive, Suite 17E08, Alexandria, VA, United States of America, Dylan.Schmorrow@osd.mil

Year: 2013

Abstract: To meet current and emerging challenges, we need a 'social radar' (integrated capabilities for situation awareness) which include alerting, course of action analysis, and measures of effectiveness). CAPT Schmorrow will describe the needs for a Social Radar, discuss leading technical challenges, and share some early successes realizing the vision.
