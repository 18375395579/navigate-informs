Title: Saul Gass: A Walking, Talking Encyclopedia of O.R.

Author: Peter Horner, Editor, Lionheart Publishing, 506 Roswell St, Suite 220, Marietta, GA, 30060, United States of America, horner@lionhrtpub.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: While Saul Gass played an instrumental role in the lives of countless O.R. students through his teaching, tutelage and textbooks, he also served the greater O.R. community in his unofficial yet widely recognized role as an O.R. pioneer, statesman, ambassador and historian. This talk focuses on the latter, particularly his encyclopedic knowledge of all things O.R. and his many contributions over the past two decades to OR/MS Today, as observed and appreciated by the editor.
