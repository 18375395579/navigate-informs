Title: Panel Discussion: Disaster Preparedness and Response and Inter-Organizational Collaboration
Author: Elizabeth Critchley, Red Cross, CritchleyE@usa.redcross.org
Coauthor(s): Louiqa Rashid, Rosemary Parnell, Eric Smith
Year: 2008
Abstract: The panelists will discuss the challenges and opportunities in disaster preparedness and response, and the role of intra- and inter-enterprise collaboration and coordination.
