Title: Modeling Diversion From Truck to Rail Intermodal Service

Author: Gene Tyworth, Professor Supply Chain Management, Penn State University, 454B Business Bldg, University Park, PA, 16802, United States, jet@psu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: The U.S. DOT developed the Intermodal Transportation and Inventory Model (ITIC) for policy analysis concerning long-haul freight traffic in its Comprehensive Truck Size and Weight Study submitted to Congress in 2000. A recently released version for estimating potential truck diversion to rail intermodal service is critically reviewed.
