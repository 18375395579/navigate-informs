Title: A Cooperative Investment Decision Model for Renewable Energy
Author: Alireza Ghalebani, PhD Student, University of South Florida, 4202 East Fowler Avenue, ENB 118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, alireza@mail.usf.edu
Coauthor(s): Tapas Das
Year: 2013
Abstract: Using the financial incentives, neighborhood size and demand characteristics, alternative technologies for capital equipment, and weather data as input, a MIP decision model is developed. The model determines the optimal level of investment in renewable energy production, storage, and distribution for the members, while minimizing the annual cost of energy.
