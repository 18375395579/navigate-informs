Title: Optimize Treatment Outcome through Personalized Health Management

Author: Chih-Lin Chi, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, chihlin.chi@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Studying patterns among patient characteristics, treatment options, and outcomes enable optimizing treatment options and outcome based on patients?characteristics. We will discuss a two-step approach to speed the implementation of personalized care in a clinical setting: (1) discovering the pattern and evidence from medical data, and (2) producing transparent clinical rules which can thereafter be confirmed by clinical experts and for which clinical trials can be executed to test the results.
