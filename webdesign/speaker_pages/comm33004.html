Title: Estimating the Insurer’s Willingness-to-Pay for Colorectal Cancer Screening Tests
Author: Reza Yaesoubi, PhD Student, North Carolina State University, 421 Daniels Hall, Edward P. Fitts Department of Industrial, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, ryaesou@ncsu.edu
Coauthor(s): Stephen Roberts
Year: 2008
Abstract: Among the factors influencing Colorectal Cancer (CRC) screening test utilization, the role of health insurers has gained considerable attention. We propose an analytical model for the market of CRC screening tests which can be used to estimate the insurer’s willingness-to-pay to acquire one additional life year by covering the CRC screening tests.
