Title: Development of a Human-based Metric Selection Protocol

Author: Gratchen Macht, Pennsylvania State University, 244 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, gam201@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): David A. Nembhard

Year: 2012

Abstract: Relevant solutions to current complex problems require the incorporation of highly functional collaborative skills within a team-like framework. A human-based selection protocol was developed through a mixture of technical skills and psychological profiles to address interdisciplinary teams. The results impact team formulation to improve the overall team performance.
