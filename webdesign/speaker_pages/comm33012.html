Title: The Services Factory: Models and Challenges
Author: Abhijit Bose, Member of Research Staff, IBM T. J. Watson Research, 19 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, NY, 10532, United States of America, bosea@us.ibm.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: Manufacturing has benefitted tremendously from advancements in automation, standardization, inventory control and supply chains. The services sector has yet to achieve such economies of scale, quality and efficiency. There has been significant interest in understanding how IT, healthcare and other service domains can benefit from initiatives undertaken in manufacturing. This session will identify the issues faced by the services sector today in achieving factory-scale efficiencies.
