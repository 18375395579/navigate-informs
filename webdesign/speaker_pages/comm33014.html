Title: Service Systems with Anecdotal Reasoning Consumers
Author: Ying-Ju Chen, UC Berkeley, Berkeley, United States of America, chen@ieor.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Yimin Yu, Tingliang Huang
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study service systems where consumers have boundedly rational expectations. In particular, we assume that consumers lack full capability or ample opportunity to perfectly infer the waiting time, and thus can only rely on past experiences and anecdotal reasoning to make their joining or balking decisions.
