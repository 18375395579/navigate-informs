Title: Bicycle Tours: Modeling the Perceived Exertion of a Daily Path

Author: Katherine Carl, Graduate Associate, University of Arizona, 1130 E. Helen Street, PO Box 210108, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, kcarl@email.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Moshe Dror

Year: 2012

Abstract: Bicycling has garnered interest in recent years. Perceived exertion is an important consideration when selecting bicycling tours. We propose a model for generating measures of perceived exertion for bicycling tours for 5 categories of riders. We present results of a survey designed to verify the accuracy of our model and demonstrate a good fit with exertion values reported by cyclists.
