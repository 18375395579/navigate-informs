Title: Conducting Behavioral OM Experiments via Multi-media Discrete Choice Analysis
Author: Rohit Verma, Associate Professor, Cornell University, School of Hotel Administration, 338 Statler Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, rv54@cornell.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Discrete choice analysis has its roots in economics but is being widely used to study a variety of research topics in operations management, marketing, product and service development and other related fields. The purpose of this presentation is to introduce the participants with the theory and different applications of discrete choice analysis. Examples from manufacturing, healthcare and service sector industries will be presented.
