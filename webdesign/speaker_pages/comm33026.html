Title: Modeling Papient Migration Behavior for Congestive Heart Failure Readmissions

Author: Sezgin Ayabakan, PhD Student, University of Texas at Dallas, 800 W. Campbell Road, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, ayabakan@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Indranil Bardhan, Zhiqiang Zheng

Year: 2012

Abstract: The literature does not consider the migration of patients across hospitals, which leads to an underestimation of readmission rates. We model the readmission of CHF patients by accounting for patient migration across hospitals in North Texas. We develop a Hidden Markov model to predict readmission rates associated with CHF patients.
