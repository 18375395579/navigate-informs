Title: Panel Discussion: Academic Job Search
Author: Talisa Murray, Systems Analyst, The Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory, 11100 Johns Hopkins Road, Laurel, MD, 20723, Talisa.Murray@jhuapl.edu
Coauthor(s): Dessislava Pachamanova, Ana Muriel, Gilvan Gil Souza, Andrew Loerch
Year: 2008
Abstract: The panel will lead an informal discussion concerning the academic interview process, the appropriate steps to take during the job search, and the challenges that may be faced along the way. Time will be provided for questions and answers.
