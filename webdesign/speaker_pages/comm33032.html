Title: Saul's Encyclopedia of OR/MS

Author: Michael Fu, Professor, University of Maryland, Smith School of Business, Van Munching Hall, College Park, MD, United States of America, mfu@umd.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Saul Gass' last project was the 3rd edition of the Encyclopedia of Operations Research and Management Science, published by Springer this year posthumously. The 1st and 2nd editions were published in 1996 and 2001, respectively, by Kluwer Academic Publishers, with Carl Harris as co-editor. This talk will be about my dear friend and colleague Saul and the Encyclopedia, for which I was privileged to be able to serve as co-editor.
