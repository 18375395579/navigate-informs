Title: Machine Learning Applications in Social Media Analytics
Author: Rick Lawrence, Manager, Machine Learning, IBM Research, Business Analytics & Mathematical Scienc, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, ricklawr@us.ibm.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: We will discuss applications of machine learning to several challenges inherent in the analysis of social media including (a) prediction-based approaches to characterizing the influence of participants in a social media context, (b) combined lexical and machine-learned sentiment models, and (c) NMF-based techniques for extracting a lower-order topic space that best reconstructs a document corpus. We will also describe a current UNICEF project in classifying SMS text messages in Uganda.
