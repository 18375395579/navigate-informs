Title: A Decision Support Tool for Generation Capacity Investments in Restructured Power Markets

Author: Vishnu Nanduri, Ph.D. Candidate, Industrial Engineering, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave., Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, vnanduri@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Tapas Das, Patricio Rocha

Year: 2008

Abstract: With a significant number markets trading electricity in restructured settings, a significant proportion of capacity expansion in the future will have to take place in market based environments. However, since a majority of the expansion literature is focused on regulated market structures, there is a critical need for multiperiod, multiplayer, capacity expansion models. We present a 3-Tier game theoretic model to obtain expansion plans, while considering several relevant market features.
