Title: Graphene Growth Process Modeling

Author: Jian Wu, USC ISE, 3715 McClintock Avenue, GER 236, Los Angeles, CA 90089-0193, United States of America, wujian@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Qiang Huang

Year: 2012

Abstract: Graphene growth on Cu foils shows four-lobed-symmetric shape and angle dependent growth velocity. We decompose the growth process and develop a modeling approach to describe the area coverage and geometric shape of graphene islands.
