Title: How to Use Healthcare Operational Data for Benchmarking and Efficiency Improvement

Author: Samuel Hohmann, Senior Manager, University HealthSystem Consortium, 2001 Spring Road, Suite 700, Oak Brook, IL, 60523, United States of America, hohmann@uhc.edu

Coauthor(s): Shital Shah

Year: 2008

Abstract: University Healthcare Consortium is a rich source of both administrative and operational data that provide unique insight into the healthcare operations in Academic Medical Centers. The applicability of the healthcare operational data for benchmarking and efficiency improvement is demonstrated on laboratory services using data and other quantative apporaches.
