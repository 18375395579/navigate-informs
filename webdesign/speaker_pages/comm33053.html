Title: Panel DIscussion: ETMERC - Engineering and Technology Management Education and Research Council
Author: Antonie de Klerk, President ETMERC, University of Pretoria, Pretoria, South Africa, amklerk@eng.up.ac.za
Coauthor(s): Tinus Pretorius, Anthony Bailett, John Aje, William Flanner
Year: 2006
Abstract: ETMERC (Engineering and Technology Management Education & Research Council) addresses educational and research issues common to all educational institutions in this field. Activities include conducting benchmark studies, developing curriculum guidelines, establishing quality criteria, defining research agenda among others. ETMERC will be explained and activities described; an open discussion will be held on strategic issues, future directions and members?pro-active roles in defining the field.
