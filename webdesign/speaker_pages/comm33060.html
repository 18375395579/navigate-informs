Title: Marketing Budget Allocation
Coauthor(s): Randy Erdahl, Erik Miller
Year: 2008
Abstract: Today's retail marketers must allocate their advertising spend across customer segments, marketing channels, and time periods to optimize short & long term financial metrics. This solution leverages historical customer behavior with simulation & optimization techniques to create an effective marketing budget allocation tool. With a catalog retailer example, the allocation tool will recommend the allocation strategy to meet current & future year goals.
