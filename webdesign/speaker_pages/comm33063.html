Title: Carbon-Optimal and Carbon-Neutral Supply Chains

Author: Felipe Caro, University of California-Los Angeles, Anderson School of Management, 110 Westwood Plaza, Los Angeles, CA, 90095, United States of America, fcaro@anderson.ucla.edu

Coauthor(s): Charles Corbett, Tarkan Tan, Rob Zuidwijk

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper we explore the differences between making a supply chain carbon-neutral by offsetting all emissions vs. making it carbon-optimal by inducing all parties to invest appropriately in reducing GHG emissions. We examine conditions under which first-best can and cannot be achieved, and examine various decentralized outcomes.
