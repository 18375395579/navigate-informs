Title: From Process Changes to Reduced Re-admissions: A Financial ROIC Calculator for Project BOOST

Author: Itai Gurvich, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, 2001 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, United States of America, i-gurvich@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Eric Park, Jan Van Mieghem, Jiajun Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: Project BOOST (Better Outcomes by Optimizing Safe Transitions), led by Northwestern Memorial Hospital, promotes a toolkit of process changes to reduce patient re-admissions. Our collaboration with the hospital studies how BOOST tools impact patient outcomes and financial results. A product of this collaboration is a return on investment calculator (ROIC). The ROIC assists hospital administrators in making informed decisions regarding the adoption of specific elements of the BOOST toolkit.
