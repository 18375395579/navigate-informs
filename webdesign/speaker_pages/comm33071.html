Title: Assesment of Risk of Supplier Disruption

Author: Peter Trkman, University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Economics, Kardeljeva ploscad 17, Ljubljana, Slovenia, peter.trkman@ef.uni-lj.si

Coauthor(s): Kevin McCormack

Year: 2008

Abstract: The ability to identify which supplier has greater potential of a disruption is a critical first step in managing the frequency and impact of these disruptions. A presented approach enables the classification of suppliers based on a supplier’s attributes, performance and supply chain characteristics, modified by factors in the supplier’s specific environment. A new portfolio approach for the assessment and classification of suppliers is presented.
