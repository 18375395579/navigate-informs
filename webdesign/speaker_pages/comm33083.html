Title: An Expert Model of Pandemic Influenza Risks
Author: Wandi Bruin de Bruin, Research Scientist, Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, wandi@andrew.cmu.edu
Coauthor(s): Baruch Fischhoff, Larry Brilliant, Denise Caruso
Year: 2006
Abstract: Predicting the potentially looming influenza pandemic requires an exercise in judgment, the expertise for which is distributed over many individuals and disciplines. We developed a model of pandemic influenza, with input from an interdisciplinary group of influenza experts, including estimates for key variables potentially affecting the extent of a pandemic.
