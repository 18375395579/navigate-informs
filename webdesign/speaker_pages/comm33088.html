Title: Empirically Derived Service Principles- The Ten Commandments of the Service Management

Author: Annibal Scavarda, Visiting Associate Professor of Business Management, Marriott School of Management/ Brigham Young University, Brigham Young University, 660 TNRB, Provo, UT, 84602-3131, United States of America, annibal@esp.puc-rio.br

Coauthor(s): Tatiana Bouzdine-Chameeva, Arthur Hill, Susan Meyer Goldstein

Year: 2008

Abstract: Service management is an amorphous field in need of a common structure. The macro view of this field currently relies on practitioners and academics from a wide variety of disciplines, viewpoints, and geographies to develop both practical and theoretical perspectives. A three round service management research study is presented, reflecting collective understanding of collaborators from more around 100 countries. An analysis of Rounds 1 and 2 of this study will be presented here.
