Title: Learning Profiles Through Strategic Website Design
Author: Pelin Atahan, University of Texas at Dallas, 2601 N. Floyd Rd., SM 33, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States, pxa041000@utdallas.edu
Coauthor(s): Sumit Sarkar
Year: 2007
Abstract: Personalization and recommendation systems play an important role in online business settings. An important determinant of the performance of these systems is the user profile available to the system. We study how user profiles can be implicitly learnt based on a user’s traversal of a web site.
