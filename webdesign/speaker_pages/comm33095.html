Title: Fleeting with Robust Scheduling Design
Author: Diego Klabjan, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, Ind. Eng. and Mgmt. Sci., d-klabjan@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Yu-Ching Lee, Milind Sohoni
Year: 2008
Abstract: We study the problem of capacity planning and schedule design. In particular, we focus on block times and passenger connections. These decisions are integrated with capacity planning. The underlying concept is under production consideration at a major software vendor.
