Title: The Critical Asset and Portfolio Risk Analysis Methodology: Overview, Implementation, and Directions
Author: William McGill, Center for Technology and Systems Management, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, wmcgill@umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Bilal Ayyub
Year: 2006
Abstract: This paper describes the Critical Asset and Portfolio Risk Analysis (CAPRA) methodology for informing critical infrastructure protection resource allocation decisions. The results from the CAPRA analysis are communicated to all relevant stakeholders as actionable risk assessments. A case study highlighting implementation issues will be provided.
