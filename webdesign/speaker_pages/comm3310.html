Title: Recent Developments in SDP and SOCP Software
Author: Hans Mittelmann, Professor, Arizona State University, Box 871804, Tempe, AZ, 85287-1804, United States, beck@plato.la.asu.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: After an intense development period some years ago, further improvements of SDP and SOCP codes have taken considerable time. We will try to assess the current state-of-the-art.
