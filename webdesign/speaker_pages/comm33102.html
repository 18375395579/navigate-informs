Title: Uncertainty and Surprise in Energy Forecasts: The Cost of Photovoltaics

Author: Max Henrion, CEO, Lumina Decision Systems, 26010 Highland Way, Los Gatos, CA, 95033-9758, United States of America, henrion@lumina.com

Coauthor(s): Evan Sherwin

Year: 2012

Abstract: Power system planners and other energy analysts would like to know the future performance and costs of renewable and fossil energy technologies. Several research groups have used expert elicitation to assess uncertainty using probability distributions for these quantities. We compare projections for photovoltaics. Recent dramatic and unexpected cost reductions suggest systematic overconfidence in expert assessments.
