Title: Equilibrium Model-based Comparison of CO2 Allowance Allocation Schemes in Multiple Markets

Author: Benjamin Hobbs, JHU, 313 Ames DoGEE, Baltimore, MD, 21218, United States of America, bhobbs@jhu.edu

Coauthor(s): Jinye Zhao, Jong-Shi Pang

Year: 2007

Abstract: When different allowance allocation rules apply to different jurisdictions, but their industries compete in a common market, output-based allocation can sometimes be superior to auctioning. A complementarity model of a long run electricity market in which submarkets are subject to different allocation rules is used to examine how alternative schemes affect electricity prices, generation mix, emissions, and economic efficiency.
