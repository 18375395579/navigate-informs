Title: Ethical Issues in Supply Chain Competition: A Game Theoretical Approach
Author: Brian Lee, Operations and Information Management Dept, School of Business, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road Unit 1041, Storrs, CT, 06268, United States of America, brian.lee@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Jose Cruz
Year: 2013
Abstract: Today businesses are trying to strike a difficult balance between service level and ethical (responsible) operation due to growing competition and short product life-cycles. While focusing on maximizing profit, firms face the risk of boycotts as a result of falling short of the stakeholder's expectation by their partners in labor or environmental issues. This paper studies, under competition, the effects of the firms?decision to disclose or not social information of their suppliers.
