Title: Empirical Analysis of Inventory Display in Retail

Author: Antonio Moreno-Garcia, Kellogg School of Management, 2001 Sheridan Road, Evanston, 60208, United States of America, a-morenogarcia@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Santiago Gallino

Year: 2012

Abstract: Using a combination of observational data and a field experiment, we explore the consequences of inventory display strategies in retail environments across different categories.
