Title: Perspectives on Research in Supply Chain Security and Resiliency
Author: Alan Erera, Assistant Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, alan.erera@isye.gatech.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: This talk will provide some perspectives on the role of operations research and management science in the area of supply chain security and resiliency. Using examples, the presentation will attempt to identify interesting problem domains for study, and where enhancements are needed in exisiting methodological solution approaches. Some ideas generated from an NSF workshop on this topic will also be discussed.
