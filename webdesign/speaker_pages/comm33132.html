Title: Nursing Home Performance Evaluation on Patient-Centered Outcomes

Author: Paul Hong, Professor, University of Toledo, 2801 W. Bancroft, Toledo, United States of America, hong@utoledo.edu

Coauthor(s): Yue Zhang, Huey-Shys Chen, Diane Salvador

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this research, we apply the Data Envelopment Analysis (DEA) technique to performance evaluation of nursing homes with the focus on patient-centered outcomes. The databased for our analysis contains 9622 nursing homes in the U.S. We find that for-profit nursing homes and nursing homes belonging to a chain are more efficient, and that the larger the nursing home, the more efficient.
