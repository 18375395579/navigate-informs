Title: Appointment Access & Engineering Control of Healthcare Systems
Author: James Benneyan, Northeastern University, 360 Huntington Ave., 334 Snell Engineering Center, Boston, MA, 02120, United States of America, benneyan@coe.neu.edu
Year: 2012
