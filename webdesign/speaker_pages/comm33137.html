Title: Optimizing a Portfolio of Credit Derivatives

Author: Jack Kim, Stanford University, Terman Center, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, jackkim@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Kay Giesecke

Year: 2008

Abstract: We develop computationally tractable methods for optimizing the future mark-to-market value of a portfolio consisting of credit derivatives such as credit swaps or collateralized debt obligations.
