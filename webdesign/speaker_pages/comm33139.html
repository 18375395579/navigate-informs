Title: Workforce Optimization for IT Application Service Providers
Author: Rohit Lotlikar, Research Staff Member, IBM India Research Lab, EGL Block D,, Domulr Inner Ring Rd, Bangalore, KA, 560034, India, rohitmlo@in.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Guruduth Banavar, Munish Goyal, Nandakishore Kambhatla, Debapriyo Majumdar, Gyana Parija, Soujanya Soni, Sambuddha Roy
Year: 2008
Abstract: In large IT Services organizations, efficient matching of practitioners to project openings is critical to profitability. Practitioners and project openings are characterized by certain structured and unstructured attributes. Complexity arises due to inter-relationships between attributes, missing or incorrect data, as well as the need to be customize to user needs. We use constrained optimization and machine learning techniques, packaged in a customizable web based user interface.
