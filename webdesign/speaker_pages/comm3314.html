Title: Technology Adoption and Performance Among Medical Service Providers

Author: J. Lamar Pierce, Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, jlp1@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Aaron Chatterji

Year: 2006

Abstract: When and how to acquire new medical equipment is an important strategic decision for health care firms. Especially for new firms, significant tradeoffs exist between financial stability and quality of services. This study uses data on medical equipment to investigate the impact of technology adoption and financing on firm performance.
