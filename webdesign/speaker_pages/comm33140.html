Title: Experimental Evidence on Time-money-tradeoffs

Author: Katrin Burmeister, Ph.D Student, Humboldt-Universitaet zu Berlin, Spandauer Str. 1, Berlin, 10178, Germany, burmeister@wiwi.hu-berlin.de

Coauthor(s): Christian Schade

Year: 2007

Abstract: We measure time-money-tradeoffs in the laboratory as well as in the field. Relevance for entrepreneurship research is discussed.
