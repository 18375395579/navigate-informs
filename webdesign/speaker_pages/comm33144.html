Title: Determinants of Crowdfunding Success: A Social Influence Perspective

Author: Eun Ju Jung, PhD Candidate, Michigan State University, Broad College of Business, Dept. of Accounting&Information systems, East Lansing, United States of America, jung@bus.msu.edu

Coauthor(s): Vallabh Sambamurthy, Anjana Susarla

Year: 2013

Abstract: The emergence of crowdfunding could unleash substantial changes in the business environment. Worldwide crowdfunding raised almost $2.7 billion in 2012 (crowdsroucing.org). Although there are several academic studies on crowdfunding, it is important to highlight the collective evaluation mechanisms of crowdfunding. In this paper, we empirically investigate the impact of social influence and network characteristics on the likelihood of funding success, using data collected from Kickstarter.com.
