Title: Treatment Decision Flow of Acute Myeloid Leukemia (AML)
Author: Andrew J. Blair, Portland State University, PO Box 751, Portland, OR, United States, blairaj@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Baqer Alali
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper discusses the various leukemia and posts statistical numbers that show how this disease is contributing to cancer fatalities. The most common types of treatment are reviewed. Through literature reviews and interviews, a decision flow diagram is proposed that shows steps and options available to the patient and oncologist to use in treatment of Acute Myeloid Leukemia.
