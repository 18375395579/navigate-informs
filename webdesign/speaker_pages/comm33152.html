Title: Operationalizing Sustainable Production Strategies Subject to Diminishing Resources

Author: Manuel Nunez, Associate Professor, School of Business, UConn, 2100 Hillside Road Unit 1041, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, mnunez@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Hsiao-Hui Lee

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a two-stage dynamic model to analyze different sustainability strategic decisions such as starting a recycling program or developing alternative technologies to replace a diminishing resource. We investigate the impact of the parameters on the optimal decision and discuss how to operationalize the decisions in the supply chain.
