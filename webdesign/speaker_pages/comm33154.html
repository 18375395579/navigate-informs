Title: Risk Aversion and the Single Server Queue

Author: Martin Lariviere, Kellogg School of Management, Evanston, IL, United States of America, m-lariviere@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Wuqin Lin

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider managing a queuing system when the decision maker is risk averse. Of particular interest is whether risk aversion results in too little capacity (a standard result for newsvendor models) or too much capacity relative the risk neutral case.
