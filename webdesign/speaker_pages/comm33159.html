Title: Sensor Data Characterization
Author: Soundar Kumara, Allen E. Pearce/Allen M. Pearce Chaired Professor, The Pennsylvania State University, 310 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, skumara@psu.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this talk the importance of sensor data analysis and real-time aspects as important components of quality control curriculum will be discussed. Ideas on the aspects of real time signal characterization will be presented.
