Title: You Can Do It, We Can Help: An OR Toolkit for Patient Scheduling

Author: Sherry Weaver, Haskayne School of Business, University of Calgary, 2500 University Drive NW, Calgary, AB, T2N1N4, Canada, sherry.weaver@haskayne.ucalgary.ca

Coauthor(s): Michael Carter, Tom Noseworthy

Year: 2008

Abstract: You are familiar with this scenario: after developing a successful simulation/DSS for one location, you're asked to create a similar system for a slightly dissimilar location. We're in the fortunate situation of knowing we'll be asked to create similar patient scheduling systems at multiple facilities performing hip/knee arthroplasty. Hence, our goal is to create a flexible 'OR toolkit' for efficient implementation across multiple sites. Lessons 'learned' and 'learning still' will be presented.
