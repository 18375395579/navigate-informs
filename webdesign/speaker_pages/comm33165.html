Title: Data Sharing, Information Privacy and Incentive-compatibility

Author: Xiaobai (Bob) Li, College of Management, University of Massachusetts Lowell, One University Ave., Lowell, MA, 01854, United States, xiaobai_li@uml.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We analyze the conflict between data gathering/usage and information privacy using a game-theoretic approach. An incentive-compatible mechanism is developed for sharing/disseminating confidential data.
