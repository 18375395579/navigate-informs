Title: Cost and Quality of Healthcare Supply Chains

Author: Edward Pohl, Assoc Professor, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering Ctr, Fayetteville, AR, United States of America, epoh@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Heather Nachtmann, Brian Smith

Year: 2008

Abstract: The cost and quality of healthcare is a much debated issue. Early studies have shown the healthcare supply chain to be inefficient and expensive when compared to supply chains in other sectors. Our study seeks to identify opportunities for cost and quality improvement in the healthcare supply chain.
