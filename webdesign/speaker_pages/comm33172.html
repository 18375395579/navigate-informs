Title: Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) Treatment Algorithm
Author: Dean Hartley, Principal, Hartley Consulting, 106 Windsong Lane, Oak Ridge, TN, 37830, United States, DSHartley3@comcast.net
Year: 2006
Abstract: PTSD affects both civilians and military personnel and is of concern to the international health community. The International Psychopharmacology Algorithm Project (IPAP) is using OR in creating treatment algorithms for mental disorders, including PTSD. The PTSD algorithm will be described, along with lessons learned about the algorithm creation process.
