Title: Dynamic Congestion Pricing Strategies with Drivers' Stochastic Dynamic Behavior

Author: Fan Yang, City University of New York (CUNY), fyang@ccny.cuny.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Drivers?behavior plays an important role in the success of a congestion pricing strategy. This study aims at providing insight on how to force the traffic system to evolve from the status quo to a stationary state of system optimal, considering a general drivers' stochastic and dynamic behavior adjustment process.
