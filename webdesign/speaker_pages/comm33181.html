Title: Panel Discussion: OR Entrepreneurship

Author: Bruce Golden, University of Maryland, R.H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, bgolden@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Jean-Marc Rousseau, Yossi Sheffi, Sridhar Tayur, Michael Hooban, Irv Lustig

Year: 2006

Abstract: Many members of INFORMS have worked in an entrepreneurial capacity. Some have started and/or grown companies on a full-time basis. Others (e.g., professors) have started companies on a part-time basis. The purpose of this session is to tell some of the stories associated with these activities and to discuss common experiences, obstacles, and strategies.
