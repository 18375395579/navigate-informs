Title: Information Technology and Firm Efficiency: A Non-Parametric Analysis

Author: Indranil Bardhan, Assistant Professor, The University of Texas at Dallas, School of Management, SM 41, 2601 N. Floyd Road, Richardson, TX, 75083-0688, United States, bardhan@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Shu Lin, Sunil Mithas

Year: 2006

Abstract: We apply data envelopment analyses to study the impact of information technology investments across a large sample of global firms from 1998 to 2004. Using panel data on IT spending, we explore the impact of IT on firm efficiency and study trends in firm efficiencies over time.
