Title: Effectiveness of Utilizing Expected Mortality Information in Attestation Note

Author: Xuan Huang, Univ of Alabama at Birmingham, 1150 10th avenue south, Birmingham, United States of America, nuoxu@uab.edu

Coauthor(s): nuo xu

Year: 2013

Abstract: A pilot study of presenting expected mortality information for patients in attestation template started from November 2012, in which one group of attending physicians used the attestation notes while the other did not. The paper looks at the collected data to evaluate the effectiveness of utilizing such information in improving the quality of care.
