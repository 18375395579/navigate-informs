Title: History of Operations Research Applied to Search and Rescue
Author: John Frost, Information/Search Planning Specialist, U. S. Coast Guard, Office of Search and Rescue, Suite 3106, 2100 Second St. SW, Washington, DC, 20593-0001, United States of America, John.R.Frost@uscg.mil
Year: 2008
Abstract: This presentation reviews the application of operations research, and search theory in particular, to search and rescue since search theory's initial development during the Second World War.
