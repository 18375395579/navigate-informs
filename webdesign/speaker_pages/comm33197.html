Title: Analysis of Aggregated Signals for Multiple Embedded Operations using Wavelets

Author: Yong Lei, University of Michigan, IOE department, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, ylei@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Judy Jin

Year: 2008

Abstract: In many complex systems, the measured sensor signals are usually the aggregated responses of multiple embedded operations. This research is develop a new multi-resolution analysis method to enhance the monitoring capability of individual embedded operation based on the aggregated sensing signals.
