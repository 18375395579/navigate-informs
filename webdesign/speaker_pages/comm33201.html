Title: Valuation of Startup Firms Under Market, Technology and Time Uncertainties
Author: Leonardo Santiago, Assistant Professor, Federal University of Minas Gerais - UFMG, Production Engineering Department, Av. Antonio Carlos, 6627.Pampulha., Belo Horizonte, MG, 31270-901, Brazil, lsantiago@ufmg.br
Coauthor(s): Henrique Mendes Silva
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider the problem of valuating a startup firm in the early stages of its creation under market, technology, and development time uncertainties. Our model takes into account the management value of the startup development process, and the opportunity created by those uncertainties. We analyze the impact of these uncertainties on the value of a startup and discuss the model’s practical implementation.
