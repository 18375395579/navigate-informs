Title: Teaching Decision Analysis with the Case Method

Author: Casey Lichtendahl, University of Virginia, Darden School of Business, 100 Darden Blvd., P. O. Box 6550, Charlottesville, VA, 22906, United States of America, lichtendahlc@darden.virginia.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: I will address a question I am frequently asked, How do you teach Decision Analysis, or any quantitative subject, without lecturing in the classroom? I will discuss the power of the flipped classroom approach that the Darden School of Business and other case-method schools/teachers have been using for decades.
