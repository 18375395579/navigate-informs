Title: Current Challenges in Kidney Exchange

Author: Itai Ashlagi, Assistant Professor, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, E-62, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, iashlagi@mit.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Kidney exchange has grown into an enterprise involving many hospitals and overlapping exchange networks, and in the process the set of strategic players has changed, and so has the patient pool. I’ll discuss recent market design developments and further challenges.
