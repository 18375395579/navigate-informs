Title: Measuring Energy Efficiency in the Context of an Emerging Economy: The Case of Indian Manufacturing

Author: Kankana Mukherjee, Department of Management, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, 100 Institute Road, Worcester, MA, 01609, kmukher@wpi.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In recent years the Indian economy has been striving to achieve a rapid growth rate. However, one of the challenges facing the economy is the high levels of energy supply that would be needed to sustain this growth path. Hence attaining energy efficiency is crucial for the economy. This paper examines the Indian manufacturing sector and utilizes Data Envelopment Analysis to obtain comprehensive measures of performance given the joint goals of achieving GDP growth as well as energy conservation.
