Title: Markets for Innovation: An Equilibrium Models of R&D Contests
Author: Christian Terwiesch, Associate Professor, University of Pennsylvania, 548 JMHH, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, terwiesch@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Yi Xu
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present an analytical model of a community of R&D problem solvers. Firms can post R&D problems to this community and offer potential rewards for satisfactory solutions. We explain why such markets exist, how the function, and how the should me managed.
