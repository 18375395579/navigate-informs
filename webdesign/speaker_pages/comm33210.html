Title: Managing Integrated Main and Regional Carriers Recovery Decisions

Author: Julian Pachon, Principal O.R. Scientist, Navitaire, Inc., 9130 Jollyville Rd, Suite 100, Austin, TX, 78759, United States of America, Julian.Pachon@navitaire.com

Coauthor(s): Stuart Smith

Year: 2007

Abstract: A common regional carrier model is to transport passengers who book directly with the main carrier. Once a flight schedule is determined, all operational decisions are generally done independently. Since disruptions to the schedule affect both the main and regional carriers, independent recovery decisions lead to sub-optimal solutions. We discuss approaches that integrate both main and regional decisions. We describe the integrated recovery problem and present its technological challenges.
