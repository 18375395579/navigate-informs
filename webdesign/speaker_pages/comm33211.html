Title: Generalized Maximum Commonality Problems

Author: Yunxia Zhu, Doctoral Student, University of Texas at Dallas, Richardson, Richardson, tx, 75070, United States of America, yunxia.zhu@student.utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Milind Dawande

Year: 2008

Abstract: Motivated by applications in software project management, we generalize the notion of commonality presented in Dawande et al. (2008) in the context of pair programming. Preliminary algorithmic results on the generalized commonality problem are discussed.
