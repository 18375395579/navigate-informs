Title: Capacity Leadership in a 2-Level Supply Chain with Asymmetric Demand Information
Author: Ozge Islegen, Stanford University, oislegen@stanford.edu
Coauthor(s): Erica Plambeck
Year: 2007
Abstract: In the nascent organic cotton and PV solar industries, suppliers have a long leadtime to add capacity and manufacturers have private information about the demand distribution. We characterize conditions under which a manufacturer should build capacity earlier than necessary, to induce her supplier to add capacity.
