Title: Schedule Planning using an Interactive Analysis Approach

Author: Edwin R Chip Kraft, TEMS, Inc., 116 Record Street, Frederick, MD, 21701, United States of America, ckraft@temsinc.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Interactive Analysis is a strategic assessment whereby the service and equipment attributes for each scenario and the interaction between infrastructure, demand and operations are appraised simultaneously. The result optimizes the financial and economic returns associated with a project and balances equipment technology, capital, operating cost and ridership. This presentation will describe how demand, operations and trains schedules are linked in the capacity planning process.
