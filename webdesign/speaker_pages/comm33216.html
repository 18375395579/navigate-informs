Title: Import Security: Assessing the Risks of Imported Food
Author: Vicki Bier, Professor, University of Wisconsin, 1513 University Avenue, Room 3270A, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, bier@engr.wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Jonathan Welburn
Year: 2012
Abstract: There is rising concern for risks associated with imported foods. We use import violations data from the FDA Operational and Administrative System for Import Support (OASIS) to address these concerns. We quantify risks by both product type and country of origin, and explore the usefulness of OASIS data for risk quantification.
