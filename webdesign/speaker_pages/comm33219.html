Title: Flexible Products for Dynamic Preferences
Author: Aydin Alptekinoglu, Associate Professor of Supply Chain Management, Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA, United States of America, aydin@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Karthik Ramachandran
Year: 2013
Abstract: Consumers often have needs that change in a dynamic fashion over time due to physiological, mental or environmental variations. We address a product design dilemma in satisfying such dynamic preferences: should a firm offer multiple standard products, each designed for a specific purpose, or a flexible product that can be reconfigured by consumers as their preferences change?
