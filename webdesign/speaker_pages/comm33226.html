Title: On Keeping Theoretical OR Models Relevant

Author: Sasa Pekec, Duke University - The Fuqua School of Business, 1 Towerview Road, Durham, NC, 27708-0120, United States of America, pekec@duke.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Mike Rothkopf had a unique blend of skills and experiences that allowed him to assess applicability of theoretical models to address concrete business problems. He was successful in transferring his critical reasoning to his students. I will illustrate this with several examples of my interactions with Mike who was my teacher, mentor, and coauthor.
