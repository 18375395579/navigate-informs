Title: Service Six Sigma (Case Studies)

Author: Fugee Tsung, Professor, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, clear water bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong, Hong kong, HK, China, season@ust.hk

Year: 2008

Abstract: Since 1997, Citibank has begun its Six Sigma initiative for defect reduction and cycle time reduction. After that, AIG, American Express, Bank of America, etc. are all in various stages of Six Sigma deployment. People now realize that Six Sigma is every bit as applicable to service processes as it is to manufacturing. The speaker will give an introduction to Service Six Sigma and demonstrate some real cases from telecommunications, banking, hotel, healthcare, etc.
