Title: Strategic Initiatives in Analytics
Author: Anne Robinson, Director, Supply Chain Strategy and Analytics, Verizon Wireless, Basking Ridge, NJ, United States of America, Anne.Robinson@VerizonWireless.com
Coauthor(s): Jack Levis
Year: 2012
Abstract: Anne Robinson, President-Elect of INFORMS, and other committee members will provide information about multiple INFORMS analytics-related strategic priorities and goals in this talk. Topics such as certification, continuing education, and outreach will be covered.
