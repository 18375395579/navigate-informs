Title: Exploring Impact of Management of Chronic Illnesses through Prevention on U.S. Healthcare System

Author: Sameer Kumar, Professor, University of St Thomas, Opus College of Business, 1000 LaSalle Ave, Minneapolis, MN, 55403, United States of America, sameerkumar724@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Explores how dynamical growth of chronic diseases contributes to increasing chronic treatment costs, negatively affecting available funds for preventive measures and subsequently affecting the growth of health risk factors. Treatment of chronic diseases presents a major cost burden to the U.S. National Healthcare. Without aggressive intervention into root causes of these chronic diseases and their costs, these trends are expected to worsen.
