Title: Meet-pass Planning Model for Rail Capacity Analyzer

Author: Hyun-suk Yoon, Operations Research Specialist, Norfolk Southern Corporation, 1200 Peachtree St NE, Atlanta, GA, 30022, United States of America, Hyun-Suk.Yoon@nscorp.com

Coauthor(s): Clark Cheng, Akshat Dimri

Year: 2013

Abstract: We describe a railroad corridor capacity planning model that determines the practical capacity of a line segment on freight railroad. A tool based on this model has been utilized for the last few years at Norfolk Southern to assist with decision making in infrastructure improvement.
