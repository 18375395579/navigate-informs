Title: Wine Futures and Advance Selling under Quality Uncertainty
Author: Tim Noparumpa, Assistant Professor of Management, Providence College, School of Business, 1 Cunningham Square, Providence, RI, 02918, United States of America, tnoparum@providence.edu
Coauthor(s): Burak Kazaz, Scott Webster
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this study we examine the use of wine futures and advance selling as a form of operational flexibility to mitigate quality rating risk in wine production. While aging in the barrel, expert reviewers create a barrel score, indicating the potential quality of the wine. Based on the barrel score, the wine producer determines what proportion of its wine should be sold as futures, and what proportion should be sold in retail.
