Title: Ross Recovery in Markovian Economies I

Author: Vadim Linetsky, Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Rd, Evanston, IL, 60202, United States of America, linetsky@iems.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Likuan Qin

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk will survey our work on extending the Ross recovery theorem to general Markovian economies.
