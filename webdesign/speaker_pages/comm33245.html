Title: SDPA: Leading-edge Software for SDP

Author: Makoto Yamashita, Tokyo Institute of Technology, 2-12-1-W8-29 Oh-okayama Meguro-ku, Tokyo, 152-8552, Japan, Makoto.Yamashita@is.titech.ac.jp

Coauthor(s): Kazuhiro Kobayashi, Masakazu Kojima, Maho Nakata, Kazhide Nakata, Katsuki Fujisawa, Mituhiro Fukuda

Year: 2008

Abstract: SDPA (SemiDefinite Programming Algorithm) is an Open Source Software to solve SemiDefinite Programs. SDPA has contributed to various fields, such as control theory and quantum chemistry. Since its first release in 1995, it has been continuously updated. The latest version (SDPA7) attains outstanding speed-up mainly due to the new data structures. SDPA is now distributed under GPL and users also can access SDPA via SDPA Online Solver on the Internet. [http://sdpa.indsys.chuo-u.ac.jp/sdpa/]
