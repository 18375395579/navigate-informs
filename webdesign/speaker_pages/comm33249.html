Title: Pedagogical Research ?Working on a Course Design Project

Author: Donna Llewellyn, Director, Georgia Tech, CETL, 225 North Avenue, Suite 4, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0383, United States, donna.llewellyn@cetl.gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this session, we will go through the steps of working on the most common type of pedagogical research ?that related to the design or redesign of a course. We will work from course objectives through assessment of student learning to the actual teaching activities. This is the third in a series of four sessions at this conference on pedagogical research. Feel free to come even if you missed the first two!
