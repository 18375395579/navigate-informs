Title: Theoretical and Practical Aspects of Modeling Network Equilibrium with Road Pricing
Author: Peter Vovsha, Principal, PB Consult, 5 Penn Plaza, 19th Floor, New York, NY, 10001, United States, Vovsha@pbworld.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: The current paper is intended to systematically analyze possible operational approaches to a more consistent and theoretically sound modeling of road pricing. It starts with a formulation of a “seed?equilibrium model, discuss some basic aspects and pitfalls associated with practical implementation of this model, and then gradually adds features and discuss possible enhancements.
