Title: Benefit of Capacity Pooling in Second-Production-Opportunity with Random Yields

Author: Saurabh Bansal, Pennsylvania State University, 405 Business Building, University Park, PA, United States of America, sub32@psu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We model seed corn supply chain where a firm has two opportunities to produce seed corn before observing the demand. The capacity for the second production opportunity must be determined in advance. We discuss algorithms to determine the optimal reservation amount given the possibility of using the same capacity to produce multiple varieties of seed corn.
