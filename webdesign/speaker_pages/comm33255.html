Title: Narrative Analysis in Support of a Rapid Fielding Process
Author: Michael Jaye, Naval Postgraduate School, Monterey, CA, United States of America, Mjjaye@nps.edu
Coauthor(s): Donnie Choe, Vic Diaz`, Anthony Keller
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Warfighter Technology Tradespace Methodolgy applies technology acceptance theories to rapid fielding activities which focus on working with developers to gather system data relative to warfighter expectations in several dimensions. Collecting and analyzing soldier end user narratives can inform that process: we gathered soldier narratives to create multiple sense-making items which can be used to provide quantitative research material as well as impact analysis thereby informing the process.
