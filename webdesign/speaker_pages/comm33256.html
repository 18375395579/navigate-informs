Title: Service Convergence: A New Phenomenon?

Author: Christian Wernz, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, 205 Durham Hall (0118), Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, cwernz@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Pooja Thakur, Kongkiti Phusavat

Year: 2012

Abstract: We investigate the phenomenon of service convergence. Service convergence is defined as the tendency of previously separate service offerings to evolve towards an integrated product. We analyzed the business strategy of a Thai hospital and identified service convergence as a result of the firm's quality improvement and innovation initiative. We discuss how the integration of medical services with hospitality services contributed to the hospital's success in the medical tourism industry.
