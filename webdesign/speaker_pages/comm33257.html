Title: New Degree Enrollment Forecasting in Higher Education Industry
Author: Tao Kang, Operations Research Scientist, University of Phoenix, 4025 S Riverpoint Pkwy, CF-K301, Phoenix, AZ, 85040, United States of America, tao.kang@phoenix.edu
Coauthor(s): Roger Gung
Year: 2012
Abstract: Revenue forecast in higher education industry is based on the new degree enrollment forecast. In this research, we developed Cox survival models to forecast the conversion rates of marketing leads to new degree enrollments.
