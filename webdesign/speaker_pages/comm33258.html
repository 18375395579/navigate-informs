Title: Evaluation of Ambulance Transfers into UCCs to Improve Ambulance Availability & Reduce Offload Delay
Author: Ali Vahit Esensoy, ali.esensoy@utoronto.ca
Coauthor(s): Michael Carter
Year: 2008
Abstract: Through the UCC initiative, selected patients with low risk of inpatient admission traveling via ambulance will be transported to a UCC instead of being sent to an ED. This study uses a probabilistic linkage between EMS and ambulatory care databases to propose changes to the patient mix, operating hours and catchment areas of these facilities given operational and medical constraints.
