Title: Herding in a Queue: A Laboratory Experiment
Author: Mirko Kremer, Assistant Professor, Pennsylvania State University, Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, muk22@smeal.psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Laurens Debo
Year: 2013
Abstract: We report results from a set of laboratory experiments designed to understand the impact of waiting time on human purchasing behavior when the quality of the product for which the human has to wait is uncertain. We confirm that the purchasing frequency may increase in the waiting time, i.e. human subjects `herd'.
