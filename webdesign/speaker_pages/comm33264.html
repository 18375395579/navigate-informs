Title: Business Analytics in the Liberal Arts
Author: Avijit Sarkar, Associate Professor, University of Redlands, 1200 E. Colton Avenue, Redlands, CA, 92373, United States of America, avijit_sarkar@redlands.edu
Coauthor(s): Hindupur Ramakrishna
Year: 2013
Abstract: Due to the big data and analytics revolution, business analytics programs and coursework are evolving in many B-schools. This research contains an illustration of a business analytics course in a graduate program for management professionals in a B-School situated in a liberal arts university. We examine what analytics means in a liberal arts context and discuss challenges due to the accelerated nature of the course. An innovative aspect of the course is an introduction to location analytics.
