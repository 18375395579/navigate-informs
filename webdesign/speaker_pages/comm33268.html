Title: Competition and Coordination in Online Marketplaces
Author: Daewon Sun, University of Notre Dame, 359 MCOB, Notre Dame, IN, United States of America, dsun@nd.edu
Coauthor(s): Jennifer Ryan, Xuying Zhao
Year: 2012
Abstract: Online market places, such as those operated by Amazon, eBay and Google, serve as an intermediary, providing the service of matching buyers with sellers, while control of the good is left to the seller. We study a new form of channel conflict caused by online marketplace systems.
