Title: Delivering the Sustainable Way

Author: Gerard Cachon, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut Street, Philadelphia, United States of America, cachon@wharton.upenn.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: People need to have goods brought to their homes. They can do this themselves - drive to a store, purchase a basket of items and then return back home. Or, they can order on-line and have the goods delivered to their doorstep. With a view to sustainability, which of those approaches is better?
