Title: Simulating Pedestrian and Vehicle Evacuation at Large Events
Author: Zhuoyang Zhou, Research Assistant, Arizona State University, 699 S Mill Ave., TA/RA Mail Room, Tempe, AZ, 85281, United States of America, zhuoyang.zhou@asu.edu
Coauthor(s): Hong Zheng, Pitu Mirchandani, Ning Wang
Year: 2013
Abstract: Currently, most evacuation management studies focus on vehicular traffic where their departure curves are assumed to be some analytical functions. This research developed a simulation model that included pedestrian evacuation process along with a proposed parking strategy to improve the evacuation efficiency. Performance comparison shows improved clearance time to safe boundaries.
