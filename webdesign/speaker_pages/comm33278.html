Title: Operations Research and Network Science forming a Modern, Emerging Conception of Information
Author: David Arney, United States Military Academy, Department of Mathematics, West Point, United States of America, david.arney@usma.edu
Coauthor(s): Kathryn Coronges
Year: 2013
Abstract: Progress is being made in solving problems and understanding issues in our information-centric world to build systems and make decisions. This progress comes from integrating modes of understanding (theory, application, and practice) and perception (data, information, intelligence, knowledge, and wisdom). Operations research and network science play a role. Our model focuses on these two disciplines for information problem solving and informatics.
