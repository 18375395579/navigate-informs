Title: Chinese Relationship Marketing in Retailing: The Case of Wet Market in Taiwan

Author: Stephen Huang, Associate Professor, National Kaohsiung First University of Science and Technology, 2 Jhuoyue Rd., Nanzih, Kaoshiung, Taiwan - ROC, howhowone2000@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Nai-Hua Chen, Tung-Sheng Wang, Paul S.H. Wu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The current research aims to explore how customer values and retail service quality impact the customers' relationship quality and their loyalty behavior in the Chinese setting. The findings indicate that Taiwanese customers concern the freshness of the products and the social factors are critical for them to visit traditional markets.The satisfaction positively affects the trust and the latter in turn positively influence the word-of-mouth and repurchasing intention.
