Title: Multiple-stage Screening Game in the Face of Strategic Applicants
Author: Cen Song, Teaching Assistant, University at Buffalo, 435 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, United States of America, censong@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): Jun Zhuang
Year: 2012
Abstract: The security screening system becomes a big concern to identify and deter potential attacks. Based on analyzing the security and congestion in the face of strategic applicants, this paper is further studied in an imperfect and multi-stage screening game. In series queueing network, the error screen probabilities are considered. We provide analytical optimal level of screening strategies for the approver and compare the discriminatory and non-discriminatory screening policies.
