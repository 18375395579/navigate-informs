Title: Optimal Contracts for Outsourcing of Repair and Restoration Services

Author: Nitish Jain, INSEAD, Singapore, 138676, Singapore, nitish.jain@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Dana Popescu, Sameer Hasija

Year: 2013

Abstract: Outsourcing of repair services creates double-sided moral hazard issues since the system performance is determined by joint efforts of the client and the vendor. Additionally, performance-based contracts coupled with stochasticity in the service performance may raise financial concerns for the vendor. We present optimal contracts that resolve such agency issues.
