Title: Career: Monotone Optimal Policies in Parallel Processing Networks

Author: Mark Lewis, Professor, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, mark.lewis@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Hampshire

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, I will discuss some of the technical aspects of my proposal, expand on how those questions were answered, what extensions were pursued and how the CAREER grant 'jumpstarted' my career. I will then discuss how the education component continues to guide my approach to service to the community through teaching and mentorship.
