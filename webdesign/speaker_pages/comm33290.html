Title: Transmission Investment Incentives: Economic Analysis by Example
Author: Robert Entriken, Manager Policy Analysis, EPRI, 3420 Hillview Avenue, Room B-1007, Palo Alto, CA, 94304-1338, United States, rentrike@epri.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper summarizes the use of agent-based simulation as a tool for studying transmission investment incentives, and it is meant to illuminate the source and reasoning for the difficulties in aligning incentives for enhancing the transmission system. The technology behind this tool can be broadly applied to help negotiators in transmission planning quantify their counterparties benefits and costs, as well as their own.
