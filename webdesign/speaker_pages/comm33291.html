Title: Multi-resource Allocation and Pricing in the Cloud

Author: Soumya Sen, Univ. of Minnesota, C319E, EQuad, Olden Street, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, soumya.sen@datami.com

Coauthor(s): Carlee Joe-Wong

Year: 2013

Abstract: We discuss the issues of pricing and fair allocation of multiple datacenter resources among heterogenous clients by introducing an analytical framework and empirical studies on workload traces form a Google cluster.
