Title: Modeling, Analysis, and Redesign of Primary Care: A Simulation Approach

Author: Xiang Zhong, University of Wisconsin - Madison, 1513 University Avenue, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, xzhong4@wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Sally Kraft, Elizabeth Strutz, Molly Williams, Jingshan Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: This work is dedicated to redesigning primary care at University of Wisconsin Health. A standard set of workflows that enable consistent and patient centered processes for the delivery system and provide a consistent clinical experience for patients would lead to improved outcomes. A discrete event simulation model is developed to analyze the patient flow in the primary care processes with complex interactions among patients, care providers, and resources.
