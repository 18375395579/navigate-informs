Title: A Medium Term Multireservoir Hydrothermal Coordination Model by Stochastic Programming
Author: Andres Ramos, Professor, Universidad Pontificia Comillas, Alberto Aguilera 23, Madrid, 28015, Spain, Andres.Ramos@iit.icai.upcomillas.es
Coauthor(s): Santiago Cerisola, Jesus M. Latorre, Rafael Bellido, Alejandro Perea
Year: 2007
Abstract: Medium term operation planning of hydrothermal systems is an important task in the context of generation production planning. Its purpose is coordinating the releases of hydro basins along the year and allocating them to hydro reservoirs of each basin. Stochasticity is due to hydro inflows although fuel and electricity market prices and demand can also influence the operation. Non linear water head effects are considered. A realistic case study based in the Spanish electric system is presented.
