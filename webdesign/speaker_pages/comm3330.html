Title: Revisiting Vertical Integration

Author: Augusto Ruperez Micola, Research Fellow, IMD - Institute for Management Development, Chemin de Bellerive, 23, Lausanne, 1007, Switzerland, Augusto.RuperezMicola@imd.ch

Coauthor(s): James Henderson

Year: 2007

Abstract: We revise and provide new vertical integration motives including creating of upstream/downstream markets, supply chain optimisation, importance of quality, market power and risk factors. The analysis is based on the systematic examination of a wide array of industries including, among others the commodities, luxury goods, hospitality and paper sectors.
