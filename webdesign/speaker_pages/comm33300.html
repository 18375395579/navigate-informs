Title: Service Science, Human Resources, Information Systems and Information Distortion Interactions

Author: Orhan Güvenen, Professor, Bilkent University, Bilkent University DSEE, FEASS, Z-03 06800 Bilkent, Ankara, Turkey, gorhan@bilkent.edu.tr

Year: 2013

Abstract: In service science applications optimization, endogenization of the following subsystems can be considered as complementary conditions: (i) Transdisciplinary science methodology, (ii) “Third Generation University?impact on human resources, (iii) Information systems and information distortion interactions. This paper analyses the interactions of the mentioned subsystems.
