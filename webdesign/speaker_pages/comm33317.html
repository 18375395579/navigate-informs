Title: Information Acceleration for Alternative Fuel Vehicles

Author: Rosanna Garcia, Northeastern University, Hayden Hall, Room 202, Boston, MA, 02115, United States of America, r.garcia@neu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Automobile manufacturers are interested in what drives consumers to purchase alternative fuel vehicles. However, consumers are unable to provide informed choice decisions on unfamiliar technologies. Marketing managers have looked at how multi-media technological advancements can be utilized to develop a method for introducing consumers to these innovations. This method is known as information acceleration. This is an NSF sponsored project MUSES:Environmental Policy, Auto Design & Materials Flow.
