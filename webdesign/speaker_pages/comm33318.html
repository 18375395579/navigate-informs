Title: A Model to Estimate the Effects of Behavioral Change and Subsequent Relapse in HIV Prevention
Author: Stephen Sorensen, Operations Research Analyst, Centers for Disease Control, 1600 Clifton Road, Mail Stop E48, Atlanta, GA, 30333, United States, ssorensen@cdc.gov
Coauthor(s): Stephanie Sansom L
Year: 2007
Abstract: Many interventions for HIV prevention apply behavioral modification programs to promote barrier protection with condoms. We constructed a dynamic model to estimate the long-term effects of these behavioral modification programs. We included an estimate of long-term benefits even when behavioral relapse occurs after the end of the program.
