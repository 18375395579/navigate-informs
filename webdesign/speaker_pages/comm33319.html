Title: Flight Planning - The Forgotten Field in Airline Operations

Author: Stephen Altus, Enterprise Manager, Technical Sales, Jeppesen, 225 West Santa Clara Street, Suite 1600, San Jose, CA, 95113, United States, steve.altus@jeppesen.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Although it is a central part of the operation of every flight, and includes multiple challenging optimization problems, flight planning has been largely ignored by the OR field. This presentation broadly introduces the flight planning problem and topics of current research. Relevant aspects of the domain are described, specific problems are identified, and various approaches to resolving them are discussed.
