Title: Optimization: Seeing is Believing
Author: Peter Nieuwesteeg, Senior AIMMS Specialist, AIMMS (Paragon Decision Technology), 500 108th Avenue NE, Suite 1085, Bellevue, WA, 98004, United States of America, Peter.Nieuwesteeg@aimms.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: “Seeing is Believing?also holds true for optimization. Just saying that it can bring value to your organization is not enough. Your colleagues need to be able to see it. This web-based publishing framework provides a portal for centralized optimization. Once logged in, they will be able to see optimization with their own eyes!
