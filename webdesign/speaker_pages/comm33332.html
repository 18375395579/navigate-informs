Title: Analytical Challenges in the Petroleum and Petrochemical Industry

Author: Marlize Meyer, Principal Operations Researcher, Sasol Technology, 1 Klasie Havenga Street, Sasolburg, 1947, South Africa, marlize.meyer@sasol.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Petrochemical and Petroleum environment is very complex with many components, products and interfaces. In 2011 Petrochemical and Petroleum analytics found a place as a subgroup in the ENRE section. Applying analytics consistently is a challenge in this diverse environment. A panel will discuss the challenges decision professionals face in this environment. Please bring your challenges and join in the discussion.
