Title: Calibration of Deviation Measures in General Portfolio Theory
Author: Sergey Sarykalin, University of Florida, saryk@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): R. Tyrrell Rockafellar, Stanislav Uryasev
Year: 2007
Abstract: We will make a connection between the portfolio theory with general deviation measures and the classical asset pricing theory by examination of CAPM-like relations. In particular, we derive discount factors, corresponding to the CAPM-like relations and consider the risk-neutral pricing form of CAPM-like relations. This will lead to a method of calibrating deviation measures from market data.
