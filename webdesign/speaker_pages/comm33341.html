Title: The Entrepreneurial Intentions of Founders and Joiners
Author: Henry Sauermann, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 W Peachtree St., Atlanta, GA, United States of America, henry.sauermann@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Michael Roach
Year: 2012
Abstract: Using a sample of over 4,000 science and engineering PhD students close to graduation, we provide descriptive insights into founder and joiner intentions and explore similarities as well as differences with respect to three sets of potential antecedents: individual characteristics such as preferences for freedom or risk aversion, characteristics of the social environment, as well as perceived commercial opportunities.
