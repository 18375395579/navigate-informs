Title: Dynamic Games in Complex Transportation Networks

Author: Jürgen Scheffran, ACDIS, Adjunct Associate Professor, Political Science and Atmospheric Sciences, University of Illinois, 359 Armory Building, MC 533, 505 East Armory Avenue, Champaign, IL, 61820, scheffra@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Kieran Donaghy

Year: 2006

Abstract: A framework of complex dynamic transportation networks is presented to examine the evolution of goods movement and network externalities, including the increased fragmentation of production processes. Based on data, we analyze the solutions to non-cooperative dynamic games between producers (shippers), carriers, and environmental policy makers.
