Title: Virtualizing Medicaid Application Process in Georgia: A Test of Process Virtualization Theory

Author: Abhay Mishra, Georgia State University, Robinson College of Business, Atlanta, GA, 30030, United States of America, amishra@gsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Patricia Ketsche, Angela Snyder, Susan Mclaren, James Marton

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper examines the virtualization of Medicaid application process in the state of Georgia. We apply the process virtualization theory, which is particularly apt in this context, to examine the extent to which the Medicaid application process can be virtualized. We also examine the key moderating role that information technology can play in virtualization.
