Title: Contingency Strategies in Managing Supply Systems with Uncertain Lead Times

Author: Panos Kouvelis, Professor, Washington University in St Louis, Olin School of Business, Campus Box 1133, 1 Brookings Lane, St Louis, MO, 63130, United States, kouvelis@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Jian Li

Year: 2007

Abstract: Conventionally in the literature and practice, the strategy of Safety Leadtime (SL) is used to deal with leadtime uncertainty, ignoring future available information on the delivery status and other possible resources for emergency response. In this paper, we study the potential use of two contingency strategies on top of the SL approach: Strategic Inventory (SI) and Dynamic Emergency Response (DER). We provide sensitivity analysis and numerical experiments to illustrate the effectiveness of these strategies and to derive managerial insights.
