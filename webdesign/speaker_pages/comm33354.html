Title: Optimization Society Student Paper Prize
Author: Katya Scheinberg, Associate Professor, Lehigh University, 200 W Packer Avenue, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, katyas@lehigh.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: The INFORMS Optimization Society Student Paper Prize was established in 2006 and is awarded annually at the INFORMS Fall National Meeting to one or more student(s) for an outstanding paper in optimization that is submitted to and received or published in a refereed professional journal during the three calendar years preceding the year of the award. The prize serves as an esteemed recognition of promising students who are looking for an academic or industrial career.
