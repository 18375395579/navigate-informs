Title: Impacts of Multiple Interactive Behavioral Factors on Neuronal Population Dependency

Author: Hang Zhang, Arizona State University, PO Box 875906, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, hzhang12@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Jiping He, Nong Ye, Auttawut Roontiva, Jose J. Aguayo

Year: 2008

Abstract: Brain neural activities interact with behaviors. We use two-way ANOVA to identify the significance of the impacts of two behavioral factors (motion direction and detouring for obstacle) and their interaction on the dependency among a neuronal ensemble in the dorsal premotor (PMd) area of a monkey during a reaching motion.
