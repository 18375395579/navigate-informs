Title: Appointment Scheduling: Implementing Patients Flow Policies
Author: Linda LaGanga, Mental Health Center of Denver, 4141 East Dickenson Place, Denver, CO, United States of America, Linda.Laganga@MHCD.org
Coauthor(s): Wes Williams, Michele Samorani, Stephen Lawrence
Year: 2012
Abstract: We present our evolving research and implementation of scheduling policies that improve patient flow. Our models consider the value of serving more patients sooner and the impacts of uncertainty and variability on provider utilization, patient wait time, and provider productivity. We will present the solutions we have implemented and innovations we are piloting, and discuss how to develop and implement scheduling practices and sustain positive service delivery experiences.
