Title: Designing and Delivering a Better Management Science Course for MBA Students
Author: Robert Freund, Professor, MIT Sloan School of Mgmt., Building E53-357, 50 Memorial Drive, Cambridge, MA, 02142, United States, rfreund@MIT.EDU
Year: 2007
Abstract: We will share key strategies for designing and delivering a successful management science curriculum for MBA students, based on our successes in the (MIT) Sloan MBA program. We will share how to deliver a curriculum to educate future managers about the value of operations research, and how to develop and use case-like materials that work.
