Title: Revenue Management Performance Drivers: An Empirical Analysis in the Hotel Industry

Author: Carrie Crystal, Notre Dame, Mendoza School of Business, South Bend, IN, United States, carrie.crystal@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Ferguson, Jeff Stratman

Year: 2007

Abstract: We developed and administered a survey instrument tailored to the hotel industry to measure both drivers of RM and RM performance. In line with expectations, we found evidence that forecasting and organizational focus positively impact RM performance. On the other hand, the results showed evidence that higher organizational structure scores negatively impact RM performance. We provide a few explanations for this non-intuitive result and proposals for future research.
