Title: Nicholson Student Paper Prize
Author: Jose Blanchet, Professor, Columbia University, 500W 120th Street, New York, NY, United States of America, jose.blanchet@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Huseyin Topaloglu
Year: 2013
Abstract: The top participants in the Nicholson student paper competition are selected as finalists by the prize committee and invited to present their work in these sessions. This competition comprises papers of the highest quality drawn from virtually all areas represented by INFORMS.
