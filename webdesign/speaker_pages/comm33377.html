Title: Panel: Overarching OR as a Catalyst for Grand Challenges

Author: Suvrajeet Sen, Professor, University of Southern California, Daniel J. Epstein Department of ISE, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, S.sen@USC.edu

Coauthor(s): Cynthia Barnhart, John Birge, David Yao, Michael Fu, Stefanos Zenios, George Nemhauser

Year: 2013

Abstract: This panel is the second in a series of panels devoted to the role of OR in Engineering Grand Challenges. This panel will go beyond Science and Engineering to cover Social Sciences, and Economics. Panelists will also discuss how the grand challenges pose important opportunities for the OR community to work on overarching problems that cross many boundaries, both theoretical and applied.
