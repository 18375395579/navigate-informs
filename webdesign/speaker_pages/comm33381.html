Title: Unconscious and Implicit Biases and the Impact on Women and Underrepresented Groups in S&E
Author: Joyce Yen, University of Washington, Box 352180, Loew 367, Seattle, WA, 98195-2180, United States, joyceyen@u.washington.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this session we will explore how we all have unconscious biases, how those biases manifest themselves in experiences such as evaluation, recruitment, selection, etc., the impact of unconscious and implicit biases on women and underrepresented minorities, and strategies for counteracting these biases.
