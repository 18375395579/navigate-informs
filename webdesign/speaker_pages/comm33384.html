Title: Analyzing Discrepancies in the 2004 Presidential Vote in Ohio

Author: Doug Samuelson, President, InfoLogix, Inc., 8711 Chippendale Court, Annandale, VA, 22003, United States, samuelsondoug@yahoo.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: We analyzed reported discrepancies between exit polls and actual vote totals in Ohio in the 2004 Presidential election, using spreadsheets and a popular statistical package (JMP). While we found indications of problems in the voting and vote counting, we concluded that there was insufficient evidence to call the outcome into serious question.
