Title: Optimal Crop Planting Schedules and Financial Hedging Strategies
Author: Farid AitSahlia, University of Florida, Weil Hall 303, Gainesville, FL, United States of America, farid@ise.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Chung-Jui Wang, Victor Cabrera, Clyde Fraisse, Stan Uryasev
Year: 2008
Abstract: We investigate the impact of ENSO-based climate forecasts on optimal planting schedules and financial yield-hedging strategies in a CVaR framework. In our context, insurance and futures contracts are available to hedge against yield and price risks, respectively. In a study based on actual data, we derive efficient frontiers for each of the three ENSO phases (Neutral, La Niña, El Niño) and highlight the contrast between their related hedging strategies.
