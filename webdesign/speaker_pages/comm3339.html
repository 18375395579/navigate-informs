Title: Minimizing U.S. Energy Dependency From Foreign Energy Sources

Author: Darin Colby, Portland State University, PO Box 751, Portland, OR, United States, darin.colby@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Edwin Garces

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper analyzes the reduction of U.S. dependency on foreign energy sources using the Hierarchical Decision Model. Utilizing pair wise comparison methodology, the paper identifies the energy technologies that the U.S. should pursue. The findings support that the U.S. should exploit coal technologies and that they should invest in renewable technologies.
