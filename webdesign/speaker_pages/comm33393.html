Title: HSR: Paying Our Way Forward

Author: Nicolas Norboge, Assistant Transportation Researcher, Texas Transportation Institute, 3135 TAMU, Room 334, College Station, TX, 77840, United States of America, n-norboge@ttimail.tamu.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: With renewed interest in intercity passenger rail brings the emergence of fresh efforts to develop comprehensive system plans. However, many plans fail to explain clearly how such capital-intensive projects will be paid for. This research provides a list of viable funding and financing strategies transportation professionals and policymakers to consider for paying for new HSR networks.
