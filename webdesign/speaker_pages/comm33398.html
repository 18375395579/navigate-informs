Title: Multimodal and Multiresolution Analysis and Visualization of Experimental Data

Author: Daniela Ushizima, PhD, Lawrence Berkeley Lab, 1 Cyclotron, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, dushizima@lbl.gov

Year: 2013

Abstract: Advancements in image acquisition allows collection of unprecedented amounts of medical data, including modalities at different space-time scales. The challenge is to develop/evaluate protocols to quantify these datasets, e.g., characterization of individuals and colocalization of features under distinct imaging modalities. This talk will describe work in quantitative image analysis, including description of microstructure of materials and biomedical images (ophthalmologic and neurological data)
