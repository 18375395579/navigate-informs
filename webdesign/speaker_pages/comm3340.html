Title: Operations Analysis Workforce Development Capabilities Assessment
Author: William Nanry, Member, Group Technical Staff, Lockheed Martin Missiles and Fire Control, 1701 W. Marshall Drive, Grand Prairie, TX, 75265, United States of America, william.nanry@lmco.com
Coauthor(s): Steve Dininger
Year: 2012
Abstract: To enhance operations analysis (OA), the Lockheed Martin OA Workforce Development Team assessed the value each OA capability / skill provides to anticipate needs, assess shortfalls, and articulate mission understanding to generate affordable and relevant solutions for decision-makers and end-users. A multi-attribute decision support exercise was conducted to identify those key critical OA capabilities / skills across Lockheed Martin.
