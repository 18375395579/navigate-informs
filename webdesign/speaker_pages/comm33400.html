Title: Evaluating Marketing Strategies for International Logistics Zones in Taiwan

Author: Chin-Shan Lu, Professor, Cheng Kung University, No 1, University Road,, Tainan, 701, Taiwan - ROC, lucs@mail.ncku.edu.tw

Year: 2007

Abstract: This research empirically evaluates marketing strategies of international logistics zones in Taiwan. Results suggest simplified customs procedures as the most important criterion, followed by efficiency of port operations, port facilities, geographic location, and tax incentives. Theoretical and practical implications of the research findings are discussed.
