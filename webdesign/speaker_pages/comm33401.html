Title: Application of Strategic Management Tools in Public Organizations

Author: Nate Sledge, PhD, Principal, Trophy Management Solutions LLC, 7021 Ashleigh Manor Court, Alexandria, VA, 22315, United States of America, natesledge@tms.us.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Strategic management can help shape the future of an organization, guide it through change, and help it meet strategic challenges. This talk will demonstrate how selected strategic planning and management tools can be tailored to specific contexts. Strategic planning tools are applied to real world examples. The tools are used to define roles, missions, and functions; describe processes; identify issues, gaps, and inefficiencies; and finally, to develop strategic improvement plans.
