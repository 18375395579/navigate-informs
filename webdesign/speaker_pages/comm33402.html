Title: Network DEA Pitfalls
Author: Joe Zhu, Professor, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, 100 Institute Rd, Worcester, MA, 01609, United States of America, jzhu@WPI.EDU
Coauthor(s): Yao Chen, Wade Cook, Chiang Kao
Year: 2012
Abstract: Network DEA models been developed to examine the efficiency DMUs with internal structures. Pitfalls in network DEA are discussed with respect to the determination of divisional efficiency, frontier type, and projections.
