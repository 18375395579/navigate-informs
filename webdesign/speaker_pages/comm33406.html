Title: Resource Planning in Professional Service Organizations
Author: Vincent Hargaden, PhD Student, Centre for Doctoral Studies, UCD Michael Smurfit School of Business, University College Dublin, Blackrock, Co. Dublin, Ireland, vincent.hargaden@ucd.ie
Coauthor(s): Jennifer Ryan
Year: 2008
Abstract: A model is developed for the human resource planning and allocation process in professional service organisations. This provides insights into the strategic issues surrounding the matching of firm resources to client requirements and allows the impact of changes in utilisation levels, skills acquisition, cross training and team formation on the optimal solution to be evaluated.
