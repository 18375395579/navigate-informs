Title: Provision of Services and Information to the Tax Payer

Author: Kathleen Carley, Professor, School of Computer Science, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, 1323 Wean Hall, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213-3890, United States of America, kathleen.carley@cs.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Patricia McGuire

Year: 2008

Abstract: Service related resource and operational decisions are often made by taking a regional approach or by doing a minor variation on what was done in the past. Making these resource and operational decisions is exceeding difficult due to the complexity of the situation and the limitations of available data. We explore how computational reasoning can be used to support this process.
