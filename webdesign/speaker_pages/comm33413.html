Title: Biclustering EEG Data from Epileptic Patients Treated with Vagus Nerve Stimulation

Author: Nikita Boyko, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, P.O. Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611-6595, United States, nikita@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Stanislav Busygin, Mike Bewernitz, Panos Pardalos, Georges Ghacibeh

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a pilot study of EEG recordings from two epileptic patients undergoing treatment with Vagus Nerve Stimulator (VNS). Raw EEG signal was transformed to a sequence of maximum Lyapunov Exponents (STLmax). Applying consistent biclustering to STLmax time series allowed us to separate stimulation states versus non stimulation states. The result of the experiment is a step forward towards developing a physiologic marker for optimal VNS parameters based on measures of scalp EEG signals.
