Title: Innovation, Openness & Platform Control
Author: Geoffrey Parker, Tulane University, Freeman School of Business, New Orleans, LA, 70115, United States of America, gparker@tulane.edu
Coauthor(s): Marshall Van Alstyne
Year: 2013
Abstract: We examine how exercising control over a technology platform can increase profits and innovation. By choosing how much to open and when to bundle enhancements, platform sponsors can influence ecosystem partner choices. Results can inform innovation strategy, choice of organizational form, antitrust and intellectual property law, and competition management.
