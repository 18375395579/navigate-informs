Title: Learning from My Success and from Others?Failure

Author: Bradley Staats, Assistant Professor, University of North Carolina-Chapel Hill, McColl Building, CB 3490, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599, United States of America, Bradley_Staats@kenan-flagler.unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Diwas KC, Francesca Gino

Year: 2012

Abstract: Drawing on attribution theory, we investigate how individuals learn from failure and success. We use 10 years of data from 71 surgeons using a new cardiac surgery technology. We find that individuals learn more from their own successes than from their own failures, while they learn more from the failures of others than they do from others?successes. We also find that individuals?prior successes and others?failures help individuals overcome their inability to learn from their own failures.
