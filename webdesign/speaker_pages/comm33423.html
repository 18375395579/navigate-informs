Title: Mechanisms for Mitigating Quality Risk with Outsourced Manufacturing

Author: Sean Handley, The Ohio State University, 600 Fisher Hall, 2100 Neil Ave, Columbus, OH, United States of America, handley.20@osu.edu

Coauthor(s): John Gray

Year: 2008

Abstract: Increasingly, companies in the food, drug and medical device industries are outsourcing manufacturing. Previous research has shown that there is an increased quality risk with outsourced production relative to internal production. We empirically evaluate the conditions under which various quality risk mitigation approaches are most effective.
