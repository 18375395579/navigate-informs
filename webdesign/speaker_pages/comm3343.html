Title: Risk Handling Pattern of Large-scale Construction Project
Author: Nengpai Lin, Professor, National Taiwan University, 6th Floor,NO.17, Suchow Rd., TAIPEI, 100, Taiwan - ROC, nengpai@mail2000.com.tw
Coauthor(s): Miao Fan
Year: 2007
Abstract: The Uncertainty of a project will increase as the scale of the project increase so as the importance of project risk management. From the management perspective, risk handling plays the key role in the project risk management process. Based on case study method, this article explored factors that influence the risk handling selection. After analyzing several risk events and their handling of two large scale construction projects, a framework described the selection of risk handling is created.
