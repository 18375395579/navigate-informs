Title: Introducing the INFORMS Analytics Maturity Model for Improving Organizational Decision Support

Author: Norm Reitter, Senior Director, Analytics, Syngineering, LLC, 1892 Denver West Court, Denver, CO, 80401, United States of America, norm@reitter.net

Year: 2013

Abstract: Decision makers need assurance that their analytics supports their objectives. INFORMS has developed an analytic maturity model (AMM) designed to help organizations assess themselves, set goals, and justify investments to support improvements. This presentation will provide an overview of the INFORMS AMM and the approach that INFORMS is taking to help members improve organizational conditions, analytical capabilities, and data support to achieve greater business and military outcomes.
