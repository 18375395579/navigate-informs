Title: House Allocation with Fractional Endowments

Author: Stergios Athanassoglou, Ph.D Student, Columbia University, New York, NY, United States, sa2164@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Jay Sethuraman

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine a generalized version of the house allocation problem in economics, allowing for arbitrary fractional endowments of houses to agents. In this context, we provide an efficient network flow based algorithm that computes an allocation satifying ordinal efficiency, individual rationality and no justified envy. We further discuss impossibility results related to strategyproofness and the core of the associated cooperative game.
