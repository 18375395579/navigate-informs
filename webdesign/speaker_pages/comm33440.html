Title: Smart Services in the Waste and Resource Industry: A Best Practice Case Study

Author: Willibald Erhart, IT Project Manager, Saubermacher AG, Hans-Roth-Straﬂe 1, Feldkirchen bei Graz, 8073, Austria, W.Erhart@saubermacher.at

Year: 2012

Abstract: The pioneer in Austria's waste industry early recognized the value of waste as a resource. In addition to the process steps that are necessary to close the entire lifecycle from waste to product, time and costs are the central key drivers for success. At this stage Smart Services offer a great possibility not only to optimize the production/service delivery but even to create superior customer experience by a proactive Smart Service approach. Conceptual/strategic considerations are highlighted.
