Title: Understanding IT Adoption in Homehealth Domain by System Dynamics

Author: Mehmet Serdar Kilinc, University of Arkansas, 4207 BELL Engineering Center, Fayetteville, AR, United States of America, serdarmehmet@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Nebil Buyurgan

Year: 2012

Abstract: Homecare organizations have been struggling with many problems such as financial issues, changes in reimbursement methods, and lack of human resource. Integration and utilization of IT can improve efficiency and reduce costs in both clinical and supportive operations. This study attempts to analyze IT diffusion over mobile devices and telehomehealth. A system dynamics model is utilized to investigate the barriers and outcomes of each technology adoption and explore the interaction between them.
