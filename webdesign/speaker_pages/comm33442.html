Title: MBA Revenue Management Course at Columbia University
Author: Costis Maglaras, Associate Professor, Columbia University, Graduate School of Business, 409 Uris Hall, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, c.maglaras@gsb.columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Garrett van Ryzin
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present the structure of a full semester MBA elective course on Pricing and Revenue Management that we teach at Columbia, highlighting two new cases that we wrote recently.
