Title: The Impacts of Network Characteristics on Content Diffusion in Social Media
Author: Hyelim Oh, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke St. W., Montreal, Canada, hyelim.oh@mail.mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Animesh Animesh, Alain Pinsonneault
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study investigates how network characteristics influence content diffusion in social media. Specifically, we focus on individual-level content retransmission decision and examine how network characteristics between a sender and a receiver affect the likelihood of a receiver’s content retransmission in Twitter’s subscription network. We provide practical implications for designing effective social media advertising campaigns.
