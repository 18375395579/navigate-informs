Title: A Bayesian Network Approach to a Supply Chain Risk Management Framework

Author: Myles Garvey, Ph.D Student, Rutgers Business School, 1 Washington Park, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States of America, myles.garvey@rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Steven Carnovale, Sengun Yeniyurt

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research advances a framework for supply chain risk management and derives structural network measures of risk, leveraging a Bayesian Belief Network (BBN). The composition of the supply network allows for the development of a BBN in order to formally and analytically capture the risk profile of a supply network. Further, we extend current understanding of supply chain risk management by incorporating supply network structure and causality of risk for a holistic perspective.
