Title: A Congestion Pricing Problem with Embedded Mobile Source Emission Models
Author: Tao Yao, Pennsylvania State University, 349 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, taoyao@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Ke Han, Hongcheng Liu, Terry Friesz
Year: 2012
Abstract: An emission model will be integrated into the Lighthill-Whitham-Richards traffic flow model. Aim of this work is to understand the impacts of network flow pattern and driving behavior on transportation emissions, as well as to exploit the engineering aspect of emission control.
