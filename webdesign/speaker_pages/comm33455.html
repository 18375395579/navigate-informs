Title: Uncertainty Model for Rapid Updating of Target Positions

Author: Eva Regnier, Naval Postgraduate School, 699 Dyer Road, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, eregnier@nps.edu

Coauthor(s): Dashi Singham

Year: 2012

Abstract: In many security contexts, forces must allocate their assets based on incomplete information about targets?positions. They frequently receive human intelligence describing past or future events related to target locations. We present an uncertainty model that allows for rapid updating, conditioning on past or future events.
