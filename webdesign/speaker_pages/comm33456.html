Title: Generating Interest in OR among Undergraduate Mathematics Majors
Author: Kevin Hutson, Furman University, Greenville, SC, United States of America, kevin.hutson@furman.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Undergraduate mathematics students sometime get frustrated by an abstract major and long to use their mathematical skills in some applied setting. Often, though, the mathematics major allows for a very limited coverage of OR concepts. Here I talk about introducing OR to students in a variety of courses and through undergraduate research. I will discuss success stories of projects as well as finding appropriate projects for these undergraduate students.
