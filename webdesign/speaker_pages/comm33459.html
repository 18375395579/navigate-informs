Title: Development of Repeat Offender Model for Colorado Springs Police Department

Author: Aaron Burns, United States Air Force Academy, United States of America

Coauthor(s): Rebekah McKenna, Zachary Smith, Zebulon Hanley

Year: 2013

Abstract: The idea of Intelligence Led Policing grows in popularity throughout the country. A repeat offender model could help prioritize policing activities. This project created a model incorporating all types of individual offenses to determine the propensity to commit repeated crimes and prioritizes results with each crime’s weighted societal impact factor.
