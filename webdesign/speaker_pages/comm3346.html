Title: How to Create Change within the Camelot?
Author: Christopher Tang, Edward W. Carter Chair in Business Administration, UCLA Anderson School, 110 Westwood Plaza, Los Angeles, CA, 90095, United States, ctang@anderson.ucla.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: The world is changing, and OR/MS must change along with it. Previous sessions have examined pertinent developments and emerging opportunities and challenges for OR/MS practitioners, researchers, and educators. This presentation discusses possible concerted actions to promote faster progress in some of these areas especially in education.
