Title: Business Mail Discount Verification for the U.S. Postal Service
Author: Zachary Waltz, IBM Global Business Services, Fairfax, VA, 22033, zwaltz@us.ibm.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: This discussion will focus on the business mail environment and the Operation Research techniques employed in the U.S. Postal Service’s new method to verify the validity of claimed work share discounts.
