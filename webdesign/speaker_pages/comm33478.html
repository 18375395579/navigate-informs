Title: Differences in lifetime expectancy and costs among persons diagnosed with HIV in different settings
Author: Vimalanand Prabhu, P.E. Fellow, CDC, MS E48, Clifton Rd., Atlanta, Ga, 30033, United States of America, gmf9@cdc.gov
Coauthor(s): Angela Hutchinson, Stephanie Sansom, Paul Farnham
Year: 2008
Abstract: We use a Markov model of HIV disease progression and transmission to determine the most cost-effective hospital setting for HIV screening. Based on average CD4 counts among those diagnosed after hospital admission compared with those diagnosed in emergency departments, emergency department screening was more cost-effective.
