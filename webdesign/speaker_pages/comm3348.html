Title: Disaster logistics and DHS/FEMA Logistics Management Directorate (LMD)
Author: Eric Smith, Assistant Administrator for the Logistics Management Directorate, FEMA, DHS/Federal Emergency Management Agency, Washington, DC, 20472, United States of America, eric.smith@dhs.gov
Year: 2008
Abstract: LMD is responsible for policy, guidance, standards, execution and governance of logistics support, services, and operations. FEMA transformed its logistics management and created a responsive and robust logistics capability by rightsizing inventory, enhancing its relationships with federal partners as the integrator for coordinating national logistics response operations, and establishing an effective pre-positioning plan. The preparations for recent disasters validated the new strategies.
