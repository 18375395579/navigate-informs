Title: Dynamics of Bucket Brigades in Systems with Fixed Stations
Author: Esma Gel, Associate Professor, Arizona State University, Dept of Industrial Engineering, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, Esma.Gel@asu.edu
Coauthor(s): Erika Murguia, Dieter Armbruster
Year: 2008
Abstract: We study the dynamics and throughput of a bucket brigade when work has to be conducted in stations that can only be occupied by a single worker. We first analyze the case of two worker lines, and extend some results to more general systems. We provide several managerial insights on the performance of bucket brigades in such environments using the amount of blocking that the line incurs under various assumptions.
