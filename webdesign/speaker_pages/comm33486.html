Title: IT-Marketing Coordination Decisions under Dynamic Inter-firm Competition

Author: Dengpan Liu, Assistant Professor, Iowa State University, College of Business, Ames, IA, 50011, United States of America, dliu@iastate.edu

Coauthor(s): Subodha Kumar, Vijay Mookerjee

Year: 2013

Abstract: Extant research has shown that firms can resolve the over-spending problem through effective coordination between IT and Marketing departments. In this study, we examine whether the firms in a differential game theoretical setting can still benefit from the intra-firm coordination.
