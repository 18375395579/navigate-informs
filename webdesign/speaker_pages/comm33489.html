Title: Revenue Management Research That is Used in Practice: Past, Present and Future
Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, 412 Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, gjv1@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Robert Phillips, Andy Boyd, Jeff McGill, Richard Ratliff
Year: 2007
Abstract: Revenue management research has grown rapidly over the past decade. So has revenue management practice. But which research ideas have had the most influence on industry practice and why? What accounts for the success of these ideas? What lessons can we learn from these examples about doing research that has real impact? The panel will address these questions in an open discussion format.
