Title: 40 Years of Forrest and Tomlin

Author: John Forrest, Faster Coin, 19 Wix's Lane, London, SW4 0AL, United Kingdom, john.forrest@fastercoin.com

Coauthor(s): John Tomlin

Year: 2012

Abstract: We review the the impact of the updating of triangular factors, and in particular our F-T method, on the speed of the simplex method, both at the time of publication 40 years ago, and in the years since on many mathematical programming (optimization) systems.
