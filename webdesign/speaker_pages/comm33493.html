Title: RFID with Shelf-space Dependent Demand

Author: Jiang Zhang, Assistant Professor, Adelphi University, School of Business, Adelphi University, Garden City, NY, 11530, United States of America, zhang@adelphi.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Szmerekovsky, Vera Tilson

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the case of RFID adoption and management when demand is shelf-space dependent. We compare the performance of RFID enabled and non-RFID enabled suppliers. We determine optimal policies for both the management and adoption of RFID technology.
