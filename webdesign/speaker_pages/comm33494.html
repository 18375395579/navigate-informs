Title: Supply Chain Management in the Context of Humanitarian Disasters
Author: Luk Van Wassenhove, Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, IF, 77300, France, luk.van-wassenhove@insead.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: This presentation will introduce supply chain management in the humanitarian context, and outline the need for better preparedness and response to disasters. We also discuss opportunities for more effective collaboration among humanitarian organizations, as well as between humanitarians and private organizations. The presentation uses a number of real cases to highlight opportunities to do interesting research with a high potential impact.
