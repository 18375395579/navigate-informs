Title: Mike Rothkopf's Contribution to the Analysis of Bidding in Sequential Auctions

Author: Shmuel Oren, Professor, UC Berkeley, IEOR Department, Rm. 4119 Etcheverry Hall, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, oren@ieor.berkeley.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper will detail an early contribution by Mike Rothkopf and myself to the analysis of multiplicative bidding strategies in sequential auctions. The work examines the affect of competitive interaction where bidders who interact repeatedly in sequential auctions adjust they strategies in response to prior bidding behavior of their rivals.
