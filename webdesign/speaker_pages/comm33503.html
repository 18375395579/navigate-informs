Title: Feedback for Process Improvement in B2B E-commerce: Constructs and Measurement
Author: Hua-Hung(Robin) Weng, Doctoral Student, Clemson University, College of Business and Behavioral Scien, 101 Sirrine Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634-1305, United States, whua@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Aleda Roth, Janis Miller
Year: 2007
Abstract: Feedback systems are important for quality improvement because they offer companies the opportunity to interface with customers and suppliers, and as a result believed to facilitate the double-loop learning. The purpose of this research is to present a conceptual model of B2B feedback systems, B2B service quality, and customer satisfaction.
