Title: Active Learning Exercises for a Service Operations Management Course
Author: Mark Davis, Professor, Bentley University, 175 Forest St., Waltham, MA, 02452, United States of America, mdavis@bentley.edu
Coauthor(s): Ravi Behara
Year: 2012
Abstract: In today’s hi-tech environment, students are familiar with the Internet where they actively participate in the generation and consumption of information. Thus, if business schools want to stimulate student interest, they have to actively engage them in the learning process. This presentation introduces exercises, some of which are part of the course structure, that incorporate active learning in the classroom, and which align well with topics that are in a service operations management course.
