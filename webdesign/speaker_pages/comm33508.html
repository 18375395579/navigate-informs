Title: Trail Network Design at Harvest Areas
Author: Mikael Ronnqvist, Professor, Universit?Laval, Pavillon Adrien-Pouliot, 1065, avenue de la Médecine, Quebec, QC, Canada, mikael.ronnqvist@gmc.ulaval.ca
Coauthor(s): Petrus Jonsson, Patrik Flisberg
Year: 2013
Abstract: We develop a decision support tool to construct road network at harvest areas for harvesters and forwarders. The objective is to minimize ground damage and at the same time have efficient operations. We use high quality GIS information on individual trees through laser scanning, soil information, machine characteristics and information on creeks etc. The system is tested on case studies from Swedish forest companies.
