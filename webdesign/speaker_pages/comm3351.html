Title: The Rise of Cities

Author: James Felli, Eli Lilly and Company, Lilly Corporate Center, Indianapolis, IN, United States of America, felli_james_c@lilly.com

Coauthor(s): John Andersen

Year: 2013

Abstract: We will discuss the results of the INFORMS Cities game and award the prize to the game winner(s)! We will share our observations of the game and participants will be invited to share their experiences.
