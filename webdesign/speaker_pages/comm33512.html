Title: An Uncertain Future: Where Will Fuel Prices Go and How Can OR Help the Airline Industry Respond?
Author: Stefan Karisch, Director, Operations Research and Optimization, Jeppesen, 1800 McGill College Avenue, Suite 1930, Montreal, QC, H3A 3J6, Canada, Stefan.Karisch@jeppesen.com
Coauthor(s): James Bonn, Erik Andersson, John Heimlich, Krishnan Saranathan, John-Paul Clarke
Year: 2008
Abstract: IATA has predicted airline losses of up to $6.1 billion this year at current oil prices. At the 64th Annual General Meeting and World Air Transport Summit in Istanbul, IATA DG and CEO Giovanni Bisignani spoke about an “extraordinary crisis?faced by the world’s airlines brought on by soaring fuel prices and slowing traffic growth. Is there anything that operations research can do? This panel of experts will try to answer this and related questions.
