Title: Social Media, Crowdsourced Data and Health Informatics
Author: Maksim Tsvetovat, Founder & CTO, Open Cancer Network, 2230 George C. Marshall Dr, Falls Church, VA, 22043, United States of America, maksim@tsvetovat.org
Year: 2013
Abstract: Social media and crowdsourcing presents an opportunity for researchers and decision makers in the health and medical fields to learn orders of magnitude more about their audiences, about spread and incidence of diseases and about effect of healthcare policies. In this session, we invite presentations that use social media and crowdsourced data to solve real problems in healthcare, disease prevention, public health, epidemiology and health research.
