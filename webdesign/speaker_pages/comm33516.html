Title: City Pair Block Time Analysis

Author: Esther Hernandez, Analyst, FAA, 800 Independence Avenue SW, Rm 939, Washington, DC, 20591, United States of America, Esther.Hernandez@faa.gov

Coauthor(s): Tony Diana

Year: 2008

Abstract: The study investigates whether block and buffer times for selected city pairs have been increasing from 2000 to 2007 and how changes may have impacted delays and congestion.
