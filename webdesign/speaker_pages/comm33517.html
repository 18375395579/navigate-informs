Title: Using Simulation Software to Drive Conceptual Understanding in Undergraduate OM Course
Author: Elizabeth Durango-Cohen, Illinois Institute of Technology, 565 W. Adams, Chicago, IL, United States of America, durango-cohen@iit.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this talk, we'll discuss various simulation packages, including Littlefield Technologies, the EBeer game as well as the use of clickers, and their impact on student understanding and participation.
