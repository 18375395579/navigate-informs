Title: Using the Missions and Means Framework to Determine Value Added of Systems of Systems
Author: Britt Bray, Senior Military Analyst, DRC, 106 S. 5th Street, Leavenworth, KS, 66048, United States, BBray@drc.com
Coauthor(s): Paul Tanenbaum
Year: 2006
Abstract: The MMF enables front end analysis of missions to determine required capabilities and measure effectiveness. The MMF helps analysts generate the most important questions to be answered. It provides a logical framework to store and understand the logical relationships between the data collected and document and understand the relationships between Systems of Systems (SOS) and the processes that employ them. This paper will describe how the MMF can be applied to determine value added of SOS.
