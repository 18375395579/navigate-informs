Title: Quantity Decisions Under Customer Learning Behavior
Author: Senthil Veeraraghavan, Assistant Professor, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, senthilv@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Serguei Netessine
Year: 2007
Abstract: We explore pricing and quantity decisions of products whose exact specification are noisy. We analyze optimal decisions in market with strategic consumers who face trade-off between availability and possibility disutility in purchasing the product.
