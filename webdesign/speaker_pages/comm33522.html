Title: Improving Patient Access to Surgical Care at the Juravinski Hospital Through Informed Decision Making
Author: Daphne Sniekers, PhD Candidate, University of Toronto, 5 King's College Road, Toronto, ON, M5S 3G8, Canada, daphne.sniekers@mail.utoronto.ca
Year: 2012
Abstract: A generic simulation-based perioperative decision support tool was implemented at the Hamilton Health Sciences Juravinski Hospital to demonstrate how changes to the operating room schedule and resource availability affect patient flow. The tool was used in negotiations with the various stakeholder groups in order to come to consensus on changes to implement. The tool provided invaluable support in their decision-making.
