Title: Outsourcing for the Risk Adverse Business: A Resource Factorization Analysis
Author: Nathan Caswell, Janus Consulting Inc, One Research Court, Suite 450, Rockville, MD, 20850, United States of America, nathan@myjanus.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: As global options move beyond simple supply sourcing to joint operations and reliance on service providers for critical functions it becomes important to understand, design, and manage the virtual enterprise. We present a service approach using ownership based resource factorization that substantially simplifies and clarifies B2B interfaces while enabling proactive risk reduction and retrospective accountability. Several real world examples demonstrate the benefits of the approach.
