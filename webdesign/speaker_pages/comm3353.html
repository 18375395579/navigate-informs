Title: Is the Tendency to Engage in Entrepreneurship Genetic?
Author: Nicos Nicolaou, University of Cyprus & Cass Business School, City University London, Department of Business Administration, P.O.Box 20537, Nicosia, 1678, Cyprus, nicos.nicolaou@ucy.ac.cy
Coauthor(s): Scott Shane
Year: 2013
Abstract: Perhaps the most common question that practitioners ask about entrepreneurship is ‘Are entrepreneurs born or made??Unfortunately, despite more than 50 years of research, scholars have only recently tried to provide an answer to this question. This paper reviews the recent findings on the genetics of entrepreneurship. It examines the evidence for the effect of genes on the tendency to engage in entrepreneurial activity and outlines the direction of future research in this area.
