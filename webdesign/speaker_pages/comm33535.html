Title: Closed-loop Supply Chain With Quality Differentiation
Author: Patrick Qiang, Assistant Professor, Pennsylvania State University, Great Valley, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, qzq10@gv.psu.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We model the CLSC that captures consumer's heterogeneous valuation on the remanufactured product and the new product. The model captures the interaction among various players, including manufacturers, retailers, and consumers. Numerical examples are used to provide managerial implications.
