Title: Do High and Low Inventory Turnover Retailers React Differently to Demand Shocks?

Author: Saravanan Kesavan, Assistant Professor, University of North Carolina, Kenan-Flagler Business School, Chapel Hill, NC, United States of America, Saravanan_Kesavan@kenan-flagler.unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Tarun Kushwaha, Vishal Gaur

Year: 2013

Abstract: In the study, we identify quantity- and price-responsiveness as two mediating mechanisms that distinguish how high- and low-inventory-turnover retailers (HIT and LIT retailers, respectively) can manage demand shocks. In addition, we examine the implications of these mechanisms to excess and shortage of inventories as well as return on assets (ROA)for retail firms.
