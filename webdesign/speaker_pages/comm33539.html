Title: Determining the Most Influential Factors to the Severity of Casualties in Iraq

Author: Douglas Hoffman, USMC, 3300 Russell Road, Quantico, VA, United States of America, douglas.hoffman@usmc.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: Our objective in this research is to analyze data contained in Personnel Casualty Reports (PCRs) and Significant Event reports in order to determine the most influential factors to the severity of casualties in Iraq. We describe the process of transforming data contained in PCR files into a structure that is suitable for analysis and our procedure to link each PCR to the event that caused the casualty. We present some of the initial findings resulting from this ongoing research.
