Title: On the Value of Lead-Time Information and Managerial Strategies in Managing Uncertain Supply

Author: Panos Kouvelis, Professor, Washington University in St. Louis, Olin School of Business, One Brookings Drive, Campus Box 1133, St. Louis, MO, 63130-4899, United States, kouvelis@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Jian Li

Year: 2006

Abstract: Conventionally in the literature and practice, the strategy of Safety Lead-time (SL) is used to deal with lead-time uncertainty, ignoring future available information on the delivery status and other possible resources for emergency response. In this paper, we study the alternative strategies of Strategic Inventory (SI) and Dynamic Emergency Response (DER) employing future available information.
