Title: Models and Managers: Then, Now, and in the Future
Author: John Little, Institute Professor, MIT Sloan School, Room E56-308, 38 Memorial Drive, Cambridge, MA, 02142, United States, jlittle@mit.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Decision support systems have evolved rapidly due to the explosion of available data, increased computer power, and advances in modeling methods. I shall review highlights of the evolution of DSSs from the point of view of managerial models, focusing on marketing, and offer a few thoughts about what may lie ahead.
