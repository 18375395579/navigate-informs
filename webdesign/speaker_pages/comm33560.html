Title: The Sales Process and Online Auction Outcomes

Author: Jason Kuruzovich, Assistant Professor of Management Information Systems, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 110 8th Street, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, kuruzj@rpi.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Online auctions frequently occur as part of a sales process in which the same item is offered both across multiple auctions and even across multiple channels. In this study, we investigate how aspects related to the sales process influences auctions outcomes. We conduct the study using data from the online auction market for used vehicles.
