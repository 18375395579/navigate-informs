Title: The Impact of Export Restrictions on the Implications of E-Waste Legislation

Author: Isil Alev, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, isilalev@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Atalay Atasu, Vishal Agrawal

Year: 2012

Abstract: We explore the impact of export restrictions on manufacturer's design choices under e-waste take-back legislation, and their implications on manufacturer profits, consumer surplus and economic welfare in the exporting and importing countries.
