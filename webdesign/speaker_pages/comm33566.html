Title: A Bayesian Approach for Predicting the Popularity of Tweets

Author: Tauhid Zaman, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, zlisto@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Emily Fox, Eric Bradlow

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a Bayesian approach for predicting the popularity of tweets from the micro-blogging site Twitter. Our approach predicts how many people will interact with a tweet, along with how quickly its popularity will rise using only observations of when users interact with the tweet and their basic social network information. We find our method can produce relatively accurate predictions even when observing only a small number of interactions.
