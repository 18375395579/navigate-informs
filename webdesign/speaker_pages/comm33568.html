Title: The Value of Online Social Commerce: An Empirical Investigation
Author: Mingfeng Lin, University of Maryland, mingfeng@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Siva Viswanathan
Year: 2008
Abstract: Social commerce, or e-commerce building upon online social networks, is starting to attract significant interest among practitioners and researchers. Drawing upon social-network theories and methodologies, we empirically examine the network features of some online social commerce websites and investigate in particular, their role in mitigating risk.
