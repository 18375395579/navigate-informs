Title: Measuring Fairness Considerations with the Ultimatum Game: An Experimental Investigation

Author: Claudia Rangnow, Ph.D Student, Humboldt-University Berlin, Spandauer Str. 1, Berlin, 10178, Germany, rangnowc@wiwi.hu-berlin.de

Coauthor(s): Christian Schade

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present results of an experiment on ultimatum bargaining and discuss the relevance of these findings for entrepreneurship research.
