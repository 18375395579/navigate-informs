Title: Scheduling Priority Classes in Subscription-based Rental Service
Author: Apurva Jain, University of Washington Business School, Box 353200, Seattle, WA, 98195-3200, United States, apurva@u.washington.edu
Coauthor(s): Kamran Moinzadeh, Aussadavut Dumrongsiri
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper offers a model and analysis of the rental process at a subscription-based business like Netflix. We analyze a priority scheduling scheme, currently in use, that gives priority to light renters over heavy renters and show that, in some situations, it may be more profitable for the firm to give priority to heavy renters. Several extensions are discussed.
