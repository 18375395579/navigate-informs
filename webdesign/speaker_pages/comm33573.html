Title: Childbirth and Pelvic Floor Dysfunction: An Integrated Decision Analysis
Author: Sejal Patel, University of Michigan, L4000 Women's Hospital, 1500 E. Medical Center Drive, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109-0276, United States, sejalnp@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Divya Patel, Julie Ivy, Scott Ransom, Dee Fenner, Xiao Xu, John DeLancey
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present a decision model for comparing elective cesarean section and planned vaginal delivery, while considering long-term complications including pelvic floor dysfunction. Published data on costs and probabilities of maternal and infant outcomes were used to model cost-effectiveness. Our findings should inform the current debate surrounding elective cesarean section.
