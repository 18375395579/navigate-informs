Title: Assessing Market Structure by Listening to Consumers Using Text Mining

Author: Ronen Feldman, School Of Business Administration, Hebrew University, Jerusalem, ronenf@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Jacob Goldenberg, Oded Netzer

Year: 2007

Abstract: Can one organize the information posted by consumers on the Internet to allow managers to assess market potential and market structure? The wide spread of the internet has lead to a colossal amount of information posted by consumers on the web through mediums such as forums, blogs, and product reviews. This type of information offers the firm an opportunity to listen to consumers. We rely on large network analysis techniques in converting the text mining data into market structure analysis. The networks in our research are not consumer networks but rather semantic ones in which the nodes consist of concepts, products, or commonly used terms.
