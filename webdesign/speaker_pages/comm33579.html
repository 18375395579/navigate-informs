Title: Simple Case Study of Multi-level Manufacturing Flexibilities Decision Making during Planning Stage

Author: Yingxia Yang, MIT, 550 memorial drive, apt 11b-3, cambridge, 02139, United States of America, yingxia@MIT.EDU

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper will use a simple case to study (1) the importance to incorporate uncertainty analysis during planning stage to make manufacturing flexibility decisions (2) the value of flexibility to manufacturing systems (3) the interactions between different flexibility sources and their impacts on decision making during planning stage.
