Title: Free Allowances and Investments in a CO2 Constrained Restructured Electricity Market
Author: Yves Smeers, Professor, Universite Catholique de Louvain CORE/UCL, 34 voie du Roman Pays, Louvain La Neuve, Be, B-1348, Belgium, smeers@core.ucl.ac.be
Coauthor(s): Andreas Ehrenmann
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider a stylised investment model of generation in a restructured electricity system with a CO2 cap and trade. We discuss the incentive to ivnestments arising from granting free allowances on the base of output or fuel consumption.
