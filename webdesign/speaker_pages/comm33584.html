Title: The Micks: Strong Knapsack Facets
Author: Sangho Shim, Research Associate, Kellogg School of Management/Northwestern University, 2001 Sheridan Road Suite 548, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, shim@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Wenwei Cao, Sunil Chopra
Year: 2013
Abstract: We perform the worst case analysis of the knapsack facets and identify the strongest knapsack facets which we call the Micks. We develop a linear time separation algorithm to identify the most violated Micks.
