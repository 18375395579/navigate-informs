Title: Consumer Bounded-Rationality: Evidence from the Click Stream Data
Author: Jun Li, Assistant Professor of Technology and Operations, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, United States of America, junwli@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Serguei Netessine
Year: 2012
Abstract: Using click stream data of online search for air travels, we study how price volatility affects strategic purchase delays and search frequencies.
