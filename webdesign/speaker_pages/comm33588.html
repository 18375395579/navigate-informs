Title: TSA Pre-check Expansion (Capstone)

Author: Florian Tafa, Southern Polytechnic State University, 1100 South Marietta Pkwy, Marietta, GA, 30060-2896, United States of America, ftafa11@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Rubi Gutierrez

Year: 2013

Abstract: The purpose of this study is the design and simulation of the proposed locations of the TSA Pre-Check program in the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. By simulating the proposed locations, we will then determine the ideal placement location for the new Pre-Check queues. This implementation will be affected by the results of this study as well as other factors concerning all stakeholders.
