Title: CSDP: An Open Source SDP Solver
Author: Brian Borchers, New Mexico Tech, Department of Mathematics, 801 Leroy Place, Socorro, NM, 87801, United States of America, borchers@nmt.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: CSDP is an SDP solver that has been under development since 1997. The software is now available through the COIN-OR repository of open source software. This talk will discuss recent improvements to the package.
