Title: Doing Good with OR: Energy/ Environment

Author: John Weyant, Stanford University, weyant@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Richard Richels, James Sweeney, Brian McLean, Jae Edmonds, Howard K. Gruenspech

Year: 2008

Abstract: This special series on “Doing Good with Good OR?is aimed at demonstrating how operations research can (and does) provide important insights that can be used to inform and shape public policy. We focus on three daunting societal challenges: energy and the environment; public health; and air congestion. Each focus area will feature a plenary presentation by a leader in the field, followed by a panel discussion involving key stakeholders from government and industry.
