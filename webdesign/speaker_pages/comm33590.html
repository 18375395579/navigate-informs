Title: The Firm-Boundary Decision and Its Performance In the Double Helix Model

Author: Jin-Kyu Park, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Florida International University, 10555 West Flagler Street / EC 3100, Miami, FL, 33174, United States, jinkp@engin.umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Young Ro

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study incorporates the concepts of technological uncertainty and the Double Helix Model to investigate how various product architectures affect a firm¡¯s boundary decisions. Through industry observations, we test the hypotheses that product architecture and firm boundary are interrelated and that their joint effect plays a critical role in firm dominance. Test results supporting our propositions are mixed.
