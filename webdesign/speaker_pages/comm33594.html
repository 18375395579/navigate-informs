Title: Statistics and Algorithms for Prediction of Protein Side-chain Conformations

Author: Roland Dunbrack, Member, Fox Chase Cancer Center, 333 Cottman Avenue, Philadelphia, PA, 19111, United States of America, roland.dunbrack@fccc.edu

Coauthor(s): Maxim Shapovalov, Georgy Krivov

Year: 2008

Abstract: A new version of the backbone-dependent rotamer library for protein side chains has been developed using kernel density estimates and kernel regression to produce smoothly varying rotamer probabilities and mean dihedral angles and variances. This library has been incorporated into SCWRL4 for side-chain conformation prediction. SCWRL4 has several algorithmic improvements, including graph tree decomposition to solve the combinatorial problem of rotamer choice given an input backbone structure.
