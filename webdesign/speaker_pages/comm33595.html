Title: Management and Probability Theory at the Bell System: 1900-1929: Traffic Operations and Quality Control

Author: Paul J. Miranti, Professor, Rutgers Business School, 111 Washington Street, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States, miranti@business.rutgers.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper analyzes two antecedents to the emergence of the operations research and management science disciplines that evolved from the adaptation of probability theory to resolve problems in traffic management and manufacturing quality control at the Bell System prior to 1929.
