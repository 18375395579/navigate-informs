Title: Managing Blood Collection Operations
Author: Ali Ekici, University of Houston, Department of Industrial Engineering, Houston, TX, United States of America, aekici@central.uh.edu
Coauthor(s): Orsan Ozener, Gultekin Kuyzu
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this paper, motivated by the practices in blood supply management, we study a variant of the vehicle routing problem. Considering processing requirements of donated whole blood in order to extract platelets, we analyze the pickup operations from donation centers and develop algorithms to maximize the platelet production.
