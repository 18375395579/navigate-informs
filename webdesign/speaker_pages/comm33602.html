Title: Optimizing Locations for Ocean Wave Energy Farms under Uncertainty
Author: Lawrence V. Snyder, Associate Professor, Lehigh University, 200 West Packer Ave., Mohler Lab, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, larry.snyder@lehigh.edu
Coauthor(s): Lizhou Mao
Year: 2013
Abstract: Our models seek optimal locations of ocean wave-energy conversion (WEC) devices within a wave farm under uncertainty, accounting for the hydrodynamic interactions between ocean waves and the WECs. We obtain solutions that are significantly more robust than those in the literature, which perform poorly when the wave environment changes only slightly. We also discuss qualitative results concerning how the optimal layouts change as the ocean characteristics change.
