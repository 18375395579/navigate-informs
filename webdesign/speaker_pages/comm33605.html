Title: Some Results on Availability Designs using Hadamard Matrices
Author: Damaraju Raghavarao, Laura H. Carnell Professor of Statistics, Temple University, Speakman Hall 343, Philadelphia, PA, 19122, United States of America, draghava@temple.edu
Coauthor(s): James Wiley
Year: 2008
Abstract: When a set of brands of a product class are presented to respondents, the presence or absence of brands may affect the choice of other brands. Availability experiments are used to infer these effects. In this talk some properties of availability designs constructed from Hadamard matrices will be discussed.
