Title: Network-level Reliability and Connection to Network Flow Theory
Author: Hani S. Mahmassani, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, masmah@u.northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Tian Hou, Meead Saberi
Year: 2012
Abstract: Robust relations between travel time reliability and flow variables are established at the network level. The relations are validated using actual data and simulation results in several networks.
