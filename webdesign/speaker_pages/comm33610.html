Title: Trends and Fashions in Strategy Support Tools
Author: Robert Dyson, Professor, Warwick Business School, University of Warick, Coventry, CV4 7AL, United Kingdom, robert.dyson@wbs.ac.uk
Year: 2008
Abstract: The paper identifies fashionable strategic management tools that have appeared in the academic literature over the past 50 years. The popularity of each tool through time was measured by analysing the content of relevant academic and professional journals. The tools are also related to the support they provide for particular strategic activities. The aim was to demonstrate patterns and infer causes through time.
