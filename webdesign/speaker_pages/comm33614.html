Title: IS Outsourcing to China: The Perspective of Hong Kong SME
Author: C.H. Cheng, Associate Professor, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dept of Syst Engg & Engg Mgmt, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shatin, NT, 00000, Hong Kong - ROC, chcheng@se.cuhk.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): J. Motwani, K.F. Wong
Year: 2008
Abstract: We conduct a study to examine the working relationship between Chinese Software Developers and Hong Kong SMEs through interviewing eight executives. Specifically through our study, we hope to increase our understanding of how the outsourcing decisions are made, how the operations are managed, and how the concerns are addressed. We believe that their experience will be also useful to non-Hong Kong companies.
