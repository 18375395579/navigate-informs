Title: Store Brands and Channel Power

Author: Ruixia Shi, Assistant Professor, University of Richmond, Robins School of Business, 28 Westhampton Way, Richmond, VA, 23229, United States of America, rshi@richmond.edu

Coauthor(s): Jun Zhang, Jun Ru

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper relates a retailer’s store brand strategy to the relative bargaining powers of channel members. It shows that the store brand becomes less appealing to the retailer and the quality threshold for the store brand to be introduced becomes higher as power shifts from a manufacturer to a retailer.
