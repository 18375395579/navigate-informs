Title: Assessing the Needs of the Working Poor during a Pandemic

Author: Vicki Bier, Professor, University of Wisconsin - Madison, 3234 Mechanical Engineering Building, 1513 University Ave, Madison, WI, 53706, United States of America, bier@engr.wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Lorna Zach, Terrence O'Sullivan, Susan King

Year: 2008

Abstract: A pandemic could cause enormous suffering. One intervention for controlling disease is closing of non-critical businesses. Hospitality and entertainment could therefore experience dramatic downturns. Counties dependent on tourism would be hard hit; the average wages of lost jobs would be extremely low. We identified policy options to address these concerns.
