Title: Making Decision Analysis Routine in Organizations
Author: Onder Guven, PhD Candidate, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, onderguven@stanford.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Most organizations utilize decision analysis as one-off interventions. By utilizing interviews and surveys with decision makers in institutional settings, along with the organizational behavior literature, this research aims to develop a decision analytic framework that can incorporate decision analysis into routine business practices.
