Title: An Interactive Panel/Audience Discussion: Grant Writing for New Faculty

Author: Erick Moreno-Centeno, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, 4034 ETB, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, e.moreno@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sheldon Jacobson, Simge Küçükyavuz, Edwin Romeijn, Sergiy Butenko

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this interactive panel, we will discuss successful strategies for writing grants. The panel comprises NSF program directors, and successful grant-writing/winning faculty (both at NSF and other agencies). Panel members will share strategies for writing grants including choosing the appropriate agency, preparing for and writing a grant. A large part of the panel will be an interactive discussion with the audience. Panelists: Sergiy Butenko, Sheldon Jacobson, Simge Küçükyavuz, Edwin Romeijn
