Title: Combinatorial Auctioneering

Author: Charles A. Holt, Professor, University of Virginia, Department of Economics, Charlottesville, VA, United States, cah2k@cms.mail.virginia.edu

Coauthor(s): Paul J. Healy, Christoph Brunner, Jacob Goeree

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper reports laboratory tests of combinatorial auction formats that have been proposed as alternatives to the FCC’s simultaneous multi-round procedure. Package bidding formats generally improve performance. In environments where collusion is likely, it is effectively broken by a two-stage Anglo-Dutch auction (a combinatorial clock followed by sealed bids).
