Title: Demand Management in Smart Grid

Author: Mona Asudegi, University of Maryland, 9348 Cherryhill Road, College Park, MD, 20740, United States of America, asudegi@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Ali Haghani

Year: 2012

Abstract: Climate change concerns, high oil prices, and energy security are major concerns in recent decades. Using renewable energies in addition to demand side management of the consumers are inevitable contributors which can help overcoming these concerns. This study is proposing a Demand Response model to manage different resources of generation and consumption including plug in electrical vehicles in the Smart Grid which is the future of energy systems.
