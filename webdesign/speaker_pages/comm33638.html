Title: Overcoming the Planning Fallacy
Author: Yael Grushka-Cockayne, University of Virginia, 100 Darden Boulevard, Charlottesville, VA, 22903, United States of America, GrushkaY@darden.virginia.edu
Coauthor(s): Bert De Reyck, Ioannis Fragkos, Daniel Read
Year: 2013
Abstract: How an organization manages its projects is critical to its success. Yet, firms routinely experience the Planning Fallacy: projects are delivered late, over-budget, or with reduced scope. We investigate project performance and describe our work with UK Network Rail on how to plan for the planning fallacy.
