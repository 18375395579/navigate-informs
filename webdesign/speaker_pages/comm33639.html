Title: How Delay Announcements Shape Customer Behavior: A Field Experiment Study
Author: Qiuping Yu, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, QiupingYu2009@u.northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Gad Allon, Achal Bassamboo
Year: 2013
Abstract: We explore the impact of delay announcements by conducting a field experiment at a medium sized call center. Specifically, we study and compare the call center performances where announcements of different granularity are provided. We also contrast this against the benchmark where no information is provided.
