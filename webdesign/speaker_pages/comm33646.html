Title: Modeling Long-term Prices under Uncertainty
Author: Jorge Valenzuela, Associate Professor, Auburn University, 3304 Shelby Center, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States of America, jvalenz@eng.auburn.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Knowledge of the statistical distribution of long-term prices is useful in risk assessment and improved decision-making. In this talk, a stochastic model that considers uncertanties of generator availability, demand, and spot price is described. A numerical example is given.
