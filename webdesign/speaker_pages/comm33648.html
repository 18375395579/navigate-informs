Title: Study Results: How IT Service Providers in Styria/Austria use Smart Services
Author: Eugen Brenner, Professor, Graz Technical University, Inffeldgasse 16, Graz, 8010, Austria, brenner@tugraz.at
Coauthor(s): Ernst Kreuzer, Helmut Aschbacher
Year: 2012
Abstract: Based on a survey which was conducted 2010 and 2011 in Styria/Austria among 55 IT Service Providers the acceptance and the practical use of Smart Service approaches were researched. The survey results show good insight in the practical usage of preemptive service approaches (Smart Service concepts) of Styrian IT Service Providers. Furthermore strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats were examined due to the use of Smart Services in the B2B and B2C sector.
