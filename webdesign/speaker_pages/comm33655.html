Title: Using Decision Analysis Modeling to Improve Navy Infrastructure Planning
Author: Ronald (Fred) Woodaman, Principal Operations Research Specialist, CTC, 17 Planters Place, Stafford, VA, 22554, United States, woodamar@ctc.com
Coauthor(s): Norman Reitter, Lisa Moss
Year: 2007
Abstract: The Navy is focused on improving the efficiency of its shore infrastructure, facilities, and services. We describe efforts to improve Base Support Vehicle and Equipment (BSV&E) fueling operation and management. This capability is not operated and managed at an enterprise level, with a patchwork of providers, reporting and accounting practices, regional and national stakeholders. The authors describe how decision analysis methods were used to illuminate a path forward.
