Title: Decision Making in Time Series Forecasting
Author: Brent Moritz, Assistant Professor, Pennsylvania State University, 469 Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, bmoritz@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Enno Siemsen, Mirko Kremer
Year: 2013
Abstract: Experiments show that individuals exhibit a number of errors in time series forecasting. This includes acting on perceived trends, even when none exist. We investigate how this is impacted by a number of factors, including differences in the time series.
