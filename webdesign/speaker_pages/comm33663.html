Title: Days of Supply vs. Inventory Turns: A Behavioral Perspective

Author: Ulrich Thoneman, University of Colgone, Germany, Ulrich.Thonemann@uni-koeln.de

Coauthor(s): Tobias Stangl

Year: 2013

Abstract: The two most common inventory performance metrics are “days of supply?and “inventory turns? Although, from normative perspective, both metrics measure the same (with one being the inverse of the other), they can differ substantially when used by human decision makers: We show that days of supply is leading to better resource allocation decisions while inventory turns motivates subjects to exert more effort.
