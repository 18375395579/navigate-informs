Title: How to Improve the Energy Sector in Developing Countries

Author: Syed Shahabuddin, Professor, Central Michigan University, Smith 203C, Mt Pleasant, MI, 48858, United States of America, shaha1s@cmich.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: A country continual economic development requires energy. If a county is unable to provide enough, cheap energy, it could jeopardize its economic future. Operations Research has many techniques that can help develop efficient processes to improve productivity and reduce cost. The question is: does the availability of energy and cost effect economic growth and how can OR help? My paper will evaluate this question in the context of a few developing countries.
