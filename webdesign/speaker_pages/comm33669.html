Title: Market Level Price Optimization for Hotels and Gaming Resorts
Author: Ahmet Kuyumcu, akuyumcu@prorize.com
Coauthor(s): Amar Duggasani
Year: 2006
Abstract: Many hotel chains and gaming resorts own multiple properties in close proximity and need abilities to shift demand to optimize their profits. This requires new control capabilities such as free upgrades, cross-sells, and down-sells. In addition, forecasting, overbooking, and optimization models could utilize market-level information. This presentation discusses challenges and opportunities in implementing a market-level price optimization approach.
