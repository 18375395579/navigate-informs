Title: 57 Years of Close Encounters with George

Author: Harold W. Kuhn, Department of Mathematics, Fine Hall, Washington Road, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, kuhn@math.princeton.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: A personal account of eleven encounters with George Dantzig over 57 years. These range from a meeting in his office at Project SCOOP in the summer of 1948 to preparing a citation for his Linear Programming and Extensions as one of the Princeton University Press's Notable Centenary Titles in 2005.
