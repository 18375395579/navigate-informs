Title: Elwood Buffa’s Pioneering Contributions to the Evolution of Production and Operations Management
Author: Martin Starr, Professor Emeritus, Crummer Graduate School of Business, Rollins College, 1000 Holt Avenue, Winter Park, FL, 32789, United States, mstarr@cfL.rr.com
Coauthor(s): Jaya Singhal, Kalyan Singhal
Year: 2006
Abstract: Elwood S. Buffa, a pioneer in production and operations management (P/OM), passed away in the summer of 2005, leaving a valuable legacy. During his academic career Buffa made lasting contributions to teaching and research of P/OM. His 1961 textbook, Modern Production Management provided structure and foundation for many P/OM courses in business schools.
