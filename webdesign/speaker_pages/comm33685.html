Title: Ivy Software Inc. - Quantitative Pre-Matriculation Materials for MBA Programs
Author: Rob Holt, Marketing Representative, Ivy Software, Inc., PO Box 15776, Richmond, VA, 23227, United States of America, rob@ivysoftware.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: Ivy Software will present three online courses, Business Math and Statistics, Math and Statistical Concepts, and Excel for MBAs. In addition, Ivy will present Excel for undergraduate business majors. Each course consists of a text and interactive software. The courses are self-paced, online and utilize extensive assessment testing. Topics covered are regression, probability and decision analysis. Our new courses will run on PC, Mac and iOS.
