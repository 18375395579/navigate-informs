Title: New Product Development Evolution: The Search for the Best Alternative

Author: Gulru Ozkan, Assistant Professor, Clemson University, Department of Management, 120B Sirrine Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States of America, gulruo@clemson.edu

Coauthor(s): David Hall, Jeremy Hutchison-Krupat, Fred Switzer

Year: 2013

Abstract: Using a behavioral approach, we examine the strategy of decision makers who search for the best alternative to develop into a new product. The impacts of facing a set of radical or incremental set of alternatives and the level of time-pressure on the evolution of decision makers?performance are analyzed.
