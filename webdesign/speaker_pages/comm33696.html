Title: The Legacy of Richard E. Rosenthal, Distinguished Professor of OR, Naval Postgraduate School

Author: Gerald Brown, Distinguished Professor, Naval Postgraduate School, Operations Research Department, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, GBrown@nps.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Dell, Johannes Royset, Matthew Carlyle, Ronald Fricker, Kevin Wood, Javier Salmeron

Year: 2008

Abstract: The presenters have all been close colleagues of Rick Rosenthal. We each share and admire the passion Rick had for exposition of our OR art. Rick had a rare combination of good people skills and intellectual depth, and he employed these to the benefit of scores of colleagues and acquaintances. We will share with you the astonishing breadth, passion, and committment of Rick's contributions to OR, and to INFORMS.
