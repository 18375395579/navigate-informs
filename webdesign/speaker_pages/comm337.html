Title: Revenue Management Implementation Challenges: Case of Swagatam Hotel in Kolkata, India

Author: Goutam Dutta, Professor, IIM Ahmedabad, W-3, IIM Campus, Vastrapur, Ahmedabad, GU, 380015, India, goutam@iimahd.ernet.in

Coauthor(s): Shilpy Singh, Aditya Challa

Year: 2008

Abstract: Any organization that wants to implement a RM system must have 2-3 years data. In case data is not available, RM cannot be implemented. This is the basic message that is discussed in this case. Students learn how to use concepts of booking curve. For a two session course, we demonstrate formulation of Hotel RM as Stochastic Linear Programming Problem.
