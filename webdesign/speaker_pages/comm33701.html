Title: A Data-driven Method for Robust Water Allocation under Uncertainty

Author: David Love, University of Arizona, 617 N Santa Ria Avenue, PO Box 210089, Applied Matematics, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, dlove@math.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Guzin Bayraksan

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Colorado River Basin, which supplies water to over 25 million people in the southwestern US, has experienced a sustained period of drought in recent years, raising questions about its ability to meet future demands. We adapt the Likelihood Robust Optimization method recently proposed by Wang,Glynn and Ye to examine water allocation under the ambiguous distribution of future rainfall. We examine the properties of the model, relate it to risk models, and present computational results.
