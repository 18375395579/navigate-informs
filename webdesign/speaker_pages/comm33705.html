Title: Optimal Hedging of Prediction Errors using Prediction Errors

Author: Yuji Yamada, Associate Professor, University of Tsukuba, 3-29-1 Otsuka, Bunkyo-ku, Tokyo, 112-0012, Japan, yuji@gssm.otsuka.tsukuba.ac.jp

Year: 2008

Abstract: Predicting the future weather conditions is considered important in real businesses for many industries including electricity producers and suppliers, because their profit or loss is largely affected by the weather conditions. Under these circumstances, we may have a new risk when the prediction error exists. In this work, we will propose a new type of weather derivative to effectively hedge the loss caused by prediction errors.
