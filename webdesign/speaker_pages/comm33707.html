Title: Connecting the Students to the World

Author: Michael Racer, University of Memphis, Memphis, TN, United States of America, mracer@memphis.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: “This material was hard?- how often do we hear that phrase from students unfamiliar with statistics and operations research techniques. In this talk, we will discuss the approach we take in a two course sequence in the business school. The goal is to make the students comfortable with using the tools were teaching.
