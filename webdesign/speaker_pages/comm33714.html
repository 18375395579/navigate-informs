Title: Complex Networks in Business and Economics
Author: Arun Sundararajan, Professor, New York University, 44 West 4th Street, KMC 8-93, New York, NY, 10012, United States, asundara@stern.nyu.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: This session will feature a joint tutorial (with Professor Ravi Bapna)on the applications of ideas from complex network theory to problems in OR, business and economics. Assuming a basic understanding of graph theory and random graphs, we will cover selected recent advances in the theory of complex networks that can be used in applied problems, and outline promising high-impact open research areas. This tutorial is especially recommended for doctoral students.
