Title: Elicitation of Likelihoods, Priors and Utilities
Author: Jay Kadane, Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, Department of Statistics, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, kadane@stat.cmu.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: This talk reviews the current state of frameworks and methods for elicitation. As our ability to compute advances, our ability to elicit is likely to become a most urgent issue for the advancement of Bayesian methods.
