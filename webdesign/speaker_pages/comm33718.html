Title: Service Science Section: Student Best Paper Award Session - Finalist Competitive Presentations
Author: Gregory Heim, Associate Professor, Texas A&M University, INFO Department Mays Business School, 320 Wehner Building 4217 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843-4217, United States of America, GHeim@mays.tamu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Service Science Section annually sponsors a “Best Student Paper?research award competition for students. The presenters in this session will be invited by a committee of service scholars, based on the students?submitted manuscripts. In this session, invited finalists will present their research studies, and a panel of distinguished service scholars will determine the rank ordering of winners. Winners will then be announced at the Service Science Section’s business meeting.
