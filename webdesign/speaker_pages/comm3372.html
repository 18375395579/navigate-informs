Title: Evidence Contrary to the Statistical View of Boosting

Author: David Mease, Assistant Professor, San Jose State University, mease_d@cob.sjsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Abraham Wyner

Year: 2007

Abstract: The statistical perspective on boosting focuses on optimization, drawing parallels with logistic regression. We present empirical evidence that raises questions about this view. Although the statistical perspective provides a theoretical framework within which it is possible to derive theorems and create new algorithms for general contexts, we show that there remain many unanswered important questions.
