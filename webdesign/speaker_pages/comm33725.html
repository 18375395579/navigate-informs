Title: Panel Discussion: Recommendations of the INFORMS Computing Society Education Committee
Author: Jill Hardin, Virginia Commonwealth University, 1001 W. Main St., P.O. Box 843083, Richmond, VA, 23284-3083, United States, jrhardin@vcu.edu
Coauthor(s): David Rader, Cesar Rego, Kevin Furman, Allen Holder
Year: 2007
Abstract: At its January 2007 business meeting, the INFORMS Computing Society proposed forming an Education Committee. This committee has been charged with recommending a model undergraduate curriculum which would produce graduates who are well prepared for work at the OR/CS interface. The draft report detailing the committee’s recommendations and rationale will be presented, followed by a panel discussion with the members of the committee.
