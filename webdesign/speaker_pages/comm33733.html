Title: Managing Conversion Rates for Better Retail Store Performance

Author: Olga Perdikaki, PhD Candidate, The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, Kenan-Flagler Business School, CB# 3490, McColl Bldg., Chapel Hill, NC, 27599-3490, United States of America, perdikao@kenan-flagler.unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Jayashankar Swaminathan, Saravanan Kesavan

Year: 2008

Abstract: In a service oriented retail environment conversion rate defined as sales to traffic ratio is a key performance metric for retailers. In this study we investigate factors that affect conversion rates and its role in driving store profitability.
