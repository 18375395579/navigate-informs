Title: Perspectives on the Journal

Author: Linda Argote, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, argote@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The session will focus on INFORMS journal, Organization Science's Current Status and Future Directions. The session will provide an overview of Organization Science, including its editorial team, the vision for the journal, objectives, and its operation. Statistics about review time and acceptance rates will be presented. Future directions including special issues will be described.
