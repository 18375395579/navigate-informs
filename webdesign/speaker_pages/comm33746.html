Title: Online-offline Strategies in Retail

Author: Santiago Gallino, The Wharton School, 3720 Walnut St, Philadelphia, 19104, United States of America, sgallino@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Antonio Moreno-Garcia

Year: 2012

Abstract: Using a dataset from a leading retailer, we study the effect on customer behavior of online-offline integration strategies, such as showing inventory information online and store pickup of online orders.
