Title: Hospital Business Analytics in an Era of Healthcare Reform

Author: Don Kleinmuntz, Chief Analytics Officer, Strata Decision Technology, 200 E Randolph St 49th Floor, Chicago, IL, 60654, United States of America, DNK@stratadecision.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Reform is transforming U.S. hospitals & health systems. Analytics can help, but this requires careful balance of clinical, operational, and financial objectives, and massive investment in IT. This presentation draws on years of experience with financial decision making in hospitals and health systems to illustrate the opportunities and challenges, discussing applications of descriptive, predictive, and prescriptive analytics.
