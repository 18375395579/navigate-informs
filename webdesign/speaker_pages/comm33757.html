Title: Emergency Personnel Resource Allocation In Mass Casualty Disasters

Author: Susan Heath, Assistant Professor, Naval Postgraduate School, 1 University Circle, Monterey, CA, 93943, United States of America, skheath@nps.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This research addresses the allocation of emergency personnel in mass casualty disaster situations. This research uses queueing theory and simulation to develop rules of thumb that an Incident Commander could use in a variety disaster situations to quickly determine how many of their emergency response personnel resources to assign to the 3 primary tasks of triage, patient movement, and initial patient treatment and preparation for transport. The focus is on the initial disaster response.
