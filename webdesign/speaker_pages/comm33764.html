Title: Integrating Routine Data to Reduce Acute Attendances and Admissions
Author: Derek Ceglarek, University of Warwick, Institute of Digital Healthcare, International Digital Laboratory, Warwick, United Kingdom, D.J.Ceglarek@warwick.ac.uk
Coauthor(s): Sudi Lahiri
Year: 2013
Abstract: ]: Unnecessary variations in care processes at an emergency department can result in patients diverting from care pathways triggering avoidable acute attendances and admissions. This is especially common among patients with co-morbidities. We used routine hospital data to model patient healthcare journeys to reduce avoidable acute contacts and healthcare system inefficiencies.
