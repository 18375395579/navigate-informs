Title: The Next Frontier for Portfolio Management - Med Tech Companies

Author: Jack Kloeber, Principal, Kromite, Chruchville, PA, United States of America, jkloeber@kromite.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Some industries have had decades of experience with decision analysis and rigorous portfolio analysis- Healthcare and Oil and Gas are two of them. However, within Healthcare, Pharmaceuticals and Hospital systems have seen the greatest use of DA and portfolio management. Pharmaceutical supply chain has seen huge strides in quality and efficiency. Medical decision making has also seen a dramatic increase in solid DA work. Medical devices and diagnostics, however, have not seen the same use.
