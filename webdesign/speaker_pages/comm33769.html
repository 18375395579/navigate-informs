Title: How Does Organization Structure Affect Supply Chain Performance?

Author: Anupam Agrawal, University of Illinois, 363 Wohlers Hall, Champaign, IL, 61821, United States of America, anupam@illinois.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We explore a detailed real life experiment in which a fi rm had diff erent organizational structures in its two divisions, and these diff erent structures infuenced the quality of the sourced components in the two divisions diff erently. We show how buyer organization redesign can create a learning environment at the suppliers, and if this redesign is not done, then the learning environment and thereby the incoming quality can be di fferent.
