Title: Equipment and Material Process Streams in Receiving to Patient Healthcare Supply Chains

Author: Ronald L. Rardin, White Distinguished Professor, University of Arkansas, Industrial Engineering, Bell 4169, Fayetteville, AR, 72762, United States of America, rrardin@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Angelica Burbano, Behlul Saka, Nebil Buyurgan, Josh Eno, Craig Thompson

Year: 2008

Abstract: Equipment process streams in hospitals involve what types of portable equipment to track and levels of aggregation, whereas materials process streams involve integration of receiving, replenishment, utilization and billing of the med/surg supplies and implantables. Business processes and the technology options are explored for both streams. Findings from literature, site visits and expert consultations are presented along with preliminary design support models.
