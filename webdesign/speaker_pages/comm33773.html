Title: The Carbon Reduction Effect and Policy Choices of Adjusting Industrial Structure
Author: Zengkai Zhang, School of Management, Xi’an Jiaotong University, No.28, Xianning West Road, Shaanxi, Xi'an, China, zengkaizhang@sina.com
Coauthor(s): Ju'e Guo
Year: 2012
Abstract: Reducing carbon emissions and adjusting industrial structure are China’s two strategic tasks, while adjusting industrial structure is the essential way of carbon reduction. Based on China’s SAM of 2007, this paper develops China’s dynamic CGE model to evaluate the contribution of the industrial structure adjustment in carbon reduction, and proposes policy recommendations on adjusting industrial structure.
