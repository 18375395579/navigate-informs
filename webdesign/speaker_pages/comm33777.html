Title: Transitioning to a Service-oriented Business Model: A Technology Roadmapping Approach

Author: Robert Harmon, Professor of Marketing & Technology Management, Portland State University, School of Business, Portland, OR, 97207, United States of America, corevalue@comcast.net

Coauthor(s): Bill Hefley, Haluk Demirkan

Year: 2012

Abstract: For technology firms, the evolution from manufacturing to service-dominant business models as a primary driver of innovation has become an important trend. This research reviews the literature on the key dimensions, frameworks, and organizational considerations for the service transition process. It utilizes a technology roadmapping approach to illustrate the issues and strategy dimensions faced by a technology manufacturer as it navigates the service transition process.
