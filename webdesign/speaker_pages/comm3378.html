Title: Operations Research in Vehicle Development: A Panel Discussion by Women in General Motors Research &
Author: R. Jean Ruth, General Motors Research & Development, MC 480-106-256 R&D VDR, 30500 Mound Rd., Warren, MI, 48090-9055, United States, rjean.ruth@gm.com
Coauthor(s): Hallie Kintner, Peiling Wu, Gulcin Sengir, Qi Hommes, Janet Goings
Year: 2007
Abstract: What is it like to work in an R&D lab in industry? In the auto industry? To start the discussion, we’ll give a brief overview GM R&D and some of our O.R.-related projects in vehicle development, business strategy and planning, and design and technology. Most of the session will be open discussion with Q&A, for example, about projects, vehicle development, working in industry, women in R&D, career paths and choices, and the automotive business.
