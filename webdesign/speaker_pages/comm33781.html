Title: Performance Measures of Manual Multi-modal Traffic Signal Control
Author: Qing He, SUNY Buffalo, 313 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, qinghe@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): Nan Ding, Sean Wu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Traffic control agencies (TCA), including police officers, firefighters or other traffic law enforcement officers, who override automatic traffic signal control, are crucial to mitigate non-recurrent traffic congestions, caused by planned and unplanned events. This work assesses the performance of TCA-based manual multi-modal traffic signal control under special events.
