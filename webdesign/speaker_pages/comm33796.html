Title: MINLP Formulations for the Euclidean Steiner Problem
Author: Nelson Maculan, Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, PESC/COPPE, CP 68511, Rio de Janeiro, RJ, 21941-972, Brazil, nelson.maculan@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Marcia Fampa, Brígida Sartini
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present two Mixed Integer Non-Linear Programming (MINLP) formulations for the Euclidean Steiner problem and analyze the performance of MINLP solvers on the solution of test instance.
