Title: Approximate Dynamic Programming for Adapting Radiotherapy to Hypoxia

Author: Fatemeh Saberian, University of Washington, 5209 15th AVE NE Apt No 303, seattle, Wa, 98105, United States of America, saberian@uw.edu

Coauthor(s): Archis Ghate, Minsun Kim

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present an approximate dynamic programming methodology to adapt to the spatiotemporal evolution of tumor hypoxia. Results on prostate, lung and head and neck cancer cases will be discussed.
