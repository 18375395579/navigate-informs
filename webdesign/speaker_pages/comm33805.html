Title: Improving Healthcare Supply Chains and Logistics (Panel)

Author: Ronald Rardin, White Chair and Distinguished Professor, University of Arkansas, Ctr for Innov in Healthcare Logistics, 4207 Bell Engineering Center, Fayetteville, AR, 72701, United States of America, rrardin@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Russell Meller, Bill Hardgrave

Year: 2007

Abstract: Huge unnecessary costs in healthcare supply chain and logistic systems result from overlapping providers, duplication, and under-investment in IT. As important, confusion in logistic systems has direct implications for safety and quality of patient care. The new Center for Innovation in Healthcare Logistics (CIHL) is a university-industrial partnership to catalyze solutions to these challenges. This panel of key CIHL personnel will discuss opportunities and future directions.
