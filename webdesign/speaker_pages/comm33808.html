Title: Supplier Finance –The Case of Nestle Russia

Author: Daniel Corsten, Professor, IE Business School, Maria de Molina 13, Madrid, 28004, Spain, daniel.corsten@ie.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Whilst factoring is a very traditional financing instrument, reverse factoring was developed more recently in response to the need for innovative financing instruments, particularly in emerging markets with its opaque credit market conditions. This session will outline Nestle Russia’s supplier finance program, discuss teaching applications and ideas for a research agenda.
