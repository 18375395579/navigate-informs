Title: From OR to PR and Back
Author: Sam Savage, Consulting Prof., Stanford University, Dept. of MS&E, 326 Terman, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, savage@stanford.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Many important OR concepts lie fallow because they are not understood by the general public. Small interactive OR models designed with Public Relations in mind can serve as channels of communication with your client. Once you understand what they understand, you will know what kind of OR is really needed.
