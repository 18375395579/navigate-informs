Title: Vehicle Sharing Programs in China: User Preferences on Travel Modes and Vehicle Types
Author: Wen Jin, Tsinghua University, Department of IE, Shunde Building Roon 519A, Beijing, 100084, China, jinw11@mails.tsinghua.edu.cn
Coauthor(s): Erica Klampfl, Hai Jiang, Yimin Liu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Vehicle sharing is a sustainable travel mode which has made great success in decreasing the number of vehicles in some of the cities in North America and Europe. This research investigates consumers'preferences among different travel modes when vehicle sharing comes into China. Also consumers' preferences towards types of vehicles and affiliated equipment when using vehicle sharing. The results of this research can be used for government and automakers when bringing in vehicle sharing to China.
