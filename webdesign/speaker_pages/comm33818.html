Title: The Search for a New Waffle House Principle- A Case Study in Business Operation after Hurricane Sandy
Author: Tom Darlington, University at Buffalo, The State University of New York, 328 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14228, United States of America, tedarlin@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): John Coles, Jun Zhuang, Matthew Brondum
Year: 2013
Abstract: FEMA currently makes use of WaffleHouse statuses in the southeastern United States to gauge the condition of a region in order to efficiently administer disaster relief. However, the “WaffleHouse Principle?cannot be applied everywhere due to the scarcity of WaffleHouse locations outside of the southeast. In this research we explore whether data from the Hurricane Sandy response about other businesses?status could be used to develop an alternative measure for assessment.
