Title: Certified Analytics Professional Certification
Author: Jack Levis, Director, Process Management, UPS, 2311 York Road, Timonium, MD, 21093, United States of America, jlevis@ups.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: After working for more than two years, INFORMS held its first Certified Analytics Professional (CAP) certification exam in San Antonio. This presentation will discuss CAP certification program and plans going forward. The session will be an interactive session with the audience to inform as well as seek feedback.
