Title: Potential Competition for Biomass between Biopower and Biofuel under RPS and RFS2
Author: Mohammad Rahdar, Iowa State University, Black Engineering, Ames, IA, 50011, United States of America, rahdar@iastate.edu
Coauthor(s): Lizhi Wang, Guiping Hu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Driven by RPS and RFS2, biopower generation and biofuel production will increasingly compete for biomass in the future. We study this competition as well as other interactions between two policies. Our model describes the U.S. renewable energy portfolio by explicitly accounting for all major renewable energy resources and policy requirements in all states through 2035. Our modeling results were used to address five important questions regarding the impact on the U.S. renewable energy portfolio.
