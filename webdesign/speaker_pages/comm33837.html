Title: Community Library of Cutting Planes for Re-use in Teaching, Research, and Business
Author: Robin Lougee-Heimer, IBM Research, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, robinlh@us.ibm.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: A community library of cutting plane implementations is available for use in teaching, research, and business. We will describe the 8yr old initiative to establish a cut generation library (CGL), report on recent progress, and explain how to contribute new cut implementations. More details on CGL can be found at https://projects.coin-or.org/Cgl
