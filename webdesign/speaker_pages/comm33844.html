Title: Using Design Thinking to Create Meaningful Learning Experiences

Author: Paul Szwed, Professor, U.S. Coast Guard Academy, Department of Management, 15 Mohegan Avenue, New London, CT, 06320, United States of America, Paul.S.Szwed@uscga.edu

Coauthor(s): Laurel Goulet, Charles Coiro

Year: 2012

Abstract: How do you go about developing a new course or improving an old one? One emerging method is to use concept and principles from design thinking. We had an award-winning capstone business consulting course that we knew was no longer getting us the desired results. Using design thinking concepts and principles, we conducted a “deep dive?on our course and developed an entirely new model. We will share the philosophies used and our experiences and lessons learned. Design thinking can be applied to a wide variety of courses or even modules in virtually any discipline.
