Title: Diffusion Wavelets and Applications

Author: Rajesh Ganesan, Assistant Professor, George Mason University, 4400 University Drive, ST2, MS4A6, Fairfax, VA, 22030, United States, rganesan@gmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Poornima Balakrishna

Year: 2006

Abstract: This talk presents the concept of diffusion wavelets and some of its applications in function approximation and dimensionality reduction.
