Title: Shared Minds: How Patients use Collaborative-Based Information Sharing

Author: Lu Yan, Indiana University, Kelley School of Business, Bloomington, IN, United States of America, lucyyan@uw.edu

Coauthor(s): Yong Tan

Year: 2012

Abstract: Social media has changed patients?role in managing their health. In this study, we investigate how patients utilize and educate themselves by learning others?shared experiences in the online healthcare communities. The results provide evidence for the existence of social contagion on the Internet for health information seeking and knowledge creation. Implications of these findings in relation to both practitioners and researchers are also discussed.
