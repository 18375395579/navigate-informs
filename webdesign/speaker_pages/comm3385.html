Title: EZ Publish - Website Management Tools and Hosting for INFORMS Communities
Author: Shirley Mohr, INFORMS, 7240 Parkway Drive, Hanover, MD, 21076, United States of America, shirley.mohr@informs.org
Year: 2012
Abstract: Learn about 'EZ' Website Management Tools and Hosting for INFORMS Communities This session is for INFORMS Community officers and webmasters and introduces EZ Publish, an easy but powerful web management tool for quickly creating and updating web sites without the need for web design experience or software. All you need is an internet connection and a login and you can get started. Any INFORMS Community or Subdivision that wishes to utilize a ready-made INFORMS template and intuitive tools to create and maintain their website should attend. Special features include blogs, forums, password-protected areas, real-time twitter feeds, and analytics reports about your site.
