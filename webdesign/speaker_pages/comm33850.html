Title: Optimal Selection of Countermeasures in IT Security Planning
Author: Tadeusz Sawik, Professor, AGH University of Science & Technology, Dept. OR & IT, Al.Mickiewicza 30, Krakow, 30059, Poland, ghsawik@cyf-kr.edu.pl
Year: 2012
Abstract: A mixed integer programming and conditional value-at-risk are applied for selection of countermeasure portfolio in IT security planning to prevent or mitigate cyber-threats. Given a set of potential threats and a set of available countermeasures, the selection of countermeasures is based on their effectiveness of blocking different threats, implementation costs and probability of potential attack scenarios.
