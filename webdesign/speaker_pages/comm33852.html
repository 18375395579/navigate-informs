Title: Determining Topic Sentiments Based on a Two-level Financial Sentiment Corpus
Author: Oskar Ahlgren, Aalto University, Runeberginkatu 22-24, Helsinki, 00100, Finland, oskar.ahlgren@aalto.fi
Year: 2013
Abstract: Sentiment analysis is a highly domain specific. Therefore, to be able to utilize the recent advances in statistical natural language processing, a high-quality financial corpus is necessary. In this paper, we introduce specific sentiment models for both document and sentence levels, and apply them on a newly constructed human annotated financial news corpus.
