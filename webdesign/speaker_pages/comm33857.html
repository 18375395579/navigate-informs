Title: Managing Change in Organizations where Mission Failure is not an Option

Author: Richard Staats, Senior Fellow CSA SSG, US Army, Box 1232, McLean, VA, 22101, United States of America, dr_games@yahoo.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper describes the fundamentals for successful organizational change in practical terms. The paper includes both theory and practical suggestions from experience. The methods used are derived from a variety of sources ranging from David Garvin to Sun Tsu. The foundation for most organizational change is measurement of the proper factors. The paper describes a standard metrics attribute approach that has been applied in practical situations world wide.
