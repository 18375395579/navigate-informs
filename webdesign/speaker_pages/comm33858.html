Title: Financing Eco-innovation ?The Determinants of Cooperation to Bridge the Valley of Death

Author: Friedemann Polzin, EBS Business School Strascheg Institute for Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Rheingaustr. 1, Oestrich-Winkel, 65375, Germany, polzin@instoec.de

Coauthor(s): Paschen von Flotow, Florian Taeube, Benjamin Weber

Year: 2012

Abstract: A main obstacle to effective commercialization and diffusion of clean technologies lies in the absent cooperation between financiers and innovators. I conduct expert interviews among project managers and survey stakeholders of government supported cooperative research projects (CRP) to explore this phenomenon. As results I expect a framework for the management of CRP and complementary industrial regulations to reduce information asymmetries in order to mitigate this innovation system failure.
