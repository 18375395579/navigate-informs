Title: Hospital Pediatric Specialization: Implications for the Delivery of Patient Care

Author: Mitch Harris, Ph.D. Candidate, NACHRI, 401 Wythe Street, Alexandria, VA, 22314, MHarris@nachri.org

Coauthor(s): Yasar A. Ozcan

Year: 2006

Abstract: Policy makers, payors and patients have shown increased interest in the concept of hospital specialization and its implications on effectiveness and quality of care. We present a model with levels of care complexity and hospital specialization that must be in fit for outcomes to be maximized.
