Title: A Model of Fair Process and its Limits

Author: Yaozhong Wu, Assistant Professor, NUS Business School, BIZ2 #03-08, 1 Business Link, National University of Singapore, Singapore, 117592, Singapore, bizwyz@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Christoph Loch, Ludo Van der Heyden

Year: 2007

Abstract: Organizational justice research has shown that fair process, or employee engagement and transparency, enhances both employee motivation and performance. However, work to date has not addressed why fair process is so often violated in practice and why in these instances the hoped for benefits not pursued. We develop a principal-agent model that links fair process to social preferences, and we show that indeed fair process will not always be used.
