Title: Panel Discussion: Essential Topics in Teaching Decision Analysis

Author: Ronald Howard, Professor and Director Decisions and Ethics Center, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, rhoward@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Ali Abbas, James Smith, Ralph Keeney, Eric Bickel

Year: 2007

Abstract: Decision analysis is taught in various contexts with different objectives. The question of which topics should be covered in texts and classes will be discussed by a panel of those experienced in teaching and then by the audience. The comments of practitioners are particularly welcomed.
