Title: Using Decision Analysis to Make Effective Decisions in Local Government

Author: Dan Pitzler, Consulting Economist, Decision Analysis, CH2M HILL, PO Box 91500, Bellevue, WA, 98033, United States, Dan.Pitzler@ch2m.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Decision analysis is becoming more popular as an approach to decision making in local government. This presentation will provide some examples of how decision analysis is currently being practiced in local government, and explore some of the unique challenges experienced by decision analysts in this venue.
