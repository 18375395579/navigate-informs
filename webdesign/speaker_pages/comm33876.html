Title: Online Routing in Vehicular Ad Hoc Network
Author: Lili Du, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, Troy, NY, dul@rpi.edu
Coauthor(s): Satish Ukkusuri
Year: 2008
Abstract: Online routing is critical in decentralized Advanced Traveler Information Systems (ATIS) based on Vehicular Ad Hoc Networks (VANET. VANET provides information of different qualities in various traffic conditions. This research studies online routing under Closed-loop Adaptive Routing (CAR) rule in VANET incorporating the variation of information reliability and time delay.
