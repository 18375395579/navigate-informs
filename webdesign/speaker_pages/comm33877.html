Title: Experiences with Market Clearing Optimization for Competitive Electrictiy Markets

Author: David Sun, AREVA T&D, 10865 Willows Rd, Redmond, WA, 98052, United States of America, david.sun@areva-td.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Mathematical optimization provides a formal framework that enables systematic and transparent decision process which is essential for successful operation of competitive electricity markets. Practical experiences with the development and operation of optimization-based market clearing applications, as typical business process activitity time-lines and data flows, and in terms of mathematical formulation, solution performance, and software products.
