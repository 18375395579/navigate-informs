Title: Reimplementing SeDuMi: A Progress Report

Author: Imre Pólik, Lehigh University, Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, 200 West Packer Avenue, Bethlehem, PA, 18015-1582, United States of America, imre.polik@gmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: SeDuMi is an open source package for optimization over symmetric cones, written originally by Jos Sturm. Int his talk we summarize our efforts to reimplement SeDuMi package using Python. Issues regarding the input format and design layout will be discussed.
