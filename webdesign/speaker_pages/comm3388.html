Title: Advances in Terrorism Risk Analysis
Author: Detlof von Winterfeldt, Professor, University of Southern Califonia, RTH 310, University Park, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, detlof@aol.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: The Center for Risk and Economic Analysis of Terrorism Events (CREATE) has made several advances in risk analysis in the past few years. These include advances in game theory and dynamic decision analysis. This presentation will summarize these advances.
