Title: Dynamic Intermodal Operations
Author: Karen Smilowitz, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, TECH M233, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, ksmilowitz@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Alan Erera, Guangming Zhang
Year: 2008
Abstract: This paper investigates models for planning urban freight drayage operations, and focuses specifically on challenges arising from the informational and operational dynamics inherent in these systems.
