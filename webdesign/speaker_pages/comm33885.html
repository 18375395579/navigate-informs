Title: Insights From Network Models of a Needle Exchange

Author: Joel Sokol, Associate Professor, School of ISyE, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States, jsokol@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Yao-Hsuan Chen

Year: 2007

Abstract: We model disease transmission in a network of needle exchange participants, and obtain insights into preventing disease spread within and outside of the test population.
