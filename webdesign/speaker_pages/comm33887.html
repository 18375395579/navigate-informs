Title: Pakistan Energy Crisis -- Breaking the Vicious Cycle

Author: Raza Rafique, PhD Student, Rutgers Unviersity, 1 Washington Park, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States of America, rrafique@pegasus.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Yao Zhao, Kwon Gi Mun

Year: 2013

Abstract: In recent years, Pakistan suffers a 25-40% shortfall of electricity supply nation-wide which hampers the economic growth, and give rise to political turmoils and social instability. Meanwhile, Pakistan has a huge reserve of coal nearly un-explored. We build a dynamic model to establish the energy supply chain based on coal and compare the result with the government's current plan.
