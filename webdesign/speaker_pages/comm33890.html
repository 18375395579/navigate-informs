Title: Incorporating Uncertainties into Product Development and Engineering Design
Author: R. Jean Ruth, General Motors Research & Development, MC 480-106-256 R&D VDR, 30500 Mound Rd., Warren, MI, 48090-9055, United States, rjean.ruth@gm.com
Coauthor(s): Artemis Kloess, Sharon Zielinski
Year: 2007
Abstract: For product applications that require balancing competing attributes and features, methods for incorporating uncertainties about consumer market research information and technology capabilities in the future, eliciting expert judgments, and combining sources of information are key. We use vehicle examples to discuss sources of uncertainty and how uncertainties evolve, consider methods for handling key uncertainties, and suggest areas for further research and development of applied methods.
