Title: Revenue Management as a Strategic Tool

Author: Maarten Oosten, SAS Institute Inc., SAS Campus Drive, Cary, NC, 27513, United States of America, Maarten.Oosten@sas.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: There are many examples where having a revenue management system has proven to be a strategic advantage. However, revenue management also makes it possible to translate strategic decisions into operational pricing decisions. Today we will discuss some options.
