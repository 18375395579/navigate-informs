Title: Data Association using Fuzzy Knowledge

Author: Cleber Oliveira, DSc, Brazilian Navy, Ilha de Mocangue s/n, Niteroi, 24040-300, Brazil, cleber.almeida@casop.mar.mil.br

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper presents a maritime tactical picture, which was assembled by the association of the reports from surface targets provided by multiple sensors of a Brazilian vessel. The decision-making process conjugates the gating technique and the conceptual structure of decisions in a fuzzy environment to solve the data association problem considering four dimensions.
