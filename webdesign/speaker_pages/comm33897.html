Title: Multi-channel Retailing: The Value of Online Channel
Author: Ashish Agarwal, McCombs School of Business, U T Austin, Austin, TX, 78759, United States of America, ashish.agarwal@mccombs.utexas.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a panel of publicly traded US dual channel retailers and evaluate how their online and offline business impacts their Tobin’s q. We find that the market only values the offline growth and not the online growth for dual channel retailers. We also find that several online features help offline sales which justifies the value of online channel for a multi-channel retailer.
