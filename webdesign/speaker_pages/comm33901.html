Title: Measuring Forecast Precision in RM Applications, or “How Do I Know This Stuff is Working??

Author: Edward Kambour, Director of Science and Research, PROS Revenue Management, 3100 Main Street, Suite 900, Houston, TX, 77002, United States, ekambour@prosrm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: It is clearly important to deliver quality forecasts. However, in practice the process of measuring forecast effectiveness tends to be problematic, even though to the lay person it seems simple: just compare the forecasts to actual observations. In this presentation we will examine methods and metrics, and also touch on the pitfalls associated with the commonly used “common sense?approaches.
