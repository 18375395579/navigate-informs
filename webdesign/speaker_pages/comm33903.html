Title: Modeling Multi-echelon Inventories for Naval Repair Parts in the ROK Navy
Author: Sung-Pil Kim, Yonsei University / R.O.K Navy, Yonseiro 50, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, senseksp@gmail.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: In comparison to the rapid modernization of defense equipment, government resources are restricted, necessitating an efficient solution for managing all national resources, including naval repair parts.This paper investigates the modeling of efficient multi-echelon inventory management of naval repair parts in the ROK Navy.
