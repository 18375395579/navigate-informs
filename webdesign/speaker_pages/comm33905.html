Title: Peer-induced Fairness and Pricing
Author: Xuanming Su, Haas School of Business, University of California, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, xuanming@haas.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Teck Ho
Year: 2008
Abstract: We introduce the concept of peer-induced fairness because people have a drive to make social comparisons with their peers. We theoretically analyze and experimentally validate our model. We relate our findings to existing models of fairness and discuss implications for pricing.
