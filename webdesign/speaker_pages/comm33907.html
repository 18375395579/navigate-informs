Title: Green Logistics in the Batch Process Industry: An Integrated Model

Author: Nagham El-Berishy, PhD Student, Universität Bremen, Intl. Grad. School for Dynamic Logistic, c/o BIBA, room no. 0540, Bremen, 28359, Germany, elb@biba.uni-bremen.de

Coauthor(s): Bernd Scholz-Reiter

Year: 2013

Abstract: Green logistics within the batch process industry is a promising up-to-date theme that enables sustainability and efficiency in supply chains. Optimizing production and transportation processes by considering green aspects are the main objectives of our research. This paper formulates a mathematical model which is capable to present the related issues in batch industry. Model results show that the total related cost can be minimized. These results have been verified for several problem sizes.
