Title: Recommendations of the INFORMS Computing Society Education Committee
Author: Jill Hardin, Virginia Commonwealth University, 1001 W. Main St., PO Box 843083, Richmond, VA, 23284-3083, jrhardin@vcu.edu
Coauthor(s): David Rader, Cesar Rego, Chris Beck, Kevin Furman, Allen Holder, Arthur Hanna
Year: 2008
Abstract: At its January 2007 business meeting, the INFORMS Computing Society proposed forming an Education Committee. This committee has been charged with recommending a model undergraduate curriculum which would produce graduates who are well prepared for work at the OR/CS interface. The committee's current findings will be presented, along with recommendations and rationale, followed by a panel discussion with the members of the committee.
