Title: Experiences Teaching Spreadsheet Modeling & Optimization in Different Cultures

Author: Jonathan P. Caulkins, Professor, Carnegie Mellon Heinz School, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, caulkins@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Is managerial spreadsheet modeling & optimization a universal skill ?akin to algebra or calculus ?or something best taught in a “culturally contextualized?manner and, if the latter, how so? I will reflect on but not answer this question based on experiences teaching on four continents. Audience input welcome!
