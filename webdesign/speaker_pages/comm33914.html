Title: Scheduling Major League Baseball Umpires
Author: Tallys Yunes, University of Miami, Department of Management Science, Coral Gables, FL, 33124-8237, United States, tallys@miami.edu
Coauthor(s): Michael Trick, Heather Barringer, Jonathan Levine
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this talk we address the problem of constructing schedules for MLB umpire crews. We will discuss a few heuristic approaches for this problem and present computational results on real data. Our work created the 2006 MLB umpire schedule.
