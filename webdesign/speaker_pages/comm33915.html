Title: Performance of Commercial and Noncommercial Optimization Software
Author: Hans Mittelmann, Arizona State University, School of Math and Stats, Box 871804, Tempe, AZ, 85287-1804, United States of America, MITTELMANN@asu.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We will report on selected results from our ongoing benchmarking effort. Discrete benchmarks are based on MIPLIB2010 and own selections. Continuous benchmarks include LP, QP, QCQP, SOCP, and SDP problems and solvers.
