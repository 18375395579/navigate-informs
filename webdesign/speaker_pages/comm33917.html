Title: A Week/Month in the Life of an OR Practitioner

Author: Warren Lieberman, Veritec Solutions, warren@veritecsolutions.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: So, what’s it like to depend on your wits (and OR skills) to make a living? In this exciting presentation, Warren Lieberman will reveal what his last 20 years have been like, ranging from his early days in the Operations Research Group at American Airlines to taking on responsibility for running a small consulting and software development firm. Come early for a good seat. You won’t want to miss this!
