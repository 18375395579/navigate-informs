Title: Balancing Protection from Terrorism and Natural Disasters
Author: Jun Zhuang, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 3155 Engineering Centers Building, 1550 Engineering Drive, Madison, WI, 53706-1572, United States, jzhuang@wisc.edu
Coauthor(s): Vicki Bier
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this paper, we apply game theory to identify equilibrium strategies for both attacker and defender in a fully endogenous model of resource allocation for countering terrorism and natural disasters. Our results provide insights into optimal defensive strategies in a post-9/11 and post-Katrina world.
