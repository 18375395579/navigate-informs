Title: Flexibility and Fragility in Supply Chain Network Design
Author: Michael Lim, Northwestern University, Department of IE/MS, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States, m-lim2@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Mark Daskin, Achal Bassamboo, Sunil Chopra
Year: 2007
Abstract: We examine the impact of disruptions on alternative chained manufacturing/product configuration. We introduce fragility to quantify the change in system performance resulting from a disruption. Simulation insights for both node and link failures are discussed as is a decomposition scheme for systems with multiple disruptions.
