Title: Global Product Development Processes
Author: Steven Eppinger, Professor and Deputy Dean, MIT Sloan School of Management, 50 Memorial Drive, Cambridge, MA, 02142, United States, eppinger@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Anshuman Tripathy
Year: 2006
Abstract: This research explores various modes of implementing global product development, including the outsourcing and offshoring of engineering services. We have observed two distinct ways to distribute work: process decomposition and component decomposition. Through several case studies, we have identified particular challenges for coordinating the product development process deriving from each type of work decomposition.
