Title: Saul Gass as Writer, Expositor, and Co-author
Author: Arjang Assad, Dean and Professor, University at Buffalo, State University of New York, 160 Jacobs Managemt Center, Buffalo, NY, 14260-4000, United States of America, aassad@buffalo.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Saul Gass was a prolific writer and an award-wining expositor of OR. This talk will review Saul's various contributions to the history, traditions, and teaching of OR. I will also describe my experience of working with Saul on the history of OR resulting in two books: Annotated Timeline of OR (2004) and Profiles in Operations Research: Pioneers and Innovations (2011).
