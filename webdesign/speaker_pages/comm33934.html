Title: Determinants of Voluntary Disclosure of Quality of Care Information among California Hospitals

Author: Jiban Khuntia, PhD Student, Information Systems, Fellow, CHIDS, Robert H. Smith School of Business, 4360, Van Munching Hall, University of Maryalnd, College Park, MD, 20783, United States of America, jiban@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Guodong(Gordon) Gao, Corey Angst, Ritu Agarwal

Year: 2008

Abstract: This study investigates a hospital’s decision to participate in voluntary quality information disclosure initiatives, by modeling the participation decision as function of financial, institutional, quality, technological and market factors. The modeled 60% California hospitals participating in the CHART initiative exhibited higher quality and better financial performance than non-participating hospitals.
