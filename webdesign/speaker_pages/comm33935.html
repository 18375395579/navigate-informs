Title: Network Theoretic Characterization of Corporate Structures
Author: Hao Fu, Vassar College, 124 Raymond Avenue, Box 2555, Poughkeepsie, NY, 12604, United States of America, markfu1022@gmail.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: It is unclear whether traditionally hierarchal corporate structure meets today’s demand for innovation. This research provides a framework for comparing different corporate structures using network theoretic characterizations. Some prevalent social networks are examined and compared to hierarchical structure both analytically and numerically. The result shows structures resembling social networks had more advantage in innovation. A general algorithm is provided to design desirable structures.
