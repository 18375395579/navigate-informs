Title: Digital Video Technology to Reduce Communication Apprehension in Undergraduate Business Students
Author: Elke Leeds, Asst Professor of MIS, Coles College of Business, Kennesaw State University, 1000 Chastain Rd, Bulding #4 - Rm 234, Kennesaw, GA, 30144, United States of America, eleeds@kennesaw.edu
Coauthor(s): Maurer Ruth
Year: 2007
Abstract: Communication apprehension (CA) is a negative factor in oral presentation. We introduce DV to substitute for in-class oral presentation in an undergraduate information systems course as a way of reducing the negative effects of CA. Faculty and student perceptions of the DV treatment suggest use in high enrollment classes.
