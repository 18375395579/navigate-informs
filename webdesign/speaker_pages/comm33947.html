Title: Mobile Commerce in the New Tablet Economy

Author: Sang Pil Han, Assistant Professor, City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Ave, Kowloon, Hong Kong - PRC, sangphan@cityu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Anindya Ghose, Kaiquan Xu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper quantifies the economic contribution of tablets in ecommerce and m-commerce markets. We use an archival data from the largest online and mobile ecommerce website in China and exploit a quasi-natural experiment. Results show that the introduction of the tablet enhances the overall growth of ecommerce markets with its annualized impact of US$4.88 billion. The tablet channel is a substitute for the existing PC channel and a complement for the smartphone channel.
