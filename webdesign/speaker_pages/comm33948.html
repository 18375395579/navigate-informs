Title: Canal Line Consideration for the Thai Canal

Author: Dr. Satapon Keovimol, Associate Professor, King Mongkut’s Institute of Technology, North Bangkok, 101 Satapon Mansion, Raumchid Rd., Dusit, Bangkok, 10300, Thailand, keovimol@hotmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: If Thailand builds a Thai Canal, Thailand will become an important centre for international sea transport, it would be considering the best canal line, and the aspects of consideration are 1.Freedom in managing the canal 2.National economy 3.Military Strategic reasons 4.Social & Environmental aspects and 5.Engineering aspects.
