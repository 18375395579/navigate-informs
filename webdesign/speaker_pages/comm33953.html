Title: Social Innovation Models in Digital Entertainment

Author: Reina Yahya Arakji, CUNY - Baruch College, One Bernard Baruch Way, New York, NY, United States of America, Reina_Arakji@baruch.cuny.edu

Coauthor(s): Karl Reiner Lang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We examine new forms of organization for innovation that are emerging in the digital entertainment space. Taking advantage of Web 2.0 technologies, firms are successfully engaging in outsourcing parts of their digital content design and development to digital consumer networks. Applying economic analysis, we explore the potential risks and benefits to both producers and consumers and propose innovation organization models for the video-game, music and moving image industries.
