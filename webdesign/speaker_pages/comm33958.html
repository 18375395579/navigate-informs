Title: A Comparison of Classical Wavelets with Diffusion Wavelets

Author: Rajesh Ganesan, Asst. Professor, George Mason University, 4400 Univ. Dr. MS 4A6, Fairfax, VA, 22030, United States of America, rganesan@gmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Poornima Balakrishna

Year: 2008

Abstract: This talk presents the analogies and differences between classical and diffusion wavelets. A case study to decompose multidimensional data is also presented.
