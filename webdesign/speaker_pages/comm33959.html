Title: Ford Motor Company Transforms Its Business using OR and Analytics

Author: Erica Klampfl, Technical Leader, Ford Motor Company, 2101 Village Road, Dearborn, MI, United States of America, eklampfl@ford.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Ford was awarded the INFORMS Prize for extensive and sustained use of operations research (OR) and analytics throughout its business. We will describe the importance of OR and analytics to Ford’s past, present, and future and how OR helped during our transformation in a competitive environment, a crashing economy, and fast-changing market conditions. We will provide an overview of OR and analytics at Ford and highlight some key projects that have transformed our business.
