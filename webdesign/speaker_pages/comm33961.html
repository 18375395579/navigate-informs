Title: The Future of Spreadsheet Modeling: A Presentation and Facilitated Discussion

Author: Jeffrey Keisler, Assoc. Professor, Univ of Massachusetts Boston, 100 Morrissey Blvd, Boston, MA, 02125, United States of America, Jeff.Keisler@umb.edu

Coauthor(s): Roger Blake, Patrick Noonan

Year: 2008

Abstract: What could spreadsheet modelers do, what would they like to do, and what will they do in the future? We will speculate on how spreadsheet modeling may change with changes in technological capabilities, user capabilities, user needs and user generated ideas. In the near term, some concepts that have already been demonstrated will become mainstream. To think about the long term, we need to think broadly about the concept of the spreadsheet.
