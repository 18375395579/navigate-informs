Title: Global Carbon Markets and Implications for Forestry
Author: Darek Nalle, Assistant Professor, University of Idaho, Dept. of Forest Products, Moscow, ID, 83844, United States, nalle@uidaho.edu
Coauthor(s): Miguel Henry
Year: 2007
Abstract: The new commodity of carbon may be difficult to value in that its contract prices are largely a function of evolving regulations within separate market places about the globe. Opportunities and challenges in maximizing NPV from a carbon portfolio will be highlighted, using the University of Idaho's membership in the Chicago Climate Exchange as a specific example.
