Title: Analysis of Water Losses: A Group Decision Sorting Approach
Author: Danielle Morais, Assistant Professor, Federal University of Pernambuco, Rua Conselheiro Portela, 169/701, Recife, 52020-030, Brazil, daniellemorais@yahoo.com.br
Coauthor(s): Jos?Figueira, Adiel Almeida
Year: 2013
Abstract: The immediate consequence of an inadequate maintenance plan of water supply systems is a high index of water losses. In these kind of problems is frequent to consider opinions of different managers from Water Companies. This study proposes a group decision sorting multicriteria model, based on SMAA TRI method, for categorizing the network in zones where losses are intense and thus focus the managers?effort on the most critical regions.
