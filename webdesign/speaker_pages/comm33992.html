Title: Two-Dimensional Barrier and Partial Barrier Options

Author: Steve Kou, Associate Professor, Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, Columbia University, 312 S.W. Mudd, New York, NY, 10027, United States, sk75@columbia.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk we shall give an overview of two recent papers by Cao and Kou (2006) and Huang and Kou (2006). The first one is on pricing discrete two-dimensional barrier and partial barrier options. The second one on pricing continuous two-dimensional barrier options with jump riks.
