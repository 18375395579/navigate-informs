Title: An Ortho-Normalized Continuous GRASP

Author: Michael Hirsch, Raytheon Company, Orlando, FL, United States of America, mjh8787@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Daniel Schroeder

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this research, we discuss recent extensions to Continuous GRASP, a heuristic for continuous global optimization. We test these extensions on numerous standard test functions, as well as some difficult real-world optimization problems.
