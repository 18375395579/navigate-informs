Title: Firm Value of Product Innovation in Service Ecosystems: A Study of Smartphones
Author: Rahul Basole, Associate Director and Senior Research Scientist, Tennenbaum Institute, Georgia Institute of Technology, 75 Fifth Street NW, Suite 600, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, rahul.basole@ti.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Hyunwoo Park
Year: 2012
Abstract: We examine the asymmetric impact of product innovation on the market value of manufacturers (e.g. device manufacturers) and service providers (e.g. mobile network operators) in the mobile service ecosystem. Our analysis is based on a comprehensive dataset comprising over 1,500 smartphones and considers the influence of product characteristics, the frequency and timing of product innovation, and the network structure of the service ecosystem. Theoretical and managerial implications are discussed.
