Title: Timing Managerial Hires for New Venture Expansion

Author: John Angelis, Case Western Reserve University, 2680 Euclid Heights Blvd., #107E, Cleveland, OH, 44106, United States of America, john.angelis@case.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: As entrepreneurs develop their new business ventures, they typically face a larger pool of qualified managerial candidates to help them successfully grow these ventures. But delaying top management hiring limits the speed at which these ventures can expand. We investigate this timing-of-hiring and talent-availability tradeoff and offer recommendations as to when and who entrepreneurs should hire.
