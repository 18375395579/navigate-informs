Title: Sales Team Effort Allocation Analytics
Author: Ban Kawas, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, bkawas@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Dharmashankar Subramanian, Mark Squillante, Kush Varshney
Year: 2013
Abstract: One of the major sources of expense of a company is its salesforce. In this work, we ask the question of how incremental dollars of sales expense should be invested to maximize revenue. In particular, we develop a predictive analytics solution for allocating effort to sales opportunities based on client attributes, seller attributes, and business constraints.
