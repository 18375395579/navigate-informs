Title: Pat Rivett (1923-2005); British OR Pioneer

Author: Graham K. Rand, Senior Lecturer, Department of Management Science, Lancaster University Management School, Lancaster, LA1 4YX, United Kingdom, g.rand@lancaster.ac.uk

Year: 2006

Abstract: Pat Rivett was first secretary of the OR Society, built up the OR group at the National Coal Board, and founded the first Department of OR in the UK, at Lancaster. He played a leading role in the first International Conference in OR, leading to the creation of IFORS. Using his outstanding communication skills he persuaded private and public sector bodies to apply OR. The nature of OR in Britain, an emphasis on application and a society with a majority of practitioner members, is his legacy.
