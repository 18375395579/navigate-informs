Title: Development of a Feasibility Model for a High Speed Rail Line

Author: Reinaldo C. Garcia, Associate Professor, Universidade de Brasilia (UnB), Industrial Engineering Department, Faculty of Technology, Brasilia, DF, 70910-900, Brazil, rcgar@yahoo.com

Coauthor(s): Aline Goulart

Year: 2013

Abstract: We have developed a model to determine the feasibility of a High-Speed Rail (HSR) line. Issues including the uncertainty of the demands and price elasticity are addressed, and the model is implemented for the HSR to be built in Brazil, connecting the cities of São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro.
