Title: Funding Opportunites at NSF and Proposal Writing Tips

Author: Cerry Klein, Program Officer, NSF, 4201 Wilson Blvd, Arlington, VA, 22230, United States of America, cklein@nsf.gov

Year: 2008

Abstract: Service, manufacturing and operations research are key areas essential to the continued growth of our economy. This presentation will discuss the efforts and funding opportunities at the National Science Foundation in these areas, give hints on proposal writing and allow for an open discussion related to the future of these areas.
