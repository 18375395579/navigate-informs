Title: Service Modularization Calls for Special Roles

Author: Markku Kuula, Professor, Aalto University School of Business, POB 11000, 00076 Aalto, Helsinki, Finland, markku.kuula@aalto.fi

Coauthor(s): Antero Putkiranta, Pirjo Tulokas

Year: 2013

Abstract: The purpose of this paper is to examine how public services can be split into modules and, if is it possible to identify different roles for these modules. As research framework ‘the strategic role of the plant?model by Ferdows (1997) is used. This study is a single case study using one case in the public sector. ‘Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA)?was selected to the case organization, because it is currently developing its services.
