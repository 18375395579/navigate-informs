Title: Time is Money in Trading: Rapid Development of Algorithms Using Excel

Author: Andy Kumiega, Illinois Institute of Technology, akumiega@stuart.iit.edu

Coauthor(s): Ben Van Vliet

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation will discuss a spreadsheet development methodology for quantitative analysts and traders in financial markets. The methodology specifically applies to the development real-time trading tools, and its repetitive design structure allowed a market making firm to quickly develop optimization systems for trading complex products. More generally, the methodology illustrates how to take a mathematical algorithm from initial concept, to working prototype in Excel, to working systems in a C++ client/server environment. The rapid development techniques reviewed will enable firms to quickly derive revenue by implementing new algorithms.
