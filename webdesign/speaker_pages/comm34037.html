Title: The Sick Supply Chain: When the Pandemic Hits

Author: Yossi Sheffi, Professor of Engineering Systems, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 1 Amherst Street, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, sheffi@MIT.EDU

Year: 2006

Abstract: How will supply chains be affected by an pandemic outbreak? The US is now more dependent on Chinese manufacturing than on Mideast oil! Which products will be affected by an outbreak in that region of the world? How will US retailers, manufacturers and distributors react? We will discuss these and related questions.
