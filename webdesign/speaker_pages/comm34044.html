Title: NextGen High Density Operations Case Study
Author: Shahab Hasan, Program Manager, LMI, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, 22102, United States of America, SHASAN@lmi.org
Coauthor(s): Gregory Carr, Yuri Gawdiak
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present the results of a case study of NextGen High Density Operations. The purpose of the case study was to exercise a portfolio process, test coordination and integration across JPDO divisions and FAA, test workflow integration, and identify lessons, gaps, and requirements.
