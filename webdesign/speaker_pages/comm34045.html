Title: Motivating and Framing “Analytics? A Fusion of Decision and Information Analysis
Author: Patrick Noonan, Professor & Associate Dean, Emory University, Goizueta Business School, 1300 Clifton Road NE, Atlanta, GA, 30322, United States of America, patrick.noonan@emory.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: When teaching analytics at a core, introductory level, there is no need to reinvent the wheel. With a well-crafted integrating framework that puts decision-making at the forefront, some topics from traditional statistics, decision analysis and OR/MS can be combined seamlessly to provide a stronger, more intuitive foundation than either topic in isolation.
