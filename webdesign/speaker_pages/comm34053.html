Title: OR and Strategy

Author: Peter Bell, Professor, Management Science and Information Systems, Richard Ivey School of Business, University of Western Ontario, London, ON, N6A 3K7, Canada, PBell@ivey.uwo.ca

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation presents results from a longitudinal study of Edelman Prize finalist applications from both the corporate and not-for-profit sectors and suggests a number of different ways that OR work can be linked to an organization's strategy.
