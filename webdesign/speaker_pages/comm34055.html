Title: Econometric Analysis of Effect of Re-admission Rates on Marginal Costs in Hospital Services
Author: Sriram Venkataraman, Doctoral Student, Clemson University, 100 Sirrine Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634-130, United States of America, svenkat@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Lawrence Fredendall, Aleda Roth
Year: 2012
Abstract: We investigate the effect of re-admission rates on marginal cost incurred by a hospital. We use secondary data derived from multiple sources, including Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services. We apply an inversion method and demand-supply-side estimation procedures developed in the empirical Industrial Organization literature to estimate marginal cost of a hospital.
