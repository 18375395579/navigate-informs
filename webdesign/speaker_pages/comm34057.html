Title: Obtaining Mutual Fund Performance Measures From Fund Networks
Author: Anton Braverman, Cornell University, 289 Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, United States of America, ab2329@cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Andreea Minca
Year: 2013
Abstract: Using a network of mutual funds, we examine several mutual fund performance measures that capture the systemic risk a fund faces from other funds with common asset holdings.
