Title: The Art of Teaching Cases Using Spreadsheets

Author: Srini Krishnamoorthy, Assistant Professor, Ivey School of Business, skrishnamoorthy@ivey.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk will focus on methods of integrating spreadsheets for teaching cases. These include techniques for involving students in the process of building and using models for resolving real life business problems. In addition, specific tips will be offered on managing classroom dynamics.
