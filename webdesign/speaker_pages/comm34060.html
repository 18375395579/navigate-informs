Title: ILOG Optimization Decision Management System (ODMS) in the Classroom
Author: Rosemary Berger, ILOG, Inc, 1195 West Fremont Avenue, Sunnyvale, CA, 94087, United States, rosemary.berger@verizon.net
Coauthor(s): Thomas Dong
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this talk, we describe the opportunities and resources available for incorporating ILOG software into undergraduate and graduate courses in optimization. We highlight the OPL-CPLEX Analyst Studio and the ILOG Optimization Decision Manager (ODM), which make it easy for students to develop, debug and tune optimization models and then create decision support applications.
