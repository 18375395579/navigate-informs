Title: Enhancing Adolescents?Lives through Decision-making Skills
Author: Ali Abbas, Associate Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Ave, Urbana, IL, 61822, United States of America, aliabbas@illinois.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: On the path to a successful career and happy personal life, adolescents face many complex decisions that significantly influence their lives. Improving their decision-making skills can have a positive impact with wider benefits across society. We discuss our learning and teaching experiences with children, adolescents and young adults and spotlight the questions of 'How can we professionally support adolescents improve their individual decision-making competence?'.
