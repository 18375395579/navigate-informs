Title: Implementing Real-time Pricing in Wholesale Electricity Markets
Author: Jingjie Xiao, Purdue, Grant St., APT 16, West Lafayette, IN, 47906-3682, United States of America, jingjie.xiao@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Andrew Liu
Year: 2013
Abstract: A key hurdle for implementing real-time pricing of electricity is the lack of consumers?response. Solutions include energy management systems that can automatically optimize household appliance usage such as EV/PEV charge (and discharge with V2G) via two-way communication with the grid. This talk will present how real-time retail pricing, aided by control automation devices, can be integrated into wholesale electricity markets under various uncertainties through approximate dynamic programming.
