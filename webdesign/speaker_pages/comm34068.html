Title: Aiding Washinton Policy Makers with Analytic Tools

Author: Rex Brown, Distinguished Senior Fellow, School of Public Policy, GMU, 2018 Lakebreeze Way, Reston, VA, 20191, United States of America, Rexvbrown@aol.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: A consultant’s perspective and lessons learned from helping government executives to make and defend high level environmental, energy and other decisions. Analytic, political, administrative, legal and private considerations.
