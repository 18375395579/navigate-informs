Title: Can Daily-deal E-coupon Sites Survive? An Empirical Investigation of Key Factors

Author: Gang Wang, Phd Student, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside road, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, gang.wang@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Bill Ross, Xue Bai, James Marsden

Year: 2013

Abstract: While daily-deal websites appear to be gaining prominence, analysts and academic researchers continue to question their long term viability. To date, little analysis has focused on the existing business model or on identifying a business model that would enable long term viability. In our empirical analysis, we investigated key relationships that must be taken into account in developing a business model of the industry. The study offers a variety of managerial insights for industry members.
