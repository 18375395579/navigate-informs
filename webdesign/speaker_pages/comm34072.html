Title: Spreadsheet Formula Generation

Author: Raymond Payette, Retired, 107-3825 ave des Compagnons, Quebec, QC, G1X 5E4, Canada, raypayette@hotmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Spreadsheets are intelligent and clever. They can detect many input errors and propose solutions. They can monitor data input from a text, a database or the Internet and trigger a specified action. They can generate the appropriate formulas when a specific term is entered; accordingly terms used in formulas should be standardized; if the user enters the word “Total?at the top of a column, the spreadsheet would enter the correct formula in the proper cells without the user’s intervention.
