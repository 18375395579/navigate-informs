Title: Aiming for a Successful Experience
Author: Richard Burton, Duke University, Fuqua School of Business, 1 Towerview Drive, Durham, NC, 27708, rmb2@mail.duke.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: The session will discuss how to increase the likelihood of a successful experience when submitting a manuscript to Organization Science. Characteristics of manuscripts that editors and reviewers regard positively will be identified. Common shortcomings of manuscripts will be described. How to respond successfully to editor's and reviewers' comments will be discussed.
