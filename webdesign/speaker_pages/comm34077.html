Title: Integrating Communication in an Industry Sponsored Capstone Course

Author: Anita Vila-Parrish, North Carolina State University, Campus Box 7906, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, arvila@ncsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Julie S. Ivy, Sarah Egan Warren

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk will be focused on threading communication tools throughout an industrial engineering capstone course. In this semester-long course, projects are industry sponsored which require students to communicate to different audiences (internal and external). The course leverages industry standard templates and project management methods to facilitate communication from concept to implementation phase.
