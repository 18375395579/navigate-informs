Title: Analysis of NextGen and New Vehicle Types

Author: Dr. Frederick Wieland, Senior Research Engineer--ATM Systems, Sensis Corporation, 600 Maryland Ave SW, Washington, DC, 20024, United States of America, fwieland@sensis.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: This talk (and the rest of the papers in this session) will focus on the impact of new vehicle types on the NextGen air transportation system, focusing on the 2025-2040 time frame. The new vehicle types to be discussed include commercial supersonic transports, very low-cost jets, cruise efficient short takeoff and landing vehicles, rotorcraft, and unmanned aerial systems. Impacts in the performance, environment, and safety areas will be discussed.
