Title: New Wine in Old Bottles? Print-newspapers?Overlooked Role in Digital Media Diffusion

Author: Bo Kyung Kim, Assistant Professor, Southern Methodist University, Cox School of Business, 6212 Bishop Boulevard 323 Fincher, Dallas, TX, 75205-0006, United States of America, kimb@smu.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: During the implementation process of discontinuous technology, incumbents can emphasize the similarity between new and current technologies to have better adoption performance by making their current assets more valuable in the new market. In the U.S. daily newspaper industry, incumbent newspapers that deemphasized interactivity on the web, the most distinctive character of digital media from print media, indeed had better online readership, potentially creating “print-media-like?digital media.
