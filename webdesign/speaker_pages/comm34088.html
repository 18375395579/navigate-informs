Title: An Operations-based Model for Predicting Bankruptcy Risk in Retailing

Author: Vishal Gaur, Professor of Operations Management, Johnson School, Cornell University, 321 Sage Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, vg77@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Yasin Alan

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate whether historical operational performance can be a predictor of probability of bankruptcy for U.S. retailers. Focusing on large retail bankruptcies in U.S., we show that inventory productivity and associated operational variables are predictive of bankruptcy probability and improve accuracy of existing bankruptcy prediction models.
