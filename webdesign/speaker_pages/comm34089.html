Title: Extracting Actionable Intelligence from Social Media Streams

Author: Chase Murray, Auburn University, Industrial & Systems Engineering, Auburn, AL, United States of America, ccm0022@auburn.edu

Coauthor(s): Justin Yates

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk explores strategies for collecting, filtering, and (hopefully) making sense of data broadcast through social media streams (e.g., Twitter and Facebook). Such streams may provide early notice of critical homeland security-related events, enabling faster response to threats. We provide preliminary results from our efforts to incorporate information from embedded URLs and demonstrate the use of natural language processing to infer context from the data.
