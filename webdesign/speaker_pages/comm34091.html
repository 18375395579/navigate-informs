Title: Evaluation of Immobilization Alternatives for Eurex Process Waste Stored in Saluggia, Italy
Author: Alan Brothers, Senior Research Scientist, Battelle Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, 902 Battelle Boulevard, P.O. Box 999 MSIN: K6-08, Richland, WA, 99352, United States, alan.brothers@pnl.gov
Coauthor(s): Shas Mattigod, Denis Strachan, Gordon Beeman
Year: 2007
Abstract: Using technical descriptions of alternative performance, Sicieta Gestione Impianti Nucleari (Sogin) staff evaluated three alternatives to treat and immobilize radioactive Saluggia nuclear waste. Battelle staff mapped technical performance to a broader set of decision objectives and used a modified SMART decision process without explicitly defining measurement scales and value functions. Through this process, the Battelle staff verified the selection made by the Sogin staff.
