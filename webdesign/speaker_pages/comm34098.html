Title: Alan S. Manne: Energy Vulnerability
Author: Hung Po Chao, Director of Market Monitoring, Independent Systems Operator, New England, One Sullivan Road, Holyoke, MA, 01040, United States, hchao@iso-ne.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: Alan Manne made many fundamental contributions to energy policy issues. His work characteristically offers simple and yet profound insights. This talk highlights his prescient insights of energy vulnerability in a modern economy characterized by complex interactions, interdependence and uncertainties, a common theme that can be found in many of his path-breaking works.
