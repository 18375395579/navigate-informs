Title: The Impact of Monetary and Non-monetary Incentives in Platform-based Innovation Contests
Author: Annika Mueller, Harvard University, Boston, MA, United States of America, amueller@fas.harvard.edu
Coauthor(s): Karim Lakhani, Kevin Boudreau
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this study we examine the effect of pecuniary and non-pecuniary incentives on the participation, effort and productivity of individuals on an open crowdsourcing contest platform. Specifically, we conduct a unique field-experiment in which subjects are randomly allocated to nine treatment groups to investigate the causal impact of various combinations and levels of money, status and job market signalling as prizes on the participation and performance of coders in an online programming contest.
