Title: An Empirical Investigation of Environmental Announcements and the Market Value of the Firm

Author: Brian Jacobs, Georgia Tech, 800 W. Peachtree NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, brian.jacobs@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Vinod Singhal, Ravi Subramanian

Year: 2008

Abstract: We categorize press announcements related to positive environmental performance into two major types -- awards & certifications, and initiatives. We use event study methodology to estimate the average abnormal stock returns. We find that although the stock market does not react to announcements in such broad categorizations, it does react to certain specific announcement types such as ISO14001 certification, voluntary emission reductions, and environmental philanthropy.
