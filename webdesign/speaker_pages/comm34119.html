Title: Integrated Design of Order Picking and Sortation Systems
Author: Fahrettin Eldemir, Assistant Professor, Yildiz Technical University, Industrial Engineering Dept, Yildiz, Besiktas, Istanbul, Turkey, feldemir@fatih.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Recep Kizilaslan, Elif Karakaya
Year: 2012
Abstract: Order picking and order sorting are the two main operations that are mostly encountered within a warehouse. The research in the literature generally addresses the operational issues considering the both system as independent systems. In this study a performance evaluation model is suggested for the integration of the Order Picking Systems and Order Sortation Systems. Detailed aspects of design and operational parameters are addressed.
