Title: The Facets of the Tree Partition Problem

Author: Sangho Shim, Research Associate, Kellogg School of Management/Northwestern University, 2001 Sheridan Road Suite 548, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, shim@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Dabeen Lee, Sunil Chopra

Year: 2013

Abstract: We identify the facets and a minimal representation of the tree partition problem. We develop a linear time separation algorithm to identify the most violated one among the exponentially many facets.
