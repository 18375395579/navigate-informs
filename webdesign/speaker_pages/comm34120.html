Title: The Role of Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis in Helping the Committee on Radioactive Waste

Author: Lawrence Phillips, Professor, London School of Economics & Political Science, Department of Operational Research, Houghton Street, London, NW3 1AH, United Kingdom, larry_phillips@msn.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper reports on the largest public consultation exercise ever undertaken in the United Kingdom, with emphasis on the role of multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA) in helping the Committee on Radioactive Waste Management (CoRWM) to recommend how the country's accumulating high-level and medium-level radioactive waste should be managed.
