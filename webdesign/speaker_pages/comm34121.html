Title: An Empirical Investigation of the B2B e-Service Chain
Author: Pedro Oliveira, School of Economics & Management , Catholic University of Portuga,, Palma de Cima, Lisboa, Portugal, poliveira@fcee.upc.pt
Coauthor(s): Aleda Roth
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper addresses the e-service capability in a B2B environment. Empirical results indicated that companies with a service orientation are more apt to have better performance.
