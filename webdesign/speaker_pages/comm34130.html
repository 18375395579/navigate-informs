Title: Performance Improvement for Emergency Medical Services via Systems Thinking and GIS
Author: Jiun-Yu Yu, Department of Business Administration, No. 1, Section 4, Roosevelt Road, Da-An, Taipei, 106, Taiwan - ROC, jyyu@ntu.edu.tw
Year: 2013
Abstract: Emergency Medical Services refers to both patient transport and medical support for people with illnesses or injuries. Most past research about EMS is conducted from clinical view. To verify that the patient’s health benefits can be enhanced from a better designed and implemented EMS process and to identify achievable potentials for performance improvement, this study proposes a model based on tools of OR/MS, operations management,systems thinking, and Geographical Information System.
