Title: Dynamic Pricing of Fashion Goods under Strategic Consumer Behavior
Author: Mike Wei, Washington University in St. Louis, 1 Brookings Dr., Campus Box 1133, St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States of America, weim@wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Yossi Aviv, Fuqiang Zhang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study pricing models of fashion goods under strategic consumer behavior. Using a rigorously developed game theoretical framework, we demonstrate that acquiring the ability to resolve demand uncertainty via early sales observations may potentially be detrimental to revenue performance. We discuss the underlying reasons behind this phenomenon, the conditions under which it prevails, and its practical ramifications. We also discuss the efficacy of a “reward program?as a segmentation mechanism.
