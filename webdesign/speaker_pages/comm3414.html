Title: Influence: Do Birds of a Feather Retweet Together?

Author: Shawndra Hill, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, shawndra@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Adrian Benton

Year: 2013

Abstract: We explore influence by gender across a variety of contexts ?in particular, we focus on influence when talking about brands from TV commercials on Twitter immediately after they have been aired. We find that men are more likely to retweet men and women are more likely to retweet women even after controlling for diversity and size of the original tweeters network. We also find that the type of language explains the pairwise difference in retweeting even further.
