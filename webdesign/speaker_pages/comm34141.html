Title: Project Management Decisions with Uncertain Targets
Author: Jeffrey Keisler, Professor, University of Massachusetts Boston, Boston, MA, United States of America, jeff.keisler@umb.edu
Coauthor(s): Robert Bordley
Year: 2013
Abstract: Many decision making methods in project management center on identifying courses of action that are likely to meet specified targets. In practice, such decisions are much more complicated when targets themselves are subject to change. Using target-oriented utility functions can help handle these complications, as will be shown in several examples.
