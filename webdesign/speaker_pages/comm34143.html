Title: Challenges and Solutions in Designing Mass Customization Strategies for Large Scale Supply Chains: The Medco Story

Author: Art Onge, St. Onge Company, 1400 Williams Road, York, PA, 17402, astonge@stonge.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Medco delivers over two million distinct prescriptions each week through a supply chain that extends thousands of miles. The challenges, including inventory distortions, encountered in developing an automated, error-free prescription preparation and delivery system for MEDCO are identified. Creative solutions deploying mass customization strategies are presented for this remarkable example.
