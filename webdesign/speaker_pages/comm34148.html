Title: Recent Improvements in IBM ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio

Author: Laszlo Ladanyi, CPLEX R&D, IBM, PO Box 800, Lakeville, CT, 06039, United States of America, ladanyi@us.ibm.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present new features that have been added to IBM ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio and give benchmarking results that demonstrate the performance improvements in CPLEX 12.5.
