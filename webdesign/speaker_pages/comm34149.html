Title: Values of Artists under Equilibrium Framework

Author: Jialie Chen, PhD Student, Marketing, Samuel Curtis Johnson Graduate School of Management, Cornell University, 301P Sage Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14850, United States of America, jc2428@cornell.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper estimates music artists?values identified as difference in profits under competitive equilibrium between two scenarios (market and counter-factual), where one artist “loses?value. Using BLP methodology and sales data for 2010-11, we find that Group artists have higher value than female or male solo artists.
