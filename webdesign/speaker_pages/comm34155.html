Title: Branch-and-cut for Piecewise Linear Optimization
Author: Ismael Regis de Farias JR., State University of New York, Dept. of Ind. and Syst. Eng., 403 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, defarias@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): Ming Zhao
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present new inequalities that define facets of the continuous separable piecewise linear polytope. We also present computational results that demonstrate the effectiveness of the inequalities.
