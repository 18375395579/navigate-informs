Title: Panel Discussion: Teaching Experimental Design and Statistical Process Control Methods in IE/OR/Statistics Programs
Author: Jeff C. F. Wu, Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 765 Ferst Drive, Campus Box 0205, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, jeffwu@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Bruce Ankenman, Jye-Chyi (JC) Lu, Russell Barton, George Runger, Yu Ding, Dan Apley
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this session a panel of faculty who teach undergraduate and graduate level courses in design of experiment (DOE) come together to present and exchange information about their courses, in terms of the course design, topics covered, data supplied, and software and text books used. This session is intended for anyone who is currently teaching or hoping to create a course on the subject of DOE.
