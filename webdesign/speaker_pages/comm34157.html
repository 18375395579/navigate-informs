Title: The Impact of Comments on Microblogging

Author: Yingda Lu, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, United States of America, yingdal@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yong Tan, Junjie Wu

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper, we investigate the impact of comments on user behavior on microblogging platform. We illustrate that comments of microbloggs could influence the way users decide whom to follow, whether to post microblogs. The comments can also influence the way new microblogs spread.
