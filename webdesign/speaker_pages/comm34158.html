Title: The use of VRP Solution Distance Measures in various DSS applications
Author: Arne Løkketangen, Professor, Molde University College, Britveien 2, Molde, -, 6411, Norway, Arne.Lokketangen@hiMolde.no
Coauthor(s): Jarl Korsvik, Kjetil Fagerholt, Johan Oppen, David Woodruff
Year: 2008
Abstract: We describe structurally based solution distance measures developed for rich VRP-like problems, to be used in decision support systems. Our measures have been tested on a set of real-life problems from land and sea, and it provides valuable decision support flexibility for the planner.
