Title: Within-cycle monitoring and diagnosis of cyclic nonlinear profile signals

Author: Weihong Guo, PhD Candidate, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Avenue, IOE Building, ANN ARBOR, MI, 48105, United States of America, graceguo@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Judy Jin, S. Jack Hu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Existing research on cyclic profile monitoring is limited to between-cycle detection made for each finished part based on its entire cycle of signals. In manufacturing processes with critical part quality and high cost, however, process changes require detection before the part is produced so that within-cycle adjustments can be triggered. This research develops a monitoring and diagnosis methodology that is capable of making within-cycle decisions and guiding real-time process adjustments.
