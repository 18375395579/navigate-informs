Title: Humanitarian Logistics- Challenges and Opportunities

Author: Prashant Yadav, Professor of Supply Chain Management, MIT-Zaragoza International Logistics Program, Gomez Laguna 25, Zaragoza, AR, 50009, Spain, pyadav@zlc.edu.es

Year: 2008

Abstract: This talk gives an overview of the current challenges in humanitarian logistics and discusses how operations research and management science applications can have a positive impact. Topics include modeling the spread of flu pandemic and preparing for food distribution and pre-positioning inventory for emergency response.
