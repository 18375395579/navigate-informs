Title: Estimating Customer Price Elasticity Curves: Methodologies, Challenges and Solutions

Author: Julia Morrison, Marriott International, MD, Julia.E.Morrison@marriott.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: One of the first steps in Pricing Optimization is defining customer segmentation, and estimation of customer elasticity curves by segment. Application of the traditional theory around these methods in practice presents many challenges: statistically significant segments might not make sense from business perspective; price elasticity curves estimated from the data might not be elastic enough. In our presentation we illustrate these challenges, and discuss some of the methods to address them.
