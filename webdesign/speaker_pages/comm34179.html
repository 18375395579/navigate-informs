Title: Electronic Reverse Auction Project Satisfaction: A Model and Empirical Test

Author: Willem Standaert, Ghent University, Gent, 9000, Belgium, Willem.Standaert@UGent.be

Coauthor(s): Steve Muylle

Year: 2013

Abstract: We develop and empirically test a model that hypothesizes positive relationships between electronic reverse auction (ERA) project satisfaction and different project outcomes. We find that ERA project satisfaction is positively related to financial, operational, and relationship outcomes. Our model and field study also reveal that project team experience and procedural fairness contribute to realizing potentially conflicting price, quality, and relationship outcomes.
