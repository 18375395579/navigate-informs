Title: Privacy Preserving Optimization

Author: Ram Gopal, Professor, University of Connecticut, OPIM Department, School of Business, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States, Ram.Gopal@business.uconn.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We develop protocols that can help participants maximize a joint objective function in an environment where participants do not trust each other. Our protocol can help participants in optimally solving many traditional optimization problems in a privacy preserving manner. We also analyze our protocols against various types of threats including malicious participants.
