Title: Panel Discussion: How to Undertake Collaborative Research
Author: Halit Uster, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Texas A&M University, College Station, TX, United States of America, uster@tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Diego Klabjan, Eva Lee, Jon Lee, Warren Powell
Year: 2012
Abstract: Timely with increasing attention and excitement about collaborative and interdisciplinary research, the panel will discuss the making and the sustainment of successful collaborations among academics as well as between academics and practitioners.
