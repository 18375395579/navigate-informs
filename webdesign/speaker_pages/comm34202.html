Title: Novelty Detection based on Distance and Topological Relation
Author: Sungzoon Cho, Professor, Seoul National University, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, zoon@snu.ac.kr
Coauthor(s): Pilsung Kang
Year: 2008
Abstract: A distance-based novelty detector classifies a new data pattern as novel if its distance to normal patterns is larger than a threshold. It is intuitive, is easy to understand and implement, and fits naturally with incremental leaning. Yet, its performance is limited since it relies on distance only. In this talk, we propose a new distance measure that takes topological relation into consideration. A preliminary experiment is promising.
