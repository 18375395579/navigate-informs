Title: Network Structure, Time and Timing in Online Social Networks

Author: Charles Weber, Associate Professor of Eng & Tech Mgmnt, Portland State University, PO Box 751 ETM, Portland, OR, 97207, United States of America, webercm@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Nitin Mayande

Year: 2012

Abstract: An empirical study of connectivity in online social network has identified a link between network structure and the time and timing of information propagation. The findings of the study suggest that the timing of surges in network performance may be predictable.
