Title: Determinants of Airline's Efficiency: A Comparative Study Over Three Categories in a Panel Data
Author: Maria Cristina Gramani, Insper Institute of Education and Research, Rua Quata, 300, Vila Olimpia, Sao Paulo, SP, 04546-042, Brazil, mariacng@insper.edu.br
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study seeks to investigate the performance of three airline’s categories: regional, low cost and full service. Empirical results are based on a panel data for 13 Brazilian airlines and 3 international airlines within the period from 2005 to 2010. The contribution of this work consists of -put side by side the results of operational and financial performances for airlines in a panel data; -verify the potential improvements for each segment; and -analyze the benchmarking best practices.
