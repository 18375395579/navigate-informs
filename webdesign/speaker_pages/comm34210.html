Title: Learning to Price Airline Seats under Competition

Author: Andrew Collins, PhD Student, School of Management, University of Southampton, University Road, Southampton, SO17 1BJ, United Kingdom, bittelsworth@hotmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: A simple stochastic dynamic airline pricing game has been constructed for comparison of several reinforcement learning method. Both theoretical and empirical results are presented, for the game and learning models. These include some interesting behaviours observed from the computer agents during the learning process. The presentation concludes the research presented at INFORMS 2006 of the same name.
