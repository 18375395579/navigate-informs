Title: Who's On the Line? Indian Call Center Agents Pose as Americans for U.S.-Outsourced Firms

Author: Winifred Poster, poster@uiuc.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Indian agents in international call centers often pose as Americans, sometimes deceptively, while talking on the phone to U.S. customers. Through ethnographic case studies of three Indian call centers, and interviews with 80 call center agents, CEOs, and industry officials, I ask why this “national identity management?has arisen as an organizational strategy, and what the implications are for the actors involved in terms of varying benefits and costs.
