Title: BPM Collaborates with Packaged Enterprise System for Balanced IT System
Author: Qinhua Wang, IBM Research China, Zhongguancun Software Park, Beijing, China, wangqinh@cn.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Qiang Wei, Feng Chen
Year: 2013
Abstract: Enterprises?IT requirements have been evolved from improving efficiency to enabling collaboration and optimization. To fulfill them, business process management (BPM) tool is no longer competitive to packaged enterprise system, but complementary. This talk will introduce the solution how they work together to achieve a balanced IT system: deploying standard business processes in stable enterprise system and agile processes in BPM, and integrating them to achieve performance improvement.
