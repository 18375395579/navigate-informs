Title: Forecasting Outpatient Visits to More Effectively Allocate Clinical Resources
Author: Stephen De Lurgio, Professor, Henry Bloch School of Business, University of Missouri, Kansas City, MO, 64110, United States, delurgios@umkc.edu
Coauthor(s): Arthur Williams, Brian Denton, Rosa Cabanela, Sandra Bruggeman
Year: 2006
Abstract: A system is developed to forecast outpatient visits for 26 divisions of the Mayo Clinic. Univariate, multivariate, and combining methods are found to perform best for 15.4%, 42.3%, and 42.3% of the divisions respectively. Also discussed are improved personnel schedules using a priori determination of division-specific best methods.
