Title: Resilience Building Lessons from Super-storm Sandy

Author: Jose Emmanuel Ramirez-Marquez, Director, Stevens Institute of Technology, 1 Castle Point Terrace, Hoboken, NJ, United States of America, jmarquez@stevens.edu

Coauthor(s): Qing Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: Supper-storm sandy impacted New Jersey early on October 29th severely affecting the state for the following weeks. Throughout the state electric power outages affected the population. In this study, we have compiled data of outages per day to develop resilience curves associated to the response by county. based on these resilience graphics, we present a vulnerability reduction approach that accounts for the economic impact of power outages.
