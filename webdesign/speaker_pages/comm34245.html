Title: The Use of Framing in Inventory Decisions

Author: Ken Schultz, Associate Professor, University of Alberta, 340-G Business School, Edmonton, Ab, T6G 2R6, Canada, klschult@ualberta.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: We investigate the use of “framing?to influence order quantities in inventory decisions. We fail to achieve the expected results based and investigate why. We increase the complexity of the situation to gain insight into behavioral reactions. In the process we discuss both the relevance of the findings and some of the difficulties inherent in behavioral research in operations management.
