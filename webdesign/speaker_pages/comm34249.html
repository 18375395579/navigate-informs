Title: Use of Open Source Software in Teaching Computer Programming to OR/IE Students
Author: Suleyman Karabuk, Assistant Professor, University of Oklahoma, School of Industrial Engineering, 200 West Boyd, Room 124, Norman, OK, 73019, United States, karabuk@ou.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: This talk includes an account of the author's personal experience in teaching a graduate level computer programming course with an OR/IE flavor. The course, including the management software, is based on open source programming languages, software tools and libraries. The author will share his experiences and his answers to the questions of why open source and how it helps to teach OR/IE.
