Title: Making Decisions with Multiple Attributes: A Case in Sustainability Planning
Author: Joe Hahn, Associate Professor, Pepperdine University, Malibu Campus, Malibu, CA, United States of America, Joe.Hahn@pepperdine.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: Decisions involving issues of sustainability tend to include an array of objective attributes along with subjective value judgments. In this paper, we present a Multi-Attribute Decision Analysis model which was developed to assist Pepperdine University with prioritizing a number of competing projects to address a goal of developing a more sustainable energy policy on campus.
