Title: Healthcare Operations Research: Research, Teaching and Service
Author: Mariel Lavieri, Assistant Professor, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave, 2783 IOE, Ann Arbor, MI, 48105, United States of America, lavieri@umich.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: As a 2006 Bonder Scholar, I share my experiences doing research in the field of healthcare operations research and discuss some of my most resent work in the field of medical decision making for chronic disease patients. I will conclude the talk by describing teaching and outreach activities developed to motivate underrepresented students to consider this growing field of research.
