Title: Post-implementation Review of RNAV Standard Terminal Arrival Routes into Washington National Airport
Author: Jonathan Green, Operations Research Analyst, FAA, 800 Indepenence Ave., SW, Washington, DC, United States of America, jonathan.m.green@faa.gov
Year: 2008
Abstract: In an effort to examine the value of Area Navigation (RNAV) Standard Terminal Arrival Routes (STARs), a Post-Implementation Review (PIR) was conducted of Washington National Airport (DCA) using analysis of flight data.
