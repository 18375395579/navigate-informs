Title: Optimal Risk Mitigation Strategies in Project Management

Author: Carlos Raoni Mendes, IBM Research Brazil, Pontifical Catholic University of Rio de Janeiro, Av. Pasteur 138-146, Rio de Janeiro, RJ, 22290240, Brazil, craoni@br.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Bruno Flach

Year: 2013

Abstract: The construction of large power plants entails a huge challenge in terms of project management involving owners, contractors and suppliers. Time, budget and resource constraints coupled with potential risk sources and inter-dependencies create a very complex environment. In this work, we describe the development of optimization models aimed at supporting the decision-making process faced by project managers.
