Title: Merge of Large Number of Service Specific Distributions and Robustness of Sequential Test

Author: Genady Grabarnik, Assistant Professor, St John's University, Dept. Math & CS, 8000 Union, Queens, NY, United States of America, grabarng@stjohns.edu

Coauthor(s): Larisa Shwartz, Yefim Michlin

Year: 2013

Abstract: We trying to explain why the merge of 14 or more service specific distributions resulting distribution is closed to exponential distribution. We use this result to conclude robustness of the sequential test for large group of servers.
