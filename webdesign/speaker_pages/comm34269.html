Title: Corporate Travel Services: A Triadic and Modularity Perspectives
Author: Anu Bask, Postdocoral Researcher, Aalto University, Runeberginkatu 22-24, Helsinki, 00100, Finland, anu.bask@aalto.fi
Coauthor(s): Anne-Maria Holma, Antti Laakso, Dan Andersson
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this study we take the buyer’s perspective and explore how modularity and service triads are used in service development in indirect sourcing context. We provide a case study where the focal service triad consists of a buyer, technology provider and travel management company. To increase understanding of modular service supply chain networks, we develop a framework for modular service architecture. As methodology we apply longitudinal case study and insider action research.
