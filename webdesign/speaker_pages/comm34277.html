Title: Beyond Feedback Scores: Measuring Bidder Migration and Its Price Effects

Author: Mingfeng Lin, University of Maryland, mingfeng@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Wolfgang Jank

Year: 2008

Abstract: We argue that the movement of bidders “migrating?from one auction to the other creates an important linkage among them. Such activities are not captured by the feedback score measures widely used in online auctions. We propose a series of measures of bidder migration, and then empirically examined their price effects using eBay data. We show that bidder migration carries rich information about bidders and can help predict auction outcomes.
