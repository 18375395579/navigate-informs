Title: Bringing Spreadsheet Reality into the Business Analytics Classroom
Author: Ozgur Ozluk, Associate Professor, San Francisco State University, 835 Market Street Suite 589, San Francisco, CA, 94103, United States of America, ozgur@sfsu.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We examine the use of large-scale spreadsheet modeling in different industries, then discuss and present how the learnings can be brought into the classroom where we study business analytics. The talk will have an interactive discussion component.
