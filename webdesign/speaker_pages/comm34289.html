Title: A Testbed for HP’s Dual Channel Policies

Author: Ozalp Ozer, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, United States of America, oozer@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Murat Kaya

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present results from collaboration between Hewlett Packard and Stanford University. We study how HP can manage its dual sales channels: a direct channel, hpshopping.com, alongside an independent retail channel. We determine optimum channel policies for HP under different channel scenarios. To this end, we develop a game-theoretic model and an experimental study. The approach allows HP to test channel policies with human decision makers before implementing it in the market place.
