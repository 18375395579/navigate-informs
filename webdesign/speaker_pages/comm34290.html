Title: An Overview about Journal of Systems Science and Systems Engineering
Author: Jian Chen, Professor, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, jchen@mail.tsinghua.edu.cn
Year: 2013
Abstract: During this presentation, the editor of JSSSE will give an overview of the Journal. He will give some facts about the journal, explain the review process, and point out the topics. Some general questions will be welcome after the presentation.
