Title: A Strategic Tool for Achieving Compatibility between Quality and Supply Chain Drivers in Healthcare Industry
Author: Gangaraju Vanteddu, Southeast Missouri State University, Cape Girardeau, MO, United States of America, gvanteddu@semo.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: With the emphasis shifting to reduce the healthcare delivery costs ,while improving the quality at the same time , it is essential to consider different interfaces between important supply chain cost drivers and the relevant quality drivers in a given context. In this research, a strategic tool is presented for identifying the degree of compatibility between the quality and supply chain drivers to facilitate effective decision making.
