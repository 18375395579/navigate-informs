Title: OR in Public Programs and Processes
Author: John Hall, Assistant Vice President - Fire Analysis & Research, National Fire Protection Association, 1 Batterymarch Park, Quincy, MA, 02169-7471, jhall@NFPA.org
Coauthor(s): Doug Samuelson
Year: 2008
Abstract: The mission of the InfORMS Section on Public Programs and Processes embraces a wide range of national, state, and local government functions, with special emphasis on criminal justice, public safety, homeland security, and the operations involved in government services. Two current officers of the section will give examples of particular relevance to humanitarian applications and to chapter officers looking to find speakers.
