Title: Cross-discipline and Cross-cultural Challenges in Customer-driven Vehicle Development
Author: R. Jean Ruth, General Motors R&D, MC 480-106-256, 30500 Mound Road, Warren, MI, 48090-9055, United States of America, rjean.ruth@gm.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: We focus here on the interplay of design, market research, and engineering in new vehicle development and the additional challenges of understanding consumers in global product development across cultures, languages, countries, infrastructures, and continents: differences in customer preference and needs, in information sources and methods, in mature markets (with a large base of customers familiar with the products) and in fast-growing developing markets of potential customers.
