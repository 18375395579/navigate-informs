Title: Social Media Usage in Disaster Relief Routing

Author: Emre Kirac, PhD Student, University of Arkansas, Department of Industrial Engineering, Fayetteville, AR, 72701, United States of America, ekirac@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Ashlea Bennett Milburn

Year: 2013

Abstract: The information available for disaster planning includes that from sources such as on the ground assessment teams. Data may also be obtained through social media, enabling a more needs to be identified in less time. However, this information is not verified and may be inaccurate. We construct models to assess tradeoffs associated with considering this new information in planning efforts. Alternative decision approaches are presented and compared across wide variety of request scenarios.
