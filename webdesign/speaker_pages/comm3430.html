Title: Modeling the Transmission of Pandemic Influenza

Author: Marc Lipsitch, Associate Professor, Harvard School of Public Health, Department of Epidemiology, 677 Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA, 02115, United States, mlipsitc@hsph.harvard.edu

Coauthor(s): Christina E. Mills

Year: 2006

Abstract: Mathematical/computational epidemiology has been pushed to new frontiers by the effort to prepare for pandemic influenza. Key findings will be presented in the context of what questions remain, with examples including logistics, decision-making, ecological understanding, and mathematical characterization of the transmission system.
