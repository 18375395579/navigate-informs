Title: Evaluation of Events Involving a Natural Disaster and Hazardous Materials in the OR/MS field

Author: Chelsea Greene, Research Assistant, University at Buffalo, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 339A Bell Hall, Amherst, NY, 14260, United States of America, cgreene3@buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Changhyun Kwon, Rajan Batta

Year: 2013

Abstract: In the past few years there has been various events that involved both a natural disaster and man-made hazardous materials. With the continuously rising population and reliance on hazmat goods the probability of these events and their prospective environmental, socio-economic and human-health consequences are increasing. This study identifies relevant issues, future research directions and provides a starting point for researchers to address this growing concern in the OR/MS field.
