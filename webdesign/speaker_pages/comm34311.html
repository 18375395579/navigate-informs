Title: Predictive Maintenance for Railroad MoW Vehicles

Author: Aihong Wen, Manager of Operations Research, CSX Transportation, 500 Water st, Jacksonville, FL, 32202, United States of America, Aihong_Wen@csx.com

Coauthor(s): Kin-Ming Kam

Year: 2013

Abstract: Predictive maintenance offers cost savings over routine or time-based preventive maintenance. This presentation discusses how data mining techniques can be used to enable railroads practice predictive maintenance for the Maintenance of Way vehicle fleet by finding associated repair items.
