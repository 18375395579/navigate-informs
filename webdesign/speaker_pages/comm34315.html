Title: An Interactive Panel/Audience Discussion: Balancing Research and Teaching - An Academic Dilemma
Author: Gal Raz, Assistant Professor, Australian Graduate School of Management (AGSM), Australian Graduate School of Management, UNSW, Sydney, NS, Australia, galr@agsm.edu.au
Coauthor(s): Gilvan Souza, Tasha R. Inniss, Feryal Erhun
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this interactive panel discussion, we will discuss the balance of research and teaching. Panel members will discuss their experiences as junior faculty in managing this balance in different environements such as business schools, Engineering departments and OR/Math Departments. A large part of the panel will be dedicated to interactive discussion with the Audience.
