Title: An Application of Risk Analysis of an R&D Pharmaceutical Portfolio
Author: Jack Kloeber, Partner, Kromite, LLC, 82 Nelson Drive, Churchville, 18966, United States of America, jkloeber@kromite.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: The risk of an R&D Portfolio in the Pharmaceuticals Industry is most often tied to the technical risk of launching a new drug. The uncertainty of the marketplace, once a drug is approved, provides another source of risk. A risk analysis of a large portfolio (>50 drugs),should also include scientific, medical, manufacturing, regulatory, and marketplace dependencies. I will briefly discuss an effort in which we attempted such an analysis, and the challenges and opportunities for future efforts.
