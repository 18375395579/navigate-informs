Title: Offshore Facility Location for Remanufacturing

Author: Michael Galbreth, Assistant Professor of Management Science, Moore School of Business, University of South Carolina, 1705 College Street, Columbia, SC, 29208, United States of America, galbreth@moore.sc.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Blackburn

Year: 2007

Abstract: The potential to locate remanufacturing facilities offshore presents an important strategic decision for many remanufacturers. We describe the tradeoffs inherent in offshoring decisions in general and discuss how these can change in a remanufacturing context. Using a simple model, we derive some high-level insights regarding the value of offshoring for a remanufacturer facing variability in used product condition.
