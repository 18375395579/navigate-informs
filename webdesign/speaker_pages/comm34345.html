Title: Multiple Criteria Decision Making: Foundations and Some Approaches
Author: Murat Köksalan, METU, IE Dept., METU, Ankara, Turkey, koksalan@ie.metu.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Jyrki Wallenius
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this tutorial we cover the foundations of Multiple Criteria Decision Making and define and demonstrate basic concepts. We classify approaches based on the problem structure as well as on the way decision maker’s preferences are elicited. We discuss some approaches, emphasizing the interactive approaches. We demonstrate the concepts and approaches together with illustrative examples and graphs and we provide information about further resources and references.
