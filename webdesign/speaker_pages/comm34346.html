Title: Accelerating Decision Concensus on Complex Problems in Large Enterprises- An Experiential Approach

Author: Mike Cramer, Director, Operations Research, McDonald's Corporation, 1253 North Schmidt Rd, Romeoville, IL, 60446, United States of America, mike.cramer@us.mcd.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: McDonald's operates over 34,000 restaurants in 118 nations. The pursuit of continued growth in markets where outlet expansion is mature, creates conflicting growth strategies. The problem is aggrevated by the time to market, fragmentation and lost opportunity cost inherent in large enterprises. This session adresses the use of a combination of behavorial and decision analysis science to accelerate of Time to Concensus of complex and conflicting growth strategies.
