Title: Security Risk Management in Healthcare: A Case Study

Author: Humayun Zafar, Assistant Professor of Information Security and Assurance, Kennesaw State University, 1000 Chastain Road. MD 1101, Kennesaw, GA, 30144, United States of America, hzafar@kennesaw.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Effective security risk management (SRM) programs in organizations can help balance operational necessities and economic costs associated with IT-based systems. SRM is not a standalone activity; it should be integrated with all processes of an organization. The purpose of this study was to identify the perceived effectiveness of an existing security risk management program at a large healthcare institution.
