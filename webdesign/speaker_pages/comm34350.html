Title: Capacity Planning for Manufacturing in the Biotech Industry
Author: Micah Siegel, Associate Production Planner, Global Biologics Supply Chain, 200 Tournament Drive, HTD-6-1, Horsham, PA, 19044, United States of America, MSiegel1@GBSUS.JNJ.COM
Year: 2007
Abstract: We will discuss the factors to be considered in making capacity decisions in the Biotech industry. The topics we will cover are: Description of manufacturing process, factors affecting capacity allocation and expansion decisions, practical issues arising from the implementation of capacity expansion, and description of challenges facing a capacity planning group in the industry.
