Title: Understanding and Designing for the Uncertainties in the Development of Large Complex Products
Author: Qi Van Eikema Hommes, General Motors, 30500 Mound Rd., MC 480-106-256, Warren, MI, 48322, United States of America, qi.hommes@gm.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: This study surveyed a number of literatures concerning design for flexibility. A summary of the approaches in the literatures is made to answer the following questions: (1) what uncertainties does product development have to address? (2) Which existing design methods address which type of the uncertainties? (3) At what time of the product development process is each design for flexibility method effective? Areas for further research are discussed.
