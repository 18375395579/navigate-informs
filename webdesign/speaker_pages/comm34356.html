Title: Error Cancellation Modeling and Its Application to Machining Process Control

Author: Qiang Huang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, 4202 East Fowler Ave., ENB118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States, huangq@eng.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Hui Wang

Year: 2007

Abstract: The product quality in a manufacturing process can be affected by multiple error sources. One error source could cancel the effects of others on certain quality characteristics. To obtain insights into this fundamental phenomenon, we developed error equivalence modeling and analysis approach for improved dimensional control in manufacturing.
