Title: Mechatronics and Metrology to Improve Quality and Reliability in Nanomanufacturing

Author: Jingyan Dong, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Systems Engineering, North Carolina State University, 414-C Daniels Hall, Campus Box 7906, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, jdong@ncsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Placid Ferreira

Year: 2008

Abstract: Progresses in nanoscale sciences and technologies impose critical requirements of high accurate and high repeatable nanomanfacturing systems. As novel product and process designs involve the large-scale integration of nano, micro and macro structures, the ability to position, manipulate and sense across multiple dimensional scales becomes extremely important. Technologies including nano positioning, advanced control, sensing and calibration are key factors to bridging the gap between emerging nano technologies and their manufacturing potential.
