Title: Chapels in Bazaar? Latent Social Structure in Open Source Projects
Author: Premkumar Devanbu, UC Davis, 1672 Joshua Tree St, Davis, CA, United States of America, devanbu@cs.ucdavis.edu
Coauthor(s): Chris Bird
Year: 2008
Abstract: Project managers organize teams carefully, mindful of skills, tasks, geography, etc. Such constructed cathedrals contrast with the protean, chaotic ``bazaar of open-source. Yet, in the email social networks of the better OSS projects, we find empirically that social``cathedrals do emerge; we also find that these subgroups manifest strongly in technical discussions, and are significantly connected with collaborative work.
