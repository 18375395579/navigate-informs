Title: The Effect of Unplanned Donations on Humanitarian Logistics Coordination
Author: Lauren Davis, Assistant Professor, North Carolina A&T State University, 1601 East Market Street, Greensboro, NC, 27411, United States of America, lbdavis.ncat@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Nolan Applewhaite
Year: 2008
Abstract: The management of unplanned donations, stemming from the public’s response to an event, is a challenging process within humanitarian logistics. Through this research, we model the flow of unplanned donations and analyze the impact of these unpredictable donations on the distribution process of a disaster relief coordinating agency.
