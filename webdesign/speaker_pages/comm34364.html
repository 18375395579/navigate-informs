Title: Competing Keyword Auctions

Author: Jianqing Chen, The University of Texas at Austin, CBA 5.202, IROM, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, chenjq@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): De Liu, Andrew B. Whinston

Year: 2006

Abstract: Most research done on keyword auctions is under monopoly settings. This paper models an unaddressed but critical issue: the competition between search engines in the keyword advertising market. We analyze the advertisers?equilibrium participation and bidding strategies and the auctioneers?strategies on the ranking rules, and eventually predict the market segmentation under the competition.
