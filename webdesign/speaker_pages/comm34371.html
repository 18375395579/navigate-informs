Title: Anticipative Long Term Forest Planning

Author: Sophie D'Amours, Universit?Laval, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Pavillon Adrien Pouliot, Québec, QC, G1K 7P4, Canada, sophie.damours@forac.ulaval.ca

Coauthor(s): Mathieu Bouchard, Martin Simard, AchilleBenjamin Laurent, Sebastien Lemieux

Year: 2012

Abstract: This presentation will describe the challenging link between long term forest planning and industry development. It will showcase web-based advanced planning tools (SylviLab and LogiLab) supporting group decision making. Anticipation of the impact of the long term forest plan on industry and carbon balance will be explained. Multiple objectives are considered when selecting the best forest plans. Case studies from Canada will be presented.
