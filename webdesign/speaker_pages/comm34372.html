Title: Pierskalla Award
Author: Vishal Ahuja, University of Chicago, United States of America, vahuja@chicagobooth.edu
Coauthor(s): John Birge
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Health Applications Society of INFORMS sponsors an annual competition for the Pierskalla Award, which recognizes research excellence in the field of health care management science. The award includes a $500 honorarium for the best paper presented in a Health Applications Society sponsored session at the annual INFORMS conference. The award is named after Dr. William Pierskalla to recognize his contribution and dedication to improving health services delivery through operations research. The Pierskalla award information can be found on the website at: https://www.informs.org/Community/HAS/Pierskalla-Award
