Title: Predict Congestion Status of a Hump Yard using Data-mining
Author: Yu Wang, Manager OR-Simulation, CSX, 500 Water St, J300, Jacksonville, FL, 32202, United States of America, yu_wang@csx.com
Coauthor(s): Aihong Wen
Year: 2013
Abstract: The objective of this project is to utilize data mining techniques to predict the near future status of a hump yard and to pinpoint the leading factors of yard congestion. The motivation of this research is to provide insights to yard operation teams as well as resource providers so that they can be proactive in solving congestion issues. The experiences obtained from this research would lay down a foundation for future dynamic service planning system development.
