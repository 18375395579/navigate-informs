Title: Simulation of a Tariff Flexible Scheme (TOU) in Chile, a Win ?Win Situation
Author: Sonia Vera, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile, Av. Vicuña Mackenna 4860, Macul, Santiago, Chile, sverao@uc.cl
Coauthor(s): Enzo Sauma
Year: 2013
Abstract: A flexible tariff scheme could allow making better use of the power system in Chile. In this country, the residential sector faces a fixed pricing scheme. It does not include the increased cost of the system produced by the high variability of the load curve characteristic. We have simulated the effects it could have on the beneficial of DISCOs a new TOU system for electricity consumption in Chile, obtaining a win-win situation among DISCOs and consumers.
