Title: Learning Consumer Tastes: A Nonparametric Bayesian Model

Author: Canan Ulu, Assistant Professor, University of Texas at Austin, 2110 Speedway Stop B6500, Austin, TX, United States of America, Canan.Ulu@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Dorothee Honhon

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a nonparametric Bayesian learning model for a firm that can gather information about consumer tastes through sales of its product assortment. The firm can dynamically change its product assortment from period to period to gather better information about consumer tastes. We consider heuristics to solve large problems.
