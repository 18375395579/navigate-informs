Title: Metaphor Mapping
Author: James Felli, Sr. Research Advisor, Eli Lilly & Company, Lilly Corporate Center, Indianapolis, IN, United States, felli_james_c@lilly.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: One obstacle faced by decision makers is a limited ability to visualize data. We present a new direction in data visualization: the use of metaphorical images to represent data sets. We will discuss the use of metaphor maps and their ability to succinctly convey information to a human observer.
