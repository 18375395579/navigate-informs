Title: Information Technology and Firm Boundaries: Impact on Risk-return Profile

Author: Sanjeev Dewan, Associate Professor, UC Irvine, Paul Merage School of Business, Irvine, CA, 92617, United States of America, sdewan@uci.edu

Coauthor(s): Fei Ren

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper, we empirically investigate the effects of information technology (IT) investment on the risk-return profile of firms, emphasizing interactions between IT and the firm boundary strategies of diversification and vertical integration.
