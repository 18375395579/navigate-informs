Title: Sustainable Infrastructure Modeling and Policy Analyses

Author: Hakob Avetisyan, Assistant Professor, Cal State Fullerton, Department of Civil and Environmental, Fullerton, CA, United States of America, havetisyan@fullerton.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Sustainable infrastructure operation assumes consideration of interrelated elements and problems within interacting industries in which the decisions made for one industry may affect those in related industries. Therefore, it is vital to analyze the problem in a systematic fashion to find cost-efficient, technically well-designed and constructed sustainable solutions. A set of models was developed to allow such analysis for considering the deterministic cases followed by probabilistic versions.
