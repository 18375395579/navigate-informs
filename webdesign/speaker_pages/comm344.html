Title: The Use and Abuse of the Negotiation Process with an Application to a Supply Chain
Author: Krishnan Anand, Assistant Professor, Operations and Information Management, The Wharton School, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, anandk@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Maurice Schweitzer, Pnina Feldman
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study Negotiations from a process perspective, using an analytical model, with a focus on (i) The abuse of the negotiations process leading to its potential breakdown, (ii) Ways of forestalling abuse, and (iii) Policy implications. We experimentally test our findings. Finally, we apply these ideas to a supply chain context.
