Title: CPMS Practitioners Offer Tips for Success

Author: Peter Bell, Professor, University of Western Ontario, Richard Ivey School of Business, Londo, ON, N6A 3K7, Canada, pbell@ivey.uwo.ca

Coauthor(s): Nicholas Nahas, Manoj K. Chari, Doug Samuleson, Vijay Mehrotra

Year: 2012

Abstract: This panel will share why advanced analytics are needed today more than ever. Business leaders lack abilities to apply analytical tools and data structure to address their challenges. Despite years of talk about scorecards and metrics, most organizations still rely on gut feelings and experience for urgent critical matters. We can glean insights from data and guide them along innovative ways as analytical thinking can lead their organization into the future.
