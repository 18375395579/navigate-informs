Title: Locating Cell Phone Towers in a Rural Environment

Author: H. A. Eiselt, University of New Brunswick, Fredricton, NB, Canada, haeiselt@unb.ca

Coauthor(s): Vladimir Marianov

Year: 2006

Abstract: The presentation investigates the location of cell phone towers in a district in Nepal. In addition to provide widespread coverage, planners must also guard against losses due to insurgents. Our objective is a combination of average survival and worst-case survival of towers in case one of the towers is destroyed.
