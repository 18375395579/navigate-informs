Title: Opinion Formation in Social Media Platform

Author: Yingda Lu, Assistant Professor, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 110 8th Street, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, luy6@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Yong Tan, Junjie Wu

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we collect an innovative dataset on user posting microblogs from a social media website. Using natural experiment method, we demonstrate how individual decision of retweeting a microblog is influenced by the posts from the public as well as those from their friends. Furthermore, we also illustrate how public opinions and peer opinions have different impact on the formation of individual opinions on a social media platform.
