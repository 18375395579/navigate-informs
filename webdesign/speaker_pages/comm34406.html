Title: Advanced Data Analytics for Lighting Energy Savings Estimation and Visualization

Author: Vikrant Vaze, Member Research Staff, Philips Research North America, 345 Scarborough Road, Briarcliff Manor, NY, 10510, United States of America, vikrant.vaze@philips.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Though occupancy sensing & daylight harvesting controls are believed to have large energy savings potential, there is high variability in actual savings across customer sites. A major challenge in a reliable, site-specific assessment is the tradeoff between low cost and data accuracy. We develop a robust analytical approach based on grid-search and regression. Validation results show that believable visualizations and reliable savings estimates can be generated using inexpensive hardware.
