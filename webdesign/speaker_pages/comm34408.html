Title: Practical Aspects of Network Bid Price Control

Author: Darius Walczak, PROS, 3100 Main Street, Suite 900, Houston, TX, 77002, United States of America, dwalczak@prospricing.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Many network carriers rely on bid price control in their day-to-day revenue management. At the same time they are also often task with providing estimates of expected revenue, load factor or other measures. We discuss challenges that arise in deriving those business metrics under bid price control in the network setting.
