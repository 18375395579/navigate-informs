Title: Adaptive Web Presence and Evolution Through Web Usage Mining

Author: Xueping Li, Assistant Professor, University of Tennessee, Knoxville, Xueping.Li@utk.edu

Coauthor(s): Laigang Song

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper considers establishing adaptive web presence and evolution through web mining. It presents a workload characterization study for Internet web servers. Several workload invariants are identified which are deemed important since they potentially reveal the universal truth for all web servers. These regularities and patterns shed light on how to design a better Web and enhance its performance.
