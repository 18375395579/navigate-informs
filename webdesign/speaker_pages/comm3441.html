Title: What Fine Wine to Carry? The Influence of Weather on the Futures and Bottle Prices of Bordeaux Wines
Author: Mert Hakan Hekimoglu, Syracuse University, Whitman School of Management, 721 University Ave, Syracuse, NY, 13244, United States of America, mhekimog@syr.edu
Coauthor(s): Burak Kazaz, Scott Webster
Year: 2013
Abstract: It is well known that weather influences the quality of wine; however, earlier studies have shown no impact on the price and quality indicator of en primeur Bordeaux wines. Using data from Liv-ex.com, we confirm this finding; however, complement the impact of changes in weather information influencing the futures price and bottle price of young en primeur Bordeaux wines.
