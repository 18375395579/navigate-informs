Title: Recent Developments of Dynamic Pricing Research in Revenue Management

Author: Ming Chen, California State University-Long Beach, 1250 Bellflower Boulevard, CBA 313, Long Beach, CA, 90840, United States of America, Ming.Chen@csulb.edu

Coauthor(s): Zhi-Long Chen

Year: 2013

Abstract: In the last fifteen years, numerous success stories of dynamic pricing applications have motivated a rapidly growing research interest in a variety of dynamic pricing problems. In this paper, we present a survey of recent developments of dynamic pricing models in revenue management and identify several interesting research areas.
