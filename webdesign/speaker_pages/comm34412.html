Title: Visualizing Innovation Networks

Author: Rahul C. Basole, Georgia Institute of Technology, 760 Spring Street NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0210, United States of America, rahul.basole@ti.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Verna Allee

Year: 2008

Abstract: Today’s innovations occur in large, complex inter and intra-firm value networks. Visualization provides valuable insight to the structure, dynamics, and effective design of complex innovation networks. We present salient properties of innovation networks and provide illustrative visualization examples. Challenges and future research opportunities are discussed.
