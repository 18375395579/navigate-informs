Title: A Unified View of Lift-and-Project Type Procedures
Author: Endre Boros, Professor, RUTCOR, Rutgers University, 640 Bartholomew Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08854-8003, United States, boros@rutcor.rutgers.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: We prove a common generalization of several lift-and-project type approaches. This result also can be viewed as an algebraic realization of disjunctive programming in the binary case.
