Title: Using Multiple Objective Decision Analysis for Cyber Risk Management
Author: Don Buckshaw, Innovative Decisions, Inc., 1945 Old Gallows Road, Suite 215, Vienna, VA, 22182-3931, United States of America, dbuckshaw@innovativedecisions.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: Risk Management is complicated when the threat includes an intelligent adversary. Cyber risk management is further complicated by the need to trade off the security enhancing features of network defenses and corresponding loses of capabilities that may accompany them. This presentation examines the use of multiple objective decision analysis to measure both the attractiveness of attacks to adversaries and the trade-offs required for striking the proper balance between security and functionality.
