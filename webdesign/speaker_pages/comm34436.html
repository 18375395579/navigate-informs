Title: An Interactive Panel/Audience Discussion: Planning, Designing, and Preparing to Teach a New Course

Author: James Cochran, Louisiana Tech University, College of Administration & Business, PO Box 10318, Ruston, LA, 71055, United States, jcochran@cab.latech.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Lewis, Mary Beth Kurz, Jill Hardin

Year: 2006

Abstract: We discuss our experiences organizing and preparing to teach courses at all levels-introductory undergraduate through advanced graduate. Topics may include designing a course to accomplish pedagogical goals, deciding on the scope of a course, selecting a text/support material, choosing methods for assessing student performance, etc. Most of the session will be devoted to contributions and questions from the audience (who will be encouraged to participate and contribute throughout the session!).
