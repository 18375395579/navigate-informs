Title: Expected Interference and Concurrent Transmission in Vehicular Ad Hoc Networks

Author: Shivkumar Kalyanaraman, Professor, shivkuma@ecse.rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Lili Du, Satish Ukkusuri

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk will address the issue of interference caused by concurrent transmissions in a vehicular ad hoc network. Specifically, we will discuss analytical expressions of expected interference due to vehicular mobility and driver behavior. In addition an integer programming model to determine concurrent transmissions will be discussed. Finally, the implications on network throughput and open research questions in this area will be highlighted.
