Title: Mining Medical Discussion Board Data
Author: Shawndra Hill, Assistant Professor, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19103, United States of America, shawndra@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Lyle Ungar, John Holmes, Adrian Benton
Year: 2012
Abstract: We discuss medpie a tool to extract drugs and side effects from user generated content about medical conditions.
