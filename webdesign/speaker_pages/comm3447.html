Title: Structural Analysis and its Application to Multi-unit Sequential Dutch Auctions

Author: Yixin Lu, PhD Candidate, Erasmus University, Rotterdam School of Management, Rotterdam, Netherlands, ylu@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Eric van Heck, Alok Gupta, Wolf Ketter

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a structural model for multi-unit sequential Dutch auctions where multiple units of identical products are auctioned in sequential rounds. Using the real-world transaction data from a complex Business-to-Business (B2B) auction market, we demonstrate the effectiveness of our model in characterizing the dynamics of winning bids.
