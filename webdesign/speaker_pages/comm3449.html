Title: Efficient Funding: An Exploratory Look at the Nonprofit Sector

Author: Natalie Privett, Stanford University, 67 Olmsted Road #108, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, nprivett@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Feryal Erhun

Year: 2006

Abstract: Nonprofit organizations are a critical part of the American society as well as a growing sector of the national economy. In this paper, we model the process of funders (foundations or the government) awarding grants and allocating funds between nonprofit organizations using a contracting perspective. We show that the effectiveness of the funding process and the nonprofit sector overall can be significantly improved by designing efficient contracts.
