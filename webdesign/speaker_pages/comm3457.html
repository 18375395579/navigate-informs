Title: Competitive Marketing Strategies over Social Networks
Author: Kostas Bimpikis, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, United States of America, kostasb@stanford.edu
Coauthor(s): Asuman Ozdaglar, Ercan Yildiz
Year: 2012
Abstract: Recent advances in information technology have allowed firms to gather vast amounts of data regarding consumers' preferences and the structure and intensity of their social interactions. This paper examines a model of competition between firms, which can target their marketing budgets to individuals embedded in a social network. We provide a sharp characterization of the optimal targeted marketing strategies and highlight their dependence on the underlying social network structure.
