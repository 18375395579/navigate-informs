Title: The Topology of Central Counterparty Clearing Networks and Network Stability

Author: Richard Sowers, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Urbana, IL, United States of America, r-sowers@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Rui Song, Jonathan Jones

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study a simple model of a trading network with central counterparties and Gaussian exposures. We are interested in the structure of rare events (Maximum Exposure at Risk). We identify a large-deviations-type result which captures some of the structure of rare events.
