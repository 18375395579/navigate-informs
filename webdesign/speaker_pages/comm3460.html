Title: City Logistics: Advanced Urban Frieght Transportation Systems
Author: Teodor Gabriel Crainic, Professor, Department Management & Technology, ESG, UQAM, C.P. 8888, succ. Centre-ville, Montreal, QC, H3C 3P8, Canada, theo@crt.umontreal.ca
Year: 2008
Abstract: City Logistics aims to reduce the nuisances associated to freight transportation in urban areas while supporting their economic and social development. The main idea is to view individual stakeholders and decisions as components of an integrated logistics system to be optimized, through coordination and consolidation of decisions, loads, and movements. The tutorial presents an overview of City Logistics concepts, planning issues, and models, and identifies research avenues and challenges.
