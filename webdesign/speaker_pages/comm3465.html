Title: OR, Analytics and the Academic: The Path Forward

Author: Terry Harrison, Penn State University, 459 Business Building, Univeristy Park, United States of America, tharrison@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Barrett Thomas, Nicholas G. Hall

Year: 2013

Abstract: This panel discussion focuses on the value delivered by the analytics movement to the academic membership of INFORMS. Opportunities to be discussed include new academic programs, new research problems, funding of research, collaboration with industry, and enhancement of classroom content.
