Title: Teaching Unconstraining of Demand Data

Author: Mark Ferguson, Associate Professor, Georgia Tech, 800 W Peachtree St, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, Mark.Ferguson@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Carrie Queenan

Year: 2008

Abstract: Accurate unconstraining of demand data is essential to the success of a revenue management system. We demonstrate the dangers of ignoring unconstraining and present a simple unconstraining method that can be easily used as a classroom exercise.
