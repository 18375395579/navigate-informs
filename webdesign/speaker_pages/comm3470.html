Title: An Evaluation of Competitive Bids in Drywall Construction Trades

Author: Gerald H. Williams, R. Brown Consulting Group, Construction & Engineering Management Research, Inc., 2300 SW First Avenue, Suite 102, Portland, OR, 97212-5047

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper presents the final results of a study comparing the estimated and actual labor productivity for drywall trades on 400 construction projects in the western US. We document a wide variance in estimated productivities in these trades and attempt to account for variance in actual labor productivities.
