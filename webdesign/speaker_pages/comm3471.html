Title: Evaluation of Hotel Demand Forecasting Techniques
Author: Ahmet Kuyumcu, Prorize, 12138 Madison Dr, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, akuyumcu@prorize.com
Coauthor(s): Utku Yildirim
Year: 2008
Abstract: Although accurate demand forecasting is one of the most critical component of any revenue management application, it has not received enough attention. This presentation focuses on the commonly used demand forecasting techniques and compares their performances based on real-world hotel booking data.
