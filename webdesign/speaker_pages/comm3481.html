Title: Applying Theory of Constraints to Software as a Service

Author: John Ricketts, Distinguished Engineer, IBM, 650 Third Ave S, Minneapolis, MN, 55402, United States of America, jaricke@us.ibm.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Theory of Constraints (TOC) introduced unconventional methods for managing operations and inventory in Manufacturing and Distribution. TOC has been extended to labor-based services, including Professional, Scientific, Technical, Healthcare, and Educational Services. TOC is also being applied to technology-based services, including Software as a Service (SaaS). This paper includes SaaS examples from Analytics, Commerce, and Cities.
