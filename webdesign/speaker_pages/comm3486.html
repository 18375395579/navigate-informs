Title: Opportunities for Decision Support in the Very Light Jet Market
Author: Dennis Mathaisel, Professor, Babson College, Babson Hall, Babson Park, MA, 02457-0310, United States of America, Mathaisel@Babson.edu
Coauthor(s): Clare Comm
Year: 2008
Abstract: How do you initiate and sustain service in a very light jet air taxi market? There are four challenges that present opportunities for decision support: marketing the service and changing consumer behavior; forecasting the demand for the service; scheduling the real-time on-demand operation; and optimal aircraft routing.
