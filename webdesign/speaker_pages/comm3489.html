Title: On the Relative Strength of Different Generalizations of Split Cuts

Author: Marco Molinaro, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, United States of America, molinaro@cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sanjeeb Dash, Oktay Gunluk

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper we show the complete relationship between the split, cross, crooked cross and t-branch split closures. We also study the strength of cross cuts from basic and 2-row relaxations. This answers three open questions left open by Day, Dash and Gunluk (2010).
