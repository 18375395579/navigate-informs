Title: Verifying Spreadsheets by Rewriting into Analytica

Author: Max Henrion, CEO, Lumina Decision Systems, Inc, 26010 Highland Way, Los Gatos, CA, 95033, United States of America, henrion@lumina.com

Coauthor(s): Surya Swamy

Year: 2008

Abstract: As researchers develop more thorough auditing methods for spreadsheets, they find correspondingly higher error rates. What fraction of errors remain undetected? We find that rewriting spreadsheets into Analytica reveals many otherwise undetected errors. This approach gives rigorous verification of spreadsheets for high-stakes applications.
