Title: Next Generation Analytics Applications for Railroads

Author: Ravindra Ahuja, President & CEO, Innovative Scheduling, 2153 SE Hawthorne Road, Gainesville, FL, 32641, United States of America, ravindra.ahuja@innovativescheduling.com

Coauthor(s): Jason Montgomery

Year: 2013

Abstract: CSX Transportation has realized the need of advanced analytic applications such as data analytics, data mining, and simulation to build game changing applications for railroads resulting in higher asset utilization. We will give overview and demonstrations of some such applications where we have been involved and outline our vision moving forward.
