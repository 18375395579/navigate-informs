Title: OR & Undergraduate Research in Mathematics

Author: Allen Holder, Trinity University Mathematics, One Trinity Place, San Antonio, TX, United States of America, aholder@trinity.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Undergraduate mathematics students are often interested in applied mathematics, and we will discuss how OR is especially well suited to undergraduate research/senior projects. We will detail success with applications in medicine, marketing, and biology as well as in OR itself, of which several have ended in peer-reviewed publication.
