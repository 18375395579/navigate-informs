Title: Setting Prices on Priceline
Author: Chris Anderson, Cornell University, School of Hotel Administration, Ithaca, NY, 14850, United States of America, cka9@cornell.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Priceline.com is the original name your own price auction mechanism for service provision. A brief introduction into how Priceline works as well as how service firms are selected is provided. An illustration of the data provided to service firms e.g. consumer bids is provided. Lastly we detail how this data can be used to set optimal prices and inventory levels.
