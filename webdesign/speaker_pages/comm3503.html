Title: Service Operations Classification for Risk Management

Author: Jonathan Hosking, IBM Research Division, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, PO Box 218, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, hosking@watson.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Farhad Shafti, Tim Bedford, Nicoleta Serban, Haipeng Shen, Lea Deleris, Lesley Walls

Year: 2008

Abstract: We describe a study in the classification of services to gain insights into operational risk management. For each of 36 service operations scores were assessed for 6 service dimensions and 8 risk factors. Statistical clustering methods were applied, and dimension reduction was used to refine the clustering. The results indicate connections between the service operations, service dimensions, and risk factors. Initial findings and a plan for follow-up work will be discussed.
