Title: Monitoring in Offshored Processes

Author: Subhajyoti Bandyopadhyay, shubho.bandyopadhyay@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Praveen Pathak, Ravi Aron

Year: 2006

Abstract: The emergence of a new class of technology-enabled monitoring ?thanks to recent advances in Information Technology and telecommunications ?has enabled organizations to effectively track the quality of outsourced processes. We present a game-theoretic model to analyze this new development, especially in the context of offshore processes. A multi-year survey across several countries back our analytical results.
