Title: Implement OR Model-driven Information Systems at Norfolk Southern
Author: Clark Cheng, Director Operations Research, Norfolk Southern Corporation, 1200 Peachtree St NE, MS 12-117, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States of America, clark.cheng@nscorp.com
Coauthor(s): Jignesh Patel
Year: 2012
Abstract: Operations research (OR) models are generally complicated by nature and therefore they are hard to understand and difficult to use for business users. In this presentation, we’ll discuss how to combine operations research and information technology to develop strategic and tactical information systems. The goal is to provide practical and usable tools to assist management with better decision-making, enhance business competitive advantage, and improve operating efficiency.
