Title: Capacity Investment under Competition: Evidence from US Hospitals
Author: Diwas Kc, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, diwas@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Christian Terwiesch, Chris P. Lee
Year: 2008
Abstract: Operations Management theory suggests that competition tends to spur capacity investment. We test this theory in the context of healthcare delivery using capacity and demand data from a cross-section of US hospitals.
