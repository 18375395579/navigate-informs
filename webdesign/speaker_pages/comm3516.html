Title: Security Risk Management of Confidential Information in Social Networks

Author: Manuel Nunez, Associate Professor, School of Business, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road Unit 1041, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, mnunez@business.uconn.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Information sharing in social networks could lead to security risks when snoopers can gain indirect access to or collude to infer confidential information through the network. Releasing information to a well-connected user could represent a security threat. We study mathematical models relating disclosure risk to social network topology.
