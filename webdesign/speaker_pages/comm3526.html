Title: A Network Approach to Mining Market Basket Data
Author: Yaquan Xu, Assistant Professor, Virginia State University, 12400 liverpool lane, #104, chester, va, 23836, United States of America, yxu@vsu.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Market basket analysis has been an important part of decision support process in retail market for many years. In this paper, we construct the market graph and study the evolution of the structural properties of the market graph over the transactions and draw conclusions regarding the dynamics of the market basket development based on the interpretation of the obtained results.
