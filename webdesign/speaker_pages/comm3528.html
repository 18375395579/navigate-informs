Title: How to Publish Your Paper in the INFORMS Journal on Computing
Author: John Chinneck, Carleton University, Systems and Computer Engineering, Ottawa, On, K1S 5B6, Canada, chinneck@sce.carleton.ca
Coauthor(s): David L. Woodruff, Allen Holder, Edward Wasil, Karen Aardal, Subramanian Raghavan, Robert Fourer
Year: 2012
Abstract: What do we look for in evaluating a paper for publication in the INFORMS Journal on Computing? What are the characteristics of successful papers? How can you increase your chances of acceptance? What are the up-and-coming topics of interest? The Editor-in-Chief and senior editors will address these issues and answer your questions.
