Title: Adding Practical Experience to the OR/MS Classroom
Author: Matt Drake, Assistant Professor, Duquesne University, School of Business, Pittsburgh, PA, United States, drake987@duq.edu
Coauthor(s): John Mawhinney
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this presentation we will discuss various ways in which we have incorporated practical experience into our Supply Chain Management curriculum at Duquesne University.
