Title: Multiscale Hierarchical Modeling of Nanowire Growth Processes
Author: Qiang Huang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, huangq@eng.usf.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Quality control of nanomanufacturing processes faces new challenges of scale effects. This talk presents multiscale hierarchical modeling of nanowire growth processes. The model integrates growth kinetics which facilitates in situ control in nanomanufacturing.
