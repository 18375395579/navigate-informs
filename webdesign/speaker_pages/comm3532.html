Title: The Impact of Supplier Development on Buyer-supplier Relationship
Author: Ying Fan, PhD Candidate, Ivey School of Business, University of Western Ontario, Apt. 2005, 2 Forest Laneway, North York, ON, M2N5X7, Canada, yfan@ivey.uwo.ca
Coauthor(s): Prahinski Carol, WC Benton
Year: 2007
Abstract: Little is understood about how supplier development programs (SDPs), which include competitive pressures, evaluations, incentives and direct involvement, influence supplier performance. Using structural equation modeling (SEM) and data collected from 141 automotive suppliers, the results suggest that the relationship between SDP and performance is mediated by communication, cooperation, and commitment. Competitive pressures were found to have a negative influence in the communication model.
