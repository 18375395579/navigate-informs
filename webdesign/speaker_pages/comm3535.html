Title: Call Center Performance Indicators and Computations

Author: Ger Koole, Professor, VU University Amsterdam, De Boelelaan 1081 a, Amsterdam, -, 1081 HV, Netherlands, koole@few.vu.nl

Year: 2007

Abstract: Call center performance is usually measured by a number of performance indicators such as abandonment rate, service level, productivity, first-time-fixed ration, etc. However, different definitions are used for the same performance indicators. For example, how to account for abandonment in teh service level definitions? We discuss the different options and show the pros and cons of different choices, both from the managerial and the computational perspectives.
