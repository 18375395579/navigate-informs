Title: Empowering Decision Makers through Process Data Analysis
Author: Anca Chandra, Research Staff Member, T.J.Watson Research Center, IBM, 650 Harry Rd, San Jose, CA, 95120, United States of America, anca@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Neil Boyette
Year: 2012
Abstract: Once a client company and a service provider sign a high deal spanning many years, the client creates a large number of small TCV short term deals. These deals are short term and the service provider must quickly react. Often, deal managers are not able to react fast because there are multiple organizations working on the deal. This paper describes a system built to assist managers when making daily decisions by exposing the current status of a deal though data aggregation and analysis.
