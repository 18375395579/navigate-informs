Title: Coordination of Ambulance to Emergency Room Transitions

Author: Vera Tilson, Assistant Professor, Simon School of Business, University of Rochester, Rochester, NY, 14627, United States of America, Vera.Tilson@simon.rochester.edu

Coauthor(s): David Tilson

Year: 2008

Abstract: In the management and operations literature, examining coordination across a network of codependent tasks has been consistently recognized as an important way to study communication in the management and operations literature. We build upon coordination theory and process analysis to conceptualize the patterns of dependencies and present and potential means for coordination in ambulance to emergency rooms hand-overs.
