Title: Using a Decision Component Registry to Manage USMC Logistics Decision Support

Author: Jeff Eaton, Director, Operations Research Solutions, Concurrent Technologies Corporation, 9570 Regency Square Boulevard, Suite 400, Jacksonville, FL, 32225, United States of America, eatonj@ctc.com

Coauthor(s): Aaron Burciaga

Year: 2012

Abstract: The USMC must ensure validated M&S capabilities are developed to support decisions to a myriad of critical logistics issues that face senior leaders. HQMC LX and CTC have developed a Decision Component Registry to improve the M&S decision support maturity across the logistics analytics community. This presentation will highlight this approach in managing their logistics decision support tools ?ensuring that there is a clear, multi-echelon mapping between data systems, M&S tools and decisions.
