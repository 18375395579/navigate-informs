Title: E-Sourcing Capability Model for Service Providers (eSCM-SP) v2: An Overview

Author: Elaine Hyder, Systems Scientist, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, 1311 Wean Hall, Pittsburgh, PA, 15217, United States, ehyder@cs.cmu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Developed by a consortium led by Carnegie Mellon University's Information Technology Services Qualification Center (ITSqc), the eSCM-SP v2 contains best practices to address the critical capabilities needed by IT-enabled sourcing service providers. The purpose of this paper is to provide an overview of this model, including its intent, its audience and scope, and its structure.
