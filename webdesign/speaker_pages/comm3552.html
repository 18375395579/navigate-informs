Title: Forecasting the Demand for Emergency Department Services

Author: Murray Côt? Associate Professor, UCDHSC, Health Care Policy and Research, Department of Medicine, Aurora, CO, 80045, United States of America, murray.cote@uchsc.edu

Coauthor(s): Dave Eitel, Elif Akcali

Year: 2007

Abstract: The first step in understanding why emergency departments become congested is an examination of the demand for services. Using hourly arrival data to an emergency department in Pennsylvania, we develop a set of forecasting models to show health care decision makers how this hourly demand translates into operational, tactical, and strategic capacity issues.
