Title: IBM's Text-mining Model Powers Quick Disability Determination at the Social Security Administration

Author: Alissa Eichinger, 12902 Federal Systems Park Dr., Fairfax, VA, 22033, United States, alissa.eichinger@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Spyros Kontogiorgis, Thomas P. Hart

Year: 2007

Abstract: IBM Global Business Services and Research crafted a production solution that enables the SSA to process disability insurance applications efficiently. Our optimization engine provides state-of-the-art text classification: The trainer processes millions of records with thousands of features; the predictor offers fast response with high accuracy.
