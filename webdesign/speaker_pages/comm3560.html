Title: Improving the Distribution System for the Food for Kids Backpack Program at the RFBO

Author: Emre Tokgoz, Faculty - Lecturer, University of Oklahoma, 601 Elm Ave. Room 423, Norman, OK, 73019, United States of America, Emre.Tokgoz-1@ou.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Regional Food Bank of Oklahoma (RFBO) is a major non-profit organization providing food to people at no cost in Oklahoma. This paper presents a new distribution system designed for one of the programs of the RFBO; the Food for Kids Backpack program (FKBP). The model is expected to result in considerable savings and a more efficient staff workload.
