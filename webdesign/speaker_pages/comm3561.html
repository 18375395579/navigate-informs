Title: GAMS Development Corporation - Model Building and Deployment with GAMS
Author: Steve Dirkse, Director of Optimization, GAMS Development Corp., 1217 Potomac St. NW, Washington, DC, United States of America, sdirkse@gams.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: We demonstrate many of the capabilities of the GAMS software as we start with a simple optimization model and build it out by adding nonlinear and integer variables to the model and connecting it with a GUI in a sample application.
