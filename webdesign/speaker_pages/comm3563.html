Title: Decentralized Control of Plug-in Electric Vehicle Charging via Multi-agent Coordination in Microgrid
Author: Jianhui Wang, Affiliate Professor, Auburn University, 9700 S. Cass Avenue, Bldg. 221, Argonne, IL 60439, USA, Argonne, IL, 60439, United States of America, jianhui.wang@anl.gov
Coauthor(s): Chen Chen
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this work we propose a decentralized control scheme, which utilizes the new development of distributed optimization algorithms in operation research, for plug-in electric vehicle charging in microgrids.
