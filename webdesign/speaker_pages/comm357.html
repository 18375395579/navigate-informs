Title: Application of Data Mining Methods to Service Process Control and Monitoring
Author: Maggie Ning, ningxh@ust.hk
Coauthor(s): Fugee Tsung
Year: 2008
Abstract: Service data, like credit card data, has been receiving more research attention in recent years. Treatment of this sort of data needs special skills because it contains a mixed type of variables and is usually high-dimensional. In the presentation, SVM-based approach and density-based method are addressed to perform analysis for service process data.
