Title: Complementarities Between IT and HR Practices in Knowledge Work
Author: Ali Reza Tafti, Ph.D Student, University of Michigan, Ross School of Business, E0401 Elec Educ Resources Bldg, Ann Arbor, MI, 48502, United States, atafti@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): M. S. Krishnan, Sunil Mithas
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop and validate a framework of Human Resources (HR) and Information Technology (IT) alignment. First, we examine the interaction between innovative HR-related work practices and specific IT-related practices in firm productivity outcomes. Next, we examine quantitative firm productivity impacts of IT investment and innovative HR-related work practices in the work of IT professionals. We utilize both objective economic metrics and managers' subjective assessments of firm productivity.
