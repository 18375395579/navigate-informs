Title: Consequences and Control of Procrastination in Projects
Author: Yaozhong Wu, National University of Singapore Business School, 15 Kent Ridge Dr., Singapore, Singapore, yaozhong.wu@nus.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Vish Krishnan, Karthik Ramachandran
Year: 2012
Abstract: We analyze how managers can design project tasks to influence procrastinating workers. We also report findings from an experiment on the effects of different task designs on individual worker’s behavior and on the quality of their performance.
