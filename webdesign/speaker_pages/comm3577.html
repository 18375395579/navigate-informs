Title: A Nonparametric Analysis of CEO Compensation
Author: John Ruggiero, Associate Professor of Economics, University of Dayton, John.Ruggiero@notes.udayton.edu
Coauthor(s): Nancy Mohan
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this paper, we apply DEA to perform a compensation gap analysis for CEOs. The approach measures a range of CEO value based on comparison of wages and company performance measures.
