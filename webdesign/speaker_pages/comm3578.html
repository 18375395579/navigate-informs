Title: To Sell and to Provide? The Implications of the Auto Manufacturer’s Involvement in Car Sharing

Author: Ioannis Bellos, Assistant Professor, George Mason University, ISOM Area, 4400 University Drive MS 5F4, Fairfax, VA, 22030, United States of America, ibellos@gmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Beril Toktay, Mark Ferguson

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper we study the auto manufacturer’s choice regarding whether to provide mobility service (e.g., car sharing) in conjunction with the traditional sales channel. We explicitly model the consumer’s choice of transportation mode as well as vehicle usage decisions and we characterize the environmental and economic implications of the auto manufacturer's strategy.
