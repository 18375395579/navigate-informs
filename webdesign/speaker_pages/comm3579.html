Title: COIN-OR/GAMSlinks: Hooking Your Solver to GAMS
Author: Steven Dirkse, GAMS Development Corp., 1217 Potomac St., NW, Washington, DC, 20007, United States of America, sdirkse@gams.com
Coauthor(s): Stefan Vigerske, Michael Bussieck
Year: 2007
Abstract: The GAMSlinks project at COIN-OR is dedicated to the development of links between GAMS and open source (e.g. COIN-OR) solvers. These links broaden the audience for COIN-OR solvers and provide solver developers with the quality assurance and benchmarking tools that come with GAMS. We show how to use the GAMSlinks project, highlight non-standard features of the present interfaces, give a short introduction on how to link your own solver to GAMS, and speculate about future developments.
