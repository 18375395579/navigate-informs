Title: Optimizing Flexible Products Over the Internet

Author: David Post, SigmaZen GmbH, Bommersheimerweg 31, Bad Homburg, 61348, Germany, davidpost@mac.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: Flexible products have attracted interest in industries where the product/service is perishable and where there is a high degree of price transparency. In the online world, suppliers have less power as information is put into consumers?hands. The cost-effective interactivity functionality of the Internet has been suggested as a method to involve consumers in the price-product determination process. This presentation discusses the optimization of the price-product mix for flexible products.
