Title: Regenerative Medicine Manufacturing ?A Systems Engineering Perspective
Author: Richard A. Wysk, Dopaco Distinguished Professor, North Carolina State University, Department of ISE, Raleigh, NC, United States of America, rawysk@ncsu.edu
Coauthor(s): Molly Purser, Paul Cohen, Rohan Shirwaiker, Ola Harrysson
Year: 2012
Abstract: This tutorial introduces the basic concepts associated with a regenerative medicine manufacturing (RMM) system. A background of regenerative medicine, the critical factors involved in its scale-up translation, and an overview of a systems engineering approach for RMM system design are presented.
