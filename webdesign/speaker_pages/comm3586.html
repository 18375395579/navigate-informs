Title: Decision Analysis as part of Educational Reform

Author: Carl Spetzler, CEO, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, cspetzler@sdg.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this session we will review how training youth in decision analysis fits into 21st Century Education models. We will see that decision skills are the keystone for integrating the three basic domains of creativity, SEL (Social Emotional Learning), and Critical Thinking into a practical skill. Seen with this perspective, decision skills are central to most of the educational reform movements.
