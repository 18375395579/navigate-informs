Title: Single Sourcing vs Multisourcing

Author: Sameer Hasija, Insead, 1 Ayer Rajah Avenue, Singapore, 138676, Singapore, sameer.hasija@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Shantanu Bhattacharya, Alok Gupta

Year: 2013

Abstract: We compare two strategies for service outsourcing: multisourcing and single-sourcing. Our focus is to identify the interplay between effort distortion and free-rider issues in determining the optimal strategy.
