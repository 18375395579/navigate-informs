Title: EM Algorithm for Product Mix of Business Customers
Author: Timothy Au, Cornell University, Department of Mathematics, Ithaca, NY, United States of America, tca4@cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Wei Jiang
Year: 2008
Abstract: In order to understand the relationship among different products that a customer purchases, this paper proposes a clustering method for the product mix of business customers using linear regression models and the EM Algorithm. By identifying the nature of the product mix, we can pinpoint important customer behaviors and develop appropriate product bundles to improve business.
