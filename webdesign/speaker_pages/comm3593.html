Title: A Mathematical Approach to Identifying and Forecasting Shifts in the Mood of Social Media

Author: Les Servi, The MITRE Corporation, 202 Burlington Road, Bedford, MA, 01730, United States of America, lservi@mitre.org

Coauthor(s): Sara Beth Elson

Year: 2012

Abstract: Public mood is examined by: (i) running an existing system, LIWC, to estimate the emotional intensity of individual tweets; (ii) infer the dynamics of the aggregate mood after first detecting times where those mood changed abruptly, and (iii) forecast future moods. This work is illustrated and validated with real data.
