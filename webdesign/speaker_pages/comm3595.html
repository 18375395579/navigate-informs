Title: The Electronic Decision Enhancement Leverager Plus Integrator (E-DEL+I? Analytic Process

Author: Carolyn Wong, RAND Corporation, 1776 Main Street, Santa Monica, United States of America, wong@rand.org

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Electronic Decision Enhancement Leverager plus Integrator (E-DEL+I ?? provisional patents, RAND) is a capability for integrating diverse views on complex issues involving many stakeholders and multiple critical dimensions. Often technical knowledge must be blended with understandings of operations and policy to arrive at solutions acceptable to all stakeholders. E-DEL+I has been used in consensus building for solution generation, conflict resolution, and strategic definition of next steps.
