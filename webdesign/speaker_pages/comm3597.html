Title: Presentations of Finalists #3 and #4 for the Seventh Annual INFORMS Case Competition

Author: Tasha R. Inniss, Assistant Professor of Mathematics, Spelman College, 350 Spelman Lane, SW, Box 320, Atlanta, GA, 30126, United States, tinniss@spelman.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The FOUR finalists for the 2006 INFORMS Case Competition will deliver final presentations of their teaching cases to a panel of pre-selected judges and the audience. All are welcome to attend and observe these presentations as well as ask questions of the finalists. At the end of the four presentations, the judges will select the winner of the Case Competition. The winners and runners-up will be announced and presented with plaques as well as cash awards at the INFORM-ED Annual Business Meeting.
