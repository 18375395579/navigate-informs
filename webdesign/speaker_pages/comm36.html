Title: A Carbon Emission Cost Based Economic Production Quantity Model

Author: Zhi Tao, Kent State University, 475 Terrace Drive, Kent, OH, United States of America, ztao@kent.edu

Coauthor(s): Alfred Guiffrida, Felix Offodile

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper integrates carbon emission cost into the economic production quantity model and investigates the tradeoff results of both economic concern and environmental concern. The findings provide optimal decisions on production quantity, carbon emission and minimum budget for carbon emission conscious manufacturers and also shed light on carbon emission management.
