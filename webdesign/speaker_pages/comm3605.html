Title: Risk Management and Decision Analysis Tools for Military and Homeland Security Applications

Author: Igor Linkov, Senior Scientist, Cambridge Environmental, 58 Charles Street, Cambridge, MA, 02141, United States, Linkov@cambridgeenvironmental.com

Coauthor(s): F. Kyle Satterstrom

Year: 2006

Abstract: Available frameworks for homeland security risk management are often applied without adjustment for unique environmental, social, political, and economic conditions. This presentation will illustrate several case studies which apply MCDA tools to homeland security and related areas. It will also report the results of the NATO workshop on current practices and options to address the complex challenges of protecting infrastructure at industrial ports.
