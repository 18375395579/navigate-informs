Title: Effects of Electronic Payment Services on Consumer Behavior in Electronic Retailing: A Research Prop
Author: Irfan Kanat, ikanat@asu.edu
Coauthor(s): Ajay Vinze, Paul Steinbart
Year: 2012
Abstract: e-payment services advanced rapidly in the recent years. Major e-retailers like e-bay are now players in the market, traditional financial institutions are trying to defend their positions with new products. Each service offers a different set of features suited for different transactions. The effects of the features sets on consumer behavior has not been investigated in the literature. We present a review of the literature and propose a quasi experimental research design to address this gap.
