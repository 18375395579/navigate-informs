Title: An Empirical Analysis of the Triples Formulation of the Network Design Problem
Author: Eli Olinick, Associate Professor, Southern Methodist University, PO Box 750123, Dallas, TX, 75275, United States of America, olinick@lyle.smu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present an empirical study comparing a new, compact formulation of the network design problem to the classical node-edge and edge-path formulations.
