Title: Fostering New Technology Adoption

Author: Feryal Erhun, Assistant Professor, Stanford University, MS&E, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, ferhun@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Ozgen Karaer, Hau L. Lee

Year: 2007

Abstract: Faced with a new technology introduced in a market, a firm has to account for uncertainties regarding the future of the technology, network effects, and steep starting costs while making the adoption decision. RFID adoption has been going through this process and suffering from this individual perspective of each firm. We study this adoption problem by finding the gap due to lack of coordination and analyze the impact of intervention methods like external funds, mandates, and quantity discounts.
