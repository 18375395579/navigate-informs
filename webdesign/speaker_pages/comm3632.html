Title: Optimal Focus Group Design to Augment Demand Data

Author: Shih-Hsien Tseng, Graduate Research Associate, The Ohio State University, 1971 Neil Avenue, Columbus, OH, 43210, United States, shih_hsien@mail2000.com.tw

Coauthor(s): Theodore Allen

Year: 2006

Abstract: Focus groups facilitate forecasting of the demand for a new product. We explore the optimal planning of which prototypes to build and which questions to ask to augment data about demands for existing products. Also, the relationship of this problem with multifidelity modeling and accelerated testing is discussed.
