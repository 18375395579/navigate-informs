Title: Internal Benchmarking of the Divisions of the Wyoming Highway Patrol using DEA

Author: Mehmet E. Ozbek, Assistant Professor, Colorado State University, Guggenheim Hall Campus Delivery 1584, Fort Collins, CO, 80523, United States of America, mehmet.ozbek@colostate.edu

Coauthor(s): Maral Jalili

Year: 2013

Abstract: Wyoming Highway Patrol (WHP) is a data-driven organization which uses multiple measures that are outlined in its Balanced Scorecard to assess performance. WHP consists of 18 divisions which undertake the patrol operations under different external conditions such as traffic volumes, climate, etc. This presentation discusses a DEA-based performance assessment system that will enable WHP to identify its best-performing (i.e., most efficient) divisions to be able to use those as internal benchmarks.
