Title: Differential Variational Inequalities and Differential Nash Games
Author: Jong-Shi Pang, Department Head and Caterpillar Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Industrial and Enterprise Systems Engin, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, jspang@illinois.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We examine the relationship between differential variational inequalities and differential Nash games and provide some recent results.
