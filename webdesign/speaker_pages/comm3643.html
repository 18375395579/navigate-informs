Title: Risk Analysis of Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs)
Author: Tim Leung, Assistant Professor, Columbia University, IEOR Department, 500 West 120th Street MC4704, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, tl2497@columbia.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: In the US, the Exchange-traded fund (ETF) market has grown from a dozen funds in 1995 to over a thousand funds with over $1.5 trillion in assets in 2013. For many investors, ETFs provide various desirable features such as liquidity, diversification, low expense ratios and accessibility to different markets and positions. In this talk, we will discuss the price dynamics and risks of ETFs as well as their options.
