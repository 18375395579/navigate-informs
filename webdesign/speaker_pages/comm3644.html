Title: Improving the Effectiveness and Lowering the Cost of the US Military Medical Distribution System
Author: Adam Resnick, RAND Corporation, 1776 Main St, Santa Monica, United States of America, adam_resnick@rand.org
Year: 2012
Abstract: The US Military delivers medical supplies from suppliers in the United States to its overseas operating locations, including treatment facilities, distribution centers, and operational units. We identify opportunities to gain efficiency in: contracting, vendor delivery, intermediate warehousing, and final distribution. We discuss policies the military should consider to improve its global medical supply operations.
