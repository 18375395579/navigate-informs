Title: A MAUT-based DEA Approach: Evaluating Retail Stores Performance Considering Corporate Preferences
Author: Luis Dias, Ph.D, Univeristy Coimbra, INESC Coimbra and Faculty of Economics, Rua Antero Quental 199, Coimbra, 3000-033, Portugal, lmcdias@fe.uc.pt
Coauthor(s): Pedro Almeida
Year: 2007
Abstract: Data Envelopment Analysis (DEA) has been widely used to assess the relative efficiency of homogeneous units such as schools, hospitals, bank branches, etc. We propose MAUT-based variants of Additive-DEA to incorporate preferences. As a pilot study, we evaluated stores from a retailing chain in Portugal. The top executives?preferences shaped the conversion of inputs and outputs into value scales, constrained the acceptable trade-offs, and constrained the benchmark targets that were defined.
