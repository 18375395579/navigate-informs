Title: Crowdsourcing Real-time Traveler Information Services: An Exploratory Analysis of Issues and Challenges

Author: Karthik Charan Konduri, Assistant Professor, University of Connecticut, 261 Glenbrook Road, Unit 3037, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, kkonduri@engr.uconn.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Crowdsourcing is gaining in popularity as a solution to providing traveler information services replacing traditional sensor networks. Despite its potential, issues about its feasibility and applicability to provide complete traveler information services remain. The main focus of this paper is to present results from an exploration of one of the key issues of crowdsourcing namely, quality and validity of crowdsourced data.
