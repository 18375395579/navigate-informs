Title: What Makes a Good Game?

Author: Joel Sokol, Associate Professor, School of ISyE, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States, jsokol@isye.gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Creating educational games is hard. Creating good educational games is even harder. In this talk, we suggest some guidelines for making good games (including a definition of what good is), and illustrate using examples of our own games (including several bad ones).
