Title: Dynamic Oligopolistic Competition in a Disruptive Supply Chain Network

Author: Reetabrata Mookherjee, Sr. Scientist, Zilliant Inc, 3815 S Capital of Texas Hwy, Suite 300, Austin, TX, 78704, United States, reeto.mookherjee@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Terry Friesz

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk we describe a family of competitive models of supply chains operating in realistic decision environments reflecting demand uncertainty and supply side disruptions.
