Title: Service Scorecard- An Implementation Strategy

Author: Rajesh Tyagi, Assistant Professor, HEC Montreal, 3000 Chemin de la Côte-Sainte-Catherine, Montreal, PQ, 60604, Canada, rtyagi@depaul.edu

Coauthor(s): Marie-Helene Jobin

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Service Scorecard, a newly designed PMS, is aimed specifically at service business context capturing the intricacies of services. The seven elements of Service Scorecard are Growth, Leadership, Acceleration, Collaboration, Innovation, Execution, and Retention. Several companies have implemented aspects of the Service Scorecard well and have realized the benefits. This paper presents lessons from these experiences and best practices in business service performance measurement.
