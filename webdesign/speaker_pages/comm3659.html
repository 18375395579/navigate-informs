Title: A Decision Support System on Surgical Treatments for Rotator Cuff Tears
Author: Weihong Guo, PhD Candidate, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Avenue, IOE Building, ANN ARBOR, MI, 48105, United States of America, graceguo@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Kamran Paynabar, Judy Jin
Year: 2013
Abstract: Treatment of patients with rotator cuff tears usually starts with physical therapy, but some patients may eventually require surgery. Ineffective physical therapy increases the time, cost, and pain for patients while undergoing treatment in addition to continuing pain after the therapy. This research developed a decision support system to help identify the patients who will not respond to physical therapy by effectively analyzing patients?examination data.
