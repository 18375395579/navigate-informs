Title: Panel Discussion: What Should We Teach Students about Auctions?
Author: Michael H. Rothkopf, Professor, Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, 640 Bartholomew Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States, rothkopf@rutcor.rutgers.edu
Coauthor(s): Charles A. Holt, Karla Hoffman, Sandy Jap, Charles W. Smith, Tuomas Sandholm
Year: 2006
Abstract: Different disciplines have different ways of thinking about auctions, and real auctions don't always oblige by fitting completely into any of these particular ways of thinking. This presents a challenge for course content decisions. What's a poor professor to do?
