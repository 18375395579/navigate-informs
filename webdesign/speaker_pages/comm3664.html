Title: Discerning Patterns of Aircraft Usage and Spare Parts Demands

Author: George Kuhn, Senior Research Fellow, LMI, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, 22102, United States, gkuhn@lmi.org

Year: 2006

Abstract: Report on research exploring whether patterns of aircraft (helicopter) usage and spare parts demands may be better described in order to support better forecasts. Progress toward greater differentiation among usage and spares behaviors, and away from traditional approaches relying on highly aggregated views, will be discussed.
