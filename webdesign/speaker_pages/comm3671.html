Title: Aligning Incentives in the Multi-level Health Care System

Author: Hui Zhang, PhD student, Virginia Tech, 236 Durham Hall, Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, VA, 24060, United States of America, corinnaz@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Christian Wernz

Year: 2013

Abstract: Current incentive structures in the U.S. health care system contribute to the overuse of diagnostic technologies, such as Computed Tomography. Using multiscale decision theory, we analyze the interaction between health care stakeholders across multiple levels. We identify incentive mechanism given multiscale interdependencies that can align stakeholders?actions. Our findings can inform incentive alignment for Accountable Care Organizations and the Medicare Shared Savings Program.
