Title: Valuing Renewable Energy Resources
Author: Michael Pavlin, Assistant Professor, Wilfrid Laurier University, 75 University Avenue West, Waterloo, ON, Canada, Michael.Pavlin06@Rotman.Utoronto.Ca
Coauthor(s): John Birge
Year: 2012
Abstract: Wind, solar, and other renewable energy resources such as tides and rain have intermittent availability that makes effective operation and valuation challenging. We consider the valuation of wind generation for electricity given the structure of this market and the risk associated with commitment to production.
