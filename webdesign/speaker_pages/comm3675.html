Title: Observations Along the Road to Business Analytics
Author: Cliff Ragsdale, Bank of America Professor, Virginia Tech, 1007 Pamplin Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24060, United States of America, Cliff.Ragsdale@vt.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Several strategies exist for the integration of business analytics in undergraduate business and MBA degree programs. This paper discusses the changes being contemplated and implemented at the author's institution and discusses reactions of faculty, students, recruiters, and academic advisory boards to date.
