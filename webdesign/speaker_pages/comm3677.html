Title: Enhancing the Value of Business2Business Services

Author: Paul C. van Fenema, Netherlands Defence Academy, Hogeschoollaan 2, Breda, Netherlands, pc.v.fenema@nlda.nl

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study concerns a project in the maritime and offshore sector. Its focus is to reorganize cooperation between regional and major asset-related stakeholders. A method is developed and implemented that pays attention to B2B value cycles, services, and interorganizational and organizational capabilities. Representing organizational, interorganizational and network-level entrepreneurship, Business2Business services are redesigned to achieve strategic objectives of the stakeholders involved.
