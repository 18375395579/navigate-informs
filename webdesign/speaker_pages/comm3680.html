Title: On the Robustness of the Scoring Rule in Multi-attribute Auctions

Author: Otto Koppius, Erasmus University Rotterdam, Rotterdam School of Management, PO Box 1738, Rotterdam, ZH, 3000 DR, Netherlands, OKoppius@rsm.nl

Year: 2008

Abstract: Using multi-attribute reverse auctions in empirical settings requires the elicitation of the buyer’s utility function, or scoring rule. As such scoring rules can be elicited in various ways with potentially different resulting scoring rules, this may affect the outcome of the auction. Using a dataset obtained from a reverse auction provider, we test how sensitive the observed outcomes are to changes in the weights of the scoring rule.
