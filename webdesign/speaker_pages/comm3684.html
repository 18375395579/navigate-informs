Title: Performance Improvements by Implementing Best Practices in an IT Outsourcing Service Provider
Author: William Hefley, Associate Teaching Professor & Associate Director, IT Services Qualification Center, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, hefley@cmu.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: The eSourcing Capability Model for Service Providers (eSCM-SP) is a best practices model for IT-enabled services. This case addresses the impact of eSCM-SP adoption on both service delivery and business effectiveness. This case addresses two units of LG CNS, located in Korea, that first achieved certification against the eSourcing Capability Model for Service Providers (eSCM-SP) in late 2003.
