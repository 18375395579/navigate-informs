Title: Lean Safety Assessment of Airborne Merging & Spacing Technologies

Author: Robert Hemm, Research Fellow, LMI, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, 22102, United States of America, rhemm@lmi.org

Year: 2008

Abstract: Using the assessment of airborne merging and spacing in the terminal area as an example, we describe the functional and quantitative framework for preliminary, “lean?safety assessments (LSAs) of NextGen operational improvements, The assessment includes a system description, hazard/failure identification, and both qualitative and quantitative risk analyses.
