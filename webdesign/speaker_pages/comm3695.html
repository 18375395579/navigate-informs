Title: Panel Academic Search
Author: Diwakar Gupta, Professor, University of Minnesota, 130B Mechanical Engineering, 111 Church Street SE, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, guptad@me.umn.edu
Coauthor(s): Sameer Kumar
Year: 2013
Abstract: The panel will discuss the academic interview process and do’s and don’ts associated with the job search. In addition to comments by current and former search chairs, time will be provided for questions and answers.
