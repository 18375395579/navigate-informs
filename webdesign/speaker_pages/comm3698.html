Title: Reference Groups and Product Design
Author: Jiong Sun, Illinois Institute of Technology, 10 West 35th Street, 18th Floor, Chicago, IL, 60616, United States of America, jsun22@iit.edu
Coauthor(s): Jinhong Xie, Tao Chen
Year: 2013
Abstract: Recent technological advances are creating unprecedented opportunities for consumer technology products to assume a new social function, representing users?social status, in addition to satisfying users?functional needs. We develop a model to study how such reference group effect may impact technology firms?product pricing, design, and targeting strategies in a setting of sequential introduction of upgraded product versions.
