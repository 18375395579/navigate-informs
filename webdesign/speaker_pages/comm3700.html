Title: Scheduling and Control of Individuals in Service Environments
Author: John Goodale, University of Oregon, Lundquist College of Business, Eugene, OR, United States, jgoodale@LCBmail.uoregon.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this paper we consider the employee scheduling problem in service environments in which agents are unique. We introduce a new scheduling model that accounts for individual characteristics and control. We show that solutions to the new formulation are effective in allocating service capacity based on economic performance, and we provide insights into the scheduling issues in this environment.
