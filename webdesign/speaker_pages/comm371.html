Title: Investments in Carbon Capture from Gas-fired Electricity Generation Under Uncertainty

Author: John Bistline, Stanford University, 475 Via Ortega, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, bistline@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Anshuman Sahoo, Stefan Reichelstein, Stephen Comello

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research investigates how uncertainty will impact the deployment of carbon capture and storage (CCS) on gas-fired electricity generation. Using a real options approach, this work examines how uncertainties in natural gas price paths, climate policy stringency, cost of CCS-related technologies, and costs of competing zero-emission technologies will influence investment dynamics from the perspective of a firm specializing in merchant electricity generation.
