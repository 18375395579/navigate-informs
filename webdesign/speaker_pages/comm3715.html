Title: Impact of Bayesian Learning and Externality on Strategic Investment
Author: Wenxin Xu, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Urbana, IL, United States of America, wxu9@illinois.edu
Coauthor(s): Anupam Agrawal, Suresh Muthulingam, Dharma Kwon
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a game of investment in uncertain projects. There exists postive and negative externality between the two firms' investments. We find that the interplay between externalities and Bayesian learning lead to counterintuitive results of firms' entry strategies.
