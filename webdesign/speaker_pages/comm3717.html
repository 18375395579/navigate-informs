Title: Martingale Dual Methods for Zero-Sum Games

Author: Chun Wang, Columbia University, 500 West 120th Street, Mudd 323, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, cw2519@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Martin Haugh

Year: 2012

Abstract: Martingale duality based on information relaxations has recently been developed for zero-sum optimal stopping games. In this talk, we consider generalizing this duality for more general zero-sum dynamic games.
