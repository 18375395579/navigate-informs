Title: Trust in E-commerce

Author: Rosine Hanna, Graduate Student, Portland State University, Eng. & Tech Management, PO Box 751, Portland, OR, 97207, United States of America, rosine@pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Charles Weber

Year: 2007

Abstract: An investigation of trust in e-commerce finds trust in traditional trust mechanisms is greater than trust in Internet-based institutions among the IT-savvy and among non-IT-savvy. Residents of nations that are not completely industrialized expressed severe distrust in e-commerce, where as residents of industrialized nations trust e-commerce. Policy makers in developing nations must build trust in institutions, if e-commerce is to be used as a vehicle for economic development.
