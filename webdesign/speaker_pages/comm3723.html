Title: Modeling the Contract Manufacturers' Strategic Crossroad

Author: David Wu, Dean of Engineering, Lehigh University, 19 Memorial Drive West, Bethlehem, PA, 18017, United States, sdw1@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Chung-Yee Lee, Banu Gemici

Year: 2007

Abstract: Contract Manufacturer (CM) often faces the strategic options of devoting its capacity entirely to a brand-carrying customer (BC), or leveraging its capabilities to develop its own market presence. We propose an analytical model that analyzes market demand dynamics, process and resource synergies, as well as market-entry timing.
