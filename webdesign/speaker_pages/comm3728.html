Title: The LRIM Tool for Evaluating the GS1 Data Standards Implementations at Healthcare Providers

Author: Vijith Varghese, Postdoctoral Fellow, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering, Fayetteville, AR, United States of America, vvarghe@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Ron Rardin, Danny Dixon, Nebil Buyurgan, Mehmet Serdar Kilinc, Kaan Okyay, Paiman Farrokhvar

Year: 2012

Abstract: Supply chain automation complemented with data standards adoption, streamline operations and improve efficiency in healthcare supply chain. However, the struggle to justify returns on those investments hinders the widespread adoption. In this presentation, we describe the Levels, Requirements and Impact Model (LRIM), a spreadsheet decision tool that helps prospective provider adopters meet the critical need for information about the potential returns on investments in GS1 data standards.
