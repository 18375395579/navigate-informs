Title: Google Online Marketing Challenge Scoring and Results

Author: Alex Scott, Quant Analyst, Google, Inc, 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy, Mountain View, CA, 94043, United States of America, alexscott@google.com

Coauthor(s): Lizzy Van Alstine

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Google Online Marketing Challenge is a global student AdWords competition developed by professors from across the globe in collaboration with Google. We will give an overview of the challenge and then briefly discuss the scoring algorithm employed to score student Advertisers around the world. We will also show distributions and results by region for the competition and end with key insights we took away from the competition and its results.
