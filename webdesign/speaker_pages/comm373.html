Title: Incentive Contracts Between 4PLs and 3PLs: An Application of the Principal-agents Model

Author: Qin Zhu, City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon, Hong Kong SAR, Kowloon, Hong Kong - PRC, qinzhu4@student.cityu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Y.K., Richard FUNG

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper applies the principal-agent theory to the logistics industry, studying the horizontal cooperation between 4PLs and 3PLs through incentive contracts, where the compensation is a combination of a fixed fee and a bonus based on the performances of the 3PLs, in terms of the service delivery rate and customer satisfaction level.
