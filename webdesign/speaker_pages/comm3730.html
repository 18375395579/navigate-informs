Title: The Optimization Prize for Young Researchers

Author:

Coauthor(s):

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Optimization Prize for Young Researchers, established in 1998 and administered by the Optimization Society of INFORMS. It is awarded in even numbered years to one (or more) young researchers for the most outstanding paper in optimization that is submitted to or published in a refereed professional journal. The prize serves as an esteemed recognition of promising colleagues who are at the beginning of their academic or industrial career.
