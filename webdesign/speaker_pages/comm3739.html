Title: Teaching Business Analytics: Lessons-Learned

Author: Jim Van Scotter, Associate Professor, Louisiana State University, Dept of ISDS, Baton Rouge, LA, 70803, United States of America, jvanscot@lsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Helmut Schneider

Year: 2013

Abstract: An M.S.in Analytics program needs more than “big data?and sophisticated software to create value. Students and faculty must focus on understanding the business case for analytics, the potential gains and implementation costs, and how to communicate results effectively to decision makers. Evaluation strategies must emphasize team work and results over individual testing. Finally, establishing and maintaining close relationships with companies that use analytics is critically important.
