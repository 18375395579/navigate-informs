Title: Optimization Model and Decision Support to Implement Health Interventions to Diminish Readmissions

Author: Andres Garcia-Arce, University of South Florida, 4202 E. Fowler Avenue, ENB 118, Tampa, Fl, 33620, United States of America, andresg@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Jose Luis Zayas-Castro

Year: 2013

Abstract: The 30-day preventable readmissions rate is a concept that has been linked to a problem of quality of service in the American healthcare system. From FY 2012 CMS has established a penalization factor in the IPPS for those hospitals that have higher rates of readmissions for three diseases. This work aims to study a new option from the health intervention approach that could help in the long run to develop a more sustainable improvement in the performance of the healthcare system.
