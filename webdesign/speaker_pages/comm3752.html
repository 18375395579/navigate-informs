Title: Analysis and Modeling of the U.S. Marine Corps OIF and OEF Ground Equipment Reset Requirements

Author: Matt Reuter, Head, Logistics Operations Analysis Branch, USMC / Installations and Logistics Department, HQMC, 2 Navy Annex, Washington, DC, 20380-1775, United States of America, matthew.reuter@usmc.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: The fulfillment of OIF and OEF ground equipment Reset requirements is critical to future warfighting capabilities. Modeling the cost of ground equipment Reset supports the budgetary requirements of the Planning, Programming, Budgeting and Execution System. Modeling and analysis of the ground equipment Retrograde and Reset process supports operational-level planning and decision-making.
