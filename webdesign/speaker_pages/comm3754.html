Title: Optimal Multiperiod Portfolio Selection with Trading Costs and Parameter Uncertainty

Author: Alberto Martin-Utrera, University Carlos III of Madrid, Calle Madrid, 126, Getafe, Madrid, 28903, Spain, amutrera@est-econ.uc3m.es

Coauthor(s): Victor DeMiguel, Javier Nogales

Year: 2013

Abstract: We address the effects of parameter uncertainty in multiperiod portfolio selection in the presence of transaction costs. We propose a novel four-fund portfolio that takes parameter uncertainty optimally into account. This portfolio diversifies the effects of estimation risk across four different funds and mitigates the impact of parameter uncertainty in the investor’s expected utility. Finally, we characterize the out-of-sample performance of four-fund portfolios in an empirical application.
