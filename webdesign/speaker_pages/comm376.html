Title: A Case for a New Financial Modeling Metaphor

Author: Dennis Moritz, Advantage for Analysts LLC, 2 Harrison Street, 6th Floor, San Francisco, CA, 94105, dennis.moritz@advantageforanalysts.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: The evolution of spreadsheet technology has been paralleled by an increasing complexity of modeling needs. We propose that the modeling and execution of sophisticated financial structures require a new modeling metaphor. We outline and provide examples of a new metaphor and the advanced analytics needed to support contemporary financial decisions.
