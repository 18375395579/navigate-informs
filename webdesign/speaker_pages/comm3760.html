Title: Demand Estimation under Substitution and Basket Shopping Effects

Author: Vidya Mani, Assistant Professor, Pennsylvania State University, University Park, State College, PA, 16802, United States of America, vmani@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Douglas Thomas, Saurabh Bansal

Year: 2013

Abstract: We use detailed transactional data from a big box retailer to estimate demand when there are substitution and basket shopping effects. We explore the implications of these effects on changing assortments for remodeled stores.
