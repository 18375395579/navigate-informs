Title: Understanding the Patterns of Inter- and Intra-day Price Movements of Emerging Stock Markets in Asia

Author: Yoon Hak Lee, KAIST, Yusung-gu Gusung-Dong, Daejeon, Korea, Republic of, yhl3145@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): WooChang Kim, Young Re Song, Yong Jun Yang

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper explains the dynamics of inter- and intra-day return series of emerging stock markets in Asia by conducting hypothesis tests, regression analysis, and simulation while focusing on spillover and overreaction effects before and after the 2008 financial crisis. The results indicate a pattern specific to emerging markets in Asia; we find that a positive performance of the U.S. market has a larger effect on Asia markets than a negative one, which stands out from general market behavior.
