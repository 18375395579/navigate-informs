Title: Streaming Intelligence: Machine Learning and Reasoning in Daily Life

Author: Eric Horvitz, Principal Researcher, Microsoft Research, One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA, United States of America, horvitz@microsoft.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: I will discuss how technical developments and trends are providing a fertile climate for integrating machine learning and reasoning into applications and services. I will review several illustrative efforts and prototypes that highlight promises with the flow of machine intelligence into daily life, including efforts in traffic forecasting, mobile commerce, search, operating systems, and call routing.
