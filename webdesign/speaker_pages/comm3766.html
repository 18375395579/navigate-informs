Title: Switching Options: Empirical Evidence from the Electricity Industry

Author: Stein-Erik Fleten, Professor, Norwegian University of Science and Technology, Industrial Economics and Technology Mgm, Alfred Getz v 3, Trondheim, NO-7491, Norway, stein-erik.fleten@iot.ntnu.no

Coauthor(s): Carl Ullrich, Erik Haugom

Year: 2012

Abstract: We examine empirically the real options to shutdown, startup, and abandon peaking power plants. The unique dataset details information for 1,337 individual plants for the period 2001?009. Reduced form regressions provide evidence of real options effects for shutdown and abandonment decisions. Moreover, uncertainty about the outcome of ongoing deregulation in retail electricity markets significantly decreases the likelihood of shutting down operating plants.
