Title: Optimal Strategies for Exploiting Product Innovations

Author: Sinan Erzurumlu, Doctoral Student, University of Texas at Austin, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, Sinan.Erzurumlu@phd.mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Karthik Ramachandran, Stephen Gilbert

Year: 2006

Abstract: We analyze the issue of when and how an innovator would transfer its technology to another firm with which he also competes in the product market. Our analysis focuses on the structure of the network effects, the transferability of the innovation, and the market position of the innovator, and we explore how these parameters affect the optimal strategy for exploiting an innovation.
