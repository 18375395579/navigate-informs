Title: Selecting Operational Improvements for European Air Traffic Management

Author: Yael Grushka-Cockayne, London Business School, Regents Park, London, UK, NW106DP, United Kingdom, ygrushka.phd2003@london.edu

Coauthor(s): Zeger Degraeve, Bert De Reyck

Year: 2006

Abstract: We develop a multi-stakeholder, multi-objective decision model for Eurocontrol, the European Air Traffic Management organization, in efforts to cope with forecasted increase in traffic, while maintaining safety and protecting the environment. We frame this strategic multicriteria problem and develop a method for eliciting stakeholders' preferences across a variety of criteria, combining qualitative and quantitative assessments, to determine a recommended set of system enhancements.
