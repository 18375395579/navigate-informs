Title: Predicting the Clinical Outcome in Patients with Traumatic Brain Injury Using Clinical Pathway Score

Author: Monica Puertas, University of South Florida, 18125 Birdwater Dr., Tampa, FL, 33647, United States of America, mpuertas@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Fabri, Jose Luis Zayas-Castro, Jennifer Mendoza-Alonzo

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Veterans Affairs Hospital treats patients with Traumatic Brain Injuries. These patients have major motor and cognitive disabilities. The progress is assessed using Functional Independence Measures and Clinical Pathway. This work develops a clinical outcome predictive model. The results will allow healthcare providers to improve the current resource management, as well as to provide the family more accurate and timely information about the status and needs of the patient.
