Title: A Market Design Approach to the Adoption of Children in Pennsylvania

Author: Mustafa Akan, Assistant Professor of Operations Management, Carnegie Mellon University, United States of America, akan@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Onur Kesten, Utku Unver

Year: 2013

Abstract: Adoption is an important phenomenon in the U.S. Notwithstanding the growing demand and prominence of the issue, little is known about what assignment protocol needs to be employed by agencies. Inspired by our ongoing collaboration with Pennsylvania's Statewide Adoption Network, we study the incentives in child adoption through the lens of operations and economics, and make recommendations for alternative systems that can ameliorate the deficiencies of current assignment plans.
