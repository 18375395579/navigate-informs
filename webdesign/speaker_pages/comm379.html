Title: Risk Aversion in Decision-making for Strategic and Tactical Investment Options

Author: Scott Mathews, Associate Technical Fellow, The Boeing Company, PO BOX 3707 MC 42-54, Seattle, WA, 98124, United States of America, scott.h.mathews@boeing.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: There is frequently confusion in corporate decision-making about whether investment choices contemplated by senior management are strategic or tactical options. Confusion about the intent and structure of the two investment choices can result in muddled decision-making and poor project governance structuring. New option modeling tools help simplify the quantification of risk-aversion impact under both strategic and tactical investment options, and provide insight to better project governance.
