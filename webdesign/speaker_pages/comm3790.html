Title: Effect of Resource Capacity on Utilization in Hospital Emergency Departments

Author: Jillian Berry Jaeker, Harvard Business School, Soldiers Field, Boston, MA, 02163, United States of America, jjaeker@hbs.edu

Coauthor(s): Anita Tucker, Michael Lee

Year: 2013

Abstract: Our work focuses on how the availability of ultrasounds (US), a medical test, in a hospital emergency department (ED) affects their demand by physicians. We take advantage of a natural experiment at two Rhode Island EDs, which are staffed by the same physicians and located within 5 miles of each other. Over the course of our study period, US availability was increased at one hospital and not the other.
