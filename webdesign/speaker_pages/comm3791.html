Title: Optimal Screening Strategies of Patients on the Kidney Transplant Waiting List

Author: Alireza Sabouri, University of British Columbia, Vancouver, BC, Canada, alireza.sabouri@sauder.ubc.ca

Coauthor(s): Steven Shechter, Tim Huh

Year: 2012

Abstract: The health condition of patients on the kidney transplant waiting list deteriorates while they are waiting for an organ arrival and hence they may no longer be suitable for transplant. Therefore, transplant centers screen waiting patients at various intervals to identify ineligible patients. We propose a model for finding screening strategies that minimizes the probability of offering a transplant to ineligible patients.
