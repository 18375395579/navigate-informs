Title: Panel Discussion: Quality and Productivity Improvement in Nanomanufacturing

Author: Qiang Huang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, 4202 East Fowler Ave., ENB118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States, huangq@eng.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Way Kuo, Ashok Kumar, JC Lu, C. F. Jeff Wu, Placid Ferreira

Year: 2007

Abstract: Nanomanufacturing poses significant research challenges for quality and productivity improvement. A panel of faculty will introduce nanomanufacturing and discuss the challenges and research opportunities in designed experiment, reliability, and process control.
