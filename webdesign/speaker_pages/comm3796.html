Title: Development of AHP Methodology to Improve the Resilience of Barranquilla International Airport

Author: Luis Ramirez, Docente, Universidad Autonoma del caribe, Cra 46 No 88-15, Barranquilla, Colombia, luis.ramirez@uac.edu.co

Coauthor(s): Fabian Sanchez, Andrea Roa, Daniel Romero, Daniela Esmeral

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper shows the implementation of the methodology AHP (Analytic hierarchy process) to improve resilience to factors that alter the normal operations of the International airport of the city of Barranquilla Colombia. The paper specifies the internal and external factors, social environmental or mechanical that influence operations, and how to evaluate the proposed strategies to build resilience to these.
