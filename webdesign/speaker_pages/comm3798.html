Title: Severe Weather and Automobile Assembly Productivity
Author: Marcelo Olivares, Columbia Business School/ Universidad de Chile, Republica 701, Santiago, Chile, mo2338@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Gerard Cachon, Santiago Gallino
Year: 2013
Abstract: Using panel data on weekly production of US assembly plants over a period of 10 years, we estimate the impact of severe weather on the productivity of these plants. We find that severe weather can add substantial variability to the production of new vehicles. Using our estimates, we project the potential impact of climate change over the next decades.
