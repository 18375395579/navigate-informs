Title: Evaluating Emerging Technologies for National Technology Policy

Author: Pisek Gerdsri, Doctoral Student, Portland State University, PO Box 751, Eng. & Tech Mgmt., Portland, OR, 97207, United States, pisekg@etm.pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Dundar Kocaoglu

Year: 2007

Abstract: A decision model assesses the contributions of emerging technologies and evaluates their impact on the country’s objective. The relative impact value of each technology is determined by a six-level hierarchical decision model (national objective, industry’s goals, industry’s strategies, technological benefits, technological factors, and technologies). The model is applied to a case study of nanotechnology in agriculture.
