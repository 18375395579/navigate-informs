Title: Making Strategic Sourcing Decisions to Support Restructuring Efforts

Author: Erica Klampfl, Operations Research Group, Ford Research & Advanced Engineering, Room 3011, RIC Building, MD 2122, 2101 Village Rd, Dearborn, MI, 48124, eklampfl@ford.com

Coauthor(s): Mike Wolcott, Chip McDaniel, Yakov Fradkin

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a method for enabling strategic decisions to support restructuring of a portfolio of automotive components, where part cost varies with plant utilization, freight and re-sourcing costs are minimized, and parts may be constrained to particular families. We provide a case study for successful application of this method.
