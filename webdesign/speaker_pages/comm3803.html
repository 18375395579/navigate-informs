Title: Optimal Inbound Trailer Allocation at a Crossdock ?Optimizing Operations and Balancing Workload

Author: Claudia Rosales, University of Cincinnati, 523 Lindner Hall PO Box 210130, Cincinnati, OH, 45221, United States, rosalecr@email.uc.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Fry, Rajesh Radhakrishnan

Year: 2007

Abstract: Transfreight, LLC is a third-party logistics provider that supports Toyota’s lean manufacturing operations in North America. Our work provides the optimal allocation of inbound trailers to docks at a crossdocking facility operated by Transfreight. We focus on improving the efficiency of operations as well as balancing workload among crossdock workers. Model implementation is performed using both a spreadsheet-based solver and CPLEX. The advantages/disadvantages of each option are discussed.
