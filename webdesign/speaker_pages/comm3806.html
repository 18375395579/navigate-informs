Title: Tales from the Decision Analysis Industrial Complex

Author: Chris Dalton, CEO, Syncopation Software, 6 State Street, Suite 402, Bangor, ME, 04401, United States of America, cdalton@syncopation.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk will define what it means to be a Decision Analysis based business, and give examples of successful and unsuccessful DA business models. At the end of the session, we will have a panel discussion with the speakers and other members of the DA business community.
