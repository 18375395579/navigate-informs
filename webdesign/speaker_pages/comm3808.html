Title: Roles of Collective Classification for Data Mining Tasks

Author: David Aha, Head, Adaptive Systems Section, Naval Research Laboratory, Code 5514, 4555 Overlook Ave., SW, Washington, DC, 20375, United States of America, david.aha@nrl.navy.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: One approach for mining entity and event relations is to use collective classification, a learning method that does not assume data are drawn independently from an identical distribution. I will summarize recent research on this topic and its application in our projects concerning maritime vessel activity analysis and chat room message highlighting.
