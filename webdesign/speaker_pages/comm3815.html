Title: Analytical Support to 3ACR in Iraq

Author: Michael Kwinn, Associate Professor, DSE, USMA, West Point, NY, 10996, United States, michael.kwinn@usma.edu

Coauthor(s): Jason Wolter

Year: 2006

Abstract: Authors will present analytical work conducted in Iraq in support of the 3rd Armor Cavalry Regiment whose operations in Tal Afar were noted by many senior leaders, including President Bush. Analysis dconducted on operations center layout and design and operational level assessments.
