Title: Panel Discussion: Research Challenges in Aviation
Author: Stefan Karisch, VP Operations Research, Jeppesen Systems AB, 1800 McGill College Avenue, Suite 1930, Montreal, QC, H3A 3J6, Canada, stefan.karisch@jeppesen.com
Coauthor(s): Barry Smith, Amy Cohn, Michael Ball, Timothy L. Jacobs
Year: 2007
Abstract: The panel discussion will address current research challenges in aviation. Representatives from industry, regulators, suppliers, and academia will present current and future challenges for the research community, and engage the audience in a discussion.
