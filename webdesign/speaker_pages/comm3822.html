Title: Recent Improvements in IBM ILOG CPLEX Optimization Studio
Author: Tobias Achterberg, CPLEX Optimization Developer, IBM, Zuse Institute Berlin, Takustr. 7, Berlin, 14195, Germany, achterberg@de.ibm.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present new features that have been added to CPLEX and give detailed benchmarking results that demonstrate the performance improvements in recent CPLEX versions.
