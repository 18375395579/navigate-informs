Title: An Airline Revenue Management Project
Author: Anton Kleywegt, Associate Professor, ISyE - Georgia Tech, 755 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, anton@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Jim Dai, Weijun Ding, Yi Zhang, Xinchang Wang, Haiyue Yu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We discuss a revenue management project with a large airline for a very busy and competitive market. We discuss how we estimated models of customer arrivals, competitor decisions, customer choices, booking to ticketing conversion, cancellations, no-shows, and go-shows. These were used as input in an optimization model to optimize fareclass opening decisions throughout the booking horizon.
