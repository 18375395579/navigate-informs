Title: Product Counterfeiting - A Buying Personnel Behavioral Model

Author: Jillian Watson, PhD Candidate, Clemson University, 100 Sirrine Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States of America, jilliad@clemson.edu

Coauthor(s): Aleda Roth

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research presents the development of a behavioral experiment to examine how buying personnel incentives and other factors interact to influence the buyer's likeliness to accept a situation where product counterfeit risk is high.
