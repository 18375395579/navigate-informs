Title: Modeling Emerging Challenges in Healthcare

Author: Diwakar Gupta, Professor, University of Minnesota, 130B Mechanical Engineering, 111 Church Street SE, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, guptad@me.umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Sandra Potthoff

Year: 2013

Abstract: In an effort to curb growth in healthcare expenditures, payers are increasingly demanding better health outcomes. They are also transferring risk to providers by moving away from fee-for-service to other payment models, such as prospective payment and bundled payment paradigms. These changes are serving as catalysts for innovation in service delivery. This talk will focus on new challenges for hospital administrators, relevant OM literature, and authors?efforts to model emerging challenges.
