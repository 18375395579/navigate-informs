Title: Benchmarking of Municipality Service in Denmark
Author: Peter Bogetoft, Copenhagen Business School, Copenhagen, Denmark, pb.eco@cbs.dk
Year: 2013
Abstract: We use DEA with weight restrictions to construct a benefit-of-the-doubt measure of the overall service levels in Danish municipalities. We also introduce costs to get an evaluation of their efficiency. Lastly, we show how Interactive Benchmarking IB support learning and strategic planning in the individual municipalities and we discuss how the municipalities have reacted to the results and the tool.
