Title: Design of Online Course Interface for Student Usability and Satisfaction
Author: Renee Butler, Assistant Professor, Southern Polytechnic Stat Univ, Division of Engineering, 1100 South Marietta Pkwy, Marietta, GA, 30060, United States of America, rbutler@spsu.edu
Coauthor(s): Christina Scherrer
Year: 2008
Abstract: In recent years, online education has increased dramatically, however there has not been much study of the usability and satisfaction of the online student interface. We study current online course interfaces from several disciplines to generate sample interfaces that are tested for usability and satisfaction. The goal of this presentation is to present several steps instructors/designers can implement to increase the satisfaction of students with the technology used in online courses.
