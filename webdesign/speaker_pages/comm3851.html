Title: Optimal R&D Portfolios: A Risk Management Perspective

Author: Leonardo Santiago, Federal University of Minas Gerais, Av. Antonio Carlos, 6627, Pampulha. Belo Horizonte, MG., 30270-901, Brazil, lsantiago@ufmg.br

Coauthor(s): Pirooz Vakili

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider the problem of optimal R&D project selection and portfolio formation based on a two-stage static model. Strategies for risk management, the influence of the degree of risk aversion of the firm on project selection at each stage, and a comparison with other ranking and selection methods will be discussed.
