Title: The Development of an Industry-focused Research Center Focused on Medical Devices

Author: Ozan Ozcan, University of South Florida, Fairway Oaks Apts., 14231 Les Palms Circle Apt No:202, Tampa, FL, 33613, United States, oozcan@mail.usf.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: The role of the university in promoting local innovation and economic growth is oftentimes limited to technology transfer via patenting and licensing intellectual property to local firms. We will focus on the current development of the Center for Applied Research in Medical Devices (CareMed) at the Univ. of South Florida, the structure of the center and how the university interacts with local industry to benefit the local economy and contrast this with other regionally located research centers.
