Title: Pre-operative Activities and Operating Room Planning: A Local Hospital Department Case Study

Author: Paolo Tubertini, PhD Student, University of Bologna, Viale Risorgimento 2, Bologna, 40136, Italy, paolo.tubertini@unibo.it

Coauthor(s): Andrea Lodi

Year: 2012

Abstract: A local hospital department has to jointly plan operating room assignment to surgeons and patients admission planning taking in consideration, for a subset of patients, some compulsory pre-operative activities such as outpatient anesthetist appointment and blood pre-storage. We propose and test an MIP model with real data instances and we compare the results with actual planning procedures.
