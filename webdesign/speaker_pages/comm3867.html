Title: Detection of Changes in Blogger Sentiment using SVM-based Control Charts
Author: Sungim Lee, Associate Professor, Dankook University, Korea, Republic of, silee@dankook.ac.kr
Year: 2012
Abstract: These days hundreds of millions of Internet users show personal emotions, feelings or thoughts through Blog posts on social media services. In this paper, we will show how to detect the changes in the blogger sentiment data using mulitvariate control charts.
