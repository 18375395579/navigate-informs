Title: Sustainability for a Manufacturing SME in Ohio: Women Perspective

Author: Anne McMahon, Professor Emeritus, Youngstown State University, One University Plaza, WCBA 3303, Youngstown, OH, 44555, United States of America, ammcmahon@ysu.edu

Coauthor(s): Birsen Karpak

Year: 2012

Abstract: This study implements an Analytical Network Process (ANP) based framework developed for small /medium manufacturing enterprises to prioritize sustainability measures to a woman owned manufacturing company in Ohio. A female expert has judged the impact of each factor on other success factors for her business. Sustainability measures are prioritized. The study results will guide the authors as they pursue distinctive issues related to women owned small businesses in Ohio.
