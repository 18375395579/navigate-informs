Title: Food Security for Emerging Economies ?An OR Perspective

Author: G Raghuram, Professor, Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad, Wing 15, IIMA Campus, IIMA Campus, Ahmedabad, 380015, India, graghu@iimahd.ernet.in

Coauthor(s): Ankan Pal

Year: 2012

Abstract: Food security is provided by the Food Corporation of India which procured 50 million tons of food grains in 2011-12 with a spend of over Rs 80,000 crores. Towards achieving this, the primary activity is managing logistics, including the type of storage (bulk/bagged, covered/open, own/outsourced), allocation from procurement to storage to demand points, mode choice, and quality management. This paper provides a perspective on OR methods which can help achieve food security while optimizing costs.
