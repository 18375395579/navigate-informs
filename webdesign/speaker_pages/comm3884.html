Title: Quantitative EEG Analysis in Epilepsy Research
Author: Petros Xanthopoulos, University of Central Florida, Department of Industrial Engineering, Orlando, FL, United States of America, petrosx@ucf.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this presentation we will discuss some of the key applications of signal processing and machine learning in quantitative electroencephalographic (EEG) analysis. Topics to be discussed include brain EEG connectivity, non linear timeseries analysis and applications of wavelets in scoring and predicting and discriminating between different EEG states.
