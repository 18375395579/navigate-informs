Title: Development of Planning Tools for the Supply Chain of Fresh Produce
Author: Omar Ahumada, Research Associate, ASU, Dept. of Industrial Eng., P.O. Box 1906, Tempe, AZ, 85202, United States of America, omar.ahumada@asu.edu
Coauthor(s): J Rene Villalobos
Year: 2007
Abstract: We discuss an analytical planning tool for the fresh produce industry that will use relevant information to render a plan for growing, harvesting and distributing the products in an agricultural cycle. The objectives is to help the farmer make decisions based on traditional factors such historical price information and resource availability, but also on those factors that are usually not considered by the farmers such as price dynamics, transportation and inventory costs.
