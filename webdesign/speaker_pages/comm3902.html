Title: Product Quality or Service Quality? The Impact of Customer Heterogeneity

Author: Jose Guajardo, Assistant Professor, Haas School of Business, University of California Berkeley, Berkeley, United States of America, jguajardo@haas.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Morris Cohen

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study how customer heterogeneity influences the effect of product quality and service quality perceptions on customer loyalty, in an application in the consumer electronics industry. We find that customer characteristics have a significant moderating effect, and document significant heterogeneity in service-sensitivity in the population.
