Title: An Empirical Analysis of Software Auctions
Author: Christian Terwiesch, Professor, The Wharton School, 3730 Walnut St, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, terwiesch@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Elena Krasnokutskaya
Year: 2007
Abstract: We econometrically study a global market place for software projects. We analyze how the buyer is choosing which coder bid to accept. We are specifically interested in the role of location and distance, two attributes that have been shown in traditional research on trade, but require a new interpretation in the digital world.
