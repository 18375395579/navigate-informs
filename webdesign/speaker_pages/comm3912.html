Title: Maintaining and Evolving a Taxonomy with Social Tagging

Author: Harris Wu, Old Dominion University, hwu@odu.edu

Coauthor(s): Kurt Maly, Mohammed Zubair

Year: 2008

Abstract: Browsing large digital collections can benefit from well-maintained taxonomies. This presentation talks about how social tagging can be utilized systematically in maintaining and evolving a taxonomy: 1) to complement a taxonomy with new category entries, 2) to categorize items into the taxonomy.
