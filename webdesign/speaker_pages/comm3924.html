Title: Secrets of Success with Optimization
Author: Robert Dell, Professor, Naval Postgraduate School, Operations Research Department, Monterey, CA, 93940, United States of America, Dell@nps.edu
Coauthor(s): Gerald Brown
Year: 2008
Abstract: Rick Rosenthal was always excited to see operations research at work in the real world and had developed a list of secrets for success with optimization. This talk will present Rick’s list using a presentation Rick recently prepared.
