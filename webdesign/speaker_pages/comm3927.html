Title: The Transformation of Innovation Ecosystems in Global Metropolitan Areas: A Data-Driven Perspective
Author: Rahul Basole, Associate Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 85 Fifth St NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, basole@gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Martha Russell, Jukka Huhtamaki, Kaisa Still, Neil Rubens
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study provides a comparative, multiscopic study of the structural transformation of innovation ecosystems in select major U.S. and worldwide metropolitan areas from 1990-2012. Our results reveal distinct patterns of ecosystem formation, growth, and evolution. We complement our findings using an interactive network visualization approach.
