Title: An Intelligent Workflow Engine for Critical Parts Management
Author: Sreeram Ramakrishnan, IBM, sramakr@us.ibm.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: It has been observed that quarter-end business pressures yield revenue opportunities, which are jeopardized by critical parts shortages. A real-time, automated workflow tool, based on communicating finite-state machines, that links departments or functions with critical parts shortages to those whose supply will support revenue generation is discussed in this talk.
