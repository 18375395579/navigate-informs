Title: Smart Services in the Austrian Biomass Boiler Industry: A Smart Services use Case Study

Coauthor(s): Stefan Kroath
Year: 2012
Abstract: It can be observed that the biomass boiler industry uses service approaches which are very much reactive oriented. In this case study one of the leading industrial biomass boiler manufacturer with worldwide export shows ways how to use Smart Services to improve the customer contact (getting closer to customer) and to optimize internal service process. A technical concept is presented and strategic considerations are highlighted.
