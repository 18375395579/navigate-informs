Title: Percentile Optimization in Portfolio Management
Author: Aurelie Thiele, Associate Professor, Lehigh University, aurelie.thiele@lehigh.edu
Coauthor(s): Elcin Cetinkaya
Year: 2012
Abstract: We investigate portfolio management problems where the decision-maker seeks to maximize a left-tail percentile of the return distribution. We discuss data-driven techniques to incorporate the percentile in a tractable formulation and present insights into the resulting strategy, both from a theoretical standpoint and in numerical experiments.
