Title: Operations Planning with Life-Cycle Environmental Considerations
Author: Ravi Subramanian, Assistant Professor, College of Management, Georgia Tech, 800 West Peachtree Street NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, ravi.subramanian@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Brian Talbot, Sudheer Gupta
Year: 2006
Abstract: Taking the life-cycle view, we consider tradeoffs to the manufacturer as a result of environmental regulation starting with product design, through production, to the end of the product’s economic life. We present a rich and flexible modeling approach that facilitates optimal decisions within a spectrum of compliance options.
