Title: Facility Layout and Design for Mass Dispensing for Biological Attack or Pandemic Outbreaks
Author: Hannah Smalley, Georgia Institute of Technology, Inustrial and Systems Engineering, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, hkolberg3@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Eva Lee
Year: 2008
Abstract: We analyze the clinic layout for mass dispensing of the general population against biological attack or pandemic outbreaks. This work is in collaboration with CDC researchers.
