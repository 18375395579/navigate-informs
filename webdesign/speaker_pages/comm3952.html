Title: Tracking Political Extremists through the Web: The Case of White Extremist Movement

Author: Jean Mark Gawron, Professor, San Diego State University, San DIego, CA, United States of America, gawron@mail.sdsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Dipak Gupta, Brian Spitzberg, Kellen Stevens, Ming Hsiang Tsou, An Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: These days political extremism is spreading through the Internet, by creating virtual communities various extremist groups attempt to recruit new members. This paper combines linguistic tools with the insights of social psychology to develops a new methodology for understanding how these movements gain strength of they disappear.
