Title: Research Opportunities Related to Behavioral Operations in Healthcare
Author: Anita Tucker, Associate Professor, Harvard Business School, Morgan Hall 413, Soldiers Field, Boston, MA, 02163, United States of America, atucker@hbs.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Healthcare organizations struggle to provide high quality care as efficiently as possible. These organizations seek to standardize the work of healthcare providers while also maintaining flexibility and creativity to treat patients as individuals. Patient compliance is also a major concern. This talk will explore research opportunities to use field and laboratory to examine issues related to behavioral operations in healthcare settings.
