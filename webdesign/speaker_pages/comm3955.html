Title: The Effect of Team Diverity and Structure on Project Termination
Author: Stylianos Kavadias, Georgia Institute of Technology, College of Management, 800 West Peachtree Street, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308-0520, United States, Stylianos.Kavadias@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Nektarios Oraiopoulos
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this presentation we analyze the impact of new information interpretation (“interpretative scheme? on the project stopping decision. We view NPD teams as groups of decision makers that evaluate the incoming information differently, and therefore result in diverse proposals on the project continuation. Our results reveal an interpretation regime under which the team diversity delays stopping consistently. Thus, we offer an alternative explanation to escalation of commitment phenomena.
