Title: Improving Distributor Performance in IBM's Extended Supply Chain

Author: Mamnoon Jamil, Dr., IBM, 525 Lincoln Drive West, Marlton, NJ 08053, NJ, 08053, United States of America, mamnoon@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Markus Ettl

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk we present a service-oriented architecture based collaboration system that was recently implemented in IBM's extended supply chain to improve the operational efficiency of distribution channel partners. We also discuss the use of innovative forecasting and inventory analytics for risk-optimized optimized replenishment decisions based on up-to-date inventory and channel sales information.
