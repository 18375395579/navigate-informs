Title: Fracture Failure Analysis of Advanced High Strength Steel by Utilizing Microscopic Properties

Author: Qingyu Yang, Assistant Professor, Wayne State University, 4815 Fourth Street, Detroit, MI, 48202, United States of America, qyang@wayne.edu

Coauthor(s): Nailong Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: Advanced high-strength steels (AHSS) has received increased interest in a large variety of industry applications due to its light-weight structures without compromising safety. The properties of AHSS steel are affected not only by the multi-phase material properties, by also by material microstructure. In this research, we developed an efficient model to analyze the failures of AHSS by utilizing its microscopic properties that are derived from its microscopic images.
