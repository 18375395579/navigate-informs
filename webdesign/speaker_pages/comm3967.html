Title: Defense Reform in the Republic of the Philippines

Author: Greg Parlier, Dr., SAIC, 255 Avian Lane, Madison, AL, 35758, United States, GREG.H.PARLIER@saic.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation is a summary of defense reform efforts supported by the Institute for Defense Analyses since the end of the Cold War. Emphasis will be placed upon current efforts, including ongoing support to the Philippine Defense Reform Project, a joint effort undertaken by the United States and the Government of the Philippines during the past four years.
