Title: Practical Technology Forecasting Tools ?A Review of Recent Tools and Applications

Author: Tugrul Daim, Portland State University, Portland, OR, 97201, United States, tugrul@etm.pdx.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper presents a comparative review of recent Technology Forecasting applications. Several new and existing methodologies were introduced into this field in the recent years, providing a wide range of options for researchers and practioners. These methods include bibliometrics, system dynamics and roadmaps. Applications that the paper reviews include optical storage, switches, nano technologies, fuel cells, food safety technologies and interconnect technologies.
