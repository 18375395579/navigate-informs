Title: The Impact of Emerging Technology on Global Sourcing

Author: Howard Sachar, Director, SO Innovation, Americas, IBM Global Services, 17 Skyline Drive, Hawthorne, NY, 10532, United States, hsachar@us.ibm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: As the costs associated with operational and maintenance functions dominate IT budgets, these functions are becoming the target of emerging technologies. Increasing research and entrepreneurial focus is directed to the high economic value of replacing support labor with technology. Over the next five to ten years, service technologies will alter the IT landscape, changing the quantity and quality of resources demanded. This talk will discuss these trends and projections.
