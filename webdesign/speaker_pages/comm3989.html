Title: A Decision-Analytic Approach to Frame Regulatory Decisions Regarding Over-the-Counter Statins

Author: Sara Eggers, Carnegie Mellon University, Baker Hall 129, 4800 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, sara.eggers@alumni.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Baruch Fischhoff

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a behavioral-realistic, decision-analytic research framework to assess the impact of Over-the-counter availability of statins on consumers' welfare and aid FDA in making judgments on its acceptability. We first normatively model choices facing consumers. We compare those decisions to a descriptive model integrating existing empirical evidence on actual consumer decision making.
