Title: The Social Side of Decision Analysis: Is Scientific Knowledge Possible?
Author: Alec Morton, Dr, London School of Economics and Political Science, Operational Research Group, London, WC2A 2AE, United Kingdom, A.Morton@lse.ac.uk
Coauthor(s): Gilberto Montibeller
Year: 2007
Abstract: What do people learn from modelling? Are there good ways to structure models? Why do some organisations institutionalise orderly approaches to decision making and others fail? This talk will discuss what lessons Decision Analysis can draw from other areas of management science where serious efforts have been made to study such questions systematically.
