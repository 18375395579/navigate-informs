Title: Test for Detecting Arbitrarily Shaped Clusters based on Adaptive Minimum Spanning Tree

Author: Ruoyu Zhou, Faculty of Business Administration,University of Macau, A203,Block 1,Faculty of Business, Administration,University of Macau, Macau, Macau, amyzhou@umac.mo

Coauthor(s): Lianjie Shu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Spatial scan statistic is the most popular test in detecting spatial clusters.However,it has low power to detect irregularly shaped clusters due to use of circular scan window.So,more attentions are placed in searching clusters with arbitrarily shapes.In this paper,we give a test inspired by minimum spanning tree(MST).We cut edges of the MST in the process of finding clusters,relying on the smallest validity index.With this method single or multiple clusters of any shapes can be detected.
