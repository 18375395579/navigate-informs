Title: Efficient Simulation for American Option Pricing Under Stochastic Volatility
Author: Farid AitSahlia, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Systems Engineering, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, farid@ise.ufl.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this talk I will present a Markov Chain Monte Carlo approach that integrates the Longstaff-Schwartz technique to price American options when the volatility of the underlying is stochastic.
