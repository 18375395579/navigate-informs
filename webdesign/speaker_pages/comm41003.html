Title: A Mixed Packing-sequencing Problem in the Delivery of Chemotherapy Drugs

Author: Federico Della Croce, D.A.I. Politecnico di Torino, Corso Duca degli Abruzzi 24, Torino, 10129, Italy, federico.dellacroce@polito.it

Coauthor(s): Andrea Grosso, Jean-Charles Billaut

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a problem arising in chemotherapy drugs delivery where drugs are stored in vials. Once a vial is opened, it must be used before a time limit. The aim is to minimize the drugs waste and satisfy the treatment requests. The problem can be seen as a 2-constraint bin packing problem with additional due dates on the treatments execution inducing jobs sequencing constraints. A matheuristic and a recovering beam search are proposed and tested on bin packing instances with/without due dates.
