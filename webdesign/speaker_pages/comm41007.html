Title: Expediting with Expiry under RFID
Author: Diego Klabjan, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, Ind. Eng. and Mgmt. Sci., d-klabjan@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): David Simchi-Levi, Chiwon Kim
Year: 2008
Abstract: Expediting of outstanding orders is facilitated by RFID deployments. We show how to improve inventory control under expediting. We also assume that goods have an expiry date.
