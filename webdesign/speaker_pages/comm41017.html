Title: Quick Response Rules for Updating Inventories of Substitutable Products with Short-Term Forecast Updating
Author: Saurabh Bansal, Assistant Professor, Pennsylvania State University, 405 Business Building, University Park, PA, United States of America, sub32@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): James Dyer
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper presents results for determining optimal inventory levels for a newsvendor who (i) makes a joint-inventory decision for multiple substitutable products for the next replenishment period using latest forecasts, and (ii) knows the forecasts and the corresponding optimal inventory levels for the current replenishment period. These results are extremely easy to implement when faced with sudden demand shocks.
