Title: A Stochastic Optimization Model to Dynamically Reroute Flights in Presence of En Route Weather
Author: Avijit Mukherjee, Associate Project Scientist, U.C. Santa Cruz, MS 210-8, NASA Ames Research Center, Moffett Field, CA, 94035, United States, itsavijit@gmail.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: A stochastic optimization model is presented for strategic planning of ground delay and rerouting decisions in presence of en route weather. The model is applied to a problem consisting of weather impact at one or more arrival fixes of an airport. Experimental results suggest significant delay saving by virtue of dynamic rerouting compared to a static ground delay program without any options to reroute aircraft.
