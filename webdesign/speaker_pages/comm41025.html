Title: New Computational Results on Solving the Sequential Procedure with Feedback

Author: David Boyce, Professor Emeritus, University of Illinois at Chicago, Chicago, IL, 60607, United States, dboyce@uic.edu

Coauthor(s): Christopher O'Neill, Wolfgang Scherr

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk presents findings for alternative feedback solution procedures applied to a 1,000 zone model. Three methods were tested: Naïve Feedback (no averaging of trip matrices or link flows); Averaging with Constant Weights, applied to the trip matrices; Method of Successive Averages (MSA), also applied to trip matrices.
