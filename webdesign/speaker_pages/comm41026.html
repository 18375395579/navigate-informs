Title: Vehicle Trade Studies with Commonality
Author: Stephen Henry, Systems Analyst, Sandia National Laboratories, PO Box 5800-1188, Albuquerque, NM, 87185, United States of America, smhenry@sandia.gov
Year: 2013
Abstract: When optimizing the design of new military vehicle platforms, it is often desirable to make certain components common across platform variants to reduce procurement and maintenance costs. However, the incorporation of commonality into trade space analyses brings some unique challenges. In this presentation we will examine various commonality analysis approaches, including a variety of commonality measures along with the advantages and disadvantages of integrated vs. post-hoc commonality.
