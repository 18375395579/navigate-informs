Title: A Practical Approach to Force Progressive Hedging Convergence for Stochastic Unit Commitment

Author: Garret LaBove, Research Assistant, Arizona State University, PO Box 875706, GWC 206 School of ECEE ASU, Tempe, AZ, 85287-5706, United States of America, garret@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Kory Hedman

Year: 2013

Abstract: Computationally efficient methods to solve stochastic unit commitment will be required in order to ensure system reliability at least cost for systems with high levels of intermittent, non-dispatchable renewable resources. This talk will present a parallelized progressive hedging framework for unit commitment while considering N-1 reliability and renewable uncertainty combined with out-of-market correction procedures to improve convergence.
