Title: An Efficient Service Policy for IaaS in Cloud Computing

Author: Jin Young Choi, Associate Professor, Ajou University, San 5, Woncheon-dong, Yeongtong-gu, Suwon, Korea, Republic of, choijy@ajou.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Moon-kyung Kim

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present an efficient heuristic policy for IaaS in cloud computing, while considering two objectives maximizing profits and minimizing customer’s rejections simultaneously. It is examined for its performance by generating some example configurations, including IBM cloud computing services.
