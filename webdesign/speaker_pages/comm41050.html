Title: Optimal Dynamic Assortment Planning
Author: Denis Saure, Columbia University, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, United States of America, dsaure05@gsb.columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Assaf Zeevi
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider a dynamic assortment planning problem under the framework of utility maximizing customers. We develop efficient algorithms that balance exploration--exploitation trade offs in a suitable manner, and prove that in certain instances the performance of said algorithms is best possible.
