Title: On-line Traveling Salesman with Duration and Distance Considerations
Author: Thibaut Vidal, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Room 32-D566, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, vidalt@mit.edu
Coauthor(s): Patrick Jaillet
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider on-line traveling salesman problems in which the objective is to minimize a weighted sum of completion time and total travel distance. This objective captures both the customer-centric time-to-service objective and the carrier costs. On-line algorithms are examined, and new upper and lower bounds on competitive ratios are introduced.
