Title: Optimization Models for Planning and Scheduling a 24/7 Workforce
Author: Shrikant Jarugumilli, Sr. Operations Research Specialist, BNSF Railway, 2400 Western Center Blvd, Ft. Worth, TX, 76131, United States of America, Shrikant.Jarugumilli@bnsf.com
Coauthor(s): Siva Veluchamy, Kruthika Nagaraj, Juan Morales, Mike Swindler
Year: 2013
Abstract: The BNSF Train Dispatching office is a 24/7/365 operation that has 105 dispatching districts covered by approximately 550 distinctly qualified train dispatchers. This discussion will concentrate on the optimization models for workforce planning and scheduling in these offices.
