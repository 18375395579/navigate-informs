Title: Approximate Dynamic Programming for Generalized Joint Replenishment
Author: Dan Adelman, Professor of Operations Management, University of Chicago, Graduate School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States, dan.adelman@gsb.uchicago.edu
Coauthor(s): Diego Klabjan
Year: 2006
Abstract: We provide an approximate dynamic programming approach to solving the generalized joint replenishment problem to within any desired optimality tolerance, using ridge functions.
