Title: Data-driven Order Set Optimization in the Pediatric Environment
Author: Yiye Zhang, Carnegie Mellon University, 4800 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, ichiyoz@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Rema Padman, James Levin
Year: 2012
Abstract: Order sets have the potential to improve care delivery by making it faster, easier and safer for the clinician to enter orders, guided by known best practices. This research combines stochastic optimization with clustering to model the development of automated, data-driven order sets with the objective of minimizing usage costs.
