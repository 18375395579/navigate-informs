Title: Capacity Investment Decisions with Multiple Competing Technologies: A Portfolio Approach

Author: Gilvan Souza, Indiana University, Kelley School of Business, Bloomington, IN, 47405, United States of America, gsouza@indiana.edu

Coauthor(s): Wenbin Wang, Mark Ferguson, Shanshan Hu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider investments in sustainable technologies, which require a higher investment but have lower operating (fuel) cost. Savings fluctuate due to uncertain fuel prices, and the technology is not always utilized at 100% due to demand fluctuations. We provide structural and numerical results about technology choice and corresponding investment levels.
