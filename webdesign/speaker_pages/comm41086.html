Title: On the Minimum k-Connected Subgraph

Author: Chitra Balasubramaniam, Texas A&M University, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, bcvaidyanath@neo.tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sergiy Butenko

Year: 2013

Abstract: Node connectivity is a property of a network that can be used as a good measure of cohesion in social networks. We explore the problem of finding the smallest possible subgroup in a social network that can uniformly maintain high connectivity, and be robust to disruptions.
