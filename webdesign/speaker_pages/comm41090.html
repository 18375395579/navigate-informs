Title: Mean-field Limits for some Load-balancing Algorithms

Author: Kavita Ramanan, Professor of Applied Mathematics, Brown University, Box F, 182 George Street, Providence, RI, 02912, United States of America, kavita_ramanan@brown.edu

Coauthor(s): Arnab Ganguly

Year: 2013

Abstract: We establish mean-field limits for some load-balancing algorithms, and describe the scheduling insights obtained from these limits. Load-balancing algorithms of a similar flavour have mostly been studied under the assumption of exponential service distributions with the exception of recent work that uses the so-called cavity method. We introduce a new method, which might be of independent interest, that allows us to consider general service distributions.
