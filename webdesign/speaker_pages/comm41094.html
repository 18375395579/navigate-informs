Title: Competitive Agent Scheduling and Its Applications

Author: Michael Pinedo, New York University, IOMS Department, 44 West 4th Street, New York, NY, 10012, United States, mpinedo@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Y. Leung

Year: 2006

Abstract: Two agents have to schedule two sets of jobs on a single resource. The two agents have different constraints and objectives. We consider the problem of minimizing the value of the objective function of agent A, while keeping the value of the objective of agent B less than or equal to a fixed value Q.
