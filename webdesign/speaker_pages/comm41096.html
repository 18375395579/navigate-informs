Title: A Condition-Based Maintenance Policy for Multi-Component Systems with High Setup Cost of Maintenance

Author: Qiushi Zhu, Ir.MSc, TU Eindhoven, De Lismortel, Paviljoen E.9a, Eindhoven, 5612 AR, Netherlands, q.zhu@tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Geert-Jan van Houtum, Hao Peng

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose a new CBM policy for multi-component systems with stochastically and continuously deterioration. To reduce the setup cost of maintenance, a joint maintenance interval is proposed. The optimal maintenance interval and control limits of components are determined by minimizing the average long-run cost rate related to maintenance and downtime. Moreover, a numerical study with a case of wind farm maintenance is performed based on a large number of non-identical components.
