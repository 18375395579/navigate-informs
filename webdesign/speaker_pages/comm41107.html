Title: Vehicle Trajectory Estimation using Mobile Sensor Data

Author: Zhanbo Sun, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 2162, 14th Street, 2nd Floor, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, sunz2@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeff Ban

Year: 2012

Abstract: We aim to estimate trajectories of unsampled vehicles from those sample vehicle trajectories around a signalized intersection. The estimated trajectories together with the sample trajectories can provide a more complete picture of the intersection traffic flow. The proposed method is based on the variational formulation for traffic flow.
