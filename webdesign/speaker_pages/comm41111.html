Title: Distributed Multi-agent Convex Optimization

Author: Angelia Nedich, UIUC, angelia@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Alex Olshevsky, Asu Ozdaglar, John Tsitsiklis

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study the problem of optimization of a sum of convex functions in a network of agents. Each agent maintains and updates his estimate based on his own cost function, and communicates with a subset of agents (his neighbors). We consider the case when agents update the estimates using sub-gradient directions and a constant step-size rule. Our main result provides an error bound on sub-optimality of the agent estimates.
