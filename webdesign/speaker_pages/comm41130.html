Title: Integrating a One-time Probabilistic Special Purchasing Opportunity with On-going Decisions
Author: Hussein Naseraldin, University of Calgary, 2500 University Drive NW, Calgary, Alberta, Calgary, AB, T2N1N4, Canada, hnaseral@ucalgary.ca
Coauthor(s): Edward Silver
Year: 2007
Abstract: An inventory controller, who replenishes the inventory of an item on a regular basis, may encounter special opportunities to purchase the item at a reduced price. Of particular interest is the case where there is a one-time opportunity that may, with known information, occur in the short-term. In this paper we integrate a one-time, probabilistic opportunity with the on-going decisions. We show that order-up-to-level is the optimal policy and we obtain the exact form of the optimal solution.
