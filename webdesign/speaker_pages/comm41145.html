Title: A Markov Chain Modulated Inventory Model

Author: Michael Katehakis, Professor and Chair, Rutgers University, MSIS Department, 94 Rockafeller Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, mnk@rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Isaac Sonin

Year: 2013

Abstract: We model a firm that consumes a fixed amount of certain commodity per one unit of time. At any period the firm can buy any amount of the commodity at the current, Markov modulated, price, or use stored?commodity paying storage cost. A strategy is a vector of levels specifying the minimal commodity amount to keep for a given price. We present a polynomial time algorithm to calculate a nearly optimal strategy as well as the value function, using, at each step, only one-dimensional optimization.
