Title: Workforce Scheduling with Learning by Doing and Learning from Others
Author: Frank Bentefouet, Project Leader-Distribution Insights, Liberty Mutual, 175 Berkeley Street, Boston, MA, 02116, United States of America, frank.bentefouet@libertymutual.com
Coauthor(s): David Nembhard
Year: 2013
Abstract: We investigate the allocation of workers to tasks based on individual learning characteristics to improve system throughput. The worker productivity includes learning by doing and learning by transfer of knowledge from others. The allocation problem is composed of: a selection phase , a grouping phase and an assignment phase. At each phase, heuristic approaches are suggested. Two systems are of interest: a parallel system and a serial system.
