Title: Designing Production-Inventory-Transportation Systems with Capacitated Cross-docks and Suppliers

Author: Hossein Abouee-Mehrizi, PhD Candidate, Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto, 105 St.George st., Toronto, ON, M5S 3L1, Canada, H.AboueeMehrizi07@rotman.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Oded Berman, M.Reza Baharnemati

Year: 2008

Abstract: We analyze a production-inventory-transportation system problem where capacitated suppliers and cross-docks are to be established to satisfy the demand of retailers for different products. We consider the pipeline and retailers?inventory together with transportation, facility location and truck capacity costs in determining the optimal number and location of suppliers and cross-docks, allocation of retailers to suppliers, and truck capacity.
