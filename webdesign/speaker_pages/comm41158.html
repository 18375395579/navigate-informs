Title: Identification of Optimal Measurement Sets for Complete Metabolic Flux Elucidation

Author: YoungJung Chang, Postdoctoral scholar, The Pennsylvania State University, 147B Fenske Laboratory, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, yjchang@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Costas Maranas, Patrick Suthers

Year: 2008

Abstract: Metabolic flux analysis (MFA) estimates intracellular fluxes from isotopic measurements. In this talk, we present OptMeas optimization formulation that identifies the measurement sets that lead to unique flux elucidation while minimizing overall measurement cost. OptMeas utilizes incidence structure analysis, and considers both stationary and isotopically nonstationary MFA experimental conditions. OptMeas also accounts for partial isotopic measurements such as mass distributions.
