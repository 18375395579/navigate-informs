Title: Ventilation Map Based Radiotherapy for Locally Advanced Lung Cancer with Motion Management

Author: Hamid Ghaffari, Postdoctoral Fellow, University of Maryland, 22 S Greene, Baltimore, United States of America, ghaffari@mie.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Hao Zhang, Warren D'Souza

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a radiotherapy optimization method for lung cancer by considering organ motion as well as the organ function. We used probability function masses to present organ motion uncertainty while utilizing the personalized utility function in the optimization objective during radiation treatment planning. Although the plans were comparable with conventional dose/dose-volume metrics, reductions of 25% and 38% in fV20 and fV30, respectively, were achieved by FPMF-based plan (p-value < 0.01).
