Title: More Equitable Congestion Pricing and Tradable Credit Scheme for Managing Rush Hour Travel Choices

Author: Mohammad Miralinaghi, Research Assistant, Purdue University, 2156 tortuga ln, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, smiralin@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta, Satish Ukkusuri

Year: 2013

Abstract: Equity is an important factor to promote congestion pricing and tradable credit schemes. Motivated by this objective, we investigate a comprehensive approach to obtain an optimal pricing toll scheme for congestion pricing and tradable credits in the single bottleneck model with general user heterogeneity in a discrete time setting.
