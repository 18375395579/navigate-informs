Title: Advertising Inventory Allocation Based on Multi-Objective Optimization

Author: Jimmy Yang, Yahoo! Inc., 4401 Great America Parkway, Santa Clara, CA, 95054, United States of America, jianyang@yahoo-inc.com

Coauthor(s): John Tomlin

Year: 2008

Abstract: Allocation of advertising inventory is one of the critical issues in revenue management for media industry. Given a set of advertising demand, a media company must decide how to allocate limited inventory to achieve certain business objectives. This research reviews various allocation objectives and different approaches to optimize for these objectives. A framework based on multi-objective optimization is developed to test different objectives and analyze the trade-off.
