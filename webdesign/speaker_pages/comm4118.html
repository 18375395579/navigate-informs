Title: Dynamic User Equilibrium with Elastic Demand and Bounded Rationality: Formulation, Qualitative Analysis, and Computation

Author: Hongcheng Liu, PhD Candidate, Pennsylvania State University, 240 Leonhard, University Park, PA, United States of America, hql5143@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ke Han, Tao Yao, Terry Friesz

Year: 2013

Abstract: Simultaneous route-and-departure choice dynamic user equilibrium with elastic demand, and with bounded rationality, are both formulated as variational inequality and differential variational inequality. Existence results are given. We further demonstrate that both models can be computed using fixed-point algorithms with satisfactory convergence results.
