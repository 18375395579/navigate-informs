Title: Flexible Yard Storage for Transshipment Hub Port

Author: Loo Hay Lee, Associate Professor, National University of Singapore, 10 Kent Ridge Crescent, Singapore, 119260, Singapore, iseleelh@nus.edu.sg

Year: 2013

Abstract: Consignment strategy is one of the common yard strategies use to address how to assign container types to yard locations. This strategy facilitates fast vessel turnaround, but it is not efficient in space utilization. We will present a flexible container storage that increases space utilization and retains the consignment concept.
