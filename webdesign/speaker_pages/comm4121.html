Title: An Efficient Evacuation Strategy for a High-rise Building Evacuation of Heterogeneous Population

Author: Byung-In Kim, Associate Professor, POhang University of Science and Technology (POSTECH), Hyoja-Dong, Pohang, Korea, Republic of, bkim@postech.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Jeongin Koo, Yong Seog Kim

Year: 2013

Abstract: Evacuating a heterogeneous population consisting of residents with various mobility limitations and space requirements take a much longer time than a homogeneous population because of the blocking effect of slowly moving people. This study proposes a dedicated route selection strategy for an effective evacuation in a 24-story building and optimally solve it via a time-expanded network flow model. Simulation experimental results confirm the effectiveness of the proposed strategy.
