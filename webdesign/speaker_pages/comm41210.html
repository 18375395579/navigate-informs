Title: Information concealing games

Author: Saswati Sarkar, Associate Professor, University of Pennsylvania, 200 S. 33rd St., Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, swati@seas.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Eitan Altman

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a system with two players that have different information about the state of the system. Each player needs to chose the amount of information it reveals about the system state to the other player subject to certain system constraints. We show that the decision problems for the entities can be formulated as a signaling game, and provide policies that attain the perfect Bayesian equilibrium of this game.
