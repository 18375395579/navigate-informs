Title: A Logarithmic Safety Staffing Rule for Contact Centers with Call Blending

Author: Ohad Perry, Assistant Professor, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, ohad.perry@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Guodong Pang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a large-scale contact center that handles inbound and outbound calls. Inbound calls arrive as an exogenous stream of customers with impatience, while outbound calls are modeled as an infinite queue of jobs, waiting to be processed. The goal is to serve inbound calls subject to service level constraints while keeping the outbound throughput rate at a desired level. We show that a logarithmic safety staffing is sufficient to meet these objectives.
