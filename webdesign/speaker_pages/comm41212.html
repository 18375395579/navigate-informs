Title: Tactical Crew Recovery During Irregular Airline Operations
Author: Chunhua Gao, PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, cgao@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Alex Kalyta
Year: 2006
Abstract: During irregular operations it is often possible to revise schedules, reroute aircraft, and reaccommodate passengers to get back to normal operations by the next morning. However, this is not the case for crew. It typically takes several days to get crew back onto their planned rosters. We discuss tactical crew recovery.
