Title: Robust Pricing for Networks with Demand Uncertainty

Author: Lauren Gardner, Graduate Research Assistant, The University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Station C1761, ECJ Hall Suite 6.2, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, lmgardner@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Jennifer Duthie, Avinash Unnikrishnan, S. Travis Waller

Year: 2007

Abstract: Robust pricing is of interest due to the uncertainties that arise in networks, specifically demand uncertainty. We will use a stochastic optimization model to determine optimal link tolls for a given network, and provide some insight into the robustness of this pricing scheme by examining UE preformance under various origin-demand matrices.
