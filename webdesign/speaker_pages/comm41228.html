Title: Analyzing the Influence of Minimum Requirements on Coupon Selling in Groupon’s Daily Deal

Author: Gang Wang, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road, Storrs, CT, United States of America, gang.wang@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): James Marsden, Xue Bai, Bill Ross

Year: 2012

Abstract: Minimum requirements have been one of the key features in Groupon’s daily deal setting in which a certain number of coupons must be bought to activate the deal. Comparing two groups of Groupon deals, those with and those without minimum requirements, we isolate the influence of minimum requirements on social sharing, indicated by Facebook likes, and then analyze possible impacts on coupon sales and revenue for various retail categories.
