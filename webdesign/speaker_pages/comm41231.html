Title: Cycle Time Constrained Priority Mix Optimization Based on Decomposition Approximation Model
Author: Shi-Chung Chang, Professor, National Taiwan University, Rm. 245, Dept. of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan University, Taipei, 10617, Taiwan - ROC, scchang@cc.ee.ntu.edu.tw
Coauthor(s): Shin-Shyu Su, Yu-ting Kao, Bo-Jiun Liao
Year: 2008
Abstract: We optimize the priority mix of semiconductor manufacturing under cycle-time target constraints. It is built on top of a novel, hybrid decomposition-approximation-based priority queueing network for quick evaluation of cycle-times under given priority mix, price, and capacity utilizations. Comparisons with simulation models for applications to supply chain management are performed.
