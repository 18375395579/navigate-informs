Title: Use of Telemetry in Stochastic Routing

Author: Amit Verma, University of Iowa, W244 John Pappajohn Bus Bldg, Iowa City, IA, United States of America, amit-verma@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Ann M. Campbell

Year: 2012

Abstract: Telemetry units can be used to gauge inventory levels at customers. Having these readings can help prevent stockouts and unnecessary deliveries. We present a model for the problem of where to place a limited number of telemetry units to minimize routing costs. We model the customers without telemetry as having a predetermined delivery frequency but allow the delivery dates for telemetry customers to vary according to realizations of usage. We will present a heuristic and computational results.
