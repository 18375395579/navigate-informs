Title: Berth and Quay Crane Scheduling: A Multi-objective Formulation

Author: Michail Golias, Graduate Research Assistant, Civil and Environmental Engineering, Rutgers University, 623 Bowser Road, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States, golias@rci.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Maria Boile, Sotiris Theofanis

Year: 2007

Abstract: A formulation for simultaneous berth and quay crane scheduling with multiple objectives is presented. A genetic algorithm based heuristic is proposed for solving the resulting problem. We consider the minimization of costs from not meeting agreed (un)loading vessel performance, waiting and handling, and tardiness of completion of service.
