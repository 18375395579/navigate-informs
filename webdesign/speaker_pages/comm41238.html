Title: P-additive Games: A Class of Totally Balanced Games Arising From Inventory Situations with Temporary

Author: Ana Meca, Professor, Universidad Miguel Harnandez, Edificio Torretamarit, Avda. Universidad s\n, Elche, 03202, Spain, ana.meca@umh.es

Coauthor(s): Luis Guardiola

Year: 2007

Abstract: We introduce a new class of totally balanced cooperative TU games, namely p-additive games. It is inspired by the class of inventory games that arises from inventory situations with temporary discounts (Toledo, 2002) and contains the class of inventory cost games (Meca et al., 2003, 2004). It is shown that p-additive games are totally balanced. The modified SOC-rule is proposed as a core-allocation for p-additive games.
