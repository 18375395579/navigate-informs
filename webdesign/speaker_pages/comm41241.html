Title: Scheduling of Simple Linear Deteriorating Jobs to Single Machine with Rate-modifying Activities

Author: Hui-Chih Hung, Assistant Professor, National Chiao Tung University, 1001 University Road, Management Bldg 2, IEM, Hsin-Chu, 300, Taiwan - ROC, hhc@nctu.edu.tw

Coauthor(s): Hsi-Mei Hsu, Hsiao-ning Lu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a scheduling problem of non-resumable deteriorating jobs on a single machine with rate-modifying activities (RMAs). Multiple RMAs with setup time can be selected to resume the processing speed of machine. The objective is to minimize makespan. We reduce the problem with k-RMAs to a k-way partitioning problem, which is shown to be NP-hard. Bounds on the number of RMAs are examined and a fast heuristic is proposed. Numerical studies are implemented to verify the performances.
