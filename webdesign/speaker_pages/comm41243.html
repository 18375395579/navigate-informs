Title: Throughput Maximization for Tandem Queues with Synergistic Servers

Author: Xinchang Wang, Georgia Institute of Technology, Room 316 Main Building, 755 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, xwang336@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Sigrún Andradóttir, Hayriye Ayhan

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider tandem queues with servers that work more efficiently in teams than on their own. The synergy among collaborating servers can be station-dependent. We provide a complete characterization of the optimal policy for Markovian systems with two stations and two servers. For larger systems, we provide sufficient conditions that guarantee that the optimal policy has all servers working together at all times.
