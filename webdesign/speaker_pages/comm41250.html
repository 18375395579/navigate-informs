Title: Slow-server Problem in Many-server Heavy Traffic with Non-exponential Service Times

Author: Tolga Tezcan, University of Illinois, Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, 117 Transportation Building, 104 South Mathews Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61801, ttezcan@uiuc.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the slow server problem. We assume that service times have a certain phase-type distribution. Specifically, service time of a server is assumed to have an exponential distribution with rate mu_i with probability p_i. Specific values of the parameters depend on the server pool the server belongs to. We show that a static priority policy is asymptotically optimal in many-server heavy-traffic.
