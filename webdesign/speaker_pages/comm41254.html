Title: Screening for Hepatocellular Carcinoma: An Application of Dynamic Allocation Indices

Author: Elliot Lee, Doctoral Pre-Candidate, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, elliot.dy.lee@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Michael Volk, Mariel Lavieri

Year: 2012

Abstract: The problem of allocating a fixed screening capacity amongst a set of patients at risk for hepatocellular carcinoma is modeled in the language of the multi-armed bandit problem. While the current screening protocol uses fixed intervals, the policy proposed here incorporates individual patient information which is gathered sequentially over time. An expression for the allocation index is derived, and the policy's performance is tested on surveillance data on 1025 patients followed over 5.3 years.
