Title: Minimax Regret Covering Location on a Network under Uncertainty

Author: Oleksandr Shlakhter, University of Toronto, Joseph L. Rotman School of Management, 105 St. George Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 3E6, Canada, alex.shlakhter@Rotman.Utoronto.Ca

Coauthor(s): Dmitry Krass, Oded Berman

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider p-cover problem on a network with uncertain weights of nodes. For each node only interval estimate of its weight is known. The problem is to find the “minimax regret?covering location, i.e. to minimize worst case loss in objective function because a location decision is made without knowing the actual weights at nodes. We present the algorithm for this problem on a general network. We also consider two special cases, when the solution algorithm is polynomial.
