Title: Distributed Backup Scheduling: Modeling and Optimization

Author: Bo Zhang, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Route 134, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, zhangbo@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Peter van de Ven, Angela Schoergendorfer

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a novel Markov chain model to describe the (distributed) data backup process and analyze its stability conditions and stationary behavior. Then we study how to choose a backup scheduling policy that strikes the right balance between performing frequent backups to improve data safety and reducing backup costs.
