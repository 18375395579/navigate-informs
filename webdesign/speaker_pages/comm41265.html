Title: Pre-pack Optimization in Fashion Retail

Author: Shivaram Subramanian, IBM Research, 1101 Kitchawan Road, TJ Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, United States of America, subshiva@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Andrew Vakhutinsky, Alexander Kushkuley

Year: 2013

Abstract: The problem of pre-pack optimization arises when a fashion retailer has to deliver merchandise items to multiple stores in the chain having various demand profiles for size-color combinations. Items of the same style are packed using very few style-color configurations (pre-packs) determined prior to the selling season, then combined and shipped to each store to match its demand curve. We formulate a problem of determining an optimal pre-pack set and analyze several solution approaches
