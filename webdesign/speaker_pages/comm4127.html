Title: Core Deviation Minimizing Auctions
Author: Isa Hafalir, Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, 15217, United States of America, isaemin@cmu.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study dominant strategy implementable direct mechanisms that minimize the expected surplus from core deviations. Using incentive compatibility conditions, we formulate the core deviation miminimization problem as a calculus of variations problem and then numerically solve it for some particular cases.
