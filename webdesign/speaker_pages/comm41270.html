Title: Spatial Analysis in OR/MS Curriculum

Author: Avijit Sarkar, University of Redlands, 1200 East Colton Avenue, Redlands, CA, United States of America, Avijit_Sarkar@redlands.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Several OR/MS problems such as site location, shortest path, vehicle routing and other network optimization problems are inherently spatial in nature. This presentation will use freeware and specialized GIS software to demonstrate infusion of spatial analysis in site location, supply chain management and logistics.
