Title: Operation and Performance of a Last Mile Transportation System

Author: Hai Wang, PhD candidate, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 2D, 550 Memorial Drive, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, haiwang@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Amedeo Odoni

Year: 2013

Abstract: We describe a model that considers a last mile transportation system as a batch arrival, batch service queueing system. We present an approach for deriving analytical expressions for some critical measures of system performance, as well as an analysis of an operating strategy.
