Title: Triage or Not to Triage?

Author: Zhankun Sun, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, Statistics and Operations Research, CB#3260, Chapel Hill, NC, 27514, United States of America, szk@unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Nilay Argon, Serhan Ziya

Year: 2012

Abstract: Inspired by the mass-casualty triage problem, we consider a clearing model with two customer types. Customer types are initially unknown but the server has the option of triaging the customers to obtain imperfect information on their types before serving them. With the objective of minimizing the total holding costs, we obtain analytical characterizations of optimal dynamic and static policies. For example, we prove that the optimal dynamic policy is of threshold type.
