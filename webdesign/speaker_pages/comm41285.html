Title: Flexible Servers in Tandem Lines with Fewer Servers Than Stations

Author: H. Eser Kirkizlar, Georgia Tech, H. Milton Stewart School of ISyE, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, ekirkizl@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Sigrun Andradottir, Hayriye Ayhan

Year: 2007

Abstract: Consider a tandem line with infinite or finite buffers between stations, infinite supply of jobs in front of the first station, and infinite room for storage after the last station. We study optimal and heuristic server assignment policies when the number of servers is smaller than the number of stations, and also compare the performance of such systems as a function of how flexible the servers are.
