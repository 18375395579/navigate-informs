Title: Algorithms for Dynamic Resequencing of En Route Flights to Relieve Terminal Congestion

Author: James Jones, University of Maryland, Department of Civil and Environmental En, College Park, MD, United States of America, jonesjc1@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): David Lovell, Michael Ball

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a set of algorithms designed to dynamically resequence flight arrival times of en route flights to both transfer and eliminate terminal delay. We also propose a strategy for transferring data between the systems and command centers and develop a process for assigning Controlled Times of Arrivals to flights en route. Analysis suggests that RBS-based algorithms provide strong throughput performance. This improvement is likely achieved, however, at the expense of fuel usage.
