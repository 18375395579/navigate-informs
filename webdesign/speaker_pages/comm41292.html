Title: Multi-criterion Dynamic User Equilibrium for Road Pricing Applications with Heterogeneous Users
Author: Hani Mahmassani, Professor, The University of Maryland at College Park, Department of Civil & Environmental Engi, College Park, MD, 20742-3021, United States, masmah@umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Chung-Cheng Lu
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop an efficient parametric approach for the assignment of heterogeneous users with continuously distributed value of time, value of schedule delay and value of reliability, in a network with dynamic prices and managed lanes on certain roadway facilities. The algorithm determines equilibrium user responses in terms of path and departure time choices, and the associated network flow patterns.
