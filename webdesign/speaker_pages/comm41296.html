Title: Real-time Inter-modal Diversion for Airline Schedule Perturbation Recovery

Author: Yu Zhang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, 1023 Foster City Blvd,, Apt.C, Foster City, CA, 94404, United States of America, zhangyu@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Hansen

Year: 2008

Abstract: Propose a new analytical framework and supporting optimization model that will encourage the aviation industry to incorporate alternative transportation modes when major airports in the system encounter temporary closures or severe capacity deficiencies, provide a way to reduce passenger disutility, help airlines reduce operating cost and recover schedule more promptly, and assist air traffic flow managers to utilize and distribute scarce resources more efficiently and equitably.
