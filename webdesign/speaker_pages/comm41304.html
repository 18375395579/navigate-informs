Title: Tuition Exchange

Author: Utku Unver, Professor of Economics, Boston College, Boston, MA, United States of America, unver@bc.edu

Coauthor(s): Umut Dur

Year: 2013

Abstract: We introduce a new matching model to mimic inter-college tuition exchange programs for dependents of faculty to attend other colleges tuition-free. Programs use decentralized markets making it difficult to achieve balance. We show that stable equilibria discourage net-exporting colleges from exchange. We introduce two-sided top-trading-cycles as the unique mechanism that is balanced-efficient, student-strategy-proof, and respecting priority bylaws regarding dependent eligibility.
