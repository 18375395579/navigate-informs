Title: The Effect of Climate Policy on Facility Location, Production and Shipping

Author: Ozge Islegen, Assistant Professor, Northwestern University, Kellogg School of Management, 2001 Sheridan Rd, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, o-islegen@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Erica Plambeck, Terry Taylor

Year: 2013

Abstract: One region imposes either a tax on GHG emissions or a cap-and-trade system in which the cost of a permit for emissions is uncertain and has mean equal to the tax. We show how variability in the permit cost affects the “leakage?of emissions, trade quantities, and social welfare in the region with climate policy under border adjustment.
