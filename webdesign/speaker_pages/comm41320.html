Title: Capacity Planning for a Class of Large-scale Loss Networks

Author: Yingdong Lu, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, yingdong@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Yue Tan, Cathy Xia

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of determining the optimal capacity levels in a general class of large-scale loss networks.
