Title: Reliability Acceptance Test based on Accelerated Life Testing
Author: Elsayed A. Elsayed, Professor, Rutgers University, 96 Frelinghuysen Rd, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, elsayed@rci.rutgers.edu
Coauthor(s): Yao Cheng
Year: 2013
Abstract: Traditional design of reliability acceptance test is performed under the product’s working condition which requires a large sample size and long test duration. In this paper, we investigate an accelerated life testing reliability acceptance test based on accelerated life testing. We design the reliability acceptance test by utilizing different types of stress loading and types.
