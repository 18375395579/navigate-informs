Title: Collusive Flow Equilibria are Not Unique

Author: Lisa Fleischer, Dartmouth College, 6211 Sudikoff, Hanover, NH, United States of America, lkf@cs.dartmouth.edu

Coauthor(s): Umang Bhaskhar, Darrell Hoy, Chien-Chung Huang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study congestion network games where agents controlling small amounts of flow may form coalitions to improve their average delay. We show that unlike the case without collusion, there may be multiple equilibria. And, we give a complete characterization of network topologies that have a unique equilibria.
