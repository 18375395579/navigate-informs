Title: Locating Noisy Counting Sensors to Estimate Network Flows

Author: Pitu Mirchandani, Professor, Arizona State University, 699 S Mill Ave., Tempe, AZ, 85281, United States of America, pitu@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ning Wang

Year: 2013

Abstract: The following problem is addressed. There are unknown flows on known routes. There is some probabilistic prior knowledge on the flows. We can locate some sensors that provide flow rates with known reliabilities. We seek the optimal locations of these sensors using a Bayesian framework for estimating flows.
