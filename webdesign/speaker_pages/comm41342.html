Title: Commercial Vehicles Fleet Replacement Optimization: Electric Trucks vs Conventional Diesel Trucks

Author: Miguel Figliozzi, Associate Professor, Portland State University, P.O. Box 751 CEE, OR 97207-075, Portland, OR, United States of America, figliozzi@pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Wei Feng

Year: 2012

Abstract: This research presents a vehicle fleet replacement model that aims at minimizing fleet total net cost in a planning time horizon, including purchase cost, energy cost, operating and maintenance cost, emissions cost and salvage revenue. A set of economic and technological factors, as well as fleet initial configuration are incorporated into the model, and their impacts on the competitiveness between electric trucks and conventional trucks are analyzed.
