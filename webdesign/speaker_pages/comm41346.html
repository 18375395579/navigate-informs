Title: Polyhedral Results for 1-restricted Simple 2-matchings

Author: Yanjun Li, Assistant Professor, Purdue University, 403 W. State Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, li14@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): David Hartvigsen

Year: 2007

Abstract: A simple 2-matching in a graph is a subgraph whose connected components are paths and cycles. A 1-restricted simple 2-matching is a simple 2-matching with no isolated edges. Such matchings are related to the TSP. We study a family of graphs on which the convex hull of 1-restricted simple 2-matchings has a simple description, introduce a new class of valid blossom-type inequalities, and show that the general polytope could be complex.
