Title: A Push-and-pull Based Distributed Control of Stream Processing Networks
Author: Cathy H. Xia, Research Scientist, IBM Research, TJ Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, cathyx@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Li Zhang, Hanhua Feng, Zhen Liu
Year: 2007
Abstract: A fundamental problem in stream processing systems is how to meet various quality of service (QoS) requirements using effective admission control and resource allocation mechanisms. We consider a stream processing system with a network of cooperating servers, collectively providing processing services for multiple data streams. We present a pull-and-pull based distributed control algorithm that guarantees the network stability and achieves the maximum system utility in the long run.
