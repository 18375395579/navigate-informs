Title: Stochastic Sequential Scheduling without Fixed Appointment Slot for Outpatient Clinics

Author: Santanu Chakraborty, Purdue University, 203 Martin Jischke Drive, Mann Hall, Suite 225, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, santanu@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Lawley, Kumar Muthuraman

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper we develop a sequential scheduling algorithm for the case where the service period is not divided into slots. We assume that the service time distribution is exponential and each patient is characterized by a certain no-show probability.
