Title: A Hybrid Algorithm and Compaction Process for Railcar Movement Reconstruction
Author: Ingrid Schultze, Sr Mgr Rail Industry Analysis, Union Pacific Railroad, 1400 Douglas St. Stop 1350, Omaha, NE, 68179, United States of America, ischultze@up.com
Coauthor(s): John Ransom, Chris Sanford, John Gray
Year: 2008
Abstract: Charged with accurate reconstruction of railcar movement over select rail corridors, a Union Pacific team supporting regulatory compliance initiatives developed a hybrid algorithm and compaction process capable of routing over 10.5 million railcars in less than one hour while adhering to historical event reportings.
