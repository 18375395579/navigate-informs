Title: American Options: Upper Bounds on the Cost of Delayed Exercise

Author: Arun Chockalingam, Assistant Professor, Eindhoven University of Technology, Den Dolech 2, Eindhoven, 5612AZ, Netherlands, A.Chockalingam@tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Haolin Feng

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this talk, we consider the cost of delaying the exercise of American options. Leveraging on the moving boundary approach to American option pricing, we characterize upper bounds on this cost in terms of only the option’s intrinsic value and the value function associated with delayed exercise for a variety of market models from the simple Black-Scholes model to complex Stochastic Volatility Jump-diffusion models. We present illustrations of these bounds for both put and call options.
