Title: Pricing Scheduling Flexibility of Electricity Generation Facilities
Author: Valeriy Ryabchenko, valeriy@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Stanislav Uryasev
Year: 2007
Abstract: The paper presents a heuristic for valuation of tolling agreement contracts on power generation assets. We build a LP model to find approximations of optimal operating boundaries. We consider different operational constraints, such as start-up and shut-down costs, ramp-up period etc. We consider general markovian martingale processes for electricity and fuel price dynamics. In order to make the model robust we incorporate a set of optimal boundary shape constraints.
