Title: Planning for Availability with Service Level Requirements

Author: Hari Natarajan, Associate Professor, University of Miami, Miami, FL, United States of America, hari@miami.edu

Coauthor(s): Yalcin Akcay, Hernan Awad

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a seller's inventory planning problem for an assortment of products facing random customer demand. The seller chooses the optimal inventory levels so as to satisfy product-specific and assortment-based service level requirements. The service level measure used in this study is the ready rate of the assortment.
