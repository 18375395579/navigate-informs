Title: Design of Inventory Networks with Dynamic Sourcing
Author: Ho-Yin Mak, University of California, Berkeley, 1117 Etcheverry Hall, University of California, Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, makho@calmail.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Max Shen
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the problem of designing an inventory network in which sourcing decisions are made dynamically over time. This class of networks allows inventory sharing (risk-pooling) through sourcing flexibility. The main focus is on the trade-off between the cost and benefit of adding flexibility (i.e. sourcing options) to the system.
