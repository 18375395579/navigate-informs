Title: Fortifying the Existing Unreliable Facilities and Monotonic Optimization
Author: Lezhou (Roger) Zhan, CitationShares Management, 5 American Ln, Greenwich, CT, 06831, United States, zhan@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Max Shen
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider the problem of fortifying the existing unreliable facilities. Various modeling and solution techniques, including a monotonic branch-reduce-bound algorithm, are outlined with extensive computational studies.
