Title: Upper Bounds on Assortment Optimization Problems under Mixtures of Logits

Author: Jacob Feldman, Cornell University, School of OR & IE, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, jbf232@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Huseyin Topaloglu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider assortment optimization problems where customers choose according to a mixture of multinomial logit models. We show how to obtain bounds on the optimal expected revenue and computationally test the performance of these upper bounds.
