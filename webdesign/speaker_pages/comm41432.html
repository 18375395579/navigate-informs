Title: Approximate Policies for Lagged Asset Acquisition Problems in Energy Resource Management
Author: Xingyuan Ethan Fang, Princeton University, Sherrerd Hall Charlton St, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, xingyuan@princeton.edu
Coauthor(s): Warren Powell
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this talk, we will illustrate a new strategy to solve the multistage lagged asset acquisition problem motivated from energy market. The strategy is a hybrid of deterministic lookahead policy and value function approximation.
