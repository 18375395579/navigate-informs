Title: Data-based Analysis of Delay Estimators and Announcements in a Call Center

Author: Oualid Jouini, Assistant Professor, Ecole Centrale Paris, Grande Voie des Vignes, Chatenay-Malabry, 92290, France, oualid.jouini@ecp.fr

Coauthor(s): Zeynep Aksin, Yves Dallery, Fikri Karaesmen, Mohamed Salah Aguir

Year: 2012

Abstract: The problem of estimating delays experienced by customers with different priorities, and determination of the appropriate delay announcement, in a multi-class call center with time varying parameters, abandonments and retrials is considered. Making use of real data from this call center, the delay announcements from the estimated delay distributions that minimize a newsvendor-like cost function are considered. The performance of these announcements are also compared to announcing the mean delay.
