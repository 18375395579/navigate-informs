Title: Integrating Production Scheduling with Packing of Finished Orders for Delivery

Author: Zhi-Long Chen, Associate Professor, University of Maryland, Robert H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, ZChen@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Guruprasad Pundoor

Year: 2006

Abstract: A set of orders are processed first in a production facility, and then packed in batches for delivery to a customer. We consider the production scheduling and order packing decisions jointly. Solvability of various problems is investigated and solution algorithms are proposed.
