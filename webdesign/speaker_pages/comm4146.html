Title: Evaluating Non-combatant Evacuation Operations (NEO) Using Optimization

Author: Steven Sparling, Military Analyst, Center for Army Analysis, 6001 Goethals Road, Fort Belvoir, VA, 22060, United States of America, steven.j.sparling@us.army.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: The U.S. Government is committed to evacuating its citizens and others authorized from host nation countries when conditions exist that place their lives in danger. Given the potential for violence, it is desirable to complete evacuations as fast as possible. This paper presents an integer program optimization model for evaluating non-combatant evacuation alternatives that considers several unique and complicating aspects to this type of problem.
