Title: Analyzing the Impacts of Traffic conditions, Adaptive Traffic Signal Control, Transit Signal Priority

Author: Miguel Figliozzi, Associated Professor, Portland State University, PSU - CEE, P.O. Box 751, Portland, OR, 97207-0751, United States of America, figliozzi@pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Wei Feng

Year: 2013

Abstract: We integrate several sources of data including bus automatic vehicle location (AVL), automatic passenger count (APC), SCATS signal timing data and traffic movement data at each intersection along a corridor. We present the results of regression models that quantify the joint impacts of traffic conditions, adaptive traffic signal control, transit signal priority and bus stop location (near-side or far-side bus stops) on bus travel times.
