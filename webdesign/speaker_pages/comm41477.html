Title: Disaster Response and Relief Facility Location for Istanbul
Author: Nihan Görmez, Research Assistant, Middle East Technical University, Middle East Technical University, Dept. of Industrial Engineering, Ankara, 06531, Turkey, nihan@ie.metu.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Murat Köksalan, F. Sibel Salman
Year: 2008
Abstract: We study disaster response and relief facility location problem for the anticipated earthquake in İstanbul. The municipality plans to locate disaster response facilities to execute post-disaster relief operations. We develop a mathematical model that optimizes the travel time and the number of facilities to be opened. We analyze the trade-off between these two objectives under various circumstances and present the results.
