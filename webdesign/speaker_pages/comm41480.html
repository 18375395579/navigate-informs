Title: Electricity Market Models: Addressing Uncertainty
Author: Uday Shanbhag, Assistant Professor, Department of Industrial and Enterprise Systems Engineering, University of Illnois, Urbana-Champaign, Urbana, 61801, United States, udaybag@uiuc.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: Several models of competitive behavior in electricity markets are considered in an uncertain setting. In each case, decomposition and sampling-based algorithms are suggested, accompanied by convergence results and computational experience.
