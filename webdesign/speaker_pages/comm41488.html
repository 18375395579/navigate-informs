Title: Continuum Models for Broadcasting Over Sensor Networks

Author: Sanjay Shakkottai, Assistant Professor, The University of Texas of Austin, ECE Department, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, shakkott@ece.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Sundar Subramanian, Ari Arapostathis

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study the role of geographic information and state information (i.e. memory of previous messages or transmissions) in reducing the redundant transmissions while broadcasting over a large-scale sensor network. We show that the absence of geographic/state information leads to broadcasting strategies that require an exponential number of transmissions, whereas networks with very little geographic or state information require only a polynomial number of transmissons.
