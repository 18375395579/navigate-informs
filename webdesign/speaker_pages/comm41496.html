Title: Valuation and Hedging of Actual Balance Interest Rate Swaps

Author: Adrian Becker, PhD Candidate, MIT Operations Research Center, 77 Mass Ave, Bldg E40-130, Cambridge, MA, 02139-4307, United States of America, adrianbb@mit.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In an asset-backed securitization deal, when investors demand interest payments in a different basis than that of the securitized assets, it is necessary to engage in a swap on the actual asset balance. This differs from traditional swaps in that the notional amounts are stochastic. We propose a valuation method based on the relationship between changes in the market cost of borrowing and underlying obligor payment activity. We then develop a robust optimization approach to hedge risk exposure.
