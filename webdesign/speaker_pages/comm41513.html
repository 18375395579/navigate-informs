Title: Group-strategyproof Mechanisms for Network Design Games
Author: Jochen Konemann, Kunlun Tan Department of Combinatorics and Optimization University of Waterloo, Waterloo, ON, Canada, jochen@math.uwaterloo.ca
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this talk we first define a class of cost-sharing games arising from network design optimization problems. We then show how group-strategyproof (i.e., collusion resistant) mechanisms for such games can be derived from existing primal-dual algorithms for the underlying optimization problems in an automatic way.
