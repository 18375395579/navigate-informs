Title: Theoretical Extensions of the Graph Theoretic Cell Transmission Model

Author: Srinivas Peeta, Professor, Purdue University, Civil Engineering, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, West Lafayette, IN, 47907-2051, United States, peeta@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Georgios Kalafatas

Year: 2007

Abstract: The Graph Theoretic Cell Transmission Model (GTCTM) provides an exact graph theoretical structure for modeling traffic flow and production planning. We now show that time-expanded or dynamic graph networks are instances of the GTCTM, thus the GTCTM is a generalized dynamic graph structure. Moreover, inspired by known concepts from the building evacuation problem, we show that for the single destination GTCTM three commonly used optimization objectives are equivalent.
