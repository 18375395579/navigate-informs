Title: Redesign of Elderly Service Network

Author: Osman Ozaltin, Doctoral student, University of Pittsburgh, Department of Industrial Engineering, Pittsburgh, PA, 15260, United States, oyo1@pitt.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Johnson

Year: 2007

Abstract: Senior centers coordinate and offer a variety services to make independent living a viable option for older adults . In this paper, we present novel math models to redesign a county-wide senior center service network by considering some innovative service mechanisms. There are three tasks associated with this goal: determining the best locations for the senior centers of different types, designing staffing schedules among the different senior centers, and redistributing government funding among the service centers.
