Title: Optimal Stock Allocation for Production-Inventory Systems with Multiple Impatient Customer Classes

Author: Yasar Levent Kocaga, Assistant Professor, Sy Syms School of Business, Yeshiva University, 2495 Amsterdam Avenue, New York, NY, 10033, United States of America, kocaga@yu.edu

Coauthor(s): Yen-Ming Lee

Year: 2013

Abstract: We address the production and inventory control of a make-to-stock system that serves multiple impatient customer classes. We assume Poisson arrivals and an exponential production time. Orders that are not satisfied immediately wait up to an exponentially distributed amount of time and abandon the system if not satisfied within this time. We show that the threshold inventory rationing policy is still optimal under certain conditions including a requirement on the order of abandonment rates.
