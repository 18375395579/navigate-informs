Title: Optimally Stationing and/or Reducing the US Military using a Mixed Integer Linear Program (MILP)

Author: Michael Teter, Major, USA, Colorado School of Mines, 1500 Illinios Ave, Golden, CO, 80401, United States of America, mteter@mines.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Unit Stationing and Reduction Model optimally stations and/or reduces the United States Military over a given time horizon while meeting operational, personnel, facility, range, flight line and/or budgetary constraints. This model has been validated over several data sets including the last Base Realignment And Closure (BRAC) from 2005.
