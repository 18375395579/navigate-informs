Title: Comparing Symmetry Breaking Techniques

Author: James Ostrowski, University of Tennessee, 1425 S Stadium Dr, Knoxville, TN, 37996, United States of America, jostrows@utk.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: There are a variety of ways to break symmetry in an MILP problem. We will discuss several of these methods, and test them on a variety of highly symmetric problems.
