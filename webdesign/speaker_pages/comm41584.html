Title: An Optimization Framework for Resource Management and Course of Action Analysis

Author: Michael Hirsch, Principal Engineer, Raytheon, PO Box 12248, M/S 61, St. Petersburg, FL, 33733, United States of America, Michael_J_Hirsch@raytheon.com

Coauthor(s): Rakesh Nagi, David Sudit

Year: 2008

Abstract: COA problem domain considered with support of fusion technology. Assume that level 1/2 fusion technology provides a set of suspicious situations currently unfolding and associated objects of interest participating in these situations. Limited resources are available to gather additional information. COA analysis and RM prescribe placement of resources to accomplish desired tasks. Problem formulated from an optimization framework, resulting in an NP-hard problem.
