Title: Return-on-investment (ROI) Criteria for the Network Design of Retail Chains

Author: Seokjin Kim, Suffolk University, ISOM Department, 8 Ashburton Place, Boston, MA, 02108, United States of America, kim@suffolk.edu

Coauthor(s): Rongbing Huang, Mozart Menezes

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a model for the optimal location of retail stores operating as an M/M/1/K queue on a discrete network. The objective is to maximize the profit of the chain subject to a minimum required ROI. Our model determines simultaneously the location and number of stores, the allocation of demands to the stores, and investment costs at the stores. We present managerial implications from parametric analysis. We also develop heuristic procedures and computational results are promising.
