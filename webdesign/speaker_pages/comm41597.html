Title: A Ranking and Selection Procedure for a Transient Mean Performance Measure
Author: Doug Morrice, Professor, University of Texas at Austin, Red McCombs School of Business, 1 University Station, B6500, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, Douglas.Morrice@mccombs.utexas.edu
Coauthor(s): Mark Brantley, Chun-Hung Chen
Year: 2008
Abstract: We develop a Ranking and Selection procedure for selecting the best configuration based on a transient mean performance measure. The procedure extends the OCBA approach to systems whose means are changing over time. We discuss a few motivating examples for this approach.
