Title: An Optimization Model for the Switchgrass-Based Biomass Production

Author: Halil Cobuloglu, PhD Student, Wichita State University, 1627 Fairmount St, Wichita, KS, 67208, United States of America, halil.cobuloglu@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Esra Buyuktahtakin

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this study, we propose an optimization model, which defines the best decision strategies for biomass production regarding seeding time and methods, land allocation, harvesting amount and time under limited budget while considering the environmental impacts. Results will be presented for switchgrass cultivation as a source of biomass in the State of Kansas.
