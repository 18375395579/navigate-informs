Title: Static Repositioning in Bike-sharing System

Author: Satish Ukkusuri, Professor, Purdue University, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, West lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, sukkusur@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Binh Luong

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we develop a strategy for repositioning the bikes in a Bike Sharing System. The objective is to minimize the amount of unmet demand at the stations and at the same time, to minimize the cost of the repositioning given the capacity constraints.
