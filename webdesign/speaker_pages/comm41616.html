Title: Modeling and Solutions for Seasonal Transmission Switching
Author: Masood Jabarnejad, PhD Student, Auburn University, 333 East Magnolia Ave, Cavell Court, Unit 44, Auburn, AL, 36830, United States of America, mzj0008@auburn.edu
Coauthor(s): Jianhui Wang, Jorge Valenzuela
Year: 2013
Abstract: Recently, optimal transmission switching is proposed for cost reduction in the power generation and dispatch. Contemporary transmission infrastructure favors the infrequent periodic switching. We propose seasonal transmission switching (STS) to meet power demands and N-1 reliability requirements throughout a season and to manage technological limitations in transmission systems, while providing cost savings. To this end, we develop a decomposition algorithm to solve the STS problem efficiently.
