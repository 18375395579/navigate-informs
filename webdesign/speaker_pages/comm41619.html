Title: Short-term Allocation of Shipping Contracts in Strategic Transportation Procurement Auctions
Author: Yassin Feki, Laval University, 2325 Rue de la Terrasse, Montreal, QC, Canada, fekihyassin@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Adnene Hajji, Monia Rekik
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a context where the strategic selection of TL carriers uses a combinatorial auction mechanism. We address the problem of allocating shipping contracts to these strategic carriers at the operational level where the objective is to minimize not only transportation costs but also inventory and backorder costs. In this case, one should manage the tradeoff between using the strategic carriers with which the shipper already engage on at the strategic level and spot carriers.
