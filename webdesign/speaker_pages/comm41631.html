Title: Revenue Management for Negotiated Prices

Author: Miguel Lobo, INSEAD, 1 Ayer Rajah Avenue, Singapore, 138676, Singapore, Miguel.LOBO@insead.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the problem of determining the optimal policy for offering an individual discount to a buyer. Buyers have random delays to purchase and to find alternative sellers. We discuss issues of identifiability and robustness when the model parameters are estimated from historical data.
