Title: FPTAS for Continuous Knapsack with Generalized Upper Bounds
Author: Diego Klabjan, Associate Professor, Northwestern University, Ind. Eng. and Mgmt. Sci., d-klabjan@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Bill Pun
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider a very general continuous knapsack problem with generalized upper bounds. An FPTAS is presented based on a very complex and technical dual problem.
