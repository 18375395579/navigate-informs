Title: Intermodal Fleet Sizing

Author: Maia Pykina, Manager Forecasting and Planning, TTX Company, 101 N Wacker Drive, Chicago, IL, 60606, United States, Maia_Pykina@ttx.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: The railroad industry faces a challenge of investing in an intermodal railcar (a fifty-year asset) a purpose of which is to carry a trailer/container (a ten-to-fifteen year asset). How many intermodal railcars and of what configuration should the industry invest in? In this presentation we show how TTX uses its intermodal fleet sizing process to recommend future investments in intermodal capacity.
