Title: Joint Control of Emissions Permit Trading and Production with Fixed-cost Considerations

Author: Jian Yang, Associate Professor, Rutgers University, 1 Washingtong Park RM 1084, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States of America, jyang@business.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Quan Yuan, Frank (Youhua) Chen, Yun Zhou

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the optimal coordination of permit trading and permit-consuming production for a firm facing both Markov permit price process and random demand during a multi-period planning horizon. Fixed setup costs first in trading and then in production are considered. We achieve partial characterization of control policies. A heuristic based on decomposition is found to be effective from computational studies.
