Title: Operation of Signalized Diamond Interchanges with Frontage Roads using Dynamic Reversible Lane Control Signs

Author: Yue Liu, Department of Civil Engineering and Mechanics, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, Milwaukee, WI, United States of America, liu28@uwm.edu

Coauthor(s): Jing Zhao

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper presents an innovative design and operational model for signalized diamond interchange by dynamically reversing certain lanes in the internal link on a regular basis with the deployment of overhead reversible lane control signs. A Binary-Mixed-Integer-Linear-Program (BMILP) is formulated to simultaneously optimize lane markings, dynamic usage of the reversible lane, and signal timings for the new system.
