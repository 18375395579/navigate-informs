Title: A Network Flow Algorithm for Mass Evacuation Traffic Flow Optimization

Author: Hong Zheng, Graduate Student, University of Arizona, 1209 E 2nd St, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, hzheng@email.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Yi-Chang Chiu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We show that the cell transmission model (CTM) based system optimal evacuation problem for infinite horizon equals to the earliest arrival flow/universal maximum flow problem under certain condition.We propose a dynamic network flow algorithm to solve it on time expanded network.It applies scaled flow scheme taking account of ratio of backward wave speed over free flow speed;solution overcomes holding phenomena for diverge and general cells. Numerical examples will be discussed.
