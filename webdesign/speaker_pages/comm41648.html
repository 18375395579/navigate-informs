Title: Optimal Control in a Netflix-like Closed Rental System
Author: Achal Bassamboo, Managerial Economics & Decision Sciences, Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, a-bassamboo@kellogg.northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Ramandeep Randhawa
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider the introduction of a new product in a closed rental system, such as Netflix, with two types of customers who differ on their mean rental time. All customers desire this new product. We assume that if the customer's request for this product is denied, she receives a substitue product and requests for the new product upon return. We study the control problem of minimizing the mean delay encountered before obtaining the new product.
