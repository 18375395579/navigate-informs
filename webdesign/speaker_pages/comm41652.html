Title: Stochastic Optimal Control of Dynamic Resource Allocation Problems

Author: Xuefeng Gao, Georgia Institute of Technology, H. Milton School of ISyE, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, gxf1240@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingdong Lu, Mayank Sharma, Mark Squillante, Joost Bosman

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a class of dynamic resource allocation problems that intrinsically involves different resource types. The goal is to determine the resource allocation capacities for serving uncertain demand that maximize the expected net-benefit based on rewards and costs associated with the different resources. We derive the optimal control policy within a singular control setting, which includes simple expressions for governing the dynamic adjustments to resource allocation capacities over time.
