Title: A Combined Scheduling Transportation Problem

Author: Johan Oppen, Associate Professor, Molde University College, Specialized University in Logistics, P O Box 2110, Molde, 6402, Norway, johan.oppen@himolde.no

Coauthor(s): Markus Brachner

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a real-world planning problem that occur, for example, in the construction industry. Given a number of jobs or projects, each with a known location, estimated duration and demand of materials, the problem is to assign projects to teams of workers, schedule the projects for each team, and construct a transportation plan for how to supply the projects with the needed materials. Modeling and solution approaches are discussed.
