Title: Two Product Inventory Control Systems with Substitution

Author: Olga Ortiz, PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, oortiz@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Erera, Chip White

Year: 2006

Abstract: Consider a two-product inventory system where demand is observed only through sales. When a stock-out occurs, substitution can take place further reducing demand observability. We present approaches based on partially observed Markov decision processes to assess the potential benefit from improving demand observability in this setting.
