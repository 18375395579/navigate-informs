Title: Algorithms for Finding Bayes-Nash Equilibria in Core-selecting Auctions
Author: Mark Schneider, University of Connecticut, Uconn School of Business, 81 Cheney Drive, Storrs, CT, United States of America, mschneider@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Robert Day
Year: 2012
Abstract: Determining Bayes-Nash equilibria in combinatorial auctions is quite difficult, and to date, the analytical approach to finding an exact equilibrium has been successful only in very limited settings. In this work, we explore the alternative possibility of approximating equilibrium behavior using numerical methods, with a focus on the class of core-selecting auctions.
