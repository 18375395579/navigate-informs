Title: Allocating Slots for Import Containers in Container Terminal Yards with Uncertain Pick-up Sequences
Author: Pengfei Zhou, United States of America, pfzhou@yeah.net
Coauthor(s): Anne Goodchild
Year: 2013
Abstract: An uncertain pick-up order model is considered to reduce rehandling in container terminals, while also capturing uncertainty in truck arrival times. A least-second reshuffle based heuristic algorithm is proposed to reduce the reshuffle ratio. The experiments reveal that the proposed method could improve the relocation performance in reasonable time.
