Title: On a Class of Random Walks in the Quarter Plane

Author: Mark Squillante, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, mss@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Tomasz Nowicki, Grzegorz Swirszcz

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a general class of random walks in the quarter plane and derive some fundamental properties of the stationary distribution for this class of random walks.
