Title: A Tight Iteration-complexity Bound for IPM via Redundant Klee-Minty Cubes

Author: Murat Mut, Doctorate Student, Lehigh University, 200 W. Packer Ave, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, mhm309@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Tamás Terlaky

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider two curvature integrals for the central path of a polyhedra. Redundant Klee-Minty cubes (Nematollahi et al., 2007) have central path whose geometric curvature is exponential in the dimension of the cube. We prove an analogous result for the curvature integral introduced by Sonnevend et al. 1990. Within an algorithmic framework, we rigorously prove that the iteration-complexity upper bound for the Klee-Minty cubes is tight.
