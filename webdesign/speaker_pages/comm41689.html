Title: Linear and Second Order Approximations to the SOS Cone

Author: Amir Ali Ahmadi, Goldstine Fellow, IBM Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, Yorktown Heights,, NY, United States of America, a_a_a@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Anirudha Majumdar

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present linear and second order inner approximations to the sum of squares (SOS) cone and new hierarchies for polynomial programs that are considerably more scalable than the SOS hierarchies.
