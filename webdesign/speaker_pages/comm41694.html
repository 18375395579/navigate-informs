Title: Optimal Sequential Decision Making Relative to a Benchmark

Author: Assaf Zeevi, Professor, Columbia Business School, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, assaf@gsb.columbia.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a simple class of problems involving online decision making with a goal of outperforming a given and known benchmark. Casting this as a ``one armed'' bandit problem, we develop strategies whose performance over any time horizon is as good as that of an oracle up to a finite fixed loss.
