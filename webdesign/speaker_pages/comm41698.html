Title: The Exploration-exploitation Trade-off in the Newsvendor Problem

Author: Juan Chaneton, PhD Candidate, Columbia University, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, United States of America, jmc2274@columbia.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the multiperiod Newsvendor problem, where unmet demand is censored and demand distribution is initially unknown. We focus our analysis in assessing two gaps of interest: the full exploitation versus optimal policy gap and the uncensored versus censored optimal costs. We show that, for one of the few known tractable cases, neither gap is substantial, and we characterize their structural behavior. In particularw we conclude that there is no potential gain from exploring in this problem.
