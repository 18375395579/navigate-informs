Title: Covering Models for Sensor Placement in Water Distribution Systems under Uncertainty

Author: Jianhua Xu, Doctoral Candidate, Department of Engineering and Public Policy, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213-3890, United States, mumu@cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeanne VanBriesen, Michael Johnson, Paul Fischbeck, Mitchell Small

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present math programming models for location of sensors across a water distribution network. Multiple objectives include minimizing placement cost, maximizing system coverage and minimizing the population at risk, all subject to probability thresholds on detection time. The model is applied to distribution systems of varying sizes. Computational results and policy implications are reported.
