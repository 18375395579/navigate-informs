Title: Crew and Equipment Optimization for Passenger Rail

Author: Marc Meketon, Vice President, Oliver Wyman, 100 University Square, Suite 100, Princeton, NJ, 08540, United States of America, marc.meketon@oliverwyman.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Specific approaches work well for equipment and crew planning and optimization for passenger rail that can reduce total labor costs and equipment needs - or increase the total number of trains that can be operated with the same number of trains. A set of specific techniques that have been applied at actual passenger operators will be presented.
