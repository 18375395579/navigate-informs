Title: Dynamic Pricing with Heterogeneous Users

Author: Hani Mahmassani, Professor, Northwestern University, Transportation Center, Evanston, Il, 60208, United States of America, masmah@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Jing Dong

Year: 2008

Abstract: This study presents a dynamic pricing scheme taking into consideration of heterogeneity in users?route choice behavior and in value of time (VOT). A multi-criterion traffic assignment model is adapted to achieve traffic management goal through time-dependent link pricing strategies.
