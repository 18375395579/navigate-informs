Title: A Parallel Development of Nonparametric Models
Author: Andy Johnson, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, 237K Zachry Engineering Center, College Station, TX, 77845-3131, United States of America, ajohnson@tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Timo Kuosmanen
Year: 2008
Abstract: The single output multi-input DEA model will be shown to be a constrained special case of convex nonparametric least squares. Also, DEA will be shown to be a nonparametric generalization of parametric programming. Through similar arguments Stochastic Nonparametric Envelopment of Data (StoNED) is also a nonparametric generalization of SFA. A new model that generalizes COLS to the nonparametric setting called corrected convex nonparametric least squares (C2NLS) is described.
