Title: Upgrading Policy after the Redesign of a Component for Reliability Improvement

Author: Geert-Jan van Houtum, Eindhoven University of Technology, PO Box 513, Eindhoven, 5600MB, Netherlands, g.j.v.houtum@tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Kurtulus Oner, Gudrun Kiesmuller

Year: 2013

Abstract: Consider an installed base of technical systems maintained by one party, which has to meet a given target system availability level. For a given component, a more reliable version has been designed. Should one immediately replace the current components in the systems by new components, or should one do this gradually, e.g., after failures of the current components? We will study multiple upgrading policies and showing their effect on system availability, downtime costs and maintenance costs.
