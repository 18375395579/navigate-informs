Title: Strategies for Intermingling of High Speed and Legacy Railway Services

Author: Steven Harrod, Assistant Professor, University of Dayton, 1143 Ashburton Dr., Dayton, OH, 45459, United States of America, a2soft@aol.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Most proposals for high speed railway services in North America seek to intermingle high speed passenger services with existing conventional railway services on shared routes. This presentation discusses how these various proposals plan to address specific operating plans and technology. The session will present details on operating rules and modifications to the infrastructure for each system. Relevant European shared network operations will also be presented.
