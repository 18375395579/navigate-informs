Title: Network Design Optimization for Canada Post

Author: Scott Sutherland, Consultant, IBM Global Business Services, 12902 Federal Systems Park Dr., Fairfax, VA, 22033, United States, ssutherland@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Hua Ni, Bianica Pint

Year: 2007

Abstract: The postal operation is very complex, with time-sensitive mail products under different processing and transportation requirements. We developed an MIP model for Canada Post to determine the optimal mix of network, flow and machinery based on fixed and variable costs. The model also considers network expansion from potential facilities.
