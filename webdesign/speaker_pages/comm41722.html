Title: Optimal Locomotive Shop Location and Capacity Planning
Author: Kamalesh Somani, Manager Operations Research, CSX Transportation, 500 Water St, Jacksonville, FL, 32202, Kamalesh_Somani@csx.com
Coauthor(s): Weijun Xie, Yanfeng Ouyang
Year: 2012
Abstract: Multiple levels of locomotive maintenance and repair are done by Locomotive Shops with various capabilities located at some major yards in the railroad network. Strategically, it is of interest to find the optimal location of locomotive shops and the capacity of these facilities. We have designed a MIP formulation to solve this problem. Numerical study is conducted to verify the model.
