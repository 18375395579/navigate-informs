Title: Meta-heuristic for Multi-trip Pickup & Delivery Problem with Time Windows and Synchronization
Author: Teodor Gabriel Crainic, Professor, School of Management & CIRRELT, Universite du Quebec a Montreal, Montreal, PW, Canada, TeodorGabriel.Crainic@cirrelt.ca
Coauthor(s): Michel Toulouse, Phuong Nguyen Khanh
Year: 2013
Abstract: Multi-zone Multi-trip Pickup and Delivery Problem with Time Windows and Synchronization (MZT-PDTWS) aims to address the integration of outbound traffic with the classical inbound traffic into a single city logistic system. We present the problem description and a meta-heuristic for the MZT-PDTWS.
