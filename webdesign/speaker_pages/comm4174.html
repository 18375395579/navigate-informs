Title: Inventory Control with Observation Costs

Author: Vidyadhar Kulkarni, Professor, Dept. of Stat and OR, University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599-3260, United States, vkulkarn@email.unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Nan Liu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider an inventory system where there is a fixed cost to observe the inventory level. After observing the level one needs to decide how much to order and when to observe the inventory level next. We present results about the structure of the optimal policy.
