Title: Fluid Models and Large Deviations for Monotone Bandwidth Sharing Networks
Author: Bert Zwart, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, bertzwart@gatech.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider uniqueness of fluid models of overloaded monotone bandwidth sharing networks. And apply our fluid limit results to a large deviations problem.
