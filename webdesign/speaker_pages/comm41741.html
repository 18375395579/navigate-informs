Title: A Model of Facility Hardening in the Presence of Disruptions
Author: Michael Lim, Northwestern University, Department of IE/MS, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States, m-lim2@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Mark Daskin, Sunil Chopra, Achal Bassamboo
Year: 2007
Abstract: We formulate a location problem incorporating facility hardening in the presence of random disruptions. We show the presence of two regimes: one using only hardened facilities and one using hardened and non-hardened sites. We examine the impact of misestimating the failure probability and derive managerial policies to mitigate facility disruptions.
