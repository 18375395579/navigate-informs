Title: Matching and Forecasting US Inland Trade

Author: Bengt Muten, Principal, Global Insight, 24 Hartwell Ave, Lexington, MA, 02421, United States, bengt.muten@globalinsight.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: We have matched and reconciled a number of public and private date sources to show and forecast commodity flows through US ports by ultimate origin, destination, and commodity. The author will present some of the data sources, describe the methodology used to match them, discuss assumptions, and present samples of the results.
