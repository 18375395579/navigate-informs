Title: Safe Tractable Approximation of Chance Constrained Randomly Perturbed LMIs

Author: Arkadi Nemirovski, Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States, nemirovs@isye.gatech.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present an approximation scheme which allows to build tight, in certain precise sense, computationally tractable sufficient conditions for the validity of chance constrained randomly perturbed Linear Matrix Inequalities.
