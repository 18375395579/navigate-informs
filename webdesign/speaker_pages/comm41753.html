Title: On the Exponential Rate of Convergence to Stationarity of the M/M/n Queue in the Halfin-Whitt Regime

Author: David Goldberg, MIT, Operations Research Center, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, dag3141@MIT.EDU

Coauthor(s): David Gamarnik

Year: 2008

Abstract: We analyze the rate of convergence to stationarity in total variation of the M/M/n queue in the Halfin-Whitt regime, with applications to dynamically staffing call centers. We compute exactly, in a simple closed form, the limiting value of the spectral gap for the M/M/n queue in this regime. We also show that there is a very interesting phase transition , unifying earlier efforts to asymptotically characterize the spectral gap.
