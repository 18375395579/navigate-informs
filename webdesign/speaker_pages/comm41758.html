Title: Maximizing the Value of Windpower in Electricity Systems: A Complementarity Programming Approach

Author: Scott Rogers, University of Toronto, Department of Mech and Ind Eng, Toronto, m5s3g8, Canada, scott.rogers@utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Paul Dabrowski

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper describes an approach to maximizing the value of wind-based generation in a hydro-thermal electricity system under different types of market designs. This work extends the Murphy and Smeers (2005) approach. Examples from the Ontario electricity system are described.
