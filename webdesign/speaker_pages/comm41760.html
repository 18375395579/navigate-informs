Title: Integrated Planning of Spare Parts and Tools for Corrective Maintenance of Capital Goods
Author: Geert-Jan van Houtum, Professor, Eindhoven University of Technology, PO Box 513, Eindhoven, 5600 MB, Netherlands, G.J.v.Houtum@tue.nl
Coauthor(s): Ana Busic, Alan Scheller-Wolf, Ingrid Vliegen
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present on the inventory planning of spare parts and service tools by an Original Equipment Manufacturer in the semiconductor supplier industry. For the corrective maintenance of machines installed at customers, a quick provisioning of parts and tools is essential in order to realize high availabilities of the machines. We compare two methods that integrate the planning of parts and tools and show the benefits relative to a non-integrated planning.
