Title: An Improved Algorithm for the Generalized Quadratic Assignment Problem

Author: Artur Pessoa, Adjunct Professor, Universidade Federal Fluminense, Rua Passo da Patria 156 Bloco D, sala 306, Sao Domingos, Niteroi, RJ, 24210-240, Brazil, artur@producao.uff.br

Coauthor(s): Monique Guignard-Spielberg, Yi-Rong Zhu, Peter Hahn

Year: 2008

Abstract: In the Generalized Quadratic Assignment Problem (GQAP), given M facilities and N locations, one must assign each facility to one location so as to satisfy the given facility space requirements, minimizing the sum of installation and facility interaction costs. In this paper, we propose a new Lagrangean relaxation and a lower bounding procedure for the GQAP. The new method allowed us to optimally solve 17 out of 19 instances from the literature with up to 35 facilities, 6 of which were open.
