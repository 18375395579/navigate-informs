Title: A Class of Normative Link Flow Control Problems in Transportation Networks
Author: Henry Liu, University of Minnesota, Department of Civil Engineering, Minneapolis, MN, United States, henryliu@umn.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Transportation network equilibrium problems describe how individuals travel through a transportation system. In many cases, the analysis is motivated by the need to decide on a transportation investment, regulation, or policy, to achieve system objectives desired by public authorities. These analyses are normative, not descriptive. We present an inverse variational inequality formulation and solution method based on a proximal point algorithm (PPA) for a class of network flow control problems.
