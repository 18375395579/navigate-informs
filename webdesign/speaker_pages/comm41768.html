Title: Solution Approaches for a Capacitated Multi-Product Lot-Sizing Problem with Remanufacturing

Author: Ibrahim Karakayali, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611-6595, United States of America, ibrahimk@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Sila Cetinkaya, Halit Uster, Elif Akcali

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a capacitated multi-product lot-sizing problem for a hybrid manufacturing and remanufacturing system. We focus on a setting where manufactured and remanufactured items are perfectly substitutable; manufacturing, remanufacturing, and disposal activities are capacitated; and manufacturing capacity is shared among multiple product types. We develop decomposition-based solution approaches, and present computational results illustrating the efficiency of the proposed solution approaches.
