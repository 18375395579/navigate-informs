Title: Management of a Fleet of Tow Trucks in Real-time
Author: Eric Duchenne, Professor, University of Valenciennes, LAMIH, Le Mont-Houy, Valenciennes, 59313, France, eric.duchenne@univ-valenciennes.fr
Coauthor(s): Frédéric Semet
Year: 2008
Abstract: When a fleet of tow trucks has to be managed in real-time, a key question is the selection of the pending call to which a free tow truck is assigned. In this talk we show how this problem can be modeled and we propose various approaches for its solution.
