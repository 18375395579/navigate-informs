Title: Reducing Workload Differences for Ambulances with Alpha Reliable Coverage

Author: Hari Rajagopalan, Assistant Professor, Francis Marion University, School of Business, P.O. Box 10, Florence, SC, 29501, United States of America, hrajagop@fmarion.edu

Coauthor(s): Frances Vergara, Cem Saydam

Year: 2008

Abstract: Emergency administrators face task of locating a limited number of ambulances to yield the best service to a population. To maintain coverage when demand fluctuates, EMS operators are practicing dynamic redeployment strategies by relocating the fleet during the day. The more busy an ambulance is the greater the chance of crew fatigue and increasing attrition. We formulate a probabilistic model to minimize the workload differences within the fleet while maintaining coverage requirements.
