Title: The c-mu Rule with Abandonments

Author: Mark Lewis, Associate Professor, School of Operations Research and Information Engineering, Cornell University, 226 Rhode Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14850, United States of America, mark.lewis@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Douglas Down

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the problem of allocating a single server to two parallel queues. Customers that arrive to each queue abandon if they have not begun service after an exponential amount of time. Conditions under which a c-mu type rule is optimal are presented.
