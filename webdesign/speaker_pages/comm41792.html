Title: Resource Management in Data Centers with Non-Homogeneous and Multi-class workload

Author: Soongeol Kwon, Texas A&M University, 3021 ETB, 3131 TAMU, College Station, 77843, United States of America, soongeol@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Natarajan Gautam

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider resource management in data centers where multiple classes of requests arrive at a dispatcher at time-varying rates. This paper develops strategies to: (i) assign classes to servers, (ii) determine when each of servers would be powered on and off, (iii) set the processing speed of each server and (iv) route requests from dispatcher to servers. The objective is to achieve time-stable performance while maintaining high performance and cost-effectiveness with time-varying arrivals.
