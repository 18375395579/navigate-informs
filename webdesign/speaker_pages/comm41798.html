Title: Dynamic Allocation to Multiple-class Demand with Continuous Review

Author: Joseph Milner, University of Toronto, 105 St. George Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 3E6, Canada, milner@rotman.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Panos Kouvelis, Qing Ding

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider how a firm should dynamically allocate inventory to different customer classes that are differentiated by price and willingness to wait for delayed delivery as well as reorder in a continuous review environment with positive lead times. We establish the optimal policy and introduce efficient and robust heuristics.
