Title: Capacity Investment with Demand Learning

Author: Anyan Qi, University of Michigan, 701 Tappan St. R4421, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, anyqi@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Hyun-Soo Ahn, Amitabh Sinha

Year: 2013

Abstract: How should a profit-maximizing firm adjust its capacity for a product with a finite life cycle when the market information is incomplete but can be learned? We characterize the firm’s optimal policy about when and by how much to adjust the capacity in a multi-period model. We also propose simple data-driven heuristics, show the asymptotic convergence, and characterize the convergence rate. We finally illustrate the benefit of demand learning using data of Ford Focus.
