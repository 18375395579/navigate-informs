Title: A Stochastic Humanitarian Inventory Model of Fixed Lifetime Goods for Disaster Planning

Author: Miguel Jaller, Ph.D Student, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, jallem@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Jose Holgiun-Veras, Satish Ukkusuri

Year: 2007

Abstract: Disasters, whether natural or manmade, impact different regions of the world on a fairly frequent basis, making global emergency relief operations an important subject to study. This paper introduces a cost minimization multi-commodity, multiple supplier, two echelon, multi-period stochastic inventory model for disaster planning of fixed lifetime commodities.
