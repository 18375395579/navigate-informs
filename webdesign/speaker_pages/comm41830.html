Title: A Single Settlement Energy only Market to Harness Wind Generation
Author: Golbon Zakeri, University of Auckland, 70 Symonds Street, Auckland, New Zealand, g.zakeri@auckland.ac.nz
Coauthor(s): Geoff Pritchard, Andy Philpott
Year: 2012
Abstract: We discuss a stochastic-programming-based method for scheduling electric power generation subject to uncertainty. Such uncertainty may arise from either imperfect forecasting or moment-to-moment fluctuations, and on either the supply or the demand side. The method gives a system of locational marginal prices which reflect the uncertainty, and these may be used in a market settlement scheme in which payment is for energy only. We show that this scheme is revenue-adequate in expectation.
