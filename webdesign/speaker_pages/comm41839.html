Title: Scheming the Postal Service: An Algorithm for Delivery Point Sequencing

Author: Mariah Jeffery, IBM Global Business Services, Fairfax, VA, mjeffery@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Michael Gibbs

Year: 2006

Abstract: We discuss the development of a tool for creating sort schemes for the United States Postal Service’s Delivery Point Sequencing (DPS) operation. The tool uses a polynomial time algorithm to assign ZIP codes to DPS machines for processing with an objective of minimizing the total equipment required while meeting service standards.
