Title: Adaptive Spare Part Replenishment Driven by Condition Monitoring Technique

Author: Haitao Liao, Assistant Professor, Wichita State University, 1845 Fairmount St., Industrial & Manufacturing Eng Dept, Wichita, KS, 67260-0035, United States, haitao.liao@wichita.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Just-in-time spare part management reduces the total downtime of a machine by carrying minimal spare part inventory and replenishing it only when and as needed. This paper presents a sensor-driven adaptive spare part replenishment strategy for several machines of the same type. A joint estimation approach is utilized to quantify the health status of each machine for updating the system-level replenishment strategy. An example of a bench top CNC milling machine is provided for demonstration.
