Title: A Sampling Approach for Minimizing Emissions in Urban Logistics

Author: Jan Fabian Ehmke, Assistant Professor, Freie Universität Berlin, Garystr. 21, Berlin, 14195, Germany, JanFabian.Ehmke@fu-berlin.de

Coauthor(s): Ann Campbell, Barrett Thomas

Year: 2013

Abstract: We discuss the determination of emissions optimized paths in urban areas. Here, frequent changes of speed as often observed in peak times increase emissions significantly. To this end, Dijkstra’s algorithm is extended by time-dependent sampling, which allows for more realistic estimation of emission and arrival time functions. Based on a large real-world data set, results will show that our approach yields less emissions than common distance and travel based optimization.
