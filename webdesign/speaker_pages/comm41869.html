Title: Approximation Algorithms for Stochastic Revenue Management

Author: Ana Radovanovic, Research Staff Member, Stochastic Analysis Group, IBM, Mathematical Sciences Department, IBM TJ Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Route 134, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States, aradovan@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Retsef Levi

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a system with a single resource pool of capacity C that is facing demands from M different classes of customers. Each customer requests one unit of the resource for a certain period of time which is a-priori random; if served, he/she is willing to pay for the resource. Our goal is to find admission policies that (i) Can be computed efficiently; (ii) Are intuitive conceptually; (iii) Admit worst-case performance guarantees for any value of capacity C.
