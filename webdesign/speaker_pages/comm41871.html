Title: Logistic Planning for the Mining Company LKAB

Author: Mikael Ronnqvist, Professor, Universit?Laval, Pavillon Adrien-Pouliot, 1065, avenue de la Médecine, Quebec, QC, Canada, mikael.ronnqvist@gmc.ulaval.ca

Coauthor(s): Dick Carlsson, Patrik Flisberg

Year: 2013

Abstract: LKAB is a large iron ore mining company and a world-leader in pellet manufacturing. It produces more than 20 million tons which is transported with trains to two harbours. In order to increase the production, LKAB needs to optimize its train logistics. We report on a modeling system and experiences that is used to schedule locomotives and wagons including maintenance. This is the basis for what-if-scenarios including new locomotives, new terminals and the opening of two mines.
