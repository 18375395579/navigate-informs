Title: A Day-to-Day Dynamic Congestion Pricing Policy Towards System Optimal

Author: Fan Yang, ESRI, Inc., 380 New York Street, Redlands, CA, 92373, United States, fyang@esri.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study proposed a simple and socially efficient day-to-day dynamic pricing policy for transport agencies to guide the day-to-day dynamic traffic flow movement to system optimal instead of user equilibrium, considering a more general adjustment behavior and less restrictive assumptions on the link travel time function.
