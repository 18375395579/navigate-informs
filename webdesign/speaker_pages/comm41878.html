Title: Asymptotic Properties of a Class of Stochastic Loss Models

Author: David A. Goldberg, Assistant Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, dgoldberg9@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingdong Lu, Mayank Sharma, Mark Squillante, Dmitriy Katz

Year: 2013

Abstract: Stochastic models in which an opportunity is lost when insufficient resources are available to fulfill demand arise naturally in workforce management applications, where the lead times on resource availability can be quite large. We derive asymptotic properties of a class of such stochastic workforce loss models.
