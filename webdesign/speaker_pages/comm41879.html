Title: Storage Operations and Investments in an Electrical System
Author: Santhosh Suresh, PhD, Ross School of Business, University of Michigan, 701 Tappan St, ANN ARBOR, MI, 48109, United States of America, sssandy@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Roman Kapuscinski, Owen Wu
Year: 2012
Abstract: Storage technology is becoming increasingly prevalent in today's electric grid. We discuss the optimal siting and sizing of storage units in the view of minimizing overall operating costs accounting for transmission losses unlike individual profit maximization setting in most previous literature. We show the degree of storage pooling to be related structurally with: the efficiency of storage and transmission, variance, correlation and mean in the demand distribution; using a three node model.
