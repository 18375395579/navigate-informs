Title: Design of Resilient Network Topologies with Balanced Rings
Author: Halit Uster, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, uster@tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Sarath Sasi Kumar
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider the design of telecommunications network topologies with ring components balanced with respect to various criteria. We provide efficient heuristic solution approaches and present promising computational results.
