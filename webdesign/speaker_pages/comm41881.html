Title: Reliability Analysis & Optimization of Cluster Based Ad-hoc Networks
Author: Jose Ramirez-Marquez, Assistant Professor, Stevens Institute of Technology, Castle Point on Hudson, Hoboken, NJ, 07030, United States, Jose.Ramirez-Marquez@stevens.edu
Coauthor(s): Jason Cook
Year: 2007
Abstract: A method to analyze the reliability of a cluster-based MAWN is presented. The method considers that in such networks, communication among mobile nodes is enabled through one or more backbone sub-networks, which have significant impact on the reliability of the MAWN and thus, are critical for its reliable operation. This method is the basis of an optimization model where the objective is to maximize reliability via the design of backbone networks yet constrained by cost and/or weight.
