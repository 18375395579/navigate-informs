Title: Global Dual Sourcing under Exchange-rate Uncertainty

Author: Shahryar Gheibi, Syracuse University, Whitman School of Management, 721 University Ave., Syracuse, NY, 13244, United States of America, sgheibi@syr.edu

Coauthor(s): Burak Kazaz, Scott Webster

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a firm’s capacity reservation decisions under exchange rate and demand uncertainty. After observing exchange rates, the firm determines what capacity to utilize for manufacturing, but this decision is still made under demand uncertainty. We identify the set of potentially optimal solutions, and the conditions that lead to onshoring, offshoring, and dual sourcing.
