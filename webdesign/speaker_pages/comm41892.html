Title: Reliable Facility Location Design under Uncertain Correlated Disruptions

Author: Mengshi Lu, PhD Student, University of California at Berkeley, Department of IEOR, University of California, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, mengshi@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Z. Max Shen

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a reliable facility location model that allows disruptions to be correlated with an uncertain joint distribution, and minimizes the expected cost under the worst-case distribution. We believe this robust optimization model could serve as an alternative approach for solving reliable facility location problems. Compared to the traditional approach, it better captures disruption propagation effect, requires much less computational effort, and delivers more favorable designs.
