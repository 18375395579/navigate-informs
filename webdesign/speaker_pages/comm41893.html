Title: Energy Station Location Problem in a Transportation Network with Autonomous Users
Author: Yingyan Lou, Assistant Professor, The University of Alabama, 260 H.M. Comer, Tuscaloosa, AL, 35487, United States of America, ylou@eng.ua.edu
Coauthor(s): Burcu Keskin, Mohammad Miralinaghi
Year: 2012
Abstract: This study addresses the energy station location problem (SLP) in a transportation network. We first develop a capacitated SLP with constant travel time and stepwise delay function at each station. We also extend this model to consider varying travel time and continuous delay function with autonomous users following the user equilibrium conditions to better capture travelers?behaviors in the network. These models are solved by specialized branch-and-bound and Lagrangian relaxation algorithms.
