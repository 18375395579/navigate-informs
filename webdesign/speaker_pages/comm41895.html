Title: Multiple Scenario Approaches for Pick-up and Delivery Problems with Time Windows

Author: Niels Agatz, Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University, Rotterdam, Netherlands, nagatz@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Jordan Srour

Year: 2013

Abstract: Due to the asymmetric nature of distances in the pick-up and delivery problem, previous versions of Multiple Scenario Analysis (MSA), which focused on the vehicle routing problem, require adaptation. This presentation demonstrates the capabilities of an MSA strategy that has been tailored to the pick-up and delivery problem with time windows. We provide numerical results based on several instances.
