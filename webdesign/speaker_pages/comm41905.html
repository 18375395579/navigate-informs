Title: Expense Constrained Bidder Optimization in Repeated Auctions

Author: Ramakrishna Gummadi, Stanford University, 475 Via Ortega, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, gummadi@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Key, Alexandre Proutiere

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the online bidding problem of an agent involved in repeated auctions against a stationary random environment. The bidder has expense constraints modeled by a time varying balance and a non-negative income that augments the balance after each auction. For a limiting scenario motivated by sponsored search auction markets, we present a convergence result that reduces the optimal bidding strategy in repeated auctions to its equivalent static version.
