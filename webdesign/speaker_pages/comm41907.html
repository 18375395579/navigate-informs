Title: Extremely Hard to Borrow (Bankrupt) Stocks

Author: Richard Sowers, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Urbana, IL, United States of America, r-sowers@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiao Li, Michael Lipkin

Year: 2012

Abstract: We extend a model of Avellaneda and Lipkin to cover extremely hard-to-borrow (bankrupt) stocks. The only source in stochasticity is Poisson relaxations after short squeezes. We find asymptotic formulae and a risk-neutral model.
