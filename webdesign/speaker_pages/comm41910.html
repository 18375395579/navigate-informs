Title: Order Batching and Throughput Models Considering Picker Congestion
Author: Rakesh Nagi, Professor, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, 438 Bell Hall, University at Buffalo (SUNY), Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States, nagi@buffalo.edu
Coauthor(s): Rajan Batta, Melih Temel
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider the order batching problem and picker assignment problem in distribution facilities. We formulate an objective that considers total travel, picking and picker blocking time and formulate the order batching problem to minimize this objective.
