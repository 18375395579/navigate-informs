Title: Some Future Directions in Vehicle Routing Research
Author: Bruce Golden, University of Maryland, Van Munching Hall, College Park, MD, United States of America, bgolden@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Ed Wasil, Chris Groer
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this tutorial, we discuss some of our recent work in vehicle routing. First, we discuss two multi-period vehicle routing problems. The first arises in the small package shipping industry. The second is important in the utility industry. In addition, we present a cooperative parallel algorithm for generating high quality vehicle routing solutions.
