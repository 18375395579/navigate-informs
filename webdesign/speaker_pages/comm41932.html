Title: Appointment Scheduling for a Server with Sequence-dependent Deteriorating Effects

Author: Eduardo Perez, Assistant Professor, Texas State University, 601 University Drive, San Marcos, TX, 78666, United States of America, eduardopr@txstate.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We describe a mixed integer programming model for scheduling jobs on a single-server with deteriorating effects. The deterioration (fatigue) of the server and the job processing times are functions of the sequence of jobs that have been processed by the server. We focus on the simultaneous effects of sequencing jobs and scheduling start times by considering the overtime cost. We create a set of test problems and provide a computational procedure that evaluates the optimal job schedules.
