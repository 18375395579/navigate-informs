Title: On a Least Absolute Deviations Estimator of a Convex Function
Author: Eunji Lim, Assistant Professor, University of Miami, University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL, United States of America, lim@miami.edu
Coauthor(s): Yao Luo
Year: 2013
Abstract: When estimating a performance measure of a complex system from noisy data over a domain of interest, the underlying function is often known to have a certain shape characteristic such as convexity. We propose a computationally efficient way of fitting a convex function by minimizing the sum of least absolute deviations. We present numerical examples to illustrate the performance of the proposed estimator. We also establish the consistency of the proposed estimator and its derivative.
