Title: Variance Minimization for Constrained Discounted Continuous-time Markov Decision Processes
Author: Jun Fei, Stony Brook University, Dept of Applied Mathematics & Statistics, Stony Brook, NY, 11794, United States of America, royfei@yahoo.com
Coauthor(s): Eugene Feinberg
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper compares variances of optimal policies for constrained discounted Continuous-Time Markov Decision Processes (MDPs). According to the existing theory, optimal policies exist in the form of switching and randomized policies. In the paper we interpret discounting as exponentially distributed stopping time. We provide an index-type formula for the switching policy with the minimal variance and show that it has the smaller variance than the optimal randomized policy.
