Title: Scheduling MRI Patients for a Radiology Department

Author: David Phillips, Assistant Professor, The College of WIlliam & Mary, Math Department, Jones Hall 114, Williamsburg, VA, 23187, United States of America, phillips@math.wm.edu

Coauthor(s): Grace Phillips, Lawrence Leemis, Cliff Stein, Eyjolfur Asgeirsson, Adam Carpenter

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a bi-criteria scheduling problem that arises in Seattle Children's Hospital radiology department when they schedule Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) patients. The objectives represent the trade-offs between scheduling enough time for the MRI exam and revenue generated by the exams. Algorithms to solve the problem are tested on collected and simulated patient data.
