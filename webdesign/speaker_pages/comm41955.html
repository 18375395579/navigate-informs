Title: Optimal Replenishment Policies for Multi-product Stochastic Inventory Systems
Author: Vinayak Desphande, Purdue University, vinayak@exchange.purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Kumar Muthuraman, Haolin Feng
Year: 2007
Abstract: We analyze optimal replenishment policies for multi-product inventory systems with joint-replenishment costs and correlated demand. Our focus is on computing and finding the structure of the optimal policies. An efficient method to compute the optimal policy that uses a moving boundary based policy improvement scheme is analyzed. A new heuristic, called an (s, c, d, S) policy is proposed, and is shown to significantly outperform the simple (s,c,S) policy analyzed in prior literature.
