Title: Routing in Systems with Flexible Multi-server Pools
Author: Alexander Stolyar, Bell Labs, Alcatel-Lucent, 600 Mountain Ave, 2C-322, Murray Hill, NJ, 07974, United States of America, stolyar@research.bell-labs.com
Coauthor(s): Tolga Tezcan
Year: 2008
Abstract: A general model with multiple input flows (classes) and several flexible multi-server pools is considered. We propose a robust, generic algorithm for routing new arrivals, which optimally balances server pools' loads, without the knowledge of the flow input rates and without solving any optimization problem. We study behavior of the algorithm in the fluid limit and Halfin-Whitt (or, QED) asymptotic regimes.
