Title: Forecasting Demand at Union Pacific Railroad
Author: Mark Czerwinski, General Director Business Planning, Union Pacific, 1400 Douglas Street, Stop 1120, Omaha, NE, 68179-1120, United States, MARKCZERWINSKI@UP.COM
Year: 2007
Abstract: The United States economy and overall international trade is booming placing the entire North American transportation infrastructure under stress. The challenge for the North American railroad network is matching resources to meet diverse and growing customer demand. This presentation highlights the process and challenges Union Pacific Railroad faces in forecasting rail demand and integrating the rail demand forecast into the railroad’s business planning function.
