Title: Optimizing Origin-to-destination Flow of Freight in Lake Superior
Author: Hongyi Chen, Assistant Professor, U. of Minnesota, Duluth, MIE Department, Duluth, MN, 55812, United States, hongyi@pdx.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: A method that combines utility theory and nonlinear network modeling is proposed to optimize the origin-to-destination flow of freight in Lake Superior. The method determines the shipping routes with the highest company value.
