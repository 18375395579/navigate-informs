Title: Satisficing Measures for Analysis of Risky Positions
Author: David Brown, Assistant Professor, Duke University, Fuqua School of Business, 1 Towerview Drive, Durham, NC, 27708, United States of America, dbbrown@duke.edu
Coauthor(s): Melvyn Sim
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this work we consider a class of measures for evaluating the quality of financial positions with uncertain payoffs based on their ability to achieve desired financial goals. We call these measures satisficing measures and show that they are dual to classes of corresponding risk measures. We demonstrate the use of these measures in portfolio optimization problems.
