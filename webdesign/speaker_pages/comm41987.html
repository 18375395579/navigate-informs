Title: Travelers Prefer Airline Frequent Flier Programs Over Hotel Frequent Guest Programs

Author: Rex Toh, Professor, Director of the Marketing Program, Seattle University, 901 12th Avenue, PO Box 222000, Seattle, WA, 98122, United States of America, rextoh@seattleu.edu

Coauthor(s): Frederick DeKay

Year: 2007

Abstract: Based on a survey of 304 hotel guests, 81% belong to frequent flier programs, while only 62% belong to hotel frequent guest programs. And of those who belong to frequent guest programs, 90% belong to airline frequent fli er programs while only 69% of airline frequent fliers who stay in hotels belong to hotel frequent guest programs. Why have hotels lagged the airlines in attracting hotel guests to their loyalty programs?
