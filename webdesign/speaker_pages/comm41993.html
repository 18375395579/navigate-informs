Title: Delayed Resolution of Uncertainty: Further Evidence

Author: Enrico Diecidue, INSEAD, Bd. de Constance,, 77305 Fontainebleau cedex, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, enrico.diecidue@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Mohammed Abdellaoui, Ayse Onculer

Year: 2007

Abstract: In most real decisions a considerable time between the choice and the resolution of uncertainty may occur. We capture the impact of delayed resolution of uncertainty through the probability weighting function. We propose an elicitation of individual probability weighting functions with and without delayed resolution of uncertainty. The case of unknown time of resolution is presented. Applications and modeling implications are discussed.
