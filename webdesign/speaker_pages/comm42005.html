Title: A Simple Expansion Formula for Local Volatility Model
Author: Yingda Song, PhD Candidate, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Department of Industrial Engineering, Clear Water Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, ysong@ust.hk
Coauthor(s): Ning Cai
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper proposes a simple expansion formula for European options under local volatility model. The expansion series are given explicitly in terms of Black Scholes greeks. The convergence of the formula is justified both theoretically and numerically.
