Title: On the Flow-level Dynamics of a Packet-switched Network

Author: Ciamac Moallemi, Assistant Professor, Columbia Business School, Uris 416, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, ciamac@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Devavrat Shah

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study the dynamics of a network that employs congestion control at both at the level of flows injecting packets into the network and at the level of packets being scheduled within the network. We investigate how dynamics at these two levels interact without any assumption of time scale separation, and propose algorithms that trade-off the maximization of utility with the minimization of delay.
