Title: A New Tunnel Function Method for Global Optimization

Author: Yong Wang, SAS Institute Inc., 100 SAS Campus Drive, Cary, NC, 27513, United States, yong.wang@sas.com

Coauthor(s): Lian-Sheng Zhang, Shu-Cherng Fang

Year: 2007

Abstract: We introduce a new tunnel function for finding the global minimum of a general coercive C1 function. A tunnel function is constructed at a local minimizer of the objective function such that it achieves local maximum at the current solution. Moreover, a local minimizer of the tunnel function leads to a new solution to the original problem with lower objective value. Iteration follows in this manner to reach a global minimizer. Promising computational results are included and discussed.
