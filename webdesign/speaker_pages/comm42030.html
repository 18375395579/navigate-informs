Title: A Parallel Algorithm for Dynamic Routing and Intersection Control

Author: Shih-Fen Cheng, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, 80 Stamford Road, 178902, Singapore, sfcheng@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Robert L Smith, Marina A. Epelman

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper, we study the challenging problem of finding coordinated intersection control policy and routing choices simultaneously in a network with flow-dependent link travel times. We employ the game-theoretic paradigm of fictitious play to iteratively converge to a locally optimal solution. The algorithm can be parallelized to scale to realistic size networks modeled with high-fidelity simulations such as city of Troy, Michigan. Significant savings can be achieved by this approach.
