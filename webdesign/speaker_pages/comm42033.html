Title: Prevalence of Anchor Points in DEA
Author: Jose Dula, Virginia Commonwealth University, School of Business, Richmond, VA, 23284, United States, jdula@vcu.edu
Coauthor(s): Marie-Laure Bougnol
Year: 2007
Abstract: Anchor points define the transition from the efficient frontier to the non-efficient part of the production possibility set. A recent realization is that these points are prevalent. We propose explanations for this phenomenon.
