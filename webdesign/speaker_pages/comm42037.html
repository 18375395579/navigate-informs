Title: Determining Convex Objective Functions and Importance Factors in Multi-criteria IMRT Planning

Author: Taewoo Lee, University of Toronto, 5 King's College Road, Toronto, Canada, taewoo.lee@utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Michael Sharpe, Timothy Chan, Tim Craig

Year: 2012

Abstract: In multi-criteria optimization, objective function parameters are often determined by a trial-and-error process. Multi-criteria IMRT planning typically involves many objective functions, which leads to a large parameter space to search over. We develop an inverse optimization method to determine convex objective functions and parameters that are most critical in treatment planning. Results show the potential to both streamline the planning process and increase the treatment effectiveness.
