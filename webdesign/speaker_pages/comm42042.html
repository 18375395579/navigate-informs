Title: Robust Dynamic Portfolio Selection with Learning

Author: Andrew Lim, Professor, University of California, Berkeley, Department of Industrial Engineering, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, lim@ieor.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): J. George Shanthikumar

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the problem of dynamic robust portfolio selection with learning in the framework of relative regret, and provide a Bayesian characterization of the solution. The solution involves a tilted posterior distribution, where the tilting is defined in terms of a likelihood ratio that involves the benchmarks in the definition of the problem.
