Title: Integrated Operations Planning and Revenue Management for Rail Freight

Author: Gilles Savard, Ecole Polytechnique de Montréal, CP 6079 Succursale Centre Ville, Montréal, QC, H3C3A7, Canada, gilles.savard@polymtl.ca

Coauthor(s): Jean-François Cordeau, Benoit Crevier

Year: 2006

Abstract: Profit maximization relies heavily on the integration of logistics activities with an improved management of the revenues. The operational policies chosen by the carrier have also an important impact on freight transit time. To achieve this integration, we propose operational and tactical bilevel mathematical models. Solution approaches will be discussed.
