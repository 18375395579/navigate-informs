Title: Risk-Sensitive Real Options Decisions

Author: H. Dharma Kwon, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 1206 South Sixth Street, Champaign, IL, 61820, United States of America, dhkwon@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Lim

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a class of stopping problems under uncertainty with non-linear utility functions. We focus on stationary stopping problems and consider stationary stopping policies. We formulate credible stationary stopping policies, from which the decision-maker will have no incentive to deviate.
