Title: A Dynamic Programming Approach to Optimal Boltzmann Sampling

Author: Archis Ghate, Assistant Professor, University of Washington, Industrial Engineering, Box 352650, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States of America, archis@u.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert L Smith

Year: 2007

Abstract: MCMC is widely applied to sample approximately from low-temperature Boltzmann distributions. Given phases defined by a cooling schedule and the number of iterations allowed, the key is to find the number of iterations in each phase to ensure a high-quality approximation. We present a dynamic programming approach to achieve this.
