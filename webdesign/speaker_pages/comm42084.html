Title: Optimal Paths and Recharging Policies for Electric Vehicles in a Network

Author: Timothy Sweda, PhD Candidate, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Rd., Rm. C210, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, tsweda@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Diego Klabjan, Irina Dolinskaya

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of finding both a path and recharging policy for an electric vehicle traveling in a network such that the total cost of traveling and recharging is minimized. Charging stations are available at every node, and the vehicle's charge level must remain between upper and lower thresholds along the entire path. We propose efficient solution methods and analyze their performance.
