Title: Integrated Replenishment and Inbound Transportation Problem with Batch Ordering

Author: Erhan Kutanoglu, The University of Texas at Austin, ORIE, Austin, United States of America, erhank@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Ying Chen

Year: 2013

Abstract: The problem is motivated by an actual project with a high-mix assembly plant, which is currently located far from most of its contract suppliers. Each supplier provides various parts to the plant, and the parts may have package quantities that satisfy demands for different time horizons. We formulate and solve the problem to find the most beneficial integrated policy of inbound shipping and inventory management when order quantities are restricted to be integral multiples of package quantities.
