Title: Solving the Turkish Crew Scheduling Problem

Author: Melda Ormeci Matoglu, Ozyegin University, Kusbakisi Cad No 2, Altunizade, Istanbul, Turkey, melda.ormeci@ozyegin.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Orsan Ozener, Gunes Erdogan, Mohamed Haouari

Year: 2012

Abstract: Crew scheduling problem is considered to be one of the most challenging problems in airline planning. Problems for the Turkish airline companies are different than those faced by the North American and European companies. The Turkish crew scheduling problem has its specific properties such as fixed crew salaries, only one crew base, and other considerations besides cost minimization. We develop heuristic based novel solution methods to address Turkish non-generic crew scheduling problem.
