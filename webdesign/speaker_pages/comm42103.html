Title: Development of Postive Train Control at CSX Transportation
Author: Suneil Kuthiala, CSX Transportation, 500 Water Street, Jacksonville, FL, 32202, United States of America, suneil_kuthiala@csx.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: All railroads have been manadated to install PTC by 2015 by an Federal law. To achieve this aggressive timetable, significant amounts of planning, research, network analysis, systems design, testing, and capital planning must be undertaken. This presentation will explore the actions CSX is taking to identify which lines will require PTC and get in installed prior to the deadline.
