Title: Estimating the Quantity of Hazardous Material Release from Railroad Tank Cars Involved in Accident

Author: Xiaonan Zhou, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 205 North Mathews Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, xzhou27@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Rapik Saat

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study describes a statistical model to estimate the quantity of release in a train accident from a railroad tank car as a function of its design characteristics and accident environments.
