Title: Online DTA and Anticipatory Pricing for Real-time Traffic Management
Author: Hani Mahmassani, Professor, The University of Maryland at College Park, Department of Civil & Environmental Engi, College Park, MD, 20742-3021, United States, masmah@umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Chung-Cheng Lu, Sevgi Erdogan, Jing Dong
Year: 2007
Abstract: To avoid breakdown on managed lanes, we show how to set anticipatory prices on the basis of short-term predictions generated by an on-line DTA-based traffic estimation and prediction system. The effectiveness of the approach is illustrated using the Washington-Baltimore corridor network.
