Title: A New Approach for Integrating Inventory into Capacitated Facility Location Modeling

Author: Kayse Maass, University of Michigan, 503 Greenwich DR, Apt M, Howell, MI, 48843, United States of America, leekayse@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Siqian Shen, Mark Daskin

Year: 2013

Abstract: While current capacitated fixed charge location problems use hard and fast capacities, in reality, facility managers have many operational tools to extend capacity or to allow the facility to accept demands in excess of the capacity constraint for short periods of time. The model we present begins to capture these operational extensions and is motivated by the explosion in the availability of massive amounts of transactional data. Computational results will be provided.
