Title: Robust Ordinal Regression Approach to MCDA: Principles and State of the Art

Author: Roman Slowinski, Professor, Poznan University of Technology, 60-965 Poznan, Poznan, Poland, roman.slowinski@cs.put.poznan.pl

Coauthor(s): Salvatore Greco, Vincent Mousseau

Year: 2012

Abstract: Robust Ordinal Regression (ROR) finds a whole set of value functions compatible with preference information supplied by a Decision Maker (DM) in terms of pairwise comparisons of some alternatives, and applies this set on a considered set of alternatives. In result, ROR gets two preference relations: necessary and possible, corresponding to preferences holding for all or for at least one compatible value function. We present basic principles as well as a state of the art of ROR applied to MCDA.
