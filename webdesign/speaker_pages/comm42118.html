Title: Skills Planning for Resilient Service Delivery
Author: Rohit M Lotlikar, IBM India Research Lab, Embassy Golf Links, Block D, 4th Floor, Domlur Intermediate Ring Road, Bangalore, 560034, India, rohitmlo@in.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Anuradha Bhamidipaty, Guruduth Banavar, Anees Shaikh, Prashant Pradhan
Year: 2006
Abstract: In remote delivery of IT services from multiple locations, proper skills planning helps mitigate the impact of non availability of personnel due to a disaster at one location. We propose a model for optimal allocation of skills (job roles) across these locations to meet the guaranteed service availability levels during disasters at least cost. Various operational aspects like critical workloads, skill levels, mobile workers, cross-training, and wage costs are factored into the model.
