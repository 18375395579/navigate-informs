Title: Learning the Dynamics of Social Network Diffusion from Limited Data

Author: Andrew Li, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Ave, Bldg E40-130, Operations Research Center, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, aali@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Vivek Farias, Tauhid Zaman

Year: 2013

Abstract: In many real-world settings, the underlying structure of a network is known, but the process on the network is not available and must be estimated from observations. Given an information diffusion process over a network, we consider the problem of learning the process from limited adoption data. We demonstrate conditions on the network structure for such inference to be possible, and we propose efficient learning algorithms which we apply to data from an online marketing campaign on Facebook.
