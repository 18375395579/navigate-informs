Title: Operating Room Scheduling Heuristics to Improve Post-operative Patient Flow
Author: Carter Price, University of Maryland, 446 Ridge Road Apt 5, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, price@math.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Bruce Golden, Edward Wasil, Timothy Babineau, Michael Harrington, Ramon Konewko
Year: 2008
Abstract: Operating room schedules generally neglect to consider the down-stream effects of the block assignment. Mixed-integer programming and simulation can be used to develop block assignments that better accommodate known sources of variation. Using hospital data, we found that that minor scheduling alterations can significantly reduce the census variability.
