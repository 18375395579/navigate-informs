Title: Accurate Forecasts of Spare Part Demand
Author: Thomas Willemain, Vice President, Smart Software, Inc., 4 Hill Road, Belmont, MA, 02478, United States of America, tomw@smartcorp.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: The US military manages vast inventories of spare parts. Many parts have demand that is “intermittent?and therefore difficult to forecast. We describe a patented method that combines Markov modeling with bootstrapping to make the accurate estimates of high percentiles of lead time demand that are required for efficient inventory management.
