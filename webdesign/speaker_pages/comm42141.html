Title: Solution Approaches for the Load Leveling Appointment Scheduling

Author: Azadeh Mobasher, Science Analyst, Manhattan Associates, 2300 Windy Ridge Pkwy SE, Atlanta, GA, 30339, United States of America, azadeh.mobasher@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Elham Mortazavi, Maryam Aliakbari

Year: 2013

Abstract: Appointment scheduling for the patients of surgical physicians in a cancer center is a complex problem due to the limited available clinical time of physicians and the unexpected long hours of surgeries. These complexities result in pushing appointments to limited days of a week (unbalanced schedule). In Load Leveling Appointment Scheduling Problem, we try to determine the balanced schedule of appointments for surgical physicians and to satisfy the preferences of physicians and patients.
