Title: Diffusion Limit of Fair Resource Control--Stationarity and Interchange of Limits

Author: Hengqing Ye, Associate Professor, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Kowloon, Hong Kong - PRC, lgtyehq@inet.polyu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): David Yao

Year: 2012

Abstract: Consider a network in which each job requires the concurrent occupancy of a subset of servers, and the service capacity is shared among job classes following the proportional fair allocation. We demonstrate that the usual traffic condition of the diffusion limit is necessary and sufficient for both the diffusion limit and prelimit networks to have stationary distributions, and justify the diffusion approximation of the stationary distribution and p-th moment under a bounded workload condition.
