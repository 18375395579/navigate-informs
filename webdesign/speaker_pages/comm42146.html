Title: Binomial Method for Optimal Portfolio Liquidation

Author: Jingnan Chen, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Champaign, IL, United States of America, jchen98@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Liming Feng, Jiming Peng

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the optimal execution of assets where the objective is to minimize trading cost and risk. Two types of risk criteria, quadratic variation and value-at-risk, are taken into account. We propose a binomial method to obtain the optimal trading strategy numerically. The effect of some factors, such as price impact and correlation between assets, are analyzed.
