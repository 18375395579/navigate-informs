Title: Dynamic Model of Urban Freight Flows and Demand Formation
Author: Hongcheng Liu, PhD Candidate, Pennsylvania State University, 240 Leonhard, University Park, PA, United States of America, hql5143@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Terry Friesz, Bo Zhang, Tao Yao
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper we model congested urban freight transportation for a hypothetical urban street network in a dynamic setting. The model may be described as a dynamic spatial price equilibrium model that views freight transportation demand as derived from the spatial separation of inventories and consumption of an abstract good. We solve a numerical example.
