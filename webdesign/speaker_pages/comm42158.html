Title: Logic-based Multi-objective Optimization for Restoration Planning

Author: Jing Gong, Ph.D Candidate, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 110 8th St., Troy, NY, 12180, United States, gongj@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Earl Rusty Lee, John E. Mitchell, William Wallace

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a Benders decomposition approach to schedule the tasks that need to be completed in order to restore services following a major disruption. Efficient solutions for the multiple objective function problem are determined using convex combinations of the three (classical) objectives involved in scheduling problems: cost, tardiness and makespan.
