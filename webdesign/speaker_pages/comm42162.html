Title: Multi-objective Model to determine the Location of a Food Factory

Author: Markku Kuula, Professor, Aalto University School of Business, POB 11000, 00076 Aalto, Helsinki, Finland, markku.kuula@aalto.fi

Coauthor(s): Antero Putkiranta, Ville Hallavo

Year: 2013

Abstract: Infrastructure of the current factories of a Finnish food company will come to the end of their life cycles within the next 10 years. In this paper the optimal location for the future factories are estimated and the optimal value chain; animal production ?butchery ?processing ?warehousing ?distribution are defined. Decisions are based on multiple objectives, such as the consumer expectations, transportation costs and investment and operating costs of the animal production plants.
