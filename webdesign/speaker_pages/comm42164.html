Title: Computational Time Estimation of SDP Solvers
Author: Makoto Yamashita, Kanagawa University, 3-27-1 Rokkakubashi Kanagawa-ku, Yokohama, Japan, Makoto.Yamashita@is.kanagawa-u.ac.jp
Year: 2007
Abstract: When solving extremely large SemiDefinite Programs (SDPs), it is favorable to have some knowledge of computational time in advance. From some information of input SDP files and its result files of SDP solvers, we specify parameters to compute estimation time. We formulate the computational time estimation model into an SDP. By solving this SDP, we obtain acceptable estimations of SDPA (SemiDefinite Programming Algorithm) and SDPARA (its parallel version).
