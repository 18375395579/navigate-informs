Title: Server Assignment Problem on Unreliable Networks

Author: Abdullah Konak, Associate Professor, Penn State Berks, Tulpehocken Road PO Box 7009, Reading, PA, 19609, United States of America, konak@psu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper addresses the problem of server deployment on a network with unreliable edges and nodes. A new measure of service availability is developed, and it is used within a search algorithm to find good solutions to the problem.
