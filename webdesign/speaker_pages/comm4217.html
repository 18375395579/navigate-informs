Title: Facility Location and Revenue Optimization under Equilibrium-driven Demand and Congestion
Author: Dehui Tong, University of Toronto, 105 St. George St. PhD Program, Toronto, ON, M5S3E6, Canada, dehui.tong@rotman.utoronto.ca
Coauthor(s): Dmitry Krass, Oded Berman
Year: 2008
Abstract: We focus on several important strategic decisions for service facilities operating on a congested network, including finding the locations of the facilities, determining the capacity of the service units and choosing the price to charge for service. The distribution of the customer flows is guided by the traffic equilibrium. The objective is to maximize the profit rate of the facilities.
