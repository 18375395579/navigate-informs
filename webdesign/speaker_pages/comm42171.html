Title: Numerical Solution of the Versatile Batch-service Geo(X)/G(a,Y)/1 Queue
Author: Jing Gai, Ms., Royal Military College of Canada, PO Box 17000, Station Forces, Kingston, ON, K7K7B4, Canada, Jing.Gai@rmc.ca
Coauthor(s): Mohan Chaudhry
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present numerical results for the bulk-arrival bulk-service single-server Geo(X)/G(a,Y)/1 queue by inverting of the probability generation function ( p.g. f) of the number in the queue.
