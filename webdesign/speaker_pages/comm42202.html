Title: Network Migration Strategies
Author: Warren Sutton, PhD Candidate, Unversity of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, wsutton@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Lawrence M. Seiford
Year: 2008
Abstract: This study examines several properties of network topologies and how these properties change during periods of transition. Transitions periods could include a redesign of the existing network topology or merging of two different topologies. Case studies will be given in each scenario to determine efficiency strategies for change.
