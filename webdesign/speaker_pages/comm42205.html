Title: Concurrent Optimal Scheduling in Skills-based and Non-skills Based Systems

Author: Liz Turner, ac2 Solutions, Inc., 12 Crown Plaza, Suite 207, Hazlet, NJ, 07730, United States of America, lturner@ac2solutions.com

Coauthor(s): Turgut Aykin

Year: 2012

Abstract: It is known that staffing costs account for 60-70% of the operating budget at a typical service center. Given this, considerable emphasis was put on optimal shift scheduling problems in the literature. This talk focuses on scheduling in skills and non-skills based systems and discusses Integer Programming based approaches for these problems.
