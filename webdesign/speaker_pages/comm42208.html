Title: Efficient Optimization of Assemble-to-order Systems with Period Order Batching

Author: Ton de Kok, TU Eindhoven, PO Box 513, Paviljoen E4, Eindhoven, 5600 MB, Netherlands, A.G.d.KOK@TM.TUE.NL

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this presentation we consider an Assemble-To-Order (ATO) system under periodic review base stock policies. Review periods for different components may differ due to setup costs. We assume a so-called ideal product structure, implying that long lead time components are more common than short lead time components. We derive an expression for the long-run average costs consisting of holding and penalty costs and derive optimality equations through marginal analysis.
