Title: Maximal Accessibility Network Design in the Public Sector

Author: Robert Aboolian, California State San Marcos, 2771 Palmetto Drive, Carlsbad, CA, United States of America, raboolia@csusm.edu

Coauthor(s): Oded Berman, Vedat Verter

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper is on the maximal accessibility facility network design problem for the public sector. The governments?mandate is to maximize the societal benefit by acting as agents of the public. Participants often patronize a publicly funded facility with highest accessibility. We assume that the time spent for receiving the service from a facility is a good proxy for its accessibility. We formalize the basic network design problem for the public sector and develop an exact approach to solve it.
