Title: Algorithm to Solve N-k Secure Unit Commitment Problem with Transmission Switching

Author: Kathryn Schumacher, PhD Candidate, University of Michigan, Industrial & Operations Eng. Dept., 1205 Beal Ave, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, kaschu@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Amy Cohn, Richard Chen

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the unit commitment problem subject to the requirement that a certain threshold of consumer demand must be met in the event that any k or fewer elements simultaneously fail. Additionally, when a disruption occurs, the network operators have the option to switch out of service any transmission elements to help direct flows. We present an algorithm that can solve this problem and analyze the algorithmic performance.
