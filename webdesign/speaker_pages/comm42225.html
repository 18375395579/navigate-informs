Title: Dynamic Routing and Scheduling in Drayage Operations

Author: Guangming Zhang, g-zhang@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Karen Smilowitz

Year: 2006

Abstract: This research explores modeling and solution techniques for dynamic routing and scheduling problems in drayage operations. In intermodal drayage applications, it is typical that about 60% of task requests are known before the day of operation and the remaining 40% become known over the day.
