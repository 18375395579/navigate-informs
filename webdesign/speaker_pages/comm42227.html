Title: Robust Pricing to Minimize Maximum Regret
Author: Zhi-Long Chen, Associate Professor, University of Maryland, R.H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, zchen@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Nicholas G. Hall
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a robust pricing model where the objective is to minimize the maximum regret. We consider several problems that involve both production and pricing decisions and derive solution algorithms for them. We will also discuss related managerial insights.
