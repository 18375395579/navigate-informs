Title: Joint Optimal Ordering and Weather Hedging Contract Decisions: A Newsvendor Model

Author: Fei Gao, Ph.D Candidate, The Chinese Univ of Hong Kong, M.W. Mong Engg Bldg, Shatin, N.T., Dept of Systems Engg & Engg Mgmt, Hong Kong - ROC, fgao@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Frank Chen, Xiuli Chao

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper considers the problem of hedging inventory risk using weather options for a seasonal product whose demand is sensitive to the seasonal weather, such as the average seasonal temperature. The newsvendor not only decides the order quantity, but also adopts a weather hedging strategy thru weather derivatives markets. We address the joint decision problem under conditional value at risk (CVaR) and Mean-CVaR framework.
