Title: Specialization and Competition in Healthcare Delivery Networks
Author: Vikram Tiwari, Indiana University, Kelley School of Business, Operations & Decision Technology, Bloomington, IN, 47405, United States, vtiwari@indiana.edu
Coauthor(s): H. Sebastian Heese
Year: 2006
Abstract: Hospital networks that offer multiple services at multiple locations can improve cost and quality by creating specialized facilities; however, this may be at the cost of market share. Using a spatial model, we study when specialization is profitable, and which facilities and services provide the greatest value through specialization.
