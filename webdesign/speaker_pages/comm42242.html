Title: Efficient Flow-Shop Scheduling for Large Print Shops with Heavy-Tail Job Size Distributions

Author: Rong Zhou, Member Research Staff, Palo Alto Research Center, 3333 Coyote Hill Road, Palo Alto, CA, 94304, United States of America, Rong.Zhou@parc.com

Coauthor(s): Sudhendu Rail

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this talk, we focus on the flexible flow-shop scheduling problem that arises in large print shops. Special attention is given to heavy-tail job size distributions and sequence-dependent setup costs that can be found in many print-shop environments. We describe a number of computationally efficient approaches to improving the productivity of large print shops while keeping the number of late jobs low.
