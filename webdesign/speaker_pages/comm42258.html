Title: A Neurogenetic Approach for Vehicle Routing Problem

Author: Selcuk Colak, Cukurova University, Department of Business, Balcali, Adana, 01330, Turkey, scolak@cu.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Anurag Agarwal, Huseyin Guler

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Vehicle Routing Problem (VRP) consists of constructing minimum cost routes that includes a depot and customers where the vehicles leave the depot, visits each customer exactly once, and returns to the depot. In this study, the NeuroGenetic approach, hybrid of augmented neural networks and the genetic algorithms, are used to solve the VRP.
