Title: Planning the Pre-Disaster Strengthening of Transportation Networks for Disaster Response

Author: Sibel Salman, Assistant Professor, Koc University, College of Engineering, Sariyer, Istanbul, 34450, Turkey, ssalman@ku.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Kannan Viswanath, Srinivas Peeta, Dilek Gunnec

Year: 2006

Abstract: We address an investment problem in which a budget is allocated to strengthen a subset of network links to optimize the objectives of the post-disaster emergency response agencies such as minimizing time to reach affected areas using the surviving transportation network. Application to the city of Istanbul is also discussed.
