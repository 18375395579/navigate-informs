Title: Tractable Robust Expected Utility and Risk Models for Portfolio Optimization
Author: Melvyn Sim, Professor, NUS Business School, Singapore-MIT Alliance, 1 Business Link, Singapore, 117592, Singapore, dscsimm@nus.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Joline Uichanco, Karthik Natarajan
Year: 2008
Abstract: We derive exact and approximate optimal trading strategies for a robust or maximin expected utility model where the distributions of the random returns are practially characterized. The investor's utility is modeled as a piecewise-linear concave function. We also provide connections of our results with robust or ambiguous convex risk measures, in which the investor minimizes his worst case risk under distributional ambiguity.
