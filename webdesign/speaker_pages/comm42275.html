Title: Computation of Optimal Order Quantities in an Inventory System with Censored Demand Data

Author: Arnab Bisi, Assistant Professor, Purdue University, Krannert School of Management, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, abisi@krannert.purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Surya Tokdar

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the problem of computing the optimal order quantities in a newsvendor model with censored demand. The demand distribution is updated over time based on historical sales data. We develop a computation method which can compute approximate optimal policies and costs for any demand distribution. We show how the method can be implemented for various demand models. Comparison with the exact solutions confirms high accuracy of our computing scheme.
