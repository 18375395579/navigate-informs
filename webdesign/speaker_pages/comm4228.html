Title: Optimal Distribution of Medical Backpacks and Health Surveillance Assistants in Malawi
Author: Amber Kunkel, Rice University, 6100 Main Street, Houton, TX, 77005, United States of America, agkunkel@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Elizabeth Van Itallie, Duo Wu
Year: 2012
Abstract: The Malawian people face severely limited healthcare access. To address this issue, we used large-scale p-median and capacitated facility location problems to create a scalable, three-tiered plan for optimal allocation of Health Surveillance Assistants (HSAs), HSA designated medical backpacks, and backpack resupply centers in Malawi. This plan will be used to direct the Beyond Traditional Borders' HSA backpack program scale-up.
