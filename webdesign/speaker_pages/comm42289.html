Title: Commercial Cartography of Trains and Yield Managment

Author: Mariane Riss, SNCF - Direction de l'Innovation et de la Recherche, 45 rue de Londres, Paris Cedex 08, -, F 75379, France, mariane.riss@sncf.fr

Year: 2006

Abstract: In order to optimize the revenue of each SNCF's TGV we perform a cartography of TGV based on their historical datas of reservations, which keep the temporal aspect of customer's demand. Then we forcast the appartenance of a TGV to a group via others datas known before the train's departure.
