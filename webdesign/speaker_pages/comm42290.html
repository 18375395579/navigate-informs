Title: Network Interdiction via a Critical Disruption Path

Author: Greg Steeger, Colorado School of Mines, Golden, CO, United States of America, gsteeger@mymail.mines.edu

Coauthor(s): Steffen Rebennack, Donatella Granata

Year: 2012

Abstract: Network interdiction models inhibit network operations through impeding the network’s connectivity, e.g. by deleting nodes or arcs in the network. In our case, we remove the path that minimizes the number of nodes in the largest connected component. This we term the Critical Disruption Path (CDP). We develop a tailored Branch and Price algorithm to solve large problems more efficiently than standard algorithms.
