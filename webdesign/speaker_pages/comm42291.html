Title: Online Integrated Network Design and Scheduling Problems with Flexible Release Dates

Author: Sarah Nurre, Assistant Professor, Air Force Institute of Technology, 2950 Hobson Way, WPAFB, OH, 45433, United States of America, Sarah.Nurre@afit.edu

Coauthor(s): Thomas Sharkey

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of allocating resources to tasks within a network over time. The motivating application is repairing damaged infrastructure components after an extreme event. Each task has an associated release date, which can be shifted earlier in time by allocating special resources to it. We discuss an online optimization setting where the release dates are not known, dispatching rules to determine the allocation of resources to tasks, and promising computational results.
