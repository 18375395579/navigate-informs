Title: A Cooperative Gaming Model for Vehicle Sharing in Perishable Product Deliveries

Author: Xiaoqiang Cai, Professor, Chinese University of HK, Dept of SE&EM, Shatin, NT, Hong Kong - ROC, xqcai@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Jun Li

Year: 2007

Abstract: A group of distributors, each requiring to deliver a perishable product from location A to location B, can cooperate in vehicle utilization subject to vehicle capacity and fair cost sharing. The total cost of a distributor is the expenses of using a vehicle and the loss of the product during transportation, which increases exponentially as time grows. We develop a cooperative game model for the problem and derive the characteristics of the solutions.
