Title: Aggregate Planning of Fleet Deployment for Liner Shipping

Author: Zhou Xu, Department of Logistics and Maritime Studies, Faculty of Business, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, PQ506, The Hong Kong Polytechnic Univers, Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, lgtzx@polyu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Kevin Li, Jun Xia

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present an aggregate planning model to deploy container ships to liner shipping services rotated around different regions of the world, with an aim to maximize the total profit. It takes into account a joint optimization of decisions on the numbers and speeds of ships deployed for each service, together with the cargo flows. The model can be solved to optimal for up to five regions, which are commonly faced by practitioners in liner shipping.
