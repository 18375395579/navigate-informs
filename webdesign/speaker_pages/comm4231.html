Title: Nonnegative Pareto-improving Tolls with Multiclass Network Equilibrium
Author: Ziqi Song, University of Florida, Weil Hall 508, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, ziqi@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Yafeng Yin, Siriphong Lawphongpanich
Year: 2007
Abstract: This study considers nonnegative Pareto-improving toll vectors that improve the social benefit without making anyone worse off in a network with multiple user classes. Since requiring tolls to be nonnegative and anonymous may not be feasible in all cases, the existence conditions for such toll vectors are discussed.
