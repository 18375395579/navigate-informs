Title: A New Facility Design Method for Third-party Warehouse Based on Optimal Revenue Management
Author: Yeming Gong, Mr., Rotterdam School of Management, Versijdenstraat 6, Rotterdam, 3033TK, Netherlands, ygong@rsm.nl
Coauthor(s): Rene de Koster
Year: 2008
Abstract: Based on a leading 3P(third-party) warehouse case, we identify the relation between market segment and facility type, and present a new facility design method. We show new facility designed by our method can significantly improve the revenue. This paper reports a novel RM application industry,3P warehouse, is the first to apply RM theory as a methodological foundation to facility design, and identifies a new direction,the interface between RM and facility logistics.
