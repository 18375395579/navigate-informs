Title: Tractable Optimization Subject to Service Level Constraints
Author: Assaf Zeevi, Professor, Columbia Business School, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, assaf@gsb.columbia.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a simple and tractable approach to optimizing service centers ' capacity planning decisions, subject to service level constraints. A salient feature of the proposed approach is that it allows to deal with uncertainty in the basic model primitives (such as demand volume and other business drivers).
