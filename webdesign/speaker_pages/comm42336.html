Title: Pricing in Freight Transportation
Author: Huseyin Topaloglu, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, 223 Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, huseyin@orie.cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Warren B. Powell
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present a model to jointly make the pricing and vehicle allocation decisions for fleet management systems. We obtain stochastic gradients of the objective function with respect to the prices and use this information to search for a good set of prices.
