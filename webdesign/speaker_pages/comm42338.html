Title: Small Worlds by Design
Author: Donald P. Gaver, Professor Emeritus of Operations Research, Naval Postgraduate School, Monterey, CA, United States of America, dgaver@nps.org
Coauthor(s): Patricia Jacobs
Year: 2013
Abstract: A large group (size n) of entities is to be linked together efficiently. Forming optimal subgroups, size O(n^(1/3)) each with one representative, and representatives in higher subgroup, total connections is O(n^( 4/3)) so efficiency of two levels over one is O(n^(2/3)). Further efficiency can be achieved.
