Title: Domain Protection Methodologies
Author: Donald Gaver, Distinguished Professor, Naval Postgraduate School, DGaver@nps.edu
Coauthor(s): K.C. Chng, Patricia Jacobs
Year: 2007
Abstract: An important domain (e.g. straits towards a Blue seaport) contains many benign(White)usually-friendly vessels; it is transited by fewer possibly dangerous (e.g. LNG) ships aimed to cause port damage. We analyze requirements and CONOPS for Blue defense forces: UAVs, USVs, Helos, and Patrol Craft, to minimize port damage.
