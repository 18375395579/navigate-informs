Title: Joint Optimization of Virtual Capacities and Bid-Prices for Revenue Management
Author: Gustavo Vulcano, Associate Professor, Leonard N. Stern School of Business - NYU, 44 West Fourth St, Suite 8-76, New York, NY, 10012, United States of America, gvulcano@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Alex Weil
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a network RM problem where the physical capacity of the legs is uncertain. The controller sets a virtual capacity and a bid-price for each of the legs at the beginning of the finite horizon, and takes reservations. At the end of the horizon, the effective capacities and customer show-ups are realized. The controller incurs a penalty cost for each bumped reservation. We present a stochastic gradient algorithm for the adjusted revenue maximization problem (revenue minus penalty cost).
