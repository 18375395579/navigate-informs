Title: Bargaining for Resources in Large-scale Wireless Sensor Networks

Author: Abhijit Deshmukh, Program Director, NSF, 4201 Wilson Blvd, Arlington, VA, 22230, United States, adeshmuk@nsf.gov

Coauthor(s): Timothy Middelkoop

Year: 2007

Abstract: Wireless sensor networks pose interesting challenges when coordination between sensors is required. This talk presents a bargaining protocol designed specifically for resource coordination in distributed sensor networks. This talk will present the bargaining market formulation, characterize the equilibrium solution and present computational results for a large-scale wireless sensor network.
