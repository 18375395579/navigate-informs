Title: The Probabilistic Gradual Covering Problem on a Network with Discrete Demand Weights
Author: Jiamin Wang, Professor, Long Island University, Department of Management, 720 Northern Blvd, Brookville, NY, 11548-1300, United States of America, jiamin.wang@liu.edu
Coauthor(s): Oded Berman, Dmitry Krass
Year: 2008
Abstract: We investigate the covering problem on a network with uncertain demand. Instead of assuming that demand points are either covered or not covered at all, we allow partial coverage where the coverage level is a function of distance. The objective of the problem is to locate a facility so as to maximize the probability that the total covered demand weight is beyond a pre-selected threshold value. The problem is shown to be NP-hard and an exact solution procedure and a heuristic are suggested.
