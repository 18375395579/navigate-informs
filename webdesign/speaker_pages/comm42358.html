Title: Biobjective Pharmacy Duty Scheduling Problem

Author: Ozgur Ozpeynirci, Izmir University of Economics, Sakarya Cad. No:156, Izmir, 35330, Turkey, ozgur.ozpeynirci@ieu.edu.tr

Year: 2012

Abstract: In Turkey, only on duty pharmacies provide service at the nights of weekdays and at the weekends. The pharmacy duty scheduling (PDS) problem assigns duties to the pharmacies to provide the best possible service to the public. PDS is a special multi-period facility location problem and it is NP-Hard. In this study, we propose the secondary objective that balances the workloads of the pharmacies. The study is supported by the Scientific and Technological Council of Turkey (TUBITAK).
