Title: Dynamic Allocation to Multiple-Class Demand with Continuous Review
Author: Joseph Milner, Professor, milner@rotman.utoronto.ca
Coauthor(s): Panos Kouvelis, Ding Qing
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider how a firm should dynamically allocate inventory to different customer classes that are differentiated by price and willingness to wait for delayed delivery as well as reorder in a continuous review environment. We establish the optimal policy and introduce efficient and robust heuristics.
