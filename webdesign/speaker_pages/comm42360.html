Title: Revenue Management when Supply is Flexible
Author: Douglas Bish, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, 221 Durham Hall, drb1@vt.edu
Coauthor(s): Ebru Bish, Lingrui Liao
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this paper, we study the problem of revenue management for a two fare-class setting when the airline has the flexibility to swap aircraft (with different capacities) between two legs. We determine the structure of the optimal policy and perform a comparative statics analysis.
