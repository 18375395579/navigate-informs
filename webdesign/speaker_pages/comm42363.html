Title: Interdicting Nuclear Smugglers and Priorities

Author: David Morton, Associate Professor, The University of Texas at Austin, Graduate Program in Operations Research, 1 University Station, C2200, Austin, TX, 78712-0292, United States, morton@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Feng Pan

Year: 2006

Abstract: We describe an interdiction model for locating sensors that detect nuclear material. We model a nuclear material smuggler who selects a path to maximize the probability of avoiding detection. An interdictor installs sensors to minimize that maximum probability. We discuss how to form an optimal priority list of candidate sensor sites.
