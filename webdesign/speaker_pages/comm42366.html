Title: Optimal Utility-based Control of a Network Supporting Multi-rate Multicast

Author: Alexander Stolyar, Bell Labs, Alcatel-Lucent, 600 Mountain Ave, 2C-322, Murray Hill, NJ, 07920, United States of America, stolyar@research.bell-labs.com

Coauthor(s): R. Srikant, Loc Bui

Year: 2007

Abstract: The model is a queueing network shared by a number of multicast sessions, each with a single sender and multiple receivers. Key feature: receivers (even within same session) may have different (concave) utility functions of the average received rate. We present a joint scheduling and congestion control algorithm, asymptotically maximizing the total utility.
