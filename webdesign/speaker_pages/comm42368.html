Title: Fluid and Diffusion Approximation of Limited Processor Sharing Queue

Author: Jiheng Zhang, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Dr., Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, jrz@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Bert Zwart, Jim Dai

Year: 2007

Abstract: Motivated by application in computer systems, we study a modification of a processor sharing queue where only up to a limited number of jobs can equally share the server's full capacity at a time, excess jobs have to wait in a FIFO buffer. We first study the existence, uniqueness and invariant manifolds of fluid model solution. Then based on these, we establish fluid and diffusion approximations of the system.
