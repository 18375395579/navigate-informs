Title: Robust Optimal Stopping under Volatility Uncertainty

Author: Song Yao, University of Pittsburgh, 404 Thackeray Hall, Pittsburgh, PA, 15260, United States of America, songyao@pitt.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a robust optimal stopping problem with respect to mutually singular probabilities. This can be interpreted as a zero-sum controller-stopper game in which the stopper is trying to maximize its pay-o ff while an adverse player wants to minimize this payoff by choosing an evaluation criteria. We show that the fi rst time when the reward process meets its the upper Snell envelope is the optimal stopping time for the robust optimal stopping problem.
