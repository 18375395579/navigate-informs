Title: Assortment Optimization in Response to New Product Introductions in Grocery Retail
Author: Ying Liu, New York University, 44 West Fourth Street, New York, NY, United States of America, yliu2@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Srikanth Jagabathula, Andrew Vakhutinsky
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider the problem of assortment optimization when there are new product introductions. Solving this problem requires addressing two main challenges: (a) predicting demand for new and existing items in response to the new product introduction, and (b) finding the optimal assortment in response to the demand predictions. We propose a new approach to predicting demand and an associated algorithm for assortment optimization. We report results on real-world supermarket transaction logs.
