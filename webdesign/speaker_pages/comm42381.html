Title: Cargo Driven Container Logistics: A Cross-Docking Facility Location Problem with Economies of Scale
Author: Yann Bouchery, Eindhoven University of Technology, P.O. Box 513, Eindhoven, 5600 MB, Netherlands, y.bouchery@tue.nl
Coauthor(s): Jan Fransoo
Year: 2013
Abstract: Flow consolidation is a key aspect in container logistics as volumes are critical for economies of scale. In our research, we explore how cross-docking may improve flow consolidation by focusing on the cargo inside the containers. We thus propose a cross-docking facility location problem for container logistics in a hub-and-spoke network. Moreover, we model flow dependent economies of scale both for transportation and for cross-docking operations.
