Title: Infinite Horizon Strategies for Replenishment Systems with a General Pool of Suppliers

Author: Nan Yang, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, 310 Sage Hall, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, ny38@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Awi Federgruen

Year: 2008

Abstract: We characterize the structure of optimal infinite horizon strategies in replenishment systems, with an arbitrary pool of suppliers with distinct prices and yield characteristics. We design exact solution methods as well as simple but accurate heuristics.
