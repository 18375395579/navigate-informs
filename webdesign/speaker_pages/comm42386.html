Title: Maximum Likelihood Estimation for Large Interacting Stochastic Systems
Author: Justin Sirignano, Stanford University, 3 Gibbs Court, Irvine, CA, United States of America, jasirign@stanford.edu
Coauthor(s): Kay Giesecke, Gustavo Schwenkler
Year: 2012
Abstract: Parameter estimation for a large system is facilitated by using the asymptotic SPDE to which the system weakly converges as well as a conditionally Gaussian first-order correction. We develop a particle filtering method for the asymptotic SPDEs. Theoretical convergence of the finite system's likelihood to the asymptotic likelihood is discussed. Important credit risk and mortgage applications motivate the method.
