Title: Optimization of Logistic Operations of a Company that Carries Environmentally Hazardous Materials.
Author: Lauren Castro, FCIMEC, Carrera 53 No.74-86 Office 402, Barranquilla, At, 080020, Colombia, ljcastro@fcimec.org
Coauthor(s): Jairo Montoya, Linda Castro, Santiago Nieto, Diana Ramirez, Nilson Herazo
Year: 2013
Abstract: In a company that transports environmentally hazardous materials, the optimization was aimed for the assignment and distribution of their tanks, through the use of metaheuristics. It both considers a high service level and the minimization of the costs of transportation. The results for this research project demonstrate an efficient use of the tanks through optimized logistic operations.
