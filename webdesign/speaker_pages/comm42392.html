Title: Parking Search, Information, and Online Routing Problems
Author: Shoupeng Tang, Unversity of Texas-Austin, Austin, TX, United States of America, shouptang@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Tarun Rambha, Stephen Boyles, Avinash Unnikrishnan
Year: 2013
Abstract: In some urban areas, a substantial fraction of traffic volume consists of drivers searching for parking. We model this search process as a Markov decision process which can be efficiently solved with variants of the online shortest path problem, and consider alternative ways to reflect past history.
