Title: Analysis of (Q,r)-inventory Models with Exponential Lead Times by Successive Lumping
Author: Flora Spieksma, Leiden University, Mathematical Institute, Niels Bohrweg 1, Leiden, 2333, Netherlands, spieksma@math.leidenuniv.nl
Coauthor(s): Dwi Ertiningsih
Year: 2012
Abstract: We assume that the demand is Poisson with rate lambda and the state of the system is the current inventory position. If the position is r-nQ, we place a new order of size Q, where the number of outstanding orders allowed is at most K. Sales get lost, if the inventory position is less then r-(K-1)Q. This can be modelled as a successively lumpable PH/M/K/K-queueing model. We will compare its performance with the (Q,r)-model with deterministic lead times.
