Title: Investigating Random Keys Representation in a Particle Swarm Optimization Metaheuristic
Author: Vinodh Sankaran, Dr.Mary Elizaberth Kurz/Clemson university, Clemson, SC, United States of America, vsankar@clemson.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: This talk considers how the combinatorial optimization problem of scheduling flexible flow line to minimize makespan can be attacked with particle swarm optimization by representing the combinatorial solution with random keys. Extensive computational experiments are facilitated through the use of high-throughput computing via Clemson’s Condor grid.
