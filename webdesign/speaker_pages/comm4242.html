Title: Utility Bayesian Networks
Author: Ali Abbas, Associate Professor and Director, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Ave, Urbana, IL, 61822, United States of America, aliabbas@illinois.edu
Coauthor(s): Sewoong Oh, Zhengwei Sun
Year: 2013
Abstract: We introduce a graphical representation of multiattribute utility functions that represents an expansion order of the multiattribute utility tree (Abbas 2011). The new graph consists of two directed acyclic graphs superimposed: the first represents the utility independence relations and the second represents the boundary independence relations. New rules for arc reversals in utility networks are introduced to facilitate the elicitation process.
