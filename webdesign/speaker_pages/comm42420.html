Title: A Customer-item Decomposition Approach to Stochastic Inventory Systems with Correlation

Author: Saif Benjaafar, Professor, University of Minnesota, Industrial & Systems Engineering Divisio, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, saif@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Yimin Yu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a general class of periodic review stochastic inventory problems with demand and leadtime correlation. We use a customer-item decomposition approach to characterize the structure of the optimal policy and construct efficient solution algorithms. We show how the analysis can be extended to systems with multiple stages, advance demand information, and batch ordering, among others
