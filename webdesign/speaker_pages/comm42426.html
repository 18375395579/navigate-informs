Title: Dynamic Capacity Investment in Sustainable Technologies
Author: Gilvan Gil Souza, Associate Professor, Indiana University, Kelley School of Business, Bloomington, IN, 47405, United States of America, gsouza@indiana.edu
Coauthor(s): Shanshan Hu, Mark Ferguson, Wenbin Wang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a firm’s dynamic capacity decisions given a portfolio of technology options (e.g., HEV and diesel trucks) when demand and fuel costs are stochastic, and there is usage-based capacity deterioration. We provide the analytical structure of the optimal policy, and provide managerial and policy insights based on actual data.
