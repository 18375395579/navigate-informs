Title: Multi-objective Semiconductor Manufacturing Scheduling

Author: Mary Beth Kurz, Clemson University, 110 Freeman Hall, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States, mkurz@CLEMSON.EDU

Coauthor(s): Letitia Pohl, Scott Mason, John Fowler, Michelle Pfund

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine a complex multiobjective semiconductor manufacturing scheduling problem with 2 batch processing steps linked by a timer constraint, which requires that any job completing the first processing step must be started on the second machine within a time limit; otherwise, the job must repeat the first step. We develop deterministic and probabilistic algorithms which are evaluated through the use of the Integrated Convex Preference measure over a wide range of experimental problem instances.
