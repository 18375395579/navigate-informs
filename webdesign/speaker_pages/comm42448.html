Title: Data-driven Newsvendor with Temporally Dependent Demand

Author: Alp Akcay, Bilkent University, Ankara, Turkey, aakcay@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Bahar Biller, Sridhar Tayur

Year: 2013

Abstract: Considering a newsvendor problem with temporally dependent demand data, we characterize a probabilistic guarantee for the expected cost of an inventory-target estimate to be close to the optimal expected cost with an arbitrarily small relative error. Our analysis sheds light on the role of temporal dependence in inventory-target estimation.
