Title: Airlines Strategic Competition: A Network Equilibrium Approach
Author: Ahmed Hassan, Research Assistant, Southern Methodist University, P.O. Box 750340, Dallas, tx, 75275-0340, United States, aehassan@engr.smu.edu
Coauthor(s): Khaled Abdelghany, Ahmed Abdelghany
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present a network equilibrium approach for modeling airlines competition at the strategic level. The model replicates air carrier decisions regarding network configuration and service frequency considering resources availability and system capacity limitations. Problem formulation, solution algorithm and Texas preliminary results are presented.
