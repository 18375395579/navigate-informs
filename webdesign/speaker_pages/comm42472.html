Title: Big Triangle Small Triangle Method for the Estimation of Parameters of Probability Distributions

Author: Atsuo Suzuki, Professor, Nanzan University, 27 Seirei-cho, Seto, Japan, atsuo@nanzan-u.ac.jp

Year: 2012

Abstract: We apply the Big Triangle Small Triangle (BTST) algorithm for the estimation of the parameters of probability distributions such as the logistic distribution. The maximum likelihood method is common method to estimate the parameters of the distribution. However, a likelihood function is not necessary concave. That means the likelihood function has many local maxima. To find the maximum of the likelihood function, we resort the BTST algorithm.
