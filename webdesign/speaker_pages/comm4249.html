Title: Competitive Two-agent Scheduling with Simple Linear Deterioration

Author: Lixin Tang, Professor, Northeastern University, The Logistics Institute, Shenyang, 110819, China, lixintang@mail.neu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Xiaoli Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper considers a scheduling simultaneous with two-agent and simple linear deterioration. Two agents compete to process their respective jobs on common machine and each agent has his own criterion to optimize. The goal is to determine a schedule for two agents?jobs such that the objective of one agent is minimized while keeping the objective of the other agent no exceeding a maximum value. We obtain different problems complexities of various scheduling environments.
