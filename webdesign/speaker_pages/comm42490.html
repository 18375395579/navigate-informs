Title: Matching Supply from Multiple Components to Competing Demands with Changing Attributes

Author: Alfred Degbotse, IBM, 1000 River Street, Essex Junction, VT, 05452, United States, adegbo@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Robert Orzell, Brent Elmer, Chi-Tai Wang, R. John Milne

Year: 2006

Abstract: Demand attributes such as quantity and priority can vary as a function of delivery date. We describe an element of our Supply Chain software which assigns supply to demand in the situation where demand attributes vary and multiple components are present. The software is used to plan IBM's semiconductor operations and those of an external client.
