Title: On-line Algorithms for Maximizing Throughput with Equal Length Jobs in the Presence of Look-Ahead Information

Author: Ishwar Murthy, Professor, Indian Institute of Management Bangalore, IIM Bangalore, Bannerghatta Road, Bangalore, 560076, India, ishwar@IIMB.ERNET.IN

Coauthor(s): Sumit Sarkar

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we consider the problem for maximizing throughput of jobs whose processing times are all equal to p. We have information about release times and due dates of jobs Kp time periods into the future, where K = 1. However, we have no information about jobs that might arrive beyond Kp time periods into the future. We present on-line algorithms for this problem and establish their competitive ratio.
