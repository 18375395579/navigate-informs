Title: Reducing Airport Gate Blockage in Passenger Aviation: Models and Analysis

Author: Jeremy Castaing, PhD Student, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109-2117, United States of America, jctg@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Ishan Mukherjee, Amy Cohn

Year: 2013

Abstract: A Gate blockage occurs when a flight arrives to its scheduled gate but has to wait because an aircraft is still occupying it. We formulate an optimization problem to assign flights to gates so as to minimize the expected impact of gate blockage. We use historical data to predict delay distributions and conduct experiments to assess both the computational tractability of our approach and its potential for improvement in solution quality over existing approaches.
