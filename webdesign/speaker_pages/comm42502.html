Title: Solving the Multi-criteria, Multiple Choice Problem

Author: Luiz Autran Gomes, Professor, Ibmec/RJ, Av. Presidente Wilson, 118,, Room 1110,, Rio de Janeiro, RJ, 20030020, Brazil, autran@ibmecrj.br

Coauthor(s): Maria A.S. Machado, Luis A.D. Rangel

Year: 2013

Abstract: The multiple choice problem arises when one must select at least two alternatives at the same time. A variation is the multi-criteria, multiple choice problem. It is shown that the bipolar Choquet integral is the most recommended approach for tackling that problem. The paper closes with suggestions for future research.
