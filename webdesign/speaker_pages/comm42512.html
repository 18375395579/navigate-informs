Title: G-Brownian Motion and Optimal Stopping
Author: Xin Guo, University of California-Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, United States of America, xinguo@ieor.berkeley.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We consider optimal stopping problems under the G-B.M. framework. Well-posedness of the problem and characterization of the solution are discussed. Based on joint work with Chen Pan and Shige Peng.
