Title: On the Network Design Problem with Mixed Connectivity Requirements
Author: Elham Sadeghi, University of Arizona, 1127 E. James E. Rogers Way Rm 111, Tucson, United States of America, sadeghi@email.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Neng Fan
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this talk, we present and prove several equivalent theorems to ensure a minimum-cost network to be (K,l)-connected. A graph is (k,l)-connected if removal of certain numbers (related to k,l) of vertices and edges will not disconnect the graph. Based on these theorems, we present formulations and algorithms to solve the minimum-cost network design problem.
