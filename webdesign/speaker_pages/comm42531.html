Title: Index Priority Rules with Variable Job Processing Times
Author: George Kyparisis, Florida International University, 11200 SW 8th Street, Miami, FL, 33199, United States of America, kyparis@fiu.edu
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider single-machine makespan minimization problems with time-dependent job processing times given by general multiplicative/additive functional forms. We show that the linear function is the only function leading to an index priority rule for such problems with multiplicative/additive job deterioration functions.
