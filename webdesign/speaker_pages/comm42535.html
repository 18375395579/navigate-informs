Title: Flow Balancing with Uncertain Demand for Cross-Docking Facilities

Author: Luis Novoa, PhD Student - Decision Sciences, The George Washington University, 2201 G Street, NW, Duquès Hall, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, ljnovoa@gwu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ahmad Jarrah, David Morton

Year: 2012

Abstract: Packages arriving at a facility are routed by primary sorters to secondary sorters based on the freight’s final destination. We look at the problem of assigning package destinations to secondary sorters in a fashion that balances the workload within the facility while incorporating the day-to-day volume fluctuation. Robustness of the operation will be assessed based on solutions from a stochastic optimization model and a deterministic optimization one that assumes average package volumes.
