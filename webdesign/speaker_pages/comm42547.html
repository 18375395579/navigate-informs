Title: Alert Dissemination through Social Networks in Extreme Events
Author: Xin Ma, Texas A&M University, 3006 ETB, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, xma@tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Justin Yates
Year: 2013
Abstract: Social networks are playing an increasingly important role in extreme event management. We propose to integrate independent subscription networks with social networks for message/alert dissemination in extreme events. We develop a multi-network, multi-period model to maximize message dissemination and reliability while minimizing message starts and test our model through experimentation
