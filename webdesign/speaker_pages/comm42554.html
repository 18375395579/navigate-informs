Title: The Maximum Ratio Maximal Clique Problem

Author: Samyukta Sethuraman, Texas A&M University, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, samyukta@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sergiy Butenko

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce and study the maximum ratio maximal clique problem in graphs, which, given benefit and cost weights associated with each vertex, asks for a clique maximizing the benefit to cost ratio. This problem is motivated by network-based data mining applications.
