Title: Cluster-based Optimal Location Planning of Urban Transit Hubs

Author: Yue Liu, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, P.O. Box 784, Milwaukee, WI, 53201, United States of America, liu28@uwm.edu

Coauthor(s): Jie Yu, Zhenke Luo

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper presents an optimization model planning urban transit hub locations. The model features a bi-level structure with the upper-level determining the hub locations to minimize the total transportation and facility cost. The lower-level models the behavior of buses and passengers in choosing optimal hubs and transportation routes. GA is employed to yield meta-optimal solutions.
