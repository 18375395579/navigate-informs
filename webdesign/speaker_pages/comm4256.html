Title: The Groupon Effect: Threshold-Discounting with Strategic Customers
Author: Serguei Netessine, Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fountainbleau, 77305, France, Serguei.Netessine@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Simone Marinesi, Karan Girotra
Year: 2012
Abstract: We analyze the business model of Groupon which offers discounts to customers if a sufficient demand for service is reached. We show when Groupon business model works best and suggest improvements for its design.
