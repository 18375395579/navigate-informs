Title: AC Chance Constrained Optimal Power Flow

Author: Michael Chertkov, Los Alamos National Laboratory, MS B213, Los ALamos, NM, 87545, United States of America, chertkov@lanl.gov

Coauthor(s): Russell Bent, Daniel Bienstock

Year: 2013

Abstract: Typical implementations of the Optimum Power Flow (OPF) minimize the cost of generation, subject to linearized, so-called DC, power flows, thermal capacity, ramping and security constraints. Here we extend our recent results and develop the Chance Constraint (CC) OPF which manages risk in a principled fashion by allowing physical constraints to be violated with small probability while keeping some of the nonlinearities, in the underlying AC power flow equations, present.
