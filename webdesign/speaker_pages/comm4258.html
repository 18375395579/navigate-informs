Title: The M/D/s queue, the Halfin-Whitt regime and the Gaussian random walk

Author: Johan Leeuwaarden, Technical University of Eindhoven, Netherlands, j.s.h.v.leeuwaarden@TUE.nl

Year: 2008

Abstract: To obtain insight in the quality of heavy-traffic approximations for queues with many servers, we consider the M/D/s queue in steady state as s goes to infinity. In the Halfin-Whitt regime, the queue converges to a Gaussian random walk. We present series expansions that characterize the difference between the queue and this Gaussian limiting process. Joint work with A.J.E.M. Janssen and B. Zwart.
