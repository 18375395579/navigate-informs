Title: Robust Inventory Management Using Tractable Replenishment Policies

Author: Chuen-Teck See, NUS, Singapore, Singapore, Singapore, see_chuenteck@yahoo.com.sg

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose tractable replenishment policies for a multiperiod inventory control problem under ambiguous demands via solving a second order cone optimization problem. Our framework extends to correlated demands and is developed around a factor-based demand model, which incorporates business factors and time series forecast effects of trend, seasonality and cyclic variations.
