Title: Strongly Agree or Strongly Disagree? Rating Features in Support Vector Machines

Author: Romero Morales, Professor, University of Oxford, Said Business School, Oxford, United Kingdom, dolores.romero-morales@sbs.ox.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Emilio Carrizosa, Amaya Nogales Gomez

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose the Integer Support Vector Machine (ISVM), where the feature scores can take only a discrete amount of values. The ISVM classifier can be seen as a collection of Likert scales each rating the level of agreement with the positive class. We propose a Mixed Integer Linear Programming formulation and three reduction strategies. Our computational results show that the 3-point and 5-point ISVM classifiers have comparable accuracy to that of SVM, with a gain in interpretability.
