Title: Formulations and Reformulations for Network Design Problems with Reload Costs
Author: Luis Gouveia, Faculdade de Ciencias da Universidade de Lisboa, DEIO-CIO, Bloco c/2 - Campo Grande, Lisboa, Portugal, legouveia@fc.ul.pt
Coauthor(s): Ioannis Gamvros, S. Raghavan
Year: 2006
Abstract: We present the notion of reload costs that can appear in several network design and planning problems in telecommunication systems and the transportation industry. We discuss the challenges in modeling such costs with standard approaches and present several reformulations and comment on their strengths and weaknesses
