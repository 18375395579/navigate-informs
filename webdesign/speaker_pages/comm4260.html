Title: Dynamic Pricing and Input Control in Multi-Class Queues
Author: Sriram Dasu, Assistant Professor, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, sriram.dasu@marshall.usc.edu
Coauthor(s): Chunyang Tong
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider multiple servers system with multiple customer classes. The duration of service is class dependent. We are interested in pricing and input control policies that maximize revenues. Based on diffusion approximations we derive properties of the optimal policies
