Title: Dividing a Territory with Obstacles

Author: Raghuveer Devulapalli, Research Assistant, University of MInnesota, 310 8th St SE #205, MInneapolis, MN, 55414, India, devul002@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): John Carlsson

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the problem of dividing a territory into sub-regions so as to balance workloads of $n$ fixed facilities over that territory. We assume that the territory is a simply connected polygon containing a set of simply connected obstacles. We give a fast analytic center cutting plane method that divides the territory into $n$ compact, connected sub-regions, each of which contains a facility, such that the workloads in each sub-region are balanced.
