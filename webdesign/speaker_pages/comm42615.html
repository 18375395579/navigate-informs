Title: Dynamic Crossdocking Optimization

Author: Monique Guignard, Professor, University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School, OPIM Department, 3730 Walnut Street, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, guignard_monique@yahoo.fr

Coauthor(s): Peter Hahn, Heng Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: Crossdocks in a supply chain aim at reducing transportation and inventory costs. Freight from incoming trailers must be sorted and loaded into outbound trailers. We concentrate on assigning trucks to doors to avoid congestion on the floor and improving the use/cost of the workforce. Our prototype system assigns trailers dynamically through a workday, updating the loading/unloading operations and solving quadratic door assignment models. It has been tested on realistic data from one company.
