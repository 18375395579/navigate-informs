Title: Multiperiod Portfolio Optimization with General Transaction Costs

Author: Mei Xiaoling, Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Department of Statistics, Avda. de la Universidad, 30, Leganés, 28911, Spain, xmei@est-econ.uc3m.es

Coauthor(s): Javier Nogales, Victor DeMiguel

Year: 2013

Abstract: We analyze the optimal portfolio policy for a multiperiod mean-variance investor who faces multiple risky assets, and is subject to general transaction costs. We find the no-trade region is a parallelogram for proportional transaction costs. For market impact costs, the optimal trading strategy is characterized by a superellipse that shrinks along the horizon, which may result in trading occurring throughout the horizon. Illustrative numerical results are provided.
