Title: A Linear Storage-Retrieval Policy for Robust Warehouse Management

Author: Marcus Ang, Research Fellow, Singapore-MIT Alliance, 4 Engineering Drive 3, Singapore, 117576, Singapore, g0300273@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Yun Fong Lim, Melvyn Sim

Year: 2008

Abstract: In a unit-load warehouse, assigning products to and retrieving products from proper storage locations is crucial in minimizing its operating cost. The problem becomes intractable when demand is uncertain. We assume a factor-based demand and introduce a robust optimization model that minimizes the worst-case expected travel under distributional ambiguity. By restricting to a linear decision rule, we can derive an efficient policy. The results are verified via numerical studies.
