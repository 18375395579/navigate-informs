Title: Statistical Depth and Outlier Detection
Author: Yixin Chen, Assistant Professor, Department of Computer and Information Science, University of Mississippi, University, MS, 38677, United States, ychen@cs.olemiss.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Statistical depth functions provide from the deepest point a center-outward ordering of multi-dimensional data. We propos a novel statistical depth, the kernelized spatial depth (KSD), and a outlier detector based on KSD.
