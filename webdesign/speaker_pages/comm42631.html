Title: Day-to-day Dynamic Congestion Pricing Strategies under Elastic Travel Demand

Author: Fan Yang, Assistant Professor, City University of New York, 140th St and Convent Ave, New York, NY, 10031, United States, fyang@ccny.cuny.edu

Coauthor(s): Jiangang Lu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Day-to-day dynamic tolls were proposed to guide the day-to-day dynamic traffic flow moving towards system optimal under the fixed travel demand. In this talk, we investigate the social efficiency of day-to-day dynamic tolls under the elastic demand and a general driver��s rational behavior adjustment process.
