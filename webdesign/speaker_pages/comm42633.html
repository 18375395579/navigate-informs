Title: A Progressive Hedging Approach to Managing Harvesting Operations on Flammable Forest Landscapes

Author: David Martell, University of Toronto, Faculty of Forestry, Toronto, ON, Canada, david.martell@utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Luis Fernando Solari, Jean-Paul Watson, Roger Wets, David Woodruff, Andres Weintraub

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of deciding when and where to harvest stands on flammable forest landscapes. Stands that are harvested before they burn provide fibre and also act as firebreaks that reduce the flammability of the forest and the likelihood that the residual stands will burn before they are harvested. We develop a set of fire ignition and spread scenarios and illustrate the use of progressive hedging methods to solve such problems by applying them to a small hypothetical forest landscape.
