Title: Stochastic Control for Smart Grid Users with Flexible Demand

Author: Long He, University of California, Berkeley, 1117 Etcheverry Hall, Berkeley, CA, United States of America, longhe@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Yong Liang, Xinyu Cao, Z. Max Shen

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the optimal control problem for the demand-side of the Smart Grid under time-varying prices with general structures. We assume that users are equipped with smart appliances that allow delay in satisfying demands, and one central controller that makes energy usage decisions on when and how to satisfy the scheduled demands. We formulate a dynamic programming model for the control problem. We also propose an approximation approach based on Q-learning and a decentralization-based heuristic.
