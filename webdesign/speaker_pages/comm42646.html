Title: Inventory Control for a Distribution System with Real-time Visibility
Author: Diego Klabjan, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 205 North Mathews Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, klabjan@uiuc.edu
Coauthor(s): Jinxiang Pei
Year: 2007
Abstract: RFID enables suppliers to have complete visibility of their shipments. We assume that the underlying distribution system is serial and we study the related inventory control policies that exploit full shipment information such as the location.
