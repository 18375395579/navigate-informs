Title: A Scenario Based Approach for Scheduling in a Fractional Ownership Program

Author: Fei Qian, ISyE Georgia Tech, Atlanta, GA, gtg120s@mail.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Ozlem Ergun, Ellis Johnson, William Hall

Year: 2008

Abstract: In the recent years although the fractional aircraft ownership programs as a whole has experi-enced significant growth, most of the major fractional jet management companies have been un-profitable. Two major drivers for high operating costs are the stochasticity in demand and the high number of daily unscheduled maintenance events. We discuss a stochastic programming approach based on generating scenarios and solving the scheduling problem for minimizing the expected cost over these scenarios.
