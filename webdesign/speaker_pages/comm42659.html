Title: Optimal Storage and Retrieval Policies for Unit-Load Warehouses
Author: Fang Liu, Assistant Professor, Nanyang Technological University, Nanyang Business School, Singapore, Singapore, Liu_Fang@ntu.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Yun Fong Lim
Year: 2013
Abstract: Optimizing storage and retrieval decisions is crucial for minimizing the operating cost of a unit-load warehouse. The problem is especially challenging when the warehouse faces time-varying product arrivals and random demand in a multi-period setting. We identify optimal storage and retrieval policies for a warehouse with general layout and storage classes. We will discuss some insights on these policies.
