Title: Dispatching and Routing Automated Transporters in Port Container Terminals
Author: Kap Hwan Kim, kapkim@pusan.ac.kr
Coauthor(s): Soo Min Jeon, Vu Duc Nguyen
Year: 2007
Abstract: This study is to provide a method for dispatching and routing automated transporters in container terminals considering container delivery requirements. The objectives of this problem are to satisfy delivery requirements and to minimize the travel time considering not only the moving time on guide paths but also the waiting time at intersections, merging positions, and transfer points.
