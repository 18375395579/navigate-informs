Title: Customer Order Rationing in Shipment Consolidation
Author: Benhür Satir, Assistant Professor, Çankaya University, Yenimahalle, Ankara, 06810, Turkey, benhur@cankaya.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): James Bookbinder, Fatih Safa Erenay
Year: 2012
Abstract: A delivery company faces two classes of demand for dispatching a single package type, viz. standard and express; the latter is more profitable. Shipments are made by capacitated vehicles. The optimal customer rationing policy for shipment consolidation is modeled and solved as a continuous-time Markov Decision Process.
