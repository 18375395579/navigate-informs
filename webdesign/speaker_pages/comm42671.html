Title: Interesting Use Cases for the CPLEX Remote Object
Author: Laszlo Ladanyi, IBM, 11 Interlaken Rd, Lakeville, CT, 06039, United States of America, ladanyi@us.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Daniel Junglas
Year: 2013
Abstract: CPLEX 12.5 has introduced the CPLEX Remote Object. In this presentation we discuss ways to employ remote objects in order to design powerful distributed algorithms like decomposition based approaches without having to worry about details of the communication between the machines.
