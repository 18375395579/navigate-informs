Title: Multi-objective Robust Adaptive Scheduling for Hospital Staffing

Author: Kibaek Kim, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Rd., C210, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, Kibaek.kim@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Jonathan Turner, Sanjay Mehrotra

Year: 2012

Abstract: For given mid-term (or long-term) schedules, it is necessary to make short-term adjustments, either by rescheduling staffs when shortages are anticipated or by canceling staffs when patient volume drops are anticipated. We develop a multi-objective robust adaptive scheduling model for the hospital staffing problem.
