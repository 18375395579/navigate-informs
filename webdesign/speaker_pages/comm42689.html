Title: Detection of Approximate Periodicity in Categorical Time Series with Time Intervals
Author: Jian Liu, Assistant Professor, The University of Arizona, Rm 221 ENGR Building, 1127 E. James E. Rogers Way, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, jianliu@sie.arizona.edu
Coauthor(s): Zhenrui Wang, Mingyang Li, Sean Dessureault
Year: 2012
Abstract: Categorical time series are symbol sequences arranged in the order of occurrence. When time of occurrences of symbols is also available, order and time information should be fused to detect periodic patterns. In practice, symbols often repeat with only approximately equal time intervals. This paper employs statistical method for the detection of such approximate periodicity. A case study from mining industry demonstrates the effectiveness of the proposed approach.
