Title: Generating better Inpatient Dialysis Schedules

Author: Ruben A. Proano, Assistant Professor, Rochester Institute of Technology, Rochester Institute of Technology, Rochester, NY, 14623, United States of America, rpmeie@rit.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Kuhl, Manuel Tolentino

Year: 2013

Abstract: Scheduling dialysis procedures for inpatients is a complex process that requires determining when a patient should receive treatment, while considering a limited number of dialysis units, isolation requirements, and other more critical medical procedures already scheduled for the patient. This study proposes an optimization approach to minimizes tardiness associated with scheduling dialysis procedures
