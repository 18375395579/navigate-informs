Title: Dynamics of New Product Introduction in Closed Rental Systems

Author: Ramandeep Randhawa, The University of Texas at Austin, Austin, TX, 78704, United States of America, Ramandeep.Randhawa@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Sunil Kumar, Achal Bassamboo

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a rental system, like Netflix, where a fixed number of users rent one product at a time from a collection of re-usable products. We study transient behavior in this system following the introduction of a new product. Analyzing the usage process for this product, we show that an increase in the variability can allow the firm to set lower capacity levels to meet a desired QoS. We also show that the firm is better off not imposing any deadlines for the return of the product.
