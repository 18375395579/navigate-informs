Title: Network Security and Contagion

Author: Azarakhsh Malekian, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, malekian@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Daron Acemoglu, Asuman Ozdaglar

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the network security problem and compare the properties of the equilibrium and the socially optimal solutions.An infection percolates over the network.Agents protect themselves by investing in the security.We delineate conditions under which there is underinvestment in the equilibrium and also show which sorts of networks lead to more severe market failures. When attacks are strategically targeted,we show that there is a new mechanism for overinvestment.
