Title: Fair Allocation Algorithm to Support Proposed LGA Slot Reassignment

Author: Shin-Lai Tien, University of Maryland, Dept of Civil and Environmental Eng, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, alextien@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Ball

Year: 2008

Abstract: The FAA has proposed the use of market-based mechanisms to both alleviate delay and maintain competitive access at LaGuadia airport. Under the proposal, a percentage of air carrier slots revert to the FAA over time. We propose a fair allocation process for specifying this slot transfer.
