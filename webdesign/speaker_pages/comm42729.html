Title: Movement Planner Algorithm Design for Dispatching on Multi-Track Territories
Author: Kamalesh Somani, Manager Operations Research, CSX Transportation, 500 Water St, Jacksonville, FL, 32202, Kamalesh_Somani@csx.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: A dispatcher’s task is to give track authorities to trains to maximize overall system efficiency while abiding by a number of business rules. This is done by deciding which train takes the siding when two trains traveling in opposite directions meet each other, or when a faster train is to overtake the leading slower train. They must also decide on the departure and hold times at the yard where train originate terminate or stop for work events. The three finalists will compete at this session.
