Title: Analysis of a Generalized Fork/Join Station with Inputs from Multi-server Stations

Author: Nico Vandaele, KU Leuven Campus Kortrijk, Etienne Sabbelaan 53, Kortrijk, WV, 8500, Belgium, nico.vandaele@kuleuven-kortrijk.be

Coauthor(s): Nico Goossens, Ananth Krishnamurthy

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper presents an exact analysis of a fork/join station with inputs from stations composed of multiple exponential servers. The queue length process at the input buffers is analyzed in terms of the underlying Markov process. Expressions for the marginal and joint distributions of inter-departure times from the fork/join station are derived from the analysis of the semi-Markov kernel characterizing the departure process.
