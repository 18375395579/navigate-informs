Title: An Operational Statistical Approach for Informed Pricing

Author: Justin Azadivar, Graduate Student, University of California, Department of IEOR, University of California, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, jazad@uclink.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): George Shanthikumar

Year: 2006

Abstract: In order to price products or services, information must be gathered, often at cost. A complete statistical approach should account for the value of additional information dynamically and in relation to the decision. An Operational Statistical approach is presented to avoid the limitations of both the frequentist estimator approach, which does not generally integrate decision-making, and the subjective Bayesian approach, which requires knowledge that is often unavailable.
