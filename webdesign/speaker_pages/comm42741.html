Title: The Effect of Variance Reduction and Risk Aversion on Bidding in Auctions
Author: Elena Katok, Associate Professor, Penn State University, 465 Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, ekatok@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Tim Salmon
Year: 2008
Abstract: Bidding above th risk-neutral Nash equilibrium has long been attributed to risk aversion. We present two experiments. One showing that consistent with risk aversion, variance reduction increases certainty equivalence of lotteries. The other showing overbidding in sealed-bid first price auctions is not affected by variance reduction.
