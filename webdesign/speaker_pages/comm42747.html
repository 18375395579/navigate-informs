Title: Fifth Generation Methods for Forecasting Spare Parts

Author: Thomas Willemain, Senior Vice President, Smart Software, Inc., 4 Hill Road, Belmont, MA, 02478, United States of America, TomW@smartcorp.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Management of spare parts inventories is complicated by the intermittent nature of demand. All four prior generations of intermittent demand forecasting methods processed each part in isolation. Gen5 methods discover and exploit demand correlations across parts. These correlations define the social network of parts. Besides providing a new way to forecast demand, this collective approach will also be useful in non-forecasting tasks by giving a big picture view of an entire fleet of parts.
