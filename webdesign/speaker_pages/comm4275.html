Title: Bidding Algorithms for Simultaneous Transportation Procurement Auctions

Author: Gultekin Kuyzu, PhD Candidate, ISyE, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, gkuyzu@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Özlem Ergun, Martin Savelsbergh

Year: 2006

Abstract: We explore bidding strategies for a carrier in multiple simultaneous single-lane auctions. The challenge is that the operating cost of the carrier depends on the synergy between the set of lanes the carrier wins, which is not known until the end of the auction. The emphasis is on modeling the strategic bidding behavior of competitors. We model a problem with the objective of maximizing the carrier’s expected profit.
