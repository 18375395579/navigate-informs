Title: A Shipper-Carrier Freight Transshipment Network Model: A Stackelberg Game-Theoretic Approach
Author: Tao Yao, Pennsylvania State University, 349 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, taoyao@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Bo Zhang, Amir Meimand, Terry Friesz
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper considers the shipper-carrier freight transshipment problem by a leader-follower game. The problem is formulated as a MPEC. Numerical experiments are conducted and some interesting insights about carrier’s optimal pricing strategy are discussed.
