Title: Transit Vehicle Routing for Evacuation of the Carless

Author: Mark Hickman, Associate Professor, University of Arizona, P.O. Box 210072, Tucson, AZ, 85721-0072, United States, mhickman@engr.arizona.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: There is a role for public transit vehicles in evacuating low-mobility persons during an emergency. We explore the problem of how buses might be routed dynamically to evacuate the carless. In this on-line problem context, various vehicle routing heuristics are proposed and compared.
