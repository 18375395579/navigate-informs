Title: Optimal Dynamic Portfolio Construction with Transaction Costs

Author: John Birge, Professor, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, john.birge@chicagobooth.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Challenges for practical portfolio optimization include both time-varying return distributions and the presence of transaction costs. We consider simultaneous portfolio optimization and parameter estimation in a GARCH model with proportional transaction costs using consistent estimates of the opportunity cost of sub-optimal positions.
