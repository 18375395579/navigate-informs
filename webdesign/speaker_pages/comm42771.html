Title: Locomotive Fleet Planning System at Norfolk Southern

Author: Clark Cheng, Sr. Manager Operations Research, Norfolk Southern Railway, 1200 Peachtree St. NE, MS 12-117, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States of America, clark.cheng@nscorp.com

Coauthor(s): Ricardo Fiorillo, Junxia Chang, Sourav Das, Belgacem Bouzaiene-Ayari, Warren Powell

Year: 2008

Abstract: In order to meet the forecasted demand, Norfolk Southern needs to identify the optimal locomotive fleet size. On one hand, locomotive shortage can result in train delays and lost business. On the other hand, locomotives are very expensive assets. NS and Princeton University jointly developed a locomotive fleet sizing model. The model has been integrated into the existing NS planning framework and provides an effective tool to identify locomotive requirements for various service levels.
