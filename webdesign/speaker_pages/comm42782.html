Title: Nash Equilibria of Sealed-bid Package Auctions

Author: Marion Ott, RWTH Aachen University, Templergraben 64, Aachen, 52056, Germany, marion.ott@rwth-aachen.de

Coauthor(s): Marissa Beck

Year: 2013

Abstract: We characterize the sets of Nash equilibria (NE) of the Vickrey package auction (VA), minimum-revenue core-selecting auctions (MRCSA), and the pay-as-bid auction (PBA). The set of NE of the VA contains the set of NE of MRCSA, which contains all NE of the PBA. Moreover, the set of potential NE outcomes for all MRCSA and the VA includes any feasible assignment together with any payments weakly below the winners?true values.
