Title: Dynamic Pricing for Hotel Yield Management of Multiple-Day Stay
Author: Ding Qing, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore, 178899, Singapore, dingqing@smu.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Kum Khiong Yang
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study an application of yield management to the hotel industry of multiple-day stay. The problem is formulated as a stochastic programming model. We develop a dynamic pricing system with demand reshaping and capacity rationing. The policy is almost-optimal and the updated information is revealed in real time. The mechanism is applicable in both spot and online system.
