Title: Optimization of Vaccine Packaging and Storage to Improve Vaccine Supply Chain Logistics
Author: Bryan Norman, University of Pittsburgh, Pittsburgh, PA, United States of America, banorman@pitt.edu
Coauthor(s): Jayant Rajgopal, Shawn Brown, Jung Lim, Bruce Lee, Sunghoon Lim, Erin Claypool
Year: 2013
Abstract: Cold storage space is a significant constraint in many vaccine supply chains. We consider the problem of determining vaccine packaging quantities and storage configurations in order to optimize cold storage space utilization with an emphasis on lower and middle income countries EPI vaccine distribution systems. Results are shown for different country contexts.
