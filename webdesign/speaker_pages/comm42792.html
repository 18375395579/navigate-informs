Title: A Sensor Location Model to Optimize Bayesian OD Estimation

Author: Pitu Mirchandani, Arizona State University, 699 South Mill Avenue, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, pitu@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ning Wang

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Sensor-Location Flow Estimation is a bilevel problem, where the upper level decides the optimum subset of links to locate counting sensors, and the lower level computes the best estimates of the flows of interest by using anticipated data from given candidate sensors locations. This paper proposed a sensor location model (upper level) when the lower level uses a Bayesian approach to update the prior distribution of OD volumes from observed link flows
