Title: Non-Markovian State-dependent Networks in Critical Loading
Author: Chihoon Lee, Colorado State University, 221 Statistics Building (CB#1877), Fort Collins, CO, 80523-1877, United States of America, chihoon@stat.colostate.edu
Coauthor(s): Anatolii Puhalskii
Year: 2013
Abstract: We establish heavy traffic limit theorems for queue-length processes in critically loaded single class queueing networks with state-dependent arrival and service rates. A distinguishing feature of our model is non-Markovian state dependence. The limit stochastic process is a continuous-path reflected process on the nonnegative orthant. We give an application to generalized Jackson networks with state-dependent rates.
