Title: Reliable Real-time Travel Time Prediction with Sparsely Distributed Detectors`
Author: Nan Zou, Research Associate, University of Maryland, 9716 Luguna Road, Middle River, MD, 21220, United States, nanzou@umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Jianwei Wang, Gang-Len Chang
Year: 2007
Abstract: This study aims to develop a reliable and cost-effective travel time prediction system that needs only a small number of traffic detectors and does not rely on the concurrent measurement of travel times to perform accurate real-time travel time predictions under recurrent traffic conditions and some scenarios with missing data.
