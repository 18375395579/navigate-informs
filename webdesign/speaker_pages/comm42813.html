Title: Capacity Reservation Policies in Cloud Computing Marketplaces
Author: Aaron Yan, Gravitant, Inc., 11940 Jollyville Rd. Suite 325-N, Austin, TX, 78759, United States of America, aaron.yan@gravitant.com
Coauthor(s): Ilyas Iyoob, Ton Dieker
Year: 2013
Abstract: Many IT organizations now choose to go to the Cloud for their computing needs. While Cloud computing allows practically infinite scaling of capacity based on demand, such “on-demand?capacity is relatively expensive. Instead, IT organizations may choose to reserve some capacity for some portion of their demand and then resort to “on-demand?capacity only for unsatisfied demand. In this paper, we define the optimal capacity reservation policy that minimizes cost subject to demand variability.
