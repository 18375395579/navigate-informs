Title: Flexible Leasing Contracts in a Sustainable Fleet Replacement Model

Author: Fernando Oliveira, Professor, ESSEC Business School, 100 Victoria Street, Singapore, Singapore, oliveira@essec.fr

Coauthor(s): Amir Hossein Ansaripoor

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the fleet replacement problem taking into account the minimization of cost and risk, simultaneously, in a stochastic multi-period setting, using conditional value at risk. New engine vehicles are considered as an alternative solution to reduce fossil fuel consumption and greenhouse gas emission. We develop a model that considers the possibility of early termination of the leasing contracts using real options. We validate the results by a real world case study.
