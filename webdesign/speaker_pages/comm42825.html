Title: Reliable Networks for Smarter Systems

Author: Justo Puerto, Professor, Universidad de Sevilla, Reina Mercedes n.1, 2-A, Sevilla, 41012, Spain, puerto@us.es

Coauthor(s): Maria Albareda-Sambola, Yolanda Hinojosa

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce formulations to location problems where facilities may fail, and service is provided on scene. Service depends of the number of fails encountered by the users. The criterion for the assignments of customers is the distance to the facility previously visited. We compare different exact formulations. Then, we introduce an approximation that works with much lower computing time. Computational experiments show the performance of our formulations.
