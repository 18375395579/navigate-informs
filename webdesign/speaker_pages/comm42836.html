Title: New Facets for the K-median Problem
Author: Wenhui Zhao, Research Fellow, Washington University at St. Louis, John M. Olin School of Business, One Brookings Drive, St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States of America, zhao@olin.wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Marc E. Posner
Year: 2008
Abstract: The polyhedral structure of the K-median problem is examined. We establish two basic properties of facets. Then, we apply these properties to generalize three existing classes of facets. Also, we establish that a class of k-cover inequalities are facet inducing.
