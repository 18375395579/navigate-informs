Title: Towards a New Approach to Seat Inventory Control at Air Canada
Author: Anne Mercier, Senior Scientist, ExPretio Technologies, 200 Laurier Ouest, suite 400, Montréal, QC, H2T 2N8, Canada, anne.mercier@expretio.com
Coauthor(s): Jean-François Pag?, Jean-Philippe Côt?, Louis-Philippe Bigras
Year: 2008
Abstract: The proposed integrated approach incorporates a segmentation procedure, a discrete choice model that computes choice probabilities for each alternative of the passengers' choice set, and an optimization tool for the airline's seat allocation maximizing revenue in accordance with the airline's business strategies.
