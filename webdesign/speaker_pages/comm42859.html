Title: On the Stability of a Boundedly Rational Day-to-day Dynamic
Author: Xuan Di, Graduate Asistant, University of Minnesota, 500 Pillsbury Dr SE, Civil Engineering, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, dixuan@umn.edu
Coauthor(s): Jong-Shi Pang, Henry Liu, Jeff Ban
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper provides the stability analysis for the boundedly rational day-to-day dynamic. TBR dynamic is a variable structure system consisting of multiple subsystems. Within each subsystem, the dynamic is linear time-invariant with exponential stability. Along the discontinuity surfaces between subsystems, the dynamic will slide along it and stabilize at one equilibrium. Therefore, the BR dynamic is asymptotic stable. A small network example is given to illustrate the stability analysis.
