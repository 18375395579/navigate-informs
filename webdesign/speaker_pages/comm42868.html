Title: Convergence of Sequential Sampling Policies for Bayesian Information Collection Problems
Author: Peter Frazier, Princeton University, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, pfrazier@princeton.edu
Coauthor(s): Warren Powell
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider adaptive sequential sampling policies in a general Bayesian framework. We present new sufficient conditions for convergence, or asymptotic optimality, in this sequential setting, and apply these conditions to show asymptotic optimality of several existing policies for ranking and selection.
