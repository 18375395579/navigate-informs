Title: Modeling of and Numerical Solution to Optimal Control of Diabetes Mellitus Under Uncertainty

Author: Zhen Liu, Assistant Professor, University of Missouri - Rolla, 1870 Miner Circle, Rolla, MO, 65409, United States, zliu@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): John Birge

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the continuous control of insulin for diabetic patients to minimize the cumulative deviations of blood glucose level from basal level. The dynamics we use is the 'minimal model' of Bergman subject to uncertainties. A Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman (HJB) equation is derived and corresponding numerical methods are discussed.
