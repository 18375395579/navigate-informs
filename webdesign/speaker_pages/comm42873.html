Title: The Branchwidth of Graphs and their Cycle Matroids

Author: Illya Hicks, Associate Professor, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering MS 3131, Zachry Engineering Center, College Station, TX, 77843-3131, United States, ivhicks@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Nolan McMurray

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk, we prove a conjecture stating that the branchwidth of a graph and the branchwidth of the graph's cycle matroid are equivalent if the graph has a cycle of length at least two.
