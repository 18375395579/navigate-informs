Title: Semidefinite Programming Approaches in Valuing Energy Derivatives
Author: Georgios Dalakouras, DalakouG@fhlbsf.com
Coauthor(s): Roy Kwon, Richard Ghazarian
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider SDP approachces for valuing path dependent energy derivatives. Several SDP formulations will be presented based on a technique for generating the moments of the risk-neutral distributions of energy (e.g. gas) prices.
