Title: Manpower Reduction Heuristic for Automotive Assembly Planning
Author: Kacie Theisen, Ford Research & Advanced Engineering, 2101 Village Rd, Room 3011, RIC Building, MD 2122, Dearborn, MI, 48124, ktheise1@ford.com
Coauthor(s): Erica Klampfl, Oleg Gusikhin
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present a heuristic to minimize manpower on a mixed-model assembly line by reassigning work within a neighborhood of the current assignment. This heuristic is based on current operation assignments and does not require explicit representation of precedence constraints. We present an example where good starting manpower assignments are provided.
