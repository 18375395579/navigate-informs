Title: Strong Formulations for Convex Functions Over Nonconvex Sets
Author: Daniel Bienstock, Columbia University, Dept. of IEOR, New York, NY, United States of America, dano@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Alex Michalka
Year: 2012
Abstract: We describe continuing work for obtaining the convex hull of sets representing quadratic polynomials over nonconvex sets, such as the complement of a polyhedron, the complement of a paraboloid, or the complement of a union of polyhedra and paraboloids.
