Title: Pricing under General Choice
Author: Srikanth Jagabathula, New York University, Kaufman Management Center, 44 W 4th Street, Rm 8-74 KMC, New York, NY, 10012-1126, United States of America, sjagabat@stern.nyu.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We consider the problem of optimal pricing for a given assortment of products when the demand is determined by a nonparametric choice model. We assume we are given historical transaction data. The choice model is assumed to be a distribution over preference lists and the choice probability is assumed to be related to the price through a logistic function. The talk will present the results on the optimal price structure under these conditions.
