Title: Appointment Scheduling with Nonlinear Waiting Costs and No-shows

Author: Michael Pinedo, Professor and Chair, New York University, Stern School of Business, 44 West 4th Street, New York, NY, 10012, United States of America, mpinedo@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Christos Zacharias, Kangbok Lee

Year: 2012

Abstract: Consider an appointment scheduling problem with a fixed number of slots and a number of customers to be assigned to the slots. Each customer has a weight and a given probability of not showing up. The waiting cost function of the customers is nonlinear. We establish conditions under which certain priority rules are optimal for certain segments of the schedule.
