Title: A Single-server Queue with a Delayed Service Option and Abandonment
Author: Amy Ward, USC, Marshall School of Business, BRI 401 H, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-0804, United States, amyward@marshall.usc.edu
Coauthor(s): Sriram Dasu, Vasiliki Kostami
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a single-server queueing model in which customers may choose between waiting for service in real-time, and returning for service at a dynamically specified future time. Some of the customers that promise to return for service do not return. We show the system exhibits state-space collapse in the heavy-traffic limit, and develop a one-dimensional diffusion approximation with state-dependent drift.
