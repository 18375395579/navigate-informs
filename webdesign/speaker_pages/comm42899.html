Title: A Decision Support Framework for Optimizing a Bank's Currency Network
Author: Yiwei Huang, Texas A&M University, Mays Business School, 320R Wehner, 4217 TAMU, College Station, TX, United States of America, yhuang@mays.tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Neil Geismar, Divakar Rajamani, Suresh Sethi, Chelliah Sriskandarajah, Marcelo Carlos
Year: 2013
Abstract: A Bank supplies currency to satisfy the demand of the financial institutions. The decisions to be made are selecting and prioritizing the locations of retail vaults that will be upgraded, selecting the most efficient transportation mode between branches and vaults, and optimizing the sourcing for the new currency network. The objective is to minimize the total cost including the upgrading cost, the transportation cost and the incremental capacity cost to select locations for new regional vaults.
