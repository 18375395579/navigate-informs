Title: Stochastic Risk-factor Model for Portfolio Optimization
Author: Miguel Lejeune, Assistant Professor, George Washington University, United States of America, mlejeune@gwu.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: We propose a stochastic risk-factor asset allocation model. We consider the cases in which the asset returns have skewed probability distribution and/or investment constraints are imposed. We derive a solution method. Numerical results will illustrate its efficacy.
