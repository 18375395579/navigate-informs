Title: Service and Day-differentiated Load Planning for the LTL Industry

Author: Mike Hewitt, Georgia Institue of Technology, 765 Ferst Dr, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, mhewitt@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Martin Savelsbergh, Alan Erera

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider extensions of the traditional load planning problem faced by LTL carriers, namely the generation of service-level and day-of-week differentiated load plans, which are necessary to remain competitive in the current market. We investigate integer programming based approaches augmented with local improvement heuristics.
