Title: Minimizing Total Weighted Tardiness on Burn-in Ovens in Semiconductor Manufacturing

Author: Shubin Xu, Charles H. Lunquist College of Business, University of Oregon, Eugene, OR, 97403-1208, United States, sxu@uoregon.edu

Coauthor(s): James Bean

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study burn-in oven scheduling in semiconductor manufacturing with parallel, non-identical ovens and total weighted tardiness objective. Jobs have non-zero ready-time and variable processing times. We present a random keys genetic algorithm that finds good solutions in reasonable computation time.
