Title: Bidding Optimally Across Multiple Simultaneous Vickrey Auctions
Author: Enrico Gerding, University of Southampton, Southampton, S017 1BJ, United Kingdom, eg@ecs.soton.ac.uk
Coauthor(s): Rajdeep Dash, Nicholas Jennings
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider optimal best response strategies for bidding in simultaneous Vickrey auctions with perfect substitutes. We prove that it is optimal to place non-zero bids in all available auctions, and we characterise the optimal strategy with and without budget constraints.
