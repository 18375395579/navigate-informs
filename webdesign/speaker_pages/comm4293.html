Title: Using Sensor Data to Improve the Management of Spare Parts
Author: Jennifer Ryan, Associate Professor, RPI, 110 8th Street, CII, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, ryanj6@rpi.edu
Coauthor(s): Xi Kan, Rong Li, Zhi Zeng
Year: 2012
Abstract: This talk will discuss methods for incorporating real-time sensor information obtained through condition monitoring into inventory management and replacement decisions for service parts. We consider the problem faced by a manufacturer who manages inventory for a machine part that is used on a large number of geographically-dispersed machines and which is subject to deterioration. The manufacturer's goal is to use the real-time sensor information to improve his service parts inventory management.
