Title: Corrected Diffusion Approximations in the Halfin-Whitt Regime

Author: Bert Zwart, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Dr., Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, bertzwart@gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We give corrected diffusion approximations and bounds for the no-wait probability in the M/M/s and M/D/s queue. These results can be used to evaluate the quality of the square root staffing formula. We show analytically why this staffing formula works so well - even for small systems. In addition, we show how the Halfin-Whitt approximation can be adapted in such a way that it becomes accurate for systems which are not in heavy traffic or systems with a small number of servers.
