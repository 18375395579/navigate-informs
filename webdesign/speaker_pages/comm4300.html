Title: Joining (and leaving) Observable and Unobservable Queues - An Experimental Investigation

Author: Mirko Kremer, Pennsylvania State University, State College, PA, United States of America, muk22@smeal.psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Laurens Debo

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present results from a set of laboratory experiments designed to test some of the key predictions of queuing theory. Specifically, we study how customers form expectations about queue lengths and waiting times when they arrive at a service facility, and their subsequent queue joining decisions.
