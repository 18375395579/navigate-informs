Title: Block Coordinate Descent-type Decomposition in Multiobjective Programming

Author: Brian Dandurand, Clemson University, O-110 Martin Hall, Box 340975, Clemson, SC, 29631, United States of America, bdandur@clemson.edu

Coauthor(s): Margaret Wiecek

Year: 2012

Abstract: Decomposition of multiobjective programs (MOPs) in the objective space, the decision space, and simultaneously both spaces according to the principles of block coordinate descent is investigated. Coordination algorithms and conditions for their convergence to the Pareto set of the original MOP are developed. Computational examples are given.
