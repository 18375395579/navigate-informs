Title: Approximations for Information Propagation Speed in Delay-tolerant Connected Vehicle Networks
Author: Kai Yin, Texas A&M University, College Station, TX, United States of America, yinkai1000@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Bruce Wang
Year: 2013
Abstract: In a delay-tolerant connected vehicle network, the message carried by vehicles propagates at vehicles' travel speed when vehicles disconnect with each other. Otherwise, the message propagates instantaneously through the multihop connectivity. In this study, we propose some approximation methods to obtain estimates of information propagation speed on bidirectional highways. The methods enable us to address not only Poisson but also general vehicle headway distributions.
