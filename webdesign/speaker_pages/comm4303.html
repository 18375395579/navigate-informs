Title: An Integrated Model for Ship Routing and Berth Assignment with Transshipments

Author: Anthony Pang, Lecturer, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, M613, Li Ka Shing Tower,, Department of Logistics, Hung Hom, Hong Kong - ROC, lgtapang@polyu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Chung-Lun Li

Year: 2007

Abstract: Shipping companies that offer feeder services often seek to coordinate their ship routing and berthing operations. In this work, we consider an integrated model to support joint decisions in ship routing and terminal berth assignment, with some terminals serving as transshipment facilities. We propose a mathematical programming based heuristic, together with column generation techniques and local improvement schemes, for solving the problem.
