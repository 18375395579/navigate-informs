Title: Network Fairness in Air Traffic Flow Management (ATFM)
Author: Douglas Fearing, Ph.D Student, MIT Operations Research Center, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Building E40-149, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States, dfearing@MIT.EDU
Coauthor(s): Dimitris Bertsimas, Amedeo Odoni
Year: 2007
Abstract: We extend a network formulation for ATFM with a measure of fairness. This extension is consistent with highly successful practices. Additionally, we show how we can incorporate a three-stage Collaborative Decision-Making (CDM) approach. For the single-airport case, we compare our three-stage approach to the three-stage approach utilized in a CDM-enhanced GDP. In our computational results, we use simple network scenarios to demonstrate the trade-off between system delay and fairness.
