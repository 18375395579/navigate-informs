Title: Mixed-integer and Linear Approximation Models for Continuous Path Treatment Planning for Stereotactic

Author: Kimia Ghobadi, University of Toronto, 5 King's College Road, Toronto, On, M5S 3G8, Canada, kimia@mie.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Dionne Aleman, David Jaffray, Mark Ruschin

Year: 2013

Abstract: We explore continuous dose delivery in treatment plans for Gamma Knife Perfexion. We first select a path in the tumor, along which the radiation will be deposited, and then develop mixed-integer models (and their linear approximations) to find the optimal beam durations for the chosen path. Several criteria are considered, including speed and movement accuracy. Our models yield clinically acceptable treatments while improving treatment time compared to forward and inverse step-and-shoot plans.
