Title: No Soup for You! Intentionally Unsatisfied Demand and Future Production

Author: Reynold Byers, University of California, Irvine, SB 309, The Paul Merage School of Business, Irvine, CA, 92697, United States, RByers@uci.edu

Coauthor(s): David Huff

Year: 2006

Abstract: Intentional shortages are a surprising practical reality. There are several examples of fashion labels that specifically leave known demand unfilled. We explore the relationship of demand with fulfillment in a two-period setting. We assume that unfulfilled demand in the first period creates a ``buzz that increases demand in the second period. We model the problem first with deterministic demand and then again incorporating demand variability.
