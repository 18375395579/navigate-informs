Title: Simultaneous Berth and Quay Crane Scheduling for Container Ports

Author: Aykagan Ak, Georgia Institute of Technology, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, aak@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Erera

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper concentrates on the scheduling problem of berth and quay cranes, simultaneously because of the fact that the service time of vessels depend on the crane allocation. We determine berthing positions and times of vessels as well as the assignment of quay cranes to holds of vessels so that the total service time and total lateness penalty is minimized. A MIP is formulated and used to solve small problems optimally. Tabu search methods are proposed for large problems.
