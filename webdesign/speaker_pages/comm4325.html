Title: Bandwidth Sharing Networks in Overload

Author: Bert Zwart, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Dr., Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, bertzwart@gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a multiclass bandwidth sharing network assuming weighted alpha-fair scheduling and at least one of the traffic links is overloaded. Building on work by Gromoll&Williams, we obtain a multidimensional fixed point equation for the growth rates of the number of users. Uniqueness of solutions to this fixed point equation is shown by identifying an associated convex programming problem, which is shown to imply uniqueness of the GW fluid model solution assuming an empty network at time 0.
