Title: Yield Management in Freight Transportation

Author: Long Gao, Assistant Professor, School of Business Administration, University of Dayton, Dayton, OH, 45469, United States of America, gaolong@udayton.edu

Coauthor(s): Mike Gorman

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study an order acceptance and resource allocation decision problem in freight transportation with random, nonperishable supply and multiple shipping dates. The study provides insights on the value of inventory rationing, order batching, costs of using suboptimal policies, as well as justifications for the management to adopt advanced order acceptance policies.
