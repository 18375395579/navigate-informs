Title: No-holdback Allocation Rules for Continuous-time Assemble-to-order Systems
Author: Yao Zhao, Rutgers University, 180 University Ave, Newark, 07102, United States of America, yaozhao@andromeda.rutgers.edu
Coauthor(s): Yingdong Lu, Jeannette Song
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider a class of dynamic component allocation rules, termed no-holdback (NHB) rules, in continuous-review, multi-product assemble-to-order (ATO) systems. The lead times are positive and not necessarily identical. We analyzed the optimality condition for this class of rules and developed tools for performance evaluation. We also compared NHB rules to the common used FIFO allocation rule.
