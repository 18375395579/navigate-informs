Title: Call Center Workforce Optimization Through Simulation Incorporating Retrial Calls
Author: Hideki Yamanaka, Fujitsu Laboratories Ltd., 4-1-1 Kamiodanaka, Nakahara-ku, Kawasaki, 211-8588, Japan, hyamanaka@jp.fujitsu.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: A call center model with retrial calls was validated by analyzing the CTI log with notified customer telephone numbers in real call centers, with the result that hourly average retrial call ratios and retrial intervals for abandoned calls vary considerably with human behavior, giving us another room for workforce optimization.
