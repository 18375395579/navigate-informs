Title: Optimal Scheduling of Products with Two Subassemblies and Changeover Cost

Author: Feng Zhou, student, Indiana University Bloomington, 1309 E. 10th Street, Bloomington, IN, 47405, United States of America, fengzhou@indiana.edu

Coauthor(s): James Blocher, Sebastian Heese, Xinxin Hu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the problem of scheduling products with two subassemblies on a common resource, where changeovers incur fixed costs. The objective is to minimize the weighted sum of flow time and changeover cost. We provide properties of optimal solutions and characterize optimal schedules. Our results have interesting implications for practice, primarily that the structure of the optimal schedule is robust to changes in demand.
