Title: Geometric Programming Based Shape-Preserving Cubic L1 Splines

Author: Shu-Cherng Fang, Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, North Carolina State University, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States, fang@eos.ncsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Wei Zhang, John Lavery

Year: 2006

Abstract: Splines have been widely used for real world applications. One fundamental requirement for these applications is that they should have minimum nonphysical oscillations for data with arbitrary changes in magnitude and in node spacing. We present a geometric programming based cubic L1 splines to serve this purpose.
