Title: Is Capacity Rationing Optimal When Customers Use Anecdotal Reasoning?

Author: Tingliang Huang, Assistant Professor, University College London, Gower Street, Department of Management Science & Innov, London, WC1E 6BT, United Kingdom, t.huang@ucl.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Qian Liu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study strategic capacity rationing when customers have boundedly rational expectations in the sense of anecdotal reasoning. Our S(K) model generalizes the existing literature by allowing customer expectations to be boundedly rational.
