Title: Delivery of Video-on-demand Over a Transport Channel with Limited Capacity
Author: Peter Kubat, Distinguished MTS, Verizon Technology, 40 Sylvan Rd, Waltham, MA, 02451, United States, peter.kubat@core.verizon.com
Year: 2007
Abstract: Two video-on-demand delivery schemes (a) “streaming?and (b) “fast load and store?are compared. It is shown that under moderate traffic loads “fast load and store?method is more efficient, namely having a better throughput, less blocking and provides a better transport utilization.
