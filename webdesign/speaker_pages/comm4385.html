Title: An Optimal Edge Search for a Stationary Hider with Imperfect Detection Information

Author: Shantih Spanton, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, sspanton@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Geunes, J. Cole Smith

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a searcher who seeks an immobile hider on an undirected network. The searcher may only traverse a finite number of edges along the search path. With each traversal of a network edge, the likelihood of discovering the hider upon that edge is reduced. A search path that maximizes the probability of discovering the hider is found.
