Title: Barriers to Relocating Intercity Bus Stations in Chinese Mega-cities

Author: Elenna Dugundji, Universiteit van Amsterdam, Faculty of Social & Behavioral Sciences, Nieuwe Prinsengracht 130, 1018 VZ, Amsterdam, Netherlands, E.R.Dugundji@uva.nl

Year: 2007

Abstract: Several mega-cities in China are faced with relocating their intercity bus stations due to problems caused by the mismatch between transport and land use systems. This paper takes Wuhan as an example to illustrate requirements for relocating the stations; to analyze the major barriers in the decision-making process for the relocation; and to propose likely information solutions to remove the barriers.
