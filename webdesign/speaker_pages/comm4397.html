Title: Convexity Results for Capacitated Stochastic Inventory Systems

Author: Woonghee Tim Huh, Assistant Professor, Columbia University, 500 W 120th Street, MC 4704, New York, NY, 10027, United States, th2113@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Ganesh Janakiraman

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper, we study a capacitated assembly system managed using an echelon order-up-to policy, and show that the shortage penalty cost over any horizon is jointly convex with respect to the base-stock levels and production capacity levels. This property is useful in developing algorithms for making optimal inventory and capacity decisions.
