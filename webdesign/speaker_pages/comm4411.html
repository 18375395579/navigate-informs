Title: Inventory Control with Observable and Unobservable Demands
Author: Mariana Olvera-Cravioto, Assistant Professor, Columbia University, 306 S. W. Mudd Building, New York, NY, 10027, United States, molvera@ieor.columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Woonghee Tim Huh, Ozalp Ozer
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study a peridic-review inventory model where a portion of demand is not observable. We assume that both observable and unobservable demands are stochastic with known distributions. In each period, the manager makes inventory replenishment and count decisions.
