Title: A Heuristic for an Integrated Inventory and Delivery Planning Problem in a Retailer's Supply Chain
Author: Jiayi Chen, Graduate Student, The Chinese Univ of Hong Kong, Dept of Syst Engg & Engg Mgmt, The Chinese Univ of Hong Kong, Shatin, NT, 00000, Hong Kong - ROC, jychen@se.cuhk.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): C.H. Cheng
Year: 2008
Abstract: An integrated inventory and delivery problem in a retailers supply chain involves the delivery of multi-products to satisfy retailers demands for a planning horizon. An MIP model is formulated and an optimization-based solution will be developed. Computational experiments will also be presented.
