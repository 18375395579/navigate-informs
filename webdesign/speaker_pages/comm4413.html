Title: A Resilience Framework for Collaborative Supply Networks

Author: Shimon Nof, Purdue University, 315 North Grant St, West Lafayette, IN, United States of America, nof@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Rodrigo Reyes Levalle

Year: 2013

Abstract: Research on resilient design and operation of supply networks is at its early stages; existing approaches are based on a trade-off between costs and fault-tolerance. The Fault Tolerance by Teaming (FTT) principle of Collaborative Control Theory appears as a promising solution, not based on the aforesaid trade-off. An FTT-inspired Resilience Framework for Supply Networks is presented and applied in two case studies; results are compared to those of traditional approaches for resilience.
