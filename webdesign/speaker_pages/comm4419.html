Title: Performance of Zero-Memory Policies in a Single Product Inventory System with Inaccurate Counts

Author: Olga L. Ortiz, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of Industrial and Systems Engineering, oortiz@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Chelsea C. White III, Alan Erera

Year: 2007

Abstract: We model an inventory system with inaccurate counts as a partially observed Markov decision process and show that under certain conditions, a zero-memory policy that is equivalent to an optimal policy for its completely observed counterpart is adaptive. A simulation study is used to assess the value of improved inventory accuracy.
