Title: Heuristic Approaches for the Multi-period Location Routing Problem

Author: Marco Lam, The University of Alabama, PO Box 870226, Tuscaloosa, AL, United States, mlam@cba.ua.edu

Coauthor(s): John Mittenthal

Year: 2007

Abstract: The objective of the Multi-Period Location Routing Problem (MP-LRP) is to minimize transportation and warehousing costs by selecting warehouses out of a set of possible warehouses. Because the problem is NP-hard, we propose heuristic approaches to solve it. We develop lower bounds and assess the performance of our heuristic approaches.
