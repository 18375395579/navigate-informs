Title: Level Workforce Schedules for 2-stage Transfer Lines
Author: George Vairaktarakis, Case Western Reserve University, Weatherhead School of Managament, Department of Operations, Cleveland, OH, 44106, United States of America, gxv5@case.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: We define two workforce leveling objectives for serial transfer-lines where the workers required per operation is such that the operation completes in precisely c periods. For both objectives we develop efficient algorithms for 2-stages and examine the trade-off between the maximum workforce size and the fluctuations from cycle to cycle.
