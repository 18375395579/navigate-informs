Title: Daily Routing for Customer Relationships in the Textbook Industry

Author: Shu Zhang, University of Iowa, Tippie College of Business, S321 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, shu-zhang-1@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeffrey Ohlmann, Barrett Thomas

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study an orienteering problem with stochastic wait times motivated by the daily decision making faced by textbook sales representatives. We explore structural properties of solutions and specifically investigate the existence of a control limit policy to govern the balk or join decision upon arrival at a queue outside a professor’s office. We focus on developing an approximate dynamic programming scheme to obtain dynamic routing solutions to maximize the expected textbook sales.
