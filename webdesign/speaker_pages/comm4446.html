Title: Optimal Job Replication Policies on Homogeneous Parallel Servers

Author: Yusik Kim, Ph.D. student, UC Berkeley, 4141 Etcheverry Hall, IEOR Department, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, ykim@newton.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Rhonda Righter, Ronald Wolff

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider scheduling jobs over a system of homogeneous servers with stochastic job processing times. When job replication is allowed, we study the degree of replication that yields the best system performance under various processing time distributions.
