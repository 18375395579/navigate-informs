Title: On the Benefit of the Sacrifice Bunt: Getting your Objectives Straight

Author: J. Eric Bickel, University of Texas, 1 University Station, C2200, ETC 5.128D, Austin, TX, 78712-0292, United States of America, ebickel@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Nobuyoshi Hirotsu

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk we analyze the benefit of the sacrifice bunt in Major League Baseball games under two different objectives: maximizing the expected number runs scored or maximizing the probability of winning. We demonstrate that focusing on the true objective (i.e., winning) leads to attempting the sacrifice in more situations than maximizing the expected number of runs.
