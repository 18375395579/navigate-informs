Title: Optimal Trade Execution using Limit Order Book Information

Author: Rolf Waeber, Cornell University, 206 Rhodes Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, rw339@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Sasha Stoikov

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider an asset liquidation problem at the market microstructure level, given observations of the limit order book. The optimization is formulated in terms of a sequence of stopping times, at which we submit market sell orders. We describe the shape of the trade and no trade regions for different price and latency assumptions. In the empirical section, we show that our policy signicantly outperforms a benchmark TWAP algorithm on US treasury bonds.
