Title: A Periodic Vehicle Routing Problem with Inventory

Author: Qiong Zhang, University of Iowa, 108 John Pappajohn, S343 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, qiong-zhang@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Luca Bertazzi, Jeffrey Ohlmann

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a periodic vehicle routing problem with inventory considerations. We examine the relationship between our model formulation and the more general inventory routing problem. We present preliminary results on a solution approach.
