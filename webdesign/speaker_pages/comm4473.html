Title: Decomposition Results for Queues in Random Environment

Author: Melike Baykal-Gursoy, Associate Professor, I&SE Department Rutgers, 96 Frelinghuysen Rd., Piscataway, NJ, 08854-8018, United States of America, gursoy@rci.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Zhe Duan

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study the infinite server Markovian queue with two-state Markov modulated arrival and service processes and the M/M/1/1 retrial queue. We show that the Fuhrman and Cooper type decomposition holds for the stationary occupancy distributions in both queues. We derive the explicit form of the occupancy distributions.
