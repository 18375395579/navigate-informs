Title: Bi-Criteria Scheduling on Parallel Machines with Machine Costs

Author: Michael Pinedo, Professor and Chair, New York University, Stern School of Business, 44 West 4th Street, New York, NY, 10012, United States of America, mpinedo@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Leung, Kangbok Lee

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider parallel machine scheduling problems where the processing of the jobs involves two types of costs. The first type is one of the classical scheduling objectives (i.e., makespan, flow time). The second type is an actual cost of processing a specific job on a specific machine. We present an overview of all the complexities of all problems considered in this bi-criteria scheduling framework.
