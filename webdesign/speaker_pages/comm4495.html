Title: Demand Response with Parameterized Supply Function Bidding
Author: Yunjian Xu, Singapore University of Technology and Design, Engineering Systems and Design, Singapore, Singapore, xuyunjian@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Na Li, Steven Low
Year: 2013
Abstract: When electricity supply and demand are imbalanced, a certain amount of load has to be adjusted by consumers. We seek to allocate the total amount of load adjustment to multiple consumers so as to minimize the aggregate consumer disutility. We propose a simple market-clearing mechanism that encourages consumers to reveal their private information by compensating their utility losses. We show that the proposed market mechanism yields bounded efficiency loss at a Nash equilibrium.
