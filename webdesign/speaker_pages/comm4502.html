Title: Smart Solutions: Identifying Top-Selling Product Configurations Using Branch And Bound
Author: Jule Drew, HP, jpward@hp.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a branch-and-bound based algorithm for identifying top-selling product configurations from order history. This problem, an example of frequent itemset counting problem in data mining, arose at HP through efforts to improve sales productivity and reduce the number of iterations required to perfect a customer sales quote.
