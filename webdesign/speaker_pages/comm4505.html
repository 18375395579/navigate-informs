Title: Sparse Mean Reverting Portfolios

Author: Alexandre d'Aspremont, Princeton University, E-Quad E424, Princeton, NJ, United States, aspremon@princeton.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the problem of extracting a sparse portfolio with maximum mean reversion given historical prices time series on a given set of assets. We derive a semidefinite relaxation for this problem and study its performance on numerical examples.
