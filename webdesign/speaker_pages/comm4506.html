Title: Structure from Local Optima: Learning Subspace Juntas via Higher Order PCA

Author: Ying Xiao, Georgia Institute of Technology, 266 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, ying.fe.xiao@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Santosh Vempala

Year: 2012

Abstract: Our result is an algorithm for generalized Independent Component Analysis that uses local optima of high moments to recover the component subspaces of a product distribution. If one component is k-dimensional while the other is “noise?modeled as an (n-k)-dimensional Gaussian, the algorithm is polynomial in n. We use this to learn a k-subspace junta, a labeling function determined by an unknown k-dimensional subspace, which is a generalization of learning a junta.
