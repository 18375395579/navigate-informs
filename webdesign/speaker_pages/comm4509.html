Title: Lower Bounds and Metaheuristics for a Bounded Vertex Coloring Problem.

Author: Enrico Malaguti, DEIS - University of Bologna, viale Risorgimento, 2, Bologna, 40136, Italy, emalaguti@deis.unibo.it

Coauthor(s): Albert Einstein Fernandes Muritiba, Manuel Iori, Paolo Toth

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a bounded version of the Vertex Coloring Problem, where each vertex i has an associated positive weight w(i), and the total weight of the vertices assigned to the same color is bounded. The problem can be also seen as a Bin Packing Problem subject to compatibility restrictions. We propose new lower bounding procedures and an effective metaheuristic approach for the problem.
