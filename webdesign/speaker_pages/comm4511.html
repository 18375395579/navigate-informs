Title: A Review on Stochastic Optimization Algorithms for Power Systems Optimization

Author: Steffen Rebennack, University of Florida, 303 Weil, Gainesville, FL, United States of America, steffen@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Mario PEREIRA, Niko Iliadis, Panos Pardalos

Year: 2008

Abstract: We provide a review of the existing algorithms that treat stochastically the problem of power systems operation optimization for a centrally dispatched (cost minimization) and a locally dispatched in a liberalized market (revenue maximization).
