Title: A Model for Optimal Maintenance Policies of Multi-component Transportation System with Demand Response
Author: Pattharin Sarutipand, Ph.D Candidate, Northwestern University, p-sarutipand@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Pablo Durango-Cohen
Year: 2007
Abstract: Optimal maintenance policies for a system of functionally interdependent components are considered. The problem is formulated as a constrained quadratic programming. The proposed model captures economic interdependencies that link the components that comprise these systems while accounting for the implications of explicitly considering the interrelationship between service level and demand of components.
