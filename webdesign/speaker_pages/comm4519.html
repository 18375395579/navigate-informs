Title: Shortest Routes with Refueling Stops

Author: Pitu Mirchandani, Arizona State University, 699 South Mill Avenue, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, pitu@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Jonathan Adler, Guoliang Xue

Year: 2012

Abstract: Unlike the problem of shortest paths and shortest path trees, when refueling stops are not required, and where well known algorithms exist for finding shortest path trees, the problem of finding shortest routes with refueling may require detouring for refueling, which may result in walks with cycles. A polynomial algorithm is presented for finding the set of shortest walks for all node pairs in the network.
