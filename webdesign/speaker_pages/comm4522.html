Title: Sequential Allocation Problems for Non-Profit Agencies
Author: Robert Lien, Doctoral Student, Department of Industrial Engineering and Management Sciences, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, Tech C210, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States, robertlien@northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Seyed Iravani, Karen Smilowitz
Year: 2006
Abstract: In this paper, we consider allocation decisions that arise in sequential inventory problems. In particular, we consider the Food Rescue Program operated by the Greater Chicago Food Depository.
