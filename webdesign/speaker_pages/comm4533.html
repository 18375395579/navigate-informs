Title: Finding Supply Function Equilibrium in Electricity Markets Using an AC Approach

Author: Miguel Anjos, Assistant Professor, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, ON, N2L 3G1, Canada, anjos@stanfordalumni.org

Coauthor(s): Anthony Vannelli, Guillermo Bautista

Year: 2006

Abstract: Using an AC transmission network, oligopolistic competition in power markets is formulated as a Nonlinear Programming (NLP) problem, and characterized by a multi-leader single-follower game. The transmission network is modelled with a detailed nonlinear system. For the game setting, a supply function equilibrium approach is used.
