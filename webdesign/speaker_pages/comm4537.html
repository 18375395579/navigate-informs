Title: Assessing Joint Distributions with Isoprobability Contours

Author: Ali Abbas, Assistant Professor, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Ave, Urbana, IL, 61820, United States of America, aliabbas@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): David Budescu, Yuhong Gu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a method to elicit joint probability distributions using isoprobability contours: the set of points with equal cumulative probability. This approach dispenses with the need to assess conditional probabilities, correlation coefficients or concordance probabilities, and reduces the joint probability assessment into a one-dimensional assessment. We present the results of an experiment to elicit the contours using pairwise choices; comment on consistency, and show how to infer dependence.
