Title: Probabilistic Interpretation for the Elasticity of Carried Load Revenue from the Erlang Loss Model

Author: William Massey, Professor, Princeton University, Sherrerd Hall Princeton University, Princeton, NJ, 08540, United States of America, wmassey@princeton.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Suppose that we know the demand function for some telephone service as a function of the phone rate. If we model the number of busy channels as an Erlang loss model, then we have a formula for the carried load revenue rate as a function of this price rate. The explicit formula for the elasticity of this carried load rate can be expressed in terms of the elasticity of the corresponding offered load rate.
