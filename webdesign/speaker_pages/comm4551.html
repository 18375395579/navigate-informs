Title: Throughput Optimization for Epicyclic Cycle in Dual-Gripper Robotic Cells under a Circular Layout

Author: Kyung Sung Jung, PhD Candidate, University of Texas at Dallas, Jindal School of Management, 800 W Campbell Rd., SM30, Richardson, TX, 75083, United States of America, kxj083000@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Pinedo, Chelliah Sriskandarajah, Neil Geismar

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the problem of scheduling operations to find the optimum k-unit cyclic sequence of robot movements in a dual-gripper bufferless robotic cell under circular layout, free-pickup criterion and the additive-travel-time metric. Given a small swithcing time for grippers , Epicyclic cycle proivdes a 3/2-approximation to the optimum cycle time.
