Title: Probability Modeling for Heuristics to Beam Selection in Radiotherapy

Author: Allen Holder, Trinity University Mathematics, One Trinity Place, San Antonio, United States, aholder@trinity.edu

Coauthor(s): Evan O'Dea

Year: 2006

Abstract: Selecting optimal beams in the design of radiotherapy treatments often requires values (probabilities) to be assigned to angles. We show that a balanced solution provides a suitable distribution and characterize when this distribution correctly estimates zero probabilities.
