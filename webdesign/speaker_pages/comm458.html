Title: Buy Low, Sell High with Mutually Exciting Processes, Adverse Selection and Short-Term-Alpha

Author: Sebastian Jaimungal, Associate Professor, University of Toronto, Department of Statistics, U. of Toronto, 6005 - 100 St. George Street, Toronto, On, M5S 3G3, Canada, sebastian.jaimungal@utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Alvaro Cartea

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce a multi-factor mutally exciting process for market-making strategies which allows for feedback and clustering effects in market orders and the shape of the limit order book and also accounts for the adverse selection effect. We pose and solve the stochastic control problem for the market marker and show how the strategy outperforms (i) a Poisson model which does not account for clustering (ii) a model which does not distinguish between influential and non-influential events.
