Title: Event-driven Sensing for Enterprise Reconfigurability and Optimization

Author: Christos Cassandras, Professor, Boston University, Dept. of Manuf. Engineering, 15 St. Mary's St., Brookline, MA, 02446, United States of America, cgc@bu.edu

Coauthor(s): Weibo Gong, Robert Gao, Yannis Paschalidis, Azer Bestavros

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose a systematic reconfigurability framework for an enterprise viewed as a large-scale dynamic system. A key element is the replacement of traditional fixed-interval, time-driven systems by reconfigurable event-driven systems better suited for asynchronous distributed environments. To achieve this, we develop event-driven mechanisms for data acquisition, sensor networking with node localization, and system level optimization that includes mobility coordination for delay tolerant networks.
