Title: A Multi-Carrier, Intermodal (IM) Freight Train-Scheduling Problem

Author: April Kuo, University of Maryland, aprilkuo@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Elise Miller-Hooks

Year: 2006

Abstract: We formulate a multi-carrier, IM train-scheduling problem in a network with multiple O-D pairs as an IP with multiple objectives. A technique is proposed based on network optimization theory to determine the timetable for the IM network. This technique will be illustrated on a network representation of a corridor in Europe.
