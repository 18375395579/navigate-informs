Title: Optimal Investment of Renewable and Conventional Assets
Author: Wuhan Desmond Cai, California Institute of Technology, 1200 E. California Blvd., MC 305-16, Pasadena, CA, United States of America, wccai@caltech.edu
Coauthor(s): Yunjian Xu, Steven Low
Year: 2013
Abstract: It is expected that the costs of solar energy will be on par with conventional energy in the coming years. However, a large fraction of conventional energy costs are due to fixed assets which are amortized over at least twenty years. We study the problem of optimal investment of two fixed assets (conventional and renewable generation) where the price of renewable generation decreases over time. We characterize the optimal investment strategy and give insights on optimal cost allocation rules.
