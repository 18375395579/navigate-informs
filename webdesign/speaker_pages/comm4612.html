Title: Survey of Dynamic Pricing Research on Selling a Given Inventory over a Finite Horizon

Author: Ming Chen, Assistant Professor, California State University, School of Business Administration, Long Beach, CA, 90840, United States of America, Ming.Chen@csulb.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a review of existing research on dynamic pricing problems involve selling a given amount of inventory over a finite time horizon without inventory replenishment. We classify these problems into several classes based on a number of problem attributes and summarize main findings and managerial insights.
