Title: Parallel Server Queueing Systems with Server Arrivals and Departures

Author: Mayank Sharma, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, mxsharma@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Yingdong Lu, Mark Squillante, Bo Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a parallel server queueing system where customers arrive according to a stochastic process, new servers arrive according to another stochastic process, and existing servers depart according to yet another stochastic process.
