Title: Structural Estimation of Callers' Delay Sensitivity in Call Centers Under Delay Announcement

Author: Che-Lin Su, University of Chicago Booth School of Business, 5807 S Woodlawn Ave, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States of America, che-lin.su@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): Baris Ata, Zeynep Aksin, Seyedmorteza Emadi

Year: 2012

Abstract: We estimate callers' reward and cost parameters from the data of calls made to a call center with delay announcement. We consider the setting in which the call center provider chooses the information level of the announcement messages. We compute the steady state equilibrium of the system under different information levels, and analyze changes in call center performance measures.
