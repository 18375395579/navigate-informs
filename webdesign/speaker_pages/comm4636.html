Title: Coordinated Scheduling of Production and Storage/Retrieval in the Steel Industry

Author: Feng Li, PhD, The Logistics Institute, Northeastern University, Shenyang, 110819, China, fengli055@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Lixin Tang

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk discusses a coordinated scheduling of production and storage/retrieval that commonly occurs in the production and warehousing operations in the steel industry. The problem can be classified as follows: 1) Coordinated scheduling of steelmaking-continuous casting production and slab storage; 2) Coordinated batching scheduling of hot rolling production and slab retrieval; 3) Coordinated batching scheduling of cold rolling production and coil storage/retrieval.
