Title: A Multiscale Model of High-frequency Trading

Author: Richard Sowers, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Urbana, IL, United States of America, r-sowers@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiangqian Meng, Andrei Kirilenko

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose and study a model of high-frequency trading. Our effort centers around a multiscale asymptotic analysis which depends on fluid and diffusive limits for order queues.
