Title: The Aisle Design Problem for Unit-Load Warehouses
Author: Kevin Gue, Associate Professor, Auburn University, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States, kevin.gue@auburn.edu
Coauthor(s): Russell Meller
Year: 2006
Abstract: We introduce and build models for the aisle design problem for unit-load warehouses, where the challenge is to construct picking and cross aisles in a way that minimizes expected retrieval time. Our results include a simple warehouse design that promises a 20 percent reduction in expected labor cost for warehouses of reasonable size.
