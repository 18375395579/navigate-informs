Title: Analyzing Fire Service Deployment in Los Angeles County
Author: Richard Church, Professor, University of California, Santa Barbara, 1832 Ellison Hall, Santa Barbara, CA, 93106-4060, United States of America, church@geog.ucsb.edu
Coauthor(s): WenWen Li
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper presents the use of a combination of approaches, including cybersearch, GIS, and optimization to analyze fire service deployment within the county of Los Angeles, covering all municipal jurisdictions. Station coverage is analyzed in terms of whether imporovements can by limited station relocation.
