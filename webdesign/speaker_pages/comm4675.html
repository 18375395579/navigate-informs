Title: New Results on Inventory Management of Perishables

Author: Ismail Civelek, icivelek@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Itir Karaesmen, Alan Scheller-Wolf

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a new model that subsumes many of the inventory issuance models in the literature on inventory management of perishables. We analyze properties of optimal replenishment policies and optimal issuance rules for this model.
