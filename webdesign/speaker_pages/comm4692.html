Title: A Facility Location Model for Remanufacturing Network System
Author: Qingli Da, School of Economics and Management, Southeast University, Si Pai Lou 2, Nanjing, JS, China, dqlseunj@126.com
Coauthor(s): Wen-ping Wang, Wei-da Chen, Hao Sun
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this paper, a remanufacturing network system with six types of facility to be located is constructed, in which both the forward and the reverse logistics are of two-level structure and all the logistics facilities are capacitated. A mixed-integer non-linear programming facility-location model of the remanufacturing network is then developed to minimize the total cost of the system. A numerical example is solved by means of Lingo 8.0 to show the application of the model.
