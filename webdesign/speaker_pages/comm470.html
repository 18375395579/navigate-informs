Title: Local and Connecting Traffic in Single and Multiple Airport Cities

Author: Cumhur Gelogullari, Operations Research Analyst, American Airlines, 4333 Amon Carter Boulevard MD 5358, Fort Worth, TX, 76155, United States, Cumhur.Gelogullari@aa.com

Coauthor(s): Tassio Carvalho

Year: 2006

Abstract: The presence of commercial service at multiple airports in a metro area is frequently due to capacity constraints at the main facility. We present a study on the impact of the presence of commercial service at multiple facilities on local and connecting traffic in large metro areas.
