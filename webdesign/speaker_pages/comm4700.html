Title: A Clustering Approach to Bid Optimization on Behalf of Advertisers

Author: MohammadHossein Bateni, Research Scientist, Google Inc., 76 Ninth Ave, New York, NY, 10011, United States of America, bateni@gmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the problem of maximizing the number of clicks subject to budget constraints for advertisers. Given bid landscapes that are typically provided by some ad-serving systems, we formulate the problem and optimize the profit given budget constraints. We consider bidding strategies that consist of no more than k different bids for all the keywords. We present a PTAS when k is constant and constant-factor approximation otherwise.
