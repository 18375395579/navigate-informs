Title: Two Dimensional Load Balancing

Author: Kangbok Lee, Research Associate Professor, Rutgers University, Business School, Piscataway, NJ, United States of America, kangblee@business.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Leung, Michael Pinedo

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a bi-criteria parallel machine scheduling problem in which the first objective is to minimize the makespan of the schedule and the second objective is to minimize the maximum machine cost. Since the problem is strongly NP-hard, we propose a fast heuristic and derive its worst-case performance bound.
