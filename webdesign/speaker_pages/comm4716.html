Title: Risk Taking over Networks: Insufficient Precautions

Author: Azarakhsh Malekian, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Ave, 32D-632, Cambridge, MA, 02138, United States of America, azarakhsh.malekian@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Asuman Ozdaglar, Daron Acemoglu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We introduce a theoretical model of the security investments in a network of interconnected agents and characterize the impact of network structure on equilibrium and optimal investments. We also show that when the attack location is endogenized, there is an additional incentive for overinvestment: greater investment by an agent shifts the attack to other parts of the network.
