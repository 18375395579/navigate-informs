Title: Design of Multi-period Credit based Pricing

Author: Mohammad Miralinaghi, Research Assistant, Purdue University, 2156 tortuga ln, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, smiralin@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this study, we assume that a congestion management authority allocates credits to travelers in multiple periods and specifies a deadline for their validity while enabling transfer to the next period free of charge. Also, we investigate the possibility of an alternative design to provide flexibility for the authority to control the market price of the credits.
