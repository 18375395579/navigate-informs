Title: The Co-2-plex Polytope

Author: Benjamin McClosky, Rice University, 6100 Main St. - MS 134, Houston, TX, 77030, United States, bjm4@rice.edu

Coauthor(s): Illya Hicks

Year: 2007

Abstract: 2-plexes are cohesive subgraphs which were introduced to relax the structure of cliques. A co-2-plex is the complement of a 2-plex and is therefore similar to a stable set. We discuss the co-2-plex analogue for certain properties of the stable set polytope.
