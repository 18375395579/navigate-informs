Title: Minimum Discrimination Information (MDI) for Confidentiality Protection in Tabular Data

Author: Lawrence Cox, lgc9@CDC.GOV

Year: 2006

Abstract: Cox et al. (2004) proposed a disclosure limitation method for tabular data that preserves analytical properties of original data--quality-preserving controlled tabular adjustment. Their approach replaces each sensitive cell with a safe value subject to linear constraints to preserve additivity and parameters such as mean, variance and covariance. We have implemented an alternative algorithm using Kullback-Leibler minimum discrimination information (MDI).
