Title: Capacity Decisions in a Food Court Environment
Author: Haiyan Wang, Olin School of Business, Washington University in St. Louis, PhD Campus Box 1133, Saint Louis, MO, 63130, United States, hwang27@wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Tava Olsen
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider two competitive food providers in a food court, where the customer arrival rate usually increases before the peak dinner time and then decreases. Customers have different valuations on the services of the two providers and are time sensitive. We employ a fluid model to analysis the equilibrium service rates of the two providers.
