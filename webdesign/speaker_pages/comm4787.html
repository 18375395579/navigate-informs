Title: The Managers Problem Under Uncertainty
Author: Anshul Sheopuri, PhD Student, Stern Business School, 44th West 4th Street, New York, NY, 10012, United States, asheopur@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Eitan Zemel
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study a single decision maker, single period problem which has wide applications in the study of crime, service level and inventory decisions. We study important properties of this problem and identify conditions for uniqueness of the solution. Finally, we extend the analysis to a non-linear case and study the finite horizon version of problem.
