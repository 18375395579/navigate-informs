Title: Optimizing the Allocation of Surgeries to ORs in an Open-Booking Scheduling System

Author: Brian Denton, Assistant Professor, Mayo Clinic College of Medicine, 200 First Street SW, Rochester, MN, 55906, United States, Denton.Brian@mayo.edu

Coauthor(s): Andrew Miller, Huschka Todd

Year: 2006

Abstract: Hospitals that operate according to open-booking policies allocate large numbers of procedures across many operating rooms each day. The allocation problem is analogous to a stochastic bin-packing problem, where the objective includes expected costs of overtime and fixed costs of opening ORs. We describe our model, solution methods, and numerical results based on real data.
