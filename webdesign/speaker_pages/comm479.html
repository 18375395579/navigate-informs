Title: Unconstraining Airline Sales Data using Choice Sets

Author: Ger Koole, Professor, VU University, De Boelelaan 1081a, Amsterdam, Netherlands, ger.koole@vu.nl

Coauthor(s): Alwin Haensel, Jeroen Erdman

Year: 2012

Abstract: Forecasting airline demand is a problem involving censored data because not all historic demand is observed and sometimes observed in different price classes because of the availability of tickets in different price classes. Forecasting the unconstrained sales is a problem that is crucial for airline revenue management. In this we present new methods based on maximum likelihoods and the expectation maximization algorithm. These methods are applied to real airline data.
