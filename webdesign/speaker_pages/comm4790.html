Title: A Simple Heuristic for Joint Inventory and Pricing Problems with Lead Time

Author: Yang Li, PhD Student, Duke University, 100 Fuqua Drive, Durham, NC, 27708, United States of America, yang.li2@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Kevin Shang, Fernando Bernstein

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study a joint inventory and pricing problem in a single-stage system with positive lead time. This problem is, in general, intractable due to its computational complexity. We develop a simple heuristic that resolves this issue. The heuristic first generates a pricing policy which depends on the initial inventory level. We then transform the joint problem into a standard inventory problem. This heuristic enables us to explore the impact of lead time on the joint decision.
