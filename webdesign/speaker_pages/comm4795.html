Title: Long Run Power Sector Response to CO2 Allowance Allocation Methods

Author: Benjamin Hobbs, The Johns Hopkins University, Dept. Geography & Env. Engineering, Baltimore, MD, United States, bhobbs@jhu.edu

Coauthor(s): J.-S. Pang, Jinye Zhao, Yihsu Chen

Year: 2006

Abstract: By basing the allocation of CO2 emissions allowance on investment decisions, carbon trading systems (including the EU) risk distorting investments. A long run capacity equilibrium problem is formulated subject to allocation rules, and is analyzed for existence of solutions. Simulations show a potential for large distortions.
