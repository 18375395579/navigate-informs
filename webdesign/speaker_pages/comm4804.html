Title: Optimal Lignocellulosic Biomass Feedstocks Allocation for Biofuel Production in California

Author: Chien-Wei Chen, graduate student, University of California, Institute of Transportation Studies, Davis, CA, 95776, United States of America, scwchen@ucdavis.edu

Coauthor(s): Yueyue Fan

Year: 2008

Abstract: A well designed Biofuel production system may alleviate greenhouse gas emission and energy security issues. In this paper, we propose an optimization model for allocation of multiple lignocellulosic biomass feedstock resources to satisfy long-term bioenergy demand in California. The impact of biofuel production and delivery on transportation infrastructure is also analyzed.
