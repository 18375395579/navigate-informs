Title: Revenue Management in Public Transportation

Author: Ban Kawas, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Road, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, bkawas@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Olivier Gallay

Year: 2013

Abstract: Public transportation demand forecasting is an integral part in setting the base price of travel tickets. In practice, it is a complex task as it involves a deep understanding of customers?travel behaviour and their sensitivity to tickets' prices. We propose an approach that tackles this challenge when managing revenues for a public transport operator. We derive a quadratic program that maximizes total revenues and explicitly addresses demand uncertainty. Numerical results are encouraging.
