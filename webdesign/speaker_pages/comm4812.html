Title: Traffic Management in a Dynamic Evacuation Network: Trade-off between Signal and Cross-eliminations

Author: Yue Liu, Department of Civil Engineering and Mechanics, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, Milwaukee, WI, United States of America, liu28@uwm.edu

Coauthor(s): Zhenke Luo

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper develops a bi-level model to best trade-off between signal control and uninterrupted flow strategies for traffic management in a dynamic evacuation network. The upper level searches for the best distribution strategies with the objectives of minimizing the total evacuation clearance time. The lower level problem handles routing of evacuation traffic with the cell transmission model adopted to mathematically represent dynamic traffic flow evolution and queuing in an evacuation network.
