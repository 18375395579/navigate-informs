Title: Efficient, Fair, and Group Strategy-proof (Re)Allocation under Network Constraints

Author: Karol Szwagrzak, Graduate Student, University of Rochester, 280 Hutchison Road, Box 270156, Rochester, NY, 14627, United States of America, karol.s.flores@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: We examine the strategy-proof (re)allocation of commodities among a group of agents in a network. Agent can receive an amount of a commodity only if the network contains an arc connecting her to it. The relevant fairness notion in reallocation problems is ``fair net trades [Schmeidler, D. and K. Vind. 1972. Fair net trades. Econometrica, 40, 637-642]. When a single commodity is to be reallocated, we identify the only strategy-proof Pareto-efficient allocation rule satisfying fair net trades.
