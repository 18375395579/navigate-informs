Title: Influential Multi-armed Bandits

Author: Kemal Gursoy, Associate Professor, Kean University, Willis Hall, 305-G, 1000 Morris Avenue, Union, NJ, 07083, United States, kgursoy@kean.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: A multi-armed bandit is a sequential decision making model, utilized to select the best one among many independent stochastic processes [Gittins and Jones, 1972]. In this work, an approach to express influences between the constituent stochastic processes of multi-armed bandits was considered.
