Title: Optimal Portfolio with Regime Switching and Transaction Costs
Author: Jussi Keppo, Assistant Professor, University of Michigan, College of Engineering, 1205 Beal Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States, keppo@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Erhan Bayraktar
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider the portfolio and consumption optimization problem when the risky asset price follows a regime switching process and when the agent faces transaction costs. The optimal solution is compared with the cases without the transaction costs and/or regime switching.
