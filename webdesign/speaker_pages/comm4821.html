Title: Modeling Containerized Cargo Shipments Between Ocean Port and Handling Facilities
Author: Anne Goodchild, Assistant Professor, University of Washington, 121E More Hall, Box 352700, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States, annegood@u.washington.edu
Coauthor(s): Kaori Fujisawa, Eric Jessup
Year: 2007
Abstract: This research presents a model of container freight movement between ocean ports and handling facilities. We estimate shipper behavior by estimating logistics costs for a set of transportation choices based on shipper volume and value. The model is a practical tool, primarily for policy makers, to understand the impact of land value changes, transportation capacity, and other inputs on logistics strategies and therefore freight flows.
