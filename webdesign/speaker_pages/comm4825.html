Title: On Track?Solving the Strategic Railway Crew Scheduling at Deutsche Bahn (Part II)
Author: Simon Schäfer, University of Cologne, Department of Information Systems, and Operations Research (WINFORS), Cologne, 50969, Germany, simon.schaefer@uni-koeln.de
Coauthor(s): Dominik Malcherek, Ulrich Derigs
Year: 2008
Abstract: Solving large-scale crew scheduling problems is extremely difficult. We report on a joint project with Deutsche Bahn, where we have developed a MIP-crew scheduling model and a column generation based optimization system to perform scenario analyses on future timetables with respect to alternative crew base configurations and service levels.
