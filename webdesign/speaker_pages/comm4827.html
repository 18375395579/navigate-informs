Title: Arbitrary Throughput and Complexity Tradeoffs in Presence of Delay Guarantees in Wireless Networks

Author: Saswati Sarkar, Associate Professor, University of Pennsylvania, 200 S. 33rd St., Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, swati@seas.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Saikat Ray

Year: 2008

Abstract: We develop a class of parameterized policies that given any positive constant a that is between 0 and 1, approximates the maximum throughput region in a large class of wireless networks within a factor of a, using a computation time that depends only on the maximum node degree and a. These policies also attain asymptotically optimal delays in these networks.
