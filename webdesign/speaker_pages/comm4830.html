Title: Accelerated Burn-in and Condition-based Maintenance for N-subpopulations Subject to Stochastic Degradation

Author: Yisha Xiang, Sun Yat-Sen University, Guangzhou, China, xiangysh@mail.sysu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): David Coit, Qianmei Feng

Year: 2013

Abstract: For some manufacturing applications, populations of manufactured components can consist of several sub-populations. A new model is developed to determine accelerated burn-in and condition-based maintenance policies for heterogeneous populations. We also compare the proposed joint accelerated burn-in and condition-based maintenance policy with two benchmark policies. Sensitivity analysis is performed to investigate the value of joint accelerated burn-in and condition-based maintenance policy.
