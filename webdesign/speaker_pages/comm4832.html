Title: Multi-period Shipment Consolidation Strategy

Author: Wooseung Jang, Professor, University of Missouri, E3437 Lafferre Hall, Columbia, MO, 65211, United States of America, JangW@missouri.edu

Coauthor(s): Na Deng

Year: 2013

Abstract: Recent transportation activities have significantly changed the design and operation of international logistics networks. In particular, shipment consolidation has become a key factor that affects the cost and service quality of logistics operation. In this work, we consider a multi-period multi-commodity consolidation problem and propose math-programming based solution approaches.
