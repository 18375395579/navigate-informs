Title: Large Graph Limits for Configurational Constructions of Random Networks - Applications to SIR Epidemy
Author: Pascal Moyal, Universit?de Technologie de Compiègne, Rue Roger Couttolenc, CS 60319, Compiègne, 60203, France, pascal.moyal@utc.fr
Year: 2013
Abstract: We present two constructions of random networks inspired by the configuration model: the course of a SIR epidemy on a human population, and the processing of a CSMA algortithm on a set of interacting nodes. In both cases, we construct, and represent the population by partially constructed random graphs, described in turn by point measure-valued processes. We then obtain fluid limits for these processes under large graph limit.
