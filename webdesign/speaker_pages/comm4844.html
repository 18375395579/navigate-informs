Title: Plug-in Electric Vehicular Flows in Equilibrium Networks
Author: Chi Xie, Research Associate, The University of Texas at Austin, 1616 Guadalupe St, UTA 4.403, Austin, TX, 78701, United States of America, chi.xie@mail.utexas.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: This presentation discusses a set of new network equilibrium models and methods that incorporate alternative travel restrictions and cost compositions of battery electric vehicles and plug-in hybrid electric vehicles into the traditional urban transportation network modeling paradigm.
