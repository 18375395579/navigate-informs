Title: Order Policies for a Perishable Product with Age-Dependent Demand
Author: Amar Sapra, University of Florida, Industrial and System Eng. Department, Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, sapra@ise.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Li-Ming Chen
Year: 2007
Abstract: Motivated by recent advances in bakery industry that have increased shelf-life, we consider a single stage periodic review inventory system for a perishable product whose demand depends on age. We characterize the form of the optimal ordering policy.
