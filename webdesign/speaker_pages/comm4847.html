Title: Optimal Aviation Security Screening Strategies with Dynamic Passenger Risk Updates
Author: Sheldon Jacobson, Professor, University of Illinois, 201 N. Goodwin Avenue (MC258), urbana, IL, 61821, United States of America, shj@uiuc.edu
Coauthor(s): Adrian Lee, Alexander Nikoleav
Year: 2008
Abstract: Multistage Sequential Passenger Screening Problem is formulated for aviation security systems, with passenger risks initialized upon arrival and updated as passengers pass through multiple screening stages. Optimal screening policies are obtained using sequential assignment theory. Monte Carlo simulation-based heuristics are developed and compared with other algorithms for a two-stage MSPSP.
