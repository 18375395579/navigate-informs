Title: Freight Transportation In Turkish State Railways (TCDD) High-Speed Train System
Author: Alp Ertem, Cankaya University, Eskisehir Yolu 29.km Yenimahalle, Ankara, 06810, Turkey, alpertem@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Merve Keskin
Year: 2013
Abstract: High speed trains (HSTs) are faster than trucks and cheaper than airlines for freight transportation. Freight is added to the existing HST system of Turkey which is currently used only for passengers. A scheduling model is constructed to observe the effects of freight transportation in two cases: adding separate freight trains to the system and using the same trains with passengers for freight transportation. Several factors were determined to choose between two cases at certain conditions.
