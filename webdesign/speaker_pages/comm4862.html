Title: New Conditions and Proofs for (s, S) Policies
Author: Liming Liu, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, LGT, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong, HK, China, lgtliulm@polyu.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): Xiaolan Xie
Year: 2007
Abstract: With a proposed DXING-convexity, we show by K-convexity that for a wide range of demand distributions, the (st, St) policies or the modified (st, St) policies are optimal for finite-horizon periodic-review inventory systems with both backlogging and lost sales, and with both proportional and lump-sum shortage penalty costs.
