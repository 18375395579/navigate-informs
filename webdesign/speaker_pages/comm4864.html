Title: An Analytical Approach to Solving Load Balancing Problem in Cloud Computing

Author: Emrah Zarifoglu, Optimization Scientist, IBM, 1 Franklin Parkway, Building 910, San Mateo, CA, 94402, United States of America, emrah.zarifoglu@utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Ilyas Iyoob

Year: 2012

Abstract: The dynamic cost structure increases the importance of intelligent provisioning and management in cloud computing. We develop an optimization model to decide high-mark utilization and low-mark utilization values to turn on and off VMs while minimizing provisioning and maintenance costs. Solving the same optimization problem at different points in time provides us with a dynamic VM load balancing policy. The robustness of the policy relies on the agility of periodical model corrections.
