Title: Optimal Dynamic Pricing of Account-Based Services with Customer Switching Costs
Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States, gjv1@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Qian Liu
Year: 2006
Abstract: We look at a model of pricing account-based services, such as telecome service and financial services, in which switching costs play a critical role. We show the optimal pricing strategy can be characterized simply in terms of a critical account level. We then analyze a duopoloy version of the model.
