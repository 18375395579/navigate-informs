Title: Cost Allocation of Capacity Investment Game
Author: Xin Chen, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Ave., Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, xinchen@uiuc.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Consider a set of manufacturers, which need to build capacities and then make productions to satisfy their demand. By taking advantage of the economies of scale of capacity investment, the manufacturers may build joint capacity and make joint productions to reduce their operating costs. Under rather general assumptions on the capacity investment cost, we show that the resulting capacity investment game has a nonempty core.
