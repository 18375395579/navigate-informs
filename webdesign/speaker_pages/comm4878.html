Title: Dynamic Simulation Assignment Model for Intermodal Multi Product Freight Transportation

Author: Hani Mahmassani, University of Maryland, 3128 J. H. Kim Building, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, masmah@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Kuilin Zhang, Jing Dong, Chung-Cheng Lu

Year: 2006

Abstract: A simulation-assignment model with joint mode and route choice is developed for multi-product freight shipments. Terminal operations are modeled as bulk queueing systems. Intermodal route choice is addressed by solving a multi-product intermodal shortest path problem. The model is applied to a trans-European corridor linking the Nordic countries to Southeastern Europe.
