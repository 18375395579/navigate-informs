Title: The Directed Pairs Connection Problem
Author: Guy Kortsarz, Rutgers University, Buisness and science building, Pen and T, Camden, NJ, United States of America, guyk@camden.rutgers.edu
Coauthor(s): Zeev Nutov, Moran Feldman
Year: 2008
Abstract: We deal with the problem of connecting a collection of pairs in a directed graph at minimum cost. More generally given a number k the goal is to connect at least k of the pairs at minimum cost. We give improved results for both problems.
