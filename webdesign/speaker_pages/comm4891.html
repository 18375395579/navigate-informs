Title: The Multi-product Newsvendor Problem with Customer Choice

Author: Amr Farahat, Washington University-St. Louis, 1 Brookings Drive, Campus Box 1133, St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States of America, farahat@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Joonkyum Lee

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the stocking problem facing a newsvendor offering multiple products where customer choice depends on the set of available products at the time of purchase. We present a tractable approach, guaranteed to yield upper bounds on the optimal value, and numerical results that outperform current benchmarks.
