Title: Convex Extensions, Inclusion Certificates and Disjunctive Programming
Author: Mohit Tawarmalani, Assistant Professor, Purdue University, School of Management, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, mtawarma@purdue.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: We define inclusion certificate based on convex multipliers required to assert the inclusion of a point in the convex hull of a disjunctive set. The inclusion certificates are shown to be related to the product-factors used in relaxation schemes such as Lift-and-Project and the Reformulation Linearization Techniques. Insights into relaxation hierarchies are developed.
