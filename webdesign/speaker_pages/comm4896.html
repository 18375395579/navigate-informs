Title: Services Costing in a Complex Environment: A Hierarchical Dichotomy
Author: Toby Lehman, Research Staff Member, IBM Research, 650 Harry Road, San Jose, CA, 95120, United States of America, toby@almaden.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Greg Eyres
Year: 2008
Abstract: Costing requires adopting one of two opposing modeling methodologies: tops-down or bottoms-up. Tops-down provides a quick turn-around for estimates, but bottoms-up provides a more accuracy. The ideal solution is to take the benefits of each method without incurring any of the problems. We present a mechanism that balances the two approaches and derives benefits from both.
