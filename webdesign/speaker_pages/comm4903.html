Title: Structural Break Estimation for Non-Stationary Time Series Signals

Author: Thomas Lee, Associate Professor, Colorado State University, Department of Statistics, Fort Collins, CO, 80523, United States, thomas.cm.lee@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Gabriel Rodriguez-Yam, Richard A. Davis

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper considers the problem of detecting break points for a broad class of non-stationary time series models. In this formulation, the number and locations of the break points are assumed unknown. Each piece is assumed to be stationary and can be modeled from a class of parametric time series models. The minimum description length is used as a criterion for estimating the number of break points, the location of break points, and the parametric model in each segment.
