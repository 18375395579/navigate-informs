Title: Simple and Asymptotically Optimal Policies for Capacitated Serial Systems with High Service Levels

Author: Ganesh Janakiraman, N.Y.U., gjanakir@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Woonghee Tim Huh, Mahesh Nagarajan

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study a multi-echelon inventory system in series with capacity constraints at all stages. The optimal policy for this system is unknown. We propose three intuitive, echelon order-up-to policies and show that these policies are asymptotically optimal as the backorder cost parameter grows infinitely large.
