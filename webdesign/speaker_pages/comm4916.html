Title: Tracking Error Models for Multiple Benchmarks

Author: Zhichao Zheng, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, 50 Stamford Road, Singapore, 178899, Singapore, danielzheng@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Chung Piaw Teo, Karthik Natarajan, Yunchao Xu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a new multiple-benchmark tracking-error model for portfolio selection to address the agency issue. The tracking error is defined as the difference between the portfolio’s return and the highest return from a set of benchmarks. We obtain the closed-form solution by linking our model to Stein's Identity. Extensive numerical tests show that our portfolio has consistent higher net Sharpe ratio and lower turnover rate than its benchmarks, in particular, the 1/n portfolio.
