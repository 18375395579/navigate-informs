Title: Weakly Cooperative Systems
Author: David Jeffcoat, Principal Engineer, Air Force Research Lab, 101 W. Eglin Blvd, Eglin AFB, FL, 32542, United States of America, david.jeffcoat@eglin.af.mil
Coauthor(s): Pavlo Krokhmal
Year: 2008
Abstract: We introduce a concept of weakly cooperative systems that can represent a wide range of multi-agent systems. Our mathematical model is the Markov process. We present a general framework and illustrate it on a number of search and engage systems. Our analysis shows that the impact of cooperation on overall system performance is inherently bounded. Finally, we introduce a measure of cooperation that enables one to quantify the “degree of cooperativeness?in a given multi-agent system.
