Title: Flight Crew Economic Analysis at Boeing

Author: Scott Ryan, The Boeing Company, PO Box 3707, MC 21-33, Seattle, WA, 98124-2207, United States, scott.b.ryan@boeing.com

Coauthor(s): Roman Fresnedo, Sharon Arroyo, Dan Abraham

Year: 2007

Abstract: Boeing acquires and develops methods and tools to estimate the value of flight deck commonality for business case analysis. Current focus is on the development of models to efficiently estimate the number of crew needed to fly a schedule, approximate the cost of crew ladders (or how crew are promoted through the ranks), and estimate the benefits (as related to crew) of mixed fleet flying under irregular operations. An overview of the activities in these three areas will be given.
