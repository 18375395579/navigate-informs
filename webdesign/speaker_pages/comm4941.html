Title: Optimal Hedging Under Convex Risk Measures and Application to Shortfall Risk

Author: Antoine Toussaint, Princeton University, ORFE, Princeton, NJ, United States, atoussai@Princeton.EDU

Coauthor(s): Ronnie Sircar

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study the hedging of contracts in an incomplete financial model. The criteria for optimal hedging is determined by a suitable convex risk measure. Under some assumptions on the set of trading strategies, we prove the existence of optimal hedging strategies and we give an expression for the minimum capital requirement. Applied to the framework of shortfall risk, it provides a bridge to minimal variance pricing.
