Title: Streamlining Reasoning for Solution Finding and Counting

Author: Carla Gomes, Associate Professor, Cornell University, 4130 Upson Hall, Ithaca, NY, United States, gomes@cs.cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Ashish Sabharwal, Bart Selman, Meinolf Sellmann

Year: 2006

Abstract: We will introduce streamlining reasoning, a technique for effective solution finding and solution counting for hard combinatorial problems.
