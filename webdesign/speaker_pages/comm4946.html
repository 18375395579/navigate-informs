Title: Robust Gate Assignment to Reduce Tarmac Waiting Time

Author: Amy Cohn, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, United States of America, amycohn@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Ryan Chen

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the problem of assigning flights to gates at an airport, with the goal of reducing delay associated with an inbound flight waiting on the tarmac because its gate is still occupied by the preceding outbound flight. This can happen because the outbound flight is late leaving, the inbound flight is early arriving, or both. We develop MIP models to assign flights to gates so as to recognize the inherent stochasticity of the system, and provide empirical analysis based on these models.
