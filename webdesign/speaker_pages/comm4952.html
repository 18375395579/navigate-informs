Title: Allocation of Trucking Requirements to Carriers

Author: Xu Yang, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Louisville, 305, JB Speed Bldg, Louisville, KY, 40292, United States of America, x0yang07@louisville.edu

Coauthor(s): Sunderesh Heragu, Gerald Evans

Year: 2007

Abstract: Several linear programming models were formulated to aid a leading multinational company in the allocation of their trucking requirements among various carriers. Each of the models determined the number of trips assigned to each carrier on each shipping lane in order to minimize total shipping costs.
