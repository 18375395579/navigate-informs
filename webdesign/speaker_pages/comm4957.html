Title: Improving Network Connectivity in Ad-Hoc Networks Using Intelligence Agents

Author: Abdullah Konak, Professor of Information Sciences and Technology, Penn State Berks, Tulpehocken Road, P.O. Box 7009, Reading, PA, 19610, United States of America, konak@psu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper presents a decentralized approach to maintain the connectivity of a MANET using autonomous, intelligent agents that collaborate. The presentation introduces several network metrics, including ones form the social network analysis, to maintain the connectivity of mobile nodes. Computational results are presented to demonstrate the effect of various metrics on the global network connectivity.
