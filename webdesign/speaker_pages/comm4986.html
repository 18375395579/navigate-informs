Title: Optimal Fuel Switching and Contract Decisions in Electricity Generation

Author: Michael Rice, Colorado School of Mines, 816 15th St, Golden, CO, 80401, United States of America, mrice@mines.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: The shale gas boom and proposed plant emissions regulations could accelerate a switch from coal to gas in electricity generation portfolios. We analyze optimal fuel contracts for power plants facing price and regulatory uncertainty using stochastic programming.
