Title: A New Facility Layout Model with Congestion and Rerouting

Author: Rakesh Nagi, Professor and Chair, University at Buffalo, 438 Bell Hall, Department of Industrial and Systems Eng, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, nagi@buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Rajan Batta, Min Zhang

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a facility layout model that explicitly considers flow congestion and alternate path routing to simultaneously minimize total expected travel time. We demonstrate that our model is capable of generating superior layouts to QAP with a 10% improvement in true material handling (MH) time if only routing is considered and a 14% improvement in true MH time if both layout and routing are designed simultaneously.
