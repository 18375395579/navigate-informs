Title: Strategic Redeployment of Emergency Helicopters based on Weather and Visibility

Author: Rajan Batta, Professor, Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering, University at Buffalo (SUNY), 420 Bell Hall, Buffalo, NY, 14260, United States of America, batta@eng.buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Elif Tokar-Erdermir

Year: 2008

Abstract: Emergency helicopters may not be dispatched under bad weather/visibility conditions, and night dispatch requires larger helipads. We study the problem of locating ambulances and emergency helicopters under changing weather/visibility conditions, and whether to redeploy the helicopters at different states of the system. We consider three coverage types: only ground, only air, and joint ground-air. Objective is to maximize accident coverage at each state without exceeding relocation cost limit.
