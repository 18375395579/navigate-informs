Title: Accurate Lead Time Demand Modeling in Coordinated Supply Chains

Author: Barry Cobb, Virginia Military Institute, 334 Scott Shipp Hall, Economics and Business, Lexington, VA, 24450, United States of America, cobbbr@vmi.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Johnson

Year: 2013

Abstract: Under uncertain demand, a mixture distribution approach can be used to model lead time demand in a two-level supply chain. Using this technique, incentives that allow the members to divide the cost savings resulting from coordinating their inventory policies can be determined without assuming normally distributed demand.
