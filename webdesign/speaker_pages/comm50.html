Title: Fighting Market Power by Auctioning Generation: How Much, How Often? A System Dynamics Approach

Author: Juan Jose Sanchez, IIT, Comillas University, Santa Cruz de Marcenado, 26, Madrid, Ma, 28015, Spain, JuanJose.Sanchez@iit.upcomillas.es

Coauthor(s): Julian Barquin, Efraim Centeno

Year: 2007

Abstract: The effectiveness of auctions of long-term contracts in mitigating market power is related both to the amount and to the tenor of the contracts, but there is not a consensus on the optimal figures and on the long-term effects. This paper proposes to address these issues by means of a dynamic model. In particular, a detailed realistic model of the power system, conjectured price responses computation, and forward trading with price formation based on expected spot plus risk premium are considered
