Title: Strategic Price Bundling in Competitive Markets: Complements, Substitutes, and Correlated Values

Author: Cary Deck, University of Arkansas, 402 WCOB, Fayetteville, AR, 72701, United States, CDeck@walton.uark.edu

Coauthor(s): John Aloysius

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study how customer product valuation affects strategic mixed bundle pricing in competitive markets where a fraction of buyers comparison shop. The model allows for jointly distributed values where the super- or sub-additivity of the bundle (degree of substitutability or complementarity) is parameterized. Behavioral Study 1 manipulates bundle additivity and Study 2 varies value correlation. The impact of control variables on market outcomes is measured and the implications are discussed.
