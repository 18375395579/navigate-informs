Title: Hide or Show: Can eTailers Benefit from Providing More Information about Their Products?

Author: Han Zhang, Associate Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree Street, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, Han.Zhang@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Peng Mei, Minxue Huang

Year: 2008

Abstract: Based on Economics of Information (EOI) theory and Signal theory, this research combines two competitive theories to explore how e-Tailers¡¯ information provision about their products influences consumers¡¯ cognition and products¡¯ price premium in electronic markets. The results indicate that providing more information, regardless of product information (e.g., reviews of products) or price information (e.g., competitor price information), all can lead to higher price premium.
