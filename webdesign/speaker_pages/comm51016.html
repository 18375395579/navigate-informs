Title: The Inventory Billboard Effect on Information Sharing in Competing Supply Chains

Author: Zhengping Wu, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, 50 Stamford Road, Singapore, Singapore, zpwu@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Rong Li, Xin Wei

Year: 2012

Abstract: The inventory billboard effect in operations management indicates that the increasing shelf-space allocated to a product has a positive effect on the product demand. This paper studies the billboard effect on the vertical information sharing strategy of competing supply chains in an environment with production diseconomies. We analyze how equilibrium information sharing strategy, wholesale price and retail quantity are affected by the billboard effect coefficient.
