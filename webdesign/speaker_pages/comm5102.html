Title: Firm Competition Under Supply Uncertainty
Author: Fuqiang Zhang, Assistant Professor, University of California, Irvine, CA, United States, fzhang@uci.edu
Coauthor(s): Shaoxuan Liu, Rick So
Year: 2007
Abstract: We use a game-theoretical framework to study the impact of supply uncertainty on the joint marketing and inventory decisions between two competing retailers. We derive some analytical results that provide managerial insights on the equilibrium behavior of these joint decisions and the corresponding profitability of the two retailers
