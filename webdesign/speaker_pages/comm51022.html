Title: Orchestrating Procurement Service in Third-Party Logistics
Author: Gangshu Cai, Santa Clara University, OMIS Department, Santa Clara, CA, 95053, United States of America, gcai@scu.edu
Coauthor(s): Jeannette Song, Xiangfeng Chen
Year: 2013
Abstract: In the innovative procurement service, a 3PL firm can actually escalate the whole supply chain performance by orchestrating both procurement process and shipping for the manufacturer and the retailer. This paper extends the classic newsvendor model to include a manufacturer, a retailer, and a 3PL firm. Our analysis indicates that there exists a Pareto zone where all firms benefit from the procurement service compared with the traditional pure logistics service.
