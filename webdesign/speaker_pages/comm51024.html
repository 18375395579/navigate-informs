Title: Social Media Blog Sentiments and Brand Performances: Competitive and Asymmetric Effects

Author: Jie Zhang, University of Texas at Arlington, 701 S West St. #19437, Arlington, TX, 76019, United States of America, jiezhang@uta.edu

Coauthor(s): Xueming Luo, Bin Gu, Chiwei Phang

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this study, we investigate the competitive effects of social media on consumer sentiments and brand performances of firms operating in the same industry. We assembled a novel, comprehensive dataset that combined the extent of social media presence of competing brands in the personal computer industry; and individual consumers?evaluations of the brands collected at a daily level. Our analyses of this unique dataset unveil three key insights.
