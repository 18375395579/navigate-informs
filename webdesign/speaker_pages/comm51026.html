Title: Multi-objective Supply Chain Scheduling
Author: Tadeusz Sawik, Professor, AGH University of Science & Technology, Dept of Oper. Res. & Inform. Techn., Al.Mickiewicza 30, Krakow, PL, 30059, Poland, ghsawik@cyf-kr.edu.pl
Year: 2008
Abstract: A hierarchy of mixed integer programs is proposed for a multi-objective supply chain scheduling. The supply chain consists of multiple suppliers of parts and one producer. Given a set of orders, the objective is to select supplier of parts for each order, determine manufacturing and supply schedules for suppliers and assembly schedule for producer to reach a high customer service level at low cost.
