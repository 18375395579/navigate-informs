Title: Investigating Strategic Customer Behavior through an Interactive Supply Chain Game

Author: Pelin Pekgun, Assistant Professor, University of South Carolina, Moore School of Business, 1705 College Street, Columbia, SC, 29208, United States of America, Pelin.Pekgun@moore.sc.edu

Coauthor(s): Pinar Keskinocak, Tosanwunmi Maku, Mani Janakiram

Year: 2012

Abstract: We investigate the strategic forecasting and ordering behavior of customers through an interactive supply chain game with the motivation of continuously improving supply chain metrics and planning for various scenarios. We discuss the effectiveness of different inventory allocation mechanisms in reducing forecast inflation based on our insights gained from experiments conducted on over 200 human subjects.
