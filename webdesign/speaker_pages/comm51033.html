Title: Market Uncertainty and Innovation Incentives in Pharmaceutical R&D

Author: John Cavallaro, PhD Candidate, Management Science & Engineering, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, john.cavallaro@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Stefanos Zenios

Year: 2006

Abstract: The newly launched Medicare Part D has renewed both interest in pharmaceutical pricing policies and concerns about innovation incentives. This work quantifies pharmaceutical R&D risk, and then investigates how price reductions affect R&D spending and innovation. Finally, it explores how policy levers can reduce expenditures while maintaining innovation incentives.
