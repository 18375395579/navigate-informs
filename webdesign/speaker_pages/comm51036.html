Title: Cascade Reuse Over Product Life Cycle

Author: Kate Li, Doctoral Student, Penn State University, 419A Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States, jzl120@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Gilvan Souza, Daniel Guide

Year: 2006

Abstract: We explore the options for product reuse over the life cycle of a product by taking into account volume, quality and timing of product returns, technology diffusion, and market potential. We develop a model focused on profit maximization for reverse supply chains based on the system of cascade reuse.
