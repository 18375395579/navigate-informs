Title: Timining of Service Investments under Competition and Demand Uncertainty
Author: Olga Perdikaki, Assistant Professor, Mays Business School at Texas A&M University, 320 Wehner Building, College Station, TX, 77843-4217, United States of America, operdikaki@mays.tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Jayashankar M. Swaminathan, Dimitris Kostamis
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study how competition and demand uncertainty affect firms' timing of service level decisions relative to demand realization. We model a symmetric duopoly in which firms compete on price and service levels and analytically characterize the Nash equilibrium in the timing of service level decisions.
