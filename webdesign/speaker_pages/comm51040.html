Title: Dynamic Customer Base Management: Acquisition, Retention and Risk Diversification
Author: Lu Huang, Duke University, 215 Butler Court, Chapel Hill, NC, 27514, United States of America, lu.huang2@duke.edu
Coauthor(s): Jeannette Song
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a manufacturer with finite capacity serving business customers in a dynamic environment. The customers differ in business volume, profit margin, loyalty and disruption risk profiles. We characterize the optimal customer portfolio and capacity rationing policy for the manufacturer. Some of the findings are counter-intuitive.
