Title: Procurement Auctions with Error-Prone RFQs

Author: Yan Yin, HEC Paris, 1 rue de la Libération, Jouy-en-Josas, France, yin@hec.fr

Coauthor(s): Damian Beil, Hyun-Soo Ahn

Year: 2013

Abstract: Many requests for quotes (RFQs) contain errors that will trigger re-design (and windfall supplier profits) after production has begun. We study how the buyer’s payment changes in the error rate, supplier’s design knowledge, and competition intensity. Surprisingly, we find that a higher error rate can sometimes shrink the buyer’s total payment by mitigating supplier windfall profits. We also show pre-paying re-design profits can be an alternative strategy to stem windfall profits.
