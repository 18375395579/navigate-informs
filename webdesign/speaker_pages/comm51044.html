Title: A Two-echelon Inventory System when Retailers are Jointly Replenished: Analysis and Insights
Author: Banu Yuksel Ozkaya, Hacettepe University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, byuksel@hacettepe.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Emre Berk, Ulku Gurler
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study a single-item, two-echelon inventory system with one warehouse and multi- retailers in the presence of replenishment economies at the lower echelon. We analyze the system when the retailers are jointly replenished under policies within the Inventory Position Regenerative Ordering Policy class. We address a number of questions such as impact of warehouse function as a cross-dock, impact of policy choice on intra-chain dynamics, impact of policies on distribution of costs across echelons.
