Title: Innovative Pricing Strategies Under Strategic Consumer Behavior

Author: Yossi Aviv, Professor, Washington University, The Olin School of Business, Campus Box 1133, 1 Brookings Dr., St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States, aviv@olin.wustl.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In recent years, there has been an increasing interest in using OR models to explore strategic consumer behavior in the context of revenue management. In this talk, we will discuss ways in which sellers can adopt creative pricing schemes to optimally price products in the face of strategic consumer behavior. Such innovative pricing schemes should be tailored to discourage strategic waiting for price discounts, but should not harm the sellers?ability to segment customers.
