Title: Strategies for Profiting from Technologies with Weak Patent Rights
Author: Leonardo Santiago, Federal University of Minas Gerais, Escola de Engenharia da UFMG, Av. Antonio Carlos, 6627. Pampulha, Belo Horizonte, MG, 31270-901, Brazil, lsantiago@ufmg.br
Coauthor(s): Henrique Rocha
Year: 2012
Abstract: We investigate a set of strategies that companies adopt in order to profit from innovations. The strategies depend not only on inherent features of the innovation at stake but also on the incentives potential competitors have to enter in the target market. Our main focus is on technologies characterized by weak patent rights.
