Title: Retail Pricing Strategy in Competitive Market

Author: Xingchu Liu, VP, Data Analytics and Research, Blacklocus, Home Depot Innovation Lab, 804 Congress Ave, Austin, TX, 78701, United States of America, xingchu@blacklocus.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Retail pricing becomes more competitive as online services bring more transparency to prices and product availability. In this paper, we investigate retailer's pricing strategies and their impact.
