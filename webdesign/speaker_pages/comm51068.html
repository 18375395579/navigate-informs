Title: Resource Allocation for a New Product Introduction: Joint Facility Location and Marketing Strategies
Author: Vahideh Sadat Abedi, PHD Candidate, University of Toronto, 105 St. George Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 3E6, Canada, VahidehSadat.Abedi07@Rotman.Utoronto.Ca
Coauthor(s): Oded Berman, Dmitry Krass
Year: 2012
Abstract: We analyze the problem of joint design of the network of retail facilities and marketing strategies of a firm introducing an innovative product or service to a network of markets. After introducing a framework for the joint study of these decisions, we study the effect of different types of marketing media and their substitutive behaviour. We also investigate allocation of resources of the firm between different types of media and opening facilities.
