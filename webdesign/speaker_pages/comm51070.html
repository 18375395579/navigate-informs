Title: Revenue Management by Sequential Screening
Author: Mustafa Akan, Ph.D Student, Kellogg School of Management, Managerial Economics & Decision Sciences, Leverone 581, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States, m-akan@kellogg.northwestern.edu
Coauthor(s): Baris Ata, James Dana
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a dynamic model of revenue management, where the consumers are strategic, i.e., forward looking, and learn their valuations over time. A system manager strives to maximize her profits by sequentially screening consumers. Using a mechanism design approach, we characterize the optimal mechanism as a refund contract.
