Title: The Role of Supply Chain Financing beyond Financing
Author: S. Alex Yang, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Sussex Place, London, NA, United Kingdom, sayang@london.edu
Coauthor(s): Wenhui Zhao
Year: 2013
Abstract: Supply chain financing, or reverse factoring, is a method that allows the supplier to receive cash from a bank on invoices validated by the buyer. In this talk, we study how supply chain financing can be used as a tool to balance bargaining powers between different supply chain parties and hence potentially improve supply chain efficiency.
