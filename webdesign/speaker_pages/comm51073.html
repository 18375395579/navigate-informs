Title: A Distribution Game Model with Reciprocity Behavior and its Applications
Author: Qinglong Gou, Dr., University of Science and Technology of China, School of Management, USTC, Jinzhai Road 96#, Hefei, AH, 230026, China, tslg@ustc.edu.cn
Coauthor(s): Yan Zhang, Juan Li
Year: 2013
Abstract: We propose a distribution game framework with consideration of the reciprocity behavior of human being. We use it to explain the Ultimatum game. We also use it to investigate the pricing decisions in a supply chain. Analysis shows the impact of a retailer’s reciprocity behavior on the supply chain decisions.
