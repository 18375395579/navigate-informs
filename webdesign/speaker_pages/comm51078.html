Title: On the Value of Commitment and Availability Guarantees when Selling to Strategic Consumer

Author: Fuqiang Zhang, Assistant Professor, Washington University, Olin Business School, St. Louis, MO, United States of America, FZhang22@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Xuanming Su

Year: 2008

Abstract: The possibility of stockouts discourages patronage when consumers incur a sunk cost before visiting the store. We formulate a rational expectations game to model this situation. We find that commitment and availability guarantees are useful strategies that can increase the seller's profits.
