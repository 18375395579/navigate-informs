Title: Flexible-Discount Pricing for Computing Utilities

Author: Giuseppe Paleologo, IBM Research, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States, gappy@us.ibm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: The initial capacity investment decision is critical to the success of a new computing utility offering. The problem of capacity allocation under a linear pricing contract resembles that of a newsvendor problem. I introduce a new pricing schedule which attains full information revelation, and results in the highest possible utilitarian welfare for the system.
