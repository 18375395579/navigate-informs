Title: Optimal Suppy Diversification Under General Supply Risks

Author: Nan Yang, Assistant Professor of Operations Management, Cornell University, 310 Sage Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, ny118@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Awi Federgruen

Year: 2006

Abstract: A firm needs to cover uncertain demand for an item by procuring supplies before the sales season from multiple suppliers with random yields. The suppliers differ in yield distributions, costs and capacity levels. The model determines which potential suppliers are to be retained and what size order is to be placed with each. We consider both the problem of minimizing procurement costs subject to a service level constraint and that of minimizing procurement plus inventory and backlogging costs.
