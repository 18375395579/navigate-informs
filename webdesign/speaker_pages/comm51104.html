Title: Intent-based Online Recommendation
Author: Changrui Ren, Senior Manager, IBM Research China, Zhongguancun Software Park, Beijing, China, rencr@cn.ibm.com
Coauthor(s): Tao Qin, Miao He, Jinfeng Li, Bing Shao
Year: 2013
Abstract: The prevailing of collaborative filtering recommendation brings about tougher marketing competition for e-B2C retailers. The failure to capture and fulfill a customer’s intents becomes an observed and announced pain point of a great many retailers. In contrary, the understanding of customer intents becomes a differentiator to cement the customer relationship, increase conversion rate and win market share. We share in-depth intent-based online recommendation in this work.
