Title: Dynamic Pricing and Inventory Management under Fluctuating Procurement Costs

Author: Renyu Zhang, WashU, Business School, Campus Box 1133, One Brookings Drive, St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States of America, renyu.zhang@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Nan Yang, Guang Xiao

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a periodic review joint pricing and inventory control model in which the firm orders from a spot market with fluctuating procurement costs to meet price-dependent stochastic demand. We show that a cost-dependent order-up-to/pre-order-up-to/list-price policy is optimal. We offer the unique insight on the risk-seeking attitude of a risk-neutral firm and show that dual-sourcing and dynamic-pricing are strategically complementary in the presence of procurement cost fluctuating.
