Title: Risk Measures for Breach of Contracts
Author: Cagri Haksoz, Dr., Cass Business School, City University of London, Operations Management & Quantitative Met, London, United Kingdom, C.Haksoz@city.ac.uk
Coauthor(s): Ashay Kadam
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop a risk measure, coined as Supply-at-Risk (SAR) for commodity buyers who procure via fixed-price long-term contracts in the presence of demand uncertainty. In case of breach of the contract by the commodity sellers, the buyers needs to know how large a risk they are exposed to in the spot procurement market.
