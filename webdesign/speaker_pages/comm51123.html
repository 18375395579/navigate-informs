Title: The Effects of Auction Design on the Performance of Online Markets for IT Services

Author: Eric van Heck, Professor of Information Management and Markets, RSM Erasmus University, 50 Burg Oudlaan, Rotterdam, 3062PA, Netherlands, eheck@fbk.eur.nl

Coauthor(s): Otto Koppius, Uladzimir Radkevitch

Year: 2006

Abstract: Small business increasingly uses online marketplaces to alocate IT projects to suppliers via reverse auctions. The market and auction design affects auction outcomes and market performance. We draw on electronic markets theory and auction theory to investigate the role of entrance barriers for bidders. Transaction data from two major marketplaces are used to explore barriers' impact on market performance, i.e. percentage of awarded auctions, percentage of return buyers and buyers?satisfaction.
