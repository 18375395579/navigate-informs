Title: Market Segmentation of Call Centers: The Case for Back-sourcing

Author: Yu Xia, Associate Professor, Northeastern University, 214 Hayden Hall, 360 Huntington Ave, Boston, MA, 02115, United States of America, y.xia@neu.edu

Coauthor(s): Bintong Chen, Vaidy Jayaraman, Charles Munson

Year: 2013

Abstract: We construct a theoretical model to study call centers with different wage rates and productivity levels that compete for contracts of multiple clients. We develop conditions for a call center to dominate the market and conditions for several call centers to coexist in the market. These results are then used to explain the back-sourcing phenomenon in the market. We survey an international service provider and find a strong match between our theoretical results and the empirical evidence.
