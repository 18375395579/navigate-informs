Title: Optimal Inventory-trading Policy for Commodity Storage Assets

Author: Nicola Secomandi, Assistant Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, ns7@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This talk presents the structure of the optimal inventory trading policy for a storage asset when the commodity price state-variable evolves according to a Markov process and the storage technology features inventory dependent capacity functions, and provides managerial insights on the meaning of high and low price state-variable values.
