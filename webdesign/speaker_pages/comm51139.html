Title: Nonatomic-game Models for Timing Clearance Sales under Competition

Author: Jian Yang, New Jersey Institute of Technology, Department of IME, Newark, 07102, United States of America, yang@njit.edu

Coauthor(s): Yusen Xia, Xiangtong Qi

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study a problem in which a continuum of sellers try to sell the same product over the same time horizon. Each seller is allowed to lower his price once at the time of his own choosing. For both deterministic and stochastic models, we show the existence of well-behaved equilibria. Computational results verify that our approach is valid when there are only a finite number of competing firms.
