Title: The Optimal Advertising Policy on Facebook
Author: Anwesha Bhattacharjee, University of Texas at Dallas, 2200 WATERVIEW PKWY APT 1836, RICHARDSON, TX, 75080, United States of America, axb094820@utdallas.edu
Coauthor(s): Vijay Mookerjee
Year: 2013
Abstract: Advertisers on Facebook can control their advertising effort to maximize profit. This paper analyzes the optimal advertising strategy for firms on Facebook. The findings suggest that a firm with a bigger initial market will spend less on the campaign. Results also indicate there are distinct regions where Facebook’s revenue decreases as sharing increases and Facebook has an opportunity cost for providing viral impressions to an advertiser for free.
