Title: Transferring and Hedging Foreign Exchange Risk in a Global Supply Chain
Author: Kun Soo Park, Assistant Professor, Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, 85 Hoegiro, Dongdaemun-gu, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, kunsoo@kaist.ac.kr
Coauthor(s): Kyoung-Kuk Kim
Year: 2013
Abstract: We analyzes risk management contracts used to handle foreign exchange risk in a decentralized supply chain of a multinational firm. Particular contracts of interest are transferring risk to a third party by using currency options contracts and re-arranging risk between supply chain members using risk-sharing contracts. We study if and when these risk management contracts be be beneficial to each division and the total supply chain.
