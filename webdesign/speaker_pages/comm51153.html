Title: The Simple Economics of Approximately Optimal Auctions
Author: Nima Haghpanah, PhD Student, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, United States of America, nima.haghpanah@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Saeed Alaei, Hu Fu, Jason D. Hartline
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider mechanism design in environments where the agents have multi-dimensional and non-linear preferences. In these environments maximizing marginal revenue may not be optimal, and furthermore, there is sometimes no direct way to implementing the marginal revenue maximization mechanism. We characterize the settings for which marginal revenue maximization is optimal and we show that marginal revenue maximization is approximately optimal.
