Title: Asymmetric Supply Function Equilibria with Forward Contracts

Author: Eddie Anderson, Professor of Operations Management, Australian School of Business, University of New South Wales, Sydney, NSW 2052, Australia, eddiea@agsm.edu.au

Coauthor(s): Xinmin Hu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Recent work has characterized the behaviour of supply function equilibria for asymmetric firms. This paper extends this work by exploring the nature of such equilibria when there are forward swap contracts, which are common in electricity markets. There are surprisingly far reaching effects: e.g there is no longer a natural ordering between different equilibria. We show uniqueness with three firms, even without capacity constraints. On the other hand duopoly solutions are not unique.
