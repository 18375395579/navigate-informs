Title: Pricing and Capacity Rationing with Stragetic Customers
Author: Matulya Bansal, Graduate Student, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, mb2431@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Costis Maglaras
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider a retailer that faces a market of heterogeneous customers that strategize over their time of purchase in response to the retailer's dynamic pricing and capacity rationing strategies. We formulate the retailer's revenue maximization problem as one of mechanism design, which allow us to unify old results, and derive several new insights about the structure of the optimal policies.
