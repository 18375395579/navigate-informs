Title: Supply-side Rationale for Bundling
Author: Qingning Cao, University of Texas-Dallas, School of Management, 800 West Campbell Road, Dallas, TX, 75080, United States of America, qingning.cao@utdallas.edu
Coauthor(s): Jun Zhang, Xianjun Geng, Kathryn Stecke
Year: 2012
Abstract: We examine a firm's capacity decisions and restrictions on a popular product when the market is uncertain and the firm has an option to bundle two products. Deriving the firm's optimal bundling strategy, this talk sheds light on the interplay between a firm's capacity (supply-side) and bundling (demand-side) decisions.
