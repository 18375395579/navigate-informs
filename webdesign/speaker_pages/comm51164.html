Title: Inventory Models with Lead-time Choice and Supply Capacity Constraints

Author: Haifeng Wang, Chinese University of Hong Kong, William M. W. Mong Engineering Bldg., Room 615, Shatin, N.T., Hong Kong - ROC, hfwang@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Houmin Yan

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a multi-period inventory model in which a supplier provides two alternative lead-time choices to customers: a short or a long-lead time. The supplier operates in a batch-production mode with a supply capacity constraint. Orders from long lead-time customers can be satisfied in the next production cycle; while orders from short lead-time customers have to be satisfied from the on-hand inventory. We characterize the optimal production-inventory policies.
