Title: Managing Engineering Design for Competitive Sourcing in Closed-loop Supply Chains
Author: Tolga Aydinliyim, Lundquist College of Business, University of Oregon, Eugene, OR, 97403, United States of America, tolga@uoregon.edu
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study the design (integral vs. partitioned) and procurement decisions of a buyer and its suppliers?pricing decisions. The integral design requires more virgin materials and eliminates yield loss due to final joining. However, the simpler partitioned design allows a more competitive supplier base to rely less on reverse flows.
