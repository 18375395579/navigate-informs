Title: Signaling via Prices in a Congested Environment
Author: Laurens Debo, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, laurens.debo@chicagobooth.edu
Coauthor(s): Senthil Veeraraghavan
Year: 2012
Abstract: High quality of new, innovative products could be signaled to an uninformed consumer segment via high prices. We show that low prices, but, long queues can be more efficient signals of high quality.
