Title: Consumer Loyalty, Strategic Behavior and Retailers' Dynamic Pricing Strategy: Theory Sand Experiments
Author: Benny Mantin, University of Waterloo, Waterloo, ON, Canada, bmantin@uwaterloo.ca
Coauthor(s): Mirko Kremer, Anton Ovchinnikov
Year: 2012
Abstract: We investigate the impact of strategic consumer behavior and loyalty on the dynamic pricing strategies of competing retailers. We present both modeling results as well as the results of behavioral experiments in which subjects played as retailers in either a monopoly or duopoly environments. We compare subjects?decisions with the optimal model predictions and explain the effects of consumer loyalty and strategic behavior.
