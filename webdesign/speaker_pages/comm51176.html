Title: Dynamic Assortment Customization with Limited Inventories
Author: Fernando Bernstein, Professor, Duke University, 100 Fuqua Drive, Box 90120, Durham, NC, 27708, United States of America, fernando.bernstein@duke.edu
Coauthor(s): Safak Yucel, Lei Xie
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a retailer with limited inventories of substitutable products. The firm decides which subset of products to offer to each arriving customer. The optimal policy is to offer all available products if the customer base is homogeneous. However, with multiple customer segments characterized by different product preferences, it may be optimal to limit the choice set of some customers. Using a case study, we find that the revenue impact of assortment customization can be significant.
