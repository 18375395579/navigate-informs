Title: Real Options Analysis of Investment in Carbon Capture and Sequestration Technology

Author: Afzal Siddiqui, University College London, Department of Statistical Science, London, WC1E 6BT, United Kingdom, afzal@stats.ucl.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Somayeh Heydari, Nick Ovenden

Year: 2008

Abstract: We take the perspective of a coal-fired power plant owner that can either invest in carbon capture and sequestration technology or carry out more modest energy-conversion efficiency upgrades. Both mitigation measures have irreversible fixed costs, and the prices for coal and carbon dioxide are stochastic and correlated. Given the two perpetual, mutually exclusive mitigation options, we find the optimal decision rule and the value of the option to reduce carbon dioxide emissions.
