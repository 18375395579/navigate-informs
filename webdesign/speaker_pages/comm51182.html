Title: Exchange of Demand Information Across Retailers in a Supply Chain
Author: Jianghua Wu, Assistant Professor, Renmin University of China, School of Business, 59 Zhongguancun St., Beijing, 100872, China, jwu@ruc.edu.cn
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the impact of information sharing across decentralized retailers in a supply chain. The manufacturer supplies similar products to multiple retailers and each retailer serves its independent end market. We show that, under constant market conditions, the number of retailers may affect their incentives to share information.
