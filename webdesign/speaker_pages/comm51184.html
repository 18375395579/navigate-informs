Title: Inventory Management Under Advance Selling: Optimal Order and Pricing Policies
Author: Michelle MH Seref, University of Florida, Stuzin Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32606, United States, michelle.hanna@cba.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Aydin Alptekinoglu, Selcuk Erenguc
Year: 2007
Abstract: Under advance selling, in which an advance sales period precedes the spot period, customers are uncertain about their future product valuation. We consider an inventory management decision, in which we decide a one-time inventory order quantity at the beginning of the advance sales period as well as a portion of this inventory to reserve for advance sales. We use an expected profit maximization model to find the optimal order and pricing policies.
