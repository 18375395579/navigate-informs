Title: Delivering Profitable Growth for HPDirect.com using Operations Research

Author: Arnab Chakraborty, Director, Global Analytics, HP, No. 66/2, Ward No. 83, Bagmane Tech-Park, Embassy Prime, CV Raman Nagar, Bangalore, Ka, 560093, India, arnab.chakraborty@hp.com

Coauthor(s): Manav Shroff, Rohit Tandon, Girish Srinivasan

Year: 2012

Abstract: HP's online business, is meant to build robust multi-channel presence given the evolution in shopping behavior. The business benefitted from OR Solutions that overcame problems faced along the e-commerce value chain -Traffic Driver Identification(impacts marketing & budget management),Purchase Pattern Prediction(effective campaign design) AND Order Forecasting(Optimize warehouse inventory holding & order fulfillment).Deploying these solutions since '09 have driven additional revenue of $117Mn.
