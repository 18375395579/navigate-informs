Title: Contracting for Supply in an Unpredictable World

Author: Fang Liu, Assistant Professor, Nanyang Technological University, Nanyang Business School, Singapore, Singapore, Liu_Fang@ntu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Tracy Lewis, Jing-Sheng Song

Year: 2013

Abstract: A group of retailers contract with a manufacturer for the eventual delivery of materials over T future time periods. They each undertake private investments to provide for and utilize the manufacturing capacity. We identify dynamic agreements that induce uncooperative members to make efficient private investments and capacity allocation decisions under unpredictable and unverifiable market conditions. We demonstrate the supply agreement through IFRC's warehouse capacity allocation problem.
