Title: The Revenue Driven Newsvendor under Information Asymmetries

Author: Adam Zhu Wansha, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, 50 stamford 0401, Singapore, 178899, Singapore, adamzhu@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Zhengping Wu, Pascale Crama

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study the inventory decisions of a manufacturer who derives revenue not only from its product sales, but also from a second source such as advertisement revenue of a magazine publisher, cell phone carriers?subsidize revenue of a phone maker, and etc. We use the principle-agent model to study the manufacturer’s relationship with the second revenue source provider. The manufacturer’s optimal decisions are derived and its managerial insights are discussed.
