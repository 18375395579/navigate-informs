Title: Competition in Network Revenue Management

Author: Nishant Mishra, London Business School, London, United Kingdom, nmishra.phd2003@london.edu

Coauthor(s): Victor DeMiguel

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a model for oligopolistic competition in network revenue management. For such a system, we show the existence and uniqueness of equilibrium under certain conditions. We also perform numerical experiments and show how competition will affect revenue management practices in comparison to the case of a monopoly making centralized decisions.
