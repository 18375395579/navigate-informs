Title: The Value of Perfect Hedging

Author: Thomas Seyller, Consultant, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301-2411, United States of America, tseyller@sdg.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: I will define the value of perfect hedging, which captures the decision-maker’s willingness to pay for the hedges that best complement his portfolio. The analysis provides three benefits. First, it shows the decision-maker how much of his resources he should devote to searching for hedges. It also helps identify the uncertainties on which it is most valuable to hedge. Finally, it gives an upper bound on his personal indifferent buying price for any uncertain deal he might consider acquiring.
