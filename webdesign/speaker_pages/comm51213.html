Title: Product Line Design in e-Commerce Marketplaces

Author: Vibhanshu Abhishek, Assistant Professor, Carnegie Mellon University, 4800 Forbes Avenue, Hamburg Hall, Pittsburgh, Pe, 15213, United States of America, vibs@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: There has been an emergence of online Marketplaces that allow suppliers to use the retailer’s online infrastructure to sell products directly to consumers. We consider a marketplace for vertically differentiated goods with heterogeneous consumers. Our analysis suggests that the quality offered to the high-type customers when the supplier sells through a marketplace is always lower than the first-best quality. We further show that the product line always becomes shorter in this marketplace.
