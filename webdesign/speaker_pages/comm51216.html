Title: Optimal Incentives for Delegated Portfolio Selection

Author: Stephan Sturm, Worcester Polytechnic Institute, c/o Princeton University, 116, Sherrerd Hall, Princeton, NJ, 08544, United States of America, ssturm@princeton.edu

Coauthor(s): Maxim Bichuch

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the problem of an investor who lets a fund manager manage his wealth. The latter is paid by an incentive scheme based on the performance of the fund. Manager and investor have different risk aversions; the manager may invest in a financial market to form a portfolio optimal for his expected utility whereas the investor is free to choose the incentives ?taking only into account that the manager is paid enough to accept the managing contract.
