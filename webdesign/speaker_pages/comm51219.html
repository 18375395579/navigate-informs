Title: Data Driven Market Pricing and Portfolio Management

Author: Jose Beltran, HP Labs, 1U-2, HP Labs, 1501 Page Mill Road, Palo Alto, United States of America, jose-luis.beltran@hp.com

Coauthor(s): Kay-Yut Chen, Guillermo Gallego, Ruxian Wang, Jay Wang, Shelen Jain

Year: 2012

Abstract: Managing and pricing a portfolio of hardware products is a complex but essential task in competitive markets with small margins. Considerations include demand estimation, brand and feature valuation for multiple products in a rapidly changing environment, among other factors. We present a pragmatic approaches and optimization models of joint decisions of portfolio selection and product pricing.
