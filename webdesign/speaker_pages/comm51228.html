Title: Inter-temporal Price Dispersion in Airline Markets: an Empirical Analysis
Author: Benny Mantin, Assistant Professor, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, ON, N2L 3G1, Canada, bmantin@uwaterloo.ca
Coauthor(s): Bon Koo
Year: 2008
Abstract: We empirically investigate inter-temporal price dispersion in the airline industry on 1000 US domestic routes. By measuring price dispersion about the average observed airfares for same periods, we examine the factors that affect dispersion, such as competitive, route, and carrier specific variables.
