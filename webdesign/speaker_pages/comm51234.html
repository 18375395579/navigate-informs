Title: Using Procurement Service Providers (PSP) in Supplier Selection
Author: Sripad Devalkar, Indian School of Business, Gachibowli, Hyderabad, 500032, India, sripad_devalkar@isb.edu
Coauthor(s): Zhixi Wan
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this research, we address the question of a how a buyer can make the best use of procurement service providers (PSP) in the supplier selection process. Specifically, we consider how the buyer should set the service requirement (number of pre-qualified suppliers recommended), and provide incentives to motivate the PSP to better screen the recommended suppliers. We characterize the optimal decisions for the buyer under different conditions of supplier quality and cost heterogeneity.
