Title: Optimal Contract Design with Information Sharing and Competition

Author: Aditya Jain, Assistant Professor, Indian School of Business, Gachibowli, Hyderabad, AP, 500032, India, Aditya_Jain@isb.edu

Coauthor(s): Milind Sohoni, Sridhar Seshadri

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the case when retailers, engaged in horizontal competition, source from a common supplier. Each retailer privately observes her market signal and chooses to reveal her information to the common supplier. Her information accuracy is common knowledge. The supplier then sets a wholesale price for the retailers. We design an optimal, linear, mechanism that enforces truthful revelation and ensures individual rationality. We also show that the entire supply chain is better off.
