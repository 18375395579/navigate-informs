Title: The Impact of Consumer Returns on a Manufacturing Multichannel Strategy
Author: Paolo Letizia, Erasmus University, Burgemeester Oudlaan 50, Rotterdam, Netherlands, letizia@rsm.nl
Coauthor(s): Terry Harrison
Year: 2013
Abstract: Products sold over Internet, thanks to product variety and customization, can offer higher marginal value to the consumers than the ones sold at the store. However, there might be uncertainty about product fit. The manufacturer has to strategically control, through prices and a returns policy, the flow of consumers buying his products through the on-line channel vs. the retail channel.
