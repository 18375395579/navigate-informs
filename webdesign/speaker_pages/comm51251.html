Title: Strategic Consumers, Myopic Retailers
Author: Anton Ovchinnikov, University of Virginia, Darden School of Business, 100 Darden Blvd, Charlottesville, VA, 22903, United States of America, aovchinnikov@darden.virginia.edu
Coauthor(s): Mirko Kremer, Benny Mantin
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study competitive dynamic pricing with strategic consumers. We present a stylized two-period model, followed by a set of behavioral experiments. Our main insight is that subject behaved myopically: they price so that to obtain higher revenue and sell more inventory in the main selling season, but by doing so significantly limit their ability to generate revenue in the clearance season, which, in the presence of strategic consumers is a major source of revenue
