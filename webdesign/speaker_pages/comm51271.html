Title: Price & Revenue Optimization for One of the Largest Acquiring Banks in South America

Author: Suman Kumar Singh, Fiserv, Bangalore, India, kumarsum@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Aditya Khandekar, Tarang Goyal

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Merchant Acquirer had ~1million merchants in mass market. An opportunity existed to selectively optimize price for merchants based on certain attributes. A statistical segmentation was developed following which non-linear differential price elasticity function was built. For each segment, price elasticity along with an optimization algorithm with complex constraints was developed to optimize price and achieve optimal revenue.
