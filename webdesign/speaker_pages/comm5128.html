Title: Price Containment in Cap and Trade Policies
Author: Yihsu Chen, Assistant Professor, University of California, Merced, 5200 N. Lake Rd, Merced, CA, 95343, United States of America, yihsu.chen@ucmerced.edu
Coauthor(s): Andrew Liu
Year: 2012
Abstract: Various instruments are available in existing or proposed policies to contain price volatility of permits. We examine the effect of a price ceiling & floor on the investment of new capacity. Besides undesirable emission consequences, we find that the price-ceiling could also lead to over-investment due to the inflated consumption induced by low power prices when the permit prices are capped. On the other hand, a permit floor would result in an under-investment in new capacity.
