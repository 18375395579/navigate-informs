Title: The Incentive Effect of Acceptance Sampling Plans in a Supply Chain with Endogenous Product Quality

Author: Tian Ni, PhD Student, Purdue University, 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, ni10@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Hong Wan, Xiaowei Xu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper studies a firm that procures a product from a supplier with endogenous product quality. The firm determines the acceptance sampling plan and the supplier determines the quality effort level in either a simultaneous or a Stackelberg leadership game. We identify the Nash equilibriums, and show the firm's and the supplier's optimal strategies are sensitive to both the recall loss sharing ratio and the game format.
