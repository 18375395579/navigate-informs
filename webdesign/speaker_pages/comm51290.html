Title: Capacity Management for Oilseeds Processors

Author: Quang Dang Nguyen, Carlson School of Management, University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, United States of America, nguy1762@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Tong Wang, Onur Boyabatli

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper analyzes the capacity investment decisions of an oilseed pressing plant that uses a commodity input (oilseed) to produce a commodity output (crude vegetable oil) and a commodity byproduct (protein meal) in proportions. In a multi-period framework, we characterize the optimal capacity investment portfolio and the subsequent sourcing, processing and storage decisions under the input and the output spot price uncertainties.
