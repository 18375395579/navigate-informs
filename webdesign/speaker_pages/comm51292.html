Title: Managing New Product Development Projects In the Presence of a Competitive Threat

Author: Issariya Sirichakwal, University of Washington, 423 Paccar Hall, Seattle, WA, 98195-5852, United States of America, issars@u.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Theodore Klastorin, Gary Mitchell

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the introduction of a new product or service where the innovator firm faces competition to the market and it only knows a distribution representing the market entry time of a possible rival. The first firm to enter the market is rewarded with a single payment or a per period monopoly profit. We consider the trade-off between the development cost and the risk of a rival entering the market first, and provide insights into managing the internal activities of the development projects.
