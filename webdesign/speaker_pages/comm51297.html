Title: An Examination of Competition and Efficiency for Hospital Industry

Author: Yasar A. Ozcan, Professor, Virginia Commonwealth University, P.O. Box 980203, Richmond, VA, 23298-0203, United States of America, ozcan@vcu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ismet Sahin, Menderes Tarcan, Mustafa Narci, Hacer Özgen Narci

Year: 2013

Abstract: Technical efficiency of hospitals was estimated using Data Envelopment Analysis using five outputs and five inputs. The intensity of competition among hospitals was measured by objective and subjective measures. The relationship between competition and efficiency was examined by controlling the effects of demand and supply characteristics of the market and hospital traits. Tobit results showed competition did not have a significant relationship with hospital technical efficiency.
