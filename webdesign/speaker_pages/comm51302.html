Title: Impact of Operations Flexibility on Contract Design

Author: Li Jiang, Assistant Professor, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Department of Logistics, Hung Hom, KN, Hong Kong - ROC, L.Jiang@inet.polyu.edu.hk

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a supply chain consisting of one manufacturer and one retailer. The retailer offers a revenue share to the manufacturer who has the capability to postpone price and production decisions until demand uncertainty is resolved. We characterize optimal contract terms and the resultant channel performances, and compare them to the results in existing literature to evaluate the impact of flexibility.
