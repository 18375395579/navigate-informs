Title: Competing Auctions in Online Advertising

Author: De Liu, University of Kentucky, 425D Gatton B&E Building, Lexington, KY, 40506, United States of America, de.liu@uky.edu

Coauthor(s): Jianqing Chen, Andrew Whinston

Year: 2013

Abstract: What happens when there are competing offerings of keyword auctions? We moves beyond a monopolistic setting to study two simultaneous keyword auctions that may differ in their ranking rules. Under this framework, we examine the effect of competing auctions on advertisers' participation, the optimal design of keyword auctions, and social welfare.
