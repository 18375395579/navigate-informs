Title: Promoting Competition in Procurement Mechanisms

Author: Gabriel Weintraub, Columbia Business School, Uris Hall 402, New York, NY, United States of America, gyw2105@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Daniela Saban

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk we discuss recent work that studies effective mechanisms to enhance competition in procurement settings and reduce buying prices. Portions of this work have been done in collaboration with the Chilean government procurement agency, in charge of buying US$9.1 billion worth of products and services every year.
