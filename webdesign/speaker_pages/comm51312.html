Title: Economic Impact of Recommender Systems on Firms and Customers

Author: Abhijeet Ghoshal, University of Minnesota, 19th Av. S., Minneapolis, United States of America, abhijeet@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Sumit Sarkar, Vijay Mookerjee

Year: 2013

Abstract: Recommendation systems provide firms with an increased ability to target customers with products and services that match their needs. However, is a better recommender system always beneficial for a firm? To study this question, we consider monopolistic and duopolistic markets where customers are heterogeneous in their search costs. We derive conditions and provide managerial insights for situations when improving recommendation systems may increase or decrease profits of firms in these markets.
