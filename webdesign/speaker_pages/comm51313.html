Title: Supply Contracts with Competition
Author: Richard Engelbrecht-Wiggans, eplus17@uiuc.edu
Coauthor(s): Elena Katok, Valery Pavlov
Year: 2007
Abstract: We present and analytical and an experimental investigation of several supply chain contracts, with full and incomplete information. Our main finding is that with cometition, wholesale price contracts can perform well, and in some cases better than coordinating contracts.
