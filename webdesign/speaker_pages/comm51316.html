Title: Coordinated Pricing and Inventory Management with Deterministic Demand and Costly Price Adjustment

Author: Xin Chen, Assistant Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 216C Transportation Bldg, 104 S. Mathews Ave., Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, xinchen@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Peng Sun

Year: 2008

Abstract: We develop an inventory and pricing model with deterministic demand and costly price adjustment. Different from the majority of the literature, a cost is incurred if there is a price change. The objective is to find a pricing and ordering plan so as to maximize the total profit over the planning horizon. Under rather general assumptions, we develop polynomial time algorithms to solve the model, which interestingly vary significantly with the cost structure.
