Title: Economic Analysis of the Right of First Refusal

Author: Shehzad Wadalawala, swadalawala@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Shmuel Oren

Year: 2008

Abstract: Right of First Refusal (ROFR) in an auction provides its owner the opportunity to match a third party's winning offer. We analyze the economic consequences of such a right and show possible negative consequences on sellers?revenue and on social welfare due to suppression of competition. We also describe circumstances where an ROFR is beneficial and should be awarded as part of a contract negotiation.
