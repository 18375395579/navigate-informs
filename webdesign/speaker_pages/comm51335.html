Title: Experimental Evaluation of Quantity-discount and Price-only Contracts Under Asymmetric Information

Author: Feryal Erhun, Assistant Professor, Stanford University, MS&E, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, ferhun@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Basak Kalkanci, Kay-Yut Chen

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study all-unit quantity discount (QD), incremental QD, and price-only contracts in a setting where a manufacturer, who is better informed about demand, procures a component from a supplier. With experiments, we test the relative performance of all-unit and incremental discounts (theory predicts that all-unit discount leads to higher profits) and the effect of number of price breaks (theory predicts that increasing the number of price breaks leads to higher profits) on supplier’s profits.
