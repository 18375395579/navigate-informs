Title: Product Line Design Under Demand Uncertainty: Market Segmentation Versus Risk Pooling

Author: Ying Rong, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, No. 535 Fahua Road, Shanghai, 200052, China, yrong@sjtu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Ying-Ju Chen, Z. Max Shen

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper investigates the impact of demand uncertainty on the firm's product line decision and production strategy. We argue that the presence of supply-demand mismatch may significantly influence the product line design decisions due to the interplay between the market segmentation and risk pooling effects.
