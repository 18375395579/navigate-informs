Title: Revenue Management in the Presence of Staying Customers

Author: Lei Xie, Dr, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Room 307, 1954 Hua Shan Road, Shanghai, Shanghai, China, leixie.nhb@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Xiaoying Liang, Houmin Yan, Yimin Yu

Year: 2013

Abstract: The well-observed staying (screening-and-waiting) behavior has received little attention in revenue management. We consider two inventory models characterized by a personalized pricing and a dynamic pricing strategies, respectively. We show that the optimal selling strategy is a static multi-level protection policy. By evaluating models with/without staying customers, we report the importance of including the staying behavior in revenue management models.
