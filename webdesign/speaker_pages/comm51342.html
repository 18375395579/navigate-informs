Title: Endogenous Flexibility Installation Under Asymmetric Cost Structure and Demand Uncertainty

Author: Li Jiang, Assistant Professor, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Department of Logistics, Hung Hom, KN, Hong Kong - ROC, L.Jiang@inet.polyu.edu.hk

Year: 2007

Abstract: We provide a detailed analysis of flexibility decision by firms in monopoly and competitive settings facing demand uncertainty. By defining flexibility on a continuous spectrum to allow it to affect production cost after demand uncertainty is resolved, we completely characterize flexibility and expected profit of firms with asymmetric cost structures, and discuss the effects of demand, competition, decision sequence, and cost parameters on flexibility, capacity and profitability.
