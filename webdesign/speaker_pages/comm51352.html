Title: Inventory Competition for Newsvendors Under the Profit and Revenue Satisficing Objective

Author: Xuan Zhao, Assistant Professor, Wilfrid Laurier University, 75 University Ave. West, Waterloo, ON, N2J 6C1, Canada, xzhao@wlu.ca

Coauthor(s): Victor Shi

Year: 2007

Abstract: The classical Newsvendor (NV) model has been widely used to understand the single-period inventory-management problem. Recent years have witnessed an extensive study of NVs under competition. Most of the literature, if not all, has assumed NVs' objectives are to maximize profits. In reality, however, many inventory managers' objectives are to achieve the pre-determined profit and (or) revenue targets. This paper aims to understand competitive NVs with profit or profit and revenue satisficing objectives.
