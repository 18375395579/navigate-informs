Title: Dynamic Model of Quality Improvement in Manufacturer-supplier Contract

Author: Sidong Zhang, Ph.D Candidate, Syracuse University, 721 University Ave., Syracuase, NY, 13244, United States, szhang01@syr.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Usually quality is a static requirement in a contract rather than an evolving process, though quality improvement is a continuous process. This paper studies a dynamic contractual mechanism to motivate the supplier to improve quality continuously and spontaneously given price is exogenous and consumer’s quality expectation is increasing. This paper studies the supplier’s quality improvement decision making behavior given the dynamic contractual mechanism.
