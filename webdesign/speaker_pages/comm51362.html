Title: Quality Decisions in Sustainable Manufacturing

Author: Farhad Azadivar, Professor, University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, 40 William Street, South Dartmouth, MA, 02748, United States of America, fazadivar@umassd.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: A solution is presented to decide on the optimum quality when products are replaced with new ones and returned products are recycled. The paper explores the tradeoff between the costs of the targeted quality versus the additional cost of remanufacturing using returned parts.
