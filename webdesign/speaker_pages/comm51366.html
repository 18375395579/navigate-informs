Title: Product Design in a Market with Satisficing Customers
Author: Matulya Bansal, Student, Columbia Business School, Uri Hall, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, mb2431@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Costis Maglaras
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the product design problems of monopolist that serves a market of heterogeneous customers that behave as follows: each customer selects the cheapest product among those whose price is less than his valuation and their product quality exceed a customer-specific quality threshold. We characterize the optimal product menu for such a market, contrast it to results based on more traditional models of choice behavior, and specialize our findings to several application settings.
