Title: Optimal Bulk to Case Product Mix in a Supply Chain
Author: Ananth Iyer, Professor, Purdue University, Rawls Hall, Room 4001, 100 S Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, aiyer@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Joo Yol Maeng
Year: 2008
Abstract: We model the optimal choice of production in bulk or in individual product in the presence of demand forecast errors, production cost differences and inventory control choices. The model suggests that demand correlation with forecast, inventory turns and production variation may determine the benefit to product postponement. Data from a bottling company is used to estimate the impact of such strategies.
