Title: Supply Chain Revenue Management

Author: Yusen Xia, Assistant Professor, Georgia State University, Atlanta, GA, 30303, United States, mgtyxx@langate.gsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Qiying Hu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk, we study revenue management for a one-supplier and one-retailer supply chain where consumers can buy either from the supplier or from the retailer. Both centralized and decentralized settings are investigated.
