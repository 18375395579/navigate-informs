Title: Quality-based supplier selection with information asymmetry
Author: Xinghao Yan, Purdue University, Krannert School of Management, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States, yan1@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Hui Zhao, Kwei Tang
Year: 2007
Abstract: We explore quality-based supplier selection when quality is suppliers' private information and quality itself also exhibits uncertainty. We analyze quality promise scenario (QP), a signaling game, and quality requirement scenario (QP), a screening game. We find that under certain conditions, both QP and QR may achieve the ideal outcome to the buyer due to the competition among suppliers. Surprisingly, simple requirement strategy with no screening may outperform both QP and QR with screening.
