Title: Joint Production and Economic Retention Quantity Decisions in Capacitated Production Systems
Author: Abhilasha Katariya, PhD Student, Texas A&M University, Industrial and System Engineering, TAMU 3131, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, abhilashapk@neo.tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Eylem Tekin
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider production/inventory management decisions of a firm that sells its product in two market segments during a finite horizon. The objective is to maximize the total expected net revenue by determining the optimal production quantity in each period, and the optimal inventory amount to carry to the next period after the sales in primary and secondary markets. We characterize the structure of the optimal policy and establish lower and upper bounds on the optimal policy parameters.
