Title: Contracting Mechanisms for Airline Code Share Alliances

Author: Achal Bassamboo, Northwestern University, a-bassamboo@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Sunil Chopra, Sandeep Juneja, Milind Sohoni

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study revenue-sharing contracts for airline alliances. An airline privately observes local demand on a single leg and reveals information about remaining capacity, which is used to accept passengers with 2-flight itineraries (thru-passengers.) We show conditions under which the max-allocation contract, for thru-passengers, is an optimal truth-telling contract. We develop conditions when the min-allocation contract achieves optimality. We extend the results for larger alliances.
