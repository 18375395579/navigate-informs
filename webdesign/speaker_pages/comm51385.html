Title: Quality-adjusted Price Comparison Across Internet Retailers

Author: Sangkil Moon, Assistant Professor, North Carolina State University, College of Management, Raleigh, NC, 27695-7229, United States of America, smoon2@ncsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Wagner Kamakura

Year: 2008

Abstract: We use a stochastic frontier model to compare retailers in their ability to offer the best deals to consumers considering product assortment quality. Empirically, we compare the price efficiencies of three major online travel agents and demonstrate that interactions between the agent’s information presentation mode and the consumer’s search strategy have a strong impact on the relative price efficiency.
