Title: Using Integer Programming to Evaluate the Impact of the Fed’s New Currency Recirculation Policy

Author: Mili Mehrotra, The University of Texas at Dallas, 800 West Campbell Road, Richardson, TX, United States of America, mxm057000@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Milind Dawande, Chelliah Sriskandarajah

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Fed’s new currency recirculation policy came into effect from June 2007. We evaluate the impact of two main components of this policy: recirculation fees and custodial inventories. Using a mixed-integer linear programming model, we analyze the currency supply network and offer managers of depository institutions with effective cyclic policies for their transactions with the Fed.
