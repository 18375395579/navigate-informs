Title: Competitive Pricing with Reference Effects

Author: Srinivas Krishnamoorthy, Assistant Professor, Ivey School of Business, University of Western Ontario, 1151 Richmond Street N., London, ON, N6A 3K7, Canada, skrishnamoorthy@ivey.ca

Coauthor(s): Brian Coulter

Year: 2008

Abstract: This study examines the effect of reference prices on the pricing strategies of two competing firms ?an entrant and an incumbent. The analogy drawn within the paper is to the MP3-Player market, wherein incumbents have large reference price advantages over effectively equivalent new entrants.
