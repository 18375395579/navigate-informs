Title: Demand Response in the California ISO market
Author: Guillermo Bautista Alderete, Manager of Market Validation and Quality Analysis, California ISO, 250 Outcropping Way, Folsom, CA, 95630, United States of America, bautista.guillermo@gmail.com
Year: 2012
Abstract: This presentation explains the initiatives and mechanisms in place to facilitate the participation of retail demand response programs in the California ISO wholesale market, including the implementation of a new demand response product known as the proxy demand resource. In conjunction with the ISO’s existing participating load product, the proxy demand resource product will add to the demand response capability available to market participants.
