Title: Empirical Analysis of Multi-attribute Procurement Auctions for the Public Healthcare Sector in Chile
Author: Bernardo Quiroga, PhD Candidate, The Pennsylvania State University, Smeal College of Business, 454 Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, bquiroga@psualum.com
Year: 2013
Abstract: The Chilean Public Procurement Agency, ChileCompra, implements multi-attribute reverse auction mechanisms to purchase all governmental acquisitions in the country, producing a very interesting real-life experiment on multi-dimensional bidding. Using the analytical structure of the bidding game, and existing data from auctions conducted by ChileCompra on behalf of the Public Healthcare sector, we study the empirical effect on incentives and the procurement cost of this bidding mechanism.
