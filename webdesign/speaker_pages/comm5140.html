Title: A Network Prescott Model of Capacity Pricing
Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Professor, Columbia University, 412 Uris Hall, NY, NY, 10027, United States of America, gjv1@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Dan Mallet
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider a network version of the Prescott model of capacity pricing, in which firms commit to capacity and price under a zero-profit equilibrium condition. While stylized, the model has interesting implications for cost sharing and price dispersion in airline networks.
