Title: Integrating Long-term and Short-term Contracting in Fed-cattle Supply Chains
Author: Onur Boyabatli, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore, oboyabatli@smu.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Paul Kleindorfer, Stephen Koontz
Year: 2008
Abstract: This paper analyzes the optimal contracting portfolio choice of a meatpacker in fed-cattle supply chains. The meatpacker can source cattle from option-like, unreliable marketing agreement contracts and from the spot market on the spot day. We incorporate several particularities of fed-cattle supply chain (quality and cost differences between different contracted cattle, substitutability between beef products etc.).
