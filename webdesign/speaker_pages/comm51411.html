Title: Contracting for Initial Acquisition and After-sales Service in the Presence of Lifetime Buy Planning

Author: Dong Li, Erasmus University, Rotterdam School of Management, PO Box 1738, Rotterdam, Netherlands, dli@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Nishant Mishra

Year: 2013

Abstract: We look at contracting under warranty + transaction-based contract (W+T) and performance-based contract (PBC), in a supply chain where equipment downtime impacts the customer’s revenue, and lifetime buy planning is considered to manage component obsolescence. We find that PBC can result in higher product availability, but may result in lower efficiency. However, with low obsolescence cost, longer warranty can result in higher profit, despite spare parts inventory backorder.
