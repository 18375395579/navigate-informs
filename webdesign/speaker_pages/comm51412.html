Title: Harvesting and Refurbishing Decisions of a Remanufacturer

Author: Oznur Ozdemir, Sabanci University, Sabanci University Faculty of Management, Orhanli Tuzla, Istanbul, 34956, Turkey, oznurozdemir@su.sabanciuniv.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Ferguson, Meltem Denizel

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the disposition decisions of a remanufacturer. There are three options: a core can be refurbished, harvested for spare parts or salvaged at a certain value. The demand for the refurbished products and the spare parts are uncertain over time. The core availability is limited to meet all demand. We address the problem in a revenue management framework to maximize the firm’s revenue over a T period horizon.
