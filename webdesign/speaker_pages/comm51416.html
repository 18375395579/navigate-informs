Title: A Stochastic Multiple Leader Stackelberg Model of Competition in the Telecommunications Industry

Author: Victor DeMiguel, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Regents Park, London, NW14SA, United Kingdom, avmiguel@london.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study an oligopoly consisting of M leaders and N followers that supply a homogeneous product noncooperatively. Leaders choose their supply levels first, knowing demand only in distribution. Followers make their decisions after observing the leader supply levels and realized demand. We prove existence and uniqueness of equilibrium under mild assumptions, propose a computational approach to find the equilibrium, and apply this framework to model competition in the telecommunication industry.
