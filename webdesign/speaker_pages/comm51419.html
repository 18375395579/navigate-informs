Title: Queueing Game Models for Differentiated Services

Author: Parijat Dube, IBM T J Watson Research Center, Yorktown Heights NY, 10598, pdube@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Rahul Jain

Year: 2008

Abstract: We develop queueing game models and a framework to study competition between network providers, each of whom offer multiple (differentiated) classes of service.We study the pricing (or Bertrand) game between the providers. A Nash equilibrium may not exist in general but we provide sufficient conditions for its existence.
