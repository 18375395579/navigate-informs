Title: Coordinating the Supply Chain in the Agricultural Seed Industry

Author: Timothy Lowe, Professor, University of Iowa, S210 Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, tlowe@iowa.uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Philip Jones, Samuel Burer

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present and analyze contract parameters used between suppliers and retailers in the agricultural seed industry. Many of these contracts include certain bonus?and “penalty?features. We establish sufficient conditions for contract parameters to guarantee coordination. Under the assumption of uniform demand, we fully characterize all coordinating contracts.
