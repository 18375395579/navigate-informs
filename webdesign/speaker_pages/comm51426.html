Title: Improving Supplier Environmental Performance

Author: Tim Kraft, Assistant Professor, University of Virginia, Darden School of Business, Charlottesville, VA, United States of America, kraftt@darden.virginia.edu

Coauthor(s): Ozgen Karaer, John Khawam

Year: 2013

Abstract: We examine how a firm can induce higher environmental quality from a supplier under varying cost and market sensitivity conditions. We consider three methods: supplier competition, cost sharing, and revenue sharing. Our research is based on our work with an NGO as it markets to industries a tool to safely share chemical information.
