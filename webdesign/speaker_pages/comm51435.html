Title: The Value of Bundling in Vertical Markets

Author: Mihai Banciu, Bucknell University, School of Management, 119 Taylor Hall, Lewisburg, PA, 17837, United States of America, mmb018@bucknell.edu

Coauthor(s): Prakash Mirchandani

Year: 2012

Abstract: We investigate the possible optimal bundling strategies in a market characterized by vertical differentiation and resource scarcity (such as the media market), and compute the value of bundling (VoB) for the buyers, seller(s), and overall. We define the VoB as the net benefit derived by either a buyer or a seller when a bundle is offered, versus when no bundle is available for purchase. We find that the overall bundling benefit is influenced by scarcity and degree of differentiation.
