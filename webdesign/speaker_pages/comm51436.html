Title: Valuation of Downstream Liquefied-natural-gas Storage

Author: Mulan X. Wang, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave Posner Hall A27, Tepper School of Business, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, mulan@cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Nicola Secomandi, Sunder Kekre, Alan Scheller-Wolf

Year: 2007

Abstract: Motivated by current developments in the liquefied-natural-gas (LNG) industry, we study the operations of LNG supply chains facing both supply and price risk. We model the upstream LNG production, ocean shipping logistics and dynamic spot price, and characterize the structure of the optimal inventory management policy.
