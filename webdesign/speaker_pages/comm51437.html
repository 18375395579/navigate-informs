Title: Sourcing and Pricing Strategies under Capacity Uncertainties

Author: Qi Feng, Assistant Professor, The University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Station, B6500, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, Annabelle.Feng@mccombs.utexas.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We examine simultaneous ordering and pricing decisions in a periodic review inventory system with random supply capacities. We show that there is a reorder point for each of the suppliers. The reorder points are in the increasing order of the procurement costs, indicating that cost consideration dominates the risk consideration in the sourcing decision. Additional insights of the problem are also obtained by discussing several special applications of the problem.
