Title: Joint Replenishment Problem with Transportation Capacity
Author: Ulku Gurler, Professor, Bilkent University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Ankara, Turkey, ulku@bilkent.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Cagdas Buyukkaramikli, Osman Alp
Year: 2007
Abstract: In this study, we examine a coordinated replenishment problem in the presence of transportation capacity. We discuss a multi-retailer, single-echelon setting under the quantity based joint replenishment policy (Q,s) with positive leadtimes and full backorders and consider extensions.
