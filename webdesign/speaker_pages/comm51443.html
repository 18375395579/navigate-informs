Title: The Effect of Competition on Reserve Price Strategies in Auctions

Author: Peter Popkowski-Leszcyc, Associate Professor of Marketing, University of Alberta, 4-20 F School of Business, Edmonton, AB, T6G 2R6, Canada, ppopkows@ualberta.ca

Coauthor(s): Terry Elrod, Junjun Xie

Year: 2007

Abstract: We model the effect of competitive reserve prices strategies on prices in online auctions, and show that competition matters. However sellers tend not to adapt to competition or different market conditions. To determine sellers?profit maximizing reserve price strategies under different market conditions we build a comprehensive simulation model. This model incorporates competitive sellers and bidder segments who vary in their propensity to bid in auctions with different reserve price strategies.
