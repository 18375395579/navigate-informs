Title: Dynamic Assortment Planning
Author: Rene Caldentey, Stern School of Business, New York University, New York, NY, rcaldent@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Felipe Caro
Year: 2008
Abstract: We investigate optimal assortment planning strategies for a retailer with limited shelf space. The retailer can choose among basic and fashion items with low and high risk (and return) respectively. Our motivation is in the apparel industry, and we explicitly model the vogue as a stochastic process that the retailer tries to follow. The objective is to maximize the long-term value of the retail business by dynamically adjusting the menu of products on display.
