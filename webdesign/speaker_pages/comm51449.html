Title: Determining the Value of Manufacturing Flexibility for a Multi-Plant System
Author: Mark Everson, Technical Leader, Ford Research and Advanced Engineering, RIC Building, MD # 2122, 2101 Village Road, Dearborn, MI, 48124, United States, meverson@ford.com
Coauthor(s): David A. Ostrowski, Charu Chandra, Janis Grabis
Year: 2006
Abstract: Obtaining the value of manufacturing flexibility for a multi-product manufacturer like an automotive company is a problem that has many facets. Important issues include: consumer demand uncertainty characterization, the product-specific level of flexibility of assembly plants, and capacity in the supply base. This talk will give results of a study of an assembly plant system with several different levels of flexibility.
