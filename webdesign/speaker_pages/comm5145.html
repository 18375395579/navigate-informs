Title: Six Sigma & Monte Carlo Simulation to Improve Service Delivery while Optimizing Profits

Author: Tayfun (Ty) Avni, IBM Global Business Services, SCM Operations Analytics, tavni@us.ibm.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: IBM is engaged in providing Information Technology Outsourcing Services at global corporations. Level of service delivered not only impacts client satisfaction, but IBM’s bottom line through contract obligations. Six Sigma tools are combined with Monte Carlo simulation analysis to not only improve IT Service Level Availability through variation reduction, but also quantify and mitigate risk of contract defaults to optimize profits.
