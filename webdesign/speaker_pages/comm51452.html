Title: Sales Competition and the Value of Capacity

Author: Navid Sabbaghi, MIT, 77 Massachusetts Ave. #32-D712, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, sabbaghi@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Yossi Sheffi, John Tsitsiklis

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a supply chain model with supplier competition. Using the model, we motivate and define the value of capacity, show existence and uniqueness, and characterize that value. Finally, we show how the value of capacity can be calculated for a related model.
