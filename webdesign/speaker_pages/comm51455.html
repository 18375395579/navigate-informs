Title: Effective Contracted Logistics Utilization
Author: Engin Alper, University of California at Berkeley, 5816 San Diego St. Apt.3, El Cerrito, CA, 94530, United States, engin@ieor.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Hyun-Soo Ahn, Phil Kaminsky
Year: 2007
Abstract: Third party logistics (3PL) providers have typically guaranteed a high level of flexibility to their customers. We demonstrate how customers can effectively utilize less flexible contracts, and argue that by offering less flexible contracts, 3PL providers can more effectively utilize their resources.
