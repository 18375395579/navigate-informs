Title: Retail Assortment and Price Competition with Effect of Brand Reputation

Author: Nan Xia, Ph.D Candidate, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, Nan.Xia.2008@marshall.usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Raj Rajagopalan

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a modeling framework to analyze the assortment and price competition between two retailers. Our framework incorporates several important factors influencing customers?purchasing behaviors: assortment, price, firm differentiation, product substitution and brand reputation. We provide insights into equilibrium pricing and assortment decisions and analyze the effects of important factors on the equilibrium decisions.
