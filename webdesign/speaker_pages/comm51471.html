Title: Emissions Trading and Electricity Prices in an Agent-based Simulation Model
Author: Daniel Veit, Professor, University of Mannheim, Chair of BA and Information Systems, Schloss, Mannheim, D, 68131, Germany, veit@uni-mannheim.de
Coauthor(s): Anke Weidlich
Year: 2008
Abstract: The contribution presents an agent-based simulation model of the German wholesale electricity market, which takes the market for CO2 emission allowances into account. It is analyzed how profit-seeking generator agents carry over allowance prices to electricity market bids and how this ultimately influences wholesale power prices.
