Title: Revenue Management with Highly Influential Seller Recommendations: A Study of OTA Websites

Author: Ori Marom, RSM Erasmus University, Burgemeester Oudlaan 50, Rotterdam, 3062PA, Netherlands, omarom@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Roman Peskin

Year: 2013

Abstract: Online travel agents such as Orbitz often have tremendous power to direct online buyers to a small subset of available products. Our paper studies OTA selections of top-listed hotels. Over a period of 135 days we tracked price and OTA page-rank data for 2581 different hotels. Our analysis indicates that while OTA websites provide buyers with valuable search assistance by locating good-valued deals they also influence them to purchase relatively expensive products.
