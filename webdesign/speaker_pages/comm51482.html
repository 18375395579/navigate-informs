Title: Take Responsibility of Building an Ethical Supply Chain and the Influence

Author: Yu Xia, Associate Professor, Northeastern University, 214 Hayden Hall, 360 Huntington Ave., Boston, MA, 02115, United States of America, Y.Xia@neu.edu

Coauthor(s): Xingxing Zu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Manufacturers in the global supply chain is facing tremendous challenge-on one hand, intensified competition and compelling drive for cost containment; and on the other hand, the ever increasing demand of corporate social/ethical responsibility that isn’t constrained by geographic and political border. This research proposes a framework to discuss the influence of building an ethical supply chain to a manufacturer’s competitive advantage in the global market.
