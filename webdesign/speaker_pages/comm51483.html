Title: An Experimental Investigation of Buyer Determined Auctions

Author: Elena Katok, Associate Professor, Penn State University, 465 Business Building, Penn State University, University Park, PA, 16802, United States, ekatok@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Ernan Haruvy

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a laboratory study of buyer determined auctions with non-monetary exogenous attributes. We find that generally sealed-bid auctions generate higher buyer surplus levels than dynamic auctions. We also find that when suppliers' non-monetary attributes are known to all suppliers, dynamic auctions perform particularly poorly.
