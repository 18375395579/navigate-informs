Title: Dynamics of Social Learning with Consumer Reviews

Author: Bar Ifrach, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, Uris Hall, 4 West, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, BIfrach14@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Costis Maglaras, Marco Scarsini

Year: 2012

Abstract: A monopolist offers a product to a market of consumers with heterogeneous quality preferences. Although initially uninformed about the product quality, they learn by observing past purchase decisions and reviews of other consumers. We characterize the dynamics of this learning process for markets with high demand intensity. This analysis can be put to use to study various operational and marketing decisions (for example pricing) when consumers engage in social learning.
