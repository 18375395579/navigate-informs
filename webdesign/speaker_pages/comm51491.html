Title: Towards a Counterfeit-proof Global Supply Chain

Author: Morteza Pourakbar, Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University, BurgmasterOudlaan 50, Rotterdam, 3031AA, Netherlands, mpourakbar@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Rob Zuidwijk

Year: 2013

Abstract: Counterfeiting has become one of the major risks in supply chains. In this paper, we investigate the role of Customs in the fight against counterfeiting. We develop game-theoretic models to study how Customs and supply chain stakeholders can collaborate in order to mitigate the risk of counterfeit products entering the legitimate supply chains.
