Title: Optimal Investment Time in Mining Projects Under Uncertainty and Competition
Author: Leonardo Santiago, Associate Professor, Federal University of Minas Gerais, Escola de Engenharia da UFMG, Av. Antônio Carlos, 6627 - Pampulha, Belo Horizonte, MG, 31270-901, Brazil, lsantiago@ufmg.br
Coauthor(s): Alexandre Rodrigues
Year: 2013
Abstract: We assess the managerial flexibility in competitive markets under uncertain demand. Our model takes into account the fact that the investment decision of a firm affects not only its profit, but also the profit of its competitors. Our focus is on analyzing investments of capacity expansion of mining projects.
