Title: Alliance Formation for Durable Products

Author: Yuhong He, Ph.D. Student, University of California, Irvine, 1334 Verano Plac, Irvine, CA, 92617, United States of America, hhe08@exchange.uci.edu

Coauthor(s): Shuya Yin, Saibal Ray

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a two-period model involving a value chain comprising two upstream firms (selling complementary goods) and one downstream firm (selling a durable end product). We are interested in understanding the role of the end product durability and consumer behaviors (forward-looking and/or patient to wait and buy in the future) in shaping the incentives of the upstream firms to form an alliance.
