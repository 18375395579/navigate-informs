Title: Games Among Manufacturers Producing Complementary Products

Author: Xiang Fang, Assistant Professor, University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee, Lubar School of Business, 3202 N Maryland Ave, Milwaukee, WI, 53201, United States of America, fangx@uwm.edu

Coauthor(s): Yunzeng Wang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a general industrial setting where multiple manufacturers each produce a different product and sell it to the markets. These produts are partially complementary. Facing multiple uncertain demand streams, each manufacturer chooses a production quantity to maximize its own expected profit. The manufacturers' equilibrium solutions are studied. Managerial insights are drawn as to how system parameters and control mechanisms affect firms¡¯ decisions and performance.
