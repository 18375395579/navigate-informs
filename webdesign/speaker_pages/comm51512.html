Title: Bid and List Price Trends in RM and Forward-Looking Consumers

Author: Ming Hu, Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto, Toronto, Canada, Ming.Hu@rotman.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Oded Berman, Zhan Pang

Year: 2012

Abstract: We characterize a probabilistic property of the bid price process driven by an optimal policy in a general RM framework: the bid price process always follows an upward trend. We explore a couple of applications of this structural property. For example, we show that the optimal list price process also follows an upward trend if the customers' WTP does not decrease over time. We draw implications on forward-looking behavior of strategic consumers.
