Title: Modeling Multicategory Online Video Demand in Social Media

Author: Qian Tang, University of Texas at Austin, Austin, TX, 78703, United States of America, qian.tang@phd.mccombs.utexas.com

Coauthor(s): Liangfei Qiu, Andrew Whinston

Year: 2012

Abstract: The popularity of social media has greatly increased the demand for online videos. The demand for online videos may be influencd by online WOM, social influence, the provider’s reputation, and competition. Our paper disentangles the influence of these factors. We model a viewer’s decision as first choosing categories and then videos within the categories. The hierarchical model allows videos within a category to have correlated demand.
