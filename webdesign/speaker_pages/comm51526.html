Title: Operations Research Helps Reshape Operations Strategy at Standard Register Company

Author: Michael Gorman, Associate Professor, University of Dayton, Department of MIS, OM and DSC, 300 College Park, Dayton, OH, 45419-2130, United States, michael.gorman@udayton.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Standard Register Company is facing the strategic challenge of “minimizing the total landed costs?to offer competitive pricing in the highly competitive traditional print market segment. We used a trio of operations research techniques - regression, optimization and simulation - to help SR minimize the cost of production orders across its production-distribution network. We estimate annual savings of over $10 million across SR’s major product segments.
