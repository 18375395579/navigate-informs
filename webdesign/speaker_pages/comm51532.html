Title: Mechanism Implementing in Nash Equilibria the Optimal Centralized Solution of a Supply Chain Problem

Author: Shrutivandana Sharma, PhD Student, University of Michigan, Department of EECS, 1301 Beal Ave., Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, svandana@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Volodymyr Babich, Mark Van Oyen, Demosthenis Teneketzis

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study the coordination of production decisions for multiple products among many manufacturers and many suppliers, each with private information about its own objective and its own production capabilities. We do not assume a probabilistic model on the beliefs of each decision maker regarding asymmetric information. We discover a game form (mechanism) which implements in Nash Equilibria the solution of the corresponding centralized supply chain problem.
