Title: Price Competition Under Yield Uncertainty

Author: Awi Federgruen, Professor, Columbia University, 419 Uris Hall, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, af7@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Nan Yang

Year: 2007

Abstract: We analyze the equilibrium behavior in an industry with N suppliers with random yield processes, competing for the business of one or several buyers who face uncertain demands. The suppliers have different yield distributions and procurement costs, and are engaged in price competition. The buyers react to the selected prices and the suppliers' yield characteristics by determining a total order size and its allocation among the suppliers, minimizing purchasing costs under a service constraint.
