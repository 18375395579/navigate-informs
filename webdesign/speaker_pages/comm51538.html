Title: Revenue Maximization in Queues via Service Differentiation

Author: Costis Maglaras, Columbia University, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, United States of America, c.maglaras@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): John Yao, Assaf Zeevi

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study a revenue maximization problem for a service system facing a market of heterogeneous, price- and delay-sensitive customers. Using a deterministic relaxation we characterize the optimal (price, delay) menu, and derive a condition in terms of demand elasticities under which service differentiation is optimal. We propose a set of prices, a priority rule, and a strategic delay control policy that is incentive compatible and asymptotically optimal in large systems, and discuss its properties
