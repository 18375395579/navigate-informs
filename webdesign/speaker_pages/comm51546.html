Title: Demand and Supply Management in Services and Manufacturing Supply Chains

Author: Turgut Aykin, ac2 Solutions, 935 Route 34, Suite 3A, Matawan, NJ, 07747, United States, taykin@ac2solutions.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk, we present a framework for demand and supply management in services and manufacturing supply chains. Challenges faced in demand and supply management in services supply chains are discussed together with a number of new approaches for forecasting and resource optimization. We also present new approaches for demand and supply management in CTO/BTO/ATO type manufacturing supply chains when products do not have fixed bills of materials.
