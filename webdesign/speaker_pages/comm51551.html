Title: Prudent Pricing for Sequential Sales

Author: Xiaowei Xu, Associate Professor, Rutgers Business School, 1 Washington Park, RM 958, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States of America, xiaoweix@business.rutgers.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper studies a multi-stage dynamic pricing model, in which a state variable evolves as a stochastic process and affects price-sensitive customer demand at each stage. A firm has a limited amount of inventory to liquidate and sets the price at each stage after observing the state variable. We study a prudent pricing strategy to sell the stock sequentially. The optimality of such a pricing strategy depends on the curvature of the marginal revenue function.
