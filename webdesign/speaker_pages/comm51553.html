Title: Correlated Disruption Risk in Supply Networks
Author: Alireza Tahbaz-Salehi, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, 418 Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10023, United States of America, alirezat@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Kostas Bimpikis, Douglas Fearing
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper shows how in the presence of incomplete contracts, dual sourcing by a firm's suppliers may reduce its profits. It also shows how divergence between the retailer's and its suppliers' sourcing incentives can reduce overall welfare and lead to ineffiently fragile supply chains.
