Title: Pricing and Production Planning Under Supply and Demand Uncertainty

Author: Burak Kazaz, Associate Professor, Syracuse University, Whitman School of Management, Syracuse, NY, 13244, United States, bkazaz@syr.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We provide a comprehensive review of pricing and production planning decisions under supply and demand uncertainty. Two modeling approaches are presented for the price-setting behavior. The paper identifies how these decisions change with backlogging and lost sales independently, and later with the yield-dependent purchasing cost. The results are generalized by considering: 1) linear and iso-elastic demand functions, and 2) additive and multiplicative forms of supply and demand uncertainty.
