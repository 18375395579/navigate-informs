Title: Optimizing Product Launches in the Presence of Strategic Consumers
Author: Jigar Patel, New York University, 44 West 4th Street, KMC 8-152, New York, NY, 10012, United States of America, jigar@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Ilan Lobel, Gustavo Vulcano, Jiawei Zhang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider the problem of a technology firm deciding the optimal timing of its product launches. Consumers are strategic and weigh the value of purchasing improved products in light of future technological improvements. We completely characterize the optimal launch policies for two cases: one where the firm pre-announces the product launch times in the market, and the second where no information about the future launches is divulged. Revenue gap between both cases can be very significant.
