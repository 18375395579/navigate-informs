Title: Dynamic Pricing for Selling a Fixed Inventory of Perishable Products: Research Overview

Author: Ming Chen, Ph.D Student, University of Maryland, Robert H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, chenming@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Zhi-Long Chen

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present an overview of dynamic pricing models that consider selling a fixed inventory of perishable products. We particularly focus on models that consider clearance sales of seasonal products in retail industry. Our purpose is to provide a stat-of-the-art review and identify future research directions.
