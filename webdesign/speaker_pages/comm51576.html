Title: Is Using Target Rebates Good for Manufacturers?

Author: Wenqiang Xiao, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, United States, wx2004@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Fangruo Chen

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper studies target rebates in a manufacturer-retailer model with retailer sales efforts. We show that the manufacturer is better off by offering a single wholesale price contract, rather than a target rebate coupled with a wholesale price contract.
