Title: A Comparison and Equilibrium Analysis of Push, Pull, and Trade Credit Contracts
Author: Lingxiu Dong, Associate Professor, Olin Business School, Washington University, One Brookings Drive, Saint Louis, MO, United States of America, dong@wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Danko Turcic, Xiaomeng Guo
Year: 2012
Abstract: We consider three types of wholesale price contracts in a supply chain, including push, pull, and trade credit. The research focuses on understanding the implication of those contracts for the allocation of inventory risk among supply chain members, and the driving forces for the equilibrium contractual arrangement in the supply chain.
