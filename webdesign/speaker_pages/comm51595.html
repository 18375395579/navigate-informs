Title: Returns Policies between Channel Partners for Durable Products with Used Goods Markets
Author: Mehmet Gumus, Assistant Professor, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke West, Montreal, QC, H3A1G5, Canada, mehmet.gumus@mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Saibal Ray, Shuya Yin
Year: 2012
Abstract: Many durable products with relatively short selling seasons have been using returns policies between manufacturers and retailers as contractual protocol for quite some time. More recently, these sectors have witnessed the emergence of online used goods markets as important transaction channels. Given that these two issues are linked, in this work, we study the role of the used goods market in shaping a manufacturer's incentive to offer a returns policy option to a retailer.
