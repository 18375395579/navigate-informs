Title: Avoiding Pitfalls When Outsourcing Commodity IT Services

Author: Cynthia Beath, Department of Information, Risk and Operations Management, McCombs School of Business, CBA 5.202, University of Texas at Austin, Austin, TX, 78712-1175, United States, Cynthia.Beath@mccombs.utexas.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Outsourcing commodity IT services has proven to be problematic for both buyers and vendors. This presentation argues that successful buyers and vendors 1) Identify commodity services differently; 2) Find a partner with overlapping interests; and 3) Take actions throughout the process to ensure both parties achieve long-term benefits.
