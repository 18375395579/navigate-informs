Title: Supply Allocation to Maintain Supplier Health in Development-intensive Supply Chains

Author: Mericcan Usta, PhD Candidate, Stanford University, 245 Huang Engineering Center, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, usta@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Feryal Erhun, Warren H. Hausman

Year: 2013

Abstract: We analyze the impact of the buyer's financial health concerns of suppliers on his supplier selection. We formulate a mathematical model where multiple suppliers compete for a divisible supply across multiple periods. Inclusion of the buyer's financial health concerns reveals dynamics that have been overlooked in the previous literatures on supplier selection and supply risk.
