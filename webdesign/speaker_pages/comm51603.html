Title: An Empirical Study for Estimating Price Elasticities in the Travel Industry
Author: Pelin Pekgun, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of Industrial and Systems Engng, 765 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, ppekgun@isye.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Paul Griffin, Pinar Keskinocak
Year: 2007
Abstract: We address the challenges in estimating price sensitivity of customers in passenger travel industry through an empirical study. We show the improvement when endogeneity is accounted for, which arises as a result of simultaneous determination of supply and demand leading to high demand-high price and low demand-low price pairs in the observed data.
