Title: Fee-for-Service Contract in the Pharmaceutical Supply Chains
Author: Hui Zhao, Smeal College of Business, Penn State University, University Park, United States of America, huz10@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Chuanhui Xiong, Srinagesh Gavirneni, Adam Fein
Year: 2013
Abstract: Pharmaceutical industry takes an important role in healthcare but has received relatively less attention from supply chain researchers. In this talk, we will provide an overview of the pharma supply chain with an analysis of the Fee-for-service (FFS) contracts which dramatically changed the way pharma distribution supply chains are designed, managed, and operated. Our results address a few challenges faced by pharma manufacturers and distributors.
