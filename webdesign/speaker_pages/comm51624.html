Title: Supply Chain Power Structure and Performance

Author: Ruixia Shi, School of Management, University of Texas at Dallas, Richardson, TX, 75083, United States, rxs048100@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Jun Zhang

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the impacts of supply chain power structure on the performance of a supply chain. We model three different scenarios where the supply chain members' powers are different in the supply chain and characterize the impact of the power structure on the performance of the supply and its members.
