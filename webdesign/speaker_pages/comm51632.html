Title: Centralized vs. Decentralized Supply Chains
Author: Ann Chan, Assistant Professor, Virginia Tech, 250 Durham Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24061-0118, United States, achan@vt.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: When customer demand is stochastic and price sensitive, we study the loss of expected profit due to double marginization. We also analyze and compare the supply chain performances under different operations mechanisms: buy-back, inventory sharing, coordination and competition.
