Title: Quantity Discounts in Two Echelon Supply Chains with Transshipping Retailers
Author: Frank Ciarallo, Associate Professor, Wright State University, 3640 Col Glenn Hwy, Dayton, OH, 45435, United States of America, frank.ciarallo@wright.edu
Coauthor(s): Gregory Noble
Year: 2013
Abstract: Pricing contracts between suppliers and retailers allow a supplier to coordinate the actions of competing retailers facing uncertain demand. We study quantity discount contracts between a supplier and two competing retailers that already agree to transship stock to maximize service levels. We study different forms of discount contracts to determine which give the supplier successful coordinating influence, thus improving the performance of either suppler, retailer or the entire supply chain.
