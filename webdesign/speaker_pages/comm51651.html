Title: Supply Chain Contracting Under Asymmetric Information and Competition

Author: Shilu Tong, Department of ISMT, 408A, Tower B, HKUST, Hong Kong, China, tongsl@ust.hk

Coauthor(s): Albert Ha

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider the problem of designing optimal contracts for supply chains under horizontal competition and asymmetric information. Based on a three-stage contracting game model, we conduct a comprehensive comparison of the performance of the supply chains as well as the individual firms under different information structures. We also consider the issue of investing in vertical information sharing in the context of supply chain competition.
