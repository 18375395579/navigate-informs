Title: Managing Long-term Supplier Relationship under Changing Productivity

Author: Long Gao, Assistant Professor, UC Riverside, 221 AGSM, UCR, Riverside, United States of America, long.gao@ucr.edu

Coauthor(s): Elodie Adida

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a procurement problem where the supplier's private productivity is dynamically evolving. The buyer commits to a long-term contract for profit-maximizing. We show that the optimal contract spreads out the needed distortions over time to limit information rents. The contract is asymptotically efficient and easy to implement.
