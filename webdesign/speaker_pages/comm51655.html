Title: Impact of Item-level RFID on Product Availability and Forecasting in Retail

Author: Gary Gaukler, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, College Station, TX, United States, gaukler@tamu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk, we consider a two-stage retail supply chain with a periodic-review, order-up to inventory system with lost sales. In this setting, we explore the impact that item-level RFID has on the availability of products on the retail shelf, as well as on the demand forecasts made by a retailer.
