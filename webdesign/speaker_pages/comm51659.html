Title: Incentive Contracts in Sequential Projects

Author: Tony Chen, University of Washington, Box 353200, Foster School of Business, Seattle, WA, 98195-3226, United States of America, tony0906@uw.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Wagner, Ted Klastorin

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate how incentive contracts and delayed payment terms affect a contractor’s decision when managing a large scale sequential project which is outsourced to n independent subcontractors. Our paper provide efficient linear time algorithms for the contractor to find optimal decision variables and explains how these contract can lead to undesired results when misused.
