Title: A Product Line Problem in a Retail Environment with Stochastic Demand

Author: Gal Raz, Associate Professor, Darden Graduate School of Business, University of Virginia, Charlottesville, VA, 22906-6550, United States, razg@darden.virginia.edu

Coauthor(s): Oded Koenigsberg

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper investigates a retailer's decision on the variety of products to offer while simultaneously deciding on their prices and quantities under a stochastic demand newsvendor environment that takes into account the customer heterogeneous valuation of the products in the product line. We derive the retailer’s optimal strategy and examine the effect of the problem parameters on this strategy.
