Title: Understanding the Appeal of Suboptimal Contracts
Author: Anna Devlin, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, United States of America, adevlin@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Wedad Elmaghraby, Rebecca Hamilton
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this research, we design an experiment to test behavioral biases driving supplier preferences for economically inferior contracts. The experiment incorporates a two period selling season during which the computerized retailer may sell items on sale. The results indicate subjects are averse to contracts that expose them to retailer effort.
