Title: Inventory Information Disclosure Tactics to Leverage Stock-out Risk

Author: Tolga Aydinliyim, Lundquist College of Business, University of Oregon, Eugene, OR, 97403, United States of America, tolga@uoregon.edu

Coauthor(s): Elliot Rabinovich, Michael Pangburn, Min Choi

Year: 2012

Abstract: We investigate whether an online retailer should divulge its stocking level, or merely indicate availability via the “in stock?cue, while optimally pricing its product. We find, analytically, that disclosing accurate inventory information can mitigate strategic consumer behavior, and support this finding via an empirical study of two online retailers.
