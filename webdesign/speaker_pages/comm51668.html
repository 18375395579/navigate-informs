Title: Operational Impacts of Monopolistic and Competitive WEEE Collection Schemes
Author: Fuminori Toyasaki, Postdoctoral Research Fellow, McGill University, Faculty of Management, 1001 Sherbrooke Street West, Montreal, H3A 1G5, Canada, fuminori.toyasaki@mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Vedat Verter, Tamer Boyaci
Year: 2006
Abstract: This research investigates the operational efficiency of a monopolistic and a competitive collection system for WEEE from the perspective of EEE manufacturers and consumers. We assume that EEE manufacturers compete with each other strategically in a Bertrand oligopoly form. We assume that in the both systems the behavior of the EEE manufacturers and collection schemes can be represented by a leader-follower game.
