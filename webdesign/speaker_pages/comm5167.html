Title: Sales Force Compensation for Remanufactured Products
Author: Jeremy Kovach, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree Street NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, jeremy.kovach@scheller.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Atalay Atasu, Sumitro Banerjee
Year: 2013
Abstract: We investigate sales force management practices for a firm that engages in remanufacturing. The firm sells new and remanufactured products in the same market and incentivizes sales agents to increase demand. We determine the structure of the ideal compensation plans to be offered to the sales agents and how sales force incentives affect the profitability of remanufacturing.
