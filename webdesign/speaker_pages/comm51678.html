Title: Endogenous Price Leadership in a Competition Supply Chain Comprising an OEM and Its Competitive ODM

Author: Baozhuang Niu, Assistant Professor, Sun Yat-Sen University, Lingnan College, No. 135, Xingangxi Road, Guangzhou, China, niubzh@mail.sysu.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Yulan Wang, Pengfei Guo

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study three price competition games between an original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and an original design manufacturer (ODM). We investigate their endogenous price timing game. The ODM provides manufacturing service and competes with its OEM by selling self-branded products. We consider two markets: the ODM market and the OEM market.
