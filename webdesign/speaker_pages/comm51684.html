Title: Impact of Introducing a Direct Channel on Supply Chain Performance

Author: Xuemei Su, College of Business Administration, California State University, Long Beach, Long Beach, CA, 90840, United States, xuemeisu@uwm.edu

Coauthor(s): Lifang Wu, Xiaohang Yue

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the incentives for a manufacturer to introduce a direct channel in a stochastic demand environment. We find that the customer acceptance of direct channel, cross channel price sensitivity and the difference in distribution costs of the two channels play important roles in determining the existence of dual channel in equilibrium.
