Title: Partner Selection in Contracting for Capacity under Renegotiation
Author: Eda Kemahlioglu Ziya, UNC-Chapel Hill, CB # 3490, Chapel Hill, NC, United States of America, Eda_KemahliogluZiya@unc.edu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Consider a supply chain with a single supplier of capacity and two buyers. The buyers first negotiate their unit prices with the supplier and place orders. After demand realization they are allowed to trade capacity. The questions we are interested in include: How does capacity trading change the optimal order quantities? Do the players prefer to partner with strong or weak negotiators?
