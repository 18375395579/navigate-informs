Title: Funding Liquidity Risk in a Game Theoretic Model with Recovery

Author: Andrey Krishenik, Cornell, 292 Rhodes Hall, Hoy Rd, Ithaca, United States of America, ak757@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Andreea Minca, Robert Jarrow

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a one period model for a company that finances its operations through short-term debt. The company receives the money from a continuum of short-term lenders and invests in a risky, divisible asset, it can also hold a certain amount of liquid reserves, that we refer to as ``cash. We find that it can be profitable for the company to hold some riskless cash as the investment.
