Title: Strategies for Bundling Durable Products with Contingent Services or Consumable Products

Author: Sinan Erzurumlu, Doctoral Student, University of Texas at Austin, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, Sinan.Erzurumlu@phd.mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen Gilbert

Year: 2006

Abstract: We investigate the issue of whether and how a durable goods monopolist should bundle his product with contingent services or consumable products depending upon on the costs of production and distribution and the competitiveness of the industry for the contingent products. Our analysis identifies conditions under which the durable goods monopolist should bundle the two products, sell them separately, or exit the market for the contingent products.
