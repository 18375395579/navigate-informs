Title: Managing Suppliers: Joint Audit and Shared Supplier Information

Author: Xin Fang, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, xfang@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Soo-Haeng Cho

Year: 2013

Abstract: Product safety incidents in recent years have compelled many manufacturers to rethink approaches to manage product quality of their suppliers. In this paper, we consider two approaches: joint audit and sharing quality information of suppliers. Based on a model with both cooperative and competitive interactions between firms, we investigate the impacts of the two approaches on product quality and the incentives of manufacturers to conduct joint audit or share their private supplier information.
