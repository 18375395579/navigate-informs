Title: Markets for Remanufactured Products: An Empirical Analysis of Buyer and Seller Characteristics

Author: Ravi Subramanian, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree St. NW, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, ravi.subramanian@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Ramanath Subramanyam

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present an empirical analysis of market characteristics of remanufactured products, including willingness to pay (WTP), buyer expertise, seller reputation, and post-purchase buyer satisfaction, using a dataset of actual purchases of such products.
