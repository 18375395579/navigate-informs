Title: When Does Advance Selling Benefit Manufacturers, Retailers, or Both?
Author: Xuying Zhao, University of Notre Dame, Mendoza College of Business, Notre Dame, United States of America, Xuying.Zhao.29@nd.edu
Coauthor(s): Zhan Pang, Kathryn Stecke
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a supply chain with a retailer who has advance selling capability. We show having the capability of advance selling can hurt the retailer's profit, the supply chain profit, and social welfare, while always benefits the manufacturer.
