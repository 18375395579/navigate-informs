Title: Comparative Analysis of Markup and Markdown Pricing Policies in Revenue Management Problems

Author: Wei Guo, University of Minnesota, Twin Cities, 111 Church Street, Minneapolis, MN, 55414, United States of America, guo@aem.umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Zizhuo Wang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We compare the expected revenue generated from two pricing policies for a finite horizon revenue management problem: markup only and markdown only. We study the optimal solutions for each policy and show that the markdown policy is superior to the markup policy when inventory level is low and the remaining time is ample; otherwise the markup policy is superior. We verify our findings through numerical tests.
