Title: Procurement Auctions with Costly Supplier Qualification Screening

Author: Zhixi Wan, PhD candidate, University of Michigan, 701 Tappan Street, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, wanzhixi@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Damian Beil

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider reverse auctions in combination with supplier qualification screening to determine which qualified supplier will be awarded a contract. The buyer seeks to minimize the contract payment plus qualification costs. We explore benefits of qualifying some suppliers only after the auction, and the role offully pre-qualified incumbents.
