Title: Try Before You Buy: Consumer Learning in Rental Markets

Author: Pnina Feldman, University of California-Berkeley, 545 Student Services Bldg, #1900, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, feldman@haas.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Jose Guajardo, Dimin Xu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Rental markets have expanded to non-traditional settings, including markets for exclusive goods. While introducing rental markets may cannibalize sales, it may also benefit firms. We investigate how the interplay between the sales and rental markets is affected by the presence of consumer learning.
