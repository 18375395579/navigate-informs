Title: Revenue Management with Last Minute Getaways
Author: Srinivas Krishnamoorthy, Assistant Professor, Ivey School of Business, The University of Western Ontario, 1151 Richmond Street North, London, ON, N6A 3K7, Canada, skrishnamoorthy@ivey.ca
Year: 2006
Abstract: In recent years the travel industry has seen the development of a whole new market: the last minute leisure travelers. We show that the opportunity to sell “distressed?inventory through online channels to such last minute impulse travelers allows providers to increase revenues and load factor. While there is a chance of some revenue leakage because of travelers postponing purchases in anticipation of last minute sales, providers can overall benefit by this selling strategy.
