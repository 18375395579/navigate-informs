Title: Optimal Bundling with Dependent Valuations

Author: Fredrik Odegaard, Ivey Business School, Western University, London, ON, Canada, fodegaard@ivey.uwo.ca

Coauthor(s): Mihai Banciu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Bundling together two or more separate products and selling as a whole, is a prevalent industry and revenue management practice, e.g. airlines offering add-on services. In this talk, we introduce a novel approach of pricing bundles which explicitly addresses the relationship between the individual valuations of the components. We contrast our approach with the typical assumption of independent valuations and discuss the revenue implications of ignoring dependence.
