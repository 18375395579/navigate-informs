Title: Valuation of Natural Gas Storage Capacity Incorporating Forward Price Curve

Author: Jieyun Zhou, jzhou@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Shi-Jie Deng

Year: 2008

Abstract: We propose a model for natural gas storage capacity valuation in which the operational strategies of the storage consist of both spot and forwards trading actions.We derive a market-based valuation and the corresponding optimal operating and trading strategy, taking account of various operational constraints and realistic market characteristics.
