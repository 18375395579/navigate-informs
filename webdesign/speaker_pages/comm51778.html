Title: Dynamic Pricing with Patient Customers

Author: Yan Liu, University of Minnesota, 111 Church ST, ME L123, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, liux0984@umn.edu

Coauthor(s): William Cooper

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider an infinite horizon dynamic pricing problem with patient customers. Each arriving customer will purchase a product immediately if the price is below his valuation. Otherwise, such a customer will wait a fixed number of time periods and purchase a product as soon as the price goes below his valuation. If price remains above his valuation, the customer will depart without making a purchase. We identify the structure of an optimal policy and discuss extensions.
