Title: Efficient Channel Contracting in Vertically Differentiated Environments

Author: Mehmet Sekip Altug, PhD Candidate, Columbia University, 3022 Broadway, Uris Hall 4J, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, msa2104@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Garrett van Ryzin

Year: 2007

Abstract: We model a manufacturer that supplies high and low quality versions of a product to a retailer. In this vertically differentiated environment all consumers prefer higher quality, though they have different valuations for quality. We analyze contracts the supplier can offer to achieve channel coordination. We also analyze coordination with competing retailers.
