Title: A Multi-source Inventory System with Order Tracking and Expediting
Author: Jeannette Song, Professor, Duke University, Fuqua School of Business, 1 Towerview Dr., Durham, NC, 27708, United States of America, jssong@duke.edu
Coauthor(s): Kamran Moinzadeh, Yongpin Zhou, Paul Zipkin
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider an inventory system with multiple supply sources and expediting options. The replenishment leadtimes from each supply source are stochastic, representing congestion and disruption. We develop performance evaluation tools for a family of reasonable ordering and expediting policies that take into account real-time supply information,
