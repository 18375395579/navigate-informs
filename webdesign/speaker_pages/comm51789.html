Title: The Implications of Customer Purchasing Behavior and Display Formats

Author: Rui Yin, Assistant Professor, Arizona State University, Department of Supply Chain Management, W. P. Carey School of Business, Tempe, AZ, 85287-4706, United States, ryin@anderson.ucla.edu

Coauthor(s): Yossi Aviv, Amit Pazgal, Christopher Tang

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider two types of customer purchasing behavior (myopic and strategic) and two types of display formats (Display All and Display One) on a retailer’s pricing and ordering decisions and his expected profit. We consider a Stackelberg game between the retailer and the customers as well as the competitive sub-game between customers. The existence and uniqueness of a threshold type Nash equilibrium for the sub-game between customers are established.
