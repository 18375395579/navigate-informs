Title: How Does Buyer’s Knowledge Impact Outsourcing Strategies? A Game Theoretic Model
Author: Qiong Chen, Clemson University, 100 Sirrine Hall, Clemson University, Clemson, SC, 29634, United States of America, qiongc@clemson.edu
Coauthor(s): Shouqiang Wang, Gulru Ozkan, Aleda Roth
Year: 2013
Abstract: We introduce a signaling game to examine the outsourcing strategies of a buying firm that faces two options in determining a manufacturer to produce its new product: search directly or indirectly (through an intermediary). We demonstrate that buyer’s knowledge about supply market influences its strategy and the intermediary’s effort level.
