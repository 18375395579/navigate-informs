Title: Effects of Product Architecture on Market Segmentation and Pricing Strategies
Author: Weiyan Liu, School of Management Xi'an Jiaotong University, No.28 XianNing West Road, Xi'an ShanXi, 710049, China, 2567090358@qq.com
Coauthor(s): Qin Su
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider the impact of product architecture on pricing strategies of a monopoly and his suppliers where the monopoly’s product line includes two substitutes which are different in product architecture .We analyze the differences of market shares when the monopoly buys components separately or modules as a whole from the suppliers. We find that the product architecture has such a significant effect that higher profit is actually realized at the sacrifice of one substitute’s market share.
