Title: Sequential Innovation with Strategic Consumers and Suppliers
Author: Karthik Ramachandran, Assistant Professor, Southern Methodist University, Cox School of Business, PO Box 750333, Dallas, TX, 75275, United States, karthik@cox.smu.edu
Coauthor(s): Vish Krishnan
Year: 2007
Abstract: Modular upgradability has been shown to be valuable for both manufacturers and consumers of improving products. However, a time-inconsistency problem arises if (i) Strategic consumers if the future versions of the product design would continue to be modular, or (ii) Strategic suppliers of stable subsystems seek to appropriate gains from product improvement. We formalize this two-pronged design inconsistency problem and offer an integrated product-design and module-pricing based solution.
