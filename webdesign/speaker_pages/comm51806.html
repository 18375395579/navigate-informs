Title: Coordinated Dynamic Control of Marketing and Production
Author: Jian Yang, Assistant Professor, New Jersey Institute of Technology, Department of Industrial Engineering, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States, yang@adm.njit.edu
Coauthor(s): Xiaolong Zhang
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study the dynamic profit maximization problem for a firm who exercises control on both marketing and production. The firm's marketing effort impacts the demand it faces, and the current-period demand in turn affects future demands in a subdued fashion. We find characterizations of optimal control policies.
