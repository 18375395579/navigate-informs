Title: Rank Versus Full Feedback in Procurement Auctions
Author: Wedad Elmaghraby, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, Wedad_Elmaghraby@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Elena Katok
Year: 2008
Abstract: There has been a move in recent years away from full feedback auctions (where bidders can see each others prices in realtime) towards rank feedback auction. Under Rank feedback, bidders see only their current bid ranking. Via experiments, we test the theoretical prediction of rank and full feedback when bidders have asymmetric quality and costs. We find that rank feedback, combined with uniform reserve prices, sometimes induces bidders to behave equivalently to a sealed-bid format.
