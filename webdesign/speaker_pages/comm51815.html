Title: Reverse Supply Chain Design for Multiple Channel Product Returns
Author: Aejaz Khan, amkhan4@mail.uh.edu
Coauthor(s): Daniel Guide
Year: 2008
Abstract: Manufacturers manage a growing volume of product returns, while retailers offer multiple sales channels and convenient return policies to build customer loyalty. The need to design strategies for manufacturer-retailer coordination motivates the discussion in this paper. We consider three reverse supply chain designs and determine parameters that influence the design choice.
