Title: Auctions for Deliberative Agents
Author: David Thompson, University of British Columbia, 201-2366 Main Mall, Vancouver, BC, V6T 1Z4, Canada, daveth@cs.ubc.ca
Coauthor(s): Kevin Leyton-Brown
Year: 2007
Abstract: In auctions, agents might not know their exact valuations because of transaction costs or bounded rationality. We show settings where if agents don't coordinate value discovery, it can affect efficiency and revenue. For sealed-bid auctions, we show revenue and value-of-information bounds and where revenue equivalence applies.
