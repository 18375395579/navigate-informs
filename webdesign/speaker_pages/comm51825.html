Title: Peak Policy for Reparable Parts

Author: Carol DeZwarte, Logistics Management Institute, 2000 Corporate Ridge, McLean, VA, 22102-7805, United States of America, cdezwarte@lmi.org

Coauthor(s): Tovey Bachman

Year: 2007

Abstract: Some repair parts with sporadic demand are vital to maintaining weapon systems, but their demand uncertainty makes buy and repair decisions hard. Our Peak Policy for buying consumable parts reduces wholesale delay 20 to 50% for a given inventory investment. We now extend Peak Policy to reparable items with sporadic demand. We discuss the challenges, show how we adapted the Peak Policy to improve support for these items, and conclude with management insights. Peak Policy is a trademark of LMI.
