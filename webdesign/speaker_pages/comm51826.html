Title: Coalition Stability in Assembly Models

Author: Greys Sosic, Assistant Professor, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, Bridge Hall 401, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, sosic@marshall.usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Mahesh Nagarajan

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this talk, we examine a decentralized assembly system where n component manufacturers sell to a single downstream assembler who faces deterministic price sensitive demand. We look at three types of competition -- Supplier Stackelberg, Vertical Nash, and Assembler Stackelberg -- and in each type allow the suppliers to freely form coalitions among themselves. Using dynamic concepts of stability, we predict the structure of the stable supplier coalitions.
