Title: Bounded Rationality in Newsvendor Models
Author: Xuanming Su, University of California, Haas School of Business, Berkeley, 94720, United States, xuanming@haas.berkeley.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: We propose a decision framework of bounded rationality. While the best decision need not always be made, better decisions are made more often. We identify systematic over-ording and under-ordering biases, and we analyze a data-set of newsvendor-type decisions made by individual subjects in a laboratory setting. We also investigate the impact of these biases on other inventory settings, such as supply chain contracting, the bullwhip effect, and inventory pooling.
