Title: Dynamic Optimal Pricing and Platform Evolution

Author: Sinit Vitavasiri, PhD Candidate, Stanford University, Knight Management Center, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, sinit@stanford.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: I introduce a dynamic framework to analyze platform's optimal pricing strategy for a network product. In the Markov perfect equilibrium, the platform's market power is weakened by the network effect and the damage to future sale. The intensity of the network effect and the ability to commit to a future price path determine the optimal pricing rule, the long-run distribution of the market share, and the risk of failure to launch.
