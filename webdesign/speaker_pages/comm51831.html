Title: Stage-Gate Contracts for New Product Development

Author: Chunlin Wang, David Eccles School of Business, University of Utah, Salt Lake City, chunlin.wang@business.utah.edu

Coauthor(s): Krishnan S. Anand, Glen Schmidt

Year: 2012

Abstract: We gain new insights into why a firm might use a stage-gate contract when outsourcing New Product Development. The contract helps address both adverse-selection and moral hazard issues. We study the optimal number and placement of gates, and compare the performance of stage-gate contracts with traditional outsourcing contracts.
