Title: Supply Diagnostic Incentives in New Product Development
Author: Mehmet Gumus, McGill University, Montreal, QC, Canada, mehmet.gumus@mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Mohammad Nikoofal
Year: 2013
Abstract: In launching new products, the newness of the production leads to unexpected reliability issues on supply side. The adverse effect of supply risk can be mitigated via test production. We explore how such a diagnostic technology investment may affect both incentive and information asymmetries across channel partners.
