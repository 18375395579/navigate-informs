Title: Managing Revenues in Alliances under Incomplete Information
Author: Christopher Wright, Simon Graduate School of Business, University of Rochester, Rochester, NY, 14627, United States of America, WRIGHTC1@simon.rochester.edu
Coauthor(s): Harry Groenevelt, Robert Shumsky
Year: 2008
Abstract: We examine dynamic transfer price schemes in airline alliances, under the assumption that the airlines do not share information about demand forecasts and inventory. We explore the airlines?equilibrium policies under this game and compare the policies, as well as the overall performance of the alliance, to both the centralized control (first-best) alliance and an alliance that shares complete information.
