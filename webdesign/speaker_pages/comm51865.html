Title: Optimal Front-End Fare Discounts in Airline Corporate Contracts

Author: Murat Erkoc, merkoc@miami.edu

Coauthor(s): Julian Pachon

Year: 2006

Abstract: We propose a nonlinear programming model to design optimal corporate contracts for airlines stipulating front-end discounts for all nets, which are defined by combination of routes, cabin types, and fare classes. The airline’s profit is modeled using a multinomial logit function that captures the client’s choice behavior in a competitive market. We investigate the impact of price elasticity, demand, and competition on optimal discounting policies.
