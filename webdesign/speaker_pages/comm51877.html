Title: Optimal Pricing for Services Bundles with Down-payments: Monopoly vs. Duopoly

Author: Juan-Carlos Ferrer, Assistant Professor, P. Universidad Catolica de Chile, Casilla 306 Correo 22, Santiago, 00, Chile, jferrer@ing.puc.cl

Coauthor(s): Hugo Mora, Gabriel Bitran

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine the pricing decision of a provider that offers a bundle that includes a physical product with a high setup cost, and its associated services by means of a subscription. A two-part tariff scheme is used which consists on a down-payment (a fraction of the setup cost) and a periodic fee. We examine the monopoly and duopoly market scenarios, finding near optimal fixed-price policies, and that duopoly firms use the down-payment to protect themselves from the effect of customers switching.
