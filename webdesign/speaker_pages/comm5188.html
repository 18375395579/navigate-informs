Title: A Combined Auction-bargaining Model in Procurement

Author: Kun Soo Park, Ph.D Student, IEOR Department, Columbia University, 305 S.W.Mudd Building,, 500 West 120th Street, New York, NY, 10027, United States, kp2143@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Woonghee Tim Huh

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a two-phase procurement system where a risk-neutral buyer procures one unit of an indivisible good from one of many competing sellers. In the auction phase, the buyer selects the seller with the most competitive bid; in the subsequent bargaining phase, the buyer enters into the bargaining process with the selected seller after discovering his true cost.
