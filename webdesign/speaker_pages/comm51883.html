Title: Managing Switching Behavior with Presence of Price Tracking Customers

Author: Fouad H. Mirzaei, Ivey Business School, Western University, London, ON, N6G4X3, Canada, fhm.phd@ivey.ca

Coauthor(s): Fredrik Odegaard, Xinghao Yan

Year: 2013

Abstract: Many airlines permit passengers to change a purchased ticket for another flight by paying a penalty. This switching penalty is a revenue item for the airline, however a high fee could increase the risk of lost sales. The question that arises is how much penalty should be set for switching, knowing that the magnitude of switching fee impacts on switching behaviour of ticket holders. We model a single firm, which triggers customers?switching behavior by markdown pricing policy.
