Title: Startegic Consumer Impact on Bankruptcy and Competition
Author: John Birge, Professor, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, john.birge@chicagobooth.edu
Coauthor(s): Rodney Parker, Song Alex Yang
Year: 2012
Abstract: In a previous model of firm competition under the threat of one firm's bankruptcy, we showed how that firm could use bankruptcy strategically to gain concessions from a dominant supplier. In this work, we consider the effect on the supply chain from consumers who anticipate future price discounts in the event of a bankruptcy.
