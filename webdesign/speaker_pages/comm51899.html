Title: Competition and Diversification in Supply Chains

Author: Shanshan Wang, PhD Student of Management Science/Operation Management, University of Chicago, Graduate School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, CHICAGO, IL, 60637, United States of America, swang5@chicagogsb.edu

Coauthor(s): Dan Adelman

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper, we consider a decentralized supply chain with multiple unreliable suppliers and one buyer. We study conditions that elicit the buyer to diversify her orders across suppliers, and the effect of competition between suppliers on this behavior.
