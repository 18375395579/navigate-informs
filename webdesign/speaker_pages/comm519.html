Title: Measuring Consumer Willingness-to-pay for Socially Responsible Products

Author: Leon Valdes, PhD Student, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Sloan School of Management, Boston, MA, United States of America, lvaldes@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Yanchong Zheng, Tim Kraft

Year: 2013

Abstract: We experimentally study whether consumers are willing to pay higher prices for products with more accurate information regarding their social benefits. We show that consumers?willingness to pay does not decrease with higher information accuracy, and it is non-decreasing in the expected value of social benefits. We compare our results to a scenario where competitive products with different prices and information accuracy are presented to the consumers.
