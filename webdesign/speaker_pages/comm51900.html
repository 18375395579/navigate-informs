Title: Randomized Supply Contracts

Author: Rene Caldentey, New York University, Stern School of Business, 44 West Fourth Street, New York, United States of America, rcaldent@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Martin Haugh

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the performance of a supply chain with multiple competing retailers and a single Producer. We assume that retailers are budget-constrained and are therefore limited in the number of units they may purchase from the producer. We study the optimal design of time-dependent procurement contracts. We also quantify the value that financial markets bring to the operations of a cash-constrained supply chain.
