Title: Time Series Forecasting for Perishable Product Prices

Author: Heping Liu, Senior Science Analyst, SignalDemand Inc., 101 California Street, Suite 1950, San Francisco, CA, 94121, United States of America, hepingliu@yahoo.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: This study explores how to apply the time series forecasting model to predict the prices of perishable products. The time series approach is based on the auto-regressive moving average frame, and the seasonality and trend hidden in the price data are captured by the seasonality and trend terms (or functions), respectively. The suggested model has the good generalization structure and is shown to have the potential application value for the price forecast of perishable products.
