Title: Analysis of Supplier's Benefits under Vendor Managed Inventory
Author: Secil Savasaneril, Assistant Professor, Middle East Technical University, Industrial Engineering Department, Ankara, Turkey, secil@ie.metu.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Nesim Erkip
Year: 2007
Abstract: The benefits of Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) are often attributed to information sharing between the supplier and the retailer, however, there is more to VMI than just the information availability; there are benefits hidden in the increased flexibility of the supplier's manufacturing operations. We analyze how the supplier can translate this flexibility into benefit, and determine the conditions that make VMI a viable strategy for the supplier.
