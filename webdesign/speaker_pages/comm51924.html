Title: A Descent Method for Differential VIs Arising in Non-cooperative Differential Nash Equilibrium

Author: Changhyun Kwon, Ph.D Candidate, The Pennsylvania State University, 310 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States, chkwon@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Terry Friesz

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper, we consider a gap function for differential variational inequalities (DVIs) and study an equivalent optimal control problem. In particular, we employ a differential gap function -- the so-called D-gap function -- and its gradient to form a descent method for DVIs. An application of DVIs to differential Nash games is also investigated. In particular, we solve an abstract linear-quadratic differential Nash game and a model of oligopolistic competition.
