Title: Price Commitments with Strategic Consumers
Author: Pnina Feldman, University of California-Berkeley, 545 Student Services Bldg, #1900, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, feldman@haas.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Gerard Cachon
Year: 2013
Abstract: Firms may benefit from making price commitment when consumers are strategic. But which price commitment strategy should firms implement? We examine commonly used pricing strategies and propose a new pricing strategy in which the firm commits to discount more frequently than optimal. In a setting where consumers are strategic and incur a cost to search the firm, we show that discount frequently performs the best and in many cases it is optimal (equivalent to maximum social welfare).
