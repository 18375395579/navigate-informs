Title: A Multi Attribute Model for Supplier Performance for an Industrial Equipment Manufacturer
Author: Richard J. Titus Jr., Industrial Engineering Department, The Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA, 16802, rjt186@psu.edu
Year: 2006
Abstract: An empirical study of an industrial equipment manufacturer’s supplier quality and delivery performance against select supplier attributes such as quality certifications (ex. ISO9000, etc.), the use of quality techniques (such as Statistical Process Control), financial ratings, product complexity, product differentiation, supplier size, distance between supplier and customer location and financial leverage or purchasing power was performed in order to evaluate the relationship of these key attributes to supplier performance.
