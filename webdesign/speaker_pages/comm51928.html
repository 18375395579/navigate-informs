Title: A Strategic Approach to Hedging and Contracting
Author: Danko Turcic, Assistant Professor of Operations, Washington University in St Louis, Olin Business School, St Louis, MO, 63105, United States of America, turcic@wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Panos Kouvelis, Guang Xiao
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a model, where two identical firms compete in the output market and identify conditions under which neither firm hedges its production inputs, only firm hedges, and both firms hedge. In doing so, we provide a new rationale for hedging that is based on competitive behavior in product markets.
