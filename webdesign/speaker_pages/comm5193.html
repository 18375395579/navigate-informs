Title: Capacity Investment and Pricing Strategy under Product Evolution
Author: Xiao Huang, PhD Candidate, University of Southern California, 3670 Trousdale Pkwy, Bridge Hall 401, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States of America, xiao.huang.2009@marshall.usc.edu
Coauthor(s): Greys Sosic
Year: 2008
Abstract: Consider a market with two products, in which the new product is on some attributes inferior to the old one. Our model consists of an entrant who can only provide the new product, and an incumbent who can provide both. A segment of the customers are dedicated to the new product, while the rest can purchase either. We study how the firms strategically determine their capacities and prices under product evolution, and characterize equilibria under different assumptions about the uncertainty.
