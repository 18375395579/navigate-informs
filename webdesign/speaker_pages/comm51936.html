Title: Real Options Analysis of Revenue Management: The Case of Pre-sale Housing Market
Author: Zhan (James) Pang, Ph.D Candidate, SE&EM CUHK, R609 M. M. W. Engineering Building, Chinese University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong - ROC, zpang@se.cuhk.edu.hk
Coauthor(s): Youyi Feng
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a house developer who designs an options contract in a presales (sales-before-completion) housing market. The spot price in the existing housing market is assumed to follow a diffusion process. The demand approaches housing systems as a Poisson stream and the arrival rate is sensitive to the premium and affected by spot price. The premium of each house is dynamically adusted. Finally, the optimal contract design issue and the optimal capacity investment strategy are developed.
