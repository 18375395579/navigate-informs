Title: Decentralization in Dynamic Pricing and Network Revenue Management

Author: Peng Li, University of California, Berkeley, Department of Industrial Engineering, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, pengli@ieor.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): J. George Shanthikumar, Andrew Lim

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the problem of decentralization in dynamic pricing and network revenue management problems. We consider both the case where agents are risk neutral as well as when they are risk averse (with different aversion levels), and determine conditions under which decentralization which achieves a centralized optimal can be achieved.
