Title: Nash Equilibria in Duopolistic Power Markets under Diverse Pricing and Uplift Mechanism
Author: Uday Shanbhag, Pennsylvania State University, 353 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, udaybag@engr.psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Sean Meyn, Gui Wang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a capacitated duopolistic power market in which firms bid their offer functions. Allocation decisions are specified through commitment problems while pricing is considered via either marginal cost or convex hull pricing mechanisms. Furthermore, most markets are complicated by varying uplift mechanisms. The resulting Nash games are characterized by discontinuous payoff functions and existence is not immediate. We investigate the existence of of Nash equilibria in such settings.
