Title: Distributing a Product Line in a Decentralized Supply Chain

Author: Jing Shao, University of British Columbia, Vanouver, Vancouver, BC, Canada, jing.shao@sauder.ubc.ca

Coauthor(s): Thomas Mccormick, Harish Krishnan

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study a manufacturer who distributes a horizontally differentiated product line through competing retailers. We investigate how retailers?incentives are affected by the substitution between products as well as between retailers. The incentive distortions in a decentralized supply chain are characterized, and various contracts that coordinate the channel are provided.
