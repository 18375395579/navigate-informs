Title: Inter-temporal Product Line Strategies under Refurbishing of Consumer Returns
Author: Narendra Singh, Ph.D. Candidate, Georgia Institute of Technology, Scheller College of Business, 800 West Peachtree NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, Narendra.Singh@scheller.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Ravi Subramanian, Karthik Ramachandran
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study product line decisions for a firm offering a new product in the first period and both new and refurbished products in the second period to strategic consumers. The refurbished products offered in the second period are from consumer returns of the new products sold in the first period. Under certain conditions, we find that the profit of the firm is non-monotone in the return rate.
