Title: Corn or Soybean: Optimal Farm Space Allocation under Yield and Price

Author: Cerag Pince, cerag.pince@the-klu.org

Coauthor(s): Onur Boyabatli, Javad Nasiry

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider farm space allocation decision between corn and soybean in a multi-period setting. In each period the farmer decides the allocation due to yield and spot price uncertainties given the cross-farming benefit, i.e, the yield is stochastically larger when a product is not grown in the same farm space for two consecutive periods. We investigate the impact of yield and price variability for each product, and the correlation among these uncertainties on the optimal farm allocation decision.
