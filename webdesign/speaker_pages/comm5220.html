Title: Supporting Travelocity’s Transition to Travel Retailer and Economic Turn-Around
Author: Barry Smith, Sabre Holdings, 3150 Sabre Drive, Southlake, TX, 76092, United States, Barry.Smith@sabre-holdings.com
Year: 2006
Abstract: In 2002, Travelocity experienced revenue and share loss due to competition and a changing business environment. Travelocity and Sabre collaborated to improve performance through modeling of customer behavior, supplier agreements and product pricing. Since then, these models have contributed over $54 million to Travelocity earnings, at a current rate of $43 million per year.
