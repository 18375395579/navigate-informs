Title: Retail Assortment Competition: Effect of Inventory Costs on the Assortment Decisions

Author: Nan Xia, Ph.D Candidate, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, Nan.Xia.2008@marshall.usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Yehuda Bassok

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a modeling framework to analyze the assortment competition between two retailers. From a given set of product variants of a category, both retailers must determine what variants to stock as well as the purchasing quantities. Taken into account of the consumer choice, we obtain interesting structural properties of the optimal assortment strategy and the equilibrium assortments. We also analyze the effects of various factors on the equilibrium assortments.
