Title: Push or Pull? Auctioning Supply Contracts

Author: Cuihong Li, University of Connecticut, School of Business, Storrs, CT, United States, Cuihong.Li@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Scheller-Wolf

Year: 2007

Abstract: Consider a buyer that sources from multiple potential suppliers via a competitive bidding process. The suppliers have heterogeneous production costs, which are private information. The buyer faces uncertain demand, and the winning supplier has to invest in production capacity before this uncertainty is resolved. We analyze and compare the outcomes of auctions for push and pull contracts.
