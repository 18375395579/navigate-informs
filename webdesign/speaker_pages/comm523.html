Title: Competition and Coordination in a Two-channel Supply Chain

Author: Amy David, University of Illinois at Chicago, 1615 Monroe Street, #1S, Evanston, IL, 60202, United States of America, amydavid@uic.edu

Coauthor(s): Elodie Adida

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study competition and coordination in a supply chain in which a single supplier both operates a direct channel and sells its product through multiple independent retailers competing in quantities. We find that the two-channel supply chain may be subject to inefficiencies not present in the traditional supply chain, and suggest tactics for remediation.
