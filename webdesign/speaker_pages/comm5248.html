Title: The Implications of Product Modularity for Leasing vs. Selling

Author: Sezer Ulku, Georgetown University, Washington, DC, United States of America, su8@georgetown.edu

Coauthor(s): Vishal Agrawal, Atalay Atasu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Product architecture (i.e., integral vs. modular) and business model (i.e., selling vs. leasing) choices are two key strategic decisions. In this paper, we investigate a firm's joint choice of product architecture and business model, and identify when and how leasing and product modularity enhance or hurt the economic and environmental performance of a firm.
