Title: An Empirical Analysis of Power in Supply Networks: A Buyer Supplier Perspective

Author: Steven Carnovale, Ph.D. Candidate, Rutgers Business School, 1 Washington Park, Room 1057B, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States of America, scarnova@pegasus.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Sengun Yeniyurt

Year: 2013

Abstract: A common control mechanism in supply-networks are equity-based arrangements such as joint-ventures. Yet, firms are often at the behest of powerful suppliers or buyers. We take a network-theory perspective of power in supply-chain-networks and empirically investigate the role that power plays for buyers and suppliers in new manufacturing joint-venture formations.
