Title: Cargo Revenue Management with Allotments

Author: Huseyin Topaloglu, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, School of ORIE, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, ht88@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Mikhail Nediak, Yuri Levin

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a problem faced by an airline that operates several parallel flights to transport cargo between a particular origin destination pair. The airline can sell its cargo capacity either through allotment contracts or on the spot market. The goal is to simultaneously choose allotment contracts among available bids, assign these contracts to flights, and find a booking control policy for the spot market so as to maximize the total expected profit.
