Title: Effect of Manufacturer Rebates in the Automobile Industry - An Empirical Study
Author: Banu Yuksel Ozkaya, Hacettepe University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, byuksel@hacettepe.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Pinar Keskinocak, Julie Swann
Year: 2007
Abstract: We analyze the effect of manufacturer rebates using several sources of data from automotive industry. We investigate how rebates impact the sales for different vehicle types. We differentiate the strategic behavior of several customer segments over time. We also analyze factors to determine the pass-through rates of rebates by dealers.
