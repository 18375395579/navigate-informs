Title: Coordinating Positioning and Pricing in a Supply Chain

Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, 412 Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, gjv1@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Wei Ke

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the incetives to optimally differentiate and price products in a supply chain when manufacturers sell through intermediaries (retailers). The model has interesting implications for category management and related supply chain contracting mechanisms.
