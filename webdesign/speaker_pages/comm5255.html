Title: Stage-gate Contracts for Screening Agents for New Product Development

Author: Chunlin Wang, PhD Student, The David Eccles School of Business, University of Utah, 1645 E Campus Center Dr, Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States of America, chunlin.wang@business.utah.edu

Coauthor(s): Glen Schmidt, Krishnan S Anand

Year: 2013

Abstract: A principal wants to outsource an NPD project to an agent. In our setting, the agent has inside information on a project’s viability and/or his own competence to execute the project. We study the principal’s use of stage-gate contracts as a screening device, and compare the performance of stage-gate contracts with traditional outsourcing contracts.
