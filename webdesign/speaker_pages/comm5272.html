Title: Gray Markets, Strategic Consumers and Supply Chain Coordination
Author: Hamed Mamani, University of Washington, Box 353226, Foster School of Business, UW, Seattle, WA, 98195-3226, United States of America, hmamani@uw.edu
Coauthor(s): Foad Iravani, Reza Ahmadi
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this paper we consider the effect of contracts for a supply chain under the threat of gray market activities. If internal gray markets are ignored we show the sub-optimality of a number of otherwise coordinating contracts when consumers are strategic. We propose a contract to coordinate this supply chain.
