Title: Incentive Contracts in Project Teams

Author: Murat Bayiz, Information and Operations Management, Marshall School of Business, University of Southern California, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-0809, bayiz@marshall.usc.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study explores optimal linear incentive contracts in projects where activities are outsourced to subcontractors. I assume that activity durations are exponentially distributed, which allows transforming project networks to continuous time Markov chains. I use this structure to calculate optimal contract parameters and study how project network configurations change the optimal incentive contract types in which compensations can be tied to joint or individual performances.
