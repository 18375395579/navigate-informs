Title: Markdown Management: Pricing as a Signaling Device

Author: Gad Allon, Professor, Northwestern University - Kellogg, 2001 Sheridan Rd., Evanston, IL, United States of America, g-allon@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Achal Bassamboo, Ramandeep Randhawa

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the classical revenue management scenario of a monopolist retailer selling to customers over a finite horizon. The retailer possesses information about the aggregate demand, whereas customers have private information on their valuations and are strategic. We study how the profit maximizing retailer strategically uses price as a signaling device.
