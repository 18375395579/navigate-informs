Title: Are Strategic Customers Bad for a Supply Chain?
Author: Yen-Ting Lin, University of San Diego, School of Business Administration, 5998 Alcala Park, San Diego, CA, 92110, United States of America, linyt@sandiego.edu
Coauthor(s): Ali Parlakturk, Jayashankar Swaminathan
Year: 2013
Abstract: We examine the impact of having strategic customers on the performance of a two-tier supply chain. We find that customers?strategic behavior can increase firm profitability as well as the total supply chain profit.
