Title: Inventory Financing and Trade Credit
Author: Sridhar Seshadri, Professor, Indian School of Business, Hyderabad, India, sridhar_seshadri@isb.edu
Coauthor(s): Qi Wu, Kumar Muthuraman
Year: 2013
Abstract: Suppliers often sell products through credits, and trade credit is one of the common ways to finance inventory for operational firms. As an early payment discount, trade credit can be costly from a cash flow perspective. In this paper, from the operations perspective, we answer the question why trade credit is widely used and benefits the supply chain.
