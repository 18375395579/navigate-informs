Title: Category Pricing in the Presence of Reference Price Effects

Author: Soulaymane Kachani, Professor, Columbia University, 334 Mudd Building, 500 W. 120th Street, New York, NY, 10027, United States, kachani@ieor.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Attakrit Asvanunt

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine the effects of reference prices on a firm’s dynamic pricing policy. We provide closed-form solutions of optimal pricing policies for a single-product and for multiple products within the same category. We extend our results to the competitive setting and study the price of anarchy.
