Title: The Impact of Changes in Demand Variability on Logistics Strategy with Two Modes of Supply

Author: Ana Vila-Parrish, North Carolina State University, 400 Daniels Hall, Campus Box 7906, Raleigh, NC, United States of America, anita.vilaparrish@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Julie Simmons Ivy, Russell E. King

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a make-to-order firm, with a geographically distant supplier, that decides the order quantity for each of two supply modes on a periodic basis. We explore the relationship of product life cycle demand characteristics, through the use of Markovian demand process, and the impact on ordering strategies using a Markov decision process.
