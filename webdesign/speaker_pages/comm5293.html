Title: An Experimental Study of Collaborative Forecasting

Author: Doug Thomas, Smeal College of Business, 463 Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, dthomas@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Taylor Randall, Elena Katok

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this study, we examine the extent to which human subjects acting as retailers accurately share demand forecasts with human subjects acting as their suppliers. We investigate how the length of the retailer-supplier partnership, the presence of an auditing mechanism, the inherent value of the forecast information and the presence of a coordinating contract affect the level of collaboration.
