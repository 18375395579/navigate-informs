Title: Dynamic Pricing with Behavioral Considerations
Author: Javad Nasiry, PhD Student, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, Javad.NASIRY@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Ioana Popescu
Year: 2008
Abstract: We study a dynamic pricing problem where consumers purchase decisions are anchored on a reference price, derived from past prices. We focus on asymmetric reference price formation mechanisms, behaviourally motivated by the peak-end rule and loss aversion. The firm's optimal pricing strategies are characterized and compared to the literature.
