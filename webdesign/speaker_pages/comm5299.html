Title: An Inter-Temporal Model for Lease Returns
Author: V. Daniel R. Guide, Jr., Dept. of SC & IS, Pennsylvania State University, University Park, PA, United States, dguide@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Susan Xu, Zheng Jia
Year: 2007
Abstract: We explore the problem of a firm with leased product returns during the lease period. The firm seeks to maximize the total profits over the lifecycle of the product.The faces the choice of remanufacturing or salvaging the product. We explore scenarios that considers cases of remanufacturing with various lead times, and various time between decisions.
