Title: Integrated Inventory, Pricing and Refund Policy Decisions for Online Retailers
Author: Mehmet Sekip Altug, Assistant Professor, George Washington University, 2201 G. Street NW, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, maltug@gwu.edu
Coauthor(s): Tolga Aydinliyim
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study how retailers?return policy choices are affected by consumers?discount-seeking behavior. We study this problem in a newsvendor setting in which a monopolist retailer needs to make a stocking, pricing and a refund decision. Interestingly, when the return rate is below a threshold, we find out that retailers will stock more and price higher. We then study multiple competing retailers.
