Title: Recovery Legislations versus Taxation/Subsidy Policies for Product Remanufacturing

Author: Shumail Mazahir, PhD Candidate, HEC Paris, Campus HEC, 1 rue de la libération, Jouy en Josas, France, shumail.mazahir@hec.edu

Coauthor(s): Sam Aflaki

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper studies the effect of incorporating recovery/remanufacturing targets on the performance of firms and compares them with incentive schemes such as taxation/subsidy policies. We characterise conditions under which the two types of policies outperform each other in terms of prices, manufactured and recovered quantities etc.
