Title: Capacity Planning with Financial Hedging in Offshoring/ Outsourcing
Author: Letian Wang, PhD student, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke Street West, Montreal, QC, H3A1G5, Canada, letian.wang@mail.mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Shanling Li
Year: 2008
Abstract: Multinational firms often sell their products in foreign countries and the prices of the products have to be denominated to local currencies. When the exchange rate and demand for the product in foreign countries are uncertain, the firms may engage in both operational and financial hedging. In this research, we develop an analytical model to demonstrate when and how a risk-averse firm may use financial hedging contract to mitigate the risk in the exchange rate.
