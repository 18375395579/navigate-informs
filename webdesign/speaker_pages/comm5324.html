Title: Cost and Demand Uncertainties and Their Impacts on Pricing and Production Decisions

Author: Sechan Oh, IBM Research, 650 Harry Road, San Jose, United States of America, seoh@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Ray Strong

Year: 2012

Abstract: In contrast to manufacturing firms, service firms often face cost uncertainty as well as demand uncertainty when making pricing and production decisions. In this talk, we introduce a unified framework capable of examining the impacts of cost and demand uncertainties on the pricing and production decisions under different scenarios.
