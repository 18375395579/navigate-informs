Title: Supply Chains in the Presence of Store Brands
Author: Xiang Fang, Assistant Professor, University of Wisconsin--Milwaukee, Lubar School of Business, Milwaukee, WI, 53201, United States of America, fangx@uwm.edu
Coauthor(s): Vithala Rao, Srinagesh Gavirneni
Year: 2013
Abstract: We analyze a supply chain in which a retailer accepts (with the appropriate contractual agreements) a national brand for resale and then determines whether to introduce a store brand, how to price the store brand, and what quantities of the product(s) to order. We find that the efficiency loss in the decentralized supply chain becomes smaller when a store brand is introduced.
