Title: When and How Much to Give: Impact of Donation Percentage on Selling Prices in Charity Auctions
Author: Peter Popkowski Leszczyc, Associate Professor of Marketing, School of Business, University of Alberta, Edmonton AB, T6G 2R6, Canada, ppopkows@ualberta.ca
Coauthor(s): Michael H. Rothkopf, Leo Wong
Year: 2006
Abstract: We study the effects of percentage of selling price donated, product type, information, and gender on outcomes in charity auctions. Controlled field experiments indicate that auctions with 25% of revenue donated to charity had higher net revenue than non-charity auctions.
