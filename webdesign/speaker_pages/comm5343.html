Title: Hewlett Packard's Personal Systems Group Shapes its Distribution Strategy Using Optimization
Author: Alper Sen, Assistant Professor, Bilkent University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Bilkent, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, alpersen@bilkent.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Dirk Beyer, Shailendra Jain
Year: 2006
Abstract: In 2005, HP's PSG decided to evaluate its supply chain strategy in North America for its commercial division and investigate opportunities to decrease its supply chain costs and improve its order cycle times. As part of a larger project, a detailed optimization model was set up to help PSG develop a distribution strategy. A software tool is created to evaluate various distribution alternatives and understand the trade-off between the transportation costs and order cycle times.
