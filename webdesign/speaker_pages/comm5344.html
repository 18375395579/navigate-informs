Title: On the Selection of Supply Chain Coordinating Contracts: The Role of Capital Constraints

Author: Yi Xu, Assistant Professor, Robert H. Smith School of Business, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, United States of America, yxu@exchange.sba.miami.edu

Coauthor(s): Jun Zhang

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a supply chain with a financially constrained retailer, and examine the implications of the retailer's capital constraint on the selection of supply chain coordinating contracts. We demonstrate that several well-accepted results on supply chain coordinating contracts need to be reexamined. We then propose and study a new coordinating contract form: manufacturer financing contract in which the manufacturer provides financing for the retailer's purchase.
