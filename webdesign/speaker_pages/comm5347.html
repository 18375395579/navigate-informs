Title: The Impact of Budget Constraints on Flexible versus Dedicated Technology Choice

Author: Onur Boyabatli, Singapore Management University, 50 Stamford Road, Singapore, Singapore, oboyabatli@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Tiecheng Leng, Beril Toktay

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper analyzes the impact of financial constraints in a capacity investment setting. We model a two-product firm that decides on its technology choice (flexible versus dedicated) and capacity level under demand uncertainty, and the production quantities after this uncertainty is resolved. Differing from the majority of the stochastic capacity investment literature, we assume that the firm is budget-constrained both in the capacity investment and production stages.
