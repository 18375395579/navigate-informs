Title: Price and Lead Time Quotation for Contract and Spot Customers

Author: Baykal Hafizoglu, Arizona State University, 699 S Mill Avenue, Tempe, AZ, 85281, United States of America, baykal@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Pinar Keskinocak, Esma Gel

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider dynamic price and lead time quotation for a make-to-order company with demand from contract customers as well as spot purchasers. Contract customers are offered a uniform price and lead time, whereas, spot purchasers are subject to dynamically quoted price and lead times. We discuss the potential of dynamic quotation for such environments, various properties of optimal control policies and the optimal mix of contract customers and spot purchasers.
