Title: Valuing Flexibility in Multi-generation New Product Development
Author: Yael Grushka-Cockayne, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, United Kingdom, ygrushka.phd2003@london.edu
Coauthor(s): Janne Kettunen, Bert De Reyck, Zeger Degraeve, Ahti Salo
Year: 2008
Abstract: We present a model for valuing a flexible new product development program consisting of multiple product generations, taking into account uncertainty regarding product performance and market requirements, and cannibalization affects across product generations. We derive managerial insights regarding possible decisions the firm can make throughout the program, in the form of possible product line abandonment, development enhancement and flexible launching times of each product generation.
