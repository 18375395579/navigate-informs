Title: New Product Pricing Strategies with Social Media Influences and Reference Price Effect
Author: Xiuli He, Assistant Professor, Univeristy of North Carolina-Charlotte, 9201 University City Blvd, BISOM Dept., Charlotte, NC, 28223, United States of America, xhe8@uncc.edu
Coauthor(s): Juzhi Zhang, Qinglong Gou, Subodha Kumar
Year: 2013
Abstract: We propose a two-period model to study the reference price effect under social media influences. The consumers' utility valuation is uncertain and is affected by the online word-of-mouth effect and reference price effect.
