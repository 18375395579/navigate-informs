Title: Upgrades, Upsells and Pricing in Revenue Management

Author: Catalina Stefanescu, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, cstefanescu@london.edu

Coauthor(s): Guillermo Gallego

Year: 2007

Abstract: Upgrades and upsells can improve revenues when there is a mismatch between supply and demand particularly under heterogeneous sales commissions. We discuss upgrades fairness and characterize instances when limiting upgrades to the next highest quality product is optimal. Under multinomial choice demand, we investigate optimal pricing of products and upgrades.
