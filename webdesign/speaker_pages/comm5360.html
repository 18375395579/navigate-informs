Title: Product Quality Choice, Competition and Supply Chain Design
Author: Stylianos Kavadias, Georgia Institute of Technology, College of Management, 800 West Peachtree Street, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308-0520, United States, Stylianos.Kavadias@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Mark Ferguson
Year: 2007
Abstract: We explore the interplay between product design choices and their effect on the supply chain transactions. A large number of examples indicates that product design choices, and the overall product performance (quality), is relying on “off the shelf?standardized components, and it affects component pricing. We develop a normative model that highlights the profitability drivers for the different industrial settings.
