Title: IS-enabled Reduction of Disruptions in Supply Chains

Author: Pamela Rogers, Teaching Fellow, University of North Texas, Department of Management, PO Box 305429, Denton, TX, 76203, United States, RogersP@unt.edu

Coauthor(s): Arun Madapusi

Year: 2007

Abstract: A tightly integrated supply chain is crucial to the sustainability of a firm’s competitive advantage. However, failure to integrate the objectives of all the supply chain members results in sub-optimal supply chain performance. This study examines the use of information systems (IS) as a solution to reduce supply chain disruptions.
