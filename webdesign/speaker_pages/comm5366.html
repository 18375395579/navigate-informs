Title: Cost Allocation in Combinatorial Auctions for Bilateral Procurement Markets

Author: Michel Gendreau, Universite de Montreal, Montreal, Canada, michelg@crt.umontreal.ca

Coauthor(s): Monia Rekik, Jacques Robert, Teodor Gabriel Crainic

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a bilateral procurement market where multiple buyers and sellers trade indivisible contracts through a one-sided combinatorial auction where sellers are the only bidders. We propose, evaluate and compare different allocation methods to determine each buyer’s portion of the total cost incurred at the end of the auction.
