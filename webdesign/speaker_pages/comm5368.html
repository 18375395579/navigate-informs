Title: Price Matching Negotiation versus Simultaneous Negotiation
Author: Gangshu Cai, Associate Professor, Santa Clara University, Department of OMIS, Santa Clara, CA, United States of America, Gangshu@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Weixin Shang
Year: 2012
Abstract: Price matching negotiation has been seen in practice; however, no theoretical analysis has been yet dedicated to this phenomenon. This article investigates the impact of negotiation schemes on firms via comparing price matching negotiation and simultaneous negotiation in a variety of situations. We find price matching negotiation can outperform simultaneous negotiation for all firms. We also discuss the impact of ownership, asymmetric bargaining power, and the firms?merger incentives.
