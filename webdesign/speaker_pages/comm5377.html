Title: Strategic Sourcing Based on Contract Selection
Author: Jie Li, National University of Singapore, Singapore, 117576, Singapore, g0304872@nus.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Mukta Bansal, Iftekhar Karimi, Rajagopalan Srinivasan
Year: 2006
Abstract: Selecting contracts for raw material purchases is an important problem, as raw material costs are significant for many companies and contracts allow risk sharing in the presence of uncertainties. However, contracts come with different terms & conditions, hence selection is a non-trivial problem. We consider the optimal selection of raw material contracts using a mathematical programming approach. We also treat procurement planning horizon as a potential decision.
