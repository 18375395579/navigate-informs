Title: Price Negotiation and Extended Warranty in a Distribution Channel
Author: Chia-Wei Kuo, National Taiwan University, No. 1, Sec 4, Roosevelt Road, Taipei, 106, Taiwan - ROC, cwkuo@ntu.edu.tw
Coauthor(s): Sean Zhou, Kwei-Long Huang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a distribution channel in which a manufacturer sells the product through a retailer to the end market. The manufacturer uses the wholesale-price-only contract while the retailer can determine the pricing policy ?posted pricing or negotiation, and whether to bundle the product with extended warranty. We discuss effects of various forms of cost structures on the optimal decisions and equilibrium outcome.
