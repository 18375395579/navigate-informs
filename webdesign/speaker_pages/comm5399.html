Title: Retailer’s Multichannel Strategies under Random Yield and Demand Uncertainty
Author: Weijun Yang, Xi'an Jiaotong University, School of Management, No. 28 Xianning West Road, Xi'an, 710049, China, ywj0126@126.com
Coauthor(s): Shulin Liu
Year: 2013
Abstract: Consider a supply chain that consists of one supplier with random yield and one retailer with demand uncertainty. This paper is motivated from the widely use of Online To Offline (OTO) business model in electronic commerce. We focus in this paper on the case that the retailer with yield and demand uncertainty to make channel strategy choice. Two major questions are addressed. When should a retailer adopt a multichannel strategy? What is the impact of multichannel on price and profit problems?
