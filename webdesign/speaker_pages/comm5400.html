Title: Commodity Processing and Financial Hedging: Under Correlated Price and Demand

Author: Ankur Goel, Case Western Reserve University, Cleveland, Cleveland, United States of America, axg312@case.edu

Coauthor(s): Fehmi Tanrisever

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a publicly traded commodity processor, under perfect financial market, with an objective to maximize the value of its stakeholders when demand of output is correlated with its price. In a multi-period model, we develop optimal hedging and procurement policies in the presence of yield uncertainty and correlation between input and output price of the commodity.
