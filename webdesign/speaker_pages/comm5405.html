Title: Congestion Management of Interregional Electricity Markets: Review of Methodologies and a Proposal

Author: Marcelino Madrigal, Senior Energy Specialist, The World Bank, 1818 H St NW, Washington, DC, 20433, United States of America, marcelino_madrigal@yahoo.ca

Coauthor(s): Jos?A. Aguado, William Rosehart

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation will summarize the scope of the panel, and will review methodologies for interregional electricity market trading and congestion management. Following, a particular proposal of the integration of a forward market with a balancing (spot) market based on nodal pricing will be presented. The interaction of TSOs belonging to adjacent markets is efficiently taken into account through a decentralized Optimal Power Flow solved by interior point methods.
