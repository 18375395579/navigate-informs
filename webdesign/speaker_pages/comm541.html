Title: Price and Quantity Competition between Outlets and Retail Stores

Author: Xuying Zhao, Assistant Professor, University of Notre Dame, 361 Mendoza College of Business, Notre Dame, IN, 46556, United States of America, xzhao1@nd.edu

Coauthor(s): Jennifer Ryan, Daewon Sun

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study a dual channel supply chain in which a manufacturer sells a single product to end-users through both a traditional retail channel and a manufacturer-owned outlet store. Each channel faces a newsvendor problem, with price and order quantity as decision variables. A unique equilibrium is provided for the system. We propose a modified revenue sharing contract to enable supply chain coordination. We use numerical tests to answer when the manufacturer should delay sales at the outlet store.
