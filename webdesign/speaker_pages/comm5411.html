Title: Intertemporal Price Discrimination: Structure and Computation of Optimal Policies
Author: Ilan Lobel, New York University, 44 West Fourth Street, New York, NY, 10012, United States of America, ilobel@stern.nyu.edu
Coauthor(s): Omar Besbes
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider the problem of a firm selling goods over time to customers with heterogeneous patience levels. We let customer valuations be correlated with their willingness-to-wait and look for a dynamic pricing policy that maximizes the long-term revenue of the firm. We prove several structural properties of the optimal pricing policy and develop a dynamic programming algorithm that computes the optimal policy in polynomial time.
