Title: Material Flow Variability in Supply Chains
Author: Wei Luo, IESE Business School, Avenida Pearson, 21, Barcelona, Spain, wluo@iese.edu
Coauthor(s): Li Chen, Kevin Shang
Year: 2013
Abstract: We study the variability of shipment and sales in a multi-echelon supply chain. We show the conditions under which this variability will amplify or dampen along the supply chain. Our study bridges the gap between the theoretical and empirical research on the bullwhip effect.
