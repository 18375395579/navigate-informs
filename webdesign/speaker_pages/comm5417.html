Title: Stochastic Depletion Problems: Product Line Design

Author: Vivek Farias, Stanford University, vivekf@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Carri Chan

Year: 2007

Abstract: A firm that is capable of producing a variety of products and serves a heterogenous pool of customers must decide on the product line it offers at every point in time. We ask: In the face of dynamic market conditions how might a firm best select product assortments over time?
