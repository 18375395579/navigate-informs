Title: Managing Value and Uncertainty in Service-Driven IT Investments

Author: Ryan Sougstad, Doctoral Student, University of Minnesota, Carlson School of Management, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States, rsougstad@csom.umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Kauffman

Year: 2006

Abstract: Service-driven IT solutions require a shift in IT investment evaluation from methods that assess fixed costs of acquisition toward methods that emphasize variable cost-based service solutions for which contract structure is key. Contracts must balance flexibility and quality with profit and risk tolerance. We apply a technique called value-at-risk to evaluate the profitability of IT service contracts, along with the impact a portfolio of contracts will have on the firm’s overall IT risk profile.
