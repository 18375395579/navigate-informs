Title: Supply Chain with Outsourcing and Quick-response Production under Demand and Cost Uncertainty

Author: Zugang Liu, Assistant Professor of Business Administration, Pennsylvania State Hazleton, 76 University Drive, Hazleton, PA, 18202, United States of America, zxl23@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Anna Nagurney

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop a modeling framework for supply chains with global outsourcing and quick-response production under demand and cost uncertainty. We considers multiple off-shore suppliers, manufacturers, and demand markets. Our results reveal important insights regarding how uncertainty affects the profits, risks, as well as decisions of supply chain firms under competition.
