Title: A Multi-supplier Sourcing Model with Learning and Disruption Risk

Author: Lena Silbermayr, University of Vienna, Brünner Strasse 72, Vienna, 1210, Austria, lena.silbermayr@univie.ac.at

Coauthor(s): Stefan Minner

Year: 2013

Abstract: A buyer has to deal with the risk of supply disruptions. We study a multi-period order allocation problem of a buyer sourcing from unreliable suppliers which reduce production cost over time through learning. By considering supply disruption risk and learning, we analyze the trade-off between risk reduction via multiple sourcing and learning benefits induced by long-term relationships with a single supplier.
