Title: A Newsvendor Pricing Game with Fulfillment Obligations

Author: Soulaymane Kachani, Department of Industrial Engineering and Operations Research, Columbia University, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, kachani@ieor.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Woonghee Tim Huh, Ali Sadighian

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a duopoly game between two price-setting newsvendor-type firms. The firms compete over price and fulfillment rate, which is different from the classical quantity-price game. We consider this game under different make-to-order and make-to-stock scenarios and show existence and uniqueness of equilibria. We also provide a characterization of the optimization problem of each firm in the form of a general newsvendor problem with piecewise-linear overage and underage costs.
