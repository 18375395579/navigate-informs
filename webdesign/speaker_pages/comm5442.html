Title: Bricks and Clicks: Managing In-Store Product Assortment and Online Returns
Author: Elie Ofek, Harvard Business School, Soldiers Field, Boston, United States, eofek@hbs.edu
Coauthor(s): Miklos Sarvary, Ozge Turut
Year: 2006
Abstract: Operating an online arm in addition to physical stores allows retailers to appeal to customers that value the conveniences of online shopping. However, online shopping increases the likelihodd of product mismatch. Returning mismatched merchandise is a costly process for firms and consumers. Retailers operating dual channels need to decide how much in-store product assortment to offer, how costly to make product returns, and how to price their merchandise online and offline.
