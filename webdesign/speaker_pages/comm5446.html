Title: The Effects of Executive Stock Option Grants on Financing Decisions
Author: Kyoko Yagi, Akita Prefectural University, 84-4 Ebinokuchi, Tsuchiya, Yurihonjo, Akita, 015-0055, Japan, yagi@akita-pu.ac.jp
Coauthor(s): Ryuta Takashima
Year: 2012
Abstract: This paper considers the capital structure of a firm with stock option grants as managerial compensation in real options framework. We examine how the separation of ownership and control affects the firm’s financing decisions. Especially, we analyze the effect of the stock option grants on capital structures and the share of stock option in managerial portfolios of firm’s stock and options for financing with debt.
