Title: Customer Revenue Sharing Program in Online Social Media

Author: Fredrik Odegaard, Assistant Professor, Richard Ivey School of Business, 1151 Richmond St. N, London, On, N6G4X3, Canada, fodegaard@ivey.uwo.ca

Coauthor(s): Fouad H. Mirzaei, Xinghao Yan

Year: 2012

Abstract: Online social media (OSMs) have become a popular and growing phenomenon on the Internet. Given high competition over the Internet to attract users, a question that arises is whether OSMs should reward their contributing users. We model this competition by a duopoly game where users are either active or passive with respect to each OSM. We show this game has a unique Nash equilibrium in pure strategies and specify how OSMs derive the optimal reward payments.
