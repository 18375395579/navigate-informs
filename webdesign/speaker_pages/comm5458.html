Title: Quality and Pricing Decisions when Consumers Perceive Recycled Content Differently

Author: Tolga Aydinliyim, Assistant Professor, Baruch College, Zicklin School of Business, New York, NY, United States of America, tolga@uoregon.edu

Coauthor(s): Monire Jalili, Nagesh Murthy

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a monopolist selling ordinary and green product variants to consumers whose differential (dis)utility vary by consumer type, and is a function of the firm’s quality decision (i.e., the amount of recycled content.) We discuss how the optimal quality and pricing decisions drive demand and profit.
