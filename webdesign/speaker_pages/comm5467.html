Title: A Fluid Analysis for Service Capacity Decisions
Author: Haiyan Wang, Olin School of Business, Washington University in St. Louis, Saint Louis, MO, United States, wangha@olin.wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Tava Olsen
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study capacity decisions in a service environment where arrival rates are highly seasonal and customers are time sensitive. We begin by studying a monopolist's capacity decision where the trade off is between the cost of extra capacity for low demand periods or loss of revenue for high demand periods. We then study a duopoly model where lost demand for one firm may become increased demand for the opponent. In both models we use a fluid model for the analysis.
