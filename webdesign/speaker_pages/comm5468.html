Title: Designing Public Storage Warehouses With Customer Choice

Author: Yeming Gong, Associate Professor, EM Lyon Business School, 23 avenue Guy de Collongue, Ecully, 69134, France, GONG@em-lyon.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Customer choice behavior is an important element in revenue management of public storage warehouses. To consider this behavior, the customer choice model is used to specify the probability of purchase for each fare product as a function of the set of fare products offered. This paper proposes models and solving algorithms to design public storage warehouses with considering the choice behavior of customers.
