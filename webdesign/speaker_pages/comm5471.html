Title: Informative Product Demonstrations and Prices

Author: Haresh Gurnani, Professor, University of Miami, Department of Management, Coral Gables, FL, 33124, United States of America, haresh@miami.edu

Coauthor(s): Ralph Boleslavsky, Chris Cotton

Year: 2013

Abstract: Firms use product demonstrations to educate prospective retail consumers about product valuation. While more informative demonstrations reduce valuation risk, it may reduce competition and make consumers worse off. This has important implications for firm profits, consumer protection, and for better understanding the release of “niche?products with uncertain customer appeal.
