Title: Carrot vs. Stick: The Effectiveness of Contract Mechanisms on Incentivizing Supplier Innovation
Author: David Wuttke, EBS University, Konrad-Adenauer-Ring 15, Wiesbaden, Germany, david.wuttke@ebs.edu
Coauthor(s): Karen Donohue, Enno Siemsen
Year: 2013
Abstract: Using a combination of analytical models and laboratory experiments, we study the effectiveness of buyer contract mechanisms, including breach penalties and innovation rewards, on incentivizing product innovation at the supplier level. Our results provide insight into how the mechanisms can be altered to better account for supplier-specific behavior.
