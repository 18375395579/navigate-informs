Title: Bidding and Learning in Repeated Auctions
Author: S. Karti Puranam, Assistant Professor, La Salle University, 1900 W Olney Ave, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, puranam@lasalle.edu
Coauthor(s): Michael Katehakis
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider the problem of a firm that procures items in repeated auctions by bidding against the “market.?The firm and the “market?learn from each winning bid. We study bidding strategies for the firm when the objective of the firm is to minimize the expected total cost of acquiring items to meet demand.
