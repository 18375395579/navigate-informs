Title: Contracting and Performance Outcomes in Software Development Outsourcing: A Field Study

Author: Sandra Slaughter, Associate Professor of Information Systems, Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, Tech & Frew Streets, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, sandras@andrew.cmu.edu

Coauthor(s): Donald Harter, Soon Ang

Year: 2006

Abstract: This study traces the complete chain of effects of ex ante contractual conditions and ex post performance outcomes of three types of contracts in software development outsourcing: time and materials, fixed price, and a hybrid contract. We examine archival data collected on the quality, budget and schedule performance of software development contracts completed by a service provider for a major client. Our findings suggest that different contract types maximize different performance outcomes.
