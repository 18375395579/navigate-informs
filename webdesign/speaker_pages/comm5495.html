Title: Optimal Price and Product Quality Decisions in a Distribution Channel

Author: Xiaowei Xu, Assistant Professor, Rutgers University, 180 University Avenue, ACK208, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States of America, xiaoweix@andromeda.rutgers.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study a joint pricing and product quality decision problem in a distribution channel, in which a manufacturer sells a product through a retailer. The manufacturer jointly determines the wholesale price and quality of the product and the retailer determines the retail price. We find that the curvature of the marginal revenue function determines the comparative statics in the distribution channel.
