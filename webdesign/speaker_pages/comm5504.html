Title: Implications of Product Returns on Channel Relations

Author: Canan Savaskan, Northwestern University, r-savaskan@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeff Shulman, Anne Coughlan

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper examines the implications of commercial product returns on channel relations between a manufacturer and a retailer. We develop a game theoretic bilateral supply chain model to identify conditions under which manufacturer (or retailer) would be in a better positon to assume product returns responsibility. We solve for the restocking fee decisions under different channel formats and discuss the role of restocking fees on channel incentives.
