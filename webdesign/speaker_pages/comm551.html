Title: Ordering Behavior under Supply Risk: An Experimental Investigation
Author: Yusen Xia, Associate Professor, Georgia State University, J. Mack Robinson College of Business, Atlanta, GA, 30303, United States of America, ysxia@gsu.edu
Coauthor(s): Haresh Gurnani, Karthik Ramachandran, Saibal Ray
Year: 2012
Abstract: In this presentation, we show results from a study where human subjects were asked to place orders from two suppliers who differ in their costs and reliabilities to satisfy a fixed amount of end customer demand. Under such a scenario, it is optimal to sole-source exclusively. However, subjects in our experiment show a systematic tendency to diversify their orders between the sources even if such an action increases their risk and reduces their expected pro t.
