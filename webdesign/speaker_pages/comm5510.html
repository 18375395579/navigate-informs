Title: Outsourcing of Warranty Contracts
Author: Wei Huang, SAS, wei.huang@sas.com
Coauthor(s): Vidyadhar Kulkarni, Jay Swaminathan
Year: 2006
Abstract: Firms are outsourcing their business processes in order to gain greater flexibility in their supply chains. In this paper, we study a firm that is outsourcing its warranty services to vendors. We provide insights on alternative leadership, competition and contract settings.
