Title: Usage Restriction and Subscription Services: Operational Benefits with Rational Users

Author: Ramandeep Randhawa, The University of Texas at Austin, Austin, TX, United States, ramandeep.randhawa@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Sunil Kumar

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study a rental firm, e.g. Netflix or Blockbuster, that offers reusable products to price and quality-of-service sensitive customers. We study the alternatives of offering either a subscription option that limits the number of concurrent rentals in return for a flat fee per unit time or a pay-per-use option with no such restriction. In each option, customers adjust their request rate to obtain a nominal usage rate. We show that offering the subscription option is more profitable for the firm.
