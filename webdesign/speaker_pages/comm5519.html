Title: Learning to Price from Bounded Rational Competitors
Author: Kay-Yut Chen, Principal Scientist, HP Labs, 1U-2, HP Labs, 1501 Page Mill Road, Palo Alto, CA, 94304, United States of America, kay-yut.chen@hp.com
Coauthor(s): Shelen Jain, Guillermo Gallego, Jay Wang, Jose Beltran
Year: 2012
Abstract: We propose a new pricing method in markets where observed prices may be near, but not on equilibrium. A firm can “learn?from the limited intelligence in the market place. Simulated results show that the level of benefits depends on relative levels of intelligence between the firm, and the market.
