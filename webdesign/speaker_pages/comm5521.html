Title: The Impact of Low End Competition with a Strategic Supplier

Author: Liwen Chen, PhD Candidate, University of Texas at Austin, IROM Department, McCombs School of Business, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, liwen.chen@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen Gilbert, Yusen Xia

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider how the entry of a lower quality rival affects an incumbent OEM when there is a strategic supplier. Interestingly, we find that if the rival's quality is neither too high nor too low, his entry can benefit the incumbent OEM. Although the rival cannibalizes some of the incumbent's demand, his entry also causes the strategic supplier to lower the wholesale price. This latter effect dominates the former when the rival's quality is not too high.
