Title: Offerings and Investment Strategy

Author: Chuck Maniaci, Senior Financial Analyst, IBM Global Technology Services, One Michigan Avenue, Lansing, MI, 48933, United States, cmaniaci@us.ibm.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: We discuss important steps in developing market-based price for IT services. These involve identifying target markets for different types of offerings and then determining customer’s willingness-to-pay through market studies. This is then used to construct a market-based price for the offering. We also describe how the on demand pricing structure can provide considerable savings to the customers on their overall IT spending through a case study employing a TCO financial estimate.
