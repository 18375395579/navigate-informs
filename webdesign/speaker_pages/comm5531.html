Title: Analysis of Online Product Pricing and Shipping Charges
Author: Mehmet Gumus, Assistant Professor, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke Street West, Montreal, QC, H3A1G5, Canada, mehmet.gumus@mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Wonseok Oh, Shanling Li, Ray Saibal
Year: 2008
Abstract: We analyze the interaction between product and shipping pricing decisions under two scenarios. In first (second) scenario, retailer presents shipping (product) price first and then product (shipping) price. We develop stylized model that captures this asymmetric effect on decisions and conduct empirical analysis to validate analytical predictions.
