Title: Scheduled Ordering in Decentralized Distribution Supply Chains
Author: Gongtao (Lucy) Chen, Cornell University, 401 Sage Hall, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States, gc84@cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Srinagesh Gavirneni
Year: 2006
Abstract: We analyze two different scheduled ordering polices in a supply chain with one supplier providing a single product to multiple retailers facing identical stochastic demands. Both the cases with and without information sharing among the supply chain are considered. We develop the conditions under which those scheduled ordering policies will reduce the total supply chain cost. We also compare the performances of the two scheduled ordering polices.
