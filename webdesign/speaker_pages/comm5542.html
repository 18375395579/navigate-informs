Title: Farm-yield Management When The Production Rate is Yield Dependent
Author: Onur Boyabatli, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, 50 Stamford Road 04-01, Singaport, 178899, Singapore, oboyabatli@smu.edu.sg
Coauthor(s): Kwan Eng Wee
Year: 2013
Abstract: This paper analyzes the impact of farm-yield uncertainty when the unit production rate is yield dependent. We model a firm that procures an input through a contract in advance of the selling season; and through the open market on the day in a single period so as to maximize its expected profit. We investigate the impact of yield uncertainty on the optimal contract volume and the profitability of the firm, and underline the role of yield-dependent production rate on our results.
