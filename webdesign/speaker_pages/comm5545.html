Title: Are US Airlines Being Relegated to a Supporting Role in the Global Market?

Author: Bill Swelbar, MIT International Center for Air Transportation, 77 Mass. Ave., Cambridge, MA, 02139, swelbar@mit.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: As US network carriers shift some of their focus away from the US domestic market and increase capacity to international markets, we assess their competitive position vis-?vis non-US competition. Can US flag carriers be competitive internationally without a meaningful US domestic presence? Can the US carriers afford the necessary investments in their product to be competitive?
