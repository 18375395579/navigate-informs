Title: Incentive Mechanisms in Healthcare

Author: Mohsen Bayati, Assistant Professor, Stanford University, Graduate School of Business, Stanford, United States of America, bayati@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Braverman, Ramakrishna Gummadi, Ramesh Johari

Year: 2013

Abstract: We investigate healthcare provider payment mechanisms with the aim of incentivizing optimal provider behavior. We show that adding a small performance-based incentive on top of the existing mechanisms can have a dramatic effect on overall efficiency. Our results provide theoretical support for the recent push by Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services to add small performance-based incentives into its reimbursement system.
