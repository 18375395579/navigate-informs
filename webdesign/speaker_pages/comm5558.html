Title: Value of Retail Pricing Flexibility in a Decentralized Supply Chain
Author: Saibal Ray, Associate Professor, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke West, Montreal, QC, H3A1G5, Canada, saibal.ray@MCGILL.CA
Coauthor(s): Tamer Boyaci, Yuyue Song
Year: 2007
Abstract: There are a number of papers in the OM literature which establishes the value of retail pricing flexibility in a centralized system. In this research we investigate the value of such flexibility to the individual channel members (and the whole chain) in a decentralized setting. We also show how dynamic contracting affects the value of retail pricing flexibility.
