Title: Service Differentiation in Large-Scale Systems

Author: John Yao, Columbia University, New York, NY, United States of America, jyao14@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Costis Maglaras, Assaf Zeevi

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the problem of a monopolistic service provider who offers a menu of services to a market of heterogeneous price- and delay-sensitive customers. We highlight how a simple deterministic problem reveals the heavy-traffic operating regime induced by the revenue maximizing or social welfare maximizing optimal policy. Moreover, we show that the optimal service offering may be well-approximated by one with many fewer service classes.
