Title: Revenue Management of Consumer Options for Tournaments

Author: Caner Gocmen, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, Uris Hall, 4V, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, fgocmen13@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Phillips, Guillermo Gallego, Santiago Balseiro

Year: 2012

Abstract: We analyze tournament options where the event manager sells team-specific options for a tournament final, such as the Super Bowl, before the finalists are determined. These options guarantee a final game ticket to the bearer if her team advances to the finals. We develop an approach by which an event manager can determine the revenue maximizing prices and amounts of tickets and options to sell for a tournament final. We show that offering options can increase expected revenue and social welfare.
