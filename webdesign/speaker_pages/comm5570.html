Title: Leasing & Selling: The Environmental Implications of Product Recovery & Marketing Strategies

Author: Vishal Agrawal, PhD Student, Georgia Tech, 800 W Peachtree St, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, gth767p@mail.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Mark Ferguson, Beril Toktay, Valerie Thomas

Year: 2008

Abstract: We evaluate the environmental implications of the lease versus sell decision based on the total environmental impact in the life cycle of the product. We extend the results on durable goods from the economics literature and on closed-loop supply chains in the operations literature. We find the optimal recovery, disposition & remarketing strategies under both leasing and selling. Next we examine conditions under which each strategy is both profitable and environmentally superior.
