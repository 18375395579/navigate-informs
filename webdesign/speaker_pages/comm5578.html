Title: Pricing, Product Variety and Inventory Decisions in the Presence of Supply Disruptions
Author: Saed Alizamir, University of Florida, 2909, SW 13th St., Apt 34, Gainesville, FL, 32608, United States, saed@ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Aydin Alptekinoglu, Amar Sapra
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a manufacturer that offers multiple variants in two brands of a product with a long life-cycle. Both brands use a critical component that is sourced from two different suppliers, which are prone to disruption. As a consequence of this supply uncertainty, the manufacturer may not be able to offer product variants of a brand in the marketplace. We examine how this uncertainty in supply affect her pricing, product variety and inventory decisions.
