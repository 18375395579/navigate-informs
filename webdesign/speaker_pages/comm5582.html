Title: Agricultural Supply Chain of Price-maker with a Case Study of Colombian Coffee
Author: Jim Shi, Assistant Professor, Georgia State University, 35 Broad Street, Altanta, GA, 30303, United States of America, shigsu@gmail.com
Coauthor(s): Yao Zhao, Rose Karimi Kiwanuka
Year: 2013
Abstract: This study is motivated by an empirical study on Colombian coffee supply chain and investigates general agricultural products with price-maker. We develop optimal selling policies for price maker.
