Title: Competitive Due-date and Capacity Management in Internal Markets

Author: Murat Erkoc, Assistant Professor, University of Miami, Dept. of Industrial Engineering, P.O. Box 248294, Coral Gables, FL, 33124, United States of America, merkoc@miami.edu

Coauthor(s): David Wu, Haresh Gurnani

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this study we consider a make-to-order (MTO) firm where marketing and manufacturing divisions simultaneously make due-date and capacity investment decisions, respectively. We study the impact of how two divisions share the costs associated with tardy deliveries, and derive the equilibrium policies. We investigate the value of capacity flexibility.
