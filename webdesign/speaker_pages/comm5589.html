Title: Information Quality and Competition in the Supply Chain Network

Author: Deanna Kennedy, Assistant Professor, University of Washington Bothell, 18115 Campus Way NE, Bothell, WA, 98028, United States of America, dkennedy@uwb.edu

Coauthor(s): Qiang Qiang, Zugang Liu, Trisha Anderson

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper focuses on competition and the quality of information shared among supply chain players. We investigate the dynamics between the firms in a general supply chain network. We model the network to capture interactions among various information quality dimensions including information sharing, information distortion, and accuracy. We derive the equilibrium conditions and use numerical examples to provide managerial implications.
