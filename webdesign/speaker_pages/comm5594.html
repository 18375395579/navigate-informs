Title: Crowdvoting the Timing of New Product Introduction

Author: Rene Caldentey, New York University, Stern School of Business, 44 West Fourth Street, New York, United States of America, rcaldent@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Victor Araman

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a seller who is considering introducing a new product into the marketplace. In order to improve demand forecasts, the seller uses an online voting system that allow potential buyers to comment on the product's attributes and their likelihood of purchase if the product is made available on the market. We study the design of such a voting system and the pricing of the product.
