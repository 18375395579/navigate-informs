Title: Incentives for Retailer Forecasting: Rebates versus Returns

Author: Wenqiang Xiao, Assistant Professor, New York University, 3 Washington Square Village, #4B, New York, NY, 10012, United States, wxiao@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Terry Taylor

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper studies a manufacturer that sells to a newsvendor retailer who can improve the quality of her demand information by exerting costly forecasting effort. We focus on two forms contracts: a rebates contract and a returns contract which are mirror images of one another. We show that the manufacturer is better off offering returns.
