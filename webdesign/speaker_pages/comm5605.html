Title: Store Brand Positioning in the Presence of Retail Competition
Author: Bo Liao, PhD Candidate, Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley, Haas School of Business, UC Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States of America, bo_liao@haas.berkeley.edu
Coauthor(s): Candace Yano
Year: 2013
Abstract: We characterize a retailer’s equilibrium store-brand quality level when competing with another retailer also offering a store brand; both retailers carry the same national brand. All parties choose their respective prices. We find the retailer’s key tradeoff is between product differentiation and bargaining power vis-?vis the national brand manufacturer.
