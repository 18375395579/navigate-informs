Title: Capacity Contracts in Air-Cargo Revenue Management

Author: Kannapha Amaruchkul, Doctoral Student, University of Minnesota, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Room 1101 MechE 0691, 111 Church Street, SE, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States, amar0017@me.umn.edu

Coauthor(s): William L. Cooper, Diwakar Gupta

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study air-cargo capacity contracts between a carrier and a freight forwarder in settings where the forwarder possesses some private information. Working in the principal-agent paradigm, we propose contract schemes that overcome inefficiencies associated with the asymmetric information.
