Title: Evaluating the Environmental Performance of Product Leasing

Author: Mark Ferguson, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree Street NW, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, Mark.Ferguson@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Vishal Agrawal

Year: 2007

Abstract: We evaluate the impact of the lease versus sell decision on environmental performance measures such as the quantity of products remanufactured versus recycled or disposed. We extend the results on durable goods from the economics literature and on closed-loop supply chains in the operations literature.
