Title: Sourcing Strategies under Supply Yield Uncertainty

Author: Guang Xiao, Wahsington University in St. Louis, One Brookings Drive, Campus Box 1133, St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States of America, xiaoguang@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Lingxiu Dong, Nan Yang

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a one-buyer-multi-supplier system in the presence of supply yield uncertainty. We explore the impact of various supply contractual arrangements and different yield uncertainty profiles on the equilibrium supply chain performance.
