Title: Optimal Policies for an Unreliable Serial System with Dual External Sourcing
Author: Amar Sapra, University of Florida, Industrial and System Eng. Department, Weil Hall, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, sapra@ise.ufl.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider a two-stage serial system in which the supply to the the upstream stage from the external supplier is prone to disruptions. As a result, the upstream stage is unable to ship to the downstream stage. The downstream stage continues functioning by ordering directly from an emergency supplier. We show that the optimal policy takes a simple form only when all the lead-times are equal.
