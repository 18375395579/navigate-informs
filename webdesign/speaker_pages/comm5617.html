Title: Impact of Process and Pricing Decisions on Successive Generations of New Products
Author: Janice Carrillo, Associate Professor, Warrington College of Business, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, United States of America, janice.carrillo@cba.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Michelle MH Seref
Year: 2007
Abstract: We develop an analytical model of coordinated product and process development when there are multiple generations of a product. Critical strategic decisions include not only the timing of successive product generations, but also introduction of advances in production technology. Trade-offs considered in the coordinated decision include demand and pricing implications for the next generation product.
