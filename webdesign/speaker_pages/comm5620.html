Title: Online Advertising: Revenue Management Approach
Author: Kristin Fridgeirsdottir, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, kristin@london.edu
Coauthor(s): Victor Araman
Year: 2006
Abstract: Internet advertising is the fastest growing area in the advertising industry. Webhosts providing advertising space for their customers face complex problems of advertising planning, pricing and capacity management. We develop a stylized model for revenue management of a webhost considering the uncertainty of both the demand and supply side.
