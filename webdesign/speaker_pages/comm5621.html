Title: A Win-win Pricing Strategy in Social Networking Advertising

Author: Yingying Kang, Senior Operations Research Consultant, JDA Software Group Inc., 3200 Windy Hill Road, Suite 1550, Atlanta, GA, 30339, United States of America, eing.008@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Social network has become a powerful medium for business to achieve their marketing goals. How to balance the benefits between both advertisers and social networking sites, is a key point to gain maximum revenue but at the same time gaining the long-term relationship with advertisers. This achieves a “win-win?marketing strategy. This model maximizes their total revenue by setting various advertisement prices, while balancing the benefits of individual advertisers.
