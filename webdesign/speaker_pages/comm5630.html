Title: Multi-period Procurement Strategies in the Presence of Unreliable Suppliers

Author: Awi Federgruen, Professor, Columbia University, 419 Uris Hall, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, af7@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Nan Yang

Year: 2007

Abstract: We analyze multi-period planning models for a firm which needs to cover uncertain demands by procuring supplies from multiple sources with random yields. The suppliers differ in terms of their yield distributions, prices and capacities. A procurement strategy determines, in each period, how much to order and from whom, as a function of the prevailing inventory. We characterize the structure of an optimal procurement strategy and develop efficient algorithms to compute the strategy.
