Title: Communicating Price Changes
Author: Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States, gjv1@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Wei Ke
Year: 2006
Abstract: We look at a model of dynamic pricing with reference price effects in which a firm making a price change decides whether or no to broadcast the information. Broadcasting the change attracts customers but lowers the perceiped reference price for the product. We study this tradeoff.
