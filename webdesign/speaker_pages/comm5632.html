Title: Lead Time and Price Quotation Mode Selection: Uniform or Differentiated?

Author: Xuying Zhao, University of Texas at Dallas, School of Management, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States, kxz024000@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Ashutosh Prasad, Kathryn Stecke

Year: 2006

Abstract: Firms in make-to-order industries often provide lead time and price quotations to customers. When a firm offers a single lead time and price, it is called uniform quotation mode. The differentiated quotation mode is when a firm offers a menu of lead times and prices for customers to choose from. By modeling, analyzing, and comparing these two quotation modes, we provide guidelines for a manager to choose a suitable mode for his/her firm.
