Title: Contract Timing with Forecast Updates
Author: Ozge Sahin, University of Michigan, ozge@bus.umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Izak Duenyas, Bowei Cheng
Year: 2008
Abstract: The goal of the research is to investigate the value of forecasting the demand of the retailer in a decentralized supply chain. We consider a two period, single retailer and single supplier problem. The retailer's type and subtype after the forecast update are private information. We compare and contrast three contract types and show that contrary to the common belief the supplier and retailer do not always benefit from more information.
