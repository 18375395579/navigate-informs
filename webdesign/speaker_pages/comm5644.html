Title: Bundling Strategies for Vertically Differentiated Products
Author: Dorothee Honhon, Eindhoven University of Technology, Den Dolech 2, Eindhoven, Netherlands, D.B.L.P.Honhon@tue.nl
Coauthor(s): Xiajun Amy Pan
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider a retailer selling vertically differentiated products which can be offered individually or as part of a bundle in order to maximize profits. We show that the optimal bundling strategy can be pure bundling, mixed bundling or pure components depending on the quality relationship between the products and component scarcity.
