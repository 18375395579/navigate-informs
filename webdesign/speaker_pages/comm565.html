Title: Buyer-Supplier Relationships in Global Sourcing Networks

Author: Elena Belavina, Assistant Professor, University of Chicago, Booth School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60611, United States of America, Elena.Belavina@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): Karan Girotra

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study general bipartite buyer-supplier networks, where buyer demand and supplier supply are uncertain. We identify network structures that are most amenable to establish relational equilibria in sourcing transactions. Our results illustrate challenge a number of widely held beliefs on supplier management strategies, for instance considering the network structure shows that wider supplier bases may be inimical to buyers.
