Title: The Supply Chain Effect of Bankruptcy Reorganization
Author: S. Alex Yang, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Sussex Place, London, NA, United Kingdom, sayang@london.edu
Coauthor(s): John Birge, Rodney Parker
Year: 2013
Abstract: Focusing on the interaction of supply chain structures and the cost of reorganization, this paper studies the influence of bankruptcy reorganization on both ex ante and ex post operations and performances of the financially distressed firm, its competitor, and its supplier under three supply chain structures.
