Title: Product Line Design With Strategic Customers and Co-production Manufacturing Technology
Author: Saurabh Bansal, Pennsylvania State University, 405 Business Building, University Park, PA, United States of America, sub32@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Karthik Ramachandran
Year: 2013
Abstract: We consider the product line design faced by firms who obtains various units simultaneously with random quality levels, and classify these units into different products based on the quality levels. All units of a product have quality levels greater than the tagged quality level. We investigate how this feature determines the product-line, and the contrast it to the traditional case where the quality of each unit is equal to the tagged quality.
