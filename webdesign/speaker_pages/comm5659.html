Title: The Effects of Risk Aversion and Service Constraints on a Two-period Newsvendor Policy

Author: Tatyana Chernonog, Bar Ilan University, Ramat Gan, 52900, Israel, Tatyana.Chernonog@biu.ac.il

Coauthor(s): Matan Shnaiderman

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper presents a two-period newsvendor problem with postponed pricing strategy. Risk aversion and service constraints are taken into account. These constraints may affect the optimal inventory capacity as well as the optimal prices. The effects of the constraints on supply chain performance are compared. The current two-period model leads to complicated and non-trivial solutions and insights, compared with one-period models, found in the literature. Exact analytical formulas are given.
