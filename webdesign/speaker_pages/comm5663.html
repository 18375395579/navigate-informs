Title: New Product Introduction and Services

Author: Gad Allon, Assistant Professor, Northwestern University, Kellogg School of Management, 2001 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States, g-allon@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Achal Bassamboo

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a monopoly that sells a durable product and a complementary perishable product or service. We first study the pricing problem under the assumptions that customers form rational expectation regarding future prices and quality levels of the service. We then study the “new product introduction?problem faced by the monopolist in the presence of aftermarket products or services.
