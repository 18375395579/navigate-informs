Title: Impact of RFID Technology on the Bullwhip Effect

Author: Pedro Reyes, Assistant Professor, Baylor University, One Bear Place #98006, Waco, TX, 76798, United States of America, Pedro_Reyes@baylor.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this research, we investigate the impact of RFID technology on the bullwhip effect in a decentralized and centralized supply chain. Using the traditional Beer Game, we study the availability of information captured using RFID and effect of order variance amplification and supply chain performance. Our simulation results demonstrate the use of RFID for improving supply chain operation performance may help dampen the bullwhip effect.
