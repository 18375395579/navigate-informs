Title: A Multi-Period Inventory Duopoly with Substitutable Inventory
Author: Tava Olsen, Washington University in St. Louis, Olin Business School, 1 Brookings Dr., St. Louis, MO, 63141, United States of America, olsen@wustl.edu
Coauthor(s): Rodney Parker
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider multiple period competition between retailers who stock substitutable inventory. We compare the equilibrium policy to that found in the static game. We show the sustained equilibrium policy is base-stock and that competition increases total system stock.
