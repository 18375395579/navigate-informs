Title: Repeat Procurement Auctions with Bidder Defection
Author: Christopher Held, PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, College of Management, 800 West Peachtree St NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, Christopher.Held@mgt.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Atalay Atasu, Mark Ferguson
Year: 2008
Abstract: We explore the impact of entrant bidder defection on the outcome of repeat procurement auctions. We develop a model that includes bidding pool defection and analyze the characteristics of policies that perform well in the presence of defection. We simulate traditional policies and compare their performance in the presence of bidding pool defection to a policy developed specifically for defection. We show that when defection is present, existing policies perform poorly.
