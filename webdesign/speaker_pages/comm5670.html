Title: Outsourcing Structures and Information Flow in a Three Tier Supply Chain

Author: Jeannette Song, Professor, Duke University, Fuqua School of Business, 1 Towerview Drive, Durham, NC, 27708, United States, jssong@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Pengfei Guo, Yulan Wang

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a supply chain consisting of an OEM, a contract manufacturer and a supplier. We examine the effectiveness of three outsourcing structures currently implemented by top-tier OEMs. We characterize each party’s optimal decision and the intermediary party's information distortion behavior under each structure. We then compare each party’s profit across three outsourcing structures and identify who benefits and when.
