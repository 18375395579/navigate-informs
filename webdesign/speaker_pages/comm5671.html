Title: Joint Inventory and Pricing in Product Bundling

Author: Ek Peng Chew, National University of Singapore, 10 kent Ridge Crescent, Singapore, Singapore, isecep@nus.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Qiang Wang, Loo Hay Lee

Year: 2008

Abstract: This research aims to study how product bundling performs for a retailer in terms of effects on expected profit in a supply chain who orders products from his supplier and sells them to customers. Separate products as well as bundled form of them can be provided by the supplier. Hence, the retailer needs to design its selling strategy and decide the order quantities simultaneously.
