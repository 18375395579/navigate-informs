Title: Optimal Search Behavior of Strategic Customers under Deadlines
Author: Marco Castillo, Assistant Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 685 Cherry Street, N.W., Atlanta, GA, 30332-0345, United States of America, marco.castillo@pubpolicy.gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): Laurie Garrow, Misuk Lee
Year: 2008
Abstract: We investigate optimal search and purchase behavior of a buyer under a deadline. We show strategic customer have consequences on the optimal pricing of monopolists that are themselves strategic and time-consistent. While mark-down pricing is always optimal for a large class of models of heterogeneous preferences commonly used in the discrete choice literature, we find that mark-up pricing might be optimal when buyers are time-inconsistent.
