Title: Vendor Selection, Contract Efficiency, and Performance Measurement in Outsourcing

Author: Zhijian Cui, Assistant Professor of Operations Management, IE Business School, Calle de Maria de Molina, 12, Madrid, Spain, Zhijian.Cui@ie.edu

Coauthor(s): Sameer Hasija

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study compares the efficacy of some commonly observed vendor selection and contracting mechanisms with respect to two key challenges in outsourcing of information and knowledge intensive service processes: vendor selection and contract efficiency. We also highlight the implications of performance-based contracts in services.
