Title: Information Acquisition and Production Planning

Author: Wenqiang Xiao, Assistant Professor, New York University, 3 Washington Square Village, #4B, New York, NY, 10012, United States, wxiao@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Fangruo Chen

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a salesforce compensation problem where the salesperson is engaged in two types of activities, gathering information about the market condition and promoting sales. The information gathered by the salesperson can be used by the firm to improve its production planning. We study several types of contracts, and find that a contract that requires the salesperson to submit a sales forecast maximizes the firm's expected profit.
