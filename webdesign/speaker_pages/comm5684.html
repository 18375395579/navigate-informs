Title: Value of Information in a Three Player System
Author: Manoj Vanajakumari, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, 3367 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, manojuv@neo.tamu.edu
Coauthor(s): Subodha Kumar
Year: 2013
Abstract: In this research we analyze a supply chain system consisting of a manufacturer, one dedicated and another temporary supplier. Under various levels of information exchange in the system, we derive the order quantities placed by the manufacturer to each of the suppliers.
