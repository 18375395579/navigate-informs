Title: Doing while Learning: Data Driven Production Strategies under Ambiguity
Author: Soroush Saghafian, Arizona State University, 699 S. Mill Avenue, Tempe, AZ, 85281, United States of America, Soroush.Saghafian@asu.edu
Coauthor(s): Brian Tomlin
Year: 2013
Abstract: Demand and/or supply uncertainty is a key challenge in production planning. An crucial complication in many settings is that the firm does not even have full information about the distribution of demand or supply random variable (i.e., ambiguity). Managers therefore have to produce (“do? while learning about the random demand or supply. We present a new data-driven approach to production planning when there is ambiguity about the underlying distribution of a relevant random variable.
