Title: Push, Pull, and Trade Credit Contracts in Competitive Markets

Author: Lingxiu Dong, Associate Professor, Washington University, Olin Business School, One Brookings Drive, St. Louis, MO, 63130, United States of America, dong@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Danko Turcic, Xiaomeng Guo

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the use of three basic wholesale price contracts, push, pull, and trade credit, in a competitive retail environment. The research focuses on understanding the implications of product offerings and downstream retail competition for the supply chain’s equilibrium contract.
