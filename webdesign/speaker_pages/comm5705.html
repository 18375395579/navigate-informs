Title: A Price-Dependent Demand Substitution Rule with Applications to joint Pricing and Inventory Game

Author: Ye Lu, MIT, 77 Mass. Ave. E40-130, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, yelu@MIT.EDU

Coauthor(s): David Simchi-Levi

Year: 2008

Abstract: In a multi-product market, if one product stocks out, consumers may substitute to its competing products. In this paper, we use an axiomatic approach to characterize a price-dependent demand substitution rule, and give a sufficient and necessary condition for demand models where our demand substitution rule applies. We use this demand substitution rule to study joint pricing and inventory game. We also extend the existing work in the literature for pricing game.
