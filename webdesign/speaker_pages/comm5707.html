Title: A Single Period Model with Multiple Ordering Opportunities and Fixed Ordering Cost

Author: Kamran Moinzadeh, Professor, University of Washington Business School, Box 353200, Seattle, WA, 98195-3200, United States, kamran58@comcast.net

Coauthor(s): Yong-Pin Zhou, Apurva Jain

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a fixed-life cycle product with random demand. Orders can be placed at any time, and the replenishment cost has both fixed and variable components. At the end of the product’s life cycle, leftover has salvage value and shortage incurs penalty cost. Examples of such products exist in retailing, hi-tech products, and healthcare. We first propose an optimal replenishment policy for a single retailer location, and then expand to include the case with one supplier and many retailers.
