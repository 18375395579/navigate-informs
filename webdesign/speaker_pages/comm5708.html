Title: Using Nash Bargaining to Design Project Management Contracts under Cost Uncertainty

Author: Steven Lippman, Professor, University of California-Los Angeles, Anderson School, 110 Westwood Plaza, Suite D517, Los Angeles, CA, 90095-1481, United States of America, steven.lippman@anderson.ucla.edu

Coauthor(s): Kevin McCardle, Christopher Tang

Year: 2012

Abstract: For procurement contracts in which the cost is uncertain, cost sharing is common. We determine the best cost-sharing contract between a risk-neutral project manager and a risk-averse contractor when negotiation proceeds in accord with Nash bargaining.
