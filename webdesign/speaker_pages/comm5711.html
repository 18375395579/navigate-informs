Title: Offering New and Remanufactured Products to Strategic Consumers

Author: Narendra Singh, PhD Candidate, Georgia Institute of Technology, 800 West Peachtree Street NW, Atlanta, GA, 30308, United States of America, Narendra.Singh@mgt.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Ravi Subramanian

Year: 2012

Abstract: Using a two-period model, we consider a firm that offers new and remanufactured products to strategic consumers. The firm offers a new product 1 in the first period, and an improved new product 2 and a remanufactured version of product 1 in the second period. We analyze profit-maximizing strategies for the firm under different scenarios.
