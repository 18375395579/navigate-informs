Title: Global Facility Network Design Under Price Flexibility

Author: Lingxiu Dong, Associate Professor, Washington University in St. Louis, Olin School of Business, Saint Louis, MO, United States of America, dong@wustl.edu

Coauthor(s): Ping Su, Panos Kouvelis

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a global firm who sells as a monopolist in both the domestic and foreign markets. We characterize the optimal global network design, and investigate the impact of price flexibility and the uncertainties in exchange rate and/or demand on the firm's facility location and capacity decisions.
