Title: Multi-sided Analysis of Airport - Airline Risk Sharing Contract

Author: Katsuya Hihara, U-Tokyo, 2nd Admin Bureau BLGD 7-3-1 Hongo Bunkyo, Tokyo, To, 113-0033, Japan, hihara@pp.u-tokyo.ac.jp

Year: 2013

Abstract: We analyze linear risk sharing contract between airport and airline with multi-sided aspects. With modeling their contract including demand uncertainties, we show how such contracts are formed and illustrate the impacts of changes of, for example, risk aversions, land-side productivities of airports, on prices and welfares.
