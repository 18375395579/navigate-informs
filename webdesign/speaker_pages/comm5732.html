Title: Where is the Missing Money?: The Impact of Ramping Costs in Dynamic Electricity Markets

Author: Matias Negrete-Pincetic, University of California-Berkeley, Berkeley, CA, United States of America, mnegret2@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Sean Meyn, Luciano De Castro

Year: 2013

Abstract: Can spot markets provide the necessary incentives for flexible generation? We analyze the difficulties of incentivizing flexible generation in a model of a dynamic electricity market with capacity and ramping costs. We show that average prices coincide with average marginal costs of capacity. Hence, ramping costs are not covered by the market. If the market includes a ramping price, then the competitive equilibrium is no longer unique. We give a complete characterization of possible prices.
