Title: Selling to Conspicuous Consumers
Author: Senthil Veeraraghavan, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, senthilv@wharton.upenn.edu
Coauthor(s): Necati Tereyagoglu
Year: 2008
Abstract: We model the value of stockouts for products which are consumed or purchased conspicuously. We examine the influence of scarcity on the valuation of the customer for the product, leading to its effects on the demand, and pricing and production decisions of a monopolist.
