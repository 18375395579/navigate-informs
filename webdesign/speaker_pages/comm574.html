Title: Price-only Contract with Backup Supply

Author: Yao Zhao, Rutgers University, 180 University Ave, Newark, NJ, 07102, United States, yaozhao@andromeda.rutgers.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiaowei Xu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We explore a new potential way to mitigate the ``double marginalization effect by a backup supply. Specifically, we consider a supply chain with one manufacturer and one retailer in newsvendor settings. In addition to regular supply, the manufacturer may offer a backup supply to satisfy demand not met by the retailer. We study when to offer backup supply, how it affects regular supply and overall supply chain profit.
