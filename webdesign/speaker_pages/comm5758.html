Title: Designing Pricing Policies for Cloud Service Providers

Author: Megha Sharma, Assistant Professor, Indian Institute of Management Calcutta, Diamond Harbour Road, Joka, Kolkata, WB, 700104, India, megha@iimcal.ac.in

Coauthor(s): Sumanta Basu, Soumyakanti Chakraborty

Year: 2012

Abstract: Infrastructure-as-a-Service (Iaas) is expected to grow at a rapid pace in the next few years because of the flexibility offered to online platform providers in terms of meeting demand fluctuations. Pricing this service naturally becomes a critical issue for IaaS providers. We explore different pricing options for IaaS providers and their acceptance conditions for online businesses. We conclude by comparing our findings from different pricing plans and offer some guidelines on pricing IaaS.
