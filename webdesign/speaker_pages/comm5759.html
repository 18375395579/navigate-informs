Title: An Experimental Study on Quantity Discounts in Supply Chains
Author: Diana Wu, Assistant Prof. of Decision Sciences, University of Kansas, 1300 Sunnyside Ave. 313D, Lawrence, KS, 66045, United States, dianawu@ku.edu
Year: 2007
Abstract: Supply chain contracts have been well studied analytically, yet less is known about their practical performance. Using a controlled laboratory setting, we investigate the performance of one particular type of contract that is widely applied in practice: quantity discounts. Our research question asked is which form of discount schedule is most effective in improving supply chain coordination.
