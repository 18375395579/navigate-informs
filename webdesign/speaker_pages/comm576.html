Title: Dynamic Pricing of Experience Goods of Unknown Quality: The Impact of Consumer Reviews

Author: Roman Kapuscinski, Professor, Ross School of Business, University of Michigan, 701 Tappan St, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, kapuscin@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Man Yu, Laurens Debo

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider pricing of goods whose quality is unknown to the firm, but, is imperfectly assessed by consumers that purchased the good and who transmit their assessment via consumer reviews to consumers that did not buy yet the good.
