Title: Reference Prices and Bidder Heterogeneity in Secondary Market Online B2B Auctions
Author: Wedad Elmaghraby, Associate Professor, University of Maryland, Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, welmaghr@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Ali Pilehvar, Anand Gopal
Year: 2012
Abstract: Using data collected from a large wholesale liquidator who runs auctions to sell the bundles of returned goods and excess inventory, we study the behavior of these secondary market B2B auctions. We empirically investigate how formed reference prices by bidders, moderated by bidder heterogeneity, impact their bids.
