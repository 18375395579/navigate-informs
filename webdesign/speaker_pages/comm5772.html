Title: The Structural Properties of Pricing Optimization for a Network of Products

Author: Hong Zhi He, Ph.D Student, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Dept. of SEEM, CUHK, Hong Kong, hzhe@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Youyi Feng

Year: 2007

Abstract: We explore the structural properties of the pricing optimization for an airline's travelling network. Our results disclose how the physical configuration of the network affects ticket sales in a dynamic manner, and in particular, how optimal dynamic pricing rules balance demand and supply on substitutable or complementary itineraries.
