Title: Subsidizing Risky Suppliers: A Comparison of Supply Chain Structures
Author: Adam Wadecki, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, awadecki@umich.edu
Coauthor(s): Volodymyr Babich
Year: 2008
Abstract: Supplier financial distress affects supply risks faced by manufacturers. To alleviate such risks, manufacturers may provide suppliers with financial subsidies. We examine the optimal subsidy decisions and supply chain profits when manufacturers deal with dedicated and shared suppliers. We also examine the affects of competition among manufacturers.
