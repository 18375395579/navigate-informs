Title: Retail Budget Allocations for Store Labor and Promotions

Author: Olga Perdikaki, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, 320 Wehner Building, College Station, TX, 77843-4217, United States of America, operdikaki@mays.tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Subodha Kumar, Chelliah Sriskandarajah

Year: 2013

Abstract: The performance of a retailer depends on its ability to attract traffic into its stores and convert the incoming traffic into sales profitably. We establish the relationship between weekly sales performance of a retailer with respect to weekly store traffic, labor force deployment, and intra-day traffic variability. We devise a strategy to allocate limited funds for advertising, early-bird promotions, and labor force for weekly operation in order to maximize store profit.
