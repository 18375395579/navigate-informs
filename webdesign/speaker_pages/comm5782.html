Title: Iterative Auction Design for Graphical Valuations

Author: Ozan Candogan, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 32 Vassar St, Room D640, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, candogan@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Daron Acemoglu, Asuman Ozdaglar, Pablo A. Parrilo

Year: 2012

Abstract: Multi-item iterative auctions are commonly employed in practice, for instance in the context of spectrum auctions and procurement auctions, often without efficiency guarantees. In this work, we focus on a special but realistic class of valuation functions, which we refer to as graphical valuations, and develop simple efficient iterative auctions. The auctions we provide are applicable in various practical environments and allow for items that exhibit value complementarity and substitutability.
