Title: Connecting CBM to the Supply Chain
Author: Greg Parlier, Dr., SAIC, 255 Avian Lane, Madison, AL, 35758, United States, GREG.H.PARLIER@saic.com
Coauthor(s): Michelle Liddon
Year: 2007
Abstract: This paper examines creative methods for “connecting?CBM to the supply chain utilizing usage data from the CBM Data Warehouse to improve supply chain operations and management. We also outline how CBM Data Warehouse information can be combined with supply chain initiatives to significantly increase part demand forecast accuracy, improve supply chain inventory management, and enhance readiness.
