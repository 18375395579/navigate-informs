Title: Dynamic Acceptance Sampling Plans in a Supply Chain with Endogenous Product Quality

Author: Tian Ni, PhD student, Purdue University, 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, ni10@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Xiaowei Xu, Hong Wan

Year: 2013

Abstract: This article studies a firm that procures a product from a supplier in a repeated setting. In each period, based on the historical public information the firm determines the optimal acceptance sampling plan and the supplier strategically determines the quality effort level. Internal product failure and external product failure coexist in our model. Sufficient conditions that guarantee the existence of Nash equilibrium are also provided.
