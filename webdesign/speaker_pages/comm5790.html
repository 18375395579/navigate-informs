Title: Robust Markdown Pricing with Two Products
Author: Ming Chen, University of Maryland, Robert H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, chenming@rhsmith.umd.edu
Coauthor(s): Zhi-Long Chen
Year: 2008
Abstract: We consider a markdown pricing problem with two dependent products. The demand for one product is a function of the prices of both products, and only a limited information about this function is known. We propose a solution approach for maximizing the revenue under the worst-case demand scenario.
