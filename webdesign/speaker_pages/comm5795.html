Title: Supply Chain Risk Assessment Applying System Dynamics Approach, Case Study: Apparel Industry

Author: Marzieh Mehrjoo, University of Windsor, 335 Detroit St., Apt 6, Windsor, ON, n9c2p4, Canada, mehrjoo@uwindsor.ca

Coauthor(s): Zbigniew J Pasek

Year: 2013

Abstract: A remarkable increase in the demand and supply uncertainty have increased the necessity of assessing and managing the risk in the supply chain. The purpose of this study is to investigate the impact of different categories of risk on the performance of an apparel supply chain. A system dynamics approach was used to study the behavior and relationships within the supply chain. The proposed model provides a tool to generate multiple business scenarios for effective decision making.
