Title: An Analysis of Pricing and Leadtime Policies within the Marketing/Operations Interface
Author: Pelin Pekgun-Cakmak, Senior Consultant, Operations Research Affiliation: JDA Software Group, pelin.pekgun@jda.com
Year: 2008
Abstract: In this thesis, we analyze the impact of the decentralization of price and leadtime decisions made by marketing and production, respectively, under monopolistic and duopolistic settings. We also address the challenges encountered in estimating the price elasticity in the passenger travel industry, and empirically show the improvement when endogeneity is accounted for.
