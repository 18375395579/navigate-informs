Title: Subsidy Strategies in a Location Model
Author: H.A. Eiselt, Professor, University of New Brunswick, P.O. Box 4400, Fredericton, NB, Canada, HAeiselt@unb.ca
Coauthor(s): Joy Bhadury, Steve Peng
Year: 2007
Abstract: The paper considers a location model with three levels of decision makers: customers who purchase products, firms that locate, and a regional planner who awards subsidies. Customers buy at the cheapest source, firms maximize profits from sales plus subsidies and planners maximize social benefit. The resulting location patterns are examined.
