Title: A Method to Systematically Articulate the Value of IT Outsourcing Services to Clients

Author: Rama Akkiraju, Distinguished Engineer, Service Solution Design, IBM Research, 650 Harry Road, Almaden Research Center, San Jose, CA, 95120, United States of America, akkiraju@us.ibm.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: IT outsourcing service providers are exploring various ways to offer innovative, high quality and differentiated services to their clients to increase their profit margins in an increasingly commoditizing market. However, they struggle to articulate the value of these services to the client to win the bid. We present a systematic method-based approach to enable IT outsourcing service providers to articulate the value of their services to the client to increase their changes of winning a bid.
