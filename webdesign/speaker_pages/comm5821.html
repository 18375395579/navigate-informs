Title: Designing Platforms for Supplier Aggregation and Integration

Author: Burcu Tan, Assistant Professor, Tulane University, Freeman School of Business, New Orleans, LA, 70115, United States of America, btan@tulane.edu

Coauthor(s): Amiya Chakravarty, Geoffrey Parker

Year: 2013

Abstract: We focus on an e-marketplace that matches buyers and suppliers to study the subscription fee and the context-service investment that determine the viability of the marketplace. Although others have studied the basic business-to-business exchange investment problem, this paper is novel because we incorporate heterogeneous complementary externalities between sellers. We characterize conditions for the exchange operator to set fees such that not all suppliers are willing to join.
