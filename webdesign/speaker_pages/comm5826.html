Title: How Consumers Shop Online: Data, Models and Pricing Decisions

Author: Zachary Leung, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, zacleung@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Georgia Perakis, Pavithra Harsha

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study an Internet retailer selling fashion items. We propose a demand model, where customers may not buy the item on their initial visit, but return over time and may purchase items on subsequent visits. We propose and analyze a markdown optimization pricing model with returning customers. Using clickstream data collected from an online fashion retailer, we estimate our demand model and quantify the benefit of applying our model.
