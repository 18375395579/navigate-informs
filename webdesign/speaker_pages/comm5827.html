Title: Stochastic Fluid Models for Production-Inventory Systems

Author: Vidyadhar Kulkarni, Professor, Dept. of Stat and OR, University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599-3260, United States, vkulkarn@email.unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Keqi Yan

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a production-inventory system where the production and demand rates are modulated by an external environment that evolves as a continuous time Markov chain. Orders can also be placed at an external supplier. We present methods of computing the optimal ordering policies in the presence of leadtimes and backorders.
