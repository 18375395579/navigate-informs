Title: Cardinality Bundles for Spence-Mirrlees Reservation Prices
Author: Jianqing Wu, Purdue Univercity, 403 W. State Street, Room 412, W Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, wu35@purdue.edu
Coauthor(s): Mohit Tawarmalani, Karthik Kannan
Year: 2013
Abstract: We develop optimal prices for cardinality bundling (CB) when customers are ordered by their preferences. In CB, the price depends on the number but not on the specific goods purchased; e.g., when songs are sold with CB, consumers may choose any songs up to the specified number. We develop polynomial time algorithms for various cost structures assuming that a customer purchases at most one bundle. Subsequently, we relax this assumption and study the impact of this assumption on seller profits.
