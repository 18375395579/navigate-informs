Title: Push, Pull, or Both? A Behavioral Study of Inventory Risk on Supply Chain Performance
Author: Andrew Davis, Assistant Professor, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, 14853, United States of America, adavis@cornell.edu
Coauthor(s): Elena Katok
Year: 2013
Abstract: We experimentally investigate how the allocation of inventory risk affects supply chain performance. We first evaluate two wholesale price contracts that differ in which party holds the inventory and develop a behavioral model. We then apply this model to a third contract and find that it qualitatively matches behavior.
