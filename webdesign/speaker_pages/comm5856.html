Title: Pricing Policy in a Stackelberg Game on Supply Chain Network with Imperfect Information

Author: Tao Yao, Pennsylvania State University, 349 Leonhard Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, taoyao@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Amir Meimand, Terry Friesz

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper we present a model for stackelberg game on supply chain network in which supplier is a leader and retailers are followers and have uncertain observability of leader’s policy which is the whole sale price. Then we study the effect of this uncertainty on the profitability of supplier and will discuss how different pricing policies can mitigate the negative effect of uncertainty.
