Title: Are Incentives Enough? The Value of Audit in Managing Supplier's Process Improvement
Author: Mohammad Nikoofal, PhD Student, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke Street West, Bronfman Building, Montreal, QC, H3A 1G5, Canada, mohammad.nikoofal@mail.mcgill.ca
Coauthor(s): Mehmet Gumus
Year: 2013
Abstract: Besides the benefits of outsourcing, firms are concerned about the lack of information regarding their suppliers. Moreover, suppliers may take certain actions that affect their supply risks without informing their buyers. We study the effectiveness of incentive- and audit-based contracts in dealing with such suppliers.
