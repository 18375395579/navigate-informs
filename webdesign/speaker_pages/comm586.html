Title: Strategic Pricing and Capacity Planning in Cloud Computing Markets
Author: Manuel Nunez, Associate Professor, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, mnunez@business.uconn.edu
Coauthor(s): Linna Du, Xue Bai
Year: 2013
Abstract: We develop pricing and capacity planning models for a cloud provider considering trade-offs between service reliability and capacity utilization. The provider's objectives are to optimally allocate resources, price different types of cloud services, and extract the maximum revenue from the market. We analyze two basic types of cloud services: contract and on-demand; and propose hybrid systems in which the two basic services simultaneously co-exist.
