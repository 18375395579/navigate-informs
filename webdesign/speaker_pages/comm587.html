Title: Analysis of Tailored Base-Surge Policies in Dual Sourcing Inventory Systems
Author: Ganesh Janakiraman, Associate Professor, Jindal School of Management, The University of Texas at DallasThe University of Texas at Dallas, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, ganesh@utdallas.edu
Coauthor(s): Sridhar Seshari, Anshul Sheopuri
Year: 2012
Abstract: We study a firm managing its inventory by sourcing supplies from two supply sources, a low cost, slow regular supplier and a high-cost, emergency, supplier. Firms often use a Tailored Base-Surge(TBS) Policy (Allon and Van Mieghem 2010) in such cases: Procure a constant quantity from the regular supplier in every period and choose the emergency procurement quantity dynamically. We present a set of analytical results to understand when these policies are most effective.
