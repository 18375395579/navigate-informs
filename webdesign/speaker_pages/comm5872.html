Title: Effects of Inventory Inaccuracy in Joint Marketing and Inventory Decisions

Author: Shaoxuan Liu, UC Irvine, 419 The Paul Merage School of Business, UC Irvine, Irvine, ca, 92697, United States, sliu02@merage.uci.edu

Coauthor(s): Fuqiang Zhang, Rick So

Year: 2007

Abstract: We develop a mathematic model to study how supply uncertainty due to inventory inaccuracy affects a firm’s marketing effort and stocking quantity decisions. We use our model to analyze the value of coordination between marketing and inventory decisions and quantify the benefits of emerging technology such as RFID.
