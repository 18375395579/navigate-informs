Title: Leasing and Its Relationship with Company Growth and Technology Innovation
Author: Kate Li, The Pennsylvania State University, Smeal College Business, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, jzl120@psu.edu
Coauthor(s): Susan Xu
Year: 2008
Abstract: Leasing helps to expand consumer base and eliminate the uncertainty of product return. Moreover, by coordinating lease design with product introduction, companies can effectively guide customers through desired product migration path. We use a dynamic programming framework to study leasing and its relationship with company growth, secondary market development and product innovation, and provide insights on the impact of leasing under different scenarios.
