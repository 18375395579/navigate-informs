Title: Product Differentiation through Environmental Quality

Author: Ozgen Karaer, Assistant Professor, Middle East Technical University, Turkey, okaraer@ie.metu.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): John Khawam, Tim Kraft

Year: 2012

Abstract: We examine a firm's investments in environmental quality when prices are fixed but a shift in market segmentation has occurred. Our research goals are to 1) better understand how a firm can differentiate its product through quality investments and 2) identify how a firm should work with a supplier to improve the environmental quality of the firm's product.
