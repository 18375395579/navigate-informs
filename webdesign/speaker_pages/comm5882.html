Title: Price-setting Newsvendor Problems with Uncertain Supply and Risk Aversion
Author: Scott Webster, Syracuse University, Whitman School of Management, 721 University Ave., Syracuse, NY, 13244, United States of America, stwebste@syr.edu
Coauthor(s): Burak Kazaz
Year: 2013
Abstract: The price-setting newsvendor problem, which models the economic tradeoffs associated with uncertain demand, is fundamental to supply chain analysis. However, in settings such as agriculture, there is significant economic risk associated with supply uncertainty. We extend technical advances in the literature to accommodate supply uncertainty and risk aversion.
