Title: Contracts and Product Choice

Author: Wedad Elmaghraby, Associate Professor, University of Maryland, RH Smith School of Business, Van Munching Hall Room 4352, College Park, MD, 20742-1815, United States of America, wedad_elmaghraby@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Anna Devlin, Rebecca Hamilton

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this research we study experimentally whether or not the type of contract offered influences the retailer’s choice between two products where one product is obviously riskier. We hypothesize that when offered a risk reducing contract, the retailer will be more likely to select the riskier product as compared to a case when he is offered a wholesale price contract. These findings could have implications for future contract design as well as product assortment.
