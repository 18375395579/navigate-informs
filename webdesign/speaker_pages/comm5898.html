Title: Impact of Transmission Congestion on the Forward Risk Premium in Electricity Markets

Author: Shi-Jie Deng, Associate Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, Industrial & Systems Engineering, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, deng@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Haibin Sun

Year: 2008

Abstract: We examine the optimal hedging and equilibrium pricing of electricity with the presence of both forwards and spot markets subject to transmission system constraints. Given the day-ahead and spot markets?clearing rules set by a system operator and the fact that LSEs?and GenCo’s maximize their risk-adjusted profits, we derive the forward and spot market equilibrium prices which incorporate the transmission congestion information. Empirical analysis is conducted to test our model.
