Title: A Simple Ascending Auction for Assignment Problems

Author: De Liu, University of Kentucky, 425D Gatton B&E Building, Lexington, KY, 40506, United States of America, de.liu@uky.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We introduce a simple ascending auction that allocates heterogeneous objects among bidders with purely private unit demands. Our auction design economizes information needed from bidders, uses a simple and intuitive price adjustment procedure, and accommodates explicit reserve prices. Despite these features, it stops in a finite time, has a sincere bidding ex-post Nash equilibrium, and the ending prices and revenue depend only on reserve prices.
