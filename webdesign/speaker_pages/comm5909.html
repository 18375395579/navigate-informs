Title: A Kernel Based Approach to Storage and Swing Contracts Valuations in High Dimensions

Author: Denis Mazieres, Head of Methodology & Modelling, LCH.Clearnet & Birkbeck, University of London, Aldgate House, 33 Aldgate High Street, London, EC3N 1EA, United Kingdom, d.mazieres@ems.bbk.ac.uk

Year: 2012

Abstract: This work expands upon the original work from Hubbert & Mazières, Tensor of Radial Basis Functions:..., where the Tensor of Radial Basis Functions (TBF) was first introduced and from Boogert & Mazières, A Radial Basis Function Approach to Gas Storage Valuation, 2011, where it was later applied in gas storage valuation in two dimensions. This work introduces multi-factor price and volume dimensions to storage and swing contract valuation by using Kernels: RBF and TBF.
