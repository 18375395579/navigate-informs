Title: Optimal Bundle Formation and Pricing of Two Products with Limited Stock
Author: Alper Sen, Assistant Professor, Bilkent University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Bilkent, Ankara, 06800, Turkey, alpersen@bilkent.edu.tr
Coauthor(s): Ulku Gurler, Salih Oztop
Year: 2006
Abstract: We consider a retailer that sells a fixed stock of two products not only as independent items but also as a bundle over a season. The retailer decides how many bundles to form and what prices to charge for the bundle so that expected profits over season are maximized. Product demands follow a Poisson Process and customer reservation prices are assumed to have a joint distribution. We study the impact of reservation prices, inventory levels, arrival rates and bundle formation costs.
