Title: Analysis of Propagation of Disruptions Produced by Terrorist Acts on Global Supply Chains Performance

Author: Alfredo Bueno Solano, Researcher, COMIMSA - National Council of Science & Technology, Calle ciencia y tecnología N?790, Satillo , Choahuila méxico, Mexico, alfredo.bueno@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Rosa G. Gonzalez-Ramírez, Miguel Gaston Cedillo-Campos

Year: 2012

Abstract: Understanding the security disruptions and how their effects propagate through the supply chain is critical to promote security and efficient movement of goods. This research proposes systems dynamic as an effective modeling technique for analyzing the effects of the materialization and simultaneous propagation of disruptions of security at various points in supply chains. The article shows that the impact on inventory levels in the chain can increase 600% compared to normal operating conditions
