Title: OR Challenges in the Wine Supply Chain
Author: Alejandro Mac Cawley, Georgia Tech, 765 Ferst Dr NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, amac@gatech.edu
Coauthor(s): John Bartholdi
Year: 2012
Abstract: The international supply chains for wine are complex. Wine is a highly branded product produced in a few countries and shipped thousands of miles to consumers. Furthermore, there is a limited supply and at least one large consumer, the US, legally restricts distribution through a three-tier system. We will describe some OR challenges in this supply chain, from the winery to the importer, the distributor, and the retailer.
