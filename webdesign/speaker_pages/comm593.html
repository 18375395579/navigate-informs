Title: Dynamic Two-Sided Pricing under Sequential Innovation

Author: Xiajun Amy Pan, University of Florida, PO Box 117169, Gainsville, FL, United States of America, amypan@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Mei Lin

Year: 2013

Abstract: Many two-sided platforms offer innovative hardware products, which improve in quality and enter the marketplace sequentially. We analyze the impact of reductions in future production cost and network effects on a monopolist's dynamic pricing strategies for a durable good facing strategic buyers.
