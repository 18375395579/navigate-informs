Title: Near Optimal Design of Price and Delay Differentiation

Author: Assaf Zeevi, Associate Professor, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, assaf@gsb.columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Costis Maglaras

Year: 2006

Abstract: A service provider offers substitutable products to a segmented market comprised of customers with heterogenous preferences. The ``products'' are differentiated based on price and delay. How many such products to offer and how to design them are some of the questions addressed in this talk.
