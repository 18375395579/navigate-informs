Title: Firm's Optimal Resource Portfolio Under Consumer Choice, and Supply and Demand Uncertainties
Author: Weiping Chen, Virginia Tech., Blacksburg, VA, United States, wpchen@vt.edu
Coauthor(s): Ebru Bish
Year: 2007
Abstract: We study the firm's optimal resource investment portfolio when facing both supply and demand risks under a consumer choice model. The firm produces two substitutable products, and can invest in product-dedicated resources as well as a more expensive, flexible resource. We investigate how the firm can utilize resource diversification and product differentiation strategies to hedge against supply and demand risks.
