Title: Modeling the Impact of Competitive Index Sharing in a Grocery Supply Chain

Author: Ananth Iyer, Professor, Purdue University, Rawls Hall, Room 4001, 100 S Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, aiyer@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Arnd Huchzermeier, Daniela Schmitz-Wiehenbrauk

Year: 2008

Abstract: We model the impact of a manufacturer sharing a retail competition index, based on shipment to retailers, with all retailers in a grocery supply chain. The model suggests conditions under which such information can improve supply chain performance in a Pareto manner. Data from the German grocery industry is used to estimate the impact and suggest managerial insights.
