Title: Cooperation for Sustainability

Author: Fang Tian, Ph.D. Candidate, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, IOM Department, BRI 401, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-0809, United States of America, tianfang@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Marco Slikker, Greys Sosic

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider the Design for Environment (DfE) setting and study manufacturers who cooperate by sharing their technology for product design and manufacture in order to improve products?recyclability. We discuss the incentives that could facilitate manufacturers?cooperation under different circumstances by analyzing their impact on manufacturers?profits, consumers?surplus, and the social welfare.
