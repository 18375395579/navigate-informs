Title: Having your Cake: An Empirical Study of the Impact of Workload on Worker Productivity
Author: Tom Tan, Cox Business School, Southern Methodist University, 6212 Bishop Blvd, Dallas, TX, 75275, United States of America, ttan@cox.smu.edu
Coauthor(s): Serguei Netessine
Year: 2013
Abstract: We find inverted-U shaped relationships between workload and performance. In the focal restaurant chain we find that this saturation point is generally not reached and, counter-intuitively, the chain can reduce the staffing level and achieve both significantly higher sales (an estimated 3% increase) and lower labor costs (an estimated 17% decrease).
