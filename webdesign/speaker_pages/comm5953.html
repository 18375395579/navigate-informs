Title: Supplier Selection and Order Quantity Allocation with Quantity Discounts and Price-Sensitive Demand

Author: Hamza Adeinat, The Pennsylvania State University, 201 Old Main, University Park, State College, 16802, United States of America, hma5104@psu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: A MINLP model is present for a replenishment problem that consists of a single buyer and multiple potential suppliers. Each supplier offers all-unit quantity discounts. The buyer is allowed to submit multiple orders to the selected suppliers during a repeating order cycle. The buyer’s demand rate is price sensitive. The model finds the optimal number of orders and corresponding order quantities, and the optimal demand rate and selling price that maximizes the buyer’s total profit per time unit.
