Title: Experimental Investigation of Salesforce Pricing Decisions

Author: Rashmi Sharma, Pennsylvania State University, 463 A Business Building, University Park, PA, United States of America, ros5253@smeal.psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Elena Katok, Saurabh Bansal, Peter Rimshnick

Year: 2013

Abstract: We model sales force pricing decisions under the uncertainty of getting a contract, and various incentive schemes. We identify three different classes of projects, and the incentive schemes best suited for each class. Subsequently, we test pricing decisions in a laboratory environment to identify behavioral factors that influence sales force pricing decisions.
