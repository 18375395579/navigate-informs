Title: Multi-channel Pricing of Subscription-based Products with Consumer Choice
Author: Ali Sadighian, Columbia University, 313 Mudd Building, 500 W. 120th Street, New York, NY, 10027, United States, as2446@columbia.edu
Coauthor(s): Soulaymane Kachani, Woonghee Tim Huh
Year: 2007
Abstract: We address the problem of a magazine publisher facing stochastic demand over multiple periods. Magazines are offered through both online and traditional channels, and consumers choose their preferred medium based on a specific choice model (e.g. multinomial logit). We model the dynamics of customer subscription and attrition, and derive optimal pricing and production quantity decisions for magazine publishers.
