Title: The Dynamics of Electric Vehicle Adoption Systems
Author: Buket Avci, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77300, France, buket.avci@insead.edu
Coauthor(s): Karan Girotra, Serguei Netessine
Year: 2012
Abstract: We analyze the dynamics of electric vehicle adoption decision in a market where customers are offered different ownership structures (purchasing miles vs. purchasing batteries). We compare these ownership structures in terms of market adoption and environmental impact. We also investigate how the changes in future gasoline and electricity prices and the advances in battery technology influence the dynamics of electric vehicle adoption.
