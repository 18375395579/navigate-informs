Title: Dynamics of Sustainable Business Relationship
Author: Pavel Izhutov, PhD student, Stanford University, 655 Knight Way, Stanford Graduate School of Business, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, IZHUTOV@stanford.edu
Coauthor(s): Hau L. Lee
Year: 2012
Abstract: A retailer is interested in designing a contract that ensures the use of responsible production process by the supplier. The key features of the examined contract are its duration and continuation/termination conditions. The optimal contract ensuring responsible supplier behavior is derived.
