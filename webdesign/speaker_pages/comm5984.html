Title: Competition and Innovation in Technology Markets

Author: Bo Van der Rhee, University of Utah, 1645 E. Campus Center Drive, Salt Lake City, UT, 84112-9349, United States, pmgtbv@business.utah.edu

Coauthor(s): Glen Schmidt

Year: 2006

Abstract: We developed an analytical model to examine the case where technology based products consist of two attributes, quality and a new feature, which are negatively correlated. Firms compete in a duopoly on these attributes as well as on price and we examine the conditions under which either the high or the low quality firm would introduce the new feature.
