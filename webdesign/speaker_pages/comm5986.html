Title: Product Design and Channel Choice Under Extended Producer Responsibility

Author: Sudheer Gupta, Simon Fraser University, 8888 University Drive, Burnaby, BC, Canada, sudheerg@sfu.ca

Coauthor(s): Brian Talbot, Ravi Subramanian

Year: 2007

Abstract: As environmental concerns mount, an increasing number of durable goods fall under the umbrella of extended producer responsibility (EPR). We examine the impact of EPR policies on manufacturers?choice of optimal product design attributes and channel coordination incentives. We show how some commonly used contractual forms, such as two-part tariffs, quantity discounts, leasing, and buy-backs perform under EPR and their effect on product design, channel profits and environmental outcomes.
