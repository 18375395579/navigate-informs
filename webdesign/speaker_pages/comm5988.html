Title: Managing Technology Uncertainty under Multi-Firm New Product Development

Author: Sreekumar Bhaskaran, Assistant Professor, Southern Methodist University, Cox School of Business, Dallas, TX, 75275, United States, sbhaskar@cox.smu.edu

Coauthor(s): Vish Krishnan

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this paper, we conceptualize and formulate the co-development of products involving two firms and examine the implications of two collaboration mechanisms found in industrial practice which involve sharing of the development cost and sharing of the development work. We consider both exogenous and endogenous technology uncertainty, and study the impact of these mechanisms on a firm’s technology consideration set, product qualities, and profits.
