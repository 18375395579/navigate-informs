Title: Order-timing Strategies and Incentives in a Multi-retailer, Single-period Setting
Author: Joseph Geunes, Associate Professor, University of Florida, Department of Industrial and Systems Eng, 303 Weil Hall, P.O. Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, geunes@ise.ufl.edu
Coauthor(s): Ismail Bakal
Year: 2007
Abstract: We consider order-timing preferences in a single-supplier, multi-retailer system facing a single period of uncertain demand. We characterize conditions under which the supplier and a given retailer prefer that the retailer order pre- or post demand realization. When the retailer prefers ordering after demand realization, we consider mechanisms the supplier can use to induce an early order, as well as the conditions under which it is in the supplier's best interest to apply these mechanisms.
