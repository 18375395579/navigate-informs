Title: Price Equilibrium of Online Advertisement and Click Fraud Problem

Author: Wei Zhou, University of Florida, 305 Diamond Vlg., Apt 20, Gainesville, FL, 32603, United States, wzhou@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Praveen Pathak, Shubho Bandyopadhyay

Year: 2007

Abstract: We develop a model to find the price equilibrium for internet advertisement industry among the three parties: publisher, coordinator, and advertiser. We find the publisher-coordinator contract to be a double-sided moral hazard problem, and the coordinator-advertiser contract a double moral hazard problem with agent¡¯s effort observed. We further show that an efficient pricing strategy has the power to deter click fraud, which is a major problem facing the internet advertising industry recently.
