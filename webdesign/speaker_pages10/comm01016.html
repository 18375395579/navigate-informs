Title: Combining Multiple Subjective Probabilities: Applications to Aviation Safety Risk Assessment

Author: Kazim Topuz, PhD Student, Rutgers University, Department of Industrial & Systems Engin, 96 Frelinghuysen Road CoRE Building, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, mktopuz@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): James T. Luxhoj

Year: 2013

Abstract: We focus on combining subjective probabilities in aviation safety. We used Subject Matter Experts in probability elicitation for separate risk assessment models. A composite model requires combining subjective probabilities. We proposed three probability combining protocols: Rational Combination is inspired by the Expected Utilization Theory; Irrational Combination is inspired by Prospect Theory; Realistic Combination involves using the results of the individual models selected to combine.
