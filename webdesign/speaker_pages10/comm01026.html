Title: A Fast Swap-based Local Search Procedure for Location Problems

Author: Renato Werneck, Researcher, Microsoft Research Silicon Valley, 1065 La Avenida, Mountain View, CA, 94043, United States, renatow@microsoft.com

Coauthor(s): Mauricio G. C. Resende

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a new implementation of a widely used swap-based local search procedure for the P-median problem, proposed in 1968 by Teitz and Bart. It produces the same output as the best alternatives described in the literature and, even though its worst-case complexity is similar, it can be significantly faster in practice: speedups of up to 3 orders of magnitude were observed. We also show that our method can be easily adapted to implement path-relinking and tabu search.
