Title: Determining Tolerable Dosages with Multiple Responses and Multiple Predictors

Author: Edward Boone, Associate Professor of Statistics, Virginia Commonweath University, 1001 West Main Street, Richmond, VA, 23112, United States of America, elboone@vcu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Determining tolerable dosages/exposures to toxic substances is often complicated by ill defined endpoints that can be measured by multiple outcome variables. This is further complicated by having multiple stressors/predictors. This talk presents a novel method for determining tolerable dosages using a Bayesian approach for this situation.
