Title: Banking Efficiency with Stochastic Data

Author: John Ruggiero, University of Dayton, 517 Miriam Hall, Dayton, OH, United States of America, John.Ruggiero@notes.udayton.edu

Coauthor(s): Alex Totomanov, Erin Landers

Year: 2007

Abstract: We analyze the technical efficiency of U.S. banks while accounting for statistical noise. This is achieved by aggregation of panel data.
