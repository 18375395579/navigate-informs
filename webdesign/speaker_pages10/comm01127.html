Title: Monitoring Nonlinear Profiles using Wavelet Filtering and B-Spline Approximation

Author: Shing Chang, Associate Professor, Kansas State University, 228 Durland Hall, Manhatta, Ka, 66506, United States of America, changs@ksu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: A statistical process control framework is proposed to monitor nonlinear profiles. The proposed methodology identifies mean shifts or “shape changes?in a profile. Discrete wavelet transformation is applied to separate variation or noise from profile contours. B-splines are adopted to generate critical points to define the shape of a profile.
