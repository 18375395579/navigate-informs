Title: On Nonparametric Statistical Process Control

Author: Peihua Qiu, Professor, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, qiuxx008@umn.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider statistical process control (SPC) when the parametric form of the process distribution is unavailable. In the literature, some nonparametric SPC charts have been proposed based on the ordering information of the observed data. We present an alternative framework for constructing nonparametric control charts using categorical data analysis. This framework is demonstrated to work well in various applications.
