Title: Performance-based Regularization in Mean-CVaR Portfolio Optimization

Author: Gah-Yi Vahn, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, United Kingdom, gvahn@london.edu

Coauthor(s): Noureddine El Karoui, Andrew Lim

Year: 2012

Abstract: We introduce performance-based regularization (PBR), a new approach to addressing estimation risk in data-driven optimization, to mean-CVaR portfolio optimization. We assume the available log-return data is iid, and detail the approach for two cases: nonparametric and parametric (the log-return distribution belongs in the elliptical family). We show that the PBR methods produce efficient frontiers that are, on average, closer to the population efficient frontier with less variability.
