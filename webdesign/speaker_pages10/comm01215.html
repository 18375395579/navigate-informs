Title: On the Development of a Fraud Rate Estimation Method

Author: Jing Ai, The University of Hawaii at Manoa, FEI Dept, Shidler College of Business, 2404 Maile Way, Honolulu, HI, 96815, United States of America, jing.ai@hawaii.edu

Coauthor(s): Patrick Brockett

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper proposes a method for insurance fraud rate estimation, which contributes to solving the current controversy in assessing the amount of fraud in the market place. The proposed estimation method can be used in a host of applications and is shown to produce accurate results in a cost-effective manner.
