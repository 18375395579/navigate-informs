Title: Reliability and Maintenance for Dependent Competing Failure Processes with Shifting Thresholds

Author: Qianmei Feng, Associate Professor, Houston University, E206 Engineering Bldg 2, Houston University, Houston, TX, 77204-4008, United States of America, qmfeng@uh.edu

Coauthor(s): Lei Jiang, David Coit

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present reliability and maintenance models for systems subject to multiple dependent competing failure processes with a changing and dependent failure threshold. Three cases of dependency between shock process and hard failure threshold level are studied. Reliability models are developed based on degradation and random shock modeling. Two preventive maintenance policies are also applied and compared. Then an MEMS example is given to demonstrate the reliability models and maintenance polices.
