Title: What You May Not Know About Information Value

Author: Gordon Hazen, Professor, Northwestern University, IEMS Department, McCormick School of Engineering, Evanston, IL, 60208-3119, United States of America, gbh305@northwestern.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Information value has a long history in decision analysis and related fields. We summarize lesser known properties of perfect and sample information value, and examine the relation to Shannon information, as well as conventional and unconventional treatment of information value under expected utility. We also consider information value as a sensitivity analysis tool, and introduce the concept of information density.
