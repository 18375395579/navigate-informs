Title: Click Logs Analysis for e-Commerce

Author: Si-Chi Chin, The University of Iowa, 3087 LIB, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, si-chi-chin@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): W. Nick Street

Year: 2012

Abstract: Click logs from e-Commerce sites provide a rich opportunity to acquire implicit feedback from users. Patterns derived from the time between a posted query and a click provide information on the ranking quality, reflecting the perceived relevance of a retrieved URL. This presentation applies the Kaplan-Meier estimator to study click patterns. The observed results demonstrate the potential of using click curves to predict the quality of the top-ranked results.
