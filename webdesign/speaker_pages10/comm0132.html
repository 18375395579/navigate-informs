Title: Using Tags for Collaborative Filtering

Author: Iljoo Kim, University of Utah, pactik@business.utah.edu

Coauthor(s): Gautam Pant, Olivia Sheng

Year: 2008

Abstract: While tagging has been widely deployed to support search and categorization applications, studies that investigate the use of tags for improving the performance of recommender systems are limited. We propose a modified collaborative-filtering recommendation system that uses tag-based profiling and present an empirical evaluation of the proposed system.
