Title: Advances in Hypergame Theory

Author: Russell R Vane III, IBM Global Business Services, 2102 Capston Circle, Herndon, VA, 20170, United States of America, russ@vaneteam.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Hypergame Theory, using a cardinal value approach, explores more dimensions of the outguessing problem by constructing a model of perceptions. Adversaries are allowed to create new options unknown by their opponents to calculate the value of information. Hypergame Expected Value is proposed as a confidence indexed decision criteria.
