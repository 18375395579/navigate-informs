Title: Large Portfolio Loss: Conditionally Gaussian Approximations

Author: Justin Sirignano, Stanford University, 3 Gibbs Court, Irvine, CA, United States of America, jasirign@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Kay Giesecke, Konstantinos Spiliopoulos

Year: 2012

Abstract: We extend previous asymptotic results for the loss in a large credit portfolio by proving a first-order correction. This conditionally Gaussian approximation satisfies a stochastic partial differential equation and is shown to offer accurate approximations to the finite portfolio loss distribution. Numerical methods are developed to simulate the approximating loss.
