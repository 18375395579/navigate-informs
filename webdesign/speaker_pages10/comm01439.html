Title: Monitoring Correlation within Nonlinear Profiles Using Mixed Models

Author: Jeffrey Birch, Professor, Virginia Tech, 406-A Hutcheson Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States, jbbirch@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Willis Jensen

Year: 2007

Abstract: Previous work on profile monitoring of nonlinear profiles has assumed that the measurements within a profile are uncorrelated. We propose the use of a nonlinear mixed model (NLMM) to account for the correlation structure. Our approach results in charts with good abilities to detect changes in Phase I data.
