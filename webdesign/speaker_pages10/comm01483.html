Title: Forecasting Financial Time Series via Mining the Underlying Transactions Data

Author: Errol Caby, AT&T, 180 Park Ave, Florham Park, NJ, United States, ecaby@att.com

Coauthor(s): Tom Au, Felix Eze

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper looks at ways of forecasting monthly or quarterly financial time series by using the underlying transactions data. First, it establishes a statistical framework in which the concept of a gap time series (essentially the difference between the financial time series and the time series derived from the transactions data) appears. This paper analyzes financial forecasts produced by combining forecasts of the gap time series with transactions data (available some time in advance).
