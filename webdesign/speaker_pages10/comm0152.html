Title: Constructing Objectives for Homeland Security

Author: Ralph Keeney, Duke University, Fuqua School of Business, 101 Lombard Street Suite #704W, San Francisco, CA, 94111, United States, keeneyr@aol.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation discusses a procedure to identify the fundamental objectives of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and construct an objective function that combines them. This would provide a basis for developing consistent evaluation procedures for allocating DHS funds and for analyses of DHS alternatives.
