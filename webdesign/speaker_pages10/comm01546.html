Title: Modeling and Solving Non-convex Robust Portfolio Optimization Problems

Author: Daniel Bienstock, Columbia University, dano@ieor.columbia.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Robust optimization is a technique for building 'robustness', in a qualitative sense, into solutions to optimization models. Real-world data uncertainty arising in the portfolio optimization context can exhibit non-convexities as well as hidden correlations among groups of assets. In this talk we describe continuing work in the solution of robust optimization problems with these features.
