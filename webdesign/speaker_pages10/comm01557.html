Title: To Find the Distribution of the Number of Renewals of a Renewal Process Using Laplace Transforms

Author: Boon Ong, Dr, Royal Military College, P.O. Box 1700 Stn Forces, Kingston, ON, K7K 7B4, Canada, ong-b@rmc.ca

Coauthor(s): Mohan Chaudhry

Year: 2007

Abstract: The distribution of the number of renewals of a renewal process over a time period 0 to t is found by inverting a Laplace transform using the roots method. Where a distribution has no Laplace transform, continued fractions are used to approximate the Laplace transform.
