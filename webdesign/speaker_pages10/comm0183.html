Title: Introduction to the Data Mining Process

Author: George Runger, Arizona State University, School of Computing, Informatics, and D, Tempe, AZ, United States of America, runger@asu.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This tutorial introduces data mining with a focus on the data mining process. Descriptions of the objectives, steps, terminology, differences from traditional statistical analysis, and common applications are presented. The breadth of scope is illustrated. The process of model, estimate, validate is outlined with some illustrative methods.
