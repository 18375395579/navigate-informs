Title: Performance-based Regularization for Data-driven Optimization

Author: Gah-Yi Vahn, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, United Kingdom, gvahn@london.edu

Coauthor(s): Noureddine El Karoui, Andrew Lim

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present an extension of the regularization technique that is currently popular in statistics and machine learning. We apply this to mean-CVaR portfolio optimization to reduce the estimation risk of the performance.
