Title: A Behavioral Measure of Risk

Author: Philippe Delqui? Associate Professor of Decision Sciences, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, F-77300, France, philippe.delquie@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Alessandra Cillo

Year: 2006

Abstract: We develop a measure of risk based on the behavioral assumption that individuals care about differences among the outcomes of a gamble. This new risk measure includes variance as a special case, among many other forms. It also leads to Rank-Dependent Utility and Disappointment models of choice. We discuss properties of the risk measure.
