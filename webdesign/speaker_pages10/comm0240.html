Title: Supply Chain Progress Monitoring Based on Individualized Trace Data

Author: Jun Shu, Assistant Professor, Penn State, Department of Supply Chain and Informati, 462 Business Building, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, junshu@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Dr. Russell Barton

Year: 2008

Abstract: Although quality control is widely used in manufacturing, its use in supply chains is practiced ad hoc, rather than rigorously defined using statistical characterizations. We develop a mathematical framework to represent and manipulate individualized trace data at multiple levels of aggregation. Using this framework, we propose several generic quality measures for the progress of entities through a supply chain and describe methods to monitor these measures.
