Title: Evaluation of Batched Quantile Estimation Merhods

Author: Dave Goldsman, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of ISYE, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, sman@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Christos Alexopoulos, James Wilson

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the performance of asymptotically valid confidence intervals (CIs) methods for steady-state quantiles computed from nonoverlapping batches. Our results form the basis for the development of fully sequential procedures that yield CI estimators of steady-state quantiles with user-specified absolute or relative precision.
