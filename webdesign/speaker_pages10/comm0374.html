Title: Statistical Monitoring of Microblog Media Streams

Author: Jeremy D. Ezell, Auburn University, Auburn, AL, United States of America, jde0009@auburn.edu

Coauthor(s): L. Allison Jones Farmer

Year: 2013

Abstract: Modern statistical methods for detecting true signals for monitored activity in high-velocity social media streams are considered. We survey and assess the applicability of existing statistical surveillance methods and investigate potential refinements and applications using multiple microblog data streams. Challenges and opportunities for research are discussed.
