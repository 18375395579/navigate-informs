Title: A Multivariate Statistical Framework for Monitoring the Performance of Transportation Infrastructure

Author: Yikai Chen, PhD Candidate, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, A320, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, yikai.chen@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Pablo Durango-Cohen

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a statistical framework for the analysis of realtime performance data of transportation infrastructure. It applies statistical process control tools to simultaneously monitor multiple interrelated facilities and alarm changes in their structural conditions to support decisions in resource allocation. We provide results of implementing the framework on the Hurley Bridge in Wisconsin.
