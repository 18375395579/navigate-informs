Title: Ross Recovery in Markovian Economies II

Author: Likuan Qin, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, United States of America, LikuanQin2012@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Vadim Linetsky

Year: 2013

Abstract: This talk will survey our work on extending the Ross recovery theorem to general Markovian economies.
