Title: Population Model-based Optimization

Author: Xi Chen, PhD student, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 205 Transportation Bldg.,, 104 S. Mathews Ave., Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, xchen37@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Enlu Zhou

Year: 2013

Abstract: Model-based methods are effective for solving optimization problem with little structure. To better capture the multi-modality of the objective functions than traditional model-based methods which use only a single model, we propose a framework of using a population of models with an adaptive mechanism to propagate the population. The mechanism is derived from estimating the optimal parameter of the model in a Bayesian manner, which provides a proper way to determine the diversity of the models.
