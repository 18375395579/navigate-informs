Title: Database Structure for a Stochastic Optimization based DSS for ALM in Life Insurance Firms

Author: Harish Rao, Doctoral Student, Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad, India, harishrao@iimahd.ernet.in

Coauthor(s): Goutam Dutta

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we introduce a stochastic optimization based DSS for the strategic planning needs (asset-liability management) of a life insurance firm using a multi-period stochastic optimization model. We discuss the issues related to database structure, DSS interface design, SLP development, database updation and solution reporting.
