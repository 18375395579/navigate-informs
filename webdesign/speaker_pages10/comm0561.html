Title: Model-based Multivariate Monitoring Charts for Autocorrelated Processes

Author: Xuan Huang, University of Massachusetts Amherst, Isenberg School of Management, 121 Presidents Drive, Amherst, MA, 01002, United States of America, xuan@som.umass.edu

Coauthor(s): Soren Bisgaard

Year: 2008

Abstract: Autocorrelation may seriously impact the performance of conventional control charts. For univariate process, it has been proposed to use a special cause control chart on the residuals after fitting a time series model. We generalize this idea to multivariate scenario. We produce plot on fitted values to provide a guidance of process adjustments and propose applying the Hotellings T2 to the residuals after fitting a multivariate time series model. The Average Run Length performance is reported.
