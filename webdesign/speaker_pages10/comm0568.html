Title: Analysis and Prediction of Aggregate Demand for Electricity

Author: John Hobby, Alcatel-Lucent Bell Labs, 600 Mountain Avenue, Murray Hill, NJ, 07974, United States of America, hobby@research.bell-labs.com

Coauthor(s): Gabriel Tucci, Mustafa Dogru

Year: 2012

Abstract: We analyze the aggregate electricity consumption of many consumers, and extract components for weather and natural illumination. Least-squares fits for Steadman apparent temperature and log-scale illumination proceed independently for each hour of the day. We use cubic polynomials and spline surfaces, with FFT spectral analysis for residual consumption. We do a full comparison with simpler methods based on exponential smoothing.
