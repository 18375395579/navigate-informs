Title: Time Series Search and Retrieval for Printer Diagnosis and Repair

Author: John Handley, Member of Research Staff, Xerox Corporation, 800 Phillips Road, MS 128 27E, Webster, NY, 14580, United States of America, john.handley@xerox.com

Coauthor(s): Sankar Virdhagriswaran

Year: 2008

Abstract: Many printers are connected to the Internet and send time-dependent data comprising sensor values and usage statistics to a data warehouse. When diagnosing faults within a machine, service engineers find it valuable to plot a time series of a particular variable and search a database for machines with similar behavior. We present a system for search and retrieval that uses global similarity based on dynamic time warping and local similarity based on a modified Smith-Waterman metric.
