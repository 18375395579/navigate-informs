Title: Integer-ordered Simulation Optimization using R-SPLINE

Author: Bruce Schmeiser, Professor, Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN, 47905, United States of America, bruce@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Honggang Wang, Raghu Pasupathy

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider simulation-optimization problems where the decision variables are integer ordered. We develop R-SPLINE---an algorithm that alternates between a continuous search and a discrete neighborhood search, to asymptotically identify a local minimum. R-SPLINE converges almost surely, with exponentially decaying likelihood of returning an incorrect solution. With no parameter tuning, R-SPLINE compares favorably with existing algorithms.
