Title: Calibration and Validation of the Anisotropic Mesoscopic Simulation (AMS) Model

Author: Yi-Chang Chiu, Assistant Professor, University of Arizona, PO Box 210072, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States, chiu@utep.edu

Coauthor(s): Houbing Song

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Anisotropic mesoscopic Simulation (AMS) model is a mesoscopic traffic simulation approach that is based on directional influence of car-following mechanism. This talk gives an overview of the model with the focus on the calibration and validation procedure and results of the AMS model using the NGSIM data.
