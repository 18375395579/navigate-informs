Title: Boundary Evolution Equations for American Options

Author: Daniel Mitchell, University of Texas, McCombs School of Business, 1 University Station, B6000, Austin, TX, 78712, United States of America, Daniel.Mitchell@phd.mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Kumar Muthuraman, Jonathan Goodman

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the problem of finding optimal exercise policies for American options in constant and stochastic volatility. We derive and use boundary evolution equations that characterize the evolution of the optimal exercise boundary. Using these boundary evolution equations we show how one can construct very efficient computational methods for pricing American options.
