Title: Deciding Where to Establish Fuel Breaks on Flammable Forest Landscapes

Author: David Martell, Professor, Faculty of Forestry, University of Toronto, 33 Willcocks Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 3B3, Canada, martell@smokey.forestry.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Wenbin Cui, Cristian Palma, Andres Weintraub

Year: 2006

Abstract: We partition a flammable forest landscape into cells and use a simple localized cell assessment procedure to rank cells with respect to their fire protection values. We then compare our results with the results obtained by using more exact models. Our cell ranking procedure can be used to help determine where to establish fuel breaks.
