Title: Robust Assessment of Dependency

Author: Henry Lam, Assistant Professor, Boston University, 111 Cummington Street, Boston, MA, 02215, United States of America, khhlam@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a general nonparametric framework to gauge the local effect of serial dependency in the input model on stochastic simulation. The key instrument is an optimization formulation on the mutual information of the underlying input distributions. The associated optimal values form a basis for constructing output confidence intervals, and connect to analysis-of-variance (ANOVA) estimators which can be efficiently computed.
