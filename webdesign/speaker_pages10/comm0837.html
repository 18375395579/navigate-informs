Title: A Local Algorithm for Detecting Evolving Communities in Large Weighted Networks

Author: Bin Zhang, Assistant Professor, Temple University, 209J Speakman, 1810 N. 13th St., Philadelphia, PA, 19122, United States of America, bzhang@temple.edu

Coauthor(s): Zoran Obradovic, Mladen Nikolic

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present an effective method for community detection in large directed weighted dynamic networks. Our method scales well to networks with millions of nodes observed over time. It is applied to synthetic and real networks and experiments show that the proposed method is the proposed method is as efficient and accurate as the leading algorithms for static unweighted graphs, more importantly it is also effective to identify dense communities in large weighted and in large evolving networks.
