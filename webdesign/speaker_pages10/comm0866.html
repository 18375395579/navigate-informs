Title: Planning Under Uncertainty

Author: Patrik Flisberg, Norwegian School of Economics, Helleveien 30, Bergen, Norway, pafli@mweb.co.za

Coauthor(s): Mikael Frisk, Mikael Ronnqvist

Year: 2012

Abstract: There are many applications in forestry with uncertain data. We explore solution methods and strategies for forest transportation and harvesting when some data is uncertain. Results from case studies are presented.
