Title: Real Life AHP. Issues and Trouble Shooting

Author: Claudio Garuti, Fulcrum Ingenieria, L.T. Ojeda 0180 Of.1004, Santiago, Chile, cgaruti@fulcrum.cl

Coauthor(s): Isabel Spencer

Year: 2006

Abstract: This presentation try to put light in the way that we are using AHP in real life problems. What are his benefits and what his claim. His application area and its limitations. Also put some light in few of the criticisms made. The most of them, due to a bad understanding of the method and its axioms, resulting in a lack of modelling knowledgement for real life problems representation.
