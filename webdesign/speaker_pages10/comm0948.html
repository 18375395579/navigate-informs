Title: Condition-based Maintenance for Degrading Systems

Author: Petek Yontay, Arizona State University, 699 S. Mill Ave., Tempe, AZ, 85281, United States of America, pyontay@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Rong Pan

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this research, we use offline condition monitoring to model and analyze degrading components. We investigate some performance parameters to assess reliability of the system. In order to illustrate our methodology, we will apply it on a real life industrial problem.
