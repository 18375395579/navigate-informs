Title: EWMA and CUSUM Control Chart Using Wavelets for Complicated Functional Data

Author: Young S. Jeong, Student, University of Tennessee, 309 EST, Knoxville, TN, 37996, United States, yjeong@utk.edu

Coauthor(s): Myong K. Jeong, Mary Leitnaker

Year: 2006

Abstract: This paper extends the adaptive thresholding to the versions of the exponentially weighted moving average (EWMA) and cumulative sum (CUSUM) control for process monitoring based on functional data. Simulation studies show that proposed methods quickly detect smaller local and vertical shifts.
