Title: Unified Heuristic for the Location-Routing Problem

Author: Abdulrahman Alenezi, aalenezi@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Leyla Ozsen

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a unified cluster first route second heuristic for the Location-Routing Problem. The special cases of this heuristic solves the VRP and MDVRP as well. We present preliminary results for all three problems.
