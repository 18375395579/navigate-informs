Title: Route Design in a Fast-Bus Urban Transportation System

Author: Andrés L. Medaglia, Centro de Optimización y Probabilidad Aplicada (COPA) Departamento de Ingeniería Industrial Universidad de los Andes, amedagli@uniandes.edu.co

Coauthor(s): German Riano, Juan Camilo Acero

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a mixed-integer programming model to design the set of routes for a fast-bus urban transportation system by minimizing overall passenger travel time while limiting the number of routes so that the system remains manageable and understandable. Several solution techniques are explored taking advantage of the model’s structure.
