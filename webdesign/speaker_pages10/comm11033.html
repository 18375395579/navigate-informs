Title: Airline Crew Pairing is Solved

Author: Curt Hjorring, Senior Optimization Specialist, Jeppesen, Odinsgatan 9, Gothenburg, SE-411 03, Sweden, curt.hjorring@jeppesen.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: The airline crew pairing has a long history in the OR literature. I will provide an overview of the significant contributions. I now believe that the basic crew pairing problem is solved so well in practice that further algorithmic improvements will provide little impact for production problems. However, there are a number of extensions and integrated problems for which benefits are possible, and algorithmic improvements are needed.
