Title: The Coordinated Scheduling of Production and Two-stage Transportation

Author: Feng Li, PhD, The Logistics Institute, Northeastern University, Shenyang, 110819, China, fengli055@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Lixin Tang, Zhi-Long Chen

Year: 2013

Abstract: Motivated by applications in the steel industry, we study an integrated scheduling model of production and two-modal transportation. A set of jobs are first processed in a plant, delivered from the plant to the port by vehicles, and delivered from the port to the customers by ships. The problem is to find a joint schedule of production, vehicle transportation, and ship transportation such that an objective function is optimized.
