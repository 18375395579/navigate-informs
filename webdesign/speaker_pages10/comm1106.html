Title: Routing Autonomous Aerial Vehicles Away from Populated Regions using Road Network Data

Author: Sam Thangiah, Professor, Slippery Rock University, Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Lab, 250 ATS, Slippery Rock, PA, 16057, United States of America, sam.thangiah@sru.edu

Coauthor(s): James Mullen, Shawn Forsythe, Daniel Arnett, Shane Chestnutt

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this study a method to route autonomous aerial vehicles away from populated regions using road network data is described. An autonomous, small-scale quadrotor with an onboard GPS is used to demonstrate the routing process.
