Title: The p-median with Construction Costs

Author: Vladimir Marianov, Professor, Pontificia Universidad Catolica de Chile, Department of Electrical Engineering, Vicuna Mackenna 4860, Santiago, Sa, Chile, marianov@ing.puc.cl

Coauthor(s): Gabriel Gutierrez-Jarpa, Carlos Obreque, Oscar Cornejo

Year: 2008

Abstract: A known number of facilities are located in a rural zone. Minimum cost roads are built, and the total customer-facility travel distance over these roads is minimized. We formulate the problem using a multicommodity flow formulation and propose a Lagrangean-based solution procedure. Computational experience is provided.
