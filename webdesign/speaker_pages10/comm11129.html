Title: A Fast Response Mechanism for Airspace Capacity Windfalls

Author: Andrew Churchill, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Maryland, 3117 AV Williams, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, churchil@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): David Lovell, Michael Ball

Year: 2007

Abstract: We propose a dynamic fast-response mechanism to help airspace users take advantage of unanticipated capacity opportunities in strategic en-route congestion management initiatives. These types of programs would typically be triggered by the presence of severe weather in the en-route airspace. User preference regarding alternate and preferred routings is incorporated, as are various realistic airspace constraints.
