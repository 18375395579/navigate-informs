Title: Value of Travel Information: Exploring Psychological Effects of Real-Time Travel Information on Traveler Decision-Making Behavior

Author: Dong Yoon Song, Graduate Research Assistant, Purdue University, 3000 Kent Ave, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, song50@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Srinivas Peeta, Yu-Ting Hsu

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study explores the value of real-time travel information in terms of how it may psychologically influence traveler route choice behavior. An analytical framework is developed and driving simulator-based experiments are used to observe and study traveler responses to the provided information.
