Title: Considering Hazardous Materials Vehicles as a Terrorist Weapon: A Geographically Motivated Model

Author: Justin Yates, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, Dept of Industrial & Systems Engineering, College Station, TX, United States of America, jtyates@buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Rajan Batta, Chase Murray, Irene Casas

Year: 2008

Abstract: Modeling various sensor parameters, detection capability within a geographic region is formulated as a bi-level integer program and integrated with a queuing schema that allocates limited interception resources responsible for alarm response. A geographic information system is used to store, manipulate and display geographic and network data. The presentation culminates with a case study examining the geographic region of Los Angeles County, California.
