Title: The Single Assignment Follower p-Hub Location Problem with Attraction Functions

Author: H.A. Eiselt, Professor, University of New Brunswick, Faculty of Business Administration, University of New Brunswick, Frederickton, NB, E3B 5A3, Canada, haeiselt@unb.ca

Coauthor(s): Vladimir Marianov

Year: 2008

Abstract: We solve a competitive hub location problem in which a firm is entering a market in which other airlines have hubs already located and seeks maximum capture. Customers choose routes according to gravity-like utility functions that depend on travel time and fare. Each demand is assigned to a single hub.
