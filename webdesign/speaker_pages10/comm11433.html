Title: Identification and Adjustment of Inaccurate Airport Arrival Capacities

Author: Andrew Churchill, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, churchil@umd.edu

Coauthor(s): David Lovell, Michael Ball, Avijit Mukherjee

Year: 2006

Abstract: Modeling flight delays and cancellations at an airport using statistical and analytical methods require as input the airport capacity during different time intervals of a day. In this paper, we present a statistical model to estimate the arrival capacity of an airport during any time interval, based on several factors such as runway configuration, meteorological conditions, etc. We show that these estimates reduce the prediction error of existing models for delays and cancellation.
