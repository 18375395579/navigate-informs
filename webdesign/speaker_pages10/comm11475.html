Title: Planning Models for City Logistics

Author: Teodor Gabriel Crainic, University of Quebec in Montreal, Ecole des sciences de la gestion, C.P. 8888, succ. Centre-Ville, Montreal, QC, H3C3P8, Canada, theo@crt.umontreal.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: We address planning issues in two-tier City Logistics systems. We focuss on models for the short-term planning of operations, which may also be part of system-evaluation procedures. We'll devote most of the talk to routing issues, models, and solution procedures.
