Title: Revenue Management in the Parking Industry

Author: Kostas Triantis, Professor, Virginia Tech, 7054 Haycock Road, Falls Church, VA, 22043-2311, United States of America, triantis@vt.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This research explores how revenue management (RM) principles would integrate into a parking system, and how advanced reservations, coupled with dynamic pricing (based on booking limits) could be used to maximize parking revenue. Furthermore, it evaluates how the application of RM in the parking industry and within urban parking districts can mitigate traffic congestion.
