Title: An Integer Programming Model for Generating Vehicle Fleet Purchase Recommendations

Author: Daniel Reich, Operations Research Analyst, Ford Motor Company, 2101 Village Road, MD 2122, Dearborn, MI, 48124, United States of America, dreich8@ford.com

Coauthor(s): Sandy Winkler, Erica Klampfl

Year: 2012

Abstract: Sustainability and environmental impact are areas of growing importance to many of Ford’s fleet customers. New green vehicle technologies have emerged, which present organizations with an opportunity to increase the fuel economy of their fleets. “Fleet Purchase Planner (FPP)?(patent pending) is a software system designed to assist Ford’s fleet customers in planning their purchases. This talk introduces FPP and the integer programming model we use to provide customized purchase recommendations.
