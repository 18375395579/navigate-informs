Title: Scheming the Postal Service: An Algorithm for Delivery Point Sequencing

Author: Mariah Jeffery, IBM Global Business Services, 12902 Federal Systems Park Dr, Fairfax, VA, 22033, United States, mjeffery1@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Mariah Jeffery

Year: 2007

Abstract: We discuss the development of a tool for creating sort schemes for the United States Postal Service’s Delivery Point Sequencing (DPS) operation. The tool uses a polynomial time algorithm to assign mail to DPS machines for sorting with an objective of minimizing the total equipment required while meeting service standards.
