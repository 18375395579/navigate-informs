Title: Equipage-based Resource Allocation in Air Traffic Flow Management

Author: Shervin AhmadBeygi, Lead Operations Research Analyst, Metron Aviation Inc., 45300 Catalina Ct, Dulles, VA, 20166, United States of America, shervin.ahmadbeygi@metronaviation.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Successful implementation of the Next Generation Air Transportation System (NextGen) requires a critical mass of aircraft to be equipped with a range of communication, navigation, and surveillance avionics. In this research we study different mechanisms to incorporate equipage information in Traffic Flow Management resource allocation algorithms.
