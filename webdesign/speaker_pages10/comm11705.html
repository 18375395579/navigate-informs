Title: Installation Access Control Analysis

Author: J. Gregory Heck, Operation Research Analyst, Center for Army Analysis, US Army, 6001 Goethals Road, Fort Belvoir, VA, 22060, United States of America, john.heck2@us.army.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Office of the Provost Marshal General is required to ensure all Army installations have proper security regarding Anti-Terrorism/Force Protection. This requirement includes access control of installations. Requirements and standards must be reviewed to support reprogramming of future funding. The Center for Army Analysis assisted in identifying efficiencies, increasing effectiveness and reducing requirements for installation security.
