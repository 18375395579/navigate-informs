Title: Heuristics for the Location Routing Problem with Intermediate Storage Facilities

Author: Stephen Hill, Ph.D Candidate, The University of Alabama, Box 870226, Tuscaloosa, AL, 35487, United States, shill@cba.ua.edu

Coauthor(s): John Mittenthal

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this work we discuss the characteristics of and potential solution approaches for the Location Routing Problem with Intermediate Storage Facilities. We present several heuristic approaches to generate high-quality solutions to the problem.
