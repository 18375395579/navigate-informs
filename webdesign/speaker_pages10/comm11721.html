Title: Innovative Operating Strategies for Para-transit Services

Author: Luca Quadrifoglio, Texas A&M University, CE/TTI Bldg. - Room 301-I, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, lquadrifoglio@civil.tamu.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: A significant inefficiency of ADA Paratransit services using zoning strategies is related to the large amount of empty trip miles driven for serving cross-zonal customers. We explore innovative strategies for operating them by enabling providers to serve both trips of these customers in need of round trip rides and quantify the benefits in terms of a significant reduction of the empty trip miles and an improvement of the level of service. We investigate both static and dynamic scenarios.
