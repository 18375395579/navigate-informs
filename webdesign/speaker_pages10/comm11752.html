Title: Scheduling Ship Operations in Container Terminals

Author: Kap Hwan Kim, kapkim@pusan.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Dong Won Jang, Da Hun Jung

Year: 2007

Abstract: For the efficient operation of container terminals, it is essential to synchronize all the operations of quay cranes, transporters, and yard cranes. This study addresses scheduling of ship operations by considering both the quay side operation and the yard side operation. A mathematical formulation is provided and a solution method is suggested.
