Title: Online Point of Distribution Location in Disaster Relief

Author: Ashlea Bennett Milburn, Assistant Professor, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering Center, Fayetteville, AR, 72701, United States of America, ashlea@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Chase Rainwater

Year: 2012

Abstract: Information regarding affected populations, roads, buildings, and bridges becomes known on a rolling basis in the hours following a disaster. We develop online models that use real-time information regarding demand and infrastructure status to specify shelter locations and supply distribution points as a specific damage scenario is realized. Approximate solution approaches are employed to obtain good plans quickly. Computation results are presented for a New Madrid earthquake scenario.
