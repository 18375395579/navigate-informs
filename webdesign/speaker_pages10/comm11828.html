Title: Analysis of LTL Routes in Congested Urban Areas

Author: Miguel Figliozzi, Assistant Professor, Portland State University, figliozzi@pdx.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Data from the daily activity of less than truckload (LTL) delivery tours is used to identify route patterns, estimate travel distances, and analyze the impact of congestion. Route characteristics, data collection challenges, and the potential of GPS based collection methods are also discussed.
