Title: Scheduling a 24/7 Distinctly Qualified Workforce

Author: Mike Swindler, Director Transportation Process, BNSF Railway, 2400 Western Center Boulevard, Fort Worth, TX, 76131, United States of America, michael.swindler@bnsf.com

Coauthor(s): Tony Craig, Shrikant Jarugumilli

Year: 2013

Abstract: The BNSF Train Dispatching office is a 24/7/365 operation that has 105 dispatching districts covered by approximately 550 distinctly qualified train dispatchers. This discussion will concentrate on difficulties the BNSF encountered when developing a scheduling application. BNSF’s scheduling algorithm will be discussed at a high level, as will our vacancy allocation and workforce planning models.
