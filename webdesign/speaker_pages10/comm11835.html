Title: Dynamic Long-Term Planning of Ambulance Locations and Assignments

Author: Oleksandr Shlakhter, Senior Researcher, Alberta Health Services, Edmonton, AB, Canada, alex.shlakhter@rotman.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Dmitry Krass

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present new perspectives on the problem of long-term planning of emergency medical services, including station locations and resource assignments. Available approaches are reviewed and a new approach is formulated. The model allows for dynamic multi-year resource planning.
