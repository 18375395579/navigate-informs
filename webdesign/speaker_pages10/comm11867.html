Title: Optimization of Distribution Networks in Postal Logistics

Author: Hans- Jürgen Sebastian, Professor, RWTH Aachen University, Templergraben 64, Aachen, 52062, Germany, Sebastian@or.rwth-aachen.de

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation gives an overview of the area of postal logistics for letter, parcel, express and freight mail.The subsystems of a typical distribution network mail collection,sorting centers and delivery stations, long-haul transportation and the last mile will be discussed and typical optimization problems of the network planning phases will be presented.Finally ,an overview of case studies together with Deutsche Post World Net will be given .
