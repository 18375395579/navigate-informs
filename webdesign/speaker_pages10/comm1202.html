Title: Accounting for Flexibility in Transportation Network Design Under Uncertainty

Author: Gopal Patil, PhD Candidate, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 4002 JEC, Department of Civil Engineering, Troy, NY, 12180, United States, patilg@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Satish Ukkusuri

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study a multiperiod flexible network design problem (F-NDP) accounting for demand uncertainty. Here the planner gets the flexibility of increasing/reducing the investment on any link at later stages based on the available recourse. We introduce the concept of transportation network flexibility and present two MPEC formulations for F-NDP. The implications of the model and results on test network are discussed.
