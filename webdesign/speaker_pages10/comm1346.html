Title: Maximizing Trading Area of a New Facility: The Planar Case

Author: Dmitry Krass, Professor, Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto, Toronto, ON, M5S3e6, Canada, Krass@Rotman.Utoronto.Ca

Coauthor(s): Atsuo Suzuki

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider adding a new facility to a region containing competitive facilities. The demand is assumed to be uniformly distributed. The objective is to find the location for a new facility that maximizes its trading area, which is equivalent to maximizing the area of the Voronoi cell for the new facility. We develop an exact solution method based on the Big Triangle - Small Triangle algorithm. Novel approach is used to develop upper bounds.
