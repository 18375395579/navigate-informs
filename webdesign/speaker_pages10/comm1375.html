Title: Tailoring Logistics Strategies to Demand

Author: Valerie Tardif, SmartOps, 12 Federal Street, Suite 400, Pittsburgh, PA, 15212, United States, VTardif@smartops.com

Coauthor(s): Sridhar Tayur

Year: 2007

Abstract: Given the agility of current distribution networks, logistics optimization has become a tactical planning activity for many companies. Since 2005, Deere’s C&CE Division has been dynamically aligning its downstream logistics to its seasonal demand pattern. Using optimization models developed by SmartOps, Deere optimally redesigns its network twice a year, and executes optimized replenishment decisions to its dealers daily, achieving overall improved service while significantly reducing cost.
