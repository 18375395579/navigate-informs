Title: Mathematical Model for Airline Flight Re-scheduling Problem under Disruptions

Author: Mohamed Kharbeche, Postdoctoral Associate, Qatar University, Mechanical and Industrial Engineering, Doha, 2713, Qatar, mkharbec@qu.edu.qa

Coauthor(s): Ameer Al-Salem, Abdel Magid Hamouda, Fajr Ali Akbar Ansari, Sara Yousuf Al-Haidous, Nayla Ahmed Al-Thani

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose airline re-scheduling model that simultaneously develop recovery plans by determining alternate flight schedules with a minimal change from original schedules. This model produces the optimal track, level and departure time for each flight of a schedule after a disruption. Computational results are investigated for this exact method.
