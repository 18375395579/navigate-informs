Title: Robust Storage Assignment in Unit-Load Warehouses

Author: Yun Fong Lim, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, 50 Stamford Road, #04-01, Singapore, SG, Singapore, yflim@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Melvyn Sim, Marcus Ang

Year: 2012

Abstract: Assigning products to and retrieving them from proper storage locations are crucial in minimizing the operating cost of a unit-load warehouse. We develop a robust optimization model to solve the problem with variable supply and uncertain demand in a multi-period setting. Our approach significantly outperforms existing methods in the literature.
