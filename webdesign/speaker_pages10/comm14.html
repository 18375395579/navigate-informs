Title: Optimal Staging Area Locations and Material Recycling Strategies for Sustainable Highway Reconstruction

Author: Ryan Smith, University of Illinois, Champaign, IL, United States of America, smith103@illinois.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Sustainable highway reconstruction requires strategies that optimally procure material from supply locations, select locations to process material, and ship material to their demand locations to reduce the consumption of energy and material resources. Our model determines (i) optimal locations for fixed and mobile staging areas, and (ii) material recycling and shipment strategies.
