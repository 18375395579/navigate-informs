Title: The Progression of Analytics in Motor Racing ?In The Beginning ?

Author: Karl Kempf, Intel Corporation, Phoenix, AZ, United States of America, karl.g.kempf@intel.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: When I arrived in the offices of the Ferrari Formula One Racing team, I was surprised to find that the total data set I was given to work with was lap times and driver comments. Three years later the car was fully instrumented, with useful models at home base and trackside, and microprocessors were on the car doing interesting things as it sped around the track. I will recount the high points (and the low ones) in that journey that has turned out in hindsight to be just the beginning.
