Title: Robust Design of Hub-and-Spoke Network with Congestions

Author: Yu An, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave, ENB 118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, yan2@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Yu Zhang, Bo Zeng

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a hub-and-spoke system design scheme that simultaneously includes the practical factors of hub congestion and hub disruptions on the basis of the classical design model. Specifically, robust optimization technique is applied to handle the random hub failures. Demand loss factor will also be introduced to model the affected flows that can not be served in a failure scenario.
