Title: Pre-departure Sequence Planning

Author: Mourad Boudia, Research Engineer, Amadeus, 485, Route du Pin Montard BP 69, Sophia Antipolis, 06902, France, mourad.boudia@amadeus.com

Coauthor(s): Olivier Ratier, Baptiste Chatrain

Year: 2012

Abstract: High performance departure sequence planning contributes to improve air traffic and aircraft operations at airports by maximizing usage of available runways. We present here an innovative approach based on linear programming and discuss its contribution and performances.
