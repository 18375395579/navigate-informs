Title: Bike-sharing System: A Strategy to Dynamically Solve the Unbalancing Problem

Author: Satish Ukkusuri, Professor, Purdue University, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, West lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, sukkusur@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Binh Luong, Pulkit Parikh

Year: 2013

Abstract: A challenge problem in public bike-sharing system is the unbalance of bikes at stations. In this work, we present a strategy to dynamically deal with the problem by maximizing the level of users satisfaction while minimizing the cost to redistribute the bikes
