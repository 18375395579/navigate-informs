Title: Vehicle to Infrastructure Communication based Yellow Onset Decision Assistance System

Author: Anuj Sharma, Assistant Professor, University of Nebraska Lincoln, 330 F Whittier Building, Lincoln, NE, 68583, United States of America, asharma3@unl.edu

Coauthor(s): Sepi Aria, Liu Chenhui

Year: 2013

Abstract: The effect of information systems on the probability of conflict at onset yellow at high-speed intersections will be discussed. This results were used to develop a prototype Yellow Onset Driver Assistance (YODA) system using COTS hardware. The prototype design insights, challenges and future directions will be presented.
