Title: Transit Prices Negotiation: Repeated Game Approach

Author: Loubna Echabbi, INRIA/LORIA, 615 Rue du Jardin Botanique, Villers Les Nancy, 54602, France, Loubna.Echabbi@loria.fr

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a game theoretic approach for the transit price negotiation problem in the interdomain routing framework. We analyze the behavior of providers on a specific scenario mainly by considering the simple but not simplistic case of one source and one destination.
