Title: OR in Action for Rotterdam's Automated Container Terminals

Author: Rommert Dekker, Erasmus University Rotterdam, PO Box 1738, Rotterdam, ZH, 3000 DR, Netherlands, rdekker@few.eur.nl

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this presentation we give an overview of the Operations Research work done for the world's first automated container terminals at ECT in the Port of Rotterdam. We tackle both AGV and ASC planning, scheduling and routing. Other topics covered are stacking, terminal transport planning and rail / barge interfaces. Next we show the evolution of the terminal concepts in time and compare them with another automated terminal, HHLA in Hamburg. Finally we sketch a number of open OR problems.
