Title: A Solution Procedure for Broadband Convergence Access Network Design Problem

Author: Hyunjung Yun, Korea University, Sungbuk Ku Anamdong, Seoul, Korea, Republic of, hjyun@korea.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Youngho Lee, Youngjin Kim, Junghee Han, Kyunam Chang, Youngwook Kim, Bongju Kwon, Kyungsoo Kong

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a physical network design problem of broadband convergence access network. We formulate the problem as a three-level capacitated facility location-allocation model that minimizes the total cost, while satisfying nonlinear QoS constraints. The proposed branch and bound procedure finds the optimal solution within ten minutes for real problem instances.
