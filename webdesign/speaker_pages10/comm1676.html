Title: Flight Schedule Adjustments Through Departure and Block Times

Author: Diego Klabjan, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 205 North Mathews Avenue, Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, klabjan@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Milind Sohoni, Yu-Ching Lee

Year: 2007

Abstract: Schedule planning divisions determine flight frequencies and preliminary departure times. We provide models that compute exact departure and block times based on the provided stochastic estimates. Operating cost and passenger revenue are captured. Service level based on connecting traffic and the DOT measure is also taken into account.
