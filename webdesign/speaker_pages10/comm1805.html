Title: Revenue Management in Inland Container Transport

Author: Nishant Mishra, Assistant Professor of Operations Management, Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University, Rotterdam, Netherlands, nmishra@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Niels Agatz, Rob Zuidwijk

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider the application of revenue management in the inland transport of shipping containers, and show how the problem setting differs from the traditional airline network revenue management problem. We formulate a dynamic optimization model to support accept / reject decisions for the various types of transportation products, and also discuss possible extensions of this model.
