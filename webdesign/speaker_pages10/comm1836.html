Title: A Mathematical Model for Optimizing the Management of Empty Railcars

Author: David De Almeida, Research Project Manager, SNCF, 45 Rue de Londres, 75379 Paris Cedex 08, Paris, France, david.de_almeida@sncf.fr

Coauthor(s): Romain Wlodarczyk, Christian Weber

Year: 2007

Abstract: This presentation describes a mathematical model designed to optimize the management of empty railcars. Available railcars of different types must be allocated to demands and positioned with a minimal travelling cost and a maximum reliability (travel times are not deterministic). In some instances, demands may actually be intermediate stops where railcars need to be charged in order to fulfill final demands.
