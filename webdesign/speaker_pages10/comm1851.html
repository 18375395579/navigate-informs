Title: A New Railway Rolling Stock Planning System

Author: Jesper Hansen, Senior Consultant, Jeppesen, Købmagergade 53, 3., Copenhagen, 1150, Denmark, Jesper.Hansen@jeppesen.com

Coauthor(s): Mattias Grönkvist, Tomas Lidén, Erik Hasselberg, Jens Kjerrström

Year: 2007

Abstract: A newly developed rolling stock planning system is presented. The system solves several optimization problems resulting in train compositions, vehicle rotations including local activities and depot planning taking all relevant aspects into account. The system is customizable for any railway operator. Results are presented from the Danish State Railways.
