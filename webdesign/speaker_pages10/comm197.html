Title: Predictive Speed Harmonization and Control of Flow Breakdown in a Connected Vehicle Environment

Author: Alireza Talebpour, Northwestern University, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, AlirezaTalebpour2014@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Hani S. Mahmassani

Year: 2012

Abstract: Microscopic simulation is used to evaluate the effectiveness of speed harmonization strategies intended to avoid or delay the onset of breakdown and shock wave formation on freeways. Implementation in a connected vehicle environment is discussed.
