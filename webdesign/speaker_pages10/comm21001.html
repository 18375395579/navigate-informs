Title: Evaluation of Choices for Rural Electrification in Emerging Economies: A Multi-criteria Approach

Author: Md Mizanur Rahman, Researcher, Aalto University School of Engineering, Department of Energy Technology, Otakaari 4, Espoo, FI-02150, Finland, mdmizanur.rahman@aalto.fi

Coauthor(s): Jukka Paatero

Year: 2012

Abstract: Making decisions concerning rural electrification is rather complex and it is limited by economic and technical constraints, socio-cultural dynamics, and environmental consequences. Here a multi-criteria approach that utilizes a stochastic multi-criteria acceptability analysis tool is presented to support decision makers concerning the extension of electricity services to rural areas.
