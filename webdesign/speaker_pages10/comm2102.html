Title: Technology Level Assessment in Korea

Author: Chang Won Lee, Professor, Hanyang University, School of Business, Korea, Republic of, leecw@hanyang.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Sang Hyung Ahn

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper is to present Technology Level Assessment (TLA) in Korea. Certain industry is selected for the TLA study. Regression analysis is used for developing TLA model. The study results will provide decision-makers and policy-makers of technology level planning with strategic insights.
