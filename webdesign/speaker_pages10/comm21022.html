Title: Counterinsurgency Wargaming

Author: Jason Southerland, Center for Army Analysis, 6001 Goethals Road, Fort Belvoir, VA, 22060, United States of America, jason.a.southerland@us.army.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: Counterinsurgency is a multi-faceted problem in which all sides attempt to leverage military, political, diplomatic, and economic tools. Wargaming the political, economic, and diplomatic aspects of a counterinsurgency are not as well understood as the military and population security components. The Center for Army Analysis continues to mature a methodology to integrate these aspects into a wargame used to address important issues facing senior military commanders.
