Title: 2006 Decision Analysis Society Practice Award Winner and Finalists

Author: Kazuo Ezawa, PhD, R&D Decision Analysis, Daiichi Sankyo Pharma Development, 399 Thornall Street, Edison, NJ, 08837, United States, kezawa@daiichisankyo-us.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Decision Analysis Society (DAS) of INFORMS is seeking entries for the DAS Practice Award competition every year. The intent of this award is to recognize, promote, and publicize good decision analysis practice. We will announce the winner of DAS Practice Award for 2006.
