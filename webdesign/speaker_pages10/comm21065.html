Title: Technology Road-mapping process with Feature-based Analysis and Cluster-based Grouping in Healthcare

Author: Nima Behkami, Researcher, Oregon Health & Science University, 3181 S.W. Sam Jackson Park Rd., Mail Code: BICC, Portland, Or, 97239, United States of America, behkamin@hotmail.com

Coauthor(s): Noah Third, David Dorr

Year: 2008

Abstract: Technology Road Maps are difficult to model in large fields such as HealthCare. Shortcoming include: 1)visually representing a comprehensive TRM for decision making purposes 2)systematically identifying links between market drivers and products. A new three-phased process is purposed that includes Feature-based Analysis and Cluster-based Grouping. For demonstration it’s applied to Care Management Plus(CMP) a care management product under development at Oregon Health & Science University(OHSU).
