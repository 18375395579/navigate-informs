Title: Implementing the CPAT Mixed-integer Fleet Modernization Optimization Model Formulation

Author: Darryl Melander, Sandia National Laboratories, PO Box 5800-1188, Albuquerque, NM, 87185, United States of America, djmelan@sandia.gov

Year: 2013

Abstract: Military fleet modernization schedules and investment strategies must assure optimal fleet performance while respecting cost, schedule, production, and programmatic constraints. We will present formulation insights into the Capability Portfolio Analysis Tool (CPAT) mixed-integer optimization model used to provide decision support for military fleet modernization planning, focusing on the translation of customer business motivations into implementation choices.
