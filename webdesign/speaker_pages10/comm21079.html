Title: Sustaining a Multi-attribute Decision Model Over Time

Author: Jim Lowe, Professor, US Air Force Academy, 2354 Fairchild Dr Ste 6H-238, US Air Force Academy, CO, 80840, United States of America, jim.lowe@usafa.edu

Coauthor(s): Gregory Seely

Year: 2012

Abstract: While the process of developing an initial Multi-Attribute Decision Model is well defined, sustaining such a model from year to year becomes an art. As Decision Makers change, every aspect of the model becomes open for discussion since the new stakeholders have no vested interest in the original model. We present results of a resource allocation model based on 25 years of implementation.
