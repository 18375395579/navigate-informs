Title: Use Demand Forecast for Operations and Asset Planning at Norfolk Southern

Author: Clark Cheng, Senior Manager Operations Research, Norfolk Southern Corporation, 1200 Peachtree St. NW, MS 12-117, Atlanta, GA, 30309, United States of America, clark.cheng@nscorp.com

Coauthor(s): Viraj Karnik, Ilya Lavrik, Kitty Vollbrecht

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this presentation, we will give an overview of NS demand forecasting system. We will describe the statistical models that are being used to facilitate business processes and improve forecasting accuracy. The system is tightly integrated into a suite of planning tools and drives the downstream planning processes to optimize operations and assets.
