Title: Road Blocks to the Adoption of Patient Decision Support

Author: Glyn Elwyn, Professor, The Dartmouth Center for Healthcare Delivery Science, 37 Dewey Field Road, 2nd Floor, Dartmouth Center for Healthcare Delivery, Hanover, NH, 03755, United States of America, glynelwyn@gmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Two decades of work has shown the positive impact of patient-targeted decision support interventions. Yet, despite demonstrated efficacy, their effectiveness in routine clinical practice has yet to be established. We reviewed existing feasibility studies and found evidence of significant challenges to implementation, including professional indifference, a lack of confidence in their content and disruption to established work flows, all of which contributed to organizational inertia.
