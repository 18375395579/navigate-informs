Title: PLATO Helps Athens Win Gold

Author: Pericles Loucopoulos, Professor, The University of Manchester, School of Informatics, PO Box 88, Manchester, M60 1QD, United Kingdom, p.loucopoulos@manchester.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Kostas Zografos

Year: 2006

Abstract: The Athens 2004 Olympic Games organizing committee commissioned the PLATO project (Process Logistics Advanced Technical Optimization) to bring about a major shift in the way that planning, design, and operation of venues were carried out. We developed a systematic process for planning and designing venue operations by using knowledge modeling and resource-management techniques and tools. We developed a rich library of models that is directly transferable to future organizing committees.
