Title: Determining the Optimal Number of Suppliers in Global Supply Chain Networks under Supply Disruption

Author: Seong-Hyun Nam, Professor, University of North Dakota, 293 Centennial Drive Stop 8377, Grand Forks, ND, 58202-8377, United States of America, snam@business.und.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: The research paper focus on the trends in competitive business environments in that companies are reducing their number of suppliers and the impacts that it makes. Through this study we attempt to develop an analytical model for determining the optimal number of suppliers to minimize the total cost considering the risks of catastrophic events.
