Title: Uncertainty and Risk Analysis in Federal Cost Estimating Agencies

Author: Tomeka Williams, Senior Operations Research, Optimum Performance Solutions, 2486 Glengyle Drive, Vienna, VA, 22181, United States of America, tomeka.williams@gmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Uncertainty and risk analysis is a process that is unique, tailorable, and often times misunderstood within Federal Departments and Agencies. In working in three different cost estimating organizations, each has had several initiatives to bridge that gap. This session is intended to share the initiatives of each of these agencies, and practical tools that have been employed to ensure that uncertainty and risk analysis is included in Federal cost estimating products.
