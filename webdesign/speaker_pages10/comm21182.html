Title: Advances in Decision Analysis ?The Book

Author: Detlof von Winterfeldt, Professor, USC, Ronald Tutor Hall 316A, University Park, Los Angeles, CA, 92651, United States of America, Detlof@aol.com

Coauthor(s): Ralph F. Miles

Year: 2007

Abstract: Thirty-five prominent decision analysts contributed chapters to this book, which was published by Cambridge University Press in August of 2007. The editors will provide a brief overview of the book and how it was started and several chapter authors will comment on their chapters and the future of decision analysis.
