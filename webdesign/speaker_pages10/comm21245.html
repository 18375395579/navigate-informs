Title: The Hyper-Hypergame and Evidence-Based Evaluation

Author: Doug Samuelson, Homeland Security Institute, 8711 Chippendale Court, Annandale, VA, 22003, United States, samuelsondoug@yahoo.com

Abstract: As models?complexity approaches that of the actual systems of interest, traditional methods of validation are often ineffective or ethically indefensible.
