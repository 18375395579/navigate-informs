Title: An Information Asymmetry Model of Information Technology (IT) Governance

Author: Joseph Vithayathil, Washington State University, 6238 Adobe Circle, Irvine, CA, 92617, United States of America, joev80@gmail.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: This paper proposes an information asymmetry based definition of IT governance. Agency theory is employed in conjunction with organizational theory to develop a conceptual model, and an empirically testable model.
