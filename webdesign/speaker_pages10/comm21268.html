Title: Forging Successful INFORMS/SMDM Collaborations

Author: Douglas Owens, VA/Stanford, 117 Encina Commons, Stanford, CA, 94305-6019, United States of America, owens@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Margaret Brandeau

Year: 2012

Abstract: This talk describes examples of successful collaborations between INFORMS members and SMDM members in the area of operations research applied to public health. We also identify key aspects of successful collaboration, and suggest a road map for future joint work.
