Title: The Future Chinese Outbound Tourism Market

Author: Richard Harrill, Director, Sloane Travel and Tourism Center, Univ South Carolina, Columbia, SC, 29208, United States of America, RHARRILL@mailbox.sc.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Alfred P. Sloan Travel & Tourism Industry Center at the University of South Carolina, in partnership with the Travel Industry Association (TIA) and the U.S. Department of Commerce, Office of Travel & Tourism Industries (OTTI), launched a major research project addressing the anticipated future increase in Chinese travelers visiting the United States. This presentation provides an overview of the project’s results.
