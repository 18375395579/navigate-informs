Title: Answer the Question Clearly and On Time

Author: Michael Wilmer, COL, USA, Studies & Analysis Program Director, Army Capabilities Integration Center, USA, ARCIC, Fort Monroe, VA, 223651, United States of America, michael.wilmer@us.army.mil

Year: 2008

Abstract: Informing policy decisions requires that analysts are in tune with changing political and organizational dynamics, are directly engaged with the decision maker, and answer the key questions clearly and on time. This talk will describe how the analyst can inform senior military and civilian leaders' decision processes by employing a range of qualitative and quantitative techniques; scoping the work to the question at hand and time available; and delivering the results in a clear, concise manner.
