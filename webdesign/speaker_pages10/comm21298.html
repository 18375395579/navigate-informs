Title: Using Analytical Hierarchy Process (AHP) to Determine the Most Outstanding Sports Record

Author: Bret Myers, Assistant Professor of Management and Operations, Villanova University, 204 Eagle Glen Drive, Coatesville, PA, 19320, United States of America, bret.myers@villanova.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Nydick, Howard Weiss, Matthew Liberatore, Brian Coughlin

Year: 2013

Abstract: What is the greatest sports record of all time? This is an ageless debate that continuously garners attention from media and sports fans alike. We present an AHP-based approach to address this question. We build on the work of Golden and Wasil (1987) and limit our focus to the four major American sports: MLB, NBA, NFL, and NHL. Our ranking process involves input from “experts?including renowned Sports Illustrated writer Jack McCallum.
