Title: Not Just for Calculating Fast Anymore: Spreadsheet as Platform

Author: Jeffrey Keisler, Assistant Professor, University of Massachusetts Boston, MSIS Department, 100 Morrissey Boulevard, Boston, MA, 02125, United States, Jeff.Keisler@umb.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In default mode, spreadsheets look like balance sheets so we use them for arithmetic, or with add-ins to solve well-structured problems. In fact, spreadsheets are application development platforms, with sophisticated language and interface capabilities, allowing different families of functions to be creatively combined to form complex tools. We propose a regimen for exploring the possibilities and developing expertise in this regard.
