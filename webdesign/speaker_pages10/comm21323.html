Title: Performance Metrics in Outpatient Healthcare: A Framework

Author: Ken Klassen, Professor, Brock University, 500 Glenridge Ave, St Catharines, ON, L2S3A1, Canada, kklassen@brocku.ca

Coauthor(s): Tugba Cayirli, Elham Torabi, Craig Froehle, Michael Magazine, Denise White

Year: 2013

Abstract: Precisely defined performance metrics are essential for managing and improving operations in an outpatient clinic. We review OM and healthcare literature and develop a comprehensive framework of performance metrics for outpatient operations. We demonstrate how the framework can be applied to prior research and can inform and motivate future work.
