Title: The First Ten Years of Using The Analytic Network Process (ANP): A Literature Review

Author: Enrique Mu, Director, MIS Program, University of Pittsburgh, 345 Mervis Hall, Pittsburgh, PA, 15260, United States, enmu@katz.pitt.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Since its formal proposal by Saaty, ten years ago, the ANP has had only a few applications and has appeared rather sparsely in the literature. Still, the body of ANP articles is growing and at this point merits careful examination. This paper will review the current literature on ANP with an emphasis on its applications.
