Title: Network Modeling and Planning Implementations in Starbucks Supply Chain

Author: Stephen Stoyan, Analytics Manager, Starbucks Coffee Company, 2401 Utah Ave. S., Seattle, WA, 98134, United States of America, sstoyan@starbucks.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The supply chain network at Starbucks Coffee Company is facing new complexities on how products are shipped, stored, and distributed in the network. The advanced analytics team at Starbucks has developed an approach that tackles new challenges faced in the network as well as a tool that integrates with current systems to interact with business partners and aid with complexities. We present Phase I of the system that computes a production distribution hierarchy and inventory hedging strategy.
