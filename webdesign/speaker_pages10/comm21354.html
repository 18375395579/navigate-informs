Title: Humanitarian Relief Organization Procurement Needs in Light of Donor Forecasting

Author: Luther Brock, Doctoral Student, North Carolina A&T State University Department of Industrial and Systems Eng., 1401 East Market Street, Greensboro, NC, United States of America, lbrock@ncat.edu

Coauthor(s): Lauren Davis

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation will provide guidance for humanitarian relief organizations in regards to procurement decisions for field operations in light of expected donations. It highlights a mathematical model developed to determine the capital investment necessary to satisfy field requirements for supplies during each of three phases of relief operations.
