Title: Panel Discussion on Women in Engineering: Publishing, Recruitment, and Retention

Author: Laura McLay, Virginia Commonwealth University, Statistics & Operations Research, 1015 Floyd Ave, Box 843083, Richmond, VA, 23284, United States of America, lamclay@vcu.edu

Coauthor(s): Susan Albin, Mary Anderson-Rowland, Robyn McKay, Robin Keller

Year: 2012

Abstract: The panelists will discuss issues for women in OR/MS as they relate to publishing, recruitment, and retention of women. The panelists are listed as co-authors in this talk.
