Title: Panel: OR for Science and Engineering Grand Challenges

Author: Barry Nelson, Chair Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Road, Room C216, Evanston, IL, United States of America, nelsonb@northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Dorit Hochbaum, David Morton, Warren Powell, Christine Shoemaker, Andrew Boyd

Year: 2013

Abstract: The report on Engineering Grand Challenges commissioned by the National Academy of Engineering identified several important research areas in which breakthroughs would transform our world in one or more ways, including sustainability, security, human health, and our joy of living in general. This panel will discuss how OR might play the role of a catalyst for advances in the above research areas.
