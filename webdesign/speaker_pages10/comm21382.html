Title: Cost of Software Failure

Author: Fusun Gonul, Dr., School of Business, Slippery Rock University, Slippery Rock, PA, 16057-1399, United States of America, fusun.gonul@sru.edu

Coauthor(s): Elina Petrova

Year: 2008

Abstract: This research aims to glean the users?perspective on the factors contributing to software quality and to obtain an economic assessment of the implications of software failure. Using two different datasets, we obtain $17 billion and $48 billion as conservative estimates of the annual economic loss from software failure.
