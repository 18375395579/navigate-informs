Title: Challenges and Opportunities to QSR Community (II)

Author: Yu Ding, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, yuding@iemail.tamu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: INFORMS Section on Quality, Statistics, and Reliability was founded ten years ago and has grown into one of the most active sections within INFORMS. A panel of discussants will present their views and perspectives on the challenges the QSR community currently faces and the opportunities that QSR may be able to grasp for the next phase of growth. Audiences will be actively involved in the discussion in this session.
