Title: Panel Discussion: Innovation and Product Development: Engineering, Organizational and Technology Perspectives

Author: Riitta Katila, Stanford University, Terman Engineering Center, MS&E dpt, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, rkatila@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Linda Argote, Wallace Hopp, Suresh Kotha, Christoph Loch, James Utterback

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this panel session, leading scholars of technology and innovation discuss current topics in the intersection of engineering, innovation and technology. The panel will examine current trends in product innovation research and outline fruitful future research directions.
