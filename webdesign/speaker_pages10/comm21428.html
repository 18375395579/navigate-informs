Title: Selecting an Academic Collaboration Network using Multi-Criteria Decision Analysis

Author: Owen Hall Jr, Professor, Pepperdine University, 24255 PCH, Malibu, 90263, United States of America, ohall@pepperdine.edu

Coauthor(s): Joe Hahn

Year: 2013

Abstract: Cloud-based collaboration networks provide a universal platform in which the educational community can converge to share, and exchange ideas to drive innovation in student learning. The purpose of this presentation is to illustrate how multi-criteria decision analysis can be used to help select the appropriate network collaboration system in this complex setting.
