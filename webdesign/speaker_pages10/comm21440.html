Title: Operations Research Techniques for Planning in Test and Evaluation

Author: Darryl Ahner, Air Force Institute of Technology, 2905 Hobson Way, WPAFB, OH, 45433, United States of America, darryl.ahner@afit.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: The National Research Council Panel on Statistical Methods for T&E of Defense Systems has stated many times since 1998 that current practices in the Department of Defense do not take full advantage of the benefits available from the use of state-of-the-art statistical methodology, including experimental design techniques. This presentation will discuss the use of operations research techniques in effectively and afficinetly planning and analyzing integrated testing of new systems.
