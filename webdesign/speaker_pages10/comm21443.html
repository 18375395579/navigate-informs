Title: Win Probability of Baseball Game in NPB

Author: Norio Torigoe, Associate Professor, Tokai Univeristy, 4-1-1 Kitakaname, Hiratsuka, Kanagawa, 2591292, Japan, torigoe@tokai-u.jp

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present an algorithm for calculating the Win Probability of baseball game, which is held in Japanese professional baseball league (NPB). Moreover, we consider how much does the new ball that was adopted by NPB from 2011 season have an effect on a fluctuation of Win Probability. This study is supported by Data Stadium Inc.
