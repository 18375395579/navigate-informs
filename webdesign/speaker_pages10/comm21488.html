Title: Optimization-based Railroad Blocking Case Studies at BNSF

Author: Xiaoyun Ji, Senior Operations Research Specialist, BNSF Railway, Service Design, 2400 Lou Menk Drive, Fort Worth, FL, 76131, United States, Xiaoyun.Ji@BNSF.com

Coauthor(s): Pooja Dewan

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this presentation, we describe the use of an optimization-based blocking tool to improve the current train blocking plan. We will present incremental study cases to illustrate the role of this tool, the integration into the current business process, the implementation and impact of its recommendations, finally the remaining challenges.
