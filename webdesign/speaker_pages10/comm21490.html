Title: Multimedia and Spreadsheets: Improving Statistics Instruction

Author: Janet Wagner, Dean of Business, Richard Stockton College of NJ, J-203, P.O. Box 195, Pomona, NJ, 08240, United States of America, janet.wagner@stockton.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Originally targeted to an on-line introductory statistics course, a set of home-movies for numerous spreadsheet based examples of statistical analysis were developed. These videos were then used to great acclaim in an on-ground version of the course as well. A few examples and a discussion of multi-media technologies for use in spreadsheet based courses will be discussed.
