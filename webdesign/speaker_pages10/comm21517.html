Title: Panel: Resolution of Great Unsolved Problems in OR

Author: Saul I. Gass, Professor, University of Maryland, Robert H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, sgass@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Harvey Greenberg, George Nemhauser, Heiner Müller-Merbach, Arjang Assad

Year: 2006

Abstract: This session will bring together some of the speakers in the previous Great Unsolved Problems in OR (GUPOR) sessions. Issues raised by the GUPORS speakers will be open for further discussion by the panelists and the audience.
