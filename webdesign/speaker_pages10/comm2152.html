Title: INFORMS Communities/Subdivisions Session on INFORMS Web Pages

Author: Barry List, INFORMS, Director of Communications, Barry.List@INFORMS.org

Coauthor(s): Mary Magrogan, Shirley Mohr

Year: 2007

Abstract: A special training session that introduces webmasters for INFORMS societies, fora, chapters, and student chapters to the Content Management System (CMS) that INFORMS uses to update web pages. Session includes background on how INFORMS creates web pages in CMS and how webmasters can easily make changes to their websites.
