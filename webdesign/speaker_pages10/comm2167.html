Title: Working Together, Getting Things Done ?Encouraging Collaboration Between the OR and Healthcare Communities

Author: James Stahl, Massachusetts General Hospital, Boston, MA, United States of America, jstahl@mgh-ita.org

Year: 2012

Abstract: The purpose of this session is discuss how to encourage the development of ideas and collaborations between the OR and healthcare communities and how to actually get them adopted. Experienced practitioners will share insights from their experiences designing and implementing systems ranging from the micro (e.g., redesigning operating rooms) to the macro (e.g., healthcare policy).
