Title: Potential Synergies from Positive Train Control

Author: Bruce Patty, Vice President, Veritec Solutions, 824 Miramar Terrace, Belmont, CA, 94002, United States of America, bpatty@veritecsolutions.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Given the communications platform and train status data available from PTC, many suggestions for how to leverage this information to improve management of the railway have been made. A number of these concepts will be presented and their feasibility and benefits explored.
