Title: Weaponizing Data: New Principles of Advanced Analytics and Data Stewardship in Military Decisions

Author: Aaron D Burciaga, Senior Manager, Supply Chain Analytics, Accenture LLP, 4305 Majestic Lane, Fairfax, VA, 22033, United States of America, aaron.d.burciaga@accenture.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: To compete and win future operational challenges, the DoD and Services must ‘Weaponize Data’—which is the application of advanced analytics to data through rigorous processes to maximize the application and operational impact of decision making information. We present a Big Data Analytics framework for the DoD to overcome the stubborn paradigms of siloed data, unfederated information systems, and ungoverned analytic processes.
