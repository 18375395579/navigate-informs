Title: Spreadsheet Templates for Teaching Business Statistics

Author: Thomas Groleau, Professor, Carthage College, 2001 Alford Park Drive, Kenosha, WI, 53140, United States of America, tgroleau@carthage.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Demonstration of Excel spreadsheet templates provided to students in an introductory business and economics statistics course. Rather than use a spreadsheet add-in, non-macro templates are used. Students create their own templates for some problems, use teacher provided templates for others. Also, an interactive template us used to replace one chapter's set of PowerPoint slides.
