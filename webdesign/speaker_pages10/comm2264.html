Title: Joint Force Development: Transforming for the Future

Author: Clark Heidelbaugh, Joint Chiefs of Staff, The Joint Staff, 7000 Pentagon, Washington, DC, 20318-7000, United States of America, clark.heidelbaugh@js.pentagon.mil

Year: 2007

Abstract: Through a multitude of activities, the joint military community provides insights and findings that shape the design of the future force. By looking to the future and designing Joint Operations Concepts, the Joint Experimentation Transformation Concept Division of the Joint Staff J7 introduces new ideas to improve joint operations and capabilities. Through a process known as Joint Experimentation, which is based upon the future Joint Operations Concepts, the military can gain insights designed to produce results to shape Joint Force Development. This is a deliberate, iterative and continuous process of planning and developing that transforms the current force into a future joint force.
