Title: Pedagogical Research - An Introductory How-To Workshop

Author: Donna Llewellyn, Director, Georgia Institute of Technology, CETL, 225 North Avenue, Suite 4, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0383, United States, donna.llewellyn@gatech.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: There are many reasons why faculty do educational research. There are even more reasons why faculty should do so. This session is for faculty who are interested in getting started. We will do a brief intro to what is ed. research , how to start a project, & how to think about publishing it. We will provide resources, & do activities to get people started. So, come, network with other like-minded faculty, and bring some questions that you have about what in your teaching is working or not.
