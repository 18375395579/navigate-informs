Title: Developing a Curriculum for Service Systems

Author: Tang Loon Ching, National University of Singapore, 1, Engineering Drive 2, Dept of Ind & Sys Engrg, Singapore, 117576, Singapore, isetlc@nus.edu.sg

Year: 2008

Abstract: There has been a surge in interest in service science, management and engineering in the last two years as a result of active promotion by IBM and its partner universities. It is thus timely for academic institutions to develop program that leverages on the current strength of their academic units and the state of economic development in the country. Here, we present a case in the ISE Department of NUS in which we develop an area of specialization in service systems in our MSc program.
