Title: Global (and Local Nonlinear) Optimization in Integrated Computing Systems

Author: Janos D. Pinter, Pintér Consulting Services, Inc., 129 Glenforest Drive, Halifax, NS, Canada, jdpinter@hfx.eastlink.ca

Year: 2007

Abstract: Integrated scientific-technical computing (ISTC) systems support the development of sophisticated interactive documents that include computing, programming, visualization features. ISTC systems can be put to especially good use in the context of nonlinear systems modeling and optimization, in both education and research. We demonstrate global/local optimization capabilities of Maple and Mathematica, including both built-in and add-on functionality. We also highlight engineering and scientific application perspectives.
