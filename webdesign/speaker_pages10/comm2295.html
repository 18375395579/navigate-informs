Title: DHS Risk Characterization Methodology for Global Supply Chain Security

Author: Debra Elkins, DHS Policy, Office of Strategy, Planning, Analysis and Risk (SPAR), U.S. Department of Homeland Security, Washington, DC, United States of America, debra.elkins@hq.dhs.gov

Year: 2012

Abstract: The U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is leading a U.S. Government Risk Characterization to support the White House National Strategy on Global Supply Chain Security. DHS will talk about developing a Risk Characterization Methodology, and how the OR/MS community is helping to inform and shape this global problem space.
