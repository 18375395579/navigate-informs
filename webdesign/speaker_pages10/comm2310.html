Title: Pedagogical Research ?How to Get Started

Author: Donna Llewellyn, Director, Georgia Tech, CETL, 225 North Avenue, Suite 4, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0383, United States, donna.llewellyn@cetl.gatech.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: This will be an interactive session devoted to how to get started doing your own educational research project. What are the basic steps, who can help you along the way, and what are your goals? We will talk about where some common pitfalls occur, and how to avoid them. And we will discuss some of the common techniques used in educational research. This is the second in a series of four sessions at this conference on pedagogical research. Feel free to come even if you missed the first one!
