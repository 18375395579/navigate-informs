Title: An Interactive Panel/Mentoring Session in Honor of Rick Rosenthal

Author: Feryal Erhun, Stanford University, Department of MS&E, Stanford, CA, United States of America, ferhun@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Anne Robinson, Harlan Crowder, Karla Hoffman

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this interactive session we will discuss many issues such as mentoring, work/family/community balance, dual careers, and recruiting and retaining women and underrepresented minorities.
