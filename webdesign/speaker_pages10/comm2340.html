Title: Speed Networking

Author:

Coauthor(s):

Year: 2013

Abstract: Are you looking for a new research collaborator? Eager to branch out into a new area of research? Looking to establish professional connections outside of your institution? WORMS, MIF and JFIG invite you to this Speed Networking session. Participants will be grouped according to broad research interests and will meet successively in pairs to share research backgrounds and contact information. Bring your business cards!
