Title: Effective Deployment of Scalable Information Technology Solutions for Humanitarian Relief Services

Author: Louiqa Rashid, University of Maryland, VMH, College Park, United States of America, louiqa@umiacs.umd.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Recent disasters have identified the shortcomings of ICT solutions. I will discuss the effective deployment of solutions that address the following challenges of disaster data management (disDM): Diversity of the partner or client organizations; Heterogeneity and the need for interoperability to facilitate information sharing; Data Quality requirements to ensure overall high quality of service; Support for evolving semantic dataspaces to model past disasters and to provide a flexible platform and evolving solutions.
