Title: Delivering Decision Analytic Content to Youth

Author: Chris Spetzler, Executive Director, Decision Education Foundation, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, chris@decisioneducation.org

Year: 2013

Abstract: It’s Your Choice, a 20 hour course in Decision Skills, delivers Decision Analytic content to youth through short online videos extended with activities to reinforce learning. This session will provide a course overview and cover recent and planned implementation experiences, including opportunities for Decision Professionals to offer the course.
