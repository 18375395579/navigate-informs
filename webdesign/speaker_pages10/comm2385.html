Title: A Simulation-based Optimization Framework for Pandemic Impact Mitigation

Author: Tapas K. Das, Professor, University of South Florida, 4202 E. Fowler Avenue, ENB 118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States, das@eng.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Alex Savachkin, Diana Prieto, Andres Uribe

Year: 2007

Abstract: At present, scalable mitigation strategies do not exist for pandemic outbreaks that could affect tens of millions of people in many geographically dispersed cities. We present a real-time simulation tool mimicking movements of each individual in large cities during many weeks of pandemic progress and discuss how to generate mitigation strategies at both national and local levels using a simulation based hierarchical stochastic optimization framework.
