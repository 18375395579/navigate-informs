Title: Use of Cognitive Modeling and Decision Analysis for Risk Management

Author: Igor Linkov, US Army Engineer Research and Development Center, 696 Virginia Road, Concord, MA, 01742, United States of America, Igor.Linkov@usace.army.mil

Year: 2012

Abstract: Risk management challenges facing the US DOD require integrated approaches for knowledge acquisition and decision making. This paper illustrates the use of cognitive modeling and decision-analytical approaches to: (i) flood risk perception and management in US Army Corps of ENgineers; (ii) integration of cognitive models and multi-criteria decision analysis to address course of action selection in emergency settings.
