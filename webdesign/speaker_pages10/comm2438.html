Title: Panel Discussion: Marketing Science and Revenue Management, Contrast and Commonality

Author: Robert Shumsky, Professor, Tuck School of Business, Dartmouth College, Hanover, NH, 03755, United States of America, robert.shumsky@dartmouth.edu

Coauthor(s): Guillermo Gallego, Eyal Biyalogorsky, Steven Shugan, Ioana Popescu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Those who identify themselves as “Marketing Science?and “Revenue Management?professionals often are working on the same set of problems. Yet historically the two fields have seen relatively little overlap in research methods, journal publishing, and conference participation. Lately, however, the two fields seem to be growing closer. We bring together representatives of both fields to discuss the historical roots of the two fields, current research styles, and future directions.
