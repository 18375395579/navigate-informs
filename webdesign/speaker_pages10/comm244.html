Title: The Use of New Technology during Check-in at Airports

Author: Eric Bentzen, Professor, Copenhagen Business School, Solbjerg Plads 3, Frederiksberg C, DK-2000 F., Denmark, eb.om@cbs.dk

Coauthor(s): Juliana Hsuan

Year: 2012

Abstract: Smartphone technology replaces the traditional tickets used at check in at airports. The often labour-intensive check in has developed several new ways of improving productivity. Self-service systems have been built and recently smart phones have been introduced as a new way of doing check in.
