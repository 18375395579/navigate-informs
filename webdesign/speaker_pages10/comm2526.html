Title: Forming Effective Teams using Machine Learning and Optimization

Author: Alexandros Nathan, Northwestern University, 1930 Ridge Avenue Apt. C204, Evanston, IL, 60201, United States of America, anathan@u.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Sanjay Mehrotra, Noshir Contractor

Year: 2013

Abstract: As many disciplines in today’s world are becoming team-oriented, the assembly of effective teams has become an increasingly important task. We are proposing a data driven optimization model for the assignment of individuals to teams with the objective of maximizing the quality of the weakest group. The model is utilizing the results of recent Social Networks research in the elements of successful teams, such as personality traits of individuals and trust between members of the same team.
