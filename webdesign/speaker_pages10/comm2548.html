Title: Operations and Supply Management - The Core

Author: Robert Jacobs, Professor of Operations Management, Indiana University, 1309 East Tenth Street, Bloomington, IN, 47405, United States, jacobs@indiana.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: The new book concisely covers core operations management topics in 13 chapters. A theme that is carried throughout is that success for companies today requires managing the entire supply flow, from the sources of the firm, through the value added processes of the firm, and on to the customers of the firm. The first edition book will be available from McGraw-Hill during November, 2006.
