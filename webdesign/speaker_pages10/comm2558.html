Title: Strategic Workforce Planning Models for Semiconductor Manufacturing

Author: Shrikant Jarugumilli, Sr. Operations Research Specialist, BNSF Railway, 2400 Western Center Blvd., Ft Worth, TX, 76131, United States of America, Shrikant.Jarugumilli@bnsf.com

Coauthor(s): Scott Grasman, Naiping Keng

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this work, we propose an integrated framework that links the strategic workforce planning model to the operational workforce allocation models. The strategic workforce planning model enables the planners to make decisions to retain and hire workers, along with the design of worker cross-training programs based on the long-term requirements of the organization.
