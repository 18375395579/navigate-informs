Title: McGraw-Hill Education - Introducing Practice Operations: Single-Player Game for the Introductory Operations Management Course

Author: Heather A. Kazakoff, McGraw-Hill Education, 2 Penn Plaza, New York, NY, 10121, United States of America, heather_kazakoff@mcgraw-hill.com

Coauthor(s): Thomas Hayward

Year: 2013

Abstract: McGraw-Hill Practice Operations provide a lively, interactive experience that focuses on student learning through trial and error within holistic game play, where students see how the elements of operations and production come together. Highlights the inherently interdisciplinary nature of business by demonstrating that the various functional areas of the company, human resources, manufacturing, accounting, and sales must work together in order to meet company goals. Provides reporting features that make it easy for instructors to assess player performance and decision-making.
