Title: Explanation Systems

Author: K.Nadia Papamichail, Senior Lecturer, Manchester Business School, Booth Street East, Manchester, M15 6PB, United Kingdom, nadia.papamichail@mbs.ac.uk

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper reviews explanation systems for multi-criteria decision problems. It presents an explanation system that justifies choices in emergency management. Explanation facilities add transparency into the way that value-based choices are computed. They describe what a system does, and justify why the system’s recommendations are reasonable. The paper concludes with a discussion of future trends.
