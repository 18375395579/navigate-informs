Title: Social Media Deep Analytics: Integration and Evaluation Horizons

Author: Shawn Weil, Director: Analytics, Modeling, & Simulation, Aptima, Inc., 12 Gill St., Suite 1400, WOBURN, MA, 02176, United States of America, sweil@aptima.com

Coauthor(s): Robert McCormack, Ian Yohai

Year: 2013

Abstract: Contemporary analytic tools provide analysts a macro-level view of events of interest, but often do little more than aggregate and summarize. To enable deep insight, the multi-source social media landscape (photo, video, microblogs) needs to be considered explicitly in the context of traditional data sources.
