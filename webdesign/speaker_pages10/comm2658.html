Title: Visualization of Contents-oriented Services

Author: Chie-Hyeon Lim, Pohang University of Science and Technology, Engineering Building #4-316, Pohang, Korea, Republic of, arachon@postech.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Kwang-Jae Kim

Year: 2012

Abstract: A contents-oriented service is a type of service in which service contents have major roles in service provision. Examples include car-infotainment services and internet-based education services. This talk presents issues regarding the visualization of contents-oriented services. The conventional service blueprint and other existing tools for service visualization are reviewed for the visualization of contents-oriented services; the limitations and alternatives are also discussed.
