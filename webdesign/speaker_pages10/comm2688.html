Title: The Impacts of Virtualization on IT Infrastructure: An Analysis Based on an SSME Framework

Author: Minder Chen, Associate Professor, California State University, Channel Islands, One University Drive, Camarillo, CA, 93012, United States of America, minder.chen@csuci.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Gardner called virtualization the highest-impact trend for the information technology world through 2012. In our study, we will discuss various software virtualization approaches and their benefits. A service sciences, management, and engineering (SSME) framework has been used in guiding our analysis of virtualization technology. An IT Service standard, ITIL, is used in assessing the impacts of virtualization to IT services.
