Title: OnTheMark: An Integrated Workforce Management Solution

Author: Mark S. Squillante, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, Mathematical Sciences Department, 1101 Kitchawan Road, RT 134/PO Box 218, Yorktown Heights, NY, 10598, United States of America, mss@watson.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Ta-Hsin Li, Jianying Hu, Aleksandra Mojsilovic, Ana Radovanovic, Yingdong Lu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Forward-thinking businesses are investing in advanced workforce management (WFM) solutions as a competitive differentiator. We first analyze the requirements for advanced WFM solutions, which combine managerial discipline with sophisticated analytics and IT to produce demand forecast, labor standards, optimally staffed/timely delivered projects and efficiently deployed workers. We will then review analytics and optimization capabilities behind OnTheMark, a novel WFM system developed at IBM.
