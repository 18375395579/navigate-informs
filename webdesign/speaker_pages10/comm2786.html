Title: Integrated Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear Risk Assessment (iCBRNra) Methodology

Author: Barry Ezell, IDI, barry.ezell@innovativedecisions.com

Coauthor(s): Steve Bennett

Year: 2008

Abstract: The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) developed the the Integrated Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear Risk Assessment (iCBRNra) to fulfill the objectives of HSPD-18 Medical Countermeasures Against Weapons of Mass Destruction. iCBRNra is an extension of the Bioterrorism Risk Assessment (BTRA 2006) developed by DHS in 2005. In this presentation we introduce the iCBRNra methodology and discuss the strengths and limitations.
