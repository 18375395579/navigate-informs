Title: Transforming Army Logistics: A Supply Chain Approach

Author: Greg Parlier, Gregory.h.parlier@saic.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Fully engaged in the Golobal War on Terrorism, the US Army is also committed to a comprehensive and ambitious Transformation endeavor. This presentation summarizes a three-phase project, combining elements of classical inventory theory and large-scale supply chain management concepts with several innovative approaches, culminating in ananalytical architecture to guide Logistics Tranformation for the US Army.
