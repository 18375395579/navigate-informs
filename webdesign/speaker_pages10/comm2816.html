Title: Portfolio Decision Analysis and Organizational Planning- Who Are We and What can We Offer?

Author: Alec Morton, London School of Economics, A.Morton@lse.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Martin Schilling, Ahti Salo, Jack Kloeber, Jim Matheson

Year: 2008

Abstract: Decision Analysts are not the only analytic professionals who want and have a role in organizational resource planning. Finance professionals often drive capital budgeting. Information systems provide much of the data decisions require. Cheap computation and user-friendly software make math programming approaches ever more practical. As a group with a distinct identity, how do we position ourselves in this crowded marketplace? How do/should we relate to other providers of modeling and advice?
