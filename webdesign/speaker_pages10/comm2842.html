Title: 2013 Decision Analysis Student Paper Award

Author: Lea Deleris, IBM, Paris, 75015, France, lea.deleris@ie.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Jun Zhuang

Year: 2013

Abstract: The Student Paper Award is given annually to the best decision analysis paper by a student author, as judged by a panel of the Decision Analysis Society of INFORMS. Students who did not complete their Ph.D. prior to May 1, 2012 are eligible for this year's competition.
