Title: Marketing Science Research Opportunities in Search Marketing

Author: Jim Oliver, Google, Inc, 1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy, Mountain View, CA, 94043, United States of America, joliver@google.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Thanks to Google’s massive scale of personalized search advertising, and the digital nature of the data, the researcher’s problem is no longer gathering data. Similar to the early days of scanner data, the problem is tackling the data. The challenges and opportunities are abundant. This presentation will highlight a few of the data sources, modeling issues and research opportunities associated with Google’s flagship advertising vehicle, AdWords.
