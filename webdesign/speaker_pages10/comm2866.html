Title: An Axiomatic and Teachable Warehouse Design Method

Author: Leon McGinnis, Professor Emeritus, Georgia Institute of Technology, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, leon.mcginnis@isye.gatech.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: Distinguishing function from embodiment is the key to developing a teachable warehouse design method that incorporates both analytic and empirical knowledge and is a platform for creating computer aided warehouse design tools. The method will be described and illustrated with a case from the literature.
