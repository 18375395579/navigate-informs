Title: A Travel Demand Management Strategy: The Downtown Space Reservation System

Author: Yueqin Zhao, Northern Virginia Graduate Center, 7Grado Department of ISE, 054 Haycock Road, Falls Church, VA, 22043-2311, yueqinz@vt.edu

Coauthor(s): Kostas Triantis, Dusan Teodorovic, Praveen Edara

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper, traditional concept of booking/reservation is adopted into a Travel Demand Management context. The system is known as Downtown Space Reservation System. The purpose of the system is to mitigate traffic congestion in a downtown area. It is composed of two modules ?an integer optimization programming and an artificial neural network.
