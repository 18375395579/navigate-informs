Title: Some Ideas on the Advanced Quality Control Course

Author: Jianjun Shi, The Carolyn J. Stewart Chair Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, Room 214, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States of America, jshi33@isye.gatech.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Advanced quality control is an important course in a IE graduate curriculum. Though the course is routinely offered in different universities, there are not much standard contents or coverage among different programs. This presentation presents some AQC course outlines in different IE programs. Some ideas for the course design will be presented for further discussion.
