Title: Initial Career Selection Process for Air Force Officers

Author: James Lowe, Professor, 2354 Fairchild Drive, USAF Academy, CO, 80840, Jim.Lowe@usafa.af.mil

Coauthor(s): Andrew Armacost

Year: 2006

Abstract: The USAF Academy annually commissions 1,000 men/women into the AF. The process of matching cadets to careers involved data collection, a review panel, and time-consuming matching of cadets to jobs. In 2000, we began to improve the process. The intial phase occurred over a 2-year period, full-scale use happened for the next 2 years. In 2004, the academy committed resources to integrate our models into its database. In 2006, we integrated our system with the ROTC process and provide results.
