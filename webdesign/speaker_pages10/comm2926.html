Title: Application of Hierarchical Decision Modeling: Laptop Selection

Author: Kenny Phan, Portland State University, PO Box 751, Portland, OR, 97207, United States, kenny.phan@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Rimal Abu Taha, Phakphoom Chuenparsitporn, Qian Gu, Byung Chul Choi, Adriana Cutar

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper presents the application of the hierarchical decision modeling approach to the decision making process for selection of the best laptop for a college student to buy. After establishing a hierarchy of criteria , the model is populated with the data for ten of the most popular laptops available in the market. The laptop that fits each student’s objectives best is determined by pairwise comparisons of the attributes of the alternatives.
