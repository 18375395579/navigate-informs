Title: My (Almost) 50 Years In Operations Research in Washington

Author: Howard Eisner, Professor, George Washington University, EMSE/SEAS, 1776 G St. NW, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, heisner@gwu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: The field of Operations Research (OR) has evolved and changed considerably over the past 50 years. This paper describes the author's journey in OR in Washington, in such application areas as transportation, defense, the environment, information systems, security, aeronautics and space. It also provides some suggestions for the future.
