Title: A Paradigm Shift in Data Analyses and Interpretation

Author: Dipak Gupta, Professor, San Diego State University, 5500 Campanile Drive, NH-121, Department of Political Science, San Diego, CA, 92182-4427, United States of America, dgupta@mail.sdsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Brian Spitzberg, Ming Hsiang Tsou, Jean Mark Gawron, An Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: A paradigm shift is taking place in our understanding of the society that not only changes the analytical methodology but also calls for a thorough change in academic disciplinary boundaries. This article takes a comprehensive look the promises and pitfalls of this emerging field of inquiry.
