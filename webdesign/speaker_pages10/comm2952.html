Title: Constructing Organisational Preferences: Within-Criterion Weighting in Multi-Criteria Portfolio

Author: Alec Morton, A.Morton@lse.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Lawrence Phillips

Year: 2006

Abstract: Decision Analysis often helps people discuss what precisely constitutes value for their organisation. Decision Conferencing in particular uses multiple workshops with multiple areas of the organization to develop a value model. Locally assessed valuations may require “within-criterion weights?to arrive at a global value scale. We shall interpret these weights, and explore their ability to reduce error, and the appropriateness of different ways of structuring an intervention which uses them.
