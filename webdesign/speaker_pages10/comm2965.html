Title: Every Picture Tells a Story

Author: Mukund Thapa, Optical Fusion, Inc, 2060 Old Page Mill Road, Palo Alto, CA, 94304, United States of America, mukund.thapa@sbsinc.net

Year: 2008

Abstract: George Dantzig, the Father of Linear Programming and mathematical genius, had a great sense of humor, dabbled in painting, wrote a science fiction book, designed the house that he and Anne lived in, wrote Compact City (with Saaty), had solutions for ordinary problems, as well as how to create a zero inflation economy. He could enchant, impress and captivate all with his wit, keen sense of humor, wide interests, and patience. This talk will present photos and videos of George, the man.
