Title: Career Building at the National Laboratories

Author: Genetha Gray, Sandia National Laboratories, P.O. Box 969, MS 9159, Livermore, CA, 94551-0969, United States of America, gagray@sandia.gov

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, I will discuss the basic job duties of an operations research or analytics specialist at the national laboratories. I will give examples from my own ten year career as well as examples from co-workers and peers. In addition, I will describe how to use a job at a national laboratory to either build a lifelong career at the lab or to prepare for a career industry.
