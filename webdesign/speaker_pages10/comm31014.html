Title: Multi-stage Stochastic Decomposition

Author: Zhihong Zhou, The University of Arizona, 2519 Indianola Ave. Apt. A, Columbus, OH, United States, zhzhou@email.arizona.edu

Coauthor(s): Suvrajeet Sen

Year: 2007

Abstract: Stochastic decomposition (SD) has been very successful at solving two-stage stochastic programming problems. In this talk, we generalize the ideas underlying SD to multi-stage SLP. It turns out that Rockafellar's primal-dual stochastic programming framework for SP plays a critical role in this algorithm.
