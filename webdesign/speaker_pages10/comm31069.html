Title: The De-blurring of Medical Images with Image Segmentation

Author: Dorit Hochbaum, University of California Berkeley, hochbaum@ieor.berkeley.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: An image is segmented by modifying the pixels' colors while minimizing the penalty of modification and a penalty associated with adjacent pixels having different colors. A very efficient and best possible polynomial algorithm of Hochbaum, based on parametric cut procedure, removes blur from medical images and improves significantly their quality.
