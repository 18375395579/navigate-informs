Title: Extremal-volume Ellipsoids and Their Applications, Especially in Statistics

Author: Osman Guler, Professor, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, 1000 Hilltop Circle, Baltimore, MD, 21250, United States of America, guler@math.umbc.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We discuss geometric problems, e.g. extremal-volume ellipsoids and some of their applications, in data mining, control theory, statistics, and functional analysis. We use semi-infinite duality to obtain new results as well improving and unifying some known results. May other geometric problems can be treated with our approach; we will mention several of these. We also consider symmetry properties of our problems, with a view towards their practical and theoretical aspects.
