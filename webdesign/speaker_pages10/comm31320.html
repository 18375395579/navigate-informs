Title: FilMINT: A Linearizations-based MINLP Solver

Author: Kumar Abhishek, Lehigh University, 200 W Packer Avenue, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States, kua3@lehigh.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We describe a solver for mixed integer nonlinear programs that implements a linearization-based algorithm in a branch-and-cut framework. We offer new suggestions for generating and managing linearizations that are shown to be efficient on a wide range of MINLPs. We also use the advanced features of the MILP framework. FilMINT uses MINTO for solving the MILP, and Filter-SQP for solving the NLPs. Detailed computational experiments are presented, highlighting the framework's effectiveness.
