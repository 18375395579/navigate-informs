Title: How to Solve Optimal Experimental Design Problems?

Author: S. Damla Ahipasaoglu, PhD Student, Cornell University, 222 University Ave. No:5, Ithaca, NY, 14850, United States of America, dse8@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael J. Todd

Year: 2008

Abstract: It is known that Frank-Wolfe type algorithms are very useful in finding solutions for the D-optimal design problem of statistics, and its dual, the Minimum Volume Enclosing Ellipsoid problem. We devise similar algorithms for other optimal design problems such as the A-optimal design problem. These algorithms are also locally linearly convergent and have attractive features confirmed by computational results.
