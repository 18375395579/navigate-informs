Title: Dynamic Programming-based Cuts for the Capacitated Lot-sizing Problem

Author: Esra Buyuktahtakin, University of Florida, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 303 Weil Hall, P.O. Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, iesra@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Joseph Hartman

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this study, we analyze a dynamic programming algorithm for the single-item, capacitated lot-sizing problem. Furthermore, we utilize the associated linear programming formulation to generate cutting planes for the classical integer programming formulation of the CLSP and illustrate their computational effectiveness.
