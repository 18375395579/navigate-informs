Title: An Algorithm for Bilevel Integer Interdiction

Author: Yen Tang, University of Florida, 303 Weil Hall, Gainesville, United States of America, yentang@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Jean-Philippe Richard, J. Cole Smith

Year: 2012

Abstract: We propose a new algorithm for finding globally optimal solutions to a family of bilevel integer interdiction optimization in which the upper level minimizes the maximum of the objective of the lower level problem. We show that this algorithm, which proceeds by progressively building a suitable restriction of the lower level problem, is finitely convergent. We then present the results of a computational study aimed at evaluating the quality of our algorithm.
