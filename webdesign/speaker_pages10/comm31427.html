Title: A Semidefinite Optimization Approach to Space-free Multi-row Facility Layout

Author: Miguel F. Anjos, Canada Research Chair in Discrete Nonlinear Optimization in Engineering, Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal, C.P. 6079, succ. Centre-ville, Montreal, QC, H3C 3A7, Canada, miguel-f.anjos@polymtl.ca

Coauthor(s): Philipp Hungerländer

Year: 2012

Abstract: Multi-row facility layout seeks an optimal placement of departments along rows. Large single-row problems have been solved to global optimality using semidefinite optimization. We extend the approach to space-free multi-row layout and show that if provides high-quality bounds in reasonable time.
