Title: (Fraction) Dimension of a Poset and Constraint Feedback Arc Set Problem.

Author: Kamal Jain, Senior Researcher, Microsoft Research, One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA, 98052, United States, kjain@cc.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Rajneesh Hegde

Year: 2006

Abstract: The dimension of a partially ordered set (poset) is the minimum integer k such that the partial order can be expressed as the intersection of k total orders. We prove it is hard to approximate the dimension of a poset with $n$ elements within a factor $n^{0.5 - \epsilon} for any $\epsilon > 0$.
