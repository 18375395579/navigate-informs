Title: Mini-batch Stochastic Approximation Methods for Constrained Nonconvex Stochastic Programming

Author: Saeed Ghadimi, University of Florida, P.O. Box 210020, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, sghadimi@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Hongchao Zhang, Guanghui Lan

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a randomized mini-batch stochastic approximation method for solving constrained nonconvex stochastic programming problems. Complexity analysis of finding an approximate stationary point of this kind of problems is provided. Some computational results are also presented to show practical performance of the proposed algorithm.
