Title: Taking Advantage of Problem Structure in Optimization of Radiation Therapy

Author: Anders Forsgren, Royal Institute of Technology (KTH), Department of Mathematics, Stockholm, SE-100 44, Sweden, andersf@kth.se

Coauthor(s): Fredrik Carlsson

Year: 2008

Abstract: The optimization problem in IMRT may be formulated in several ways. One approach is to solve a fluence map optimization problem and then convert the solution to machine parameters of the treatment unit. Alternatively, one may solve an optimization problem in the machine parameters, either directly, by a column generation approach or by combining these two approaches. We discuss how one may take advantage of problem structure in the above approaches.
