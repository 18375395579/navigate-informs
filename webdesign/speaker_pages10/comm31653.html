Title: Decomposition Methods for Nonlinear Programming Under Uncertainty

Author: Ankur Kulkarni, Industrial and Enterprise Systems Engg, UIUC, Urbana, IL, United States, akulkar3@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Uday Shanbhag

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the two-period stochastic nonlinear programming and consinder the use of sequential quadratic programming for the solution of such problems. To solve the quadratic approximations, we present two stochastic QP solvers. The first is based on the inexact cut version of the L-shaped method for stochastic QPs. The second is a QP extension of the trust-region method suggested by Linderoth and Wright. We test the performance of the algorithm(s) on a nonlinear programming test problem set.
