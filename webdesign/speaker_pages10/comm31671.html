Title: Using Computational Grids for Solving Stochastic Programs

Author: Jeff Linderoth, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1513 University Avenue, Madison, WI, 53706-1572, United States, jtl3@lehigh.edu

Coauthor(s): Udom Janjarassuk, Jierui Shen

Year: 2007

Abstract: The theme of this talk is how to (easily) exploit the massive computing power offered by collections of loosely-federated, nondedicated computational units for solving stochastic programs. Experience with solving two-stage and multi-stage linear recourse problems will be presented.
