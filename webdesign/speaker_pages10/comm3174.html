Title: Multi-objective Programming with Simulation Optimization to Improve Patient Flow Distribution

Author: Jie Song, Peking University, NO. 298,Chengfu Road,Haidian District, Beijing, 100871, China, songjie@coe.pku.edu.cn

Coauthor(s): Yunzhe Qiu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Our research proposed a methodology to find the optimal patient flow with multi-dimension inputs and outputs for the multi-level healthcare system. Our approach integrates multi-objective programming and simulation optimization , and an algorithm based on Stochastic Approximation and improved by the Response Surface Method is used. A local case study is carried out, and a comparison between our optimization algorithm to the Random Search Approach indicates that our method is faster.
