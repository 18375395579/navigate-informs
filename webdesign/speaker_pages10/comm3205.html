Title: Pareto Efficiency in Robust Optimization

Author: Dan Iancu, Assistant Professor, Stanford University, 655 Knight Way, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, daniancu@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Nikos Trichakis

Year: 2013

Abstract: We formalize the concept of Pareto efficiency in the context of the robust optimization (RO) methodology.We characterize Pareto robustly optimal (PRO) solutions,and extend the RO framework by proposing methods to verify Pareto optimality and generate PRO solutions.Our approach requires solving problems that are of the same complexity as the nominal RO problems,and numerical experiments demonstrate the significant potential upside of PRO solutions compared with classical RO solutions.
