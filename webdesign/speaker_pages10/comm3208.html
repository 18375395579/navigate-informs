Title: Conjectured Supply Function Equilibria Under Uncertainty

Author: Andrew Liu, Johns Hopkins University, Whiting School of Engineering, Baltimore, MD, United States, lliu@jhu.edu

Coauthor(s): Uday Shanbhag

Year: 2006

Abstract: We employ the Conjectured Supply Function approach to model a two-period stochastic game in which players compete in supply functions in the first period and make generation (recourse) decisions in the second-period. The resulting problem may be formulated as a stochastic complementarity problem. A scalable algorithm for solving such a problem is suggested and its convergence properties are discussed briefly. Finally, some results from a small electrical network are reported.
