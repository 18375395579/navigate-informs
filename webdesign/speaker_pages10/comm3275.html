Title: On the O(1/k) Convergence of Asynchronous Distributed Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers

Author: Ermin Wei, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 32-D640 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, erminwei@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Asuman Ozdaglar

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a network of agents that are cooperatively optimizing a problem with separable objective functions and linear coupling constraints. Recent literature studied distributed solutions through either subgradient based methods with slow convergence rate or Alternating Direction Method of Multipliers (ADMM) based methods, which require a synchronous implementation. In this paper, we present a novel asynchronous ADMM based distributed method and show that it converges at rate O(1/k).
