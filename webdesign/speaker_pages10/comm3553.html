Title: Recent Advances in Xpress Pre-solve

Author: Michael Perregaard, Xpresss Optimization, FICO, FICO House, International Square, Starley Way, Birmingham, B37 7GN, United Kingdom, MichaelPerregaard@fico.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The ability to reduce and tighten MIP problems significantly by the application of simple rules often makes the differences between a solvable and an unsolved problem. We will here present some of the recent presolve techniques applied by Xpress.
