Title: A Complementarity Framework for Forward Contracting under Uncertainty

Author: Uday Shanbhag, Asst. Professor, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 216 Transportation Bldg, Urbana, Il, 61822, United States of America, udaybag@uiuc.edu

Coauthor(s): Gerd Infanger, Peter W. Glynn

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider an instance of a stochastic multi-leader multi-follower equilibrium problem. By conjecturing a relationship between forward and spot decisions, we may use complementarity theory to provide an existence result. We present a scalable algorithm for solving the resulting stochastic complementarity problem by using matrix-splitting techniques. Tests show that the effort grows linearly with the number of scenarios. The model is applied to a two-settlement 6-node electricity market.
