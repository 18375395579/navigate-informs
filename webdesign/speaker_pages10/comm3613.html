Title: Developing Applications Based on CHiPPS

Author: Yan Xu, SAS Institute Inc., SAS Campus Drive, Cary, NC, 27519, United States of America, Yan.Xu@sas.com

Coauthor(s): Ted Ralphs, Laszlo Ladanyi, Matthew Saltzman

Year: 2008

Abstract: CHiPPS is the COIN-OR Open Parallel Search Framework, a framework for implementing parallel algorithms based on tree search. The current CHiPPS architecture consists of three layers: ALPS, BiCePS and BLIS. In this talk, first we briefly introduce the main concepts of CHiPPS, then we use VRP/TSP as an example to show how to develop specific applicatoins based on CHiPPS.
