Title: Recent Applications of the Multi-start Global Optimization Algorithm MSNLP

Author: Leon Lasdon, University of Texas, Austin, TX, Leon.Lasdon@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): Zsolt Ugray, John Plummer, Michael Bussieck

Year: 2008

Abstract: The multi-start global optimization algorithm MSNLP starts a local NLP solver from a filtered subset of points generated by a starting point generator or “driver.?Recent improvements include dynamic distance and merit filters and stochastic drivers as alternatives to OptQuest. We describe some recent applications of this system to GAMS models of fixed-mix multiperiod investing and to fitting a waterflooding oilfield model to field data.
