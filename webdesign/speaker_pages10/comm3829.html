Title: A Regularized Interior-Point Method for Semidefinite Programming

Author: Ahad Dehghani, McGill University, Montreal, Canada, ahad.dehghani@mcgill.ca

Coauthor(s): Jean-Louis Goffin, Dominique Orban

Year: 2012

Abstract: Interior-point methods in semi-definite programming (SDP) require the solution of a sequence of linear systems which are used to derive the search directions. Safeguards are typically required in order to handle rank-deficient Jacobians and free variables. We propose a primal-dual regularization to the original SDP and show that it is possible to recover an optimal solution of the original SDP via inaccurate solves of a sequence of regularized SDPs for both the NT and dual HKM directions.
