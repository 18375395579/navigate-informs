Title: DAKOTA: A Toolkit for Engineering Design

Author: William Hart, Distinguished Member of Technical Staff, Sandia National Laboratories, Mail Stop 1110, PO Box 5800, Albuquerque, NM, 87185-1110, United States, wehart@sandia.gov

Coauthor(s): Michael Eldred, Anthony Giunta

Year: 2006

Abstract: We summarize capabilities and applications of the DAKOTA toolkit for high performance computers. It provides a flexible interface between simulation software and algorithms for optimization, uncertainty quantification, parameter estimation, design of experiments, and sensitivity analysis. DAKOTA enables research in multilevel parallel computing, mixed integer nonlinear programming, surrogate-based optimization, optimization under uncertainty, and simultaneous analysis and design.
