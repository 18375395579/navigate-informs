Title: Non-convex QP in CPLEX

Author: Christian Bliek, IBM, 1681 HB2 Route des Dolines, Valbonne, 06560, France, bliek@fr.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Pierre Bonami

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present the techniques used in CPLEX to solve non-convex QP and will show the performance on a number of benchmarks.
