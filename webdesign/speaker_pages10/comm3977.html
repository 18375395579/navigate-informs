Title: New Cutting Planes for Cardinality Constrained Optimization

Author: Ismael de Farias, Associate Professor, Department of Industrial Engineering, Texas Tech, Lubbock, TX, United States of America, defarias@buffalo.edu

Coauthor(s): Ming Zhao

Year: 2008

Abstract: Cardinality constraints are pervasive in applications in fields as diverse as finance, bioinformatics, and data mining. We present a polyhedral study of such problems and new inequalities for use as cutting planes in LP-based branch-and-bound.
