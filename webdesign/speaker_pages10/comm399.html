Title: Hierarchical Target Setting for Large Enterprise

Author: Yingdong Lu, IBM T.J. Watson Research Center, 1101 Kitchawan Rd, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, yingdong@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Debarun Bhattacharjya, Mark Squillante

Year: 2012

Abstract: We formulate and solve a stochastic optimization problem to determine performance targets for large enterprises with a complex hierarchy. Solution techniques and structural properties of the stochastic optimization are discussed. Numerical experiments show that our approach provides better targets than several widely adopted business practices.
