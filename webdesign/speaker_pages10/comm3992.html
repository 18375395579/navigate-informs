Title: An Integrality Gap Minimization Algorithm for Binary Mixed Integer Nonlinear Programming

Author: Wendel Melo, Visiting Scholar, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Avenue, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109-2117, United States of America, wendelalexandre@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Fernanda Raupp, Marcia Fampa

Year: 2013

Abstract: This work presents a new algorithm for binary Mixed Integer Nonlinear Programming (MINLP) problems based on the idea of minimizing the integrality gap. To improve feasible solutions, a sequence of convex and nonconvex nonlinear programming subproblems is solved. Computational experiments have shown that the proposed algorithm can find feasible solutions of the original problem very fast, showing a promising feasibility heuristic.
