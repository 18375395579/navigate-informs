Title: Enterprise Inventory Optimization- The Devil is in the Details

Author: Valerie Tardif, Vice President, Algorithms, SmartOps Corporation, Suite 400, 12 Federal Street, Pittsburgh, PA, 15212, United States of America, VTardif@smartops.com

Coauthor(s): Sridhar Tayur, Lei Wang

Year: 2008

Abstract: Delivering value through inventory optimization requires three elements: a comprehensive model of the supply chain, an algorithm that optimize tradeoffs between cost and service, and end-to-end integration with execution systems. We share solution approaches, and a few lessons learned along the way, that have contributed to our success at implementing enterprise inventory optimization and bringing value across industries.
