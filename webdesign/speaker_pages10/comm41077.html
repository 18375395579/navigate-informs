Title: Generic Simulation Model for Assembly Line Supply

Author: Veronique Limere, Post-doctoral researcher, Ghent University, Technologiepark 903, Zwijnaarde, 9052, Belgium, Veronique.Limere@UGent.be

Coauthor(s): Kurt De Cock, El-Houssaine Aghezzaf

Year: 2013

Abstract: A simulation model for materials supply to mixed-model assembly lines will be discussed. The simulation model is created in FlexSim by means of an automatic model generator and demonstrates the in-plant logistical flows involved to supply parts from the warehouse to the assembly line. The results of the simulation model are compared to those of a deterministic mixed integer linear programming model. The findings of this study will be reported.
