Title: An Optimal Resource Allocation Model Aging for Bus Transit Fleet Based on Service Life and Budget Constraint

Author: Sushant Sharma, Purdue University, NEXTRANS, 3000 Kent Avenue, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, sharma57@purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Sabyasachee Mishra

Year: 2012

Abstract: An optimization model for allocation of funds among different fleet improvement programs within budget constraints over the planning period is presented. This is achieved by minimizing the Net Present Cost of the investment within the constraint of remaining life of the fleet. The model formulation and application are demonstrated with a real world case study of transit agencies
