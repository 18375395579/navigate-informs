Title: Affine Policies and Constraint Generation for the Multistage Adaptive Robust Unit Commitment Problem

Author: Alvaro Lorca, Operations Research PhD Student, Georgia Institute of Technology, 755 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, alvarolorca@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Andy Sun

Year: 2013

Abstract: The uncertain nature of multiple factors such as wind power generation and electricity demand makes the operation of power systems highly challenging. In this talk we present a multistage adaptive robust approach for the unit commitment problem under uncertain net electricity demands. We consider families of approximate affine policies for dealing with this hard problem and a solution method based on constraint generation with several algorithmic improvements.
