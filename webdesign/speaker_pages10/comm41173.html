Title: Unlocking the Value of Improved Service Level in Outsourcing Services for Optimal Capacity Decision

Author: Sechan Oh, IBM Research, 650 Harry Road, San Jose, CA, United States of America, seoh@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Ankit Shah, Anca Chandra, Sumeet Kaur, Diana Negoescu, Gerhard Satzger

Year: 2013

Abstract: In IT and business process outsourcing services, service firms often need to acquire additional capacity for new service contracts before the contracts are signed because of the long leadtime for capacity acquisition. In order to make the optimal capacity investment decision, firms need to quantify the benefit of improved service level, which does not have a direct impact on revenue. In this talk, we present a federated data analysis approach to connect financial data and operational data.
