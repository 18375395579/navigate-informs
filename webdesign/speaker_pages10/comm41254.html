Title: Inventory Control and Risk Management of Natural Gas Storage Assets

Author: Owen Wu, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, owenwu@bus.umich.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Managing a natural gas storage asset involves optimizing injection and withdrawal of natural gas and managing the risk using futures. The delivery of matured futures is typically evenly spread over the entire delivery month, which affects the injection and withdrawal rates. We study the structure of the optimal inventory control and trading strategy.
