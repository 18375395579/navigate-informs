Title: A Matrix Game Approach for FTR Allocation in Electric Power Networks

Author: Tapas K. Das, Professor, University of South Florida, 4202 E. Fowler Avenue, ENB 118, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States, das@eng.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Cihan Babayigit

Year: 2007

Abstract: We develop a matrix game theoretic modeling approach for allocation of financial transmission rights in electric power networks. The model considers the usual network constraints, contingencies, variability and the risk factor associated with the estimation of congestion cost, and the revenue adequacy condition. A learning based solution approach yields a multidimensional equilibrium bid vector for FTRs consisting of price, quantity, and mix of options and obligations.
