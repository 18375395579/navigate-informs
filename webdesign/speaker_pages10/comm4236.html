Title: Decision Making in the Operation of Smart Distribution Systems

Author: Mingguo Hong, Midwest ISO, 5407 Alvamar Place, Carmel, IN, 46033, United States of America, mingguoh@hotmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: The Smart Grid operation involves both the eletrical transmission and distribution systems where infrastructure and operational strategy changes should take place in parallel. This talk focuses on the operational decisions of the Smart Grid at the distribution system level to ensure greater grid reliability and economic efficiency. The talk also looks at a potential market design for the distribution system involving electrical utility and their customers.
