Title: Addressing Risk in a Supplier Selection Problem

Author: Lei Li, University of Washington, Industrial Engineering, University of Washington, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States, leili@u.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Zelda Zabinsky

Year: 2007

Abstract: With implementation of JIT and trends to global outsouring, the risk presented in the strategic supplier selection decision process must be considered. Risk is captured in a two-stage stochastic programming model with recourse and a chance constrained programming model to determine a Pareto set of suppliers and optimal order quantities with consideration of business volume discounts.
