Title: The MiniCAM Integrated Assessment Model

Author: Jae Edmonds, Laboratory Fellow and Chief Scientist, Pacific Northwest National Laboratory, Joint Global Change Research Institute a, 8400 Baltimore Avenue, Suite 201, College Park, MD, 20740-2496, United States of America, jae@pnl.gov

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation will describe the MiniCAM Integrated Assessment Model and present results from its analysis of the interplay between bioenergy, stabilization of CO2 concentrations, agriculture and land-use change emissions.
