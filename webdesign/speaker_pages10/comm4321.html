Title: Pre-positioning Inventory for Emergency Response

Author: Serhan Duran, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of ISyE, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, sduran@isye.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Pinar Keskinocak, Marco A Gutierrez, Adaora Okwo

Year: 2007

Abstract: In humanitarian supply chains demand is complicated with many unknown factors such as location, size, and timing of disasters. We developed a pre-positioning strategy and compared it, in terms of its effectiveness and timeliness of response, with current practices of sending resources through local and global suppliers.
