Title: Cross-border Energy and Transmission Dispatch in Central America Electricity Market

Author: Ricardo Rios, AREVA T&D, United States of America, ricardo.rios-zalapa@areva-td.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: An hourly day-ahead energy and transmission dispatch is currently in operation for the international electricity trade in the six Central American Markets, the optimization mechanism allows market participants to submit energy only bids and offers as well as requests for point to point transmission services. The mechanisms will be compared to models used in the USA.
