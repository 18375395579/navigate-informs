Title: Project Financing for Investments in Energy Technologies

Author: Ryuta Takashima, Tokyo University of Science, 2641 Yamazaki, Noda-shi, Chiba, 278-8510, Japan, takashima@rs.tus.ac.jp

Coauthor(s): Kyoko Yagi, Shohei Ohata

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper we examine how the scheme of project financing affects the decisions of the investment in energy technologies as power generations in real options framework. We analyze the interaction between the investment decisions of a special purpose company which has an investment project and the debt-paying ability. We also show the relation between public and private financial institutions with respect to the debt guarantee. Additionally, we consider a suspending risk of plants operations.
