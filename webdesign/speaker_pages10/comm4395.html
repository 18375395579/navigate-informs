Title: Discrete-time optimization of consumption and investment decisions under liquidity constraints

Author: Amit Bhandari, Northwestern University, 2145 N Sheridan Rd, Evanston, IL, United States, amit@iems.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): John R. Birge

Year: 2006

Abstract: Both individual and institutional investors face liquidity constraints in making investments in alternative assets. We study their impact on optimal investment policies under a dynamic discrete time setting.
