Title: Incorporating Uncertainty into an Optimal Selection of Suppliers and Ordering Plan

Author: Lei Li, University of Washington, Industrial Engineering, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States, leili@u.washington.edu

Coauthor(s): Zelda Zabinsky

Year: 2006

Abstract: Supplier selection is an important strategic management decision. Incorporating uncertain demand and supplier capacity into the optimization results in a more robust solution than treating them as deterministic values. A stochastic programming model with recourse and a chance-constrained model are developed to optimize the selection of supplier and ordering plan.
