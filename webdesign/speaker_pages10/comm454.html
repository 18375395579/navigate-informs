Title: Supply Chain Networks with Corporate Financial Risks and Trade Credits under Economic Uncertainty

Author: Jose Cruz, Associate Professor, University of Connecticut, School of Business, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States of America, jose.cruz@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Zugang Liu

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper provides a framework to investigate how financial risks affect the values of interconnected supply chain firms' profitability, as well as, the cash and credit transactions. From a network perspective, we develop a variational inequality equilibrium model in conjunction with capital asset pricing model and the net present value to determine the optimal supply chain prices, profits, and implicit equity values of supply chain firms under financial risks and economic uncertainty.
