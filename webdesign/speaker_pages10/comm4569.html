Title: Challenges to Market Participants

Author: Alva Svoboda, Manager, Pacific Gas and Electric Co., 77 Beale St., Rm. 2565F, San Francisco, CA, 94105, United States of America, ajsh@pge.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: The combination of continuing improvements in optimization technologies, developments in stochastic optimization methods, and ISO responses to renewables integration pose interesting O.R. challenges to market participants in the valuation of new resources, contracting, and day-to-day bidding and dispatch functions.
