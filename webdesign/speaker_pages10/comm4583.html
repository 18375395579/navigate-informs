Title: Adversarial Risk Analysis: The Somali Pirates Case

Author: Jesus Rios, IBM, Yorktown Heights, NY, United States of America, jriosal@us.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): David Rios Insua, Juan Carlos Sevillano

Year: 2012

Abstract: We show how Adversarial Risk Analysis may cope with a current important security issue in relation with piracy in the Somali coasts. Specifically, we describe how to support the owner of a ship in managing risks from piracy in that area. We illustrate how a sequential defend-attack-defend model can be used to formulate this decision problem and solve it for the ship owner. Our formulation models the pirates' behavior through the analysis of how they could solve their decision problem.
