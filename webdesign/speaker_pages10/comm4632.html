Title: Lean Production Applications at CPR Freight Classification Yards

Author: Jeff Adams, General Manager - Yards Operations, Canadian Pacific Railway, Suite 500 Gulf Canada Square, 401 9th Avenue SW, Calgary, AB, T2P 4Z4, Canada, jeff_adams@cpr.ca

Year: 2006

Abstract: Addressing railroad capacity constraints often leads to expanded infrastructure or other hard asset investments - an extremely expensive way to buy capacity. Lean production methodologies can yield significant results without resorting to capital. Experiences and results of lean applications in freight classification yards at Canadian Pacific will be discussed.
