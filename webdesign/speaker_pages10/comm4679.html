Title: Validation of Adversary Utility Assessment by Proxy

Author: Richard John, Associate Professor, University of Southern California, Department of Psychology, MC-1061, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-1061, United States of America, richardj@usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Heather Rosoff

Year: 2013

Abstract: We report two validation studies comparing MAU models for two different politically active non-profit organizations that utilize civil disobedience to achieve political objectives. In both cases, we demonstrate good convergence between the proxy model and the model assessed by direct contact with a stakeholder representative.
