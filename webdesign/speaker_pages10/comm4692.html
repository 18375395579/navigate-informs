Title: An Optimization Model for Advanced Biofuel Production based on Bio-oil Gasification

Author: Qi Li, Iowa State University, 0076 Black Engineering, Ames, IA, 50011, United States of America, qili@iastate.edu

Coauthor(s): Guiping Hu

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this research, a two-stage stochastic programming is formulated. The first-stage makes the capital investment decisions including the locations and capacities of the bio-facilities (distributed small scale fast pyrolysis plants and centralized refining facility) while the second-stage determines the biomass and bio-fuels flows. This paper aims to find the optimal design of the supply chain considering uncertainties in biomass yield, biofuel price and transportation costs.
