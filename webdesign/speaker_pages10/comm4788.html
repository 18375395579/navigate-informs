Title: Probabilistic n-K Security Criterion with Significant Renewable Energy Resources Generation

Author: David Pozo, Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile, Avenue Vicuña Mackenna # 4860, Santiago, Chile, davidpozocamara@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Javier Contreras

Year: 2013

Abstract: We present a new approach for the joint energy and reserves scheduling and unit commitment with n-K reliability constraints for the day-ahead market. The proposed method includes an n-K criterion where demand must be met with a specified probability under any simultaneous loss of K generating units. A chance-constrained method is proposed to determine the confidence level to meet the demand under K simultaneous contingencies. The effect of increasing renewable penetration is analyzed.
