Title: Energy Procurement Strategies in the Presence of Intermittent Sources

Author: Sachin Adlakha, Caltech, 1200 E. California Blvd., Pasadena, CA, 91125, United States of America, adlakha@caltech.edu

Coauthor(s): Adam Wierman, Jayakrishnan Nair

Year: 2012

Abstract: The increasing penetration of renewable energy sources, such as wind energy, pose significant challenges for utility companies. In this talk, we discuss inventory management issues that arise in the presence of renewable resources. A key insight from our work is a separation between the impact of the structure of electricity markets and the impact of increased penetration.
