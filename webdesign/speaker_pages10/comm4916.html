Title: Comparison of Approaches to Untraded Asset Valuation

Author: Steve Derby, sgderby@pacbell.net

Coauthor(s): Ross Shachter

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine a range of approaches for the valuation of uncertain cash flows from untraded assets, with trading opportunities in a no-arbitrage market. Traditional decision analytic valuation does not exploit market opportunities and provides a lower bound. Standard financial option theory imposes conditions of completeness and provides an upper bound.
