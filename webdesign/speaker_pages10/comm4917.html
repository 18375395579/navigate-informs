Title: A Mixed Integer Programming Approach for Cybersecurity Risk Planning

Author: Tadeusz Sawik, Professor, AGH University of Science & Technology, Al.Mickiewicza 30, Krakow, 30059, Poland, ghsawik@cyf-kr.edu.pl

Year: 2013

Abstract: Using a mixed integer programming with CVaR as a risk measure, a subset of available countermeasures is selected for implementation under limited budget, to minimize the expected and/or worst-case losses from successful cyber-attacks. The effectiveness of countermeasures for blocking different cyber-threats and the probability of potential attack scenarios are considered.
