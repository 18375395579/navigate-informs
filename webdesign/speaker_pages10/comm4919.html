Title: Coordination Mechanisms in Forest Value Chains

Author: Sophie D'Amours, Universit?Laval, Department of Mechanical Engineering, Pavillon Adrien Pouliot, Québec, QC, G1K 7P4, Canada, sophie.damours@forac.ulaval.ca

Coauthor(s): Mikael Ronnqvist

Year: 2012

Abstract: Logistic planning is often based on decoupled planning of several independent problems. In these cases, there is a need to introduce coordination mechanisms to find the best solution for the entire chain. We explore a number of planning problems and describe efficient coordination mechanisms based on different decomposition strategies.
