Title: Interdicting a Nuclear Weapons Project

Author: Gerald Brown, Naval Postgraduate School, Monterey, CA, 93943

Coauthor(s): Matt Carlyle, Robert Harney, Eric Skroch, Kevin Wood

Year: 2007

Abstract: We introduce a bilevel “mip-versus-mip?optimization of interdiction of a project to produce a first small batch of fission weapons, where the proliferator can anticipate our actions. We find vulnerabilities in a published proliferation project. Such insights can help policy makers evaluate the effect of interdiction options at their disposal.
