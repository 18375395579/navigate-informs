Title: Service Dynamics under Repeated Interactions

Author: Sam Aflaki, PhD Candidate, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, Sam.AFLAKI@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Ioana Popescu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We model a dynamic service relationship where the provider can adjust the service level in each period and customers decide to renew the service contract based on previous experiences and future expectations. We characterize the firm's optimal strategies under various customer behavior models. In particular, we consider the effect of gain/loss asymmetry on (a) the evolution of overall perception of service quality and (b) the decision making process for the renewal of the service contract.
