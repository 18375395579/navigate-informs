Title: Joint Stocking and Assortment Planning under the Nested Logit Model

Author: W. Zachary Rayfield, Cornell University, Ithaca, NY, United States of America, wzr2@cornell.edu

Coauthor(s): Paat Rusmevichientong, Huseyin Topaloglu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a joint inventory replenishment and assortment offering problem where customers choose among the set of offered products according to the nested logit model. We show that optimal offer assortments have a desirable structure that arises from ordering products by popularity.
