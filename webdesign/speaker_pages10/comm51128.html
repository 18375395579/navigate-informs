Title: Stock Positioning in the Flu Vaccine Supply Chain: Issues and Implications

Author: Prashant Yadav, Professor of Supply Chain Management, MIT-Zaragoza Intl Logistics Program, Zaragoza Logistics Center, Avda Gomez Laguna 25, Zaragoza, AR, 50009, Spain, pyadav@zlc.edu.es

Year: 2006

Abstract: We look at the tradeoffs involved in positioning vaccine stock at the physician's practice vs at the distributor. A scheme where pre-ordered stock is maintained at a centralized location for each healthcare provider and is dispacthed upon appointment scheduling is analyzed. The impacts of such a scheme on cold chain breaks and more efficient distribution are modeled.
