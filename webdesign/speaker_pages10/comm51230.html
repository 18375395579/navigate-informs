Title: Relationship as Coordination Mechanism in a Supply Chain

Author: Christoph Loch, Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, christoph.loch@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Yaozhong Wu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Double marginalizaton is a well-known problem in supply chains. We show that parties partially motivated by status and relationships may achieve supply chain coordination in the presence of even weak selection over time.
