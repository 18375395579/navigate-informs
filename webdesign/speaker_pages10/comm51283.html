Title: Engineering Changes and Quality Spills in Long Supply Chains

Author: Daniel Reaume, Staff Researcher, General Motors Operations Research, M/C 480-106-155, 30500 Mound Road, Warren, MI, 48090, United States of America, daniel.reaume@gm.com

Coauthor(s): Bob Bordley

Year: 2007

Abstract: Long supply chains tend to make engineering changes and quality spills more difficult to manage because disruptions lead to long pipelines of unusable parts. We present an analytic model of such disruptions, taking into account production rates, supplier capacity, pipeline length, premium freight and part repairability.
