Title: A Competitive Inventory Model under Customers' Strategies

Author: Hitoshi Hohjo, Associate Professor, Osaka Prefecture University, 1-1, Gakuen-cho, Naka-ku, Sakai, Osaka, 599-8531, Japan, hojo@mi.s.osakafu-u.ac.jp

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a competitive inventory model with two retailers and their customers, and analyze it in the game theory. Each retailer decides ordering strategy so as to maximize his profit. And customers decide the first visiting retailer under the minimization of total cost with respect to moving and purchasing a product at the beginning of planning period. Our goal is to investigate the influence of strategies between retailers and customers.
