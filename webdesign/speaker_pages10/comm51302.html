Title: Contracting Between Downside Risk Averse Supplier and Risk Neutral Retailer

Author: Sudarsan Rangan, The University of Alabama, ISM Department, Alston Hall, The University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, AL, 35487, United States, srangan@cba.ua.edu

Coauthor(s): Charles Sox

Year: 2007

Abstract: The objective of this research project is to develop a coordinating contract acceptable to all agents involved. This paper attempts to model and analyze the supplier’s downside risk in some existing types of contracts and to identify conditions under which the supply chain can be coordinated in a single period setting under demand uncertainty.
