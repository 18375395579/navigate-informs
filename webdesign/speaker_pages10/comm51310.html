Title: Market Positioning of Technologically Advanced Products

Author: John Angelis, Rochester Institute of Technology, 105 Lomb Memorial Drive, Rochester, NY, United States of America, jangelis@saunders.rit.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: A first mover sets price and product quality to attract the most profitable market segments. However, such a strategy may not be optimal if a late entrant offers higher quality. We investigate this trade-off in optimal product positioning given typical segment characteristics for a technologically advanced new product.
