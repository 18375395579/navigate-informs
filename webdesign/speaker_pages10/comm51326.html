Title: Sequential Innovation under Development Uncertainty: Inventory and New Product Launch Decisions

Author: Ankur Goel, Prof, Case Western Reserve University, ankur.goel2@case.edu

Coauthor(s): Sreekumar Bhaskaran, Karthik Ramachandran

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper, we focus on the Inventory Planning and Introduction timing decisions surrounding product rollovers in sequential innovation when a firm faces uncertainty regarding the outcome of a new product development endeavor. We consider a firm that has to make the inventory decision for an existing product while anticipating the launch of a new product . We characterize the product launch timing decision as the optimal recourse to observed new product quality and current product inventory.
