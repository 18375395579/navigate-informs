Title: Optimal Long-Term Supply Contracts with Asymmetric Demand and Inventory Information

Author: Ilan Lobel, New York University, Stern Business, 44 W 4th St, New York, NY, United States of America, ilobel@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Wenqiang Xiao

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the problem of finding optimal dynamic supply contracts when faced with a retailer with private demand and inventory information. We show that the optimal contract is very simple one: retailers pick a wholesale price, pay a fee and the manufacturer sticks to this wholesale price.
