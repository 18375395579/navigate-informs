Title: Perfusion Supply Chain Planning

Author: Dan Bu, University of California, Berkeley, IEOR Dept., Berkeley, CA, 94720-1777, United States of America, danbu@berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Phil Kaminsky

Year: 2013

Abstract: Motivated by some supply chain planning and production control problems faced by a biopharmaceutical firm with which we have worked, we consider production and supply chain planning and control where manufacturing has to be periodically restarted, production rate is random, a subset of production steps are outsourced, and products can expire at any stage of the supply chain, but the “clock?is restarted each time additional steps are completed.
