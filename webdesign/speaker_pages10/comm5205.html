Title: Optimal Design of a Pharmaceutical Price-Volume Agreement

Author: Hui Zhang, Lakehead University, 955 Oliver Rd, Thunder Bay, ON, P7B 5E1, Canada, hzhang2@lakeheadu.ca

Coauthor(s): Greg Zaric

Year: 2008

Abstract: Price-volume agreements are commonly negotiated between drug manufacturers and third-party payers for drugs. In one form of price-volume agreement a drug manufacturer pays a rebate to the payer on a portion of sales in excess of a specified sales threshold. We examine the optimal design of such an agreement under two different assumptions about pricing, each under complete and asymmetric information about the mean demand.
