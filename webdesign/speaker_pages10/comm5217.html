Title: Purchasing, Pricing, and Quick Response in the Presence of Strategic Consumers

Author: Robert Swinney, Doctoral Student, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut St. Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, rswinney@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Gerard Cachon

Year: 2007

Abstract: We address the issue of a retailer selling a short life cycle product to a strategic consumer population. The retailer sets the inventory level and the markdown price of a product, and consumers anticipate future markdowns when choosing whether to purchase an item at the full price or wait for a sale. We consider how quick response inventory practices-the ability of a retailer to rapidly replenish inventory after learning about demand-are affected by strategic consumer purchasing behavior.
