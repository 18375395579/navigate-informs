Title: Black Friday Pricing and Inventory Managment in Retail

Author: Nikolay Osadchiy, Emory University, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, nikolay.osadchiy@emory.edu

Coauthor(s): Vishal Gaur

Year: 2012

Abstract: Black Friday sales are used by retailers to generate revenue through selling at discount prices as well as gauge demand for the remainder of the holiday season. Using Bayesian framework we decouple effects of price elasticity and demand signaling and study their implications for inventory and pricing decisions.
