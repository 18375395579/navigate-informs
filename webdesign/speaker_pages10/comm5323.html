Title: The Effect of Special Orders on Manufacturer-retailer Interactions

Author: Diwakar Gupta, Professor, University of Minnesota, 111 Church Street SE, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States, guptad@me.umn.edu

Coauthor(s): Hao-Wei Chen, Haresh Gurnani

Year: 2007

Abstract: Many retailers offer to special order out-of-stock items at no additional cost to the customers assuming that special orders increase demand, reduce stock outs, and thereby increasing profits. We study the relative benefit of special ordering to the retailer under single and two-price price mechanisms with single and dual sourcing.
