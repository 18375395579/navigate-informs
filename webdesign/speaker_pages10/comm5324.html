Title: A Firm’s Supply Chain Agility for Risk Management: Cultural Antecedents

Author: Mike Braunscheidel, Assistant Professor, The College at Brockport, The State University of New York, 350 New Campus Drive, Hartwell Hall, Brockport, NY, 14420-2914, United States of America, mjbraunscheidel@verizon.net

Coauthor(s): Nallan C. Suresh

Year: 2008

Abstract: Agility has been touted as being a necessary component for risk management and mitigation in supply chains. Additionally, organizational culture is important for the adoption of organizational practices such as agility. In this paper, the effect of organizational culture, as measured by the competing values framework, upon four constructs important to agility is considered.
