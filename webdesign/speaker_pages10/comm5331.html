Title: Concealing Information in the Presence of Common Suppliers

Author: Milind Sohoni, Indian School of Business, Gachibowli, Hyderabad, 500032, India, milind_sohoni@isb.edu

Coauthor(s): Aditya Jain

Year: 2012

Abstract: A privately informed first mover placing order for supply with a common supplier faces the risk of information leakage to competing firms. Thus, first mover firm may wish to actively work with supplier to conceal information. We examine the incentives and strategic effects of such information concealment in a supply chain. We show that in general concealing information help the first mover, but may backfire hand hurt in certain cases.
