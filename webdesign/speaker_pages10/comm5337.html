Title: Integrating Long-term and Short-term Contracting in Fed-cattle Supply Chains

Author: Onur Boyabatli, Assistant Professor, Singapore Management University, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore, onur.boyabatli@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Paul Kleindorfer, Stephen Koontz

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper analyzes the optimal contracting portfolio choice of a meatpacker in the fed-cattle supply chain. The meatpacker can source cattle from options-like marketing agreements and forward contracts in advance, and from the spot market on the day. We incorporate several particularities of fed-cattle supply chains (e.g., quality and cost differences between different types of contracted cattle and substitutability between beef end products).
