Title: Mitigating Supply Risk Under Regulatory Trade Barriers

Author: Yimin Wang, UNC Chapel Hill, McColl Building, CB3490, Chapel Hill, NC, 27599, United States of America, yimin_wang@unc.edu

Coauthor(s): Brian Tomlin, Wendell Gilland

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study a firm's procurement problem when its suppliers face regulatory trade barriers. In particular, we investigate four potential supply risk mitigation strategies when its suppliers are subject to quota restrictions. We fully characterize the firm’s optimal policy under all four strategies. In addition, we provide managerial insights regarding the relative attractiveness of each strategy.
