Title: Managing Inventory for Entrepreneurial Firms with Trade Credits and Payment Defaults

Author: Wei Luo, IESE Business School, Avenida Pearson, 21, Barcelona, Spain, wluo@iese.edu

Coauthor(s): Kevin Shang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a credit-constrained firm that orders inventory to satisfy stochastic demand in a finite horizon. The firm provides trade credit to customers and receive it from the supplier. The work sheds light on the management of cash conversion cycle and demonstrates how liquidity provision can mitigate the bullwhip effect.
