Title: Impact of Replenishment Policies on the Value of Demand Information in a Serial Supply Chain

Author: Kevin Shang, Fuqua School of Busines, Duke University, Durham, NC, United States, khshang@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Sean Zhou

Year: 2007

Abstract: A supply chain includes material and information flows. Only when these two flows are managed simultaneously, the value of information can be maximized. This paper addresses this issue by investigating how the value of demand information is affected by inventory replenishment practices, i.e., responsive and periodic replenishments.
