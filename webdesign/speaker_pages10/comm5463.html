Title: Process-based Contractual Mechanisms for Collaborative Development Initiatives

Author: Nektarios Oraiopoulos, University Lecturer in Operations Management, Judge Business School, Cambridge University, Trumptington St., Cambridge, United Kingdom, n.oraiopoulos@jbs.cam.ac.uk

Coauthor(s): Vishal Agrawal

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we study initiatives for co-development of new products and technologies. In such settings, it may be difficult a priori to specify contracts contingent on the outcome. Therefore, we investigate the efficacy of different process-based contracts, which instead specify the decision-making process.
