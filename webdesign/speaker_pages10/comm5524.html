Title: Remanufacturing in Decentralized Supply Chains with Uncertain

Author: Huafan Ma, UW-Milwaukee, 1315 N Cass St. APT305, Milwaukee, WI, 53202, United States of America, mahuafan@uwm.edu

Coauthor(s): Samar Mukhopadhyay

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a decentralized supply chain with one OEM and one retailer that trade with either a push or pull contract. The OEM can process both used and new parts for the production of new products while the yield of the used parts is random. The OEM makes procurement and production decisions and the retailer makes ordering decisions based on their own cost/incentive structures. We develop different models to determine their optimal policies.
