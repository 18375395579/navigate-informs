Title: Public Policy Application of the Joint Price / Quantity Newsvendor Model: The Influenza Vaccine

Author: Gal Raz, Assistant Professor, Australian Graduate School of Management (AGSM), Australian Graduate School of Management, UNSW, Sydney, NS, Australia, galr@agsm.edu.au

Year: 2006

Abstract: The supplying of a produced good is a game that includes three players, the firm that is supplying the good, the consumers that are buying it and the government that can intervene in the transaction by imposing rules and regulations. We analyze a joint price/ quantity newsvendor model for the Influenza Vaccine market, taking a social planner perspective by maximizing the total social welfare and examine the possible government actions to support this outcome.
