Title: Global Sourcing: Impact of Sourcing Strategies on Supply Chain Resilience

Author: Nitish Jain, INSEAD, Singapore, 138676, Singapore, nitish.jain@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Karan Girotra, Serguei Netessine

Year: 2013

Abstract: Global Sourcing firms expose themselves to varied kind of disruptions such as extreme weather events, labor unrest and natural disasters. Firm supply chains that are resilient to such disruptions have an advantage. Using a novel data set of over 3.5 million import transactions by U.S. retailers, wholesalers, and manufacturers, we study role of sourcing strategies in fostering supply chain resilience.
