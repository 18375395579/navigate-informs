Title: Assortment Competition and Supply Chain Responsiveness

Author: Victor Martínez de Albéniz, Associate Professor, IESE Business School, Av. Pearson 21, Barcelona, 08034, Spain, Valbeniz@iese.edu

Coauthor(s): Gurhan Kok

Year: 2013

Abstract: In industries where customer needs quickly change, retailers can postpone their assortment decisions (amount of variety, balance across categories) if they are quick enough. We study here how assortment competition depends on the postponement capabilities of retailers.
