Title: A Two-period Newsvendor Problem with Production Cost Learning

Author: He Xiuli, Visiting Assistant Professor, School of Management, University of Texas at Dallas, Richardson, 75083, United States of America, xiulihe@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Suresh Sethi

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a supply chain with a manufacturer and a retailer who faces demand uncertainty. We adopt a price-dependent newsvendor model which takes the multiplicative form. We assume that the manufacturer can reduce the per unit product cost in the second period as a result of accumulated production experience in economy of scale. The retailer decides a retail price and an order quantity prior to facing random demand in each period. We consider two cases: a myopic and a far-sighted retailer.
