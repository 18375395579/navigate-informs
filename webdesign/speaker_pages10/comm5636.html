Title: Optimal Planning of a Reverse-Logistics Supply Chain for a Consumer Electronics Retailer

Author: Andre Calmon, Massachusetts Institue of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, E62-364, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, acalmon@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Stephen C. Graves

Year: 2013

Abstract: We describe, model, and optimize the reverse logistics that support the warranty system used by a Fortune 100 company that sells consumer electronics. Since this company uses refurbished devices to satisfy warranty claims, their reverse logistics operation is also a closed-loop supply chain. Within this setting, our main contributions are: (i) Policies for managing inventory of refurbished devices in this system; (ii) An algorithm for assigning refurbished items in inventory to customers.
