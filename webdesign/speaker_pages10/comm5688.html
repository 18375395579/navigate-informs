Title: Empirical Results on Drivers of Retail Sales and Customer Satisfaction

Author: Jayanth Krishnan, The Wharton School, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States, jayanth@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Serguei Netessine, Marshall Fisher, Nicole deHoratius

Year: 2006

Abstract: We discuss results of analysis conducted with a retailer using data from 500 stores over 29 months in which we identify factors that explain 80% of the variation in sales and 94% of the variation in customer satisfaction. We to use these models to identify changes estimated to increase sales by 5-10%.
