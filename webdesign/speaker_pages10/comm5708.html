Title: Market Share Modeling in Revenue Management

Author: Ahmet Kuyumcu, President, Prorize LLC, 3399 Peachtree Rd NE, STE 400, Atlanta, GA, 30326, United States of America, akuyumcu@prorize.com

Coauthor(s): Utku Yildirim

Year: 2012

Abstract: Market share modeling could be effectively used when demand and price data for majority of the competing products is available. This presentation briefly reviews market share modeling and provides successful real-world applications.
