Title: Optimal Pricing and Product Development Policies under Consumer Memory

Author: Hasan Arslan, Suffolk University, Boston, MA, 02108, United States of America, harslan@suffolk.edu

Coauthor(s): Soulaymane Kachani, Kyrylo Shmatov

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider pricing and product development policies for successive product generations, when demand is impacted by the reference price and reference innovation level formed by consumers. We characterize the pricing and product development policies explicitly for two successive generations, and propose a general quasi-analytic solution. We also extend the analysis to a competitive environment. We derive Nash equilibria among firms adopting different pricing and product development policies.
