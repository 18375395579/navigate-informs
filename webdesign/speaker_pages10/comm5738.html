Title: Governance for Growth: Real Options Perspectives on Supplier Transactions

Author: Nile Hatch, Marriott School - BYU, Provo, UT, 84602, United States of America, nile@byu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: In buyer-supplier partnerships, firms focus on collaboration to create joint value rather than taking value from the other firm. Real options analysis of the growth potential of the relationship between a buyer and supplier indicates the appropriate governance structure for the relationship. Traditional outsourcing emphasizes the value of the option to switch suppliers; buyer-supplier partnerships emphasize the value of the option to grow capabilities.
