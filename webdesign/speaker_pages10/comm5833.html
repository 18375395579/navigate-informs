Title: Entry Deterrence Strategies Facing Capacitated Competition

Author: Huaqing Wang, University of Miami, Miami, FL, United States of America, h.wang12@umiami.edu

Coauthor(s): Haresh Gurnani, Murat Erkoc

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study a model in which a monopolistic incumbent faces entry of a capacitated entrant. As function of the entrant’s capacity, the incumbent can adopt different strategies to deter or accommodate the entrant. We show that when entry is inevitable, the capacitated entrant (incumbent) can get higher profit in Stackelberg competition as follower (leader) than in Nash Competition. We also consider a case in which the incumbent invests in changing consumers?product preference.
