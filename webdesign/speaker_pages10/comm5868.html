Title: An Analysis of Supply Chain Using Vendor-managed Inventory in Emerging Markets

Author: SugJe Sohn, Senior Supply Chain Modeling Engineer, Intel Corporation, 6505 W Chandler Blvd MS-CH11-111, Chandler, AZ, 85226, United States, sugje.sohn@intel.com

Coauthor(s): Matt McMillan

Year: 2007

Abstract: The characteristics of emerging markets include typically the greater demand, lower sales price, higher demand uncertainty and larger transportation variability. The research analyzes the vendor-managed inventory (VMI) when implemented in the supply chain of emerging markets. An analytic comparison is performed between the conventional retailer and VMI supply chain models in developed market and emerging market.
