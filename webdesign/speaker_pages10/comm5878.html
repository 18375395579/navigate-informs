Title: Revenue and Response Time Management for Queuing Systems with Partial Demand Information

Author: Philipp Afeche, Assistant Professor, University of Toronto, 105 St.George Street, Toronto, ON, M5S3E6, Canada, Afeche@rotman.utoronto.ca

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider the design of optimal price-response time menus for service providers facing heterogeneous time-sensitive customers in the absence of complete demand information. We investigate how the distribution of and information about demand attributes impact the structure of the optimal menu.
