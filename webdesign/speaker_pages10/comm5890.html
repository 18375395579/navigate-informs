Title: Managing Revenue Risks for Industry Leaders across Automotive, High-tech and Agriculture

Author: Heiko Pieper, Principal, End-to-End Analytics, 505 Gilbert Avenue, Menlo Park, CA, 95054, United States of America, heiko@e2eanalytics.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Companies are facing significant risks to the top and bottom line with exploding raw material costs and increased competition and price pressure. The ability to effectively manage demand, cost and price risk is becoming a competitive advantage across industries. End-to-End analytics specializes in helping clients to quantify and manage these risk using OR techniques and tools in practice. We will report about our work with some of the largest companies in Automotive, High-Tech and Agriculture.
