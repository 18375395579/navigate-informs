Title: Outsourcing Games

Author: Gad Allon, Assistant Professor, Kellogg School of Management, 2001 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, g-allon@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Eyal Beigman

Year: 2007

Abstract: In many industries firms consider the option of outsourcing the service provision or the production of the good they bring to the market. In most markets, these firms may choose among multiple competing suppliers, according to the terms of the contracts offered and the quality of the product. We consider an industry with several competing retailers and several competing suppliers, and study the equilibria that arise in the multistage game.
