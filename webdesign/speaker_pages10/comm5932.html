Title: Push or Pull? Auctioning Supply Contracts

Author: Cuihong Li, Assistant Professor, University of Connecticut, 2100 Hillside Road, Unit 1041, Storrs, CT, 06269, United States, Cuihong.Li@business.uconn.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Scheller-Wolf

Year: 2006

Abstract: For a buyer that faces uncertain demand and purchases from competing suppliers, both the price and inventory risk allocation are important considerations. Based on a news vendor model, we study the auction mechanism in which suppliers bid a single price, with or without a quantity committed by the buyer. That is, the buyer auctions a push or pull contract. We chracterize the environments in which a push or pull contract auction is preferred.
