Title: The Potential of Servicing as a Green Business Model

Author: Vishal Agrawal, Assistant Professor, Georgetown University, 37th & O Streets, 200 Hariri Building, Washington, DC, 20057, United States of America, va64@georgetown.edu

Coauthor(s): Ioannis Bellos

Year: 2013

Abstract: In recent years, manufacturers in various industries have begun to orient their practices towards selling the use of the product as opposed to selling the product itself. We investigate the economic and environmental implications of the manufacturer’s design and pricing decisions under different forms of servicizing business models.
