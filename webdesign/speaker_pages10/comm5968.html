Title: Optimal Procurement Auction with Sequential Supplier Qualification

Author: Wei Chen, PhD Student, Jindal School of Management, The University of Texas at Dallas, 800 W. Campbell Road, Richardson, TX, 75080, United States of America, wei.chen@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Ganesh Janakiraman, Milind Dawande

Year: 2012

Abstract: A firm is soliciting price bids for a sourcing contract from N suppliers. The contract can only be awarded to a supplier who passes two rounds of qualification/testing, which can be performed sequentially or simultaneously. We study a two period optimal mechanism design problem in which some suppliers take the first test in the first period and some among the successful take the second test in the second period, and some of the untested suppliers take the integrated test in the second period.
