Title: A Capacitated Commodity Trading Model with Market Power

Author: Victor Martinez de Albeniz, Assistant Professor, IESE Business School, Av. Pearson 21, Barcelona, 08034, Spain, valbeniz@iese.edu

Coauthor(s): Josep Maria Vendrell Sim?

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider the operational problem of a trader between two markets of the same commodity, each one of which quotes a different price. We are interested in finding the optimal trading policy across the markets so as to obtain the maximum profit in the long-term, assuming that the trading volumes influence the price processes. We assume a finite trading capacity (fixed inventory level) and proportional costs. We determine the optimal trading strategy and the optimal capacity to be installed.
