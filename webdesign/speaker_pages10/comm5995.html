Title: Trade Credit in Supply Chains: Multiple Creditors and Priority Rules

Author: Song Alex Yang, Assistant Professor, London Business School, Sussex Place, London, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, sayang@london.edu

Coauthor(s): John Birge

Year: 2012

Abstract: Priority rules determine the order of repayment when the debtor cannot repay all of his debt. In this paper, we study how different priority rules influence trade credit usage and supply chain efficiency when multiple creditors are present.
