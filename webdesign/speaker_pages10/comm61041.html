Title: Display Advertising with an Ad Exchange and Prediction Uncertainty

Author: Eric Sodomka, Brown University, 115 Waterman Street, Providence, RI, 02912, United States of America, sodomka@cs.brown.edu

Coauthor(s): Max Lin, Vahab Mirrokni

Year: 2013

Abstract: Publishers in display advertising face difficult challenges in both prediction and optimization. A publisher must predict what users will arrive to its site and how bidders on an ad exchange will respond to different reserve prices. The publisher must then decide, as each impression arrives, how to set reserve prices and which ads to show to maximize its long-term expected profit. We propose and evaluate algorithms for acting in this domain with uncertainty in predictions.
