Title: Quality Testing for Online Programs

Author: Jaby Mohammed, Assistant Professor, Morehead State University, 215 A, Lloyd Cassity Building, Morehead State University, Morehead, KY, 40351, United States of America, j.mohammed@moreheadstate.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Online program enrollments have been growing substantially faster than overall higher education enrollments. Academic leaders do expect that these enrollments would continue their exponential growth. The biggest concern in an online program is how efficiently and effectively the online programs are run. There is need to evaluate how the online programs are delivered. Quality evaluation is an approach to ensure the online courses are met the standards.
