Title: Robust Revenue Management: Booking Control with Limited Demand Information

Author: Itir Karaesmen, Assistant Professor of Management Science, University of Maryland, Robert H. Smith School of Business, Decision and Information Technologies, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, ikaraes@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Yingjie Lan, Huina Gao, Michael Ball

Year: 2006

Abstract: We analyze the single resource (leg) revenue management problem when no or limited demand information is available. We devise both static and dynamic policies via competitive analysis of online algorithms. The policies come with performance guarantees. We further test the average performance of these policies are using extensive computational experiments.
