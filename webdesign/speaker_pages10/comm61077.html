Title: Dynamic Targeted Pricing

Author: Dan Zhang, University of Colorado at Boulder, 995 Regent Dr, Boulder, CO, 80305, United States of America, Dan.Zhang@Colorado.EDU

Coauthor(s): Chuan He

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider dynamic targeted pricing problem for a monopolist selling to two customer segments. Implications of targeting accuracy and customers' intertemporal purchasing behavior are discussed. We also compare the efficiency of dynamic pricing with that of targeting.
