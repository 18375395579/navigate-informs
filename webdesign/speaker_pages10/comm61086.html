Title: Repeat Purchase Decisions in Technology Adoption

Author: Canan Ulu, University of Texas at Austin, IROM, B6500, 1 University Station, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, Canan.Ulu@mccombs.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): James Smith

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper, we study when consumers should replace or “upgrade?a technology. The tradeoff is between using a profitable technology now and waiting for better technologies that are to come in the future. We model this problem as a dynamic programming model and study structural properties of the value function and optimal policies.
