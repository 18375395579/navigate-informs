Title: Setting Quality and Speed in Service Industry with Repeated Customers

Author: Azin Farzan, The University of Washington, The Michael G. Foster School of Business, The University of Washington, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States of America, afarzan@uw.edu

Coauthor(s): Yong-Pin Zhou

Year: 2013

Abstract: We analyze quality, capacity, and pricing decisions for a system in which customers purchase service repeatedly. We consider different aspects of quality, capacity and pricing decisions on customer behavior and provide a general model that provides insights on the optimal levels of these decisions and their relationships.
