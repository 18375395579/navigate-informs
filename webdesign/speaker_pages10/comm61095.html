Title: Overpayment in Set-system Auctions

Author: Edith Elkind, eelkind@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Kenneth Steiglitz, Paul Goldberg, Amit Sahai, Leslie Goldberg

Year: 2008

Abstract: In set-system auctions, there is a task than can be completed by several overlapping teams of agents, and the center's goal is to hire one of these teams and pay as little as possible. Examples of this setting include shortest path auctions, minimum spanning tree auctions, and vertex cover auctions. In this talk, we discuss several approaches to reducing the total payment of the center in this setting, and the associated computational challenges.
