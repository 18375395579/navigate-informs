Title: Holiday Retailing with Gift Cards

Author: Alice Lu, University of Pennsylvania, Univesity of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, xingwei@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Xuanming Su

Year: 2013

Abstract: Using gift cards, holiday gift givers can avoid offering an unwanted gift, but recipients incur the hassle of redeeming the gift card and may not find what they want. We study the profitability of gift cards and explore novel gift card policies.
