Title: What's the Buzz About: Analyzing Social Media Response to TV Ads

Author: Shawndra Hill, Assistant Professor, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, 3730 Walnut Street, Suite 500, Philadelphia, PA, 19103, United States of America, shawndra@wharton.upenn.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the relationship between Ad attributes and online Buzz. We link the attributes to buzz levels, sentiment and specific content.
