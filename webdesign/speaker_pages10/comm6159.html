Title: Abandonment and Pricing in Queueing Systems

Author: Philipp Afeche, University of Toronto, 105 St. George Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 3E6, Canada, philipp.afeche@rotman.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Vahid Sarhangian

Year: 2013

Abstract: Order cancellations can be observed in practice, but the literature on congestion pricing appears to ignore the possibility of customer abandonment. We study a queueing model in which customers may abandon the system and explore pricing implications.
