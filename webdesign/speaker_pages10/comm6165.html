Title: The Bidding Problem in One-shot Combinatorial Auctions: A Look from Decision Analysis

Author: Natalia Santamaria, Smeal College of Business, Penn State, 471B Business Building, Penn State, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, nsantam@psu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This presentation explores the problem a bidder faces to determine his bids in an one-shot sealed bid combinatorial auctions of two objects. Bidders in the auction can have superadditive or subadditive preferences for the bundle. The tool we use to model the problem is decision analysis.
