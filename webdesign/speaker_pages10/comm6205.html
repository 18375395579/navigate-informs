Title: Dynamic Pricing with Strategic Customers: The Role of Customer Heterogeneity

Author: Xuanming Su, Assistant Professor, University of California, Haas School of Business, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, xuanming@haas.berkeley.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a firm who prices and rations a finite inventory of products over a finite time horizon, and consumers who strategically time their purchases under rational expectations of future prices and availability. The optimal policy depends critically on customer heterogeneity along two dimensions: their willingness to pay, and their willingness to wait.
