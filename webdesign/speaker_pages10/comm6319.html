Title: Differential Game Models in Marketing Science

Author: Gary Erickson, Professor, University of Washington Business School, Box 353200, Seattle, WA, 98195-3200, United States of America, erick@u.washington.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: Markets are dynamic and involve oligopolistic competition, so the fit between differential games and marketing science is a natural one. This tutorial presents an overview of differential games, and discusses examples of differential game models used in marketing science, focusing on models involving advertising. The challenge of applying differential games empirically is also discussed, and example applications are presented.
