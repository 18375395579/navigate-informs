Title: The Newsvendor Model with Customer Search Costs

Author: Xuanming Su, University of California, Haas School of Business, Berkeley, 94720, United States, xuanming@haas.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Fuqiang Zhang

Year: 2007

Abstract: Customers may have to pay a search or travel cost in order to visit the store. In a newsvendor setting, the possibility of stockouts discourages patronage. We use the rational expectations paradigm to study optimal pricing and inventory decisions.
