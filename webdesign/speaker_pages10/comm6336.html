Title: Optimal Fencing in Airline Industry with Demand Leakage

Author: Mihaela Turiac, Qatar University - College of Business & Economics, P.O.Box 2713, Office B123, Doha, Qatar, michelle.turiac@qu.edu.qa

Coauthor(s): Syed Asif Raza

Year: 2013

Abstract: This research introduces the fencing mechanism in an integrated framework for fair pricing and seat inventory control decisions of an airline company. An integrated framework of fencing, fair pricing and seat inventory control optimization is proposed to investigate how an airline can retain gains under demand leakage and what would be the effects of fencing investments decisions.
