Title: Dynamic Models with Two Competitors

Author: Darius Walczak, PROS Revenue Managment, 3100 Main Street, Suite 900, Houston, TX, 77382, United States of America, dwalczak@prosrm.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present a review of dynamic programming-based methods for a problem with two expected-revenue maximizers competing in a single market. We contrast a game theory-based model with other alternatives. The former model is closely related to Dudey (1992, 2006), Talluri (2003), and Isler (2007). We further investigate some of its properties.
