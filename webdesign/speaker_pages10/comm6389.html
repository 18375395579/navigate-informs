Title: Revenue Optimization and the Generalized Second-price Auction

Author: David Thompson, PhD Student, University of British Columbia, 191-2366 Main Mall, Vancouver, BC, V6T 1Z4, Canada, davidrthompson@gmail.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Search engines generate billions of dollars in advertising revenue using the generalized second-price auction (GSP). They have tried to further increase revenue using GSP variants including different kinds of reserve prices, and quality-score squashing. Our contributions are a comparison of different reserve price regimes (with and without squashing) and a study of how equilibrium selection affects the revenue generated by these variants.
