Title: An Experimental Study of Penny Auctions

Author: Zhiling Guo, Associate Professor, Singapore Management University, School of Information Systems, Singapore, Singapore, zhilingguo@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Ben Liu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Penny auction is a special form of business-to-consumer online auction that turns shopping into a competitive bidding game. Based on controlled lab experiments, this study evaluates how budget, risk attitude, and strategic thinking of consumers would affect their bidding strategies. We found evidences that penny auction is a profitable selling mechanism only under certain conditions. We further identify a number of factors that impact auctioneer revenue and bidders' performance in the auction.
