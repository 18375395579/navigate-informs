Title: Dynamic Pricing without Priors

Author: Ying Liu, New York University, 44 west 4th street, New York, United States of America, yliu2@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Rene Caldentey, Ilan Lobel

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a setting where a firm is selling a limited inventory of items over time to strategic customers, but it does not have a prior on the customers' distributions of values. We construct policies that minimize the maximum regret the firm could face and present structural properties of such pricing policies.
