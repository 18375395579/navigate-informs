Title: The Operational Advantage of Threshold Discounting Offers in the presence of Strategic Customers

Author: Simone Marinesi, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, Simone.MARINESI@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Karan Girotra, Serguei Netessine

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the operational advantage of Threshold Discounting Offers—i.e. the practice to offer a discounted service conditional on enough customers subscribing to the offer—as employed by Groupon and its competitors, in the presence of strategic customers. We find that Threshold Discounting increases operational performance and profit compared to more traditional approaches, even in the absence of economies of scale. We also find that strategic customers are beneficial to the firm.
