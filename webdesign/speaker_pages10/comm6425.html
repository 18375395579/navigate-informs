Title: The Revenue Driven Newsvendor Under Information Asymmetries

Author: Adam Zhu, Assistant Professor of Operations Management, Singapore Management University, Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore, adamzhu@smu.edu.sg

Coauthor(s): Zhengping Wu

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider the impact of advertisement revenue on the newsvendor's inventory decision. The newsvendor also has to appropriately decide how much to charge advertisers without knowing their marginal benefit from the advertisement. We discuss managerial insights obtained from our model.
