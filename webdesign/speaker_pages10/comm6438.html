Title: Dynamic Pricing with Behavioral Considerations

Author: Javad Nasiry, INSEAD, Blvd de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77300, France, javad.nasiry@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Ioana Popescu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We model a dynamic pricing problem where consumers purchase decisions are anchored on multiple reference prices, reflecting past prices, fairness considerations and future price expectations. We characterize the firm's optimal pricing strategies under various models of consumer memory and behavior.
