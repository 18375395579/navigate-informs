Title: Discriminatory Pricing Schemes in Ascending Auctions with Anonymous Bidders

Author: Nicole Immorlica, Microsoft Research, One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA, 98052, United States, nickle@microsoft.com

Coauthor(s): Mohammad Mahdian, K. Talwar, A. Karlin

Year: 2006

Abstract: An auctioneer is selling an unlimited supply of goods to bidders with unit demand through ascending auctions and would like to maximize his profit. We show that discriminatory pricing can not significantly increase the revenue of the seller when bidders are ex-ante identical even if their values are interdependent.
