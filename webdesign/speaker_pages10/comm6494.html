Title: Leveraging Quality Information in Stock-outs for Products with Common Value Uncertainty

Author: Laurens Debo, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, laurens.debo@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): Garrett Van Ryzin

Year: 2012

Abstract: When some less-informed consumers are uncertain about a product's quality, stock-outs may convey information about the propensity of other, better-informed consumer to purchase. We explore how a firm and the less-informed consumers can exploit such information during a short sales season.
