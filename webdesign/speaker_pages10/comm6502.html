Title: The Groupon Effect on Yelp Ratings: A Root Cause Analysis

Author: Giorgos Zervas, Boston University, 595 Commonwealth Avenue, Boston, MA, 02215, United States of America, zg@cs.yale.edu

Coauthor(s): Michael Mitzenmacher, John Byers

Year: 2013

Abstract: Daily deals sites such as Groupon offer deeply discounted goods and services to millions of customers through geographically targeted daily email marketing campaigns. In our prior work we observed that a negative side effect for merchants selling Groupons is that their Yelp ratings decline significantly. In this work, we rigorously consider and evaluate various hypotheses about underlying consumer and merchant behavior in order to understand this phenomenon, which we dub the Groupon effect.
