Title: A Monopoly's Location and Spatial Pricing Strategies

Author: Binbin Liu, PhD Candidate, Rotman School of Management, University of Toronto, 105 St. George Street, Toronto, ON, M5S 3E6, Canada, binbin.liu02@rotman.utoronto.ca

Coauthor(s): Dmitry Krass, Oded Berman

Year: 2006

Abstract: A multi-store monopoly's simultaneous pricing and location decisions on networks are studied. Three pricing policies, discriminatory, uniform and mill pricing are considered. We establish nodal optimality of the location decision and find optimal locations and prices under mild conditions. We provide managerial insights regarding when a firm should use discriminatory pricing versus mill or uniform pricing.
