Title: Price and Service Competition with Transshipments

Author: Maqbool Dada, Professor, Krannert School of Management, Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, dada@mgmt.purdue.edu

Coauthor(s): Kwan Wee

Year: 2006

Abstract: Two newsvendors agree to pool inventory via transhipments at the end of the season but compete for customers on price and service during the season. The potential equilibria are fully characterized. Moreover conditions under which there is exactly one equilibrium are identified.
