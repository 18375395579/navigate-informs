Title: Operational Advantage and Design Recommendations of Threshold Discounting Offers

Author: Simone Marinesi, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, Simone.MARINESI@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Karan Girotra, Serguei Netessine

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the use of Threshold Discounting offers, commonly employed by Groupon and other players in the online discounted deals industry, when a service provider faces strategic customers. We find that these offers improve operational performance compared to traditional approaches, and we provide guidelines on how to correctly design them.
