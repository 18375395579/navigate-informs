Title: Behavioral Decision Making and Inventory Models

Author: Xuanming Su, Assistant Professor, University of California, Haas School of Business, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, xuanming@haas.berkeley.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Recent experimental findings have highlighted several anomalies in inventory models: decision makers do not behave as normative theory predicts. We propose a descriptive framework of decision biases and investigate its implications in traditional inventory models.
