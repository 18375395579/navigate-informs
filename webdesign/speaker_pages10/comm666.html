Title: Dynamic Pricing with Uncertainty in Market Response

Author: Vivek Farias, Stanford University, vivekf@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Benjamin Van Roy

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a single resource revenue management model with uncertainty in demand statistics. We present a simple heuristic that addresses the tradeoff between `exploratory' pricing that attempts to learn quickly and pricing that attempts to hedge against the possibility of a highly popular product. In addition to promising computational results we present a uniform performance guarantee.
