Title: Optimal VCG Redistribution Mechanisms

Author: Vincent Conitzer, Assistant Professor, Duke University, LSRC, Box 90129, Duke University, Durham, NC, 27708, United States, conitzer@cs.duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Mingyu Guo

Year: 2007

Abstract: It is impossible for an auction mechanism to simultaneously satisfy efficiency, strategy-proofness, individual rationality, and (strong) budget balance. Nevertheless, it is possible to satisfy the first three and come close to budget balance, using redistribution mechanisms. I will cover worst-case optimal, undominated, and optimal-in-expectation redistribution mechanisms.
