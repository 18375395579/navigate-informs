Title: Event-based Sentiment Analysis for Stock Market Prediction using Social Media

Author: Masoud Makrehchi, Associate Professor, University of Ontario, Institute of Technology, 2000 Simcoe Street North, Oshawa, ON, L1H7K4, Canada, Masoud.Makrehchi@uoit.ca

Year: 2013

Abstract: We extract significant stock movements and collect appropriate pre, post and contemporaneous text from Twitter. Then the respective label for each tweet is assigned. We aggregate the net sentiment per each day and show that it holds significant predictive power for subsequent stock market movement.
