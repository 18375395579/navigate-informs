Title: Luxury Yacht Rentals ?How to Forecast Demand and Optimize Prices?

Author: Pelin Pekgun, Assistant Professor, University of South Carolina, Moore School of Business, 1705 College Street, Columbia, SC, 29208, United States of America, Pelin.Pekgun@moore.sc.edu

Coauthor(s): Ronald Menich

Year: 2012

Abstract: Luxury yacht rentals is a niche market with a unique business model that presents rich revenue opportunities but also makes it vulnerable in a challenging economy. Traditional forecasting approaches do not work due to data sparsity and low volume of transactions, while pricing becomes difficult with hard-to-identify price elasticity from customers that already pay high prices. We present a forecasting and optimization framework that can address the unique challenges in this environment.
