Title: Enhancing Customer Service Support: A Text Mining and Machine Scheduling Model

Author: Yilu Zhou, George Washington University, 2201 G St. NW, Suite 515, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, yzhou@gwu.edu

Coauthor(s): Moshe Dror

Year: 2008

Abstract: To achieve high customer satisfaction, information requests from a customer need to be delivered to the right expert efficiently. We research the problem of finding the right expert for processing customer request in large organizations. Our framework integrates text mining and machine scheduling techniques. The text mining module estimates the processing time for each job request on each customer-contact employee. It serves as a front end of optimizing scheduling of customer request.
