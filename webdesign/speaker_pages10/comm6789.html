Title: Cost Comparison between Infrastructure Cloud Services

Author: Huan Liu, Accenture Technology Labs, 50 W San Fernando St., San Jose, CA, 95113, United States of America, huan.liu@accenture.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: Infrastructure cloud service providers, such as Amazon EC2, differ widely from each other in terms of their price structure, which makes it very difficult to perform an apple-to-apple comparison. In this paper, we describe a methodology to dissect the price structure into its individual components, including CPU, memory, and hard disk capacity. We then use the price components to compare offerings from a few dominate cloud providers.
