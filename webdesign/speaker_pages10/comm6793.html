Title: Dynamic Pricing and Revenue Management

Author: Maarten Oosten, SAS Institute, World Headquarters, SAS Campus Drive, Cary, NC, 27513, United States of America, maarten.oosten@sas.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: New technologies allow for pricies to be changed continuously, and optimal dynamic pricing algorithms take this freedom explicitly into account. In this presentation we explore several directions in which this can be taken.
