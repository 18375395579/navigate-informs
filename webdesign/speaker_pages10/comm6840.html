Title: Product Assortment under Choice Behavior

Author: Juan Jose Miranda Bront, University of Buenos Aires, Pabellón I - Ciudad Universitaria, Capital Federal, BA, C1428EGA, Argentina, jmiranda@dc.uba.ar

Coauthor(s): Isabel Méndez-Díaz, Gustavo Vulcano, Paula Zabala

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a revenue management problem with fixed prices and a general model of market segmentation. Consumers within each segment choose according to a multinomial logit model. The seller has to decide the optimal set of products to offer in order to maximize the instantaneous revenue rate. We formulate the problem as a MIP, and develop a branch-and-cut to solve it effectively. Our numerical experiments provide evidence of the potential of our approach.
