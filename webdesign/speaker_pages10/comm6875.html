Title: Price and Capacity Competition

Author: Asu Ozdaglar, Professor, EECS/MIT, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, United States, asuman@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Kostas Bimpikis, Daron Acemoglu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the efficiency of oligopoly equilibria in a model where firms compete over capacities and prices. We establish the existence of pure strategy subgame perfect equilibria and characterize the set of equilibria. We then investigate the efficiency properties of these equilibria, where efficiency is defined as the ratio of surplus in equilibrium relative to the first best.
