Title: Joint Bidding and Pricing Models

Author: Karti Puranam, Assistant Professor, LaSalle University, 1900 Olney Avenue, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, kartys.here@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Michael Katehakis

Year: 2012

Abstract: We present a class of models where a firm procures items by participating in auctions and it decides at what price to sell them. We discuss conditions for the existence of stationary optimal policies of simple structure for the present value and the average-reward optimal policies.
