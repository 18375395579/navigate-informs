Title: The Effect of Product Class on the Usage of Buy-now Price in Online Auctions

Author: Chun Qiu, Assistant Professor, McGill University, Desautels Faculty of Management, Montreal, QC, Canada, chun.qiu@mcgill.ca

Coauthor(s): Peter Popkowski-Leszcyc, Kursad Asdemir

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study the moderating effects of product class and ease of assessing the value of an item on the effectiveness of buy-now prices in online auctions. Buy-now prices may serve different purposes, which vary depending on the product class. In particular, buy-now prices may serve as external reference prices for high-end high uncertain products. On the other hand buy-now options may serve to accelerate sales for low-end products. We provide empirical results to support our conjectures.
