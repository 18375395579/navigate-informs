Title: Dynamic Pricing using Scenario Based Optimization

Author: Ruben Lobel, rlobel@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Georgia Perakis

Year: 2008

Abstract: We discuss a dynamic pricing problem under fixed capacities. Demand is a function of the price with uncertain parameters. We develop a scenario based optimization model to find a closed-loop pricing strategy that maximizes revenue robustly, without making distributional assumptions on the uncertainty. We provide a bound on the number of scenario samples that achieve a desired confidence level for this solution. We also present numerical results to support our findings.
