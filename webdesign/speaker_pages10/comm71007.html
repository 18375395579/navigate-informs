Title: Analysis of Serial Inventory Systems with Batch Ordering and Nested Replenishment Schedule

Author: Kevin Shang, Fuqua School of Business, Duke University, Durham, NC, United States, khshang@duke.edu

Coauthor(s): Sean Zhou

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider an N-stage serial inventory system where material is ordered in batches and according to a nested schedule. There are two types of fixed cost considered in this system: setup cost and order cost. The former is associated with each batch ordered; the latter is incurred for each replenishment cycle. We study two models that consider each of these fixed costs. For both models, we construct a lower bound to the optimal cost, an optimization algorithm, and an effective heuristic.
