Title: Minimizing Total Weighted Completion Time with Unexpected Machine Unavailability

Author: Yumei Huo, Associate Professor, CUNY at Staten Island, Staten Island, NY, 10314, United States of America, yumei.huo@csi.cuny.edu

Coauthor(s): Hairong Zhao, Boris Reznichenko

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the problem of minimizing total weighted completion time on a single machine without any knowledge about the unavailable interval due to breakdown or emergent jobs. For both breakdown model and emergent job model, we show the lowerbounds for any online algorithms, the tight competitive ratios of LPT algorithm, and the tight approximation ratios of First Fit LPT for offline case. Finally, our experimental results show that in practice, both LPT and FF- LPT perform very well.
