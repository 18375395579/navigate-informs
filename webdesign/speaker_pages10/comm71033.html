Title: Network Creation Games

Author: MohammadTaghi Hajiaghayi, Senior Researcher, AT&T Labs- Research, Rm: C225, 180 Park Ave., Florham Park, NJ, 08904, United States of America, hajiagha@mit.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study Nash equilibria in the setting of network creation games introduced recently by Fabrikant, Luthra, Maneva, Papadimitriou and Shenker. In this game we have a set of selfish node players, each creating some incident links, and the goal is to minimize x times the cost of the created links plus sum of the distances to all other players. Fabrikant et al. proved an upper bound O(sqrt{n}) on the price of anarchy. In this talk, we prove the first o(n^\epsilon) upper bound for general x.
