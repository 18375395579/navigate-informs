Title: The Speed Quality Dilemma in Large Call Centers with Heterogeneous Servers

Author: Amy Ward, University of Southern California, Marshall School of Business, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-0809, United States of America, amyward@marshall.usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Dongyuan Zhan

Year: 2012

Abstract: We consider a call center with heterogeneous agents differentiated by their service speed and quality, where quality is measured by the percentage of callers who call back after service. There is a natural trade-off between minimizing wait time and minimizing call-backs when routing calls. We propose a threshold routing policy based on the diffusion control problem that arises in the Halfin-Whitt many-server limit regime. Simulation results show our proposed policy outperforms other policies.
