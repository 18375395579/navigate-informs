Title: Buffer and Server Allocation in General Queueing Networks

Author: Tom van Woensel, Eindhoven University of Technology, PO BOX 513, Eindhoven, NL, Netherlands, t.v.woensel@tm.tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Ricky Andriansyah, Frederico Cruz

Year: 2007

Abstract: Optimization of manufacturing systems is the focus of a number of studies. Of interest here is the optimization of the number of buffers and servers, since both of them represent a significant amount of investment in a company. The joint buffer-server optimization is a nonlinear, multiple-objective optimization problem with integer decision variables. Numerical results are presented.
