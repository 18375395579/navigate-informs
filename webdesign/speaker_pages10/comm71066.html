Title: Inventory Control in Divergent Supply Chains with Shipment Consolidation

Author: Johan Marklund, Lund University, P.O. Box 118, Lund, SE, SE-22100, Sweden, johan.marklund@iml.lth.se

Year: 2007

Abstract: Balancing inventory and delivery costs is an important issue in many supply chains. This talk focuses on methods for joint evaluation of inventory replenishment and shipment consolidation effects in one-warehouse multiple-retailer systems. The results are applicable for both single-item and multi-item inventory systems with non-identical retailers.
