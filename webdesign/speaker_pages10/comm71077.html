Title: Dynamic Decision Making Problems When There are Different Levels of Model Uncertainty

Author: Andrew Lim, Associate Professor, IEOR Department, 4141 Etcheverry Hall, University of California, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, lim@ieor.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): George Shantikumar, Peng Li

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider intensity control problems with different levels of uncertainty for different random factors. We establish a new duality result within this setting which shows how tradeoffs should be made in decision making when there are different uncertainty levels between different factors.
