Title: Continuous Mapping Approach to Many-server Heavy Traffic Limit Theorems

Author: Jim Dai, Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive NW, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, dai@isye.gatech.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: We show that the continuous mapping theorem, and sometimes together with a state space collpase result, is sufficient to prove existing many-server heavy traffic limit theorems in the Halfin-Whitt regime. The approach applies to parallel-server systems with exponential or phase type distributions. The customer patience time distribution is assumed to be exponential.
