Title: Reducing Customer Waiting Time in Baggage Handling Systems (BHS)

Author: Hongsuk Yang, Seoul National University, 1 Gwanak-ro Gwanak-gu, Seoul, 151-916, Korea, Republic of, hongsuk@snu.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Changhee Kim

Year: 2013

Abstract: The purpose of this study is to find an operational rule to minimize customer waiting time in Baggage Handling Systems (BHS) of airports. We consider three sorting rules and analyze waiting time of customers: traditional FIFO sorting, 2 Group (Business class and Economy class) sorting and Line sorting (each line of airplane seat). We suggest an effective sorting rule for Baggage Handling Systems.
