Title: Optimal Flow-line Conditions with Worker Variability

Author: Frank Bentefouet, Research Assistant, Pennsylvania State University, 1231 South Allen Street, State College, PA, 16801, United States of America, fub3@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): David Nembhard

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this paper, we propose a framework to study the workforce scheduling problem in an assembly line also called flow line. We investigate the impact of within-worker and between workers variability and discuss the selection of a scheduling policy between fixed and work sharing system. We characterize the optimal switching time between workers in a system with two tasks and establish the maximum number of changes between assignments when considering a general floor shop.
