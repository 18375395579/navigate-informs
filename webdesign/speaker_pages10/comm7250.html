Title: Estimating Customer Service Times using GIS Information

Author: Shantih Spanton, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, sspanton@ufl.edu

Coauthor(s): Jagadish Jampani, Joseph Geunes

Year: 2012

Abstract: Currently customer service times are reported manually by crews, leading to potential data entry errors. This project automates the estimation of service times by utilizing onboard GIS data and the customer service sequence. The close proximity of sequentially serviced customers and lack of a precise work location for large industrial customers adds complexity. The derived service times can then be used to optimize local train work load balancing.
