Title: Inventory Management with Advance Demand Information and Flexible Delivery

Author: Tong Wang, Ph.D Candidate, Insead, Decision Sciences Area, Fontainebleau Cedex, 77305, France, tong.wang@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Beril Toktay

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider an inventory model with advance demand information and flexible delivery. We develop a lower-bound approximation based on an allocation assumption, and propose protection level heuristics that yield upper bounds on the optimal cost. Numerical analysis quantifies the benefit of delivery flexibility (14% on average) and the optimality gaps of the heuristics (2% on average).
