Title: A Lagrangian Approach to Single-machine, Two-agent Scheduling Problems

Author: Alessandro Agnetis, Professor, Universita di Siena, Dip. Di Ingegneria Dell'Informazione, Via Roma 56, Siena, 53100, Italy, agnetis@dii.unisi.it

Year: 2007

Abstract: Two agents must schedule their jobs on a single machine. Each agent has an objective function depending on the schedule of his/her jobs only. We present theoretical and computational results for the (NP-hard) problem of determining Nash and Kalai-Smorodinsky solutions of the underlying bargaining problem.
