Title: Centralization vs. Competition in Subcontracting Operations

Author: George Vairaktarakis, Case Western Reserve University, Weatherhead School of Managament, Department of Operations, Cleveland, OH, 44106, United States of America, gxv5@case.edu

Coauthor(s): Tolga Aydinliyim

Year: 2008

Abstract: A group of manufacturers subcontract part of their workload to a third-party, each with the objective of completing his total workload the soonest possible. We develop centralized solutions and compare them to Nash Equilibria and FCFS practices. Also, we propose incentive schemes to promote cooperation.
