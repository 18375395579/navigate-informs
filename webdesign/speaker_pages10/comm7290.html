Title: A Service-based Comparison for Stochastic Clearing Systems

Author: Sila Cetinkaya, Texas A&M University, Austin, TX, United States of America, sila@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Bo Wei, Daren B.H. Cline

Year: 2013

Abstract: Considering a Poisson demand process, we develop a unified method for calculating average order delay (AOD) under different clearing policies using the view of martingales. Next, we introduce some refined properties of truncated Poisson random variables and offer an analytical comparison of AOD expressions for a fixed clearing frequency, e.g., expected service cycle length. The problem has direct applications in freight consolidation among other stochastic clearing systems.
