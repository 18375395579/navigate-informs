Title: Integrated Queuing and Gaming Analsis of Stochastic Assembly Systems

Author: Ananth Krishnamurthy, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, CII 5015, 110 8th Street, Troy, NY, United States, krisha@rpi.edu

Coauthor(s): Jong-Shi Pang, Ram Ramakrishnan, Jinye Zhao

Year: 2007

Abstract: This paper presents a stochastic, decentralized assembly system consisting of two suppliers and one assembler, who decide on investments on capacities and inventories subject to a common throughput constraint. Queuing approximations for the relevant performance measures are integrated into the players' optimization problems, resulting in a generalized Nash equilibrium problem. Existence of a solution is established and numerical results are presented.
