Title: A Real-time Routing Policy for Call Centers

Author: Auke Pot, Assistent Professor, Vrije Universiteit, Amsterdam, Netherlands, pot@ccmath.com

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a multi-skill call centers, having different job types and several agent groups. A routing policy is presented that can handle unexpected fluctuations in the arrival intensities. We show that the policy yields the best achievable service levels under certain circumstances.
