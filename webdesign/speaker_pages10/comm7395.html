Title: Bayesian Analysis of Markov Modulated Queues

Author: Refik Soyer, Professor of Decision Sciences & Statistics, The George Washington University, 2201 G Street, NW, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, soyer@gwu.edu

Coauthor(s): Suleyman Ozekici, Joshua Landon

Year: 2013

Abstract: We consider a Markovian queueing model where both customer arrival process and the service process are modulated by an external environmental process. We present the Bayesian analysis of the stochastic system. We illustrate the implementation of our model and the Bayesian approach by using queueing data.
