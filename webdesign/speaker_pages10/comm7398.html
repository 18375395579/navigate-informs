Title: Cross-training of Team Members to Deal with Demand Mix Variation and Absenteeism

Author: Jordi Olivella, Assistant Professor, Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya, Esteve Terradas, 7, Castelldefels, 08860, Spain, jorge.olivella@upc.edu

Coauthor(s): David Nembhard

Year: 2012

Abstract: The objectives of this work are: (1) To develop a method to determine the cross-training goals of a work team in order to deal with certain levels of variation in the demand mix and absenteeism; and (2) Evaluating alternative policies of cross-training of team members. The demand mix variation is defined by establishing a maximum time to devote to each product. To solve numerically the mathematical problem generated a selection constraint based procedure is developed.
