Title: Production Control with Stochastic Processing Times and Random Demand

Author: Vladimir Krasik, Stern School of Business, New York University, 44 West 4th Street, New York, NY, United States, vkrasik@stern.nyu.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Lanker, Michael Pinedo

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this paper we consider the dynamic single class production control problem of a single make-to-stock production facility with random production times and demand. For the three cases of underlying demand and production time processes ?deterministic, exponential and Brownian we compute total average cost, obtain optimal policies and make comparisons.
