Title: An Infinite-server Queue in a Semi-Markov Environment

Author: Brian Fralix, EURANDOM, Den Dolech 2, Eindhoven, NB, Netherlands, fralix@eurandom.tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Ivo Adan

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider an infinite-server queue, where customers with general service times arrive in a Poisson fashion, with an arrival and a service rate depending on a semi-Markovian environment. Our contributions include (1) providing a procedure to compute all moments of the queue length for Erlang or hyper-exponential service times, and (2) a continuity result that gives rigorous approximations of all moments of the queue-length for general service times.
