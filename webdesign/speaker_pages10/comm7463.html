Title: Staffing Algorithms for Multi-skill Call Centers

Author: Wyean Chan, Ph.D Student, Universit?de Montréal, C.P. 6128, Succ. Centre-ville, Montréal, H3C 3J7, Canada, chanwyea@iro.umontreal.ca

Coauthor(s): Pierre L'Ecuyer, Athanassios Avramidis

Year: 2007

Abstract: We compare the performance of different staffing optimization algorithms for various multi-skill call center scenarios. We also look at other optimization aspect such as skillset construction and routing policies, and how to optimize them jointly with staffing.
