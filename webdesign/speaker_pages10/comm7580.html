Title: Distributed Resource Allocation for Fork and Join

Author: Cathy Xia, Associate Professor, The Ohio State University, Dept. of Industrial & System Engineering, Columbus, OH, 43210, United States of America, cathyhxia@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Chuck Zhao, Zhen Liu, Don Towsley

Year: 2008

Abstract: Many emerging information processing applications require applying various fork and join type operations to data streams in real-time. Effective in-network processing and resource management in a distributed heterogeneous environment is critical to achieve better scalability and quality of service provision. We present novel distributed resource allocation algorithms that achieve the global optimality in a generic fork and join network setting.
