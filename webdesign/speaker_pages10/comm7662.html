Title: Intra-Day Updating of Call Center Agent Schedules Based on Variable Arrival Rates

Author: Vijay Mehrotra, Assistant Professor, San Francisco State University, Department of Decision Sciences, COB, 1600 Holloway Avenue, San Francisco, CA, 94132, United States, drvijay@sfsu.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: In this paper, we define a framework for updating call center agent schedules on an intra-day basis, a practice that is carried out (usually in an unstructured way) in 75% of call centers. The key contribtion of this paper is accounting for arrival rate variability in both the initial schedule creation and in schedule updating.
