Title: A Few New Results About (s,S) and (s,S,p) Policies

Author: Youyi Feng, Associate Professor, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, R609 M.M.W Engineering Building, Sys. Engg. & Engg. Mgmt., Hong Kong - ROC, yyfeng@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Year: 2007

Abstract: The author reports a few new developments in the (s,S) and (s,S,p) policies. These results focus on the characterization of suitable demand models and the structure of holding-shortage cost function. They provide new proofs for the discount and average optimality of these policies in the finite and infinite horizon settings.
