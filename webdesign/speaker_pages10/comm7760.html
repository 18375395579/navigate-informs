Title: Supply Chain Distribution Logistics to Minimize Inventory Holding Cost with Dominant Upstream Partner

Author: U.V Manoj, The University of Texas at Dallas, Richardson, TX, United States, muv031000@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Chelliah Sriskandarajah, Sushil Gupta

Year: 2006

Abstract: We consider a two stage supply chain with a dominant upstream partner. Finding an optimal schedule to minimize inventory holding cost for the downstream member is NP-Hard. Under this situation, we provide efficient Genetic Algorithm using optimized crossover technique to arrive at near optimal solutions.
