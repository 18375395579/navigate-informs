Title: The Perishable (Q,r) Model with Fixed Shelf-lives and Lead Times: Renewal Demands

Author: Emre Berk, Bilkent University, Department of Management, Bilkent, Ankara, Turkey, eberk@bilkent.edu.tr

Coauthor(s): Ulku Gurler

Year: 2008

Abstract: We consider a perishable inventory system with a single product having a constant shelflife. External demands are generated by a compound renewal process with inter-arrival times having a generalized Erlang distribution. Lead times are constant and excess demand is lost. We employ the (Q,r) control policy where r > mQ for m >1. We develop the operating characteristics of the model, establish its certain analytical properties, and present some numerical results.
