Title: Scheduling Despite Inexact Job Size Information

Author: Adam Wierman, Assistant Professor, California Institute of Technology, 1200 E. California Blvd, Pasadena, CA, 91125, United States of America, adamw@caltech.edu

Coauthor(s): Misja Nuyens

Year: 2008

Abstract: Motivated by the optimality of Shortest Remaining Processing Time (SRPT) for mean response time, many computer systems have begun to favor small jobs. However, rarely do computer systems have knowledge of exact remaining sizes. In this paper, we prove new results characterizing the impact of inexact job size information on the performance of SRPT and related policies.
