Title: George-Harrison II: Dynamic Control of a Queue with Admission Control and Adjustable Service Rate

Author: John Hasenbein, Associate Professor, University of Texas at Austin, 1 University Station C2200, Austin, TX, 78712, United States, jhas@mail.utexas.edu

Coauthor(s): A. Kranthi Mitra

Year: 2007

Abstract: We resolve several open questions posed in a 2001 OR paper by George and Harrison. That paper investigated dyanamic control of a queue with general holding costs, adjustable service rates, and a cost of effort function. We relax assumptions on the cost of effort function, allowing admission control, and we prove that stationary policies are optimal in this system. It is also shown that the elegant G-H algorithm can be extended.
