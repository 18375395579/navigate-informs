Title: Worst-case Analysis of the Quality of Neighborhoods for Minimizing Makespan on Identical Machines

Author: Johann Hurink, Professor, University of Twente, Department of Applied Mathematics, PO Box 217, Enschede, 7500 AE, Netherlands, j.l.hurink@utwente.nl

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this talk we consider local search methods for a multiprocessor scheduling problem where jobs have to be scheduled on identical parallel machines such that the latest completion of jobs (the makespan) is minimized. We give worst-case performance guarantees for local optima with respect to several large scale neighborhood structures for the considered problem.
