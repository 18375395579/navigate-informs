Title: Behavioral Newsvendor Decisions: What Have We Learned and What is Left to Do?

Author: Gerard Cachon, Professor, University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, cachon@wharton.upenn.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Ask somebody to make a newsvendor problem decision and they are likely to order a quantity that is closer to expected demand than optimal. This pull to the center effect has been the focus of numerous studies. This talk discusses what we know about this effect and what seems to be missing.
