Title: Pharmaceutical A-Frame Order Fulfillment Optimization and Throughput Models

Author: Jen Pazour, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering, Fayetteville, AR, United States of America, jpazour@uark.edu

Coauthor(s): Russell Meller

Year: 2008

Abstract: Pharmaceutical distribution is characterized by high-value products requiring precision accuracy within short lead times. These requirements mean that highly-automated technologies like A-Frame systems are common in pharmaceutical order fulfillment processes. The main decisions to be addressed when designing an A-Frame are the assignment and allocation of items to the A-Frame as well as throughput considerations. We address these decisions using optimization and throughput models.
