Title: Optimal Policy for a Periodic Review Inventory System with Expediting

Author: Chiwon Kim, PhD Student, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 262 Belmont Street #2, Watertown, MA, 02472, United States, chiwon@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): David Simchi-Levi, Diego Klabjan

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study the single item periodic review inventory model with deterministic lead time and optional expediting. Under a certain expediting cost structure, we prove optimality of serialized base stock policy. The serialized base stock policy is simple, reasonable, and easily implementable. On the other hand, if the expediting cost does not have this structure, it is shown that the optimal decisions are functions of system state variables, and the corresponding optimal policy is no longer simple.
