Title: Learning Quality from Queues: A Laboratory Experiment

Author: Laurens Debo, University of Chicago, Chicago, IL, United States of America, laurens.debo@chicagobooth.edu

Coauthor(s): Mirko Kremer

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this talk, I will report the results of a laboratory experiments in which human subjects need to decide whether to join the queue or not, when the quality of the service is unknown to the subject, but known to a small fraction of the population.
