Title: Optimal Switching Time of Entertainment Shows

Author: Srini Krishnamoorthy, Assistant Professor, Ivey School of Business, Western University, London, ON, N6B3R6, Canada, skrishnamoorthy@ivey.uwo.ca

Coauthor(s): Kyle Maclean

Year: 2013

Abstract: We model a decision problem faced by the management of a theatre that exhibits entertainment shows like movies or plays. The management has to decide every week whether they should continue with the current show or replace it with a new one. We formulate the problem as a stochastic dynamic program and outline the optimal policy that maximizes expected revenue.
