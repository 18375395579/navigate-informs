Title: Joint Optimization Models for Assemble-to-order System Replenishment and Allocation

Author: Kai Huang, Assistant Professor in Operations Management, Technology University of Eindhoven, Pav. E.01, P.O.Box 513, Department of Technology Management, Eindhoven, 5600MB, Netherlands, k.huang@tue.nl

Coauthor(s): Ton de Kok

Year: 2007

Abstract: We consider a periodic-review Assemble-To-Order system assuming base stock replenishment policy. We establish stochastic programming models for the classical criteria of minimizing expected overall holding cost and backlogging cost. Numerical experiment results are compared with previous results under criteria of maximizing overall reward within a time window. We also propose a series of non-FCFS allocation rules.
