Title: Polymatroid Optimization, Submodularity, and Joint Replenishment Games

Author: Simai He, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Department of Systems Engineering and En, Hong Kong, HK, China, smhe@se.cuhk.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Shuzhong Zhang, Jiawei Zhang

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this paper we show that the optimal value maximizing a separable concave function over a polymatroid is submodular in its parameters. We discuss the application of this result in the joint replenishment games studied by Anily and Haviv (2007) and Zhang (2007).
