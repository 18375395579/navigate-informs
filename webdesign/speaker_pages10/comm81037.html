Title: Matching Supply with Demand in an Outpatient Clinic

Author: Diwas KC, Emory University, 1300 Clifton Road, Atlanta, GA, 30322, United States of America, Diwas_Kc@bus.emory.edu

Coauthor(s): Nikolay Osadchiy

Year: 2012

Abstract: We examine patient scheduling at an outpatient clinic, where a significant number of scheduled appointments end up as no-shows. In this research, we explore factors that lead to the mismatch between demand and supply, and propose potential solutions to help the clinic manage its capacity utilization.
