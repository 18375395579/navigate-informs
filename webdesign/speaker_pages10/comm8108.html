Title: Multi-criteria Location-allocation Modeling for the Primary Care System Network in Ontario

Author: Vedat Verter, Prof, Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke West, Montreal, H3A 1G5, Canada, vedat.verter@mcgill.ca

Coauthor(s): Michael Carter, Anna Graber

Year: 2013

Abstract: The primary care scheme of Ontario, Canada, involves five models of care currently being employed simultaneously. We present a multi-criteria model to plan the distribution and mix of primary care models, while incorporating accessibility, operating cost and quality of service.
