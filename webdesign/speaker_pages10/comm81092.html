Title: Using Magnetic Resonance Imaging Data to Predict and Locate Prostate Cancer

Author: David Anderson, Robert H.Smith School of Business, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, davidryberganderson@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Bruce Golden, Edward Wasil, Hao Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a method of diagnosing prostate cancer using magnetic resonance imaging data. Logistic regression and nearest neighbor classification are combined to identify the risk of cancer. Our method performs well with 79% predictive accuracy and an area under the ROC curve of 0.85.
