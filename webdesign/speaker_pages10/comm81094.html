Title: Developing a Decision Aid for Laypeople Choosing Whether to Share Their Medication Information

Author: Jenna Marquard, Assistant Professor, University of Massachusetts-Amherst, 219 Engineering Lab I, 160 Governor's Drive, Amherst, MA, 01002, United States of America, jlmarquard@ecs.umass.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Current policy discussions and technical developments suggest laypeople will need to make complex choices regarding whether to electronically share their health information through tools such as personal health records (PHRs). We created a balanced scorecard-based analysis to aid laypeople in weighing the potential clinical care benefits and information disclosure concerns related to sharing information about each of their medications.
