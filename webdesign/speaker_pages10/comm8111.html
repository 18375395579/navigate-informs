Title: A Spreadsheet Patient Scheduling Model for a Student Health Center

Author: John Kros, Assistant Professor of Decision Science, East Carolina University, 3121 Bate, Greenville, NC, 27858, United States, krosj@ecu.edu

Coauthor(s): Scott Dellana, Dave West

Year: 2006

Abstract: This work studies the procedures that determine how patient appointments are scheduled at a large university Student Health Center and introduces the concept of overbooking to alleviate the detrimental effects of no show appointments. A spreadsheet simulation model is developed and employed to suggest an efficient overbooking procedure. This spreadsheet model augments the rules that determine when appointments can be made and how those appointments are set.
