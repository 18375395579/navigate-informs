Title: Sequential Filtering of Glaucoma Progression

Author: Jonathan Helm, Assistant Professor, Indiana University, Kelley School of Business, ODT, 1309 East Tenth Street, Bloomington, IN, 47405, United States of America, jhelm@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Mariel Lavieri, Mark Van Oyen, Joshua Stein, MD

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop novel filtering algorithms for monitoring the disease progression of glaucoma patients. The models are validated using patient data from two multi-center clinical trials.
