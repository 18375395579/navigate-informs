Title: A Bayesian Network for Predicting Invasive and In-situ Breast Cancer using Mammographic Findings

Author: Jagpreet Chhatwal, Health Economist, Merck Research Laboratories, North Wales, PA, 19454, United States of America, jchhatwal@wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): Nassif Houssam, Edward Sickles, Oguzhan Alagoz, Elizabeth Burnside

Year: 2008

Abstract: We developed a Bayesian Network (BN) to predict the risk of invasive and in-situ breast cancer based on patient’s prospectively collected mammographic findings and demographic factors. The structure and parameters of the BN were trained and tested on a large clinical dataset. Understanding the risk of invasive and in-situ cancer prior to biopsy can improve the clinical management of breast cancer patients.
