Title: Multi-resource Allocation Scheduling of Elective and Emergency Surgery in Dynamic Environments

Author: Van-Anh Truong, Columbia University, vt2196@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Tim Huh, Nan Liu

Year: 2012

Abstract: We formulate the first model of allocation scheduling for emergency and elective surgeries in a dynamic environment, where distributional information about demand and resource availability is continually updated and scheduling decisions exploit new information as it arises. We allow multiple resources to be considered, and patients to renege.
