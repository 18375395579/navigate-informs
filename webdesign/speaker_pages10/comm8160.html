Title: Using Analytics at The Dow Chemical Company

Author: Joseph Czyzyk, Advanced Analytics Math Modeler, The Dow Chemical Company, 2040 Dow Center, Midland, MI, 48674, JJCzyzyk@dow.com

Year: 2012

Abstract: We will present the growth of the Advanced Analytics group within Dow, the model for serving the various internal businesses, the analytics process, areas of application (enhanced sales forecasts, early insight for business units, and quick response to deteriorating economic conditions) and a project example showing how the use of simulation and historical data can aid Human Resources in planning for the talent needs of the company.
