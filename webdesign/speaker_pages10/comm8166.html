Title: Maximizing the Value of Healthcare Output Through Learning

Author: Renato de Matta, Associate Professor, University of Iowa, 108 PBAB, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, renato-dematta@uiowa.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: We develop a model to find the number and type of patients to assign physicians that will maximize the value of healthcare output per input expended in treatment through learning. We apply our model to plan the workload of LASIK eye surgeons. Computational results are presented.
