Title: Improving Healthcare Delivery to Traveling Patients in the Veterans Health Administration

Author: Erkan Ceyhan, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, eceyhan@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Shahed Al-Haque

Year: 2012

Abstract: Performed in collaboration with the VHA, this research investigates the improvement of healthcare delivery to traveling patients. After analysis of the current state, the study aims to provide operations management strategies, through simulation of potential policy interventions, that enable more effective and efficient resource allocation to meet traveling patients?needs.
