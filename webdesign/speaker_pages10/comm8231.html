Title: Defining Service Quality in an Outpatient Clinic with Complex Constituency

Author: Swati Verma, University of Soth Florida, sverma@mail.usf.edu

Coauthor(s): Kingsley Reeves

Year: 2007

Abstract: It presents foundational work paving the way for further research in assessing the usefulness of RFID in an ambulatory healthcare setting to capture real-time data and promote continuous improvement.Factor analysis is used to determine if distinct patient classes exist that perceive service quality in a distinctive manner.The objective is to use the results of this work along with real-time data collection via RFID to permit “service quality maximization?
