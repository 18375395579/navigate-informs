Title: Privacy Protection of Microdata with Individual Identifiers

Author: Steven Thompson, Assistant Professor, University of Richmond, 1 Gateway Drive, Richmond, VA, 23173, United States, sthomps3@richmond.edu

Coauthor(s): Robert Garfinkel, Ram Gopal

Year: 2006

Abstract: The ability to disseminate individually identifiable microdata is important in areas such as health care and national security to ensure public health and safety. We develop a technique that enables the release of individually identifiable microdata in a manner that maximizes the utility of the released data while providing protection of confidential data. Computational results show that the method is practical and that useful data can be released even when the level of risk in the data is high.
