Title: Nursing Home Quality Assessment Scorecard

Author: Jenya Antonova, University of Wisconsin-Madison, 1175 WARF Building, 610 Walnut Street, Madison, WI, 53726, United States, Jenya_Antonova@chsra.wisc.edu

Coauthor(s): David Zimmerman

Year: 2006

Abstract: Quality of care scorecards are increasingly used as part of an overall Balanced Scorecard approach to nursing home performance. The concept of a multi-dimensional nursing home Quality of Care Scorecard is introduced. Illustrative data from several nursing home networks are presented.
