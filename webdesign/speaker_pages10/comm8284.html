Title: The Impact of Private Providers and Cross-border Policy on Public Healthcare Services

Author: Dimitrios Andritsos, HEC Paris, 1 Rue de la Liberation, Paris, France, andritsos@hec.fr

Coauthor(s): Christopher Tang

Year: 2012

Abstract: We examine the impact of competition from private hospitals and a recent cross-border healthcare policy on public healthcare systems in the EU. We find that in border regions, where the cost of crossing the border is low, “outsourcing?the high-cost country's elective care services to the low-cost country is a viable strategy from which both countries' systems can benefit.
