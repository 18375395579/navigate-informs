Title: Dynamic Periodic Scheduling of Home Health Practitioners

Author: Ashlea Bennett, Georgia Institute of Technology, Georgia Tech - ISyE, 765 Ferst Dr NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332-0205, United States of America, ashlea@gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Alan Erera

Year: 2008

Abstract: We study the challenge of increasing resource utilization in the home care industry by improving the scheduling of home visits by nurses. The specific resultant problems are highly constrained dynamic periodic routing problems. Solution approaches are presented and the sensitivity of productivity objectives to the enforcement of various patient service constraints is analyzed.
