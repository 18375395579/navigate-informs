Title: Analysis of Optimal Investment in HIV Prevention Programs

Author: Margaret Brandeau, Professor, Stanford University, 407 Terman Engineering Center, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States of America, brandeau@stanford.edu

Coauthor(s): Greg Zaric

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present a mathematical/economic framework to address the following question: Given a particular population, a specific HIV prevention program, and a fixed amount of funds that could be invested in the program, how much money should be invested? We illustrate our framework with analysis of several different prevention programs in different populations.
