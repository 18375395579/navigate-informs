Title: On the Access-Throughput Spiral Effects in Large Academic Centers

Author: Cecilia Zenteno, Postdoctoral Fellow, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, E62-389, Cambrdige, MA, 02139, United States of America, ceciliaz@mit.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Dunn, Ulrich Schmidt, Retsef Levi, Tim Carnes, Benjamin Christensen, Bethany Daily

Year: 2013

Abstract: We demonstrate empirically and statistically the surprisingly adverse impact of patient flow disruptions from ICU to regular bed floors in large academic hospitals. We call this the Access-Throughput Spiral Affect.
