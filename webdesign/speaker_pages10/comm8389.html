Title: e-Visits in Primary Care

Author: Hessam Bavafa, Ph.D. Candidate, The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, bavafa@wharton.upenn.edu

Coauthor(s): Christian Terwiesch

Year: 2013

Abstract: The technology for secure messaging between provider and patients, e-visits, has been utilized for several years and many view it as a potential solution for improving quality of health services while reducing costs. This project will be the first to disentangle the impact of e-visits on patient health outcomes and physician workload by using a credible empirical strategy.
