Title: Sequential Monitoring of Glaucoma Patients

Author: Mariel Lavieri, Assistant Professor, University of Michigan, 1205 Beal Ave, 2783 IOE, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, lavieri@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Gregg Schell, Mark Van Oyen, Joshua Stein, MD, David Musch, Jonathan Helm

Year: 2012

Abstract: We develop novel algorithms to determine the best Time to Next Test based on each patient's individual disease trajectory. Our algorithms integrate a dynamic linear Gaussian systems model of disease progression with optimization approaches to predict the likelihood of glaucoma progression. We will present our closed form solutions and discuss the structural properties of the algorithms. Our work has been validated using patient data from a large–scale glaucoma clinical trial.
