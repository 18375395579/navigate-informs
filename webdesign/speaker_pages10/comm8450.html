Title: EMS Team Planning: A Prediction and Evaluation Tool for Ambulance Services

Author: Martin van Buuren, Scientific Programmer, Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI), Science Park 123, Amsterdam, 1098XG, Netherlands, M.van.Buuren@cwi.nl

Coauthor(s): Rob van der Mei

Year: 2012

Abstract: In a time of increasing cost reduction emergency medical service (EMS) providers are pressured to work more cost efficiently. In the meantime EMS providers aim for better medical service. EMS team planning (EMS-TP) enables EMS service providers to monitor their past performance, and moreover enables providers to predict call volumes and the number of ambulances needed at each base location for half an hour time intervals. EMS-TP is currently being deployed by a large Dutch EMS provider.
