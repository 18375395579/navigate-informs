Title: Partnership and Contracting between Regional Medical Centers and Rural Hospitals via Telemedicine

Author: Balaraman Rajan, University of Rochester, Rochester, NY, United States of America, balaraman.rajan@Simon.Rochester.edu

Coauthor(s): Abraham Seidmann, Rajeev Ramachandran

Year: 2013

Abstract: We analytically investigate a partnership model between hospitals with the help of telemedicine. Our results explain how telemedicine may not always result in increased competition amongst hospitals serving the same general region but can also aid in forming a mutually beneficial partnership resulting in improved economic and clinical outcomes. While the regional hospitals gain by retaining their patients, medical centers win by being able to see patients who require their level of expertise.
