Title: Does the Size of the ARM Matter?

Author: Marc McDill, Associate Professor, Pennsylvania State University, School of Forest Resources, University Park, PA, 16802, United States, mem14@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sandor Toth, Sandor Toth

Year: 2007

Abstract: We explore the question of how factors like problem size, formulation type, characteristics of the forest, and side constraints affect solution times for spatially explicit area-restricted (ARM) forest planning problems. Conclusions are based on a synthesis of the literature and an experiment using both hypothetical and real forest planning problems.
