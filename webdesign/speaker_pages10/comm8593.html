Title: Expert Judgment Based Risk Ranking in Port Security

Author: Jason Merrick, Virginia Commonwealth University, PO Box 843083, 1001 West Main Street, Richmond, 23284, United States of America, jrmerric@vcu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Which incoming vessels should be boarded and inspected? Which containers should be scanned for nuclear or biological threats? These are critical questions in the area of port security. We propose and expert judgment technique involving pairwise comparisons of vessels or containers that leads to relative rankings of risk for use in inspection or screening decisions.
