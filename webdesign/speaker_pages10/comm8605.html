Title: Applications of Data Mining in Healthcare Research

Author: Samuel Hohmann, Senior Research Specialist, University Health System Consortium, 2001 Spring Road, Suite 7000, Oak Brook, IL, 60523, United States of America, hohmann@uhc.edu

Coauthor(s): Shital Shah

Year: 2007

Abstract: UHC is a rich repository of administrative, clinical, and operations data for academic medical centers. Data mining opportunities are investigated to address financial and operational performance including cost of service, length of stay, diseases patterns, etc. An application using above data is presented to illustrate the endless possibilities of data mining in healthcare research.
