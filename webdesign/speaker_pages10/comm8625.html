Title: Measuring Nursing Home Performance Using Multiple Objective Data Envelopment Analysis

Author: Ronald Klimberg, Associate Professor, Saint Joseph's University, Haub School of Business, Decision and System Sciences, Philadelphia, PA, 19131, United States, klimberg@mailhost.sju.edu

Coauthor(s): Daniel Shimshak, Melanie Lenard

Year: 2006

Abstract: Decision-makers must not only concentrate on increasing efficiency by increasing productivity, lowering costs, but also other objectives such as quality. By paying attention to the multiobjective nature of overall performance allows decision-makers to address various tradeoffs between these objectives. The results of applying several DEA models to measure the operational efficiency and quality of a data set of nursing homes will be presented.
