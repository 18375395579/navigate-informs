Title: Self-organizing Transportation Systems

Author: Donald Eisenstein, University of Chicago, Graduate School of Business, 5807 South Woodlawn Avenue, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States, don.eisenstein@ChicagoGSB.edu

Coauthor(s): Peter Francis, John Bartholdi

Year: 2007

Abstract: Transportation systems such as elevators suffer from what is commonly known as bunching, resulting in long and variable wait times. We explore a new, somewhat counter-intuitive, way to reduce bunching. Our approach is simple, yet very effective.
