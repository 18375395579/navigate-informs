Title: Pricing Strategies for Combination Pediatric Vaccines Based on the Lowest Overall Cost Formulary

Author: Banafsheh Behzad, PhD Candidate, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 117 Transportation Building, 104 S. Mathews Ave., Urbana, IL, 61801, United States of America, behzad1@illinois.edu

Coauthor(s): Sheldon Jacobson, Edward Sewell

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper analyzes pricing strategies for pediatric combination vaccines by comparing the lowest overall cost formularies for a fixed cost of injection. Three pharmaceutical companies compete over the sale of pediatric vaccines. The main contribution of the paper is to provide the lowest overall cost formularies for a fixed cost of an injection for different prices of Pentacel. The resulting analysis shows that Pentacel could have been more competitively priced compared to the Pediarix.
