Title: Location Based Healthcare Workflows

Author: Jonathan Emanuele, Product Manager, Siemens Medical Solutions, 51 Valley Stream Pkwy, Malvern, PA, 19355, United States, jonathan.emanuele@siemens.com

Coauthor(s): Kevin O'Rourke

Year: 2007

Abstract: Healthcare information systems today are designed to increase patient safety and operation efficiency, while at the same time decreasing costs. At the forefront are workflow driven healthcare information systems that track the state of a patient and push work items to the hospital staff. A missing piece in these systems has been real time location information. This session will look at how an integrated RFID solution can fill this gap.
