Title: A Real-time Decision Support System for Pandemic Response

Author: Sunderesh Heragu, Professor, University of Louisville, 580 S Preston St, Louisville, KY, 40202, United States of America, s.heragu@louisville.edu

Coauthor(s): Mehdi Zakerifar

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this talk, we present a real-time decision support system (RTDSS) that uses real-time date for pandemic response. The RTDSS includes several modules including those for location of strategic national stockpile (SNS) and their routing to points of dispense during a natural or manmade disaster, real-time allocation of emergency medical service (EMS), fire and police vehicles to patients in need, SNS distribution planning, mass vaccination simulation, emergency department (ED) planning, dispatch of mental health professionals to first responders, module to allocate patients to hospitals and others.
