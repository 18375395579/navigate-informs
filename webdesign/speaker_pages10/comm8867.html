Title: Process Management, Patient Outcomes and Hospital Efficencies: A Model Joint Replacement Program

Author: Mary Ziemba-Davis, Research Director, St. Vincent Center for Joint Replacement, 2001 W. 86th Street - Entrance #8, Indianapolis, IN, 46260, United States of America, mxziemba@stvincent.org

Coauthor(s): Lisa Brandt, Jeffery Pierson

Year: 2007

Abstract: Our success relies on preoperative patient assessment, education, and team-based planning; streamlined patient registration; dedicated aftercare planning; postoperative team rounding; standardized medical protocols; evidence-based procedures; board-certified surgeons; and empirical research to streamline processes, reduce cost and resource use and enhance patient care. We show impact on patient satisfaction; length of stay; complications; case delays, volume and duration; and process variances.
