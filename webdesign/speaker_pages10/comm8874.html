Title: Incentives in Healthcare: Predicting the Impact of Penalties for Hospital Re-admissions

Author: Song-Hee Kim, PhD Candidate, Columbia University, New York, NY, United States of America, sk3116@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Ann Bartel, Carri Chan

Year: 2013

Abstract: Under the Affordable Care Act, hospitals with higher than expected 30-day readmissions for Heart Failure, Acute Myocardial Infarction, and Pneumonia will be financially penalized. Using retrospective data on patients enrolled in different Medicare programs (e.g., Fee-for-service and Medicare Advantage), we develop an understanding of the potential impact of the new legislation and how it relates to the interplay between financial and medical incentives in the provision of hospital care.
