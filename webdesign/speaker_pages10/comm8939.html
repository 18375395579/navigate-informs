Title: Patient-centered Long-term Care Network Design

Author: Paul Intrevado, Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke Street West, Montreal, QC, H3A1G5, Canada, paulintrevado@mac.com

Coauthor(s): Vedat Verter

Year: 2013

Abstract: A dynamic co-location, capacity acquisition and patient assignment model for long-term care facilities is presented as a mixed-integer program, capturing patient quality-of-life. An illustrative example based on empirical data is presented and results discussed.
