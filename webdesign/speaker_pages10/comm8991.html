Title: Efficiency and Returns to Scale Analysis of Surgical Suites

Author: Timothy Butler, Wayne State University, School of Business Administration, Detroit, MI, 48202, United States, ad4518@wayne.edu

Coauthor(s): Marc Basson

Year: 2006

Abstract: We use data envelopment analysis to evaluate the efficiency of surgical suites in VA hospitals. We consider returns to scale and propose a methodology for assessing the returns to scale tendencies of strictly inefficient suites.
