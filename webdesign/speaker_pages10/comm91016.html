Title: An Empirical Analysis of Conversion Rate Dynamics in Online Retail

Author: Lin Hao, Assistant Professor, University of Notre Dame, Mendoza College of Business, Notre Dame, IN, 46556, United States of America, lhao@nd.edu

Coauthor(s): Ming Fan, Yong Tan

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper studies how online seller's pricing and product strategies, marketing efforts and service quality, together with reputation scores and ratings, affect conversion rates. We use a dataset from a popular online marketplace. Our estimation results show that there exist multiple states that affect the conversion rates. The relationship between conversion rate and above related factors are state dependent.
