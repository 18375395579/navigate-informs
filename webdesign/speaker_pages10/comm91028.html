Title: Development of a Service Quality Evaluation Scheme in a Service Laboratory Environment

Author: Ryeok-Hwan Kwon, Pohang University of Science and Technology, Engineering Buliding #4-316, Hyoja-Dong, Nam-Gu, Pohang, Kyungbuk, Pohang, Korea, Republic of, rh_kwon@postech.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Hyun-Jin Kim, Kwang-Jae Kim

Year: 2012

Abstract: A virtual reality (VR) laboratory for service testing has been constructed in Korea. One issue associated with its operation is how to evaluate the quality of a service tested in a laboratory environment. We present a service quality evaluation scheme for a service laboratory environment, consisting of two parts: a repository of service evaluation criteria and a guideline to select pertinent criteria for a particular case study. The result of a case study will also be presented.
