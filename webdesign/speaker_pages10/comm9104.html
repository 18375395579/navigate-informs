Title: Motivation and Performance in Professional Groups

Author: Christoph Loch, Professor, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, christoph.loch@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Yaozhong Wu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We show empirical evidence from experiemnets with human subjects that a balance of individual and team-oriented motivation mechanisms leads to highest performance in knowledge tasks.
