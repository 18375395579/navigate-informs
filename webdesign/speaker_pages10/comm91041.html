Title: Using Customer Interactions for Product and Service Innovation in a Telecom Context

Author: Athanasios Karapantelakis, Research Engineer, Ericsson, Farogatan 6, Stockholm, 16440, Sweden, athanasios.karapantelakis@ericsson.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: In order to adapt to a transforming telecom industry driven by technological evolution, network providers need to leverage their relationship with customers through personalized offerings. We present a conceptual model describing how customer interactions foster innovation in a telecom context. This framework will identify the different ways customer interactions can facilitate creation of new offerings and will include a number of case studies showing how the model can be used in reality.
