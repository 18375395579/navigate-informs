Title: Balancing Information Access and Risk with Capacitated IT Service

Author: Eric Johnson, Professor, Dartmouth College, Center for Digital Strategies, Tuck School of Business, Hanover, NH, 03755, United States of America, M.Eric.Johnson@tuck.dartmouth.edu

Coauthor(s): Xia. Zhao

Year: 2008

Abstract: Controls for information access, while needed to maintain the security, inevitably constrain the firm’s ability to respond quickly to changing needs. We introduce an escalation component to the access control process and study design and control decisions for an IT service department focused on both security risks and delay costs.
