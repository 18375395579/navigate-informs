Title: On Validating the Analytic Hierarchy/Network Processes

Author: Rozann Saaty, Creative Decisions Foundation, Pittsburgh, PA, United States, rozann@rwspublications.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: An essential fact regarding the truth of a theory is its validation in practice. One validates with: (1) A pairwise comparison matrix, (2) A hierarchy, (3) A network (both simple with market share and with benefits, opportunities, costs and risks. A compatibility index is used to compare the derived results with data.
