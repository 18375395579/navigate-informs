Title: Piracy Control in Two-sided Markets

Author: Xiuli He, Assistant Professor, Univeristy of North Carolina-Charlotte, 9201 University City Blvd, BISOM Dept., Charlotte, NC, 28223, United States of America, xhe8@uncc.edu

Coauthor(s): Sungjune Park, Peiqin Zhang

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study investigates the piracy control in two-sided markets. The third-party platform providers generate revenues both in advertising and content market. We study the optimal prices for the content and advertising market and the level of piracy control for the platform providers.
