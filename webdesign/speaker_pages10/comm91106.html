Title: Six Sigma Supply Chains with RFID Visibility

Author: Sanjay Sarma, Professor, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA, United States, sesarma@MIT.EDU

Year: 2006

Abstract: The principles of Statistical Process Control have revolutionized the yields of processes ranging from semiconductor fabrication to automotive assembly. The recent emergence of RFID however enables a new level of process measurement, control and continuous improvement in the Supply Chain. I will present some emerging results on this front and show how tighter performance, measured statistically can significantly enhance finacial metrics like revenues and earnings.
