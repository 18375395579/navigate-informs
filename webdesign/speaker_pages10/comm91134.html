Title: Building Ahoona: The Decision-Making Social Network ?

Author: Ali Abbas, Associate Professor and Director, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, 104 S. Mathews Ave, Urbana, IL, 61822, United States of America, aliabbas@illinois.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Wikipedia has shown us that people love to help others with their expertise. The result was the finest encyclopedia that exists today. Facebook has shown us that people love to connect with friends in a social network. The result was one of the largest companies that exist today. This talk will discuss the creation of Ahoona; a decision-making social network that connects people and enables them to offer decision expertise.
