Title: Social TV: Linking TV to Social Media and Sales

Author: Adrian Benton, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA, United States of America, adrian.donald.benton@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Shawndra Hill

Year: 2012

Abstract: “Social TV?is a term that broadly describes the online social interactions that occur between viewers while watching television. In order to better understand the relationship between television content, buzz on online social media, and ultimately viewership and sales, we examine the American television program, The Voice. In this talk, we present analyses linking television content presented to buzz on Twitter and sales on ITunes.
