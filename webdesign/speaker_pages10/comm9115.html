Title: What is New in Organizational Decision Quality

Author: Carl Spetzler, CEO, Strategic Decisions Group, 745 Emerson Street, Palo Alto, CA, 94301, United States of America, cspetzler@sdg.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: The adoption of Decision Quality as a deep organizational core competence is accelerating. Other oil companies are following Chevron's lead; Pharma adoption is moving beyond the traditional development portfolio decisions; Major technology companies are building capabilities; and we see many job openings for decision professionals. The key to adoption is to reach the tipping point of converts among the top management in an enterprise. We'll share stories and lessons learned.
