Title: Co-movement between Shipping Industry and Global Markets

Author: Gilbert Y. F. Wang, Senior Scientific Officer, City University of Hong Kong, Ctr of Transport, Trade and Finance, Tat Chee Ave, Hong Kong, Hong Kong - PRC, gilbertw@cityu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): John Liu, Hui Bu, Laser J. G. Yuan

Year: 2013

Abstract: Noting shipping as the artery of international trade, this paper investigates the co-movement between shipping industry and global markets. We use Multivariate DCC GARCH model to conduct this analysis. We estimate and forecast the dynamic conditional variances and correlations among the demand of shipbuilding, the Baltic Dry Index and some other economic indexes. We find that the dependencies/correlations vary over time, with significant structure changes during the crises periods.
