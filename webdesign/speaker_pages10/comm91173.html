Title: The Effect of Online Reviews on Consumer Search and Purchase Behavior

Author: Dina Mayzlin, Yale University, dina.mayzlin@yale.edu

Coauthor(s): Wendy Moe

Year: 2008

Abstract: While previous literature has analyzed the impact of reviews on sales, little is known about the effect of reviews on search. Here we analyze how an online retailer's introduction of a review tool affects the search as well as purchase behavior of its customers. We also show that a product's reviews have positive externalities on the page views and sales of similar products.
