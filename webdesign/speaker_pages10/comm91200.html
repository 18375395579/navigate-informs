Title: Website Features and Online Shopping: How Technology Usage Impacts Consumer Demand

Author: Mohammad Rahman, University of Calgary, 2500 University Dr. NW, Calgary, Alberta, T2N 1N4, Canada, mohammad.rahman@haskayne.ucalgary.ca

Coauthor(s): Prabuddha De, Yu Hu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Today virtually all Internet companies provide advanced technological features such as search functions and recommendation systems on their websites. Despite the widespread adoption of search and recommendation technologies, empirical research that tackles the economic impact of these technologies is scarce. Does consumers?technology usage have an impact on consumer demand? How does this impact vary for different technologies and across different products? Our research studies these questions.
