Title: An Empirical Analysis of Contract Structures in IT Outsourcing

Author: YuanYuan Chen, Emory University, 1300 Clifton Road, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, yuanyuan_chen@bus.emory.edu

Coauthor(s): Anandhi Bharadwaj

Year: 2007

Abstract: Outsourcing of IT services has received much attention in the information systems literature.However, there has been considerably less attention paid to actual contract structures used in IT outsourcing. Drawing on transaction cost, agency and relational exchange theories we hypothesize the effects of transaction and relational characteristics on the specific contractual provisions as well as on overall contract extensiveness.
