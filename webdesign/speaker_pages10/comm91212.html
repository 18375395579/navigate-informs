Title: Optimal Choice for Market Price Information

Author: Chris Parker, Pennsylvania State University, Smeal College of Business, University Park, PA, 16802, United States of America, chris.parker@psu.edu

Coauthor(s): Kamalini Ramdas, Nicos Savva

Year: 2012

Abstract: Information is only valuable if it is both new and actionable. We model a seller's selection of a market for which to purchase price information. We discuss the results of the model in the context of farmers in rural India and use data to analyze whether actual decisions are in line with our model.
