Title: Predicting Analytics Investment Decisions

Author: Matthew Liberatore, Professor, Villanova University, 800 Lancaster Ave, Villanova, PA, 19085, United States of America, matthew.liberatore@villanova.edu

Coauthor(s): Suzanne Clain, Carolyn Martin, Bruce Pollack-Johnson

Year: 2013

Abstract: The increasing use of data-driven decision making is leading organizations to consider whether they should invest in analytics software and services. Are organizations in specific industries or geographies more likely to invest? Does industry competitiveness influence the decision? Are organization size, performance, and analytics maturity key influencing factors? We present predictive models for the analytics investment decision and level of investment based on data obtained from IBM.
