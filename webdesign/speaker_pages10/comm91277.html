Title: Industrial Service Project Management Effectiveness for Ship Repair Project Managers

Author: Anthony Erickson, Portland State University, ericksoa@pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Timothy Anderson

Year: 2008

Abstract: Countering a lack of formal project management evaluation metrics in the ship repair industry, this paper proposes a project management effectiveness (PME) model, measuring profit contribution, schedule, specification execution/quality, strategic impact, and personal objectives on normalized scales and compares project manager performance both directly and using Data Envelopment Analysis.
