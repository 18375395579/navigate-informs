Title: Pricing and Timing Impacts on Sales: An Empirical Study on the Entertainment Industry

Author: Naragain Phumchusri, PhD student, Georgia Institute of Technology, -, Atlanta, GA, United States of America, gth752r@mail.gatech.edu

Coauthor(s): Julie Swann

Year: 2008

Abstract: In this study, we use data from an entertainment industry in Georgia to empirically examine Revenue Management practices and their impacts. Particularly, we observe how pricing for each seating zone and the timing affect sales of its zone and other zones in the same show. In addition, we study the impacts of factors such as weekday versus weekend shows and identify opportunities to increase revenue from their different popularities.
