Title: Overview of Search Engine Marketing

Author: Theresa Flaherty, Professor of Marketing, James Madison University, MSC 0205, Dept. of Marketing, COB, Harrisonburg, VA, 22807, United States of America, flahertb@jmu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This overview provides a brief introduction to search engine marketing (SEM) and discussion of some of the current trends and practices. Two distinct, but interrelated, SEM strategies will be presented: Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Paid Search (PPC). Additional topics include key phrase identification, site submission, optimization caveats, sitemap protocol, Google AdWords, relevant SEM metrics, and link building strategies.
