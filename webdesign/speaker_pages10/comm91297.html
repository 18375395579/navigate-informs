Title: Exploring University Technology Transfer Incorporating Time-lags and Institutional Characteristics

Author: Jisun Kim, Portland State University, Portland, OR, United States of America, kimjisun73@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Tugrul Daim

Year: 2012

Abstract: This paper aims to develop institutional strategy to improve the licensing practice of academic research institutions based on further understanding of licensing performance and related institutional characteristics. The results which are grounded on the comprehensive observations over multiple time durations provide an insight into the licensing practices of US research institutions.
