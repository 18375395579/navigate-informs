Title: Dynamic Customer Acquisition and Retention Management

Author: Gregory King, University of Michigan, United States of America, gjking@umich.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: In consulting, finance, and other service industries, customers represent a revenue stream, and must be acquired and retained over time. In this paper, we study the resource allocation problem of a profit-maximizing service firm that dynamically allocates its resources towards acquiring new clients and retaining unsatisfied existing ones.
