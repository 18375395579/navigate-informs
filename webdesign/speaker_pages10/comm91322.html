Title: Optimal Fundraising Categories

Author: Kevin McCardle, Professor, UCLA Anderson School, 110 Westwood Plaza, Los Angeles, CA, 90095-1481, United States, kevin.mccardle@anderson.ucla.edu

Coauthor(s): Kumar Rajaram, Christopher Tang

Year: 2006

Abstract: We provide some data and a simple model for the setting of named categories in capital campaigns for charitable 501c3 organizations.
