Title: Behavioral Product Quality Improvement

Author: Gokhan Dogan, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 30 Wadsworth Street E53-364, Cambridge, MA, 02142, United States, gdogan@MIT.EDU

Coauthor(s): Nelson Repenning

Year: 2007

Abstract: Product quality improvement process includes preventing potential problems or fixing existing ones. Using qualitative and quantitative data from a major manufacturer, we identify biases and heuristics that inhibit the effectiveness of the quality improvement process. We then test alternative policies for improvement with a simulation model.
