Title: What Can We Learn from Online Doctor Ratings

Author: Jiban Kuntia, Visiting Assistant Professor, Oakland University, Decision and Information Sciences Dept., School of Business Administration, Rochester, MI, 48309, United States of America

Coauthor(s): Guodong Gordon Gao, Ritu Agarwal

Year: 2012

Abstract: This study examines various issues related to online doctor ratings, especially how the ratings are related to physician quality.
