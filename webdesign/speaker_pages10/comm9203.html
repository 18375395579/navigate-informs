Title: Identification and Prediction of Economic Regimes to Guide Decision Making in Multiagent Marketplace

Author: Wolfgang Ketter, Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University, Rotterdam, Netherlands, WKetter@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Maria Gini, Paul Schrater, John Collins, Alok Gupta

Year: 2008

Abstract: We present an approach that enables software agents to make tactical decisions, such as product pricing, and strategic decisions, such as production planning. Using a combination of machine learning and optimization techniques, agents characterize economic regimes, which are microeconomic conditions reflecting situations such as over-supply or scarcity.
