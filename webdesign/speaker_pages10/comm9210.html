Title: From E-commerce to M-commerce: Business Value of Mobile Sales Channel

Author: Yen-Chun Chou, Arizona State University, United States of America, Yen-Chun.Chou@asu.edu

Coauthor(s): Benjamin Shao

Year: 2012

Abstract: M-commerce represents an emerging subset of e-commerce and is projected to reach 31 billion by 2016. While firms embrace mobile sales channels, one critical question is how they can reap values from the new channels. We aim to answer the question by considering prior e-commerce establishments, timing of mobile channel implementation, and a firm’s general business operations. Our study contributes to research and practice by exploring factors that influence m-commerce performances.
