Title: Enlisted Community Manager (ECM) Digital Dashboard: Inventory Tracking of Military Personnel

Author: Thomas Jones, Commander, USN, thomas.jones4@navy.mil

Year: 2007

Abstract: The ECM Digital Dashboard has been responsible for unprecedented transformational change affecting the processes and methodologies used to manage enlisted communities across the entire Active Component (AC) and Reserve Component (RC ?FTS) Navy. The ECM DD offers Senior Navy Leadership, as well as all ECMs, unprecedented visibility into 214 enlisted ratings with intuitive displays of data for 15 top-level metrics critical to community management.
