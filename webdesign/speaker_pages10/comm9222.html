Title: Understanding Process Variability Following Enterprise System Performance

Author: Mark Cotteleer, Marquette University, College of Business Administration, mark.cotteleer@marquette.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This study extends our understanding of the time-sensitivity of intent and usage following large-scale IT implementation. It focuses on perceived system misfit with organizational processes and the availability of system circumvention opportunities. The study combines case studies and controlled experiments are used to support the theoretical unpacking of organizational and technical contingencies and their relationship to shifts in user intentions and variation in work-processing tactics over time.
