Title: The Geography of Retail Inventory

Author: Richard Lai, Harvard Business School, Gallatin Hall, Boston, MA, 02163, United States, rlai@hbs.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: How different are retailers?inventory levels around the world? I test various forms of global determinism: are levels constant across countries, converging, or at least co-integrating? The tests use a novel dataset covering 4,100 retailers in 23 countries. I find evidence to reject all the three forms of global determinism. Instead, I report evidence consistent with an alternative hypothesis ?“local contingency??in which country effects are significant.
