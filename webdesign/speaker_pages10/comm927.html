Title: Social Media Intelligence for Regional Instability

Author: Tom Sabo, Sr. Solutions Architect, SAS Federal LLC, 1530 Wilson Blvd. Suite 800, Arlington, VA, 22209, United States of America, tom.sabo@sas.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: SAS has analyzed social media relevant to Arab Spring to present visually and geospatially how indications of discontentment and stressor events in a region correlate with protest and instability. Such trend analysis can preempt the resurgence of militant groups and subsequent terrorist activity.
