Title: New Quality as a Competitive Weapon in Korean Automotive Industry

Author: Youn Sung Kim, Inha University, Korea, Republic of, keziah@inha.ac.kr

Year: 2012

Abstract: Quality is one of the most important issues in many companies. After the Toyota crisis, many companies are trying to enhance the quality level and reduce the quality problem. So in this research, we try to find out how to reenergize their quality focus, especially in Korean automotive industry.
