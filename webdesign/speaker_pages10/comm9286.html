Title: Product Rollovers and Optimal Pace of Product Introductions

Author: Eylem Koca, University of Maryland, R. H. Smith School of Business, College Park, MD, 20742, United States, ekoca@rhsmith.umd.edu

Coauthor(s): Cheryl Druehl, Gilvan Souza

Year: 2006

Abstract: We present a framework to find the optimal pace of product introductions in the face of inventory and pricing considerations. We also compare the single and dual roll decision. We present numerical solutions and managerial implications using a fluid approximation model.
