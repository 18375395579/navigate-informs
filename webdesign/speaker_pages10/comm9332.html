Title: Persistence of Sustainability Capabilities: An Empirical Analysis

Author: Brian Jacobs, Assistant Professor, Michigan State University, 632 Bogue St. Rm N370, East Lansing, MI, 48824, United States of America, jacobsb@bus.msu.edu

Coauthor(s): Sriram Narayanan

Year: 2012

Abstract: In this study, we examine the persistence of sustainability capabilities and draw implications for how sustainability capabilities of firms change over time. We examine how the “ecosystem?of internal capabilities and those of industry counterparts influence the persistence of firms?sustainability capabilities.
