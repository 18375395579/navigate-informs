Title: Projection, Lifting and the Two Representations of Polyhedra

Author: Egon Balas, Professor, Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, eb17@andrew.cmu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: This talk will convey some thoughts about the relationship between projection on the one hand and various lifting techniques on the other, prompted by leafing through George Dantzig's last book.
