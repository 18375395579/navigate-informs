Title: Efficient Take-back Legislation

Author: Atalay Atasu, Ph.D Candidate, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77300, France, atalay.atasu@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Miklos Sarvary, Luk Van Wassenhove

Year: 2007

Abstract: In this paper, the economic and environmental impacts of environmental take-back directives are investigated under a general setting that applies in leading economies of the world. The conditions for optimally regulating such systems are identified and a number of possibilities for improving the existing legislation are provided.
