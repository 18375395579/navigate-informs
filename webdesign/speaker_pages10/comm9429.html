Title: Practical Methods of Leveraging Data to Increase Revenue

Author: Bob Marklein, Manager, Procter & Gamble, 2 P&G Plaza, Cincinnati, OH, 45202, United States of America, marklein.ra@pg.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: The volume of data available to retailers about their customers continues to grow, yet the capacity of resources to optimize value from this sea of data tends to remain flat. I will talk about practical ways of leveraging data to help make investment choices to increase category, department, and total store sales while avoiding “analysis paralysis?
