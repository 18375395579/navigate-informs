Title: Differential Influence of Blogs Across Different Stages of Decision Making

Author: Harpreet Singh, University of Texas-Dallas, Richardson, TX, United States of America, harpreet@utdallas.edu

Coauthor(s): Rohit Aggarwal

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the differential influence of online user generated content (UGC), specifically blogs, across the multiple stages of decision-making of venture capitalists: screening stage, choice stage, and contract stage. In particular, this study indicates that blogs can help managers in getting their products/services selected at the screening stage, but beyond that, blogs do not help directly.
