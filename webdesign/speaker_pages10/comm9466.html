Title: Analysis of Multi-criteria Decision-making Methodologies for the Petroleum Industry

Author: Lev Virine, Schlumberger Ltd., 600, 322 - 11Ave S.W., Calgary, AB, Canada, lvirine@calgary.oilfield.slb.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: Oil companies use multi-criteria decision making to balance financial performance and production with quality, safety, reserve maturation and environmental protection and other objectives. The paper provides recommendations how different multi-criteria decision-making methodologies and techniques of eliciting judgment can be applied for different types of decisions related to petroleum exploration and production.
