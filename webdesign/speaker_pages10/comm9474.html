Title: How Can RFID Fix the Challenges in Healthcare Industry?

Author: Shiwoo Lee, Oregon State University, 118 Covell, Corvallis, OR, United States of America, Shiwoo.Lee@oregonstate.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: According a new study, it is the biggest health-care players that are moving the quickest and investing in large deployments of the technology. RFID technology is not an exception. US healthcare industry is increasingly seeing adoption of RFID as a major potential contributor to improved healthcare. This presentation will talk about the various challenges in healthcare industry, which can be fixed by RFID applications in near future.
