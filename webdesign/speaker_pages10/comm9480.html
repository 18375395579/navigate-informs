Title: Analyzing the Interdependence between Web and Mobile Advertisements

Author: Sang-Pil Han, Assistant Professor, City University of Hong Kong, Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon, Hong Kong - ROC, sangphan@cityu.edu.hk

Coauthor(s): Sunghyuk Park, Anindya Ghose

Year: 2012

Abstract: This study examines whether exposing users to Internet and mobile ads for the same product has a positive or a negative effect on consumer’s affinity towards those products. Towards this end, we conduct large-scale archival data analysis, combined with randomized field trials in order to establish their causal relationships. Our results will provide valuable insights to advertisers as to whether/how they should engage in mobile and Web advertisings in a multi-channel environment.
