Title: Portfolios of Buyer-Supplier Relations in Reverse Auctions for IT Services: A Longitudinal Analysis

Author: Uladzimir Radkevitch, Ph.D Candidate, RSM Erasmus University, Room T09-11, Postbox 1738, Rotterdam, 3000 DR, Netherlands, uradkevitch@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Otto Koppius, Eric van Heck

Year: 2007

Abstract: We study an electronic marketplace for IT services. Despite the transactional nature of the reverse auction mechanism used, many buyers in this market develop relationships with their suppliers. We study the configuration of these relationships over time and show that they evolve into four distinct types of portfolios.
