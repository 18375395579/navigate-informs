Title: An Upper Bound Look at FAA NEXTGEN Benefits

Author: Marc Rose, Sr. OR Analyst, MCR LLC, 600 Maryland Ave, SW, Washington, DC, 20024, United States of America, marc.rose@baesystems.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: An examination of the claimed capabilities (capacity increase) of the FAA NEXTGEN program. This presentation will assume that NEXTGEN achieves the desired capabilities. Given that assumption, what is the magnitude of the benefits (primarily delay savings). This paper will address this in only the broadest of terms As a secondary look at some of the risks to achieving these capabilities (e.g., impact of uncertainties like wind and weather forecasts).
