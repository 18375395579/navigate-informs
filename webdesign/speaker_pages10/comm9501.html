Title: Geographical Challenges of Online Advertising

Author: Wolfgang Jank, University of Maryland, 4322 Van Munching Hall, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, wjank@rhsmith.umd.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Online firms are keen on understanding different geographical markets from where their customers come from and how their preferences vary across these markets. Such an understanding can be useful in developing the right product mix and targeting the right customers, through local market advertising, direct marketing, and cross channel promotion. We will discuss why geography matters online and we will outline different challenges of online advertising in a spatially-connected world.
