Title: Linking the Cash-to-cash Cycle to Corporate Performance

Author: David A. Menachof, Sr. Lecturer in International Logistics, Cass Business School, City University, 106 Bunhill Row, London, EC1Y 8TZ, United Kingdom, d.menachof@city.ac.uk

Year: 2007

Abstract: This study will attempt to link Cash-to-Cash(C-to-C) cycle (a key SC performance metric) and corporate performance on a global scale; an empirical link that is so far missing to date. The implication is that the clearer the link, the more incentive the SC Manager has to actively manage this recognized metric.
