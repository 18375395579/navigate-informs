Title: Temperament, Character, and Customer Loyalty

Author: Han-Chung Huang, PhD Candidate, Chang Gung University, 259, Wenhua 1st Rd, Gueishan, Taoyuan, 333, Taiwan - ROC, t756068@yahoo.com.tw;

Coauthor(s): Ching-I Teng

Year: 2013

Abstract: Previous studies have started to focus on the link between customer personality and loyalty. Psychological theories are used for developing hypotheses. This study examines how the seven dimensions of Temperament and Character dimensions are related to customer loyalty in an online context. Mixed findings are observed regarding the correlations between the seven dimensions and customer loyalty. Implications of the study findings are discussed.
