Title: Logistics Contracts in Manufacturing

Author: Osman Engin Alper, University of California, IEOR, Berkeley, CA, 94530, United States, engin@ieor.berkeley.edu

Coauthor(s): Hyun-Soo Ahn, Phil Kaminsky

Year: 2006

Abstract: The value of integrating production and logistics operations is increasingly appreciated by managers, while at the same time logistics outsourcing is becoming a more prevalent across many industries. Motivated by this observation, we develop models of logistics contracts in manufacturing systems, characterize optimal policies, provide computational results, and summarize practical implications.
