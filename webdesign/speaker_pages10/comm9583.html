Title: Team Experience and Returns to Management in Software Services

Author: Brad Staats, Doctoral Candidate, Harvard Business School, Morgan Hall 428A, Boston, MA, 02163, United States, bstaats@hbs.edu

Coauthor(s): David Upton, Robert Huckman

Year: 2006

Abstract: Using data from an Indian software services firm we examine the returns to project-level management and team experience.
