Title: DOE and Congressional Perspectives on Energy Models and Decision-making

Author: Uday Varadarajan, AAAS Science & Technology Policy Fellow, Department of Energy / House Appropriations Committee Staff, Room 2362-B, Rayburn House Office Building, Washington, DC, 20515, United States of America, Uday.Varadarajan@gmail.com

Year: 2008

Abstract: The current role of energy models in corporate decision-making at DOE is reviewed, and some personal lessons learned during the development of the FY 2009 DOE budget are discussed. These are compared and contrasted with the needs of the Congressional appropriations process, and some recommendations are made regarding effective utilization of models in the two different decision-making environments.
