Title: An Efficiency-based Approach to Stock Selection

Author: Irene Song, Columbia University, Columbia University, 120 Street, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, is2306@columbia.edu

Coauthor(s): Soulaymane Kachani, Iraj Kani

Year: 2013

Abstract: This study investigates the relationship between firm efficiency and stock performance by building an efficiency-based investment strategy and assessing its performance over time. Our empirical findings demonstrate the existence of a significant positive relationship between the two and furnish an economic explanation for the relative outperformance of the efficient firms.
