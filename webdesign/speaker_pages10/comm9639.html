Title: Modeling the Healthcare Supply Chain: The Clinical Perspective

Author: Ursula Hubner, Professor, University of Applied Sciences, Caprivistr. 30A, Osnabrück, D-49080, Germany, U.Huebner@fh-osnabrueck.de

Year: 2008

Abstract: This paper aims at presenting a model of supply chain activities in healthcare within the context of eBusiness and its four behavioral components: information distribution and search, transactions, collaboration and decision-making. The supply chain model of eBusiness in healthcare integrates a process, a document, and a functional model that are all geared to the combined view of clinical and economic issues related to the procurement, provision, and use of medical supplies.
