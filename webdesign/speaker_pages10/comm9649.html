Title: The Evolution of Software for Supply Chain Planning: A Look Forward and a Look Back

Author: Lora Cecere, founder, Supply Chain Insights, P 64 801 Key Highway, Baltimore, MD, 21230, United States of America, lora.cecere@supplychaininsights.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Supply chain managment as a practice is thirty years old. Optimization and artifical intelligence technologies have evolved and matured to solve supply chain problems. But, were are we in driving supply chain progress? What is the road ahead? In this presentation, learn the current and future state from the author of the book, Bricks Matter.
