Title: Dynamic Complexity in Love and Interfirm Rivalry

Author: John Morecroft, Senior Fellow, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, UK, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, jmorecroft@london.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: A tiny model of dynamic complexity in a love relationship paves the way for modelling and simulation of interfirm rivalry in fast-moving consumer goods. Strategic insights into the launch of liquid soap products (and their growth dynamics) are reported. The role of modelling in such strategic development is discussed.
