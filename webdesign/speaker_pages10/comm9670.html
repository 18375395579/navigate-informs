Title: Studying the Dynamics of Software Architectures

Author: Manuel Sosa, Assistant Professor of Technology and Operations Management, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, manuel.sosa@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Jurgen Mihm, Tyson Browning

Year: 2007

Abstract: We report the results of an exploratory study to investigate how the architecture of software products evolves over time. We illustrate our approach by examining the evolution over several versions of an open source product. Implications for research are discussed.
