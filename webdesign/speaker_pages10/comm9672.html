Title: Risk Adjusted Portfolio Procurement Management

Author: Chung-Yee Lee, Department Chair, Hong Kong University of Sci. & Tech., Clear Water Bay, Kowloon, Hong Kong - ROC, cylee@ust.hk

Coauthor(s): Qi Fu, Chung-Piaw Teo

Year: 2007

Abstract: Many procurement managers reported the need of meeting their performance target as the primary concern when making procurement decisions. This study proposes a risk adjusted approach to address this type of performance measurement in a portfolio procurement environment. We compare the procurement decisions under this risk adjusted approach with those of the risk neutral approach, and discuss how different performance measurements could affect the choice of procurement strategy.
