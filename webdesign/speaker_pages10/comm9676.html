Title: Group Size and Investment in Quality

Author: Julie Niederhoff, Syracuse University, 721 University Ave, WSOM 500, Syracuse, NY, 13244, United States of America, jniederh@syr.edu

Year: 2013

Abstract: Supply chains are filled with people who have often conflicting objectives of group success versus personal success. We explore the effect of group size and inter-dependency on the overall output of a system in several metrics of operations management.
