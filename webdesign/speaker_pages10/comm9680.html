Title: Developing a Strategic Policy Choice Framework

Author: Leong Chan, Adjunct Faculty, Portland State University, Portland, OR, 97201, United States of America, vleongv@gmail.com

Coauthor(s): Tugrul Daim

Year: 2013

Abstract: The objective of this research was to construct a technology policy choice framework to formulate technology development strategies, linking prospective high-tech areas and various innovative resources to promote national S&T competitiveness and foster long term technological innovation. A hierarchical decision model was developed. This research focused on the fast developing Chinese pharmaceutical industry as a case study.
