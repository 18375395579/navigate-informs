Title: The Impact of Misalignment of Organization Structure and Product Architecture on Quality

Author: Bilal Gokpinar, Assistant Professor, University College London, Management Science and Innovation, London, WC1E 6BT, United Kingdom, bilal@iems.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Seyed Iravani, Wallace Hopp

Year: 2008

Abstract: A key challenge for organizations that design complex products is matching the organization to the product being developed. Using network analysis, we characterize mismatches between the product architecture and organization structure by defining a new metric, called “coordination deficit? and show that it is positively associated with quality problems observed when the product gets to the market.
