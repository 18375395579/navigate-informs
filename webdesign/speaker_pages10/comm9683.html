Title: Social Networks and Individual Performance in Open Source Software Communities

Author: Il-Horn Hann, Marshall School of Business, USC, 3670 Trousdale Parkway, Los Angeles, CA, 90089, United States, hann@marshall.usc.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeffrey Roberts, Sandra Slaughter

Year: 2007

Abstract: We examine social network dynamics and participation in several projects of the Apache open source community. Understanding how open source developers communicate and interact, and how this social interaction affects their participation is interesting and important, given the geographic distribution of the developers and the unstructured process of software development in the open source context.
