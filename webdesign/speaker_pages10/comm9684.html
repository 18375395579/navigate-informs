Title: Evaluation, Play Entry Strategy, and Materiality in Unconventional Gas & Liquids-Rich Opportunities

Author: William Haskett, Senior Principal - Energy Strategy, Decision Strategies Inc., 10333 Richmond St., Suite 350, Houston, TX, 77005, United States of America, bhaskett@decisionstrategies.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Source rock plays revolutionized the Energy industry leading to new decision challenges and methods. Targets are a “family?of wells; a distribution of distributions. A novel method is shown to carry cell-based uncertainty across the play, which allows pilot optimization, decision thresholds, Confidence, and valuation. It has been implemented successfully world-wide. These concepts are applied to create and implement a play entry strategy, materiality, and create material competitive advantage.
