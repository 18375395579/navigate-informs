Title: Towards a Para-Economic Model of Global Outsourcing of White Collar Services

Author: Nir Kshetri, Assistant Professor, University of North Carolina at Greensboro, Bryan School of Business, Greensboro, NC, United States, nbkshetr@uncg.edu

Year: 2006

Abstract: Based on studies of BPO firms in India, this paper integrates theories from multiple sources to propose a “para-economic?model of global outsourcing of white-collar services. Conceptual frames dealing with international trade in services as well as frameworks for e-commerce based on business process outsourcing (BPO) are integrated in this paper.
