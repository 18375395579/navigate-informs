Title: Lessons Learned while Organizing International Teaching Effectiveness Colloquia

Author: James Cochran, Louisiana Tech University, PO Box 10318, College of Business, Ruston, LA, 71272, United States of America, jcochran@cab.latech.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Over the past 3 years INFORMS, IFORS, and EURO have worked with the Association of Latin-Iberoamerican Operational Research Societies, Operations Research Society of South Africa, and Operations Research Practice for Africa to organize joint Teaching Effectiveness Colloquia. These colloquia feature workshops on innovative O.R. education given by members of each organization. I will discuss differences, similarities, issues, etc. in O.R. education across cultures that participants have observed.
