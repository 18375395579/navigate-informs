Title: An Analytic Model to Evaluate a 3PL Partner

Author: Ozden Bayazit, Associate Professor of Supply Chain Management, Central Washington University-Lynnwood, 20000 68th Avenue W., Lynnwood, WA, 98036, United States of America, bayazito@cwu.edu

Coauthor(s): Birsen Karpak

Year: 2012

Abstract: This study extends a framework, a simple Analytical Network model, developed in literature to evaluate third party logistic companies. We contend that Benefits Costs Opportunities Risks model will represent the reality better. In addition, the proposed framework address triple bottom line, People, Planet, and Profit, therefore it is a framework for sustainable supply chains.
