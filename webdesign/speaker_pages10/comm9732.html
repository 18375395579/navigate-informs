Title: Technology Intelligence Sources: A New Frontier for Technology

Author: Guillermo Rueda, Ph.D Student, Intel, Hillsboro, OR, 97124, United States, grueda@pdx.edu

Coauthor(s): Pisek Gerdsri, Tugrul Daim

Year: 2007

Abstract: Abstract: This paper presents an overview of technology intelligence sources used at an increased rate by corporations and governments. The sources include citation and patent analysis. The paper includes cases from emerging technologies demonstrating use of these sources.
