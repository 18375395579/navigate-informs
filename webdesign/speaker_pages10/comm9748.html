Title: B2B Normative Pricing Strategy: Antecedents and Profitability Impact

Author: Tim Baker, Associate Professor, Washington State University, 2710 University Drive, CIC 125N, Richland, WA, 99354, United States, bakert@tricity.wsu.edu

Coauthor(s): Vaidy Jayaraman

Year: 2007

Abstract: We wish to determine via structural equation modeling (1) the drivers of adherence/lack-of-adherence to normative pricing strategies, and (2) the impact of adherence/lack-of-adherence to normative pricing strategies on financial measures of sales performance, relative to all other factors thought to impact such sales performance measures.
