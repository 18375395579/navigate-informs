Title: Recommendation System in E-commerce based on ACP Approach

Author: Chen Lu, Tsinghua University, 610A, Zijing 12#, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, shenlanghuhu@163.com

Coauthor(s): Lefei Li

Year: 2013

Abstract: Traditional recommendation methods, such as collaborative filtering, are based on historical data, and can hardly describe consumer behaviors accurately. In our study, we introduce an online recommendation system based on ACP approach to intelligently forecast products of customers' interest and provide customized recommendations. Our method has the unique advantages that it can keep track of users' property and preference changes, and provide timely response to events and environment changes.
