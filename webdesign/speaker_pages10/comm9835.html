Title: The Effect of Health Plan Type on The Computed Tomography (CT) Utilization

Author: Sarah Zheng, Doctoral Student, Boston University, 16 Gold Star Rd., Cambridge, MA, 02140, United States of America, xinzheng@bu.edu

Coauthor(s): Z. Justin Ren

Year: 2013

Abstract: In this paper, we look into the effects of health plan type on the CT utilization using Thomson-Reuters MarketScan database. We found that Health Maintenance Organization, Consumer-Driven Health Plan, High Deductible Health Plan have lower CT utilization comparing with Preferred Provider Organization. This study provides empirical evidence on how different payment systems may help reduce overutilization.
