Title: Component Modularity, Team Network Structure, and Attention to Technical Interdependences

Author: Manuel Sosa, Professor of Technology and Operations Management, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77305, France, manuel.sosa@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Martin Gargiulo, Craig Rowles

Year: 2006

Abstract: We study the determinants of teams´capability to attend to technical interdependences in complex product development projects. We found that teams?communication network structure plays a critical role on the capacity of teams to attend to their interdependences. Yet, its impact is contingent upon the modularity and redesign of product components.
