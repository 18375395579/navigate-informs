Title: Market Heterogeneity and Local Capacity Decisions in Services

Author: Dennis Campbell, Harvard Business School, Soldiers Field, Boston, MA, 02153, United States, dcampbell@hbs.edu

Coauthor(s): Frances Frei

Year: 2007

Abstract: We empirically document factors that influence how local managers balance the tradeoff between service capacity costs and customer sensitivity to service times. We find that (1) customer-sensitivities to service time vary predictably with market characteristics; (2) capacity supply decisions of local managers reflect market specific sensitivity to service time; and (3) experience affects how local managers evaluate the tradeoff between capacity costs and customer sensitivity to service time.
