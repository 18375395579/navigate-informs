Title: A Personalized Recommendation Model Based on Hybrid Method

Author: Sang Hyun Choi, chois@gsnu.ac.kr

Coauthor(s): Myong K. (MK) Jeong

Year: 2007

Abstract: In the recent trend of digital economy, firms are facing the challenges of providing differentiated products and services to each customer based on the purchasing information from the customers. This research suggests a new hybrid method for personalized recommendation that combines the collaborative filtering and content-based algorithm.
