Title: Managing Global Sourcing: Inventory Performance

Author: Karan Girotra, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, France, Karan.GIROTRA@insead.edu

Coauthor(s): Nitish Jain, Serguei Netessine

Year: 2012

Abstract: The shift towards sourcing products from global suppliers has been a key economic trend over the last three decades. This study provides the first rigorous firm-level empirical evidence, using over half a million sea shipments from global suppliers to US firms, on the operational impacts of global sourcing and provides actionable guidance for managing global sourcing.
