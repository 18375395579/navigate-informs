Title: “Look at me! Look at me!? The Competitive Dynamic of Wikis and Online Communities

Author: Brian Butler, University of Pittsburgh, 226 Mervis Hall, Pittsburgh, PA, 15260, United States of America, bbutler@katz.pitt.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Web 2.0 systems, such as wikis and online communities, are often assumed to be spontaneous, emergent, collaborative structures. Yet, because they are fundamentally dependent on the voluntary action of individuals, they are also subject to a variety of competitive forces. In this presentation we examine the nature of the competitive ecologies in Usenet and Wikipedia and discuss the implications for the design and management of social computing systems.
