Title: Quality of Service Modeling for Internet Service Providers

Author: Seyedreza Mousavi, Arizona State University, BA 319, WPC School of Business, Arizona State University, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, smousav4@exchange.asu.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: According to the literature, there are three main contributors to quality of service of internet service providers: traffic and network engineering, network architecture, and interconnection. The current study attempts to develop a model in which market and quality factors are linked together. A panel of experts rated the strength of each relationship in the model. The results revealed how the contributors to the QoS significantly impact the ISP’s profit.
