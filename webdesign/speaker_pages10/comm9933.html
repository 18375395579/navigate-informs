Title: Operational Risk and TQM in the Services Industries

Author: Michael Pinedo, New York University, Stern School of Business, New York, NY, United States, mpinedo@stern.nyu.edu

Year: 2007

Abstract: We present an overview of the Operational RIsk issues in the various services industries, in particular the transportation, health, financial services, and hospitality industries. We discuss the similarities and differences between the various industries and how the pecularities of each affects its procedures and processes. We furthermore discuss how TQM techniques common in manufacturing also can be applied in the services industries.
