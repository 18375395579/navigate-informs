Title: A Portfolio-based Approach for the Analysis of Integrated Telecommunications Service Providers

Author: Steven Powell, Professor Emeritus, California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, CIS Department, 3801 West Temple Avenue, Pomona, CA, 91768, United States of America, srpowell@csupomona.edu

Year: 2012

Abstract: As their existing domestic and foreign markets mature and emerging market opportunities dwindle, telecommunications service providers (TSPs) are seeking growth from consolidating their fixed line and mobile platforms to offer integrated data, video, and voice services, often in multiple play bundles. This paper presents a multidimensional technique for analyzing integrated TSPs strategically based on the degree of market attractiveness and competitive strength their business portfolios possess.
