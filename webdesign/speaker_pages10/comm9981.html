Title: Are Online Seller Recommendations Biased? An Empirical Investigation of OTA Hotel Listings

Author: Ori Marom, RSM Erasmus University, Burgemeester Oudlaan 50, Rotterdam, 3062 PA, Netherlands, omarom@rsm.nl

Coauthor(s): Roman Peskin

Year: 2012

Abstract: Online travel agents such as Expedia and Priceline.com often have tremendous power to direct online buyers to a small subset of available products. Our paper investigates whether OTA selections of top-listed hotels can be regarded as biased from buyers?viewpoint. Our empirical investigation indicates that that while OTA websites provide buyers with valuable search assistance by locating good-valued deals they also influence them to purchase relatively expensive products.
