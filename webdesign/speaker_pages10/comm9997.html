Title: Risk-Sensitive Strategies for Revenue Management Problems

Author: Joern Meissner, Lecturer, Lancaster University Management School, Department of Management Science, Lancaster, LA, LA1 1YX, United Kingdom, joe@meiss.com

Year: 2006

Abstract: Revenue Management systems rely on the quality of forecasts, in particular if premium demand shows mainly close to the end of the planning horizon. We develop risk-sensitive strategies for the situation where some information is known about the quality of past forecasts.
