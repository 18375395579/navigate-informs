Title: SAS Global Academic Program - High-Performance Data Mining with SAS Enterprise Miner

Author: Andr?de Waal, Analytical Consultant, SAS Institute, SAS Campus Dr., Cary, NC, 27513, United States of America, andre.dewaal@sas.com

Year: 2013

Abstract: Very large data sets with millions of rows and computationally intensive model fitting requires massively parallel processing to be effective. High-Performance analytics exploits massively parallel processing to reduce dramatically the run time needed for the construction of analytical models. A brief overview of various computing approaches is given. This workshop then highlights the similarities and differences between the High-Performance nodes in SAS Enterprise Miner 12.1 and the classical nodes that have been available for same time in previous versions of Enterprise Miner. A software demonstration is included.
