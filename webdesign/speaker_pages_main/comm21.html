Title: Lessons from a Master - The Teaching Heritage of Rick Rosenthal

Author: Terry Harrison, Penn State University, University Park, PA, United States of America, tharrison@psu.edu

Year: 2008

Abstract: Rick Rosenthal established a lifelong heritage of excellence and passion in teaching. I reflect on the very concrete lessons that I learned from Rick over a 30 year period as a student, colleague, collaborator and friend.
