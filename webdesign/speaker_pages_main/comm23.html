Title: Constraint Restriction in Constraint Satisfaction Problems

Author: Merav Aharoni, IBM Research - Haifa, Haifa University Campus, Haifa, 31905, Israel, MERAV@il.ibm.com

Coauthor(s): Wesam Ibraheem, Yehuda Naveh, Elena Tsanko

Year: 2012

Abstract: We study the usage of constraint restriction in the process of systematic solving of Constraint Satisfaction Problems. Constraint restriction combines propagation with an arbitrary narrowing of the domains. It can narrow several domains simultaneously, while preserving consistency. We show how to incorporate constraint restriction into a MAC based backtrack search algorithm without compromising the algorithm completeness. We demonstrate several cases where it is useful to apply this technique.
