Title: Efficient Simulation for American Option Pricing Under Stochastic VolatilityAuthor: Farid AitSahlia, Assistant Professor, Industrial and Systems Engineering, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States, farid@ise.ufl.eduYear: 2006Abstract: In this talk I will present a Markov Chain Monte Carlo approach that integrates the Longstaff-Schwartz technique to price American options when the volatility of the underlying is stochastic.
