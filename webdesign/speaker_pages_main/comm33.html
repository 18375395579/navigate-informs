Title: On Distance-based Clique Relaxations in Unit Disk Graphs

Author: Yiming Wang, Texas A&M University, 809 San Saba Drive, College Station, TX, 77845, United States of America, kkelvin@tamu.edu

Coauthor(s): Jeffrey Pattillo, Sergiy Butenko

Year: 2013

Abstract: Distance-based clique relaxations in unit disk graphs arising in wireless networking applications are studied. A 0.5-approximation algorithm for the maximum 2-clique problem in unit disk graphs is developed, and polynomial solvability of the minimum dominating set problem in unit disk graphs of diameter two is established. The same problem is shown to be hard in general diameter-two graphs.
