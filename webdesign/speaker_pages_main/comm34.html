Title: Generation Portfolios and Market Power

Author: Augusto Rupérez Micola, Research Fellow, IMD, Chemin de Bellerive 23, Lausanne, Switzerland, Augusto.RuperezMicola@imd.ch

Coauthor(s): Albert Banal-Estanol

Year: 2006

Abstract: We use an agent-based method to study the relationship between the degree of diversification in the generators?portfolios and their ability to exert market power in capacity-constrained price auctions. We analyse cases in which a generation duopoly own varying amounts of base-load and price-setting capacity, while keeping constant industry concentration and market base-load and price-setting capacities. Our results suggest that portfolio structure is an important determinant of market power.
