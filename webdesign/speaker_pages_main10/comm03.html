Title: The Öresund High-capacity Rail Corridor Standards

Author: Hans Boysen, KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Stockholm, Sweden, heboysen@kth.se

Year: 2013

Abstract: The existing fixed link between Sweden and Denmark and the planned fixed link between Denmark and Germany have adopted a set of new infrastructure standards, which compared with the present limitations in the corridor enable three times higher cubic capacity and 60 % higher tonnage per train, as well as higher freight train speeds. These factors, if also implemented on connecting lines in the corridor, will contribute to dramatically higher freight capacity in mixed traffic.
