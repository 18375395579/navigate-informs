Title: The Role of Services: Pricing, Product Line, and Durability

Author: Gad Allon, Assistant Professor, Kellogg School of Management, 2001 Sheridan Road, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, g-allon@kellogg.northwestern.edu

Coauthor(s): Achal Bassamboo

Year: 2007

Abstract: In recent times, due to the commoditization of goods, many firms often offer services along with the product. We study the role of these services apart from being another revenue source and understand how to manage services. We first explore their role in pricing of the underlying good. Next we study the impact of services on good variety offered by the firm. Lastly, we demonstrate the impact of services on markets of durable goods.
