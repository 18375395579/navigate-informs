Title: Product Upsell through Conditional Upgrades with Strategic Consumers

Author: Yao Cui, University of Michigan, Ross School of Business, 701 Tappan Street, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109, United States of America, cuiyao@umich.edu

Coauthor(s): Ozge Sahin, Izak Duenyas

Year: 2013

Abstract: We study the conditional upgrade strategy that is recently used by firms in travel industry. After the original booking, the firm offers an upgrade so that the consumer can pay a fee to upgrade the product to a high-quality one if there are leftovers at the end of the booking period. Consumers anticipate the upgrade probability when making booking decisions. We characterize the firm's optimal upgrade price and discuss when and why the conditional upgrade strategy is beneficial to the firm.
