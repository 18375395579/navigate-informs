Title: Dynamic Ensemble Prediction with a Classifier-based Distance Metric

Author: Senay Yasar Saglam, PhD Candidate, The University of Iowa, Depatment of Management Sciences, S210 John Pappajohn Business Building, Iowa City, IA, 52242, United States of America, senay-yasarsaglam@uiowa.edu

Coauthor(s): Nick Street

Year: 2013

Abstract: We propose a system for ensemble classification based on a new distance measure between data points. We map data points into a space defined by the class probability estimates from a pool of two-class classifiers. We define the new distance measure to be the Euclidean distance between data points in this space. We dynamically pick classifiers (features) to be used and the k-nearest neighbors of a test point by minimizing the distance between the neighbors and the test point.
