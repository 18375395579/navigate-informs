" 4 - Airlines On-time Performance and Airport Gate Policy Modeling" Jasenka Rakas, Ph.D, University of California at Berkeley, Nextor, 107B McLaughlin Hall, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, jrakas@berkeley.edu" Arnaud Boubert" Valentine Calloud" 2007"This study develops a methodology for the analysis of airlines on-time performance characteristics. Gate delays are computed by using Performance Data Analysis and Reporting System (PDARS) actual arrival times (plus taxi-in times) and the Official Airline Guide (OAG) scheduled gate arrival times. The proposed model suggests new airport gate policy alternatives as a function of gate delay variations that relate to (i) origin airports, (ii) airline characteristics, (iii) utilized runways and (iv) weather conditions."
