" 1 - Machine-learning Functional Connectivity Analysis for the Prediction of Mild Cognitive Impairment" Gianluca Gazzola, PhD Student, Rutgers University, Piscataway, NJ, 08854, United States of America, ggazzola@rci.rutgers.edu" Thomas Grabowski" Paul Borghesani" Art Chaovalitwongse" Myong K Jeong" 2013"We introduce an fMRI data classification framework for the prediction of mild cognitive impairment. We extract an array of novel features from resting-state brain activity to characterize functional associations within and between regions of interest. We PCA-transform and select features to train a Linear Discriminant Analysis classifier. We validate our technique on a data set from the Seattle Longitudinal Study, obtaining accurate predictions for more than 90% of the subjects."
