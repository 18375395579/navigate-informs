" 3 - Stochastic Optimization of Sensor Placement for Diver Detection" Michael Zabarankin, Associate Professor, Stevens Institute of Technology, Hoboken, NJ, United States of America, mzabaran@stevens.edu" Anton Molyboha" 2013"A comprehensive framework for diver detection by a sensor network in an urban harbor is presented. For each detection period, sensor outputs are aggregated into a linear statistic, whose coefficients and detection threshold are optimized for each noise scenario. On a short time scale, the serial dependence of sensor outputs is modeled by a hidden Markov chain, and optimal sensor placement is formulated as a two-stage stochastic optimization problem with respect to different noise scenarios."
