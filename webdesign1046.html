" 1 - Axiomatic Semi-nonparametric Approach to the Estimation of Stochastic Distance Functions" Timo Kuosmanen, Professor, Aalto University School of Economics, PL 21220, Helsinki, Finland, timo.kuosmanen@aalto.fi" Andrew Johnson" Mika Kortelainen" 2012"Modeling joint production of multiple outputs under stochastic noise remains an important challenge in productive efficiency analysis. This paper extends the axiomatic semi-nonparametric StoNED approach to the general multi-input multi-output setting. Our theoretical model contains both the parametric distance functions and the nonparametric DEA as its special cases. Assuming radial inefficiency and noise, consistent estimators of the input and output distance functions are developed."
