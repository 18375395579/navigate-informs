" 4 - Determining OSPF Routing Policies in the Presence of Active Queue Management" Jiaxin Liu, University of Waterloo, 200 University Avenue West, Waterloo, Canada, j69liu@uwaterloo.ca" Stanko Dimitrov" Laura Sanita" 2013"We propose a mathematical model to derive OSPF routing policies in the presence of router active queue management. We discuss the computational complexity of the proposed general model. A modified model is proposed to ensure edge weights are realizable and feasible. We conclude by showing the derived routing policies tend to perform better than those used in a real world network."
