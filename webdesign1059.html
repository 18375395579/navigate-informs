" 3 - Towards an Integrated Demand Management for Emergency Evacuation" Hossam Abdelgawad, PhD Candidate, University of Toronto, 30 Charles St. West, Apt 924, Toronto, ON, M4Y 1R5, Canada, h.abdel.gawad@utoronto.ca" Baher Abdulhai" 2008"We integrated demand scheduling and destination choice to synergize their potential benefits to emergency evacuation.The result is an optimal spatio-temporal evacuation (OSTE) model and tool that helps evacuees to decide where to go, when to go, and how to get there. A case study applying the model to a portion of Downtown Toronto in a simulated environment is also presented. The model results and findings are discussed."
