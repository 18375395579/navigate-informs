" 4 - A Statistical Analysis of Ambulance Travel Times" Dawit Zerom, Associate Professor, California State University, Fullerton, Mihaylo College of Business,P.O.BOX 6848, Fullerton, CA, 92834, United States of America, dzerom@Exchange.FULLERTON.EDU" Susan Budge" Armann Ingolfsson" 2008"An important component of virtually any model of emergency service operations (an analytic model or optimization model) is the travel time of vehicles, especially of those en route to a call. In this paper, we present a semi-parametric statistical model for estimating travel times for emergency services using unique data from ambulance operations of the city of Calgary, Canada."
