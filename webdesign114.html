" 1 - Equivalence of Two-parameter and Measure-valued Fluid Models for G_t/GI/N+GI Queues" Weining Kang, University of Maryland, Baltimore County, 1000 Hilltop Circle, Baltimore, MD, 21250, United States of America, wkang@umbc.edu" Guodong Pang" 2013"In this talk, we show the equivalence of two fluid models for G_t/GI/N+GI queues, the two-parameter fluid model by Whitt (2006) and the measure-valued fluid model by Kang and Ranaman (2010). We also explore connections between fluid models tracking residual times and fluid models tracking elapsed times."
