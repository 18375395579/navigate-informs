" 4 - Dirichlet Process Based Gaussian Mixture Model for Multi-step Forecasting in Complex Systems" Changqing Cheng, Research Assistant, Oklahoma State University, Stillwater, OK, 74078, United States of America, changqing.cheng@okstate.edu" Satish Bukkapatnam" 2012"We employed Dirichlet process based Gaussian mixture models to handle the matrix inversion and stationary covariance assumption issues in Gaussian processes, and investigated it for complex system multiple-step forecasting. The application in atomistic Monte Carlo (MC) simulation of carbon nanotube synthesis process indicates that this forecasting approach can save over 80% of the MC simulation computational expense."
