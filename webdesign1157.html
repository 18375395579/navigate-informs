" 4 - Aggregating Forecasts of Probability from Incoherent and Abstaining Experts" Joel Predd, RAND Corporation, 4570 Fifth Avenue, Suite 600, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States, jpredd@rand.org" Sanjeev Kulkarni" Daniel Osherson" H. Vincent Poor" 2006"In this talk, an algorithm is developed for aggregating forecasts of probability from incoherent and abstaining experts. The algorithm approximately solves an NP-Hard problem, admits a provable performance guarantee, and is demonstrated to be orders of magnitude faster than existing tools when tested on several real-world data sets. The tool is practically relevant to risk analysis, since it enables an elicitation methodology in which judges have freedom to choose the events they assess."
