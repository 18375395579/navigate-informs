" 1 - New Developments in Finding Maximum 2-clubs in Graphs" Foad Mahdavi Pajouh, Oklahoma State University, Industrial Engineering and Management, 322 Engineering North, Stillwater, Ok, 74075, United States of America, mahdavi@okstate.edu" Illya Hicks" Baski Balasundaram" 2012"A k-club is an induced subgraph of diameter at most k which is a distance-based graph-theoretic generalization of a clique. The k-clubs can be used to model clusters in social networks, biological networks and internet graphs. This talk will outline the research challenges associated with k-club model because of its non-hereditary nature. New complexity results, theoretical results concerning the 2-club polytope and preliminary numerical results will be presented."
