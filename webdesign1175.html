" 2 - Determining Optimal Therapy for Diabetic Patients. Evidence from the U.S. Department of Veteran Affairs" Vishal Ahuja, PhD Candidate, University of Chicago, Booth Business School, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States of America, vahuja@chicagobooth.edu" John Birge" 2012"We investigate various treatments for Type 2 Diabetes in adults using data from the Veterans Health Administration. We analyze dynamic patterns of glucose lowering therapies, and evaluate the risks of associated diabetes outcomes. We propose a predictive model to evaluate effectiveness of various glucose-lowering therapies and recommend optimal treatment based on patient characteristics. The paper addresses confounding measures such as physician bias and unobservable patient characteristics."
