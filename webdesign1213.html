" 2 - Enhanced Piecewise Least Squares Approach for Diagnosis of Ill-Conditioned Multi-station Assembly with Compliant Parts" Darek Ceglarek, Professor, 3Dept. of Industrial and Systems Engineering, University of Wisconsin-Madison, Madison, WI, 53706, United States, darek@engr.wisc.edu" Zhenyu (James) Kong" Atul Tripathi" Prakash" 2007"Assembly process-induced dimensional variation has a significant impact on product quality and functionality. Furthermore, part compliance/flexibility frequently results in ill-conditioned assembly systems posing enormous challenges to process control and fault failure diagnosis. We propose an Enhanced PLS-based diagnostic methodology which searches for a set of latent vectors with the search constrained by assembly response function–the Stream-of-Variation (SOVA) model of assembly system–and which performs decomposition of response based on the end-of-line-measurements."
