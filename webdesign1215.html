" 1 - Statistical Uncertainty Analysis for Stochastic Simulation" Wei Xie, northwestern university, 2145 sheridan Rd. c210, evanston, Il, 60208, United States of America, weixie2013@u.northwestern.edu" Barry Nelson" Russell Barton" 2013"When we use simulation to evaluate the performance of a stochastic system, the input models are often estimated from real-world data. We derive a confidence interval that accounts for both simulation and input-model uncertainty by using a metamodel-assisted bootstrapping approach. Our approach is rigorously supported with both theoretical analysis and an empirical study."
