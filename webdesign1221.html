" 1 - Product Comparison Networks for Competitive Analysis of Online Word-of-mouth" Zhu Zhang, Assistant Professor, University of Arizona, 1130 E Helen St, Rm 430, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, zhuzhang@email.arizona.edu" 2012"By integrating network analysis with text sentiment mining techniques, we propose product comparison networks as a novel construct, computed from consumer product reviews. To test the validity of the therefrom derived product ranking measures, we conduct an empirical study based on a digital camera dataset from Amazon.com. The results demonstrate significant effect of network-based measures on product sales."
