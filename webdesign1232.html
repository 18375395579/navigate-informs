" 2 - A Frequentist Assessment of Bayesian Inclusion Probabilities for Quantiative Trait Loci" Edward Boone, Assitant Professor of Statistics, Department of Statistical Sciences and Operations Research, Virginia Commonwealth University, 1001 W. Main St, Richmond, VA, 23284, United States of America, elboone@vcu.edu" Albert Lee" 2008"Bayesian inclusion probabilities have become a popular tool for variable assessment. This presentation shows how a frequentist may evaluate Bayesian inclusion probabilities. This evaluation looks at both restricted and unrestricted model spaces and develops a framework that can be utilized for inclusion probabilities that preserve Type I error rates. This framework is applied to an analysis of the Arabidopsis thaliana with respect to determining QTL associated with cotelydon opening angle."
