" 2 - Integrating Real Options and Decision Analysis: Case Studies From the Energy Sector" Eric Chung, Senior Consultant, Strategic Decisions Group, 4548 NE 27th Avenue, Portland, OR, 97211, United States, EChung@sdg.com" 2007"SDG assists many energy companies with major environmental investment decisions. While we typically use a traditional decision analysis framework to address such investments, we have found it valuable to apply real option analysis as a parallel, complementary tool. In this presentation, we will revisit differences between real options and decision analysis, build an integrated framework, and review a case study where we applied this technique to an emissions control investment decision."
