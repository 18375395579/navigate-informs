" 3 - Stochastic Patient-centered Long-term Care Network Design" Leyla Ozsen, San Francisco State University, 1600 Holloway Ave., San Francisco, CA, United States of America, lozsen@sfsu.edu" Paul Intrevado" 2013"A multistage stochastic network facility model for long-term care is presented as a mixed-integer program, capturing patient quality-of-life. Stochastic patient flows and demands are examined. An illustrative example based on empirical data is presented and results discussed."
