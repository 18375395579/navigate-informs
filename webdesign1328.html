" 4 - Error Minimization in Healthcare Settings Through the Dynamic Analysis of Near-misses" Jose Zayas-Castro, Professor & Chair, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Avenue, Industrial Engineering Department, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, josezaya@eng.usf.edu" Laila Cure" Peter Fabri" 2007"There are about 98,000 annual U.S. deaths due to medical errors. The potential to reduce this error exists by conducting systematic analysis of near-misses. A dynamic methodology is proposed to report, classify and analyze near-misses to then assess the likelihood of the occurrence of errors and trigger a timely alarm."
