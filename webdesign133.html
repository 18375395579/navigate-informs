" 1 - Two-stage Controlled Fractional Factorial Screening for Simulation Experiments" Bruce Ankenman, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Rd., Evanston, IL, United States, ankenman@northwestern.edu" Hong Wan" 2007"Factor screening with statistical control of Type I error and Power makes sense for simulation experiments since they have random error, but can be run automatically. Here, a fractional factorial design in combination with a two-stage procedure for controlling power is proposed and compared to other available methods."
