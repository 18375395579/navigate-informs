" 4 - ***NO SHOW***Challenges of Deploying an Open Access System for Outpatient Clinics via Process Mining" Ashkan Sharabiani, University of Illinois at Chicago, 842 W Taylor Street, 4209 W SEL Building, Chicago, IL, United States of America, ashara2@uic.edu" Houshang Darabi" 2013"Recent advances in process mining (PM) have provided system analysts with a set of techniques for extracting fact-based patterns of system’s processes from data. The presentation is about applying PM techniques in an outpatient clinic in an underrepresented district of Chicago to explore the possibility of deploying an open access system. After discovering the underlying processes, the open access system will be tested against the traditional pre-scheduled system using a simulation model"
