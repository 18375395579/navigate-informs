" 1 - Introduction to Risk Trading in Energy Investments and Markets - Endogenous Uncertainty" Daniel Ralph, Professor of Operations Research, University of Cambridge, Judge Business School, Cambridge, United Kingdom, d.ralph@jbs.cam.ac.uk" Gauthier de Maere d'Aertrycke" Yves Smeers" Andreas Ehrenmann" 2013"Electricity generators face huge uncertainties, relating to carbon emissions, that were not on the horizon in the early days of deregulation in the USA. We survey recent progress in models of risky power plant investments as an equilibrium process, where power generation and sales take place in a stochastic environment; and agents are risk averse & use financial products to offset their risk. Coherent risk measures are powerful in linking risk aversion to standard risk neutral equilibrium models"
