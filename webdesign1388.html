" 2 - An Analytic Model to Evaluate a 3PL Partner" Ozden Bayazit, Associate Professor of Supply Chain Management, Central Washington University-Lynnwood, 20000 68th Avenue W., Lynnwood, WA, 98036, United States of America, bayazito@cwu.edu" Birsen Karpak" 2012"This study extends a framework, a simple Analytical Network model, developed in literature to evaluate third party logistic companies.
