" 2 - A Method to Generate Image for SPC by Point Cloud Data" Ketai He, Associate Professor, University of Science and Technology Beijing, No. 30, Xueyuan Rd., Haidian Dist., Beijing, 100083, China, ketaih@vt.edu" Jaime Camelio" Fadel Megahed" 2012"Image analysis is an important method of statistical process control in manufacturing. It is difficult to obtain suitable images for defect detection and analysis when products have irregular surfaces. Three-dimension surface scanner can get the surface information, but the data is difficult to analyze directly due to massive data points. We propose a method to generate image by point cloud data from surface scanner, which can be used in defect analysis."
