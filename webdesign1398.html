" 2 - Designing and Pricing Services Based on Customer-Perceived Value: An Airline Company Feasibility Study" Francesco Moresino, Professor, Haute Ecole de Gestion de Genève, HESSO, 7 Route de Drize, Genève, 1227, Switzerland, francesco.moresino@hesge.ch" Angela Lombardi" Emmanuel Fragnière" 2013"Revenue management models heavily rely on the carrier’s historical booking patterns to forecast passenger demand and to recommend optimal inventory controls. We are presenting a new integrated model for pricing airline tickets that takes into account the perceived value of multiple services in different competitive environments. Our integrated model includes a classical aggregate planner for services as well as a share of choice model and can be implemented as a decision support tool."
