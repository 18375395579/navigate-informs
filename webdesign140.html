" 1 - Optimal Location of Public Electric Vehicle Charging Infrastructure" Ramteen Sioshansi, Assistant Professor, The Ohio State University, 240 Baker Systems, 1971 Neil Avenue, Columbus, OH, 43215, United States of America, sioshansi.1@osu.edu" Xiaomin Xi" Vincenzo Marano" 2012"We develop a simulation optimization based approach to optimize the location of public electric vehicle charging infrastructure.
