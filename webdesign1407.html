" 2 - A Charter Air Service to Minimize Total Cost of Conference Travel" Anne Goodchild, Assistant Professor, University of Washington, Dept. of Civil and Environmental Eng., 121E More Hall, Seattle, WA, 98195-2700, United States of America, annegood@u.washington.edu" Gautam Gupta" Mark Hansen" 2008"The operations of an air charter service for conference sports are modeled in competition with existing scheduled travel options. The demand subset and the routing and scheduling of aircraft are done in the same framework. Through this modeling we develop an innovative method for formulating and accommodating continuous time windows and competitive market dynamics. We apply the model to a specific sports conference, which provides insight into the market for such a service."
