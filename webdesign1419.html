" 1 - Managing Complexity and Uncertainty in Offshore Outsourced New Product Development Projects" Saikat Chaudhuri, Assistant Professor of Management, University of Pennsylvania, The Wharton School, 3730 Walnut Street, Philadelphia, PA, 19104, United States of America, saikatc@wharton.upenn.edu" Abhijit Mandal" 2013"Motivated by its rising prominence, we investigate to what extent the offshore outsourcing approach can effectively transfer to complex and uncertain knowledge-intensive work like R&D, by developing a simulation model based on a sample of such projects by a leading vendor. We contrast the global delivery model with a consulting model, and find that each approach is more efficacious at handling different types of tasks, bearing implications for notions of firm boundaries and organizational forms."
