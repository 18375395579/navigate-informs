" 2 - A Transfer Learning Approach for Network Modeling" Shuai Huang, Assistant Professor, University of South Florida, 4202 E Fowler Ave, ENC 2509, Tampa, FL, 33620, United States of America, shuai.huang.ie@gmail.com" Jing Li" 2013"Network models have been widely used in many subject areas to characterize the interactions between physical entities. A typical problem is to identify the network for multiple related tasks. We proposed a transfer learning approach that can leverage the knowledge gained during the modeling of one task to help better model another task, by adopting a Bayesian hierarchical model framework and L1-regularization. We used this model for biomarker discovery for Alzheimer's disease from fMRI data."
