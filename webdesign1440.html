" 2 - The Economics of Nosocomial Infection Controls in Hospitals" Reidar Hagtvedt, hagtvedt@gatech.edu" Paul Griffin" Pinar Keskinocak" 2007"We examine the economics of strategies to control nosocomial (hospital acquired) infections. Data from one hospital, combined with results from the literature, provide the basis for a simulation of the incidence of nosocomial infections. This model allows costs and benefits of different approaches of infection control to be assessed."
