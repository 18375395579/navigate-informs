" 4 - Behavioral Decision Making and Inventory Models" Xuanming Su, Assistant Professor, University of California, Haas School of Business, Berkeley, CA, 94720, United States, xuanming@haas.berkeley.edu" 2006"Recent experimental findings have highlighted several anomalies in inventory models: decision makers do not behave as normative theory predicts.
