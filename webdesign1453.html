" 2 - Optimal Control Policies for Ambulance Diversion Decisions" Esma Gel, Arizona State University, Department of Industrial Engineering, Tempe, AZ, 85287, United States of America, esma.gel@asu.edu" Adrian Ramirez" Baykal Hafizoglu" John Fowler" 2012"We consider optimal ambulance diversion control policies with the objective of minimizing patients' waiting time higher than a recommended safety time threshold. We model the problem using a Markov decision process formulation, and characterize the optimal ambulance diversion decisions. We compare the performance of our policy with simple heuristics using a simulation model, and analyze the value of information in ambulance diversion decisions."
