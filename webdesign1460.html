" 2 - Causal Inference for Scale-up in Nano-manufacturing" Tirthankar Dasgupta, Assistant Professor, Harvard University, Science Center, 1 Oxford Street, Cambridge, MA, 02138-2901, United States of America, dasgupta@stat.harvard.edu" Qiang Huang" 2012"Statistical inference for nanostructure growth models is typically drawn from small scale laboratory-level experiments. To scale up nanostructure growth, it is necessary to expand the scope of inference to super-populations which mimic actual manufacturing conditions. A framework for causal inference, based on potential outcomes, can provide a solution to this challenging problem."
