" 2 - ****LATE CANCELLATION***The Effects of Demand Subsidies and R&D on Cost Reductions for a Pre- commercial Low-carbon Energy Technology" Erin Baker, University of Massachusetts, Amherst, 220 ELab, Amherst, MA, United States of America, edbaker@ecs.umass.edu" 2008"The representation of technological change in large energy-economic model remains highly stylized relative to the state-of-the-art of understanding about the economics of innovation. We address this by combining expert elicitations with a bottom-up manufacturing cost model to simulate the cost reductions that result from technology-push and demand-pull technology policies for organic photovoltaics."
