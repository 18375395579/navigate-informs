Presidente Wilson, 118, Room 1110, Rio de Janeiro, 20030020, Brazil, autran@ibmecrj.br" Renata Andrade" 2012"The key objective of this paper is to show, through a case study, how managers working for a financial organization in Brazil were evaluated in their performance by using the AHP method. Based on the success of the study the organization has decided that the AHP method should definitely be considered as the analytical tool to be utilized in their future performance evaluation processes."
