" 1 - ***NO SHOW***Regression Model Estimating Annual Hotel Revenue" Christopher Keller, Assistant Professor, College of Business, East Carolina University, Greenville, NC, 27854, United States of America, kellerc@ecu.edu" 2013"This research uses partial daily occupancy and rate data to construct a regression model for estimating future annual revenues in order to evaluate the purchase of a hotel property. This presentation considers a variety of alternative modeling assumptions to provide a robust sensitivity analysis for evaluation and ultimately negotiation."
