" 1 - A Framework for Understanding Uncertainty in Graphene Growth Process Modeling" Sobambo Sosina, Graduate Research Assistant, Harvard University, 1 Oxford St, 7th floor, Cambridge, MA, 02138, United States of America, sosina@fas.harvard.edu" Tirthankar Dasgupta" Qiang Huang" Jian Wu" 2013"Graphene has many important properties and has been identified as a very efficient and cheap ingredient in a lot of industrial applications. Unfortunately, there has not been much work done in understanding the kinetics that drive the graphene growth process. We propose a rate-based model that correctly quantifies the uncertainty in our model parameters. We further extend the graphene shape model to account for possible spatial dependence using a lattice spatial model."
