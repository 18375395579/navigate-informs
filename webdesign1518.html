" 3 - Optimal Tolls for Converting General Purpose Lanes to High Occupancy Toll (HOT) Lanes" Chaitanya Paleti, Purdue University, West Lafayette, ID, United States of America, cpaletis@purdue.edu" Srinivas Peeta" Sushant Sharma" 2012"HOT lanes have a potential to generate revenue and relieve congestion. This has encouraged conversion of general purpose lanes into HOT lanes. In this study, we propose a methodology using agent based modeling for designing optimal tolls for HOT lanes by factoring the travel time and equity issues."
