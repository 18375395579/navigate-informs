" 3 - Analysis of Destructive Degradation Test for a Product with Random Degradation Initiation Time" Ye Zhang, University of Arizona, 1557 N Santa Rita Ave, Tucson, AZ, 85719, United States of America, yezhang1@email.arizona.edu" Haitao Liao" 2013"In this paper, we develop two delayed degradation models based on destructive degradation test data to evaluate the reliability of a product with a random degradation initiation time. Fixed-effects and random-effects gamma processes are considered, respectively. EM algorithm is implemented to calculate the MLE in both models. Monte Carlo approach is used to obtain the confidence intervals."
