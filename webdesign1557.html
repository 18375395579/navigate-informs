" 3 - A Data-driven Prescriptive Analytics Approach to Optimize a Portfolio" Durai Sundaramoorthi, Washington University in St. Louis, Olin Business School, Saint Louis, MO, 63141, United States of America, dsundaramoorthi@gmail.com" Jay Rosenberger" Aihong Wen" Victoria C Chen" 2013"Classification and Regression Trees (CART) is a method of predictive analytics that attempts to predict or classify using a set of continuous or categorical variables. In this research, using CART, a simulation model has been developed to observe transition states of stocks in a portfolio. Utilizing design of experiments, objective values are observed from the simulation at selected portfolio points. We then approximate the objective function to use in prescriptive analytics."
