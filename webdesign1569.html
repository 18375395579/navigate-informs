" 2 - Airport Taxi-out Time Prediction using Approximate Dynamic" Poornima Balakrishna, George Mason Univ, 4400 Univ Dr MS 4A6, Fairfax, VA, 22030, United States of America, pbalakri@gmu.edu" Rajesh Ganesan" 2008"The talk presents case studies of taxi-out time prediction at Detroit, Washington Reagan, and Tampa International airports using approximate dynamic programming (ADP). The talk focuses on the quality improvement in airport surface management systems by better departure planning that can be achieved through accurate taxi-out time predications. The talk also points out the advantages of ADP over traditional regression and queueing models."
