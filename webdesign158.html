" 2 - Motivations, Uses, and Benefits of Locomotive Models at CSX Transportation" Andy John, AVP - Locomotive Planning & Utilization, CSX Transportation, 3019 Warrington Street, Jacksonville, FL, 32254, United States of America, Andy_John@csx.com" Kamalesh Somani" 2008"CSX Transportation is partnering with Innovative Scheduling to develop and implement several locomotive models and implementing them. In this presentation, we will describe the motivation for developing these models, how we developed them, how we are using them, and what are the benefits derived from the use of these models. We will also share with the audience the lessons learnt in this process."
