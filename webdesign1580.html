" 2 - Stochastic Choice Models of Demand and Applications to Pricing" Catalina Stefanescu, Assistant Professor of Decision Sciences, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, CStefanescu@london.edu" Guillermo Gallego" 2006"In this paper we investigate several classes of stochastic models for customer choice, and their calibration from shopping data. We discuss different specifications of these models in terms of choice attributes, and study the implications for ticket pricing. These methodologies are exemplified with a large data set of customer purchases of airline tickets."
