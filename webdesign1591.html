" 1 - Time Series Forecasting for Nonlinear and Nonstationary Systems: A Comparative Study" Changqing Cheng, Oklahoma State University, 322 Engineering North, Stillwater, OK, 74074, United States of America, changqing.cheng@okstate.edu" Trung Le" Satish Bukkapatnam" Hui Yang" Zhenyu (James) Kong" 2013"Complex systems usually demonstrate nonlinear and nonstationary dynamics. Time series data from those systems can represent casual and dynamic information about the underlying dynamics, and provide a unique way to monitor the system state evolution. We reviewed the recently developed models for nonlinear nonstationary time series prediction, and provided a comparative study."
