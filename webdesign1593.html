" 2 - Selecting a Quality Set of Objectives" Ralph Keeney, Research Professor Emeritus, Duke University, 101 Lombard Street, #704W, San Francisco, CA, 94111, United States of America, keeneyr@aol.com" 2013"Selecting a quality set of objectives for a decision is itself a decision with quality defined by the planned uses of those objectives. Several potential uses are presented. Concepts and procedures to create and select a quality set of objectives for different uses are presented and illustrated with several cases."
