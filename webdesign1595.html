" 4 - Data-driven Optimization for Minimizing the Environmental Impact of Airport Deicing Activities" Victoria Chen, University of Texas at Arlington, Industrial & Manufacturing Systems Eng., Campus Box 19017, Arlington, TX, 76019-0017, United States, vchen@uta.edu" Prashant Tarun" Dan Bergman" Jay Rosenberger" Huiyuan Fan" Seoung Bum Kim" 2007"A prototype is presented for Dallas-Fort Worth International Airport (D/FW). Deicing and anti-icing fluids have a high concentration of glycol that can lead to increased bacterial growth in the receiving waters and reduced dissolved oxygen. Our prototype uses actual data for two primary tasks: (1) Construction of simulation model and (2) Development of a stochastic dynamic programming based optimization tool to dynamically assign airplanes to deicing pad locations."
