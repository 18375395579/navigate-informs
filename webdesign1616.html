" 2 - Effectiveness of Robust Optimization in IMPT for Head and Neck Cancer" Wei Liu, Assistant Professor, The University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center, 1515 Holcombe Blvd. Y2.5605, Unit 0094, Houston, TX, 77030, United States of America, franklin_liu1019@hotmail.com" Xiaorong Zhu" Steven Frank" Radhe Mohan" 2013"IMPT is highly sensitive to uncertainties in beam range and patient setup. In the worst case and nominal scenarios, robustly optimized plans showed better target coverage, improved dose homogeneity, and lower or equivalent dose to organs at risk (OARs). Robust optimization provided significantly more robust dose distributions to targets and organs than conventional optimization in H&N using IMPT. Reduction of PTV and planning directly based on CTV provided better or equivalent OAR sparing."
