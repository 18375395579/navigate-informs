" 2 - Analysis of a Threshold-based Node Sleep Scheduling Policy for Energy Harvesting Sensor Networks" Arupa Mohapatra, PhD Student, Texas A&M University, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, arupa@tamu.edu" Natarajan Gautam" 2012"Autonomous wireless sensor networks with energy harvesting nodes are gaining prominence in long-lived remote surveillance applications. Node sleep scheduling is a popular approach to ensure “energy-neutral?operation in these networks. We present a fluid model based analysis of performance of such a network under a threshold based sleep scheduling policy."
