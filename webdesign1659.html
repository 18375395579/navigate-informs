" 1 - Comparison of Procurement Options in the Healthcare Industry and Determining the Degree of Innovations" Arka Bhattacharya, apbhatta@mail.usf.edu" Kingsley Reeves" 2007"We compare various purchasing models used within the medical device and equipment supply chain based on their impact on patient cost and patient access to technological innovations. The various models analyzed include self-sourcing, exclusive use of a large national group purchasing organization (GPO), exclusive use of a regional GPO, and a hybrid structure employing both a regional and national GPO."
