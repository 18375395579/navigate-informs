" 2 - Analytical Bounds on Parameterized Improving Hit-and-Run for Adaptive Performance Enhancements" Wei Wang, Doctoral Student, University of Washington, Industrial Engineering, Box 352650, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States, wangwei@u.washington.edu" Archis Ghate" Zelda Zabinsky" 2007"We incorporate the feature of a Hit-and-Run based small world network model into Improving Hit-and-Run to obtain a parameterized IHR algorithm.
