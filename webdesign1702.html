" 3 - Nonlinear Problems in Efficiency Analysis under Multiple Perspectives" Xiaopeng Yang, University of Toronto, 200 College Street, Toronto, ON, Canada, xiaopeng.yang@utoronto.ca" Hiroshi Morita" Joseph Paradi" 2013"Multiple perspectives have different input/output classifications, which always result in nonlinear problems in either efficiency evaluation or improvement processes. To address such problems, we propose a new DEA model by incorporating SQP method into actual calculation. An application in banking sector is used to validate our model."
