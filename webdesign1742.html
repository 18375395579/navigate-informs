" 2 - Multi Attribute Utility Functions with Incomplete Preference Information" Luis Montiel, PhD, The University of Texas as Austin, 1 University Station, Austin, TX, 78703, United States of America, lvmontiel@gmail.com" Eric Bickel" 2012"We present a new method to explore multi-attribute utility functions when the decision maker's preferences are incompletely specified. We consider the multi-linear utility function and use a new simulation procedure called JDSIM to perform sensitivity analysis over the space of all possible utilities that match partially specified preferences. Finally, we present a case of study and illustrate the power of this procedure under relative elicitation."
