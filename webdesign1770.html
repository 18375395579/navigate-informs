" 1 - Outpatient Clinic Simulation Metamodeling" Mark Isken, Associate Professor of MIS, Oakland University, School of Business Administration, Rochester, MI, 48309, United States, isken@oakland.edu" 2006"We describe simulation metamodel development for use in an outpatient capacity planning tool. Issues discussed include comparison of polynomial regression models with nonlinear models containing queuing related basis functions, prediction of performance percentiles and prediction of transient performance measures."
