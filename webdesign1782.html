" 4 - Flow Modeling and Prediction of Heterogeneous Patients Using Semi-Markov Mixture Clustering" Chitta Ranjan, Georgia Institute of Technology, 755 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, nk.chitta.ranjan@gatech.edu" Kamran Paynabar" Jonathan Helm" 2013"Planning methods in hospitals rely on accurate estimation of patient flow for census predictions. We propose a hybrid clustering-classification prediction model for better estimation of patient flow. Semi-Markov model-based clustering is developed to cluster a heterogeneous mix of patients into homogeneous groups of patients with similar flow patterns. The clustering model is then linked with a classifier to predict the patient flow based on patient attributes."
