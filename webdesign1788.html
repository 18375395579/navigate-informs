" 3 - The Simulation Start-up Problem: Performance Comparison of Recent Solution Procedures" James Wilson, Professor, Edward P. Fitts Department of Industrial Engineering, North Carolina State University, Raleigh, NC, 27695-7906, United States of America, jwilson@ncsu.edu" Anup Mokashi" Emily Lada" 2012"We summarize the results of a comprehensive experimental performance comparison of some recent procedures for solving the simulation start-up problem, including the methods incorporated in MSER-5, N-Skart, WASSP, and SBatch. Substantial differences in performance are observed, especially in comparisons of MSER-5 with the latter three procedures."
