" 3 - Military Logistics Planning Prototype" Allan Manning, SAS, w.a.manning@sas.com" Paul Grant" 2007"Logistics planners must ensure the USAF, Army, Navy supply requirements are met while contending with numerous transportation and operational constraints. Using COTS software, SAS consultants developed a logistics optimization prototype called GOLDSS. By linking existing DoD legacy systems, into a new optimization model , an interactive interface was designed to support strategist planners as they analyze and evaluate the results of the optimization model."
