State Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47907, United States of America, ksarica@purdue.edu" Wallace Tyner" 2012"The purpose of the study is to compare the current US climate policy Clean Energy Standard for electricity, Renewable Fuel Standard for biofuels, and Corporate Average Fuel Economy (CAFE) standard with carbon tax. We make use of EPA MARKAL model which has been extensively modified by adding land supply from GTAP for biomass, biochemical and thermochemical conversion technologies for cellulosic fuels, new shale gas incorporated natural gas supply curves, and accurately modeling the CAFE standard."
