" 4 - Modelling the Value of Windpower in an "Ontario-like" Electricity System: A MPEC Approach" Scott Rogers, Dept of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering, University of Toronto, Toronto, M5S 3G8, Canada, rogers@mie.utoronto.ca" Paul Dabrowski" 2007"This paper describes an intermediate term planning model for valuing wind power generation in a well-balanced hydro-thermal system with a Poolco kind of market. Traditional approaches have used displacement cost or market price. Using the market models we describe alternative approaches based on the properties of the market and illustrate their usefulness through an MPEC model of an "Ontario-like" system."
