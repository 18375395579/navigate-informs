" 1 - Excel-based Decision Support Systems for Primary Care Vaccinations" John Fontanesi, Director, Center for Management Science in Health, University of California, San Diego, 9500 Gilman Drive, #0821, La Jolla, Ca, 92093, United States of America, jfontanesi@ucsd.edu" Debra Bowers" 2008"Provider Offices are presented with difficult optimization issues in deciding what combination of strategies to employ in providing influenza vaccinations. We will describe and demonstrate an Excel-based Decision Support System presently used in a number of provider offices to balance costs, number of individuals vaccinated and customer satisfaction. The DSS has embedded optimization routines that permit “as is?and “what if?analysis as well as planning and staff training guidelines"
