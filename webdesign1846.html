" 2 - Modeling Information Sharing and Cooperation in Multi-agent Systems" David Jeffcoat, Senior Research Engineer, AFRL/MNGN, 101 W. Eglin Blvd, Eglin AFB, FL, 32542, United States, david.jeffcoat@eglin.af.mil" Paul Krokhmal" 2007"This research is concerned with the modeling and analysis of cooperation within a heterogeneous, multi-agent team. Our focus is on a special form of cooperation, namely information sharing. The objective is to develop mathematical models for cooperation via information sharing in complex large-scale systems. The approach is based on the techniques of discrete-state, continuous-time Markov chain models. The primary application context is the search for targets in a combat scenario."
