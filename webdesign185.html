" 2 - A Relationship between DEA and Sabermetrics in Evaluation of Baseball Players" Hirotsu Nobuyoshi, United States of America, nhirotsu@hotmail.com" James Cochran" 2013"Data envelopment analysis (DEA) has been applied to evaluate baseball players' performance, and Sabermetrics are widely used to find characteristics of players. To understand the relationship between DEA and Sabermetrics will be an interesting topic in evaluation of players. This talk will show the relationship using data of Major League Baseball, and discuss how DEA approach could help to get a clue to find new Sabermetric measures."
