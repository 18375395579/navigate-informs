" 4 - The Development of a Spatially and Temporally Scalable Dynamic Traffic Assignment Model" Yi-Chang Chiu, Assistant Professor, University of Arizona, PO Box 210072, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States, chiu@utep.edu" Jorge A. Villalobos" Pitu Mirchandani" 2006"We present the theoretical development and implementation of a spatially and temporally scalable DTA (STS-DTA) model. The STS-DTA model utilizes a cluster-computing methodology that enables flexible scaling of the network. The Anisotropic Mesoscopic Simulation (AMS) model provides temporal scalability in traffic simulation resolution. Model semantics and research issues are discussed."
