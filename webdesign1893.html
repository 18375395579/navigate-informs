" 2 - Analysis and Enhancement of Practice-based Methods for Commodity Storage Real Option Management" Nicola Secomandi, Associate Professor, Carnegie Mellon Unversity, Tepper School of Business, 5000 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA, 15213, United States of America, ns7@andrew.cmu.edu" 2013"Practitioners manage commodity storage assets as real options using the rolling intrinsic and rolling basket of spread options heuristic policies. This research provides novel structural and numerical support for the use of these policies and enhances them by developing a simple and effective dual upper bound to be used in conjunction with these policies."
