" 4 - A Stochastic Dynamic Programming Approach for Transmission Investments" Enzo Sauma, P. Universidad Catolica de Chile, Ave. Vicuña Mackenna #4860, Santiago, RM, Chile, esauma@ing.puc.cl" 2007"We reformulate a previously-developed static model of transmission investments in restructured electricity systems using a stochastic dynamic framework. In particular, we study the optimal transmission investment strategy of a network planner based on equilibrium models characterizing the dynamic competitive interaction among oligopolistic generation firms."
