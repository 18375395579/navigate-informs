" 1 - Planning for the Future - A Model of Cardiac Surgeons in Canada" Sonia Vanderby, Assistant Professor, University of Saskatchewan, 57 Campus Drive, Saskatoon, SK, S7N 5A9, Canada, sonia.vanderby@usask.ca" Michael Carter" 2012"We present a model simulating the workforce within a single specialty at a national level which includes students training to enter the profession, providing a tool that would help to inform future resource planning. This presentation includes details of the model, developed using system dynamics modeling, and a demonstration of it using the example of cardiac surgeons in Canada, and analyzes the effect of provider motivations on system outcomes."
