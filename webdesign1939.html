" 2 - Enterprise Scale Portfolio Analysis at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration" Richard Moynihan, Principal Operations Research Analyst, MITRE Economic and Decision Analysis Center, 202 Burlington Road, M/S M230, Bedford, MA, 01730, United States of America, moynihan@mitre.org" Robert Reining" Patricia Salamone" Brian Schmidt" 2008"We describe a portfolio analysis approach to modeling the value of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration’s investment options that was used to select portfolios of environmental observing systems that best satisfy NOAA’s mission-critical observing requirements at target budget constraints. The approach is based on capability-based performance measures that satisfy recent Government acquisition and planning regulations."
