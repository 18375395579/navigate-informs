" 1 - Graph Theory-based Coarse-graining Potential Function Fitting for Atomistic Simulations" Satish Bukkapatnam, Professor, Oklahoma State University, 322 Engineering North, Oklahoma State University, Stillwater, OK, United States of America, satish.t.bukkapatnam@okstate.edu" Changqing Cheng" 2013"Atomistic molecular dynamics and Monte Carlo simulations have been widely used to study nanomanufacturing processes, but the computational overhead has limited the simulations only to the initial nano-synthesis stage. A graph theory-based coarse-graining potential fitting approach was investigated to coarse grain the atomistic simulation, and realize large-scale simulation of the nanomanufacturing processes."
