" 2 - Using Spreadsheet to Optimize Transportation Problem in a Large Consumer and Industrial Company" Xu Yang, Graduate Research Assistant, University of Louisville, Department of Industrial Engineering, J B Speed School of Engineering, Louisville, KY, 40292, United States of America, x0yang07@louisville.edu" Sunderesh S Heragu" Gerald W Evans" 2008"A large transportation model is formulated in spreadsheet and solved by XPRESS Solver Engine on Premium Solver Platform. The objective is to minimize total shipping cost in a large consumer and industrial company. The solution goes into a decision support tool which is also a spreadsheet. This user-friendly tool is developed for operations managers to make a final decision based on optimal solution given by optimization model as well as some real-world considerations."
