" 3 - Tractable Functional Response Modeling using Nonstationary Covariance Functions" Matthew Plumlee, Ph.D. Student, The Georgia Institute of Technology, 765 Ferst Drive, NW, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States of America, mplumlee@gatech.edu" 2012"Gaussian process models have become increasingly popular to model functional responses. Fitting these models requires computationally expensive matrix decomposition, a problem exacerbated with functional responses. Separability assumptions on the covariance function eases the computational burden. This work models these functions without the separability assumption while maintaining the computational advantages."
