" 2 - Information Sharing and Cooperation in Multi-Agent Systems" David Jeffcoat, Eglin AFB, jeffcoatd@eglin.af.mil" 2007"This research is concerned with the modeling and analysis of cooperation within a heterogeneous, multi-agent team. Our focus is on a special form of cooperation, namely information sharing. The objective is to develop mathematical models for cooperation via information sharing in complex large-scale systems. The approach is based on the techniques of discrete-state, continuous-time Markov chain models. The primary application context is the search for targets in a combat scenario."
