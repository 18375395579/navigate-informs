" 1 - Pool Points for Peeps: A Network Design Model for a Candy Supply Chain" Lawrence V. Snyder, Assistant Professor, Lehigh University, 200 West Packer Ave., Mohler Lab, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, larry.snyder@lehigh.edu" 2008"We discuss a network design model built and implemented for a candy company with a national distribution network. The model chooses which pool points (distribution centers) to use, which customers to serve direct and which to serve LTL, which shipping arcs to use (incurring a fixed cost), and the timing and routing of trucks through the network. We present the model and solution methods and discuss data-processing challenges and implications of the solution for the company's supply chain."
