" 2 - A Surrogate Model Approximating the Performance of the Compass Model" Gary Kochenberger, University of Colorado at Denver, Denver, CO, gary.kochenberger@cudenver.edu" Manuel Laguna" Candy Brinkman" Jim Kelly" 2007"COMPASS is an agent-based model designed to simulate the flow of sailors through the sequence of career decisions. While COMPASS holds great promise as a policy evaluation tool, it is, by its very nature, computationally cumbersome. To address these computational issues, we are developing a surrogate model to approximate COMPASS with enough accuracy to provide good starting points for policy variables."
