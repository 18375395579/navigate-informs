" 1 - A Hybrid Model for Sustaining Long Term Infrastructure Projects" Deepak Sharma, Visiting Assistant Professor, University of Baltimore, 1420 N Charles Street, Baltimore, MD, 21201, United States of America, dsharma@ubalt.edu" 2013"Many clauses in long term projects are designed using traditional methods which could be inadequate to sustain the impacts of uncertainties. This work presents a hybrid model that can design clauses for sustaining construction contracts. The model integrates multi-objective linear programming model with stochastic dynamic programming model. The model incorporates uncertainty, multiple objectives and sustainable provisions. A case study is included as a means to demonstrate the use of the model."
