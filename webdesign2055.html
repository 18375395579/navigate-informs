" 5 - Comparative Analysis of Capital Asset Pricing (CAPM) Betas Based on Tail Risk Measures" Konstantin Pavlikov, University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, kpavlikov@ufl.edu" Stan Uryasev" 2012"The paper considers Capital Asset Price (CAPM) for tail risk measures: Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR) and Conditional Drawdown-at-Risk. We compared CAPM Beta based on Standard Deviation with Betas based on CVaR and CDaR. We ranked the performance of the hedge fund stiles using these new Betas."
