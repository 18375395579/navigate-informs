" 4 - Evidence-based Incentiveneering for a Healthcare Delivery System" Donald Lee, Stanford University, Terman Engineering Center #494, Stanford, CA, 94305, United States, dkklee@stanford.edu" Stefanos Zenios" 2007"How should shareholders pay a CEO for stock performance? What strategies can a casino use to retain player loyalty? What’s the best way to pay a healthcare provider for patient outcomes? Incentive issues in OM are traditionally analyzed using principal-agent models, but these are impractical because some required components (e.g. agent cost function) are rarely ever known. This talk introduces a practical, data-driven remedy that will be applied to Medicare’s dialysis payment system."
