" 3 - Forecasting Call Center Arrivals: Mixed and Bivariate Models" Rouba Ibrahim, Department of Management Science and Innovation of University College London, Gower Street, London, UK, WC1E 6BT, London, United Kingdom, rouba.e.ibrahim@gmail.com" Pierre L'Ecuyer" 2012"We consider different statistical models for the call arrival process in telephone call centers.
