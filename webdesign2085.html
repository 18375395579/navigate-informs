" 4 - Trade-off Between Treatment Plan Quality and Beam-on-time in IMRT Using Direct Aperture Optimization" Ehsan Salari, University of Florida, 999 SW 16th Avenue Apt 64, Gainesville, FL, 32601, United States of America, esalari@ufl.edu" Edwin Romeijn" 2008"Direct aperture optimization of IMRT treatment plans allows for a much more accurate modeling of treatment delivery aspects than traditional beamlet-based treatment plan optimization. We extend a column generation approach for direct aperture optimization to include total beam-on time as one of the treatment plan evaluation criteria and use this model to determine the Pareto efficient frontier of treatment plan quality and beam-on time for clinical patient cases."
