" 4 - Understanding Psychological Effects of Real-time Travel Information on the Travel Experience" Dongyoon Song, NEXTRANS Center, USDOT Region V Regional UTC, School of Civil Engineering, Purdue University, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, song50@purdue.edu" Srinivas Peeta" 2012"Real-time travel information has been disseminated to travelers in many ways to improve the efficiency of the network and enhance the travel experience. We propose a framework to elicit the psychological mechanisms associated with real-time information. Three types of information-related stresses are identified and analyzed using a structural equation model (SEM) with latent variables."
