" 1 - Design of Experiments for Simulation Optimization" Hong Wan, Assistant Professor, School of IE, Purdue Univ., 315 N. Grant Street, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, hwan@purdue.edu" 2008"In this paper, we will discuss efficient design of experiments for simulation optimization. We have proposed a DOE-based simulation optimization framework that has nice convergence property. Based on the framework, we will discuss different designs that are suitable for simulation optimization."
