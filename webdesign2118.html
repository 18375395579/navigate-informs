" 2 - Modeling a Presidential Prediction Market" Keith Chen, Yale School of Management, New Haven, CT, United States of America, Keith.chen@yale.edu" Edward Kaplan" 2012"Using data from past Intrade US presidential prediction markets, we examined three alternative models for security prices, focusing on consistency with electoral college rules. We show that a simple diffusion model provides a good description of the overall distribution of electoral college votes, while an even simpler ranking model provides excellent predictions of the probability of winning the presidency. We derive implications for predicting the 2012 US presidential election."
