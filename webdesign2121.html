" 4 - A System Architecture for Autonomous Demand Side Load Management in Smart Buildings" Miguel F. Anjos, Canada Research Chair in Discrete Nonlinear Optimization in Engineering, Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal, C.P. 6079, succ. Centre-ville, Montreal, QC, H3C 3A7, Canada, miguel-f.anjos@polymtl.ca" Giuseppe T Costanzo" Gilles Savard" Guchuan Zhu" 2012"We present a new system architecture for demand-side load management. The system is composed of modules for admission control, load balancing, and demand/response management that operate using online operation control, optimal scheduling, and dynamic pricing respectively. It can integrate different energy sources and handle autonomous systems with heterogeneous dynamics in multiple time-scales. Simulation results confirm the viability and efficiency of the proposed framework."
