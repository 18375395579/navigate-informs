" 2 - Signal Transduction Network Inference from Double Causal Experimental Evidence" Bhaskar DasGupta, Associate Professor, University of Illinois at Chicago, 851 South Morgan Street, Chicago, IL, 60607, United States of America, dasgupta@bert.cs.uic.edu" Reka Albert" Riccardo Dondi" Sema Kachalo" Eduardo Sontag" Alex Zelikovsky" Kelly Westbrooks" Ranran Zhang" 2008"We introduce a new method of combined synthesis and inference of biological signal transduction networks. We validate the biological usability of our approach by successfully applying it to a previously published signal transduction network and by using it to synthesize and simplify a novel network corresponding to activation induced cell death in large granular lymphocyte leukemia."
