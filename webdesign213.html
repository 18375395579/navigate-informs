" 4 - Real-time Incident Management and Operations - Performance and Benefits Evaluation in Kansas City" Pei-Wei Lin, Assistant Professor, Univrsity of Missouri, Kansas City, 5100 Rockhill Rd, Kansas City, MO, 64110, United States, linp@umkc.edu" Jason Sims" 2007"This study is presented to evaluate the performance and assess benefits of the Scout system-Motorist Assist in Kansas City metropolitan area. The evaluation study consists of two phases: Phase 1 focuses on the reliable assess of system performance including incident detection, response, clearance and duration; and Phase 2 is performed to develop the methodology and to estimate resulting system benefits from data available in the incident operations record."
