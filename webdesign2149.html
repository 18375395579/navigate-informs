Khaleghi, PhD Student, University of Arizona, Systems and Industrial Engineering, The University of Arizona, Tucson, AZ, 85721, United States of America, amirreza@email.arizona.edu" Dong Xu" Young-Jun Son" 2013"A DDDAMS-based planning and control framework is proposed, which 1) devises a best control strategy via crowd detection, tracking, and motion planning in agent-based simulation, 2) controls real UAVs/UGVs via monitoring, data processing, task generation, and execution, and 3) adjusts modeling scales over time adapting to system performance and computations.
