" 4 - Model formulations for the Time-dependent Mobility Allowance Shuttle Transit (TD-MAST)" Peng Sun, PhD candidate, TU/e, Pav E 17, Eindhoven, 5600MB, Netherlands, P.Sun@tue.nl" Said Dabia" Tom van Woensel" 2013"In this paper, we study the Time dependent mobility allowance shuttle transit (MAST). Travel time function is depends on the departure time from a node and different travel time is generated and meets the FIFO property. First, we build a time dependent MAST model. Secondly , we use a time dependent shortest path algorithms to linearize the non-linear term in the proposed model. Finally, some simulation would be conducted."
