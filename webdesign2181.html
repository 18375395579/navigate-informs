" 2 - Inverse Principal-Agent Paradigm: Application to Health Care Delivery" Donald Lee, Yale School of Management, 135 Prospect St, Box 208200, New Haven, CT, United States of America, dkklee@cantab.net" Stefanos Zenios" 2008"How should dialysis providers be paid for patient outcomes along multiple dimensions? Principal-agent models can in theory be used to construct a pay-for-performance system for Medicare’s dialysis program, but little is known about the underlying model parameters. We develop a procedure for inferring the provider's cost parameters from its past outcomes, making it possible to implement the optimal payment system. Numerical results will be presented."
