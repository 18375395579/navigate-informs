" 1 - Stochastic Shortest Path Problem with Coordinated Traffic Signals " Henry X. Liu, Associate Professor, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, henryliu@umn.edu" Jie Sun" 2013"Shortest path problem (SPP) is important to many applications in ITS and has been studied extensively in the literature. However, little attention has been paid to the SPPs within a signalized traffic network, especially with coordinated traffic signals. In this talk, we will discuss a shortest path search algorithm that will consider traffic signal coordination parameters. The model is tested using the real-world data collected from the City of Pasadena in California."
