" 2 - Transfer Payments in Global Climate Policy" Florian Landis, ZEW Mannheim, L7, 1, Mannheim, 68161, Germany, landis@zew.de" Thomas Bernauer" 2013"We combine global climate modeling with regional welfare analysis to estimate regional carbon prices under various climate change, emissions and economic scenarios. Differences in regional carbon prices motivate transfer payments, if a global carbon price was to emerge. Results indicate that distributional matters can be discussed using arguments of cost-benefit analysis and we highlight to what extent the different uncertainties inherent to the analysis affect estimates of transfer payments."
