" 3 - Improving Schedule Design Through A Priori Evaluation of Robustness and Reliability" John-Paul Clarke, Associate Professor, Georgia Institute of Technology, School of Aerospace Engineering, 270 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA, 30332, United States, john-paul.clarke@ae.gatech.edu" Terran Melconian" 2006"Schedule reliability is a concern for all airlines. However, there are few tools to evaluate schedule robustness and reliability a priori. We describe such a tool and illustrate through various examples how MEANS can be used to evaluate schedule robustness and reliability, and thereby improve schedule design."
