" 6 - Performance Management Using Key Performance Indicators (KPI) in Health Service System" Chang Won Lee, Associate Professor, Hanyang University, School of Business, Seoul, 133-791, Korea, Republic of, leecw@hanyang.ac.kr" Seung-Chul Kim" 2008"This study presents a performance management model using key performance indicators (KPI) in a health service system in a leading patient-centered hospital in Korea and provides the health decision makers and policy makers with managerial insights for improving operational excellence of a health service system. The case study is invited and analyzed in detail. The results show that the proposed KPI model provides the management with strategic insights."
