" 4 - Foundations of Social Network Ad Optimization" John Turner, Assistant Professor, University of California - Irvine, The Paul Merage School of Business, Room SB 338, Irvine, CA, 92697-3125, United States of America, john.turner@uci.edu" 2013"We introduce revenue optimization models for placing ads in social networks, motivated by the connectivity structure of the underlying graph.
