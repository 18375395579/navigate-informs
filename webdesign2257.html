" 3 - National Drug Stock-out Risks in Africa: The Global Fund Disbursement Process for Procurement" Iva Rashkova, London Business School, Regent's Park, London, NW1 4SA, United Kingdom, irashkova.phd2009@london.edu" Jérémie Gallien" Prashant Yadav" Rifat Atun" 2013"We use historical data to construct and validate distributional forecasts of procurement lead times and disbursement inter-arrival times for Global Fund grants in 53 African countries. We use a validated simulation model to predict impact of these processes on central stock availability of a single product facing constant demand. We find that adjusting disbursement amounts to reflect historical disbursement schedules mitigates the risk of stockouts better than bridge financing or a buffer stock."
