" 1 - Self-Insurance Choices with INTER-period and INTRA-period Utility Functions" Matthew Sobel, Professor, Weatherhead School of Management, Case Western Reserve University, 10900 Euclid Ave., Cleveland, OH, 44106-7235, United States, matthew.sobel@case.edu" Danko Turcic" 2007"We contrast optimal self-insurance choices with alternative models having inter-period versus intra-period utility functions. After reviewing the axiomatic foundations of the models, we show that the intra-period model biases the decisions towards too little self insurance. This result has implications for diverse settings in which the chosen size of an early commitment affects the magnitude of a subsequent risky consequence."
