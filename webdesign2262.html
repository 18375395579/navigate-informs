" 3 - Stress Scenario Selection by Empirical Likelihood" Wanmo Kang, Assistant Professor, KAIST, 291 Daehak-ro, Yuseong-gu, Daejeon, Korea, Republic of, wanmo.kang@kaist.edu" Paul Glasserman" Chulmin Kang" 2012"We present the selection and analysis of stress scenarios for financial risk assessment, with particular emphasis on identifying sensible combinations of stresses to multiple variables. We consider reverse stress testing - finding the most likely scenarios - using a nonparametric empirical likelihood estimator. We apply the results to marginal expected shortfall, macro stress scenarios, and scenario sampling method. This is a joint work with Paul Glasserman and Chulmin Kang."
