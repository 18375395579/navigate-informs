" 1 - An OR/Problem-Solving Approach to Business Calculus" Bruce Pollack-Johnson, Associate Professor of Mathematical Sciences, Villanova University, Department of Mathematical Sciences, 800 Lancaster Avenue, Villanova, PA, 19085, United States, bruce.pollack-johnson@villanova.edu" 2007"We describe a federally-funded re-engineering of a business calculus course organized around the process of solving real-world problems using math models, calculus, and technology. Students learn OR-style post-optimality analysis, including verification, validation, and sensitivity analysis, in doing student-generated projects on topics from their own lives. Excel templates have been developed for 3-D graphing, fitting logistic models, and testing solutions for local and global optimality."
