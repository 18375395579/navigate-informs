" 3 - Elicitation of Multiattribute Utility Functions through High Dimensional Model Representation" Alessandra Cillo, Bocconi University, Via Roentgen 1, 20136 Milan, Italy, alessandra.cillo@unibocconi.it" Francesca Beccacece" Emanuele Borgonovo" Greg Buzzard" Stanley Zionts" 2013"Our paper exploits the connection between multiattribute utility theory and high dimensional model representation theory for investigating the properties of multiattribute utility functions without stating any a-priori assumption on an individuals' preference structure. We test the method via an experiment in a riskless context in which subjects are asked to evaluate mobile phone packages that differ on three attributes. We obtain insights on monotonicity and additivity."
