" 4 - Decision Analysis in Support of Clinical Trial Protocol Decisions" Joseph Kahn, Novartis Pharmaceuticals, 1 Health Plaza, East Hanover, NJ, 07936, United States, joseph.kahn@novartis.com" 2006"Presentation will illustrate via a case study for a phase IIa/b adaptive program design how modelers can use decision analysis to more productively assist clinical teams with protocol decisions. Topics include: Framing and developing a manageable set of compelling alternatives; Assuring information is relevant and has credence; Salient value measures; Analytic methods and software useful for implementation; Achieving buy-in from both clinical teams and colleagues in statistics."
