" 3 - Analysis of the Consistency of a MIP-based Multigroup Constrained Discriminant Model" Paul Brooks, VCU, Dept. of Stat. Sci. and O.R., PO Box 843083, Richmond, VA, 23284, United States of America, JPBrooks@vcu.edu" Eva K. Lee" 2007"A Bayes optimal classifier minimizes the probability of misclassification. Even if a Bayes optimal classifier can be obtained, the inter-group misclassification rates may be higher than desirable. Constrained discrimination models are classification models that allow for the non-classification of observations until more information is collected. We analyze the consistency of a mixed-integer programming formulation of a constrained discrimination model."
