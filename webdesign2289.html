" 1 - Optimal Capacity Planning for RHN Health Network via Queueing Models and Simulation" Kimiebi Akah, Masters Candidate, Ohio State University, 1971 Neil Ave, Columbus, OH, 43210, United States of America, akah.5@osu.edu" Yue Tan" Cathy Xia" 2013"We consider the capacity planning problem for a health network of 6 hospitals with the goal of reducing costs while keeping service quality within target. The complexity of the problem creates difficulty for OptQuest with running the model. We consider distance based probabilistic routing, study the service quality of each hospital through G.G.C approximations, and derive the capacity planning solution. The robustness of the model is further demonstrated via simulation."
