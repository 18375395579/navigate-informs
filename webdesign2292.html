" 1 - Optimal Information System Security Investment ?A Control Theoretic Approach" Jing Zhou, Assistant Professor, University of North Carolina-Charlotte, 9201 University City Blvd, Friday 353C, Charlotte, NC, 28223, United States of America, jzhou7@uncc.edu" Xun Li" Monica Johar" 2012"Most of the IT security investment literature is based on single period analysis. In addition to the initial investment, a firm continues to exert effort to maintain the effectiveness of the IT infrastructure. We use a control theoretic framework to find the optimal IT security investment to minimize the expected total cost over a planning horizon. Our results can help firms decide how to invest in IT security given the type of the information system and the efficiency of the investment."
