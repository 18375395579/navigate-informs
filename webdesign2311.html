" 2 - A Method to Assess Benefits of Transmission Expansions in the Italian Electricity Market" Paola Bresesti, Technical Director, CESI Ricerca, Via Rubattino, 54 - 20134, Milano, Italy, Paola.Bresesti@cesiricerca.it" Riccardo Vailati" Maria Vittoria Cazzol" 2007"The paper presents a novel method to assess simultaneously some technical and economic benefits of transmission expansions. The innovative feature of the method consists in integrating a power system reliability model and a market model. The paper reports the results of an application of the method that involves the Italian system."
