" 1 - Orientation Issues in Data Envelopment Analysis" Andy Johnson, Assistant Professor, Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, Texas A&M University, 241 Zachry, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, ajohnson@isye.gatech.edu" 2006"There have been at least four different orientations for measuring efficiency suggested in the data envelopment analysis literature. This talk will address the issue of selecting an orientation in the short-run analysis and suggest methods for estimating the parameters of the directional distance function."
