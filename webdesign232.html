" 3 - Using Large-scale Traffic Simulation Models to Inform the Design of Vehicle Sharing Networks " Carolina Osorio, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, 77 Massachusetts Avenue, 1-232, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, osorioc@mit.edu" Krishna Selvam" Cynthia Barnhart" Virot Chiraphadhanakul" Erica Klampfl" Yimin Liu" 2013"We develop an optimization framework to inform the design of vehicle sharing networks. The framework embeds an urban traffic simulation model, which provides a detailed description of how modal shifts impact both link and network-wide traffic metrics. We use the framework to identify locations for vehicle sharing stations that lead to maximal improvements in network-wide metrics."
