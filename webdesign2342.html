" 4 - Copulas-based Dependent Decision Lattice Approach" Tianyang Wang, Assistant Professor, Colorado State University, Finance and Real Estate Department, Fort Collins, CO, United States of America, tianyang.wang@business.colostate.edu" Joe Hahn" James Dyer" 2013"In this paper we present a general framework of a copulas-based dependent decision lattice approach for correlated uncertainties. The proposed dependent decision lattice model allows multiple dependent uncertainties with arbitrary marginal distributions to be represented in a recombining tree structure. This general framework significantly reduce the size of the tree for practical applications in decision analysis, real options valuations, and financial derivatives valuation."
