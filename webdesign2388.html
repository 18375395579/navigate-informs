" 2 - Efficient Learning of Donor Retention Strategies for the American Red Cross" Bin Han, University of Maryland, College Park, MB, United States of America, danielh@umd.edu" Ilya Ryzhov" Boris Defourny" 2013"We present a new sequential decision model for allocating campaign budget for the American Red Cross. The outcome--attributes relation is modeled using linear regression. We derive the first simulation procedure for learning unknown regression parameters and sampling noise. We apply convex approximation with a quantization procedure and semidefinte programming relaxation to reduce the computational complexity. Simulation experiments demonstrate the efficient performance of the approximation."
