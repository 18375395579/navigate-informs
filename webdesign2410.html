" 3 - Solution Framework for Hurricane Evacuation Planning and Management" Tiravat Assavapokee, Assistant Professor, University of Houston, 4800 Calhoun Street, Houston, TX, 77204, United States of America, Tiravat.Assavapokee@mail.uh.edu" Shabnam Zangeneh" MohammadReza Baharnemati" Gino Lim" 2008"This work presents the solution framework for emergency evacuation planning by utilizing mathematical models and heuristics algorithms.
