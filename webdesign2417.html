" 3 - EEG Biomarkers for Vagus Nerve Stimulation Therapy: A Data Mining Approach" Nikita Boyko, PhD Student, University of Florida, Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, 303 Weil Hall, PO Box 116595, Gainesville, FL, 32611, United States of America, nikita@ufl.edu" Vitaliy Yatsenko" Panos Pardalos" Michael Bewernitz" Georges Ghacibeh" Basim Uthman" 2008"This study presents an application of logistic regression, and support vector machines to the analysis of scalp electroencephalograms obtained from epilepsy patients implanted with the Vagus nerve stimulator (VNS) used in VNS Therapy. The separation quality for Local Lyapunov Exponents is studied as a potential electroencephalographic marker of optimal VNS stimulation parameters."
