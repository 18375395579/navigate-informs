" 2 - Identification of Nonlinear Causality for Prediction Based on Network Graph" Akkarapol Sa-ngasoongsong, Ph.D Candidate, Oklahoma State University, 245N. University Place, Apt.301, stillwater, ok, 74075, United States of America, akkarap@ostatemail.okstate.edu" Satish Bukkapatnam" 2012"The concept of Granger-Causality has been broadly regarded as a powerful method, and widely used in time series research to determine a predictability of one variable to the others. However, one of the limitations of Granger-Causality is linearity assumption. In this presentation, we introduce a methodology to identify causal relationship of variables based on nonlinearity assumption and graph theory. A prediction application using nonlinear causality graph is also presented."
