Determined NAS capacity, i.e. maximum number of aircraft traveling using the Max Flow algorithms. This model can be used to evaluate potential benefits or disadvantages of various natural and artificial interventions such as reducing en-route separation standards, increasing airport capacities, utilizing regional airports, smoothing traffic, and disruptive weather phenomena."
