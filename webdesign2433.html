" 4 - Prediction of Demand Surges for Immunization During Influenza Pandemics" Michael Beeler, University of Toronto, 5 King's College Road, Toronto, ON, M5S 3G8, Canada, mfbeeler@gmail.com" Michael Carter" Dionne Aleman" 2012"A time-series model is constructed to predict surges in turnout at mass immunization clinics during influenza pandemics.
