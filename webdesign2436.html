" 1 - Matrix Polynomial Theory applied to Markov modulated Brownian motion" Bernardo D'Auria, Visiting Professor, Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Avda. de la Universidad, 30, Leganes, MD, 28911, Spain, bernardo.dauria@uc3m.es" Offer Kella" 2008"e study the stationary buffer content of a Brownian queue whose parameters depend on an external independent Markov process. According to the literature by using martingale techniques such a problem reduces to a matrix identity that can be solved by finding an ad-hoc system of equations. We use the theory of Matrix Polynomial to go further in this analysis and give a simplified expression for the system of equations together with general conditions that assure the existence of a solution."
