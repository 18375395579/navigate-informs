" 2 - Estimating Choice Models from Airline Data" Garrett van Ryzin, Columbia University, Uris Hall, New York, NY, 10027, United States, gjv1@columbia.edu" Gustavo Vulcano" 2006"We look at the results of an empirical study of estimating choice models from available airline data.
