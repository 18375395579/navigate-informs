" 3 - System Reliability Optimization by using Monte Carlo Simulation and a Metaheuristic Algorithm" Krystel Castillo, Assistant Professor, The University of Texas at San Antonio, One Circle UTSA EB 3.04.54, San Antonio, TX, 78249, United States of America, krystel.castillovillar@utsa.edu" Harry Millwater" Ahmed Kibria" Eliseo Iglesias" 2013"This research addresses the challenge of allocating resources for inspection data acquisition for complex systems with multiple components and failure modes such as a military gas turbine engine. A nested loop approach using is proposed to determine the system’s mean time to failure distribution and a metaheuristics was used to identify the optimal inspection data to be added at each subcomponent. This methodology can be applied to assess the economic impact of changing maintenance policies."
