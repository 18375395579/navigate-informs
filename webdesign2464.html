" 2 - A Mathematical Model for Evaluating the System Impact of EV Demand" Yueyue Fan, Associate Professor, University of California-Davis, One Shield Ave., Davis, CA, United States of America, yyfan@ucdavis.edu" 2013"In this talk, we will present a network model that captures the spatial interdependence of existing power supply and demand. This model will be used to evaluate the economic and environmental impacts of increasing EV demand in a California case study."
