" 4 - MCDM Models in Project Portfolio Selection" Adiel Almeida, Professor, Federal University of Pernambuco, Cx. Postal 7462, Recife, 50.630-970, Brazil, almeidaatd@gmail.com" 2012"Selection of Project portfolio based on multicriteria decision making (MCDM) methods is analyzed. MCDM compensatory and non-compensatory methods are considered, with emphasis to outranking and additive methods. Problems related to scales used for intra-criteria evaluation are considered. Considerations are given to applications on Research and Development (R&D) project portfolio selection."
