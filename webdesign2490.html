" 2 - Network-Based Approaches in Data Mining" Sergiy Butenko, Assistant Professor, Texas A&M University, College Station, TX, 77843, United States, butenko@tamu.edu" Svyatoslav Trukhanov" Balabhaskar Balasundaram" 2006"This talk will discuss data mining techniques based on network representations of data sets. In particular, alternative ways of representing the data as a network will be described, several models describing structural properties of the network will be introduced, and solution methods for the corresponding optimization problems will be described."
