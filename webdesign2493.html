" 1 - Bellman Equations Applied to Hurricane Decision Making" Richard Larson, MIT, E40-231, Cambridge, MA, 02139, United States of America, rclarson@MIT.EDU" Michael Metzger" 2008"As a hurricane approaches the coastline, its state variables are updated periodically via satellite, airplane and other reconnaissance. Each update represents a stage, a decision making epoch, in Bellman's parlance. Stage-to-stage updates are uncertain, reflecting the probabilistic behavior of hurricanes. We characterize on-shore decision-making within such a stochastic dynamic programming framework, and replay old hurricanes to demonstrate the utility of the approach."
