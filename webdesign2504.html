" 1 - Multi Resolution Analysis for Modulus Prediction of PVA Treated Buckypaper" Arash Pourhabib, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, arash.pourhabib@neo.tamu.edu" Yu Ding" Jianhua Huang" Kan Wang" Chuck Zhang" 2012"Buckypapers (BPs) are thin sheets produced from carbon nanotubes. To improve their mechanical properties, polyvinyl alcohol (PVA) is added during the manufacturing process. We present a multi-resolution model for analysis and prediction of Young’s modulus of PVA-treated BPs. We utilize data from both the physical experiment and a simulation model. Introducing a latent variable which reflects the effectiveness of the PVA, we develop an algorithm which aggregates both sources of information."
