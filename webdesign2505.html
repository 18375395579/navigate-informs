" 1 - On Nonparametric Statistical Process Control of Univariate Processes" Peihua Qiu, Professor, University of Minnesota, 224 Church Street SE, 313 Ford Hall, Minneapolis, MN, 55455, United States of America, qiuxx008@umn.edu" Zhonghua Li" 2012"This paper considers statistical process control (SPC) of univariate processes when the parametric form of the process distribution is unavailable. We propose a new framework for constructing nonparametric control charts, by first categorizing observed data and then applying categorical data analysis methods to SPC. Some new nonparametric control charts are proposed and they are compared with several representative existing control charts in various cases."
