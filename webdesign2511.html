" 1 - Estimating Multiple Objectives in Data Mining" John Sparks, University of Illinois, Chicago, Information and Decision Sciences, Chicago, IL, 60607, United States, jspark4@uic.edu" Sid Bhattacharyya" 2007"Many data-mining situations require estimation of multiple objectives. For instance, predict which prospects will become customers, and which customers will provide more value. This study compares the performance of different statistical methods of modeling for multiple objectives using six different files from the database marketing arena."
