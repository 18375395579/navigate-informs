" 1 - Statistical Methods for Degradation Data with Dynamic Covariates & Application to Outdoor Weathering" Yili Hong, Assistant Professor of Statistics, Virginia Tech, Department of Statistics, 213 Hutcheson Hall, Blacksburg, VA, 24061, United States of America, yilihong@vt.edu" William Meeker" Yuanyuan Duan" 2012"Degradation data provide a useful resource for obtaining reliability information. It is common nowadays to dynamically record usage and other environmental variables (e.g., temperature and humidity). We introduce a class of models for analyzing degradation data with dynamic covariate. We use general path models with random effects to describe degradation paths. The proposed methods are illustrated with an application for predicting life of organic coatings in a complicated dynamic environment."
