" 1 - Screening and Diagnosis of Tuberculosis in Prisons in Russia and Eastern Europe" Diana Negoescu, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, United States of America, negoescu@stanford.edu" Douglas Owens" Daniel Winetsky" Emilia DeMarchis" Jeremy Goldhaber-Fiebert" 2013"Prisons of the former Soviet Union have high rates of multidrug-resistant tuberculosis (TB) and are thought to drive general population TB epidemics. Effective prison case detection, through employing more expensive technologies, may reduce long-term treatment costs and slow transmission. We develop a dynamic transmission model of TB matched to the epidemiology and costs in prisons, and we evaluate the cost-effectiveness of eight strategies for TB screening and diagnosis."
