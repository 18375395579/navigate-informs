" 3 - Analytical Models Enable Govt/Industry Policy Makers to Balance Impacts on Aviation Security" Russell Wooten, Department of Homeland Security, Washington, DC, 20528, United States, Russell.Wooten@dhs.gov" Bob Peterson" 2006"The United States Commercial Aviation Partnership (USCAP) created a large spreadsheet model using systems dynamics, queueing models, and econometrics to encompass the entire US commercial aviation industry including travelers, airlines, airports, airline suppliers, airport suppliers, government agencies, and travel and tourism entities. The model helps stakeholders understand the high level economic impact of policy and procedure decisions going out as much as 30 years."
