" 3 - An Approximation Model for Sojourn Time Distributions in Multi-server Queueing Networks" Kevin Gue, Associate Professor, Auburn University, Department of Industrial and Systems Eng, Auburn, AL, 36830, United States of America, kevin.gue@auburn.edu" Hyun Ho Kim" 2008"We develop an approximation model for the sojourn time distribution of customers or jobs arriving to a multi-server queueing network. The model accepts general interarrival and service times, and is based on the characteristics of phase-type distributions. Distributions produced by the model agree well with those produced by simulation for a variety of serial and general networks. We offer some potential uses of the model in retail order fulfillment systems."
