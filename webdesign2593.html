" 3 - A Categorization Model for Spreadsheet Use in Organizations" Mukul Madahar, Assistant MBA Program Director, University of Wales Institute Cardiff, Cardiff School of Management, Colchester Avenue, Cardiff CF23 9XR, United Kingdom, MMadahar@uwic.ac.uk" Pat Cleary" 2007"In this paper we expand upon a previous INFORMS paper which presented an analysis of proposed categorization models developed for spreadsheet use in organizations. We have now completed a further phase of research whose purpose was to refine the model and define specific model dimensions. We report on this further phase and outline a plan to fully validate the model."
