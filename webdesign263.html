" 3 - Multi-criteria Location-allocation Modeling for the Primary Care System Network in Ontario" Vedat Verter, Prof, Desautels Faculty of Management, McGill University, 1001 Sherbrooke West, Montreal, H3A 1G5, Canada, vedat.verter@mcgill.ca" Michael Carter" Anna Graber" 2013"The primary care scheme of Ontario, Canada, involves five models of care currently being employed simultaneously.
