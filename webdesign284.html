Grant st., West lafayette, IN, 47907, United States, ryuj@purdue.edu" Hong Wan" Sujin Kim" 2007"A new cumulative sum (CUSUM) chart is developed for unknown future shifts in the proportion of failures. The chart is based on counts between failures which can be modeled by a geometric distribution. A geometric based model is widely used for monitoring healthcare procedures. To detect a varying shift, we estimate the current failure rate and update the CUSUM statistic continuously. The simulation results show its potential to perform more effectively than other well-established methods."
