" 2 - Moment Explosions in Affine Diffusion Models" Kyoung-Kuk Kim, Ph.D Candidate, Columbia Business School, 311 Uris Hall, 3022 Broadway, New York, NY, 10027, United States, KKim11@gsb.columbia.edu" Paul Glasserman" 2007"We investigate the tail behavior of canonical affine term structure models through an analysis of their associated dynamical systems. We derive a necessary and sufficient condition for the model to have a Gaussian distribution, and we describe the regions of finite exponential moments for the stationary and transient distributions."
