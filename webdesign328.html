" 4 - Nested and Sliced Space-Filling Designs" Zhiguang Qian, Assistant Professor, University of Wisconsin at Madison, zhiguang@stat.wisc.edu" 2007"In this talk, we will discuss two novel classes of space-filling designs for computer modeling.
