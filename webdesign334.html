" 1 - A Dual-Response Approach to Robust Parameter Design for a Generalized Linear Model" William Brenneman, Senior Statistician, The Procter & Gamble Company, 8700 Mason-Montgomery Road, Mason, OH, 45040, United States, brenneman.wa@pg.com" William Myers" Raymond Myers" 2006"Robust Parameter Design has been studied and applied, in most cases, assuming a linear model under standard assumptions. More recently, RPD has been considered in a generalized linear model (GLM) setting. In this paper we apply a general dual response approach when using RPD in the case of a GLM. We motivate the need for exploring both the process mean and process variance, by discussing situations when a compromise between the two is necessary."
