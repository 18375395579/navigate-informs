" 3 - Robust Calibration for Localization in Wireless Sensor Networks" Jung Jin Cho, Texas A&M University, Industrial and Systems Engineering, TAMU3131, College Station, TX, United States, jjcho@tamu.edu" Yu Ding" Yong Chen" Jiong Tang" 2006"This paper presents a robust estimation procedure for determining between-node distances in a wireless sensor network, known as the calibration problem in sensor localization. The problem is modeled by a structured linear regression. We suggest using a robust regression estimator but need to revise it to account for the structural information. We will demonstrate that the sensor cluster pattern in a network determines the structure and will thus affect the system robustness for calibration."
