" 3 - Stochastic Optimization and Robust Ancillary Services Determination for Electricity System Operation" Alberto Lamadrid, Assistant Professor, Lehigh University, 621 Taylor Street, room 451, Bethlehem, PA, 18015, United States of America, ajlamadrid@lehigh.edu" Tim Mount" Ray Zimmerman" Carlos Murillo-Sanchez" 2013"This paper formulates a receding horizon framework, for System Operators in their daily operations, in the context of high penetration of renewable energy sources (RES). The model has an electricity network system operator seeking to maximize the welfare of consumers and producers, procuring energy and ancillary services in a robust manner using a security constrained Optimal Power Flow (SC-OPF)."
