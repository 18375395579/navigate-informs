" 4 - Combined MAU and SOFTMAX Modeling of Uncertainty in Adversary Behavior" Richard John, Associate Professor, University of Southern California, Department of Psychology, MC-1061, Los Angeles, CA, 90089-1061, United States of America, richardj@usc.edu" Heather Rosoff" 2013"We present case studies using proxy adversary values experts to model uncertainty in adversary attack behavior. Multiple adversary values expert assessments are used to compare different adversary groups and different assessments for the same adversary group. We use these models to assess the effectiveness of different counter terrorism in changing the likelihood of particular adversary attacks."
