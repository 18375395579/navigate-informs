" 1 - Evaluation of Serivicescape using VR-based Testing Laboratory: A Case Study on a Duty Free Shop" Ryeok-Hwan Kwon, Ph D. Candidate, POSTECH, Quality Systems Lab., Dept. of IME, San 31, Hyoja-Dong, Nam-Gu, Pohang, 790-784, Korea, Republic of, klh1706@postech.ac.kr" Ki-Hun Kim" Kwang-Jae Kim" 2013"Serivicescape refers to the physical surroundings that affect service activities of customers and employees. Recently, virtual reality (VR)-based testing laboratories have been introduced. Such a laboratory would be useful in service-scape evaluation in terms of the testing time and costs. This talk presents a case study on the servicescape design evaluation of a duty free shop using VR models."
