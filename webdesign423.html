" 2 - A Partially Observable Model for the Liver Transplant Waiting List" Burhaneddin Sandikci, Assistant Professor, University of Chicago, Graduate School of Business, Chicago, IL, 60637, United States of America, burhaneddin.sandikci@chicagogsb.edu" Mark Roberts" Lisa Maillart" Andrew Schaefer" 2008"The liver transplantation system in the United States publishes incomplete information about the composition of the liver transplant waiting list. We model and analyze the accept/reject decision of a liver patient under this incomplete information system as a partially observed Markov decision process (POMDP). We compare the POMDP model to a previous MDP model to obtain more precise estimates of the patient’s price of privacy. We present the results of clinically driven numerical experiments."
