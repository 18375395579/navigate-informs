" 3 - A Comprehensive Review of the Literature on Disaster Performance Analysis of Transportation Systems" Reza Faturechi, University of Maryland, 1173 Glenn L. Martin Hall, University of Maryland, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, reza@umd.edu" Elise Miller-Hooks" 2013"This talk provides a comprehensive review of the literature on transportation system performance in disaster. Performance characteristics considered include risk, vulnerability, reliability, robustness, flexibility, survivability and resilience. The works are classified based on a host of criteria, including applied measure (qualitiative vs. quantitative, assessment vs. management), conceptual approach, and methodology."
