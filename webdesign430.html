" 3 - Modeling Power Systems with a High Penetration of Renewables" Sonja Wogrin, IIT, Comillas Pontifical University, Madrid, Spain, sonja.wogrin@iit.upcomillas.es" Andrés Delgadillo" Javier Reneses" Pablo Dueñas" 2013"We propose a new modeling approach to address the challenges that arise in power systems with a high penetration of renewable energy sources by introducing system states instead of traditional load periods. This allows us to represent important operational details such as start-up and shut- down decisions in medium- or long-term models thereby overcoming the lack of chronological information predominant in current models."
