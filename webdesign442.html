" 4 - Mesoscopic Traffic State Estimation using GPS and Bluetooth Data " Hao Lei, Phd Student, University of Utah, 110 Central Campus Dr, MCE 2000, Salt Lake City, UT, 84112, United States of America, hao.lei@utah.edu" Xuesong Zhou" Wen Deng" 2012"This talk discusses how to use multiple data sources, including loop detector counts, AVI Bluetooth travel time readings and GPS location samples, to estimate mesoscropic traffic states on a homogeneous freeway segment. We extend Newell's three-detector method to approximate stochastic cumulative flow counts, by using a multinomial probit model and Clark's approximation method."
