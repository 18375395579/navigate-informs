" 1 - Open-source Quality Assurance and Performance Analysis Tools" Armin Pruessner, GAMS Development Corporation, 1217 Potomac St NW, Washington, DC, 20007, United States of America, apruessner@gams.com" Michael Bussieck" Steven Dirkse" Stefan Vigerske" 2008"Until recently, much of the math programming community has focused primarily on performance testing and benchmarking, while the general commercial environment has emphasized reliability over performance. We introduce a suite of tools to aid in both QA and performance analysis of solver software. We show how users can effectively use our tools to quality control their solver using a variety of quality metrics. In addition, we show how one can obtain benchmarking and performance information."
