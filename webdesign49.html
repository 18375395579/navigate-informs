" 1 - Unit Dose Packaging Systems Analysis" Russell Meller, University of Arkansas, 4207 Bell Engineering, Department of Industrial Engineering, Fayetteville, AR, 72701, United States of America, rmeller@uark.edu" Scott Mason" Jen Pazour" Sarah Root" Yasin Unlu" 2008"Medications are typically distributed by manufacturers in bulk, but to increase patient safety, they are often administered to patients in unit doses with electronic verification. A systems analysis is needed to determine the best manner for the implied repackaging and labeling of the medications. An optimization approach will be presented that considers the quantifiable factors directly and the non-quantifiable factors via an efficient frontier and break-even analysis."
