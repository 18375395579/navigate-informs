" 3 - Technical Efficiency of Jute Farmers in Bangladesh" Subir Bairagi, PhD Student, University of Nebraska Lincoln, 314E Filley Hall, Lincoln, NE, 68583, United States of America, subirkanti105@gmail.com" Uttam Deb" 2013"Technical efficiency of 259 Jute farmers in Bangladesh is measured using a non-parametric approach (O'Donnell index). Single output and four inputs are used, gathered from CPD field survey. The result shows that the mean efficiency is about 0.54. In the 2nd stage, a Tobit model will be used to identify the factors responsible for technical inefficiency in jute farming in Bangladesh."
