" 2 - Using Cross Entropy Methods to Determine Reliable Link Routes in MANETs" Ivan Guardiola, Assistant Professor, Missouri University of Science and Technology, 600 W. 14th Street, Rolla, MO, 65409, United States of America, guardiolai@mst.edu" Brian Schaefer" Shikhar Acharya" 2012"This presentation highlights the use of the Cross Entropy Method to determine the most reliable link routes within a Rayleigh Fading envelop. We use the cross entropy method as a mean to simulate and optimize received signal power in order to determine the least interference route in a network."
