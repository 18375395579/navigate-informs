" 3 - Matching Collection Channel with Collection Strategy in Reverse Supply Chains" Atalay Atasu, Ph.D Candidate, INSEAD, Boulevard de Constance, Fontainebleau, 77300, France, atalay.atasu@insead.edu" Beril Toktay" 2007"We discuss the impact of the collection strategy on the reverse channel choice of a manufacturer. The manufacturer has two options for collection, either he collects used products himself or delegates the collection activity to the retailer. The collecting party can use two different approaches for used product collection: (i) drop-off (ii) pick-up. We show that manufacturer's optimal reverse channel choice depends on the collection strategy used."
