" 4 - Efficiency-based Quantitative Currency Selection" Irene Song, Columbia University, Columbia University, 120 Street, New York, NY, 10027, United States of America, is2306@columbia.edu" Iraj Kani" Soulaymane Kachani" 2013"In this talk, we propose an efficiency-based currency selection methodology that combines data envelopment analysis (DEA), a non-parametric productivity analysis of relative efficiencies of economic entities, with fundamental analysis in a disciplined manner. Using this methodology, we construct and analyze several representative structural exchange rate models, including flexible-price monetary model, sticky-price monetary model, trade-weighted dollar model and their extensions."
