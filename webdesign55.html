" 4 - A Modeling Framework for Equipment Financing and Leasing with Default Risk" Vadim Linetsky, Professor, Northwestern University, 2145 Sheridan Rd, Evanston, IL, 60208, United States of America, linetsky@iems.northwestern.edu" 2008"We develop a modeling framework for financing and leasing big-ticket heavy equipment such as aircraft, ships, rail stock, etc. taking into account the risk of default as well as the collateral asset price risk. As an application, we present a model for the valuation of export credit guarantees in the aircraft sector."
