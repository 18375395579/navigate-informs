" 3 - Calibrating an Access-purpose Gravity Model for Cyclist Trip Distributions" Jeffrey LaMondia, Assistant Professor, Auburn University, 238 Harbert Engineering Center, Auburn University, Auburn, AL, 36849, United States of America, jlamondia@auburn.edu" Nathan Moore" 2013"Understanding where cyclists prefer to travel is a critical part of developing bicycle networks. This study uses trip diary records from the 2008 Chicago Travel Tracker Survey to calculate a scalable gravity model function to forecast cyclists?movements over a variety of trip purposes. The model includes a series of accessibility-characteristics, such as zone development and activity opportunities, elevation changes, traffic patterns, distances, and the existence of bicycle facilities."
