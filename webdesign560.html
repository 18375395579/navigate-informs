Significant further growth is anticipated, along with an increase in energy use and associated emissions. The Aviation Integrated Modelling (AIM) project develops an integrated assessment model to enable analysis of aviation, environment and economic interactions at local and global levels. Operations research methods are used extensively, and particularly in the estimation of future air traffic given airport capacity constraints."
