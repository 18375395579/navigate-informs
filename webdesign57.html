" 3 - Integrated Process Planning and Supply Chain Configuration Using Petri Nets" Oleg Gusikhin, Ford Research & Advanced Engineering, RIC Building, Room 3011, MD 2122, 2101 Village Rd, Dearborn, MI, 48124, ogusikhi@ford.com" Erica Klampfl" 2007"We present a Petri Net-based approach automating the MIP formulation of the Supply Chain configuration problem with alternative assembly sequences. We start with a Petri Net model of the assembly processes, which unfolds into the Supply Chain model. The MIP formulation is based on the matrix representation of this model."
