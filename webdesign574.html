" 2 - Evaluating Investment Options with Wind Integration using Markov Decision Processes" Athena Wu, PhD Candidate, The University of Auckland, 70 Symonds Street, Auckland, New Zealand, a.wu@auckland.ac.nz" Golbon Zakeri" Andy Philpott" 2012"Classical capacity planning models become problematic when evaluating investment facing wind generation intermittency. We describe a model treating the uncertainty in wind as a Markov Chain and the short-term operation of electricity plant as an average reward MDP. This problem’s LP formulation can be augmented by binary variables defining investment actions, leading to a mixed-integer program. The impact of increasing wind penetration on generation and transmission investments is explored."
