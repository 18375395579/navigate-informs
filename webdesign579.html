" 2 - A Win Prediction Model for IT Outsourcing" Daniel Greenia, IBM Almaden Research Center, 650 Harry Road, San Jose, CA, 95120, United States of America, dangreenia@us.ibm.com" Mu Qiao" Rama Akkiraju" 2013"Our research objective is to discover factors that correlate with winning Information Technology (IT) outsourcing contracts in a competitive setting. By understanding these correlations, an IT service provider can potentially increase the chances of winning a bid by improving the quality of critical situation-dependant factors. Using data from a large IT service provider, we conduct a correlation analysis to select model features, and build a predictive model using the naïve Bayesian approach."
