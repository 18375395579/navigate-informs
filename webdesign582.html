" 4 - Predictive Model of the Read Performance of EPC RFID Tags in Supply Chain Environments" J. David Porter, Associate Professor, Oregon State University, 110 Covell, Corvallis, OR, 97331, United States of America, david.porter@orst.edu" Mario Magaña" David Kim" 2007"RFID technology is quickly becoming the item-tracking solution of choice in supply chain applications. However, most RFID deployments are currently preformed on a trial-and-error basis. This research aims to develop a predictive model of the read performance of EPC RFID tags in supply chain environments. This predictive model will be used to determine the optimal number of tags that can be consistently read in a specific warehouse facility prior to the deployment of an RFID system solution."
