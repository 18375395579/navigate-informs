" 2 - Leveraging Mission Analysis and Modeling to investigate Conceptual CG VOI Location Decision Support" David Procter, Raytheon Integrated Defense Systems, 225 Presidential Way, Woburn, MA, United States of America, david_procter@raytheon.com" Fred Mott" 2012"This paper presents the approach and results of a joint Raytheon - U.S. Coast Guard mission analysis and experiment to evaluate mission analysis-derived decision support concepts supporting USCG missions. The objective was to identify and investigate, via mission analysis and modeling, the potential of candidate decision support aids and CONOPS to enhance maritime security. Raytheon’s Mission Profiling (Patented) approach was applied to a conceptual vessel of interest (VOI) location application."
