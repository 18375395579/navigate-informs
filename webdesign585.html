" 3 - Fluctuation Analysis for the Loss from Default" Justin Sirignano, Stanford Univerisity, Huang Engineering Center, Stanford, United States of America, jasirign@stanford.edu" Konstantinos Spiliopoulos" Kay Giesecke" 2013"The loss from default in large credit portfolios is typically computationally expensive to evaluate. We analyze the fluctuation of the loss from default around its large portfolio limit in a class of reduced-form models of correlated firm-by-firm default timing. We prove a weak convergence result for the fluctuation process and use it for developing a conditionally Gaussian approximation to the loss distribution."
