" 3 - Understanding the Pulse of the Workforce through Social Media Text Analytics" Wermer Geyer, IBM Research Cambridge, Cambridge, MA, United States of America, Werner.Geyer@us.ibm.com" Stephen Levy" 2013"The use of social media within the enterprise is increasing and with it the availability of unstructured text data containing the public "voice" of the workforce. We present an application that helps make sense of unstructured data by applying text analytics methods (topic extraction, sentiment analysis, etc.) Combined with structured data from corporate directories, we can effectively filter and segment social data based on demographics to provide targeted insights into the workforce."
