" 2 - Approximate Mean Value Analysis of Closed Queueing Networks with Bulk Servers" Liqiang Liu, Postdoctoral Researcher, EURANDOM, Eindhoven University of Technology, 1.03 Laplace Gebouw, Den Dolech 2, Eindhoven, 5612 AZ, Netherlands, liu@eurandom.tue.nl" Ivo Adan" 2008"Motivated by the performance analysis of the picking operations in automated distribution centers, we study a closed multichain queueing network with standard bulk servers and develop several approximations based on the mean value analysis method. We study the goodness of the approximations by numerical experiments."
