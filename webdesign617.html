" 1 - A Data-driven Predictive Analytics Approach for Transportation Portfolio Management" Durai Sundaramoorthi, Lecturer of Management, Olin Business School, Washington University in St. Louis, St. Louis, MO, United States of America, dsundaramoorthi@gmail.com" Aihong Wen" 2012"Classification and Regression Trees (CART) is a method of predictive analytics that attempts to predict or classify using a set of continuous or categorical variables. In this research, we investigate the predictability of a transportation portfolio with CART by simulating the portfolio performance. We analyze the daily performance of other markets in relation to the transportation portfolio."
