" 2 - Models for Designing Reliable Biofuel Supply Chains" Mohammad Marufuzzaman, Research Assistant, Mississippi State University, PO Box 9542, Industrial & Systems Engineering, Starkville, MS, 39759, United States of America, maruf237@gmail.com" Sandra Eksioglu" Xiaopeng Li" Jin Wang" 2013"We present a model to identify biorefinery locations which ensure a cost-efficient and reliable supply chain designs. This model takes into consideration the cost benefits and risks associated with locating biorefineries near intermodal hubs, such as, inland ports, sea ports and rail ramps. We developed an accelerated Benders decomposition approach to solve large instances of this problem. We validate the model on a case study using data from the southeast region of USA."
