" 3 - Predicting All-cause 30-day Patient Readmission" Issac Shams, Research Assistant, Wayne State University, 4815 4th Street, Detroit, MI, 48201, United States of America, er7671@wayne.edu" Kai Yang" Saeede Ajorlou" 2013"Based on the literature of risk prediction models, patient’s socioeconomic and diagnosis factors are related to the risk of readmission.In this paper we propose two patient-specific classification methods1)A multi-level logistic random regression and2)A bootstrapped random forest that can perform sampling at the patient level. We validate and compare our proposals with a large inpatient data set from four VA facilities in the state of Michigan."
