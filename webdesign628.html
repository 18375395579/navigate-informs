" 1 - The Cost-effectiveness of Herpes Zoster Vaccine" Mehdi Najafzadeh, PhD Student, University of British Columbia, 2545 West Broadway, Vancouver, BC, V6K 2E9, Canada, mehdin@interchange.ubc.ca" Eleni Galanis" David Patrick" Carlo Marra" 2007"The incidence and severity of herpes zoster and postherpetic neuralgia increase with age in association with a progressive decline in cell-mediated immunity to varicella-zoster virus (VZV). Vaccination against VZV decreases the incidence and severity of herpes zoster and postherpetic neuralgia among older adults. We used a discrete event simulation model to determine the cost-effectiveness of vaccinating against VZV in older adults in British Columba."
