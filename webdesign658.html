" 4 - Statistical Updating of Finite Element Model with Lamb Wave Sensing Data for Structural Damage Detection" Arda Vanli, College of Engineering, Florida State University, 2525 Pottsdamer St., Tallahassee, FL, 32310, United States of America, avanli@fsu.edu" Sungmoon Jung" 2012"This study proposes a new probabilistic model updating method used to improve the prediction capability of a finite element analysis (FEA) model of structural damages with experimental observations from a sensor network. A controlled damage experiment on a composite panel showed that the proposed model updating approach achieves a prediction accuracy that is superior to existing calibration approaches."
