" 2 - Dynamic Targeted Pricing in B2B Settings" Jonathan Zhang, Assistant Professor of Marketing, University of Washington, 547 Paccar Hall, Box 353226, Seattle, WA, 98195, United States of America, zaozao@uw.edu" Oded Nezter" Asim Ansari" 2013"We model the multifaceted impact of pricing decisions in B2B and show how a seller can develop optimal inter-temporal targeted pricing strategies, taking into account the latent B2B relationships. We model the B2B customer’s purchase decisions in an integrated hierarchical Bayesian framework, with a multivariate non-homogeneous hidden Markov model. We estimate our model on data from an industrial retailer, and find two latent relationship states - a “vigilant?state and a more “relaxed?state."
