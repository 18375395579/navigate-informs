" 4 - A Model to Quantify the Return on Information Assurance (ROIA)" Charley Tichenor, Operations Research Analyst, Defense Security Cooperation Agency, 201 12th St. South, Ste. 203, Arlington, VA, 22202, United States of America, charley.tichenor@dsca.mil" Ronald Greenfield" 2008"The return on investment (ROI) for any program is one indicator of the degree to which a program contributes to an organization’s strategic plan. It can help prioritize investments and can be used to help quantify an individual’s or team’s job performance. This paper presents a model to quantify the financial ROI of Information Assurance (ROIA). Potential users of the ROIA Model are encouraged to either use or modify this structure and populate the variables with their own data."
