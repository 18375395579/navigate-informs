" 1 - Cooperative ISR Utilizing Airborne Reconnaissance Platforms and Unmanned Aircraft Systems" Chase Murray, PhD Candidate, University at Buffalo, 308A Bell Hall, Department of Industrial and Systems Eng, Buffalo, NY, 14260-1900, United States of America, cmurray3@eng.buffalo.edu" Mark Karwan" 2008"A general modeling framework for dynamic re-routing of resources responding to battlespace changes (e.g. appearance of a new threat or loss of a resource) is presented. We demonstrate the model in the context of cooperative intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) missions involving multiple manned and unmanned aircraft systems. Examples and computational results are discussed."
