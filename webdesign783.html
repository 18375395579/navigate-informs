" 2 - Information Dissemination Delay in a Connected Vehicle Network Running on a Single Two-way Road " Lili Du, Assistant Professor, Illinois Institute of Technology, 3201 S. Dearborn St., Chicago, IL, 60616, United States of America, ldu3@iit.edu" Hoang Dao" 2013"This research proposes mathematical description models to capture information propagation delay in a connected vehicle network running on a two-way road. Intermittent wireless communication under dynamic traffic flow condition, and wireless communication interference among vehicles are integrated into the model development. The proposed models are validated are simulation experiments"
