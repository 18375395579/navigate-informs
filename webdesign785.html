" 2 - Improving Reverse Logistics Network for Medical Waste by Neural Networks and Genetic Algorithms" Samira Arabgol, Southern Illinois University Edwardsville, Industrial and Manufacturing Engineering, Edwardsville, IL, 62026, United States of America, sarabgo@siue.edu" Hoo Sang Ko" Sasan Hejrani" 2013"Prompt and proper management of medical wastes are critical due to the potential hazards to health and environment. This study aims at improving the performance of the reverse logistics network for medical waste management to minimize the risks. First, we predict the amount of waste generated at medical centers using an artificial neural network. Second, we optimize the distribution of the waste in the network using a genetic algorithm. The methodology is validated by an illustrative example."
