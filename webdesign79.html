The traditional indices of relationship fail to distinguish the dependence of singular models such as Marshall-Olkin and the exponential autoregressive bivariate distributions. The information measure of dependence is not defined. A weighted average of the mutual information indices for the singular and absolutely continuous parts provides a common metric for comparing the dependence of these models."
