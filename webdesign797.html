" 3 - Structural Estimation of Retail Demand Under Unobserved Out of Stocks" Marcelo Olivares, Assistant Professor, Columbia Business School, 3022 Broadway, Uris Hall #417, New York, NY, 10027, United States, molivares@columbia.edu" Eric Bradlow" Christian Terwiesch" Andres Musalem" Daniel Corsten" 2007"We develop a structural model to measure the effect of stock-outs on retail sales. We use a random coefficient logit model of consumer choice and estimate it using Bayesian methods. The methodology is tested using actual data on sales and availability of a product category (shampoo) collected from multiple stores of a Spanish retailer."
