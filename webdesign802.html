" 2 - A Network Flow Methodology to Estimate Empty Trips in Freight Transportation Models" Satish Ukkusuri, Associate Professor, Purdue University, 550 Stadium Mall Drive, G175F, West Lafayette, IN, 47906, United States of America, sukkusur@purdue.edu" Rodrigo Mesa-Arango" 2012"This research presents a novel methodology to estimate the number of empty trips useful for regional freight planning. This methodology is based on a time expanded network flow approach that captures behavioral and operational characteristics of the carriers. The model is formulated as a linear optimization problem that minimizes the system costs associated to truck trips. The model is validated with a commercial vehicle survey from Colombia."
