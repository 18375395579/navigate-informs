Harris Hall, Richmond, VA, 23284-3083, United States of America, xchen4@vcu.edu" Kai Wang" Kyoung-kuk Kim" Feng Yang" 2013"Stochastic kriging (SK) has been studied as a useful metamodeling tool for approximating the mean response surface implied by a stochastic simulation. We propose to extend the standard stochastic kriging in two directions, respectively, to enable SK to handle stochastic simulations involving both qualitative and quantitative decision variables and to upgrade SK with methods such as sectioning for better performance in predicting extreme quantiles and tail conditional expectations."
