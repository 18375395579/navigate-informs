" 2 - An Efficient Statistical Procedure for Multivariate Calibration of Sensor Arrays" Zongyu Geng, West Virginia University, P.O.Box 6070, Morgantown, United States of America, zygeng@gmail.com" Feng Yang" Minqi Li" Nianqiang Wu" 2013"One of the major challenges of calibrating a sensor array lies in the typically large samples required to estimate a high-quality multivariate calibration (MC) model, which functionally relates the array responses to the analyte concentrations. This work develops a multi-stage procedure to guide the optimal sampling in a sequential manner. The design optimization at each stage seeks to optimize the quality of the MC model, and is performed based on the new statistical inference method."
