" 4 - Modeling of Consolidation by Household for Emergency Evacuation Events" Ke Liu, Ph.D. Student, University of Maryland, 1173 Martin Hall, Dept. of Civil Engineering, College Park, MD, 20742, United States of America, ke.liu@fhwa.dot.gov" David Lovell" Peter Chang" 2008"Evacuation studies have grown in importance over the years.One effective method to evaluate different evacuation strategies is the use of simulation.In this study,we develop a computerized tool for modeling evacuation dynamics with household consolidation,and then incorporate it into a traffic-simulation software platform.This tool will allow a percentage of the population to consolidate as a family before they evacuate."
