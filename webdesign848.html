Alvarado, PhD Student, Texas A&M University, 3131 TAMU, College Station, TX, 77843, United States of America, michelle.mcgaha@gmail.com" Eduardo Perez" Tanisha Cotton" Lewis Ntaimo" 2012"Scheduling of chemotherapy treatments is a difficult task due to the cyclic nature of the prescribed treatment regimen, variability of treatment lengths, and patient acuity levels. We present a discrete event system specification (DEVS) simulation model integrated with a stochastic mixed integer programming model (SMIP) to schedule and manage patients in an oncology clinic. Preliminary results are presented from the management perspective."
