" 3 - The Foundations of Irregular Warfare (IW)" Dean Hartley, Principal, Hartley Consulting, 106 Windsong Ln, Oak Ridge, TN, 37830, United States of America, DSHartley3@comcast.net" Lee Lacy" 2012"IW operations are hard: they connect to the human domain, where our knowledge is weak. Recent conflicts have produced demands for models of IW. Ontologies provide a tool for representing IW. They specify vocabularies & semantic relationships. We show ontologies of IW actor, action, & environment concepts and state variables. They come from several sources. The semantics permit programmatic inferencing. The ontologies are useful for modeling, data interchange, interoperability, and V&V."
