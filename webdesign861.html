" 4 - An Optimization Model for Metabolic Pathways" J E Beasley, Brunel University, Mathematical Sciences, Uxbridge, United Kingdom, John.Beasley@brunel.ac.uk" F Planes" 2008"We present an integer-programming based optimisation model for metabolic pathways. Given a source compound, and a target compound, this model is capable of recovering the complete experimentally determined pathway reported in the literature. Computational results are reported for 40 metabolic pathways associated with E. Coli."
