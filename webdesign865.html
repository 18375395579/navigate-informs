" 3 - Bayesian Mortgage Default Models and Reliability Analysis" Refik Soyer, Professor, Department of Decision Sciences, The George Washington University, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, soyer@gwu.edu" Feng Xu" 2008"The Bayesian point of view is rather new in the mortgage default literature. In this talk we present some recent Bayesian mortgage models for analysis of aggregate and individual loan level data. In so doing, we also discuss how these models can be used for loan maintenance using a decision theoretic approach."
