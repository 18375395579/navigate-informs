" 3 - Robotic Cells with Parallel Machines and Multiple Dual Gripper Robots" Neil Geismar, Assistant Professor, Prairie View A&M University, PO Box 638, Prairie View, TX, United States, ngeismar@yahoo.com" Michael Pinedo" Chelliah Sriskandarajah" 2006"We consider different actual implementations of robotic cells with multiple dual gripper robots.
