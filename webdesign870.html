" 1 - Allocating CDC’s HIV Prevention Funds" Katherine Hicks, RTI International, 3040 Cornwallis Rd, RTP, NC, 27709, United States of America, khicks@rti.org" Arielle Lasry" Stephanie Sansom" Vladislav Uzunangelov" 2008"The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) funds most HIV prevention programs in the U.S. To guide CDC funding decisions, we developed a resource allocation model aimed at minimizing new infections over five years. New infections are calculated using a dynamic compartmental model structured by infection status, risk group, gender and race. We discuss this model, with a focus on challenges in applying optimization models to public health issues."
