" 4 - Bayesian Analysis of Markov Modulated Queues" Refik Soyer, Professor of Decision Sciences & Statistics, The George Washington University, 2201 G Street, NW, Washington, DC, 20052, United States of America, soyer@gwu.edu" Suleyman Ozekici" Joshua Landon" 2013"We consider a Markovian queueing model where both customer arrival process and the service process are modulated by an external environmental process.
