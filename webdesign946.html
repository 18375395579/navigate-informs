" 1 - Incorporating Client Preferences into Preventive Care Network Design" Lijie Song, Zayed University, Dubai, Dubai, United Arab Emirates, song.lijie@me.com" Yue Zhang" Vedat Verter" Beste Kucukyazici" 2013"We present a study on preventive care network design. We first estimate a population level participation function, then use stated preference discrete choice modeling to explore clients?preference concerning facility attributes. Next we apply results from econometrics analyses to an empirically based simulation model, and use meta-heuristics to demonstrate how to improve preventive care network performance by incorporating client preferences"
