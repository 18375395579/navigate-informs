" 2 - Simulation Optimization of the Screening and Treatment Decisions for Prostate Cancer" Daniel Underwood, North Carolina State University, 111 Lampe Drive, 373 Daniels Hall, Campus Box 7906, Raleigh, NC, 27695, United States of America, daniel.underwood@ncsu.edu" Jingyu Zhang" Brian Denton" 2012"We present a simulation-optimization model for finding near optimal strategies for prostate cancer screening, including decisions related to screening by prostate-specific antigen (PSA) and treatment by radical prostatectomy. We discuss a customized implementation of COMPASS for solving our problem. Results from the COMPASS algorithm are compared to results from a previously published method based on a genetic algorithm. We present results, based on longitudinal data for a large North American population, to study the influence of personalized risk factors on individualized PSA-based screening policies."
