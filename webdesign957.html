" 3 - Estimation of Long Queues using Mobile Sensors for Traffic Signals " Peng Hao, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, 108 8th Street, Troy, NY, 12180, United States of America, haop@rpi.edu" Jeff Ban" 2012"We develop methods to estimate queue lengths of signalized intersections using mobile data when the queue exceeds the upstream data collection location.
