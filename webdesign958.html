" 4 - The Impact of Delay on Risk Preferences" Ayse Onculer, ESSEC, Ave. Bernard Hirsch, Cergy, FR, 95021, France, onculer@essec.fr" Enrico Diecidue" Mohammed Abdellaoui" 2008"This study is an elicitation of the probability weighting function over time. By adding a temporal component to probability transformation, we can capture the impact of delay on risk preferences. Our experimental results imply that such a model has a better predictive power than existing models of risky intertemporal choice."
