" 1 - Introduction to Some Image Processing Problems" Peihua Qiu, Professor, University of Minnesota, Minneapolis, MN, United States of America, qiuxx008@umn.edu" 2013"Image data becomes more and more popular. In this talk, I will introduce some basic image processing problems, including edge and feature detection, and image registration, that are essential for statistical monitoring of image data. I will also describe the related statistical methods for handling these image processing problems based on jump regression analysis."
