" 1 - Time Series Search and Retrieval for Printer Diagnosis and Repair" John Handley, Member of Research Staff, Xerox Corporation, 800 Phillips Road, MS 128 27E, Webster, NY, 14580, United States of America, john.handley@xerox.com" Sankar Virdhagriswaran" 2008"Many printers are connected to the Internet and send time-dependent data comprising sensor values and usage statistics to a data warehouse. When diagnosing faults within a machine, service engineers find it valuable to plot a time series of a particular variable and search a database for machines with similar behavior. We present a system for search and retrieval that uses global similarity based on dynamic time warping and local similarity based on a modified Smith-Waterman metric."
